<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽英伦客家乡亲会 年度烧烤聚餐欢乐多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2-2-1Hakka-2--e1566578849886.jpg" />
<h6>经文处徐佑典处长(立排中着红衫)与客家会宋玉琴会长(立排左二)及广东同乡会参加者合影。(刘景烨/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年08月24日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨布鲁克林镇报导）8月17日是“纽英伦<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6.md#1">客家</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A1%E4%BA%B2%E4%BC%9A.md#1">乡亲会</a>”的年度<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A7%E7%83%A4.md#1">烧烤</a>聚餐。纽英伦地区客家人、广东同乡会及各侨胞组织共约140位乡亲来到布鲁克林镇的Larz Anderson公园，在和煦微风中烧烤聚会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11473554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11473554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2-2-2Hakka-2-e1566578637885.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11473554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2-2-2Hakka-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11473554" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6.md#1">客家</a>人热情款待宾客，准备了丰盛可口的佳肴。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11473557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11473557" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2-2-3Hakka-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11473557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2-2-3Hakka-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11473557" class="wp-caption-text">曾政明一如往年主掌烤肉。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>公园湖边不远处的亭中炊烟袅袅，香气缭绕。侨胞们皆远道开车而来，共享快乐时光。曾政泉、曾政明兄弟一如往年主掌烤肉，陈裕逢为为烤架上的玉米涂上沙茶酱，还特别提醒“辣的”。</p>
<p>餐桌上不仅有烤肉，还有多样乡亲们自制的菜式，尤其绿豆汤、龟苓膏甜品很受欢迎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11473559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11473559" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2-2-4Hakka-e1566578798143.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11473559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2-2-4Hakka-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11473559" class="wp-caption-text">“纽英伦客家<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A1%E4%BA%B2%E4%BC%9A.md#1">乡亲会</a>”的年度<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A7%E7%83%A4.md#1">烧烤</a>聚餐大合照。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长徐佑典、杜丽云夫妇到场同乐。徐佑典称赞，客家会的烤肉会食物“最棒”，气氛热闹，令他与夫人印象深刻。“今天真的很特别，哪里特别呢？目前为止还没下雨。”他说，令侨胞们大笑。</p>
<p>今年的客家会烤肉日天气稍阴，但气温宜人，而前两年都是雨天。</p>
<p>客家会会长宋玉琴说，明年是该会10周年，因而将在3月15日“天穿日”举办10周年庆祝会，希望各位共同参与同乐。◇</p>
