<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金峰乡产业升级活化 要与世界做朋友</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/bcbfb4afa51bca6df060f8df9dfe5425-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东县金峰乡产业升级活化计划成果展。（龙芳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年08月30日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）台东县<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E5%B3%B0%E4%B9%A1.md#1">金峰乡</a>在地农产丰富，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>县金峰乡30日在台东TT Style波浪屋16及17号店铺举行“红金宝赞金峰”产业体验升级活化计划成果展，透过微电影播放以及现场静态展示，可以看到红藜、小米、洛神等在地农产制作成的各种食品，还有排湾族工艺品等，成果多样而丰富。</p>
<p>“红金宝赞金峰”产业体验升级活化计划是经济部中小企业处2017年度地方产业发展基金补助为期３年的计划，以<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E5%B3%B0%E4%B9%A1.md#1">金峰乡</a>的红藜、小米、洛神等<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E7%89%B9%E4%BA%A7.md#1">农特产</a>品为主轴，辅导23家的特色业者升级，来推动金峰乡特色农特产品行销。县长饶庆铃表示，原住民文化是台东非常重要的资产，台东县希望以原住民文化跟世界交朋友。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11488063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11488063" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/5f47e54af07ea7183e087a5f5968fd49-450x321.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11488063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/5f47e54af07ea7183e087a5f5968fd49-450x321.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11488063" class="wp-caption-text">台东县金峰乡产业升级活化计划成果展，在地业者端出自家产品。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>金峰乡长蒋争光强调，受辅导的业者表示，藉由此项计划协助空间改造、包装设计后，不但增加了游客进到店内及购买商品的意愿，并有助于提升品牌意象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11488064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11488064" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/94c040ab123d797c7a73f1577f577565-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11488064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/94c040ab123d797c7a73f1577f577565-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11488064" class="wp-caption-text">台东县金峰乡生产的“洛神花啤酒”。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县府原民处指出，红金宝计划共协助了23家业者进行改造，透过了解他们经营的现况及需求，协助做空间改善、特色商品开发、通路行销、品牌意象建置等。成果展中民众只要在“红金宝赞金峰”粉丝页打卡按赞，即可免费兑换金峰专属“洛神花啤酒”一罐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11488061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11488061" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/cb37256c5f93a514948439151216b89f-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11488061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/cb37256c5f93a514948439151216b89f-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11488061" class="wp-caption-text">台东县金峰乡产业升级活化计划展现成果。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
