<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义烙画创作展 地检署展出至4/19</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/e666e3cd77ddaa383b7c63872b6537ee-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>受颁感谢状的艺术家与长官合影，左2为嘉义市美术协会理事长庄玉明负责本次展出，左3为王蚁益老师。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年03月22日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89%E5%9C%B0%E6%A3%80%E7%BD%B2.md#1">嘉义地检署</a>推广艺文活动，打开大门让艺术住进来，除减少民众对地检署严肃冰冷刻板印象，更能善化人心。嘉义市美术协会本次受邀举办“烙人抵嘉&#8211;<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%99%E7%94%BB.md#1">烙画</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9B%E4%BD%9C%E5%B1%95.md#1">创作展</a>”，22日开幕茶会，展期至4月19日在地检署1楼大厅，60件作品，上班日上午8时至下午5时半开放。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%99%E7%94%BB.md#1">烙画</a>艺术目前在嘉义可谓蓬勃发展，嘉义地区推广烙画自民国98年起迄今，十年有成，从烙画家展现艺术境界及工艺品的层次，可领会艺术家内在的修为和气质表现，欢迎各界人士踊跃参观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11132552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11132552" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/7ea93cddd36cb4b2cebad16d4e36339b-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11132552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/7ea93cddd36cb4b2cebad16d4e36339b-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11132552" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89%E5%9C%B0%E6%A3%80%E7%BD%B2.md#1">嘉义地检署</a>新任检察长缪卓然欢迎各界人士踊跃参观嘉义烙画<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9B%E4%BD%9C%E5%B1%95.md#1">创作展</a>。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次展览系由嘉义市美术协会烙画部烙画艺术家王蚁益、林姮惠、陈柏欣、李泓慧、范淑惠、吴素月及王孟涵等7人参展之外，更邀请林滋瑛、赖士魁、廖淑芬等40余位烙画名家及民雄文教基金会、嘉义社区大学、新营社区大学烙画班共同展出，展出作品计60件，件件精湛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11132553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11132553" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/dd3b5e1da19fadc50ee0f69b390ec731-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11132553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/dd3b5e1da19fadc50ee0f69b390ec731-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11132553" class="wp-caption-text">艺文界人士与现任嘉义市政府地政处处长、也是前嘉义市文化局局长饶嘉博（左5）叙旧合影。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>烙画家王蚁益老师在嘉义社区大学的首发耕耘，用对的方法作推广，所以目前在嘉义地区与烙画艺术推动有关的艺术家大多是由嘉义社区大学培育而成的种子教师，以认真教学的态度在传承这项技艺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11132555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11132555" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/182ad0bc1c1296f4a50592dd85c2b350-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11132555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/182ad0bc1c1296f4a50592dd85c2b350-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11132555" class="wp-caption-text">新港文教基金会董事长陈政鸿（右2起）、地检署纪录科长侯建彰、太平村村长等人体验烧烙作画，并展示作品合影。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>王蚁益表示，烙画艺术在嘉义地区从萌芽到知名度打开，处处留下努力过的痕迹，世界各地的网友不知台湾的地理位置，但很喜欢台湾的烙画作品，烙画一上了网站一小时内就有世界各国近500人点击来观赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11132556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11132556" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/357a7ee24a645c3b24f243283fa326d9-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11132556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/357a7ee24a645c3b24f243283fa326d9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11132556" class="wp-caption-text">烙画家王蚁益老师在嘉义社区大学的首发耕耘，用对的方法作推广，如今种子教师不少，王蚁益的作品〈回家〉。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>烙画亦称火笔画、烙花、烫画等，属民间艺术之一，初始以铁针加热为工具，至近代电力控制设备、热熔丝式烙笔和可更换笔头式烙笔等的开发，使烙画创作者更能得心应手。烙画素材可遍及各种木材、竹子、皮料、干葫芦、布料、纸类等。其乃运用碳化原理，掌握温度的高低、加热时间的长短等，促使媒材产生色泽深浅的变化，再加以点、线、面等的变化，成就丰富的层次与色调。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11132557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11132557" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/25459e8c9b9c84bf5f1b004471748075-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11132557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/25459e8c9b9c84bf5f1b004471748075-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11132557" class="wp-caption-text">60件烧烙作品件件精彩，不少人来看画。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不论烙画作品以何种纹样展现，其线条柔和、层次分明，能予人一种陶冶性情、升华意境之感。烙画具有双重特性，不但可以赏心悦目展现艺术境界（如画作品），还可以加诸于实物之上另其实用（如工艺品），另一项有趣的是烙画可以利用木板的色彩和纹理，透过巧思及想像力，将色彩和纹理化为艺术形象，如为水、为山、为云、为山峰或以形似的物像作为搭配，成就ㄧ幅具自然品味的作品，而达到“唯一”的效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11132559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11132559" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/efd5654c9f911a3e32067d9c3aae99b9-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11132559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/efd5654c9f911a3e32067d9c3aae99b9-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11132559" class="wp-caption-text">作者钟瑛与其槐木烧烙作品〈盛装的鲁凯族少女〉。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>烙画系以“温度”创造温馨质感的艺术，本次展出的烙画作品，虽无过多的色彩，却能把画面表现得淋漓尽致，尤其结合媒材的特色，展现古朴典雅的浮雕效果，形成独特的艺术风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11132560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11132560" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/ac8c5ae71d786032b4c09178e034e5b9-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11132560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/ac8c5ae71d786032b4c09178e034e5b9-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11132560" class="wp-caption-text">作者李泓慧的二件作品，左面梧桐木媒材作品〈童颜〉，右面用松木板凳媒材作品为〈我可以玩玩水吗?〉。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11132561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11132561" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/5be545ee8f02117bdcb0d409a8cfa8db-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11132561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/5be545ee8f02117bdcb0d409a8cfa8db-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11132561" class="wp-caption-text">贵宾在地检署展场大合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
