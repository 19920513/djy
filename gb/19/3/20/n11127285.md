<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《天空之城》爸爸合体《监狱医生》挑战反派</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/20190320-LINE-TV-001-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《监狱医生》主演（左起）崔元英、金炳哲、南宫珉、权娜拉。（LINE TV提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年03月20日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬报导）在韩国创下高收视率以及高口碑的韩剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E7%A9%BA%E4%B9%8B%E5%9F%8E.md#1">天空之城</a>》，剧中两位爸爸<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%82%B3%E5%93%B2.md#1">金炳哲</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%94%E5%85%83%E8%8B%B1.md#1">崔元英</a>再度在韩剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%91%E7%8B%B1%E5%8C%BB%E7%94%9F.md#1">监狱医生</a>》合体演出，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%82%B3%E5%93%B2.md#1">金炳哲</a>这次还挑战诠释超强反派，要对抗<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E5%AE%AB%E7%8F%89.md#1">南宫珉</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11127335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11127335" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/20190320-LINE-TV-003.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11127335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/20190320-LINE-TV-003-600x904.jpg" alt="金炳哲《监狱医生》" width="450" b="904" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11127335" class="wp-caption-text">金炳哲在《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%91%E7%8B%B1%E5%8C%BB%E7%94%9F.md#1">监狱医生</a>》中饰演反派。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>延续了《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E7%A9%BA%E4%B9%8B%E5%9F%8E.md#1">天空之城</a>》中车民赫总是不可一世、高高在上的模样，金炳哲再度挑战了超级精英，诠释医生家世出身、金字塔上层的先民植一角。读本时可以看出，他饰演自尊心超强，时而展现愤怒的语气神情或手势，看到了几分车民赫的身影。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E5%AE%AB%E7%8F%89.md#1">南宫珉</a>表示这次在剧中饰演的罗以济，并不是什么善良的角色，是为了和邪恶势力对抗，而选择了更恶劣的办法去报仇。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11127334" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11127334" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/20190320-LINE-TV-002-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11127334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/20190320-LINE-TV-002-2-600x395.jpg" alt="南宫珉《监狱医生》" width="600" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11127334" class="wp-caption-text">南宫珉在《监狱医生》中饰演医生。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《监狱医生》由南宫珉、权娜拉、金炳哲、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%94%E5%85%83%E8%8B%B1.md#1">崔元英</a>等人主演。剧中描述出身杂牌大学的罗以济，去掉监狱称王的医疗科长后，成为新的医疗科长的成长剧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11127337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11127337" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/20190320-LINE-TV-004.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11127337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/20190320-LINE-TV-004-600x338.jpg" alt="金炳哲《天空之城》" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11127337" class="wp-caption-text">金炳哲在《天空之城》饰演双胞胎儿子的爸爸。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11127344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11127344" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/20190320-LINE-TV-005.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11127344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/20190320-LINE-TV-005-600x338.jpg" alt="《天空之城》崔元英" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11127344" class="wp-caption-text">崔元英在《天空之城》饰演黄治英一角。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
