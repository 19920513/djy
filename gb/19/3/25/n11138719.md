<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>时光倒流 回放老上海美味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/IMG_6418-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>阿拉上海餐厅给您一桌满满的老上海美味。（湾区中餐馆，阿拉上海提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年03月25日讯】文：李翔?大纪元<br />
前阵子网络都在玩一个“十年”的游戏，对比现在和十年前，变化在哪里，没变又在哪里？阿拉上海餐厅（I-Shanghai Delight）带你倒流时光，记忆深处<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E4%B8%8A%E6%B5%B7.md#1">老上海</a>的味道依旧很浓……</p>
<p>作为旧金山湾区一家好吃又不贵的上海菜餐馆，每每开门营业必有众多食客捧场，门外小等稀松平常。<figure id="attachment_11139011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11139011" style="width: 520px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/c54ac4150df771211993bf57bc2ee8ec.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/c54ac4150df771211993bf57bc2ee8ec.jpg" alt="" width="520" b="388" class="size-full wp-image-11139011" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11139011" class="wp-caption-text">阿拉上海餐厅Fremont店。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%8A%E6%B5%B7%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">湾区上海餐馆</a>，阿拉上海提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>上海菜讲究咸淡适中，不油不腻，淡雅清爽，微甜不辣。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%AC%BC%E5%8C%85.md#1">小笼包</a>（Pork Dumplings）、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E7%85%8E%E5%8C%85.md#1">生煎包</a>（The Fried Pork Buns）、白斩鸡（The Plain Boiled Chicken）都是阿拉上海食客必点的热门佳肴。</p>
<p>到目前为止，小编吃过的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%AC%BC%E5%8C%85.md#1">小笼包</a>不说有上千个，也有上百个。一个优秀的小笼包应该晶莹剔透，皮薄嫩软，包子褶不大不小，稀疏得当，热腾腾出锅，汤汁鲜美，肉馅丰厚。卖相端庄大方，不媚俗不轻浮。<figure id="attachment_11139013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11139013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/62ad3996a9c8865e5701406cb4665120.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/62ad3996a9c8865e5701406cb4665120-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11139013" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11139013" class="wp-caption-text">晶莹剔透的小笼包。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%AD%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">湾区中餐馆</a>，阿拉上海提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E7%85%8E%E5%8C%85.md#1">生煎包</a>，沾油煎了的包子皮，有点黑但是脆脆的，咬起来咯吱咯吱，肉馅又大又厚，蘸点肉汤醋汁，三下五除二吃完一小盘。阿拉上海餐厅6个9.95美元，价格实惠。<figure id="attachment_11139014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11139014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/0d80d5d092303dd466a9fd56d633a9d4.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/0d80d5d092303dd466a9fd56d633a9d4-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11139014" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11139014" class="wp-caption-text">皮脆馅多的生煎包。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%AD%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">湾区中餐馆</a>，阿拉上海提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>白斩鸡，鸡只的选取极其重要，要不大不小，不肥不瘦，肥了皮厚油腻，瘦则骨多无肉。阿拉上海的白斩鸡，皮黄肉白，配上独家调制的姜醋汁，美味只增不减，好吃到吮指。半只16.95元的份量已经很足。<figure id="attachment_11139015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11139015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/ab44f2a061d85fdf4bd5e58dd36fb8e3.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/ab44f2a061d85fdf4bd5e58dd36fb8e3-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11139015" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11139015" class="wp-caption-text">白斩鸡。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%8A%E6%B5%B7%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">湾区上海餐馆</a>，阿拉上海提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>桂花糖藕（Sweet Lotus Roots with Sticky Rice），莲藕富含铁、钙、植物蛋白和维生素，有补气益血，增强人体免疫，美容养颜的作用。淡淡的桂花味，香甜软糯，一盘7.95元。<figure id="attachment_11139016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11139016" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/e131ff37650aabb979ea04abca716adf.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/e131ff37650aabb979ea04abca716adf-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11139016" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11139016" class="wp-caption-text">桂花糖藕。（湾区上海餐馆，阿拉上海提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>小笼包、生煎包都那么好吃，慢火熬煮的砂锅白菜蛋饺自然也不会差，汤面泛着油珠，咬上一口，鲜美的滋味不止来自高汤，还有蛋饺。砂锅白菜蛋饺（Egg Dumplings with Cabbage）一锅13.95元。<figure id="attachment_11139018" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11139018" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/6b4d38cd769dc2305366a98a5710694d.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/6b4d38cd769dc2305366a98a5710694d-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11139018" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11139018" class="wp-caption-text">砂锅白菜蛋饺。（湾区上海餐馆，阿拉上海提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11139021" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11139021" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/cea11b4215201d9e03040c84a5c30301.jpeg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/cea11b4215201d9e03040c84a5c30301-600x447.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="447" class="size-large wp-image-11139021" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11139021" class="wp-caption-text">阿拉上海餐厅沙加缅度店（Sacramento）。（湾区中餐馆，阿拉上海提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>这充满回忆的味道，大概十年都不会变吧！#</p>
