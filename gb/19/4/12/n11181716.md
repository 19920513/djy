<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宣信附幼环保市集 让孩子学到表达及沟通</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/c62716d5b401c5b53336dfa3f6f895e8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>校长黄金木(左)说，学校用瓦楞纸板自己制作动画小小兵。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年04月12日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A3%E4%BF%A1%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">宣信国小</a>附设幼儿园，于12日举办筑梦市集活动，邀请社区家长及幼儿参与，呈现统整课程教学成果，并以市集<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%93%E9%AA%8C.md#1">体验</a>方式，环境保护为主题，26个摊位，在校生、幼儿园校友在学有专精的教师安排下，玩得很尽兴。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A3%E4%BF%A1%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">宣信国小</a>校长黄金木表示，今天大家所看到的都不是为了办这个活动特别制作的，都是课程教学里面所产出的东西，也就是老师带领孩子做出来的，是由上学期开始到现在课程教学的展现，做分享。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11181718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181718" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/08c99cb86fc7b71754ae692e02061b99-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11181718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/08c99cb86fc7b71754ae692e02061b99-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181718" class="wp-caption-text">教师的价值在此，虽然在家也可以教，但附幼专业师资、专业课程，孩子发展更好。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>举办这个活动让社区民众了解宣信是很用心的在经营，老师很用心。有些东西看起来不是那么完美、不是那么完整，因为这些是小朋友的作品。宣信国小学区含盖10个里，由幼儿园开始在学区内好好经营，可造就未来国家所用的人材。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11181723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181723" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/412d9c537c6c2d3412f4767ca0cfac5d-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11181723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/412d9c537c6c2d3412f4767ca0cfac5d-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181723" class="wp-caption-text">校长黄金木参观小朋友自己涂鸭，一摊7个客人，生意相当好，关主很有成就感。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>成果展原本在教室，显得太拥挤，搬到户外，更大空间让更多人来参看，在过程中学童学到表达及沟通，自己制作的东西，引导别人也来<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%93%E9%AA%8C.md#1">体验</a>，独乐乐不如众乐乐，在其中享受成就感，提升心灵层次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11181724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181724" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/cc0625cd4d54cba4fb03549bab5b7135-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11181724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/cc0625cd4d54cba4fb03549bab5b7135-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181724" class="wp-caption-text">蛇梯棋摊位是小朋友自己设计，掷骰子决定走几步，是习得带得走的环保行动能力。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>何老师说，宣信附幼有2大班2中班1小班，每班30人，共150个学童。每学年的下学期都会办一次成果展，今年做更大把它移到户外，邀社区小朋友一起来玩，这一次比较特别的是小班的学童也自己当关主。</p>
<p>活动过程由中、大班学童担任市集小老板，与民众分享爱乡爱土爱地球的体验活动。大班结合绿能行动及参与“咱粮食堂计划”诱发出一系列爱植物、爱地球的主题“绿食生活”及“和植物做朋友”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11181726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181726" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1b0ffbc2f7bf8af6d22b7ef6777bda41-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11181726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1b0ffbc2f7bf8af6d22b7ef6777bda41-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181726" class="wp-caption-text">变装拍照区，戴领巾的关主负责拍照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学童将学习内化后，发挥创意思考想像，延伸出市集的各式体验区，如：留下脚印(打卡)、为你种一棵树、绿行动蛇梯棋、创意植物拼贴、种植体验……等精彩有趣的体验区活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11181727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181727" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/703cc29e0c54551ffcccd6a6de5ecd26-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11181727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/703cc29e0c54551ffcccd6a6de5ecd26-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181727" class="wp-caption-text">不少社区家长带小小孩来参加，一起体验宣信附幼的快乐童年。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中班从认识校园与社区发展出“向左走向右走”主题，在校园里艺起玩创意宣信熊、迷宫寻宝；走入社区巡礼~喜爱儿童福利中心的打地鼠，再自创童玩市集战斗陀螺PK擂台、梦想扭蛋机，仿佛进入了一场奇幻旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11181728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181728" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/934e4b3d6e712896c980f0ca322749f1-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11181728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/934e4b3d6e712896c980f0ca322749f1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181728" class="wp-caption-text">创意植物拼贴。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小班的学童，则从认识自我开始发展出“哇熊赞”的主题，小小孩子设计有趣的活动如:表情变变变、身体翻翻卡变装秀，让社区的小小孩们也能玩得很开心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11181730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181730" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/96995157fc01e309e5323733a9582ffa-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11181730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/96995157fc01e309e5323733a9582ffa-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181730" class="wp-caption-text">七手八脚依手印脚印依序到终点，由关主在手中盖印，就完成闯关活动。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小小市集的活动，让孩子们踏出教室的范畴、和社区接轨，让每个孩子做自己、勇敢站上梦想的舞台！不少社区家长带小小孩来参加一起体验宣信附幼的快乐童年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11181731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181731" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/6b30b590dd8576f222130c5b2fbcae50-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11181731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/6b30b590dd8576f222130c5b2fbcae50-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181731" class="wp-caption-text">手持塑胶蛋，走过包括独木桥在内的各式关卡，相当专心才能过关。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11181732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181732" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/03cdf43bd0f133ef313a6a0c3dfdeecb-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11181732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/03cdf43bd0f133ef313a6a0c3dfdeecb-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181732" class="wp-caption-text">套圈圈活动，参加人数及观看人数最多。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
