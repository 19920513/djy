<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>来意大利休憩片刻 美味就在眼前</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年04月22日讯】文：百合?美食专栏作家<br />
意大利文的Pausa就是Pause，也就是暂停的意思。餐厅的主人Steve Ugur和Giuliani希望人们在每日忙碌的步伐中，能够暂时停下来休憩一下，品尝他们的美食。这一家餐厅荣登2019年的平价米其林，今天特地来尝尝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11203674" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11203674" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Front-Door.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11203674 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Front-Door-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11203674" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">湾区意大利餐馆</a>Pausa Bar &amp; Cookery。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>餐厅坐落在圣马刁县的市区内，餐厅布置得舒服雅致，左边有一个长长的吧台，餐厅内收藏着几百瓶名酒，墙壁上挂着一条条的粗绳子做布置，走进走廊看到墙壁上的照片，都是意大利海边晒面条，才懂得他布置的意思。这一家八成是以面食类做主打，果不其然，见着火炉内出来了一个热腾腾的手工Pizza，看了就令人流口水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11203675" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11203675" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/The-restaruant.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11203675" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/The-restaruant-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11203675" class="wp-caption-text">雅致的内部装潢，墙上一条条的绳子代表晒面条的意思。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E8%85%8C%E8%82%89.md#1">意大利腌肉</a>和烤饼</h2>
<p>看着他们的菜单与很多餐厅都不太一样，我们先叫一道意大利最有名的腌肉：Salumi。十分特别的是他们自己特制的烤饼Tigella，要你用刀子从中间割开，有点像英式松饼（English muffin），又很像中国人的褡裢火烧。中间加上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E8%85%8C%E8%82%89.md#1">意大利腌肉</a>，以及特制的腌菜、手工芥末酱，好像在吃三明治一样的吃法，从来没吃过这样的口味，还真是好吃！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11203677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11203677" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Butchers-Board.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11203677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Butchers-Board-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11203677" class="wp-caption-text">硅谷意大利腌肉（Salumi）和烤饼。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他的沙拉也不错，里面几块烤牛肉，混合生菜、芝麻叶、鹰嘴豆、烤甜菜、小胡萝卜、南瓜、抱子甘蓝、柑橘、开心果、百里香和意大利乳清干酪醋汁，又香又嫩。</p>
<h2>披萨和意大利面</h2>
<p>既然这家店是面类有名，那么我们当然要叫叫披萨和意大利面。我叫了这款比萨Pizza &#8211; house made sausage, house made mozzarella, seasonal mushrooms, butternut squash, truffle buffalo ricotta, chives。有自家的香肠、马苏里拉、时令蘑菇、冬南瓜、松露水牛乳清干酪、韭菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11203679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11203679" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/House-made-sausage-pizza.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11203679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/House-made-sausage-pizza-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11203679" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E6%8A%AB%E8%90%A8.md#1">硅谷披萨</a>（House made sausage pizza）。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最值得一提的就是面点，它的颜色是番茄红，和红番茄一起混合制作，这是在别家都没有吃过，让我们啧啧称奇。Pasta &amp; Entress – tomato malloreddus yellowfoot mushrooms, green garlic, pea tendrils, snap peas, fava beans, stracciatella Dessert。有番茄、黄脚蘑菇、绿色大蒜、豌豆卷须、豌豆、蚕豆和意大利冰淇淋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11203681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11203681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Tomato-malloreddus.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11203681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Tomato-malloreddus-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11203681" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E9%9D%A2.md#1">硅谷意大利面</a>，用番茄制作（Tomato malloreddus）。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>巧克力杏仁蛋糕</h2>
<p>最后当然少不了甜点，不加面粉的巧克力杏仁蛋糕（Flourless Chocolate Almond Cake），不知道是怎么做的？旁边加上一球意大利冰淇淋，特别好吃！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11203682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11203682" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Flourless-Chocolate-almond-Cake.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11203682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Flourless-Chocolate-almond-Cake-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11203682" class="wp-caption-text">巧克力杏仁蛋糕（Flourless Chocolate almond Cake）。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>甜甜圈</h2>
<p>另外，侍者又建议了这一道Bomboloni，其实就是甜甜圈（Doughnut Hole），加上调配后的料理，像是模特儿穿上了新衣裳。水牛意大利乳清干酪甜甜圈，加上肉桂糖、苹果蜜饯、巧克力榛子酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11203684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11203684" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Bomboloni.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11203684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Bomboloni-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11203684" class="wp-caption-text">特制甜甜圈（Bomboloni）。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这一家餐厅十分值得来，因为在市区所以停车可能不太方便，必须停在公共停车场，但是为了吃美食多走几步也是应该的。#</p>
