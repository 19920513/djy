<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国际同济日 竹苗区举办才艺颁奖．孝亲传爱暨反毒活动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/6680c47eaade883b6e54f3a57e0833ce-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>竹苗区区主席何月梅（左二）感谢建功国小合唱团精彩演唱。右一为竹苗区区秘书长郭雅蕙。（林宝云／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年04月07日讯】（大纪元记者林宝云台湾新竹报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E5%90%8C%E6%B5%8E%E6%97%A5.md#1">国际同济日</a>为全球同济会员每年同步进行儿童与社区的社会服务活动。响应国际同济日，国际同济会台湾总会<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%B9%E8%8B%97%E5%8C%BA.md#1">竹苗区</a>4月7日下午，在新竹市府综合大礼堂举行竹苗区儿童<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8D%E8%89%BA.md#1">才艺</a>比赛颁奖典礼及传爱孝亲家庭推广暨紫锥花<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E6%AF%92%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">反毒活动</a>。</p>
<p>温馨五月母亲节即将到来，同济会特别办孝亲家庭推广传爱，邀请建功国小合唱团演唱-我想念你妈妈、勇气之歌等三首歌为活动揭开序幕。活动中邀家长及小朋友上台，小朋友以实际行动奉茶及捶背感谢父母的付出，希望小朋友爱要及时，行孝更不能等。并鼓励在爸妈耳边说感恩悄悄话，场面温馨感人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11169383" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11169383" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/9a8b075dbe78db99041b511c9ee16102-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11169383" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/9a8b075dbe78db99041b511c9ee16102-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11169383" class="wp-caption-text">孝亲传爱小朋友以实际行动奉茶。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>紫锥花<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E6%AF%92%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">反毒活动</a>由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%B9%E8%8B%97%E5%8C%BA.md#1">竹苗区</a>紫锥花运动（校园反毒反暴力）推广委员会主任曾慧敏宣导。她向现场的小朋友说明紫锥花运动由来，并希望小朋友用坚毅的勇气对毒品说不，也希望小朋友如莲花出污泥而不染。曾慧敏告诉小朋友毒品的种类很多并 制成泡泡糖、巧克力等各式漂亮包装，所以不要随便拿陌生人给的东西。活动中进行有奖征答及鼓励小朋友向父母以誓言承诺拒绝毒品、尊重生命，永不吸毒，永不自杀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11169384" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11169384" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/ec44ca4a15c2cf74e417a9297514e4f8-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11169384" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/ec44ca4a15c2cf74e417a9297514e4f8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11169384" class="wp-caption-text">紫锥花反毒活动进行有奖征答。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3月3日国际同济会台湾总会竹苗区在新竹市东门国小举行的儿童<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8D%E8%89%BA.md#1">才艺</a>竞赛，亦选在7日颁奖，由竹苗区区主席何月梅及各承办会会长颁发前五名及佳作。许多父母纷纷上前抢拍镜头，为小朋友抓住这荣耀的一刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11169385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11169385" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/86aacc30a7783e0c0b3fe0ca7891ba51-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11169385" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/86aacc30a7783e0c0b3fe0ca7891ba51-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11169385" class="wp-caption-text">同济会竹苗区举办<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E5%90%8C%E6%B5%8E%E6%97%A5.md#1">国际同济日</a>。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11169386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11169386" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/98897b79a2367b63209b2ce370a3970e-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11169386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/98897b79a2367b63209b2ce370a3970e-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11169386" class="wp-caption-text">父母为小朋友抢拍镜头。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
