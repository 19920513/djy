<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>布碌崙7大道公寓6级大火 华裔住户受灾</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年04月04日讯】（大纪元记者蔡溶纽约报导）3日下午4点45分，华人聚居的布碌崙日落公园附近一栋54户的公寓大楼发生火警，当天风力强劲，风助火势，大火迅速蔓延并升级到6级。消防局局长苏迪克（<span class="st">John Sudnik）亲自到场指挥，</span>经过近4个小时灭火，总共有23人受轻伤，包括4名居民，19名消防员。大楼内居住了多位华裔居民，其中两名88岁华裔长者当晚6时多被消防员救出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11162519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11162519" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132357-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11162519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132357-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11162519" class="wp-caption-text">起火地点在最顶楼一间单位，但风助火势，一直蔓延到全层楼甚至向下延烧到楼下的单位，云梯也被浓烟淹没。（蔡溶/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>起火地点位于布碌崙7大道交44街日落公园旁一栋多单元公寓大楼，开始从顶层6楼着火，当天的风速预计<span class="st">20-30mph，消防局估计阵风速度达40mph，</span>在强风的助力下，火势在着火一个小时左右就迅速上升到五级，六楼顶楼熊熊烈火，六楼多个单元陷入火海，火苗肆虐，火舌吐出到大楼外，火光冲天，屋顶被烧穿，天空中浓烟滚滚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11162521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11162521" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132358-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11162521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132358-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11162521" class="wp-caption-text">消防员将华裔耆老住户救出，耆老在风中哆嗦。（蔡溶/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>消防局总共出动250名消防员和EMS急救员到现场。大火烧了近4个小时，直到晚上8点半，消防局才宣布大火被扑灭。目击者称，火势之大，在Verrazzano 大桥都可看到。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11162522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11162522" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132359-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11162522" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132359-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11162522" class="wp-caption-text">消防员将华裔耆老住户救出，两名耆老在风中哆嗦着寻找女儿电话，向女儿报平安。（蔡溶/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该公寓楼内居住了多名华裔居民。据一名住在火灾大楼的一楼、临7大道的王姓居民说，下午约4点45分她被消防员扯出房间时，才知发生火警，“我在家只顾着玩手机，完全没听到消防车的呼啸声，只听到楼上的老外一直在喊叫，我也没当回事，只当她平时就爱嚷嚷。直到侄女说火警，我一看窗外，满是消防员。我赶紧穿衣。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11162523" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11162523" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132361-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11162523" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132361-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11162523" class="wp-caption-text">众多观众围观，观看消防员救火过程。（蔡溶/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着一男一女两名老外消防员把她扯出房间，王女士说，她都顾不上拿其它东西，“消防员都不给我时间关门，掩门都不让。还好拿着手机、眼镜，要没有眼镜我什么也看不清！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11162524" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11162524" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132365-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11162524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132365-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11162524" class="wp-caption-text">消防局总共出动250名消防员和EMS急救员到现场灭火及救援。（蔡溶/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>约晚上6点多，消防员将二楼的两名88岁的华裔老人救出来，随后放上担架送往医院检查身体。两名老人出来时只穿着拖鞋，浑身上下被消防的水淋得湿透，裹着大白浴巾，在风中哆嗦。两老人家被救出来后忙着拨打女儿的电话报平安，老婆婆用国语说，“家里漏水。我刚刚在里面，被消防员救出来了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11162526" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11162526" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132367-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11162526" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132367-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11162526" class="wp-caption-text">众多观众围观救火过程，左侧的日落公园山坡上站满了人。（蔡溶/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场围观的人群相当多，眼看到大火迟迟不灭，更蔓延至整层、甚至延烧至楼下的单位，从楼下窗口往外喷火时，许多华人居民很沮丧，对救火方式议论纷纷。消防局在推特上解释称，“当晚风况及其严重。<span class="tlid-translation translation">为了安全起见，我们将所有消防队员（从楼内）撤出来，并使用外部塔楼梯子进行操作，它现在是一个全面的外部操作。”</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11162527" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11162527" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132372-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11162527" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132372-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11162527" class="wp-caption-text">4楼到6楼都着火了，火苗肆虐，250名消防员到场救火。（消防局照片@FDNY推特）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11162529" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11162529" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132373-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11162529" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132373-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11162529" class="wp-caption-text">熊熊烈火，将公寓楼多个单元吞噬。（消防局照片@FDNY推特）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11162531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11162531" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132374-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11162531 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/132374-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11162531" class="wp-caption-text">火势凶猛。（NYC Scanner）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当局表示，火灾灾户被临时安置在附近的169小学内。◇</p>
