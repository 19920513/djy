<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《海豚湾恋人》主演戏外重逢 相约沙滩路跑</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260729412384-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>徐洁儿（左）、林韦君（右）6月26日在台北出席路跑记者会。（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年06月26日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）偶像剧《海豚湾恋人》中的沈曼青与徐珊妮戏外重逢，徐洁儿与林韦君今天（26日）一同出席记者会，宣布将参加全台最浪漫的沙滩嘉年华路跑活动，还要一睹美丽风景、大啖当地美食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11347542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11347542" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260729472384-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260729472384-ss1.jpg" alt="徐洁儿" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11347542" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11347542" class="wp-caption-text">徐洁儿6月26日在台北出席路跑记者会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11347544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11347544" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260730062384-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260730062384-ss1.jpg" alt="林韦君" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11347544" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11347544" class="wp-caption-text">林韦君6月26日在台北出席路跑记者会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该剧当年在澎湖吉贝屿拍摄，剧中饰演沈曼青与徐珊妮的两位女星合作后成为无话不谈的好闺密，徐洁儿称赞林韦君容貌如昔，而林韦君也称羡她热爱长跑的毅力，对于她健美的身材甘拜下风。</p>
<p>徐洁儿也喊话：“大家不只能参加赛事，而且单身的男女们一定要穿上自己最有自信的战袍，说不定就能在赛事当中找到海豚湾恋人，一起共度浪漫沙滩嘉年华。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11347545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11347545" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260729302384-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260729302384-ss1-600x399.jpg" alt="徐洁儿（左）、林韦君（右）" width="600" b="399" class="size-large wp-image-11347545" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11347545" class="wp-caption-text">徐洁儿（左）、林韦君（右）当年因合作《海豚湾恋人》而成为闺密。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
