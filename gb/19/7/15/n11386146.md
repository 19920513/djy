<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林翰墨传香 举办台澳书画名家作品联展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/8b96a1c7767cbe9ae5e9c5eaf4697202-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>云林县府举办 台澳书法名家作品联展。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月15日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）让世界晓得云林也是翰墨丹青传香的地方，云林县政府结合民间书画团体丶学术代表共同举办“台澳书画名家作品联展”，展出台澳38位艺术名家81件作品，15日在亲民空间举行开幕典礼，多为书画家现场挥毫作画免费赠送民众，现场充满书香画香，孤挺花民俗乐团的国乐演奏，更增添了现场热闹气忿！</p>
<figure id="11386157" aria-describedby="caption-11386157" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/b4caeb4cb26cce8e18b94825c279b3b7-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/b4caeb4cb26cce8e18b94825c279b3b7-450x338.jpg" alt="孤挺花民俗乐团国乐演奏，更增添了现场热闹气忿！" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11386157" class="wp-caption-text">孤挺花民俗乐团国乐演奏，更增添了现场热闹气忿！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="11386155" aria-describedby="caption-11386155" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2bc832fe9f9d538f355a5245bc34a9c9-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2bc832fe9f9d538f355a5245bc34a9c9-450x338.jpg" alt="书法家谢美在老师赠送民众墨宝。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11386155" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A6%E6%B3%95.md#1">书法</a>家谢美在老师赠送民众墨宝。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长张丽善表示，县府办公厅舍化身为艺文展览馆，作为拓展艺术交流与观赏平台，举办台澳书画名家作品联展，期望透过公私协力积极推动美学教育，能艺术即生活丶生活即艺术，更让喜爱艺术者多一处艺文欣赏与交流的平台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11386154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11386154" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/f6978d5c077ae081f647e3e8882e3379.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11386154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/f6978d5c077ae081f647e3e8882e3379-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11386154" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A6%E6%B3%95.md#1">书法</a>家赠送县长张丽善（中）墨宝。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11386156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11386156" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/951ed92b582815c1009fdd8c257c263c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11386156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/951ed92b582815c1009fdd8c257c263c-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11386156" class="wp-caption-text">县长张丽善观赏书法家现场挥毫。（廖素贞／大纪元）)</figcaption></figure>
<p>张丽善强调，推动传统艺术及深耕美学教育一直是县府重点艺文政策，为使艺术触角延伸至每个公共空间，提升艺术风气落实到日常生活，县府特地邀请澳门及台湾计38位艺术名家分享其书画创作81件(澳门27幅、台湾54幅)。运用县府1楼亲民空间及2楼艺文走廊，让办公室化身成为美术馆，愉悦同仁及洽公民众的心情，更欢迎乡亲前来共赏这些书画作品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11386150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11386150" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1bb0bfcb87a59eff033534eb98713848.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11386150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1bb0bfcb87a59eff033534eb98713848-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11386150" class="wp-caption-text">县府亲民空间化身艺文展览馆。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11386148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11386148" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/e9ebdb739432960199aba8617923c31a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11386148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/e9ebdb739432960199aba8617923c31a-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11386148" class="wp-caption-text">书画家现场作画。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11386152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11386152" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/3f36122adf57f5bdbca238210eccceed.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11386152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/3f36122adf57f5bdbca238210eccceed-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11386152" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%94%BB.md#1">国画</a>家现场作画。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次联展书画名家阵容坚强，澳门展出代表有黄开禄丶表铭、麦理等11位书画名家，台湾更有27位艺术家踊跃参与，除了云林在地21位外，更有来自台北、台中及彰化等其他县市者等多位书画名家联合展出，诸大师级艺术家难得齐聚一展，机会难得，作品精彩。</p>
