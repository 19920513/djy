<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿人雨中欢庆新年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1-2-1Boston-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>文协出动功夫班舞狮，给波士顿第一夜带来中国元素。(刘景烨/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年01月05日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）12月31日晚，在冷风冷雨中，波士顿上空烟花绽放，波士顿人欢呼鼓掌，迎接2019<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">新年</a>到来。</p>
<p>当天中午还没开始下雨，庆贺<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">新年</a>的表演已经开始。在科普利广场(Copley)的主舞台、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>公共图书馆、附近购物中心和教堂等处，多个表演嘉宾团体轮番登台。</p>
<p>华人团体褚玲舞蹈团在公共图书馆表演开场秀。大波士顿中华文化协会(GBCCA)亦表演腰鼓、扯铃、中国舞蹈等中华艺术，还设手工艺摊位与游客互动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10954112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10954112" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1-2-2Boston-e1546626005107.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10954112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1-2-2Boston-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10954112" class="wp-caption-text">在科普利广场有蓝紫色的灯光烘托的许多冰雕，包括中国宝塔。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>到了晚上，蓝紫色的灯光烘托冰雕，在科普利广场展现世界各地建筑的“冰冷”一面，其中有中国宝塔、英国大本钟，还有复活岛巨人像。游人们不顾泥泞的草地，纷纷挤在冰雕周围拍照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10954114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10954114" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1-2-3Boston-e1546626062994.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10954114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1-2-3Boston-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10954114" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长华殊(Marty Walsh)在科普利广场登台送上新年祝福。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚上6点，波士顿市长华殊(Marty Walsh)在科普利广场登台送上新年祝福，并带领盛大的游行队伍向波士顿公共公园(Boston Common)出发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10954117" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10954117" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1-2-4Boston-e1546626154124.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10954117" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1-2-4Boston-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10954117" class="wp-caption-text">文协会员拉起“Happy New Year”横幅。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10954121" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10954121" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1-2-5Boston-e1546626201523.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10954121" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1-2-5Boston-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10954121" class="wp-caption-text">文协出动“壮丁”表演中国传统的舞龙。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年，GBCCA依旧走在游行队伍前列。中国传统的舞龙、舞狮、扯铃表演吸引路人拍照、互动。</p>
<p>晚上7点，天上下起小雨，同时灿烂的烟花也在公园上空绽放。人们冒雨观看，热烈欢呼、鼓掌。</p>
<p>在科普利广场的倒计时钟下，人们倒数迎接新年。午夜时分，烟花再度轰鸣，庆贺2019新年到来。</p>
<p>这是波士顿第43个新年游行庆典。“人们喜欢庆祝第一夜。我想很多波士顿人也知道，波士顿这里是全国的第一个庆典。今晚这里也有来自全麻州各地的家庭。”市长华殊说。◇</p>
