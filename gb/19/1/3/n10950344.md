<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欣赏维港美景 叹米芝莲星级名厨意大利菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021110291366-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（米芝Gi提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年01月03日讯】纽约米芝莲星级名厨Michael White获公认为“纽约意大利厨神”，他将其精湛厨艺及别树一格的纽约风格意式菜肴带到香港，在海港城的AL MOLO为客人献上传统的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E8%8F%9C.md#1">意大利菜</a>、自家制意粉以及精心焗制pizza，还有甜品及多款意大利精选美酒选择。</p>
<p><strong>完整影片：</strong></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-adae7d8a-dc36-4369-68b9-ec97c8ff7f31" data-id="player-adae7d8a-dc36-4369-68b9-ec97c8ff7f31"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/adae7d8a-dc36-4369-68b9-ec97c8ff7f31?r=16x9&amp;s=1920x1080&amp;d=55&&cat=ncid1251282&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F19%2F1%2F3%2Fn10950344.md#1"></a>
<p>AL MOLO原为意大利文，是码头的意思，而餐厅正好位于海运大厦，名副其实兼尽收维港美景。餐厅采用纽约式设计，高雅又有格调，装潢、台凳以及餐具摆设都很有心思。除了室内还有露天用餐区，窗外能观望码头及维港两岸风景，餐厅的情调无论情侣、密友、三五知己共聚都很适合。餐厅门外还能隔着玻璃窗看到厨师在搅动做面机在做着意粉，光站着看已经觉得很吸引。</p>
<p>去试的主要最大原因是餐厅由丰富技术经验的纽约星级厨师Michael White主理，想试试纽约风格的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E8%8F%9C.md#1">意大利菜</a>到底有多独特。</p>

<p>坐下看餐牌，发现单是餐酒已经有很多款，除了红、白餐酒、甜酒还有多款鸡尾酒选择，可惜我和朋友们都不喝酒，所以没有点选。我们点了Cappelletti（芝士南瓜馄饨）、Spaghetti（蚬肉鲜虾意粉）、Pappardelle（黑松露香菇阔条面）、Grigliata（烤海鲜拼盘）和Parmigiana（焗千层茄子）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10950392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10950392" style="width: 439px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021110401366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10950392" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021110401366.jpg" alt="" width="439" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10950392" class="wp-caption-text">上：Pappardelle（黑松露香菇阔条面）、中：Cappelletti（芝士南瓜馄饨）、下：Spaghetti（蚬肉鲜虾意粉）。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>先上来的是Cappelletti，入口芝士味香浓而不腻，如果热一点会更好吃。再来是Spaghetti，配有鲜虾一只，蚬肉份量也恰好，自家制的意粉外形有点像即食面，入口软腍带有面粉香，果然好吃。Pappardelle入口更好吃，表面很粗目测阔度有一吋，入口比意粉烟韧一点，黑松露酱很香很creamy，第一次吃到这么好吃又特别的黑松露阔条面，真的很好吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10950477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10950477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021110361366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10950477" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021110361366-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10950477" class="wp-caption-text">餐前送的面包香脆好食。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10950464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10950464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901030036211366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10950464" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901030036211366-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10950464" class="wp-caption-text">Cappelletti入口芝士味香浓而不腻，如果热一点会更好吃。（影片撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10950409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10950409" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021110331366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10950409" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021110331366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10950409" class="wp-caption-text">Spaghetti的意粉是自家制，外形有点像即食面，入口软腍带有面粉香。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10950398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10950398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021110291366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10950398" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021110291366-600x405.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10950398" class="wp-caption-text">Pappardelle的黑松露酱很香、充满奶油味，第一次吃到这么好吃又特别的黑松露阔条面。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Parmigiana就有点失望，就是普普通通的焗茄子没什么特别，也不觉得有千层的特色。Grigliata烤得香脆入味，有虾、鱼块、鱿鱼和小扇贝，也很好吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10950402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10950402" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021111041366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10950402" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021111041366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10950402" class="wp-caption-text">Parmigiana就是普普通通的焗茄子没什么特别。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10950404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10950404" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021111081366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10950404" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901021111081366-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10950404" class="wp-caption-text">Grigliata烤得香脆入味，各款海鲜也很好吃。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里每款食物卖相都漂亮精致，自家制意粉最出色，侍应招呼也很好。我们当日去得比较早，晚上六时便入座，因还未满座，老板稍为有空还出来和我们打招呼，非常热情友善，最后结账的时候还提醒我们可储Asia Miles，我们说不懂如何加到Asia Miles账户，他还帮我们呢，真的很好人，虽然不便宜，食物质素真的不错，值得再去！#</p>
