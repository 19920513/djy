<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>赏枫何必到国外  石门水库枫红天越越冷更漂亮</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5d159a62920088beb8f77b961bec9819-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园石门水库枫林步道两旁，冬季时青枫的火红与枫香的金黄相映成趣。（北水局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年11月20日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）天气转凉，桃园<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E9%97%A8%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">石门水库</a>枫林步道两旁，栽植三千余棵树龄，超过30年的枫树及槭树，步道下方另有槭林公园，冬季时青枫的火红与枫香的金黄相映成趣，为北台湾低海拔赏枫胜地，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%B0%B4%E5%B1%80.md#1">北水局</a>副局长郭耀程说，天越越冷更漂亮，欢迎大家参观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11667699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11667699" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/86c276ec0e59969466a73dae441eb01a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11667699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/86c276ec0e59969466a73dae441eb01a-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11667699" class="wp-caption-text">桃园<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E9%97%A8%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">石门水库</a>枫林步道两旁，冬季时青枫的火红与枫香的金黄相映成趣。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%B0%B4%E5%B1%80.md#1">北水局</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>石门水库是台湾主要水库之一，源头来自大汉溪中游，是台湾著名的旅游景点，每年11月前后，石门水库槭林公园枫叶转红，与风景区之山水美景，结合成桃园地区最佳自然旅游景点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11667700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11667700" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/9205920004d0f2a1e33a06f978fbdfcd.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11667700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/9205920004d0f2a1e33a06f978fbdfcd-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11667700" class="wp-caption-text">桃园石门水库枫林步道两旁，冬季时青枫的火红与枫香的金黄相映成趣。（北水局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>说到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E6%9E%AB%E5%8F%B6.md#1">赏枫叶</a>很多人都选择去日本或韩国，其实台湾也有很多赏枫叶的好所在，石门水库是国人秋季绝不能错过的赏枫叶美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11667701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11667701" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/40ad3f607a2ff46bf0b6a0b894b6ad7e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11667701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/40ad3f607a2ff46bf0b6a0b894b6ad7e-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11667701" class="wp-caption-text">桃园石门水库枫林步道两旁，冬季时青枫的火红与枫香的金黄相映成趣。（北水局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>石门水库是低海拔赏枫地点，枫林步道两旁共有三千多棵的青枫纯林，加上下方的槭林公园景色也很迷人，枫林步道是石门水库最著名的赏枫景点之一，循着人行步道前行，映入眼帘是整排高耸的枫香，有如一条缤纷的隧道，约行500公尺处即抵达枫林步道入口，步入小径立刻被枫树包围，山坡上的枫树都有30年以上的树龄，每到深秋，红、黄、绿层层色彩缤纷交织，蝴蝶翩翩飞舞，鸟儿枝头吟唱，景色美丽如画，令人赞叹，也是网红打卡重地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11667702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11667702" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/0923cae60c2911239d8ca86b626284e0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11667702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/0923cae60c2911239d8ca86b626284e0-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11667702" class="wp-caption-text">桃园石门水库枫林步道两旁，冬季时青枫的火红与枫香的金黄相映成趣。（北水局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>石门水库民国53年建造完成，不但具有农业灌溉、民生与工业供水、防洪、发电等作用外，也是桃园重要的观光景点，尤其水坝高度达133公尺，最大溢洪量可达惊人的每秒11400立方公尺，每当水坝泄洪时，雄伟壮丽的景象，也吸引民众前来朝圣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11667703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11667703" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/ee8e802b3fab028ab47b6b3875a5ccbc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11667703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/ee8e802b3fab028ab47b6b3875a5ccbc-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11667703" class="wp-caption-text">桃园石门水库枫林步道两旁，冬季时青枫的火红与枫香的金黄相映成趣。（北水局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郭耀程欢迎大家利用假日到北水局一游，赏枫不用出国，石门水库就够美啦。◇</p>
