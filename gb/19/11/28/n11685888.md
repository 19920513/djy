<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】松鼠鱼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893528-b2eca0c0b81503be471908970027fc09-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道松鼠鱼，酥香松脆、甜酸可口。（普照荟苑提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%BC%A0%E9%B1%BC.md#1">松鼠鱼</a>”形似昂首翘尾的松鼠，色泽鲜艳，鲜嫩酥香，酸甜适口，更有趣的是还有声音。当炸好的“松鼠鱼”端上桌，随即浇上热气腾腾的卤汁，这“松鼠”便吱吱地“叫”起来。</p>
<p>这“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%BC%A0%E9%B1%BC.md#1">松鼠鱼</a>”还很有来历呢。相传有一次乾隆皇帝下扬州，微服走进了“松鹤楼”，见神台上有一尾活蹦乱跳的鲤鱼（又称元宝鱼），便指名要厨师做给他吃。当时，神台上的鱼是用来供奉用的。但因是皇帝旨意，堂馆便与厨师商议如何处理。</p>
<p>厨师发现鲤鱼的头很像松鼠的头，而且想到本店招牌的第一个字就是“松”字，顿时灵机一动，决定将鱼做成松鼠形状，以回避宰杀神鱼之嫌。乾隆细细品尝，感到外脆内嫩，酸甜可口，他赞不绝口，遂重赏厨师。自此以后，姑苏“松鼠鱼”便闻名于世。传说，乾隆每逢节日和寿辰之日，都要吃“松鼠鱼”。</p>
<p>今天，我们就来试试罗厨制作的这道“松鼠鱼”。罗厨的“松鼠鱼”酥香松脆、甜酸可口。鲜柠檬的清香和新鲜番茄自然风味融合在一起，二合一的酸味特别诱人。</p>
<p>罗厨特别强调了这道菜美味正宗的秘诀。红鱼的肉质特点为蒜子肉，炸起来较酥脆，但切起来容易烂，所以要小心，适合切旋转花，起肉时也要连着鱼尾为好。另外，腌制鱼肉的时候，要用普通生粉，因为普通生粉颗粒粗，炸起来口感更香脆。</p>
<h2>松鼠鱼</h2>
<p>主料：红鱼1尾，约3磅。</p>
<p>辅料：柠檬3颗，番茄汁40盎司，红黄椒粒各1汤匙，葱粒1汤匙。</p>
<p>调料：砂糖1汤匙（如果喜欢甜可多加一些），盐1茶匙，生粉水4汤匙。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 红鱼洗净沥干水分，起骨（去中骨）、鱼头、鱼鳍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12893510" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12893510" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893510-1-1-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12893510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893510-1-1-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12893510" class="wp-caption-text">将红鱼起骨（去中骨）、鱼头、鱼鳍。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 制作汤汁：柠檬取汁加入番茄汁内，调好后的汤汁倒入油锅内，加盐1茶匙、砂糖1汤匙、生粉水4汤匙，加一点油及红、黄椒粒各1汤匙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12893514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12893514" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893514-2-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12893514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893514-2-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12893514" class="wp-caption-text">制作汤汁 <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 红鱼切旋转花，起肉时也要连着鱼尾为好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12893518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12893518" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893518-3-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12893518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893518-3-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12893518" class="wp-caption-text">红鱼切旋转花。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 放2茶匙海盐、1茶匙胡椒粉腌制鱼肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12893519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12893519" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893519-4-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12893519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893519-4-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12893519" class="wp-caption-text">腌制鱼肉。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 加入1颗鸡蛋和生粉，鱼肉充分沾上生粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12893520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12893520" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893520-5-1-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12893520" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893520-5-1-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12893520" class="wp-caption-text">加入1颗鸡蛋和生粉。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 油加热至八成油温，放入鱼，炸至变色，反转炸另一面，鱼头、鱼鳍也放入油锅内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12893521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12893521" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893521-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12893521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893521-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12893521" class="wp-caption-text">将鱼炸至变色，鱼头、鱼鳍也放入油锅内。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 炸至酥脆捞出装盘，做好的汤汁均匀淋到鱼上面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12893523" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12893523" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893523-7-1-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12893523" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893523-7-1-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12893523" class="wp-caption-text">做好的汤汁均匀淋到鱼上面。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 最后把葱粒撒散其间，色香味俱全的松鼠鱼上桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12893525" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12893525" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893525-8-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12893525" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/id12893525-8-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12893525" class="wp-caption-text">色香味俱全的松鼠鱼上桌。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
