<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>元长国小课程发表 兵马俑、八国联军都登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/22b4e1cc97575ba98a7c8ec0796afc6d-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>元长国小期末课程成果展 23日在该校活动中心举行。（廖素贞 ／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年05月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E9%95%BF%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">元长国小</a>年度盛典-期末课程发表会，23日下午在该校学生活动中心热闹登场，除了一、三、五年级丰富的静态课程成果展示外，二、四、六年级学生的动态表演更是精彩，在班级导师及美术班老师通力指导下，不仅服装道具DIY，还搭配舞蹈、戏剧、音乐演奏及布袋戏等融合课程的演出，连兵马俑及八国联军都登场！吸引超过百位家长热烈参与！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11276762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276762" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/bbe73819242b833e3a712c415fc9b102.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/bbe73819242b833e3a712c415fc9b102-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276762" class="wp-caption-text">全身是泥浆的秦代兵马俑， 成了活动现场瞩目的焦点。（廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一群满身是泥巴的真人版“兵马俑”，成了活动现场瞩目的焦点，让大家都看傻眼“怎么这么像！”更有趣的是，一起参与活动的家长还不断的提醒孩子“不能笑哦！不然脸上的泥浆会裂开哦！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11276763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276763" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/c4328b6cb3217f9587447d112b2ebbfa.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/c4328b6cb3217f9587447d112b2ebbfa-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276763" class="wp-caption-text">“不能笑哦！”好酷的真人版兵马俑！ （廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11276770" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276770" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/b0c4846c41128b378e2dacabbe184448.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276770" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/b0c4846c41128b378e2dacabbe184448-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276770" class="wp-caption-text">八国也登场。（廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11276771" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276771" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/fa5ce82ecca7456e5700024f8fe1d3bf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/fa5ce82ecca7456e5700024f8fe1d3bf-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276771" class="wp-caption-text">由女同学演出的八国联军。 （廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>指导老师廖家贤表示：为了这次的演出，他们花了一个月的时间，最困难的是节目主题的发想，其它的道具制作、舞蹈音乐的编排都不困难，因为学生自己就会去网路找他们要的软体！</p>
<p>为了让兵马俑看起来更逼真，他们用学校之前活动用剩的白布及珍珠板制作服装道具，怕伤害学生的皮肤，还特别选用温泉泥粉加上太白粉调制成泥浆，让学生从头到脚包括衣服全部抹上一层，乍看真像个刚出土的泥人！廖老师说：每个学生全身的道具花不到30元。既环保又展现另类的创意！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11276764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276764" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/5ec1672fddc896efccdb649d7cce7d19.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/5ec1672fddc896efccdb649d7cce7d19-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276764" class="wp-caption-text">老师帮扮演兵马俑的学生戴上用塑胶杯制成的帽子！（廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11276761" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276761" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/cc6bf76c10a1f7d005083069e5c4cf21.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276761" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/cc6bf76c10a1f7d005083069e5c4cf21-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276761" class="wp-caption-text">全身是泥浆的秦代兵马俑， 在校园大合照。（廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E9%95%BF%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">元长国小</a>校长王晓萍表示，本学年元小致力发展学校本位课程，主题是“生活艺术家”希望透过期末成果发表，让老师及学生反刍一年来所学的课程，能活用在日常生活当中。透过动态表演，让孩子动手做道具、融贯跨领域课程，一起创作，在老师的引导下学会设计剧情、舞蹈音乐及美术，增加孩子的视觉、听觉的美学经验！</p>
<p>二年级演出的“动物嘉年华”，融合空污及环境保护议题，由天真可爱的小朋友扮演一群动物，聚集在森林举办歌唱大赛，动感十足的音乐、魅力四射的舞蹈，使整个森林充满欢乐和笑声，突然捣蛋鬼霾哥PM2.5及大量垃圾的出现，造成动物大量的死亡、森林渐渐的枯萎，让大家意识到爱护环境的重要，必须透过垃圾分类、减量，才能还给大自然呼吸的权利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11276766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276766" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/59b4e7d67c7704564de53f00741eacf8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/59b4e7d67c7704564de53f00741eacf8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276766" class="wp-caption-text">由二年级演出的“动物嘉年华”。（廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11276767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276767" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/6d3a8cd1aedd706545a7952b6f2cad94.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/6d3a8cd1aedd706545a7952b6f2cad94-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276767" class="wp-caption-text">“青花瓷”全体演出同学大合照！（廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11276773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276773" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/00b97c1abd674cef12bd7e1035729111.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/00b97c1abd674cef12bd7e1035729111-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276773" class="wp-caption-text">布袋戏也上场演出。（廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11276769" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276769" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/3d6db914945374d76e4753387a1c3936.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276769" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/3d6db914945374d76e4753387a1c3936-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276769" class="wp-caption-text">四年级演奏“青花瓷”，搭配整齐划一的宫廷舞队。（廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>四年级的“青花瓷”以小提琴为主轴，直笛伴奏并搭配各种不同的打击乐器，并融入舞蹈，整齐划一的宫廷舞队型，展现学生不同样貌的视觉艺术学习成果。还有布袋戏-观世音收服红孩儿、舞台剧-黑金刚花生，也展现了地方戏曲及在地产业的特色！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11276772" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11276772" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/f8ae145e934a4faff4536f4c8eff3666.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11276772" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/f8ae145e934a4faff4536f4c8eff3666-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11276772" class="wp-caption-text">全体演出同学及指导老师大合照。（廖素贞 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>压轴节目是由六年级带来的“寻找桃花源”，这出跨领域的专业历史大剧，带领观众看尽秦代兵马俑、魏蜀吴三国时代大军争战、清朝八国联军侵华与清末僵尸队的幽默，道具和服装全都是DIY。节目融入国语课的桃花源、健康与体育领域的太极六章、社会领域的朝代介绍，以及艺术与人文的舞蹈与戏剧。</p>
<p>从古至今，无论岁月如何流逝，朝代如何更迭，都避不开战乱与纷扰，人们寻找的桃花源，是不是真的能寻得呢？元长国小课程成果展呈现多元创意，也带给观众无限幽思。◇</p>
