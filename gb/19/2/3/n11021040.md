<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>200个诸事大吉福袋将送猪年宝宝 婴儿萌样惹人疼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/c174cc81f3fdf94557ba43b14cc469d7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>宏其妇幼医院副院长刘兴宽盼望新婚父母多生孩子，增“产”报国。 (徐乃义／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年02月03日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）黄历新年从除夕至<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5%E8%8A%82.md#1">元宵节</a>诞生的宝宝有福了！<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9D%9C.md#1">中坜</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8F%E5%85%B6%E5%A6%87%E5%B9%BC%E5%8C%BB%E9%99%A2.md#1">宏其妇幼医院</a>准备200个“诸事大吉”，送给除夕至<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5%E8%8A%82.md#1">元宵节</a>在该院诞生的婴儿，内有“招财开运”钱母一枚，与医护人员温馨的祝福，在婴儿室外隔着玻璃窗观看的父母亲和家属们，看到婴儿的萌样和喜气福袋都欢喜不已，也感受到浓浓的新年气氛与医护人员的用心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11021494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11021494" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/20190203-biyun-t001.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11021494 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/20190203-biyun-t001-300x533.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11021494" class="wp-caption-text">婴儿的萌样让人心都被融化了。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9D%9C.md#1">中坜</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8F%E5%85%B6%E5%A6%87%E5%B9%BC%E5%8C%BB%E9%99%A2.md#1">宏其妇幼医院</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过年连假，待产中的孕妇添个宝宝，为新的一年带来更多的幸福，桃园婴幼儿出生人数最多的中坜宏其妇幼医院，在充满新年热闹气氛中，今年准备200个诸事大吉福袋，除为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%94%9F%E5%84%BF.md#1">新生儿</a>祝福之外，也期盼能给孕妇一个惊喜。</p>
<p>宏其妇幼医院副院长刘兴宽表示，从除夕至元宵节，凡住进宏其妇幼医院婴儿室的宝宝们的小床上都会悬挂新年福袋，装满医护人员深摰的祝福和父母无止尽的爱，宝宝出院时，宝宝与父母可以将这个招财开运的钱母福袋带回家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11021045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11021045" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/f5c0aeabcbb54d2c2797bdbea36a58f5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11021045" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/f5c0aeabcbb54d2c2797bdbea36a58f5-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11021045" class="wp-caption-text">新年快乐福袋内有“招财开运”钱母一枚。（中坜宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在国内生育率不高的时刻，刘兴宽希望医护人员的爱心，让大家感受张红淇院长与医护人员温馨的祝福，也希望宝宝在爱的氛围下，快乐长大，更盼望新婚父母多生孩子，增“产”报国。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11021042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11021042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/4b232e313b036820e77f1caee679e6cc.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11021042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/4b232e313b036820e77f1caee679e6cc-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11021042" class="wp-caption-text">医护人员温馨的祝，福诸事大吉福袋希望猪年宝宝快乐长大。（中坜宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11021043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11021043" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/c77d317883c1abe9cd4664d26e7cc39c.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11021043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/c77d317883c1abe9cd4664d26e7cc39c-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11021043" class="wp-caption-text">宝宝们的小床上都会悬挂新年福袋，让人感受到浓浓的新年气氛。（中坜宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
