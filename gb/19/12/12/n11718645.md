<h6>烤火鸡腿杂菜（图/梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3><strong>烤<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%B8%A1%E8%85%BF.md#1">火鸡腿</a>杂菜</strong><br />
Roasted Turkey Thighs with Mix Vegetable</h3>
<p><strong>【材料】</strong><br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%B8%A1%E8%85%BF.md#1">火鸡腿</a> 2支（thighs 2）<br />
红萝卜 4条 （Carrot 4 Strips）<br />
合掌瓜 2个（Chayote 2 pieces）<br />
洋葱1个 （Onion 1 piece）<br />
西芹 5条 （Celery 5 strips）<br />
土豆 1个（Potato 1 piece）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718675" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718675" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1-14-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11718675 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1-14-450x338.jpg" alt="烤火鸡腿" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718675" class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【调味】</strong><br />
有味盐 3汤匙（Seasoned Salt 3 T）<br />
黑胡椒粉1汤匙（Black pepper 1 T）<br />
白酒1杯（White wine 1 C）<br />
鸡汤 2杯 （Chicken Broth 2 C）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718676" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2-10-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11718676 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2-10-450x338.jpg" alt="烤火鸡腿" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718676" class="wp-caption-text">有左至右依序为白酒、鸡汤、黑胡椒粉、有味盐。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【做法】</strong><br />
1.火鸡腿洗浄抹干水，切块后在肉的部分划上几刀，以便入味，把有味盐和黑胡椒粉均匀涂抹在鸡腿上，腌2小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718678" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3-7-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11718678 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3-7-450x338.jpg" alt="烤火鸡腿" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718678" class="wp-caption-text">把有味盐和黑胡椒粉均匀涂抹在鸡腿上，腌2小时才能入味。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将所有蔬菜切成大块，预开烤箱425℉，烤盘铺上锡箔纸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718681" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4-6-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11718681 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4-6-450x338.jpg" alt="烤火鸡腿" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718681" class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.把蔬菜铺在烤盘底部，鸡腿放在上面，倒入白酒和鸡汤，放进烤箱烤25分钟，再把烤箱温度降至350℉，多烤20分钟，插入测热针，肉的核心达到165℉便熟。取出鸡腿后，盖上锡箔纸保温，留下小部分蔬菜做配菜使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718679" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5-5-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11718679 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5-5-450x338.jpg" alt="烤火鸡腿" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718679" class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.剩下的蔬菜连汁液一起放进搅拌机打成泥状，做成酱汁并调好味道。把鸡腿去骨，肉切成厚片，蔬菜放碟中，上面铺上鸡肉淋上酱汁便可上桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718750" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/7-6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11718750 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/7-6-450x600.jpg" alt="火鸡腿" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718750" class="wp-caption-text">火鸡腿烤好后，你也可以选择保留骨头的吃法。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【梁师傅小贴士】</strong><br />
? 很多人不喜欢吃鸡胸（白肉），所以我就只是烤鸡腿（黑肉），而且只需要烤45分钟，可以节省不少时间，不需担心剩下太多肉。<br />
? 用烤完的蔬菜连汁一起打泥做成酱汁，味道好又营养。◇</p>
