<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>古装折纸娃娃</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%98%E7%BA%B8.md#1">折纸</a>是很有趣的活动，几个动作就将一张平凡无奇的纸张变化成各式各样的造型，这是折纸最迷人的亮点。</p>
<p>古装的人物头戴方帽，身穿宽袖大袍，威风凛凛的样子。当他变成纸娃娃时，威风不见了取而代之的是可爱的视觉感受。这样的经验还满有趣的，现在就拿起纸张来试试吧！</p>
<p><h2>材料：</h2>
<p>相同大小的正方形色纸二张、胶水或白胶。</p>
<p><h2>一、衣服做法：</h2>
<p>1. 将色纸两边对折，使其成一个长方形后摊开。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021346591452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />2. 将另外两边也对折，使其成一个长方形后摊开，得两条折线。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021409081452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />3.将色纸两组对角分别重叠对折，使其成一个三角形，摊开，得一组对角斜向的折线，如下图。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021402561452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021403561452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />4. 将一角往上翻折，角的顶点与纸张的中心点对齐。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021405341452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />5.将纸翻面，再分别将左右侧的角往中心点对齐折好。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021409221452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />6.将上图左侧三角形摊开，得一折线，再将这角往上翻折并将角的顶点对齐折线。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021404161452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021400341452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />7.将上图左侧的白色小三角形往上方再翻折，对折折好。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021406441452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /> 8.右侧重复步骤六、七的动作折好。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021347321452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /> 9. 将上图整张纸转向，让上侧角靠近自己，并将其往上翻折，使翻折的三角形的两斜边与左、右两侧梯形的斜边对齐。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021404051452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /> 10.将步骤9中的角A往丙边翻折对齐折好，并将整纸张翻面。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021403231452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021403091452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />11.将上图中靠自己的一边往上翻折，盖过上端三角形的一半面积。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021355551452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /> 12.将图十四的整张纸上、下转向，再将被压于中间的三角形拉出、对折(也就是顶点对齐底边)，摊开，得此三角形的中间线，再将三角形的顶点翻折与此中间线对齐重叠，如下图。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021356321452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021401381452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />13.将上图中上面的白色小角形往自己这一侧翻折，使其成一梯形。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021405471452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br />14.将步骤13中的右、左侧分别向内略斜向的翻折，使其像古装衣服的两边大袖，如下图。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021407361452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021405231452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021409541452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />15.将上图中梯形的两底角分别往上翻折出一个小三角形，翻面后，可见衣服的样式比较好看，如下图。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021356421452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021410491452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a></p>
<p><h2>二、官帽与头部折法:</h2>
<p>1. 将正方形的两组对边分别重叠对折，摊开后得十字形折线，如下图。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021401121452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021406001452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /> 2. 将纸张的一组对角重叠对折，摊开，得一斜对角的折线。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021404251452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br />3.将上图中的角A往中心点处翻折，顶点与纸张中心点重叠，摊开，得一折线。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021406341452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021401251452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />4. 将上图中角A的两边分别往上翻折，并使边与中央折线对齐，再运用折线将角A拉出。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021355461452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021411121452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /> 5.将角A压平，再向外侧翻折，摊开，得一中间折线。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021411121452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021355231452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021358241452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021407061452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021407181452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />6. 将上图中的角B往中间线处翻折好，再将角A再次往下翻折。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021402371452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021357121452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021347231452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />7. 将图中的甲、乙边分别往外翻折，并使与角A相邻的斜边与角A的底边对齐。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021400551452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021409441452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021358121452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021402171452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />8. 将纸张转九十度，这样比较好操作。再将角C往角A处对齐翻折，摊开后得一折线丙。<br /> <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1506021410341452-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /> <br />
