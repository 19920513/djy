<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：圣诞前 巴黎香榭丽舍大道富丽堂皇</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年12月08日讯】（大纪元记者张妮<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E9%BB%8E.md#1">巴黎</a>报导）眼看圣诞节快到，藉出差首都巴黎之机，来到香榭丽舍大道观夜景，只见车水马龙的街道两旁，一栋栋古老的建筑，在美丽的圣诞饰灯照耀下，更加富丽堂皇，游人们都会趁行人道亮绿灯的一瞬间，快速走到红绿灯柱旁，站在正对凯旋门方向举起相机，按下快门。</p>
<p>香榭丽舍大道由东向西延伸1.9多公里，总宽70米，中间双向车道（各有4条车道）占30米。夜幕下，往东遥望，是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E9%BB%8E.md#1">巴黎</a>最大的广场——协和广场，转头望向西边，是著名的凯旋门。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_6531700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6531700" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/151207101232100574-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6531700" class="wp-caption-text">夜幕下，往东遥望，是巴黎最大的广场——协和广场上的摩天轮。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6531713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6531713" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/151207101048100574-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6531713" class="wp-caption-text">香榭丽舍大街西边，是著名的凯旋门。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6531726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6531726" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/151207101159100574-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6531726" class="wp-caption-text">香榭丽舍大街上来往的车辆。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>街道两旁众多的名牌商店、高级餐厅人头簇拥，虽然这些商店、餐馆门口都站着魁梧的保安，当顾客步入时，均被严格检查携身物品，但从一张张神态恬静的脸孔上，能感受到巴黎人依然全心陶醉于繁华的都市生活，街边一排排的咖啡座满座，小巧的桌子上，一杯开胃酒似乎能驱走户外的寒意，人们忘我地谈笑风生。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_6531738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6531738" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/151207101037100574-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6531738" class="wp-caption-text">一家高级餐厅的夜灯装饰。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6531750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6531750" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/151207100959100574-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6531750" class="wp-caption-text">香榭丽舍大街路易威登专卖店的外观。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6531762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6531762" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/151207100947100574-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6531762" class="wp-caption-text">街道旁设有环保饰灯，由民众踩踏自行车供电照明。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>“哎，女士，晚上好，请问可以看看您的手提包吗？”刚步入一家快餐店，同事被保安叫住了。同事打开手提包并主动说：“有一个香蕉”，保安严肃的脸庞露出一个灿烂的笑容。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_6531774" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6531774" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/151207100935100574-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6531774" class="wp-caption-text">一栋栋古老的建筑，在美丽的圣诞饰灯照耀下，更加富丽堂皇。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6531788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6531788" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/151207100924100574-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6531788" class="wp-caption-text">奢侈品商店的圣诞装饰。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>地铁里，在列车的轰隆声和乘客们的步伐声中，偶尔会传来一曲曲悠扬的奏乐声。卖艺人总是喜欢把巴黎地铁当“演奏厅”。</p>
<p>在通往站台的路上，遇见一个演奏乐团在来来往往的人群前，演奏着欢快的乐曲，宏厚的男声合唱响遍隧道的每个角落。</p>
