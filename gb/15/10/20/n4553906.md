<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【折纸】孔雀</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>孔雀开屏，像是缤纷的移动式屏风，非常吸睛。纸张虽然没有孔雀尾羽的缤纷色彩，但它可以重复翻折的特性却可以营造出纸孔雀的开屏架式。趁着假日午后，在家中陪小朋友折<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%98%E7%BA%B8.md#1">折纸</a>孔雀，度过愉快富有亲情的午后时光。</p>
<p></p>
<h2>材料：</h2>
<p>正方形纸张、美工刀、剪刀。</p>
<p><h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1. 将正方形纸张对折，摊开后得折线甲。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191322221452-600x519.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />2. 分别将上图中的角A、角B往中线对齐翻折好。 <br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191320501452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />3. 将左、右两边往背面翻折。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191325501452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />4. 以上图中三角形与四边形相邻的边为基准线，翻折对齐。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191319521452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />5. 将上图中的造型翻转过来，将三角形中间隙缝打开，分别往外侧撑开，并由上往下压平后成一小正方形（如下图）。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191320581452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191319431452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />6. 将上图中的角C、角D分别往中线处对齐压褶，摊开（如下图）。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191323421452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191321501452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />7. 将上图中的小正方形打开，以丙、丁线为基准，将角C往内侧压入；角D作法同角C（如下图）。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191325301452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191324241452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191319351452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />8. 将上图中左侧的三角形往右侧翻折，再将左侧的四边形立起、压平，使其成1等腰三角形（如下图）。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191323051452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191321201452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />9. 将上图中等腰三角形以中央线为基准分成两个直角三角形分别立起，从内侧撑开后压平，并将末端的角往上翻折，使翻折出的小三角形底边与下面对齐，再将最上面的三角形对折，也就是左侧的三角形往右侧翻折，使两底角重叠（如下图）。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191323241452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191321111452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191321301452-600x471.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191320001452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /> 10. 将上图中左侧的三角形重复步骤9的动作。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191322321452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191325021452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /> 11. 将上图中的角E重复步骤9的动作，其他三角形也是这样处理方式，完成后共有8个锐角三角形（如下图）。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191322031452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191320091452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191324431452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191324521452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /> 12. 将8个锐角三角形的末端往内侧压下、阖上，使其成2个小三角形。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191325111452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191319261452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191323141452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191325211452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />13. 用类似步骤12的动作将上图中完成的7组小三角形再往回翻折，形成更小的7组2个小小三角形。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191322551452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191325411452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191319161452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />14. 将上图中中间的三角形往外翻出（这部分要当作孔雀的身体与头部），分别折向另一侧并与戊线（如下图中C、D上方的黑色虚线）对齐，摊开得仁、癸2斜向折线。<br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191324111452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191320171452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191318441452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510191322131452-600x472.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br />15. 运用仁、癸2斜向折线将两片三角形往上翻折立起，再将这部分的末端约0.5公分处以类似方法压折出头部，最后将尾部摊开－孔雀开屏。完成。@*<br />
