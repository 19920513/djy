<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台13组偶像艺人献唱生日庆活动</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年10月18日讯】台湾《Taipei Walker》杂志一年一度的生日庆活动，昨天（17日）在台北华山1914文创园区的华山剧场正式登场，集结了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%8A%B1%E5%A6%B9.md#1">豆花妹</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E9%BB%84%E6%B1%9D.md#1">蔡黄汝</a>）、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%95%E4%B9%A6%E5%B0%BD.md#1">毕书尽</a>、Popu lady等台湾偶像实力兼具艺人轮番开唱，为活动热闹展开。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6514980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6514980" style="width: 567px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510172321321487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6514980" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%8A%B1%E5%A6%B9.md#1">豆花妹</a>。（Taipei Walker提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>近来勤走宣传的豆花妹开始转换形象，现场动感开唱；最近发片且进入残酷淘汰赛的Popu lady则依旧维持演出水准，现场粉丝更是热情打气，五成员认为票数已不是重点，并私下约定五个人不解散，优胜者若发EP其他四人都要来客串。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_6514989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6514989" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510172322341487-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6514989" class="wp-caption-text">Popu_Lady。（Taipei Walker提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6515005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6515005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510172322311487-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6515005" class="wp-caption-text">Popu_Lady。（Taipei Walker提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>舞台表演从Bii<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%95%E4%B9%A6%E5%B0%BD.md#1">毕书尽</a>以韩文唱生日快乐歌展开。男歌手包括带伤上阵的柯智棠、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E6%98%95%E9%98%B3.md#1">林昕阳</a>、陈彦允、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%A4%A7%E6%96%87.md#1">王大文</a>和陈势安；而最近担任《Taipei Walker》封面人物的严爵，则带来严式情歌与快节奏feat等演出。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_6515020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6515020" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510172321521487-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6515020" class="wp-caption-text">毕书尽。（Taipei Walker提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6515033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6515033" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510172321591487-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6515033" class="wp-caption-text">陈彦允。（Taipei Walker提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>女歌手则有杨凯琳、唐宁、陈梦晨、吴汶芳等歌坛新生代歌手。当年参加台湾选秀《超级偶像》后苦熬四年发片的吴汶芳，这次带来首张小清新风格唱片的《孤独的总和》和《翘翘板》获得观众激赏。</p>
<p>“台北生活体验祭”今年落实了16周年城市新提案的精神，现场集结了30摊的好物市集，让大家体验店家的独特外，更鼓励大家要与着手作家一样，无时无刻展现创意生活。而前来的民众，皆可恣意的在草坪上野餐着，听歌逛市集。</p>
