<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】冬瓜云苓鹌鹑汤</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年08月15日讯】有40多年料理经验，曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨、海城酒楼 （Ocean City）主厨的粤菜大厨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%BF%97%E7%94%9F.md#1">梁志生</a>先生，本周为大家带来──冬瓜云苓鹌鹑汤。</p>
<p><h2> 材料:</h2>
<p>冬瓜：21/2磅<br />云苓： 2 oz<br />薏米：2 oz<br />鹌鹑：6只<br />猪骨：1 LB<br />蜜枣：5粒</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494613" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508142055382768-600x353.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494613" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><h2> 调味料:</h2>
<p>盐：适量<br />美极酱油：适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494629" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508142055402768-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494629" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><h2> 做法:</h2>
<p>1.冬瓜去瓤，连皮洗净切大块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508142055432768-600x415.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494651" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.蜜枣、云苓、薏米洗浄稍浸泡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494668" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508142055452768-600x409.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494668" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.鹌鹑、猪骨氽水后再冲洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494685" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508142055482768-600x522.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494685" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.汤煲内放进12碗水，所有材料放入，大火煲滚后转为小火煲两个小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494698" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508142055502768-600x424.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494698" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.加适量盐调味便可饮用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494714" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508142055532768-600x390.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494714" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.切少许葱、辣椒、姜丝，倒入少许酱酒，汤渣捞起沾酱油吃，较有味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494734" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508142055562768-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494734" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><h2> 梁师傅小贴士 </h2>
<p>?冬瓜消暑利水，云苓、薏米功能健脾祛湿，利水消肿。<br />?鹌鹑肉质鲜美，营养价值高，含有丰富的蛋白质和维生素、脂肪少，具有益中补气、强筋骨、消结热，利水消肿作用。长期食用对血管硬化、高血压、神经衰弱、结核病及肝炎有一定疗效。</p>
<p>若对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E8%B0%B1.md#1">食谱</a>制作步骤有疑问，可寄E-mail至大纪元美食组信箱：food.djy@gmail.com询问食谱相关问题。</p>
