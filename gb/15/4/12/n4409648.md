<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：“宝钿金花─惠风阁金银器展”</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2015年04月12日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾台北报导）台湾国立<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%86%E5%8F%B2%E5%8D%9A%E7%89%A9%E9%A6%86.md#1">历史博物馆</a>举办“宝钿金花─惠风阁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%93%B6%E5%99%A8.md#1">金银器</a>展”，展期至5月31日止，展出邱郁芬女士惠风阁珍藏巧夺天工的鎏金银器，介绍金银器中精彩绝伦的民族金细工艺─花丝镶嵌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5861034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861034" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111618382378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861034" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝镶宝腾龙牡丹蒜头瓶。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%86%E5%8F%B2%E5%8D%9A%E7%89%A9%E9%A6%86.md#1">历史博物馆</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>历史博物馆展出的惠风阁37件古典雅致的鎏金银花篮、龙船、佛塔、灯笼、熏香炉与摇钱树，皆以累丝编结出蟠龙、鸾凤、牡丹、鸿鹄、玄武与团花锦簇等吉祥纹饰，把花丝镶嵌鎏<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%93%B6%E5%99%A8.md#1">金银器</a>色彩的多变与层次，巧妙地展现在观众的眼前，件件匠心独具，美不胜收。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5861042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111618232378-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861042" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝镶宝双凤镯。（历史博物馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5861057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861057" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111617502378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861057" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝镶宝凤鸟宫灯。（历史博物馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>璀璨生辉的金银器，在中国古代是一种身份与地位的象征，主要为皇室王公贵族所使用，金银器的吉祥寓意与精美纹饰，成为引导社会流行的时尚风格。中国早在商朝就开始使用黄金，至战国时期鎏金技术已发展成熟，到汉代已掌握熔金为珠与拉金为丝的技巧。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5861064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861064" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111617362378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861064" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝镶宝团花四象足熏香炉。（历史博物馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5861076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861076" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111617202378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861076" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝镶宝华盖玄武灯笼座。（历史博物馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>唐代使用金银器的风气最盛，并与西方文化交流之下，金银器的器形与纹饰更是丰富多元，无论食器、容器、佩饰、药具及宗教用器等皆精美无比。此时已完善的制作工序，包括锤鍱、錾刻、炸珠、铆钉、浇铸、焊接，与镂空等特殊金工。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5861081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861081" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111617052378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861081" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝镶宝封侯添禄皮囊壶。（历史博物馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5861093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111616472378-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861093" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝镶宝火焰宝珠莲花灯。（历史博物馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>自宋、元以降，贵族社会流行以金银器来显示身份财富。至明、清时皇室成立金银器制作的官府，着重于细腻的花丝工艺发展，五彩斑斓的宝石镶嵌结合，把金细工艺推至“花丝镶嵌”的绚烂极致。直到今天，仍为人们喜爱佩戴的首饰与摆设。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5861105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861105" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111616332378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861105" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝镶嵌宝石牡丹花提篮。（历史博物馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5861115" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861115" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111616272378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861115" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝镶嵌宝石牡丹花提篮。（历史博物馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>这批璀璨玲珑、令人目不暇给的宝物，就是以高难度的累丝金工镶嵌宝石而成。这种金细工艺利用金、银高度延展的特性，把金、银加热后拉成细丝，再弯曲出花样焊接于器物表面，称为掐丝。若将细丝绞、扎成股，制成花丝加以编织，或层层盘绕堆叠，经焊接后连结成形，即为累丝。最后在累丝的器物上镶嵌宝石，就称为花丝镶嵌。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5861122" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861122" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111616122378-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861122" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝双塔龙船。（历史博物馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5861137" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5861137" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1504111615572378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5861137" class="wp-caption-text">鎏金累丝摇钱树。（历史博物馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
