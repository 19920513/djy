<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：法国购物天堂 中国人旅游欧洲首选</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年04月28日讯】（大纪元记者蔡颖综合报导）中国人旅游欧洲法国是首选，而且首都巴黎更是中国人心目中位列第一的购物天堂。</p>
<p>根据旅游Travelzoo年初统计，中国人在巴黎平均消费达1500欧元，是日本游客的2倍以上。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5855124" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5855124" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/150427192457100574-600x383.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5855124" class="wp-caption-text">图为去年大减价期间，顾客在巴黎拉法耶百货门口等待商店营业开门，其中有不少中国或亚洲游客。(JACQUES DEMARTHON/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5855140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5855140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/150427192410100574-600x394.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5855140" class="wp-caption-text">根据调查，计划前往巴黎的中国游客中，去老佛爷和春天百货、香榭丽舍大街上的奢侈品店以及物美价廉的大型购物中心“血拼”，是事先排定的行程。图为香榭丽舍大街上的奢侈品商店。(JACQUES DEMARTHON/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure><br />为吸引中国游客，法国不断推出宽松旅游签证的政策。自今年1月份以来，中国人到法国旅游，签证可在48小时内签发。</p>
<p>据法国相关调查数据显示，每年有超过90万中国人到法国旅游，预计到2020年，这个数字会增加至400万。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5855152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5855152" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/150427192135100574-600x357.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5855152" class="wp-caption-text">根据调查,计划前往巴黎的中国游客中，去老佛爷和春天百货、香榭丽舍大街上的奢侈品店以及物美价廉的大型购物中心“血拼”，是事先排定的行程。图为夜景中的拉法耶百货大楼。(JACQUES DEMARTHON/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5855162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5855162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/150427192327100574-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5855162" class="wp-caption-text">图为爱马仕位于巴黎的商店.(FRANCOIS GUILLOT/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5855174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5855174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/150427191917100574-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5855174" class="wp-caption-text">图为春天百货门口的大减价广告。(BERTRAND LANGLOIS/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5855186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5855186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/150427192220100574-600x409.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5855186" class="wp-caption-text">图为巴黎拉法耶百货大减价期间，顾客在选购商品。(MIGUEL MEDINA/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5855201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5855201" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/150427192432100574-600x424.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5855201" class="wp-caption-text">在众多的奢侈品中，皮包、首饰最受中国游客欢迎。图为一位游客在香榭丽舍大街的珠宝橱窗前拍摄一条砖石项链。(ERIC PIERMONT/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5855214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5855214" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/150427192515100574-600x412.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5855214" class="wp-caption-text">今年，许多中国人选择自由行，看准了法国冬季大减价期间出游，因为有些商品甚至奢侈品折扣低至3折至5折。(ERIC PIERMONT/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure><br />
