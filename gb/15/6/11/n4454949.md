<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】慈菇润肠煎鱼饼</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年06月11日讯】慈菇润肠煎鱼饼</p>
<p>材料:<br />鱼浆：1/2磅<br />润肠：1条<br />慈菇：1/2磅<br />芫茜（香菜）：少许<br />士多啤梨（草莓）：5个</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5875864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5875864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/06/1506101333352730-600x403.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5875864" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />调味：<br />陈皮粒：1/4块<br />盐：1/2茶匙<br />糖：1/2茶匙<br />粟粉（玉米粉）：1汤匙<br />麻油：少许<br />胡椒粉：少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_5875879" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5875879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/06/1506101333412730-600x394.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5875879" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>做法:</h2>
<p>1. 把菇洗净去皮，切厚片。</p>
	<figure id="attachment_5875897" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5875897" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/06/1506101334452730-600x435.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5875897" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />2.慈姑片与润肠一起放入蒸锅蒸15分钟，取出待凉。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5875914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5875914" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/06/1506101334542730-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5875914" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />3.留下10片?菇最后做垫底，把其余?菇、润肠切成小颗粒， 芫茜切碎，把所有材料放入一深碗内。</p>
	<figure id="attachment_5875931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5875931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/06/1506101335032730-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5875931" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />4.把材料搅拌均匀后，分成10份，用两个汤匙轻轻压扁成小饼型状。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5875945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5875945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/06/1506101335112730-600x401.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5875945" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />5.热镬下油，把鱼饼放入锅里铺开。鱼饼不要太挤，可分两次来煎。</p>
	<figure id="attachment_5875958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5875958" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/06/1506101335182730-600x394.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5875958" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />6.鱼饼每面煎2分半钟，煎至金黄色，翻面后才轻压鱼饼。 装盘时，把10片慈菇排放在碟底，将煎好鱼饼放在慈菇片上，士多啤梨切半排在鱼饼的旁边作装饰。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5875971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5875971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/06/1506101335252730-600x370.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5875971" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />主厨小技巧：汤匙先沾水，能避免鱼浆粘在汤匙上。在油中放入鱼饼后，不要立即拨动，等底面煎一会儿变色定型再翻面，才能不粘锅底。</p>
<p><h2>梁师傅小贴士</h2>
<p>*本身鱼浆已经有味道，加上润肠也有咸味，所以不用再加调味了。<br />*鱼浆很容易熟，加上慈菇已蒸熟，不用煎太久味道就很香浓。</p>
<p>若对食谱制作步骤有疑问，可于每周一～四晚上7时～7时15时；每周日下午6时～6时30分于AM1400《希望之声广播电台》的梁厨美食（粤）节目（普同话、粤语皆可）询问或留言给梁志生师傅。或可寄E-mail至大纪元美食组信箱：food.djy@gmail.com询问食谱相关问题。</p>
