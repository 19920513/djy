<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>爱因斯坦：“爱”是宇宙最大力量</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>艾伯特?<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%9B%A0%E6%96%AF%E5%9D%A6.md#1">爱因斯坦</a>（Albert Einstein）是公认的最伟大科学家。</p>
<p>1905年，26岁的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%9B%A0%E6%96%AF%E5%9D%A6.md#1">爱因斯坦</a>刚从大学毕业，在瑞士专利局担任三等技师，利用业余时间在一年之内完成6篇具有划时代意义的论文，提出了“狭义相对论”和“质能转换方程式（E=mc2）”。他在1921年获得诺贝尔物理学奖。</p>
<p>有一天，有位记者访问爱因斯坦，请他发表对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%97%E6%95%99.md#1">宗教</a>及神存在问题的看法。</p>
<p>正好，爱因斯坦刚送走一位客人。爱因斯坦问：“记者先生，您是否知道是谁将咖啡杯等物放于此处的？”记者答道：“自然是阁下。”</p>
<p>爱因斯坦接着说：“小如咖啡杯等物，尚且需要一种力量来安排；那么您想一想，宇宙拥有多少星球，而每一星球均按一定轨道运行无间，这种安排运行力量的即是神。”</p>
<p>爱因斯坦又说：“也许阁下会说：‘我没看见过，也没听到过神，那么我如何相信神的存在呢？’是的，您具备了五感：视觉、听觉、嗅觉、味觉、触觉，但这五种感官是有其限度的，例如声音，只有在20赫兹～20,000赫兹范围内的波长，人才能听到……”</p>
<p>访谈最后，爱因斯坦说到他相信“神”的存在：“因此，今天科学没有把神的存在证明出来，是由于科学还没有发展到那种程度，而不是神不存在。总而言之，人的五种感觉是有限制的，无法感觉出神的存在，科学也无法否认神的存在。因此，我们应该确信神的存在。”</p>
<p>或许我们现在还无能力制作<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E2%80%9C%E7%88%B1%E2%80%9D.md#1">“爱”</a>的炸弹，以消灭正在摧毁这个星球的仇恨、自私和贪婪。然而，我们每个人身上都拥有一个虽小但有威力的“爱”的发射器，能够发射爱的能量。<br />
－－艾伯特?爱因斯坦（Albert Einstein）</p>
<p>多年前，联合国曾经用世界著名的盖洛普民意调查方法进行了一项调查，调查最近300年间的300位最著名的科学家是否相信神。</p>
<p>其中除38位因无法查明其信仰而不计以外，其余262位科学家中，不<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%A1%E7%A5%9E%E8%80%85.md#1">信神者</a>仅20人，占总数的7.6％；信神者则有 242人，占92.4％。</p>
<p>而这位在历史上公认最伟大的科学家艾伯特?爱因斯坦（Albert Einstein），在给女儿的信中，明确表示：“爱是宇宙中最巨大力量，爱是神，神就是爱。</p>
<p>如果我们人类还希望存活下去，我们就应寻求生命的意义。如果我们还想拯救这个世界和这个世界中的生命，‘爱’则是唯一的答案！ ”</p>
<p>在当时进化论盛行的环境中，爱因斯坦虽具备领先科学界的声望，但仍未能将他的对道德和神明的认知公布于众，只在给女儿的信中，表达了他的真实想法。</p>
<p>大科学家牛顿也曾表示：“从诸天文系的奇妙安排，我们不能不承认这必是全知全能的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E7%BA%A7%E7%94%9F%E5%91%BD.md#1">高级生命</a>的作为。宇宙间一切有机无机的万象万物，都是从永生真神的智慧大能而来。”</p>
 
<p>艾伯特?爱因斯坦（Albert Einstein）的27封信，内容谈及对神、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%97%E6%95%99.md#1">宗教</a>、原子弹等的观点，6月11日在洛杉矶拍卖出42万美元。</p>
<p><strong>以下是爱因斯坦写给女儿的一封信：</strong></p>
<p>“当我提出相对论时，没有多少人理解我，在此信中我透露给人类的也会与世人的观念和偏见抵触。</p>
<p>因此，我恳请你尽量保藏这些信数年以至几十年，直到社会进展到能够理解我在这信中所揭示出来的。</p>
<p>在宇宙中存在着一种极其巨大的力量，至今科学还没有探索到合理的对其的解释。</p>
<p>此力量包容并主宰其他一切，他存在于宇宙中的一切现象背后，然而人类还没有认识到他。</p>
<p>这个宇宙的力量就是‘爱’。</p>
<p>当科学家们探索宇宙时，他们忽略了这最具威力却看不见的力量。</p>
<p>‘爱’是光，照亮那些给予和接受他的人。</p>
<p>‘爱’是引力，他使得人们彼此相吸。</p>
<p>‘爱’是力量，他把我们拥有最好的东西又加倍变得更好，他使人类 不会因无知、自私而被毁灭。</p>
<p>‘爱’可以揭示、‘爱’可以展现。</p>
<p>因‘爱’，我们生存及死去。</p>
<p>‘爱’是神，神就是‘爱’。</p>
<p>此力量可以解释任何事情，并赋予生命之意义。我们已经忽略他太久了，或许是因为我们惧怕‘爱’ &#8211; 这宇宙中人类尚未能随意运用的能量。</p>
<p>为让人类能了解‘爱’，我在我最著名的方程式，E=mc2，做了一个简单的替换，如我们能认可，从‘爱’乘以光速的平方而获得的能量足以治愈这个世界的话，我们将会得出这一结论：‘爱’是宇宙中最巨大的力量，因为他没有极限。</p>
<p>人类试图利用和控制了宇宙中的一些能量，然而这些能量却被用来毁灭自己。</p>
<p>我们现在急需能真正滋养我们的能量。如果我们人类还希望存活下去，我们就应寻求生命的意义。</p>
<p>如果我们还想拯救这个世界和这个世界中的生命，‘爱’则是唯一的答案！</p>
<p>我们或许现在还无能力制作一个‘爱’的炸弹，以消灭正在摧毁这个星球的仇恨、自私和贪婪。</p>
<p>然而，我们每个人身上都拥有一个虽小但有威力的‘爱’的发动机，这个发动机正等待发射爱的能量。</p>
<p>当我们学会如何给予和接纳这个宇宙的能量，我的孩子，我们将能断言‘爱’，无所不能的，超越一切，因为他就是生命的全部。</p>
<p>我后悔未能早些表达上述存于我心中的一切。</p>
<p>也许现在道歉已迟，但时间是相对的，我还是要告诉你，我爱你也谢谢你，我因你而得到了生命的最终答案！</p>
<p>你的父亲<br />
爱因斯坦 Albert Einstein (posted by Rafeal Tellez-Giron on FB) ”。</p>
<p>（推荐：<span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #993300;"><a style="color: #993300; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/17/11/16/n9848899.md#1">被拍摄下的“情感” 科学家揭人体能量场奥秘</a></span>）</p>
<p>英文原文：</p>
<p>“A letter from Albert Einstein to his daughter: on The Universal Force of Love</p>
<p>I deeply regret not having been able to express what is in my heart, which has quietly beaten for you all my life. Maybe it&#8217;s too late to apologize, but as time is relative, I need to tell you that I love you and thanks to you I have reached the ultimate answer! &#8221;</p>
<p>In the late 1980s, Lieserl, the daughter of the famous genius, donated 1,400 letters, written by Einstein, to the Hebrew University, with orders not to publish their contents until two decades after his death. This is one of them, for Lieserl Einstein.</p>
<p>I ask you to guard the letters as long as necessary, years, decades, until society is advanced enough to accept what I will explain below.</p>
<p>&#8220;When I proposed the theory of relativity, very few understood me, and what I will reveal now to transmit to mankind will also collide with the misunderstanding and prejudice in the world.<br />
There is an extremely powerful force that, so far, science has not found a formal explanation to. It is a force that includes and governs all others, and is even behind any phenomenon operating in the universe and has not yet been identified by us. This universal force is LOVE.</p>
<p>When scientists looked for a unified theory of the universe they forgot the most powerful unseen force. Love is Light, that enlightens those who give and receive it. Love is gravity, because it makes some people feel attracted to others. Love is power, because it multiplies the best we have, and allows humanity not to be extinguished in their blind selfishness. Love unfolds and reveals. For love we live and die. Love is God and God is Love.</p>
<p>This force explains everything and gives meaning to life. This is the variable that we have ignored for too long, maybe because we are afraid of love because it is the only energy in the universe that man has not learned to drive at will.</p>
<p>To give visibility to love, I made a simple substitution in my most famous equation. If instead of E = mc2, we accept that the energy to heal the world can be obtained through love multiplied by the speed of light squared, we arrive at the conclusion that love is the most powerful force there is, because it has no limits.</p>
<p>After the failure of humanity in the use and control of the other forces of the universe that have turned against us, it is urgent that we nourish ourselves with another kind of energy…</p>
<p>If we want our species to survive, if we are to find meaning in life, if we want to save the world and every sentient being that inhabits it, love is the one and only answer.</p>
<p>Perhaps we are not yet ready to make a bomb of love, a device powerful enough to entirely destroy the hate, selfishness and greed that devastate the planet.</p>
<p>However, each individual carries within them a small but powerful generator of love whose energy is waiting to be released.</p>
<p>When we learn to give and receive this universal energy, dear Lieserl, we will have affirmed that love conquers all, is able to transcend everything and anything, because love is the quintessence of life.</p>
<p>I deeply regret not having been able to express what is in my heart, which has quietly beaten for you all my life. Maybe it&#8217;s too late to apologize, but as time is relative, I need to tell you that I love you and thanks to you I have reached the ultimate answer! &#8220;.</p>
<p>Your father,Albert Einstein (posted by Rafeal Tellez-Giron on FB) ”。</p>
<p>（本文译自A letter from Albert Einstein to his daughter: on The Universal Force of Love by Katrin Chrysaphis）</p>
