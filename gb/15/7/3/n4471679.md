<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】鲜蟹肉鱼子素翅羹</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年07月03日讯】有40多年料理经验，曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨、海城酒楼 （Ocean City）行政总厨的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">粤菜</a>大厨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%BF%97%E7%94%9F.md#1">梁志生</a>先生，本周为大家带来──大展鸿图翅(鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9F%B9%E8%82%89.md#1">蟹肉</a>鱼子素翅羹)。</p>
<p><h2> 材料: </h2>
<p>素食鱼翅（已浸泡）：4oz<br />鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9F%B9%E8%82%89.md#1">蟹肉</a>：4 oz<br />鱼子：2 oz<br />金宝鸡汤：1罐<br />水：1/2杯<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5887980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5887980" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507021454272768-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5887980" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p></p>
<h2>调味： </h2>
<p>盐：1/4茶匙<br />胡椒粉：1/8茶匙<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5887997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5887997" style="width: 531px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507021454342768.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5887997" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<h2> 做法:</h2>
<p>1.称4安士已浸软的素鱼翅，这个量能分成4人份。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5888007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507021454472768-600x517.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888007" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>2.用少量姜汁酒把浸泡好的素鱼翅热水烫过，滚约5分钟后捞起备用。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5888026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507021454542768-600x387.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888026" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>3.鲜蟹肉用热水浸暖后，晾干备用。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5888046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888046" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507021455002768-600x409.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888046" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>4.煮滚稀释了的鸡汤，放入素鱼翅、 鲜蟹肉和少许胡椒粉。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5888058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888058" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507021455072768-600x431.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888058" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>5.素鱼翅和蟹肉都煮滚后，加1茶匙粟粉开水埋献，煮成鱼翅羹。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5888073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888073" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507021455162768-600x460.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888073" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>6.盛起鱼翅羹，最后撒入鱼子即完成。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5888088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888088" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507021455242768-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888088" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p></p>
<h2> 梁师傅小贴士</h2>
<p>?在香港过年时，为了起一个好意头的菜名，就把“蟹黄鱼翅羹”的名字改成“大展鸿图翅”，含有生意兴隆、风生水起的意思。在美国加州，因为禁鱼趐，也不容易找到靓蟹黄，所以我就利用有相近颜色的新鲜鱼子来代替，效果不错，并且营养健康。<br />?这里用的金宝鸡汤，因为味道醇厚，加半杯水即可；如觉味道不够，可加少许盐调味，新鲜蟹肉也具足鲜味。<br />?素食鱼翅主原料是由植物性淀粉、海草提取物和海藻粉，是一种天然高纤植物性胶原蛋白，对人体相当有益。素食鱼翅在贩卖素食材料店铺有售，一般是干货，只需用冷水浸泡。</p>
<p>若对食谱制作步骤有疑问，可于每周一～四晚上7时～7时15时；每周日下午6时～6时30分于AM1400《希望之声广播电台》的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8.md#1">梁厨</a>美食（粤）节目（普通话、粤语皆可）询问或留言给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%BF%97%E7%94%9F.md#1">梁志生</a>师傅。或可寄E-mail至大纪元美食组信箱：food.djy@gmail.com询问食谱相关问题。</p>
