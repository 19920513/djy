<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】麒麟蒸石斑鱼</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年07月10日讯】有40多年料理经验，曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨、海城酒楼 （Ocean City）主厨的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">粤菜</a>大厨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%BF%97%E7%94%9F.md#1">梁志生</a>先生，本周为大家带来── 麒麟蒸<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E6%96%91%E9%B1%BC.md#1">石斑鱼</a>。</p>
<p><h2> 材料:</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E6%96%91%E9%B1%BC.md#1">石斑鱼</a>：1条（约1 1/2磅）<br />冬菇：4朵(大)<br />冬笋：12片<br />金华火腿：12片<br />菜远：12条</p>
<figure id="attachment_5886192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5886192" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091459442768-600x344.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5886192" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2>调味: </h2>
<p>鸡汤：2汤匙<br />蒸鱼汁：1 1/2汤匙<br />盐：1/2茶匙<br />胡椒粉、麻油：少许<br />粟粉（玉米粉）：1/2茶匙，加水勾芡用</p>
<p><h2> 腌料:</h2>
<p>盐：1/2茶匙<br />胡椒粉：少许<br />粟粉：1茶匙<br />油：1汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_5886206" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5886206" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091459512768-600x427.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5886206" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2> 做法:</h2>
<p>1.石斑切去头尾留用；起出鱼肉，把鱼肉切厚块（约5公分x1.5公分）。冬菇浸透，每块斜刀切成3小片，与冬笋片一起烫过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5886217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5886217" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091459582768-600x388.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5886217" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.把鱼肉块以腌料拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5886229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5886229" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091500052768-600x381.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5886229" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.将鱼肉、冬菇、火腿、冬笋各1片相间排放碟中，分成两行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5886244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5886244" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091500132768-600x381.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5886244" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.把鱼头鱼尾放回碟中，整理成原来鱼的形状，放进蒸笼以大火蒸8分钟，出笼时倒出碟子中的汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5886252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5886252" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091500212768-600x466.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5886252" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.把菜远（即将菜除去一些菜叶和菜梗，保留中间最嫩最好吃的部分）用油盐水灼熟，分别伴在鱼的旁边。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5886266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5886266" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091500282768-600x418.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5886266" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.烧滚调味汁液，勾芡后淋在鱼身上便成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5886283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5886283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091500352768-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5886283" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2> 梁师傅小贴士</h2>
<p>?如果嫌整条鱼起肉麻烦，也可选用新鲜石斑肉代替。以菜远伴边来代替鱼头鱼尾装饰。<br />?麒麟的意思是指用冬菇、金华火腿、冬笋等，以梅花间竹的方法夹着斑块来蒸。</p>
<p>若对食谱制作步骤有疑问，可于每周一～四晚上7时～7时15时；每周日下午6时～6时30分于AM1400《希望之声广播电台》的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8.md#1">梁厨</a>美食（粤）节目（普同话、粤语皆可）询问或留言给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%BF%97%E7%94%9F.md#1">梁志生</a>师傅。或可寄E-mail至大纪元美食组信箱：food.djy@gmail.com询问食谱相关问题。</p>
