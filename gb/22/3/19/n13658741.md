<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>费城举办第250届圣帕特里克节游行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13660960-8efdeea7cf33df90084131dce599f8fc-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2022年3月13日，费城举办了第250届圣帕特里克节（St. Patrick's Day）游行。（李晓苏/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年03月20日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">（大纪元记者李晓苏费城报导）一年一度的的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E5%B8%95%E7%89%B9%E9%87%8C%E5%85%8B%E8%8A%82.md#1">圣帕特里克节</a>（</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">St. Patrick&#8217;s Day<span lang="ZH-CN">）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>因疫情被中断两年后，于</span>3<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>13<span lang="ZH-CN">日（周日）又回到费城，至今已经有</span>250<span lang="ZH-CN">年历史，是美国历史最悠久的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E5%B8%95%E7%89%B9%E9%87%8C%E5%85%8B%E8%8A%82.md#1">圣帕特里克节</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>之一。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13673859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13673859" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673859-Parade2-e1648266684623.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13673859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673859-Parade2-e1648266684623.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13673859" class="wp-caption-text">2022年3月13日，费城举办了第250届圣帕特里克节（St. Patrick&#8217;s Day）游行。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13673860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13673860" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673860-parade3-e1648266714368.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13673860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673860-parade3-e1648266714368.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13673860" class="wp-caption-text">2022年3月13日，费城举办了第250届圣帕特里克节（St. Patrick&#8217;s Day）游行。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-ideograph; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">游行大元帅迈克尔·布拉德利（</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">Michael Bradley<span lang="ZH-CN">）告诉大纪元说，今年游行的主题是信仰、家庭、友谊和文化遗产。他说：<span lang="ZH-CN">“</span><span lang="ZH-CN">我的</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">家人来自<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%B0%94%E5%85%B0.md#1">爱尔兰</a>。所以我们很激动，他们在这里，他们就在这里的手推车上。所以在圣帕特里克节上每个人都是爱尔兰人。”</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13673865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13673865" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673865-parade-Grand-Marshall-Michael-Bradley-e1648266808196.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13673865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673865-parade-Grand-Marshall-Michael-Bradley-e1648266808196.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13673865" class="wp-caption-text">3月13日，费城举办了第250届圣帕特里克节游行。本次游行大元帅是迈克尔·布拉德利（Michael Bradley）。李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13673862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13673862" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673862-Congressman-present-proclamation-and-flag-to-Mike-Bradley-e1648267240400.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13673862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673862-Congressman-present-proclamation-and-flag-to-Mike-Bradley-e1648267240400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13673862" class="wp-caption-text">美国众议院议员Brendan Boyle给游行大元帅迈克尔·布拉德利（Michael Bradley）颁发褒奖。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-ideograph; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">根据游行组织者介绍，费城第一次有记载的圣帕特里克节庆祝游行是在</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">1771<span lang="ZH-CN">年举行的，在《独立宣言》签署前五年。</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-ideograph; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">这次游行是由费城圣帕特里克日纪念协会组织的，有很多团体参加游行，包括乐队、舞蹈团体、青年团体和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%B0%94%E5%85%B0.md#1">爱尔兰</a>协会等等。很多游行的参与者和路旁的观众都表示，自己来参加圣帕特里克节游行很有意义。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13673863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13673863" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673863-Congressman-Brendan-Boyle-e1648267187870.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13673863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673863-Congressman-Brendan-Boyle-e1648267187870.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13673863" class="wp-caption-text">2022年3月13日，费城举办了第250届圣帕特里克节（St. Patrick&#8217;s Day）游行。图为美国众议院议员Brendan Boyle。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13673864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13673864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673864-Miss-Philadelphia-and-Miss-Philadelphia-outstanding-teen-e1648266866908.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13673864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673864-Miss-Philadelphia-and-Miss-Philadelphia-outstanding-teen-e1648266866908.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13673864" class="wp-caption-text">3月13日，费城举办了第250届圣帕特里克节游行。两位“费城小姐“参加了游行。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-ideograph; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">参加游行的足球运动员</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">HUGH PAT MCGEARY<span lang="ZH-CN">表示：</span>“<span lang="ZH-CN">首先它非常神圣。它是爱尔兰的精神，在这里的庆祝活动非常盛大。实际上我们真的感到很震惊，庆祝活动如此之大，可能比在爱尔兰都大。”</span><span lang="ZH-CN">足球运动员</span>TIERNAN MCCANN<span lang="ZH-CN">说：</span>“<span lang="ZH-CN">这对很多人来说意义重大。特别是在这里展现的爱尔兰文化。你可以看到在美国，这对爱尔兰人意味着什么。这很棒。来到这里真好。”</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13660965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13660965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13660965-Rich-Raspa-and-Neen-Raspa.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13660965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13660965-Rich-Raspa-and-Neen-Raspa-450x332.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13660965" class="wp-caption-text">退休夫妇RICH RASPA和NEEN RASPA来观看费城第250届圣帕特里克节游行（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-ideograph; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">退休夫妇</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">RICH RASPA<span lang="ZH-CN">和</span>NEEN RASPA<span lang="ZH-CN">来观看游行。</span>RASPA<span lang="ZH-CN">表示：</span>“<span lang="ZH-CN">我们喜欢爱尔兰人。我们去过几次爱尔兰，我们喜欢那里。我妻子有一半爱尔兰血统，所以我们来庆祝</span>&#8230;&#8230;”<span lang="ZH-CN">尼恩说：“只是为了玩得开心。”</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-ideograph; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">圣帕特里克节是为了纪念爱尔兰基督教最广为人知的人物之一</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">&#8212;<span lang="ZH-CN">圣帕特里克去世的日子。爱尔兰和世界各地的爱尔兰侨民在</span>3<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>17<span lang="ZH-CN">日庆祝这一节日。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13673858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13673858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673858-audience-e1648266902486.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13673858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13673858-audience-e1648266902486.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13673858" class="wp-caption-text">2022年3月13日，费城举办了第250届圣帕特里克节（St. Patrick&#8217;s Day）游行。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
