<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>警民同乐 华埠恢复举办灭罪安全夜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795812-DSC09784-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>8月2日波士顿华埠全美灭罪安全夜，塔夫茨医疗中心获颁社区服务奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年08月05日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）历经2年的暂停，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">波士顿华埠</a>今年恢复举办“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A8%E7%BE%8E%E7%81%AD%E7%BD%AA%E5%AE%89%E5%85%A8%E5%A4%9C.md#1">全美灭罪安全夜</a>”（National Night Out）活动。8月2日下午，政要、警察及众多居民来到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%90%8C%E6%9D%91.md#1">大同村</a>的广场，庆祝打击犯罪、维护安宁的工作顺利进行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13795813" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13795813" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795813-DSC09794.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13795813" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795813-DSC09794-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13795813" class="wp-caption-text">华埠治安巡逻队与嘉宾们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13795806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13795806" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795806-DSC09717.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13795806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795806-DSC09717-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13795806" class="wp-caption-text">中华耆英会的长者们演唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天下午，波士顿的多个社区都在同时举办灭罪安全夜庆祝活动。在华埠的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%90%8C%E6%9D%91.md#1">大同村</a>，成百上千的居民参与游乐。现场有武术、唱歌、舞蹈表演，也有多个公益团体带来的儿童游乐区、血压测量、手工艺、棉花糖等摊位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13795803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13795803" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795803-DSC09677.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13795803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795803-DSC09677-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13795803" class="wp-caption-text">公益团体为孩子们做汽球。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13795804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13795804" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795804-DSC09684.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13795804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795804-DSC09684-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13795804" class="wp-caption-text">大家都喜欢棉花糖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13795805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13795805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795805-DSC09715.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13795805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795805-DSC09715-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13795805" class="wp-caption-text">昆士小学的团队与市议会议长Ed Flynn合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）前往每一个社区参与庆祝，并于下午4:30左右来到大同村。她用中文普通话对居民们说，很高兴时隔2年能再次举办这个活动，跟大家一起庆祝。</p>
<p>“在波士顿市政府，非常非常重要的目标就是健康和安全。”她说。她感谢警察和卫生从业者们辛勤工作，为市民服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13795811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13795811" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795811-DSC09762.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13795811" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795811-DSC09762-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13795811" class="wp-caption-text">市长吴弭感谢警察和医护人员的服务。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13795808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13795808" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795808-DSC09736.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13795808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795808-DSC09736-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13795808" class="wp-caption-text">幼儿园的孩子们表演节目。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13795807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13795807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795807-DSC09732.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13795807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13795807-DSC09732-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13795807" class="wp-caption-text">很多游客观看表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到场的嘉宾还包括中华公所主席雷国辉、A-1分区警察局警长齐科罗（Robert Ciccolo），以及急救部门、公立学校、社区团体等诸多机构的代表。</p>
<p>吴弭和嘉宾们向塔夫茨医疗中心（Tufts Medical Center）颁发了社区服务奖，感谢这家医院在疫情期间服务华埠居民，提供医疗护理、疫苗接种、防疫知识普及等服务。◇</p>
