<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作刺绣：铁线莲 绣出一颗纯良的心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863383-20221110-flower_02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%e5%88%ba%e7%b9%a1.md#1">刺绣</a>是传统的手工艺，历史悠久。《<ahref="https://reurl.cc/28oVGO">初学者的自然系花草刺绣【全图解】》</a>作者用不同针法呈现各种花朵样貌，像是红色的山茶花、白色的三叶草、黄色的油菜花，甚至还有花盆与整个花篮。只需绣线、绣针，以及一个小小的绣框，就能贴近自然，享受专属的慢时光。让我们跟着本文作者一起学铁线莲<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%e5%88%ba%e7%b9%a1.md#1">刺绣</a>。</p>
<h2>铁线莲</h2>
<p>此花带有“你有一颗纯良的心”的美丽花语。<br />
我将紫色系的花朵们绣成花环的模样，还有种藤蔓延展的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863387" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863387" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863387-P.115-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863387-P.115-1-600x96.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="96" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863387" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13863392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863392" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863392-P.115-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863392-P.115-2-600x524.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="524" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863392" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>铁线莲的绣法</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13863393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863393" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863393-P.116-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863393-P.116-1-600x462.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="462" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863393" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 首先用<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F9eYSRZ_kjk">茎干绣</a>绣出主茎的曲线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863394" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863394-P.116-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863394-P.116-2-600x463.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="463" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863394" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 用<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VvytDsvvNzk">回针绣</a>来绣绿色的茎；用<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MRZQx65E44U">飞鸟叶形绣</a>紧密地绣出绿叶。</p>
<p>TIP 一遇到茎弯曲的地方就缩短那一针的长度并紧密地绣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863395-P.116-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863395-P.116-3-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863395" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 侧面的花瓣和小花蕾用雏菊绣先绣出轮廓，然后用直针绣把中间填满。<br />
TIP 在绣<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AoLSvUjuJy4">雏菊绣</a>的环时，请从稍远的地方出针，把尾端弄得尖尖的。先绣旁边两侧的花瓣，中间的花瓣留到最后绣，才能干净俐落地收尾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863396-P.117-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863396-P.117-1-600x463.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="463" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863396" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 大花苞就照着图案绣短针的茎干绣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863397" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863397-P.117-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863397-P.117-2-600x464.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="464" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863397" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 大花瓣先用雏菊绣绣好轮廓，换色线后再于中间绣上<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U20q3SI3RcI">直针绣</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863398-P.117-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863398-P.117-3-600x457.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="457" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863398" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 用<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=liZjgvlTruE">斯麦纳绣</a>来绣花瓣内部，并在保留环的状态下于正中央绣上<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Wo3UTn5IAlg">法国结粒绣</a>。之后剪断斯麦纳绣的环，稍微将线整平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863400" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863400-P.117-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863400-P.117-4-600x460.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="460" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863400" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 完成铁线莲。</p>
