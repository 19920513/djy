<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2022年西雅图国际汽车展 大人小孩齐欢乐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863920-2022_SIAS_03-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>西雅图国际汽车展。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年11月11日讯】（大纪元记者李奥西雅图报导）经过了疫情之后，今年各地车展渐渐回复正常，而每年一度的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E9%9B%85%E5%9B%BE%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md#1">西雅图国际汽车展</a>也顺利在11月举行，作为美国西北地区重要车展之一，展出汽车数量超过300多辆，与以往一样选址在Lumen Field Event Center，并于11月10日到13日公众开放。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863928-2022_SIAS_29-e1668142480218.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863928-2022_SIAS_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863928" class="wp-caption-text">Ford F150 Lightning Platinum。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13863924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863924" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863924-2022_SIAS_20-e1668142213736.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863924-2022_SIAS_20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863924" class="wp-caption-text">Toyota Crown。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13863931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863931-2022_SIAS_31-e1668142360991.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863931-2022_SIAS_31-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863931" class="wp-caption-text">全新设计Toyota Sequaia。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13863922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863922" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863922-2022_SIAS_12-e1668142185365.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863922-2022_SIAS_12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863922" class="wp-caption-text">BMW iX电动车。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展出车型种类广泛，包括轿车、卡车、SUV、跨界车、货车、电动汽车、混合动力车和超跑。当中不乏熟悉的名字，如离开市场10多年的Hummer，以纯电动车姿态出现的GMC Hummer EV Edition 1，同样是纯电动的皮卡Ford F150 Lightning Platinum，Nissan Ariya电动跨界车，这是这款量产车首次出现在西北地区。Chevrolet也展出了Blazer EV和Equinox EV两款电动跨界车。Dodge的Hornet（大黄蜂） GT，是Dodge的首款电动高性能车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863927-2022_SIAS_28-e1668142277918.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863927-2022_SIAS_28-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863927" class="wp-caption-text">Nissan Ariya电动跨界车。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新车绝不只有电动车，就像Acura，展出了它的最新一代Integra。意大利Alfa Romeo则展出了还未推出市场的Tonale，这辆小型的跨界车拥有Alfa Romeo的血统，前后比重达到50：50，使用引擎及电动马达双混合动力系统，前轮由引擎带动，而后轮由电动马达驱动，由于是Plug-In Hybrid车种，可以纯电动行驶距离为30英里，预计在2023第二季推出市场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863925-2022_SIAS_21-e1668142253154.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863925-2022_SIAS_21-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863925" class="wp-caption-text">2023 Alfa Romeo Tonale。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13863926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863926" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863926-2022_SIAS_23-e1668142265782.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863926-2022_SIAS_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863926" class="wp-caption-text">2023 Alfa Romeo Tonale。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，在展场一角可看到众多的名贵汽车，有Bentley、McLaren、Lamborghini和Rolls Royce等。而在另一角落，则有为小小驾驶员准备的试驾电动车及赛道。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863932-2022_SIAS_34-e1668142674829.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863932-2022_SIAS_34-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863932" class="wp-caption-text">众多的名贵汽车。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13863933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863933-2022_SIAS_38-e1668142316902.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13863933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863933-2022_SIAS_38-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863933" class="wp-caption-text">小驾驶员试驾区。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展览时间：<br />
11月10日（星期四）：中午12点至晚上9点<br />
11月11、12日（星期五、星期六）：上午10点至晚上9点<br />
11月13日（星期日）：上午10点至下午6点。</p>
<p>展览地点：<br />
Lumen Field Event Center<br />
800 Occidental Ave Seattle, WA 98134</p>
