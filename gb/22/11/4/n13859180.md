<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【超市食材料理】豆瓣鸡翅豆芽菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862375-010a75cde7683252ec391de482f196fc-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>豆瓣鸡翅豆芽菜。（出色文化出版社）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>生活即修炼，我这几年一直有这样的感想。人可以不带感受地生活，日复一日，然后感到疲乏无趣，也可在进行每项事务当下体会生活。好比将<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%8A%BD.md#1">豆芽</a>菜去根，心事一多，拣豆芽就烦躁；若无杂念干扰，拣豆芽就是疗愈心情的反复动作，看着拣好的豆芽清爽干净，当下也很有成就感。</p>
<p>每件家事都让我重整内心，利用家务时间转换心情（或放下某些事情），是我近期的练习。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">材料</span></h3>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E7%BF%85.md#1">鸡翅</a>（中）……300 克<br />
绿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%8A%BD.md#1">豆芽</a>（或黄豆芽）……1 包，去根部<br />
葱段……适量<br />
豆瓣酱……1 大匙<br />
酱油……1 大匙<br />
酱油膏……1 大匙<br />
热水……100 毫升</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_13862389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13862389" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862389-e9cd8c422e4723b1f28a7a44cfa84ed4.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13862389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862389-e9cd8c422e4723b1f28a7a44cfa84ed4.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13862389" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1.取一炒锅，锅热、油热后，将葱段下锅爆香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13862390" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13862390" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862390-f572121baa5ea3bf78e504abb57b0355.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13862390" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862390-f572121baa5ea3bf78e504abb57b0355.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13862390" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.放入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E7%BF%85.md#1">鸡翅</a>，将表面煎至金黄。保持中小火，将鸡翅内部慢慢煎熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13862391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13862391" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862391-74a6942c8cca24f25e8cc17a109947ca.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13862391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862391-74a6942c8cca24f25e8cc17a109947ca.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13862391" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.加入豆瓣酱、酱油、酱油膏，与鸡翅拌炒一下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13862392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13862392" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862392-4702e40f315ccb06f272f66c746e18d7.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13862392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862392-4702e40f315ccb06f272f66c746e18d7.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13862392" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.加入热水，将酱汁煮滚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13862393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13862393" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862393-6e373a6ca0a11dee2108187c1b9e90c8.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13862393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862393-6e373a6ca0a11dee2108187c1b9e90c8.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13862393" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.放入豆芽菜，盖上锅盖，以小火焖5 分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13862394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13862394" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862394-ec572373c62d7e90d587390c57f07f09.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13862394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13862394-ec572373c62d7e90d587390c57f07f09.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13862394" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.豆芽菜煮软后，与鸡翅拌炒均匀即可。</p>
