<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【超市食材料理】酱烧南瓜鸡胸肉排</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864937-381ab05d965e4a7b2dd3605def28c648-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>酱烧南瓜鸡胸肉排。（出色文化出版社）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a>较难入味，煮熟后口感又比较干柴，所以这里我用食物调理机将鸡胸肉打成绞肉，并在绞打过程加点盐与黑胡椒增添风味，再捏成容易入口的小肉排。</p>
<p>因为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>本身富含淀粉，吃这道菜时，我顶多再烫个青菜搭配，不另外准备面或白饭了，比较省事。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">材料</span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a>……300 克<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>……400 克，去皮切块<br />
盐……适量<br />
黑胡椒……适量<br />
水……100 毫升<br />
味醂……2 大匙<br />
酱油……2 大匙<br />
砂糖……2 茶匙</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13864939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13864939" style="width: 439px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864939-237a8c78249da17f13821ba324bd7dfd.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13864939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864939-237a8c78249da17f13821ba324bd7dfd.jpg" alt="" width="439" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13864939" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1.将鸡胸肉先处理成绞肉，再加入盐与黑胡椒拌匀。我用食物调理机打比较快，打之前就先加入盐与黑胡椒，这样绞肉打好也均匀调味了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13864940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13864940" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864940-429d51ef07e26135006427523ad77e32.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13864940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864940-429d51ef07e26135006427523ad77e32.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13864940" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将鸡绞肉分成 4 份，用手整理成饼状。取一炒锅， 锅热、油热后，放入鸡肉饼，将两面煎至金黄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13864941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13864941" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864941-8559aa1680f3248eb7c66730dc9ddf5f.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13864941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864941-8559aa1680f3248eb7c66730dc9ddf5f.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13864941" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.鸡肉饼煎熟定型之后，加入南瓜。将水、味醂、酱油与砂糖调成酱汁，倒入锅中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13864942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13864942" style="width: 442px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864942-15f194b0fb81a4b69c869f2d86f673af.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13864942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864942-15f194b0fb81a4b69c869f2d86f673af.jpg" alt="" width="442" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13864942" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.酱汁煮滚后，盖上锅盖，改小火，炖煮15 分钟即可。⊙◇</p>
