<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【超市食材料理】酸甜番茄鸡腿排</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864945-bb62f7fb72989dab93d6b9617f921857-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>酸甜番茄鸡腿排。（出色文化出版社）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>食欲不佳时，酸甜调味比较能提振我的胃口，而且加了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>的料理都很美丽，也是我偏爱它的原因。每次煎鸡腿排时，我都很珍惜鸡皮煎出来的香香鸡油，用来炒其他食材，能让整道菜味道更足。</p>
<p>这道用牛<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>和酱料简单做出的酸甜口味鸡腿排，酱汁浓郁，还没端上桌，我心里就喊着“开动”。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%B8%E7%94%9C%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84%E9%B8%A1%E8%85%BF%E6%8E%92.md#1">酸甜番茄鸡腿排</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>鸡腿肉（或无骨鸡腿排）……1 片<br />
牛番茄……3 颗，切块<br />
蒜末……适量<br />
番茄酱……1 大匙<br />
白醋……1 大匙<br />
酱油……1 大匙<br />
砂糖……2 茶匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_13864946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13864946" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864946-7c0ae5cd8205568316f05829276a2c07.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13864946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864946-7c0ae5cd8205568316f05829276a2c07.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13864946" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1.取一炒锅，锅热、油热后，放入蒜末爆香。保持中小火，将鸡腿肉下锅，先煎表皮，再翻面煎。改小火慢煎，将鸡腿肉煎熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13864947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13864947" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864947-ba6c3c16c70b17e570eae0b0d38d51d1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13864947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864947-ba6c3c16c70b17e570eae0b0d38d51d1.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13864947" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将牛番茄下锅翻炒一下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13864948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13864948" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864948-910841df21b6d0f708d2900f2e9948fa.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13864948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864948-910841df21b6d0f708d2900f2e9948fa.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13864948" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.盖上锅盖，将牛番茄煮到变软并出水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13864949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13864949" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864949-37a2a3fe7d93b5c0f59334c31e8ae329.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13864949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13864949-37a2a3fe7d93b5c0f59334c31e8ae329.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13864949" class="wp-caption-text">（出色文化出版社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.加入番茄酱、白醋、酱油、砂糖，与锅中所有材料拌炒均匀。⊙◇</p>
