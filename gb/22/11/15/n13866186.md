<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>李准基、朴恩斌等发行2023 Season&#8217;s Greeting</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13866225-221115032958100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>演员李准基、朴恩斌、徐玄、宋江推出2023 Season's Greeting。（Namoo Actors提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年11月15日讯】（大纪元记者乐慧萱编译报导）14日，经纪公司Namoo Actors公开了旗下艺人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%87%86%E5%9F%BA.md#1">李准基</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E6%81%A9%E6%96%8C.md#1">朴恩斌</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E7%8E%84.md#1">徐玄</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B1%9F.md#1">宋江</a>的Season&#8217;s Greeting预告海报和视频，同时宣告了2023年即将开始。</p>
<p>首先，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%87%86%E5%9F%BA.md#1">李准基</a>的海报以红色和蓝色为背景，将他多变的形象与8种颜色相结合的概念，突出了感性的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13866231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13866231" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13866231-221115032955100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13866231" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13866231-221115032955100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13866231" class="wp-caption-text">演员李准基推出2023 Season\&#8217;s Greeting。（Namoo Actors提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E6%81%A9%E6%96%8C.md#1">朴恩斌</a>的Season&#8217;s Greeting以“四季”为主题，为“Everyday，a Bright day”，不仅展现了甜美的青涩感，还呈现了清纯、成熟、可爱等模样，让人心情愉悦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13866232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13866232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13866232-221115032953100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13866232" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13866232-221115032953100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13866232" class="wp-caption-text">演员朴恩斌推出2023 Season\&#8217;s Greeting。（Namoo Actors提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E7%8E%84.md#1">徐玄</a>的主题是“经典&amp;中性”，从永恒的经典之美到精致干练的中性装扮都包含其中，展现了迷人和优雅的气质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13866229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13866229" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13866229-221115033001100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13866229" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13866229-221115033001100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13866229" class="wp-caption-text">演员徐玄推出2023 Season\&#8217;s Greeting。（Namoo Actors提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B1%9F.md#1">宋江</a>穿着舒服的运动造型看似调皮的模样，又以冰冷的黑白氛围传达了慵懒的性感美。画报上“Sketch”寓意着“宋江拥有耀眼青春的记录”，“SK”也是宋江名字首字母的缩写。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13866234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13866234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13866234-221115033004100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13866234" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13866234-221115033004100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13866234" class="wp-caption-text">演员宋江推出2023 Season\&#8217;s Greeting。（Namoo Actors提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李准基、朴恩斌、徐玄、宋江的Season&#8217;s Greeting已于14日通过Namoo Actors官网公开。</p>
