<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>朴敏英接代言再访台 应媒体要求可爱10连拍</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870253-2211210819251487.jpg" />
<h6>2022年11月21日，朴敏英为台湾服饰品牌访台，在台北出席服饰品牌上市记者会，摆出可爱的pose让媒体拍照。（黄宗茂／大纪元制图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年11月21日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）韩国女星朴敏英为服饰品牌访台，今（21）日在台北出席上市记者会，相隔三年再度访台，笑说每次来都是因为工作，听说这里的夜市很好玩，希望此行有时间能出去走一走。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870310" style="width: 348px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870310-2211210642341538.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870310" title="朴敏英" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870310-2211210642341538.jpg" alt="朴敏英" width="348" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870310" class="wp-caption-text">2022年11月21日，朴敏英为台湾服饰品牌访台，在台北出席服饰品牌上市记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13870318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870318" style="width: 373px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870318-2211210642271538.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870318" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870318-2211210642271538.jpg" alt="" width="373" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870318" class="wp-caption-text">朴敏英为台湾服饰品牌访台，在台北出席服饰品牌上市记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近期以韩剧《月水金火木土》受到关注的朴敏英，在剧中展现万事通的形象，让粉丝看见朴敏英的各种风貌，而今在发表会现场，主持人黄子佼特别准备问题，从穿搭技巧到工作生活，无一不问。朴敏英也提及自己曾梦想成为设计师，因此对时尚充满热情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870312" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870312-2211210915252188.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870312" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870312-2211210915252188-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870312" class="wp-caption-text">朴敏英为台湾服饰品牌访台，在台北出席服饰品牌上市记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13870259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870259" style="width: 457px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870259-DSC_2855.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13870259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870259-DSC_2855-600x755.jpg" alt="朴敏英" width="457" b="575" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870259" class="wp-caption-text">2022年11月21日，朴敏英为台湾服饰品牌访台，在台北出席服饰品牌上市记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>36岁的朴敏英至今累积不少代表作，包含《成均馆绯闻》、《城市猎人》、《Healer治愈者》、《七日的王妃》、《金秘书为何那样》等，今年也推出《气象厅的人们》、《月水金火木土》两部新作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870307" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870307-2211210915152188.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870307" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870307-2211210915152188-600x836.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="836" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870307" class="wp-caption-text">2022年11月21日，朴敏英为台湾服饰品牌访台，在台北出席服饰品牌上市记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>相隔3年再次访台，她今日穿着粉色外套现身记者会，并透露自己很怕冷，平时待在片场也需要穿外套，刚好她开始对羽绒外套很关注，就接到台湾品牌的代言机会，甜笑说：“可能是缘分到了，终于该来的来了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870309-2211210916102188.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870309" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870309-2211210916102188-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870309" class="wp-caption-text">朴敏英访台北出席服饰品牌上市记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13870317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870317" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870317-2211210915182188.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870317" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870317-2211210915182188.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870317" class="wp-caption-text">朴敏英访台北出席服饰品牌上市记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于最近剧组工作都已杀青，朴敏英表示需要休息一下，才能再接作品，而明天拍完代言广告后，她预告想趁机会到夜市逛逛。笑说已经换好新台币，希望可以吃美食为自己充电。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870316" style="width: 412px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870316-2211210913242188.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870316" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870316-2211210913242188.jpg" alt="" width="412" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870316" class="wp-caption-text">朴敏英访台北出席服饰品牌上市记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13870308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870308-2211210927401538.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870308" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870308-2211210927401538-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870308" class="wp-caption-text">朴敏英出席服饰品牌上市记者会，应媒体要求完成可爱十连拍。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后她也应媒体要求完成可爱十连拍，并用中文预祝大家“圣诞快乐！”、“新年快乐！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870314" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870314-2211210916202188.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870314" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870314-2211210916202188-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870314" class="wp-caption-text">朴敏英出席服饰品牌上市记者会，应媒体要求完成可爱十连拍。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
