<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉大教师研发国产发酵豆乳 现场手作体验</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870160-586399-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉大校长林翰谦 (左6)、农粮署南区分署分署长罗正宗 (左5)与参与贵宾共同推广国产发酵黑豆乳。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年11月21日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">嘉义大学</a>今（21）日下午于兰潭校区员生消费合作社前举办第一支<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%A7.md#1">国产</a>发酵<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E4%B9%B3.md#1">豆乳</a>暨食农教育活动，食科系教师杨怀文研发第一支<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%A7.md#1">国产</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E9%85%B5%E8%B1%86%E4%B9%B3.md#1">发酵豆乳</a>，其风味及安全性受到专业的肯定，校长林翰谦期许还有第二、第三支问世。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870161" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870161-586400.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870161-586400-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870161" class="wp-caption-text">嘉大校长林翰谦致词。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>今日活动由嘉大校长林翰谦、行政院农业委员会农粮署南区分署分署长罗正宗及嘉农<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E4%BA%A7%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%A5.md#1">农产加工</a>生产合作社理事主席黄家均出席支持，欢迎消费大众一起品味100%在地契作生产，新鲜且安全的健康好滋味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870162-586401.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870162-586401-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870162" class="wp-caption-text">行政院农业委员会农粮署南区分署分署长罗正宗致词。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">嘉义大学</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林翰谦校长表示，推动在地农业地产地消不但可减少碳排，更能透过由“农场”到“餐桌”的理念，让消费者买到新鲜、安全的农产品。嘉大食品科学系教师杨怀文严选国产大豆品种，精挑乳酸菌种，挑选最佳组合的豆种与菌种，将国产大豆豆浆再晋级，研发第一支国产发酵<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E4%B9%B3.md#1">豆乳</a>，让更多微量元素能被人体细胞吸收，除了风味佳，更能提升身体抵抗力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870163" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870163-586402.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870163-586402-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870163" class="wp-caption-text">媒体联访。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天除提供与会者品尝第一支国产<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E9%85%B5%E8%B1%86%E4%B9%B3.md#1">发酵豆乳</a>，主办单位为让大家更了解地产地消的在地风味，地产地消是以“当地生产、当地消费”为目标，缩短运输距离，确保农产品新鲜，保证食品安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870164" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870164-586403.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870164-586403-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870164" class="wp-caption-text">第一支国产发酵黑豆乳。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也提供手作体验教育，让大家学习如何自制发酵豆乳，体验最原始的发酵风味，吸引多位民众带着小宝贝来参与体验。将各类水果分别加入发酵黑豆乳，可提升感官敏锐度，品尝出更多风味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870165-586404.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870165-586404-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870165" class="wp-caption-text">嘉大教师将国产黑豆浆（左）再晋级，研发第一支国产发酵黑豆乳（右）。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗正宗分署长表示，国产大豆非基改且新鲜，含有丰富的钙质、卵磷脂、异黄酮及不饱和脂肪等营养成分，今天推出的发酵黑豆乳为100%具产销履历国产豆奶产品，经第三方验证机构认证，兼顾新鲜、营养。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870166-586405.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870166-586405-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870166" class="wp-caption-text">食农教育活动吸引大朋友小朋友一起体验手做发酵黑豆乳。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近年健康养生生活习惯盛行，为提倡环境保护，减少碳排，嘉大教师发挥丰沛的研究量能，辅助小农开发各项优质且健康的农特产品，今天发表的国产发酵豆乳经过多次试验，以最适温度与时间制作，让国产发酵豆乳风味及口味都能符合大众喜爱，喝到好品质好健康的在地好味道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870167-586406.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870167-586406-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870167" class="wp-caption-text">第一支国产发酵黑豆乳新鲜安全且健康非常适合全家大小饮用。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13870168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870168-586407.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870168-586407-600x560.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="560" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870168" class="wp-caption-text">两岁半的小朋友在遇到第一支国产发酵黑豆乳时也被征服了，开始与其对话。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13870169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870169-586408.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870169-586408-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870169" class="wp-caption-text">本是同根生，毛豆8分熟，成熟后依种皮颜色分为黑豆及黄豆，统称大豆。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
