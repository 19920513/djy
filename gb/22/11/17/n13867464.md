<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台商会秋季烤肉 会员联谊其乐融融</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13867468-8c2a3cfb00e7e9ef11f9c0cf536b2894-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>休士顿台商会2022秋季烤肉活动大合影。（图／TCC提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年11月17日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%91%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">休士顿台湾商会</a>（TCC）于11月13日（星期日）在Eldridge Park举办秋季户外郊游<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>活动，借着活动凝聚台商乡亲，同时丰富休闲生活，经文处副处长王韦龙夫妇、政治组副组长韦天翔，组长张家华及侨教中心副主任黄依莉等应邀出席活动，台商会会员、眷属和乡亲朋友数十人共襄盛举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13867469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13867469" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13867469-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13867469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13867469-02-600x452.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13867469" class="wp-caption-text">由右至左为张园立，李雄，周政贤，吕明雪，谭秋晴和陈适中合影。（图／TCC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会长张园立表示，理事团队数人上月以海外侨团代表返台参加双十国庆之盛会，月底规划此次活动，宣传时间仓促，也考虑到要准备大量的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>食材，依照惯例都由荣誉副会长周政贤及夫人吕明雪及理事精心备料腌制，为体恤他们的辛劳，所以此次活动规格较小，再加上活动前一日，气温骤降，参与人数比旧年略少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13867470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13867470" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13867470-T03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13867470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13867470-T03-600x452.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13867470" class="wp-caption-text">林富桂（右二）和夫婿Michael （左二）及王韦龙（左一）夫妇爱狗家族。（图／TCC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当日依旧是闻名的休士顿台商会独家腌制BBQ肉类牛小排，鸡腿和猪排；好吃的台式沙拉，马铃薯沙拉等一应俱全。还有从不缺席的味全休士顿分公司总经理李乐罡，提供好喝的饮料果汁等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13867471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13867471" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13867471-T04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13867471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13867471-T04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13867471" class="wp-caption-text">北美教授协会理事郭正泉及夫人林翆梅，与周政贤合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13867472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13867472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13867472-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13867472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13867472-05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13867472" class="wp-caption-text">由左至右是副会长徐世钢，黄荣富及夫人合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天天气虽冷，但阳光普照，前会长李雄、张智嘉，资深会员黄荣富、郭正泉及吴而立等夫妇连袂出席，担任斯坦福市议员的会员谭秋晴也和夫婿陈适中一起出席；会员们也有带着小朋友一起参加，主办单位也准备了适合小朋友玩的球类，飞盘等。</p>
