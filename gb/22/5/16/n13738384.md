<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义县长视察防疫门诊运作情形</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738386-555432-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。（嘉义县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月16日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义县强化医疗整备应变，4大医院医疗营运降载，急诊室外设置<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB%E9%97%A8%E8%AF%8A.md#1">防疫门诊</a>，分流及保全医疗量能，提供户外药来速及启动24小时儿童就医绿色通道，61家基层诊所加入视讯诊疗并提供假日时段医疗服务。</p>
<figure id="13738388" aria-describedby="caption-13738388" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738388-555434-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738388-555434-450x300.jpeg" alt="嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13738388" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长翁章梁视察<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB%E9%97%A8%E8%AF%8A.md#1">防疫门诊</a>运作情形。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长翁章梁16日上午视察嘉义长庚医院防疫门诊运作情形，指示增设服务时段、强化儿童就医绿色通道，务必让医疗服务通畅，守护县民健康。</p>
<figure id="13738389" aria-describedby="caption-13738389" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738389-555435-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738389-555435-450x300.jpeg" alt="嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13738389" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>翁章梁视察嘉义长庚医院户外设置防疫门诊情形，由院长杨仁宗说明执行流程。翁章梁表示，为避免急诊壅塞及交叉感染，提升就诊速度，长庚、慈济、朴子和湾桥4大医院开设防疫门诊，延长服务时间及加开假日时段，畅通医疗服务，兼顾防疫及便利性。</p>
<figure id="13738390" aria-describedby="caption-13738390" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738390-555436-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738390-555436-450x300.jpeg" alt="嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13738390" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>翁章梁提醒民众如有疑似染疫症状轻微者、快筛阳者需进行PCR采检，可利用防疫门诊提供快速诊疗服务，并依症状给予药物治疗。</p>
<figure id="13738391" aria-describedby="caption-13738391" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738391-555437-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738391-555437-450x300.jpeg" alt="嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13738391" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫生局局长赵纹华指出，为全力防堵疫情，维持医疗量能，避免民众大量涌入医院急诊影响医疗正常运作，4大医院已开设户外防疫门诊，嘉义长庚医院原每天上午开设，现增加周六、日时段，大林慈济医院将延长至下午，朴子医院及台中荣总湾桥分院平日上午提供防疫门诊。</p>
<figure id="13738392" aria-describedby="caption-13738392" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738392-555438-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738392-555438-450x300.jpeg" alt="嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。" width="449" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13738392" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，为优先照顾幼童，嘉义长庚和大林慈济医院开设24小时儿童就医绿色通道(防疫急门诊)，湾桥医院每周二至周四提供儿童就医绿色通道，由儿科专科医师看诊，提供学龄前儿童诊疗服务。61家基层诊所除平日外，也增加假日时段医疗服务，让有需求的乡亲免于医院奔波。</p>
<figure id="13738387" aria-describedby="caption-13738387" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738387-555433-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738387-555433-450x300.jpeg" alt="嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。" width="449" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13738387" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长翁章梁视察防疫门诊运作情形。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13738385" aria-describedby="caption-13738385" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738385-555431-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738385-555431-450x450.jpg" alt="嘉义县各医院防疫门诊。" width="451" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13738385" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县各医院防疫门诊。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
