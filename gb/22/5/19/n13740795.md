<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多儿童节本周末三天 30多种游乐设施等你来</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740988-20224-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大量户外活动景点。
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月19日】进入户外节日季了！本周<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%A4%9A%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A%E6%97%A5.md#1">维多利亚日</a>（Victoria Day）长周末三天，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E8%8A%82.md#1">多伦多儿童节</a>（KidsFest TO）春季场，将把<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%87%E9%94%A6%E5%B8%82%E5%B1%95%E8%A7%88%E4%BC%9A%E5%9C%BA.md#1">万锦市展览会场</a>（Markham Fairgrounds）变身为超级儿童乐园！<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/22/5/19/n13740795.md#1/daabb34c02164c817a64203d40f977e4" rel="attachment wp-att-13740987"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-13740987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740987-daabb34c02164c817a64203d40f977e4-450x198.png" alt="" width="768" b="338" /></a>超过30种游乐设施和景点等你来！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13740852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13740852" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/22/5/19/n13740795.md#1/attachment/20222" rel="attachment wp-att-13740852"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13740852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740852-20222-450x475.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="475" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13740852" class="wp-caption-text">大量的户外活动景点。</figcaption></figure>
<p>大人、孩子可尽情享受跳跃城堡、低龄儿童游乐场、宠物园、有趣的食物和更多！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13740848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13740848" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/22/5/19/n13740795.md#1/attachment/20221" rel="attachment wp-att-13740848"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13740848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740848-20221-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13740848" class="wp-caption-text">大量的室内活动景点。</figcaption></figure>
<p>最大亮点是超级狗，无论家长还是孩子，都会惊讶于这些宠物狗的精彩表演！</p>
<p><ahref="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/%E5%BF%AB%E8%8A%82%E5%A5%8F%E3%80%81%E4%BA%92%E5%8A%A8%E3%80%81%E6%9A%96%E5%BF%83%E5%8F%88%E6%90%9E%E7%AC%91%EF%BC%8C%E4%BD%93%E9%AA%8C%E8%BF%99%E4%BA%9B%E7%A5%9E%E5%A5%87%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97%E7%9A%84%E4%B9%90%E8%B6%A3.mp4?_=1">快节奏、互动、暖心又搞笑，体验这些神奇狗狗的乐趣！</a></p>

<p>还有，不要错过与可爱的吉祥物见面，并在巨大的充气照相场拍摄温馨的家庭照片！</p>
