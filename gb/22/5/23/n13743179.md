<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>探宝良机！澳洲顶级古董展重返墨尔本</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743379-Valentines-Antique-Gallery_AAADA-600x400.jpg" />

<p>【大纪元2022年05月23日讯】（澳洲<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>工商讯）2022年，股市动荡、楼市不稳，哪里可以捡到宝？当然是<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="http://aaada.org.au/melbourne" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">AAADA Antiques &amp; Art Fair古董艺术展</a></span>！</p>
<p>经过疫情的洗礼，“美妙的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>”终于恢复如常。备受瞩目的AAADA<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E8%91%A3.md#1">古董</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">艺术</a>展终于重返墨尔本，将在6月莅临莫尔文市政厅（Malvern Town Hall）。</p>
<p>AAADA<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E8%91%A3.md#1">古董</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">艺术</a>展是澳洲顶级艺术文化盛会，由澳洲古董和艺术品商协会（AAADA, The Australian Antique &amp; Art Dealers Association）主办。本届为期四天的展会将为各界人士提供无与伦比的收藏体验。</p>
<p>AAADA是代表澳大利亚古董和艺术经销商的领先行业机构，政府机构和收藏家协会通常就所有与古董和艺术有关的事宜咨询AAADA，其会员致力于促进澳大利亚社区对古董和艺术的理解，并提供专业建议。AAADA的所有会员都必须遵守严格的执业道德规范（code of ethics），因此每届展会上，收藏者和投资者都会蜂拥而至，放心大胆地寻获宝物。</p>

<h2>展会亮点：</h2>
<p><strong>古腾堡《圣经》中的一页（A leaf from the Gutenberg Bible）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743368" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743368-Douglas-Stewart-Fine-Books_AAADA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13743368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743368-Douglas-Stewart-Fine-Books_AAADA-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743368" class="wp-caption-text">DOUGLAS STEWART FINE BOOKS《圣经》中的一页。（AAADA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古董商：道格拉斯·斯图尔特善本商（DOUGLAS STEWART FINE BOOKS）<br />
制作者：约翰内斯·古腾堡 （Johannes Gutenberg，约1398–1468），在西方历史上被誉为活字印刷之父，曾于1455年用活字印刷机首次印刷了《圣经》。</p>
<p><strong>银制鼻烟盒（Presentation Silver Snuff Box）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743369" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743369-J.B.-Hawkins-Antiques_AAADA.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13743369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743369-J.B.-Hawkins-Antiques_AAADA-600x600.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743369" class="wp-caption-text">J.B.HAWKINS ANTIQUES银制鼻烟盒。（AAADA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古董商：J.B.霍金斯古董（J.B. HAWKINS ANTIQUES）</p>
<p>以路易十四为代表的欧洲各国国王，曾热衷于延请最出色的珠宝工匠打造盒子，赐予为国家倾力奉献的贵族或大臣。这种盒子在历史上被称为鼻烟盒“presentation snuff box”。</p>
<p>本次展会上，J.B.霍金斯古董就带来了这样一个难得一见且流传有序的银制鼻烟盒，由Joseph Forester于霍巴特（Hobart）在David Barclay工作室制造。</p>
<p>该银制鼻烟盒由Alexander Dick于1833年2月1日从诺福克岛（Norfolk Island）获释后不久在悉尼零售，并于1833年4月23日对外展示。本品长7厘米、宽5厘米、高1.7厘米，重108 克。</p>
<p><strong>澳洲雪松扶手椅（Australian cedar armchairs）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743370" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743370-Lauder-Howard-Antiques_AAADA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13743370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743370-Lauder-Howard-Antiques_AAADA-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743370" class="wp-caption-text">Lauder&amp;Howard Antiques扶手椅。（AAADA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古董商：劳德和霍华德古董（LAUDER &amp; HOWARD ANTIQUES）</p>
<p>这是两把大约制造于1840年的澳洲雪松扶手椅，出自霍巴特的约翰·麦克劳林（John McLoughlin of Hobart）之手。</p>
<p><strong>盒装古董黄金项链（Boxed Antique Gold Collar）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743372" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743372-Rutherford_AAADA.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13743372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743372-Rutherford_AAADA-600x600.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743372" class="wp-caption-text">Rutherford古董黄金项链。（AAADA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古董商：卢瑟福（RUTHERFORD）<br />
制作者：Edward &amp; Sons of Glasgow &amp; London</p>
<p>这是一件15克拉花卉纹饰古董黄金项链，配有原装首饰盒。制作时间约为1900年。总长度：40厘米。</p>
<p><strong>澳洲殖民时期银质船桨型奖品（An Australian Colonial silver rowing trophy）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743374-Trinity-Antiques_AAADA-Trophy.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13743374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743374-Trinity-Antiques_AAADA-Trophy-600x384.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="384" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743374" class="wp-caption-text">Trinity Antiques澳洲殖民时期银质船桨型奖品。（AAADA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古董商：三一古董（TRINITY ANTIQUES）</p>
<p>这支船桨镶有金色纹质，Houstone认为它是由吉朗（Geelong）的Edward Fischer制作的。这件古董曾于1876年在墨尔本帆船赛上展出。</p>
<p><strong>稀缺瓷器</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743376" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743376-Moorabool-Antique-Galleries_AAADA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13743376" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743376-Moorabool-Antique-Galleries_AAADA-600x501.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="501" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743376" class="wp-caption-text">Moorabool Antique Galleries稀缺瓷器。（AAADA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古董商：MOORABOOL古董（MOORABOOL ANTIQUE GALLERIES）</p>
<p>这是来自两家欧洲瓷器厂成立初期的几件作品，极具稀缺性。</p>
<p>韦格利时期（Wegely period）的瓷器人物，大约制于1755年，是柏林瓷器厂（Berlin porcelain factory）的早期作品。</p>
<p>另有杜帕奎尔（Du Paquier）瓷器厂的茶杯和碟子，大约制于1725年，是维也纳瓷器厂（Vienna factory）的早期作品。</p>
<p><strong>“洪流” 台钟三件套（Le Torrent）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743379-Valentines-Antique-Gallery_AAADA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13743379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743379-Valentines-Antique-Gallery_AAADA-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743379" class="wp-caption-text">Valentine&#8217;s Antique Gallery台钟三件套。（AAADA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古董商：瓦伦丁古董（VALENTINE&#8217;S ANTIQUE GALLERY）</p>
<p>这是一件铜制壁炉台钟三件套装8天钟（3 Piece Bronzed 8 Day Mantle Clock Set），属于新工艺美术风格， 由Julien Causse制作于1904年前后。</p>
