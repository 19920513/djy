<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>踩场“老屋”吃母亲节特餐 Vicky：想每集都来</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13730070-2205080247041487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《来吧！营业中》推出“母亲节特餐”，邀来屈中恒、黄小柔及Darren邱凯伟三组嘉宾带着家人前来用餐。图左四为屈中恒老婆Vicky。（TVBS提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月08日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）实境节目《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A5%E5%90%A7%EF%BC%81%E8%90%A5%E4%B8%9A%E4%B8%AD.md#1">来吧！营业中</a>》本周播出“老屋”量身打造出“母亲节特餐”，还特别派出“孩子王”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%98%A0%E6%B4%81.md#1">吴映洁</a>跟<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A2%9C%E4%BD%91%E5%BA%AD.md#1">颜佑庭</a>陪小孩玩耍，让妈妈们好好享受母亲节大餐。</p>
<p>本周屈中恒跟老婆Vicky带着女儿、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%B0%8F%E6%9F%94.md#1">黄小柔</a>跟丈夫王睿带着儿女一同前来老屋用餐，平时照顾小孩很需要放松的黄小柔跟Vicky，一到餐厅就透露刚刚在来餐厅的路上快崩溃，迫不及待地说：“不然先上酒好了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13730071" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13730071" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13730071-2205080246541487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13730071" title="来吧！营业中 庹宗康 屈宗恒" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13730071-2205080246541487-600x416.jpg" alt="来吧！营业中 庹宗康 屈宗恒" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13730071" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A5%E5%90%A7%EF%BC%81%E8%90%A5%E4%B8%9A%E4%B8%AD.md#1">来吧！营业中</a>》推出母亲节特餐，屈宗恒带着老婆跟小孩来庆祝母亲节。图左为店长庹宗康。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Vicky首次看到炎亚纶本人，立刻少女心喷发说：“看到你本人我好开心。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13730072" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13730072" style="width: 447px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13730072-2205080246591487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13730072" title="来吧！营业中" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13730072-2205080246591487.jpg" alt="来吧！营业中" width="447" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13730072" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%B0%8F%E6%9F%94.md#1">黄小柔</a>跟Vicky对老屋赞不绝口。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了能让妈妈们放松，“孩子王”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%98%A0%E6%B4%81.md#1">吴映洁</a>跟<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A2%9C%E4%BD%91%E5%BA%AD.md#1">颜佑庭</a>，陪着现场的小孩们画画、玩游戏，炎亚纶也闲不下来，首次挑战“洗奶瓶”，一旁的吴映洁则笑他：“炎亚纶洗奶瓶是奇迹。”</p>
<p>这次老屋推出“母亲节特餐”，高规格的服务让Vicky跟黄小柔说：“好想每集都来。”一旁姚元浩听了也很欣慰，觉得这是“老屋”的骄傲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13730074" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13730074" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13730074-2205080246571487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13730074" title="来吧！营业中" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13730074-2205080246571487.jpg" alt="来吧！营业中" width="448" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13730074" class="wp-caption-text">黄小柔跟丈夫王睿在《来吧！营业中》中的老屋合影。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>店长庹宗康也提醒大家，有时间有机会，一定要抱抱妈妈跟老婆，感谢她们为家庭的付出。</p>
