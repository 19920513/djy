<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>花莲平地金针花缤纷盛开 线上赏花疗愈疫情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726963-553503-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>花莲吉安嘉德花田金针花盛开，吸引民众驻足欣赏，成为拍照打卡景点。（詹亦菱／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月04日讯】（大纪元记者詹亦菱台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E8%8E%B2.md#1">花莲</a>报导）每年在4、5月间，春末夏初天气逐渐转热，是百花盛开季；有着母亲花别称的金针“萱草”，近日随着母亲节的到来朵朵灿烂绽放。花莲吉安乡“嘉德花田”花田内种植高达1.5万株的萱草，金黄相间艳红的花朵，园主脸书粉丝专页上，提供免费线上赏花；在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%96%AB%E6%83%85.md#1">疫情</a>严峻下，缤纷的花海，能带给大家<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%96%97%E6%84%88.md#1">疗愈</a>力量。</p>
<p>位于吉安乡广丰路及广贤二街交叉口的嘉德花田，在4分地的园区内种植万株花朵，包含萱草、玫瑰花、孤挺花、蜀葵花、紫藤花、吊钟花及马齿苋等，每年5月壮观景色相当吸睛，园主曾永坤8年来皆免费参观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13726968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13726968" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726968-553519.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13726968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726968-553519-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13726968" class="wp-caption-text">5号黄天鹅（黄色、花型大）（詹亦菱／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13726967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13726967" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726967-553518.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13726967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726967-553518-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13726967" class="wp-caption-text">4号俏佳人，花瓣红色、色彩艳丽、美艳动人。（詹亦菱／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曾永坤说，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%96%AB%E6%83%85.md#1">疫情</a>虽严重，但欢迎民众踏青赏花来透气，今年种植有8个品种<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%92%88%E8%8A%B1.md#1">金针花</a>，陆续种植萱草1号粉佳人、2号艳红佳人、3号甜蜜佳人、4号俏佳人及5号黄天鹅，目前园区内近1万5千株，4月中旬已开始开花，现在变一片美丽花海，将持续盛开到6月。</p>
<p>一入园区，首先映入眼帘是扑满地，黄、红、橘红与白等不同颜色的马齿苋，曾永坤提到，花田有60多种花草植物，如桂叶黄梅（米老鼠树）、鸢尾花（白色、紫色）、玫瑰花、孤挺花、扶桑花、黄金树牡丹等。而玫瑰花墙与拱门上，则开满各种不同颜色玫瑰，姹紫嫣红、绚丽色彩令人着迷。曾永坤说，花园玫瑰有20个不同品种。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13726965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13726965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726965-553516.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13726965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726965-553516-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13726965" class="wp-caption-text">1号粉佳人、可爱动人。（詹亦菱／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13726966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13726966" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726966-553517.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13726966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726966-553517-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13726966" class="wp-caption-text">3号甜蜜佳人（双色花）（詹亦菱／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13726964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13726964" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726964-553514.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13726964" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726964-553514-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13726964" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E8%8E%B2.md#1">花莲</a>吉安嘉德花田<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%92%88%E8%8A%B1.md#1">金针花</a>盛开，吸引民众驻足欣赏，成为拍照打卡景点。（詹亦菱／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于嘉德花田园区历年来都开放给民众免费参观，另外对外有贩售花苗。花田管理员说，花田有出售玫瑰花、孤挺花、玫瑰花、金针花等。花田土地肥沃，不论什么花草用对肥料，就会长得很漂亮，有提供民众买培养土及花肥回家种花。</p>
