<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美国警官和拥有数百万粉丝的勇敢警犬</h2>
<a href="https://dp5vkf7bbqo9d.cloudfront.net/M2qkCJQzu"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>马克?塔潘（Mark Tappan）警官和警犬马蒂斯（Mattis）共同破案、侦查罪恶、抓捕坏人、拯救生命。(Courtesy of Mark Tappan)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年07月26日讯】（英文大纪元记者E. S. Armstrong报导／李宇君编译）在马克?塔潘（Mark Tappan）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E5%AE%98.md#1">警官</a>的心中，警犬马蒂斯（Mattis）是不可思议的工作伙伴。他们共同破案、侦查罪恶、抓捕坏人、拯救生命。</p>
<p>马蒂斯除了获得紫心勋章外，根据美国养犬俱乐部（the American Kennel Club）记录，它曾在毒品追缉中，帮忙追回300万美元；追踪成功20起案子，并协助过200多次逮捕罪犯。</p>
<p>多年来，这只名犬和他主人在Instagram上有数千名粉丝，在TikTok（抖音国际版）上拥有数百万粉丝。塔潘和马蒂斯还参与了许多慈善活动，为Coins4CURE筹集超过10万美元，帮助对抗儿童癌症。他们还支持了乔治亚警察K9基金会和K9英雄等执法项目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13789415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13789415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789415-mattis1.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13789415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789415-mattis1-450x563.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13789415" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Mark Tappan)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13789418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13789418" style="width: 594px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789418-Mattis8.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13789418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789418-Mattis8-450x288.jpeg" alt="" width="594" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13789418" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Mark Tappan)</figcaption></figure>
<p>塔潘和马蒂斯2016年开始合作，一年后，在他们追缉两名嫌犯时，嫌犯跳过了一座30英尺（9.1公尺）高的挡土墙。当塔潘正要跟着跳过时，他紧急煞住了。然而，马蒂斯越过去了。当塔潘看着狗狗弓身倒地时，他觉得自己应该对马蒂斯的生命负全部责任。</p>
<p>但勇敢的马蒂斯起身解决一名嫌犯后，又追逐并逮捕了另一人。随后，便昏倒在地。塔潘把狗狗送到兽医院，马蒂斯肝脏撕裂、重伤和严重内出血。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13789420" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13789420" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789420-Mattis10.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13789420" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789420-Mattis10-450x563.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13789420" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Mark Tappan)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13789426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13789426" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789426-mattis11.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13789426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789426-mattis11-450x563.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13789426" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Mark Tappan)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我很害怕它会死”，中士塔潘告诉《大纪元时报》，“这对我来说真是太痛苦了。”</p>
<p>幸运的是，马蒂斯在受伤一个月后，又重返工作岗位。</p>
<p>马蒂斯获得了紫心勋章，名声也很快传开了。它和塔潘还获得了许多奖项，马蒂斯曾在HBO电视剧《守望者》（Watchmen）中出现，它和塔潘在“全美超级警犬”（America&#8217;s Top Dog）中获胜，并将继续参加冠军比赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13789427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13789427" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789427-mattis14.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13789427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789427-mattis14-450x563.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13789427" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Mark Tappan)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13789428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13789428" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789428-mattis-15.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13789428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789428-mattis-15-450x562.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13789428" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Mark Tappan)</figcaption></figure>

<p>塔潘写了两本关于马蒂斯的儿童读物——《我的狗马蒂斯》和《我的狗马蒂斯和赤脚土匪》——书中讲述了他们共同经历的不可思议的冒险。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13789430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13789430" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789430-mattis21.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13789430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789430-mattis21-450x563.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13789430" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Mark Tappan)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13789432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13789432" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789432-mattis23.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13789432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789432-mattis23-450x450.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13789432" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Mark Tappan)</figcaption></figure>
<p>后来，由于一条后腿受伤，马蒂斯退役了。然而，它每天早上仍会跟着塔潘下楼。当塔潘离开时，它会在门口哭泣；那段没有马蒂斯的日子，令塔潘感到心碎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13789433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13789433" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789433-mattis-31.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13789433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13789433-mattis-31-450x563.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13789433" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Mark Tappan)</figcaption></figure>
