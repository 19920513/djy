<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>早产14周宝宝现6岁 向奶奶学习创业技巧</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785473-Kai-Ferguson-baby-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>凯?彿格森没有因为早产三个多月而受影响，已经开始跟奶奶一起创业。（Cathy Messer提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年07月20日讯】（英文大纪元记者LOUISE CHAMBERS报导／李宇君编译）凯?彿格森（Kai Ferguson）是个典型的巴掌宝宝，出生时不到800公克。她不但没有因为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A9%E4%BA%A7.md#1">早产</a>三个多月而受影响，反而在各方面表现超群。</p>
<p>六岁的小凯可爱活泼，精力旺盛，而且已经开始跟奶奶一起创业。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13785475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13785475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785475-ET-Kai-Ferguson-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13785475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785475-ET-Kai-Ferguson-2-450x710.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="710" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13785475" class="wp-caption-text">（Cathy Messer提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13785478" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13785478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785478-ET-Kai-Ferguson-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13785478" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785478-ET-Kai-Ferguson-13-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13785478" class="wp-caption-text">（Cathy Messer提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13785479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13785479" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785479-ET-Kai-Ferguson-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13785479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785479-ET-Kai-Ferguson-17-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13785479" class="wp-caption-text">（Cathy Messer提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小凯的奶奶凯茜?梅塞尔（Cathy Messer）64岁，开了一家披萨店。自2012年起，在阿肯色州中部<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E8%90%A5.md#1">经营</a>一个13英亩的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E5%9C%BA.md#1">农场</a>。</p>
<p>奶奶告诉《大纪元时报》，这个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E5%9C%BA.md#1">农场</a>叫中土农场（Middle Earth Farm），到处都是树林、牧区、池塘和花园，像是天堂的一角。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13785481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13785481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785481-ET-Cathy-Messer.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13785481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785481-ET-Cathy-Messer-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13785481" class="wp-caption-text">（左）凯与她的父母和弟弟；（右）凯和她的奶奶。（Cathy Messer提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>奶奶从孙儿们很小时就在下午照顾小凯和她弟弟。姊弟喜欢在农场中玩乐，在农场自然的环境中，他们远离了电脑、电视和电子游戏。</p>
<p>农场的生活让他们“学会欣赏简单的事物”，奶奶说，“我认为放慢脚步对他们有好处。”</p>
<p>小凯跟奶奶要了养蜂服来照顾蜂巢。在蜂巢从2个增加到9个后，小凯发现了一个秘密。</p>
<p>原来，蜂蜡是护唇膏的重要成分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13785486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13785486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785486-ET-Kai-Ferguson-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13785486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785486-ET-Kai-Ferguson-5-600x466.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="466" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13785486" class="wp-caption-text">（Cathy Messer提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在检查大块蜂蜡、品尝其中的蜂蜜时，奶奶告诉小凯，蜡也很有用。“小凯一直很喜欢ChapStick护唇膏”，奶奶说，“当我们检查蜂蜡时，我告诉她这是护唇膏的重要成分。”</p>
<p>祖孙俩在网上找到了一种简单的护唇膏制作方式，成分包含蜂蜡和蜂蜜。她们做出护唇膏后，小凯自己把它命名为“笑容闪耀”（Smile Shine）。她说因为擦了这护唇膏会让你笑容闪耀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13785488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13785488" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785488-ET-Kai-Ferguson-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13785488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785488-ET-Kai-Ferguson-8-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13785488" class="wp-caption-text">（Cathy Messer提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>护唇膏特别之处是标签上贴有小凯的照片。这些天然的护唇膏除了在亲朋好友和邻居之间一售而空之外，小凯不忘留一些给新生儿加护病房的护士。</p>
<p>在每年新生儿加护病房欢聚活动时，小凯兴奋地送给她的护士每人一支“笑容闪耀”，并从她卖出的500支唇膏中捐出500美元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13785489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13785489" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785489-ET-Kai-Ferguson-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13785489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785489-ET-Kai-Ferguson-3-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13785489" class="wp-caption-text">（Cathy Messer提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“热心的小凯非常想帮忙”，奶奶说，“新生儿加护病房护士收到捐赠非常惊讶，也非常高兴。更令她们印象深刻的是这个捐款来自过去的一位小病人！”</p>
<p>这对勤劳的祖孙希望等她们的蜜蜂制造更多的蜂蜡后，她们可以再制造更多的“笑容闪耀”护唇膏！</p>
