<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>基隆关新添3只缉毒犬 投入缉毒行列</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773950-563269-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>缉毒犬生力军Iso。（基隆关提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年07月05日讯】（大纪元记者陈秀媛台湾基隆报导）为维护人民身心健康，强化边境<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%92%E5%93%81.md#1">毒品</a>查缉量能，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%BA%E9%9A%86%E5%85%B3.md#1">基隆关</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BC%89%E6%AF%92%E7%8A%AC.md#1">缉毒犬</a>队增编3只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BC%89%E6%AF%92.md#1">缉毒</a>犬：Belle、Custy、Iso。该关表示，犬组扩编为10组，新生力军近期接受熟悉环境及加强基础训练，并即将投入缉毒行列，透过领犬员运用缉毒犬敏锐嗅觉及机动优势，有效打击<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%92%E5%93%81.md#1">毒品</a>走私，成为台湾边境<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BC%89%E6%AF%92.md#1">缉毒</a>精英部队。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13773951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13773951" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773951-563270.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13773951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773951-563270-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13773951" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BC%89%E6%AF%92%E7%8A%AC.md#1">缉毒犬</a>生力军Custy。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%BA%E9%9A%86%E5%85%B3.md#1">基隆关</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>基隆关指出，该关缉毒犬队近5年来查缉毒品绩效卓著，成功缉获多起毒品走私案件，缉获毒品总重量约近7公吨，成绩亮眼。未来海关除持续强化关员之专业训练，运用风险管理机制加强边境毒品查缉，并将培训更多精良犬只加入缉毒团队，藉由跨机关合作及参与反毒宣导活动等方式，期以达成拒毒于境外、截毒于关口之综效，进而维护社会治安，保障人民免于毒害。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13773952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13773952" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773952-563271.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13773952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773952-563271-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13773952" class="wp-caption-text">缉毒犬生力军Belle。（基隆关提供）</figcaption></figure>
