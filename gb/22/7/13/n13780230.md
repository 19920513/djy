<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>牛仔节手记：多姿多彩的牛仔</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780250-20220710-LinCaiFeng-cow-girls-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>靓丽的牛仔女郎。（林采枫／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年07月14日讯】（记者林采枫报导）在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">牛仔公园</a>，除了吃喝玩乐，看人绝对也是一大乐趣。每天十几万的游客，熙熙攘攘，形形色色，男男女女，老老少少， 欣欣然而共乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13780253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13780253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780253-20220710-LinCaiFeng-a-family-of-3-cowboys-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13780253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780253-20220710-LinCaiFeng-a-family-of-3-cowboys-10-600x431.jpg" alt="卡尔加里牛仔节" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13780253" class="wp-caption-text">一家三牛仔。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在这里，不说全副武装，但从头至脚，至少要穿着一样沾染牛仔气息的。最普遍的自然就是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E5%B8%BD.md#1">牛仔帽</a>了。牛仔帽，宽沿高顶，上部凹陷，两侧帽沿稍稍翻翘，不仅遮风、挡雨、阻雪、隔阳，在幕天席地的草原上，还可以拿来当作枕头，实在是用途多多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13780254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13780254" style="width: 457px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780254-20220710-LinCaiFeng-cowboy-hats-02.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13780254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780254-20220710-LinCaiFeng-cowboy-hats-02-600x456.jpg" alt="卡尔加里牛仔节" width="457" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13780254" class="wp-caption-text">多姿多彩的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E5%B8%BD.md#1">牛仔帽</a>。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13780255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13780255" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780255-20220710-LinCaiFeng-cowboy-hats-03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13780255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780255-20220710-LinCaiFeng-cowboy-hats-03-450x331.jpg" alt="卡尔加里牛仔节" width="450" b="331" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13780255" class="wp-caption-text">多姿多彩的牛仔帽。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13780256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13780256" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780256-20220710-LinCaiFeng-cowboy-hats-04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13780256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780256-20220710-LinCaiFeng-cowboy-hats-04-450x338.jpg" alt="卡尔加里牛仔节" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13780256" class="wp-caption-text">多姿多彩的牛仔帽。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不管是小少年，还是美娇娥，牛仔帽一戴，立刻显得英气潇洒，平空多了一丝驰骋草原、叱咤江湖的野性味道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13780257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13780257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780257-20220710-LinCaiFeng-cowboy-hats-05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13780257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780257-20220710-LinCaiFeng-cowboy-hats-05-450x280.jpg" alt="卡尔加里牛仔节" width="450" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13780257" class="wp-caption-text">多姿多彩的牛仔帽。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
