<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【无鱼坊的摄影心视界】鼻上长角的鱼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13841666-551676_medium-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>单角鼻鱼Naso unicornis刺尾鲷科。（图／郑清海提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<figure id="attachment_13841655" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13841655" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13841655-551675_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13841655" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13841655-551675_medium-600x601.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13841655" class="wp-caption-text">体呈椭圆形而侧扁；尾柄部有二个盾状骨板，各有一个龙骨突。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%95%E8%A7%92%E9%BC%BB%E9%B1%BC.md#1">单角鼻鱼</a>Naso unicornis刺尾鲷科</strong></h2>
<p>体呈椭圆形而侧扁；尾柄部有二个盾状骨板，各有一个龙骨突。头小，随着成长，在眼前方之额部逐渐突出而形成长而钝圆之角状突起。口小，两侧或有锯状齿。背鳍及臀鳍硬棘尖锐；尾鳍截平，上下叶缘延长如丝。体呈蓝灰色，腹侧则为黄褐色，尾柄上的骨质板为蓝黑色。背鳍与臀鳍有数条暗色纵线，并具蓝缘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13841654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13841654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13841654-551674_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13841654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13841654-551674_medium-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13841654" class="wp-caption-text">背鳍与臀鳍有数条暗色纵线，并具蓝缘。栖息于潟湖、礁岸、礁区斜坡或有拂浪处。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13841659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13841659" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13841659-551673_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13841659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13841659-551673_medium-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13841659" class="wp-caption-text">尾柄上的骨质板为蓝黑色。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%95%E8%A7%92%E9%BC%BB%E9%B1%BC.md#1">单角鼻鱼</a>栖息于潟湖、礁岸、礁区斜坡或有拂浪处。幼鱼在礁区上方活动，成鱼则大多成小群活动。一般在浅水域活动，最大深度可达180公尺左右。繁殖季节时则会成对出现。以粗糙多叶的褐藻类为食，例如马尾藻。观赏及食用兼具。剥皮后，煮姜丝汤，肉质鲜美。尾柄上骨质盾板非常锐利，易伤人，处理时需小心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13841657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13841657" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13841657-551677_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13841657" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13841657-551677_medium-600x460.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="460" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13841657" class="wp-caption-text">背鳍及臀鳍硬棘尖锐；尾鳍截平，上下叶缘延长如丝。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
