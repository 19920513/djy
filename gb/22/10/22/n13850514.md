<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】跨越荒野之选 2022 Subaru Outback Wilderness</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850541-2022_Subaru_Outback_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2022 Subaru Outback Wilderness。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年10月22日讯】现时车厂好像都有两大趋势，一个是向纯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%8A%A8%E8%BD%A6.md#1">电动车</a>发展，而另一方面就是开发更多汽油越野车，毕竟电动车在越野方面的优势没有汽油车大，在越野时可带上多个备用油箱及汽油，在荒野也能有足够汽油回程，电动车就难以做到，莫非要带备发电机？</p>
<p><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://dycg1eql8aigr.cloudfront.net/U9s6eYc8g"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/gzEtA29KWhA" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Subaru的四驱系统更是有利于越野，所以Subaru在Outback推出2年后的今天，再推出一辆提升越野能力的Outback Wilderness，尺寸上Outback Wilderness比其他版Outback缩短了一点，但离地距却有明显增加，达到9.5吋，所以看上去不像以往的旅行车车型，更像一辆SUV，而事实上Subaru的官方介绍也把Outback称为SUV。</p>
<p>测试车采用深蓝色，汽车四方也伴以数个金色的配件作装饰，车侧及车后也贴上了“Subaru Wilderness”的徽章，以彰显这辆车的越野能力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13850524" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13850524" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850524-2022_Subaru_Outback_09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13850524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850524-2022_Subaru_Outback_09-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13850524" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Subaru Outback Wilderness。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%A5%A5.md#1">李奥</a>／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小圆形雾灯是由数个LED灯组成，在2022年款Outback是标准配置。</p>
<p>另外，Subaru Outback Wilderness版配有专为越野使用的Yokohama Geolandar轮胎，尺寸是225/65 R17，搭配17吋全暗黑色铝质轮圈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13850525" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13850525" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850525-2022_Subaru_Outback_17.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13850525" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850525-2022_Subaru_Outback_17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13850525" class="wp-caption-text">小圆形雾灯是由数个LED灯组成。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%A5%A5.md#1">李奥</a>／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13850526" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13850526" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850526-2022_Subaru_Outback_19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13850526" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850526-2022_Subaru_Outback_19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13850526" class="wp-caption-text">为越野使用的Yokohama Geolandar轮胎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然Outback入门版是配置2.5升Boxer引擎，但Outback Wilderness是车系的中级版本，所以使用的是较高级的2.4升四缸水平对向Boxer引擎，并有涡轮增压系统，最大马力提升到260匹，而峰值扭矩为277磅呎，于2,000转到4,800转可达到。</p>
<p>名为Lineartronic的CVT变速箱是Outback的标配，由最入门到最高级版本的Outback都使用它，在转档时有更流畅的感觉，但加速力仍是有一点柔和，可使用方向盘后方的Paddle Shift，又或变速杆的M模式，作手动介入。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13850528" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13850528" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850528-2022_Subaru_Outback_36.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13850528" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850528-2022_Subaru_Outback_36-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13850528" class="wp-caption-text">2.4升四缸水平对向Boxer引擎，附有涡轮增压系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13850531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13850531" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850531-2022_Subaru_Outback_44.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13850531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850531-2022_Subaru_Outback_44-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13850531" class="wp-caption-text">Lineartronic的CVT变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Subaru的“对称全时四驱系统”(Symmetrical AWD)，对汽车加速、减速、转向及车辆抓地力各方面监察，从而提供最佳动力予每一车轮，投入使用至今已有40年以上历史。</p>
<p>除此之外，Outback的“X-Mode”模式，启用时会对引擎、变速箱、前后轮轴、刹车及VDC等进行优化，使汽车在湿滑路面上有更好的抓地力，行车更稳定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13850532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13850532" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850532-2022_Subaru_Outback_40.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13850532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850532-2022_Subaru_Outback_40-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13850532" class="wp-caption-text">Outback的“X-Mode”模式。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>EyeSight系统，在车内挡风玻璃中间后方，装有一对摄像头，可提供“预碰撞刹车”、“预碰撞动力管理”、“自适应巡航”及“偏离行车线警告”等功效，而且在美国则是标配。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13850535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13850535" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850535-2022_Subaru_Outback_05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13850535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850535-2022_Subaru_Outback_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13850535" class="wp-caption-text">Outback Wilderness装有EyeSight系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新的自适应巡航系统（Advanced Adaptive Cruise Control）可根据前车速度及预设距离，而自动减速或加速，以保持与前车距离，配合新加入的“车道居中（Lane Centering）”功能，便可成为“半自动驾驶”了。</p>
<p>巡航系统的车速控制是电子式，每按一下是加或减5英里/时，既清楚又快捷。</p>
<p>启动“车道居中”功能后，虽然系统会自动转动方向盘，以维持汽车在行车道的中央，但是需要驾车者不时给予反应，不然系统便会发出警告声响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13850536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13850536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850536-2022_Subaru_Outback_49.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13850536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850536-2022_Subaru_Outback_49-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13850536" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Subaru Outback Wilderness。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控台的萤幕作垂直摆放，尤如一个大尺寸iPad放在中间，运行Subaru Starlink系统，功能按键排列及图案都有如App一般，在底部也有“Home”键，Wilderness版加入了显示车前摄像头影像，在攀爬山坡时也可在萤幕看到前方地形，非常好用。</p>
<p>此外，也有部分常用功能实体键设在萤幕两旁，如气温调节、音量及紧急车灯等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13850537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13850537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850537-2022_Subaru_Outback_39.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13850537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850537-2022_Subaru_Outback_39-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13850537" class="wp-caption-text">中控台的萤幕作垂直摆放。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>初时驾驶Subaru Outback Wilderness在高速公路时，感觉有点飘，相信是方向盘反应敏感，习惯了便可以。</p>
<p>在野外测试时，Outback的Symmetrical AWD应付越野路段轻松自如，上下斜坡时也很容易，而且有了前置摄像头帮助，攀爬斜坡也不会只看见天空了。</p>
<p>Outback Wilderness的离地距也对越野时有极大的优势，更不用怕被石块撞击车底。</p>
<p>所以喜欢越野的人，Outback Wilderness会是一个好选择！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13850540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13850540" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850540-2022_Subaru_Outback_13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13850540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13850540-2022_Subaru_Outback_13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13850540" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Subaru Outback Wilderness。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
