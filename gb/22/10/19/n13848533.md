<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>罕见！两座头鲸与一群虎鲸激战数小时 原因不明</h2>
<a href="https://d2tkl2xkgbxyfv.cloudfront.net/B3u9zywLh"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年10月19日讯】（大纪元记者张雨霏编译报导）日前，鲸鱼观察者意外目睹了2只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%A7%E5%A4%B4%E9%B2%B8.md#1">座头鲸</a>和一群<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E9%B2%B8.md#1">虎鲸</a>（又名杀人鲸）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BF%80%E6%88%98.md#1">激战</a>的罕见画面，双方在海上缠斗数小时后又全部神秘消失，专家们为此大吃一惊，目前还不清楚它们打斗的具体原因。</p>
<p>这起事件发生在9月29日上午11点左右，当时<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%AA%E5%B9%B3%E6%B4%8B%E9%B2%B8%E9%B1%BC%E8%A7%82%E5%AF%9F%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">太平洋鲸鱼观察协会</a>（PWWA）一行人开船驶经加拿大与美国西部边境的萨利希海（Salish Sea）时，首先注意到大约15只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E9%B2%B8.md#1">虎鲸</a>在海面上异常活跃，很快他们发现原来是2只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%A7%E5%A4%B4%E9%B2%B8.md#1">座头鲸</a>在跟它们发生激烈冲突。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13848615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13848615" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13848615-3.-Orcas-and-humpback-interact-Mollie-Naccarato-Sooke-Coastal-Explorations-PWWA.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13848615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13848615-3.-Orcas-and-humpback-interact-Mollie-Naccarato-Sooke-Coastal-Explorations-PWWA.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="355" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13848615" class="wp-caption-text">（PWWA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13848616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13848616" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13848616-4.-Biggs-orca-Mollie-Naccarato-Sooke-Coastal-Explorations-PWWA.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13848616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13848616-4.-Biggs-orca-Mollie-Naccarato-Sooke-Coastal-Explorations-PWWA.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13848616" class="wp-caption-text">（PWWA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>加拿大温哥华岛“苏克海岸探险”（Sooke Coastal Explorations）的船长兼博物学家莫莉?纳卡拉托（Mollie Naccarato）在一份声明中说：“我仍在努力接受这个事实，因为这绝对令人难以置信。起初以为虎鲸是在追赶座头鲸，但当双方拉开一段距离后，座头鲸又会回到虎鲸身边混战。”</p>
<p>从<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%AA%E5%B9%B3%E6%B4%8B%E9%B2%B8%E9%B1%BC%E8%A7%82%E5%AF%9F%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">太平洋鲸鱼观察协会</a>发布的影片中可见，多只鲸鱼不断在海面跃进跃出，掀起阵阵巨大的水花，战况十分激烈，这场冲突整整持续了3小时之久。</p>
<p>但令人不解的是，它们最后不分胜负，竟一起消失在迷雾中，现场所有的协会成员也看得目瞪口呆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13848617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13848617" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13848617-6.-Biggs-orca-T251-Mollie-Naccarato-Sooke-Coastal-Explorations-PWWA.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13848617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13848617-6.-Biggs-orca-T251-Mollie-Naccarato-Sooke-Coastal-Explorations-PWWA.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13848617" class="wp-caption-text">（PWWA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13848614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13848614" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13848614-1.-Biggs-orcas-harrass-humpback-whale-Reaper-Mollie-Naccarato-Sooke-Coastal-Explorations-PWWA.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13848614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13848614-1.-Biggs-orcas-harrass-humpback-whale-Reaper-Mollie-Naccarato-Sooke-Coastal-Explorations-PWWA.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="368" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13848614" class="wp-caption-text">（PWWA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该协会执行董事格莱斯（Erin Gless）表示，这是第一次看到座头鲸和虎鲸争吵得如此激烈，当时它们在海面上不断拍打尾巴，还发出高分贝吼声。</p>
<p>太平洋鲸鱼观察协会表示，众所周知，虎鲸以海豹、海狮等哺乳动物为食，也会捕食年轻的座头鲸，不过在鲸群里并未出现年幼座头鲸，而且在萨利希海域从未发生过虎鲸与座头鲸激烈交战的状况，因此这属于非常罕见的情形，专家们对此也很好奇。</p>
<p>纳卡拉托指出，目前许多海洋学者都在研究该起冲突发生的真正原因。</p>
