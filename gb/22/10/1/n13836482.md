<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】试驾全新Z跑车 2023 Nissan Z Performance</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836504-IMG251-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023 Nissan Z Performance。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年10月01日讯】Z是26个字母中最后一个，所以也代表终极，在Nissan品牌中也是跑车的代表。由初代的Fairlady Z到最新一代的Z，都是车迷的目标，当然还有更强的GT-R。</p>
<p><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://dervszfjn9udk.cloudfront.net/R3zoWgsxq"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/iJ1hWXxZdss" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>在外形上，新Z采用了传统的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8E%E8%BD%AE%E9%A9%B1%E5%8A%A8.md#1">后轮驱动</a>跑车设计，引擎盖加长，后部车身较低，轮廓是向第一代Z致敬。</p>
<p>设计师利用最新技术简化了Z的设计，结合了LED照明，令车身设计更为简约。LED大灯有两个半圆形，其灵感来自1970年代日本市场的240ZG。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836484" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836484-IMG265.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836484-IMG265-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836484" class="wp-caption-text">2023 Nissan Z Performance。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2023年Nissan Z侧面线条由前端流向尾部，完全继承了第一代Z的轮廓。门把手采用嵌入式设计，尾舱盖设计超薄，但却比旧有370Z的刚性更高。</p>
<p>Z Performance配备后扰流板，可在尾舱盖和扰流板处产生正压，减轻后部升力。</p>
<p>车侧贴有“Z”的徽章，特别又有型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836486-IMG261.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836486-IMG261-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836486" class="wp-caption-text">车侧贴有“Z”的徽章。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>与上一代370Z相比，2023 Nissan Z装置了3升V6双涡轮增压引擎，提高了动力输出，可提供400匹马力，以及350磅呎扭矩，增加了近68匹马力，同时由0到60英里/时的加速快约15%。</p>
<p>测试车采用6速密齿比手动变速箱，配EXEDY高性能离合器，虽然换挡是不太费劲，但仍不及之前测试的Honda Civic Si轻松。</p>
<p>另外，手动变速车型还配备了碳纤维复合传动轴，Performance型号加入了SynchroRev Match功能，一键便可控制汽车电脑自动提高引擎转速配合换档。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836487-IMG272.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836487-IMG272-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836487" class="wp-caption-text">3升V6双涡轮增压引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前后减震器都采用了新的单管减震器设计，直径比旧有370Z更大，可把震振力减少约20%，在不平坦的路面减少冲击震动，增强贴地性能，也提高了操控稳定性。</p>
<p>前部双叉臂铝制悬架采用了新的几何形状，增加了后仰角，提高了直线行车的稳定性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836488" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836488-IMG276.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836488-IMG276-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836488" class="wp-caption-text">内设计简约。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Z的车内设计与车外的一样简约，三幅式方向盘配12.3吋TFT仪表萤幕，具有三种显示模式。“正常”模式在平衡了导航、音频和车辆信息。</p>
<p>“增强”模式将转速表和速度表推到显示屏的边缘，在中心提供更多空间，可显示更大的导航地图和信息。</p>
<p>“运动”模式集中在位于中间的转速表，顶部的升档灯可提醒在驾驶者换档，运动模式还可显示增压计或G力度计。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836489-IMG281.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836489-IMG281-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836489" class="wp-caption-text">中控台顶部装有三个实体仪表。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13836490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836490" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836490-IMG279.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836490-IMG279-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836490" class="wp-caption-text">12.3吋TFT仪表萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控台顶部装有三个实体仪表，左方是涡轮压力、中间是涡轮转速，而右方是电池电压。</p>
<p>Performance版装有导航功能的9吋触摸屏，内置有Wi-Fi热点功能的NissanConnect系统，8个扬声器的BOSE音响系统，具有主动降噪和主动声音增强功能。</p>
<p>配皮革座椅，驾驶席是备有手动升降的四方向半电动座椅，乘客座椅也是四方向电动，两座椅均有加热功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836492" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836492-IMG282.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836492-IMG282-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836492" class="wp-caption-text">6速密齿比手动变速箱。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13836493" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836493" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836493-IMG283.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836493-IMG283-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836493" class="wp-caption-text">SynchroRev Match功能键。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>标准配置包括Nissan Intelligent Key智能车匙、智能巡航控制、自动温度控制、自动调暗后视镜、倒车摄像机、两个12伏电源插座、两个USB插座（Type-A和Type-C)，前后雷达感应器。</p>
<p>支持Apple CarPlay和Android Auto、蓝牙免提电话系统、SiriusXM收音机的系统。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836494" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836494-IMG280.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836494-IMG280-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836494" class="wp-caption-text">支持Apple CarPlay和Android Auto。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nissan Z双座位跑车是纯玩乐之用，车尾舱的容量不大，而且地台下方也因为有了备胎，所以下方也没有载货空暗。</p>
<p>以驾驶而言，Nissan Z的起步加速力度不错，中速及高速的加速反应更佳。</p>
<p>因有了SynchroRev Match功能，在降档过弯时，系统自动提高引擎转速，驾驶者只需注意离合器及变速杆的控制，方便不少。</p>
<p>可惜是只有数天的测试，又没能安排在赛道上测试，是有点遗憾！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836495" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836495-IMG256.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13836495-IMG256-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836495" class="wp-caption-text">前方进风口设计特别。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
