<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自制香草糖浆 学做咖啡店好喝的香草拿铁</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852436-20221025_P.108_drink_06-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>以手作香草糖浆调制出的天然香草拿铁，特别受到年轻族群的喜爱！（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>同一杯饮料为何在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1.md#1">咖啡</a>店里喝起来的味道和在家里喝的不一样？秘诀就在饮料基底，只需学会“果酱、浓缩汁、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B3%96%E6%B5%86.md#1">糖浆</a>、饮品粉、水果干”基底，你家的厨房就能变身为专业饮料吧。简单．安心．健康．美味，百变口味任你调制！本文介绍如何制作香草糖浆，用香草糖浆调制出<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1.md#1">咖啡</a>店的人气饮品“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8D%89%E6%8B%BF%E9%93%81.md#1">香草拿铁</a>”。</p>
<h2>香草<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B3%96%E6%B5%86.md#1">糖浆</a></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13852431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13852431" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852431-2fa78291cd05e707559d949fdab14758.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13852431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852431-2fa78291cd05e707559d949fdab14758-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13852431" class="wp-caption-text">香草糖浆是咖啡店经常使用到的糖浆，能活用在各种饮品中。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是咖啡店经常使用到的糖浆，能活用在各种饮品中。手作香草糖浆的香草味比市售加工过的糖浆更为浓郁。制作时，需将<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8D%89%E8%8D%9A.md#1">香草荚</a>、香草籽一起装瓶，豆荚的成分会让味道变得更好。香草荚一般使用马达加斯加和大溪地生产的，特别是马达加斯加所产的香草荚，口感相当柔和。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8D%89%E8%8D%9A.md#1">香草荚</a>2枝（5克）、糖2杯（360克）、水3杯（600c.c.）</p>
<p>1. 香草荚切半后，用刀背取出香草籽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13852432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13852432" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852432-968a75537f80575b11f4675e1a332c7e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13852432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852432-968a75537f80575b11f4675e1a332c7e-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13852432" class="wp-caption-text">香草荚切半后，用刀背取出香草籽。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 在锅中倒入水、香草籽、香草荚烹煮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13852433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13852433" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852433-a768ec983440231ab55e92f945e1792a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13852433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852433-a768ec983440231ab55e92f945e1792a-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13852433" class="wp-caption-text">在锅中倒入水、香草籽、香草荚烹煮。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 待沸腾时，加入糖以中火续煮10分钟至糖溶化。</p>
<p>4. 糖皆溶化时，充分搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13852434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13852434" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852434-20221025_P.107-03_drink_04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13852434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852434-20221025_P.107-03_drink_04-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13852434" class="wp-caption-text">待沸腾时，加入糖以中火续煮10分钟至糖溶化。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 冷却后，连香草荚一起倒入瓶子中，冷藏于冰箱，进行3天的熟成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13852435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13852435" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852435-20221025_P.107-04_drink_05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13852435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852435-20221025_P.107-04_drink_05-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13852435" class="wp-caption-text">冷却后，连香草荚一起倒入瓶子中，冷藏于冰箱，进行3天的熟成。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>TIP</strong></span><strong>单柄锅比双耳锅更适合做为糖浆用锅</strong><br />
为了避免糖浆沾黏锅底，煮的过程中需不断搅拌，因此可以一手轻易握住锅子的单柄锅比双耳锅更加方便。若使用有尖嘴的锅子，将液体倒入瓶子时会更方便。</p>
<h2><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8D%89%E6%8B%BF%E9%93%81.md#1">香草拿铁</a></strong></h2>
<p>这是咖啡店里非常热门的一款饮品。以手作香草糖浆调制出的天然香草拿铁，特别受到年轻族群的喜爱！</p>
<p>香草糖浆3大匙<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%BB%B4%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1.md#1">冰滴咖啡</a>或冷泡咖啡<br />
1/3杯（70c.c.）<br />
牛奶1杯（200c.c.）<br />
冰块1杯</p>
<p>1.在杯中加入牛奶、香草糖浆搅拌均匀。<br />
2.在成品杯中加入冰块、步骤<b>1</b>的香草牛奶并搅拌均匀。<br />
3.接着倒入咖啡。<br />
4.搅拌均匀后即完成。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>TIP??</strong></span>咖啡与牛奶的倒入顺序制作温热的香草拿铁时，请将倒入牛奶和咖啡的顺序颠倒过来。在成品杯中，先倒入咖啡，再倒入加热过的牛奶，注意需连奶泡也一起倒入。⊙◇</p>
