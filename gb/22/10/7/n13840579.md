<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：今年最畅销的25款车 日韩系占14款</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13291970-2021_Toyota_RAV4_Hybrid_02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2021 Toyota RAV4 Hybrid Limited。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年10月10日讯】（大纪元记者夏雨综合报导）在经历2022年汽车销售之后，需要仔细管理预期。由于供应链问题继续影响经销商库存，消费者买新车十分困难，更不用说以合理的价格购买了。</p>
<p>因此，与去年相比，许多汽车制造商报告销量下降也就不足为奇。尽管如此，对一些车来说，依然畅销。</p>
<p>根据美国汽车杂志《Car and Driver》评选出的今年前三个季度最畅销的25款汽车、SUV和卡车的名单，毫无悬念地，三款<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%AE%E5%8D%A1.md#1">皮卡</a>依然占据前三名；但是在前十名中，丰田品牌占四款；在前25名中，日韩系占14款。</p>
<p>以下是最畅销的前10款车介绍。</p>
<h2>1. 福特F系列（售出467,307辆）</h2>
<p>即使销量下降达13%，也无法将F系列产品从销量排行榜冠军位置上拉下来。</p>
<p>福特还开始销售名为Lightning的电动车版F-150，并表示迄今为止已售出8760辆电动<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%AE%E5%8D%A1.md#1">皮卡</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13711801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13711801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13711801-2204131729301973.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13711801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13711801-2204131729301973-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13711801" class="wp-caption-text">福特（Ford）F-150 Lightning 全电动车。（戴兵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>2. 雪佛兰索罗德（Chevrolet Silverado）（售出374,479辆）</h2>
<p>轻型Silverado 1500将在2022年进行更新，它需要重新获得一些销售势头，才能在未来超越Ram保持第二名。</p>
<p>该销售数字还包括重型车型的销量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13242632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13242632" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13242632-2021_Chevy_Silverado_LT_02-e1631922598816.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13242632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13242632-2021_Chevy_Silverado_LT_02-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13242632" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Chevrolet Silverado LT Z71。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>3. 道奇Ram Pickup（售出363,089辆）</h2>
<p>Ram的皮卡阵容包括轻型和重型版本，与去年相比销量下降了16%。尽管雪佛兰最近重新夺回第二名，但Ram和索罗德（Silverado）的竞争仍在继续，两款车几乎势均力敌，一直在为销量银牌而战。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13259417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13259417" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13259417-2021_Ram_1500_TRX_05-e1632548053521.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13259417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13259417-2021_Ram_1500_TRX_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13259417" class="wp-caption-text">2021 RAM 1500 TRX。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>4. 丰田RAV4（TOYOTA RAV4）（售出303,341辆）</h2>
<p>近年来，RAV4巩固了最畅销非皮卡汽车的地位，且这种情况预计将持续到2022年。</p>
<p>今年迄今为止，RAV销量下降了3%，但与这份榜单上排名第二的非皮卡畅销车型相比，仍有很大优势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13291973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13291973" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13291973-2021_Toyota_RAV4_Hybrid_07-e1633742595344.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13291973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13291973-2021_Toyota_RAV4_Hybrid_07-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13291973" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Toyota RAV4 Hybrid Limited。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>5. 丰田佳美（Toyota Camry，丰田凯美瑞）（售出214,403辆）</h2>
<p>尽管这份榜单上有多款SUV，但丰田佳美仍然是最畅销的车型之一，这款车既不是SUV也不是皮卡车。</p>
<p>今年迄今，佳美销量下降17%，但仍击败了许多SUV，并以显&#33879;优势超越了轿车排名第二的丰田花冠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12382108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12382108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/IMG01-e1599273603541.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12382108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/IMG01-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12382108" class="wp-caption-text">2020 Toyota Camry TRD。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>6. 本田CR-V（Honda CR-V）（售出178,687辆）</h2>
<p>新款2023本田CR-V即将上市销售，但现有车型销售依然强劲，在畅销车榜单中排名第6。与去年同期相比，这款车的销售下降了38%，但也许新车型可以帮助扭转这种局面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13356852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356852-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_03-e1636155134443.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356852-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_03-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356852" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>7. 丰田塔科马（Toyota Tacoma）（售出175,872辆）</h2>
<p>中型皮卡中，丰田在销量方面仍占统治地位，击败了福特Ranger和雪佛兰库罗德（Colorado）。但与去年这个时候相比，塔科马（Tacoma）销量确实下降了12%。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13048532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13048532" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13048532-000_Was8894197.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13048532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13048532-000_Was8894197-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13048532" class="wp-caption-text">丰田塔科马 (Jewel Samad/AFP)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>8. 吉普大切诺基（Jeep Grand Cherokee）（售出172,545辆）</h2>
<p>与丰田汉兰达（Highlander）类似，吉普的中型SUV在畅销车排行榜上名列前茅不太常见。这款车的三排座车型和新的两排座车型现已发售；在经历销售强劲的上半年之后，大切诺基的销量迄今比去年下降了9%。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10430101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10430101" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/GettyImages-517118236.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10430101" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/GettyImages-517118236-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10430101" class="wp-caption-text">吉普大切诺基TrailHawk (Bryan Thomas/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>9. 丰田花冠（Toyota?Corolla）（售出171,556辆）</h2>
<p>丰田花冠在燃油效率、可靠性和可负担性方面有很好的名声，但它的受欢迎程度正在下降。与去年相比，销售额下降了21%。刚刚推出的300马力GR Corolla热掀背车当然令人兴奋，但由于其利基地位，永远无法显&#33879;提升这款车的销量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13705720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13705720" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705720-2022_Toyota_Corolla_HB_03-e1649459178738.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13705720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705720-2022_Toyota_Corolla_HB_03-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13705720" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Toyota Corolla Hatchback XSE。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>10. 吉姆西西拉（GMC Sierra）（售出169,107辆）</h2>
<p>这款车是雪佛兰索罗德（Silverado）的高档双胞胎，在今年前三个季度跻身前10名畅销车型之列，包括重型型号和轻型Sierra 1500。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11197551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11197551" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/2019_GMC_Sierra_01-e1555647545241.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11197551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/2019_GMC_Sierra_01-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11197551" class="wp-caption-text">2019 GMC Sierra Denali。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
