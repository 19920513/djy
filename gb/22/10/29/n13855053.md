<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】1.3升的非凡动力 2023 Chevrolet Trailblazer RS</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855072-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023 Chevrolet Trailblazer RS。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年10月29日讯】Chevrolet Trailblazer是紧凑型Crossover，自2020年推出市场，这次测试的是2023年款的RS型号，事实上，2023年款Trailblazer在尺寸上及内装上都与2022年款不变，唯一是增加了数种外观颜色，Nitro Yellow Metallic金属黄色、喷泉蓝色和纯灰色金属漆，另外还有彩虹珍珠车顶颜色。</p>
<p><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d2iiqnrx4rrboe.cloudfront.net/Y3qyvogas"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/pA4jCq2TQGQ" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>新Chevrolet Trailblazer车型细小，全车尺寸为长度173.7吋、宽度71.2吋、高度65.7吋，采用GM VSS-F底盘，离地距为8吋。测试车是Trailblazer RS型号，车身是新增的纯金属灰颜色，车身上部则采用黑色，并且特别装有车顶蓝架，整体就是越野感超强，RS更独有网状前进风口栏栅，以及车尾左右双排气喉，而且有镀铬排气口端。头灯位于日间行车灯（DRL）之下，方形头灯就像雾灯一样，而真正的雾灯隐藏于防撞器下方。RS装有四个旋风形银黑两色合金轮圈，配225/55R18轮胎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13855055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855055" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855055-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13855055" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855055-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855055" class="wp-caption-text">2023 Chevrolet Trailblazer RS。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%A5%A5.md#1">李奥</a>／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>引擎分为两种，1.2升直列三缸引擎，最大马力137匹，扭矩162磅呎，只用在LS及LT前驱版本；另一款是1.3升直列三缸引擎，最大马力155匹，扭矩174磅呎，应用在其他型号，尤其是AWD型号。变速箱方面，1.2升引擎会配备CVT变速箱，而1.3升引擎就会配备9速自动变速箱。Trailblazer RS AWD版内有AWD按键，驾驶者可手动解除，只以前驱行车，进一步减少油耗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13855056" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855056" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855056-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_25.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13855056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855056-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855056" class="wp-caption-text">1.3升直列三缸引擎，最大马力155匹，扭矩174磅呎。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%A5%A5.md#1">李奥</a>／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13855059" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855059" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855059-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_31.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13855059" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855059-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_31-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855059" class="wp-caption-text">配备9速自动变速箱。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内装方面，Trailblazer备有三幅式方向盘，上方有各种按键，除了有音响操作及巡航功能之外，也有四方向按键，用以控制仪表板上的资讯显示。RS及ACTIV型号更有电热功能，而LT则是加选项目。中控台标配7吋触屏，但可升级到8吋，内置4G/LTE热点功能，可支援Apple Carplay及Android Auto，而且是可Wireless无线连接，另外也有Amazon Alexa。内置的导航地图有了改进，去年版本在运行时会占据全个萤幕，把底部的Home键及其他捷径也盖掉了，时钟也看不到，今年却改良了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13855061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855061" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855061-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_27.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13855061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855061-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_27-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855061" class="wp-caption-text">双圆仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13855062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855062" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855062-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_34.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13855062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855062-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_34-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855062" class="wp-caption-text">Chevrolet Trailblazer RS内装。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控台底部装有无线充电板，而在充电板后方有数个按键，其中一个可将Trailblazer标配有自动引擎熄火功能关闭，另一个是格子旗标志的按键，其实就是Sport模底。Trailblazer也备有前座双杯座及电子手刹车，而且是标配装置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13855063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855063-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_29.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13855063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855063-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855063" class="wp-caption-text">（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13855064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855064" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855064-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_30.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13855064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855064-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_30-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855064" class="wp-caption-text">底部装有无线充电板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了令这辆小车在载运时更有优势，Trailblazer的前乘客座位是可完全折平，可载运最长达到8.5呎的东西。后方则可分为40/60折叠，最大载货量为54.4立方呎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13855065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855065" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855065-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_22.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13855065" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855065-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855065" class="wp-caption-text">装有车顶蓝架。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最令人惊讶的是Trailblazer虽然只有一副小容积引擎，但加速绝不可小观，反应极快，在1,600转时已可达最大扭矩，9速自动变速变档时间也快，而且对减少耗油有大帮助。Trailblazer的四个轮胎接近汽车的四个角落，所以行车也很稳定，转向灵活。整体来说，Trailblazer是非常出色的小Crossover，测试车价格为31,530美元，入门LT版则由23,295美元起跳。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13855066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855066" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855066-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13855066" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855066-2022_Chevy_Trailblazer_RS_04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855066" class="wp-caption-text">2023 Chevrolet Trailblazer RS。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
