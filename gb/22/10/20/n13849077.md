<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园机场捷运宝可梦一日票 21日上巿开卖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13849197-580827-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>飞翔皮卡丘彩绘列车21日启航。（徐乃义／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年10月20日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E5%8F%AF%E6%A2%A6.md#1">宝可梦</a>迷要留意啰！21日起<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E6%9C%BA%E5%9C%BA%E6%8D%B7%E8%BF%90.md#1">桃园机场捷运</a>线21个车站开卖“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E5%8F%AF%E6%A2%A6.md#1">宝可梦</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%80%E6%97%A5%E7%A5%A8.md#1">一日票</a>”，数量有限，宝可梦迷手脚要快。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13849194" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13849194" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13849194-580824.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13849194" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13849194-580824-600x730.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="549" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13849194" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E6%9C%BA%E5%9C%BA%E6%8D%B7%E8%BF%90.md#1">桃园机场捷运</a>线21个车站开卖“宝可梦<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%80%E6%97%A5%E7%A5%A8.md#1">一日票</a>”。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园大众捷运公司表示，开卖的“宝可梦一日票”票卡上印制有宝可梦的各种角色，计有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%AE%E5%8D%A1%E4%B8%98.md#1">皮卡丘</a>、呆呆兽、熊宝宝、卡比兽、食梦梦、谢米、青绵鸟，个个萌样可掬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13849196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13849196" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13849196-580826.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13849196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13849196-580826-600x762.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="572" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13849196" class="wp-caption-text">因宝可梦角色众多，票卡分A、B、C三款。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>因宝可梦角色众多，票卡分A、B、C三款，不过只有A、B款票卡可单买，每张优惠价299元。C款票卡不单卖，包含在三张一式的套组内，售价888元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13849195" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13849195" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13849195-580825.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13849195" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13849195-580825-600x363.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13849195" class="wp-caption-text">“宝可梦一日票”票卡上印制有宝可梦的各种角色。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园大众捷运公司“宝可梦一日票”各款票卡贩售车站不同，一式三张的套组可在桃园机场捷运线21个车站冾购。A款票卡在A1至A11等11个车站开卖。B款票卡在A12至A21等10 个车站贩售。◇</p>
