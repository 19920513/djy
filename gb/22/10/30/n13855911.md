<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>越柬寮华人组团访台 经文处长黄敏境授旗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855916-5b30d7a27ac13c30936b2c9df99cdaf2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>越柬寮华人团体一行36人将于11月7日至12日参访台湾，2022年10月28日驻洛杉矶台北经济文化办事处处长黄敏境授旗。左起访问团副团长徐雄刚、侨务咨询委员梁永泰、经文处长黄敏境、团长罗明、洛侨中心主任陈敏永、副团长莫华锋。（袁玫／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年10月31日讯】（大纪元记者袁玫洛杉矶报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E6%9F%AC%E5%AF%AE%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA%E5%9B%A2%E4%BD%93.md#1">越柬寮华人团体</a>一行36人将于11月7日至12日参访台湾，10月28日下午在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E4%BE%A8%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">洛侨中心</a>举行<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%88%E6%97%97%E5%85%B8%E7%A4%BC.md#1">授旗典礼</a>。驻洛台北经文处处长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E6%95%8F%E5%A2%83.md#1">黄敏境</a>为团员们授旗时表示，访台行程因疫情睽违三年后重启意义重大，预祝大家享受台湾自由空气、到处走看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13855917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855917" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855917-05a0f0358a9a902a3ab4ac9e39621750-e1667163141653.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13855917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855917-05a0f0358a9a902a3ab4ac9e39621750-e1667163141653.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="239" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855917" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E6%9F%AC%E5%AF%AE%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA%E5%9B%A2%E4%BD%93.md#1">越柬寮华人团体</a>一行36人将于11月7日至12日参访台湾，2022年10月28日驻洛杉矶台北经济文化办事处处长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E6%95%8F%E5%A2%83.md#1">黄敏境</a>授旗。（袁玫/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>访问团团长罗明表示，台湾人民跟越南华侨相同“善良、纯直、热情”，访台好像回家乡。他感谢侨委会的邀请，大家都很开心期待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13855922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855922" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855922-b929840451b5990073be0ee32d9b5b47-e1667164029699.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13855922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855922-b929840451b5990073be0ee32d9b5b47-e1667164029699.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855922" class="wp-caption-text">越柬寮华人台湾参访团团长罗明。（袁玫/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄敏境表示，中华民国曾和越南朋友同心团结度过很长的岁月，包括接待越南难民到台湾一步步的安置，许多越柬寮侨民来美前都在台湾受过正统教育。如今大家定居在美国这个公平、只要努力奋斗都有一席之地的地方，享受可贵的自由民主。但中共20大后更要警惕中共独裁，要珍惜自由民主的可贵。他祝福力挺台湾、志同道合的访问团员们在台湾有愉快的旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13855924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855924" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855924-c24ef5cf626e692a7fa1af18f0c55a02-e1667164185615.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13855924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855924-c24ef5cf626e692a7fa1af18f0c55a02-300x430.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855924" class="wp-caption-text">驻洛杉矶台北经济文化办事处处长黄敏境。（袁玫/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13855926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855926" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855926-57ec9b0fa3fac5277938f9bd30e918e5-e1667164402988.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13855926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855926-57ec9b0fa3fac5277938f9bd30e918e5-300x302.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855926" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E4%BE%A8%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">洛侨中心</a>主任陈敏永致词，右为越柬寮华人台湾参访团副团长莫华锋。（袁玫/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侨务咨询委员梁永泰、副团长徐雄刚与莫华锋分别致词，感谢侨委会提供参访机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13855927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13855927" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855927-7644c0d5c5f0064feb08a9bc4da9d0e9-e1667164635151.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13855927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13855927-7644c0d5c5f0064feb08a9bc4da9d0e9-300x573.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="573" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13855927" class="wp-caption-text">侨务咨询委员梁永泰。（袁玫/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>洛侨中心主任陈敏永说明，侨委会精心安排的精彩行程包括：拜会侨委会、参观国父纪念馆、拜会智慧农场、高科技企业及台湾研发机构；拜访总统府还在洽谈中；参访金门。◇</p>
