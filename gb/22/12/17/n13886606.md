<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>水耕和土耕有何不同？在家以菜种菜简单又有趣</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886630-P.13-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>水耕的优点是不需浇水，缺点是能培育的蔬菜种类有限。（苹果屋出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong> <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%A5%E8%8F%9C%E7%A7%8D%E8%8F%9C.md#1">以菜种菜</a>，以园艺专业用语称为“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%8D%E7%94%9F%E6%A0%BD%E5%9F%B9.md#1">再生栽培</a>”。是指切下蔬菜的一小块根、茎或挖出种子，经过培育就能长大、重新采收的方式，又称作“蔬菜再生（reborn vegetable）”。再生栽培最大的特征是初学者也能立刻上手，无需担心失败。本文介绍在家培育蔬菜采用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E8%80%95.md#1">水耕</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%9F%E8%80%95.md#1">土耕</a>的优缺点，以及种植<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8B%92.md#1">罗勒</a>的方法。</p>
<h2>依培育方法不同而产生的优缺点</h2>
<p>蔬菜的培育方法大致可分两种：种在土里的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%9F%E8%80%95.md#1">土耕</a>，和浸在水中的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E8%80%95.md#1">水耕</a>。本书中介绍的水耕方法仅使用自来水来培育，完全不会用到液态肥料和气泵。土耕和水耕则各有其优缺点。</p>
<h2>土耕</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13886629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886629" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886629-P.13-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13886629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886629-P.13-1-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886629" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">优点</span></h3>
<p>?水和肥料积蓄在土壤里，蔬菜能长得健康又茁壮<br />
?有空间让根部伸展便不易枯萎，蔬菜能好好地生长<br />
?有土壤的支撑，根茎类蔬菜和茎比较长的蔬菜都较容易培育</p>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">缺点</span></h3>
<p>╳种完要丢的土壤处理起来比较麻烦（处理方法依地区而异）<br />
╳需要准备土壤和容器比较麻烦</p>
<h2>水耕</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13886630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886630" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886630-P.13-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13886630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886630-P.13-2-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886630" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">优点</span></h3>
<p>?就只是浸泡在水中，简简单单就能开始<br />
?不指定场所，放在厨房角落也OK<br />
?只需每天换水即可，不需浇水</p>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">缺点</span></h3>
<p>╳养分和氧气会越来越少、长不大<br />
╳能培育的蔬菜种类有限</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886743" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886743-P.128.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13886743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886743-P.128-300x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="474" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886743" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8B%92.md#1">罗勒</a></h2>
<p><strong>从春天到夏天都能收获满满</strong></p>
<p>用新鲜、清香的罗勒入菜，可以为料理增添风味，或是做成青酱、卡布里沙拉，都美味十足！由于不耐寒，春夏两季是能收获最多的季节。在日照强或是西晒的场所，也能生长得很好。</p>
<p>难易度：★<br />
场所：厨房→室内（窗边）<br />
栽种时期：整年<br />
从种植到采收的期间：约1个月<br />
栽培方法：杯子、中盆器</p>
<h3>1. 浸水</h3>
<p>食用完叶片后，将留下的茎浸泡到盛有水的杯子里。水量要盖到茎的一半。约过1星期后，根就会从茎的地方长出来。根长到5cm前都以水耕栽培。需要每天换水并清洗。即使不种在土里也能采收！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886744" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886744-P.129-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13886744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886744-P.129-1-300x366.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886744" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 准备容器和土壤</p>
<p>选用中型盆器，最下方铺钵底石（轻石），倒入约九分满的蔬菜用培养土。因选用的培养土里已经混有肥料，所以不需再加基肥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886745" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886745-P.129-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886745-P.129-2-300x147.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="147" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886745" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 移植到土壤里</p>
<p>先在土里挖个洞，把根埋进土壤里。植株之间须间隔20cm。盆栽最好放在日照充足的阳台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886746" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886746-P.129-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886746-P.129-3-300x164.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="164" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886746" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>POINT<br />
如果留下大片叶子，水分会从叶子蒸散。所以大片的叶子要先采收喔！</p>
<p>4. 浇水</p>
<p>第一次浇的水量要很多，让土壤全部都湿润，所以水要不停地浇，直到从盆器底部流出来为止。当土壤表面变干燥时，就表示需要再浇水。1个月之后再追肥就可以了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886748" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886748-P.129-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886748-P.129-4-300x304.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886748" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 采收</p>
<p>做菜需要时，再拿剪刀剪或是手摘都行。即便采收过一次，新叶还是会陆续生长出来。但因寒冬而枯萎，或是开花，就表示收获结束了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886749" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886749-P.129-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886749-P.129-5-300x289.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="289" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886749" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;">MIYAZAKI MEMO</span><br />
<span style="color: #000000;"> 采花取籽也是乐趣之一</span></h3>
<p>顺顺利利地培育，到了夏天就会开出小小的可爱花朵。可把花朵摘下做居家装饰，或是培育到可以取籽的时候，确实干燥后取下籽。到了春天，就播籽种植看看吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886751" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886751-P.129-6.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13886751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886751-P.129-6-300x192.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886751" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
