<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>以菜种菜真的很简单 在家种草莓酿果实酒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886642-20221217_strawberry_02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>以菜种菜很简单，还可以用自家栽培的草莓调配白兰地草莓酒。（苹果屋出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%A5%E8%8F%9C%E7%A7%8D%E8%8F%9C.md#1">以菜种菜</a>，以园艺专业用语称为“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%8D%E7%94%9F%E6%A0%BD%E5%9F%B9.md#1">再生栽培</a>”。是指切下蔬菜的一小块根、茎或挖出种子，经过培育就能长大、重新采收的方式，又称作“蔬菜再生（reborn vegetable）”。</p>
<p>如此简单就能做到的家庭菜园型态，正慢慢风行在我们的日常生活中。尤其因COVID-19新冠肺炎疫情的关系，待在家的时间比以往更多的今天，这种简单又有趣的种菜方式以新兴趣之姿受到大众瞩目，想要尝试的人更是有增无减。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%8D%E7%94%9F%E6%A0%BD%E5%9F%B9.md#1">再生栽培</a>最大的特征是初学者也能立刻上手，无需担心失败。每吃一次蔬菜就能有一次挑战的机会。像这样轻松就能做到也是优点之一。如果你也乐在培育蔬菜的过程，还请务必亲自采收、做成料理好好品尝，然后持续种下去唷！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886638" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886638-P.110.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886638-P.110-300x259.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="259" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886638" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93.md#1">草莓</a></h2>
<p>刚采摘的完熟<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93.md#1">草莓</a>香甜可口</p>
<p>由于不容易发芽，要取籽的话，就拿快要烂掉的草莓挑战看看吧！用镊子夹或是用刀子刮都可以。结果之后，请务必等到完熟再采收，便能享用又甜又美味的草莓。</p>
<p>难易度：★★★★<br />
场所：室内（窗边）→阳台<br />
栽种时期：整年<br />
从种植到采收的期间：约1年<br />
栽培方法：杯子、中盆器</p>
<h3>1. 取籽</h3>
<p>将草莓在常温下放至完熟之后，利用网子摩擦草莓取籽。一颗草莓可取100粒以上的种子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886609" style="width: 302px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886609-P.111-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13886609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886609-P.111-1-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="302" b="217" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886609" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>2. 准备苗盆</h3>
<p>利用钻子等尖头的工具，在塑胶杯底部钻10个左右的排水孔，作为培育苗株的苗盆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886615" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886615-P.111-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886615-P.111-2-300x304.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886615" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>3. 播种</h3>
<p>杯子里倒入八分满的培养土，放上步骤1取的籽。播下的籽要分散开来，上面可不必再覆盖土壤。将它放在室内（窗边）日照佳的地方，土壤表面干了再浇水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886616" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886616-P.111-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886616-P.111-3-300x404.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886616" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #993300;">POINT</span><br />
播种后约1个月左右会发芽。所有长出来的芽都不需摘除，继续培育即可。</p>
<h3>4. 准备容器和土壤</h3>
<p>选用中型盆器，最下方铺钵底石（轻石），倒入约九分满的蔬菜用培养土。因选用的培养土里已经混有肥料，所以不需再加基肥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886617" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886617-P.112-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886617-P.112-1-300x133.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="133" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886617" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>5. 移植</h3>
<p>当苗株分别长出3片叶子时，就可连土一起从苗盆取出移植到盆器里。先在土里挖个洞再放入苗株，并把旁边的土壤整平。植株之间须间隔20cm，中型盆器能种2、3株。盆栽最好放在日照充足的阳台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886618" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886618-P.112-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886618-P.112-2-300x129.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="129" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886618" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>6. 浇水</h3>
<p>第一次浇的水量要很多，让土壤全部都湿润，所以水要不停地浇，直到从盆器底部流出来为止。当土壤表面变干燥时，就表示需要再浇水。1个月之后再追肥就可以了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886619" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886619-P.112-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886619-P.112-3-300x250.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886619" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>7. 授粉</h3>
<p>春天来临，草莓花开时，利用掏耳棒的棉球或是材质较软的水彩笔，轻轻地在花朵中间来回刷几次以帮助人工授粉。授粉成功、花谢后就会结果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886620" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886620" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886620-P.113-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886620" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886620-P.113-1-300x237.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="237" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886620" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>8. 采收</h3>
<p>春天，果实变红就能采收。采收时用剪刀剪或是用手摘都可以。从果实取籽培育而成的草莓，会和原来的品种完全不同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886621" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886621-P.113-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886621-P.113-2-300x302.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886621" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>MIYAZAKI MEMO<br />
草莓每年都能收成吗？</h3>
<p>到了严寒的冬季，大部分的蔬菜都会枯萎，但草莓例外。即使是冬天也能安然度过存活下来。也就是说，只要栽培成功一次，每年的春天都能采收。但要注意可能因病虫害而枯萎的情形喔！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886628" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886628-P.113-3.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13886628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886628-P.113-3-300x195.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886628" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #993300;">手工酿果实酒</span></h2>
<p><strong>大人专属！另一种开心享用水果的方法</strong></p>
<p>浓缩水果的甜味及香气，令人爱不释手的果实酒，现在，就用自家栽培的水果加上喜爱的酒调配看看吧！不需要专业的酿酒知识，用简单的方式照着做，就能酿出独特风味的果实酒。</p>
<p>材料与容器（白兰地草莓酒）：冰糖、草莓、白兰地、玻璃罐</p>
<p>STEP 1：准备水果<br />
清洗草莓、去掉蒂头，整颗直接使用不用切。若要增添酸味，可加入一颗柠檬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886623" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886623-P.122-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886623-P.122-1-300x214.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="214" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886623" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>STEP 2：酿酒<br />
草莓与冰糖的比例是10颗：150g。将材料全部装入玻璃罐后，再倒满白兰地，盖上盖子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886624" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886624-P.122-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886624-P.122-2-300x214.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="214" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886624" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>STEP 3：熟成<br />
放置约1～4周后取出水果。酿渍期间可能发生水果褪色、形状崩塌、产生沉淀物等情形，尽可能把水果拿出来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886626" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886626" style="width: 232px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886626-P.122-3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13886626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886626-P.122-3.jpg" alt="" width="232" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886626" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>完成<br />
水果都取出后就可立即饮用，甚至多放几个月也行。除了直接喝，也可搭配气泡水、牛奶、优格等一起饮用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13886627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13886627" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886627-P.122-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13886627" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13886627-P.122-4-300x392.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13886627" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>酒×水果×糖的组合无上限！<br />
酿果实酒有趣之处在于，与不同食材组合就会有完全不同的味道。至今我酿过30种以上的酒。请试做看看，并开心地与家人一起享用酿好的美酒吧！◇</p>
