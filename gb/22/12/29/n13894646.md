<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>通膨和基础设施成本推高德拉华河谷水费</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年12月30日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">（大纪元费城讯）从德拉华州的威明顿到宾州郊区，德拉华河谷地区数十万居民的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E8%B4%B9.md#1">水费</a>将在</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;"> 2023 <span lang="ZH-CN">年上涨。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 12.0pt 0in 12.0pt 0in;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">涨幅最显着的是宾州美国水务公司（</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">Pennsylvania American Water<span lang="ZH-CN">），涨价</span>14%<span lang="ZH-CN">。该公司称这将有助于更换老化的供水基础设施。 </span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 12.0pt 0in 12.0pt 0in;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">今年早些时候，宾州水务公司</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">Aqua Pennsylvania <span lang="ZH-CN">涨价</span> 12%<span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 12.0pt 0in 12.0pt 0in;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">依赖公共供水设施的居民在</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;"> 2023 <span lang="ZH-CN">年面临的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E8%B4%B9.md#1">水费</a>涨幅将小得多。威明顿市将提高水费</span>6%<span lang="ZH-CN">，这几乎与费城</span> 9 <span lang="ZH-CN">月份的提价持平。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 12.0pt 0in 12.0pt 0in;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">专家说，在瘟疫大流行期间，氯和其他水处理化学品的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%9A%E8%B4%A7%E8%86%A8%E8%83%80.md#1">通货膨胀</a>导致水电费上涨。夏天给草坪浇水和洗衣用水量最大。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 12.0pt 0in 12.0pt 0in;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">德拉华大学水资源中心自</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;"> 2000 <span lang="ZH-CN">年以来一直在研究该地区的水费，表示水费是</span> 20 <span lang="ZH-CN">年前的四倍。据该大学称，私人公用事业费用大约是市政费用的两倍。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 12.0pt 0in 12.0pt 0in;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">宾州的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E8%B4%B9%E8%80%85.md#1">消费者</a>权益倡导者帕特里克·西塞罗</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;"> (Patrick Cicero) <span lang="ZH-CN">表示，客户有权就未来提议的涨价发表意见。如果客户的供水商受公用事业委员会监管，他们可以在听证会上提供证词。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 12.0pt 0in 12.0pt 0in;">责任编辑：王玫</p>
