<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>陈伟民和伍伟业就任安良工商会会长 侨界致贺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891372-DSC03431-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>12月23日晚，波士顿安良工商会新届职员及前辈们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年12月25日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）12月23日晚上，波士顿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>Hudson街<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E8%89%AF%E5%B7%A5%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">安良工商会</a>的大堂内高朋满座。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>各侨团代表纷纷向新届会长陈伟民、伍伟业，以及新就任的职员道贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13891373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13891373" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891373-DSC03411.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13891373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891373-DSC03411-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13891373" class="wp-caption-text">12月23日晚，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E8%89%AF%E5%B7%A5%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">安良工商会</a>高朋满座，众多来宾祝贺新届职员就任。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚，安良工商会的新届职员们首先向关帝敬献香烛、供品。随后在来宾们的见证下，总理陈仕维带领他们宣誓就职。锣声响起，宣告典礼完成，来宾们纷纷鼓掌向新届职员道贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13891371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13891371" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891371-DSC03405.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13891371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891371-DSC03405-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13891371" class="wp-caption-text">新届职员在总理陈仕维带领下，向关帝敬香。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天正逢强风大雨，不少人进门时身上都有水渍。全美安良工商会总理陈仕维说：“这可谓风雨同路见真心。”看到来宾们坐满了堂内的椅子，还有不少人站在后排，陈仕维说，非常感谢大家如此支持安良。</p>
<p>他亦感谢上届职员群策群力，让会务及各项活动在疫情期间也没有停顿。对于新届职员，陈仕维期待他们继续服务侨社，帮助华埠走出疫情阴影，繁荣生意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13891379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13891379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891379-DSC03421.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13891379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891379-DSC03421-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13891379" class="wp-caption-text">安良工商会全美总理陈仕维感谢各位来宾到场支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卸任会长伍辉民亦感谢同届职员们和乐相处，在任内各尽其力。</p>
<p>新届会长陈伟民、伍伟业向嘉宾们一一致谢，并期待与各兄弟侨团继续合作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13891380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13891380" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891380-DSC03428.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13891380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891380-DSC03428-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13891380" class="wp-caption-text">安良工商会2023新届会长陈伟民。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13891381" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13891381" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891381-DSC03429.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13891381" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891381-DSC03429-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13891381" class="wp-caption-text">安良工商会2023新届会长伍伟业。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚有诸多嘉宾及团体代表到场参与典礼，包括驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元、侨教中心主任潘昭荣、纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉、洪门总理余丽媖、北美舜裔笃亲公所总理陈建立、伍胥山公所总长伍焕华、安良工商会元老团主席伍健生等等。</p>
<p>孙俭元感谢安良工商会长期支持中华民国，并向在场各位致以新年祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13891378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13891378" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891378-DSC03408.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13891378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891378-DSC03408-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13891378" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元感谢安良工商会支持中华民国。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>雷国辉在祝贺新届职员之后，还谈到，安良在过去一年协助举办了不少繁荣华埠的活动，例如乒乓球赛、中国象棋赛、台湾夜市等等。在这过程中，安良与中华公所及各侨团都有着良好的合作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13891377" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13891377" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891377-DSC03410.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13891377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891377-DSC03410-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13891377" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉祝贺安良工商会新届职员就任。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>余丽媖也表示，洪门在过去一年参与举办了很多活动，与安良也多有合作。她说，这两个团体向来是互帮互助的兄弟团体，希望日后继续协力完成各项事务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13891737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13891737" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891737-DSC03414.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13891737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891737-DSC03414-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13891737" class="wp-caption-text">洪门总理余丽媖期待继续与安良工商会合作服务侨社。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在就职典礼之后，安良工商会在帝苑酒楼宴请各位来宾。宾主尽欢而散。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13891382" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13891382" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891382-DSC03407.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13891382" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13891382-DSC03407-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13891382" class="wp-caption-text">安良工商会鸣锣宣告就职典礼完成。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
