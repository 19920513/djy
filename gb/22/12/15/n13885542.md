<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这10道塞尔维亚美食 你一定想品尝</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885547-shutterstock_527270461-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>塞尔维亚传统的一席菜肴包括了肉类、蔬菜、面包、奶酪和点心等。图为传统的塞尔维亚午餐。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年12月15日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）体验欧洲的别样<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>，中部国家<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A1%9E%E5%B0%94%E7%BB%B4%E4%BA%9A.md#1">塞尔维亚</a>是个不错的选择。当地饮食融合了巴尔干、地中海、土耳其的特色，以及受时代变迁、移民文化等影响，形成非常多元的风格。在塞尔维亚，有10道经典<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>值得一尝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13885555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13885555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885555-shutterstock_1265164090-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13885555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885555-shutterstock_1265164090-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13885555" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>?evapi可以说是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A1%9E%E5%B0%94%E7%BB%B4%E4%BA%9A.md#1">塞尔维亚</a>的国民小吃，它是人们到餐厅几乎必点的食物。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一、?evapi</h2>
<p>塞尔维亚饮食受土耳其影响较深，当地著名的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>?evapi源自土耳其的烤肉k?fte kebab。?evapi是用猪肉、羊肉、牛肉或这三种肉类混合的碎肉制成，外型像一根香肠。通常搭配新鲜的洋葱、饼、奶酪或者酸奶油食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13885562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13885562" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885562-shutterstock_1132672715-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13885562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885562-shutterstock_1132672715-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13885562" class="wp-caption-text">Sarma是塞尔维亚传统食物之一，在巴尔干地区有多种作法。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>二、Sarma</h2>
<p>Sarma的作法类似亚洲的春卷，在巴尔干地区有多种作法。制作时，先将碎肉、生米及调料混合搅拌成馅料，再用卷心菜做外皮将馅料包裹起来，最后搭配酸卷心菜、排骨以及调料长时间焖煮。有些地区用鱼和熏培根代替肉类。Sarma的字面意思是“包裹”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13885561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13885561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885561-shutterstock_666882904-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13885561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885561-shutterstock_666882904-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13885561" class="wp-caption-text">Burek是一种用Phyllo酥皮制作的馅饼。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>三、Burek</h2>
<p>Burek是一种用Phyllo酥皮做成的馅饼，源自巴尔干半岛东部和土耳其，已成为塞尔维亚人的主食。它的馅料有牛肉、羊肉、猪肉或这三种肉类混合的碎肉，也有加入奶酪、菠菜等。Burek馅饼通常搭配当地的特色酸奶或咖啡食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13885565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13885565" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885565-shutterstock_1742222624-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13885565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885565-shutterstock_1742222624-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13885565" class="wp-caption-text">Pljeskavica肉饼的份量一般都比较大，和烤肉?evapi一样都是当地常见的食物。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>四、Pljeskavica</h2>
<p>Pljeskavica加面包的版本像个汉堡，很多人将之称为塞尔维亚汉堡。实际上Pljeskavica并不能算作汉堡。它是用碎牛肉、羊肉和猪肉或这三种肉类混合制成的烤肉饼，食用的时候可以不加任何饼或面包，仅搭配洋葱、当地著名酱料Ajvar、酸奶油、奶酪或辣椒等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13885564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13885564" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885564-shutterstock_1723104412-600x401.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13885564" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885564-shutterstock_1723104412-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13885564" class="wp-caption-text">酿辣椒Punjene Paprike也是塞尔维亚的常见菜肴。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>五、Punjene Paprike</h2>
<p>在塞尔维亚，酿蔬菜很受欢迎，酿辣椒Punjene Paprike是其中之一。它的馅料由不同的肉类、米饭、番茄酱、鸡蛋、香料和辣椒混合而成。</p>
<h2>六、Fi? Paprika?</h2>
<p>除了烤肉，塞尔维亚也流行吃海鲜。Fi? Paprika?是一道塞尔维亚的经典炖鱼，使用辣椒和蕃茄调味，味道鲜美。在餐厅点餐，要是觉得辣味不够，可以自己添加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13885563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13885563" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885563-shutterstock_1252730632-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13885563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885563-shutterstock_1252730632-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13885563" class="wp-caption-text">塞尔维亚的南瓜派Bundevara，外皮使用Phyllo酥皮制成。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>七、Bundevara</h2>
<p>塞尔维亚的甜食也很多元。Bundevara是当地受欢迎的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%82%B9.md#1">甜点</a>，样子与希腊的果仁蜂蜜酥相似，外皮由Phyllo酥皮制成，内馅是南瓜果肉，有时也加入葡萄干、肉豆蔻等香料。食用的时候可以撒上糖粉，热食或冷食均可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13885566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13885566" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885566-shutterstock_2151006911-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13885566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13885566-shutterstock_2151006911-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13885566" class="wp-caption-text">塞尔维亚久负盛名的红<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%A3%E6%A4%92%E9%85%B1.md#1">辣椒酱</a>Ajvar，里面有时会加入茄子。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>八、Ajvar</h2>
<p>Ajvar是当地人喜爱的一种红<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%A3%E6%A4%92%E9%85%B1.md#1">辣椒酱</a>，它可以用作调味品、酱汁、蘸酱或抹酱。Ajvar的传统配方是由红色甜椒、葵花籽油、盐和醋四种食材慢煮而成，制作1公斤Ajvar大约需要4公斤的新鲜红辣椒。红椒Kurtovska Kapija是公认最佳原料。</p>
<h2>九、Pala?inke</h2>
<p>Pala?inke类似可丽饼，但这种薄饼使用的面糊不需要静置，面糊和好后可以直接烹饪。它的内馅有奶油、巧克力酱、饼干、核桃和蜂蜜，或者咸味火腿等。</p>
<h2>十、Pule</h2>
<p>塞尔维亚原产的驴奶酪Pule，使用的并不是普通驴子的奶水，而是数量稀少的巴尔干驴子的奶水。驴奶自古以来被奉为营养佳品，产量比牛羊低，而且用驴奶制作奶酪有一定的难度，所以Pule据说是世界上最贵的奶酪。</p>
