<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台电寒冬送暖 三重办桌温饱在地弱势街友</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878818-588606-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台电送暖办桌温饱在地弱势街友。（北西区处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年12月05日讯】（大纪元记者宋顺澈台湾新北报导）新的一年即将到来，在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B2%81%E6%9C%AB.md#1">岁末</a>年终之际，为了让<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%97%E5%8F%8B.md#1">街友</a>们感受社会温馨，台电台北西区营业处与台电工会第35分会联合新北市政府社会局、新北市三重绅士协会、新北市女子美容商业同业公会等单位，于4日下午在三重区光兴市民活动中心举办“111（2022）年关怀街友<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B2%81%E6%9C%AB.md#1">岁末</a>惜福<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A4%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">餐会</a>”，由处长谢义隆带领同仁透过一连串的关怀活动，帮助街友度过寒冬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13878824" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13878824" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878824-588612.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13878824" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878824-588612-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13878824" class="wp-caption-text">办桌温饱在地弱势<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%97%E5%8F%8B.md#1">街友</a>。（北西区处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13878823" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13878823" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878823-588611.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13878823" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878823-588611-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13878823" class="wp-caption-text">办桌温饱三重在地弱势街友。（北西区处提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13878821" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13878821" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878821-588609.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13878821" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878821-588609-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13878821" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%92%E5%86%AC%E9%80%81%E6%9A%96.md#1">寒冬送暖</a>办桌温饱在地弱势街友。（北西区处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>北西区处本次活动除了发放御寒衣物、干粮、生活用品等物资，现场也提供行动沐浴车、义剪等服务，让街友们重新梳理自己并透过沐浴服务，清洗掉日积月累的生活压力，也为寒冬增添一份暖意。同时还有准备热腾腾的办桌佳肴，供街友们好好坐下来吃饱喝足。在活动的最后，发放慰问红包，象征社会不忘对他们的关心与祝福，使他们能安心地迎接新年的到来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13878820" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13878820" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878820-588608.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13878820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878820-588608-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13878820" class="wp-caption-text">台电办桌温饱在地弱势街友。（北西区处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13878822" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13878822" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878822-588610.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13878822" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13878822-588610-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13878822" class="wp-caption-text">寒冬送暖三重在地弱势街友。（北西区处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台电北西区处谢义隆处长表示，多数街友都有不得已的苦衷而露宿街头，此次活动对于散居各地的他们来说，可能是久违的饱餐时刻。台电除肩负稳定供电的使命外，也期望发挥睦邻友善的精神，以实际行动关怀弱势族群。希望借此活动抛砖引玉，呼吁更多民间公益团体加入，透过各种资源的整合，协助街友们能早日重返家庭、回归社会，共同照亮社会每个角落。◇</p>
