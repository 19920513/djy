<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>媲美花儿的观叶盆栽 让家居充满艺术气质</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890622-shutterstock_1892358949-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>观叶植物的品种繁多，有些品种的叶子色彩斑斓，比花朵还绚丽夺目，而且大部分很好养护，用来提升居家气质非常不错。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年12月23日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）家中的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%86%E6%A0%BD.md#1">盆栽</a>千篇一“绿”吗？这里有一些叶色斑斓的植物，拥有花儿般炫目的气质，居家摆上一盆，会让你的家充满艺术感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890623-shutterstock_2036284211.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890623-shutterstock_2036284211-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890623" class="wp-caption-text">霓虹合果芋拥有粉嫩的叶子，气质活泼可爱，而且好养护，新手园丁也能驾驭。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一、霓虹合果芋（Syngonium podophyllum）</h2>
<p>霓虹合果芋是很好照料的植物，适合新手园丁。它原产于南美洲，在室内可以全年种植。如果条件允许，将室温保持在60华氏度以上。它可以忍受一些直射光，喜欢潮湿的环境。在最初的几个月，它保持直立生长，之后会发展出长茎蔓。这时，你可以竖立杆子让茎蔓攀爬，或随时修剪茎蔓保持直立生长状态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890624" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890624-shutterstock_2178605943.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890624-shutterstock_2178605943-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890624" class="wp-caption-text">粉红公主蔓绿绒拥有宽大的心型叶片，叶色有不规则的粉、绿杂色，风格独一无二，是最热门的室内植物之一。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>二、粉红公主蔓绿绒（Philodendron erubescens &#8216;Pink Princess&#8217;）</h2>
<p>粉红公主蔓绿绒原产于南美热带地区，多年生，喜欢温暖、湿润的环境，以及排水良好的土壤。它的叶片有不规则的粉色、绿色等杂色，是非常受欢迎的观赏植物，在庭院或室内都可栽种。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890627" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890627-shutterstock_1928834285.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890627" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890627-shutterstock_1928834285-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890627" class="wp-caption-text">嫣红蔓的外观娇小可人，又容易养护，是十分受欢迎的小型<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%86%E6%A0%BD.md#1">盆栽</a>。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>三、嫣红蔓（Polka Dot Plant）</h2>
<p>这种植物的气质如其名，外型优雅小巧，叶色斑驳，属容易养护的小型观赏植物。它的品种不少，叶色以粉、红和白杂色较为常见。喜欢室内半阴、温暖的地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890628" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890628-shutterstock_1927890695.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890628-shutterstock_1927890695-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890628" class="wp-caption-text">白花紫露草是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%8F%B6%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9.md#1">观叶植物</a>中的明星，自带浪漫气质；可栽种在吊式花盆里作悬挂植物，是提升室内格调的不二选择。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>四、白花紫露草（Tradescantia）</h2>
<p>白花紫露草在明亮的间接光线下长势较好。如果发现叶子的颜色开始减退，表明植物没有获得足够的光照。至少将房间温度保持在50华氏度以上。它们喜欢潮湿的环境，但浇水过量容易导致根部腐烂。此外，它们的生长能力强，爆盆快，会长出许多漂亮长茎蔓；建议把它栽种在吊式花盆里，便于造型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890629" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890629-shutterstock_2029896950.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890629-shutterstock_2029896950-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890629" class="wp-caption-text">贝母叶的特点是叶色丰富。图为流行的粉红色品种。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>五、贝母叶（Caladium）</h2>
<p>贝母叶的叶色较多，有绿色、红色、粉色、白色，甚至橙色。很多人收集不同颜色的贝母叶。粉色的贝母叶也有很多样子，有的是在叶子中间长出心型粉色块，有的叶脉是粉色。它们在室内外都可以种植。若在室内栽种，需注意它对人和宠物有毒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890630" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890630-shutterstock_1982016563.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890630-shutterstock_1982016563-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890630" class="wp-caption-text">三色竹芋的叶子平滑光亮，叶子的杂色有如油画般的立体感而闻名。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>六、三色竹芋（Stromanthe Triostar）</h2>
<p>三色竹芋因叶子上的白、绿、粉色晕染恰到好处，艺术感十足而闻名。它原产于南美热带雨林，在室内温暖、潮湿的环境可以生长良好，必要时可使用加湿喷雾器来为叶片添加水分。另外，它们的叶子喜欢朝着光照的方向生长，建议每周转动一次花盆，以使叶子获得均匀的光照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890631" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890631-shutterstock_2104159685.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890631-shutterstock_2104159685-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890631" class="wp-caption-text">图为花叶万年青的红叶品种。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>七、万年青（Aglaonema）</h2>
<p>万年青是美丽又最易于护理的观赏盆栽之一。万年青的品种较多，绿叶品种为常见。这里推荐的是花叶万年青，常见叶色有银白、淡绿、粉、红等杂色。它对光照、肥料的要求不高，也无需太勤浇水。不过，它忌讳风吹，栽种的位置最好远离冷、暖气出口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890633-shutterstock_2041300787.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890633-shutterstock_2041300787-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890633" class="wp-caption-text">红掌（又称火鹤）会开出漂亮的花朵，不开花时又是极佳的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%8F%B6%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9.md#1">观叶植物</a>。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>八、红掌（Anthurium andraeanum）</h2>
<p>红掌（又称火鹤）会开出鲜艳夺目的花朵，是极具观赏价值的盆栽。若护理得好，它可以全年开花，每次花期持续长达三个月。照顾红掌并不难，只要将它种在排水良好的土壤中，适时浇水，放置在无强烈直射光照的地方，它会茁壮生长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890637" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890637-shutterstock_2021855177.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890637" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890637-shutterstock_2021855177-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890637" class="wp-caption-text">网纹草的叶色斑斓，外型小巧，非常适合用来妆点小空间。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>九、网纹草（Fittonia）</h2>
<p>网纹草的风格大方，外型小巧，适合摆在边桌这类小空间的地方。它的叶子五颜六色，有的整片叶色都是红的，有的长着粉色叶脉，呈现出如玻璃万花筒般的纹路，令人惊艳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890638-shutterstock_2048922812.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890638-shutterstock_2048922812-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890638" class="wp-caption-text">红双线竹芋的独特之处是它的条纹和会随着光线强弱开合的叶片。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>十、红双线竹芋（Calathea Pinstripe）</h2>
<p>因叶子上有两三条一组的粉、白色线条而知名。它原产于南美洲，喜欢较高的湿度，以及半阴、无强烈直射光的环境。它属祈祷植物家族的一员，因其叶子会在晚上聚拢，白天展开，这个开合过程像做祈祷的手势一样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890640" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890640-shutterstock_1217466364.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890640-shutterstock_1217466364-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890640" class="wp-caption-text">秋海棠的品种非常之多，简单分类可作观花、观叶两类。图为大王秋海棠部分品种。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>十一、大王秋海棠（Rex Begonia）</h2>
<p>秋海棠的品种繁多，通常可分成观花、观叶两类。观叶秋海棠的叶形和叶色变化多样，可谓叶比花娇，大王秋海棠是其中受欢迎的品种。它因叶片有各种色彩斑斓的花纹而闻名。将它摆放在散射光充足的地方，植株的叶色能更好地保持鲜艳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13890641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890641-shutterstock_1616750614.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13890641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890641-shutterstock_1616750614-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890641" class="wp-caption-text">橡胶树通常需要较大的种植空间。图为杂色品种。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>十二、橡胶树（Rubber Plant）</h2>
<p>橡胶树的叶面宽大、光亮，若家中有较大种植空间，它是不错的选择。常见的叶色有深绿、酒红及粉白杂色等。在过去，这种植物的汁液被用于制作橡胶，因而得名橡胶树。</p>
