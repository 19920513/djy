<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：艺术家在岩石上绘制超逼真动物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888944-The-Art-of-Roberto-Rizzo-1-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>意大利一位艺术家擅长在岩石上绘制极其细致的动物肖像。（The Art of Roberto Rizzo, Roberto Rizzo Fine Art和@robertorizzoart提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年12月21日讯】（英文大纪元记者Louise Chambers报导／张雨霏编译）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9.md#1">意大利</a>一位<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%AE%B6.md#1">艺术家</a>对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A0%E7%89%A9.md#1">宠物</a>和绘画情有独钟，多年来他结合这两种热情，在不同寻常的画布——<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B2%A9%E7%9F%B3.md#1">岩石</a>上绘制极其细致的动物肖像，看起来栩栩如生。</p>
<p>50岁的罗伯托?里佐（Roberto Rizzo）出生于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9.md#1">意大利</a>那不勒斯，现居住在西班牙南部的马拉加。高中毕业后，他就读于米兰的欧洲设计学院，之后开始在出版社担任插画师，主要创作水彩画。到了1996年，当他偶然接触到古老的岩画艺术时，他的职业生涯从此发生了重大转变。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13888935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13888935" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888935-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-8.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13888935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888935-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-8.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="429" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13888935" class="wp-caption-text">（The Art of Roberto Rizzo, Roberto Rizzo Fine Art和@robertorizzoart提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13888929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13888929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888929-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13888929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888929-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-2.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="458" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13888929" class="wp-caption-text">（The Art of Roberto Rizzo, Roberto Rizzo Fine Art和@robertorizzoart提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13888928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13888928" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888928-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13888928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888928-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-1.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="539" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13888928" class="wp-caption-text">（The Art of Roberto Rizzo, Roberto Rizzo Fine Art和@robertorizzoart提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我起初在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B2%A9%E7%9F%B3.md#1">岩石</a>上作画纯属为了好玩，在海滩上收集的鹅卵石上随便画一些东西”，里佐告诉《大纪元时报》，“没想到结果非常受欢迎，从那以后我就再没有停止过这种艺术创作。”</p>
<p>他说，每次创作时，他首先在岩石表面涂上由深到浅的多层丙烯颜料，然后结合岩石的形状“准确画出合乎比例的铅笔素描”。接着用中型画笔绘制皮毛和其它区域，最后再用最细的笔刷修饰细节部分。</p>
<p>里佐的岩画被用来当作装饰品、镇纸甚至作为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A0%E7%89%A9.md#1">宠物</a>纪念品而广受欢迎，因为他仅通过一张照片就能临摹出一幅丢失宠物的逼真作品。这些动物肖像是他最喜欢的委托作品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13888932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13888932" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888932-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-5.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13888932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888932-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-5.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13888932" class="wp-caption-text">（The Art of Roberto Rizzo, Roberto Rizzo Fine Art和@robertorizzoart提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13888931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13888931" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888931-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13888931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888931-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-4.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13888931" class="wp-caption-text">（The Art of Roberto Rizzo, Roberto Rizzo Fine Art和@robertorizzoart提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“这些宠物用纯洁、无条件的爱包围着我们，给我们带来欢乐和慰藉”，他说，“把我们去世的四足朋友描绘在岩石或画布上，通过这种独特的方式留住它们的记忆，将它们纯真的眼神永远定格。每当收到宠物主人的消息说我的艺术品很令他们感动时，我都会兴奋不已。”</p>
<p>“我不只画动物，但它们真的是我最喜欢的主题，因为这些动物肖像确实帮助我使这些冰冷的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E5%A4%B4.md#1">石头</a>焕发出别样的生机。”</p>
<p>值得一提的是，里佐以各种造型独特的天然<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E5%A4%B4.md#1">石头</a>做画材，但会保留岩石所有的坑洞和瑕疵，不会对石头的造型与棱角进行任何切割和打磨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13888933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13888933" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888933-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-6.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13888933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888933-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-6.jpeg" alt="" width="500" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13888933" class="wp-caption-text">（The Art of Roberto Rizzo, Roberto Rizzo Fine Art和@robertorizzoart提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13888941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13888941" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888941-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-18.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13888941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13888941-ET-Roberto-Rizzo-18.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13888941" class="wp-caption-text">（The Art of Roberto Rizzo, Roberto Rizzo Fine Art和@robertorizzoart提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>通常，一块小石头可能需要一天的时间画完，更大的作品可能需要数周时间，而一张超大的石头画布则可能需要几个月的时间才能完成。</p>
<p>“许多人认为这需要很大的耐心，但我认为这种想法是错误的；做自己真正喜欢的事情时不需要耐心。”里佐说。</p>
<p>自1996年以来，里佐已经创作了数千幅岩石画，包括从手掌大小的小石头到接近实物大小的动物复制品。近几年随着社交媒体平台的崛起，他也开始在<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/robertorizzoart/">Instagram</a>、<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/robertorizzoart">脸书</a>、<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC2VZBjwaBTsC5lCwnnqJcUw">Youtube</a>以及个人<ahref="https://www.robertorizzoart.net/">网站</a>上分享自己的作品。此外，他还在<ahref="https://www.etsy.com/shop/RobertoRizzoArt">Etsy</a>上出售他的精美艺术品。◇</p>
