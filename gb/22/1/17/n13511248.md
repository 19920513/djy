<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>简单烹调就能上桌 一盘猪肉片变化3种吃法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13516837-134bfab238784a51104b4221b8792a45.jpg" />
<h6>涮猪肉沾柑橘醋。（山岳文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【<strong>小编按】 每</strong>个家庭都有从市场买食材回家料理，一时吃不完的经验，这时候如果事先把食材调理好，下次上桌前，只要简单烹调就能上桌。一种食材可以变化多种吃法，例如猪肉片就可以清炒、烧烤、油煎或水煮，口味有照烧、葱爆、油渍、盐炒、杂炊等多种调理方法。餐餐有变化的饭菜肉，无时无刻都能优雅享受饮食。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">买猪肉片时（240～280g）分成2半</span></h2>
<p>冷冻保存时，尽量让猪肉以不重叠的方式放进袋子里，以方便冷冻与解冻。</p>
<h2><em><span style="color: #000080;">当天食用</span></em></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">涮猪肉沾柑橘醋</span></h3>
<p>1人份382kcal<br />
单手锅16cm</p>
<p>也可以汆烫后保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13516852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13516852" style="width: 274px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13516852-3064671c90954784a40ba83ff25865dc.png"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13516852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13516852-3064671c90954784a40ba83ff25865dc-274x400.png" alt="" width="274" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13516852" class="wp-caption-text">猪肉片川烫后，趁热拌上柑橘醋酱油、麻油之后就可以上桌。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>材料 【1人份】</strong></span></p>
<p>猪肉片 ┈ 120～140g<br />
柑橘醋酱油 ┈ 1.5大匙<br />
麻油 ┈ 1小匙</p>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>作法</strong></span></p>
<p>在锅中倒入热水煮滚，放入猪肉。迅速烫熟后捞起，放进保鲜袋里，趁热拌上柑橘醋酱油、麻油。</p>
<p>冷藏：2～3天／冷冻：1个月</p>

<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>要吃的时候搭上蔬菜</strong></span></p>

<h2><em><span style="color: #000080;">冷冻用</span></em></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">1. 炒猪肉片～甜咸好下饭！</span></h3>
<p>平底锅20cm<br />
1人份311kcal</p>
<figure id="attachment_13513624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13513624" style="width: 360px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513624-d1c234a948b0e6849a3feccf850e46ce.png"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13513624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513624-d1c234a948b0e6849a3feccf850e46ce-360x400.png" alt="" width="360" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13513624" class="wp-caption-text">甜咸软嫩的炒猪肉片，是一款简单料理又受欢迎的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%B8%E5%A4%87%E8%8F%9C.md#1">常备菜</a>，光是配饭吃就可以吃好几碗！（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<div class="mceTemp"></div>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>材料 【1人份】</strong></span></p>
<p>猪肉片 ┈ 120～140g</p>
<p>A、酒、酱油、味醂 ┈ 各2小匙。砂糖 ┈ 1小匙。蒜泥、姜泥 ┈ 各1/2小匙。</p>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>作法</strong></span></p>
<p>在保鲜袋中将A 混合，放入猪肉隔着袋子搓揉。静置约5分钟腌渍入味。</p>
<p><strong>＊保存期限</strong> 冷藏：2～3天／冷冻：1个月</p>
<figure id="attachment_13513623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13513623" style="width: 317px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513623-cf229cc01b0d3266f3b87e12c0102f52.png"><img class=" wp-image-13513623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513623-cf229cc01b0d3266f3b87e12c0102f52-450x290.png" alt="" width="317" b="204" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13513623" class="wp-caption-text">在保鲜袋中将调味料混合，放 入猪肉隔着袋子搓揉。静置约5分钟腌渍入味。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>要吃的时候…炒熟就好</strong></span></p>
<p>在平底锅中倒入1小匙麻油，开中火烧热，将猪肉炒到收汁，洒上1小匙白芝麻。将1大片切丝的莴苣、1/4包切成方便食用大小的萝卜婴拌在一起，与猪肉一同盛在盘子上。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">2. 猪肉沙嗲～香辣温和滋味，令人着迷！</span></h3>
<p>小烤箱<br />
1人份326 kcal</p>
<figure id="attachment_13513631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13513631" style="width: 312px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513631-2ffeb14d739bc8f565511cb6fb3eda0b.png"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13513631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513631-2ffeb14d739bc8f565511cb6fb3eda0b-312x400.png" alt="" width="312" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13513631" class="wp-caption-text">用小烤箱就可以做出餐厅级南亚风的猪肉沙嗲，香辣又温和的滋味，令人着迷！（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>材料 【1人份】</strong></span></p>
<p>猪肉片 ┈ 120～140g</p>
<p>A、原味优格（无糖）┈ 1大匙。酱油 ┈ 1小匙。蒜泥 ┈ 1/2小匙。粗磨黑胡椒粒 ┈ 少许。</p>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>作法</strong></span></p>
<p>在保鲜袋中将A混合，放入猪肉隔着袋子搓揉。静置约5分钟腌渍入味。</p>
<p>＊保存期限 冷藏：2～3天／冷冻：1个月＊</p>
<div class="mceTemp"></div>
<figure id="attachment_13513630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13513630" style="width: 317px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513630-e2a7c04422d359fbc8771610327bc111.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-13513630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513630-e2a7c04422d359fbc8771610327bc111.png" alt="" width="317" b="210" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13513630" class="wp-caption-text">在保鲜袋中将A混合，放入 猪肉隔着袋子搓揉。静置约 5分钟腌渍入味。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>要吃的时候…烤熟就好</strong></span></p>
<p>在每1根竹签上各插上1/4份猪肉，排进托盘里，以预热过的小烤箱烘烤约15分钟。途中若怕烧焦，可以盖上铝箔纸。⊙</p>
