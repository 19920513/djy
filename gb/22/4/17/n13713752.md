<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉市111学年新生报到人数出炉 班级数大幅增长</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713753-550454-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>	决心成就每个孩子，林森国小新生报到精彩画面，右后方为校长吴育楷。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年04月17日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市111学年度国小一年级<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%94%9F.md#1">新生</a>于4月15、16日完成报到及登记，各校将于暑假依常态编班规定，采公开抽签或电脑乱数方式，每班以25人编班及导师抽签作业。</p>
<p>受近日<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%96%AB%E6%83%85.md#1">疫情</a>持续升温影响，为减轻学校现场作业负担，及降低家长到校群聚感染风险，今年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%94%9F%E6%8A%A5%E5%88%B0.md#1">新生报到</a>采线上报到为主，现场报到为辅，并优化系统功能，提高运行速度及效率，让今年的国小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%94%9F.md#1">新生</a>线上报到人数，比较去年提高了7成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13713754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13713754" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713754-550455.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13713754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713754-550455-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13713754" class="wp-caption-text">宣信国小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%94%9F%E6%8A%A5%E5%88%B0.md#1">新生报到</a>现场。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13713755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13713755" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713755-550456.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13713755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713755-550456-600x423.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13713755" class="wp-caption-text">侨平国小校长何宪昌（左2）率领校方办理新生报到团队合影。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教育处处长林立生表示，今年的小一新生设籍人数为2,616人，实际报到人数为2,369人，依目前登记人数111学年度新生班级数预估101班，110学年度毕业班级数为85班，故预计增加16班。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13713756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13713756" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713756-550457.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13713756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713756-550457-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13713756" class="wp-caption-text">精忠国小以各式动物玩具向小朋友问好。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林立生也提醒新生家长，线上报到系统已关闭，目前尚有约240名新生未完成报到，尚未报到的家长，请尽快持户口名簿等资料向学区内学校报到。另因总量管制学校新生登记人数若超出可招收人数，将于学校公告新生入学排序名单后，协助超额的学生转介到其他邻近学校就读，新生人数仍会有所调整，也期待更多的新生加入本市教育的大家庭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13713757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13713757" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713757-550458.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13713757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713757-550458-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13713757" class="wp-caption-text">兴安国小新生报到现场。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13713758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13713758" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713758-550459.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13713758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13713758-550459-600x420.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13713758" class="wp-caption-text">教育处处长林立生（右4）也莅临兴嘉国小，关心新生报到情况与校长朱丽乖（右3）等人合照。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
