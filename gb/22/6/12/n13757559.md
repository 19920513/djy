<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宾州参议院褒奖神韵艺术团</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13757567-20220611-PA-State-Senator-Citation-copy-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>宾州参议院褒奖神韵艺术团莅临费城。图为宾州第二选区参议员Christine M. Tartaglione及参议院褒奖。（大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年06月12日讯】<span lang="ZH-TW">（大纪元费城记者站报导）</span><span lang="ZH-TW">继今年</span>2<span lang="ZH-TW">月份</span>8<span lang="ZH-TW">天</span>10<span lang="ZH-TW">场的成功巡回演出之后，应观众要求，</span><span lang="ZH-TW">?</span><span id="OBJ_PREFIX_DWT45_com_zimbra_url" class="Object"><span lang="ZH-TW"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E9%9F%B5.md#1">神韵</a></span></span><span lang="ZH-TW">艺术团于</span>2022<span lang="ZH-TW">年</span>6<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>11<span lang="ZH-TW">日和</span>12<span lang="ZH-TW">日再次莅临</span><span id="OBJ_PREFIX_DWT46_com_zimbra_url" class="Object"><span lang="ZH-TW">费城</span></span><span lang="ZH-TW">，在音乐学院剧院（</span>Academy of Music<span lang="ZH-TW">）演出</span>3<span lang="ZH-TW">场</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，</span><span lang="ZH-TW"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%BE%E5%B7%9E.md#1">宾州</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%82%E8%AE%AE%E9%99%A2.md#1">参议院</a>颁发<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A4%92%E5%A5%96.md#1">褒奖</a>状祝贺</span><span id="OBJ_PREFIX_DWT47_com_zimbra_url" class="Object"><span lang="ZH-TW"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E9%9F%B5.md#1">神韵</a></span></span><span lang="ZH-TW">艺术团</span><span lang="ZH-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A4%92%E5%A5%96.md#1">褒奖</a>信</span><span lang="ZH-CN">写道：“</span><span lang="ZH-CN">宾夕法尼亚州<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%82%E8%AE%AE%E9%99%A2.md#1">参议院</a>向<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E9%9F%B5%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%9B%A2.md#1">神韵艺术团</a>致敬，因为他们</span><span lang="ZH-CN">出色</span><span lang="ZH-CN">的服务记录</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">远远</span><span lang="ZH-CN">地</span><span lang="ZH-CN">超过了通常所称的</span><span lang="ZH-CN">模范的标准；并祝愿他们</span><span lang="ZH-CN">拥</span><span lang="ZH-CN">有一个最有价值和</span><span lang="ZH-CN">最</span><span lang="ZH-CN">充实的未来</span><span lang="ZH-CN">。”</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13757568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13757568" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13757568-20220611-PA-State-Senator-Citation-e1655007991895.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13757568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13757568-20220611-PA-State-Senator-Citation-e1655007991895.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="1003" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13757568" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%BE%E5%B7%9E.md#1">宾州</a>参议院褒奖<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E9%9F%B5%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%9B%A2.md#1">神韵艺术团</a>莅临费城。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">以下是宾州参议院褒奖全文翻译：</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext">2022<span lang="ZH-CN">年</span>6<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>11<span lang="ZH-CN">日</span><span lang="ZH-CN">和</span>12<span lang="ZH-CN">日</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span id="OBJ_PREFIX_DWT48_com_zimbra_url" class="Object"><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">巡回艺术团再度莅临费城，在</span><span id="OBJ_PREFIX_DWT49_com_zimbra_url" class="Object"><span lang="ZH-CN">音乐</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">学院剧院（</span>Academy of Music<span lang="ZH-CN">）展开演出。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">鉴于</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">世界首屈一指的中国古典舞和古典音乐艺术团神韵艺术团将于</span>2022<span lang="ZH-CN">年</span>6<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>11<span lang="ZH-CN">日和</span>6<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>12<span lang="ZH-CN">日</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">重返</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">费城音乐学院</span><span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">鉴于</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span>“<span lang="ZH-CN">神韵</span>?”<span lang="ZH-CN">意为</span><span lang="ZH-CN">“</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神舞动之美</span><span lang="ZH-CN">”</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，以及神韵艺术团对推动宾州艺术发展的重要贡献。神韵艺术团于</span>2006<span lang="ZH-CN">年在纽约州成立，其使命是复兴五千年的中国传统文化，这些文化在中共统治下</span><span lang="ZH-CN">几近</span><span lang="ZH-CN">失传。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵在中国</span><span lang="ZH-CN">仍</span><span lang="ZH-CN">被禁</span><span lang="ZH-CN">演。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">鉴于</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵每年在世界各地约</span>150<span lang="ZH-CN">个城市巡回演出</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">从东京到巴黎</span><span lang="ZH-CN">、</span><span lang="ZH-CN">从悉尼到纽约。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵的现场演出获得了雷鸣般的掌声和满堂彩的赞誉。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">有些观众驱车几百英里去看演出，有些观众则观赏</span><span lang="ZH-CN">了</span><span lang="ZH-CN">五六次</span><span lang="ZH-CN">演出。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">鉴于</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵的受欢迎程度反映了人们对善良、谐和和复兴的需求日益增长。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵代表了我们国家伟大民主的艺术自由，</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">将古老的传统</span></span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">展现在</span></span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">现代舞台</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">。古典舞、民族舞和民间舞与神韵现场乐团的原创</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">配乐完美搭配</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">数字天幕让</span><span lang="ZH-CN">观众沉浸在每</span><span lang="ZH-CN">一</span><span lang="ZH-CN">个场景中，使每场演出都美</span><span lang="ZH-CN">不胜收</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，感受</span><span lang="ZH-CN">到跌宕起伏的情节变化</span><span lang="ZH-CN">。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">今年是</span><span lang="ZH-CN">宾</span><span lang="ZH-CN">州居民第</span>15<span lang="ZH-CN">年体验神韵</span><span lang="ZH-CN">带给</span><span lang="ZH-CN">世界的精湛艺术和文化</span><span lang="ZH-CN">遗产。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">鉴于</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">在</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">展现了令人钦佩的领导力和品</span><span lang="ZH-CN">行</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，并</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">恪守</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">我们社会的原则，使我们的社区成为一个更好的</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">生活场所</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">，并为这一成就赢得了家人和朋友的钦佩，你</span><span lang="ZH-CN">们</span><span lang="ZH-CN">为自己、</span><span lang="ZH-CN">为</span><span lang="ZH-CN">家人</span><span lang="ZH-CN">、为</span><span lang="ZH-CN">我们的社区带来了荣誉</span><span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">因此，宾夕法尼亚州参议院向神韵艺术团致敬，因为他们</span><span lang="ZH-CN">出色</span><span lang="ZH-CN">的服务记录</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">远远</span><span lang="ZH-CN">地</span><span lang="ZH-CN">超过了通常被</span><span lang="ZH-CN">认</span><span lang="ZH-CN">为</span><span lang="ZH-CN">是</span><span lang="ZH-CN">模范的标准；并祝愿他们</span><span lang="ZH-CN">拥</span><span lang="ZH-CN">有一个最有价值和</span><span lang="ZH-CN">最</span><span lang="ZH-CN">充实的未来</span><span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">并指示将参议员</span>Christine M. Tartaglione<span lang="ZH-CN">提出的这份文件的副本转交给神韵</span><span lang="ZH-CN">在费城的</span><span lang="ZH-CN">主办方</span><span lang="ZH-CN">大</span><span lang="ZH-CN">费城法轮大法</span><span lang="ZH-CN">学</span><span lang="ZH-CN">会。</span></p>
