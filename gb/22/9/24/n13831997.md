<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：可爱金毛猎犬爬屋顶 吸引路人眼光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832004-Cute-Golden-Retriever-Hugh-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>金毛哈克（Huck）喜欢被关注，每天爬上屋顶问候路人。（由Lindenmuths提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年09月24日讯】（英文大纪元记者Anna Mason报导／李宇君编译）金毛猎犬哈克（Huck）喜欢吸引人注意，它找到了一个绝佳的方式。</p>
<p>这只金毛猎犬很有个性，几乎整天都待在屋顶上。如果路过的人拿起相机要拍它，这只漂亮的狗就会摆出各种超级可爱的姿势拍照。</p>
<p>“它喜欢人们向它挥手说，‘嗨，哈克，你好吗？’然后它就会摇着尾巴走下来。”主人贾斯汀?林登穆斯（Justin Lindenmuth）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13832006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832006" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832006-Huck1.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13832006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832006-Huck1-450x450.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832006" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13832009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832009" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832009-Huck3.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13832009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832009-Huck3-450x562.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832009" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13832010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832010" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832010-Huck2.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13832010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832010-Huck2-450x563.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832010" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13832012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832012-Huck4.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13832012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832012-Huck4-450x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832012" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>贾斯汀、妻子艾莉（Allie）和他们的两个小孩住在路易斯安那州，这对夫妇在2011年收养了哈克。</p>
<p>“哈克的个性非常喧闹活泼。”贾斯汀说，“它非常有爱心，喜欢和家人在一起。它很需要别人注意到它，但我们全家人都很喜欢它。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13832013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832013" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832013-Huck-collage.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13832013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832013-Huck-collage-450x272.jpeg" alt="" width="601" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832013" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哈克第一次跑到屋顶那天，贾斯汀正在家工作。他听到屋顶上传来隆隆声，以为是浣熊或其它什么动物，便跑出去看。</p>
<p>“是哈克，它边叫边摇着尾巴，从屋顶上俯视着我。”贾斯汀说，“我们后院有一个小山坡，它从小坡上跳到屋顶，就在屋顶上闲晃。”</p>
<p>贾斯汀大叫，艾莉过来看。</p>
<p>“哈克非常兴奋。”贾斯汀补充说，“因为它有了新的最佳视野，它可以从各个方向往街道看过去，还可以低头看我们。”</p>
<p>“它觉得这很酷。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13832014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832014" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832014-101499662_286999206012287_17187687782515997_n.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13832014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832014-101499662_286999206012287_17187687782515997_n-450x562.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832014" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13832015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832015" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832015-Huckk3.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13832015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832015-Huckk3-450x450.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832015" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13832016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832016" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832016-Huckk2.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13832016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832016-Huckk2-450x563.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832016" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哈克经常爬到屋顶，已经好几年了，但它仍感觉新鲜好玩。住在附近的人已习惯看它待在屋顶上，“屋顶上的哈克”已经成为一个可爱的景致。</p>
<p>艾莉说，哈克小时候，有一次从错的方向跳上屋顶，掉了下来。虽然只是四英尺（122厘米）高，但它从此以后，就一直都从对的地方跳上屋顶。</p>
<p>后来，他们还是帮哈克造了一个小坡道，让它可以毫不费力地爬上屋顶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13832018" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832018" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832018-Huckkk2.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13832018" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832018-Huckkk2-450x562.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832018" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13832017" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832017" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832017-Huckkk1.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13832017" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832017-Huckkk1-450x450.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832017" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“虽然它（上屋顶）已经跳了很长时间，但对它的臀部不好。金毛猎犬长大后可能会有臀部问题，所以我们为它造了一个小坡道。”艾莉说。</p>
<p>这家人不让哈克在下雨或酷热时留在屋顶上，但只要可以出去，它就会跑到它的快乐屋顶。</p>
<p>现在这个家庭又领养了一只五个月大的小狗，叫布鲁蓓尔（Bluebell）。</p>
<p>“它长得很像哈克。”艾莉说，“它比哈克更野，但它很可爱。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13832020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13832020" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832020-Bluebell.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13832020" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13832020-Bluebell-450x278.jpeg" alt="" width="602" b="372" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13832020" class="wp-caption-text">（由Lindenmuths提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现在布鲁蓓尔还太小，不能和它的大哥哥一起上屋顶，所以屋顶还完全是哈克的，哈克已经成了屋顶上的一个固定景致。</p>
<p>贾斯汀说，“我觉得人们在遛狗时真的很喜欢看到它，而且知道他们每次经过时，哈克一定会对着他们叫或摇尾巴。”◇</p>
