<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：一只气势如王的猫有着虎豹的皮纹</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828969-Thor-Bengal-i-1-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>这只孟加拉猫身上毛色混杂着虎豹皮纹，有很大的气魄。(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年09月20日讯】（英文大纪元记者Anna Mason报导／李宇君编译）这只住在比利时的孟加拉<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB.md#1">猫</a>托尔（Thor，北欧神话中的雷电与力量之神），气势如王。</p>
<p>托尔身上毛色混杂着虎豹<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%AE%E7%BA%B9.md#1">皮纹</a>，有着雷神般的气势。这让它一夜成名，杜拜甚至有人想用天价买这只雷神<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB.md#1">猫</a>。</p>
<p>小猫托尔拥有世界上最漂亮的皮毛：头和胸部有老虎的条纹，背部和腹部有豹子的斑点。个性上，主人说它也是有很大的气魄。</p>
<p>27岁的猫主人蕾妮?库喜科芙（Rani Cucicov）告诉《英文大纪元》，“它叫声宏亮，也非常会表达自己的情绪。”阳光照在托尔皮毛上时，会有点点金光在它身上闪烁。</p>
<p>伟绝的外表和个性，让这只9岁的孟加拉猫全球知名。它曾出过一些照片专辑，也在电视上亮相过。</p>
<p>杜拜曾经有人出100,000欧元高价想买这只明星猫，却被猫主人拒绝了。她说，“托尔是我们的家人，我们永远不会卖掉它。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13828981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828981" style="width: 508px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828981-Thor-bengal-cat-6-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13828981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828981-Thor-bengal-cat-6-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="508" b="508" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828981" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13828983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828983" style="width: 556px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828983-Thor-bengal-cat-15-450x341.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13828983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828983-Thor-bengal-cat-15-450x341.jpg" alt="" width="556" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828983" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13828982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828982" style="width: 511px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828982-Thor-bengal-cat-8-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13828982" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828982-Thor-bengal-cat-8-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="511" b="511" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828982" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<p>2013年，库喜科芙家人在荷兰的猫舍发现了5个月大的托尔。小托尔本来要成为一只表演猫，但因为尾巴上有一个凸起的小缺陷，便不能参加表演了。</p>
<p>“我们一见到小托尔，就爱上它了。它正是我们心目中最理想的猫，它是一只很有个性的小猫，非常适合我们家。”库喜科芙说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13828984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828984" style="width: 577px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828984-Thor-bengal-cat-4-450x316.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13828984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828984-Thor-bengal-cat-4-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="577" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828984" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13828985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828985" style="width: 562px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828985-Thor-bengal-cat-17-450x339.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13828985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828985-Thor-bengal-cat-17-450x339.jpg" alt="" width="562" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828985" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13828986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828986" style="width: 525px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828986-Thor-bengal-cat-22-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13828986" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828986-Thor-bengal-cat-22-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="525" b="525" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828986" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<p>她说，“有时候，我觉得它是我的小弟弟。托尔以为我母亲是它‘妈妈’，我和我兄弟们是它玩伴。”</p>
<p>这家人知道这只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%B7%E7%A5%9E%E7%8C%AB.md#1">雷神猫</a>所有的情绪、喜好，他们知道它心情好坏、什么时候想玩耍、什么时候肚子饿。</p>
<p>“我知道它喜欢什么、不喜欢什么。它知道相机要做什么，如果它不想拍照，就拍摄不了它。我们只有一条规则：绝不强迫托尔做它很不想做的事情。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13828987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828987" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828987-Thor-bengal-cat-13-450x562.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13828987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828987-Thor-bengal-cat-13-450x562.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828987" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13828988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828988" style="width: 520px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828988-Thor-bengal-cat-20-450x360.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13828988" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828988-Thor-bengal-cat-20-450x360.jpg" alt="" width="520" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828988" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<p>有一天，库喜科芙随便在即时电报（Instagram）上开设了一个账号“@bengalthor”，上传了一张托尔的冒险照片，第二天就收到朋友发讯息给她。</p>
<p>“朋友问我这只托尔是红迪（Reddit）首页上的托尔吗？”库喜科芙说，“没错，它也出现在9GAG的头版。我在网上到处都能看到它。”</p>
<p>只是上传了一张照片，托尔便在一夕间一举成名。</p>
<p>托尔的粉丝快速增加，每小时上升数千人，库喜科芙收到了来自世界各地的采访请求。</p>
<p>她说，“看到你的猫在沙发上，躺在自己身边，而全世界都有人喜欢它，感觉很好玩。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13828989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828989" style="width: 457px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828989-Thor-bengal-cat-2-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13828989" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828989-Thor-bengal-cat-2-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="457" b="457" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828989" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13828990" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828990" style="width: 428px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828990-Thor-bengal-cat-9-450x469.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13828990" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828990-Thor-bengal-cat-9-450x469.jpg" alt="" width="428" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828990" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13828991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13828991" style="width: 517px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828991-Thor-bengal-cat-18-450x302.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13828991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13828991-Thor-bengal-cat-18-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="517" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13828991" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Rani Cucicov)</figcaption></figure>
<p>收到这么多社交媒体青睐很有趣，收到的评论也很有趣。她说，“一开始，很多人不相信托尔是真的。他们说照片是我做出来的，是用老虎的头和豹的身体PS出来的。”</p>
<p>然而，托尔最让库喜科芙心动的地方却是它撒娇的方式：即使它正在睡午觉，一听到他们进房间，它会马上仰躺，四脚朝天，等着他们来抚揉它的腹部。</p>
<p>“我每次看到，心都融化了。”</p>
