<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>妙解《易》卦名</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827934-c9e02ecaef63e440abfac96e6a718683-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>妙解《易》卦名（空玄居士提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>伏羲文明祖，画卦载大道，</strong><br />
<strong> 卦画与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A6%E5%90%8D.md#1">卦名</a>，奥妙有玄机。</strong><br />
<strong> 阴阳三横爻，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%8D%A6.md#1">八卦</a>各所象，</strong><br />
<strong> 天地有山泽，水火与风雷。</strong><br />
<strong> 易与天地准，易道法自然，</strong><br />
<strong> 单卦成重卦，不易八纯卦，</strong><br />
<strong> 乾坤而艮兑，坎离与巽震。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13827917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827917" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827917-bd8e3a69646db028b7c59745b5676226.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13827917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827917-bd8e3a69646db028b7c59745b5676226.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="185" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827917" class="wp-caption-text">图1《易》卦之后天<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%8D%A6.md#1">八卦</a>（作者：空玄居士提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>变易五十六，方法很简易，</strong><br />
<strong> 卦象数三解，首解卦关联，</strong><br />
<strong> 先下而后上，可得四十六；</strong><br />
<strong> 水往山上流，蒙昧无知语；</strong><br />
<strong> 山上吹的风，渐高则渐冷；</strong><br />
<strong> 风调雷雨顺，气候盼恒常；</strong><br />
<strong> 雷打又火烧，噬嗑惨兮兮；</strong><br />
<strong> 火入地则熄，光灭是明夷；</strong><br />
<strong> 先地而后天，反序招否厄；</strong><br />
<strong> 先天而后地，正序得泰顺；</strong><br />
<strong> 地上有湖泽，生物会萃处；</strong><br />
<strong> 湖泽供取水，资源当节用；</strong><br />
<strong> 水淹氾大地，如大军出师；</strong><br />
<strong> 如前所举例，其余可类推。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13827919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827919" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827919-c8295a446001cd000fb6f67e6728df9d.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13827919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827919-c8295a446001cd000fb6f67e6728df9d.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="205" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827919" class="wp-caption-text">图2《易》卦 局部（作者：空玄居士提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>次解爻形象，六卦有三形；</strong><br />
<strong> 下天上山风，大畜小畜也；</strong><br />
<strong> 泽风中四阳，雷山中二阳，</strong><br />
<strong> 阳爻拆阴爻，大过与小过；</strong><br />
<strong> 山雷大张口，如大快朵颐；</strong><br />
<strong> 风泽谨开口，中肯孚众信。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13827923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827923" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827923-81d0aedeb38dfd6b5b6311a52617d382.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13827923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827923-81d0aedeb38dfd6b5b6311a52617d382.jpg" alt="" width="351" b="187" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827923" class="wp-caption-text">图3《易》卦 局部（作者：空玄居士提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>末解数运算，四卦两算法；</strong><br />
<strong> 上阳山地剥，初阳地雷复；</strong><br />
<strong> 山泽损经济，风雷益百业。</strong><br />
<strong> 八加四十六，再加六与四，</strong><br />
<strong> 妙解易<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A6%E5%90%8D.md#1">卦名</a>，依循此要领。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13827930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827930-c90c6b83bfb030e08fdd4814a19c7661.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13827930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827930-c90c6b83bfb030e08fdd4814a19c7661-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827930" class="wp-caption-text">妙解《易》卦名，图中暗红字所示为卦名。（空玄居士提供）</figcaption></figure>
