<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【第九届五湖四海宴】中秋节连假北港盛大登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821695-574425-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>【第九届五湖四海宴】中秋节连假北港盛大登场。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年09月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）由民间自发举办的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E6%B9%96%E5%9B%9B%E6%B5%B7%E5%AE%B4.md#1">五湖四海宴</a>”，于9月9日~11日中秋连假期间在北港盛大登场，因疫情关系，活动会场改至北港镇朝圣酒店办理，邀请云林及全国过108位优秀工艺师，以总铺师办桌的形式，将各项精湛工艺大会师来敬献给妈祖。</p>
<figure id="13821698" aria-describedby="caption-13821698" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821698-574428-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821698-574428-450x300.jpg" alt="雕塑作品" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13821698" class="wp-caption-text">雕塑作品（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主办单位云林县传统工艺文创产业发展协会总干事蔡享润表示，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E6%B9%96%E5%9B%9B%E6%B5%B7%E5%AE%B4.md#1">五湖四海宴</a>发想源自北港朝天宫妈祖过炉摆设拜神的108道菜，为传达对妈祖的虔诚敬意，工艺师们透过展现拿手的传统工艺技艺，以办桌的形式，将最好的工艺呈现给妈祖。今年迈入第九届，由民间自发办理、募集资源，感谢各界支持。</p>
<figure id="13821696" aria-describedby="caption-13821696" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821696-574426-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821696-574426-450x300.jpg" alt=" 庄博旺的粉线雕" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13821696" class="wp-caption-text">庄博旺的粉线雕（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13821697" aria-describedby="caption-13821697" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821697-574427-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821697-574427-450x300.jpg" alt="木雕工艺" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13821697" class="wp-caption-text">木雕工艺（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13821699" aria-describedby="caption-13821699" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821699-574429-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821699-574429-450x300.jpg" alt="雕塑作品" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13821699" class="wp-caption-text">雕塑作品（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13821700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13821700" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821700-574430.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13821700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821700-574430-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13821700" class="wp-caption-text">庄素月的十字绣（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13821701" aria-describedby="caption-13821701" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821701-574431-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13821701-574431-450x253.jpg" alt="捏面人" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13821701" class="wp-caption-text">捏面人（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次集结了全国超过108位优秀工艺师的精彩作品，包含木雕、妆佛、剪黏、纸雕、彩绘、花灯、锡艺等多样媒材，这民俗性活动不仅仅表现了对妈祖的崇敬，也提供全国传统工艺师们一个展示与交流的平台，对促进传统工艺能见度与薪传更多所助益，完整再现由北港妈祖信仰文化所孕育的百年艺镇特色。</p>
<p>“第九届五湖四海宴”展期自2022年9月9日起至9月11日止，现场除了有精彩的工艺作品展示外，还有工艺市集、博龟及多场工艺 DIY制作体验，内容丰富有趣，欢迎民众中秋连假期间阖家前往观赏，欣赏各工艺师的拿手绝活，体验各种工艺的制作过程。另继去年首度移展至台北华山展出获得极大回响与好评后，今年也将于10月28日至10月30日至台北华山文创园区展出。</p>
