<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中秋节烤肉 芝麻彩蔬串烧猪肉好吃又营养</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13820238-f2671800eaed417a4cdbdb00e3f66690-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中秋烤肉，芝麻彩蔬串烧猪肉好吃又营养。（曾晏均／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>“中秋月圆人团圆，家家门前<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>香。”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B%E8%8A%82.md#1">中秋节</a>除了吃月饼、剥柚、赏月外，最应景的就是与亲朋好友一起团聚吃<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>，共享美好时光。</p>
<p>今天，推荐读者一道超级好吃又健康的烤肉料理，不但充满原汁原味的烤肉香，又能搭配新鲜蔬菜兼顾蔬食营养，最重要得是料理方式超级简单，赶快来看看吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819387" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819387" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819387-25808.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13819387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819387-25808-600x548.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="548" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819387" class="wp-caption-text">各种蔬菜食材切正方块约2cm×2cm。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13819371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819371" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819371-25809.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13819371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819371-25809-600x582.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="582" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819371" class="wp-caption-text">猪肉切正方块约2cm×2cm。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>●<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB%E5%BD%A9%E8%94%AC%E4%B8%B2%E7%83%A7%E7%8C%AA%E8%82%89.md#1">芝麻彩蔬串烧猪肉</a></strong></h3>
<p><strong>材料：</strong></p>
<p>梅花肉500克，约切12块肉<br />
洋葱? 2/1颗<br />
红甜椒 ?1颗<br />
青椒 ? ? ?1颗<br />
熟白芝麻3克<br />
长竹叉 4支</p>
<p><strong>调味酱汁：</strong></p>
<p>酱油 2大匙<br />
米酒 1大匙<br />
糖 1大匙<br />
乌醋 1大匙</p>
<p><strong>做法准备：</strong></p>
<p>一、洋葱切正方片、青椒切、红甜椒、黄甜椒去除内膜切正方片，梅花肉切大方块。（各约2cm×2cm）</p>
<p>二、烤肉酱汁调味：<br />
备锅放入2/1杯水、酱油、米酒、糖，一起烧开煮到稍微浓稠即可起锅，然后倒入容器中再放入黄芥末、乌醋搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819372" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819372-timeline_20220809_184822.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13819372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819372-timeline_20220809_184822-600x508.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="508" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819372" class="wp-caption-text">放进烤箱。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三、串连组合烤肉串顺序：（4支）<br />
洋葱→猪肉→洋葱→红甜椒→猪肉→红甜椒→黄甜椒→猪肉→黄甜椒→洋葱。</p>
<p>※铁盘铺上铝箔纸，避免铁皮烧损。</p>
<p>四、备锅开小火，加入少许油，放入组串好的烤肉串，稍微煎煮上色，再撒上米酒即可起锅放入铁盘。<br />
※热锅中加上一点点水煮成汤汁，再淋上铁盘肉串上。</p>
<p>五、放入烤箱中约烤10分钟后取出，刷上烤肉酱汁之后，再放入烤箱继续烤，温度175°约烤20分钟，酱汁可随时补充上色，直至猪肉有点焦黄即为熟成，方可出炉。</p>
<p>六、肉串出炉摆盘，撒上白芝麻，妆点盘饰即完成。◇</p>
