<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【无鱼坊的摄影心视界】嘎嘎精灵灰树鹊</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823839-425688_medium-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>灰树鹊Dendrocitta formosae鸦科。（图／郑清海提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>林中嘎嘎色灰黯</strong><br />
<strong>展翼飞翔两白斑</strong><br />
<strong>飞虫树蛙加果实</strong><br />
<strong>来者不拒当三餐</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13823845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13823845" style="width: 418px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823845-425686_medium.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13823845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823845-425686_medium.jpg" alt="" width="418" b="665" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13823845" class="wp-caption-text">杂食性，以大型昆虫、蜥蜴、树蛙、雏鸟、浆果、果实等为食。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13823843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13823843" style="width: 454px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823843-425683_medium.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13823843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823843-425683_medium-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="454" b="605" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13823843" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%B0%E6%A0%91%E9%B9%8A.md#1">灰树鹊</a>Dendrocitta formosae鸦科</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%B0%E6%A0%91%E9%B9%8A.md#1">灰树鹊</a>又名，树鹊、嘎嘎仔。身长约36公分尾羽18公分，为普遍的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%99%E9%B8%9F.md#1">留鸟</a>。全身大致以黑、灰、褐三色为主，公母外表相似。嘴、脚黑色，头至后颈鼠灰色，背、胸为暗栗褐色，脸、两翼及尾部皆为黑色，两翼上并有一小块白斑。它虽生性聒噪，惟警觉性高，稍一靠近，即展翅飞离。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13823846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13823846" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823846-425687_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13823846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823846-425687_medium-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13823846" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13823844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13823844" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823844-425685_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13823844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823844-425685_medium-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13823844" class="wp-caption-text">喜欢栖息在中、低海拔山区、校园公园等杂木林以及果树的树冠上层或枝桠中层。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>飞行时，鼓翼动作大，两翼白斑清晰可见，并呈波浪状飞行。叫声短促沙哑似“嘎儿-葛哩哦”婉转喉音，或“嘎、嘎、嘎……”连续警戒声音。杂食性，以大型昆虫、蜥蜴、树蛙、雏鸟、浆果、果实等为食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13823847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13823847" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823847-425682_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13823847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823847-425682_medium-600x814.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="814" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13823847" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公母鸟会共同筑巢，也会共同育雏。繁殖期为4月至6月间，通常会在于离地约2~3公尺的树上，以树枝筑巢，巢为浅盘状，每窝约有2~4个蛋，颜色多变，有淡蓝、浅黄等色泽。喜欢栖息在中、低海拔山区、校园公园等杂木林以及果树的树冠上层或枝桠中层。</p>
