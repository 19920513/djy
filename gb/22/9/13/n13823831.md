<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>酒驾自撞电杆翻车 桃园警消救他出来送法办</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823832-574747-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>酒驾自撞电杆翻车，桃园警消救他出来送法办。（桃园警察分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年09月13日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）潘姓男子（45岁）驾驶自小客沿国际路往文中路方向行驶，行经国际路与延寿街口时，不慎自撞中央分隔岛后翻覆在车道上，人受困车中，桃园警察分局龙安派出所及桃园交通中队接获民众报案，立即赶抵现场救援，并进行路段封锁及疏导。</p>
<figure id="13823833" aria-describedby="caption-13823833" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823833-574748-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823833-574748-450x337.jpg" alt="酒驾自撞电杆翻车，桃园警消救他出来送法办。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13823833" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%92%E9%A9%BE.md#1">酒驾</a>自撞电杆翻车，桃园<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E6%B6%88.md#1">警消</a>救他出来送<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E5%8A%9E.md#1">法办</a>。（桃园警察分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>员警将潘男拖出车外，幸好他仅脸部有轻微擦伤，他说物品掉落，准备要捡拾却不小心撞上分隔岛。</p>
<figure id="13823834" aria-describedby="caption-13823834" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823834-574749-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823834-574749-450x337.jpg" alt="酒驾自撞电杆翻车，桃园警消救他出来送法办。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13823834" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%92%E9%A9%BE.md#1">酒驾</a>自撞电杆翻车，桃园<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E6%B6%88.md#1">警消</a>救他出来送<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E5%8A%9E.md#1">法办</a>。（桃园警察分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>员警闻到他身上有浓厚酒味，潘男表示在工地有喝几瓶啤酒，经实施酒精测试，测试值为0.79Mg/L严重超标，已触犯公共危险罪，全案依法移请桃园地检署侦办。</p>
<figure id="13823835" aria-describedby="caption-13823835" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823835-574750-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13823835-574750-450x337.jpg" alt="酒驾自撞电杆翻车，桃园警消救他出来送法办。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13823835" class="wp-caption-text">酒驾自撞电杆翻车，桃园警消救他出来送法办。（桃园警察分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警方呼吁民众饮用含酒精饮料后，切勿驾驶动力车辆，以免造成自身及他人危险。◇</p>
