<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美东南泛亚裔颁奖晚宴  萧柏恩获奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836323-USPAACC_SE_Gala_2022_09_23_255_b-vong.com_Boon_Vong-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>萧柏恩(左一)获奖，与两位颁奖人合影。((USPAACC))
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年09月30日讯】(大纪元记者吕尚明<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E7%89%B9%E5%85%B0%E5%A4%A7.md#1">亚特兰大</a>报导) 美国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%9B%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94.md#1">泛亚裔</a>商会美东南分会(US Pan Asian American Chamber of Commerce–South East，简称USPAACC-SE)的第六届族裔多元化行动(Diversity in Action)<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A2%81%E5%A5%96.md#1">颁奖</a>晚宴于9月23日在亚特兰大中城地区的Twelve Hotel举行，大约有200人出席。亚特兰大的华裔餐饮界名人萧柏恩（Joseph Hsiao）获得了十大亚裔企业奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836316" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836316-DSC_0181-e1664551167480.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836316-DSC_0181-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836316" class="wp-caption-text">萧柏恩(左三)与其他受奖人在台上合影。(吕尚明/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13836320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836320-DSC_0188.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836320-DSC_0188-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836320" class="wp-caption-text">USPAACC颁给H Restaurant Group的十大亚裔企业奖。(吕尚明/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>萧柏恩是餐馆集团H Restaurant Group的首席执行官，他的公司被USPAACC-SE认定为在美东南地区在过去几年中成长最快的十家亚裔企业之一。萧柏恩与弟弟萧柏(Matthew Hsiao)的父母来自台湾，曾多年辛苦经营在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E7%89%B9%E5%85%B0%E5%A4%A7.md#1">亚特兰大</a>西边Mableton的一家中餐馆。两兄弟自幼在父母经营的餐馆成长，2010年他与弟弟两人决定创业，但是心中计划的模式有别于父母所经营的中式餐馆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836317" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836317-DSC_0187-e1664549768832.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836317-DSC_0187-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836317" class="wp-caption-text">萧柏恩(右)与弟弟萧柏仁，也是他的企业H Restaurant Group的合伙人共享这个奖项。(吕尚明/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们首先接手位于Candler Park的著名亚特兰大餐馆The Flying Biscuit Cafe。这家餐馆创始于1993年，属于亚特兰大市区的地标级餐馆。后来逐步扩张，买下了位于中城的The Flying Biscuit Cafe，特色汉堡店FLIP Burger Boutique等等，成立了H Restaurant Group。他们还与大学时代的同学及好友Michael Judvytis合资购下位于Kennesaw的比萨及意大利餐馆Pisano’s Pizzeria &amp;amp; Italian Kitchen。现在H Restaurant Group旗下共有七家不同风格的餐馆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836318-DSC_0193-e1664549882517.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836318-DSC_0193-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836318" class="wp-caption-text">萧柏恩与他的比萨餐馆Pisano’s Pizzeria合伙人Michael Judvytis及Michael的女友合影。(吕尚明/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>USPAACC创立于1984年，总部位于华盛顿特区。创立的初衷是由于当时一些亚裔人士发现在美国亚裔人口快速成长，亚裔拥有的企业也不断增加的环境之下，美国缺乏代表亚裔创业族群的团体。现在USPAACC在全美根据地域分为五个分会，并致力于助益亚裔企业发展的各类培训、交流等资源与活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13836326" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836326" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836326-USPAACC_SE_Gala_2022_09_23_223_b-vong.com_Boon_Vong-e1664551295762.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836326-USPAACC_SE_Gala_2022_09_23_223_b-vong.com_Boon_Vong-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836326" class="wp-caption-text">现场印度传统舞蹈表演。(USPAACC)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13836327" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13836327" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836327-USPAACC_SE_Gala_2022_09_23_316_b-vong.com_Boon_Vong-e1664551358397.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13836327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13836327-USPAACC_SE_Gala_2022_09_23_316_b-vong.com_Boon_Vong-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13836327" class="wp-caption-text">现场舞狮表演。(USPAACC)</figcaption></figure>
