<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>月津港灯节光影盛宴 过年涌入18万人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565161-540056-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>月津港灯节光影盛宴登场，展现梦幻般光影魅力。（赖友容／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年02月09日讯】（大纪元记者赖友容台湾台南报导）今年迈入第10年的台南<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%88%E6%B4%A5%E6%B8%AF%E7%81%AF%E8%8A%82.md#1">月津港灯节</a>，去年因疫情停办，今年扩大规模重新登场，过年期间已吸引18万人次参观，除见证<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%90%E6%B0%B4.md#1">盐水</a>小镇10年来的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%95%E5%8F%98.md#1">蜕变</a>，也一同欣赏月津港限定登场的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%89%E5%BD%B1.md#1">光影</a>魅力。月津港灯节展期至2月28日。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%88%E6%B4%A5%E6%B8%AF%E7%81%AF%E8%8A%82.md#1">月津港灯节</a>是台南极具代表性的文化节庆，也是台湾水岸艺术灯节的指标品牌，近年更成为新锐艺术家崭露锋芒的重要发表平台。今年其范围扩大包括月津港、永成戏院、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%90%E6%B0%B4.md#1">盐水</a>历史街区、盐水车站、新创灯区等5大展区，作品数量也是历年最多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13565166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13565166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565166-540061.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13565166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565166-540061-600x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13565166" class="wp-caption-text">月津港灯节透过<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%89%E5%BD%B1.md#1">光影</a>流动设计，达到视觉感动效果。（赖友容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13565165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13565165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565165-540060.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13565165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565165-540060-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13565165" class="wp-caption-text">月津港灯节主体设计之一景观。（赖友容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13565168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13565168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565168-540062.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13565168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565168-540062-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13565168" class="wp-caption-text">2022月津港灯节盐水车站灯区《噪轨》。（台南市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台南市文化局表示，月津港灯节今年新开辟的两大灯区之一的“车站灯区”以盐水车站为据点，将3栋仓库摇身一变，化身为捻合历史文化与光影科技的前卫展区，3件作品《光流》、《噪轨》与《车站风景》以灯光、声效、烟雾等元素，演绎了光影流动的不同意象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13565164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13565164" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565164-540059.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13565164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565164-540059-600x301.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13565164" class="wp-caption-text">月津港灯节透过光影流动设计，呈现光耀炫目场景。（赖友容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13565163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13565163" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565163-540058.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13565163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565163-540058-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13565163" class="wp-caption-text">月津港灯节光影流动的场景，呈现静谧之美。（赖友容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，5大灯区中的艺术灯区、新创灯区、征件灯区则分别散落在月津港水域的不同区域。今年新辟的新创灯区，由艺术团队“404N.F”独立策展，提供年轻艺术团队发想与执行，共带来5件新型态的大型装置艺术；征件灯区也以“船说－One Piece航行计划”为主题全新策划，在水域后段更首度规划水上船屋，作品除了平面绘画，也涵盖立体装置、影像、声音装置、表演艺术等，不同船屋内各有丰富样貌。</p>
<p>台南市政府为扩大月津港灯节效益，自2019年起启动“月之美术馆”计划，逐步将短期的节庆活动，转型为长期艺术深耕行动。今年持续进行形象品牌建立、在地串连、艺术共创、老屋活化，让艺术创作与地方产生更多情感共鸣与连结。</p>
<p>“月之美术馆－2021漫月美行动特展”展期至2月28日，每日启灯时间为晚上6时至10时。民众可深入盐水历史街区，体验这座历史文化与艺术共创实验融汇绽放的经典小镇。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13565162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13565162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565162-540057.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13565162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565162-540057-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13565162" class="wp-caption-text">月津港灯节光影盛宴之一场景。（赖友容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
