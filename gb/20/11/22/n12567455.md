<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>田园写生比赛 云林学子彩绘口湖之美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470581-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>北港国际青商会第十三届“田园写生”比赛，小朋友认真的现场写生。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年11月22日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）北港<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E9%9D%92%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">国际青商会</a>第十三届“田园<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%99%E7%94%9F.md#1">写生</a>”比赛，22日于口湖游客中心对面的沐艺家DIY观光工厂热闹登场，报名参加的小朋友非常踊跃，今年以现场场景及自由发挥为主题，吸引一百多位来自云林各地的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F%E5%AD%A6%E7%AB%A5.md#1">国小学童</a>，在父母的陪伴下开心的用彩笔画下眼中的口湖之美！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12567460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12567460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470587.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12567460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470587-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12567460" class="wp-caption-text">充满欧风的沐艺家DIY观光工厂，成立一年多来，成了口湖的新亮点！也是最佳的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%99%E7%94%9F.md#1">写生</a>题材！（沐艺家观光工厂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12567458" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12567458" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470582.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12567458" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470582-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12567458" class="wp-caption-text">参加比赛的小朋友，用彩笔画下眼中的美景。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>北港<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E9%9D%92%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">国际青商会</a>会长许胜博表示，“田园写生比赛”行之有年，口碑甚佳，北港青商会在偏乡架构了这个平台，就是要让云林学子有机会展现绘画创意，同时促进<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%B2%E5%AD%90.md#1">亲子</a>关系。“训练自己、服务别人”是青商会的宗旨，今年特别选在口湖乡举办，照顾海口子弟，往后会持续举办此活动，继续关心偏乡学子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12567463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12567463" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470595.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12567463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470595-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12567463" class="wp-caption-text">田园写生比赛的一景（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12567461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12567461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470588.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12567461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470588-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12567461" class="wp-caption-text">沐艺家DIY观光工厂的生态池！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12567462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12567462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470589.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12567462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470589-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12567462" class="wp-caption-text">北港国际青商会第十三届“田园写生”比赛，22日于口湖游客中心对面的沐艺家DIY观光工厂热闹登场。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>沐艺家DIY观光工厂负责人连家淇说，这几年口湖乡观光起飞，渔乡风情景观相当有特色，有很棒的活海鲜及渔村生活体验，在口湖游客中心附近更有小日月潭之称的椬梧滞洪池及成龙湿地，还有台湾民主先驱李万居的故居，郑丰喜图书馆，欢迎全国乡亲有空来口湖走走。</p>
<p>带着女儿来参加写生比赛的黄姓妈妈说：我是口湖人，今天能免费的在口湖举办以我们家乡为主题的写生活动，让孩子透过画笔，把家乡的美记录下来，同时把口湖行销出去，我觉得非常有意义！不但提供偏乡孩子一个学习的机会，也增进了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%B2%E5%AD%90.md#1">亲子</a>关系！</p>
