<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>项婕如陪伴追爱李玉玺 画脸谱培养感情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200938471470-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《神之乡》戏剧发布会11月20日在台北举行。左起为李玉玺、项婕如。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年11月20日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）台湾原创漫画改编IP戏剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E4%B9%8B%E4%B9%A1.md#1">神之乡</a>》今天（20日）举办记者会，剧中<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A1%B9%E5%A9%95%E5%A6%82.md#1">项婕如</a>倒追<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E7%8E%89%E7%8E%BA.md#1">李玉玺</a>，彼此陪伴与鼓励，还有画脸谱的特别桥段。李玉玺说：“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A1%B9%E5%A9%95%E5%A6%82.md#1">项婕如</a>戏里一直勇敢追爱，我是回过头来才发现她默默陪我。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12564036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12564036" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200350072122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200350072122-ss1.jpg" alt="项婕如" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12564036" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12564036" class="wp-caption-text">项婕如出席发布会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12564038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12564038" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200938511470-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200938511470-ss1.jpg" alt="项婕如" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12564038" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12564038" class="wp-caption-text">项婕如出席发布会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两人首度合作，开拍前就一起训练培养默契，且大家都住在大溪剧组，下戏后都会一起讨论剧本，笑说他们本来都是慢熟的人，现在已经熟到不行。</p>
<p>被问到“会不会担心<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E7%8E%89%E7%8E%BA.md#1">李玉玺</a>女友吃醋？”时，项婕如笑说：“我们应该还好，为了戏剧嘛，当下不会想那么多，都满自然的。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12564045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12564045" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200349422122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200349422122-ss1-600x266.jpg" alt="神之乡" width="600" b="266" class="size-large wp-image-12564045" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12564045" class="wp-caption-text">台湾原创漫画改编IP戏剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E4%B9%8B%E4%B9%A1.md#1">神之乡</a>》11月20日举办发布记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12564050" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12564050" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200349182122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200349182122-ss1-600x374.jpg" alt="神之乡" width="600" b="374" class="size-large wp-image-12564050" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12564050" class="wp-caption-text">台湾原创漫画改编IP戏剧《神之乡》11月20日举办发布记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该剧描述台湾宫庙的在地文化，制作人郭建宏、导演庄景燊、编剧王莉雯、演员<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E6%9D%8E%E4%BB%81.md#1">李李仁</a>、李玉玺、项婕如、周采诗、Janet谢怡芬、王彩桦、廖威廉、林玟谊、林晖闵、卢以恩、黄新皓皆出席记者会现场分享拍戏趣事。</p>
<p>活动中播出独家版预告，李玉玺表示：“这是杀青后第一次大家再团聚，所以我们都很开心，而且今天第一次看到预告，看到我们努力的心血出炉，真的觉得又感动又感慨。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12564040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12564040" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200349592122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200349592122-ss1.jpg" alt="李玉玺" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12564040" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12564040" class="wp-caption-text">李玉玺出席发布会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12564041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12564041" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200938441470-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200938441470-ss1.jpg" alt="李玉玺" width="450" b="599" class="size-full wp-image-12564041" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12564041" class="wp-caption-text">李玉玺出席发布会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E6%9D%8E%E4%BB%81.md#1">李李仁</a>在剧中是吹小喇叭的乐器担当，他笑说：“不只我们两个搞音乐的，戏里从神将、陀螺、小喇叭、街舞甚至直播主都有，我都说我们可以组个大溪马戏团，因为各种担当都有。”他在戏中跟王识贤相互较劲，戏外感情则是认识20年的换帖兄弟，听到王识贤脚受伤，李李仁就去帮他按摩。</p>
<p>怀孕6个半月的Janet谢怡芬笑说：“我在戏里第一次有个二十多岁的帅儿子，戏外又有个新的家人，所以同时是两个孩子的妈。”剧中饰演她儿子的李玉玺笑说：“我还是看新闻才知道。”李李仁更说：“她怀孕3个月还出外景，只差没骑马，上个月还跟老公去潜水。”</p>
<p>王彩桦饰演发廊老板娘，不但剪遍剧中帅哥的头发，甚至剪过神像的头发，她笑称自己跟“三太子”一直都很有缘。她剧里和李李仁配对，还有场跳恰恰的戏，李李仁笑说，当天到剧组报到才知道要跳舞，算是“当场被逼就范”；加上场景完全没空调，两人跳到全身汗流浃背。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12564042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12564042" style="width: 445px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200349482122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011200349482122-ss1.jpg" alt="谢怡芬、李李仁、王彩桦" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12564042" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12564042" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）谢怡芬、李李仁、王彩桦出席新剧发布会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
