<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：沉入怀念里</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/fc5c07dc7e7a78cc52ce51df90d32c77-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>花莲吉安亲水生态公园绿映盎然，田野翠绿与壮阔山岚如诗如画。（李怡欣／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>
活在现代世间<br />
常常拢嘛会感觉有什么向我冲来，撞来<br />
譬如车辆、垃圾、噪音、烟尘<br />
譬如破病的山、浊浊的水<br />
譬如有色的广告、花红柳绿的壁墙<br />
譬如诈欺、特权、抢劫、黑枪……</p>
<p>常常设法闪避<br />
还是拢闪避不开<br />
只好把目睭眯起来</p>
<p>哪会知影目睭一眯起来<br />
反倒转看到记忆深处有什么离我而去<br />
譬如蓝天、绿野、青山、碧水<br />
譬如鸟只、蝴蝶、没喷农药的蔬菜水果<br />
譬如童年、乡情、纯真、欢笑<br />
譬如安详、诚恳、朴实、平和……</p>
<p>离我而去，离我而去<br />
唉，愈去愈远，愈去愈渺茫<br />
渐渐沉入怀念里……</p>
<p>1996/1/19台湾时报副刊</p>
<p><strong>Sink into the memory</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>At the present tine<br />
I often feel there are many things rush toward me, dash toward me<br />
For instant, the vehicle, the trash, the noise, the dust<br />
For instant, the mountain which is fell in sick, the water which in polluted<br />
For instant, the sexual advertisement, the scrabble wall<br />
For instant, the defraud, prerogative, rob, unlawful gun</p>
<p>I often try to keep off it<br />
But I always can’t keep off it<br />
As soon as to narrow my eyes</p>
<p>How can I know as soon as I narrow my eyes<br />
There are something get away from my deep memory on the contrary<br />
For instant, the blue sky, the green field, the vernal mountain, the pure water<br />
For instant, the birds, butterflies, vegetables and fruits which no farm insecticide<br />
For instant, the childhood, country feelings, innocence, laugh heartily<br />
For instant, the easiness, honest, simple, gentle…</p>
<p>Get away from me, get away from me<br />
Alas, more far more get away, more dimming more get away<br />
Sink into the memory slowly…@</p>
<p>责任编辑：林芳宇</p>
