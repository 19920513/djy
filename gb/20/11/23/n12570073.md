<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>1个动作克服圆肩 训练肩部肌群</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/fix-rounded-sholder0-1124-rrxf-health11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>1个动作克服圆肩，训练肩部肌群。（日日幸福提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #188638;"><strong>【编者按】</strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%86%E8%82%A9.md#1">圆肩</a>是很多人都有的毛病，让人显得不够精神、大方。1个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E4%BD%9C.md#1">动作</a>克服圆肩。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">克服<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%86%E8%82%A9.md#1">圆肩</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8.md#1">运动</a>（肩膀外旋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%8C%E8%82%89.md#1">肌肉</a>肌力训练）</span></h2>
<p><strong>时间和次数：</strong>10～15秒／2组</p>
<p><strong>训练部位：</strong>肩膀外旋肌群、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%A9%E8%83%9B%E9%AA%A8.md#1">肩胛骨</a>稳定、改善圆肩问题。</p>
<p><strong>作法：</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12572409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12572409" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/fix-rounded-sholder-3-1124-rrxf-health11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12572409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/fix-rounded-sholder-3-1124-rrxf-health11-600x376.jpg" alt="克服圆肩运动的作法。（日日幸福提供）" width="600" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12572409" class="wp-caption-text">克服圆肩<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8.md#1">运动</a>的作法。（日日幸福提供／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 双手抓住毛巾两端、两侧平举，手肘弯曲90度拳头朝前。</p>
<p>2. 以手肘为支点向上旋转。</p>
<p>NOTE：肩膀稳定不耸肩，手肘弯曲维持90度，速度切勿急促且避免过度旋转。</p>
