<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>本田紧凑型SUV——2021款HONDA CR-V</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>本田旗下紧凑型城市SUV——2021 Year Model CR-V。（HONDA提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年11月19日讯】（大纪元特约记者陆洋报导）CR-V是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AC%E7%94%B0.md#1">本田</a>旗下的一款紧凑型城市SUV。CR-V整个系列，自从在1995年诞生以来，已经经历了5次换代，凭借比同类实用的空间，和优秀的可靠性，一直是北美同类车型的销售冠军，可谓销量与口碑双收。第五代CR-V于2017年面世，本次2021款是第五代的facelift小改款车型。</p>
<p>2021款CR-V本次最为明显的改动之处就是进气格栅由之前的镀铬条改为熏黑设计，圆形雾灯改为直线的LED光源，转向灯整合到后视镜上。轮毂外型的变动，排气的造型由之前的圆形改为扁型设计。尾灯外型依然是采用五代车型的款式，唯一的区别改为了熏黑设计。除了这几处稍做改动之外，其余部位和上一代车型基本保持一致。轮毂有17寸、18寸、19寸可选。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12560869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12560869" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD1-450x296.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12560869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD1-450x296.jpg" alt="2021款HONDA CR-V" width="600" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12560869" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AC%E7%94%B0.md#1">本田</a>旗下紧凑型城市SUV——2021 Year Model CR-V。（HONDA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12560875" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12560875" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD7-450x286.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12560875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD7-450x286.jpg" alt="2021款HONDA CR-V" width="600" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12560875" class="wp-caption-text">本田旗下紧凑型城市SUV——2021 Year Model CR-V。（HONDA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2021款CR-V内饰也做了相应的升级，融入了大量的科技氛围。中控采用多层次设计，扶手处的盖板可以自由装卸，前门与后门处均有杯架槽。全系标配日行灯，7英寸多媒体屏幕，支持苹果CarPlay和Android。高配版本有无限充电板和手波换档片，四驱高配和7座高配均带有全景天窗，部分车型有电加热座椅和记忆模式。独特的后排车门设计可以呈90度开启，非常方便老年人上下车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12560876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12560876" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD14-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12560876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD14-450x299.jpg" alt="2021款HONDA CR-V" width="599" b="440" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12560876" class="wp-caption-text">本田旗下紧凑型城市SUV——2021 Year Model CR-V。（HONDA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12560880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12560880" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD19-450x305.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12560880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD19-450x305.jpg" alt="2021款HONDA CR-V" width="600" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12560880" class="wp-caption-text">本田旗下紧凑型城市SUV——2021 Year Model CR-V。（HONDA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新车提供两种动力，并且有7款车型。驱动系统分别有前轮驱动和四轮驱动，并有5座和7座可选，车型号后缀数字7代表是7座车型，后缀AWD字母的表示为四轮驱动。入门级的Vi2.0是四缸自然吸气发动机，输出功率为113kW/189Nm，其余6款1.5排量的均为涡轮增压引擎，输出功率为140kW/240Nm。所有型号均搭载无级变速箱。车身尺寸为4621*1855*1679mm，轴距为2661mm。基础款2.0配置的可选三种颜色，其余车型均有7种颜色选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12560878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12560878" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD18-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12560878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD18-450x299.jpg" alt="2021款HONDA CR-V" width="601" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12560878" class="wp-caption-text">本田旗下紧凑型城市SUV——2021 Year Model CR-V。（HONDA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12560882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12560882" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD20-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12560882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD20-450x300.jpg" alt="2021款HONDA CR-V" width="600" b="462" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12560882" class="wp-caption-text">本田旗下紧凑型城市SUV——2021 Year Model CR-V。（HONDA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>CR-V安全系数5颗星，安全配置有车道保持辅助系统、前撞警告、道路偏离缓解系统、车道偏离警告、坡道起步辅助、车辆稳定辅助系统、胎压检测系统、禁忌停止信号ESS、驾驶员注意监视器（辅助你驾驶时间，提醒你长时间驾驶需要停下来休息一下），在监视器上会出现Time for a break警示你需要休息，儿童安全座椅为国际公认标准。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12560884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12560884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD22-450x281.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12560884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD22-450x281.jpg" alt="2021款HONDA CR-V" width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12560884" class="wp-caption-text">本田旗下紧凑型城市SUV——2021 Year Model CR-V。（HONDA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12560886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12560886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD24-450x297.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12560886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2021HondaCR-VVTiLXAWD24-450x297.jpg" alt="2021款HONDA CR-V" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12560886" class="wp-caption-text">本田旗下紧凑型城市SUV——2021 Year Model CR-V。（HONDA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2021款CR-V在车身设计上采用了前车架结构，以吸收和偏转碰撞产生的能量，从而使驾驶者身在何处都很安全。特殊调节的悬架和动态助力转向可最大程度的提供舒适性，控制力和驾驶乐趣。由于新车全系升级配置，所以价格较上代车型整体上调A$2200-A$4500，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>市场售价由入门级A$30490-旗舰版A$47490不等。</p>
