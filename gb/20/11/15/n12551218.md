<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【日式家常菜】秋刀鱼饭好吃秘诀 经典酱汁比例</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/f1559a2bc29c7c86c3dabff7796f1ca9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>秋季的秋刀鱼很肥美，烤起来很香，搭配萝卜泥吃，非常美味。（图文／日日幸福提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E5%88%80%E9%B1%BC.md#1">秋刀鱼</a>是日本秋天美味的代表。这个季节的秋刀鱼很肥美，烤起来很香，搭配<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C%E6%B3%A5.md#1">萝卜泥</a>吃，非常美味。秋刀鱼也可以变成<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%8C%E9%A5%AD.md#1">拌饭</a>。虽然将煎好的秋刀鱼挑出鱼肉比较麻烦，但吃过的人都说值得花时间挑鱼肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6505690" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6505690" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509170500501758.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-6505690" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509170500501758-450x310.jpg" alt="秋刀鱼" width="450" b="310" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6505690" class="wp-caption-text">日式定食很常见“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E5%88%80%E9%B1%BC.md#1">秋刀鱼</a>一夜干”。（图／高雄市政府海洋局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">材料（ 4人份）</span></h3>
<p>秋刀鱼 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.2条<br />
白饭 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..2杯<br />
秋葵 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..6条<br />
白芝麻 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.1大匙<br />
柠檬 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..1/4个</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">调味酱</span></h3>
<p>酱油 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..2大匙<br />
白醋 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..2大匙<br />
糖 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;1小匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">做法</span></h3>
<p>1、秋刀鱼切2段，抹少许盐。10分钟后擦干鱼的水分，下锅煎香。</p>
<p>2、秋葵汆烫后切圆片。</p>
<p>3、用筷子挑出鱼肉，加入调味酱。</p>
<p>4、将做法3、秋葵以及白芝麻放入煮好的白米饭里一起搅拌均匀。食用时，加柠檬汁更香。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">TIPS</span></h3>
<p>秋刀鱼靠近腹部较长的刺一定要挑掉，大约1公分的刺较细的刺不需要特别挑掉，吃也不会刺到嘴巴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12551232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12551232" style="width: 112px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/af9ce79cb8a34ea133e7a458ff4f57ce.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12551232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/af9ce79cb8a34ea133e7a458ff4f57ce-450x604.jpg" alt="书封" width="112" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12551232" class="wp-caption-text">《和食家常》书封。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
