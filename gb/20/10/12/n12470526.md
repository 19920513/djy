<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>周晓涵为戏吊钢丝从4楼滑下 邵雨薇拍到背脊酸痛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120506042122-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台视、三立奇幻旗舰大戏《天巡者》“驱魔真君钟馗身边的三个女人”记者会于2020年10月12日在台北举行。图左起为周晓涵、邵雨薇、程雅晨。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年10月12日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）奇幻剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%B7%A1%E8%80%85.md#1">天巡者</a>》今天（12日）举办“钟馗身边的三个女人”记者会，包括该剧剧中钟馗的爱人“孟婆”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B5%E9%9B%A8%E8%96%87.md#1">邵雨薇</a>、钟馗的上司“秦广王”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%99%93%E6%B6%B5.md#1">周晓涵</a>，以及钟馗的妹妹<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A8%8B%E9%9B%85%E6%99%A8.md#1">程雅晨</a>齐聚一堂。由于拍剧需要吊钢丝，周晓涵超怕脚扭到，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B5%E9%9B%A8%E8%96%87.md#1">邵雨薇</a>更是拍完背脊酸痛。<br />
<figure id="attachment_12470561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12470561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120506302122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120506302122-ss1-600x399.jpg" alt="天巡者" width="600" b="399" class="size-large wp-image-12470561" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12470561" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%99%93%E6%B6%B5.md#1">周晓涵</a>、邵雨薇、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A8%8B%E9%9B%85%E6%99%A8.md#1">程雅晨</a>三位演员出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p><strong>周晓涵穿高跟鞋从4楼高飞下来</strong></p>
<p>周晓涵剧里所饰演的“秦广王”就是阎罗王，是地府第五处的最高主管、钟馗跟孟婆的直属上司。</p>
<p>而周晓涵每次出场都十分霸气，要从4层楼高很有气势的飞下来，还要脚穿高跟鞋，让她每次都担心会扭到脚，这镜头拍了7、8次才成功。</p>
<p>饰演“孟婆”的邵雨薇也不轻松，有一场戏她被另一个时空吸走，钟馗一直要抓住她的手，仿佛汤姆克鲁斯拍《不可能的任务》，她说每次拍完背脊都很酸痛。</p>
<p>最糗的是饰演钟馗妹妹的程雅晨，因为她有惧高症，每次只要高过膝盖的高度就会吓得哇哇叫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12470562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12470562" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120507252122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120507252122-ss1.jpg" alt="周晓涵" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12470562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12470562" class="wp-caption-text">周晓涵出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12470566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12470566" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120507112122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120507112122-ss1.jpg" alt="程雅晨" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12470566" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12470566" class="wp-caption-text">程雅晨出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12470565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12470565" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120507382122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120507382122-ss1.jpg" alt="邵雨薇" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12470565" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12470565" class="wp-caption-text">邵雨薇出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>邵雨薇煮孟婆汤煮成咖啡高手</strong></p>
<p>聊起最佩服男主角“钟馗”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BA%E5%86%9B%E7%BF%94.md#1">贺军翔</a>的特点，邵雨薇说他很有耐心健身，每次见面身材一天比一天更结实，因为36岁的他不愿意自己身材输给戏里年轻男演员们。</p>
<p>邵雨薇戏中为煮孟婆汤而变成煮咖啡高手，她还笑说“钟馗<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BA%E5%86%9B%E7%BF%94.md#1">贺军翔</a>）爱吃道具菜”，而且贺军翔功夫好，中间掏空都看不出来，因为理由是“不要浪费食物”。</p>
<p>《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%B7%A1%E8%80%85.md#1">天巡者</a>》是一部以钟馗伏魔降妖为改编题材的神幻戏剧，是台湾戏剧难得的尝试跟突破，将神话题材搬到现代时空框架，用高规格特效动画赋予故事奇异色彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12470568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12470568" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120506442122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010120506442122-ss1-600x304.jpg" alt="天巡者" width="600" b="304" class="size-large wp-image-12470568" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12470568" class="wp-caption-text">周晓涵、邵雨薇、程雅晨今天（12日）出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
