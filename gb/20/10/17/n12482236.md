<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【省时早餐】简易美味好消化“ 鲜蔬粥”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/e07f6428112dc69be2a6b4bd4b9ba405-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>鲜蔬粥备料简易、料理省时，相当适合吃完大餐后的隔天早上食用，好消化不失营养！（图／采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>让肠胃休战一下！周末全家人通常会上馆子吃高热量食物，或三餐不正常，因此星期一最好准备容易消化的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A5.md#1">粥</a>，记得上桌前先冷却才不会拖延吃饭的时间！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">必要材料（2人份）</span></h2>
<p>切丁蔬菜 1杯<br />
芝麻油 1汤匙<br />
白饭 1碗<br />
烤<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%8B%94.md#1">海苔</a> 1/2张</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">调味酱</span></h2>
<p>酱油 2汤匙<br />
芝麻油 1汤匙<br />
捣碎的芝麻粒 少许</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">步骤</span></h2>
<p>1. 将<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>、马铃薯、洋葱、红萝卜等蔬菜切成小丁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12482257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12482257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/afce37a2082233b769805bc2bfa4dd23.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/afce37a2082233b769805bc2bfa4dd23-450x338.jpg" alt="洋葱" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12482257" class="wp-caption-text">将<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>、马铃薯、洋葱、红萝卜等蔬菜切成小丁。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 在锅内倒入芝麻油后，以中火翻炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12482258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12482258" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/846d8c0be1a7b9046554b92d60aa41b8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/846d8c0be1a7b9046554b92d60aa41b8-450x337.jpg" alt="芝麻油" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12482258" class="wp-caption-text">在锅内倒入芝麻油后，以中火翻炒综合蔬菜。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 加入2杯水与白饭，以中火继续煮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12482259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12482259" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/bee113b87717491c2268d4ef8c5745ce.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/bee113b87717491c2268d4ef8c5745ce-450x337.jpg" alt="粥" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12482259" class="wp-caption-text">加入水、白饭，以中火煮。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 煮至饭粒变软后，将火关掉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12482260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12482260" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/db556c86fc6a21f956fc48b45f6d7a55.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/db556c86fc6a21f956fc48b45f6d7a55-450x338.jpg" alt="粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12482260" class="wp-caption-text">煮至饭粒变软后，将火关掉。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 起锅后加点捏碎的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%8B%94.md#1">海苔</a>，配上一碟小菜。（就算只是煮白<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A5.md#1">粥</a>，加调味酱跟海苔就非常好吃了！）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12482261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12482261" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/f946e7664624ae478392bd62dfd25e3c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/f946e7664624ae478392bd62dfd25e3c-450x338.jpg" alt="海苔" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12482261" class="wp-caption-text">起锅后加点捏碎的海苔或酱油就很好吃！（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
