<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新西兰大选计票显示工党暂时领先</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/image_2020-10-17T20_12_13-600x400.png" />
<h6>新西兰总理杰辛达·阿德恩于2020年10月3日在新西兰奥克兰的伊顿山战争纪念馆投票。 (Photo by Hannah Peters/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年10月17日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者安琪新西兰编译报导）新西兰</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">大选计票在新西兰晚上7点正式开始。截至新西兰时间</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">45pm</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，已清点了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">13.8%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的选票，工党的表现甚至比民调数字还要好。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">目前工党获得了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">50.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的选票，在国会中可获</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">66</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个席位；国家党</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(National)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">获得</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">26%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的选票，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">34</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">席位；绿党</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Greens)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">获得</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的选票，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">席位；行动党</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Act)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">获得</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">席位；新西兰优先党</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(NZ First)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">获得</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的选票，毛利党</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Maori Party)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">获得</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的选票。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">新保守党</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(New Conservative )1.5%，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">机会党</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(The Opportunities Party (TOP)</span><b>1.3%</b><b>。</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">新西兰进步党</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Advance NZ (Umbrella party) 0.9%.</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在晚</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">点</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">40分</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，计票显示工党候选人在国家党国</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">会</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">议员或退休的国家党国会议员所拥有的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">18</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个选区中领先。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">国家党主席彼得·古德费勒</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Peter Goodfellow)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">说，政府一直在进行应对</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Covid-19(</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">中共病毒</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">运动，但他们很难取得任何突破。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">他说，国家党的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">30%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">支持率将是一个强大的反对阵营。“我认为我们需要这样做，让政府承担责任。”</span></p>
<p>责任编辑：刘毅</p>
