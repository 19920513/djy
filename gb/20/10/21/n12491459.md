<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>泰式鲭鱼罗勒饭 异国美味料理平底锅就搞定</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/eb64fc0685d100de92e123d33595a812-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>泰式鲭鱼罗勒饭，鲭鱼取代猪肉的打抛鱼料理，风味独特。（图／大是文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>“煮鱼”是很多人的痛处，要怎么料理才会好吃够味，需要用什么器具才行？下面我们用一只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E5%BA%95%E9%94%85.md#1">平底锅</a>搞定异国料理“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%BC%8F.md#1">泰式</a>打抛鱼”和副菜“涮<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>沙拉”，好吃美味一定让你吃了就停不下来，重点是相当好料理又省时，做法简单不复杂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12502817" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12502817" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_794860645.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_794860645-450x300.jpg" alt="鲭鱼" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12502817" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%AD%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲭鱼</a>营养成分高，价格不贵，是很多家庭喜欢的食材。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%BC%8F.md#1">泰式</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%AD%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲭鱼</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8B%92.md#1">罗勒</a>饭（2人份）</span></h2>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>材料</strong></span></h2>
<p>鲭鱼的鱼肉片：1片（100公克）<br />
彩椒（红椒、黄椒）：各1／2个<br />
洋葱：1／2个<br />
沙拉油：2大匙<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8B%92.md#1">罗勒</a>叶（切末）：5片<br />
热饭：2碗份<br />
荷包蛋：2颗蛋</p>
<figure id="attachment_12502820" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12502820" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1721163883.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1721163883-450x300.jpg" alt="彩椒" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12502820" class="wp-caption-text">彩椒有各种颜色，红、黄、橘、绿等颜色；图为红椒和黄椒。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12502816" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12502816" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_554601856.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_554601856-450x300.jpg" alt="罗勒叶" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12502816" class="wp-caption-text">罗勒叶不是九层塔，类似茴香的气味，多用于南亚料理。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">A</span></h3>
<p>蒜（切末）：1小匙<br />
生姜（切末）：1小匙<br />
红辣椒（去籽切圆片）：1根</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">B</span></h3>
<p>鱼露：2小匙<br />
蚝油：1大匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法</span></h3>
<p>1. 鲭鱼、彩椒、洋葱切成1公分大小。<br />
2. 沙拉油入锅加热炒A，香气出来后放入1炒软后，加入B和罗勒叶快速翻炒调味。<br />
3. 将2放入已盛好饭的盘子里，依个人喜好放入荷包蛋。</p>
<p><strong>用这些鱼也可以！鲣鱼、生鲑鱼、剑旗鱼、大西洋鲑。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12502819" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12502819" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1631991265.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1631991265-450x298.jpg" alt="剑旗鱼" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12502819" class="wp-caption-text">剑旗鱼是一种大型鱼类，多用于煮汤或是制作成罐头食品。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">涮<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>沙拉</span><span style="color: #008000;">（2人份）</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12502755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12502755" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/p121.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/p121-450x337.jpg" alt="涮牛肉" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12502755" class="wp-caption-text">涮牛肉沙拉，搭配泰式料理也很对味！（图／大是文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>牛肉（切薄片）：100公克<br />
豆瓣菜（和兰水芹）：1束<br />
香菜：1／2束</p>
<figure id="attachment_12502815" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12502815" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_343406270.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502815" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_343406270-450x298.jpg" alt="豆瓣菜" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12502815" class="wp-caption-text">豆瓣菜是一种西洋菜，料理方式炒食、川烫、煮汤。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">A</span></h3>
<p>鱼露：2小匙<br />
橄榄油：2大匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法</span></h3>
<p>1. 在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E5%BA%95%E9%94%85.md#1">平底锅</a>里烧一锅热水，把牛肉放入热水中汆烫30秒捞起，放入滤勺。<br />
2. 豆瓣菜和香菜切成易入口的大小。<br />
3. 将1盛盘放上2，淋上混合均匀的A。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12502821" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12502821" style="width: 110px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/232f1b704bc80a551acf4e2b89bda97a-e1603710954389.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12502821" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/232f1b704bc80a551acf4e2b89bda97a-e1603710954389.jpg" alt="大是文化" width="110" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12502821" class="wp-caption-text">一只平底锅的零失败鱼料理书封（图／大是文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
