<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>太妍获首尔歌谣大赏大奖等3奖 上台好慌张</h2>
<a href="https://d19vw3711v9lks.cloudfront.net/H2tbHuALj"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>太妍出席第29届首尔歌谣大赏红毯照。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年01月30日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）第29届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%96%E5%B0%94%E6%AD%8C%E8%B0%A3%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%8F.md#1">首尔歌谣大赏</a>（29th Seoul Music Awards）今日（30日）晚间在首尔高尺天空巨蛋举行，由申东烨、赵宝儿与金希澈担任主持人。本届的专辑大奖仍由防弹少年团（BTS）拿下，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%AA%E5%A6%8D.md#1">太妍</a>则获得了音源大奖、OST奖及本赏3个奖项。</p>
<p>本日参与颁奖典礼的歌手有Super Junior、TWICE、Red Velvet 、NU&#8217;EST、MAMAMOO、MONSTA X、ITZY、TXT（TOMORROW X TOGETHER）、AB6IX、NCT DREAM、宋佳人、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%AA%E5%A6%8D.md#1">太妍</a>、请夏、河成云、金在奂、Paul Kim、Kassy、金贤哲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11833036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11833036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200130060731100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11833036" title="TWICE attend 29th Seoul Music Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200130060731100707-600x400.jpg" alt="TWICE attend 29th Seoul Music Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11833036" class="wp-caption-text">TWICE出席第29届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%96%E5%B0%94%E6%AD%8C%E8%B0%A3%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%8F.md#1">首尔歌谣大赏</a>红毯照。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11833034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11833034" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200130060743100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11833034" title="Red Velvet attend 29th Seoul Music Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200130060743100707-600x419.jpg" alt="Red Velvet attend 29th Seoul Music Awards" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11833034" class="wp-caption-text">Red Velvet出席第29届首尔歌谣大赏红毯照。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>为本届活动担任红毯及典礼压轴演出嘉宾的歌手，是出道15年的Super Junior。他们在红毯亮相时，仅有6位成员出席。对此，典礼主持人申东烨说明，希澈因录制《认识的哥哥》而未能参与红毯，Super Junior成员厉旭也说明，由于成员们有其他行程才未能共同参与红毯，但舞台表演时大家都会在一起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11832971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200130060723100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11832971" title="Super Junior attend 29th Seoul Music Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200130060723100707-600x393.jpg" alt="Super Junior attend 29th Seoul Music Awards" width="600" b="393" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11832971" class="wp-caption-text">Super Junior 出席第29届首尔歌谣大赏红毯照。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Super Junior本日除了演唱最新主打歌《2YA2YAO!》，还带来了《The Crown》、《SUPER Clap》与《Sorry Sorry》的舞台演出。</p>
<p>去年推出《四季》与《Purpose》等作品的太妍，这天一共获得音源大奖、OST奖及本赏三个奖项。为IU及吕珍九主演的人气韩剧《德鲁纳酒店》演唱的OST《?????（All about you／名为你的诗）》的太妍，在拿下OST奖时，与本日担任颁奖人的少女时代成员崔秀英相互拥抱。</p>

<p>太妍在发表得奖感言时表示，自己曾演唱过许多OST（原声带），她认为去年自己为《德鲁纳酒店》演唱的OST时，优秀的演员们的演技和歌曲创造了很棒的画面，因此若自己的声音能够为戏剧起到好的作用，今后她也会努力歌唱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11832975" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200130060749100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11832975" title="Taeyeon attend 29th Seoul Music Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200130060749100707.jpg" alt="Taeyeon attend 29th Seoul Music Awards" width="401" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11832975" class="wp-caption-text">太妍出席第29届首尔歌谣大赏红毯照。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在典礼颁发最后的音源大奖之际，被叫到名字的太妍在座位上惊讶得瞪大眼睛摀住嘴，上台后仍久久说不出话。她表示：“真的没有想到会得奖，现在觉得很慌张，真的很感谢。”</p>
<p>对于获得大奖感到意外的太妍，还表示自己真的很紧张，也想到了家人和成员们，“真的很感谢粉丝们，因为有粉丝们等待着我，我才能持续唱下去。”她笑说：“今后我真的会更努力的，谢谢SONE（少女时代官方粉丝名）。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11832977" style="width: 375px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200130060759100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11832977" title="Taeyeon attend 29th Seoul Music Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200130060759100707.jpg" alt="Taeyeon attend 29th Seoul Music Awards" width="375" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11832977" class="wp-caption-text">太妍出席第29届首尔歌谣大赏红毯照。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
