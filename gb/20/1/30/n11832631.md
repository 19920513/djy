<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】鲈鱼头骨腩汤 天然保健食物首选</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>冷冷的寒流来袭，别忘了注意保暖，避免感冒了。想要提升免疫力，不妨煮一锅鲈鱼汤，暖和全家人的身子。鲈鱼汤头鲜甜，肉质绵密让大人小孩都喜爱，而且富有丰富的蛋白质和维他命D，营养价值高。（图/梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【材料】</strong></span></p>
<p>鲈鱼头骨腩 1条（Sea bass Head &amp; Bone 1 piece）<br />
红萝卜 4安士（Carrot 4 oz）<br />
西芹 6安士（Celery 6 oz）<br />
山药 6安士（Nagaimo 6 oz）<br />
姜 4片（Ginger 4 slices）<br />
水 6杯（Water 6 C）</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【做法】</strong></span><br />
1.所有蔬菜切成大块备用，姜拍扁备用。鱼头骨腩切成小块，洗干净后用餐巾纸吸干水分，洒上少许盐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832816" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11832816" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11832816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-24-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼骨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11832816" class="wp-caption-text">新鲜鲈鱼（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11832828" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11832828" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-29.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11832828" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-29-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼骨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11832828" class="wp-caption-text">备料时，鱼头骨腩切成小块，要注意洗干净不要有血水，这样才不会有腥味，最后用餐巾纸吸干水分，再洒上少许盐。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.热锅下油，放入姜片煎香，再放入鱼头和鱼骨，中大火煎至两面呈金黄色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11832845" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-21.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11832845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-21-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11832845" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，放入姜片煎香，再放入鱼头和鱼骨，中大火煎至两面呈金黄色。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.期间烧热一锅热水，当鱼骨煎至金黄色的时候，立即加入滚水，鱼汤会出现奶白色（这就是煮出奶白色鱼汤的方法）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11832854" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11832854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-15-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼骨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11832854" class="wp-caption-text">鱼骨煎至金黄色的时候，立即加入滚水，鱼汤会出现奶白色。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.再把所有蔬菜材料放入，大火烧滚后改为中火滚约15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11832856" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/b60a206daf07085ee53cf0b69c95c159.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11832856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/b60a206daf07085ee53cf0b69c95c159-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11832856" class="wp-caption-text">煮出奶白色鱼汤才把所有的蔬菜放入锅内，用大火烧滚后改中火滚约15分钟。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.最后加少许盐和胡椒粉，鱼汤便可上桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11832867" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-11.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11832867 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11832867" class="wp-caption-text">最后加少许盐和胡椒粉，就可以准备上桌了!（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【梁师傅小贴士】</strong><br />
?汤会浓缩成大约3碗左右，但味道非常鲜甜，营养价值很高，适合大人小孩食用。◇</p>
