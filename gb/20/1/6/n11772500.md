<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台湾阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061055002378-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾阿里山区梅山乡太和村樟树湖169县道10公里路旁美丽的河津樱树。（苏家弘提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月06日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台北报导）您看过<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>上都是水滴的美照吗？台湾林务局<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>森林铁路管理处导览解说员苏家弘说：“雾雨中的樱花有另一种美”。他也分享在南投县竹山大鞍林道13K民宿前方拍摄到的美丽云海景观。</p>
<p>专业摄影师苏家弘告诉记者，前几天他在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>区梅山乡太和村樟树湖169县道10公里路旁，拍摄王姓茶农栽种的美丽河津樱树，“元旦早上刚好雾很浓，拍摄雾雨中的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>，与晴天不一样，上面都是水滴显出樱花之美。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772780" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11772780" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061053482378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11772780" title="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061053482378-600x337.jpg" alt="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11772780" class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山区梅山乡太和村樟树湖169县道10公里路旁美丽的河津樱树。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11772788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11772788" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061124292378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11772788" title="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061124292378-600x337.jpg" alt="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11772788" class="wp-caption-text">绿绣眼觅食河津樱。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苏家弘表示，去年开始观察这棵河津樱，树龄10年施肥管理好，他看到有熊蜂来采蜜；今年河津樱树花期与去年差不多，但因前几天下大雨，花瓣掉了不少，感觉比较稀疏，“拍摄当天阴天，整个雾非常浓，有雾雨的感觉；所以跟去年拍摄完全两样情，樱花上面有水滴、水珠，有生命力的感觉。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772782" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11772782" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061054572378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11772782" title="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061054572378-600x337.jpg" alt="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11772782" class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山区梅山乡太和村樟树湖169县道10公里路旁美丽的河津樱树。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每逢初春，樱花在阿里山区绽放，吸游客上山追景。苏家弘说，樱花从含苞待放，要经过寒冷及太阳光的照射，樱花花期到了就开的比较多，观赏期大约2-3星期，但樱花怕风和下雨，若遇大雨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E8%8A%B1.md#1">赏花</a>期只有1─2周。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11772781" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061053292378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11772781" title="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061053292378-600x337.jpg" alt="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11772781" class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山区梅山乡太和村樟树湖169县道10公里路旁美丽的河津樱树。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他说，从瑞里风景区到樟树湖茶园，沿路的花很迷人，除河津樱之外，还有山樱花、梅花、虎头兰、状元红等可观赏，还有一间像农村里的谷仓房很漂亮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11772784" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061122162378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11772784" title="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061122162378.jpg" alt="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" width="337" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11772784" class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山区山樱花。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11772786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11772786" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061123162378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11772786" title="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001061123162378-600x337.jpg" alt="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11772786" class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山区状元红。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他也分享，几天前早上8点在南投县竹山大鞍林道13K民宿前方，拍摄到的云海景观，“当天雾气雾茫茫的，看到云海旁边有山景感觉非常壮观，跟阿里山小笠原的云海有的拼，小笠原有2,500-3,000公尺高，大鞍高度约1,000公尺感觉比较清静，云好像海一样很漂亮。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11772792" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001060824532378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11772792" title="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001060824532378-600x337.jpg" alt="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11772792" class="wp-caption-text">台湾南投县竹山大鞍林道13K民宿前方的云海景观。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11772793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11772793" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001060824502378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11772793" title="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001060824502378-600x337.jpg" alt="组图：台阿里山区雾雨中的樱花之美" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11772793" class="wp-caption-text">台湾南投县竹山大鞍林道13K民宿前方的云海景观。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
