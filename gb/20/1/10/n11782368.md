<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>汉堡也可安心吃！掌握3点自制汉堡 营养又美味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/healthy-hamburger-homemade_644936938-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在家自制汉堡、汉堡排，有很多营养、热量低而口感好的替代品。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span class="s1">快餐店中常见的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%89%E5%A0%A1.md#1">汉堡</a>，往往被认为是热量高、对身体没有益处的食物。这是因为这类汉堡多使用白<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">面包</a>、油炸肉和糖分高的酱汁当作食材。自己在家动手，能做出美味的健康汉堡。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">自己制作<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%89%E5%A0%A1.md#1">汉堡</a>，可以选用不同种类的优质肉类。红肉和家禽肉都是优质的蛋白质来源，红肉能让人体补充铁、锌和维生素</span><span class="s2">B12</span><span class="s1">。而像是鸡肉和火鸡肉这类家禽肉，不仅容易消化，而且也含有维生素</span><span class="s2">B</span><span class="s1">、铁、锌和铜等营养。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">另外，快餐店中所贩售的汉堡，其使用的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">面包</a>多半营养价值不高。在家制作汉堡时，可以选择全麦面包、杂粮面包等较健康的面包。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">掌握肉品蔬菜、酱料和面包的来源，并采用健康的方式调味和烹调，让自己和家人吃得安全又开心！</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><span class="s1">一、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E5%88%B6%E6%B1%89%E5%A0%A1.md#1">自制汉堡</a>排</span><span class="s2"><span class="Apple-converted-space">??</span></span><span class="s1">营养美味热量低</span></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11782394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11782394" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_353736437.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11782394 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_353736437-600x398.jpg" alt="自制汉堡排，可将1/4～1/2的绞肉换成替代食材，让全家人吃下更多营养。（shutterstock）" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11782394" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E5%88%B6%E6%B1%89%E5%A0%A1.md#1">自制汉堡</a>排，可将1/4～1/2的绞肉换成替代食材。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">自己制作<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%89%E5%A0%A1%E8%82%89.md#1">汉堡肉</a>，不仅热量较低，也是帮助家人补充营养的好方式。市售的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%89%E5%A0%A1%E6%8E%92.md#1">汉堡排</a>多以牛肉为主，只是牛绞肉油脂较少，煎起来又干又硬，因此会加入脂肪量高的猪绞肉，吃起来才会湿润多汁。常见的牛、猪肉比例为</span><span class="s2"> 7</span><span class="s1">：</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">，若要让<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%89%E5%A0%A1%E6%8E%92.md#1">汉堡排</a>肉汁满满，猪绞肉量甚至要再增加。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">其实，肉饼的好口感，不一定靠提高脂肪。自制汉堡排时，可加入洋葱、青葱、迷迭香、大蒜、胡萝卜、地瓜、蘑菇、杏鲍菇，甚至是小扁豆、豆腐或牡蛎。加了这些食材，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%89%E5%A0%A1%E8%82%89.md#1">汉堡肉</a>吃起来更多汁，而且富有多层次的风味。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">煎烤汉堡肉时，要记得不要太过频繁的用铲子压肉排，才不会挤出过多的汤汁。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">设计配方时，大约可将</span><span class="s2">1/4</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s2">1/2</span><span class="s1">的绞肉换成替代食材，以下提供参考食谱，你也可自己发挥创意，利用手边有的食材来替换。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><strong><span class="s1">【材料】</span></strong><span class="s3">低脂牛绞肉</span><span class="s1">1000g、</span><span class="s1">洋葱</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">颗、胡</span><span class="s1">萝卜</span><span class="s2">1/4</span><span class="s1">根、</span><span class="s1">大蒜</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">颗、</span><span class="s3">蘑菇</span><span class="s1">100g、</span><span class="s1">盐</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">匙、</span><span class="s1">黑胡椒粉半匙。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><strong><span class="s1">【作法】</span></strong></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">1. </span><span class="s1">洋葱、蒜头、胡萝卜去皮洗净切丁，用调理机打碎。取一个不沾锅，加入少许食用油，将打碎的食材拌炒出香气，放一旁冷却备用。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">2. </span><span class="s1">将蘑菇切成细丁，取一调理盆，加入所有食材，用汤匙拌匀，再用多只筷子用力搅拌，经过搅拌的肉纤维将自然断裂，搅打到出黏性即可。如果觉得用筷子不方便，也可以直接用手抓拌，很快就会产生黏性。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">3. </span><span class="s1">依照想要的大小，将汉堡肉压成圆形。一般来说，给孩子吃的小汉堡，一个大约</span><span class="s2">40</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s2">60</span><span class="s1">克，大人的一份约是</span><span class="s2">80</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s2">100克</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><span class="s1">二、自制酱料</span><span class="s2">?不吃过多油盐糖</span></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_10937431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10937431" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/shutterstock_254351062.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10937431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/shutterstock_254351062-600x400.jpg" alt="用过熟的酪梨做沙拉酱，不仅口感细腻，而且很营养。" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10937431" class="wp-caption-text">过熟的酪梨适合做成汉堡酱，口感细腻又营养。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">有了健康的汉堡排，酱料也别马虎，否则加了过多油、盐、糖的酱料，很容易让自制汉堡肉的努力功亏一篑。不妨利用自制酱料，为汉堡增添独一无二的风味。哪天，你可能会自创出许多独门酱料，让家人或客人争相探询你的食谱呢！</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">可以尝试自制的酱料有很多种，例如：番茄酱、酪梨酱、咖喱酱和椰奶酱等。自制酱料可以按照自己和家人的口味，调整酱汁的甜咸程度。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">如果你比较忙，想用更简单的方式处理酱汁，也可以尝试使用卡路里含量相对较低的芥末酱（</span><span class="s2">mustard</span><span class="s1">）、拉差香甜辣椒酱（</span><span class="s2">Sriracha</span><span class="s1">）和牛排酱（</span><span class="s2">steak sauce</span><span class="s1">），但使用时切记不要加超过</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">汤匙的份量。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">另外，其它酱汁像是美乃滋（</span><span class="s2">mayo</span><span class="s1">）和大蒜蛋黄酱（</span><span class="s2">aioli</span><span class="s1">）的卡路里含量高，使用时的份量不能超过</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">茶匙。</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><span class="s1">三、减少碳水? 找寻面包替代品</span></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11782398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11782398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_168781877.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11782398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_168781877-600x404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11782398" class="wp-caption-text">波特菇足足有一个成人的手掌这么大，口感肥厚多汁，烤熟以后用来取代面包相当有饱足感。(shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">对想要控制体重的人来说，面包中过多的碳水化合物，可能是需要解决的问题。可以使用一些面包的替代品，不仅减少碳水化合物的摄取，还让口味更丰足。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">你可以尝试无碳水的云片松饼（</span><span class="s2">cloud bread</span><span class="s1">），或是像</span><span class="s2">In-N-Out</span><span class="s1">汉堡店的生菜汉堡（</span><span class="s2">Protein Style Hamburger</span><span class="s1">）一样，用厚厚的生菜代替面包。或是，你也可以考虑用波特菇（</span><span class="s2">Portobello mushroom</span><span class="s1">，又称龙葵菇或皇帝菇）来取代面包，波特菇足足有一个成人的手这么大，口感肥厚多汁，用来取代面包不仅营养，也相当有饱足感。</span></p>
