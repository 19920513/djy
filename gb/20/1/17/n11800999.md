<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】煎鲈鱼柳配无花果汁酱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-2-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff
- 年节缺少不了一道营养又可口的佳肴，不妨把“煎鲈鱼柳配无花果汁酱”列入菜单，酸酸甜甜好开胃！（图/梁志生提供）

<h6>年节缺少不了一道营养又可口的佳肴，不妨把“煎鲈鱼柳配无花果汁酱”列入菜单，酸酸甜甜好开胃！（图/梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>这次介绍的菜肴，适合佳节享用，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%88%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲈鱼</a>营养丰富，能让一家老小身体健康。</p>
<p>【材料】<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%88%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲈鱼</a>一条 1.5磅 （Sea bass 1.5 lbs）<br />
芦笋8条（Asparagus 8 strips）<br />
小蕃茄6粒（Small tomato 6 ）<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E8%8A%B1%E6%9E%9C.md#1">无花果</a>酱汁半杯（Fig sauce ? C）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11801027" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11801027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-14-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11801027" class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E8%8A%B1%E6%9E%9C.md#1">无花果</a>酱汁材料】<br />
红酒醋2大匙（Red wine vinegar 2 T）<br />
牛油半大匙（Butter ? T）<br />
橙皮果酱3大匙（Marmalade 3 T）<br />
无花果5颗（Fig 5 pieces）<br />
红酒半杯（Red wine ? C）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11801028" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11801028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-13-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11801028" class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【无花果酱汁做法】</p>
<p>无花果去皮后压烂成茸，加入果酱和红酒醋，用牛油煮热后，再加上红酒煮至浓稠便可。</p>
<p>【煎鲈鱼做法】<br />
1. 准备鲈鱼一条，将肉取出，用盐和胡椒粉将鱼块涂抹均匀，腌10分钟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11801029" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11801029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11801029" class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 热锅下油， 把鱼块两边煎香至熟，盛放在碟上 。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11801030" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11801030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11801030" class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 芦笋用油盐水烫熟放旁边，小蕃茄做装饰，最后淋上无花果酱汁便可上桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11801033" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11801033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11801033" class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11801032" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11801032" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11801032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11801032" class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【梁师傅小贴士】</strong><br />
? 无花果酱可以搭配鸭肉、羊肉和海鲜，也可以直接涂在饼乾和吐司上，做成小点心。<br />
? 鲈鱼富含蛋白质、维生素A、维生素B、钙、镁、锌、硒等营养元素；具有补肝肾、益脾胃、化痰止咳之效，对肝肾功能不佳的人有很好的补益作用。<br />
? 鲈鱼还可治胎动不安以及少乳等症，准妈妈和生产妇女吃鲈鱼可以补身，但又不会因为营养过剩而导致肥胖。鲈鱼可说是健身补血、健脾益气和益体安康的佳品。<br />
? 鲈鱼血中还含有较多铜元素，铜能维持神经系统的功能。建议铜元素缺乏的人可食用鲈鱼来补充。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11801036" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11801036 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9-3-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11801036" class="wp-caption-text">鲈鱼富含多种营养元素，能顾及一家老小饮食均衡。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
