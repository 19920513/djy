<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2020波士顿第一夜 游行烟火庆新年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-1FirstNight2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大波士顿区中华文化协会一如既往舞龙、舞狮，举起“新年快乐”4个中文字参与游行。(刘景烨/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年01月04日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）2019年12月31日中午开始，“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E7%AC%AC%E4%B8%80%E5%A4%9C.md#1">波士顿第一夜</a>2020”庆典在多地上演，成千上万波士顿人及游客涌入庆祝2020新年到来。这是第44届的“Boston First Night”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11766272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11766272" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-2FirstNight2-e1578073081949.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11766272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-2FirstNight2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11766272" class="wp-caption-text">冰雕之一。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天中午，华人褚玲舞蹈团携知音舞韵、波士顿京剧协会等多个团体，在Copley Place Mall带来“传统中国舞蹈”主题演出。下午，大波士顿区中华文化协会(GBCCA)在公共图书馆表演各种中华民俗艺术。晚上，波士顿公共公园的青蛙池塘还有“冰上奇观”表演，其中2018年奥运会铜牌得主Mirai Nagasu的花样溜冰引起观众们此起彼伏的喝采。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11766277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11766277" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-4FirstNight2-e1578073187809.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11766277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-4FirstNight2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11766277" class="wp-caption-text">游行队伍之一。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11766280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11766280" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-5FirstNight2-e1578073238889.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11766280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-5FirstNight2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11766280" class="wp-caption-text">游行队伍中的踩高跷。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚上6点后，全民大游行队伍从考布利广场(Copley)出发。警车摩托鸣笛开道，随后由波士顿警察局长格罗斯(William Gross)领头，乐队、古典汽车、众多社区团体以及警笛轰鸣的消防车和救护车缓缓行过。队伍最后则跟随着成千上万的民众。大波士顿区中华文化协会一如既往舞龙、舞狮，举起“新年快乐”4个中文字参与游行。</p>
<p>俯瞰游行路线的Prudential大厦亮起“20”灯光，与参与庆祝的民众遥相呼应。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11766281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11766281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-6FirstNight2-e1578073295118.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11766281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-6FirstNight2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11766281" class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E7%AC%AC%E4%B8%80%E5%A4%9C.md#1">波士顿第一夜</a>”的主舞台表演。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11766283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11766283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-7FirstNight2-e1578073344561.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11766283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-7FirstNight2-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11766283" class="wp-caption-text">冰雕欢迎2020年到来！。(艾瑞娜/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>但是，今年的“波士顿第一夜”也有不顺利的插曲。由于天气不好，主舞台下一片泥泞。而本应成为冰雕展焦点的“五月花号”模型冰雕直到31日晚上还没完成。在主舞台旁，众多游客饶有兴致地围观、拍摄工作人员切割和组装冰块。</p>
<p>另外，在搭建庆典设施过程中，34岁的建筑工人麦克斯威尼(Brandon J. McSweeney)被3500磅的灯柱底座填充物砸死。在当晚6点的大游行开始前，考布利广场主舞台的主持人郑重地与观众们一同向麦克斯威尼默哀致意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11766274" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11766274" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-3FirstNight2-e1578073142538.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11766274" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-3-3FirstNight2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11766274" class="wp-caption-text">晚上7点，波士顿公共公园上空先绽放合家欢烟火。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>当然迎接2020新年的到来，放烟火是不可少的。晚上7点，波士顿公共公园上空先绽放合家欢烟火。午夜倒数至新年来临那一刻，波士顿海港就燃放更多的烟火庆贺。◇</p>
