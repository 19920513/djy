<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>不畏寒流 卑南族南王部落狩猎祭凯旋归</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477364-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>卑南族南王部落狩猎祭，勇士凯旋归，部落族人在中途迎接。（台东市公所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年12月31日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾台东报导）不畏寒流来袭，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E5%8D%97%E6%97%8F.md#1">卑南族</a>南王部落<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%A9%E7%8C%8E%E7%A5%AD.md#1">狩猎祭</a>31日上午完成训练，带着狩猎的成果回到卑南文化公园，部落女子在中途迎接，为他们戴上花环，穿上传统服饰，再一同到“迎猎门”。</p>
<figure id="12657092" aria-describedby="caption-12657092" style="width: 604px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477365-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477365-450x300.jpg" alt="卑南族南王部落狩猎祭。" width="604" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12657092" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E5%8D%97%E6%97%8F.md#1">卑南族</a>南王部落<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%A9%E7%8C%8E%E7%A5%AD.md#1">狩猎祭</a>。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">台东市</a>公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>家家户户也都准备了麻油鸡等各种丰盛食物，迎接凯旋归来的勇士，大家吟唱传统歌谣，喝酒助兴。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">台东市</a>长张国洲也到卑南文化公园慰劳部落族人，并致赠加菜金。</p>
<p>张国洲表示，族人经过猎场2天的狩猎，学习各种狩猎的技巧，带着收获返回凯旋门，也带回快乐及健康，他期待疫情赶快过去，让台东及世界迎接新的生活。</p>
<figure id="12657093" aria-describedby="caption-12657093" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477366-450x287.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477366-450x287.jpg" alt="台东市长张国洲致赠南王部落加菜金。" width="601" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12657093" class="wp-caption-text">台东市长张国洲致赠南王部落加菜金。（台东市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
