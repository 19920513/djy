<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>冬至 谁是汤圆王？ 4款创新口味来评比</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474159-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>冬至吃汤圆是中国人传统习俗，象征团圆、圆满。图为花生双酱汤圆。（刘宸豪／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年12月18日讯】（大纪元记者施芝吟台湾台北报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E8%87%B3.md#1">冬至</a>就快到了，绝对不能错过白白胖胖的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%A4%E5%9C%86.md#1">汤圆</a>！除了花生、芝麻汤圆经典不败款之外，市面上也有许多创意口味，像是奶茶控必吃“黑糖奶茶”、有颗粒的“吉比花生双酱”、蚂蚁族最爱“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%A5%B6%E7%B3%96.md#1">牛奶糖</a>”，还有用传承百年的京都抹茶粉制成的“抹茶汤圆”，这次选4款汤圆进行PK，快来看看哪一款最好吃？最值得买？</p>
<p>自古民间即有天地的观念，天代表阳，“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E8%87%B3.md#1">冬至</a>”之后阳气开始日渐回升，?故言“冬至一阳生”为使阳气回复，于是以圆象征迎接阳气。?团圆、凡事圆满乃民间普遍的期望，吃<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%A4%E5%9C%86.md#1">汤圆</a>祈求圆满更是人民共同的心愿。</p>
<p>今天本报找了4名试吃员，进行4款汤圆实测，皆以冷冻汤圆为主；分别在全联、家乐福、全家便利商店购入，价格介于50~69元之间，10颗装，每包200公克 。</p>
<p>桂冠去年推出的“吉比花生双酱汤圆”，吉比花生汤圆与花生酱SKIPPY联名合作的商品，特别使用吉比柔滑及颗粒2种花生酱，主打花生酱的经典风味。因具有颗粒口感，限量6万盒开卖一周快速完售，今年再度回归。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12628131" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12628131" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474161.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12628131" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474161-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12628131" class="wp-caption-text">花生双酱汤圆。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12628129" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12628129" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474159.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12628129" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474159-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12628129" class="wp-caption-text">花生双酱汤圆。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花生双酱汤圆吃起来有咸甜咸甜味道令人意外，喜欢尝鲜、爱咸甜口味的民众一定要选购。不过，花生颗粒过少，多一点会更有口感；与桂冠经典花生口味差别，就是多了咸味、颗粒，但是没有传统花生口味香浓。</p>
<p>桂冠过去推出不少抹茶汤圆，今年也不例外，“天皇抹茶汤圆”第一口咬下有微苦带甜滋味，可惜抹茶内馅不够多，以至于抹茶在口腔内很快消失。外皮属于有嚼劲感的，喜欢口感软的民众，建议可以烹煮久一点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12628139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12628139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474174.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12628139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474174-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12628139" class="wp-caption-text">抹茶汤圆。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12628137" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12628137" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474173.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12628137" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474173-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12628137" class="wp-caption-text">抹茶汤圆。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>义美与全家便利商店独家推出的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%A5%B6%E7%B3%96.md#1">牛奶糖</a>汤圆”，将古早味的牛奶糖与传统吃汤圆习俗结合，迸出创新吃法。还没吃就能闻到浓郁牛奶糖味，外皮软绵内馅多，随着咬破汤圆那刻，嘴巴内充满浓郁牛奶糖味，但是甜而不腻，可以再吃几颗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12628130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12628130" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474160.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12628130" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474160-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12628130" class="wp-caption-text">牛奶糖汤圆。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12628135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12628135" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474170.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12628135" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474170-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12628135" class="wp-caption-text">牛奶糖汤圆。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12628136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12628136" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474171.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12628136" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474171-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12628136" class="wp-caption-text">牛奶糖汤圆。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>继奶茶汤圆之后，义美全新推出升级版“义美黑糖奶茶汤圆”，特别使用经典义美的浓厚奶茶、加入香纯黑糖，打造特制的黑糖奶茶内馅，让奶茶控抢先更新甜汤圆清单。原以为黑糖奶茶是滑顺的口感，内馅不像一般汤圆会流出，吃一半就觉得甜的腻口，不过奶茶味满重的。</p>
<p>对于喜欢经典花生、芝麻口味的传统派而言，创新口味不会想再回购。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12628132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12628132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474162.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12628132" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474162-600x263.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="263" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12628132" class="wp-caption-text">黑糖奶茶汤圆。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12628140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12628140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474175.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12628140" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474175-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12628140" class="wp-caption-text">黑糖奶茶汤圆。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12628142" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12628142" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474176.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12628142" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474176-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12628142" class="wp-caption-text">黑糖奶茶汤圆。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>试吃员讲评：</strong></p>
<h3>贪吃鬼</h3>
<p>4颗★? ??花生双酱：没想到是咸甜的口味，一般汤圆以甜馅居多，外皮很Ｑ软，可惜内馅颗粒少。</p>
<p>1颗★? ? 黑糖奶茶：是整坨内馅在汤圆内，咬一口就觉得腻，吃完整颗甜味停留在喉咙中。</p>
<p>3.5颗★?牛奶糖：甜而不腻，外皮软Ｑ，令人想多吃几颗。</p>
<p>3颗★? ??抹茶：味道微苦带甜，可惜的是内馅太少，尝不到抹茶的好滋味。</p>
<h3>小清新</h3>
<p>3颗★?花生双酱：入口后令人感到惊奇的是咸甜咸甜的滋味，内馅还多了一些颗粒的口感，跟传统的花生汤圆的味道完全不一样。</p>
<p>2颗★? ??黑糖奶茶：黑糖味跟奶茶味都有，可是黑糖是黑糖，奶茶是奶茶，两者的味道没有融合在一起，有点可惜。</p>
<p>4颗★? ??牛奶糖：是四款里面最喜欢的一款，把牛奶糖的味道还原得很好，虽然很甜，可是不腻口。</p>
<p>3.5颗★?抹茶：抹茶的外皮很Q弹、有嚼劲，但是内馅不多，吃到后来就没什么抹茶味了。</p>
<h3>奶茶控</h3>
<p>2颗★? ??花生双酱：虽然咸甜的口味让人惊艳，但跟传统的花生汤圆相比，较喜欢传统口味。</p>
<p>0.5颗★?黑糖奶茶：身为奶茶控，这款令人失望，满满的黑糖味盖过奶茶味。</p>
<p>3颗★? ??牛奶糖：内馅很滑顺，外皮软绵，又有淡淡的牛奶糖香味，整体而言令人感到非常满意。</p>
<p>1颗★? ??抹茶：抹茶味道很快就消失在口腔中，有种还没吃完就消失了的感觉。</p>
<h3>传统派</h3>
<p>3颗★? ??花生双酱：内馅过于稀，浓郁度不足，宁可买传统花生汤圆来吃。</p>
<p>2颗★? ??黑糖奶茶：吃起来有黑糖牛奶糖错觉，没有特别令人印象深刻的感觉。</p>
<p>1.5颗★?牛奶糖：令人想起儿时吃牛奶糖的滋味。</p>
<p>0.5颗★?抹茶：内馅偏少，未把抹茶好味道呈现出来。◇</p>
