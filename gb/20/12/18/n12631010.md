<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新店频开 新州超市竞争激烈</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年12月19日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">（大纪元费城讯）随着两家德国连锁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E5%B8%82.md#1">超市</a>新店的开业，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E6%B3%BD%E8%A5%BF%E5%B7%9E.md#1">新泽西州</a>超市的竞争日趋激烈。</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">Aldi<span lang="ZH-CN">前些日子刚在</span>Monmouth Junction<span lang="ZH-CN">开了一家新店，而</span>Lidl<span lang="ZH-CN">也即将在</span>Brick<span lang="ZH-CN">开设一家新店。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-ideograph; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">网商巨头亚马逊在上个月宣布将在</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">Woodland Park<span lang="ZH-CN">开设亚马逊新鲜食品<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E5%B8%82.md#1">超市</a>（</span>Amazon Fresh<span lang="ZH-CN">）。 </span>Lidl<span lang="ZH-CN">，</span>Aldi<span lang="ZH-CN">，</span>Trader Joe&#8217;s<span lang="ZH-CN">和</span>ACME<span lang="ZH-CN">也都宣布了扩大进军<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E6%B3%BD%E8%A5%BF%E5%B7%9E.md#1">新泽西州</a>的计划。</span>Lidl<span lang="ZH-CN">表示，他们计划将新泽西的店面数量增加一倍，</span>Brick<span lang="ZH-CN">分店是其第</span>14<span lang="ZH-CN">家。</span> Trader Joe&#8217;s<span lang="ZH-CN">于今年早些时候开设了其第</span>19<span lang="ZH-CN">家分店。</span>Acme<span lang="ZH-CN">最近在新泽西州购买了</span>23<span lang="ZH-CN">个</span>King&#8217;s Food Markets<span lang="ZH-CN">。</span>Aldi<span lang="ZH-CN">则计划在新泽西开</span>50<span lang="ZH-CN">家分店。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-ideograph; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;">?</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-ideograph; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">Aldi<span lang="ZH-CN">表示，到</span>2022<span lang="ZH-CN">年，它有望成为美国第三大超市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9E%E9%94%81%E5%BA%97.md#1">连锁店</a>。目前，它在全美</span>36<span lang="ZH-CN">个州有</span>2,000<span lang="ZH-CN">家商店。在全球范围内，</span>Aldi<span lang="ZH-CN">拥有超过一万家商店。</span> Lidl<span lang="ZH-CN">在全球拥有超过</span>11,000<span lang="ZH-CN">家商店，而在美国则仅有</span>100<span lang="ZH-CN">多家，他们正计划在新泽西州的</span>Lawrenceville<span lang="ZH-CN">开设另一家商店。</span></span></p>
