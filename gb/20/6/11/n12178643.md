<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>免烤箱铜锣烧～自制抹茶馅 香煎饼皮松软可口</h2>
<a href="https://d3twnfu6dd6xpm.cloudfront.net/L8szYgT5t"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>抹茶清香加上绵密红豆馅，经典口味的铜锣烧吃一口总是回味无穷。（图／Ｃ2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>哆啦A梦是很多人小时候的童年回忆，他最爱吃的点心就是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%9C%E9%94%A3%E7%83%A7.md#1">铜锣烧</a>。每次看卡通时，哆啦A梦吃铜锣烧的样子好像很美味，就令人很好奇铜锣烧到底是什么味道，相信这是很多小孩梦想的甜食。其实铜锣烧的做法不难，在家里用平底锅就是可以做出来喔！而且还可以变化很多不同的味道。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%9C%E9%94%A3%E7%83%A7.md#1">铜锣烧</a>在日本是很常见的和菓子。日文是“どら焼き”（dorayaki），有一种说法是来自打击乐器的铜锣。二片饼皮很像是小铜锣把小豆馅夹在中间的样子。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【面糊食材】</span></h2>
<p>中筋面粉130g<br />
泡打粉5g<br />
蜂蜜20g<br />
糖30g<br />
蛋2颗<br />
盐少许</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【内馅食材】</span></h2>
<p>鲜奶油150ml<br />
抺茶粉10g<br />
水2大匙<br />
糖10g</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 取一个碗将蛋打散，加入砂糖以及蜂蜜，拌匀后，加入牛奶拌匀。<br />
2. 接着将过筛好的面粉以及泡打粉加入，拌匀后加入少量的盐拌匀 ，冰半个小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12178661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12178661" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4490d104fa402f2bb2bbf5004fc8142a.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12178661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4490d104fa402f2bb2bbf5004fc8142a-450x357.png" alt="" width="450" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12178661" class="wp-caption-text">搅拌均匀后，放入冷藏室冰半个小时（图／Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 取一个碗将鲜奶油倒入。<br />
4. 再取一个碗倒入两大匙的水及抹茶粉拌匀。<br />
5. 将做法4以及糖加入鲜奶油里，打发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12178662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12178662" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4a4d294655a74d58a19cb9ea8f859093.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12178662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4a4d294655a74d58a19cb9ea8f859093-450x336.png" alt="C2食光,铜锣烧" width="450" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12178662" class="wp-caption-text">打发的过程（图／Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12178663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12178663" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/34d5c473ae1f602e54728dac88ac6f41.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12178663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/34d5c473ae1f602e54728dac88ac6f41-450x323.png" alt="C2食光,铜锣烧" width="450" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12178663" class="wp-caption-text">在平底锅倒入面糊，小火煎至面糊冒小泡。（图／Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12178667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12178667" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7dbfbeb25987bbb6a498294ee97b67d7.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12178667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7dbfbeb25987bbb6a498294ee97b67d7-450x324.png" alt="C2食光,铜锣烧" width="450" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12178667" class="wp-caption-text">饼皮煎制外表呈现金黄色。（图／Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将铜锣烧皮煎好后，将抹茶馅、红豆馅加入其中即可。</p>
