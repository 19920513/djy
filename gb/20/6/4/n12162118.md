<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】蜜汁叉烧鸡排</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/c9f5f8f390580039d2d6cda755609180-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>叉烧源于中国广东，是广东、香港和澳门等地常见的食材。传统的叉烧是用猪肉以叉子放在炉火上烤制而成。现代家庭用小烤箱来烤制叉烧，效果与传统制法相当。</p>
<p>制作叉烧通常是选用猪肉，猪肉表面涂上红色的叉烧酱，所以烤好的叉烧带砖红色。质素上佳的叉烧肉质软嫩多汁、色泽鲜明、香味四溢。叉烧不但可作为一道主菜，亦可配上白饭作为叉烧饭。另外，粤式酒家点心叉烧包、叉烧酥和叉烧肠粉都以叉烧作为馅料；扬州炒饭和星洲炒米粉均有加入叉烧。</p>
<p>鸡肉肉质细嫩、营养丰富、价格便宜，吃法多多。原汁原味的蒸滑鸡、色泽红亮的糖醋鸡翅、美味下饭的鸡翅包饭等等，鸡肉是我们做料理时最常用、最普遍的食材。</p>
<p>罗厨今天制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%9C%E6%B1%81%E5%8F%89%E7%83%A7%E9%B8%A1%E6%8E%92.md#1">蜜汁叉烧鸡排</a>，是以鸡腿肉代替猪肉，以海鲜酱和烧烤酱代替叉烧酱。添加了黄酒、盐、玉米粉、生抽、蜂蜜的调料后，出炉的鸡排香滑汁多，口味更加浓郁。</p>
<p>罗厨担任主厨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>，在纽约曼哈顿中城的黄金地段拥有两家分店。疫情期间，普照荟苑提供餐点外送服务。这道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%9C%E6%B1%81%E5%8F%89%E7%83%A7%E9%B8%A1%E6%8E%92.md#1">蜜汁叉烧鸡排</a>就可以在普照荟苑的网站上点餐。订餐网站：<ahref="&lt;a href=">https://www.radianceteashop.com/ </a></p>
<h2>蜜汁叉烧鸡排</h2>
<p>主料：鸡大腿6只。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/794bff094d445cea993455b9ed7b378e.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/794bff094d445cea993455b9ed7b378e-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：姜1块、葱2根、香菜4根。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/0fe14a91901ac37db83dcf963dd0a3e7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/0fe14a91901ac37db83dcf963dd0a3e7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
调料：海鲜酱、烧烤酱、黄酒、盐、玉米粉、生抽、蜂蜜。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将鸡大腿起骨。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 厚肉处切2刀，洗干净，沥干待用。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 将姜、葱拍烂，抓出水，放入海鲜酱2汤匙。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162158" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 放入3汤匙烧烤酱、1汤匙黄酒。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 放入生抽、香菜粒，一起捞匀。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 用盐、玉米粉腌制鸡大腿肉。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 加入刚调好的酱料腌制。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 再加入蜂蜜4汤匙。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 将鸡肉腌制30分钟。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 将鸡扒皮摆在烤盘中，用220℃焗约15分钟至金黄色。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/10-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/10-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 待烤到鸡肉表面焦香即可上盘。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/11-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12162169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/11-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
