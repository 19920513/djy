<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蔬菜汤最强食材 4种十字花科蔬菜低糖又抗癌</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/vegetable-soup-2-200424-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>富含植化素且含糖量低的十字花科蔬菜，是蔬菜汤的主角食材。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>富含植化素且含糖量低的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%81%E5%AD%97%E8%8A%B1%E7%A7%91%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">十字花科蔬菜</a>，是最强<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C%E6%B1%A4.md#1">蔬菜汤</a>的主角。经过充分日晒的当季<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">蔬菜</a>，含有丰富的植化素。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C%E6%B1%A4.md#1">蔬菜汤</a>食材主角：十字花科叶菜类<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">蔬菜</a></span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%81%E5%AD%97%E8%8A%B1%E7%A7%91%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">十字花科蔬菜</a>含有其他蔬菜没有的植化素——硫代葡萄糖苷。此物质会在体内转化为异硫氰酸酯，将致癌物无毒化或阻止细胞癌化。</p>
<p><strong>1. <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E4%B8%BD%E8%8F%9C.md#1">高丽菜</a></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12059089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12059089" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_429985669-e1587752871287.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12059089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_429985669-e1587752871287-600x428.jpg" alt="高丽菜有异硫氰酸酯、过氧化酶等有效抑制癌症的成分。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12059089" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E4%B8%BD%E8%8F%9C.md#1">高丽菜</a>有异硫氰酸酯、过氧化酶等有效抑制癌症的成分。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>富含提高免疫力的维生素C和保护胃黏膜的维生素U，以及异硫氰酸酯、过氧化酶等有效抑制癌症的成分。</p>
<p><strong>2. <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E8%8A%B1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">青花菜</a></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11990359" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11990359" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/160143457e9a84da_ttl7dayfu5_shutterstock_1258200229.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11990359" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/160143457e9a84da_ttl7dayfu5_shutterstock_1258200229-600x400.jpg" alt="花椰菜和青花菜中除了花青素、维生素C易因烹煮而流失，其它营养较不受高温影响。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11990359" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E8%8A%B1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">青花菜</a>是由野生甘蓝菜改良而成，花椰菜则是改良青花菜而来。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>青花菜（西兰花）是由野生甘蓝菜改良而成，花椰菜则是改良青花菜而来。青花菜含有丰富的维生素和矿物质，其中维生素C含量特别高。无论是茎还是叶子都富含膳食纤维和营养素，可整朵一起摄取。</p>
<p><strong>3. 大白菜</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12059088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12059088" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_475799716.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12059088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_475799716-600x400.jpg" alt="大白菜不仅含糖量少、低卡路里，还有丰富的维生素和矿物质。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12059088" class="wp-caption-text">大白菜不仅含糖量少、低卡路里，还有丰富的维生素和矿物质。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>不仅含糖量少、低卡路里，还有丰富的维生素和矿物质。是最强汤品冬天的强力伙伴。</p>
<p><strong>4. 小松菜</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12059090" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12059090" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1329517565.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12059090" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1329517565-600x400.jpg" alt="小松菜富含β-胡萝卜素（植化素）、维生素C 和维生素E。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12059090" class="wp-caption-text">小松菜（日本油菜）富含β-胡萝卜素、维生素C 和维生素E。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>富含β-胡萝卜素（植化素）、维生素C 和维生素E，以及钙、铁等矿物质与膳食纤维。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">其他推荐食材</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12059084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12059084" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/vegetable-soup-200424.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12059084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/vegetable-soup-200424-600x343.jpg" alt="煮蔬菜汤的推荐食材：苦瓜、白萝卜和豆芽菜。（和平国际提供）" width="600" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12059084" class="wp-caption-text">煮蔬菜汤的推荐食材：苦瓜、白萝卜和豆芽菜。（和平国际）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>● 苦瓜</strong></p>
<p>不仅含糖量少、低卡路里，还含有丰富的维生素。适合与肉和油一起烹调，是最适合夏天的最强汤品。</p>
<p><strong>●?</strong><strong>白萝卜</strong></p>
<p>含糖量少，炖煮时要留意不要加糖。</p>
<p><strong>● 豆芽菜</strong></p>
<p>植物在长出新芽的阶段，会合成出种子没有的维生素和其他营养成分，因此作为新芽的豆芽菜，其含有的维生素和矿物质是其他蔬菜的好几倍。此营养成分若释放在汤品中，防癌效果也会倍增。</p>
<p>POINT：痛风者需注意发芽食物、豆类，不宜多食。</p>
