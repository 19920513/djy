<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>邮轮致国人染疫 纽欲起诉公主号</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年04月09日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者筱康<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>编译报导）新西兰<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%BF%E5%BA%9C.md#1">政府</a>正在考虑对红宝石公主（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ruby Princess</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）游轮采取<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E5%BE%8B.md#1">法律</a>行动，该游轮已被证明是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A.md#1">澳大利亚</a>中共病毒<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%9F%E6%9F%93.md#1">感染</a>的温床。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Carnival Cruises</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">公司拥有的这艘游轮在返回<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A.md#1">澳大利亚</a>之前，于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月早些时候访问了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>的五个港口。此后，与该船有关的600多人中共病毒测试呈阳性，其中</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人死亡。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">澳大利亚新南威尔士州（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">New South Wales</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）警方证实，他们已经发起了刑事调查，原因是尽管有一些乘客出现类似流感的症状，但该游轮仍允许</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2700</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">名乘客在悉尼下船。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在新西兰，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hawke&#8217;s Bay</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">16</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个中共病毒病例与</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Napier </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">停靠的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">这艘</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">游轮有关。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">新西兰总理杰辛达</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">·</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">阿德恩（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Jacinda Ardern</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日在接受媒体采访时说，她</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">考虑新西兰是否也应采取行动。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">我已经要求总检察长戴维</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">·</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">帕克（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">David Parker</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）就有关红宝石公主在新西兰期间是否履行其所有义务的问题，向律师寻求<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E5%BE%8B.md#1">法律</a>意见</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">……</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">因为，我们现在正在承受那艘游轮在新西兰造成的后果。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">红宝石公主在新西兰各地停留了五站，先后访问了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Fiordland</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日），</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Dunedin</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日），</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Akaroa</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">13</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日），</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Wellington</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日）和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Napier</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">15</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日），然后返回悉尼。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">从</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日午夜起，新西兰禁止所有游轮进入，但已在新西兰水域的游轮，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">允许其按原行程继续走完航线</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
