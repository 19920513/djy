<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Marbella飘香纽约数十载 必点招牌炖海鲜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/22eba8efea24518102f29e559a6f47ad-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Marbella炖海鲜的汤汁浓厚，鲜甜得让人爱不释口。（林宜君/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年09月01日讯】（大纪元记者林宜君报导）我与友人还未踏进Marbella的室外用餐区，已听得竖琴的乐声，西班牙民乐的曲调，相当有异国风情。年老却干练的侍者待我们坐定，推荐了季节限定的椰奶果昔，沁凉甜爽的味道，十分开胃，引人期待当日晚餐会是如何。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12370921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12370921" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/28a1153f89ddcff456d049a09e0a7b28.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12370921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/28a1153f89ddcff456d049a09e0a7b28-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12370921" class="wp-caption-text">来一杯Marbella特调的椰香果昔，让悠扬的竖琴乐声带来清凉。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>西班牙<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C.md#1">海鲜</a>料理是一绝</h2>
<p>餐厅经理Juan Estevez为我们点了3道前菜：三种起司镶蘑菇（y Tres Quesos）、蒜香嫩煎小章鱼佐番茄糊（Chipirones a la Plancha）、酪梨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C.md#1">海鲜</a>盅（Aguacate Relleno de Marisco）。虽说是前菜，但用料和分量很足，适合小家庭分食。</p>
<p>起司镶蘑菇的起司味非常浓厚，几乎让人以为在咀嚼乳酪块。咸香软绵的起司镶料配上鲜美多汁的大蘑菇，一口咬下真是十分满足。夹一口柔嫩弹牙的小章鱼，搭一片经橄榄油微煸过的蒜片，油香、蒜香还有章鱼的鲜，越嚼越香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12370922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12370922" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/cc298cb1b1c28030e9f7629fc130d687.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12370922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/cc298cb1b1c28030e9f7629fc130d687-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12370922" class="wp-caption-text">三种起司镶蘑菇，芝士味浓略咸，正好搭配肥厚多汁的蕈菇。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>酪梨海鲜盅的虾肉块与龙虾螯满到堆在半颗酪梨外，生菜铺底，酸豆与青椒、红椒、洋葱碎洒落其间。餐厅讲究地使用了白酒醋，风味更清新淡雅，能衬托海鲜的甜味。这种海鲜沙拉看似随处可见，但是Marbella的处理更细致，酪梨油滑新鲜，入口即化；各种生菜碎没有恼人的菜味，只剩脆爽的口感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12370915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12370915" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6a2ca6b6a01843c14333e7d4227a45d0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12370915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6a2ca6b6a01843c14333e7d4227a45d0-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12370915" class="wp-caption-text">Marbella的酪梨海鲜盅用料大方，酪梨的油润搭配鲜美海味，相得益彰。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>西班牙人吃饭和法国人可是同样悠闲，如果没把前菜吃完，服务员是不可能接着上主菜。趁着侍者收拾的空隙，我轻瞥四周：用餐者聊天慢食，竖琴活泼轻快的旋律不绝于耳，红、黄布幔和外头的绿意将四周围起，侍者互相用陌生铿锵的西班牙语交谈，料理的香气一阵阵飘来。有那么一瞬间，我感觉自己置身西班牙，而不是在拥挤繁忙的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12370918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12370918" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3e6f3124170bf81b015d105303a0b408.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12370918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3e6f3124170bf81b015d105303a0b408-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12370918" class="wp-caption-text">蒜香嫩煎小章鱼的肉质鲜嫩弹牙，油煎香气还没上桌就闻得到。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>炖海鲜 嫩口汤鲜停不下</h2>
<p>联想间，主菜已经端至眼前：瓦伦西亚式龙虾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%96%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炖饭</a>（Paella Valenciana con Langosta）、瓦伦西亚式烤鸭腿（Pato a la Valenciana Flameado）、Marbella招牌炖海鲜（Zarzuela de Mariscos “Marbella”）。瓦伦西亚在西班牙东南部，是西班牙炖饭的发源地。Marbella的龙虾炖饭还放了腊肠与鸡腿肉，为炖饭增加了醇厚的肉汤滋味；米饭粒粒分明，却不至于生感。番红花独特的草本香气与蔬菜，让炖饭显得不腻，能一口接着一口吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12370920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12370920" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9e2c6a2ef156ce9c4ab1aab68dec2cc8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12370920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9e2c6a2ef156ce9c4ab1aab68dec2cc8-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12370920" class="wp-caption-text">Marbella提供多种西班牙地道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%96%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炖饭</a>，松香饱腹，适合全家分享。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>与西班牙炖饭相比，华人普遍不知道炖海鲜其实也是西班牙名菜，而且西班牙的炖海鲜与欧洲其他地方不一样，选用了大量的贝类。贝类容易煮老或带腥味，不仅考验食材的新鲜度，也考验厨师使用火侯的功力。Marbella炖海鲜的汤汁浓稠，但不是面粉勾芡，贝肉饱嘴又鲜甜。舀一匙稍咸的海鲜汤汁兑着炖饭吃，真是绝配！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12370923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12370923" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/22eba8efea24518102f29e559a6f47ad.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12370923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/22eba8efea24518102f29e559a6f47ad-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12370923" class="wp-caption-text">Marbella炖海鲜的汤汁浓厚，鲜甜得让人爱不释口。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>瓦伦西亚式烤鸭腿的分量相当惊人，竟然是两只大鸭腿相叠，还另外付上一碟黄米饭与炒时蔬！鸭肉熟透而柔软，橙汁酱甜味浓，这肯定是家中长辈和小孩喜欢的味道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12370917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12370917" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/ac3f2dbcc28889d3b58ac563c33e7628.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12370917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/ac3f2dbcc28889d3b58ac563c33e7628-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12370917" class="wp-caption-text">Marbella的瓦伦西亚鸭腿，肉质柔软，佐以香甜橙汁酱，搭配附餐黄米饭与时蔬，吃饱又吃巧。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>菜单经典丰富 无可替代</h2>
<p>西班牙料理与美式料理相比，较少油炸且很多海鲜可选择，对嗜吃海味的华人而言，是一大福音。翻开Marbella的菜单，光是炖饭就有4种选择，现点现做。素食者也有蔬菜炖饭可选，但这可不是Marbella特别为素食者制作，而是西班牙炖饭的传统菜式之一。</p>
<p>Marbella菜单上的料理多有来头，烹调手法繁复而传统，用料大方又新鲜，还有音乐家、佛朗明哥舞者现场演出，让人一秒回到西班牙，难怪这间家族经营的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">西班牙餐厅</a>即使远离市区，仍能屹立不摇44年。</p>
<figure id="12370919" aria-describedby="caption-12370919" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/bd9fec66f64db20abf3fcac46e5c4be6-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/bd9fec66f64db20abf3fcac46e5c4be6-450x300.jpg" alt="Marbella的户外用餐区虽然在酒店停车场，但布置用心不马虎。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12370919" class="wp-caption-text">Marbella的户外用餐区虽然在酒店停车场，但布置用心不马虎。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
