<h6>酸甜酥脆的凤梨虾球，料理简单不困难，又相当受欢迎。（C2食光）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【小编按】夏季来到了尾声，凤梨的产季也即将告一段落，不妨赶快抓住季节的尾端，来一盘解腻的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%A4%E6%A2%A8%E8%99%BE%E7%90%83.md#1">凤梨虾球</a>吧！这一道菜是宴客菜，小时候有流水席时就会看到他的踪影，看似简单的料理，却相当受欢迎。因为鳯梨酸酸甜甜的，开胃又帮助消化，除了促进食欲，还能减缓疲劳！赶快跟着视频做看看吧！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【小知识】</span></h2>
<p>1.鳯梨跟肉类一起烹煮，会帮助肉类使其变得软嫩可口。<br />
2.鳯梨经过炒过能增加鳯梨甜味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【食材】</span></h2>
<p>白虾15尾<br />
鳯梨1/2颗</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【腌料】</span></h2>
<p>盐1/4小匙<br />
米酒1大匙<br />
白胡椒少许<br />
面粉适量<br />
水少许</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【其他食材】</span></h2>
<p>美乃滋 适量</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1.把凤梨切片。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/292dacf0b0f2f835471ab126e8cdff03.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12383826 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/292dacf0b0f2f835471ab126e8cdff03-450x306.png" alt="" width="450" b="306" /></a><br />
2.虾子用腌料腌着备用</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/24d4c74b030a8c30ad4064821b6c5841.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12383827 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/24d4c74b030a8c30ad4064821b6c5841-450x316.png" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><br />
3.将虾仁沾上脆浆粉后，用低温小火油炸，炸成金黄色起锅备用</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/f7d86494f6a451080c24399bbee85bef.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12383828 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/f7d86494f6a451080c24399bbee85bef-450x277.png" alt="" width="450" b="277" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4243df960b7c8c40d68473cf440ec459.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12383829 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4243df960b7c8c40d68473cf440ec459-450x322.png" alt="" width="450" b="322" /></a><br />
4.放入凤梨拌炒，再加入炸好的炸虾仁拌炒</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3de0417c19912529ed16b30f621bf380.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12383830 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3de0417c19912529ed16b30f621bf380-450x320.png" alt="" width="450" b="320" /></a><br />
5.最后挤上美乃滋即可</p>
