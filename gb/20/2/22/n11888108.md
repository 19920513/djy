<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】明太子蒸豆腐?和食风味 下酒菜上选</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>明太子蒸豆腐（图／梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【小编语</strong>】</span>还在烦恼要煮什么菜肴吗？运用基本食材的搭配再变化烹调方式也能有不同滋味。这次梁厨为大家介绍的是搭配饭、面等主食都很受欢迎的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90.md#1">豆腐</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%8E%E5%A4%AA%E5%AD%90.md#1">明太子</a>。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90.md#1">豆腐</a>有丰沛的蛋白质，不论是凉拌、焗烤、蒸煮、汤品、油炸都有其风味。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%8E%E5%A4%AA%E5%AD%90.md#1">明太子</a>是鳕鱼的鱼卵，亦是日本国民美食的代表之一，可以做各种料理的佐料，适合炒面、炒饭、面包或是烧烤等。如果想要吃得营养又品尝海产的鲜味，绝对不可错过这一道“明太子蒸豆腐”，明太子浑厚的咸味，搭配软嫩的豆腐，入口能感受到明太子的颗粒和豆腐的融合，绵密又有口感，是老少皆宜的美食佳肴。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【材料】</strong></span></p>
<p>明太子2条<br />
豆腐1盒<br />
小棠菜10棵<br />
芫茜碎少许（装饰用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11888135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11888135" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-36-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11888135 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-36-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,明太子" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11888135" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【调味】</span></strong></p>
<p>Xo酱1汤匙<br />
蒸鱼豉油1茶匙<br />
麻油1茶匙</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【做法】</strong></span></p>
<p>1.豆腐切成8小块，在上面挖一小洞。明太子斜刀切成8小块，把它放在豆腐洞上面，再放上xo酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11897763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11897763" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-29.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11897763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-29-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,明太子豆腐" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11897763" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将豆腐放在烧热滚水的蒸锅内蒸5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11897766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11897766" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-29.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11897766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-29-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,梁志生,明太子豆腐" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11897766" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.将小棠菜洗净后，用油盐水烫熟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11897785" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11897785" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11897785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-24-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,梁志生,明太子豆腐" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11897785" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.豆腐蒸好后取出，把小棠菜摆在旁边，淋上蒸鱼豉油及麻油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11897767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11897767" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/94500141aa4c9611374c604eab8efb9b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11897767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/94500141aa4c9611374c604eab8efb9b-450x308.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,梁志生,明太子豆腐" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11897767" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.最后用少许芫茜碎装饰便可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11897775" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11897775" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11897775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-12-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,梁志生,明太子豆腐" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11897775" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【梁师傅小贴士】</strong><br />
·明太子可以在日本超市买到，有辛辣及普通味道两种口味。烹调时要注意，腌渍过的明太子味道稍咸，所以不要加太多。◇</p>
