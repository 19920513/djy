<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>天普市欢度76届茶花节 热情不减</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-5-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>天普市茶花节游行。（姜琳达／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者姜琳达天普市报导）每年2月的最后一个周末，是天普市（Temple City）一年一度的传统山茶花节（Camellia Festival），寓意迎接春天的到来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11890856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890856" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-5-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11890856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-5-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890856" class="wp-caption-text">未受疫情影响，天普市各族裔居民欢度76届茶花节。（姜琳达／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11890858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-4-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11890858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-4-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890858" class="wp-caption-text">未受疫情影响，天普市各族裔居民欢度76届茶花节。（姜琳达／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过，今年受<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A6%E6%B1%89%E7%96%AB%E6%83%85.md#1">武汉疫情</a>（也称新型冠状病毒，COVID-19）影响，南加州很多华裔社区在新年期间的户外活动都被取消，但天普市的这一庆典活动依然照旧。</p>
<p>在为期三天的嘉年华活动中，疫情也没有影响民众参与传统活动的热情。现场除了有双人舞表演外，市政厅旁的公园里还设有各种小吃摊位、娱乐表演和摩天轮、旋转木马等游乐设施。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11890870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890870" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-1-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11890870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-1-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890870" class="wp-caption-text">天普市茶花节游乐场。（姜琳达／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11890871" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890871" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-2-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11890871" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-2-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890871" class="wp-caption-text">天普市茶花节游乐场。（姜琳达／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而在周六（22日）上午举行的大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>中，有骑警队、童子军，还有多辆由学生以山茶花装饰的花车，以及当地不同的学校组成的仪队、旗队、鼓乐队也都备受欢迎。虽然当天飘着小雨，但沿途两旁仍可见到自备椅子、雨伞看游行的民众。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11890865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890865" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11890865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-11-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890865" class="wp-caption-text">天普市茶花节<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>。（姜琳达／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11890866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890866" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11890866" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890866" class="wp-caption-text">天普市茶花节游行。（姜琳达／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11890868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890868" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11890868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890868" class="wp-caption-text">天普市茶花节游行。（姜琳达／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11890872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890872" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11890872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-11-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890872" class="wp-caption-text">参加游行的小朋友与民众分享糖果。（姜琳达／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>山茶花节也注重鼓励当地学区的小学生参加，今年的游行还选出了包括华裔在内的各族裔10位小花车国王、皇后、王子和公主。游行队伍中不时能看到穿着各自传统服饰、由家庭组成的队伍。</p>
<p>住在天普市的周女士说，她的两个儿子都在游行队伍中，所以一家人都出动观看游行，帮儿子们拍照。“今年受疫情影响，就连新年都没怎么庆祝的感觉，整个假日期间都关在家里。这次儿子们参加游行，我们家人也算是稍微弥补一下，有了一点庆新年的氛围。”</p>
<p>今年，天普市的姐妹城市台湾花莲县代表团，也首次应邀参加游行活动。当天，8位身着台湾本地原住民族服饰的学生，也走在游行队伍中，向民众展示台湾的民俗文化。</p>
<p>早在1944年，山茶花当选为天普市的市花。因此隔年起，市府就开始举办山茶花节，该传统也一直延续至今。◇</p>
