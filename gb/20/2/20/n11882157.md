<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园产后护理之家高标准防疫  守护产妇新生儿</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c5a0b180a2046668cd1af400153ac73d-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>产后护理之家高标准防疫。 （桃园市府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年02月20日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）桃园市长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E6%96%87%E7%81%BF.md#1">郑文灿</a>20日上午到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9D%9C%E5%8C%BA.md#1">中坜区</a>宋俊宏产后<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%A4%E7%90%86%E4%B9%8B%E5%AE%B6.md#1">护理之家</a>，视察产后护理之家<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>工作时表示，宋俊宏妇幼医院从开业至今，累计接生超过6万名婴儿，考量产妇及新生儿抵抗力较弱，正值防疫期间，产后护理之家提高防疫标准，采取单一入口，人员进入医院前须量测体温、以酒精消毒手部，全程配戴口罩，并以健保卡或身份证查核旅游史等资讯，全面守护产妇及新生儿健康；郑文灿说，希望透过防疫超前部署，做最好的准备，守护市民健康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11882161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11882161" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/950af1e16b21e8fec8ddedd02e135cc6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11882161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/950af1e16b21e8fec8ddedd02e135cc6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11882161" class="wp-caption-text">访客须量测体温。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11882162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11882162" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4fb9c1bdd618b61041e5f6bb5bfe53be.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11882162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4fb9c1bdd618b61041e5f6bb5bfe53be-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11882162" class="wp-caption-text">产妇聆听<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>宣导。（桃园市府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宋俊宏妇幼医院长宋俊宏表示，医院积极针对访客进行防疫宣传，同时限缩访视时间及访客身份，仅开放直系亲属探访，且有感冒症状或曾入境中港澳者皆不得入院访视，人员进入产后护理机构，皆须穿着隔离衣、戴头帽、脚套，并登记访客登记表，院方也会加强环境清洁，确保产妇及幼儿健康。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11882164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11882164" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c6f776c91415331eb8c8dcf37b988be1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11882164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c6f776c91415331eb8c8dcf37b988be1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11882164" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E6%96%87%E7%81%BF.md#1">郑文灿</a>示范干洗手正确步骤。（桃园市府提供）</figcaption></figure>
