<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一锅到底“自制蕃茄酱”佐坚果鸡胸肉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/20200804_sally_food_05-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>坚果香煎鸡胸肉。（高宝出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>淸淡的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a>裹上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9D%9A%E6%9E%9C.md#1">坚果</a>面衣酥炸过后别有一番风味，坚果的香气与颗粒感以及番茄酱的浓醇，将带给味蕾多重的惊奇飨宴。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">?材料（2人份）<strong>493kcal/1人份</strong></span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a>．200g</p>
<p>橄榄油．适量</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">面衣</span></h2>
<p>面粉．1/2杯</p>
<p>蛋．2 颗</p>
<p>磨碎的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9D%9A%E6%9E%9C.md#1">坚果</a>．各2大匙</p>
<p>（花生、核桃、杏仁等）</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">鸡胸肉调味料</span></h2>
<p>盐与胡椒粉．各少许</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">番茄酱料</span></h2>
<p>奶油．1 大匙</p>
<p>洋葱末．3大匙</p>
<p>番茄末．1 杯</p>
<p>大蒜末．1 小匙</p>
<p>咖喱粉与辣酱．各1小匙</p>
<p>水、盐、胡椒粉．各少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_12334306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12334306" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1708111705-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-12334306 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1708111705-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12334306" class="wp-caption-text">自制<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%95%83%E8%8C%84%E9%85%B1.md#1">蕃茄酱</a>要准备蒜头、洋葱、黑胡椒、盐、番茄等材料。如果想增添清香可以增加香草类植物（例如：月桂叶）。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><span style="caret-color: #008000;">作法</span></span></h2>
<p>1 鸡胸肉调味。将鸡胸肉切成薄片后以盐、胡椒粉调味。</p>
<p>2 裹上面衣。先将鸡胸肉裹上面粉，并沾上蛋液之后再紧紧裹上磨碎的坚果。</p>
<p>3 煎鸡胸肉。在热好的锅子里倒入适量的油，并将裹好面衣的鸡胸肉放入煎熟。</p>
<p>4 制作酱料。在锅子里将奶油融化后翻炒洋葱，接着将其余的酱料食材全部放入熬煮制成番茄酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12334305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12334305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_560126905.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12334305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_560126905-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12334305" class="wp-caption-text">奶油拌炒洋葱丁（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5 摆盘。将煎熟的鸡胸肉盛盘后一旁摆上番茄酱料。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">TIP</span></h2>
<p>如果觉得自制番茄酱步骤繁复，也可以使用市售的意大利面番茄酱代替。</p>
