<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>香草烤鸡胸肉 香气十足不干柴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/20200804_sally_food_01-545x400.jpg" />
<h6>香草烤鸡胸。（高宝出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>多种不同香料除了可以去除鸡肉腥味，更能增添香气与口感。将<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%8B%E8%91%B1.md#1">洋葱</a>一起放入烤箱，风味更加绝伦。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">材料 （<strong>222 kcal / </strong>1人份）</span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a>．100g</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>．2 颗</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%8B%E8%91%B1.md#1">洋葱</a>．1 颗</p>
<p>盐与胡椒粉．各少许</p>
<p>橄榄油．1/2大匙</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">鸡胸肉腌料</span></h2>
<p>白酒．1 大匙</p>
<p>综合西式香料．2大匙</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h2>
<p>1 腌鸡胸肉。将白酒淋在在鸡胸肉上，并用香料均匀涂抹覆盖后静置一晚。</p>
<p>2 用料理纸包覆后放入烤箱。将腌渍完成的鸡胸肉和整颗番茄与洋葱一起用料理纸包覆后，放入烤箱用170°C烤约30分钟。</p>
<p>3 切鸡胸肉。将烤好的鸡胸肉切成适合入口的大小。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12334315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12334315" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_786204382.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12334315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_786204382-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12334315" class="wp-caption-text">（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4 摆盘。将番茄与洋葱洒上盐、胡椒粉、橄榄油后，和鸡胸肉一起盛盘。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">TIP</span></h2>
<p>可事先将鸡胸肉用香料腌渍后放入冷冻库保存，等到有需要时再退冰切成适当大小放入烤箱烤熟，便能轻松享用美味的料理。</p>
