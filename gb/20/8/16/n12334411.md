<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蜂蜜慢烤球芽甘蓝 甘甜焦香好滋味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1234022578-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蜂蜜慢烤球芽甘蓝。（shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>略带焦香风味的烤<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%90%83%E8%8A%BD%E7%94%98%E8%93%9D.md#1">球芽甘蓝</a>，酥脆又松软，咀嚼起来的层次感透出<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">蔬菜</a>的鲜甜，味道非常的迷人。在做这一道菜的时候，建议你调高烤箱的温度，才能烤出表皮带有褐色、口感丰富的球芽甘蓝。在这道菜肴中加入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a>，也能带出又咸又甜的多变层次，让简单的烤<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">蔬菜</a>，多了有趣又特别的要素！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p>2磅<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%90%83%E8%8A%BD%E7%94%98%E8%93%9D.md#1">球芽甘蓝</a><br />
2汤匙<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a><br />
4汤匙橄榄油<br />
1汤匙醋<br />
盐和胡椒粉适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_6495066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6495066" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1403011245032483.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-6495066" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1403011245032483-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6495066" class="wp-caption-text">球芽甘蓝用烤箱料理是零失败食谱，简单好上手又美味。(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【做法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 球芽甘蓝洗干净，清洗时挑去不健康或发黄的叶子，对半切后备用。</p>
<p>2. 烤箱预热到摄氏200度（华氏425度）。将球芽甘蓝与橄榄油、盐和胡椒粉搅拌过，切面朝下放到烤盘上，也可以直接在烤盘上拌匀。</p>
<p>3. 大约烤15 -25分钟的时间，直到球芽甘蓝的表面略呈褐色。将烤盘拿出，淋上蜂蜜和香醋即可。◇ <strong>??</strong></p>
