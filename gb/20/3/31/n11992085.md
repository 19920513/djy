<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林子弟回馈故乡 捐赠母校冷气设备</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/0289567baedf98489a2ed035839481b7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>云林县土库镇后埔国小杰出校友鑫威利股份有限公司总经理陈富用，事业有成，捐赠母校14台冷气及学校照明设备，31日举行捐赠仪式。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月31日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）云林县土库<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8E%E5%9F%94%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">后埔国小</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%B0%E5%87%BA%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B.md#1">杰出校友</a>鑫威利股份有限公司总经理<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%AF%8C%E7%94%A8.md#1">陈富用</a>，在中华民国云林同乡总会创会会长谭量吉牵线下，捐赠母校14台冷气及学校照明设备，31日举行捐赠仪式！</p>
<p>县长张丽善亲自颁发感谢状表扬他的善举！并盼借此抛砖引玉，吸引更多乡亲响应云林县推动国中小学教室全面装冷气！共同提升孩子的学习环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11992112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11992112" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/433708.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11992112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/433708-599x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11992112" class="wp-caption-text">县长张丽善颁发感谢状，表扬<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%AF%8C%E7%94%A8.md#1">陈富用</a>总经理<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%9E%E9%A6%88%E6%AF%8D%E6%A0%A1.md#1">回馈母校</a>的善举。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈富用总经理表示，他国中毕业就离乡打拼，迄今40多年，云林是他的家乡，即使在外打拼，但却仍然心系故乡的发展，他的父母在几年前相继过世，他一直想为家乡做些事情，来表达对父母的思念，感谢县府及母校让他有回馈的机会！</p>
<p>陈富用说：记得在小学的时候洗厕所是他的梦餍，今天看到学校竟把厕所变成美仑美奂的迷你美术馆，让他非常的感动！尤其是小朋友赠送的温馨感谢卡片，更让他开心不已。频说：今天真的好感动，好感动哦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11992113" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11992113" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/433711.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11992113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/433711-533x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11992113" class="wp-caption-text">厕所改造的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8E%E5%9F%94%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">后埔国小</a>校园美术馆。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="11992089" aria-describedby="caption-11992089" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/843ab6edc23c6658f8eafd0da614e9fd-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/843ab6edc23c6658f8eafd0da614e9fd-450x300.jpg" alt="小朋友赠送温馨感谢卡片， 让陈富用开心不已" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11992089" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友赠送温馨感谢卡片， 让陈富用开心不已。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长张丽善表示，教育是国家百年大业，不论是教育软实力的提升，或是学校硬体设备的改善，都是她相当重视的执行方针，完成全县国中小冷气装设便是诸多工作中的一项，但因所需经费庞大，除了县府自筹财源外，着实需要更多像陈总经理的个人或团体共襄盛举。感谢陈总经理的热心公益，让后埔国小的孩子能够有更为舒适的学习环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11992114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11992114" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/433712.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11992114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/433712-533x400.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11992114" class="wp-caption-text">校园美术馆旁的微风艺廊，充满艺术气息。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="11992091" aria-describedby="caption-11992091" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/c08b39f25955773a0e50068189fe2230-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/c08b39f25955773a0e50068189fe2230-450x300.jpg" alt="后埔国小校长黄丽鸿回赠杰出校友陈富用纪念品。感谢他对母校的回馈" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11992091" class="wp-caption-text">后埔国小校长黄丽鸿回赠<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%B0%E5%87%BA%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B.md#1">杰出校友</a>陈富用纪念品。感谢他对母校的回馈。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11992115" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11992115" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/433713.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11992115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/433713-599x400.jpg" alt="" width="499" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11992115" class="wp-caption-text">后埔国小杰出校友捐赠母校14台冷气设备。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>后埔国小黄丽鸿校长表示，相当感谢陈总经理的慷慨解囊，全校师生可于夏日酷暑下有较为舒适的学习环境，正是“今日我以后埔国小为荣，他日后埔国小以我为荣”的最佳写照。</p>
