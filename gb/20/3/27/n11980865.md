<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>吴思贤居家防疫写歌 欣赏朴叙俊演技</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月27日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）曾入围金钟奖戏剧节目新进演员的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E4%B9%90.md#1">小乐</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%80%9D%E8%B4%A4.md#1">吴思贤</a>，27日受邀担任环保戏剧竞赛推广大使，他表示因为受到疫情影响，最近几乎都待在家写歌、练舞，等待疫情过去，新专辑也一直在筹备中，敬请歌迷期待。</p>
<figure id="11980869" aria-describedby="caption-11980869" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/6e06191249afc770166cd40b2ddaebd7-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/6e06191249afc770166cd40b2ddaebd7-450x675.jpg" alt="吴思贤。" width="400" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11980869" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%80%9D%E8%B4%A4.md#1">吴思贤</a>。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="11980868" aria-describedby="caption-11980868" style="width: 361px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/c7bab15a6d6c17249d43eed52db3fee9-450x748.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/c7bab15a6d6c17249d43eed52db3fee9-450x748.jpg" alt="吴思贤。" width="361" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11980868" class="wp-caption-text">吴思贤。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E4%B9%90.md#1">小乐</a>现场分享戏剧演出的技巧，也表示非常欣赏<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E5%8F%99%E4%BF%8A.md#1">朴叙俊</a>，觉得他演技自然又精准，不夸张且生活化，各种情境与角色都能诠释得很好，无论是喜剧、需要情感张力的戏，他都能用很深层、有情感的方式表现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11980867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11980867" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/88dd8e9f774dea0768b0d478be288c93.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11980867" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/88dd8e9f774dea0768b0d478be288c93-267x400.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11980867" class="wp-caption-text">吴思贤。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于未来想挑战什么样的角色呢？小乐表示，希望尝试有特别技能的角色，挑战不同领域的极限，体验不同的人生，比如说饰演游泳选手，藉由这样的机会，了解成为游泳选手辛苦的过程，也能因为透过戏剧而学习到一项技能。</p>
<p>29岁的小乐跨足歌手与演员，现阶段就是努力把演戏及唱歌稳扎稳打地达到自己想要的目标，他说：“除了努力我会再努力。”对于未来工作事业不设限。◇</p>
