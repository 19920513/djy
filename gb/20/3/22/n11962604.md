<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳开启NDIS住房项目</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年03月22日讯】<span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">（</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">大纪元</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">记者张小凡</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲珀斯</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">编译</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">报导）</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">残疾人住房终于将被建在在城镇中心的豪华公寓楼内，此举将有助于缓解住房危机。</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">?</span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">据西澳人报网报导，长期以来，包括年轻人在内的残障人士被迫住进珀斯市郊的养老院或集体住房，远离社交圈。</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">?</span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">非营利组织</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Summer Housing</span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">将于</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">2022</span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">年之前为残障人士提供</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">300</span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">套出租公寓，这些公寓楼将位于</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Joondalup</span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">、</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Fremantle</span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">和</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Subiaco</span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">这样的活力社区。</span><span style="font-size: medium;">?</span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">在这种模式下，</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Summer Housing</span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">在原计划的基础上购买了</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">11</span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">套公寓，对其中的</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">10</span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">套进行了改造，以适应租户需求，并对租金差异给予补偿。</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">?</span></span></span></span></p>
