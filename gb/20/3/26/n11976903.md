<h6>韭菜烘蛋（图／C2食光）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】</strong>当白昼时间渐渐增长，夜晚一天比一天变短，代表一年的时节走到了“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%88%86.md#1">春分</a>”，这时候春天已经过了一半。俗话说：“春分落雨落到清明”，这段期间气候相对不稳定，春寒料峭，大家别忘了要注意身体保健，不要生病了！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%88%86.md#1">春分</a>时节</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_11976947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11976947" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/81497243a3037d2acf630a561b3f133d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11976947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/81497243a3037d2acf630a561b3f133d-450x253.jpg" alt="Ｃ2食光,春分" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11976947" class="wp-caption-text">（图／Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中医讲究顺应四季节气养生，春天容易生肝火、间接影响消化系统，饮食可以多食用温补阳气的食材，让身体新陈代谢保持稳定，避免辛辣、燥热、烧烤或是过于进补的食物，多食用绿色养肝的蔬果为佳。</p>
<p><strong>【春分建议食材】</strong>香椿、菠菜、苋菜、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%AD%E8%8F%9C.md#1">韭菜</a>、草莓、樱桃</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%AD%E8%8F%9C.md#1">韭菜</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E8%9B%8B.md#1">烘蛋</a></span></h3>
<p>韭菜有独特的辛香味加上咸蛋的咸香味，让人胃口大开！</p>
<p>这一道“韭菜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E8%9B%8B.md#1">烘蛋</a>”是在家就可以做的简易杀菌料理。春天相当适合吃韭菜，除了粗纤维促进肠胃蠕动，各类营养素让其保健作用高，可以提升人体免疫力，在中医也被视为一种药材！</p>
<h3><strong>【食材】</strong></h3>
<p>韭菜 1把<br />
蛋 4颗<br />
熟咸鸭蛋黄 2颗<br />
咸鸭蛋白 1颗</p>
<h3><strong>【做法】</strong></h3>
<p>1. 将韭菜切碎、蛋打散、咸鸭蛋白切碎，三者混合在ㄧ起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11976910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11976910" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/dac6f394d885ba65b987b6ac613ad578.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11976910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/dac6f394d885ba65b987b6ac613ad578-450x315.jpg" alt="韭菜烘蛋,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11976910" class="wp-caption-text">（图／Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 咸鸭蛋黄压成泥状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11976909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11976909" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8136ee029bdea40f970df22a75c11c66.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11976909 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8136ee029bdea40f970df22a75c11c66-450x315.jpg" alt="韭菜烘蛋,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11976909" class="wp-caption-text">咸鸭蛋黄压成泥状（图／Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 起油锅，将咸鸭蛋黄泥倒入，炒到发泡颜色变白。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11976911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11976911" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/56d2ad74f0cb77c1951200b8585ee609.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11976911 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/56d2ad74f0cb77c1951200b8585ee609-450x315.jpg" alt="韭菜烘蛋,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11976911" class="wp-caption-text">起油锅，把咸鸭蛋黄泥由黄炒至变白。（图／Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将做法1.的蛋液倒入锅中，稍微混合一下，盖锅盖用中火烘5分钟，翻面再烘5分钟即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11976912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11976912" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/91279b3e3fae5b7b06fed4238965ab01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11976912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/91279b3e3fae5b7b06fed4238965ab01-450x315.jpg" alt="韭菜烘蛋,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11976912" class="wp-caption-text">（图／Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11976913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11976913" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/10e336f8747ac9287db97cd6cb51c04f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11976913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/10e336f8747ac9287db97cd6cb51c04f-450x315.jpg" alt="韭菜烘蛋,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11976913" class="wp-caption-text">（图／Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【料理小技巧】</span></h3>
<p>起油锅的油必需要够多，那么炒咸鸭蛋黄泥要到什么状态才算好呢？犹如烘焙糕点一样，把蛋液打发从黄到变白的颜色，就代表可以了。如果咸鸭蛋黄泥炒不够白不够香，或是炒过头，也会消泡的。炒咸蛋黄和多种食材都很搭配，比方：杏鲍菇、四季豆?，建议可以去杂货店买生的咸蛋回来洗净煮熟，再取其蛋黄压碎来炒，风味更佳。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>“营养师的贴心小叮咛”-by 陈小薇营养师</strong></span></h3>
<p>鸡蛋可以提供优质蛋白质，而鸡蛋的“生物价”高达90以上，很容易被人体消化吸收、利用，鸡蛋还含有卵磷脂、维生素A、维生素B群等健康营养素。特别介绍卵磷脂（Lecithin）主要存在鸡蛋蛋黄，是人体细胞膜重要的成分，具有乳化油脂的功能，卵磷脂主要成分是磷脂酰胆碱（Phosphatidylcholine）能在体内转换为神经传导物乙酰胆碱（Acetylcholine），有效帮助学习及记忆，所以吃蛋时候记得吃蛋黄，适量摄取能增进身体健康喔！</p>
<p>最后，营养师小叮咛，每日钠摄取量2400毫克（相当于6公克食盐）；1颗咸蛋约有850毫克左右的钠含量，所以使用咸蛋调味的菜肴要适量食用，避免摄取太多钠喔！</p>
