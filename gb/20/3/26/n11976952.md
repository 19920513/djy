<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】马蹄甜豆炒石斑球～低脂清爽小菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/11-16-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>马蹄甜豆炒石斑球是一道具有多层次口感的美馔，软脆香甜，低脂健康好下饭。（图／梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【小编语】一碗热腾腾的白饭，搭配几盘热炒小菜，幸福滋味不输酒楼山珍海味！然而，日日下厨，难免对变化菜色苦恼，这时尝试把多种物料分工序处理妥当再混合热炒，可以带来不同的口感喔！</p>
<p>各种食材质感不同，为避免在锅中翻炒过长失去鲜味和口感，分工处理时必须注意细节。本道菜肴<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E6%96%91%E9%B1%BC.md#1">石斑鱼</a>球采用餐厅厨子常用方式“泡油法”最后再回锅，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E8%B9%84.md#1">马蹄</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E8%B1%86.md#1">甜豆</a>、土芹则是用油盐水烹煮，最后再起锅下油放入辛香料炒香后才放回食材一起热炒，重要是调味酱一定要淋上，香喷喷的小菜即可上桌。</p>
<p>【材料】<br />
新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E8%B9%84.md#1">马蹄</a>（荸荠）6oz<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E8%B1%86.md#1">甜豆</a>4oz<br />
土芹2oz<br />
鲜冬菇6个<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E6%96%91%E9%B1%BC.md#1">石斑鱼</a>肉12oz<br />
红椒1oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉各少许<br />
红萝卜花适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_11979746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11979746" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/1-46.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11979746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/1-46-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,甜豆,马蹄,石斑鱼肉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11979746" class="wp-caption-text">（图／<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【腌料】<br />
盐、生油少许<br />
胡椒粉、栗粉适量<br />
1颗蛋的蛋白</p>
<p>【调味】</p>
<p>鸡汤1/4杯<br />
烹大师1/2茶匙<br />
糖1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉1/2茶匙</p>
<p>【做法】</p>
<p>1. 马蹄去皮后切成片状备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11979747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11979747" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2-22.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11979747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2-22-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,甜豆,马蹄,石斑鱼肉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11979747" class="wp-caption-text">（图／<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 甜豆去除头尾及根部，土芹只用硬梗的部分，切成半吋长度，新鲜冬菇去蒂切成厚片，红椒切成小片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11979748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11979748" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/3-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11979748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/3-24-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,甜豆,马蹄,石斑鱼肉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11979748" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 石斑鱼肉清洗干净，切成厚块状，用少许盐、胡椒粉、粟粉及蛋白拌匀，腌约10分钟，最后加入少许生油拌匀备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11979749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11979749" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4-15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11979749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4-15-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,甜豆,马蹄,石斑鱼肉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11979749" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 石斑鱼肉用泡油的方法，泡熟后捞出晾油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11979750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11979750" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/5-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11979750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/5-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,甜豆,马蹄,石斑鱼肉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11979750" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 甜豆、土芹和马蹄用油盐水煮半分钟后捞出沥干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11979751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11979751" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/6-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11979751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/6-9-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,甜豆,马蹄,石斑鱼肉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11979751" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 热镬下油，放入姜、蒜蓉、红椒和鲜冬菇炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11979752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11979752" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/7-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11979752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/7-5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,甜豆,马蹄,石斑鱼肉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11979752" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 接着加入甜豆、土芹、马蹄、红萝卜花拌炒一下。放回鱼肉后小心拌匀，不要弄破鱼肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11979756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11979756" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8-6-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11979756 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,甜豆,马蹄,石斑鱼肉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11979756" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 搅匀调味汁液，加入锅中快手兜匀后便可上碟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11979758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11979758" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/9-6-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11979758 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/9-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,甜豆,马蹄,石斑鱼肉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11979758" class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【梁师傅小贴士】</p>
<p>· 石斑鱼肉无细骨，营养价值高，很适合小孩吃。<br />
· 石斑鱼肉用少许蛋白拌匀腌味，可以让肉质更嫩滑好吃。◇</p>
