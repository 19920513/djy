<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>猪哥亮纪念音乐会 众星用欢笑方式追忆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813214859100694-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>怀念猪哥亮“猪哥亮ㄟ永恒歌厅秀”纪念演唱会，贺一航、郭子乾、苗可丽、黄思婷和王彩桦同台演出。（华视提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年08月14日讯】艺人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E5%93%A5%E4%BA%AE.md#1">猪哥亮</a>离世后，为怀念这位综艺界大哥，由电视台等单位特意选在13日在台湾基隆某处墓园举办纪念音乐会，由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BA%E4%B8%80%E8%88%AA.md#1">贺一航</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%BD%A9%E6%A1%A6.md#1">王彩桦</a>联手主持，猪哥亮生前的好友<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%85%8B%E7%BE%A4.md#1">吴克群</a>、蔡小虎等超过20位艺人接力演出，让观众用欢笑怀念他生前的风采。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9526568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9526568" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813205831100694.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9526568" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813205831100694-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9526568" class="wp-caption-text">怀念<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E5%93%A5%E4%BA%AE.md#1">猪哥亮</a>“猪哥亮ㄟ永恒歌厅秀”纪念演唱会，猪哥亮生前多为艺人好友接力演出。图为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BA%E4%B8%80%E8%88%AA.md#1">贺一航</a>、苗可丽、黄思婷和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%BD%A9%E6%A1%A6.md#1">王彩桦</a>。（华视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场名为“猪哥亮ㄟ永恒歌厅秀”选在猪哥亮长眠之地以园游会的方式举办，并于现场为猪哥亮纪念铜像揭幕，猪哥亮的女儿谢金晶除了跟翁立友合唱台语歌《甲你惜命命》外，更独唱《思念你的心》献给猪哥亮，演唱中一度唱到眼眶泛红，令旁人都相当不舍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9526551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9526551" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813205813100694.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9526551" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813205813100694.jpg" alt="" width="398" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9526551" class="wp-caption-text">怀念猪哥亮“猪哥亮ㄟ永恒歌厅秀”纪念演唱会。图为翁立友和谢晶晶对唱。（华视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谢金晶泪眼表示：“刚刚有一只蛾飞进休息室，一直停在我、哥哥谢顺福跟小妈叶瑞美的身上，所以我相信爸爸一定有看到今天的演出。”翁立友则笑说，自己有梦见猪哥亮，梦中自己还跟猪哥亮一起玩扑克牌，感觉他现在过得非常开心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9526543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9526543" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813214844100694.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9526543" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813214844100694-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9526543" class="wp-caption-text">怀念猪哥亮“猪哥亮ㄟ永恒歌厅秀”纪念演唱会，猪哥亮女儿谢金晶洒泪现场。（华视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郭子乾特别扮成猪哥亮的模样出场，相似度高达90%，让王彩桦看一眼就吓到，还以为是本人现身；金曲歌王荒山亮带着布袋戏COSER出场，献唱一首《轰动武林》；康康、林美秀带来《得意的笑》，并重现电影“大尾鲈鳗”的组合诙谐桥段。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9526548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9526548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813214755100694.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9526548" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813214755100694-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9526548" class="wp-caption-text">怀念猪哥亮“猪哥亮ㄟ永恒歌厅秀”纪念演唱会，康康、林美秀和王彩桦同台演出。（华视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>跟猪哥亮生前合作电影《大钓哥》的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%85%8B%E7%BE%A4.md#1">吴克群</a>也特别出席，带来《明天过后》，并与蔡顺鹏合唱《拔萝卜》，蔡小虎则是演唱《男性的苦练》与《温泉乡的吉他》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9526555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9526555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813205758100694.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9526555" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813205758100694-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9526555" class="wp-caption-text">怀念猪哥亮“猪哥亮ㄟ永恒歌厅秀”纪念演唱会，吴克群和蔡顺鹏出席合唱歌曲。（华视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，也把猪哥亮昔日的短剧搬上舞台，由白云、黄镫辉、杨绣惠带来《爆笑理发厅》，以及方骏、林佑星、林玟谊、林义芳、阿龙、向娃演出《神偷之恋》，两出短剧内容全是家属掷筊由猪哥亮选定的，意义非凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9526553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9526553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813205808100694.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9526553" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813205808100694-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9526553" class="wp-caption-text">怀念猪哥亮“猪哥亮ㄟ永恒歌厅秀”纪念演唱会，把猪哥亮昔日的短剧搬上舞台。图为林佑星、林文谊和方骏演出《神偷之恋》。（华视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9526549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9526549" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813214822100694.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9526549" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813214822100694-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9526549" class="wp-caption-text">怀念猪哥亮“猪哥亮ㄟ永恒歌厅秀”纪念演唱会，李亚萍、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%99%E5%A4%A9.md#1">余天</a>、贺一航和王彩桦同台。（华视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纪念演唱会的压轴，由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%99%E5%A4%A9.md#1">余天</a>跟李亚萍夫妻一起登台，透过影像方式跟猪哥亮跨时空对唱《含泪的微笑》，余天不仅唱出对猪哥亮的思念，歌声也透露出两人昔日相知相惜的真挚情感，场面感人，李亚萍更是掩面痛哭，久久不能自己。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9526558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9526558" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813205740100694.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9526558" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/170813205740100694.jpg" alt="" width="398" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9526558" class="wp-caption-text">怀念猪哥亮“猪哥亮ㄟ永恒歌厅秀”纪念演唱会，金曲歌王荒山亮以布袋戏COSER出场。（华视提供）</figcaption></figure>
