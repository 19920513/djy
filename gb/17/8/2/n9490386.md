<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一看就会：教你做家常炒面（4）</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年08月03日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%83%E5%BE%B7%E8%92%99%E9%A1%BF%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87%E7%BD%91.md#1">埃德蒙顿中文网</a>记者时健报导）学做<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%B8%B8%E7%82%92%E9%9D%A2.md#1">家常炒面</a>。</p>
<p>先把荤料准备好：鸡胸肉一杯，糖一茶匙，酱油一汤匙，蛋清一个，生粉一汤匙，油二分之一汤匙，将上述拌均，腌制15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9918486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9918486" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1-copy.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9918486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1-copy-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9918486" class="wp-caption-text">先准备好材料。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再把素料准备好：胡萝卜，洋葱，包菜，切成丝，各三分之一杯。再准备香菇三个，芹菜两棵，也切成丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9918479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9918479" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/3-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9918479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/3-7-450x446.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9918479" class="wp-caption-text">蔬菜切丝。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>别忘了小葱一棵做葱花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9918470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9918470" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/10-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9918470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/10-13-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9918470" class="wp-caption-text">最后放一棵葱花。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>原料准备齐了，看视频，准备动手。</p>
<p>哇，一盘色香味俱全的炒面，好好犒劳犒劳思乡的胃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9490419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9490419" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/Noodle-x.png"><img class="wp-image-9490419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/Noodle-x-450x344.png" alt="" width="600" b="458" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9490419" class="wp-caption-text">哇，一盘色香味俱全的炒面，好好犒劳犒劳思乡的胃（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
