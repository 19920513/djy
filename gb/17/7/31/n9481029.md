<h6>温哥华枫叶街道（景浩／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年08月5日讯】（大纪元记者张雅云综合报导）秋天的绝佳去处就是赏枫，满山遍野的枫红美得令人陶醉。有“枫叶王国”之称的加拿大，每到枫红季节，沿途满山赤焰枫林与湖光山色相辉映，美景令人叹为观止。</p>
<p>加拿大东岸的枫叶每年从9月中旬开始陆续转红，赏枫季节可长达一个多月。最负盛名的“枫叶大道”（Maple Road），总长800公里，从魁北克省一路往南延烧到美加边境的尼加拉瓜大瀑布，包含川普朗度假村、圣安妮大峡谷、魁北克、蒙特利尔等赏枫胜地，是品味加拿大美丽秋色最醉人的旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6523272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6523272" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510190834261758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6523272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510190834261758-600x311.jpg" width="500" b="259" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6523272" class="wp-caption-text">加拿大最负盛名的“枫叶大道”是品味美丽秋色最醉人的旅程。（松林L／Flickr）</figcaption></figure>
<p>枫叶大道是由连结魁北克省与安大略省之间的40号、417号和407号公路串连而成的整个区域，这条蜿蜒起伏在安大略湖、圣罗伦斯河、瀑布、峡谷、高山、平原之间的路线，每年枫红时节，漆树、黄杨、山毛榉等率先以深红、鹅黄的色调，拉开了秋的序幕，随后，糖枫、红枫、西克莫枫等上百种的枫树也慢慢由黄、橙色转变成红色，与交织其间的绿松、杉树共同谱出秋的主旋律，随着越来越长的夜晚、越来越凉爽的气温，北方红栎等加东常见的橡树，也以深沉的红褐色预告了秋天的即将谢幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5782688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5782688" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1311260347581528.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5782688 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1311260347581528-600x403.jpg" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5782688" class="wp-caption-text">加拿大渥太华秋天枫叶（任乔生／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><b>川普朗度假村（Village of Mont Tremblant）山峦枫红片片</b></h2>
<p>川普朗度假村位于魁北克、渥太华及蒙特娄之间的“洛朗区”，搭乘缆车居高临下，可以远眺Monroe 湖和塔伯拉山脉的山脊，层层山峦枫红片片，景致美不胜收，是北美加东首屈一指的赏枫胜地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9481143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9481143" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310452171758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9481143" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310452171758-600x800.jpg" width="350" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9481143" class="wp-caption-text">川普朗国家公园枫红与湖光山色相辉映。（giggel／维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9481931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9481931" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310448051758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9481931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310448051758-600x494.jpg" width="500" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9481931" class="wp-caption-text">加拿大川普朗渡假村枫红片片，美不胜收。（giggel／维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5774292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5774292" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409240204131758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5774292" title="Mont Tremblant Lake and village in autumn, Quebec, Canada" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409240204131758-600x433.jpg" alt="Mont Tremblant Lake and village in autumn, Quebec, Canada" width="500" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5774292" class="wp-caption-text">川普朗渡假村搭乘缆车赏枫美景。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><b>圣安妮大峡谷（Canyon Sainte Anne）枫红漫山遍野 </b></h2>
<p>魁北克圣安妮峡谷中充满了冰河融化形成的大小瀑布。一到秋季枫红时节，红黄的枫叶漫山遍野，峡谷中呈现出丰富绚烂的层次感。配上瀑布峡谷，不愧为被称为魁北克的“枫”景之最。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9481704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9481704" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310814581758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9481704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310814581758-600x337.jpg" width="500" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9481704" class="wp-caption-text">圣安妮大峡谷枫红漫山遍野。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9481700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9481700" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310542591758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9481700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310542591758-600x400.jpg" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9481700" class="wp-caption-text">加拿大圣安妮大峡谷秋色。 (Dhinakaran Gajavarathan/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><b>阿冈昆省立公园（Algonquin Provincial Park）枫红湖泊两相映</b></h2>
<p>阿冈昆公园是安大略省最古老、最著名的省立公园，园内有数千个大大小小的天然湖泊，枫红季节，满山的枫红倒映在湖泊，美景令人陶醉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9481919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9481919" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310924541758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9481919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310924541758-600x403.jpg" width="500" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9481919" class="wp-caption-text">加拿大安大略省阿冈昆公园秋季枫红。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9481921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9481921" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310924481758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9481921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310924481758-600x395.jpg" width="500" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9481921" class="wp-caption-text">加拿大安大略省阿冈昆公园秋季枫红美景。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><b>千岛群岛（Thousand Islands）红枫染岛</b></h2>
<p>千岛群岛位于圣罗伦斯河与安大略湖的交汇处，是加拿大的三大自然奇观之一，共有大小1,865个岛屿如繁星般的散落在圣罗伦斯河上，整个水域就像是一个美丽的世外桃源，乘观光船畅游千岛群岛，欣赏渡假小岛古堡及两岸嫣红的枫叶秀丽美景，是秋季最写意的赏枫行程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9482087" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9482087" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310959081758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9482087" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707310959081758-600x336.jpg" width="500" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9482087" class="wp-caption-text">千岛群岛像是一个美丽的世外桃源，乘观光船畅游是秋季最写意的赏枫行程。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9482104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9482104" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707311022301758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9482104" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707311022301758-600x338.jpg" width="500" b="282" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9482104" class="wp-caption-text">千岛群岛位于圣罗伦斯河最美的一段，也是赏枫热点。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><b>尼加拉瀑布（Niagara Falls）枫红添<span class="algo-summary">妩媚</span></b></h2>
<p>“世界七大奇景”尼加拉瀑布，位于加拿大安大略省和美国纽约州的交界处，为全球首选的蜜月胜地。秋天，绚丽的红枫为磅薄水势增添柔媚，此时搭乘“雾中少女号”，近距离体验瀑布的壮阔气势，既刺激又浪漫。</p>
