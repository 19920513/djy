<figure id="attachment_9708641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9708641" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1103d359754b505b5f0d12469a4b2642.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9708641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1103d359754b505b5f0d12469a4b2642-600x449.jpg" alt="（网络图片）" width="500" b="374" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9708641" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>

<p>票价：免费</p>
<p>时间：10/14 11am – 5pm</p>
<p>地点：帕萨迪纳南湖大道</p>

<p>网站：southlakeavenue.org/events/calendar/taste-of-south-lake-avenue-pasadena-2017</p>

<p>第3届长滩德国啤酒节(the 3rd Annual LB Oktoberfest)将于10月14日举行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9708643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9708643" style="width: 488px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/9f6430661cbb68978d7d1c69491cf5bd.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9708643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/9f6430661cbb68978d7d1c69491cf5bd.jpg" alt="（网络图片）" width="488" b="471" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9708643" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的活动与往年不同的是：不但有新的地点，还有两个不同的品酒时段供大家选择。现场的精酿啤酒种类更多，并提供无限量品尝，还有音乐演出、啤酒杯大赛、德国美食等。</p>
<p>票价：$25 &#8211; $30</p>
<p>时间：10/14 12pm-2:30pm， 3:30pm-6pm</p>
<p>地点：The Pike Outlets； the Nike Outlet Store楼上</p>
<p>95 S Pine Ave,Long Beach,CA 90802</p>
<p>网址：tasteofbrews.com/oktoberfest.md#1</p>

<h2>橙县日本街头博览会</h2>
<p>第8届橙县日本街头博览会（OC Japan Fair）将于10月13日至15日在橙县博览活动中心(OC Fair Event Center)举行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9708644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9708644" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/c50ce57a0abb5ca92a2643e0e74744b8.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-9708644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/c50ce57a0abb5ca92a2643e0e74744b8.jpg" alt="（网络图片）" width="500" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9708644" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次活动以【日本技术：经验、享受传统、创新】为主题，汇聚地道的日本传统风味佳肴、动漫、服饰、科技、流行音乐表演等，将为民众展现丰富多样的日本文化。</p>
<p>另外主办方特别准备有日本传统花道、编织作品、茶道及书法展示，各艺廊也会分别展出各式代表日本文化的作品。</p>
<p>预计将有60多个展位及3万多观众参与。</p>
<p>票价：$8</p>
<p>6岁及以下儿童、65岁以上长者免费</p>
<p>时间：10/13~10/15</p>
<p>周五 5pm -11pm</p>
<p>周六 12pm – 10pm</p>
<p>周日 10am &#8211; 5pm</p>
<p>地点：橙县博览活动中心 OC Fair Event Center</p>
<p>88 Fair Drive,Costa Mesa, CA 92626</p>
<p>网址：http://www.oc-japanfair.com</p>

<h2>2017加州鳄梨节</h2>
<p>第31届加州鳄梨节(California Avocado Festival)于本周末在卡平特里亚(Carpinteria)盛大登场，同时加州鳄梨节也是加州最大的免费音乐节！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9708647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9708647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/9ccaa4803f5e40c72606c879684a39d1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9708647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/9ccaa4803f5e40c72606c879684a39d1-600x400.jpg" alt="（网络图片）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9708647" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
