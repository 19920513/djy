<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英国风情-王室宝宝穿什么品牌的童装</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Photo-by-HRH-The-Duchess-of-Cambridge-via-Getty-Images-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>夏洛特公主两岁生日官方照片(Photo by HRH The Duchess of Cambridge via Getty Images) 
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年06月16日讯】世界上最受瞩目的一位小公主刚刚过了两岁的生日，她的妈妈剑桥公爵夫人，在官网上发了一张夏洛特公主身着黄色开衫的近照，瞬间红遍网络和媒体。</p>
<p>那么这位公爵夫人都喜欢用哪些童装品牌，打扮可爱的小王子和公主呢？我们就来盘点一下：</p>
<h2><strong>Rachel Riley</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9274854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9274854" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Photo-by-Marty-Melville-PoolGetty-Images-450x620-300x413.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9274854 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Photo-by-Marty-Melville-PoolGetty-Images-450x620-300x413.jpg" alt="(Photo by Marty Melville-Pool/Getty Images)" width="300" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9274854" class="wp-caption-text">(Photo by Marty Melville-Pool/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Rachel Riley是一家英国本土的设计师品牌，这一品牌的设计灵感主要来自英国四、五、六十年代的儿童服饰。豪华童装的花纹和图案都很经典。乔治王子在和爸爸妈妈访问新西兰期间，就以这家的一款小背带裤迷倒了一片！</p>
<p>点击<ahref="http://www.rachelriley.co.uk" target="_blank">这里</a>进入官网</p>
<h2><strong>Amaia Kids</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9274858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9274858" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Photo-by-Chris-JacksonGetty-Images-450x660-300x440.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-small wp-image-9274858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Photo-by-Chris-JacksonGetty-Images-450x660-300x440.jpg" alt="(Photo by Chris Jackson/Getty Images)" width="300" b="440" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9274858" class="wp-caption-text">(Photo by Chris Jackson/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Amaia Kids是由分别从法国和西班牙搬迁到伦敦的妈妈Segolene和Amaia创立的，所以这一品牌充满了欧洲风情。乔治王子与妹妹夏洛特公主第一次见面时，就穿了一件Amaia的小毛衣。而夏洛特公主也佩戴过这家的发饰。</p>
<p>点击<ahref="http://amaiakids.co.uk" target="_blank">这里</a>进入官网</p>
<h2><strong>Pepa &amp; Company</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9274862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9274862" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/GettyImages-611436544-450x563-300x375.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-small wp-image-9274862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/GettyImages-611436544-450x563-300x375.jpg" alt="(Photo by Chris Jackson – Pool/Getty Images)" width="300" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9274862" class="wp-caption-text">(Photo by Chris Jackson – Pool/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Pepa &amp; Company是来自西班牙的童装品牌，和大多数王室钟爱的品牌一样，Pepa＆Company提供漂亮而经典的服装款式。夏洛特公主在加拿大参加party时，曾身着一款这家的连衣裙。</p>
<p>点击<ahref="http://www.pepaandcompany.com" target="_blank">这里</a>进入官网</p>
<h2><strong>Olivier Baby</strong></h2>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-9274864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Screenshot-2017-05-02-12.52.17-450x335-300x223.png" alt="Screenshot-2017-05-02-12.52.17-450x335" width="500" b="372" /></p>
<p>Olivier Baby是始创于2011年的年轻的英国品牌，由两个姐妹(Emma Davison 和 Alice Smith)经营，她们用手工编织样品，并在她们的孩子（常作为品牌模特儿）身上试穿；而她们的羊绒开衫则是完美的礼物！夏洛特公主在她的一岁生日的照片上穿着这个品牌的羊绒开衫。</p>
<p>点击<ahref="http://www.olivierbaby.com" target="_blank">这里</a>进入官网</p>
<h2><strong>Do?a Carmen</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9274866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9274866" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/GettyImages-611855710-450x675-300x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-small wp-image-9274866" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/GettyImages-611855710-450x675-300x450.jpg" alt="(Photo by Andrew Milligan/WPA Pool/Getty Images)" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9274866" class="wp-caption-text">(Photo by Andrew Milligan/WPA Pool/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Do?a Carmen于1970年在西班牙的桑坦德创立，品牌初衷是给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">儿童时尚</a>增添一点不同的样式和色彩。四十年后，它成为了较有规模的儿童时尚品牌，且很受王室的青睐。乔治王子曾穿着这个品牌的红色灯芯绒短裤，夏洛特公主一些可爱的小鞋子也是从这家来的。</p>
<p>点击<ahref="http://donacarmen.es/en/" target="_blank">这里</a>进入官网</p>
<h2><strong>Les petites Abeilles</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9274868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9274868" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/GettyImages-485031949-450x648-300x432.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-small wp-image-9274868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/GettyImages-485031949-450x648-300x432.jpg" alt="(Photo by Chris Jackson/Getty Images)" width="300" b="432" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9274868" class="wp-caption-text">(Photo by Chris Jackson/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Les petites Abeilles的创意总监和老板是西班牙的Désiréevon Hohenlohe公主。今年的最新设计是与YOOX独家合作的夏季运动系列，很是有范儿！剑桥公爵夫人同样是这个品牌的粉丝。</p>
<p>点击<ahref="http://www.lespetitesabeilles.com" target="_blank">这里</a>进入官网</p>
<h2><strong>M &amp; H</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9274871" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9274871" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Screenshot-2017-05-02-14.24.38-450x449-300x299.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9274871 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Screenshot-2017-05-02-14.24.38-450x449-300x299.png" alt="(Photo by HRH THE DUCHESS OF CAMBRIDGE)" width="300" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9274871" class="wp-caption-text">(Photo by HRH THE DUCHESS OF CAMBRIDGE)</figcaption></figure>
<p>M &amp; H是来自西班牙的轻奢儿童品牌，凭借着简单，经典的设计，应得的皇家（妈妈）的认可。夏洛特公主曾在曾祖母（女王）生日时穿着这个品牌的小裙衫。</p>
<p>点击<ahref="http://www.myhbaby.com/en" target="_blank">这里</a>进入官网</p>
<h2><strong>Fina Ejerique</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9274872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9274872" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/prince-george-wool-jumper-p288-865_image-1-450x306-450x306.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9274872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/prince-george-wool-jumper-p288-865_image-1-450x306-450x306.jpg" alt="（aliolikids.com 官网图片）" width="500" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9274872" class="wp-caption-text">（aliolikids.com 官网图片）</figcaption></figure>

<p>点击<ahref="http://finaejerique.es/en/" target="_blank">这里</a>进入官网</p>
<h2><strong>Trotters</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9274880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9274880" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/GettyImages-545507380-450x718-300x479.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-small wp-image-9274880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/GettyImages-545507380-450x718-300x479.jpg" alt="(Photo by Chris Jackson/Getty Images)" width="300" b="479" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9274880" class="wp-caption-text">(Photo by Chris Jackson/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>西伦敦的豪华童装零售商Trotters也常常是王室宝宝身上的亮点，由夫妻团队（Sophie Mirman和Richard Ross）经营，很受英国社会中上层主流家庭的喜爱。小王子的丁字布鞋就是这个品牌的经典之一。</p>
<p>点击<ahref="http://www.trotters.co.uk" target="_blank">这里</a>进入官网</p>
<h2><strong>Wild &amp; Gorgeous</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9274885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9274885" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/girls-cilla-dress-aquaretouched-450x450-300x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9274885 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/girls-cilla-dress-aquaretouched-450x450-300x300.jpg" alt="（Wild &amp; Gorgeous官网图片）" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9274885" class="wp-caption-text">（Wild &amp; Gorgeous官网图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Wild &amp; Gorgeous也是一家英国本土品牌，以女童装起家，后又扩展至于男童装。也许她们也没想到，这一扩展却给她们赚来了王室的粉丝！</p>
<p>点击<ahref="http://www.wildandgorgeous.co.uk/?gclid=Cj0KEQjwoqvIBRD6ls6og8qB77YBEiQAcqqHe0-BoYa_X7Kf00DeQB8ylCvraACfB-m-L7ZUeiFyBZgaAvz48P8HAQ" target="_blank">这里</a>进入官网</p>
<p>别以为王室宝宝只穿高端品牌，我们其实也可以频频看到这些未来的国王、女王们穿着“高街”哦！比如乔治王子穿着Cath Kidson，卢森堡的阿玛莉公主则以Zara作为她第一张官方照片的服饰选择，西班牙的双胞公主也经常穿着Mango的童装；而英女王外孙的千金也被拍到穿着Crocs和Gap。</p>
