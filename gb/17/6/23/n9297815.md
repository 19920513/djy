<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯司机常识差 需更多交叉口黄格线</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年06月23日讯】（大纪元记者林达澳洲珀斯编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E5%85%AC%E8%B7%AF.md#1">西澳公路</a>局（Main Roads WA）宣布，在另外12个交叉口画上醒目的黄格线标记，并说这对于缺乏常识的司机很有必要。</p>
<p>据今日西澳网报导，黄格线提醒司机不要在交叉口停车，去年已在珀斯其它交叉口做了实验。实验证实交叉口车队排长龙现象有所减少。</p>
<p>公路局发言人说，希望此举能改善交通拥挤状况，工程将于月底完工。“听起来像是个常识，但我们经常看到司机堵在交叉口，造成塞车。”他说。</p>
<p>12个黄格线实验点位于：</p>
<p>James Street and Johnson Street, Guildford</p>
<p>James Street and Meadow Street, Guildford</p>
<p>Ellen Stirling Boulevard and Scarborough Beach Road, Innaloo</p>
<p>West Coast Highway and Manning Road, Scarborough</p>
<p>Stephenson Avenue and Scarborough Beach Road, Osborne Park</p>
<p>Cedric Street and Ellen Stirling Boulevard, Innaloo</p>
<p>Stirling Highway and Bay View Terrace, Claremont</p>
<p>Stirling Highway and Leura Avenue, Claremont</p>
<p>Albany Highway and Liege Street, Cannington</p>
<p>Orrong Road and Wright Street, Carlisle</p>
<p>Canning Highway and Ardross Street, Applecross</p>
<p>Canning Highway and Preston Point Road, East Fremantle。</p>
