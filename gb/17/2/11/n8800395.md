<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>罗晋晒票力撑女友 调侃唐嫣发型似方便面</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年02月12日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%94%90%E5%AB%A3.md#1">唐嫣</a>等主演的电影《决战食神》于元宵节上映。日前，唐嫣男友<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E6%99%8B.md#1">罗晋</a>晒出数十张电影票力挺新片。他还贴上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%94%90%E5%AB%A3.md#1">唐嫣</a>的角色海报，调侃她饰演的海胆妹那一头卷发像“方便面”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8800438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8800438" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/48b56685gy1fclj2bzaxkj20yi0pwkjl-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8800438 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/48b56685gy1fclj2bzaxkj20yi0pwkjl-450x338.jpg" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8800438" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E6%99%8B.md#1">罗晋</a>晒电影票支持唐嫣新戏。（罗晋微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗晋日前在微博发文支持女友唐嫣主演的电影《决战食神》，他说：“里面有一个特别美的方便面头姑娘叫唐嫣！赶快去买票啊！不然票都被卖光了，抢不到了！”并配上数十张电影票组成的图片及唐嫣的角色海报，海报中唐嫣短短的蓬松卷发，很像罗晋所描述的“方便面”。</p>
<p>唐嫣转发罗晋微博并笑答： “还真被你说对了，我也觉得方便面头小姑娘挺好看的！”</p>
<p>早前在路演现场，唐嫣在聊到角色造型时也说：“霆锋和叶导（叶伟民导演）看到我的这个造型就笑得很开心，不过，等到这部戏杀青，我放下长发，导演都没有认出来是我。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8800443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8800443" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/48b56685gy1fclj20tuu6j20rs15swvg-300x451.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8800443 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/48b56685gy1fclj20tuu6j20rs15swvg-300x451.jpg" width="300" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8800443" class="wp-caption-text">罗晋调侃女友唐嫣卷发似方便面。（罗晋微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>去年12月6日，在唐嫣生日之际，罗晋晒出他俩的童年PS照为她庆生，同时写道：“我的肩膀永远是你的依靠，亲爱的你，生日快乐。”唐嫣随即甜蜜转发回应：“缘来是你。”二人恋情正式公开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8800450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8800450" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/48b56685gw1fagwui7qz2j21ww2pg1ky-300x424.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8800450 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/48b56685gw1fagwui7qz2j21ww2pg1ky-300x424.jpg" width="300" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8800450" class="wp-caption-text">罗晋PS的他和唐嫣的童年照。（罗晋微博）</figcaption></figure>
