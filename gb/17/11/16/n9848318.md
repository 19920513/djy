<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>想有个好气色？这12种食材帮你调理气血</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>枸杞可以改善女性气虚体质。(Fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年11月16日讯】（大纪元记者罗蓉蓉综合报导）由于生理上的原因，女人容易患<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%AB%E8%A1%80.md#1">贫血</a>，一旦<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%94%E8%A1%80%E4%B8%8D%E8%B6%B3.md#1">气血不足</a>，就会导致肤色暗淡、长斑长痘等等肌肤问题。可想而知，女人想要拥有一个好气色，补气血很重要。以下介绍几种食材，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E6%80%A7.md#1">女性</a>朋友们可多加食用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>1. 黑芝麻</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9855717" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9855717" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9855717" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-3-600x401.jpg" alt="黑芝麻。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9855717" class="wp-caption-text">黑芝麻。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>黑芝麻有健脑乌发的养血功效，很适合大量消耗体力的人。现代科学证实，黑芝麻中含有九成不饱和脂肪酸及亚麻油酸，是很好的护心食物。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>2. 枸杞</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9855743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9855743" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9855743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-5-600x400.jpg" alt="枸杞。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9855743" class="wp-caption-text">枸杞。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E6%80%A7.md#1">女性</a>若是气虚则容易畏寒、四肢冰冷、疲倦无力或是皮肤干燥，枸杞可以改善体质，对于睡眠质量不佳，难以入睡等睡眠问题均有一定的缓解作用。此外，枹杞可以帮助维持皮肤的水感，还有美白作用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>3. 甘蔗</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9799897" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9799897" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1612061511312483-e1510930753763.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9799897 size-large" title="新鲜的甘蔗汁" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1612061511312483-e1510930753763-600x330.jpg" width="600" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9799897" class="wp-caption-text">甘蔗。(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在医界有“补血之王”的美誉的甘蔗，含有丰富的维生素、脂肪、蛋白质，及大量的铁、钙、磷、锰、锌等人体必需的微量元素。特别是其含铁量，在水果中含量是最高的。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>4. 红米</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9855751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9855751" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-11.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9855751 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-11-600x400.jpg" alt="Untitled design (11)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9855751" class="wp-caption-text">红米。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>红米含有丰富的淀粉与植物蛋白质，可补充消耗的体力及维持身体正常体温。而其所含的丰富铁质，有补气养血及预防<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%AB%E8%A1%80.md#1">贫血</a>的功效。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>5. 番茄</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9855756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9855756" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9855756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-6-600x435.jpg" alt="番茄。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="435" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9855756" class="wp-caption-text">番茄。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>番茄有良好的补气养血功效。每人每天食用50到100克鲜番茄，即可满足人体对几种维生素和矿物质的需要。食用番茄可以抑制细菌、治疗干眼症、夜盲症及某些皮肤病，或是降低体内胆固醇含量。（<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/17/6/30/n9341882.md#1">做菜加番茄防前列腺病！5种饮食保护前列腺</a></span></span>）</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>6. 胡萝卜</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9848378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9848378" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1405161228202483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9848378 size-large" title="木制背景新鲜胡萝卜" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1405161228202483-600x400.jpg" alt="木制背景新鲜胡萝卜" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9848378" class="wp-caption-text">胡萝卜。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>胡萝卜皮中所含有的胡萝卜素及铁可促进血红素增加，提高血液浓度及血液质量，对治疗贫血有很大作用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>7. 黄豆</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9815308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9815308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/c771cef295f5992b70b3b489d21b3ff2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9815308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/c771cef295f5992b70b3b489d21b3ff2-600x400.jpg" alt="选购黄豆时要注意产地与构成，最好选用台湾本土生产的非基改黄豆。 (赖瑞)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9815308" class="wp-caption-text">黄豆。 (赖瑞提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄豆中含有的异黄酮素又能产生与雌激素荷尔蒙相似的效用，降低血液胆固醇，保护心脏，预防乳癌骨质疏松的情况。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>8. 葡萄</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9848383" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9848383" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/100417223330100445.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9848383 size-large" title="食物" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/100417223330100445-e1510931189504-600x414.jpg" alt="食物" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9848383" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>中医认为葡萄有补气血、强筋骨和利小便的功效。现代医学研究也表明，葡萄能阻止血栓形成，且能降低人体血清胆固醇水准，降低血小板的凝聚力，对预防心脑血管病有一定作用。买不到新鲜葡萄时，吃葡萄干也行。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>9. 南瓜</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9855778" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9855778" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9855778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-7-600x400.jpg" alt="南瓜。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9855778" class="wp-caption-text">南瓜。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>南瓜含有“钴”，是极好的补血食材，还有促新陈代谢、养颜美容等效果。（<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/16/9/18/n8313243.md#1">南瓜拿铁秋季热 专家：不要在咖啡店随便点</a></span></span>）</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>10. 龙眼肉</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9855791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9855791" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9855791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-9-600x450.jpg" alt="龙眼。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9855791" class="wp-caption-text">龙眼。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>龙眼肉即桂圆肉，含有维生素的A、B、葡萄糖和蔗糖等，而且具有丰富的铁质。龙眼汤、龙眼酒等食物，推荐孕妇和产妇食用，是颇佳的补血食物。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>11. 银耳</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_7294553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7294553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/131022153311100087.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7294553 size-large" title="桂花银耳" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/131022153311100087-600x400.jpg" alt="桂花银耳" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7294553" class="wp-caption-text">桂花银耳汤。(苏玉芬／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>现代研究证明，银耳的粗纤维有助胃肠蠕动，减少脂肪吸收，有助减肥，并有去除脸部黄褐斑、雀斑的功效。银耳红枣汤有补脾、养血、安神等作用，适合女性饮用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>12. 豆浆</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9855794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9855794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-10-e1510933107483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9855794 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Untitled-design-10-e1510933107483-600x400.jpg" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9855794" class="wp-caption-text">豆浆。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>女性想要皮肤水嫩白皙，在众多饮食当中，豆浆属于女性的黄金饮品；尤其是更年期妇女容易出现热潮红、心悸、皮肤暗沉等症状，喝豆浆可以达到改善的效果。</p>
