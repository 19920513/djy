<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【折纸】企鹅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00436-e1490812766418.jpg" />
<h6>(图/ 张芯兰 提供)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>企鹅在动物园里是最吸引目光的主角之一，不论大人、小孩都喜欢它摇摇摆摆的走路模样。今天，我们就用各种颜色的色纸来折可爱的纸企鹅吧！</p>
<p><strong>?材料：</strong>双面不同色彩花纹的包装纸或是色纸都可以，美工刀、直尺、剪刀、白胶或双面胶带、无墨水的原子笔。</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong></p>
<p>一、将纸张的对应角相叠合、对折，摊开，留下折线甲，取出纸张的中心线。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946699-wenfa-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946699-wenfa-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946700-wenfa-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946700-wenfa-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a></p>
<p>二、将上图中左、右二边的角分别往中央线翻折、对齐，如下图。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946701-wenfa-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946701-wenfa-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946702-wenfa-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946702-wenfa-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a></p>
<p>三、再将两角自甲线处分别往外侧翻出约一公分宽后折下，如下图。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946705-wenfa-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946705-wenfa-7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a></p>
<p>四、将图6中的上端角，在距顶点约4公分处，将其往下方翻折，如下图。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946707-wenfa-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946707-wenfa-9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a>五、将上端的角在距折线约半公分宽处，再反向翻折回去，如下图。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946708-wenfa-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946708-wenfa-10-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a></p>
<p>六、将下方的角在距下方顶点约3公分处，往后翻折。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946708-wenfa-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946708-wenfa-10-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946710-wenfa-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946710-wenfa-12-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_13946711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946711" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946711-wenfa-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946711-wenfa-13-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946711" class="wp-caption-text">正面的样子。</figcaption></figure>
<p>七、运用折线甲将企鹅身体两侧往背部（即后方）折叠、放开，这样企鹅就可以站立。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946697-wenfa-19.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946697" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946697-wenfa-19-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a></p>
<p>八、将上端贴着头部折下，成三角状，再打开，从背部依折痕翻折，使企鹅头部往下立体起来。如下图。将动作3所翻折出来的小三角形，也就是企鹅的两翅（上肢），分别将外侧的底角再向甲线方向翻折出另一个小小的三角形。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946695-wenfa-17.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946695" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946695-wenfa-17-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a></p>
<p>十、企鹅家族有心事要相诉，所以再折几只企鹅家的成员，让其一家团员吧！</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946698-wenfa-20.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13946698" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/id13946698-wenfa-20-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a>@*</p>
