<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>NBA安比德领军 哈里斯27分白搭</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年12月03日讯】（大纪元记者魏文彬综合报导）<span lang="EN-US">NBA</span>新生代两大中锋，同为<span lang="EN-US">213</span>公分、底特律活塞的德拉蒙德（<span lang="EN-US">Andre Drummond</span>）与号称费城<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人希望的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E6%AF%94%E5%BE%B7.md#1">安比德</a>（<span lang="EN-US">Joel Embiid</span>），虽赛前就“口水战”开打，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日正式开战后，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E6%AF%94%E5%BE%B7.md#1">安比德</a>还是技高一筹，以<span lang="EN-US">25</span>分<span lang="EN-US">10</span>篮板“双十”的优异表现，硬是将<span lang="EN-US">14</span>分<span lang="EN-US">11</span>篮板的德拉蒙德给比下去，仅管<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E9%87%8C%E6%96%AF.md#1">哈里斯</a>（<span lang="EN-US">Tobias Harris</span>）拿下全场最高的<span lang="EN-US">27</span>分，终场活塞仍以<span lang="EN-US">103</span>：<span lang="EN-US">108</span>不敌<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人。</p>
<p>前<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人球星艾佛森（<span lang="EN-US">Allen Iverson</span>）亲临现场观战，安比德、科温顿（<span lang="EN-US">Robert Covington</span>）、雷迪克（<span lang="EN-US">J.J. Redick</span>）及萨里奇（<span lang="EN-US">Dario Saric</span>）等先发大将受到鼓舞，联手缴出<span lang="EN-US">49</span>分，上半场打完<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人就以<span lang="EN-US">63</span>：<span lang="EN-US">47</span>遥遥领先。</p>
<p>活塞下半场展现韧性，杰克森（<span lang="EN-US">Reggie Jackson</span>）率先展现三分火力，连续砍进<span lang="EN-US">3</span>颗三分弹，外带<span lang="EN-US">2</span>罚，开启追分模式，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E9%87%8C%E6%96%AF.md#1">哈里斯</a>再接棒演出，狂飙<span lang="EN-US">12</span>分，打完三节将比数扳平。</p>
<p>重新洗牌的两队进入决胜节的殊死战，前几分钟两队互有领先，读秒前<span lang="EN-US">3</span>分<span lang="EN-US">57</span>秒，安比德吸引德拉蒙德犯下第五犯，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>罚全进，德拉蒙德随后切入没进，最后<span lang="EN-US">2</span>分<span lang="EN-US">35</span>秒又对安比德犯下第六犯，德拉蒙德犯满离场后，活塞内线防守减弱，<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人强势进攻，最后虽哈里斯和加洛威（<span lang="EN-US">Langston Galloway</span>）都有三分长射的演出，但是为时已晚，活塞苦吞二连败。<span lang="EN-US">**</span></p>
