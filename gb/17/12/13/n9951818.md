<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>领养流浪狗惊见“史努比” 与妈妈的第一只收藏是双胞胎</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24296793_1334670626639064_779126412514198552_n-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>狗狗和饲主的第一只史努比收藏合照，简直是双胞胎。（饲主提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年12月13日讯】一只3个月大的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E5%85%8B%E6%96%AF.md#1">米克斯</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%81%E6%B5%AA%E7%8B%97.md#1">流浪狗</a>，在桃园工厂被善心人士收养后，主人在脸书社团《米克斯传奇》为狗狗征名。由于狗狗外表和卡通角色“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B2%E5%8A%AA%E6%AF%94.md#1">史努比</a>”实在太像引发关注，网友们纷纷惊呼“根本是史努比真狗版！”</p>
<p>饲主Chen King表示，老婆从小就很喜欢卡通角色<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B2%E5%8A%AA%E6%AF%94.md#1">史努比</a>，房间里还堆满了许多收藏品。其中她国小收藏的第一只史努比玩偶，放在狗狗旁边，简直就是一个模子刻出来的，甚至连背部的斑点都和史努比一模一样，让主人一家惊喜万分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9952315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9952315" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24296484_1334037580035702_5750424407986545472_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9952315 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24296484_1334037580035702_5750424407986545472_n-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9952315" class="wp-caption-text">史努比和妈妈的第一只史努比合照，简直是双胞胎。（饲主提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9952324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9952324" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24774727_1334037693369024_8661232221536769090_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9952324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24774727_1334037693369024_8661232221536769090_n-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9952324" class="wp-caption-text">不仅白色的身体、黑色的耳朵，连背上的斑点都和史努比一模一样。（饲主提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>关于收养契机，饲主表示，他们家原先已养了一只柴犬阿肥，一直想再找只狗为他作伴，正好日前在网路上看到有网友在为狗宝宝找好主人，原本看照片就觉得狗狗长得像史努比，没想到接回家一看更是惊为天人，他们笑说“尤其刚带回来还没洗澡的时候，都脏脏的简直就是双胞胎。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_9952299" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9952299" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24301252_1334037656702361_990557420885588335_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9952299" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24301252_1334037656702361_990557420885588335_n-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9952299" class="wp-caption-text">史努比真实版现身。（饲主提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9952269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9952269" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24837287_1335493039890156_808572507279025895_o.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9952269" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24837287_1335493039890156_808572507279025895_o-450x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9952269" class="wp-caption-text">饲主妈妈小时候就是个疯狂的史努比迷。（饲主提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9952183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9952183" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24301253_1334977886608338_6667465958677162732_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9952183 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/24301253_1334977886608338_6667465958677162732_n-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9952183" class="wp-caption-text">看看可爱的史努比与双胞胎玩偶共睡的模样。（饲主提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看完照片后，网友们自然就给了狗宝宝一个最贴切的名字——史努比。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9952318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9952318" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/25353645_1341595925946534_8386311742322514388_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9952318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/25353645_1341595925946534_8386311742322514388_n-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9952318" class="wp-caption-text">不仅白色的身体、黑色的耳朵，连背上的斑点都和史努比一模一样。（饲主提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饲主还帮两只可爱宝贝开了粉丝团“<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/%E5%8F%B2%E5%8A%AA%E6%AF%94%E8%88%87%E9%98%BF%E8%82%A5%E6%9F%B4%E7%9A%84%E7%98%8B%E6%97%A5%E8%A8%98-1333989436707183/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">史努比与阿肥柴的疯日记</a>”分享照片和生活点滴。网友们全被萌翻大呼“可以跟史努比要签名吗？”、“真狗版看几次都不会腻~~~”、“史努比本身也是被查理领养的。:)”，也有网友说“希望宝宝不要和史努比一样调皮啊！”</p>
<p>不过主人透露，家中两个小兄弟经常吵架，“虽然两个都很皮。不过这两个孩子，都还是我的最爱。”</p>
