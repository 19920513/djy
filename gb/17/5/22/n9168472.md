<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波澜壮阔！从被驱逐到年入400亿公司掌门人</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: left;">从入职半年就被父亲逐出公司大门，到临危受命，被二次被请回来，大刀阔斧的改革，把一家濒临倒闭的家族公司，改造成一个年入400亿的国际集团公司。回首往事，今年56岁的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%9F%E9%87%8E%E4%BD%B3%E8%B7%AF.md#1">星野佳路</a>用了四个字形容他的前半生：波澜壮阔。</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">临危受命，将濒临倒闭的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E6%97%8F%E6%97%85%E9%A6%86.md#1">家族旅馆</a>，变成遍布世界的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E9%85%92%E5%BA%97.md#1">国际酒店</a>，这四个字，实至名归。</p>
<div>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168475 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/bef4c2f4bc7eeaf13ac222a2d17ff90a-450x357.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_03_Image_0001" width="450" b="357" /><img class=" wp-image-9168476 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/559754e000ee98ad141ca1eb9a998ce4-450x300.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_03_Image_0002" width="365" b="243" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168477 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/548d7cfa7458902e36eb3427c20051b0-450x337.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_04_Image_0001" width="365" b="273" /></p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168478 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/3a109f4f9371ec719c5476a46c420c6a-450x253.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_04_Image_0002" width="450" b="253" /></p>
</div>
<p style="text-align: left;">1960年，他出生在日本的轻井泽，是日本拥有百年历史的，星野温泉旅馆的，第四代继承人。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168479 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/54a28f23c165eb5758519fcb3db3814f-450x337.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_05_Image_0001" width="316" b="237" /></p>
<p>星野国次</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168480 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/f856416642f2af504d2a8270153b4d4a-450x354.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_06_Image_0001" width="358" b="282" /></p>
<p>1914年，佳路的曾祖父星野国次在轻井泽创办了家族企业星野温泉旅馆，从小叛逆的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%9F%E9%87%8E%E4%BD%B3%E8%B7%AF.md#1">星野佳路</a>，似乎天生反骨，热衷于打曲棍球，他的梦想不是成为继承人，而是成为运动员。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168481 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/cbcf8e50a41e82ec3534e4e459a07d6d-450x443.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_07_Image_0001" width="261" b="257" /></p>
<p>但是作为家族长子，家族责任毫无疑问落到他的肩上。当他从康奈尔大学，酒店管理系毕业回家时，家族成员也不相信，这个只会打曲棍球的小子，能管理好温泉旅馆。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168482 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/6b1b7214d70d5a14f091dedb9477f8c1-450x474.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_08_Image_0001" width="291" b="307" /></p>
<p>其实说是家族企业，此时的星野温泉旅馆，由于经营方式老化，家族成员矛盾复杂，以及新兴旅馆的冲击，已濒临倒闭的边缘。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168483 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/5e9502b8f276dfd8c6be8302271a1d16-450x274.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_09_Image_0001" width="338" b="206" /></p>
<p>当他怀着满腔热情，以副董事长的身份进入公司时，面对的是21位家族股东，和一大批资历比他年纪还大的老员工。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168484 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/11d829b686f40a111b63bc3e190a6f23-450x310.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_10_Image_0001" width="344" b="237" /></p>
<p>要想在竞争中存活，就必须改革。但是盘根错杂的家族关系，和老员工的非难，让改革举步维艰。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168485 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/cb23eec93f8954e130597691c50610db-450x300.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_11_Image_0001" width="326" b="217" /></p>
<p>更关键的是，作为掌门人的父亲，也不能接受他带来的改变。一次又一次的争吵，终于，在佳路入职6个月后，父亲指着办公室的门对他说：“你走吧，离开这里。”</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168486 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/f69d578cbdeaa2a0dcea06eee9f3af5b-450x300.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_12_Image_0001" width="356" b="237" /></p>
<p>6个月，继承人离职。星野佳路成了，一个天大的笑话。之后他在花旗银行，找到了一份工作。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168487 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/d88813c158b990dbac63951ccb2c0f9a-450x283.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_13_Image_0001" width="372" b="234" /><br />
1991年，星野温泉旅馆，面临更大的危机。星野佳路，这位遗落在外的继承人，又受到了族人们的关注。<br />
但是作为回去的条件，星野佳路提出了两项要求：一是明确总裁权限；二是，确立董事会改选制度。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168488 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/dedfbf1092f785d552500089cbb5d412-450x300.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_14_Image_0001" width="383" b="255" /></p>
<p>这个毛头小子，第二次进入父亲的公司。临危受命，星野佳路依然没有妥协：要生存就必须要发生改变。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168489 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/a657fc09ec6f75af151499301b81dc0f-450x300.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_15_Image_0001" width="437" b="291" /></p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168490 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/c4e228604b931053fe1f163a33292c17-450x300.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_15_Image_0002" width="450" b="300" /></p>
<p>“你根本不懂我的味道”，当星野佳路要一位老厨师改进菜色时，他大声说道。并且，星野佳路大刀阔斧的改革，<br />
导致了近三分之一的员工离职。</p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168491 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/006f880d3fd17f5e098eecd0d84a9091-450x346.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_16_Image_0001" width="450" b="346" /></p>
<p>全家族上下似乎，又在等着看第二次笑话。但是这次并没有。星野佳路大胆地打破，日本原有的管理方式。</p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168493 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/bb37071211c0a1dfebbf1d133119250f-450x277.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_18_Image_0001" width="450" b="277" /></p>
<p>“如果他们想成为经理，就让他们试着成为经理，想换职位就让他们换职位。”他每年给员工两次升迁和调职的机会。</p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168494 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/b782dfcf8026ce288db849ac1b2f9810-450x311.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_18_Image_0002" width="450" b="311" /></p>
<p>日本的企业，讲究论资排辈，年轻人想要升职只能熬资历。星野的策略恰恰针对这一点，一提出就吸引了大量年轻人，而且员工的积极性也大大提高。</p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168495 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/eb1930d183dd408a3a9f7d56d1e960be-450x346.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_19_Image_0001" width="450" b="346" /></p>
<p>星野集团最受欢迎的项目：“最接近天堂的咖啡厅”，这个创意就来自，一个修护缆车的员工。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168727 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/0-1.gif" alt="0" /></p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168497 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/c25dc02b98c476a66e69b1f72b116adf-450x213.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_20_Image_0002" width="450" b="213" /></p>
<p>你，很重要，哪怕是一名清洁工人。新野佳路传递给，他的员工这样的信息。</p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168498 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/ae4051d6326fd443426fbe1593ff7e00-450x355.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_21_Image_0001" width="450" b="355" /></p>
<p>所以，当后来人们统计，日本员工的离职率时，发现星野的离职率竟是最低的。数年前，他们因为改变离开，<br />
数年后，又有人因为改变留了下来。</p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168499 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/f4fec2cb1fb60bb726e87628558fb71c-450x252.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_22_Image_0001" width="450" b="252" /></p>
<p>然而，星野的歪点子还没有出完，当全球的酒店管理者，都试图让顾客有在家的感觉时，他提出了一个新的概念：<br />
“非日常化”。</p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168500 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/6b6ad93fb241a1b0195ee1b1570751f0-450x306.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_23_Image_0001" width="450" b="306" /></p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168501 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/3f78e780794fab33ccc3c8837996ed72-450x253.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_23_Image_0002" width="450" b="253" /></p>
<p>让顾客获得，在日常生活中不曾有过的体验，从日常生活中逃离出来。</p>
<p>在月光下骑马。</p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168502 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/5528a9ffa0bb4741ad35392cba632eba-450x291.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_24_Image_0001" width="450" b="291" /></p>
<p>童话般的冰雪小镇。</p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168503 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/5830e605e2f82de5421e2e74810fd8e2-450x300.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_24_Image_0002" width="450" b="300" /></p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168504 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/2399e0d0ca5f7a13362e6973ce623639-450x291.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_25_Image_0001" width="450" b="291" /></p>
<p>打造石之教堂、水之教堂，<br />
冰之教堂和高原教堂，</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168505 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/b74c74e8516c282f8fba3161e667e3da-450x257.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_26_Image_0001" width="513" b="293" /></p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168506 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/aa74af90bf83558f4ec42087c0950022-450x304.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_26_Image_0002" width="450" b="304" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168507 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/0794b07ed1187c23e176420535918f7b-450x383.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_27_Image_0001" width="508" b="432" /></p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168508 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/6987d796622988edc550b72fda05fb67-450x300.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_27_Image_0002" width="450" b="300" /></p>
<p>他经营的不再是酒店，而是一个又一个，普通人的梦。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168509 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/a6c43af7c970ac8b87dd0c25923a451a-450x266.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_28_Image_0001" width="399" b="236" /></p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-9168510 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/a0aec33bb0a60d90d11abf5269b78a9c-450x299.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_28_Image_0002" width="450" b="299" /></p>
<p>既然是以温泉起家，<br />
新野就将温泉做到极致。<br />
半开放式露天温泉，<br />
秋的华美、冬的静穆，<br />
泡汤不只是泡汤，<br />
还有了不一样的内容。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168511 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/279ebdaeadb343ee4d1f2e2015ee3c1c-450x271.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_29_Image_0001" width="505" b="304" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9168512 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/21500658e606087e8cad2df81286ea58-450x266.jpg" alt="xingsuhotel·_Page_29_Image_0002" width="506" b="299" /></p>
<p>星野的改革，<br />
很快让温泉旅馆扭转了局面，<br />
逐渐发展成为，<br />
拥有35个旅馆和度假村，<br />
年收入达400亿日元的，<br />
