<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>安心亚遛狗招桃花 期许演技进步</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709120710362384-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>安心亚9月12日在台北出席公益活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年09月13日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）台湾女星安心亚12日出席公益活动，揭晓她参与设计的公益服饰，呼吁以领养代替购买、不要看残忍马戏团等，希望略尽棉薄之力，改善流浪猫狗的问题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9624419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9624419" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709120710252384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9624419" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709120710252384.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9624419" class="wp-caption-text">安心亚12日出席公益活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9624418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9624418" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709120710312384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9624418" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709120710312384.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9624418" class="wp-caption-text">安心亚揭晓她参与设计的公益服饰。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现在有养狗陪伴，会不会希望有男友陪伴？安心亚打趣说，路上遛狗会碰到同样爱狗人士，说不定就可以招桃花，“现在虽然没有人陪伴，但还好有狗陪伴，最喜欢喂食它的时候，心情不好时摸摸它、对它好，它会很忠心陪伴你。”也欢迎爱狗男性来交友。</p>
<p>今年“植剧场”入围24项金钟大赢家，被问及是否希望自己明年也能角逐金钟，她谦称：“期待也是会，有演到傅培梅这个角色已经很荣幸，如能够入围会很开心，期许自己在戏剧方面更努力。”◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_9624422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9624422" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709120710412384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9624422" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709120710412384.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9624422" class="wp-caption-text">安心亚呼吁以领养代替购买，改善流浪猫狗的问题。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9624424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9624424" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709120710182384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9624424" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709120710182384.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9624424" class="wp-caption-text">饰演傅培梅让安心亚觉得很荣幸，也期许自己在戏剧方面更努力。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
