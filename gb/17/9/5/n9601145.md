<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美网费德勒纳达尔晋8强　有望上演经典对决　</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年09月05日讯】（大纪元记者陈煜齐综合报导）世界排名第三的“瑞士特快车”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B9%E5%BE%B7%E5%8B%92.md#1">费德勒</a>（<span lang="EN-US">Roger Federer</span>）与现任球王“西班牙蛮牛”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%B3%E8%BE%BE%E5%B0%94.md#1">纳达尔</a>（<span lang="EN-US">Rafael Nadal</span>）<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E7%BD%91.md#1">美网</a>公开赛男单皆挺进<span lang="EN-US">8</span>强，两人再赢一轮，球迷将有望目睹经典“费纳对决”。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B9%E5%BE%B7%E5%8B%92.md#1">费德勒</a>对上德国好手柯许瑞柏（<span lang="EN-US">Philipp Kohlschreiber</span>），前两盘以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">4</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>轻松拿下，决胜盘双方保发，费德勒一度被超车，随后连夺<span lang="EN-US">3</span>分，未失一盘取胜。费德勒<span lang="EN-US">8</span>强赛将对上阿根廷名将波特罗（<span lang="EN-US">Juan Martin del Potro</span>）。</p>
<p>当今球王<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%B3%E8%BE%BE%E5%B0%94.md#1">纳达尔</a>面对乌克兰好手多戈波罗夫（<span lang="EN-US">Alexandr Dolgopolov</span>），首盘快速以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>拿下。次盘纳达尔陷入拉锯，一度被追平，但很快反攻夺下次盘。决胜盘纳达尔一口气以<span lang="EN-US">4</span>：<span lang="EN-US">0</span>领先，不给对手机会，最后直落三击败对手，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>强赛将对上俄罗斯小将罗布列夫（<span lang="EN-US">Andrey Rublev</span>）。<span lang="EN-US">**</span></p>
