<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小珍妮佛偕导演男友亮相威尼斯影展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709060927341487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>导演戴伦?阿洛诺夫斯基（左起）、蜜雪儿?菲佛、珍妮佛?劳伦斯与男星哈维尔?巴登一起现身第74届威尼斯影展。 (Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images for Paramount Pictures)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年09月06日讯】（大纪元记者黄钟综合报导）第74届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A8%81%E5%B0%BC%E6%96%AF%E5%BD%B1%E5%B1%95.md#1">威尼斯影展</a>于水都登场，珍妮佛?劳伦斯（Jennifer Lawrence）和导演男友<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%B4%E4%BC%A6%E2%80%A7%E9%98%BF%E6%B4%9B%E8%AF%BA%E5%A4%AB%E6%96%AF%E5%9F%BA.md#1">戴伦?阿洛诺夫斯基</a>（Darren Aronofsky）合作的《母亲》入围竞赛片，两人与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%9C%E9%9B%AA%E5%84%BF%E2%80%A7%E8%8F%B2%E4%BD%9B.md#1">蜜雪儿?菲佛</a>（Michelle Pfeiffer）及西班牙男星哈维尔?巴登（Javier Bardem）一起出席首映会红毯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9604521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9604521" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709060927451487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9604521 size-large" title="775030836DP00079_mother_Ven" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709060927451487-600x400.jpg" alt="775030836DP00079_mother_Ven" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9604521" class="wp-caption-text">珍妮佛?劳伦斯（右）和男星哈维尔?巴登（左）一起现身第74届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A8%81%E5%B0%BC%E6%96%AF%E5%BD%B1%E5%B1%95.md#1">威尼斯影展</a>。( Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images for Paramount Pictures)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9604665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9604665" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/GettyImages-842902310.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9604665 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/GettyImages-842902310-600x400.jpg" alt="VENICE, ITALY - SEPTEMBER 05: Darren Aronofsky attends the press conference and photo call for ?mother!? during the 74th Venice Film Festival at Casino Palace on September 5, 2017 in Venice, Italy. (Photo by Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images for Paramount Pictures)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9604665" class="wp-caption-text">2017年9月5日，导演<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%B4%E4%BC%A6%E2%80%A7%E9%98%BF%E6%B4%9B%E8%AF%BA%E5%A4%AB%E6%96%AF%E5%9F%BA.md#1">戴伦?阿洛诺夫斯基</a>（Darren Aronofsky）出席威尼斯影展《母亲》记者会。(Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images for Paramount Pictures)</figcaption></figure>
<p>27岁的奥斯卡影后“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8F%8D%E5%A6%AE%E4%BD%9B.md#1">小珍妮佛</a>”珍妮佛?劳伦斯和47岁的导演戴伦?阿洛诺夫斯基交往1年，这次是恋情曝光后首度一起公开亮相。她穿着浪漫优雅的Dior礼服，全场镁光灯闪不停，她也亲切为影迷签名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9604653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9604653" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/GettyImages-843021842.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9604653 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/GettyImages-843021842-450x675.jpg" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9604653" class="wp-caption-text">2017年9月5日，27岁的奥斯卡影后珍妮佛?劳伦斯出席威尼斯电影节《母亲》首映式。(Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images for Paramount Pictures)</figcaption></figure>
<p>戴伦对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8F%8D%E5%A6%AE%E4%BD%9B.md#1">小珍妮佛</a>赞不绝口：“她的天分是与生俱来，我总是把她拿来和麦可?乔丹做比较。”小珍妮佛也表示，一直很景仰戴伦的才华，当初甚至没看剧本就答应接演，“他是一个有远见的人。”但《母亲》首映反应却不如预期，评价两极化。</p>
<p>好莱坞喜剧巨星金?凯瑞（Jim Carrey）出席纪录片《Jim &amp; Andy: The Great Beyond》首映会。55岁的他近年因忧郁症淡出银幕，但出席影展仍展现逗趣形象，夸张地打招呼、放送飞吻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9604631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9604631" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/GettyImages-843031522.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9604631 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/GettyImages-843031522-450x675.jpg" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9604631" class="wp-caption-text">好莱坞喜剧巨星金?凯瑞（Jim Carrey）出席纪录片《Jim &amp; Andy: The Great Beyond》首映会。(Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>日本男星福山雅治与导演是枝裕和、广濑铃、役所广司为竞赛片《第三次杀人》在威尼斯举办记者会，随后也穿上西装出席红毯，显得神采奕奕，广濑铃则以露肩礼服亮相水都。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9604519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9604519" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709060933491487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9604519 size-large" title="775036398DP00028_The_Third_" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709060933491487-600x400.jpg" alt="775036398DP00028_The_Third_" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9604519" class="wp-caption-text">日本导演是枝裕和（左起）、广濑铃、福山雅治、役所广司为竞赛片《第三次杀人》在威尼斯举办记者会。 (Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>而台湾女星许玮甯受精品之邀，身穿孔雀绿礼服登上红毯，频获赞“她好漂亮！”更不时有人向随行人员询问“她叫什么名字？”“来自哪里？”她也写下身为演员的真心话：“身为演员，希望有一天能靠自己的作品来参加国际影展。”◇</p>
