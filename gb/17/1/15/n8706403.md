<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英国旅游：韦奇伍德的陶艺大世界</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年01月15日讯】（大纪元英国记者天宇报导）<strong>喜欢<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%B6%E7%93%B7%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">陶瓷艺术</a>的人，必定知晓世界知名品牌——韦奇伍德(Wedgwood)，作为英国顶级陶瓷工艺企业，韦奇伍德已经享誉世界二百余年，其独到的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AA%A8%E7%93%B7.md#1">骨瓷</a>系列和半宝石系列产品，是许多收藏家的不二之选，是能够和被西方称为“瓷器”的中国相齐名的。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8706604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8706604" style="width: 424px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/World-of-Wedgwood-1-600x901.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8706604 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/World-of-Wedgwood-1-600x901.jpg" alt="world-of-wedgwood-1" width="424" b="637" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8706604" class="wp-caption-text">位于公司正门前的乔瑟夫.韦奇伍德塑像，手中仍在钻研着一个瓷瓶 (World of Wedgwood)</figcaption></figure>
<p>乔赛亚.韦奇伍德被称为英国“陶瓷之父”，一生沉醉于研发瓷器的制作以及不同材质的运用。1759年，刚达而立之年的乔赛亚在英国特伦特河畔的斯托克创立了韦奇伍德公司，并开始了他不平凡的陶瓷人生，其作品在当时就成为欧洲上流社会的奇货，甚至连皇族都以能够拥有韦奇伍德的瓷器为荣，如早期研发的乳白瓷器（Cream Ware）被英国王后特许以“王后瓷器”（Queen&#8217;s Ware）来命名，沙俄女王凯瑟琳更是韦奇伍德的忠实拥护者，曾一口气订购了近1,000套王后瓷器组。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8706602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8706602" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/wedgwood_batch2_8-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8706602 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/wedgwood_batch2_8-600x400.jpg" alt="wedgwood_batch2_8" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8706602" class="wp-caption-text">英国王后特许以“王后瓷器”（Queen&#8217;s Ware）(World of Wedgwood)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8706598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8706598" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/IMG_7001-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8706598 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/IMG_7001-600x400.jpg" alt="img_7001" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8706598" class="wp-caption-text">说道英国瓷器，当然不得不提英式下午茶，韦奇伍德公司为来客精心打造了茶屋和茶吧，用他们自己的精品享用茶点，实是一大幸事 (World of Wedgwood)</figcaption></figure>
<p>时光荏苒，韦奇伍德的主工厂迁至有着英式田园风光的巴拉斯，四周由以前的采矿场变成了牧场和森林。数百个工匠用娴熟的工艺，打造着一件件精品，正如他们的核心精神——传承和手工制造，匠人依然沿用百年前的工艺方式，来到这里参观的游客能够近距离观摩到每一个环节的制作流程，这一点韦奇伍德公司毫不吝啬地将他们引以为豪的工艺展现给世人，那些闷头苦干的匠人也会不厌其烦的停下手中作业，来给游客详细讲解个中奥秘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8706597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8706597" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/IMG_6788-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8706597 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/IMG_6788-600x400.jpg" alt="img_6788" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8706597" class="wp-caption-text">韦奇伍德员工在给人们详细讲解碧玉浮雕系列作品的工艺 (World of Wedgwood)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8706594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8706594" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/IMG_6636-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8706594 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/IMG_6636-600x400.jpg" alt="img_6636" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8706594" class="wp-caption-text">游客能够与工匠们零距离交流，将韦奇伍德的工艺之秘一览无遗 (World of Wedgwood)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8706635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8706635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/IMG_6672-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8706635 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/IMG_6672-600x400.jpg" alt="img_6672" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8706635" class="wp-caption-text">韦奇伍德员工在给人们详细讲解精品瓷器的工艺 (World of Wedgwood)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里还建立了韦奇伍德的历史博物馆，不但用众多的模型以生动的形象重新演绎从创业至今日的工艺革新，当然还不乏创始人乔赛亚一生当中各个阶段的作品展示，从默默无闻的实验作品，到价值连城的Jasper国宝级瓷器，一应俱全。同时还有来自世界各地的瓷器精品，以展现韦奇伍德公司不断借鉴各国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%B6%E7%93%B7%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">陶瓷艺术</a>的精华，勇于开拓创新的理念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8706599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8706599" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/IMG_7751-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8706599 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/IMG_7751-600x400.jpg" alt="img_7751" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8706599" class="wp-caption-text">韦奇伍德公司为来客打造的精品茶屋 （World of Wedgwood）</figcaption></figure>
