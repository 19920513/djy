<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Sun Nong Dan先农坛韩国雪浓汤 罗兰岗飘香</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/DSCN8249-1.jpg" />
<h6>SUN NONG DAN韩国先农坛雪浓汤专门店，在罗兰岗的第三家分店。（大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年01月28日讯】在华人食客中享有超高人气和美誉的SUN NONG DAN韩国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%88%E5%86%9C%E5%9D%9B.md#1">先农坛</a>雪浓汤专门店，在罗兰岗的第三家分店全新开张了。就在Gale Ave.上，99大华超市对面的Plaza内，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%85%B0%E5%B2%97%E5%88%86%E5%BA%97.md#1">罗兰岗分店</a>的就餐氛围更为舒适温馨，抓住食客的胃靠的是不折不扣的新鲜品质和持久不变的味道。住在东区的伙伴们想品味地道营养的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89%E6%B1%A4.md#1">韩国牛肉汤</a>，不必远道去韩国城或圣盖博店大排长龙了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8753373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8753373" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/DSCN8235-1-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8753373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/DSCN8235-1-450x338.jpg" alt="舒适温馨的就餐氛围。（大纪元）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8753373" class="wp-caption-text">舒适温馨的就餐氛围。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%88%E5%86%9C%E5%9D%9B.md#1">先农坛</a>是一家专营<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E9%9B%AA%E6%B5%93%E6%B1%A4.md#1">韩国雪浓汤</a>、清煮牛肉汤锅、牛尾汤专门店，店家承诺只选用最新鲜的食材。确实如此，食材不新鲜真做不出那让人食指大动的美味。先农坛养生韩国料理店以超标营养值、超高新鲜度和美味度征服食客味蕾。虽然菜色不算太多，但道道精选，样样精心烹煮。开业几周，先农坛圣盖博店也大排长龙了，看看yelp的点评，对先农坛的美味是众口一词。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8753396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8753396" style="width: 456px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f57cfd_2076040e30bf42208e15d42ba8a68435-1-450x450.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-8753396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f57cfd_2076040e30bf42208e15d42ba8a68435-1-450x450.png" alt="牛排骨汤。（商家提供）" width="456" b="456" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8753396" class="wp-caption-text">牛排骨汤。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8753419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8753419" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f57cfd_e2cd6ac054874522b93e937a814ba03f-450x450.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8753419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f57cfd_e2cd6ac054874522b93e937a814ba03f-450x450.png" alt="牛尾汤。（商家提供）" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8753419" class="wp-caption-text">牛尾汤。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8753430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8753430" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f57cfd_42383fbc52374a4baafaeaab418b0271-1-450x450.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8753430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f57cfd_42383fbc52374a4baafaeaab418b0271-1-450x450.png" alt="什锦水煮牛肉片。（商家提供）" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8753430" class="wp-caption-text">什锦水煮牛肉片。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里传统主打的养生<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E9%9B%AA%E6%B5%93%E6%B1%A4.md#1">韩国雪浓汤</a>经过去腥处理，10多小时精心熬炖，无任何添加。白白浓浓的汤，骨头融化成了液体，鲜浓润滑而不腻，汤中的味道和营养完全渗出，富含钙、蛋白质、胶质及各种营养精华，滋补又养颜。最讨人喜的是不得不尝的镇店特色招牌菜——高人气红烧牛排锅。红烧牛排锅色重味浓，鲜美诱人。什锦白煮牛肉汤锅清爽滋补，雪白的牛肉汤，加上薄薄香嫩的牛肉片，与蘑菇蔬菜配搭，散发出的纯正肉香，牛骨汤头还可以无限续加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8753382" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8753382" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f57cfd_8fe5fe18f1e445d9840183956634ed17-1-450x450.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8753382" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f57cfd_8fe5fe18f1e445d9840183956634ed17-1-450x450.png" alt="红烧牛排锅。（商家提供）" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8753382" class="wp-caption-text">高人气红烧牛排锅。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>SUN NONG DAN位于韩国城6街的先农坛1号店，7天24小时营业不打烊，和圣盖博店一样，就餐高峰和周末时间几乎都会大排长龙。店主说70%的顾客都是中国人。</p>
<p>美味背后有着悠远的美食文化。先农坛现位于韩国首尔东大门外祭基洞。每年立春之后，朝鲜国王和文武百官在先农坛举行祭祀。先农祭结束后，将作为祭品的牛进行烹煮，将熬煮而成的先农汤赐宴百姓。煮出来的骨汤浓厚乳白，香滑不腻，流传到今天现亦叫雪浓汤。◇</p>
