<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2017芝加哥车展“慈善之夜” 2月10日晚举行</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年01月31日讯】（大纪元记者李清<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E5%8A%A0%E5%93%A5.md#1">芝加哥</a>报导）1月13日，芝加哥汽<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md#1">车展</a>（Chicago Auto Show）在McGrath Acura of Chicago车行举办“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%85%88%E5%96%84%E4%B9%8B%E5%A4%9C.md#1">慈善之夜</a>”媒体预览，数十家媒体前往品尝将在晚会上提供的部分美食和饮料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8761318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8761318" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Media-Preview-300x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8761318 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Media-Preview-300x400.jpg" alt="媒体品尝美食。（李清/大纪元）" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8761318" class="wp-caption-text">媒体品尝美食。（李清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2017年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E5%8A%A0%E5%93%A5.md#1">芝加哥</a>汽<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md#1">车展</a>日期为2月11日至2月20日。其“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%85%88%E5%96%84%E4%B9%8B%E5%A4%9C.md#1">慈善之夜</a>”预览将于2月10日晚7时至11时举行。“慈善之夜”每年门票收入超过250万美元，分别捐给芝加哥的18个慈善机构。芝加哥McGrath Lexus老板小迈克?麦格拉思（Mike McGrath, Jr.）鼓励大家到汽车展看看。</p>
<p>“慈善之夜”的参加者可以指定将购票收益指定捐给18个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%85%88%E5%96%84%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87.md#1">慈善组织</a>中的任何一个或全部，每张票价275美元，其中222美元可以作为慈善捐赠免税。还提供公司赠送套餐。</p>
<p>“慈善之夜”每年吸引约上万人，其间可抢先一睹新车，并尽情享用由20多家餐馆提供的精美食品和美酒。此外，展会现场的各种制造商展示会提供现场娱乐表演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8761315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8761315" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Prosciutto-Wrapped-Asparagus-300x225.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8761315 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Prosciutto-Wrapped-Asparagus-300x225.jpg" alt="Prosciutto Wrapped Asparagus。（李清/大纪元）" width="300" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8761315" class="wp-caption-text">Prosciutto Wrapped Asparagus。（李清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8761316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8761316" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Butter-Bella-Cookies-300x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8761316 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Butter-Bella-Cookies-300x400.jpg" alt="Butter Bella Cookies。（李清/大纪元）" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8761316" class="wp-caption-text">Butter Bella Cookies。（李清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每人还有机会参加抽奖，赢得一辆新车，奖品是：Acura TLX和Lexus NX。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8761502" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8761502" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Acura-TLX-300x402.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8761502 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Acura-TLX-300x402.png" alt="参加“慈善之夜”者有机会中大奖Acura TLX。（芝加哥汽车展官网）" width="300" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8761502" class="wp-caption-text">参加“慈善之夜”者有机会中大奖Acura TLX。（芝加哥汽车展官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>详情请上网：<ahref="http://www.chicagoautoshow.com/first-look-for-charity">http://www.chicagoautoshow.com/first-look-for-charity</a>。◇</p>
