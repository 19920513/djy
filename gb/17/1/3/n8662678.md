<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>女儿睡觉摆一字马 欧弟：果然是练武奇才</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/oudi-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>欧汉声（欧弟）资料照。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年01月04日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E5%BC%9F.md#1">欧弟</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B1%89%E5%A3%B0.md#1">欧汉声</a>）自去年当了爸爸后，不时在微博上传女儿Jojo的照片，和网友分享其成长点滴。3日晚，他又在微博晒出女儿“一字马”的熟睡萌照，并大赞“果然是个练武奇才”。</p>
<p>照片中，Jojo躺在摇床上睡得很香，粉红的脸蛋十分可爱，但更吸引人的亮点还是她那标准的“一字马”睡姿。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E5%BC%9F.md#1">欧弟</a>附言大赞：“这睡觉的姿势，果然是个练武奇才！”</p>
<p>照片引来网友纷纷围观并留言：“台下十年功啊！”“高难度睡姿！”“肯定是得真传，太可爱了！”</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_8662789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8662789" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/5e421e75jw1fbdsof5537j20qo0zkjue-300x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8662789 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/5e421e75jw1fbdsof5537j20qo0zkjue-300x400.jpg" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8662789" class="wp-caption-text">欧弟赞Jojo是个练武奇才。（欧弟微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据台媒报导，2014年，欧弟与重庆妹子郑云灿结婚，2015年12月喜得女儿Jojo。Jojo的大眼睛吸引了不少网友，称赞她像“俄罗斯娃娃”。如今才一岁多的Jojo人气快超过爸爸，欧弟笑说：“在网路没放她照片两天，就被粉丝骂到臭头！”看到微博中只有他的时候，粉丝就会说：“不想看你啊！Jojo呢？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8662792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8662792" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/5e421e75jw1f9ykfi1f3kj20qo0zk4a3-300x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8662792 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/5e421e75jw1f9ykfi1f3kj20qo0zk4a3-300x400.jpg" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8662792" class="wp-caption-text">Jojo美丽的大眼睛似在看乐谱。（欧弟微博）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8662800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8662800" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/5e421e75jw1fb19jk8xm0j20ku0px793-300x373.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8662800 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/5e421e75jw1fb19jk8xm0j20ku0px793-300x373.jpg" width="300" b="373" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8662800" class="wp-caption-text">Jojo的大眼睛融化粉丝的心。（欧弟微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据悉，欧弟是个重机（摩托车）迷，收藏多台重机，光是顶级品牌Ducati就有两台。去年在出席Ducati新车发布活动时，将女儿视为宝贝的他透露，自从Jojo出生后就不再追求速度、不再做危险的事，“骑快一点就想到Jojo的脸”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8662808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8662808" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/5e421e75jw1faky1r94b6j20zk0nodla-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8662808 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/5e421e75jw1faky1r94b6j20zk0nodla-450x300.jpg" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8662808" class="wp-caption-text">欧弟幸福一家。（欧弟微博）</figcaption></figure>
