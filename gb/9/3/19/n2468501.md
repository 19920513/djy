<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第286课</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元3月19日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa286.mp3 >(MP3下载)</a></center>	</p>
<p>李华和Larry一起开车去参加人材招聘会。今天我们要学习两个常用语： butterflies in one&#8217;s stomach和go overboard.</p>
<p>LH: Larry, 我心里特别紧张。你怎么样？</p>
<p>LL: Yeah, I have to admit that I&#8217;m a bit nervous as well. Every time I have to go to one of these events, I get butterflies in my stomach.</p>
<p>LH: 肚子里面有蝴蝶？真有意思。你不是说真的吧！</p>
<p>LL: The expression &#8216;butterflies in my stomach&#8217; describes the feeling you get in your stomach when you are nervous. That fluttering feeling that can make your stomach feel a bit unsettled is a result of such nervousness.</p>
<p>LH: 这种说法真形象。就是形容心里紧张，七上八下，好像在打鼓。Well, Larry, I&#8217;ve definitely got butterflies in my stomach right now!</p>
<p>LL: I&#8217;d tell you not to worry, but I&#8217;m worrying myself. Ok, let&#8217;s take a deep breath, exit the car, and go into the job fair!</p>
<p>(Sound effect &#8211; Inside the job fair building)</p>
<p>LH: Wow，你看，这次来参加招聘的公司还真不少呢！看来我们有机会。可我怎么好像越来越紧张了呢？</p>
<p>LL: Me too! Let&#8217;s start off together, and then we&#8217;ll split up if we see other things that fit our taste. Did you bring your resume?</p>
<p>LH: 我带了三十份简历。你觉得够不够？</p>
<p>LL: I brought 20 resumes, so I think you definitely have enough. But, are you mentally prepared to make a good impression? You don&#8217;t want to show that you&#8217;ve got butterflies in your stomach.</p>
<p>LH: 我知道。虽然心里七上八下的，可表面上还得装得镇定自若。哎呀，你怎么紧张得手都在抖啊。别抖，你看，简历撒了一地。</p>
<p>LL: (Flushed) Well, I guess that&#8217;s a fast way to get my name and experience out there! Thanks for helping me pick these up. Ok, so how do I look?</p>
<p>LH: 你看起来特专业。来，放松点，喝口水，深呼吸，We need to get those butterflies out of our stomach.</p>
<p>LL: Thanks! You look fine, too. Let&#8217;s go to the first company booth over there!</p>
<p>******</p>
<p>LH: 这些人真有礼貌。他们说&#8221;keep in touch&#8221;保持联络，是不是有希望啊？</p>
<p>LL: I&#8217;m not sure, Lihua. You may have gone overboard.</p>
<p>LH: Gone overboard? 这是什么意思啊！</p>
<p>LL: I mean &#8211; you may have said and done too much to them. &#8216;Going overboard&#8217; means putting too much effort or action into something.</p>
<p>LH: 我想起来了，go overboard不是从船上掉下去的意思吗？</p>
<p>LL: It stems from the meaning of when a person walked too far on the edge of a ship &#8211; so far that they fall off of the boat and into the water!</p>
<p>LH: 你意思是说我表现得很差吗？</p>
<p>LL: No, I mean that you said and did too much. Perhaps you can keep your answers shorter &#8211; more direct. And how many resumes did you give them?</p>
<p>LH: 我怕他们u会丢，所以给了他们一人两份。</p>
<p>LL: See &#8211; one resume is enough. What you did is called &#8216;going overboard.&#8217; Understand?</p>
<p>LH: 明白了，我以后一定注意分寸，除了工作经验，什么都不提。</p>
<p>LL: Yes! That&#8217;s it! See, the first booth, you talked about your family and even your dog!</p>
<p>LH: Hey! 我可不许你说我狗宝宝的坏话。</p>
<p>LL: Ok. Watch me on this one. I&#8217;ll try not to go overboard, so you can watch and learn, Li Hua.</p>
<p>LH: 好，我就在这观摩。Good luck! You need it. Haha</p>
<p>LL: Hi there, my name&#8217;s Larry. I&#8217;ve heard a lot about your company and&#8230; (声音变弱)</p>
<p>LH: 这么快！你留下简历了吗？</p>
<p>LL: Ah! I forgot! (to booth) I&#8217;m sorry, here&#8217;s my resume, sir.</p>
<p>LH: So, did you go overboard? (laughing)</p>
<p>LL: Funny, Li Hua. Very funny!</p>
<p>LH: 你连名字都忘了告诉人家。他们谈到你，肯定会说，“就是那个不留姓名、不留简历的神秘人”。</p>
<p>LL: Enough about me! I failed miserably. Let&#8217;s see how you do this time. Let&#8217;s see if you go overboard.</p>
<p>LH: 一、二、三(数简历)</p>
<p>LL: Lihua&#8230;Why are you taking so many resumes with you?</p>
<p>LH: 我宁可go overboard, 我也不愿意忘记递简历。Wish me luck!</p>
<p>今天李华学了两个常用语。一个是butterflies in one&#8217;s stomach, 意思是心里紧张，七上八下。另外一个是go overboard，意思是做事过分，走极端。<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
