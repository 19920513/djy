<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/09/909050949471469-450x340.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />原料：<br />鸡中翅6只、洋葱1/4个、生姜4片。</p>
<p>调料：<br />白糖1小杓、蜜糖1汤匙、生抽1汤匙、蚝油1汤匙、清水1/3碗。</p>
<p>做法：<br />1、鸡翅洗干净，洋葱切碎；<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/09/909050949491469-450x340.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>

<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/09/909050949481469-450x342.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />3、加热小奶锅，放少量油，爆香洋葱；<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/09/909050949501469-450x340.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />4、放进鸡翅，加入1汤匙的蚝油；<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/09/909050949511469-450x340.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />5、加入1/3碗清水；<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/09/909051144011469-450x342.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />6、大火煮开后，中火煮8分钟，小火焖2分钟；<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/09/909050951191469-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
