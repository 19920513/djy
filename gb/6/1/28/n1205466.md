<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第160课</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元1月28日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa160.mp3>MP3下载</a><br /><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa160.rm>RealPlayer下载</a></center><br />Larry已经开始在一家公司做暑期工作。他正在向李华抱怨工作上的问题。今天李华会学到两个常用语：swamped和to get axed。</p>
<p>LL: I&#8217;ve only been working there two days and I&#8217;m already totally swamped with work! It&#8217;s unbelievable!</p>
<p>LH: Larry，你说swamped with work，是不是说你不知道怎么做这份工作？</p>
<p>LL: No, I know how to do the work. It&#8217;s just that there is too much of it. To be swamped means to have too much work to do.</p>
<p>LH: 噢，我懂了，swamp一般是沼泽地的意思，你说swamped with work就是工作太多，好象陷进了沼泽地一样，让你觉得喘不过气。那他们到底给你什么样的工作？</p>
<p>LL: It&#8217;s all relatively simple stuff like filing, typing, copying. It&#8217;s just that there are piles of it and everyone wants it finished right now.</p>
<p>LH: 啊！归档、打字、复印都是很简单的事，可要是办公室每个人都要你做这些事，每个人都要你马上给他做，这确实难以对付。Larry，也许你该建议他们再多雇个人来帮你啊！</p>
<p>LL: I doubt that. Besides, I don&#8217;t want them to think I&#8217;m lazy. I guess I just have to accept the fact that I&#8217;ll be swamped with work for the rest of the summer.</p>
<p>LH: 真是的。一个暑假就陷在这些繁琐的工作里，太没劲了。我只想在暑假里轻松轻松。</p>
<p>LL: I was hoping to relax a little, too, but it doesn&#8217;t look as though that&#8217;s going to happen.</p>
<p>LH: 不过Larry，虽然你白天在公司里工作很忙，可是你晚上回到家可以轻松一下啦，因为你不必像学期当中忙于写作业或交报告！</p>
<p>LL: That&#8217;s true, but I&#8217;m so tired by the time I get home from being swamped all day that I don&#8217;t have the energy to do anything fun.</p>
<p>LH: 这你就跟我不一样了。我呀，再累也没有关系，只要一听到玩，我就精神百倍了。不过，Larry，我真希望能帮你一点忙。</p>
<p>LL: Oh, it&#8217;s not your fault, Li Hua. I&#8217;m sorry for complaining like this.</p>
<p>******</p>
<p>LH: 嗨！Larry！你的暑期工作现在怎么样了！还是很忙吗？</p>
<p>LL: It&#8217;s even worse than ever! The management just axed one of the other employees and now they&#8217;re giving me all of his work!</p>
<p>LH: Ax不是斧头吗？你说你公司把那个雇员怎么啦？Axed？不是把他砍了吧？听起来好吓人的！</p>
<p>LL: To ax someone is to fire them. The other guy was really unreliable so management decided to ax him.</p>
<p>LH: 噢，我明白了，to ax someone就是把某人开除的意思。你真倒霉。公司把那个不可靠的人开除了，他的工作也就落在你头上。你们公司有没有考虑雇用新人来接替他的工作？</p>
<p>LL: I have no idea. In the mean time, I have so much work that I&#8217;m running around all day long.</p>
<p>LH: 你真可伶，每天忙得晕头转向的。也许你可以问问他们什么时候会雇用一个新人啊？</p>
<p>LL: I want to ask, but I&#8217;m afraid of getting axed, too.</p>
<p>LH: 你不过问问而已嘛！他们为什么要开除你呢？</p>
<p>LL: Well, I guess they wouldn&#8217;t fire me, because I&#8217;ve been doing a good job although I&#8217;m swamped with work. I just don&#8217;t want them to think that I&#8217;m lazy or that I&#8217;m complaining.</p>
<p>LH: 我肯定他们不会开除你。他们也不傻，知道你做很多工作，而且还做得很好。你要不说，他们绝对不会再顾一个人。这样不是省钱嘛！</p>
<p>LL: I guess you are right. I&#8217;m more likely to get axed for messing up rather than telling them I&#8217;m overworked.</p>
<p>LH: 没错。老老实实地告诉他们你太忙，总比你忙不过来，把事情搞砸了要好。</p>
<p>LL：Li Hua, you&#8217;re so smart!</p>
<p>今天李华学到两个常用语。第一个是swamped，是指陷入大量工作，或其他什么事。另一个常用语是to get axed，就是被开除的意思。 </p>
