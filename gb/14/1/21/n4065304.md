<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：中日窗花艺术展 三秒钟剪出樱花</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2014年01月21日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾台北报导）国立<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%88%B6%E7%BA%AA%E5%BF%B5%E9%A6%86.md#1">国父纪念馆</a>于1月21日至2月9日在农历年节期间举办“中日联合剪绫布（纸）艺术展”。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%89%AA%E7%BA%B8.md#1">剪纸</a>俗称“窗花”，在中国旧时的农业社会中占有相当地位，无论婚丧喜庆都被广泛的应用，尤其在农历过年时，家家户户在整理房舍后并贴上各式吉祥剪纸图案，增添年节气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5690936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690936" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211525222378-600x564.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690936" class="wp-caption-text">国宝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%89%AA%E7%BA%B8.md#1">剪纸</a>大师<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E7%84%95%E7%AB%A0.md#1">李焕章</a>剪纸-马。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%88%B6%E7%BA%AA%E5%BF%B5%E9%A6%86.md#1">国父纪念馆</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p><B><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E7%84%95%E7%AB%A0.md#1">李焕章</a>（1925-）</B></p>
<p>国宝剪纸大师李焕章生于山东省临沂县，是家中独子，8岁时读私塾，跟着一位清朝的秀才学习三字经、大学、中庸、论语、孟子、诗经，从小打下厚实的国学底子。他10岁时父亲过世，母亲卖豆腐含辛茹苦扶养他。1948年国共内战，母亲含泪忍悲，嘱其逃亡保命成为流亡学生，1949年随国民政府离开大陆投身军旅10年。其后自台北师范学校毕业，任教台北市中正国小、兼任辅导组长数十年间，将家乡习得的剪纸艺术发扬创新，大力推广剪纸艺术教学，甚至有远从日本来台向其取经的学生。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5690943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690943" style="width: 590px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211525282378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690943" class="wp-caption-text">国宝剪纸大师李焕章剪纸-福马。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure><br />[[3]]<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5690948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690948" style="width: 348px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211523552378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690948" class="wp-caption-text">国宝剪纸大师李焕章剪纸-百寿图。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>1989年李焕章因推展社会教育贡献良多获颁教育部“教育文化奖章”；1991年更再度获颁教育部“民族艺术薪传奖－传统工艺：剪纸”。台北市圆山儿童育乐中心更为其特别成立传统艺术区剪纸艺术馆“焕章馆”。2011年再入选新北市“传统艺术师”。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5690952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690952" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211524572378-600x145.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690952" class="wp-caption-text">剪纸大师李焕章剪纸-万马奔腾。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5690958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690958" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211523442378-600x188.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690958" class="wp-caption-text">国宝剪纸大师李焕章剪纸-老鼠娶亲。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5690962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690962" style="width: 436px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211523292378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690962" class="wp-caption-text">国宝剪纸大师李焕章剪纸-闘茶图。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>国父纪念馆为欢庆马年来临，特别规划“中日联合剪绫布（纸）艺术展”，邀请李焕章进行师生作品联展。为让大家了解剪纸艺术微妙之创作过程，将在22日至24日下午2时至4时举办李焕章师生剪纸创作分享。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5690965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690965" style="width: 354px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211524492378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690965" class="wp-caption-text">国宝剪纸大师李焕章剪纸-报喜信。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5690971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690971" style="width: 495px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211525052378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690971" class="wp-caption-text">国宝剪纸大师李焕章剪纸-花鸟生肖画册。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure><br />[[10]]<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5690978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690978" style="width: 270px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211524242378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690978" class="wp-caption-text">国宝剪纸大师李焕章剪纸-牡丹富贵。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure><br />[[12]]<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5690982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690982" style="width: 268px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211524402378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690982" class="wp-caption-text">国宝剪纸大师李焕章剪纸-梅花送香。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>如今高龄88岁的李焕章身体硬朗，视力超好，手也不抖，依然技艺精湛。他在脑中构图、无需草稿，即能剪出变化多端图样，令人惊艳与赞叹。不管图案多细多小，全难不倒他，平均一秒钟剪出星星，三秒钟剪出樱花，他沉浸在剪纸世界数十寒暑，拿起彩纸和剪刀，创意与活力不输年轻人，国父纪念馆欢迎各界人士踊跃前往观赏。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5690985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5690985" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/01/1401211523382378-600x143.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5690985" class="wp-caption-text">国宝剪纸大师李焕章剪纸-琴棋书画。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
