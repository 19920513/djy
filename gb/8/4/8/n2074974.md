<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蜜汁烤排骨的做法</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>材料：<br />猪小排500克、红薯1只、蒜2瓣（剁茸）、姜末20克、酱油、五香粉、糖、李锦记风味豆豉酱（如果没有用其它豆豉酱也行）1大匙、麦芽糖2大匙、水2大匙</p>
<p>做法：<br />1，用刀在猪排上划上十字，深及骨头<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/04/804080858131469.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />2，将蒜茸、姜末、酱油、五香粉、糖、李锦记风味豆豉酱抹匀在排骨表面，密封放入冰箱中腌制5小时以上<br />（可提前一晚腌制）<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/04/804080858141469.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />3，红薯去皮洗净切厚片，刷油铺在烤盘底部，将腌制好的排骨放在红薯片上<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/04/804080858151469.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />4，用锡纸盖好排骨，放入180度烤箱中上下火烤60分钟左右</p>
<p>5，取一只小锅，将麦芽糖、水、1匙酱油、适量五香粉用小火煮成糖汁待用<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/04/804080858161469.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />6，将烤好的排骨取出，除去锡纸，将糖汁用刷子刷在排骨表面<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/04/804080859351469.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />7，将排骨放入烤箱200度，用上火再烤10分钟左右（不盖锡纸)</p>
<p>8，取一只盘，将米饭用容器盛好倒扣在盘中央（我将红薯切成小块和米饭同煮成红薯米饭）<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/04/804080859361469.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />9，将烤好的排骨取适量放在米饭上，将烤盘底的红薯片放在排骨上<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/04/804080859371469.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />10，将剩余的糖汁浇在排骨上即可<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/04/804080859381469.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />猪排骨提供人体生理活动必需的优质蛋白质、脂肪，尤其是丰富的钙质可维护骨骼健康。猪排骨具有滋阴润燥、益精补血的功效；适宜于气血不足，阴虚纳差者。</p>
<p>麦芽糖又称糯米糖，它由小麦和糯米制成，香甜可口，营养丰富，具有健胃消食等功效，是老少皆宜的食品。</p>
<p>(来源:美食中国网)</p>
