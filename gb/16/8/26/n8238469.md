<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>六大名厨主理 美网公开赛美食登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/9a4b93d74c9ab6d6425bfbafb8740c8e-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>用缅因州龙虾做的龙虾卷。 (李大宇/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年08月26日讯】（大纪元特约记者李大宇<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>报导）即将开打的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E7%BD%91%E7%90%83%E5%85%AC%E5%BC%80%E8%B5%9B.md#1">美国网球公开赛</a>，观众不止会看到紧张刺激的比赛，还能品尝到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8D%E5%8E%A8.md#1">名厨</a>制作的美味料理。周四（8月25日），至少<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AD%E4%BD%8D.md#1">六位</a>顶尖厨艺高手亮相公开赛举办地——国家网球中心，为来宾介绍场馆内出售的精致美味。</p>
<figure id="8238475" aria-describedby="caption-8238475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/afd13f36a198f7328480a5b5ff8f0e56-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/afd13f36a198f7328480a5b5ff8f0e56-450x337.jpg" alt="届时有不同国家、口味的美食可供选择。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-8238475" class="wp-caption-text">届时有不同国家、口味的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>可供选择。(李大宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>美网公开赛预计将吸引超过70万人前来观看比赛，为了满足这一庞大需求，国家网球中心内设置了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>村，囊括了五家餐馆的250种美味，由60处摊位和100个食物贩售窗口共同呈现。</p>
<figure id="8238474" aria-describedby="caption-8238474" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/ab6333b9b8eab487c1f6690aa8b823c0-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/ab6333b9b8eab487c1f6690aa8b823c0-450x337.jpg" alt="精心制作的甜点。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-8238474" class="wp-caption-text">精心制作的甜点。(李大宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>用缅因州肥龙虾做成的三明治，酥软多汁的德州炸鸡柳，夹满炸鸡和培根的香喷喷汉堡，再配上被称为“世界最佳口感伏特加”的法国灰雁鸡尾酒，这种享受不次于在一家高档餐厅，而这些您却都可以在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E7%BD%91%E7%90%83%E5%85%AC%E5%BC%80%E8%B5%9B.md#1">美国网球公开赛</a>的场地——国家网球中心品尝到。</p>
<figure id="8238473" aria-describedby="caption-8238473" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/331e6c71191c428fa18aee46bea24dd4-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/331e6c71191c428fa18aee46bea24dd4-450x337.jpg" alt="夹满炸鸡和培根的香喷喷汉堡。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-8238473" class="wp-caption-text">夹满炸鸡和培根的香喷喷汉堡。(李大宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了让来宾品尝到美味料理，公开赛主办方特别请来多位名师掌厨，让观众饱眼福的同时，也能兼顾口上的享受，包括：百福餐饮集团创始人兼<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8D%E5%8E%A8.md#1">名厨</a>David Chang、《美国铁人料理》节目知名嘉宾和名厨David Burke等。</p>
<figure id="8238472" aria-describedby="caption-8238472" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/3484df1ce7ba5d142f602ab7da284990-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/3484df1ce7ba5d142f602ab7da284990-450x338.jpg" alt="主办方邀请名厨为球迷们设计菜单，制作美食。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-8238472" class="wp-caption-text">主办方邀请名厨为球迷们设计菜单，制作美食。(李大宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>比赛场馆既提供了精致挑剔的法国餐，也有实惠、香郁的美国饮食，还有迎合东方人口味的韩餐和日式风格的料理等。◇</p>
