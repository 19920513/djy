<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：在德国应品尝的29道美食（下）</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年08月06日讯】德国人的饮食可能不太清淡，却是货真价实的好东西。虽然各色香肠（wurst）令人眼花缭乱，还是有很多其它美食可选（多数不适合素食者），比如淋肉汁的肉丸、各种制法的土豆和各式甜点。下面就为您逐一呈现。</p>
<p>（接<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/16/8/5/n8173401.md#1" target="_blank">上文</a>）</p>
<p><strong>21. 如其名，意面冰淇淋（spaghettieis）看似一盘番茄意面，但它确实只是淋了果酱的香草冰淇淋。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173566" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051832212669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173566 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051832212669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173566" class="wp-caption-text">(Christian Cable/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>22. 尽管来自土耳其，旋转烤肉（d?ner kebab）是德国常见的街食，吃法是在口袋面包（皮塔饼）中装满烤肉、蔬菜和酱汁。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173567" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051927332669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173567 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051927332669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173567" class="wp-caption-text">(Alex Kehr/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>23. 德国香煎马铃薯（bratkartoffeln）是加培根和洋葱末的煎土豆片。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173568" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051832182669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173568 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051832182669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173568" class="wp-caption-text">(Oliver Hallmann/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>24. 半鸡（halbes hendl）是慕尼黑啤酒节期间超受欢迎的菜品，但其实在德国各地全年都能吃到。烤鸡外皮超香脆，里面则超级多汁。当然你也可以点一整只。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173569" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051718062669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173569 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051718062669-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173569" class="wp-caption-text">(Markus Jakobs/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>25. 肉桂星（zimtsterne）是德国人常吃的圣诞饼干，味道像姜饼，由杏仁、桂皮、生姜和糖衣制成。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173570" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051832252669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173570 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051832252669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173570" class="wp-caption-text">(pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>26. 酸味炖牛肉（Sauerbraten）是牛肉加醋和大量调味料的德式烧锅。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173576" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051852112669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173576 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051852112669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173576" class="wp-caption-text">(Jeremy Keith/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>27. Frikadelle是用猪肉馅、牛肉馅和碎洋葱制成的肉饼，常夹在圆面包里吃。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173585" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051843492669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173585 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051843492669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173585" class="wp-caption-text">(Schw?bin， Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>28. 在德国北部，随处可以吃到鱼肉三明治（fischbr?tchen），是以炸鱼或腌鱼加酱汁和蔬菜夹在圆面包里。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173589" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051852152669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173589 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051852152669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173589" class="wp-caption-text">(Lord Jim/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>29. 著名的年轮蛋糕（baumkuchen），是将一层薄薄的蛋奶面糊刷上炙叉烤制，重复多次，烤制出一圈圈的效果。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173592" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051852082669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173592 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051852082669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173592" class="wp-caption-text">(Klaas Brumann/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
