<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Apple Watch series 2 新品发表</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年09月07日讯】(大纪元记者王婷仪纽约报导)</p>
<p>第一代的?Apple Watch 推出后销售不如预期，9/07(三)苹果新品发布会，第二代的Apple Watch会与iphone 7 同步登场。</p>
<p>就在9/07(三)今天的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%93%81%E5%8F%91%E8%A1%A8.md#1">新品发表</a>会中提到Apple Watch 的推出震动了手表界，现今已是在手表界销量中的第二名，仅次于Rolex，同时也是Smart Watch的第一名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8277353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8277353" style="width: 1625px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/apple-watch.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8277353 size-full" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/apple-watch.jpg" alt="Apple Watch series 2新品发表" width="1625" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8277353" class="wp-caption-text">Apple Watch series 2<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%93%81%E5%8F%91%E8%A1%A8.md#1">新品发表</a>(苹果官网截图)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Apple Watch series2推出的新功能</p>
<h2>1.多核心处理器，下载应用软体及导航作业系统。</h2>
<p>多核心处理器将速度提升了50%，CPU也增强，让速度再快了2倍。让所有APP操作上都可以非常顺畅。</p>
<h2>2. 2x brighter显示增强</h2>
<p>2倍的白，有1000的nits在黑暗处或强光处都可以清晰地看到画面显示，不会看不清任何讯息。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2>3.<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E6%B0%B4.md#1">防水</a>功能增强</h2>
<p>这次改变的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E6%B0%B4.md#1">防水</a>装置，让原本最容易进水的声音孔部分进行加强，Apple Watch series 2可入水 50 m，可带着手表游泳运动等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8277354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8277354" style="width: 1882px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/apple-watch2.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8277354 size-full" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/apple-watch2.jpg" alt="Apple Watch series 2新品发表" width="1882" b="856" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8277354" class="wp-caption-text">Apple Watch series 2新品发表(苹果官网截图)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>4.加入了全球卫星定位系统（GPS），提供更精准的健身追踪。</h2>
<p>新的功能与ViewRanger合作,在散步爬山等都不必担心，结合卫星定位系统，告诉你行走路径，也可以综观整体路径。</p>
<h2>5.Watch OS3</h2>
<p>*Messenger推出动态的Sticker与手写的功能(中英文皆支持)<br />
*Activity活动看板可以定时看活动资讯也可以分享给朋友<br />
*Breathe呼吸量测试，除了心跳之外新增加的功能<br />
*SOS呼救系统，遇到紧急危险时使用<br />
*Apple Watch新推出了Pokemon go的App，将可以在Apple Watch 上看到路上遇到的怪，就不用无时无刻拿着手机走路了。<br />
*除了以往的记步系统外，新加入游泳专用的纪录系统</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2>6.与Nike合作</h2>
<p>与运动厂牌nike和做推出几款最新表带，极具运动风格的色彩与款式，还有Nike专属的life style版本，内有运动的提示功能与全新的Nike专属介面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8277357" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8277357" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/apple-watch5-300x305.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8277357 size-small" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/apple-watch5-300x305.jpg" alt="Apple Watch series 2新品发表-nike款" width="300" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8277357" class="wp-caption-text">Apple Watch series 2新品发表-nike款(苹果官网截图)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8277356" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8277356" style="width: 344px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/apple-watch4-300x265.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8277356 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/apple-watch4-300x265.jpg" alt="Apple Watch series 2新品发表-nike款" width="344" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8277356" class="wp-caption-text">Apple Watch series 2新品发表-nike款(苹果官网截图)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>7.与HERMES合作</h2>
<p>推出三款皮制表带，富有个性的皮制表带，有双扣款与单扣款，相信各位会喜欢。</p>
