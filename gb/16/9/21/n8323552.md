<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：中国功夫茶茶艺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105072343-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年09月21日讯】（大纪元记者姜斌综合报导）中国茶文化，内涵博大精深，内容丰富多彩，而茶艺则是其中不可或缺的重要部分。精湛的茶艺不仅可以让我们品尝到馥郁甘醇的茶汤，同时也带给我们赏心悦目的体验。</p>
<p>下面是茶艺师宁静女士的“中国功夫茶茶艺表演”的精彩瞬间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8323917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323917" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211104582343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323917" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211104582343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323917" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8323930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211106332343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323930" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211106332343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323930" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>孟臣沐淋</strong>：用开水烫壶温杯，提高茶具的温度，为泡茶做好准备。惠孟臣是明代的制壶名家，以擅制小壶闻名，作品很适合泡功夫茶，因此后世把泡功夫茶用的小紫砂壶也称为“孟臣壶”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8323932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211414302343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323932" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211414302343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323932" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>乌龙入宫</strong>：将适量茶叶拨入紫砂壶。“乌龙”即乌龙茶，也用来泛指茶叶。紫砂壶是茶叶的“宫殿”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8323924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323924" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105552343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323924" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105552343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323924" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8323918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323918" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105182343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323918" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105182343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323918" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>悬壶高冲</strong>：用沸水高冲，使茶叶在壶中翻滚展开，充分激发茶香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8323919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323919" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105292343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323919" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105292343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323919" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>玉液移壶</strong>：将紫砂壶中泡好的茶汤倒入公道杯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8323969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323969" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211447092343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323969" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211447092343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323969" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>分承甘露</strong>：将公道杯中的茶汤均匀地倒入闻香杯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8323926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323926" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211106042343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323926" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211106042343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323926" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>高屋建瓴</strong>：将品茗杯盖在闻香杯上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8323920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105372343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323920" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105372343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323920" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>物换星移</strong>：将盖在一起的杯组悬空翻转，并且要滴水不漏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8323923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323923" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105462343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323923" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211105462343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323923" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>敬奉香茗</strong>：将翻转后的杯组放在托盘上，敬奉客人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8323928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211106142343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323928" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211106142343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323928" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8323931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211414202343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323931" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211414202343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323931" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>空谷幽兰</strong>：将闻香杯轻轻提起，细细品味茶之幽香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8323929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8323929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211106232343.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8323929" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211106232343-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8323929" class="wp-caption-text">中国功夫茶茶艺表演。（姜斌/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>初品奇茗</strong>：以“三龙护鼎”手法（拇指和食指端起品茗杯，中指在下托住杯底，三指似三条龙）执杯，慢慢品尝茶汤滋味。</p>
