<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>87岁瘫痪骑手重返马背 临终愿望成真</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年10月19日讯】来自<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%B7%E5%85%B0.md#1">荷兰</a>的87岁雅各斯（Jacobs）女士患有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%95%E9%87%91%E6%A3%AE%E6%B0%8F.md#1">帕金森氏</a>症，长年卧病在床，且身体因为帕金森导致瘫痪无法行走。看起来这一生已经走到了尽头，但她心中一直有个深刻的愿望，那就是能再骑一次马，原来雅各斯女士一生几乎都在马背上度过，担任过各种表演的马术骑手，她想要重温当年的美好时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8410410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8410410" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/6361124394549501361654160-600x388.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8410410 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/6361124394549501361654160-600x388.jpg" alt="6361124394549501361654160" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8410410" class="wp-caption-text">年轻时的雅各斯，英姿焕发的骑着马做各种高难度动作。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这看似难以实现的愿望终于看到希望，两个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%85%88%E5%96%84%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87.md#1">慈善组织</a>Care Group和Green Cross决定帮助老奶奶完成心愿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8410409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8410409" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/6361124393819611348484043-600x411.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8410409 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/6361124393819611348484043-600x411.jpg" alt="6361124393819611348484043" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8410409" class="wp-caption-text">在这特别的一天，雅各斯女士被带到马厩，看看她等会要骑的马。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>以往<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%85%88%E5%96%84%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87.md#1">慈善组织</a>帮助实现的愿望大多是带老人去见孙子，这次可说是相当不简单的任务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8410413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8410413" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/6361124401390353018724929-600x437.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8410413 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/6361124401390353018724929-600x437.jpg" alt="6361124401390353018724929" width="600" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8410413" class="wp-caption-text">雅各斯女士因为疾病几乎不能移动或是说话，她被带到专门从事马辅助治疗的机构，这里专门治疗有特殊需要的人。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8410411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8410411" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/6361124395594920328106199-600x509.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8410411 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/6361124395594920328106199-600x509.jpg" alt="6361124395594920328106199" width="600" b="509" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8410411" class="wp-caption-text">雅各斯女士被运上马车。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们专门为雅各斯女士做了一辆敞篷马车，有两匹马和一个遮阳棚，上面放了舒适的被单，驾驶员用吊索将老太太运上马车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8410412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8410412" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/6361124398643331179830077-600x475.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8410412 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/6361124398643331179830077-600x475.jpg" alt="6361124398643331179830077" width="600" b="475" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8410412" class="wp-caption-text">雅各斯女士的儿子也在她身边，握着母亲的手向她解释接下来会由马载着她走。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>儿子Jan向记者表示：“马儿一直对母亲来说很重要，光是看着马就让她充满喜悦，从她小时候开始就是了。直到帕金森症夺走她最爱的事物，她常常坐在轮椅上一整天。”</p>
