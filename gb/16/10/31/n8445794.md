<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加西台湾艺术家举办“美的惊艳”画展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/DSC_3309-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展，现场嘉宾与26名艺术家合影。（邱晨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年10月31日讯】（<span lang="ZH-TW">记者邱晨温哥华报导）</span><span lang="ZH-TW">星期六（</span>10<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>29<span lang="ZH-TW">日），<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E8%A5%BF%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%AE%B6%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">加西台湾艺术家协会</a>，在本拿比丽晶广场中信中心举办“美的惊艳”画展，30</span><span lang="ZH-TW">名会员的油画、水彩、粉彩、压克力、国画等48作品，呈现出每一位艺术家的创意。画展吸引了众多嘉宾与艺术爱好者。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8447498" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8447498" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_142809_HDR-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8447498 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_142809_HDR-450x253.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展，经文处刘汉清副处长赞赏画展与艺术家协会起到的好传统。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8447498" class="wp-caption-text">图：<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E8%A5%BF%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%AE%B6%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">加西台湾艺术家协会</a>“美的惊艳”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%94%E5%B1%95.md#1">联展</a>，经文处<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E6%B1%89%E6%B8%85.md#1">刘汉清</a>副处长赞赏画展与艺术家协会起到的好传统。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8447496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8447496" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_142533_HDR-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8447496 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_142533_HDR-450x253.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展，廖武藏会长分享画展的美妙。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8447496" class="wp-caption-text">图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%94%E5%B1%95.md#1">联展</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%96%E6%AD%A6%E8%97%8F.md#1">廖武藏</a>会长分享画展的美妙。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">驻温哥华台北经济办事处<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E6%B1%89%E6%B8%85.md#1">刘汉清</a>副处长伉俪，侨务副主任黄丽萱、本拿比省议员候选人陈苇蓁（</span>Katrina Chen<span lang="ZH-TW">）及多位侨界领袖到场支持。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8447506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8447506" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/DSC_3273-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8447506 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/DSC_3273-450x299.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展，开幕式由叶宪年老师主持。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8447506" class="wp-caption-text">图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展，开幕式由叶宪年老师主持。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8447495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8447495" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/DSC_3309-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8447495 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/DSC_3309-450x299.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展，现场嘉宾与26名艺术家合影。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8447495" class="wp-caption-text">图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展，现场嘉宾与26名艺术家合影。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">刘副处长表示，他从艺术家学到很多，也期盼开发出自己潜在的艺术天赋。他称赞加西台湾艺术家协会成立</span>11<span lang="ZH-TW">年来，起到了集结台湾艺术家力量的好作用，是一个“好传统”，本次画展展现题材丰富，色彩斑斓，令人赞叹。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">加西台湾艺术家协会现任会长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%96%E6%AD%A6%E8%97%8F.md#1">廖武藏</a>表示，每年都举办会员联展，每年都有更好、更有深度的作品，“令人百看不厌”。他介绍道，今年联展以“美的惊艳”为主题，不仅颜色斑斓漂亮，内涵也是绚丽多彩的。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8447569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8447569" style="width: 225px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_150833_HDR-225x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8447569 size-medium_vertical" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_150833_HDR-225x400.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展画作。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="225" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8447569" class="wp-caption-text">图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展画作。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8447567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8447567" style="width: 225px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_150843_HDR-225x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8447567 size-medium_vertical" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_150843_HDR-225x400.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展画作。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="225" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8447567" class="wp-caption-text">图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展画作。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8447505" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8447505" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/DSC_3270-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8447505 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/DSC_3270-450x299.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展，创会会长卢月铅在她的三幅参赞画作前留影。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8447505" class="wp-caption-text">图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展，创会会长卢月铅在她的三幅参赞画作前留影。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">开幕式主持人、前协会副会长叶宪年介绍说，如果卢月铅没有在</span>2005<span lang="ZH-TW">年创建此协会，也就没有今天的联展。她鼓励大家享受艺术作品的美艳时，感受大自然的奇妙，同时享受小提琴演奏与美味佳肴。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">参加本次联展的艺术家有：卢月铅、叶宪年、张丽娜、曲国仲、廖武藏、廖纯如、汪咏絮、刘长富，等等。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8447565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8447565" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_151705_HDR-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8447565 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_151705_HDR-450x253.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展画作。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8447565" class="wp-caption-text">图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展画作。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8480550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8480550" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/TW-artists-association-exhibits-161029-11-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8480550 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/TW-artists-association-exhibits-161029-11-450x253.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展画作。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8480550" class="wp-caption-text">图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展画作。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p><span lang="ZH-TW">创会会长卢月铅是大师级，她明年将举办第</span>50<span lang="ZH-TW">次个展，而且负责白家画廊</span>22<span lang="ZH-TW">年，劳苦功高。卢月铅还培育众多弟子，桃李满天下，像叶宪年、房世伶等。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">张丽娜是该艺术家协会前会长，她以《枫红》一画迎接联展来宾，曾经师从阳云与周士心等大师，现任台湾客家会会长；叶宪年称自己是书法家曲国仲的粉丝；赵行方不仅绘画出色，他的烹饪更技艺超群。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8447503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8447503" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_143949_HDR-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8447503 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/20161029_143949_HDR-450x253.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展现场，廖武藏会长千金廖俐伶演奏小提琴曲《望春风》。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8447503" class="wp-caption-text">图：加西台湾艺术家协会“美的惊艳”联展现场，廖武藏会长千金廖俐伶演奏小提琴曲《望春风》。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">廖武藏会长千金、蜚声国际小提琴家女儿廖俐伶，以一曲《望春风》独奏，深深感动着现场来宾与侨胞，联展开幕式也奉献给大家，视觉、听觉与味觉的三重享受，让嘉宾满载而归。</span>◇</p>
