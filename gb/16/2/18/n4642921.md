<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新剧上档 邱凯伟李维维等四主演K歌同乐</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年02月18日讯】台湾三立、东森八点接档华剧《大人情歌》将于下周三（24日）晚间八点首播，昨（17日）该剧在台北某KTV举办“K歌同乐会”，主要演员<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B1%E5%87%AF%E4%BC%9F.md#1">邱凯伟</a>（Darren）、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E7%BB%B4%E7%BB%B4.md#1">李维维</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E9%87%87%E8%AF%97.md#1">周采诗</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B5%E7%BF%94.md#1">邵翔</a>四人一展歌喉唱情歌。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_7293885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7293885" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1602180603021487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7293885" class="wp-caption-text">《大人情歌》主演<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B1%E5%87%AF%E4%BC%9F.md#1">邱凯伟</a>（右起）、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E7%BB%B4%E7%BB%B4.md#1">李维维</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E9%87%87%E8%AF%97.md#1">周采诗</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B5%E7%BF%94.md#1">邵翔</a>齐聚高歌同乐。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>该剧剧情描写李维维从新婚开始就因老公蓝钧天至海外工作而成了寂寞人妻，之后她巧遇昔日初恋情人邱凯伟，而发展出一段温馨、趣味的剧情。而在该剧上档前，日前在脸书还举办了“哪些适合大人唱的情歌募集”活动。</p>
<p>昨天主要演员邱凯伟、李维维、周采诗与邵翔便亲自在KTV举行“K歌同乐会”，四人大展歌喉，还一起演唱《七里香》，同时这首歌亦是剧中邱凯伟与李维维的订情曲。四人未经排练，演唱却默契十足，也让邱凯伟笑说：“又重温了歌手梦。”</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_7293886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7293886" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1602180603231487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7293886" class="wp-caption-text">图为李维维与邱凯伟Darren。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_7293887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7293887" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1602180603291487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7293887" class="wp-caption-text">图为绍翔（左）与周采诗。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>最后现场三人突然向即将于19日生日的李维维庆生，让寿星意外惊喜，巧的是在剧中饰演她老公的蓝钧天竟然也与李维维同一天生日。</p>
