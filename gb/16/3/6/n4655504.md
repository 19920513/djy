<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>崔智友朴海镇等群星现身首尔品牌活动</h2>
<a href="https://di88ceki78g29.cloudfront.net/L2lRYf66f"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年03月06日讯】韩国艺人韩孝珠（又译：<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%AD%9D%E5%91%A8.md#1">韩孝周</a>）、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%94%E6%99%BA%E5%8F%8B.md#1">崔智友</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E8%83%9C%E5%85%83.md#1">车胜元</a>、高濬熙、高修，以及<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E6%B5%B7%E9%95%87.md#1">朴海镇</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E6%B2%87%E7%86%B9.md#1">李沇熹</a>与盛骏，日前出席一场在英国知名品牌首尔旗舰店举办的揭幕活动，并作为协助拍摄“Art of the Trench”风衣艺术展照片的主角，当天现场有逾500位嘉宾出席。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_7302487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7302487" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603051253471487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7302487" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E6%B5%B7%E9%95%87.md#1">朴海镇</a>。（BURBERRY提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_7302488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7302488" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603051254101487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7302488" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%94%E6%99%BA%E5%8F%8B.md#1">崔智友</a>。（BURBERRY提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_7302489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7302489" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603051254031487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7302489" class="wp-caption-text">高濬熙。（BURBERRY提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>活动现场展出在首尔创意圈引领潮流之人士，为今年风衣艺术展于首尔南山谷韩屋村等历史景点，以及鹭梁津水产市场与新沙洞地区等地拍摄的30张照片，呈现出南韩的百变面貌。</p>
<p>韩国艺人李钟硕、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E8%83%9C%E5%85%83.md#1">车胜元</a>、高修、韩孝珠、崔智友、高濬熙、李英真，模特儿金在涌、金成熙，以及创意圈颇具影响力的人士穿着该牌英国制的风衣，及使用蕾丝、丝绸、皮革和科技布料制成的季节性服饰进行拍摄活动。</p>
<p>据悉，这是该品牌在首尔举办第二次的风衣艺术展揭幕活动，现场还请来<br />韩国乐团 Jang Kiha &#038; The Faces在现场倾情献唱，后由DJ“Tom Price”白梓轩炒热气氛。</p>
