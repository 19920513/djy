<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美综艺秀《名人对嘴生死斗II》亚洲首播</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335251487-e1464063592606-600x338.jpg" />
<h6>《名人对嘴生死斗II》亚洲首播，图为查宁?塔图与碧昂丝。（杰德影音提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年05月24日讯】美国综艺节目《名人对嘴生死斗》（Lip Sync Battle）第2季将于5月24日周二晚间在杰德影音爆笑频道“COMEDY CENTRAL”全亚洲首播。第2季首集邀请到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%A5%E5%AE%81%E2%80%A7%E5%A1%94%E5%9B%BE.md#1">查宁?塔图</a>夫妻档对战，查宁?塔图更化身成《冰雪奇缘》（<span class="st">Frozen）</span>艾莎女王，高唱《Let it go》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7924841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7924841" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335291487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7924841" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335291487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7924841" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8D%E4%BA%BA%E5%AF%B9%E5%98%B4%E7%94%9F%E6%AD%BB%E6%96%97ii.md#1">名人对嘴生死斗II</a>》亚洲首播，图中为珍娜?戴温。（杰德影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7924847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7924847" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335221487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7924847" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335221487-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7924847" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8D%E4%BA%BA%E5%AF%B9%E5%98%B4%E7%94%9F%E6%AD%BB%E6%96%97ii.md#1">名人对嘴生死斗II</a>》亚洲首播，图为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%A5%E5%AE%81%E2%80%A7%E5%A1%94%E5%9B%BE.md#1">查宁?塔图</a>化身《冰雪奇缘》中的艾莎女王。（杰德影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《名人对嘴生死斗》由两度葛莱美奖（又译：格莱美）得主、连续四年主持葛莱美颁奖典礼的饶舌歌手“LL Cool J”主持战局，还有名模<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%8B%E8%8E%89%E4%B8%9D%E6%B1%80%E2%80%A7%E6%B3%B0%E6%A0%B9.md#1">克莉丝汀?泰根</a>（Christine Teigen）担任助理主持。</p>
<p>第2季首集除了邀请到查宁?塔图（Channing Tatum）与珍娜?戴温（Jenna Lee Dewan）夫妻档对战之外，包括碧昂丝（Beyoncé）、宝拉?阿巴杜（Paula Abdul）、名导昆丁?塔伦提诺（Quentin Tarantino）、喜剧演员凯文?哈特（Kevin Hart）、奥利薇亚?穆恩（Olivia Munn）等多位美剧明星都将于节目中现身。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7924842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7924842" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335381487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7924842" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335381487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7924842" class="wp-caption-text">《名人对嘴生死斗II》亚洲首播，图中为奥利薇亚?穆恩。（杰德影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7924845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7924845" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335081487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7924845" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335081487-600x405.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7924845" class="wp-caption-text">图为吉吉?哈蒂德（右二）与新好男孩（尼克、AJ）。（杰德影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《名人对嘴生死斗》集结众多好莱坞一线明星参与演出，第2季首集才一播出，即创下高达640万的收视佳绩，一周之内各大数位串流平台已累积5,500万收视次数。节目制作版权售出多个国家，包括英国、墨西哥、中国大陆、智利、印尼、菲律宾、波兰、加拿大、南非等版本，美国儿童版也在筹备中。</p>
<p>今年1月，台湾有线电视台MOD也已公布预订第3季20集，而第二季将于5月24日起每周二、周三晚上8点半在电视频道“COMEDY CENTRAL”首播。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7924860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7924860" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335421487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7924860" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605232335421487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7924860" class="wp-caption-text">《名人对嘴生死斗II》亚洲首播，图为萝伦?科汉（左二）与索妮瓜?马丁葛林（右二）。</figcaption></figure>
