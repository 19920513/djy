<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>法国老百姓：IS恐袭尼斯得逞 政府有责任</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年07月18日讯】<span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-CN">（</span><span lang="zh-TW">大纪元记者张妮法国报导</span><span lang="zh-CN">）</span>连日来，法国电视上专家学者们均在谈论和<span lang="zh-TW">分析尼斯恐袭事件为何会发生</span><span lang="zh-CN">？</span><span lang="zh-TW">当他们</span>振振有词<span lang="zh-TW">热论之际</span><span lang="zh-CN">，</span>有一名普通老百姓<span lang="zh-TW">却明言</span><span lang="zh-CN">：是</span><span lang="zh-TW">法国放松了安全戒备</span><span lang="zh-CN">，</span>国庆烟花晚会时，没有如惯例封锁通往<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B1%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%BA%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">英国人步道</a>的交通并设置水泥墩块。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">继</span>查理漫画报社<span lang="zh-CN">、</span>欧洲之星列车<span lang="zh-CN">、</span>巴塔克朗演艺场<span lang="zh-CN">、</span>布鲁斯尔机场<span lang="zh-TW">等地遭<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%81%90%E6%80%96%E8%A2%AD%E5%87%BB.md#1">恐怖袭击</a>后</span>，如<span lang="zh-CN">今</span><span lang="zh-TW">是</span>尼斯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B1%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%BA%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">英国人步道</a>，接二连三，<span lang="zh-TW">法国</span>舆论问，这到底怎么说？有专家认为，欧足赛没出事，警惕放松了，显然，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E8%89%B2%E6%B5%B7%E5%B2%B8.md#1">蓝色海岸</a>烟花会没有设置明确的安全交通管制区，就这个问题，地方当局支支吾吾。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">据法国电视信息台报导</span><span lang="zh-CN">，</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">7</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">14</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日放国庆烟花当天</span><span lang="zh-CN">，</span><span lang="zh-CN">尼斯英国人步道</span><span lang="zh-TW">部分道路预留给行人通行</span><span lang="zh-CN">，</span><span lang="zh-TW">并从下午</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">15</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">时开始封锁禁止车辆行驶</span><span lang="zh-CN">。</span><span lang="zh-TW">但该部分人行道距恐怖分子被击毙卡车最终停止的地点少于</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">500</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">米</span><span lang="zh-CN">，</span><span lang="zh-TW">即是说</span><span lang="zh-CN">，</span><span lang="zh-TW">恐怖分子驾驶卡车行凶的</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">2</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公里路程中</span><span lang="zh-CN">，</span><span lang="zh-TW">有四分之三的路段是在开放车辆行驶的范围</span><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></span></span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8109619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8109619" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/8994111.png-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8109619" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/8994111.png-450x253.jpg" alt="如图红色线条所示，恐怖分子从H?pital Lenval 到H?tel Westminster全程行驶了2公里进行恐怖袭击，深红色线处为预留给民众观赏国庆烟花的步行道“Zone Piétonne”。（FRANCETV INFO）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8109619" class="wp-caption-text">如图红色线条所示，恐怖分子从H?pital Lenval 到H?tel Westminster全程行驶了2公里进行<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%81%90%E6%80%96%E8%A2%AD%E5%87%BB.md#1">恐怖袭击</a>，深红色线处为预留给民众观赏国庆烟花的步行道“Zone Piétonne”。（FRANCETV INFO）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-size: medium">7</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-CN">月</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">14</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-CN">日晚，法国南部尼斯市（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">Nice</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-CN">）民众</span><span lang="zh-TW">在</span><span lang="zh-CN">观赏国庆烟火秀时，一辆卡车突然冲入人群，造成至少</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">84</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-CN">人丧生、</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">50多</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-CN">人生命垂危，另有约</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">200</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-CN">人受伤。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-TW">尼斯恐怖袭击事件让<span lang="zh-CN">法国政坛激起波澜，老百姓和政要们都不能安心度假了，在法国，公认的不可或缺的乃是假期，有如英国人，不能没有女王。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-CN">尼斯英国人步道</span><span lang="zh-TW">历史悠久</span></span></span></span></strong></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">恐袭事发地</span><span lang="zh-CN">——尼斯英国人步道</span><span lang="zh-TW">已有近两百年历史</span><span lang="zh-CN">。</span>在尼斯城市化以前，尼斯海岸还只是一片荒芜的海滩，被大鹅卵石所覆盖。第一所房屋位于海边的高地上。从</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">18</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">世纪下半叶开始，英国人开始来到尼斯过冬，疗养肺病，欣赏海岸的全景。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">有一年冬天特别寒冷，来自北部的乞丐大批涌入尼斯，于是一些富有的英国人提议为他们兴建一个有用的工程：沿海岸修筑一条步行道（C</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">hemin de promenade</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">）。尼斯市这条舒适的步行道的前景激起了人们的兴趣，大大增加了工作的机会。步行道最初按照当地方言尼斯语（</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">Ni?ard</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">）称为</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">Camin dei Anglès</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">（英国路）。</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">1860</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">年，尼斯被并入法国，随之这条街也按照法语改名为</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">La Promenade des Anglais</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">当地居民将盎格鲁街简称为“</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">Promenade</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">”（步行道）或“</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">Prom</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">”。每到星期天，能看见许多骑自行车的人，以及全家人<span lang="zh-TW">老少</span>沿着这条路散步。这里也是滑板和溜冰者喜爱的地方。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">盎格鲁街上会举行许多活动，例如尼斯狂欢节、鲜花游行等等。街上还布置了蓝色的靠椅和精美的小屋供游人歇息，享受地中海沿岸的闲散生活，观赏天使湾（L</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium">a Baie Des Anges</span><span style="font-family: SimSun"><span style="font-size: medium"><span lang="zh-CN">）蔚蓝色的海水。#</span></span></span></p>
