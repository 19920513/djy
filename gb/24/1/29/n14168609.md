<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>去年1266澳人死于交通事故 创七年新高</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/11/1111040658372366_2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一份新报告称，澳洲交通事故死亡人数继续攀升，2023年成近七年来全澳道路死亡人数最多的一年。图为交通繁忙的悉尼大桥。（GREG WOOD/AFP/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年01月29日讯】（大纪元记者夏楚君<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>悉尼报导）一份新报告称，澳洲交通事故<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%BB%E4%BA%A1%E4%BA%BA%E6%95%B0.md#1">死亡人数</a>继续攀升，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/2023%E5%B9%B4.md#1">2023年</a>成近七年来全澳道路死亡人数最多的一年。报告呼吁政府提高共享交通事故数据的透明度，以了解导致致命车祸激增的原因。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>汽车协会（AAA）周一（1月29日）发布的《国家道路安全战略基准》<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="http://The AAA says the data needed to make sense of Australia’s worsening road toll is being collected by state governments, but it’s not being released. This data relates to the quality of Australian roads, the causes of crashes, and the effectiveness of the various measures to prevent crashes. AAA Managing Director Michael Bradley said: “Without good data, Australia has no credible plan to understand its current road trauma problems or prevent their continuation. “While so much data relating to roads quality and crash causation remains secret, Australia can neither identify the cause of its rising road toll, nor develop the most effective measures to reduce it. “AAA research shows Australians are deeply cynical of how politicians prioritise road project funding and improved data transparency is a commonsense approach that enjoys overwhelming community support.’’ The Commonwealth is currently negotiating a new National Partnership Agreement on Land Transport Infrastructure Projects, which will take effect from July 2024 and dictate how $50 billion in federal road funding is allocated over the next five years. The AAA’s Data Saves Lives campaign is calling on the Commonwealth to use the new agreement to compel states and territories – as a condition of receiving federal road grants – to report existing road safety data. Shortly before the 2022 election, the federal Labor Party committed to “cooperate with the states and territories to improve the timeliness and quality of road trauma data and look for opportunities to extract better quality road safety data from states and territories in return for funding of road projects”. The AAA is calling on the Albanese Government to deliver on that promise because voluntary data-sharing arrangements are failing, and the only way to achieve the required transparency is to use the agreement to link data sharing with the receipt of road funding. Data Saves Lives is supported by the nation’s motoring clubs, and 18 national organisations representing motorists, motorcyclists, truckers, pedestrians, doctors, insurers, road engineers and safety advocates. The AAA’s proposal has been endorsed by all Liberal, National, Greens and Teals MPs in the House of Representatives. But with only one exception, Labor MPs have declined to support road safety data transparency. The National Road Safety Strategy 2021-30 was agreed by all Australian governments in 2021 and aims to halve Australian road deaths through the decade to 2030. The AAA’s Benchmarking Report is available at www.aaa.asn.au/knowledge-centre" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">报告</a></span>显示，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/2023%E5%B9%B4.md#1">2023年</a>，澳洲道路交通<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%BB%E4%BA%A1%E4%BA%BA%E6%95%B0.md#1">死亡人数</a>为1,266人，比2022年的交通事故死亡人数（ 1,180人）增加了7.3%，这使2023年成为自2017年1月以来澳洲道路上死亡人数最多的一年。</p>
<p>交通事故死亡人数出现增幅的州包括澳洲人口最多的两个州——新州和维州，而南澳以其最大增幅紧随两州之后。</p>
<p>去年，新州有351人在公路交通事故中死亡，与前年的281人相比，增长了24.9%，是全澳死亡人数最多的一个州。</p>
<p>维州的死亡率增加了22.5%，从2022年的249人增至2023年的294人。</p>
<p>增幅最大的州是南澳，该州去年有117人死于公路交通事故，与2022年的70人相比，增幅达64.8%。</p>
<p>与2022年相比，其余州和地区的道路死亡人数有所下降，其中，昆州减幅最大，减少了20人。</p>
<p>与此同时，报告还发现，2023年下半年的道路死亡人数（677 人）创下13年来半年内死亡人数最多的记录 。</p>
<p>澳洲汽车协会总经理布拉德利（Michael Bradley）表示，不断增加的死亡人数表明《国家道路安全战略》偏离了轨道。该策略的目标是到2030年将每年的道路交通死亡人数至少减少50%。</p>
<p>各州和领地政府一直在收集有关道路质量、交通事故原因及预防车祸措施等方面的数据，但这些数据并未公布。</p>
<p>布拉德利表示，“在有关道路质量和车祸原因的大量数据仍处于保密状态的情况下，澳洲既无法确定<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%93%E8%B7%AF%E4%BA%A4%E9%80%9A%E4%BA%8B%E6%95%85.md#1">道路交通事故</a>不断增加的原因，也无法制定最有效的减少道路交通事故的措施。”</p>
<p>联邦正就新的《陆路交通基础设施项目全国合作协议》进行谈判，该协议将于2024年7月生效，并将决定未来五年内500亿联邦道路资金的分配方式。</p>
<p>布拉德利呼吁联邦政府利用新协议，迫使各州和领地共享道路安全数据，以此作为获得联邦道路拨款的条件。</p>
