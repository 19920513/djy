<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>别开生面春联创作课程   桃园丘美珍老师创意多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163784-660595-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>小朋友开心展现作品。（丘美珍老师提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年01月22日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）担任国中美术老师的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%98%E7%BE%8E%E7%8F%8D.md#1">丘美珍</a>，欢度新年，举办一场别开生面的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E8%81%94.md#1">春联</a>创作课程，吸引了众多学员踊跃参与，同时展现了他们独到的文学创意。</p>
<p>在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%98%E7%BE%8E%E7%8F%8D.md#1">丘美珍</a>的悉心指导下，学员们以龙年为主题，巧妙地融入传统文化元素，展现出成熟而富有层次的创意和想像力。他们运用精简却充满韵味的文字，将对新一年的美好期望巧妙地融入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E8%81%94.md#1">春联</a>，营造了一片浓厚的节庆氛围。</p>
<figure id="14163779" aria-describedby="caption-14163779" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163779-660590-450x361.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163779-660590-450x361.jpg" alt="担任国中美术老师的丘美珍，欢度新年，举办一场别开生面的春联创作课程，吸引了众多学员踊跃参与。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14163779" class="wp-caption-text">担任国中美术老师的丘美珍，欢度新年，举办一场别开生面的春联创作课程，吸引了众多学员踊跃参与。（丘美珍老师提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学员们积极分享自己的作品，表达对龙年的独特理解以及对未来的美好期望。</p>
<figure id="14163782" aria-describedby="caption-14163782" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163782-660593-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163782-660593-450x600.jpg" alt="学生写“福”字。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14163782" class="wp-caption-text">学生写“福”字。（丘美珍老师提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中，有位学员来自加拿大，对中华文化深感兴趣，透过此活动更深入了解中华传统文化，同时培养了文学审美情趣，促进了语言表达能力的提升。</p>
<figure id="14163783" aria-describedby="caption-14163783" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163783-660594-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163783-660594-450x600.jpg" alt="学员来自加拿大，对中华文化深感兴趣，透过此活动更深入了解中华传统文化。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14163783" class="wp-caption-text">学员来自加拿大，对中华文化深感兴趣，透过此活动更深入了解中华传统文化。（丘美珍老师提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>创意春联课程不仅是一场欢乐迎接龙年的活动，更是学员们共同展现创造力的契机，同时也促成了一场极具水平的文化交流。</p>
<figure id="14163781" aria-describedby="caption-14163781" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163781-660592-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163781-660592-450x600.jpg" alt="小学生练习“龙”行书。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14163781" class="wp-caption-text">小学生练习“龙”行书。（丘美珍老师提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丘美珍这次的创意春联活动，为学员们带来欢乐同时也充实了他们的文学素养。丘美珍期待透过这样的活动，激发学员更多的创造力，为未来的学习奠定坚实基础。◇</p>
<figure id="14163780" aria-describedby="caption-14163780" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163780-660591-450x351.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163780-660591-450x351.jpg" alt="学生先练习再写在红纸上。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14163780" class="wp-caption-text">学生先练习再写在红纸上。（丘美珍老师提供）</figcaption></figure>
