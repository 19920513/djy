<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>全美亚裔妇女会30周年庆 熠熠生辉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167861-1-1-1NAWA-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>1月19日，昆士市长柯奇代表麻州正副州长给精英会成员颁奖。（章佩儿/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年01月28日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿昆士市报导）1月19日周五晚，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A8%E7%BE%8E%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94%E5%A6%87%E5%A5%B3%E4%BC%9A.md#1">全美亚裔妇女会</a>（NAWA）<wbr /><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%8E%E9%9B%AF.md#1">黎雯</a>会长在昆士市的龙凤酒楼隆重举办了“30经典熠熠生辉”<wbr />晚会，庆祝包括该会成立18周年、全美<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94.md#1">亚裔</a>精英领袖联盟8周年、<wbr />全美中华国粹精英领袖联盟7周年、以及“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A8%E7%BE%8E%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94%E5%A6%87%E5%A5%B3%E4%BC%9A.md#1">全美亚裔妇女会</a>日”<wbr />4周年庆典。同时，<wbr />这也标志着<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%8E%E9%9B%AF.md#1">黎雯</a>作为始创会长服务<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94.md#1">亚裔</a>社区第30年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14167862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14167862" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167862-1-2-2NAWA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14167862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167862-1-2-2NAWA-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14167862" class="wp-caption-text">全美亚裔妇女会30周年庆，恰逢黎雯会长和昆士市长柯奇生日。左起市长夫人克丽丝汀、寿星柯奇市长、寿星黎雯共切蛋糕。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚近两百位各界嘉宾及政要出席盛会，包括昆士市长柯奇伉俪（<wbr />Tommas Koch）、波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）。柯奇代表州长希莉、副州长德里斯科颁发奖状，<wbr />感谢黎雯为妇女会和亚裔社区的奉献服务，<wbr />同时也为精英会成员颁奖。费连也颁发嘉奖状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14167856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14167856" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167856-1-2-3NAWA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14167856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167856-1-2-3NAWA-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14167856" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员费连（左三）给会长黎雯（右二）褒奖。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14167859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14167859" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167859-1-2-4NAWA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14167859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167859-1-2-4NAWA-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14167859" class="wp-caption-text">主持人李宣欣夫人萧珊珊的演唱，是纽约亨特大学音乐专业毕业生，曾在百老汇积累丰富的演出经验。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>庆典主题是“携手共创 活得精彩”，由李宣欣、潘悦和李照原主持。<wbr />开场时黎雯与会员们伴随音乐俩俩携手入场。在政要嘉宾颁奖完，<wbr />华林舞狮队表演醒狮迎春接福，为庆典增添了喜庆氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14167860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14167860" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167860-1-2-5NAWA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14167860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167860-1-2-5NAWA-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14167860" class="wp-caption-text">柳叶表演中国古典舞《黄梅悠悠》。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚会现场歌舞表演不断，李宣欣为现场观众献唱了多首歌曲，包括《<wbr />悟空》、《铁血丹心》等。黎雯也为大家演唱了经典的《夜上海》。<wbr />其中，引人注目的表演之一是李宣欣夫人萧珊珊的演唱，<wbr />她以浑厚的歌声呈现了迪士尼冰雪奇缘影片中的乐曲《For the First Time in Forever》。萧珊珊是纽约亨特大学音乐专业毕业生，<wbr />曾在百老汇积累丰富的演出经验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14167858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14167858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167858-1-2-6NAWA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14167858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167858-1-2-6NAWA-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14167858" class="wp-caption-text">高慧子表演舞蹈《且吟春雨》。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14167857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14167857" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167857-1-2-7NAWA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14167857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167857-1-2-7NAWA-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14167857" class="wp-caption-text">黎雯会长在《财神到》的歌声中，为现场出席者发放红包（派利是）。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，柳叶表演了中国古典舞《黄梅悠悠》，高慧子表演了舞蹈《<wbr />且吟春雨》，为庆典增色不少。</p>
<p>当天恰好是黎雯和柯奇市长的生日，因此特地准备了大蛋糕和寿桃，<wbr />分享给每位宾客。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14167855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14167855" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167855-1-2-8NAWA.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14167855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14167855-1-2-8NAWA-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14167855" class="wp-caption-text">庆典主题是“携手共创 活得精彩”，由（右起）李宣欣、潘悦和李照原主持。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后，黎雯会长在《财神到》的歌声中，为现场出席者发放红包（<wbr />派利是），为庆典画上句点。◇</p>
