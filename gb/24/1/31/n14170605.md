<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>耆英会白礼顿楼贺龙年 老人中心歌舞欢欣</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170584-DJY03695-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>1月29日，中华耆英会白礼顿楼庆祝龙年，在布鲁克林老人中心献上舞蹈演出。（章佩儿/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年01月31日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿布鲁克林镇报导）1月29日（周一），<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E9%B2%81%E5%85%8B%E6%9E%97%E8%80%81%E4%BA%BA%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">布鲁克林老人中心</a>（Brookline Senior Center) 迎来了一场别开生面的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%B9%B4.md#1">龙年</a>庆祝活动。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E8%80%86%E8%8B%B1%E4%BC%9A.md#1">中华耆英会</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E7%A4%BC%E9%A1%BF%E6%A5%BC.md#1">白礼顿楼</a>（Brighton House）为各族裔<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E8%80%85.md#1">长者</a>们带来了一系列富有中国文化的演出。现场座无虚席。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14170581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14170581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170581-DJY03671.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14170581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170581-DJY03671-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14170581" class="wp-caption-text">1月29日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E8%80%86%E8%8B%B1%E4%BC%9A.md#1">中华耆英会</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E7%A4%BC%E9%A1%BF%E6%A5%BC.md#1">白礼顿楼</a>庆祝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%B9%B4.md#1">龙年</a>，合唱小组为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E8%80%85.md#1">长者</a>们带来表演。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14170579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14170579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170579-DJY03640.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14170579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170579-DJY03640-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14170579" class="wp-caption-text">乐赵令瑜（中）参与耆英会的新年演出及担任司仪，已经超过20年了。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下午1点，司仪乐赵令瑜女士的娴熟主持，引领着观众走进这个独特的时刻。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E9%B2%81%E5%85%8B%E6%9E%97%E8%80%81%E4%BA%BA%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">布鲁克林老人中心</a>经理杰米·詹森（Jamie Jensen）和白礼顿楼主任梅丽梨（Lili?Mei）分别发表热情洋溢的新年致辞。梅丽梨说：“今年已经是第22年在布鲁克林老人中心举办新年庆祝会了，感谢大家的支持和参与！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14170578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14170578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170578-DJY03632.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14170578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170578-DJY03632-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14170578" class="wp-caption-text">白礼顿楼主任梅丽梨（Lili Mei）为大家送上龙年祝福。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14170638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14170638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14170638-DJY03622.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14170638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14170638-DJY03622-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14170638" class="wp-caption-text">布鲁克林老人中心经理詹森（Jamie Jensen）为长者们送上新年祝福。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14170580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14170580" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170580-DJY03668.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14170580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170580-DJY03668-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14170580" class="wp-caption-text">薛国中演奏电子琴，为表演者伴奏。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14170582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14170582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170582-DJY03686.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14170582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170582-DJY03686-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14170582" class="wp-caption-text">徐子为以钢琴独奏两首经典乐曲。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">乐赵令瑜与杨泽瑞、曹文华组成的舞蹈小组以《太湖美》拉开了庆祝序幕。合唱小组紧随其后，唱响《相逢是首歌》和《快乐颂》的旋律，将欢乐传递给每一位观众。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14170583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14170583" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170583-DJY03692.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14170583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170583-DJY03692-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14170583" class="wp-caption-text">中华耆英会携布鲁克林老人中心欢庆龙年，带来蒙古舞蹈表演。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14170585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14170585" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170585-DJY03702.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14170585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170585-DJY03702-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14170585" class="wp-caption-text">赵宝玲高音独唱两首歌曲，与长者们一起欢庆龙年。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">接下来，薛国中、薛曼玲演唱了《Sha, La La La La》和《甜言蜜语》。?舞蹈小组也再度登场，用《美丽的星期日》的表演为大家带来别样的视听盛宴。紧接着，徐子为的钢琴独奏巴赫法国组曲和舒伯特奏鸣曲，用音符演绎出浓厚的艺术氛围。蒙古舞蹈的表演者Jiang Yang Wu和??Mary Ma带来了独特的文化风情。乐赵令瑜、叶英明、杨泽瑞、曹文华、陈文龙的《两巷佳人》表演展现了华丽与典雅的结合。女高音独唱者赵宝玲用悠扬、浑厚的歌声演绎了《美丽的西班牙女郎》和《亲爱的爸爸》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14170587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14170587" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170587-DJY03718.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14170587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170587-DJY03718-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14170587" class="wp-caption-text">演唱最后一首歌曲《恭喜恭喜》的同时，表演者们纷纷向观众恭贺新年。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14170588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14170588" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170588-DSC05926.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14170588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14170588-DSC05926-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14170588" class="wp-caption-text">1月29日，中华耆英会白礼顿楼庆祝龙年，表演者们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">最后，乐赵令瑜合唱小组与所有参与者共同唱响了《恭喜恭喜》。演员们一边唱歌，一边来到观众席，为满座的观众送上最诚挚的祝福。整个庆祝活动交织着歌舞、音乐和欢笑，为老人中心带来了一片温馨和欢欣的氛围。◇</p>
