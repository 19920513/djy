<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】黑线鳕鱼土豆汤~腊月暖肠胃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14165704-b5ebb129ca35ca3b7d636585987f70e8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>进入一年中最冷的大寒节气，喝热汤可以暖身体、养胃。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年01月24日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%8A%E6%9C%88.md#1">腊月</a>期间，天气寒冷，大家忙碌之余别忘了顾好肠胃。这里分享一道容易制作的热乎乎营养鱼汤，老少咸宜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14165708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14165708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14165708-7a73bc8e49d2af92ed8678818d6d81bd-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14165708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14165708-7a73bc8e49d2af92ed8678818d6d81bd-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14165708" class="wp-caption-text">食材。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>食材准备</h3>
<p>1包<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E7%BA%BF%E9%B3%95%E9%B1%BC.md#1">黑线鳕鱼</a>排（Haddock），约100克，切小方丁<br />
1杯粉质土豆，切成拇指大小的方丁<br />
1/2杯黄洋葱，切小丁<br />
1.5杯水<br />
1/2杯蛤蜊汁（Clam juice）<br />
1/4茶匙盐，分成2份<br />
一小撮现磨黑胡椒<br />
2汤匙初榨橄榄油</p>
<p>1.锅中放入油、洋葱丁拌匀，小火（simmer），加盖焖10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14165713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14165713" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14165713-bc01c06dcad183f590e7b9b37c6dc8b5-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14165713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14165713-bc01c06dcad183f590e7b9b37c6dc8b5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14165713" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.土豆丁分成两份，形状不太好的作汤底，其余待用。鱼肉丁加1/4茶匙盐拌匀，待用。</p>
<p>3.锅中加入作汤底的土豆丁、黑胡椒、1/4茶匙盐拌匀，中火炒约2分钟。洋葱和土豆都不能炒焦，否则汤色浑浊。</p>
<p>4.添加水煮沸后关火。晾至合适温度就用机器打成糊。回锅加入剩余土豆丁、蛤蜊汁煮沸约3分钟，至土豆丁变色加入鱼肉。鱼肉煮熟即可盛盘，约5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14165714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14165714" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14165714-3a3088e48dcbb599c22bedbb434c8b8d-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14165714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14165714-3a3088e48dcbb599c22bedbb434c8b8d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14165714" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>煎面包丁</h2>
<p>喝鱼汤前，可以煎面包丁作为配菜，增加风味层次。煎面包丁可以撒在汤中或拌沙拉。</p>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1.将1杯酸面包切小丁放入平底锅，加2汤匙初榨橄榄油拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14165718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14165718" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14165718-d72accc100142027489e89a904b57b4f-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14165718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14165718-d72accc100142027489e89a904b57b4f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14165718" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.小火焙香。关火撒一层辣椒粉即可使用。辣椒粉可以换用自己喜欢的香料。</p>
