<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>寻味葡萄牙10种特色美食不容错过</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161322-shutterstock_1840946806-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>葡萄牙是令人向往的旅游胜地，美食遍地。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年01月18日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84%E7%89%99.md#1">葡萄牙</a>有童话般的街景、绝美的海滩，夏季阳光充裕，冬天不太寒冷，是不少旅人心目中的度假胜地。</p>
<p>除了美景，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84%E7%89%99.md#1">葡萄牙</a>也是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>遍地的国度，南北地区都有令人印象深刻的料理。如果计划前往葡萄牙，你应该很想知道当地有哪些必尝美食。以下介绍10种<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%B9%E8%89%B2.md#1">特色</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>，前7种由当地人票选上榜。</p>
<h2>烤沙丁鱼</h2>
<p>沿着海岸线寻找美食，海鲜是最佳选择。烤沙丁鱼（Sardinha assada）是葡萄牙人喜爱的传统食物，价格经济实惠。夏天，在沿海地区，无论走到哪里，随处可以闻到烤沙丁鱼的味道。尤其6月份的圣徒庆祝节期间，人们来到广场上一边载歌载舞，一边享用烤沙丁鱼等美食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14161306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161306-shutterstock_1559156480.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161306-shutterstock_1559156480-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161306" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙烤沙丁鱼搭配面包。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>烤沙丁鱼作法简单。人们将抹上盐的新鲜沙丁鱼放在火上烤，直到鱼皮稍微烤焦，然后搭配马铃薯、烤辣椒或者面包片享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14161307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161307" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161307-shutterstock_2272298635.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161307-shutterstock_2272298635-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161307" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙传统海鲜炖饭。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>海鲜炖饭</h2>
<p>2011年，葡萄牙人票选出7大美食。刚才介绍的烤沙丁鱼是其一。起源于中部沿海地区的海鲜炖饭（Arroz de marisco）也入选了。热腾腾的炖饭由蛤蜊等贝类、虾和浓郁的香草及番茄汁制作。比起西班牙炖饭，它的汤汁更丰富。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14161309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161309-shutterstock_2075600779.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161309-shutterstock_2075600779-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161309" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙的炭烤乳猪。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14161311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161311" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161311-shutterstock_2241184697.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161311-shutterstock_2241184697-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161311" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙烤乳猪搭配薯片。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>烤乳猪</h2>
<p>葡萄牙的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%B9%E8%89%B2.md#1">特色</a>烤乳猪（Leit?o da Bairrada）也是知名美食。这道料理是将数周大的乳猪，用胡椒等香料腌制，然后用燃木慢慢烘烤至皮脆焦香。最佳品尝地点在拜拉达（Bairrada）地区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14161312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161312" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161312-shutterstock_1592838628.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161312-shutterstock_1592838628-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161312" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙米兰德拉蒜肠。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>米兰德拉蒜肠</h2>
<p>葡萄牙的香肠种类繁多，但以米兰德拉蒜肠（Alheiras de Mirandela）最负盛名。传统的香肠馅料由猪肉以外的肉类、面包、橄榄油和大蒜等组成。它们的吃法包括油煎后搭配马铃薯和鸡蛋，也可以烧烤后搭配蔬菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14161314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161314" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161314-shutterstock_2200703353.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161314-shutterstock_2200703353-600x379.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161314" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙羽衣甘蓝汤。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>羽衣甘蓝汤</h2>
<p>羽衣甘蓝汤（Caldo Verde）起源于葡萄牙北方地区。它的汤底是马铃薯、大蒜和洋葱，然后添加绿色的羽衣甘蓝，顶部放上煎过的辣香肠（chouri?o）。有时搭配玉米面包享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14161315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161315" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161315-shutterstock_1412729531.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161315-shutterstock_1412729531-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161315" class="wp-caption-text">切开顶部的埃斯特雷拉山起司。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>埃斯特雷拉山起司</h2>
<p>埃斯特雷拉山起司（Queijo Serra da Estrela）使用埃斯特雷拉山区（Serra da Estrela）的绵羊奶制成，奶质香醇。关于它的历史记载，据说可以追溯至12世纪。根据陈化情况，奶酪的质地有半硬质的，也有的非常柔软可以用来涂抹面包片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14161316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161316" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161316-shutterstock_1936655674.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161316-shutterstock_1936655674-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161316" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙蛋挞店（Pastéis de Belém）出售的蛋挞。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>蛋挞</h2>
<p>提起葡萄牙美食，很多人会不约而同的想到蛋挞。在葡萄牙随处都可以吃到蛋挞，但最著名的蛋挞店座落在里斯本。</p>
<p>据资料记载，葡萄牙最早的蛋挞配方出自修道院，从1873年开始，里斯本贝伦区的烘焙店“Pastéis de Belém”开始出售遵循该配方制作的蛋挞（Pastéis de Belém）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14161318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161318-shutterstock_2273367219.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161318-shutterstock_2273367219-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161318" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙的猪血肉汤。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>猪血肉汤</h2>
<p>葡萄牙北方地区有一道传统肉汤（Papas de Sarrabulho）。这道菜起源于米尼奥（Minho），人们常开玩笑说它不适合胆小的人品尝，因为汤里包含许多人称之为黑暗料理的猪血。</p>
<p>然而这道菜并不是将生猪血端上桌。汤的主料是煮熟的碎鸡肉、猪肉、火腿和马铃薯。当主要食材在高汤中炖煮完成，就添加玉米粉或面包增稠，并加入少量猪血将汤调成棕色。所有食材炖煮得热乎乎的端上餐桌，飘散着孜然香气，适合冬季享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14161320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161320-shutterstock_2092262896.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161320-shutterstock_2092262896-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161320" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙的特色三明治。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>三明治</h2>
<p>波尔图（Porto）的热门三明治（Francesinha）据说源于70年代，现在是游客必尝的地标美食。有人称它是豪华版的法式三明治（croque monsieur）。因为在面包夹肉的三明治基底上，多加了三个使其华丽转身的步骤——放上煎鸡蛋后用起司包裹一圈（起司中间挖洞露出蛋黄），接着浇上店家秘制的热乎乎酱汁。</p>
<p>当起司被热酱汁融化形成起司瀑布，在三明治周围堆满薯条就可以上桌了。三明治搭配的肉类虽然各餐厅有专属版本，但通常会包括葡萄牙香肠、火腿，牛排或烤肉等，十分丰盛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14161321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14161321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161321-shutterstock_1431883292.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14161321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14161321-shutterstock_1431883292-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14161321" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙的特色甜点软鸡蛋。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>软鸡蛋</h2>
<p>景色迷人的阿威罗（Aveiro）出产一种鸡蛋大小的美味甜点——软鸡蛋（Ovos moles）。制作过程通常由人工完成，需要先将熬成膏状的蛋黄和糖填入模具，按压定型后，再一个个修剪出形状。制作过程类似意大利饺（Ravioli）。和蛋挞一样，软鸡蛋也来自修道院。</p>
