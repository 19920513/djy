<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>年节前夕 桃消提醒工厂用火用电安全</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14169605-662289-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市产业园区联合服务中心姜主任致词。（桃消观音分队提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年01月30日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%92%E6%B5%81.md#1">寒流</a>来袭及新年即将来到，为提醒观音区桃科环科大潭工业区各类工作场所注意<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E9%98%B2%E5%AE%89%E5%85%A8.md#1">消防安全</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E6%B6%88.md#1">桃消</a>观音分队于30日假桃园市产业园区联合服务中心办理<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%81%AB.md#1">防火</a>管理座谈会。提醒业者注意自身场所<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E9%98%B2%E5%AE%89%E5%85%A8.md#1">消防安全</a>，提升初期火灾灭火能力，降低灾害损失。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14169606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14169606" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14169606-662290.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14169606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14169606-662290-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14169606" class="wp-caption-text">宣导装设住宅用火灾警报器之重要性。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E6%B6%88.md#1">桃消</a>观音分队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会中分享多起事故案例，包括联华彰化厂大火起诉3人、台中大甲一氧化碳中毒、屏东明扬大火及观音辖内曾发生过火灾场所等实际案例。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14169607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14169607" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14169607-662291.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14169607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14169607-662291-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14169607" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%81%AB.md#1">防火</a>管理制度说明。（桃消观音分队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>透过曾经发生的真实案例，提醒各厂家落实自卫消防编组训练，依规定实施灭火班、通报班、避难引导班任务操作，并转达员工注意自身周围消防安全，听到火警警铃务必先逃生再确认，避免憾事再度发生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14169608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14169608" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14169608-662292.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14169608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14169608-662292-600x312.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14169608" class="wp-caption-text">藉由屏东明扬大火案例，提醒业者应落实自卫消防编组训练。（桃消观音分队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>观音分队长林宏锌表示：不是会念口诀“拉、瞄、压、扫”就会使用灭火器，灭火的危险在于会影响宝贵的逃生时间，拿灭火器的同时，往往火势已经改变，现场早已浓烟密布无法逃生，千万不能逞强一定要把火灭掉。记住：“小火快逃、浓烟关门”才是保命之道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14169609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14169609" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14169609-662293.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14169609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14169609-662293-600x316.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14169609" class="wp-caption-text">低温<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%92%E6%B5%81.md#1">寒流</a>来袭，加强防范一氧化碳中毒。（桃消观音分队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第三大队长简慈彦提醒：年假前别忘了关火源、关电器、确保消防安全设备堪用并指派专人保持联络方式，万一工厂发生火灾，务必请专人到场协助提供救灾资讯、现场平面图及告知危险物品。</p>
<p>消防单位年节期间不打烊，仍持续坚守岗位，守护大家生命财产安全。◇</p>
