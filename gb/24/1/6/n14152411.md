<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>踏青好时节 墨尔本最美公园一览</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152429-Victoria-State-Rose-Garden-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>维州玫瑰花园（Victoria State Rose Garden）。（Visit Victoria提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年01月06日讯】（大纪元记者万梓清<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>编译报导）春暖花开，又到了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B8%8F%E9%9D%92.md#1">踏青</a>的好时节。墨尔本市内及周边有很多值得一去的公园，下面为大家推荐几个。</p>
<div id="da" class="articlecontent">
<p>据visitvictoria.com报导，在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>市内，最著名的公园当然是王家植物园（Royal Botanic Gardens）啦，这里绿草如茵，曲径通幽，不仅有着大量来自澳洲本土及全球各地的奇花异草，古老的建筑也十分赏心悦目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152423" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152423" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152423-fd757a3f6312022f7e623b2a183742c0.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152423" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152423-fd757a3f6312022f7e623b2a183742c0-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152423" class="wp-caption-text">王家植物园（Royal Botanic Gardens）墨尔本花园。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而卡尔顿花园（Carlton Gardens）中美丽的喷泉和传统建筑则常年吸引着许多人前来拍照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152425" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152425-carlton-gardens.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152425-carlton-gardens-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152425" class="wp-caption-text">卡尔顿花园（Carlton Gardens）。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在东墨尔本（East Melbourne）的菲茨罗伊花园（Fitzroy Gardens），库克船长小屋（Captain Cook&#8217;s Cottage）、带雕刻的仙女树、都铎村庄（Tudor Village）模型、温室花卉以及茂密的林荫大道都是非常有名的景点。</p>
<p>被列入文化遗产名录的财政花园（Treasury Gardens）是维州最古老的公园，这里以古老的针叶树、高大的棕榈树、榆树和橡树而闻名。</p>
<p>而靠近维多利亚女王市场（Queen Victoria Market）的旗杆花园（Flagstaff Gardens）则是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E9%A4%90.md#1">野餐</a>的好地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152434-d3dbf7206833da66db4b139179fd2afd.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152434-d3dbf7206833da66db4b139179fd2afd-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152434" class="wp-caption-text">旗杆花园（Flagstaff Gardens）。（林奕瑶／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，战争纪念馆（The Shrine of Remembrance）也是墨尔本最具标志性的景点之一，该景区占地13公顷，风景如画。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152426" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152426-Shrine-of-Remembrance.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152426-Shrine-of-Remembrance-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152426" class="wp-caption-text">战争纪念馆（The Shrine of Remembrance）。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你想离开墨尔本市区，到稍远一点的地方去<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B8%8F%E9%9D%92.md#1">踏青</a>，南亚拉（South Yarra）的科摩古屋及花园（Como House and Garden）及其附近的利本·李庄园（Rippon Lea Estate）都是值得一去的地方。这两个建于19世纪的庄园起初均为私人宅院，也是当时上流社会的聚会之处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152427" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152427-Como-House-and-Garden.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152427-Como-House-and-Garden-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152427" class="wp-caption-text">科摩古屋及花园（Como House and Garden）。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14152428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152428-Rippon-Lea-Estate.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152428-Rippon-Lea-Estate-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152428" class="wp-caption-text">利本·李庄园（Rippon Lea Estate）。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在靠近海边的圣基尔达（St Kilda）区，露娜公园（Luna Park）、奥东内尔花园（O&#8217;Donnell Gardens）和卡塔尼花园（Catani Gardens）都是深受游客欢迎的好去处。</p>
<p>在墨尔本西南部的华勒比（Werribee），当维州玫瑰花园（Victoria State Rose Garden）里5000朵玫瑰绽放时，整个花园就像一个巨型的万花筒。另外，华勒比庄园（Werribee Mansion）也是一个踏青和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E9%A4%90.md#1">野餐</a>的好地方。</p>
<p>如果您还想去更远一点的地方游玩，墨尔本东南区有一个Cranbourne版的王家植物园。该植物园一直延伸到海边，里面有超过17万种本土植物和一个名叫“红土中心”（Red Centre）的知名景点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152422" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152422-1d0a1a0add9dcf7dea2e84644cd77d49.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152422-1d0a1a0add9dcf7dea2e84644cd77d49-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152422" class="wp-caption-text">王家植物园（Royal Botanic Gardens）Cranbourne花园。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除此之外，丹德农山区（Dandenong Ranges）的阿尔弗雷德·尼古拉斯纪念花园（ Alfred Nicholas Memorial Garden）、丹德农山区植物园（Dandenong Ranges Botanic Garden）、特塞拉郁金香节（Tesselaar Tulip Festival）也是值得一游的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%91%E9%97%B2%E5%A5%BD%E5%8E%BB%E5%A4%84.md#1">休闲好去处</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152430-Dandenong-Ranges-Botanic-Garden.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152430-Dandenong-Ranges-Botanic-Garden-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152430" class="wp-caption-text">丹德农山区植物园（Dandenong Ranges Botanic Garden）。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
