<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】班兰叶柠檬水</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162970-371940079c4dd6cf96ae909265fcad92-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>班兰叶柠檬水。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%AD%E5%85%B0%E5%8F%B6.md#1">班兰叶</a>是一种热带植物，散发独特甜美的香气，鲜绿的叶片让人一眼就认出，在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E5%8D%97%E4%BA%9A%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">东南亚料理</a>中特别常见。因为气味特殊，所以尝过一次便难以忘怀。大多还是用在甜点或饮料中。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%AD%E5%85%B0%E5%8F%B6.md#1">班兰叶</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%A0%E6%AA%AC%E6%B0%B4.md#1">柠檬水</a></strong></span></h2>
<h3>?<span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></h3>
<p>班兰叶 6oz<br />
水 4杯<br />
蜂蜜 适量<br />
柠檬 适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法：</span></h3>
<p>1.将班兰叶洗干净后再剪碎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14162965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14162965" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162965-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14162965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162965-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14162965" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14162966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14162966" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162966-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14162966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162966-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14162966" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.剪好的班兰叶放入搅拌机中，加水4杯，搅打约5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14162967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14162967" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162967-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14162967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162967-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14162967" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.用纱布隔渣，过滤后将班兰叶汁倒入锅中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14162968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14162968" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162968-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14162968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162968-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14162968" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.将装有班兰叶汁的锅子放在炉火上煮5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14162969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14162969" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162969-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14162969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162969-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14162969" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.煮好的班兰叶汁再加入柠檬和蜂蜜，即可饮用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14162971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14162971" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162971-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14162971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162971-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14162971" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">班兰叶的益处?</span></h2>
<p>班兰叶（Pandan Leaves）又称为香兰叶，它的好处很多，除了可作为香料，加入各种甜品糕点之外，对人体也有不少有益的功效。班兰叶能润肺、清热解毒、消暑、解酒、治痛风、改善抑郁症，并且能舒筋活络。◇</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【主厨简介】</span></h3>
<p>经历：</p>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
