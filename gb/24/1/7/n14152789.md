<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>关山镇书法协会送春联 迎接新年气氛热络</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152794-657632-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>郑聘嬴擅长将图案融入书法字体，创作出独特的“创意春联”。（周颢承提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年01月07日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）为了迎接黄历<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">新年</a>的到来，台东<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E5%B1%B1.md#1">关山</a>镇<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A6%E6%B3%95.md#1">书法</a>协会于7日上午在天后宫广场，举办了一场热闹滚滚的送春联活动。气温回升的好天气，带动了现场的热络氛围，吸引了众多家长携带小朋友前来参与。书法协会还开放民众现场指定吉祥词句或喜欢的图案，由书法家即席挥毫。</p>
<p>7日上午活动由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A6%E6%B3%95.md#1">书法</a>协会理事长郑聘嬴联合书法家进行，镇长彭成丰、镇民代表会副主席李伟钰、代表谢东原、天后宫主委方瑞清、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E5%B1%B1.md#1">关山</a>同济会长吴秀琴等嘉宾也莅临现场，向书法家们致以诚挚的问候。现场免费索取春联的消息一传开，吸引了众多民众踊跃参与。郑聘嬴等书法家巧妙地将明年的龙年生肖融入春联，创作出富有创意的春联作品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152792" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152792-657630.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152792-657630-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152792" class="wp-caption-text">关山书法协会现场免费索取春联的消息一传开，吸引了众多民众踊跃参与。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14152795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152795" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152795-657633.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152795-657633-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152795" class="wp-caption-text">郑聘嬴呼吁大众学习书法，这不仅能陶冶身心灵，尤其对小朋友来说，更能培养耐性，带来意想不到的正面效果。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近年来，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>县许多送春联的活动都能见到郑聘嬴现场挥毫的身影。除了擅长书法，她还精通绘画，成为众人注目的书法家之一。郑聘嬴分享，她从国小时期就喜爱写字和画图，十多年前有机会重拾毛笔，透过学习各种书法字体，更透过书画创作解决了昔日的睡眠障碍问题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152796" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152796-657634.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152796-657634-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152796" class="wp-caption-text">将明年的龙年生肖融入春联，创作出富有创意的春联作品。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郑聘嬴擅长将图案融入书法字体，创作出独特的“创意春联”。她强调书法是一项历史悠久的文化，透过自学和将传统春联与意象空间设计相结合，让年轻一代也能在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">新年</a>时张贴别具喜气的春联。目前担任关山镇书法协会理事长的郑聘嬴，呼吁大众学习书法，这不仅能陶冶身心灵，尤其对小朋友来说，更能培养耐性，带来意想不到的正面效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152798-657636.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152798-657636-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152798" class="wp-caption-text">对小朋友来说，更能培养耐性，写书法带来意想不到的正面效果。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14152793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152793" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152793-657631.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14152793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152793-657631-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152793" class="wp-caption-text">台东关山镇书法协会于7日上午在天后宫广场，举办了一场热闹滚滚的送春联活动。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
