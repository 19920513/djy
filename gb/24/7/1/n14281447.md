<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苗县金卓越社区选拔结果出炉 表扬绩优社区</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281448-692453-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>苗栗县金卓越社区选拔表扬。（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月01日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苗栗县政府每年办理社区选拔作业，激励社区发展协会自主发展，今年选拔结果出炉，有15个社区发展协会获奖，苗栗县长钟东锦颁奖表扬表现优异的社区，并感谢社区工作伙伴无私付出，肯定他们在社区扮演重要角色，是县府的重要支柱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281449" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281449-692454-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281449-692454-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281449" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬绩优社区。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“金卓越社区奖”为卫生福利部的全国大奖，可以算是社区的奥斯卡奖，因此县府社会处每年都会办理金卓越社区选拔作业，今年共15个社区发展协会获奖，有7个绩优社区即将代表苗栗县参加今（113）年全国社区选拔，其中后龙镇溪洲社区更要争取全国选拔银质卓越奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281450" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281450-692456-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281450-692456-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281450" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬绩优社区。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另有前往全国选拔参选铜质卓越奖的竹南镇塭内社区、铜锣乡竹森社区；参选卓越奖的为竹南镇佳兴社区；绩效组为后龙镇埔顶社区、三义乡胜兴社区、铜锣乡中平社区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281452" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281452-692457-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281452-692457-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281452" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬绩优社区。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1日苗栗县长钟东锦颁奖表扬苗栗县选拔获奖社区，立委邱镇军秘书以及三义乡长吕明忠、头屋乡长徐鑫荣、南庄乡长罗春莲、铜锣乡长谢昌年、苑里镇长刘育育、西湖乡公所秘书、苗栗市公所秘书等人皆到场恭贺，并且感谢社区理事长、志工们平日对社区辛苦的耕耘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281453" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281453-692458-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281453-692458-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281453" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬绩优社。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长钟东锦表示，社区是县府支柱，在第一线照顾在地的老人、儿少、妇女等族群，也协助将政府的政策延伸到每个角落，而苗栗步入高龄化社会，更需要社区理事长、志工伙伴们的支持，让退休长辈的生活更加精彩活泼，不仅能在社区唱歌跳舞，还能参与做劳作、绘画等课程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281454" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281454-692459-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281454-692459-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281454" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬绩优社区。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长指出，县府目前朝向一村里一关怀据点的目标迈进，同时也将落实一学区一日照中心的计划，让长辈们能够就近获得专业的照顾，走出家门与邻居互动、聊天并参与健康促进的活动，延缓老化之外，更要让长辈好好享受慢慢变老的过程，期许他们能幸福快乐地变老。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281455" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281455-692460-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281455-692460-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281455" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬绩优社区。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县府社会处说明，今日共颁发银质卓越奖1名、铜质卓越奖2名、卓越奖1名、优等奖3名、甲等奖6名、服务创新奖5名，依获奖等第颁发奖励金，绩效组优等奖颁发最高奖励金10万元，卓越组银质卓越奖颁发最高奖励金14万元，希望藉由比赛机制的设计，持续提升社区量能，带动全县社区均衡发展的风气，让其他县市的社区看见苗栗社区之美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281456" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281456-692461-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281456-692461-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281456" class="wp-caption-text">苗栗县金卓越社区选拔表扬颁奖。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
