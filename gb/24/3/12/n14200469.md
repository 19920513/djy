<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苗栗县文昌祠春祭 依循古礼庄严隆重</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200470-669163-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>苗栗县文昌祠春祭，依循古礼庄严隆重。（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月12日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苗栗县定古迹文昌祠3月12日举行<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E7%A5%AD.md#1">春祭</a>及文昌帝君圣诞祝寿庆典，依循<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E7%A4%BC.md#1">古礼</a>开中门、行三献礼并演奏八音雅乐，仪式<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%84%E4%B8%A5.md#1">庄严</a>隆重，展现注重文风晴耕雨读的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F.md#1">客家传统</a>。礼成后民众和学子纷纷参拜祈福，希望文昌帝君庇佑学业进步、考试金榜题名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14200471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200471" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200471-669164.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14200471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200471-669164-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200471" class="wp-caption-text">很多民众前来观礼。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200472-669165.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14200472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200472-669165-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200472" class="wp-caption-text">民众祈福。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12日上午，文昌祠<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E7%A5%AD.md#1">春祭</a>大典由文昌祠主任委员刘国昭、苗栗县副县长邓桂菊、苗栗市代理市长叶志航等人主祭，庙方执事人员遵循<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E7%A4%BC.md#1">古礼</a>开中门、敲钟、擂鼓、鸣炮，向文昌帝君、至圣先师孔子等神位行三献礼，并恭读及焚烧疏文。文昌祠还邀请邻近侨育国小学童表演传统客家布马阵为文昌帝君祝寿，不少乡亲也到场观礼膜拜，同霑文昌帝君福泽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14200473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200473-669166.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14200473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200473-669166-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200473" class="wp-caption-text">文昌祠内考生祈福金牌。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>坐落于南苗市场旁的文昌祠创建于清光绪8年（公元1882年），建庙已有一百余年历史，过去曾是书院、县衙临时办公处所、文人诗社，见证苗栗县治与文化的发展，1985年被列为苗栗县定古迹。文昌祠主祀文昌帝君，配祀制字先师仓颉及昌黎先生（韩愈），每年黄历2月3日、8月3日地方各界举办春、秋两季祭典。◇</p>
