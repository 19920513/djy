<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：春暖花开 到高雄赏九重葛及雨豆树</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211333-2403260805262378-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾高雄桥头糖厂多彩的九重葛盛开。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月27日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾采访报导）台湾南部天气炎热，摄影师宋永泰分享，喜爱高温的植物在高雄盛开，游客可以到桥头糖厂观赏多彩的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%9D%E9%87%8D%E8%91%9B.md#1">九重葛</a>，以及壮观的亚马逊<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E8%B1%86%E6%A0%91.md#1">雨豆树</a>。</p>
<p>台湾高雄桥头糖厂，是全台第一座现代化机械式制糖工厂，于1902年成立，至今已有122年历史，被列为高雄市定文化景观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14211306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211306" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211306-2403260736322378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14211306" title="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211306-2403260736322378.jpg" alt="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" width="337" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211306" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄桥头糖厂壮观的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E8%B1%86%E6%A0%91.md#1">雨豆树</a>。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14211312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211312" style="width: 347px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211312-2403260736282378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14211312" title="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211312-2403260736282378.jpg" alt="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" width="347" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211312" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄桥头糖厂多彩的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%9D%E9%87%8D%E8%91%9B.md#1">九重葛</a>盛开。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14211335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211335" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211335-2403260806452378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14211335" title="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211335-2403260806452378-600x338.jpg" alt="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211335" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄桥头糖厂多彩的九重葛盛开。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰说，桥头糖厂搭高雄捷运红线到桥头糖厂站即可到达，门票免费。糖厂腹地很大，可以散步、租脚踏车来骑，里面也有电动车出租，还有冰棒等美食，每到假日人潮都很多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14211334" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211334" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211334-2403260806412378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14211334" title="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211334-2403260806412378-600x338.jpg" alt="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211334" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄桥头糖厂多彩的九重葛盛开。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14211338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211338" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211338-2403260736352378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14211338" title="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211338-2403260736352378-600x329.jpg" alt="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" width="600" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211338" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄桥头糖厂多彩的九重葛盛开。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他表示，桥头糖厂里的主题景点“铁园迷城”正盛开多彩的九重葛，颜色有橘色、黄色、橘色偏黄、白色、白里透红、粉色、偏红、偏紫、紫色的花。你仔细看九重葛的颜色很缤纷，花朵交错盛开很美丽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14211337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211337" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211337-2403260736302378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14211337" title="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211337-2403260736302378-600x338.jpg" alt="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211337" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄桥头糖厂多彩的九重葛盛开。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14211305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211305" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211305-2403260736392378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14211305" title="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211305-2403260736392378-600x338.jpg" alt="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211305" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄桥头糖厂壮观的雨豆树。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰分享，主题景点“雨豆树广场”，种植喜爱高温的雨豆树，大约一百年前引进高雄，是原产地为中南美洲的亚马逊大树。青翠沉静的雨豆树，树形优美，树冠呈宽阔伞形，高大枝干令人惊艳，可说是“树大好乘凉”。他说：“桥头糖厂的雨豆树非常有名。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14211307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211307" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211307-2403260736372378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14211307" title="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211307-2403260736372378.jpg" alt="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" width="337" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211307" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄桥头糖厂多彩的九重葛盛开。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14211336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211336" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211336-2403260806432378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14211336" title="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211336-2403260806432378-600x338.jpg" alt="组图：高雄赏多彩九重葛及壮观的雨豆树" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211336" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄桥头糖厂多彩的九重葛盛开。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“桥头糖厂是很适合休憩的地方。”宋永泰说，糖厂有122年的历史，有制糖工场、日式木屋等建筑，当看到一些古物会有怀旧的感觉。</p>
