<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林草岭麝香木花海缤纷 迎接春游赏花热潮</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192286-667169-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>云林草岭地区，麝香木花正盛开，呈现一片浪漫的粉紫色花海。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）在云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E5%B2%AD.md#1">草岭</a>地区，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%9D%E9%A6%99%E6%9C%A8.md#1">麝香木</a>花正盛开，呈现一片浪漫的粉紫色花海，吸引了大批游客前来赏花。位于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E5%9D%91.md#1">古坑</a>草岭万年峡谷和草岭公路149甲土地公庙附近的麝香木林中，几万棵麝香木正在盛放，营造出令人叹为观止的景色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14192283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14192283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192283-667166.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14192283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192283-667166-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14192283" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%9D%E9%A6%99%E6%9C%A8.md#1">麝香木</a>花期约为两个月，开花后需进行裁剪和管理，才能维持花木的健康生长。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>，嘉义<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>乡的迷糊步道沿路，也是另一处麝香木花海的所在。这片花海还有茶园陪伴，展现了春日的缤纷与浪漫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14192284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14192284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192284-667167.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14192284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192284-667167-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14192284" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>乡的迷糊步道沿路，也是另一处麝香木花海的所在。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>空拍达人nina Chen表示，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E5%B2%AD.md#1">草岭</a>这片麝香木七、八成盛开了，一片粉紫，颜色相当美丽，觉得主人张惴腾对这植物很了解、很有心，把这路段种满，目前已经成林，花况很好，搭配粉嫩椿寒樱，还有浓浓的麝香味，她极力推荐大家一起来赏花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14192281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14192281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192281-667164.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14192281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192281-667164-600x461.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="461" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14192281" class="wp-caption-text">麝香木种植者张惴腾2023年为了扩大面积种植，脚还受了伤。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>澎湖来的游客首次目睹这种迷人花海，麝香木的细碎花朵，以及特有的香气让她难以忘怀。摄影玩家张先生分享了拍摄技巧，将麝香木花作为前景，搭配远处的人物，增添了朦胧感，使得拍摄作品更具艺术性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14192285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14192285" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192285-667168.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14192285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192285-667168-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14192285" class="wp-caption-text">澎湖来的游客首次目睹这种迷人花海，麝香木的细碎花朵，以及特有的香气让她难以忘怀。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>麝香木种植者张惴腾曾透露，多年来他已栽种了数万棵麝香木。受天候影响，今年的麝香木花期早了大约两周，但因种植量增加，开花量比去年增加好几倍。麝香木花期约为两个月，开花后需进行裁剪和管理，才能维持花木的健康生长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14192282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14192282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192282-667165.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14192282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14192282-667165-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14192282" class="wp-caption-text">草岭公路149甲土地公庙附近的麝香木。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>无论是云林草岭，还是嘉义阿里山，麝香木花海的绽放都为春游赏花增添了一抹亮丽的风景，也让游客们感受到了大自然的美好与浪漫。◇</p>
