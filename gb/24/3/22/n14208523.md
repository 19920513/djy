<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>俄罗斯500吨“悬岩”紧贴悬崖达10万年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208525-34-000-0-hanging-rock-035841-1080x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>西伯利亚南部西萨彦山脉的悬岩“挂”在半空中长达10万年。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月22日讯】（英文大纪元记者Anna Mason报导／赵孜济编译）这块50英尺长、500吨重的花岗岩摇摇欲坠地坐落在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%AC%E5%B4%96.md#1">悬崖</a>峭壁上，看起来随时都可能坠落。这个自然奇观隐藏在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%84%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF.md#1">俄罗斯</a>西伯利亚南部偏远的山脉中。</p>
<p>这块巨大的岩石在只有1平方码的表面上，位于埃尔加基国家公园（Ergaki National Park），似乎“悬挂”在拉杜日诺耶湖（Lake Raduzhnoye，也称为彩虹湖）深水区约半英里处的半空中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%AC%E5%B2%A9.md#1">悬岩</a>出现在距今大约11.5万年前的末次冰河时期，当时由冰川沉积在现在被称为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%90%A8%E5%BD%A6%E5%B1%B1.md#1">西萨彦山</a>脉（the Western Sayan Mountain）的地区。</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607370-3409-hanging-rock-02835841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607370-3409-hanging-rock-02835841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">西伯利亚南部<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%90%A8%E5%BD%A6%E5%B1%B1.md#1">西萨彦山</a>脉的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%AC%E5%B2%A9.md#1">悬岩</a>。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607380-3409-hanging-rock-0285241.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607380-3409-hanging-rock-0285241.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">这块巨石万年悬而不倒。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607374-34-09-hanging-rock-035841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607374-34-09-hanging-rock-035841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Stardancer／公共领域）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种奇特景象令人费解。在广袤的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%84%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF.md#1">俄罗斯</a>西伯利亚省，有关悬岩的传说比比皆是。根据当地神话，沉睡的萨彦是一位女战士，她的轮廓是由山脉本身形成的。她仰卧着，双臂交叉在胸前，是魔法区域的勇敢保护者，而悬岩是被众神夺走的她跳动的心脏。传说就这样开始了。</p>
<p>除了传说之外，还有由呼啸的风引起的裸露巨岩的像幽灵一样心跳般的振动。任何能够移动这万年巨岩的人，都将取代沉睡的萨彦成为和平与和谐的捍卫者，将她从永恒的职责中解放出来。</p>
<figure style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607373-3409-hanging-rock-02352841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607373-3409-hanging-rock-02352841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607375-3409-hanging-rock-02852841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607375-3409-hanging-rock-02852841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607378-3409-hanging-rock-023542841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607378-3409-hanging-rock-023542841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在过去的二十年里，人们对这块岩石最终可能松动和掉落的担忧有所增加。2012年，地质学家报告说，岩石中出现了新的裂缝，可能是由于自然侵蚀造成的，这可能导致它随时掉落。然而，到目前为止，还没有这样的事件发生。即使是该地区频繁发生的地震也没有使它滑落。近几十年来，这块石头在强风的压力下确实会摇晃，但后来停止了。</p>
<p>许多游客不惧风险，他们尝试过移走巨岩的运气，有些人甚至带来了千斤顶和绞盘，但没有一个成功。一个30多人的小组来到被称为Visjay Stone的巨大丘峦上，所有人一齐发力推动，但无济于事。</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607376-3409-hanging-rock-023852841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607376-3409-hanging-rock-023852841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607371-3409-hanging-rock-0235841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F03%2F14%2Fid5607371-3409-hanging-rock-0235841.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">悬在岩石堆上的巨岩。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>萨彦原住民和突厥民族的古老传说认为，只有时机成熟，沉睡的巨人才会醒来。当她醒来时，悬岩会从半英里的高空坠落，然后落入下面的冰湖，水花将洗净战士的脸，历史的新时代将开始。</p>
<p>游客可以徒步前往迷人的Visjay Stone，但只能在6月至9月之间，届时该地区严酷和无常的环境条件会有所缓解。通往巨岩的上坡小径全长7.5英里，徒步需要5到7个小时。</p>
<p>“俄罗斯最有趣的野生自然景点之一”、“真正的自然奇迹”和“疯狂的美丽”，这些是参观过悬岩的人们留下的感受。</p>
