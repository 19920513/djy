<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】圣派翠克节来了~爱尔兰棕色苏打面包</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203203-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>爱尔兰棕色苏打面包麦香浓郁，搭配黄油享用别有风味。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月15日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%B0%94%E5%85%B0.md#1">爱尔兰</a>棕色<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%8F%E6%89%93%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">苏打面包</a>属于快速面包。透过白脱牛奶（buttermilk）与小苏打反应产生气体，使面包免揉免静置发酵也蓬松。苏打面包外脆内软、麦香迷人，用手掰开或切片涂抹爱尔兰黄油享用十分美味。</p>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>2杯全麦面粉（whole wheat flour，224克）<br />
1杯无漂白通用面粉（unbleached all-purpose flour，120克）<br />
1/2茶匙细盐<br />
3/4茶匙小苏打<br />
1+1?4杯白脱牛奶（buttermilk），使用前摇匀</p>
<h2>料理步骤：</h2>
<p>1. 预热烤箱400华氏度。烤盘上筛少许干面粉。</p>
<p>2. 搅拌碗中放入面粉、盐和小苏打混合均匀。面粉中间留出一个空洞，倒入白脱牛奶（杯中留出约1汤匙涂抹面包表面）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14203204" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203204" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203204-2-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14203204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203204-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203204" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 用餐叉由外而内将干面粉拌入中间的白脱牛奶，约30秒。看不到干面粉后，将面团移到烤盘上。</p>
<p>4. 手沾一些烤盘上的干面粉，将面团聚拢成厚度约2英寸的圆形。用刀在面团上划一个深而大十字。表面涂抹刚才剩下的白脱牛奶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14203206" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203206" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203206-3-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14203206" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203206-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203206" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 400华氏度烘烤10分钟，再降至375华氏度烤25分钟。出炉放在架子上冷却，即可享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14203207" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203207" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203207-4-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14203207" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203207-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203207" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>料理贴士</strong>：有机面粉的吸水量不同，这里使用King Arthur非有机面粉。此外，混合面团的方法类似制作美式马芬，不需要揉面，以免增强面筋。</p>
