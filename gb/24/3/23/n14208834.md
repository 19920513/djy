<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】科幻兼小巧 2024 Hyundai Kona Limited FWD</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208874-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月23日讯】Hyundai的SUV/CUV种类不少，既有中大型的Palisade，尺寸小一点又有Santa Fe及Tucson，再往下就有Kona，以及最迷你的Venue，令人目不暇给。</p>
<p><a title="【新车试驾】科幻又小巧 2024 Hyundai Kona Limited FWD" src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/zh-TW/embed/1gj6qhl68rt12BQHivTDZT7e712r1c" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>这次是更新了的Kona，其实是全新设计才真，2024年 Hyundai Kona是第二代车款，从设计时已经决定同时推出Hybrid油电版及Electric纯电动版，这次测试车是汽油版Kona。虽然Kona的设计是以运动化为主，但这一代的设计加上了科幻元素，横跨前方的LED灯饰成为了主角，前头灯就像配角般藏在前方两角位，像极雾灯。车后方也是以同一设计语言，以灯饰条为主，再将刹车灯、倒车灯放到角落，整体就更为科幻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14208837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208837" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208837-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208837-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208837" class="wp-caption-text">2024 Hyundai Kona Limited FWD。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车内空间感也大，由于仪表板是萤幕，与中控台萤幕连成一片，所以整个中控台像“前移”了一样，所以前座的空间特别多。另一令人印象深刻的地方，是仪表样左方的一块灰黑板。仪表板虽是萤幕，但是Hyundai没有花太多心思在仪表的设计，简单的两圆设计，右圆的引擎转速也只是以“个位数”再x1000的方式表达，还好是使用指挥灯时，左右侧镜的影像会显示在仪表板，减少了盲点，也不让萤幕浪费了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14208838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208838" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208838-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208838-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_08-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208838" class="wp-caption-text">设计加上了科幻元素。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14208839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208839-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208839-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_09-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208839" class="wp-caption-text">头灯在前方角位。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14208840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208840" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208840-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_15.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208840-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_15-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208840" class="wp-caption-text">后灯也是以同一设计语言。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Kona的变速杆来自IONIQ 5，细小而粗的变速杆设在方向盘后的右下方，减省了中控台位置，而且使用也方便，毕竟在按下引擎启动，右手很快就可放到变速杆上，顺时针扭动便可到D档，逆时针是R档。只有两种驾驶模式按键则在中控台下方，旁边有座位加热及冷冻设定。方向盘加热有两段，冬日来说绝对是恩物。无线充电板也在中控台底部，每当关上引擎后，系统也会贴心提醒手机在充电，不要忘了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14208841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208841" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208841-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208841-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208841" class="wp-caption-text">1.6升涡轮增压引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>引擎方面有两种汽油选择，一款是2.0升直立四汽缸擎，可产生约147匹马力，并且有132磅呎扭矩，配备Smartstream智慧可变传动系统（IVT）。N Line和Limited型号，则装配了另一款1.6升引擎，配涡轮增压器，可产生190匹马力，并有195磅呎扭矩，峰值扭矩会由1,700转开始并持续到4,500转，1.6升涡轮增压引擎配备8速自动变速器，有平稳换档及迅捷的加速。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14208861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208861" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208861-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_36.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208861-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_36-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208861" class="wp-caption-text">车内空间感大。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这辆测试车最大问题是只有前驱，作为一辆SUV／CUV，没有四驱就是欠缺了最重要的东西，而且在测试期间，有天下着暴雨，只有前驱的Kona，在高速公路上的表现有点飘，不够实在；另外在市内上坡路，由于雨水多，转弯也有点打滑，相信装配有HTRAC-AWD的版本，应付坏天气时会表现更好。至于其他，只是Kona的车内储物空间少而已。总体来说，对Kona的表现相当满意。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14208863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208863" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208863-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_27.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208863-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_27-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208863" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆设计来自IONIQ 5。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14208864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208864-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_32.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208864-2024_Hyundai_Kona_Ltd_32-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208864" class="wp-caption-text">杯座收纳后成为大储物空间。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
