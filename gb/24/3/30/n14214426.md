<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>芦竹警扩大临检威力扫荡不法  净化辖区治安</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14214427-673052-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>芦竹分局于29日起举办全市性扩大临检威力扫荡。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月30日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）为净化治安环境、扫荡枪械、防制街头暴力、压制帮气焰及特定行业之不法行为，芦竹分局于29日起举办全市性扩大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B4%E6%A3%80.md#1">临检</a>威力扫荡，编组投入35名精干警力，针对辖区内“青少年易聚集滋事”、“高风险涉毒场所”、“帮派组合经常活动场所”及“帮派分子经营行业”之治安顾虑场所临检，严密执行清查行动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14214428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14214428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14214428-673053.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14214428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14214428-673053-600x387.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14214428" class="wp-caption-text">针对辖区内“青少年易聚集滋事”、“高风险涉毒场所”、“帮派组合经常活动场所”及“帮派分子经营行业”之治安顾虑场所<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B4%E6%A3%80.md#1">临检</a>，严密执行清查行动。（桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%A6%E7%AB%B9%E8%AD%A6.md#1">芦竹警</a>分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芦竹分局主秘陈永成指出，临检的主要目标在于发掘<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%BB%E5%AE%89%E6%AD%BB%E8%A7%92.md#1">治安死角</a>，揭露可能存在的不法活动场所。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14214429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14214429" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14214429-673054.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14214429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14214429-673054-600x357.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14214429" class="wp-caption-text">芦竹分局主秘陈永成指出，临检的主要目标在于发掘<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%BB%E5%AE%89%E6%AD%BB%E8%A7%92.md#1">治安死角</a>，揭露可能存在的不法活动场所。（桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%A6%E7%AB%B9%E8%AD%A6.md#1">芦竹警</a>分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同时对于进入芦竹分局辖内主要联外干道路设立临检点，执行闭锁式路检以达对外封锁对内扫荡”之效，同时进行区域联防。利用口袋战术，强化执法力道，展现芦竹分局<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E5%87%BB%E7%8A%AF%E7%BD%AA.md#1">打击犯罪</a>的决心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14214430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14214430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14214430-673055.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14214430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14214430-673055-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14214430" class="wp-caption-text">警方将持续打击不法帮派组合，以强力行动扫荡犯罪温床。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芦竹分局长林鼎泰强调，经历近期媒体大幅报导有关枪战之报导、人心浮动，警方强力执法以稳定社会治安氛围变尤为重要。警方将持续打击不法帮派组合，以强力行动扫荡犯罪温床。◇</p>
