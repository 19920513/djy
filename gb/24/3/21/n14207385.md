<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>紫色山城瑞里 民宿步道 宁静清新怡然自得</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207386-671039-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>高帝园茶业民宿（嘉义县文化观光局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月21日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>报导）花团锦簇的春天，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%9E%E9%87%8C.md#1">瑞里</a>紫藤花季也是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>县每年例行的文化活动之一，为了拓展文化观光，更为了减低人口老化等诸多问题，近年来县长翁章梁将施政方向作修正，一步步将嘉义县的发展由农业大县导向农工大县，县府团队齐心为嘉义县的发展翻开崭新的一页，水涨船高，相信将带动人口的移入及地区的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89%E4%BA%A7%E4%B8%9A.md#1">观光产业</a>快速成长，打算走入经营的店家或观光客，都有必要多了解嘉义的美及潜力所在。</p>
<p>嘉义<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%9E%E9%87%8C.md#1">瑞里</a>风景区位于嘉义县梅山乡，海拔1,200公尺左右，举目望去日出、云海、梯田与竹林等处处可见的美景，每年一至四月，为了欣赏这满山遍野的梅、李、桃、梨、山樱等等大自然的恩赐，许多游客迫不及待的或携家带眷或结伴驱车前往。</p>
<h2>嘉义瑞里旅游精选</h2>
<p>三月中、四月初嘉义瑞里紫色山城浪漫如画，除了赏花，瑞里还有咖啡、步道跟住宿点，嘉义县文化观光局特别推荐6间本地自产高山咖啡“青叶咖啡庄园”、“瑞里印象咖啡”、“茶壶咖啡语茶”、“100%咖啡庄园”、“晋扬欣茶亩田”及“若兰市集&amp;山坡咖啡”，以及4条166沿线人气步道，包括可看见一览无遗的茶园景色“太兴岩步道”、能走步道、赏萤的“圆潭生态园区”、宛如置身电影“卧虎藏龙”竹林里的“绿色隧道”，以及由绿色小栈旁石阶往上走，感受被竹林绿意包围的幽静的“瑞太古道”等。</p>
<p>除了赏花拍美照、品尝紫色山城咖啡，推荐游客一定要来瑞里居住一晚，以慢游感受瑞里的人文风情，享受山林的宁静及清新的空气，可入住多元特色的民宿包括“青叶山庄”、“高帝园茶业民宿”、“阿汉的家”、“朵丽丝森林民宿”、“山间茶墅民宿”及“许家茶园民宿”等，漫步在这座忘忧山城，感受瑞里一景一物的怡然自得！</p>
<h2>咖啡店</h2>
<p>1.青叶咖啡庄园：“水洗一哥-叶世远”的“青叶咖啡庄园”，不只在比赛中连年得奖，还创下台湾水洗咖啡最高竞标价，户外更有主人精心栽种的花草，坐在围栏边上，可将连绵群山一览无遗，享受山城的恬静。</p>
<figure id="14207403" aria-describedby="caption-14207403" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207403-671053-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207403-671053-450x338.jpg" alt="青叶咖啡庄园" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207403" class="wp-caption-text">青叶咖啡庄园（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14207402" aria-describedby="caption-14207402" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207402-671052-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207402-671052-450x338.jpg" alt="青叶咖啡庄园" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207402" class="wp-caption-text">青叶咖啡庄园（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.瑞里印象咖啡：来到瑞里，若要满足少女心就来“瑞里印象民宿”，在串串紫藤花廊下喝杯咖啡，扑鼻而来的浓浓咖啡香，让经过的旅客驻足停留，还有超美的玻璃木造窗景得来速服务可外带咖啡，民宿主人热情的问候，即使是来到异地也有了归属感，这是瑞里印象的热情之处。</p>
<figure id="14207398" aria-describedby="caption-14207398" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207398-671048-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207398-671048-450x338.jpg" alt="瑞里印象咖啡" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207398" class="wp-caption-text">瑞里印象咖啡（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.茶壶咖啡语茶：老字号茶壶餐厅推咖啡新品牌“茶壶咖啡语茶”，粉紫花串装饰从屋檐泄下，不论坐在窗内或坐在窗外木椅上，低头啜饮一口香醇咖啡都像走入日剧场景，对面的紫藤廊道更是热门打卡点，盛开时一串串花穗茂密的足以形成一条花瀑隧道。</p>
<figure id="14207399" aria-describedby="caption-14207399" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207399-671049-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207399-671049-450x338.jpg" alt="茶壶咖啡语茶" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207399" class="wp-caption-text">茶壶咖啡语茶（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.100%咖啡庄园：瑞太古道旁的“100%咖啡庄园”，开放式货柜小屋是吧台也是咖啡师的伸展台，而全户外露天座位旁栽种鲜艳整齐的花卉，挨着绿油油的茶园，一边喝咖啡一边欣赏优美景色让人好放松，处处能见对咖啡和美的坚持。</p>
<figure id="14207404" aria-describedby="caption-14207404" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207404-671054-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207404-671054-450x338.jpg" alt="100%咖啡庄园" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207404" class="wp-caption-text">100%咖啡庄园（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.晋扬欣茶亩田：位在“文峰游客中心”1楼的“晋扬欣茶亩田”，是从爱喝咖啡到开始学种咖啡，再成为有产销履历认证的咖啡农，以自然农法造就了咖啡带着水净香气、甘果醇香，让人体验到丰富的层次风味。</p>
<figure id="14207400" aria-describedby="caption-14207400" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207400-671050-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207400-671050-450x338.jpg" alt="晋扬欣茶亩田" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207400" class="wp-caption-text">晋扬欣茶亩田（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.若兰市集&amp;山坡咖啡：同样位于文峰游客中心2楼的“若兰市集&amp;山坡咖啡”，除了单品咖啡，也开发花式咖啡、花草茶、气泡饮等，还有热情的若兰阿姨指导如何摆拍美照！嘉义县文化观光局推荐到瑞里赏花外，更要点一杯咖啡尝尝这属于瑞里的独家风味。</p>
<figure id="14207401" aria-describedby="caption-14207401" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207401-671051-450x315.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207401-671051-450x315.jpg" alt="若兰市集&amp;山坡咖啡" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207401" class="wp-caption-text">若兰市集&amp;山坡咖啡（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>步道</h2>
<p>1.太兴岩步道：步道入口就在公路旁，视野辽阔，穿梭于茶园及竹林之间，沿途景色清幽雅致，适合亲子同游。</p>
<figure id="14207397" aria-describedby="caption-14207397" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207397-671047-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207397-671047-450x300.jpg" alt="太兴岩步道" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207397" class="wp-caption-text">太兴岩步道（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.圆潭生态园区：能走步道、赏巨石、赏萤和观鲴鱼，园区内有坡度平缓的无障碍步道，适合阖家来此散步。</p>
<figure id="14207396" aria-describedby="caption-14207396" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207396-671046-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207396-671046-450x338.jpg" alt="圆潭生态园区" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207396" class="wp-caption-text">圆潭生态园区（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.绿色隧道：两旁竹林整齐高耸媲美日本岚山的竹林绿色隧道，走在其中，犹如置身于电影《卧虎藏龙》里的竹林，沿途除了孟宗竹、桂竹、柳杉林外还有一片茶园，夕阳染红天际时更是唯美。</p>
<figure id="14207394" aria-describedby="caption-14207394" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207394-671044-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207394-671044-450x337.jpg" alt="绿色隧道" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207394" class="wp-caption-text">绿色隧道（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.瑞太古道：不同于绿色隧道，瑞太古道的竹林步道是沿着山径石阶一路往上，更能感受被竹林绿意包围的幽静，穿越林子后便是一片视野辽阔的山坡茶园。</p>
<figure id="14207395" aria-describedby="caption-14207395" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207395-671045-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207395-671045-450x338.jpg" alt="瑞太古道" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207395" class="wp-caption-text">瑞太古道（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>住宿点</h2>
<p>1.青叶山庄：外观是希腊风情的蓝白色，散发着地中海的异国氛围，主人家用心栽种的花卉植栽，大大提升游客的视觉享受！</p>
<figure id="14207389" aria-describedby="caption-14207389" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207389-671041-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14207389-671041-450x338.jpg" alt="青叶山庄" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14207389" class="wp-caption-text">青叶山庄（嘉义县文化观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.高帝园茶业民宿：像是一座隐身在山林里的欧风城堡，就像住进公主与王子的城堡里，一圆游客的公主梦跟王子梦！</p>
