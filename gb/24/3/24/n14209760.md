<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林、屏东山友交流 落实登山教育与环境保护</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209761-671744-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>云林、屏东山友24日上午前往林内龙过脉步道，参加山林教育活动。（云林山岳协会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县山岳协会与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>笠顶兴平山友协会，于24日在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E5%86%85.md#1">林内</a>乡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E8%BF%87%E8%84%89%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">龙过脉步道</a>展开盛大的登山交流活动。活动由山岳协会总干事陈永德带领工作人员，共同标示登山路线，以确保远道而来的山友能够安全舒适地享受登山之乐。</p>
<p>上午，共计11部游览车陆续抵达<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E5%86%85.md#1">林内</a>乡大气广场停车场，随后进行队伍整编及行前登山教育宣导。从济公庙出发，山友们展开了一场充满挑战与乐趣的登山之旅，并于中午前抵达坪顶村成功国小稍作休息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14209762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209762" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209762-671745.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14209762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209762-671745-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209762" class="wp-caption-text">约500位山友参与此次山林教育活动。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>山岳协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14209766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209766-671753.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14209766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209766-671753-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209766" class="wp-caption-text">共计11部游览车陆续抵达林内乡大气广场停车场，随后进行队伍整编及行前登山教育宣导。（云林山岳协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14209763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209763" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209763-671746.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14209763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209763-671746-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209763" class="wp-caption-text">活动从林内神社公园出发。（云林山岳协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14209765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209765" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209765-671748.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14209765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209765-671748-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209765" class="wp-caption-text">云林山岳协会以山会友活动，受到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>山友的肯定。 （云林山岳协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>在成功国小，两会山友互相联谊，以山会友，并分享彼此的登山经验。活动结束后，约500位山友义务参与了成功国小环境整理与清洁工作，并携带垃圾下山，以实际行动落实正确的登山观念与环境保护。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14209764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209764" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209764-671747.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14209764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209764-671747-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209764" class="wp-caption-text">会后约500位山友一同将成功国小环境整理清洁完毕，并把垃圾带下山。（云林山岳协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下山后，山友们顺道逛遍林内街道商圈，促进了当地商家的经济发展。林内街道商圈涌入了背着登山背包的游客，带来了一番热闹景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14209767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209767" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209767-671754.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14209767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209767-671754-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209767" class="wp-caption-text">林内街道商圈涌入了背着登山背包的游客，带来了一番热闹景象。（云林山岳协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云林县山岳协会总干事陈永德表示:“此次活动不仅是一场友好的登山交流，更重要的是我们共同落实了登山的环境教育。我们提醒登山者应具备自我行为责任，充分了解登山潜在风险，并制定完善的登山计划，确保登山活动的安全。”</p>
