<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿洪门春宴 豪杰聚会贺新年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195553-DSC00453-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿洪门致公堂3月4日欢度春宴，洪青体育会舞狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月06日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）“红花会豪杰，碧血染蛮夷。”波士顿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E9%97%A8.md#1">洪门</a>致公堂的每次<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%AE%B4.md#1">春宴</a>，总会响起这首荡气回肠的《书剑恩仇录》。在3月4日晚上的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%B9%B4.md#1">龙年</a>宴席间，洪门职员们再一次为来宾们献上这首“洪门主题曲”，赢得热烈掌声。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当晚在华埠帝苑酒楼，来自社会各界的近500人欢聚一堂，庆祝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%B9%B4.md#1">龙年</a>新年，传递美好祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14195561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195561-DSC00509.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14195561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195561-DSC00509-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195561" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E9%97%A8.md#1">洪门</a>致公堂3月4日欢度<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%AE%B4.md#1">春宴</a>，帝苑酒楼众宾云集。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14195554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195554-DSC00454.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14195554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195554-DSC00454-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195554" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿洪门致公堂3月4日欢度春宴，洪青体育会舞狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>洪门春宴</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>欢度新年</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">随着主持人翁伟健和陈国航宣布晚会开始，洪青体育会的5头彩狮跳跃进场，伴随着震耳欲聋的锣鼓声为来宾们献瑞。宴会厅内礼花绽放，彩狮们齐齐站立，赢得热烈掌声和欢呼。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">洪门主席胡振胜、黎亦置分别欢迎来宾，感谢大家出席春宴，并送上新年祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14195558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195558" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195558-DSC00486.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14195558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195558-DSC00486-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195558" class="wp-caption-text">麻州众议员黄子安（中）颁赠贺状，洪门主席胡振胜（左）、黎亦置（右）接受。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14195564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195564" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195564-DSC00477.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14195564" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195564-DSC00477-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195564" class="wp-caption-text">美国洪门致公堂总堂总理李志联来到波士顿，向春宴来宾们致贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">美国洪门致公堂总堂总理李志联亦带领多位总堂嘉宾，来到波士顿参加春宴。他盛赞这场宴席“高朋满座，可喜可贺”，向所有来宾致以祝福。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当晚的第一项娱乐节目，就是由洪门职员们献唱的歌曲《书剑恩仇录》。“新节奏乐队”在现场奏乐伴奏，更为表演增添气势。接下来，这个乐队的成员们连续演唱多首经典流行歌曲，使宴会厅内的气氛热闹不绝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14195555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195555-DSC00467.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14195555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195555-DSC00467-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195555" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾、职员及舞狮团体合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">宴席间，波士顿的洪门总理余丽媖携本地职员及外埠洪门义兄义姊上台敬酒，祝愿所有来宾新年安康。洪门还向施莱纳斯医院（Shriners Hospital）、广教学校、波士顿亚裔青年组织等公益团体捐赠了红包。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14195565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195565" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195565-DSC00512.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14195565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195565-DSC00512-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195565" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿洪门春宴贺龙年，职员们上台敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14195560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195560" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195560-DSC00496.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14195560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195560-DSC00496-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195560" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿洪门向施莱纳斯医院（Shriners Hospital）赠送红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>嘉宾致词</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>庆贺龙年</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">周一晚到场参与宴席的嘉宾包括麻州众议员黄子安（Donald Wong）、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任潘昭荣、中华公所主席雷国辉，以及波士顿地区各侨团及社会机构的代表等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14195556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195556" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195556-DSC00481.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14195556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195556-DSC00481-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195556" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉向洪门成员及全体来宾致以新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">雷国辉谈到，洪门致公堂历史悠久，比中华公所的100周年还要长得多。这么久的历史以来，洪门为侨社服务良多，尤其是培养了年轻一代“洪青”，并鼓励这些年轻人参与服务。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">黄子安作为洪门的一员，表示很荣幸与大家一起在这里服务数十年。他也代表众议院向洪门赠送了贺状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14195552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195552-DSC00442.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14195552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195552-DSC00442-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195552" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏参与洪门春宴，向来宾们致意。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14195559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195559" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195559-DSC00487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14195559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195559-DSC00487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195559" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏感谢洪门长期支持中华民国台湾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">廖朝宏还称赞，洪门与中华民国的关系已经延续了上百年，他们至今也仍然大力支持台湾政府和人民。为此，廖朝宏向洪门成员们深表感谢。◇</p>
