<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>健康扫除力“不生病打扫法”：窗户和家电篇</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211995-078ec71b3ef87bd6bba6de13fb644dc3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>难以清洁的窗框里充斥大量含有霉菌孢子的泥垢。善用工作手套，再狭窄的区域也能轻松打扫。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>编者按：</strong>“不生病打扫法”，亦即是预防生病的打扫方式，追求的是定期清除这些病原体，减少其数量，让疾病远离人体。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>各个空间的不生病打扫法</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>窗户周围</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14212000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14212000" style="width: 257px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212000-9f4d17fdf9d019eb065ff4c1cd51acc7.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14212000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212000-9f4d17fdf9d019eb065ff4c1cd51acc7-450x420.jpg" alt="" width="257" b="240" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14212000" class="wp-caption-text">窗户周围。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>打扫工具：</strong>黏性滚筒、工作手套</p>
<p><strong>清洁剂：</strong>小苏打粉</p>
<p><strong>POINT 1.</strong> 窗帘轨道上方容易累积花粉及PM2.5</p>
<p><strong>POINT 2.</strong> 从室外飘进来的霉菌及孢子，会附着在纱窗上</p>
<p><strong>POINT 3.</strong> 容易积聚雨水的窗框，常会滋生黑色霉菌</p>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>阻挡从外面入侵的“呼吸道病原体”</strong></span></h3>
<p>到了春暖花开的季节，粒子又细又轻的花粉和PM2.5的悬浮微粒就会从外面进入屋内，聚集在室内空间相对较高的地方，像是窗帘轨道上方。</p>
<p>堆积在窗帘轨道上的灰尘也比其他灰尘更干燥、轻盈，如果放着不管，人或物品移动时带起的气流就会扬起花粉跟PM2.5，让它们再度飞散到空气中。一旦被人体吸入，就容易提高花粉症、肺炎等疾病的风险。</p>
<p>打扫的时候，为了预防让上述混合着花粉和PM2.5的灰尘扬起，会建议使用兼具弹性及黏性的黏性滚筒最为适合。</p>
<p>除此之外，大家常以为霉菌只会在室内滋生，但其实霉菌原本生存在土壤中，而霉菌的孢子也可能会乘着风飘进室内，因此纱窗与窗框的清洁特别重要。</p>
<p>清洁纱窗时，请用黏性滚筒由上往下、统一方向地移动清除脏污。至于打扫窗框时，就戴着工作手套、用手指头擦拭。遇到比较难清理的污垢，则可以先将工作手套浸泡在弱碱性洗洁剂中，之后再擦拭，效果绝佳。</p>
<p><strong>POINT 1.</strong> 使用黏性滚筒清理窗帘轨道等狭窄区域的灰尘，特别方便。</p>
<p>担心打扫时会扬起轻盈灰尘？用黏性滚筒就能把它们一网打尽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14211993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211993" style="width: 424px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211993-c121c7347211cc013b0e640e8df3082c.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14211993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211993-c121c7347211cc013b0e640e8df3082c-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="424" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211993" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>POINT 2.</strong> 从纱窗上方往下、统一方向地缓慢移动黏性滚筒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14211994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211994" style="width: 170px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211994-1cf99c07ba6b35b2ba2c6ef48bc1c52c.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14211994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211994-1cf99c07ba6b35b2ba2c6ef48bc1c52c-450x727.jpg" alt="" width="170" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211994" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>POINT 3.</strong> 难以清洁的窗框里充斥大量含有霉菌孢子的泥垢。善用工作手套，再狭窄的区域也能轻松打扫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14211995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211995" style="width: 263px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211995-078ec71b3ef87bd6bba6de13fb644dc3.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14211995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211995-078ec71b3ef87bd6bba6de13fb644dc3-450x398.jpg" alt="" width="263" b="233" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211995" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>家电周围</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14211996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211996" style="width: 223px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211996-bf8d7c7405e78064820e224d29d3cc85.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14211996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211996-bf8d7c7405e78064820e224d29d3cc85-450x363.jpg" alt="" width="223" b="180" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211996" class="wp-caption-text">家电周围。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>打扫工具：</strong>化纤除尘掸、黏性滚筒</p>
<p><strong>清洁剂：</strong>厨房用中性清洁剂、消毒用酒精</p>
<p><strong>POINT 1.</strong> 空调滤网可能会成为霉菌的温床</p>
<figure id="attachment_14211997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211997" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211997-a535e33e35a026aba62c075432757e15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14211997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211997-a535e33e35a026aba62c075432757e15-450x216.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="216" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211997" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>POINT 2.</strong> 加湿器的储水盒容易滋生细菌与霉菌</p>
<figure id="attachment_14211998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211998" style="width: 263px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211998-842786245072e344542aabc25ec681a4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14211998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211998-842786245072e344542aabc25ec681a4-450x457.jpg" alt="" width="263" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211998" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>POINT 3.</strong> 电视或电视柜周遭常常累积大量的“尘螨灰尘”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14211999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211999" style="width: 244px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211999-a260d5cd5b81d8f7fea50802aefa86b5.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14211999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211999-a260d5cd5b81d8f7fea50802aefa86b5-450x481.jpg" alt="" width="244" b="260" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211999" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>在家电周围繁殖的病原体也不可忽视</strong></p>
<p>家里只要有使用冷气，就无法避免空调内部滋生霉菌。每次启动冷气时，滤网就会变冷，空气中的水汽会转成水滴附着在上面；过一阵子，霉菌就会在那个位置上繁殖。想清洁空调的滤网，需要用厨房中性清洁剂和牙刷来刷洗，晾到完全干燥后再装回去。</p>
<p>容易受潮的加湿器，或是附有加湿功能的空气清净机储水盒，常会出现混合著黑色霉垢和细菌的粉红色污垢，这些也要定期清洁。请使用厨房中性清洁剂清洗，再喷一层消毒酒精后静置晾干。</p>
<p>电视机等家电产品周围，也是经常堆积灰尘的地方，尤其是电线密集的电视机背后，常有大量“尘螨灰尘”。请用化纤除尘掸轻轻地拂去灰尘，清洁时小心别让灰尘到处乱飞。</p>
<p>最后还有一个就是大家容易忘记清洁的灯罩，推荐可以用黏性滚筒来清洁灰尘，因为它能确实服贴灯罩曲线，打扫起来更方便。</p>
<p><strong>POINT 1. </strong>拿厨房中性清洁剂及牙刷，将卡在滤网孔中的灰尘及霉菌刷洗干净。</p>
<p><strong>POINT 2. </strong>用厨房中性清洁剂与海绵清洗后，喷一层消毒用酒精，静置晾干。</p>
<p><strong>POINT 3. </strong>化纤除尘掸弄脏后就旋转杆子，将脏污面转入宝特瓶，每次都要用干净面清洁。</p>
