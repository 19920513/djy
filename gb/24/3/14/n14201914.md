<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>世华澳分会妇女节活动 推广海外台商精品选拔</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201921-70ec20dfeecb292c8d58a9ed0e6d1660-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>世华澳洲分会妇女节活动 协助推广第三届海外台商精品选拔。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月14日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA%E5%B7%A5%E5%95%86%E5%A6%87%E5%A5%B3%E4%BC%81%E7%AE%A1%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E5%88%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">世界华人工商妇女企管协会澳洲分会</a>于2024年3月8日下午在黄金海岸Broadbeach文化中心举办庆祝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E5%A6%87%E5%A5%B3%E8%8A%82.md#1">国际妇女节</a>多元文化交流活动，昆士兰台湾中心主任欧阳群应邀出席，并于活动中透过简报方式向在场百余位各界人士介绍说明侨务委员会2024年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AC%AC3%E5%B1%8A%E6%B5%B7%E5%A4%96%E5%8F%B0%E5%95%86%E7%B2%BE%E5%93%81%E9%80%89%E6%8B%94%E6%9A%A8%E8%BE%85%E5%AF%BC%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">第3届海外台商精品选拔暨辅导活动</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14201922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14201922" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201922-bf7b5c2d31af865c14901a079f7a48c2-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14201922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201922-bf7b5c2d31af865c14901a079f7a48c2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14201922" class="wp-caption-text">世华澳洲分会妇女节活动 协助推广第三届海外台商精品选拔。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14201925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14201925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201925-c42d6698bda3c37c66abac5ee56f0cc2-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14201925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201925-c42d6698bda3c37c66abac5ee56f0cc2-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14201925" class="wp-caption-text">世华澳洲分会妇女节活动 协助推广第三届海外台商精品选拔。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>欧阳群首先诚挚感谢<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA%E5%B7%A5%E5%95%86%E5%A6%87%E5%A5%B3%E4%BC%81%E7%AE%A1%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E5%88%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">世界华人工商妇女企管协会澳洲分会</a>的大力协助，让各界能够进一步了解海外台商精品选拔的内容，随后说明作业规划时程、企业报名条件与评选流程等相关注意事项，以及获奖后的辅导资源，鼓励侨台商企业踊跃报名参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14201924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14201924" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201924-cc122cedff1a301f2189f18cf5ac20d3-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14201924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201924-cc122cedff1a301f2189f18cf5ac20d3-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14201924" class="wp-caption-text">世华澳洲分会妇女节活动 协助推广第三届海外台商精品选拔。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14201923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14201923" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201923-f7d585eb059ec9aba39d4376a2b3ee57-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14201923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201923-f7d585eb059ec9aba39d4376a2b3ee57-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14201923" class="wp-caption-text">世华澳洲分会妇女节活动 协助推广第三届海外台商精品选拔。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侨务委员会为辅导海外台商事业发展、链接台湾优势、协助海外台商产品品牌及行销，2024年继续办理<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AC%AC3%E5%B1%8A%E6%B5%B7%E5%A4%96%E5%8F%B0%E5%95%86%E7%B2%BE%E5%93%81%E9%80%89%E6%8B%94%E6%9A%A8%E8%BE%85%E5%AF%BC%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">第3届海外台商精品选拔暨辅导活动</a>，期以共同通路整体行销概念，带动海外台商产业品牌发展；本年新增参赛产品类别至11类，以及更加丰富辅导推介措施，期透过台湾精品协助侨台商与台湾产业共荣发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14201920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14201920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201920-7d143e02504dff94d86faa51598ce0c2-600x849.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14201920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14201920-7d143e02504dff94d86faa51598ce0c2-600x849.png" alt="" width="600" b="849" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14201920" class="wp-caption-text">世华澳洲分会妇女节活动 协助推广第三届海外台商精品选拔。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“2024年海外台商精品选拔”报名自即日起至3月31日（台湾时间）截止，有意报名的海外台商企业可至活动专网<span style="color: #0000ff;">（<a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://taiwanprime.org/">taiwanprime.org）</a></span>及侨委会官网海外台商精品专区<ahref="https://tp.taiwan-world.net/"><span style="color: #0000ff;">TP.Taiwan-World.Net</span>）</a>浏览相关资讯，也欢迎透过“海外台商精品”选拔暨辅导活动LINE服务专线（ID：@TaiwanPrime），将有专人线上服务，并请透过海外台商精品选拔线上报名系统<ahref="https://event.taiwanprime.org/boutique"><span style="color: #0000ff;">event.taiwanprime.org/boutique</span>）</a>送件。</p>
<p>（昆士兰台湾中心供稿）</p>
