<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>开南大学全国反毒K-POP创意舞蹈大赛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193184-667360-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>国际扶轮3521地区第四分区社长与社员等捐赠开南大学新台币六万元。（开南大学提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月02日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E5%8D%97%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">开南大学</a>2日举办第三届“全国反毒K-POP<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9B%E6%84%8F%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%9B.md#1">创意舞蹈大赛</a>”，这群来自全国16 校11 队，65 名舞蹈好手及专业舞者“舞告厉害”，齐聚开南大学颜文隆国际会议中心竞技决选。</p>
<p>K-POP分为大学与高中组赛，众多好手齐聚一堂大展身手，带来动感青春、活力渲染力十足的精彩演出，吸引校内外同学们观赛。最后大学组由致理科技大学、实践大学、中国文化大学共组的SYM2获得冠军，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E5%8D%97%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">开南大学</a>的Cypress与KNUSTAR则获得亚军和季军。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193185" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193185-667361.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14193185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193185-667361-600x217.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="217" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193185" class="wp-caption-text">第三届“全国反毒K-POP<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9B%E6%84%8F%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%9B.md#1">创意舞蹈大赛</a>”，来自全国16 校11 队，65 名舞蹈好手。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14193186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193186-667362.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14193186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193186-667362-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193186" class="wp-caption-text">明台高中-Girl monster。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开南大学萧君华学务长表示，为提倡学生跳舞运动的发展，并宣示反毒的决心，在国际扶轮3521地区第四分区社长与社友们、耀胜电子公司热情捐赠学校新台币六万元，以及法律系陈炳良教授的共同协助下，让开南大学这项活动已经连续主办三届。不但让年轻学生挥洒青春魅力，更重视毒品对人们伤害，藉由现代舞蹈的活力，大家一起反毒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193187-667363.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14193187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193187-667363-600x284.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="284" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193187" class="wp-caption-text">桃园高中-CF twenty。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参赛的大学队伍，除了开南大学热门舞蹈社、啦啦舞蹈队外，包括致理科技大学、实践大学、中国文化大学、启英高中、育达高中、永平工商、华冈艺校、庄敬高职、复兴高中、泰山高中、嘉义家职、南强工商、桃园高中、明台高中、卓兰高中等，都参与竞技。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193188" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193188-667364.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14193188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193188-667364-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193188" class="wp-caption-text">启英工商 、育达高中、永平工商-N?vis。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14193189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193189" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193189-667365.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14193189" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193189-667365-600x302.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193189" class="wp-caption-text">开南大学-Cypress。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参与队伍舞风各异，舞林高手热情尬舞，展现K-POP舞曲中的多元类型，从现代节奏蓝调、流行电音、嘻哈音乐等的创意，尽情舞动嗨翻全场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193190" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193190-667366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14193190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193190-667366-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193190" class="wp-caption-text">国际扶轮3521地区第四分区社友们热情捐赠。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主办的学务处课外活动组说，感谢桃园市政府青年事务局的指导，活动能引导青少年从事正当休闲育乐活动并宣扬反毒理念。高中组竞赛总成绩的前两名队伍将于开大校庆当天一决高下。大专与高中的名次如下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193191" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193191-667367.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14193191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193191-667367-600x347.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193191" class="wp-caption-text">舞者宣示拒绝毒品。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
