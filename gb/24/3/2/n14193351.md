<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：那时候</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193534-shutterstock_2267022629-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>(Shutterstock)  
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 180px;">那时候，我住在乡下<br />
一个不到十户人家的小农村</p>
<p style="padding-left: 180px;">那时候，乡下很瘠瘦<br />
我们住在茅草平房里<br />
总是打赤脚走在泥土路上<br />
穿着粗陋破旧的衣服<br />
食物不足，加放番薯或什么杂粮<br />
可却身强体健，很有活力<br />
一大早便起床打扫庭除<br />
然后开始一天的生活<br />
大人下田，小孩子到处乱窜<br />
玩水、捉鱼、拈蜻蜓、赛手技什么的<br />
甚至帮忙放牛、下田等等<br />
每天总是土头土脸的</p>
<p style="padding-left: 180px;">大人都忙，哪有余力管？</p>
<p style="padding-left: 180px;">时光一溜烟过去了<br />
现在水泥丛林繁殖进乡间了<br />
各式各样的轿车奔驰着<br />
电视、冰箱、洗衣机、电脑等等<br />
各种生活用品唾手可得<br />
各方面便利多了<br />
但是同样的乡间，地方没变<br />
我还是喜欢那时候，怀念那时候<br />
至少以前没那么杂乱<br />
没那些诈骗、勾心斗角<br />
人们<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%AF%E7%9C%9F.md#1">纯真</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E5%AE%9E.md#1">朴实</a>，笑脸常开……</p>
<p style="padding-left: 180px;">我还是喜欢那时候，怀念那时候<br />
那时候，我小的时候</p>
<p style="padding-left: 180px;">At That Time Hsu ChiCneng</p>
<p style="padding-left: 180px;">At that time I lived in a little countryside<br />
Where was not more than ten inhabitants</p>
<p style="padding-left: 180px;">At that time, it&#8217;s very poor in the countryside<br />
We lived in the thatched cottage<br />
Always on the mire road we walked<br />
The worn-out clothes we put on<br />
Added sweet potato or food grains other than wheat and rice etc. while there&#8217;re no enough food<br />
But we were strong and in good health, full of great vitality<br />
Do washing and cleaning when we got up in the early morning<br />
Then begun the living of the whole day<br />
Ploughing and weeding the adults, running and chasing the children<br />
Play-water, catch fish, chase dragonfly, wrestling etc.<br />
Even graze cattle, help ploughing and weeding etc.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 180px;">The time past in a trance<br />
The jungle of the cement propagates into the countryside<br />
There runs all type of the bus<br />
Can be easily obtained every kind of the necessaries of life<br />
Just as the television, refrigerator, washing machine, storage system in a computer etc.<br />
Very convenience<br />
However, it’s the same of the countryside, no change of the place<br />
I like that time still, cherish that time still<br />
At least it’s not so disorder<br />
No those much swindle, no so much to intrigue against each other<br />
People are pure, simple, always smile on their faces</p>
<p style="padding-left: 180px;">I like that time still, cherish that time still<br />
At that time, the time I’m in my childhood</p>
