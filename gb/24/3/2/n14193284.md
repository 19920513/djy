<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】鹰嘴豆胡萝卜煎饼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193286-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>鹰嘴豆营养丰富，煎烤炖煮样样美味。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月02日讯】想在正餐中添加健康零食吗？今天分享的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%B0%E5%98%B4%E8%B1%86.md#1">鹰嘴豆</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C.md#1">胡萝卜</a>迷你<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%85%8E%E9%A5%BC.md#1">煎饼</a>，富含蛋白质、维生素和膳食纤维。吃不完的煎饼冷冻保存，微波炉加热风味不变。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193287-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14193287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193287-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193287" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>200g低盐罐头<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%B0%E5%98%B4%E8%B1%86.md#1">鹰嘴豆</a>，大约1杯；也可以自己浸泡生豆子并煮熟替换</p>
<p>1杯新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C.md#1">胡萝卜</a>碎末</p>
<p>1个大号鸡蛋</p>
<p>1/4茶匙盐</p>
<p>适量意大利综合调料</p>
<p>2汤匙玉米淀粉</p>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1.鹰嘴豆用流动的水冲洗，沥干水后压碎、混合其它食材。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193288-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14193288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193288-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193288" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14193291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193291" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193291-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14193291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193291-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193291" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.用1汤匙容量的饼干勺（可做20个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%85%8E%E9%A5%BC.md#1">煎饼</a>）将混合物逐个放入煎锅，煎至两面金黄，中间加锅盖煮1分钟。</p>
