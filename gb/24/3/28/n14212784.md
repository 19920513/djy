<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃教产邀请特殊及弱势学生 参与“真爱宝贝”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212785-672573-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市教育产业工会选在中坜区的“羊世界牧场”举办“真爱宝贝”活动。（桃园市教育产业工会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月28日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）今年桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">教育</a>产业工会选在中坜区的“羊世界牧场”举办“真爱宝贝”活动，在充满阳光的午后，有一百多位的学生、老师和家长们，一起<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B1%E8%A5%84%E7%9B%9B%E4%B8%BE.md#1">共襄盛举</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14212786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14212786" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212786-672574.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14212786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212786-672574-600x363.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14212786" class="wp-caption-text">“真爱宝贝”活动大家开心合影。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">教育</a>产业工会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动内容有：园区导览、认识可爱小动物、挤羊奶体验、DIY-吉羊彩绘等，让孩子们度过一个充实又欢乐的下午。桃园市教育产业工会感谢学校的带队教师和抽空前来的家长，陪伴特殊及弱势学生参与活动，让孩子们在课间也能喘口气，一起提前欢庆<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E8%8A%82.md#1">儿童节</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14212787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14212787" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212787-672575.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14212787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212787-672575-600x609.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="355" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14212787" class="wp-caption-text">DIY-吉羊彩绘。（桃园市教育产业工会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市教育产业工会自2011年成立以来，就集合所有会员的力量，共同建立了“公益救助基金”，至今已援助全市九百多个家庭，让突遭变故的家庭得以喘息，避免影响学生的就学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14212788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14212788" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212788-672576.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14212788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212788-672576-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14212788" class="wp-caption-text">认识可爱小动物、挤羊奶体验。（桃园市教育产业工会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市教育产业工会在维护学生受教权、教师工作权、关注教育议题之外，也将持续着“关怀、协助”的精神，继续公益的脚步！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14212789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14212789" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212789-672577.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14212789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212789-672577-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14212789" class="wp-caption-text">特殊及弱势学生参与活动，让孩子们在课间也能喘口气，一起提前欢庆<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E8%8A%82.md#1">儿童节</a>。（桃园市教育产业工会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动过程中，先让孩子们一一发挥自己的创意，完成吉羊彩绘的DIY作品，接着井然有序地跟着导览人员，逐一认识园区内的小动物：有水豚、兔子、羊驼、孔雀、天竺鼠等等，孩子们可以透过喂牧草等活动，跟小动物们开心地互动，导览人员也会利用有奖征答和团体活动寓教于乐，当然还有最令人期待的挤羊奶体验，看着孩子们紧张又兴奋的样子，真的好可爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14212790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14212790" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212790-672578.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14212790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212790-672578-600x365.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14212790" class="wp-caption-text">孩子们可以透过喂牧草等活动，跟小动物们开心地互动。（桃园市教育产业工会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动在孩子们愉悦的神情中圆满落幕，最后桃园市教育产业工会感谢桃园汽车客运股份有限公司鼎力赞助，免费提供专车载送老师及学童。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_14212791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14212791" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212791-672579.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14212791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212791-672579-600x369.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14212791" class="wp-caption-text">认识可爱小动物。（桃园市教育产业工会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14212792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14212792" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212792-672580.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14212792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14212792-672580-600x592.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="345" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14212792" class="wp-caption-text">喂牧草等活动，跟小动物们开心地互动。（桃园市教育产业工会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>感谢彭俊豪议员、魏筠议员亲自前来为大家加油打气，以及黄敬平议员助理蔡宗锜、劳动局专门委员张哲航、劳动局助理员范光濠、教育局特殊教育科施力中科长、自主工联刘庆裕执行长及陈德亮监察长的莅临，为活动增添光彩。◇</p>
