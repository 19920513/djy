<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>家庭消暑玩乐好去处 波士顿青蛙池塘开放</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277431-DSC03720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿公共公园的青蛙池塘6月25日新季开放。市长吴弭带着两个儿子一起来游玩。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年06月26日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）夏季到来，全美最古老的公共公园开放了备受欢迎的“青蛙池塘”（Frog Pond）喷水池，欢迎家庭一同游玩和消暑。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src="https://www.ganjing.com/embed/1gro5svanou7yNumW9PmrbYJ91871c" allowfullscreen></a>
</div>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">6月25日中午，波士顿公共公园（Boston Common）的青蛙池塘，在动感的音乐声和人们的欢笑声中迎来新一年的开放季。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277425" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277425-DSC03656.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14277425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277425-DSC03656-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277425" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿公共公园的青蛙池塘6月25日新季开放。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14277428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277428-DSC03661.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14277428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277428-DSC03661-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277428" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿公共公园的青蛙池塘6月25日新季开放。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14277426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277426" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277426-DSC03657.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14277426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277426-DSC03657-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277426" class="wp-caption-text">青蛙池塘禁止狗狗下水。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">青蛙池塘喷水池每天上午11点至下午6点免费开放，直到劳动节（Labor Day）关闭。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在今年开放的第一天，波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）带着两个孩子参与了庆祝，并下水游玩。现场有青蛙池塘的吉祥物，青蛙“弗莱迪”（Freddie）；还有Hood公司带来的冰淇淋、乐高公司带来的积木游戏等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277427" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277427-DSC03658.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14277427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277427-DSC03658-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277427" class="wp-caption-text">吉祥物青蛙“弗莱迪”与游客们拍照。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14277429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277429" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277429-DSC03703.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14277429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277429-DSC03703-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277429" class="wp-caption-text">市长吴弭与游客相谈甚欢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“欢迎来到全国最美丽的地点之一！”吴弭说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">青蛙池塘在夏季和冬季皆有不同风景：夏天是消暑喷水池，冬天则是溜冰池。池边还有旋转木马游乐场，门票为4美元。◇</p>
