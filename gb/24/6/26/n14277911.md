<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】榨菜冬菇红枣蒸牛腱～养生补气</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277920-6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>榨菜冬菇红枣蒸牛腱。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：夏天吃“蒸牛腱”滋补养生不过头、不上火，加上红枣可以补中益气、驱除湿热。这道简单料理一锅到底，对于忙碌的现代人，是再好不过的煲汤，而且配饭也很搭。</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">榨菜冬菇红枣蒸牛腱</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></h3>
<p>榨菜3 oz（切片）<br />
珍珠冬菇3 oz<br />
红枣8颗<br />
姜、蒜片、葱段各少许<br />
牛腱1磅<br />
苏打粉1/4茶匙<br />
开水适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味：</span></h3>
<p>蚝油1汤匙<br />
糖1茶匙<br />
蘑菇粉1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
花雕酒2汤匙<br />
粟粉2汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277925" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277925-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277925-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277925" class="wp-caption-text">调味。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法：</span></h3>
<p>1. 牛腱切成薄片，红枣去核，冬菇剪去蒂部，榨菜切薄片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277926" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277926-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277926-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277926" class="wp-caption-text">牛腱切成薄片。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 苏打粉加开水调匀，拌入牛肉腌半小时备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277927" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277927-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277927-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277927" class="wp-caption-text">苏打粉加开水调匀，拌入牛肉腌半小时备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 牛肉加所有调味料、榨菜、冬菇、红枣、姜、蒜片拌匀，装入蒸盘中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277921" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277921-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277921-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277921" class="wp-caption-text">材料铺平后，放在蒸锅内蒸18分钟，最后放上少许葱段装饰。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 材料铺平后，放在蒸锅内蒸18分钟，最后放上少许葱段装饰。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277922" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277922-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277922-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277922" class="wp-caption-text">牛肉加所有调味料、榨菜、冬菇、红枣、姜、蒜片拌匀，装入蒸盘中。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></h2>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
