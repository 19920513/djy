<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>六四35｜港警方加强布防 多国领事到维园“行吓”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264165-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>警方在铜锣湾加强布防。（余钢／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年06月05日讯】（大纪元记者唐健丰香港报导）4日是六四事件35周年，以往曾经点起烛光悼念的地方已经成为“禁地”，警方在铜锣湾加强布防。今日记者下午1时许在铜锣湾崇光百货外观察，暂时气氛平静，每隔百多米便有数名警员驻守，对出一条东角道停泊了3辆警车。本报记者在场拍摄期间，一度有警员上前了解。</p>
<p>下午约2时许，一名袋上插着白色菊花的年轻男子在渣甸街路口被警员截查，约一分钟后获放行。该男子其后在港铁铜锣湾站出口外停留，期间展示白纸，隔20步外有约10名军装及便衣警驻守，有警员一度注视男子的举动，该男子约近半小时后离开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264166" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264166-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14264166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264166-2-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264166" class="wp-caption-text">警方“剑齿虎”装甲车亦在附近一带戒备。（余钢／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264167" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264167-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14264167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264167-3-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264167" class="wp-caption-text">警方不时截查传媒。（蔡文昕／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>踏入下午，有零星市民到场悼念，其中社运常客王婆婆（王凤瑶），下午3时许在铜锣湾港铁站外手持鲜花，高呼“平反六四！人民不会忘记！”等口号，随即被大批警员包围同带上警车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264168" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264168-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14264168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264168-4-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264168" class="wp-caption-text">现场不断有市民被截停搜查。（余钢／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264169" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264169-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14264169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264169-5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264169" class="wp-caption-text">一名女士被带上警车。（蔡文昕／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一边厢，88岁的“旺角鸠呜团”吴伯在崇光百货外手持写上“念89!悼64! 悲痛的日子!慷慨的岁月!那些年维园烛光如火海!但现在 人杳 烛灭!”的标语。他展示标语后不久，也被在场警员警告，其后被带上警车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264170" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264170-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14264170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264170-6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264170" class="wp-caption-text">88岁的“旺角鸠呜团”吴伯在崇光百货外展示语后不久，也被在场警员警告，之后带上警车。（余钢／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警方傍晚时分仍在铜锣湾部署重兵，派出“剑齿虎”装甲车于区内巡逻，警务处长（国家安全）简启恩较早时到达维园，与执勤警员交谈，逗留约数分钟后离开。直至晚上8时许，记者目测有至少5名市民分别被警员截查和搜索随身物件，其中一名女子被搜出一些印刷品，包括数字式样的日历，其后获警员放行。</p>
<p>有市民入夜后在维园足球场外围以不同方式悼念，统统被警察截查，其中一名身穿哲古华拉上衣的男子，傍晚6时于维园内的“第二届同乡社团家乡市集嘉年华”被大会保安开伞遮挡并抬走，其间双方扰攘，引起市民及传媒拍摄。警员以橙带开路，将该名男子带上警车。据了解，男子同时持有写上“社会主义核心价值观 自由、平等、公正、法治、富强”的纸张。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264171" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264171-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14264171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264171-7-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264171" class="wp-caption-text">据了解，男子同时持有写上“社会主义核心价值观 自由、平等、公正、法治、富强”的纸张。（唐健丰／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>荷兰、德国驻港总领事及欧盟驻港澳办事处署理主任晚上约7时半到达维园，三人逗留约10分钟后离开，其间面对传媒提问，只表示到场“行吓”（Walking around）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264172" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264172-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14264172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264172-8-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264172" class="wp-caption-text">图左至右为荷兰、欧盟及德国领事。（蔡文昕／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264173" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264173-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14264173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264173-9-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264173" class="wp-caption-text">多国领事先后到维园。（余钢／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
