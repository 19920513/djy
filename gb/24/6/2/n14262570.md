<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“狮子来 Run” 障碍关卡活动热闹精彩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262574-686268-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>狮子来 Run-障碍闯关文化系列活动开幕式。（蒋义男提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）屏东县狮子乡南世村运动场，1日举办了一场充满活力与热情的活动——“2024年狮子来 Run-障碍闯关文化系列活动”。这活动结合体能与智力挑战，由狮子乡运动发展协会主办，理事长谢志强暨全体会员共同邀请大家参与这场盛会。当天活动现场人潮汹涌，气氛热闹非凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262572" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262572" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262572-686266.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14262572" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262572-686266-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262572" class="wp-caption-text">南世村女士们精彩的表演。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动从早上八点开始，一直到下午四点结束，期间设有四项极具挑战性的关卡，包括障碍闯关超越、陷阱制作、掷矛闯关及负重团体接力赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262573" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262573-686267.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14262573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262573-686267-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262573" class="wp-caption-text">团体障碍赛。（蒋义男提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些项目不仅考验参赛者的体能，更需要他们展现出高超的智力和团队合作精神。为了激励参赛者，本次活动特别设立了公开组和乡内组的奖项。公开组第一名将获得勇士奖杯一座及高达一万元奖金，而乡内组第一名则可获得勇士奖杯一座及奖金五千元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262577-686271.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14262577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262577-686271-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262577" class="wp-caption-text">非常漂亮的奖杯。（蒋义男提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次活动的亮点之一，是利用比赛行销在地的观光景点及农特产，特别是在地特产爱文芒果。所有报名参加的队伍，都获得了一箱爱文芒果，和一包爱文芒果干作为赠品，以感谢在比赛中表现优异的队伍和个人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262576-686270.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14262576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262576-686270-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262576" class="wp-caption-text">6月1日南世妇女会舞蹈表演。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了紧张刺激的障碍赛，活动场外还有丰富多彩的市集活动，共设有十五个摊位。这些摊位不仅提供了美味的食品和精致的手工艺品，更展现了狮子乡的丰富文化和农业资源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262579-686273.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14262579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262579-686273-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262579" class="wp-caption-text">狮子来RUN 陷阱制作。（蒋义男提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在活动中心会场，主办方还准备了多项亲子游戏区，让家长和孩子们能够一起参与并享受欢乐时光。树攀体验区吸引了不少勇敢的孩子，足球九宫格则让大家在游戏中锻炼了技术和耐心。而族语好好说活动区，则让参加者在娱乐中学习和传承了狮子乡的本土语言和文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262571-686264.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14262571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262571-686264-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262571" class="wp-caption-text">“射山猪闯关活动”。（蒋义男提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次“狮子来 Run-障碍闯关文化系列活动”，不仅是一次体能和智力的挑战，更是一场文化的盛宴。透过这样的活动，参赛者不仅展示了他们的体能和团队合作精神，还在互动中深入体验了狮子乡的文化魅力和社区的热情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262581-686286.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14262581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262581-686286-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262581" class="wp-caption-text">展示了他们的体能和团队合作精神。（蒋义男提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>无论是比赛的参赛者还是现场的观众，都沉浸在这场活动带来的欢乐和感动中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262575" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262575-686269.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14262575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262575-686269-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262575" class="wp-caption-text">狮子来RUN开幕式，南世青年会的表演。（蒋义男提供）</figcaption></figure>
