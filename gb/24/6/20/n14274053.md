<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>密西沙加Port Credit社区欢庆加拿大日</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274987-11-600x400.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月20日讯】<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz JxVs2d ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">“Paint the Town Red”是?Port Credit?社区、密西沙加以及大多伦多地区的免费加拿大日（Canada Day）国庆庆祝活动之一。</span></span></span></p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a style="border: none; overflow: hidden;" src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/video.php?height=314&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2FPaintTheRedPortCredit%2Fvideos%2F717237593699101%2F&amp;show_text=false&amp;width=560&amp;t=0" width="560" b="314" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></div>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz JxVs2d ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">“Paint the Town Red”活动自 2003 年开始，数万人从大多伦多各地前来参加，以引人入胜的加拿大国庆日游行开始。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz JxVs2d ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/20/n14274053.md#1/dragon-2" rel="attachment wp-att-14274978"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14274978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274978-Dragon-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="643" b="428" /></a></span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz JxVs2d ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">充满活力的“Paint the Town Red”活动，还提供现场表演、吸引孩子的活动、美味的烧烤美食，以及壮观的烟花表演。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz JxVs2d ChMk0b"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/20/n14274053.md#1/bison-transport-transportation-sponsor" rel="attachment wp-att-14274984"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14274984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274984-Bison-Transport-Transportation-Sponsor-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="643" b="362" /></a></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz JxVs2d ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">游行从 Stavebank Rd 出发，沿着 Lakeshore Rd E 行进，穿越 Port Credit 社区美丽的风景区，一直到 Seneca Ave。</span></span></span></p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/20/n14274053.md#1/lighthouse-float-1200x800" rel="attachment wp-att-14274979"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14274979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274979-Lighthouse-Float-1200x800-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="643" b="428" /></a></p>
<p>于加拿大国庆日7月1日，来大多伦多地区密西沙加的 Port Credit 社区，参加令人难忘的“Paint the Town Red”活动吧！</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/20/n14274053.md#1/b0b21cddb9a694353a0dd06f4a74b9df" rel="attachment wp-att-14274981"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14274981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274981-b0b21cddb9a694353a0dd06f4a74b9df-450x231.png" alt="" width="643" b="330" /></a></p>
