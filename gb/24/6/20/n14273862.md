<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>等待250天！ 鲤鱼潭水库出现锯齿堰溢流瀑布</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273863-689937-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>等了250天！鲤鱼潭水库出现锯齿堰溢流瀑布。（简玮廷提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月20日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾苗栗报导）相隔250天，苗栗鲤鱼潭水库在17日下午，蓄水量再度超出满水位，出现了溢流现象。18日清晨，蓄水率更是“破百”，比满水位多出3公分，让锯齿堰再现满库溢流的壮观景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14273868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14273868" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273868-689942.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14273868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273868-689942-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14273868" class="wp-caption-text">总蓄水量达1亿2600万立方公尺。（简玮廷提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鲤鱼潭水库锯齿堰溢洪道，等了250天水库满了，终于又现有如瀑布的景色。去年10月开始，鲤鱼潭水位持续下降，加上今年春雨少，到了4月初蓄水率一度剩下32.6%。幸好4月下旬锋面接续上阵，刚好下在集水区，终于在6月17日蓄水量超过100%，全台唯一的锯齿堰溢洪道再度重现壮观景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14273864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14273864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273864-689938.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14273864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273864-689938-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14273864" class="wp-caption-text">苗栗鲤鱼潭水库在17日下午，蓄水量再度超出满水位。（简玮廷提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>民宿业者简玮廷说：“气势非凡，经过二百多天，最低34%的蓄水量到17日满水位，山上水量持续流入水库，近期都可以观赏季节限定美景，而日出与夕照若搭配水量，更是一绝。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14273867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14273867" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273867-689941.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14273867" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273867-689941-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14273867" class="wp-caption-text">山上水量持续流入水库，近期都可以观赏季节限定美景。（简玮廷提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水充足才有的限定美景，鲤鱼潭水库主要供应苗栗县和台中市的用水，总蓄水量达1亿2600万立方公尺，是苗栗县内最大的水库。</p>
<p>鲤鱼潭水库管理中心表示：“今年最低蓄水量出现在4月初，目前的溢流量会持续多久？就要看后续的降雨情况。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14273869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14273869" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273869-689943.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14273869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273869-689943-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14273869" class="wp-caption-text">壮阔的蓝天与水库景色相呼应。（简玮廷提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5、6月几波梅雨助攻，还有上游台电士林堰发电引水挹注，水位持续上升。管理中心主任补充，根据历史资料统计，除了百年大旱之外，每年其实都会有满库溢流的情况。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14273866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14273866" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273866-689940.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14273866" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273866-689940-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14273866" class="wp-caption-text">全台唯一的锯齿堰溢洪道再度重现壮观景象。（简玮廷提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次的满库溢流现象吸引了不少游客前来观赏，大家都期待能多观赏几天这壮观的景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14273865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14273865" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273865-689939.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14273865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273865-689939-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14273865" class="wp-caption-text">鲤鱼村景色。（简玮廷提供）</figcaption></figure>
