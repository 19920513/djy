<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新海诚《秒速5公分》上映17周年重返大银幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274275-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-04-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《秒速5公分》（5 Centimeters per Second）剧照。（车库娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年06月20日讯】（大纪元记者杨逸帆报导）曾荣获亚洲太平洋电影奖的最佳动画片《秒速5公分》（5 Centimeters per Second）上映17周年即将重返大银幕。本片将于7月5日在台湾上映。</p>
<p>这部日本动漫片由新海诚导演执导，剧中纤细悲伤的惆怅情感是不少观众心目中的“胃痛”经典，而新海诚导演也于今年初在X（前Twitter）放上当年的未公开海报，海报上盛开的樱花树再度勾起广大粉丝的回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14274268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14274268" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274268-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-11.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274268-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-11-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="1067" class="size-large wp-image-14274268" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14274268" class="wp-caption-text">《秒速5公分》（5 Centimeters per Second）新海诚曝光当年的未公开海报。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14274269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14274269" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274269-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-12.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274269-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-12-600x857.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="857" class="size-large wp-image-14274269" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14274269" class="wp-caption-text">《秒速5公分》（5 Centimeters per Second）海报。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《秒速5公分》故事讲述远野贵树和篠原明里在国小毕业后就分隔两地。某日在大雪之中，贵树终于决定要去跟明里见面……</p>
<p>新海诚导演曾在访谈中分享自己在制作本片时，与团队造访栃木县JR两毛线沿线的岩舟站，拍摄了4000多张照片作为取景参考。新海诚导演还独自在当地待了一个月，实际观看火箭升空、目睹学生骑机车上课等场景，发现岛上的更多细节，对电影有着巨大影响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14274274" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14274274" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274274-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-06.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274274-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-06-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-14274274" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14274274" class="wp-caption-text">《秒速5公分》（5 Centimeters per Second）剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14274276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14274276" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274276-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-08.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274276-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-08-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-14274276" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14274276" class="wp-caption-text">《秒速5公分》（5 Centimeters per Second）剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14274273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14274273" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274273-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-05.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274273-20240620-5-Centimeters-per-Second-05-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-14274273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14274273" class="wp-caption-text">《秒速5公分》（5 Centimeters per Second）剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而《秒速5公分》的主题曲《One more time, One more chance》由山崎将义所演唱，许多粉丝皆表示一听到前奏眼泪便不自觉盈框；而配乐部分则由新海诚导演长期合作的音乐家友人天门操刀，至今仍是不少影迷心目中的经典。</p>
<p>想一品青春时间对于恋爱的憧憬，以及让人无限回味的惆怅尾韵，就不能错过新海诚导演的绝美代表作《秒速5公分》。</p>
