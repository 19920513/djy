<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>来义乡义林部落 首次烘烤芋头窑成功测试</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273778-689909-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>义林部落首次烘烤芋头窑成功测试。（新来义部落发展协会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月20日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）屏东来义乡义林部落新建的烘烤芋头窑还未正式启用，部落厨房志工们大家齐聚工寮，为了测试窑烤，准备一整天的烘芋头工作。早上，穿着传统服饰的ina （女长辈）和ama（男长辈） 们陆续到达，有的带来生鲜芋头，有的带着已经预先烘过的芋头，还有人带来中午要煮的食材。</p>
<figure id="14273790" aria-describedby="caption-14273790" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273790-689921-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273790-689921-450x600.jpg" alt="烤山芋干的工序很繁琐。" width="350" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273790" class="wp-caption-text">烤山芋干的工序很繁琐。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14273782" aria-describedby="caption-14273782" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273782-689913-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273782-689913-450x338.jpg" alt="60公斤的小山芋经过烘烤，缩水成为10公斤的芋头干。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273782" class="wp-caption-text">60公斤的小山芋经过烘烤，缩水成为10公斤的芋头干。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14273780" aria-describedby="caption-14273780" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273780-689911-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273780-689911-450x600.jpg" alt="这次准备了60公斤的小山芋要烘烤。" width="350" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273780" class="wp-caption-text">这次准备了60公斤的小山芋要烘烤。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>负责烘烤的赵钱山ama和大家一起祷告，祈求今天的芋头干顺利又好吃。他感谢祖先传授制作烘烤窑的技艺，以及大自然赐予的粮食。</p>
<figure id="14273789" aria-describedby="caption-14273789" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273789-689920-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273789-689920-450x338.jpg" alt="负责烘烤的赵钱山 ama 带大家一起祈福，祈求今天的芋头干顺利又好吃。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273789" class="wp-caption-text">负责烘烤的赵钱山ama带大家一起祈福，祈求今天的芋头干顺利又好吃。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14273779" aria-describedby="caption-14273779" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273779-689910-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273779-689910-450x338.jpg" alt="ama男长辈们先升火。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273779" class="wp-caption-text">ama男长辈们先升火。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>ama事先采收了60公斤的小山芋，因为过了采收期，他担心烤出来的芋头干会有口感问题。果然，在他的监控下，60公斤的小山芋经过烘烤，缩水成为10公斤的芋头干。</p>
<figure id="14273783" aria-describedby="caption-14273783" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273783-689914-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273783-689914-450x338.jpg" alt="先将小山芋摆好，避免重叠。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273783" class="wp-caption-text">先将小山芋摆好，避免重叠。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14273784" aria-describedby="caption-14273784" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273784-689915-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273784-689915-450x600.jpg" alt="ama男长辈用特制的木棍，翻动小山芋。" width="350" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273784" class="wp-caption-text">ama男长辈用特制的木棍，翻动小山芋。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动中，古万报 ama 担任解说员，介绍过去怎么烘烤芋头及相关故事。赵钱山专心生火，指挥大家依序放好大小山芋。接近中午，大家拿起篮子，将烘烤好的芋头干倒进“去皮”筛网上，合力去皮。</p>
<figure id="14273787" aria-describedby="caption-14273787" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273787-689918-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273787-689918-450x338.jpg" alt="芋头干倒进“去皮”筛网上，合力去皮。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273787" class="wp-caption-text">芋头干倒进“去皮”筛网上，合力去皮。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>挂在芒果树上的“芋头干去皮”器具是古董级的传家宝，由赵钱山的父亲建造，使用至今已近一甲子。在去皮后，ina们教大家如何挑选烘好的芋头干，因超过采收期，有些芋头干不太理想。</p>
<figure id="14273781" aria-describedby="caption-14273781" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273781-689912-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273781-689912-450x338.jpg" alt="将山芋过筛。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273781" class="wp-caption-text">将山芋过筛。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>挑选芋头干的过程中，大家听着原民歌谣，聊起部落务农和在台北工地做工的故事。中午时刻，大家一起享用排湾经典料理“腌肉树豆芋头佐刺葱叶杂菜汤”，族语称为pinu ljecengan ada vasa a valeng。</p>
<figure id="14273788" aria-describedby="caption-14273788" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273788-689919-450x381.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273788-689919-450x381.jpg" alt="腌肉树豆芋头佐刺葱叶杂菜汤" width="499" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273788" class="wp-caption-text">腌肉树豆芋头佐刺葱叶杂菜汤（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次活动让我们了解到，一包百元的芋头干，从采收、整理、分类、烘烤、去皮到拣选，都是一段艰辛合作的过程。希望未来能透过互动体验，让更多人了解部落的重要食农文化。</p>
<figure id="14273785" aria-describedby="caption-14273785" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273785-689916-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273785-689916-450x338.jpg" alt="加砂糖再炒过，这是芋头干的最后工序。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273785" class="wp-caption-text">加砂糖再炒过，这是芋头干的最后工序。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14273786" aria-describedby="caption-14273786" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273786-689917-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14273786-689917-450x338.jpg" alt="芋头干成品。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14273786" class="wp-caption-text">芋头干成品。（新来义部落发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
