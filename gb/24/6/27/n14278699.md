<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国中教育会考榜首突破纪录 表扬优异学子</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278700-691783-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>国中教育会考成绩激励表扬典礼。（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月27日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苗栗县国中生在国中教育会考表现亮眼，今年共有7位榜首，突破以往纪录。6月27日苗栗县政府办理“国中教育会考成绩激励方案”颁奖典礼，苗栗县长钟东锦表扬142位达到奖励标准的学子，总计颁发奖励金170万9000元，鼓励学生再接再厉、持续精进。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_14278701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14278701" style="width: 563px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278701-691784-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14278701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278701-691784-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="563" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14278701" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬优秀学子。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年国中教育会考达“5 A10＋，写作测验6级分”共计7名同学，分别为苑里高中赖以甯同学、建台高中江沂庭同学及郭子妍同学、君毅高中赖宇扬同学、大湖国中牛淳箴同学、照南国中张心又同学及陈岱亨同学。“5 A 10＋，写作测验5级分”者，计有君毅高中、明仁国中、建国国中、通霄国中、竹南国中及照南国中等6校共11位同学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14278703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14278703" style="width: 570px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278703-691785-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14278703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278703-691785-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="570" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14278703" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬优秀学子。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>27日苗栗县长钟东锦颁奖表扬142位考到5 A 5＋，写作测验5级分以上同学，并颁发5 A 10＋，写作测验6级分者各10万元奖金，其余则5000元到1万元不等，议员孙素娥、陈品安、张志宇、曾美露、卓兰镇长詹锦章、大湖乡长黄惠琴、苗栗市公所秘书、苑里镇公所秘书等人，皆到场肯定学生们的优异表现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14278704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14278704" style="width: 545px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278704-691786-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14278704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278704-691786-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="545" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14278704" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬优秀学子。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长钟东锦表示，满级分不容易，期许今日获奖的同学们在高中阶段更努力、更认真规划自己的未来，并且对父母、师长怀抱感恩的心，相信未来在学业以外，做人处事、应对进退方面也会表现良好，发挥专长创造精彩的人生，也期待未来有机会对苗栗也能有所贡献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14278705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14278705" style="width: 557px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278705-691787-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14278705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278705-691787-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="557" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14278705" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬优秀学子。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长指出，苗栗许多企业都看见学子们的优异表现，教育基金的捐赠越来越热络，未来将有更好的办法奖励学生读书，体育以外，也将酌量提升出国的补助，希望让孩子都有机会到国外交流，打开眼界，明年也将调整国中会考成绩激励方案的奖金，希望能够激励学子，带动好的读书风气，为自己的未来打拼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14278706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14278706" style="width: 557px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278706-691788-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14278706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278706-691788-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="557" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14278706" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬优秀学子。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县府教育处说明，5A10+人数较去年增加27%，5A学生比例与历年相较皆有不错表现，写作测验成绩5级分以上人数也提升4%；透过学习扶助等各项计划资源，协助学生建构基础学力稳定学习，将持续降低各校待加强比例，相信在亲、师、生的共同努力下，为苗栗培育更多优秀人才。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14278707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14278707" style="width: 540px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278707-691789-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14278707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278707-691789-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="540" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14278707" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬优秀学子。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14278708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14278708" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278708-691790-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14278708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278708-691790-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="359" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14278708" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬优秀学子。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
