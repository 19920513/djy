<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>菲律宾文化之旅TAYO盛大户外活动回归多伦多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14267224-1-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月09日讯】TAYO，在菲律宾语中意为“我们”，这是一个在多伦多举行的盛大户外活动，向加拿大其他族裔展示菲律宾文化，邀请各族裔一起分享菲律宾的骄傲，展开一场菲律宾文化之旅。</p>
<p class="p1">
<div class="video_fit_container"><a style="border: none; overflow: hidden;" src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/video.php?height=316&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2F61558796223790%2Fvideos%2F2319620874910942%2F&amp;show_text=false&amp;width=560&amp;t=0" width="560" b="316" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></div>
<p class="p1">2024年6月15日和16日，在多伦多北约克区的赖士民广场（Mel Lastman Square），共同体验在加拿大社区的菲律宾文化吧！</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/9/n14267218.md#1/df6b0fe5dc3798572340539d10afb126" rel="attachment wp-att-14267225"><img class="aligncenterwp-image-14267225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14267225-df6b0fe5dc3798572340539d10afb126-450x300.png" alt="" width="608" b="405" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">在这场庆祝加拿大和菲律宾友谊75周年以及菲律宾遗产月的活动中，每个人都能找到适合自己的活动！</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/9/n14267218.md#1/98110d3c373dc6c375028dd68464497f" rel="attachment wp-att-14267226"><img class="aligncenterwp-image-14267226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14267226-98110d3c373dc6c375028dd68464497f-450x295.png" alt="" width="607" b="398" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">到赖士民广场，体验紫薯茶点Harana All About Ube High Tea、用手在香蕉叶上品尝摆放的菲律宾美食Kamayan、公园里的面包店Panaderia吧。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/9/n14267218.md#1/rsw_1968h_1476" rel="attachment wp-att-14267238"><img class="aligncenterwp-image-14267238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14267238-rsw_1968h_1476-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="608" b="405" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">与Mama Sita一起烹饪，使用这个受欢迎的菲律宾食品品牌的调味料，创造加拿大的美食体验。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/9/n14267218.md#1/b59d6c13af56d65703bd23f875959675" rel="attachment wp-att-14267227"><img class="aligncenterwp-image-14267227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14267227-b59d6c13af56d65703bd23f875959675-450x295.png" alt="" width="607" b="398" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">遗产市场，提供菲律宾食品、商品和礼品。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/9/n14267218.md#1/e685ebded20bae1df013ad14a57214cf" rel="attachment wp-att-14267228"><img class="aligncenterwp-image-14267228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14267228-e685ebded20bae1df013ad14a57214cf-450x295.png" alt="" width="607" b="398" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">菲律宾游戏以及丰富的音乐表演，都将带给您独特的沉浸式享受！</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/9/n14267218.md#1/e7b9e9a5a9b0011f37fd34a125e09c12" rel="attachment wp-att-14267242"><img class="aligncenterwp-image-14267242" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14267242-e7b9e9a5a9b0011f37fd34a125e09c12-450x386.png" alt="" width="607" b="521" /></a></p>
