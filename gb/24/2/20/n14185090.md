<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>绽放的樱花结合伯爵茶 员林湖水古厝飘香</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185091-665155-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>石阶小径上的樱花。（谢五男／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月20日讯】（大纪元记者谢五男台湾彰化报导）位于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%98%E6%9E%97.md#1">员林</a>市湖水路422号（集兴宫旁）的“吴家古厝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>季”于2月17日登场，主办者是一名退休的女警吴淑女，有英式茶品及美丽的樱花吸睛之外，空气中更散发着吴淑女对父亲生前无私奉献的感念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185105-665169.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185105-665169-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185105" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>与古厝交相掩映。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185104" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185104-665168.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185104" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185104-665168-600x392.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185104" class="wp-caption-text">“樱”姿绽放。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185103" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185103-665167.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185103-665167-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185103" class="wp-caption-text">蓝天下的“樱”姿。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185102" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185102-665166.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185102" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185102-665166-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185102" class="wp-caption-text">绿意衬托下的“樱”姿。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动内容包括：奉茶、戏服穿搭、古典舞蹈表演、古厝蔬食热汤品尝，免费限量招待柠檬醋饮，以及英国下午茶套餐。中西合璧，古典与自然融合，精彩可期。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185100" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185100-665164.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185100-665164-600x469.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="469" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185100" class="wp-caption-text">首批(17日)造访吴家古厝的游客。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“吴家古厝樱花季”的发想，源自于吴淑女的父亲吴信夫。吴淑女的父亲曾担任湖水的里长多年，她表示，父亲在里长任期内，在百果山湖水路旁，沿路种植许多樱花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185093-665157.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185093-665157-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185093" class="wp-caption-text">吴信夫在湖水路的两旁种满樱花。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴淑女拿着1850年的银制茶壶，语带哽咽的说，她之所以在此冲茶，就是受了父亲的感召。她的父亲种花，种了二、三十年，常常免费招待大家赏花，不幸在两年前因种花跌倒，从此一病不起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185094" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185094" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185094-665158.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185094" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185094-665158-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185094" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑女拿着1850年的银制茶壶诉说对父亲的感念。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴淑女指着招牌说明“湖水幸福分店”中的“幸福”，就是取父亲名字“信夫”的谐音来命名的。每到樱花季节，看到樱花盛开，就会感念父亲生前的无私奉献，于是就有了“吴家古厝樱花季”的构想。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185101" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185101-665165.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14185101" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185101-665165-600x788.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="591" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185101" class="wp-caption-text">湖水“幸福”分馆是以吴“信夫”的谐音命名。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“吴家古厝樱花季”结合了英式茶点的品尝。说到吴淑女与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%AF%E7%88%B5%E8%8C%B6.md#1">伯爵茶</a>和早安茶的因缘，她表示，在4年前，疫情期间，她为了生病的女儿远赴英国，赫然发现女儿住所对面，正是戴安娜王妃及威廉王子夫妇居住的肯辛顿宫，于是常常造访，买了很多漂亮的英式茶具。同时，她在英国期间，到一家老茶馆喝茶，觉得茶品很棒，就购买回台分送亲友，也获得亲友的喜爱，这就开启了她事业第二春，现在她在台中拥有一家小小的店面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185097" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185097-665161.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185097-665161-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185097" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑女说明她与早安茶、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%AF%E7%88%B5%E8%8C%B6.md#1">伯爵茶</a>邂逅过的程。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴淑女表示，她从英国购得的早餐茶和伯爵茶，是她写信去说服英国的那家老式茶馆，经过三个月的努力，终于获得老板同意她在台贩售。她很自豪的说，这茶品从未卖到亚洲来，她是台湾唯一代理。</p>
<p>从集贤宫旁小径的石阶拾级而上，就可到达吴家古厝的三合院，沿途已有数棵樱花绽放，一股幽静迎面而来。记者踏上石阶不久，就遇到吴淑女的母亲，淑女的母亲笑脸迎人，对记者有问必答。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185092" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185092" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185092-665156.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185092" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185092-665156-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185092" class="wp-caption-text">石阶小径“樱”姿绽放。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴淑女的母亲表示，吴信夫投入相当多的心力在种花，美化环境上，甚至不辞辛劳，从雾峰远道运来樱花树苗加以栽种。</p>
<p>吴淑女的母亲指出，吴淑女是她的第二个女儿，相当的孝顺。她说，附近的古厝都是吴家宗族所有，不过近年有些宗亲变卖古厝，到外地发展，吴淑女便将该古厝买下，想加以整修运用，但遭到其父亲吴信夫的反对，认为没有必要浪费精力，吴淑女就顺从了父亲，打消整修古厝的念头。不意两年前，吴信夫摔倒长卧病榻，在离世前的十几天，吴淑女梦到吴信夫同意她整修古厝，并告诉吴淑女，以后那里将会非常美丽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185096" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185096-665160.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185096-665160-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185096" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑女与母亲合影。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴淑女的母亲甚至热情地带着记者去看吴淑女买的古厝，古厝前的庭院中也有两棵盛开的樱花，虽然古厝年久失修，透露出岁月斧凿的破旧，却是又一处充满古朴风味的秘境，这个秘境在前往吴家古厝三合院的石阶途中，一条不起眼的小岔路内，若没有淑女母亲的带领，游客肯定与其失之交臂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185095" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185095-665159.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185095-665159-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185095" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑女从宗亲手中购得的古厝。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185098" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185098-665162.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14185098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185098-665162-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185098" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑女拥有的古厝，庭院中的樱花。（谢五男／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴淑女不忘提醒游客，山上边坡陡峭，请勿攀爬樱花树或折枝拍照。同时，赏樱人潮众多，停车不易，请注意安全，共乘或错开时段上山最为好。</p>
