<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>满满正能量 会稽国中援助尼泊尔希望小学文具</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171049-662773-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>会稽国中援助尼泊尔希望小学文具。（桃园市会稽国中校长李文义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月01日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市会稽国中李文义校长、张慧珍主任、詹彦仪团长跟谢智谋教授相见欢在位于台北市民族实验中学的华人磐石领袖协会，将会稽国中亲师生募得援助<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%BC%E6%B3%8A%E5%B0%94.md#1">尼泊尔</a>希望<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%AD%A6.md#1">小学</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%85%B7.md#1">文具</a>，送达到谢智谋教授手中，在听取谢智谋的简报，真的充满感动与满满<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A3%E8%83%BD%E9%87%8F.md#1">正能量</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14171050" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171050" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171050-662774.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14171050" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171050-662774-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171050" class="wp-caption-text">家长会支持让孩子们心怀世界关注需要帮忙的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%BC%E6%B3%8A%E5%B0%94.md#1">尼泊尔</a>山区学校学生。（桃园市会稽国中校长李文义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李文义引用狄更斯名言，世界上能为别人减轻负担的都不是庸庸碌碌之徒。他说，会稽国中《国际慈善家》本位课程运用九年级综合领域课程核心素养实践能力，加上家长会支持让孩子们心怀世界关注需要帮忙的尼泊尔山区学校学生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14171051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171051" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171051-662775.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14171051" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171051-662775-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171051" class="wp-caption-text">会稽国中逐日登记募款所得。（桃园市会稽国中校长李文义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14171052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171052" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171052-662776.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14171052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171052-662776-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171052" class="wp-caption-text">会稽国中精准的替尼泊尔希望学校募得<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%85%B7.md#1">文具</a>、水壶、护唇膏、水彩笔、色笔、圆规，更替老师们募集教具，协助海外偏乡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%AD%A6.md#1">小学</a>。（桃园市会稽国中校长李文义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由詹彦仪童军团长、林淑雅家政老师通力合作，藉由尼泊尔的资源不平等课程现状，让会稽国中同学们见苦知福，老师们的解说让学生由感动化为行动，串起爱的接力与循环。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14171053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171053" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171053-662777.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14171053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171053-662777-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171053" class="wp-caption-text">老师们的解说让学生由感动化为行动，串起爱的接力与循环。（桃园市会稽国中校长李文义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14171054" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171054" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171054-662778.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14171054" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171054-662778-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171054" class="wp-caption-text">教育是翻转人生的机会。（桃园市会稽国中校长李文义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会稽国中《国际慈善家》将九年级规划慈善与教育结合的设计能力，透过自主学习讨论与华人磐石领袖协会合作，精准的替尼泊尔希望学校募得文具、水壶、护唇膏、水彩笔、色笔、圆规，更替老师们募集教具，协助海外偏乡小学、关怀海外弱势学童，启迪孩子们助人感动的人生观，藉由小谋老师助人的喜悦，很开心会稽国中亲师生能搭上助人感动送爱到尼泊尔列车。◇</p>
