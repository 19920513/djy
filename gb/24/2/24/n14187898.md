<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】时尚的美式越野 2024 Bronco Sport Big Bend</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187921-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2024 Bronco Sport Big Bend。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月24日讯】在年初时，与家人有事情需要到纽约一趟，除了要预定机票及油店之外，也需要租用一辆车，由于纽约步入冬季，要带的衣服也较多，所以这次决定租一辆SUV，价格虽较高，但空间大了，也有了四轮驱动，还是付出多一点吧。</p>
<p>在预定时，只显示出Nissan Rogue，这车最近也没试过，也是一个好选择，也就决定了。想不到，在到达租车公司时，往往也会有惊喜，这次也不例外，租车公司给了一辆同级的Bronco Sport，Bronco是Ford品牌下的越野车，现时有Bronco及Bronco Sport，前者像Jeep Wrangler，而后者是SUV，有点像Jeep Renegade。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14187903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187903" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187903-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14187903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187903-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187903" class="wp-caption-text">2024 Bronco Sport Big Bend。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Bronco车系最容易分辨是车头设计，双圆灯有点像Jeep，但Bronco的圆灯会另有横线向中间伸延，栏栅中间也有大大的“Bronco”字体；车尾闸也同样是有“Bronco”，反而没有任何“Ford”的厂徽。前下方有两个小圆雾灯，租用的Bronco Sport是全黑色，轮圈是五幅式设计，款式有点特别，轮圈中间是Bronco的徽章，是一匹野马，虽然同是马，与Mustang的不同，当然也与Ferrari的不一样，Bronco的是提起后脚的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14187904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187904" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187904-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14187904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187904-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187904" class="wp-caption-text">双圆头灯有点像Jeep。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14187905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187905" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187905-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14187905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187905-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187905" class="wp-caption-text">栏栅中间“Bronco”字体。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Bronco车身细小，但空间却不小，可放入两大一小的行李箱，乘坐空间也很足够，但后排的脚部空间也不错，只是车门很小，上下车有点不便。租用的Bronco是最低版Big Bend，仪表板是双圆实体仪表，加上中间小萤幕，没有十分很特殊的东西。Bronco Sport也用上了智能匙，开门开车也是不用拿出车匙，中控台萤幕为8吋，触碰控制，内里运行Ford的SYNC 3系统，反应灵敏，支援Apple Carplay及Android Auto，虽不是无线版本，但只要接上USB线，便可同时可充电。当接上手机后，也可用上手机上的地图Apps，就算车内没内置导航也没问题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14187906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187906" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187906-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14187906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187906-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_08-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187906" class="wp-caption-text">Big Bend徽章。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在萤幕下有一个颇大的储物空间，可放置手机，笔者就用来放钱包，手机则放在中控台底部，再接上USB线，所以空间非常实用。座椅是布质物料，因为前座有三段加热，在稍冷的天气非常有用。变速是圆盘设计，可节省一点空间，但要记消楚转动方向，由P、R、N到D档是顺时针，手刹车是电子式，拉起是启动手刹车，按下去是解除。Bronco Sport有一区恒温，也是足够有余，毕竟Bronco Sport是一辆小型SUV。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14187909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187909-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_30.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14187909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187909-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_30-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187909" class="wp-caption-text">内部空间却不小。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>既然Bronco是以越野为主的车系，所以标准是4X4四轮驱动，行车模式被称为“G.O.A.T”，全写是“Goes Over Any Type of Terrain），也代表Bronco可行走不同的越野路，模式共有五种，分别为Sand（沙地）、Slippery（湿滑）、Sport（运动）、Eco（省油）及Normal（一般）。这次驾驶主要是高速公路及市区，所以只试用了Normal及Sport，实际上差别不大。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14187910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187910" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187910-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_16.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14187910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187910-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_16-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187910" class="wp-caption-text">1.5升直立三汽缸引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Bronco Sport有两种引擎选择，但Bronco Sport Big Bend版只有1.5升直立三汽缸，配EcoBoost功能，化腐朽为神奇，小小的引擎可发出181匹马力，以及达到190磅呎峰值扭矩。配8速变速箱，可达到更细微的动力输出，又可省油。事实上，高速公路行车绝对没问题，但要猛力加速就不用想了，会有点吃力，所以要加速快，只有选择2升EcoBoost引擎版本了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14187912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187912-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_20.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14187912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187912-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_20-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187912" class="wp-caption-text">“G.O.A.T”模式。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14187913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187913" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187913-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_22.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14187913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187913-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_22-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187913" class="wp-caption-text">前座有加热功能。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在最后一天到机场的路上，终于下起鹅毛大雪，在一众汽车也减速的时候，Bronco Sport仍能给予笔者信心，就算是以平常车速行驶，也是依旧行车稳定，这也是四驱车受欢迎的原因，也能在有限时间回到机场，不会因此错过了飞机起飞。而且，最后300多英里路程只花费了约20美元汽油，算是非常节约的了！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14187914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187914" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187914-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_18.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14187914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187914-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_18-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187914" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板是双圆实体仪表。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14187915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187915" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187915-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14187915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187915-2024_Bronco_Sport_BB_19-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187915" class="wp-caption-text">萤幕下有一个颇大的储物空间。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
