<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2024年度俄州波特兰国际汽车展 更多的电车选择</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187967-2024_OIAS_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>俄州波特兰国际汽车展。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者李奥西雅图报导）每年初春时分，在樱花渐开之时，Oregon的Portland也份外热闹，各大车厂也聚集于市中心的Oregon Conventio Center，展开为期四天的车展。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187968-2024_OIAS_02.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187968-2024_OIAS_02-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-14187968" /></a></p>
<p>由2月22日到2月25日，从早上9时到晚上10时，展出车型种类广泛，包括轿车、卡车、SUV、跨界车、货车、电动汽车、混合动力车和超跑。当中不乏熟悉的名字，今年电车的比例也渐渐增多，Honda就在场内展出了新设计电车Prologue，有两驱及四驱选择，续航距离为296英里，另外，Honda也有自家提供的Home Charging Station。Toyota则展出了最新设计的Land Cruiser，而且是配置前圆灯的1958版，有着各项先进的越野装备，越野是没难度，动力系统是采用Hybrid系统，具有省油的好处。Toyota同时也展出了Tacomo的TrailHunter版，车灯、车侧及前轮上摇臂均有TrailHunter标记，车卡侧也有照明系统，车卡内也装有气泵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14187970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187970" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187970-2024_OIAS_04.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187970-2024_OIAS_04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-14187970" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187970" class="wp-caption-text">Honda Prologue。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14187971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187971-2024_OIAS_05.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187971-2024_OIAS_05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-14187971" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187971" class="wp-caption-text">Toyota Land Cruiser。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14187972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187972-2024_OIAS_06.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187972-2024_OIAS_06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-14187972" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187972" class="wp-caption-text">Toyota Tacoma TrailHunter。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同样是日系的Subaru，则展出了全新一代Forester，车身更为宽阔，动力系统仍然是2.5升Boxer引擎，变速箱经过调校，最大扭矩可更早达到。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14187973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14187973" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187973-2024_OIAS_07.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14187973-2024_OIAS_07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-14187973" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14187973" class="wp-caption-text">Subaru Forester。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，在展场其他品牌展厅也展出了不同汽车，Volkswagen展出了欧洲版的ID.Buzz，Kia有全新的EV9。要一尝电车的，场内也有室内的试坐体验，由专业的驾驶人员驾驶，并会介绍汽车特点。 </p>
<p>展览时间：2月22-25日（星期四到星期天）：上午9点至晚上10点展览地点：Oregon Convention Center777 NE MLK Jr. BlvdPortland, OR 97232</p>
