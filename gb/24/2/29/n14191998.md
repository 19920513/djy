<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>福乐中医新诊所酬宾 免费试用“螯合超能量共振舱”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14191996-IMG_5422-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蔡熹洛医师的福乐中医威尔明顿（Wilmington）新诊所开业，推出免费试用“螯合超能量共振舱”活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月01日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨报导）在波士顿华埠执业已久、备受赞誉的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A6%8F%E4%B9%90%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB.md#1">福乐中医</a>诊所（Bless and Joy Clinic / Wellness）”近期在威尔明顿镇（Wilmington）<wbr />开设新诊所，并推出每月一次周六免费试用“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9E%AF%E5%90%88%E8%B6%85%E8%83%BD%E9%87%8F%E5%85%B1%E6%8C%AF%E8%88%B1.md#1">螯合超能量共振舱</a>”<wbr />保健活动。日期为3月16日、4月20日、<wbr />5月18日和6月22日，需提前预约，网址：<ahref="https://blessandjoyclinic.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?q=https://blessandjoyclinic.com/&amp;source=gmail&amp;ust=1709312625732000&amp;usg=AOvVaw1HAU_XsFBjurUebUUHengD">https://<wbr />blessandjoyclinic.com/</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14191993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14191993" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14191993-IMG_5402.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14191993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14191993-IMG_5402-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14191993" class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9E%AF%E5%90%88%E8%B6%85%E8%83%BD%E9%87%8F%E5%85%B1%E6%8C%AF%E8%88%B1.md#1">螯合超能量共振舱</a>”是得到美国食品药物管理局（FDA）批准的尖端保健仪器。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A6%8F%E4%B9%90%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB.md#1">福乐中医</a>诊所提供针灸治疗、处方中药治疗、<wbr />螯合超能量共振舱治疗以及专业营养支持等服务。</p>
<p>驻诊医师<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E7%86%B9%E6%B4%9B.md#1">蔡熹洛</a>（Hai Lok Choy）为麻州执业<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB.md#1">中医</a>及针灸医师，美国自然医学医师，<wbr />台湾西医及<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB.md#1">中医</a>专科医师，有30年主治经验。</p>
<p>他目前分别在波士顿华埠和近郊威尔明顿镇的诊所执业。<wbr /><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E7%86%B9%E6%B4%9B.md#1">蔡熹洛</a>医师精通粤语、普通话、台语、英语。该诊所接受各种保险。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_14191995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14191995" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14191995-IMG_5420.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14191995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14191995-IMG_5420-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14191995" class="wp-caption-text">蔡熹洛医师是得到权威机构认可的执业中医师。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>螯合超能量共振舱免费试用</strong></h3>
<p>值威尔明顿诊所新开张之际，福乐中医诊所为了酬宾，<wbr />推出每月1次周六“螯合超能量共振舱”免费试用活动。<wbr />这是得到美国食品药物管理局（FDA）批准的尖端保健仪器，<wbr />能有效促进身体排毒和改善健康。</p>
<p>坐在这种共振舱内(类似露营小帐蓬，全身密闭在内，只露出头)，<wbr />使用者可体验穿透力达15釐米的能量共振。<wbr />这种保健仪器的效果包括加速新陈代谢，对抗老化疾病及癌症，<wbr />扩张血管及预防心血管疾病，减少疲劳，排除体内重金属等毒素，<wbr />减少脂肪和体重，以及调节荷尔蒙和美容抗老等等。</p>
<p>蔡熹洛医师说，很多人做完半小时的“螯合超能量共振舱”<wbr />保健之后，全身大汗淋漓，接下来几天都觉得身体很轻松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14191994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14191994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14191994-IMG_5405.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14191994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14191994-IMG_5405-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14191994" class="wp-caption-text">福乐中医诊所有着典雅、宁静的环境。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>两诊所交通便利</strong></h3>
<p>华埠诊所地址:65 Harrison Ave.,Suite 201B,Boston, MA 02111。看诊时间为每周一、三、五，上午10点至下午4点，<wbr />电话：617-451-7500。</p>
<p>威尔明顿诊所地址:420 Main Street, Wilmington, MA 01887。看诊时间为每周二、四、六，上午9点至下午6点。<wbr />电话：978-729-5857。</p>
<p>威尔明顿诊所也有着优雅、宁静的环境，<wbr />可以为患者提供非常舒适的体验。尤其交通便捷，<wbr />门口路边可免费停车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14191997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14191997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14191997-IMG_5429.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14191997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14191997-IMG_5429-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14191997" class="wp-caption-text">福乐中医威尔明顿新诊所开业，附近停车方便，而且对面就是通勤火车站。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开车者，也可从橙线或绿线地铁站North Station，搭乘紫色通勤火车Commuter Rail/Lowell Line,在Wilmington站下车，诊所即在车站对面。<wbr />预约网址：<ahref="https://blessandjoyclinic.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?q=https://blessandjoyclinic.com/&amp;source=gmail&amp;ust=1709312625732000&amp;usg=AOvVaw1HAU_XsFBjurUebUUHengD">https://<wbr />blessandjoyclinic.com/</a>。◇</p>
