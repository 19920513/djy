<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】怀旧八宝饭~年节甜食</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175658-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>自制八宝饭虽然费工时，却十分美味。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>一些地方庆祝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E8%8A%82.md#1">年节</a>的时候会食用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%AE%9D%E9%A5%AD.md#1">八宝饭</a>。过去的八宝饭使用猪油混合糯米，中间夹一层自家炒制的红豆沙。当中的馅料包括青梅丝、糖冬瓜条、葡萄干、瓜子仁、桂花酱等。由于有些馅料现在不容易买到，食谱的馅料与过去有些不同。</p>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>糯米饭：200克圆糯米、260克水、5克猪油（约1/2汤匙）、30克白砂糖</p>
<figure id="attachment_14175660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14175660" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175660-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14175660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175660-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14175660" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>豆沙馅：60克乾红小豆（浸泡12小时得到90克湿豆子）、60克白砂糖、10克淡味食用油</p>
<figure id="attachment_14175661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14175661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175661-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14175661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175661-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14175661" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>八宝馅料：25克核桃、20克金色葡萄干、葵瓜子仁和南瓜籽共6克、桂圆10克、40克无核枣、20克生腰果、20克通心干莲子</p>
<figure id="attachment_14175664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14175664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175664-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14175664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175664-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14175664" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桂花糖浆：1/2杯水、1汤匙白砂糖、1/2汤匙桂花酱、湿淀粉（1茶匙玉米淀粉混合1茶匙水）</p>
<p>餐具：2个容量1000毫升的深边碗、1个10英寸盘子</p>
<p>料理贴士：食谱中高压锅的烹煮时间从冒蒸汽开始计算。另外，豆沙馅不能炒太干，保留一些水分吃起来较顺滑。</p>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1. 称量食材，洗净。</p>
<p>2. 核桃用开水浸泡，剥去外皮。桂圆切碎。红枣对半切，去核。</p>
<p>3. 小锅烧开少量水，放入莲子后加锅盖以最小火煮20分钟。出锅沥干水，用牙签掰半待用。</p>
<p>4. 红小豆提前浸泡12小时（冬天）至用手一捏即碎。泡好的红豆放入小碗，加10汤匙水（没过豆子）以高压锅蒸15分钟。出锅用机器打成豆泥。开小火，不沾锅中加10克油和豆泥翻炒，直到水分蒸发使豆泥呈顺滑的稀泥状，加60克糖拌匀后熄火，待用。</p>
<p>5. 200克糯米洗净，放入其中一个深边碗，加水260克，以高压锅蒸12分钟。</p>
<p>6. 在另一个深边碗中涂抹一层薄薄的猪油，用八宝馅料摆出图案。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14175665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14175665" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175665-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14175665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175665-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14175665" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 余下的猪油与30克糖一起拌入蒸好的热糯米饭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14175667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14175667" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175667-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14175667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175667-6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14175667" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 取约一半糯米饭盖住图案，然后铺上所有豆沙馅，上面摆上剩余的莲子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14175681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14175681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175681-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14175681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175681-7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14175681" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14175682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14175682" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175682-8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14175682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175682-8-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14175682" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 周边摆上腰果和核桃，填入余下糯米饭，铺平。最后将切碎的桂圆铺在饭上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14175683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14175683" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175683-9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14175683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175683-9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14175683" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 用盘子盖住碗，高压锅蒸20分钟。煮好的糯米饭倒扣盘中。<br />
11. 煮桂花糖浆：小锅中加入糖和水烧开，将锅移开火灶，加入桂花酱拌匀。然后加湿淀粉（下锅前拌匀），边加边搅拌。最后重新加热糖浆即可浇在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%AE%9D%E9%A5%AD.md#1">八宝饭</a>上。</p>
