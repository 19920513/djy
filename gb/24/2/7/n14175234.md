<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多最热门宝石展3月8日-10日凯悦酒店举行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175236-1-600x400.jpg" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月07日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E7%9F%B3%E5%8D%9A%E8%A7%88%E4%BC%9A.md#1">宝石博览会</a>（Gem Expo）将于3月8日至10日在优雅的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%AF%E6%82%A6%E9%85%92%E5%BA%97.md#1">凯悦酒店</a>举行，为新手宝石爱好者和经验丰富的收藏家带来难忘的体验。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/7/n14175234.md#1/d207c283af817f6ed52426a8757f37df" rel="attachment wp-att-14175238"><img class="aligncenterwp-image-14175238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175238-d207c283af817f6ed52426a8757f37df-450x242.png" alt="" width="602" b="324" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">来自世界各地的近 40 家供应商，将展示蕴含各地丰富历史和文化特色的宝藏，种类有用于收藏的原始宝石和矿物，治疗作用的水晶，家具装饰品，以及在珠宝店难以找到的纯手工珠宝成品。</p>
<p class="p1">在多伦多<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E7%9F%B3%E5%8D%9A%E8%A7%88%E4%BC%9A.md#1">宝石博览会</a>上，您更有机会目睹珠宝设计师和工匠的艺术才华，其中许多人的创作赢得了国际赞誉。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/7/n14175234.md#1/1f5195a69a643c986a0920a40e8f15d6" rel="attachment wp-att-14175237"><img class="aligncenterwp-image-14175237" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175237-1f5195a69a643c986a0920a40e8f15d6-450x237.png" alt="" width="602" b="317" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">无论您是在寻找适合特殊场合的个性首饰，还是想为您的日常装束增添独特的风格，多伦多宝石博览会都提供无与伦比的手工珠宝选择，超越了传统商店中批量生产的产品。</p>
<p class="p1">
<p class="p1">准备好会见宝石切割大师吧！他们提供宝石和半宝石，包括红宝石、蓝宝石、祖母绿、海蓝宝石、月光石、碧玺、波罗的海琥珀、珍珠等等。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/7/n14175234.md#1/8f426dc9d3ad9709bde7409315ca8d66" rel="attachment wp-att-14175240"><img class="aligncenterwp-image-14175240" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175240-8f426dc9d3ad9709bde7409315ca8d66-450x246.png" alt="" width="602" b="329" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">从月光石的虹彩，到天然碧玺的炙热色调，与会者将有机会探索万花筒般的颜色和纹理，感受每种宝石独有的瑰丽。</p>
<p class="p1">无论您是经验丰富的收藏家，还是小荷才露尖尖角的新手爱好者，或者只是对宝石世界感到好奇，多伦多宝石博览会都能满足每个人的需求。</p>
<p class="p1">这不仅是一场珠饰秀，更是一场令人难忘的美丽的文化和慈善庆典。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/7/n14175234.md#1/f225cfb774c0eb72d639e50c0ab96a49" rel="attachment wp-att-14175239"><img class="aligncenterwp-image-14175239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175239-f225cfb774c0eb72d639e50c0ab96a49-450x230.png" alt="" width="603" b="308" /></a></p>
