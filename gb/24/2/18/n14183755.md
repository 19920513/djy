<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】沙茶西芹炒鲜鱿 一吃就停不下来</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183769-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>沙茶西芹炒鲜鱿。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E8%8C%B6.md#1">沙茶</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%8A%B9.md#1">西芹</a>炒<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E9%B1%BF.md#1">鲜鱿</a>”这一道菜是经典热炒菜，是家常菜，也是很多厨师的私房菜，因为调味的比例稍有点不一样，吃起来就好像不一样。可是，重点是大部分都非常好吃！而且，让你一口接一口停不下来。</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E8%8C%B6.md#1">沙茶</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%8A%B9.md#1">西芹</a>炒<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E9%B1%BF.md#1">鲜鱿</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>西芹12 oz<br />
甜红椒3 oz<br />
鲜鱿10 oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉少许<br />
沙茶酱2汤匙<br />
水3汤匙<br />
盐少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183772" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183772" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183772-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183772" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183772-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183772" class="wp-caption-text">材料（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>鸡汤1/4杯<br />
糖1/2茶匙<br />
盐1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉1/2茶匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 西芹刨去少许根，切成小片。甜红椒切块。鲜鱿洗净，用少许姜汁酒腌10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183773" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183773-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183773-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183773" class="wp-caption-text">西芹刨去少许根，切成小片。甜红椒切块。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 锅中烧热水，放入鲜鱿烫1分钟，沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183774" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183774" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183774-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183774" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183774-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183774" class="wp-caption-text">锅中烧热水，放入鲜鱿烫1分钟，沥干备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜、沙茶酱，放入西芹、红椒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183775" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183775" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183775-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183775-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183775" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜、沙茶酱，放入西芹、红椒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 加入3汤匙水及少许盐，炒1分钟。接着再放回鲜鱿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183777" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183777-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183777" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183777-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183777" class="wp-caption-text">加入3汤匙水及少许盐，炒1分钟。接着再放回鲜鱿。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 搅匀调味汁，一边倒入一边翻炒均匀后便可上碟。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183778" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183778" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183778-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183778-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183778" class="wp-caption-text">搅匀调味汁，一边倒入一边翻炒均匀后便可上碟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【主厨简介】</span></h3>
<p>经历：</p>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
