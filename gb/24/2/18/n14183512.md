<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】素食腐皮卷 香酥脆嫩 煮1次变2道菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183537-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香酥脆嫩的腐皮卷，煮一次变成两道菜的聪明料理法，赶快学起来！（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】</strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%90%E7%9A%AE.md#1">腐皮</a>卷里头总是包裹着各种蔬菜和菇类，油煎捞起，趁热一口咬下，酥香嫩脆的口感，不只是营养满满，连味蕾也马上被满足。尤其是这一道菜，还有2个优点，就是步骤简单，快速<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%81%E6%97%B6.md#1">省时</a>，而且煮1次还可以变化成2道菜，家常菜或宴客菜都可以，一举两得，怎么能不赶快学起来呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183526" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183526" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183526-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183526" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183526-9-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183526" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%90%E7%9A%AE.md#1">腐皮</a>卷用油煎成金黄色，单吃就很好吃，而且营养丰富。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%A0%E9%A3%9F.md#1">素食</a>腐皮卷</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【主材料】</span></h3>
<p>鲜腐皮16oz（约10块）<br />
木耳4oz<br />
杏鲍菇4oz<br />
秀珍菇4oz<br />
冬菇4oz<br />
冬笋4oz<br />
红萝卜4oz<br />
荷兰豆4oz</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>素上汤2杯<br />
素蚝油2汤匙<br />
糖1茶匙<br />
鸡粉1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉2汤匙（加开水勾芡用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183528" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183528" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183528-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183528" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183528-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183528" class="wp-caption-text">调味品。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【其它材料】</span></h3>
<p>面粉适量<br />
西兰花10朵</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 先把腐皮以外的所有材料切成幼粒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183531" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183531-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183531-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183531" class="wp-caption-text">先把材料切成幼粒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 用素上汤加调味料煮切好的材料，备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183532" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183532-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183532-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183532" class="wp-caption-text">用素上汤加调味料煮切好的材料，备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 大腐皮一开六小块，并准备好面粉水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183533" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183533-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183533-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183533" class="wp-caption-text">大腐皮一开六小块，并准备好面粉水。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 取一块腐皮，尖向上，铺上一小匙饀料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183534-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183534-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183534" class="wp-caption-text">取一块腐皮，尖向上，铺上一小匙饀料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 两边向内折，再从下往上卷成长条型，用面粉水黏口。用上述步骤做好所有腐皮卷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183535-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183535-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183535" class="wp-caption-text">两边向内折，再从下往上卷成长条型，用面粉水黏口。用上述步骤做好所有腐皮卷。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 腐皮卷用油煎成金黄色，直接可以食用，很脆口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183536-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183536-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183536" class="wp-caption-text">腐皮卷用油煎成金黄色，直接可以食用，很脆口。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>另一种吃法：把煎好的腐皮卷放在深碟中，加入素上汤，放入蒸笼蒸约3分钟，取出之后，倒出汁液，用粟粉水勾成薄芡。腐皮卷排放在碟上，西兰花围在旁边，淋上芡汁便成。◇</strong></span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【主厨简介】</span></h3>
