<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】寒天凤梨果冻～冰过更好吃！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183814-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>寒天果冻（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%92%E5%A4%A9.md#1">寒天</a>做的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%9C%E5%86%BB.md#1">果冻</a>Ｑ弹爽口，而且是减肥圣品，加上微酸香甜的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%A4%E6%A2%A8.md#1">凤梨</a>汁和果肉，真的很吸引人！在天气不稳定的春天里，自己动手做，放在冰箱里，想吃就有，方便、卫生又省钱。</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%92%E5%A4%A9.md#1">寒天</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%A4%E6%A2%A8.md#1">凤梨</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%9C%E5%86%BB.md#1">果冻</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>凤梨汁270克<br />
凤梨小块适量<br />
水230克<br />
寒天4克<br />
砂糖30克<br />
蓝莓适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183806" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183806-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183806-1-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183806" class="wp-caption-text">寒天（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 寒天条放入冷水中浸软。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183807" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183807-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183807-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183807" class="wp-caption-text">寒天条放入冷水中浸软（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 凤梨去皮之后切小块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183808" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183808-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183808-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183808" class="wp-caption-text">凤梨去皮之后切小块。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 留一些凤梨块备用，其它的凤梨块放入搅拌机打碎，过筛后取需要的凤梨汁份量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183809" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183809-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183809-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183809" class="wp-caption-text">留一些凤梨块备用，其它的凤梨块放入搅拌机打碎，过筛后取需要的凤梨汁份量。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 水加入寒天和砂糖后，一边加热，一边搅拌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183810" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183810-13072d7e2acbc87a0f4c5fef9a3dbbde.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183810-13072d7e2acbc87a0f4c5fef9a3dbbde-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183810" class="wp-caption-text">水加入寒天和砂糖后，一边加热，一边搅拌。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 趁寒天还未凝结，加入凤梨汁搅匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183811" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183811-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183811" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183811-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183811" class="wp-caption-text">趁寒天还未凝结，加入凤梨汁搅匀。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 准备几个小容器，放入凤梨小块和蓝莓，然后加入寒天凤梨汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183812" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183812" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183812-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183812-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183812" class="wp-caption-text">准备几个小容器，放入凤梨小块和蓝莓，然后加入寒天凤梨汁。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 果冻放在室温下便会凝结，或者也可以放入冰箱冷藏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183813" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183813" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183813-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183813" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183813-8-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183813" class="wp-caption-text">果冻放在室温下便会凝结，或者也可以放入冰箱冷藏。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>食谱说明：</strong></span></h3>
<p>? 如果是用寒天条，就先要用冷水浸软才好用。如果是用寒天粉，直接加入水中煮溶便可。<br />
? 寒天比鱼胶粉的凝结强度高出三倍，所以用的份量不用太多。<br />
? 日本人喜欢用寒天做甜品。◇</p>

3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
