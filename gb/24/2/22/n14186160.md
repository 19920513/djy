<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>密市国际中心举办第29届春季度假屋生活展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186269-1-600x400.png" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月22日讯】春季来临，很多人开始憧憬夏日的度假屋生活。准备好在第29届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%AD%A3%E5%BA%A6%E5%81%87%E5%B1%8B%E7%94%9F%E6%B4%BB%E5%B1%95.md#1">春季度假屋生活展</a>（Spring Cottage Life Show），与建筑商、承包商和参展商会面，开启2024年度假屋生活之旅吧！</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/22/n14186160.md#1/1-2868" rel="attachment wp-att-14186239"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14186239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186239-1-450x408.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="408" /></a></p>
<p class="p1"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%AD%A3%E5%BA%A6%E5%81%87%E5%B1%8B%E7%94%9F%E6%B4%BB%E5%B1%95.md#1">春季度假屋生活展</a>在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%86%E8%A5%BF%E6%B2%99%E5%8A%A0.md#1">密西沙加</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">国际中心</a>举办，从3月21日至24日，为期4天。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/22/n14186160.md#1/cl_workshop" rel="attachment wp-att-14186242"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14186242" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186242-CL_Workshop-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">众多参展商将介绍度假屋相关产品，包括船只、船坞、建造商、承包商、绿色方案、装饰与装修、水上玩具。现场还有美食和娱乐节目。度假屋厨房全天提供美味的娱乐创意和烹饪示范。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/22/n14186160.md#1/clss22-49-600x400" rel="attachment wp-att-14186243"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14186243" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186243-CLSS22-49-600x400-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">正在寻找有关购买、出售和享受度假屋的一流专业知识、建议和技巧；准备装修、展开自己动手DIY项目；想了解最新的度假屋设计趋势；在春季度假屋生活展上，专家将助您圆梦，打造属于您的度假屋生活。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/22/n14186160.md#1/20190323-dsc_0963-e1675195794976-600x400" rel="attachment wp-att-14186240"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14186240" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186240-20190323-DSC_0963-e1675195794976-600x400-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">与全家人一起参加展会现场举行的友好比赛！ 在真人大小的叠叠乐、梯子球等游戏中展示您的致胜技巧。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/22/n14186160.md#1/20191026-dsc_4300-1024x684" rel="attachment wp-att-14186241"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14186241" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186241-20191026-DSC_4300-1024x684-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">到展会的杂货店和贸易站区，浏览最好的手工、工艺品、加拿大制造的小众商品，包括铁制品、艺术品、装饰品、待客零食以及送给任何度假屋邻居的精美礼物。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/22/n14186160.md#1/shutterstock_371943640_1800x1800-600x400" rel="attachment wp-att-14186244"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14186244" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186244-shutterstock_371943640_1800x1800-600x400-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">充气天文馆，提供近距离了解恒星、行星、卫星、彗星、星系等体验。</p>
