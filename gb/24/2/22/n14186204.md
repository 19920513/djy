<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黄金海岸与台北市进行姊妹市图书交流活动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186211-263d92139aa3234c36b8b7334d4433cf-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>世华澳洲分会参与黄金海岸与台北市姊妹市图书交流活动。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月22日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E6%B5%B7%E5%B2%B8.md#1">澳洲黄金海岸</a>市于2024年2月18日上午在该地区Southport图书馆举办黄金海岸市与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">台北市</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%8A%E5%A6%B9%E5%B8%82.md#1">姊妹市</a>之图书交换暨欢庆<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">中国新年</a>活动。</p>
<p>驻布里斯本台北经济文化办事处处长范厚禄及昆士兰台湾中心主任欧阳群应邀出席，当天参加人士包括侨务咨询委员林淑敏、昆士兰台侨团体联合会柯文耀秘书长、世界华人工商妇女企管协会澳洲分会会长郑丽环、台湾原住民排湾族族长施雄伟先生、政治大学颜敏仁教授，以及黄金海岸市议员Brooke Patterson、多位世华澳洲分会前会长及会员，以及台湾乡亲与澳洲当地人士共近两百人次一同参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14186210" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14186210" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186210-98d67306b35fe97760cd8351c721d604-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14186210" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186210-98d67306b35fe97760cd8351c721d604-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14186210" class="wp-caption-text">世华澳洲分会参与黄金海岸与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">台北市</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%8A%E5%A6%B9%E5%B8%82.md#1">姊妹市</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BE%E4%B9%A6%E4%BA%A4%E6%B5%81.md#1">图书交流</a>活动。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14186209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14186209" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186209-515ed6c735e27d1a3e32835102f0e586-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14186209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186209-515ed6c735e27d1a3e32835102f0e586-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14186209" class="wp-caption-text">世华澳洲分会参与黄金海岸与台北市姊妹市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BE%E4%B9%A6%E4%BA%A4%E6%B5%81.md#1">图书交流</a>活动。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天世界华人工商妇女企管协会澳洲分会也在现场设置文化摊位，推广台湾原住民特色文化，同时在现场展示原住民族服饰，亦安排传统书法艺术写作、书签及龙年小提灯制作，现场家长及孩子玩得不亦乐乎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14186208" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14186208" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186208-41a8f35e98211ae9f8529be62b3c0288-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14186208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186208-41a8f35e98211ae9f8529be62b3c0288-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14186208" class="wp-caption-text">世华澳洲分会参与黄金海岸与台北市姊妹市图书交流活动。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14186207" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14186207" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186207-c804c3b2577fcec60541e5ec37298f5b-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14186207" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14186207-c804c3b2577fcec60541e5ec37298f5b-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14186207" class="wp-caption-text">世华澳洲分会参与黄金海岸与台北市姊妹市图书交流活动。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动当天由世界华人工商妇女企管协会澳洲分会Theresa带领进行丰富有趣的亲子共读、儿歌欢唱、儿童彩绘竞赛等诸多丰富亲子活动内容，另外台北市与黄金海岸市图书交换环节，亦促进两市的艺文发展与图书交流合作，意义深远。</p>
<p>（昆士兰台湾中心供稿）</p>
