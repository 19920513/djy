<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>张善政视察鱼市场果菜批发市场 确保供货稳定</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171906-662964-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市长张善政一大清早凌晨4点，实地至桃园鱼市场及桃园果菜市场关心新年前鱼货及蔬果的供应情况。（桃园市农业局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月02日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">新年</a>将至，2日天色未亮，桃园市长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E5%96%84%E6%94%BF.md#1">张善政</a>一大清早凌晨4点，实地至桃园鱼市场及桃园<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%9C%E8%8F%9C%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">果菜市场</a>关心<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">新年</a>前鱼货及蔬果的供应情况，现场交易热络，供货充裕。张善政也向市场供应承销人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%9C%E6%97%A9%E5%B9%B4.md#1">拜早年</a>，祝福大家平安过好年！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14171907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171907" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171907-662965.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14171907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171907-662965-600x331.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="331" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171907" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E5%96%84%E6%94%BF.md#1">张善政</a>也向市场供应承销人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%9C%E6%97%A9%E5%B9%B4.md#1">拜早年</a>。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张善政现场了解年节农产品供货状况，鱼货平日交易量约20公吨，新年前1至2周则约增加至40公吨，而<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%9C%E8%8F%9C%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">果菜市场</a>平日交易量约100公吨，新年前则约增加3成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14171908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171908" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171908-662966.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14171908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171908-662966-600x332.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171908" class="wp-caption-text">张善政发送春联。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市场也会增加一些年节应景食材，如象征“昌盛”“昌隆”之意的白鲳，代表“事事如意”且肉质细嫩的鲈鱼及“福寿长久”的台湾鲷，“多子多孙”的乌鱼子，“五福临门”的午仔鱼，还有富含EPA及DHA土魠鱼，都是民众喜欢买来应景的食材，让大众“年年有鱼（余）”过好年，部分鱼货是民众喜欢购买的食材，导致供不应求，稍微上涨，仍有多种鱼种可供民众挑选。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14171910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171910" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171910-662968.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14171910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171910-662968-600x344.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171910" class="wp-caption-text">张善政开心与大家合影。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14171909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171909-662967.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14171909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171909-662967-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171909" class="wp-caption-text">张善政向摊贩致意。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>果菜市场则有过年必备的芥菜（长年菜），象征好彩头的白萝卜，还有代表“大吉大利”的椪柑、桶柑，以及香蕉、苹果、水梨、莲雾、枣子等优质水果，预期目前果菜市场供应量充足，菜价维持平稳。</p>
<p>2023年桃园鱼市场全年交易量达五千多公吨，交易额约5亿8000万元；桃园果菜市场全年交易量达四万五千多公吨，交易额约6亿9000万元，除服务桃园市各区外，也扩及邻近新北市地区之消费者，为民众提供稳定的农产品供货，未来也会持续为农产品把关。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14171911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171911" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171911-662969.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14171911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171911-662969-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171911" class="wp-caption-text">民众喜欢买应景的食材，让大众“年年有鱼（余）”过好年，部分鱼货是民众喜欢购买的食材，导致供不应求。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现今民众高度关注食品安全，农业局长陈冠义表示，桃园市积极落实推动食安五环政策，在加强查验水产品用药监测部分，鱼市场每月皆执行水产品自主检测，确保鱼货来源及落实追溯管理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14171912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171912-662970.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14171912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171912-662970-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171912" class="wp-caption-text">张善政购买年货。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园果菜市场亦加强把关蔬果批发交易前的安全性，以质谱快检方式进行可快速、精准检验农药，让市民吃到品质把关健康的安心蔬果，让大众选用蔬果均能吃得安心、健康、有信心。◇</p>
