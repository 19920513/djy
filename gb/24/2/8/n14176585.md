<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】烤西葫芦~美味蔬食</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14176589-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>烤番茄和西葫芦都很美味，两种食材一起烘烤，酸甜可口。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年02月08日讯】西葫芦整个烘烤能保留甘甜汁液，搭配甜番茄一起烘烤，滋味酸甜。如下<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%A5%BF%E8%91%AB%E8%8A%A6.md#1">烤西葫芦</a>食谱的准备时间约10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14176593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14176593" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14176593-2-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14176593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14176593-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14176593" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>2个绿色西葫芦<br />
9个甜型小番茄（sugar或honey bombs），约半磅<br />
1/4茶匙盐<br />
1瓣大蒜，压泥<br />
1小撮现磨黑胡椒<br />
2汤匙初榨橄榄油<br />
1杯马苏里拉起司，可换用植物起司<br />
罐头黑橄榄（用之前冲洗），可选项</p>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1.预热烤箱350华氏度。<br />
2.准备9英寸烤盘。<br />
3.小番茄切半，西葫芦切成厚度1厘米左右的圆片，一起放入搅拌碗中，加盐、橄榄油、黑胡椒、蒜泥拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14176600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14176600" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14176600-3-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14176600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14176600-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14176600" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.先将西葫芦摆入烤盘，再把番茄（切面朝下）摆在西葫芦上面，碗中剩余调味料涂抹在番茄和西葫芦中间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14176603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14176603" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14176603-4-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14176603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14176603-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14176603" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 350华氏度烤30分钟。<br />
6.出炉撒起司和橄榄（若橄榄太咸则少添加），再烤5分钟至起司融化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14176604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14176604" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14176604-5-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14176604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14176604-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14176604" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
