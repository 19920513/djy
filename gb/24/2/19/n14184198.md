<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>学子技艺竞赛夺金手奖 翁章梁颁奖助金鼓励</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184199-664956-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义县学子技艺竞赛夺金手奖，19日荣获嘉义县长翁章梁（前排左5）颁奖助学金表扬后大合照。（蔡上海／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月19日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）嘉义县学子参加112（2023）学年度全国高中学生<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%80%E8%89%BA%E7%AB%9E%E8%B5%9B.md#1">技艺竞赛</a>告捷，勇夺13项<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E6%89%8B%E5%A5%96.md#1">金手奖</a>，嘉义县政府筹措经费提供<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%96%E5%8A%A9%E5%AD%A6%E9%87%91.md#1">奖助学金</a>，19日上午在府内招商中心由县长翁章梁亲自颁发表扬，总金额13万8,000元，让已取得保送台科大入场券的5名学生，以及斩获金手奖其它名次，在未来升学获得加分的8位同学，无后顾之忧进入理想学校继续大放异彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14184208" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14184208" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184208-664972.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14184208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184208-664972-600x659.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="384" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14184208" class="wp-caption-text">图为嘉义县学子<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%80%E8%89%BA%E7%AB%9E%E8%B5%9B.md#1">技艺竞赛</a>夺<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E6%89%8B%E5%A5%96.md#1">金手奖</a>，荣获嘉义县长翁章梁颁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%96%E5%8A%A9%E5%AD%A6%E9%87%91.md#1">奖助学金</a>表扬的名单。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14184206" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14184206" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184206-664968.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14184206" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184206-664968-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14184206" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长翁章梁19日亲自颁奖表扬学子技艺竞赛夺金手奖者，并致词。（蔡上海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教育处指出，日前前往各学生家中贴贺榜时，发现多数学生因北上就学费用较高，因此考虑就近留在云林或台中就读，为协助这群孩子努力追梦，特别筹措奖助学金，同时也洽请学生就读的各国中及高中职校友会在必要时提供协助，减轻未来生活、就学的双重负担，勇敢翻转人生。</p>
<p>教育处长李美华说，除了向所有获奖的同学给予诚挚祝福，也感谢嘉义县各国中携手嘉义区高职，提供试探与培养的环境，未来这群孩子只要想回来，转型为农工大县的嘉义县也欢迎大家返乡就业，贡献所长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14184201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14184201" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184201-664959.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14184201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184201-664959-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14184201" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义高工黄博文（右2）化验金手奖第一名，19日荣获嘉义县长翁章梁（左2）颁奖助学金表扬。（蔡上海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14184202" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14184202" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184202-664960.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14184202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184202-664960-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14184202" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义高工赖圣展（右2）机械手臂技术金手奖第一名，19日荣获嘉义县长翁章梁（左2）颁奖助学金表扬。（蔡上海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14184203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14184203" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184203-664965.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14184203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184203-664965-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14184203" class="wp-caption-text">东石高工蔡晋轩（右2）室内配线金手奖第一名，19日荣获嘉义县长翁章梁（左2）颁奖助学金表扬。（蔡上海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>室内配线金手奖第一名，东石高中电机科蔡晋轩表示，在老师用心教导之下拿到第一名，这笔奖助学金可以应用在未来求学、往上学习，读书过程更顺利。东高校长蔡吉郎更说，感谢县府挹注经费重视技职教育，这群孩子以技艺核心“做中学，学中做”的态度，进入科大或职场一定能发光发热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14184200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14184200" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184200-664957.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14184200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184200-664957-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14184200" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义高工林祐安（右2）夺冷冻空调金手奖第一名，19日荣获嘉义县长翁章梁（左2）颁奖助学金表扬。（蔡上海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14184204" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14184204" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184204-664966.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14184204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184204-664966-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14184204" class="wp-caption-text">东石高工吴学鼎（右2）车床金手奖第二名，19日荣获嘉义县长翁章梁（左2）颁奖助学金表扬。（蔡上海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14184205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14184205" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184205-664967.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14184205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14184205-664967-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14184205" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义家职李亭蓁（右2）美颜金手奖第三名，19日荣获嘉义县长翁章梁（左2）颁奖助学金表扬。（蔡上海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>模具金手奖第四名，嘉义高工张志豪分享，制作模具的过程只要差一点点就配合不了，很讲求精细度，这笔奖助学金除了能更精进钻研专业外，也能减轻家中负担，未来想就读高科大完成大学学业，毕业后也能回到嘉义服务。</p>
<p>翁章梁说，这些孩子在同侪中已是佼佼者，希望这笔奖助学金能让他们更投入学习，未来在业界贡献技术与心力，成为顶尖人才，也勉励他们真心投入所爱发挥热情，后续自然就会迎来名声、金钱等附加价值，但初衷仍不会改变。</p>
<p>教育处说明，全国高中学生技艺竞赛由教育部主办，共分为工业类、农业类、商业类、家事类及海事水产类等5大类，是全台高中教师与学生展现长期投入技能学习成果的重要舞台。这次针对金手奖前3名者颁发1万2,000元、第4名至第6名者1万元、其余名次8,000元。</p>
