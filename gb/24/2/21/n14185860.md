<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】乡村蛋饺锅~鲜味十足</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185877-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蛋饺放入肉汤中炖煮，汤汁鲜味十足。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>中国一些地方逢年过节或办家宴，会制作蛋饺（或蛋卷）与肉汤、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E5%91%B3.md#1">鲜味</a>应季食材炖煮，汤汁鲜美，家中老人和小孩都适合享用。</p>
<p>蛋饺皮用鸡蛋或鸭蛋制作，馅料是手工剁碎的新鲜猪肉和佐料。如果正值新年家中杀猪祭祀，汤锅的食材更为丰富。如下分享一道简化版本的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A1%E6%9D%91.md#1">乡村</a>风味<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E9%A5%BA%E9%94%85.md#1">蛋饺锅</a>，食材简单，汤汁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E5%91%B3.md#1">鲜味</a>十足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185878" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185878-2-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14185878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185878-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185878" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>汤底：400克五花肉、1小撮花椒和白胡椒粒、白萝卜和中硬度的豆腐各1磅、1茶匙盐（给汤调味）、适量带皮姜片（用刀拍散）</p>
<p>制作10个蛋饺：100克三七肥瘦猪肉（去皮）、5个鸡蛋、1/8茶匙盐、1/4杯切碎的细香葱（取香葱中段）、2汤匙鲜味生抽、1汤匙油</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1.大锅中放入整块五花肉、花椒、白胡椒粒和2升水烧沸，然后改中小火煨30分钟，煮成清汤。</p>
<p>2.准备蛋饺肉馅：用刀将肉切成绿豆大小的碎肉，加盐、油、生抽拌匀。碎香葱铺在肉馅上面，用时再拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185879" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185879-3-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14185879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185879-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185879" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.煮好的肉汤过滤香料等杂质，留下清汤，肉则切厚片铺在锅底，上面铺白萝卜片、豆腐片。煮沸几分钟后再阖上锅盖，期间撇去浮末。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185880" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185880-4-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14185880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185880-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185880" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185881" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185881-5-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14185881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185881-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185881" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.以中小火慢炖至白萝卜颜色透亮，豆腐分布小裂纹，全程约50分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185882" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185882-6-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14185882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185882-6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185882" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.炖肉汤时制作蛋饺：室温鸡蛋加入1/8茶匙盐打散。肉馅与香葱碎拌匀，分成10份。</p>
<p>煎蛋锅（6英寸）中放油，加入约2汤匙蛋液摊成蛋皮，蛋液未完全凝固时将肉馅放在蛋皮一侧，另一侧对折即成饺子形状。未凝固的蛋液帮助粘合蛋皮，锁住肉汁，味道更鲜美。</p>
<p>动作较慢可以先将锅离火或将蛋皮煎好放入盘中再包裹肉馅，也可以直接卷成长条的蛋卷。煎蛋皮时多放油将蛋皮煎老一点比较香。</p>
<p>6.肉汤快炖好时加盐调味；如果水烧干了，添加热水。煮沸后将蛋饺铺在表面，以中小火煮15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185884-7-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14185884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185884-7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185884" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.蘸酱：小碗中放入芝麻酱、辣椒粉、香菜（可选）；取适量葱白和油放入锅中，开火煸成葱油，捞出干葱不用。热油浇入小碗拌匀食材。添加鲜味生抽和炖肉汤调味。</p>
<p><strong>料理贴士</strong>：机器绞肉加热后会结成硬块，刀切肉馅更松软多汁，此法也适合料理蒸肉饼。如果肉块太软不好切，可以先冷藏一会。</p>
