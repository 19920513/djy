<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北昆侨界急难救助协会 举办初二回娘家活动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14181401-e8e418124d0d7a3fbad83528df2a65f6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>北昆侨界急难救助协会，热闹举办初二回娘家春节活动。（昆士兰台湾中心）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月15日讯】澳洲<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%86%E5%A3%AB%E5%85%B0%E5%87%AF%E6%81%A9%E6%96%AF%E5%9C%B0%E5%8C%BA.md#1">昆士兰凯恩斯地区</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%98%86%E4%BE%A8%E7%95%8C%E6%80%A5%E9%9A%BE%E6%95%91%E5%8A%A9%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">北昆侨界急难救助协会</a>于2024年2月11日中午在当地海景公园举办“新春<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9D%E4%BA%8C%E5%9B%9E%E5%A8%98%E5%AE%B6%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">初二回娘家活动</a>”，吸引众多侨胞携家带眷踊跃参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14181399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14181399" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14181399-b35dcf926295d593ae3c3cd11e0a95dd-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14181399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14181399-b35dcf926295d593ae3c3cd11e0a95dd-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14181399" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%98%86%E4%BE%A8%E7%95%8C%E6%80%A5%E9%9A%BE%E6%95%91%E5%8A%A9%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">北昆侨界急难救助协会</a>，热闹举办初二回娘家春节活动。（昆士兰台湾中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-14181403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14181403-0a3acf07216d8ee974f10865323e5e8f-600x842.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="842" /></p>
<p>主办单位北昆侨界急难救助协会王钦弘会长、薛镜华副会长、凯恩斯台湾联谊会黄卉逸会长、凯恩斯正体中文班叶亭均副会长、华文教师Winnie Ju、公关Felicie Yeh、Stephanie Chuang、Amelia、Abby Huang财务组长、Patrick活动组长及干部与乡亲近百人出席。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14181400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14181400" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14181400-bfe69ea0bf9a45751505e7e49abefe6c-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14181400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14181400-bfe69ea0bf9a45751505e7e49abefe6c-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14181400" class="wp-caption-text">北昆侨界急难救助协会，热闹举办初二回娘家春节活动。（昆士兰台湾中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻布里斯本台北经济文化办事处范厚禄处长及昆士兰台湾中心主任欧阳群特别以网路连线方式与当地乡亲共襄盛举。</p>
<p>范厚禄向凯恩斯地区的台湾侨胞乡亲拜年，祝福大家金龙报喜、龙年吉祥如意，祝福活动圆满成功，并期待下半年与乡亲们再次相见。</p>
<p>欧阳群代表侨务委员会感谢王钦弘会长带领协会干部于新春期间筹备本次活动，让在凯恩斯地区侨胞与打工渡假台湾青年能一同感受到充满台湾特色的年味。</p>
<p>在主办单位精心策画下，当天活动内容丰富有趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14181404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14181404" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14181404-586ab279c91f4222b3b300fc41250fa0-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14181404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14181404-586ab279c91f4222b3b300fc41250fa0-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14181404" class="wp-caption-text">北昆侨界急难救助协会，热闹举办初二回娘家春节活动。（昆士兰台湾中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了准备传统春节美食与侨胞共享之外，另有台湾传统文化体验，包括侨委会提供的龙年灯笼、彩绘扇子灯笼、体验书写春联，以及有趣的麻将宾果亲子游戏等。现场另备有总统府春联、福袋，以及侨委会月历给予侨胞，现场气氛热闹，并在欢笑声中圆满落幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14181398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14181398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14181398-50885813d9a4ed23b0edebfcb44f2552-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14181398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14181398-50885813d9a4ed23b0edebfcb44f2552-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14181398" class="wp-caption-text">北昆侨界急难救助协会，热闹举办初二回娘家春节活动。（昆士兰台湾中心）</figcaption></figure>
