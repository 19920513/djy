<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>海外港人荷兰办“流亡中的香港”研讨会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258217-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-01@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5月23日，国际学者与艺术家在荷兰海牙众议院国际新闻中心（Nieuwspoort）出席“想像：流亡中的香港”研讨会。（NGO DEI提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年05月27日讯】（大纪元记者曾莲综合报导）百多名国际学者、艺术家于5月23、24日齐聚荷兰海牙众议院国际新闻中心（Nieuwspoort），出席“想像：流亡中的香港”（ImagiNation: Hong Kong in Exile）研讨会，探讨香港人面临的人权、言论自由等问题。活动由捷克港人组织“我地”（NGO DEI）主办，盼扩大国际社会对香港议题的关注。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14258216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14258216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258216-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-02@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14258216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258216-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-02@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14258216" class="wp-caption-text">丹麦雕塑家高志活展示“国殇之柱”模型。（NGO DEI提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“想像：流亡中的香港”研讨会云集知名人士，包括荷兰联合国教科文组织委员会主席Kathleen Ferrier、《国殇之柱》艺术家高志活（Jens Galschiot）以及“审查制度和言论自由委员会成员”（Censorship and Freedom of Expression Committee）华立强（Dr. Eric Otto Wear）博士等，举行“耻辱之柱：记忆、压迫与赋权”小组讨论。香港政治艺术家黄国才博士表示：“世界不能对独裁主义视而不见！我们必须为正义而战！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14258215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14258215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258215-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-03@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14258215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258215-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-03@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14258215" class="wp-caption-text">研讨会期间“耻辱之柱：记忆、压迫与赋权”小组讨论，左起：Dean C.K. Cox、黄国才博士、华立强博士和丹麦雕塑家高志活。（NGO DEI提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14258214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14258214" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258214-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-04@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14258214" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258214-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-04@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14258214" class="wp-caption-text">荷兰联合国教科文组织委员会主席Kathleen Ferrier在研讨会上致辞。（NGO DEI提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“香港很美丽，不单是因为自然风光，还因为这里的社区和人。”（Hong Kong is beautiful, not just because of the nature scenario, but also the people and the communities.）曾经居港五年、近年亲眼目睹香港自由急速恶化的荷兰联合国教科文组织委员会主席Kathleen Ferrier在研讨会上致辞，并声援因极权统治而流散世界各地的港人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14258213" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14258213" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258213-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-05@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14258213" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258213-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-05@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14258213" class="wp-caption-text">“法庭文化灭绝审判”（The Courtroom a Trial on Cultural Genocide）互动环节。（NGO DEI提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今次活动设有一个“法庭文化灭绝审判”（The Courtroom a Trial on Cultural Genocide）的互动环节，邀请维吾尔族、西藏人和香港人的代表参与模拟法庭，为中共统治下对他们的文化灭绝作证，在陈述证词过程中，一些听众情绪激动，荷兰自由西藏学生会（Students for a Free Tibet Netherlands）主席Rinchen Dorjee也流下了泪水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14258212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14258212" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258212-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-06@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14258212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258212-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-06@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14258212" class="wp-caption-text">“法庭文化灭绝审判”（The Courtroom a Trial on Cultural Genocide）互动环节。（NGO DEI提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14258211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14258211" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258211-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-07@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14258211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258211-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-07@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14258211" class="wp-caption-text">“法庭文化灭绝审判”（The Courtroom a Trial on Cultural Genocide）互动环节。（NGO DEI提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主办单位“我地”总监刘慧婷（Loretta Lau）表示：“今次研讨会是代表香港艺术共同体的一个重要时刻。随着极权压力的增加和自我审查的扩散，我们必须提供一个让艺术得以蓬勃发展平台，令声音被世人听到。”她提到，今次研讨会标志着组织旅程中的一个重要里程碑，“加入海牙正义社区对‘我地’来说是梦想成真，组织致力于继续宣传工作，为边缘化的声音提供一个平台。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14258210" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14258210" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258210-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-08@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14258210" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258210-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-08@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14258210" class="wp-caption-text">艺术家Ricker Choi现场演奏。（NGO DEI提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我地”近日在荷兰海牙Westinde 25设立新会址，今次的研讨会属于“我地”和“香港文化艺术”（Art and Culture Hong Kong）发起的倡议活动一部分，5月18日至26日期间，在Westinde 25举办理想家园展（Home Ideal Exhibition），让人们一睹香港的文化景观。@</p>
<figure id="attachment_14258209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14258209" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258209-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-09@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14258209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14258209-Nieuwspoort-Hong-Kong-in-Exile-20240527-09@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14258209" class="wp-caption-text">“想像：流亡中的香港”研讨会嘉宾合照。（NGO DEI提供）</figcaption></figure>
