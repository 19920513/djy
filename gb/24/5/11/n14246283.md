<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>芦竹分局提前庆祝母亲节 妈妈员警享用美味餐宴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246284-681445-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>邀请妈妈同仁前往当地知名的“我家牛排馆”，享受一顿丰盛的午餐。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年05月11日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）充满爱的五月，芦竹分局特别为辛勤工作的妈妈员警们举办了一系列温馨的母亲节庆祝活动，分局不仅准备了象征母爱的康乃馨和精美丝巾作为礼物，更邀请妈妈同仁前往当地知名的“我家牛排馆”，享受一顿丰盛的午餐及水果礼盒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14246285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14246285" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246285-681446.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14246285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246285-681446-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14246285" class="wp-caption-text">邀请妈妈同仁前往当地知名的“我家牛排馆”，享受一顿丰盛的午餐。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14246286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14246286" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246286-681447.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14246286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246286-681447-600x712.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="712" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14246286" class="wp-caption-text">芦竹分局长林鼎泰送水果礼盒。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芦竹分局诚挚地祝福妈妈们“母亲节快乐”，希望大家都能与家人共度美好时光，在此特别的日子里仍有同仁在职位上牺牲奉献，因此邀请分局所有的妈妈们，在辖内“我家牛排馆”享用美味餐宴，及赠送每位妈妈们水果礼盒，在准备了象征母爱的康乃馨和精美丝巾礼盒，慰勉在这个特别的日子里奉献的同仁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14246287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14246287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246287-681448.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14246287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246287-681448-600x422.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14246287" class="wp-caption-text">邀请分局所有的妈妈们，在辖内“我家牛排馆”享用美味餐宴。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14246288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14246288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246288-681449.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14246288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246288-681449-600x509.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="509" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14246288" class="wp-caption-text">芦竹分局长林鼎泰送象征母爱的康乃馨和精美丝巾礼盒。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芦竹分局长林鼎泰表示，庆祝活动是对妈妈员警们无私奉献和辛勤工作的一份小小心意，希望透过一系列的活动，让她们感受到分局的关怀和尊重。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14246289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14246289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246289-681450.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14246289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14246289-681450-600x236.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="236" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14246289" class="wp-caption-text">芦竹分局长林鼎泰与“妈妈警察”合影。（桃园市芦竹警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“妈妈警察”除了是扮演除暴安良的警务工作，也扮演着家庭中养儿育女的重要角色，这份双重责任应值得我们所有人的钦佩。◇</p>
