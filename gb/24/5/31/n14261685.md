<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>韩籍啦啦队员李多慧 首次坐上主播台还演8点档</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261705-20240531-FTV-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年05月31日讯】（大纪元记者杨逸帆报导）台湾中华职棒味全龙啦啦队韩籍成员李多慧今天（31日）参访电视台，录制节目《决策者》。她也跟着民视的美女主播刘方慈体验新闻播报，并在虚拟摄影棚和谢京颖大跳啦啦队舞；而在参观八点档摄影棚时，还上演了一段吵架的剧情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14261704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14261704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261704-20240531-FTV-3.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261704-20240531-FTV-3-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" class="size-large wp-image-14261704" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14261704" class="wp-caption-text">李多慧参访电视台。（民视提供）<br /></figcaption></figure>
<p>首次坐上主播台的李多慧表示，平常在啦啦队要带动气氛比较轻松，忽然要转换严肃专业的一面，顿时觉得相当紧张，惊呼刘方慈超专业。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14261703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14261703" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261703-20240531-FTV-4.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261703-20240531-FTV-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-14261703" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14261703" class="wp-caption-text">李多慧(右)快闪民视跟刘方慈(左)一起播报民视新闻。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14261701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14261701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261701-20240531-FTV-6.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261701-20240531-FTV-6-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" class="size-large wp-image-14261701" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14261701" class="wp-caption-text">民视美女主播刘方慈(左)教啦啦队女星李多慧(右)播报新闻，两人互动热络。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李多慧除了与主播刘方慈一起播报新闻外，还跟艺人谢京颖一起在虚拟摄影棚大跳职棒的啦啦队舞，即兴要求跳时下流行的16蹲。</p>
<p>曾在啦啦队应援9年的谢京颖表示，自己后来都专心在拍戏的演艺工作，太久没跳舞了，真的是献丑了。不过第一次近距离看到李多慧，谢京颖大赞她长得漂亮人也很亲切。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14261700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14261700" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261700-20240531-FTV-7.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261700-20240531-FTV-7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-14261700" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14261700" class="wp-caption-text">李多慧(中)赞叹民视虚拟摄影棚把味全龙棒球场搬进来了。左为主播刘方慈，右为演员谢京颖。（民视提供）<br /></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14261706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14261706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261706-20240531-FTV-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261706-20240531-FTV-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-14261706" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14261706" class="wp-caption-text">李多慧(中)教刘方慈(左)、谢京颖(右)一起跳啦啦队应援舞。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谢京颖不只带李多慧参观八点档摄影棚，还带她上演了一段《爱的荣耀》安琪跟爱嘉反目成仇的番外篇，由李多慧饰演的爱嘉发现安琪（谢京颖饰演）勾引她老公，小动作频频，让她怒甩安琪巴掌。这一小段的演出，引起现场欢声雷动，也让李多慧直喊太有趣了。</p>
<p>李多慧表示第一次参观电视台，觉得很新鲜，但时间太短了，期待以后还有时间再来跟大家交流互动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14261702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14261702" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261702-20240531-FTV-5.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261702-20240531-FTV-5-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-14261702" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14261702" class="wp-caption-text">谢京颖(左)带李多慧(右)参观8点档《爱的荣耀》摄影棚，也教她甩巴掌技巧。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14261699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14261699" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261699-20240531-FTV-8.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14261699-20240531-FTV-8-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-14261699" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14261699" class="wp-caption-text">李多慧(右2)跟谢京颖(左2)及《娱乐超skr》主持人小鹿(左1)跟阿甘(右1)一起上演八点档《爱的荣耀》番外篇。（民视提供）<br /></figcaption></figure>
