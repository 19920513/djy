<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>兰屿乡运动会 选手成绩亮眼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242071-680366-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>东清社区获得精神总锦标。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年05月06日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）台东兰屿乡113年度全民运动大会，4日在朗岛国小运动场隆重举行，年度体育盛事吸引众人的期待。开场时，首先向今年的模范母亲，以及去年的乡运破纪录者致敬，他们的付出和努力，充分展现了部落妇女的表率精神，和运动家的奋斗精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14242078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14242078" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242078-680373-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14242078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242078-680373-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14242078" class="wp-caption-text">首先向今年的模范母亲，以及去年的乡运破纪录者致敬。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郑匡婆、曾施秀凤、曾秀花、宋玉梅、张钟秀兰，以及谢益等杰出妇女的表现得到了肯定，他们为家庭和部落贡献良多。同时，施宇喆和黄家俊等运动员也因去年的杰出表现而受到表彰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14242079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14242079" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242079-680374-600x451.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14242079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242079-680374-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14242079" class="wp-caption-text">兰屿乡113年度全民运动大会。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来，朗岛和东清部落的女士们，带来了传统舞蹈和现代社区广场舞的精彩演出，这展现了兰屿多元文化的魅力。此外，趣味游戏活动也为比赛预先暖场，缓解了比赛前的紧张氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14242074" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14242074" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242074-680369-600x451.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14242074" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242074-680369-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14242074" class="wp-caption-text">达悟族传统舞蹈表演。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管天空飘着雨滴，但这并未损坏选手们的热情。在一片激烈的竞争中，一位古稀之年的部落长辈，依然坚持参加马拉松比赛，这种不屈不挠的精神令人感动。张清长耆老因其对赛事的支持而获得特别奖，这是对他不懈努力的肯定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14242077" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14242077" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242077-680372-600x451.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14242077" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242077-680372-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14242077" class="wp-caption-text">渔人部落张清长耆老获颁特别奖。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的比赛中，有8位选手创下了9项大会纪录，其中包括施宇喆和黄家俊。他们的出色表现不仅为自己带来了荣耀，也为兰屿的体育运动注入了新的活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14242072" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14242072" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242072-680367-600x473.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14242072" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242072-680367-600x473.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="473" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14242072" class="wp-caption-text">接力赛跑竞争相当激烈。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>比赛场上不仅是个人成绩的竞争，更是团队荣誉的较量。各个部落的选手团结一致，共同缔造了令人振奋的佳绩。尤其是社会组男女混合的大队接力，东清社区最终夺得冠军，这是部落间友好竞争的最佳体现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14242073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14242073" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242073-680368-600x449.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14242073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242073-680368-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14242073" class="wp-caption-text">亲友团在场外为选手们加油。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在颁奖台上，得奖代表骄傲地站在上面，他们的成就代表了整个社区的努力和付出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14242076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14242076" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242076-680371-600x451.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14242076" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242076-680371-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14242076" class="wp-caption-text">椰油国小荣获国小男子组及女子组田赛总冠军。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动在晴朗的下午圆满结束，社区居民以运动会友，彼此连结，共同凝聚社区向心力。期待来年的比赛再创佳绩！</p>
