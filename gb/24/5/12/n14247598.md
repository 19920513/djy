<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>关山母亲节健行活动 300人漫步亲水公园</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247600-681696-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>活动前一颗热气球缓缓通过亲水公园的大门口。（周颢承提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年05月12日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）12日清晨五点多，关山亲水公园就热闹滚滚，台东关山镇体育会在母亲节这天举办了“台东县运动I 台湾2.0计划～亲子健行活动”。报名参与人数从原本设定的200人持续增加，最后竟超过300人。母亲节这一天在“达谷梵秘境”也举办了“亲子i环境”植物解说活动，希望一起珍惜生活环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14247608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14247608" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247608-681706.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14247608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247608-681706-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14247608" class="wp-caption-text">健行出发前，先做暖身操。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>母亲节市场送康乃馨祝福 婆婆妈妈大喊镇长我也要</h2>
<p>稍早，关山零售市场周边充满幸福的氛围。镇长彭成丰准备了康乃馨送给市场摊商和购物民众，并逐一献上祝福“母亲节快乐！”婆婆妈妈看到镇长的到来都相当开心，纷纷赞扬他不仅能干还很亲切。市场旁水果店老板陈春敏也来帮忙推“花车”，由于反应热烈不到半小时就发送一空，让关山镇今年“母亲节”的‘幸福快闪’活动充满甜蜜气息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14247604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14247604" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247604-681702.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14247604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247604-681702-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14247604" class="wp-caption-text">母亲节关山镇长，到市场送康乃馨祝福。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14247607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14247607" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247607-681705.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14247607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247607-681705-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14247607" class="wp-caption-text">婆婆妈妈大喊镇长我也要（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>健行活动贵宾们与民同乐</h2>
<p>关山镇民代表会副主席李伟钰介绍参与健行活动来宾包括镇长彭成丰、农会总干事邱美玲、议员陈宏宗、天后宫主委方瑞清、县长秘书洪威光等地方社团负责人。关山镇体育会理事长林秀玲则致词感谢地方对于运动活动非常支持，虽然人数超乎预期经费有限的情况下，感谢镇长彭成丰、陈宏宗议员、邱美玲总干事等协助解决。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14247605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14247605" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247605-681703.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14247605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247605-681703-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14247605" class="wp-caption-text">陈宏宗议员表示赤脚健走，可以感受土地的呼吸。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>关山镇体育会前理事长谢建泉校长说明健行活动注意事项和健行路线，并报告好消息，多年急待解决的猪粪味道和稻秆处理，都在这次镇代会定期会，通过支持价购土地来设置处理厂，等于顺利跨出一大步。今年鲨鱼公园也将建置成为关山镇第一座亲子共融公园，火车站前圆环意象、亲水公园再造等计划，乡亲们拭目以待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14247601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14247601" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247601-681697.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14247601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247601-681697-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14247601" class="wp-caption-text">母亲节健走活动，超过三百人参加。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>亲子健行活动 热闹温馨</h2>
<p>活动前一颗热气球缓缓通过亲水公园的大门口，空中搭乘者也与参加健行活动的民众热情打招呼。参与今日健行的民众有的慢跑、有的漫步方式在亲水公园间。关山镇体育会发送每位参与活动包括毛巾、矿泉水、早餐或蛋糕择一和关山米等，让超过300位的乡亲，度过不一样的母亲节清晨时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14247603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14247603" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247603-681701.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14247603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247603-681701-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14247603" class="wp-caption-text">关山镇亲水公园。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14247602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14247602" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247602-681699.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14247602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247602-681699-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14247602" class="wp-caption-text">每位参与活动者，可以获赠丰富的礼物。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>探索达谷梵秘境自然美景</h2>
<p>母亲节这一天 ，关山镇达谷梵秘境也举办了“亲子i环境活动”，由水利署台东管理处志工导览解说秘境内的植物；参加者随后抽签决定任务，分别找寻3至5种指定植物拍照，完成者便可以换取轻便型背包精美礼物。镇长彭成丰表示，希望每个月都能办理让更多人认识“达谷梵”的自然美景，也呼吁大家一起来珍惜爱护生活环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14247599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14247599" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247599-681695.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14247599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247599-681695-600x343.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14247599" class="wp-caption-text">达谷梵秘境，举办植物解说活动。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14247606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14247606" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247606-681704.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14247606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14247606-681704-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14247606" class="wp-caption-text">镇长彭成丰表示，希望每个月都能办理让更多人认识“达谷梵”的自然美景。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
