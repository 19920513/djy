<h6>背痛、肩颈酸痛、膏肓痛？两招放松按摩，舒缓紧绷上背，再来三招运动，活化肩胛骨。（健康养成记/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>腰酸背痛是现代人普遍的毛病，尤其是在办公桌前久坐而罹患“膏肓痛”一族，会感到异常疼痛。膏肓痛指的是两侧肩胛骨内缘和脊椎之间的疼痛，并且还会连带从肩颈到上背的疼痛，而大多数的这种疼痛，其实是因为姿势不良所引发的。本文介绍五招上背伸展运动，教你活化肩胛骨，可以有效舒缓上背酸痛。</p>
<div class="google-auto-placed ap_container">膏肓痛通常发生在年长者及长期姿势不正确的低头族身上。一般来说，膏肓痛都会伴随着肩颈酸痛及上背痛的症状，这主要是因为圆肩驼背的不正确坐姿导致脖子往前伸，让颈椎承受过多的压力。</div>
<p>颈椎承压会进一步造成肩胛骨的前引及外翻，让后背的菱形肌及斜方肌因为拉长造成肌肉无力的紧绷，也会让胸大肌和胸小肌因为紧缩而造成紧绷。那么，修正的方式，首先是要先放松酸痛的肌肉，再针对无力的肌肉做反向的运动练习。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14255077" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255077" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255077-shutterstock_2316186389.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14255077" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255077-shutterstock_2316186389-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255077" class="wp-caption-text">膏肓痛指的是两侧肩胛骨内缘和脊椎之间的疼痛，并且还会连带从肩颈到上背的疼痛。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">第一步 放松紧绷肌肉</span></h2>
<h4><strong>1. 按摩球放松法</strong></h4>
<div class="google-auto-placed ap_container">小肌肉的酸痛可以通过按摩球按压的方式，放松酸痛点。</div>
<p>Step1：平躺地上，将按摩球从肩胛骨上缘开始，放置在肩胛骨酸痛的地方，用按压的方式做放松，因为肌肉内有高尔肌腱器的神经组织，在肌肉被按压超过7秒后，肌肉会自动发出放松讯号。</p>
<p>Step2：将手臂慢慢上抬到耳边再放下，重复15次，增加肌肉按摩及放松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14240257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14240257" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240257-2.png"><img class=" wp-image-14240257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240257-2-600x357.png" alt="" width="599" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14240257" class="wp-caption-text">按摩球放松法。（健康养成记/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2. 滚筒放松法</strong></p>
<p>圆肩驼背让大圆肌拉长、阔背肌紧缩而紧绷，大面积的肌肉可透过滚筒放松。</p>
<p>Step1：平躺地上，将滚筒按压在腋下的位置。</p>
<p>Step2：透过手臂的内旋与外旋增加筋膜的放松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14240259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14240259" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240259-4.png"><img class=" wp-image-14240259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240259-4-600x337.png" alt="" width="599" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14240259" class="wp-caption-text">滚筒放松法。（健康养成记/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">第二部分 强化肌肉训练</span></h2>
<h4><strong>1. 胸背前伸外展</strong></h4>
<p>圆肩驼背是指肩胛骨往前伸并往外翻，同时胸大肌、胸小肌紧缩，所以透过反向动作练习，强化菱形肌、斜方肌的力量，伸拉胸大肌与胸小肌。</p>
<p>Step1：两手往前伸做拱背的姿势。</p>
<p>Step2：回正后双手平举，大臂与小臂呈现90度，肩外璇，做双臂向后展的扩胸动作，有挤压肩胛骨的感觉。注意锻炼过程中不要耸肩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14240263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14240263" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240263-7.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14240263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240263-7-600x339.png" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14240263" class="wp-caption-text">（健康养成记/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14240261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14240261" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240261-6.png"><img class=" wp-image-14240261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240261-6-600x340.png" alt="" width="598" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14240261" class="wp-caption-text">（健康养成记/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="img-caption"><strong>2. W型训练</strong></p>
<p>Step1：坐姿，身体微倾45度，双手呈现Ｗ型放在胸侧。</p>
<p>Step2：先将双手臂往后，有挤压肩胛的感觉，并作为原始设定点。</p>
<p>Step3：双手往前伸，停留3秒后，再回到原始设定点。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240265-8.png"><br />
</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_14240266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14240266" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240266-9.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14240266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14240266-9-600x337.png" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14240266" class="wp-caption-text">（健康养成记/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<div class="image-container">
<p class="img-caption"><strong>3. 肩膀旋转</strong></p>
</div>
<p>双手摸肩膀，做肩膀的内转及外转，注意过程中不要耸肩。</p>
