<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台故宫展览 看见《红楼梦》的人情物趣</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260744-2405300618062378-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾国立故宫博物院推出“看得见的红楼梦”特展，图为清朝的白绸彩绣嵌珠宝翠玉花蝶团扇。（台湾故宫提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年05月30日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾报导）台湾国立故宫博物院推出“看得见的红楼梦”特展，展览以经典文学巨&#33879;《红楼梦》为题，引领观众从物件反映出来的人情物趣，走进文学、艺术交织的美学世界，重新想像盛清贵族世家的荣景，看见《红楼梦》的绝美与哀戚。</p>
<p>台湾故宫策展人器物处助理研究员王亮钧表示，展览以三个轴线展开叙事：</p>
<figure id="attachment_14260746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14260746" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260746-2405300538452378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14260746" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260746-2405300538452378-600x548.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="548" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14260746" class="wp-caption-text">清 改琦撰 红楼梦图咏 清光绪五年(1879)刊图绘本-元春。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一，“大雅可观”，通过作者曹雪芹生于江宁织造世家的人生经验，呈现《红楼梦》中贵族阶级精致的物质文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14260745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14260745" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260745-2405300619532378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14260745" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260745-2405300619532378-600x557.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="557" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14260745" class="wp-caption-text">清朝改琦撰红楼梦图咏，清光绪五年（1879）刊图绘本。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二，“异物奇货”，展出书中对应的外国舶来品，揭示作者所处的时代，宫廷热衷于“洋货”的仿效风潮，以此衬托贾府的时尚风华。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14260747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14260747" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260747-2405300622552378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14260747" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260747-2405300622552378.jpg" alt="" width="455" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14260747" class="wp-caption-text">欧洲18世纪的铜嵌玛瑙钟烛台。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第三，“一番梦幻”，根据书中使用及相关联物品的描绘，呈现作者塑造各具特色的人物性格与隐喻，展现十二金钗短暂却华丽灿烂的生命姿态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14260752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14260752" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260752-2405300547022378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14260752" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260752-2405300547022378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14260752" class="wp-caption-text">清 乾隆 金镶东珠猫睛石嫔妃朝冠–顶。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14260750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14260750" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260750-2405300553192378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14260750" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260750-2405300553192378-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14260750" class="wp-caption-text">明朝晚期至清朝的铜夔纹炉、瓶、盒。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾故宫表示，《红楼梦》离不开“情”，有亲情、爱情、友情、主仆之情。情又与“人”之间的互动紧密交织。人通过“物”的点缀，显得立体而有温度。“物”让小说有了画面，成为看得见的剧情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14260751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14260751" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260751-2405300552032378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14260751" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260751-2405300552032378-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14260751" class="wp-caption-text">图为北宋的汝窑青瓷碟。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14260749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14260749" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260749-2405300554562378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14260749" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260749-2405300554562378-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14260749" class="wp-caption-text">清朝乾隆的玛瑙扭丝纹碟。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾故宫说，这次展览特别委请董阳孜女士为展览题字。本展汇聚故宫、国家图书馆及国立台湾大学图书馆馆藏珍品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14260748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14260748" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260748-2405300621082378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14260748" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260748-2405300621082378-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14260748" class="wp-caption-text">欧洲18世纪的铜嵌金星玻璃鼻烟盒。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展览的精彩展件如“清 乾隆 金镶东珠猫睛石嫔妃朝冠顶”累丝金凤奢华富丽，是富贵及身份的象征；“北宋 汝窑 青瓷碟”天青釉色温润典雅，釉薄处隐现浅粉色光泽，展现内敛高雅品味；“欧洲 十八世纪 铜嵌金星玻璃鼻烟盒”盒盖嵌长方金星玻璃片，密布金属微粒闪烁星点光泽，是当时最新潮、时尚的舶来品。</p>
