<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】长豇豆炒虾滑~咸甜味浓 超级下饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221197-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>咸甜香热炒菜——“长豇豆炒虾滑”，超级下饭！（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】鲜虾搭配蔬菜拌炒特别鲜甜，这一道家常菜简单煮、随便煮都不难吃，而且非常下饭，在气候多变的春夏，如果食欲不振，更是开胃的好朋友——“长豇豆炒虾滑”。今天梁厨要介绍的是如何把简单的蔬菜炒成餐厅佳肴，记得调味也非常重要，因为咸甜要搭配妥当，才会一口接一口不中断！</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">长豇豆炒虾滑</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></h3>
<p>长豇豆12 oz<br />
虾滑12 oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉少许<br />
柱侯酱2汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14221199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221199" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221199-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14221199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221199-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221199" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味：</span></h3>
<p>鸡汤半杯<br />
糖半茶匙<br />
美极鲜酱油半茶匙<br />
古月粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉1茶匙</p>
<h3><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法：</span></strong></h3>
<p>1. 长虹豆切去头尾后切段，洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14221200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221200" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221200-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14221200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221200-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221200" class="wp-caption-text">长虹豆切去头尾后切段，洗干净备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 急冻虾滑解冻后弄散备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14221196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221196" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221196-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14221196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221196-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221196" class="wp-caption-text">急冻虾滑解冻后弄散备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 长虹豆用姜汁酒烫2分钟，捞出后沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14221208" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221208" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221208-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14221208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221208-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221208" class="wp-caption-text">长虹豆用姜汁酒烫2分钟，捞出后沥干备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，虾滑放入锅中炒至七成熟，拨到锅边。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14221209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221209" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221209-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14221209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221209-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221209" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，虾滑放入锅中炒至七成熟，拨到锅边。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 接着加入姜、蒜蓉和柱侯酱爆香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14221210" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221210" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221210-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14221210" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221210-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221210" class="wp-caption-text">接着加入姜、蒜蓉和柱侯酱爆香。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放回长虹豆，翻炒均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14221216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221216" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221216-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14221216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221216-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221216" class="wp-caption-text">放回长虹豆，翻炒均匀。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 搅匀调味汁液后倒入，同时一边炒匀，完成后便可上碟。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14221215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221215" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221215-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14221215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221215-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221215" class="wp-caption-text">搅匀调味汁液后倒入，同时一边炒匀，完成后便可上碟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></p>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
