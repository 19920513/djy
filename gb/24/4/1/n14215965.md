<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳政府敦促老年卡持卡人登记领补贴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215968-08d2f737af49e151712df51a6c592e19-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>西澳政府鼓励老年卡持卡人确保登记注册个人信息，以领取2024年生活费补贴。图为示意图。图为看到银行存折感到惊讶的老年人。（shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年04月01日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者夏墨竹澳洲珀斯报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E6%94%BF%E5%BA%9C.md#1">西澳政府</a>鼓励老年卡持卡人确保登记以领取</span></span>2024<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E6%B4%BB%E8%B4%B9%E8%A1%A5%E8%B4%B4.md#1">生活费补贴</a>。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">该持卡人必须在</span></span>2024<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>31<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日之前注册，才能领取今年的款项，该款项将于</span></span>7<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月下旬发放。以前注册过的持卡人无需重新注册，除非其银行信息或家庭状况发生了变化。尚未注册的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E8%80%81%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%A1.md#1">西澳老年卡</a>持卡人已收到申请提醒函。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">去年，单身老年人领取了</span></span>104<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，老年夫妇领取了</span></span>156<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。该补贴于</span></span>2009<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年首次推出，已为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E8%80%81%E5%B9%B4%E4%BA%BA.md#1">西澳老年人</a>提供了超过</span></span>3.94<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">亿澳元的援助。</span></span></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E8%80%81%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%A1.md#1">西澳老年卡</a>持卡人可通过注册，在线访问其账户，或下载申请表来申请<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E6%B4%BB%E8%B4%B9%E8%A1%A5%E8%B4%B4.md#1">生活费补贴</a>，请访问西澳老年卡网站：<ahref="https://www.seniorscard.wa.gov.au/forms-guides/">https://www.seniorscard.wa.gov.au/forms-guides/</a></p>
