<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>急难救助协会换届 急救医疗讲座获赞</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232278-DSC01492-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>4月20日，多个团体联合在侨教中心举办急救讲座。持照急救师姚英伦和丁心格讲解急救包扎。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年04月23日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨牛顿市报导）-4月20日中午，人们在波士顿侨教中心内互相为同伴包纱布、缠止血带。这是一场紧急医疗讲座的实践环节。</p>
<p>波士顿地区侨界急难救助协会、波克莱台湾商会、新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会、波士顿台湾世卫协进会及侨教中心合作举办了这场讲座。</p>
<p>参与者们在学习和实践急救知识之后，亦在现场见证急难救助协会换届，林丽珠接任会长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14232286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14232286" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232286-DSC01540.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14232286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232286-DSC01540-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14232286" class="wp-caption-text">多个团体联合举办急救讲座，并见证急难救助协会换届。参与者们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14232276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14232276" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232276-DSC01479.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14232276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232276-DSC01479-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14232276" class="wp-caption-text">多个团体联合在侨教中心举办急救讲座，30多人参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14232288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14232288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232288-DSC01547.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14232288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232288-DSC01547-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14232288" class="wp-caption-text">4月20日，波士顿急难救助协会换届，林丽珠接任会长。左起：李孟洁、王志维、陈奕如、欧阳露、林丽珠、郭竞儒、蒋宗壬、潘昭荣。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>急救讲座受欢迎</strong></h3>
<p>当天，30多名与会者首先参加了急救讲座及实践，主讲嘉宾是获美国急救员认证资格、目前在纽英崙中华耆英会服务的姚英伦和丁心格。</p>
<p>两位主讲人讲解了颈部出血、身体出血、脱臼、骨折等紧急情况下的包扎方法，也介绍了常用的急救工具，如普通绷带、三角绷带、以色列绷带、止血带、夹板等等。</p>
<p>“血液就像是我们身体生命的燃料。”姚英伦说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14232277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14232277" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232277-DSC01485.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14232277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232277-DSC01485-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14232277" class="wp-caption-text">持照急救师姚英伦和丁心格讲解急救止血。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14232280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14232280" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232280-DSC01520.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14232280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232280-DSC01520-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14232280" class="wp-caption-text">姚英伦为听众讲解急救包扎。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两位主讲人谈到，若遇到颈部出血的情况，要让伤者用手按压伤口，然后用纱布绕过手臂包扎。切忌用纱布完全缠绕颈部，以免造成窒息。</p>
<p>关于大腿动脉出血，需要使用止血带绑紧止血的情况，两位主讲人谈到，肌肉可以在缺氧状态下保持活性1小时，这段时间内一定可以得到救援，所以不要担心止血带会导致肢体坏死。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14232281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14232281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232281-DSC01527.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14232281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232281-DSC01527-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14232281" class="wp-caption-text">丁心格指导听众实践急救包扎。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>姚英伦与丁心格不仅讲解理论，还亲身示范各种包扎方法，并指导听众实践。他们的讲解幽默风趣，听众亦乐于动手尝试，现场气氛热络，欢笑声不断。布鲁克林镇（Brookline）镇议员王力远也携家人参加了讲座，并表达赞许。</p>
<p>讲座之后，急难救助协会首任会长王志维与第二任会长欧阳露一同向两位主讲人颁赠感谢状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14232285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14232285" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232285-DSC01536.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14232285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232285-DSC01536-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14232285" class="wp-caption-text">多个团体联合在侨教中心举办急救讲座，随后向两位主讲人姚英伦和丁心格致谢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>林丽珠接掌急难救助协会</strong></h3>
<p>当天波士顿地区侨界急难救助协会也举办了换届会议，由林丽珠接替翁逸虹，就任会长。</p>
<p>“我们要让波士顿附近各社团都知道，我们有这个急难救助协会，我们的宗旨。我们能够多举办活动，让大家聚在一起。”林丽珠说。她说，希望联结各界人士，尽最大力量，为遭遇急难的台湾侨胞、留学生提供帮助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14232289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14232289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232289-DSC01550.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14232289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232289-DSC01550-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14232289" class="wp-caption-text">林丽珠接任波士顿急难救助协会会长。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>波士顿侨教中心主任潘昭荣感谢急难救助协会新、旧会长的服务，也感谢讲座嘉宾和参与者们的支持。</p>
<p>“急难救助协会没有强迫说你一定要是会员，只要你愿意针对某个突发事件，愿意伸出援手，愿意帮忙，我们都是欢迎的。”他说。</p>
<p>潘昭荣还介绍了侨委会为侨胞们设计的“i侨卡”，即电子版的侨胞卡。他欢迎侨胞们上网登记，并享受各项侨胞优惠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14232287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14232287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232287-DSC01545.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14232287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14232287-DSC01545-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14232287" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任潘昭荣向大家介绍“i侨卡”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周六来到侨教中心参与活动的嘉宾，还包括波士顿侨务委员郭竞儒、侨务咨询委员蒋宗壬、波克莱台湾商会会长陈奕如、世卫协进会共会长李孟洁、纽英崙中华专业人员协会会长卢彦君等等。◇</p>
