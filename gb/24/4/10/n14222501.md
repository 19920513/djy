<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2024年 LA东方明珠广场“台湾好食日”免费试吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222505-ef889755625edcd418ddb0efa4075e36-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>LA东方明珠广场体验“台湾好食日”惊喜连连。（大纪元提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年04月10日讯】LA东方明珠广场将于4.26（五）-4.28（日）举办“台湾好食日”免费试吃活动，去年佳评如潮，今年欢迎与家人好友结伴同行，以免向隅！精心挑选台湾好吃又优质的风味美食、伴手礼……错过就可惜啰！喜欢尝鲜钟情台湾美食的伙伴们，快来一饱口福！体验“台湾好食日”，肯定带给你惊喜连连。</p>
<p><strong>【活动地点】LA东方明珠广场</strong><br />
<strong> 欢迎莅临、大享口福！ TEL:626-401-1828</strong><br />
<strong> 18888 Labin Ct, Rowland Heights, CA 东方明珠</strong><br />
<strong> 【Free Tasting！免费试吃 】4.26（五）-4.28（日）</strong></p>
<p>▍庙口好滋味 道地台湾味</p>
<figure id="attachment_14222506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14222506" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222506-9c4299328d02e113ff27410402d00bc6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14222506" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222506-9c4299328d02e113ff27410402d00bc6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14222506" class="wp-caption-text">越吃越有味。（台湾美味食品提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾美味食品是面食专家，专注创新研发，专门做面类、米粉、冬粉、罐头等南北杂货批发生意，并自创“庙口”品牌，供应全台盘商及量贩卖场。◆关庙面◆刀削面◆香菇风味鸡丝面 ◆ 香菇风味锅烧意面，吃了回味无穷。</p>
<p>▍最熟悉的家乡味 让人恋恋不忘</p>
<figure id="attachment_14222507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14222507" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222507-39bdd55db6819585ea9a70207b7657c0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14222507" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222507-39bdd55db6819585ea9a70207b7657c0-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14222507" class="wp-caption-text">将传统美食大众化。（奇美食品提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>奇美食品成立于1971年，导入新饮食生活概念，以轻食主义、友善环境及健康减负担为核心，为调理食品开启崭新的一页。推出◆素香菇菜包◆麻糬芝麻包◆高丽菜豆腐饺◆枝豆饺，成分单纯，以食材最原始的风味征服众人味蕾。</p>
<p>▍台湾珍珠粉圆专家 口感最难忘</p>
<figure id="attachment_14222508" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14222508" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222508-6e37cb10f974ef96436763827fc32357.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14222508" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222508-6e37cb10f974ef96436763827fc32357-600x400.png" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14222508" class="wp-caption-text">黑润Q弹有细节。（国际实业提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_14222509" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14222509" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222509-23ebf8e37b3c814404012467c8230962.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14222509" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222509-23ebf8e37b3c814404012467c8230962-600x400.png" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14222509" class="wp-caption-text">风行世界的零食。（璧合提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_14222510" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14222510" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222510-85ae079ee1f2bd4d26062ea815edfe89.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14222510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14222510-85ae079ee1f2bd4d26062ea815edfe89-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14222510" class="wp-caption-text">完熟酵素纯蜂蜜。（嘟嘟家蜂蜜提供）</figcaption></figure>
