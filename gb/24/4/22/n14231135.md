<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园国小美术班“TOTEM?图腾传说”毕业美展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231136-677273-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>林辰洋-图腾奇缘。（桃园市文化局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年04月22日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园国小是桃园市最早成立的小学，创校迄今126年，亦是桃园市最早成立美术班的学校，自民国70（1981）年至今已是第43届。</p>
<figure id="14231137" aria-describedby="caption-14231137" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231137-677274-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231137-677274-450x338.jpg" alt="陈品馨-狂想的设计。" width="351" b="468" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14231137" class="wp-caption-text">陈品馨-狂想的设计。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃小美术班一路走来，融合传统与创新教学方式，致力培养小小艺术人才。第43届毕业美展将于2024年4月24日至5月5日假桃园市政府文化局2、3楼画廊展出，欢迎市民朋友们莅临欣赏。</p>
<figure id="14231138" aria-describedby="caption-14231138" style="width: 497px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231138-677275-450x326.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231138-677275-450x326.jpg" alt="曾瀚筠-图腾传说。" width="497" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14231138" class="wp-caption-text">曾瀚筠-图腾传说。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本届毕业美展以“图腾（TOTEM）”为发想与创作主轴，同时结合桃园市“结合大学资源打造艺术美感知识城”计划，与铭传大学商业设计系合作，导入该系专业资源，进行创新的艺术课程活动。</p>
<figure id="14231140" aria-describedby="caption-14231140" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231140-677277-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231140-677277-450x338.jpg" alt="魏钰涵-幻象图腾柱。" width="499" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14231140" class="wp-caption-text">魏钰涵-幻象图腾柱。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14231139" aria-describedby="caption-14231139" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231139-677276-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231139-677276-450x338.jpg" alt="蔡佳伃-立体野兽派系。" width="351" b="468" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14231139" class="wp-caption-text">蔡佳伃-立体野兽派系。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>跨学习阶段相互支持与协助，以大手牵小手模式，技艺相传、带领共学、共作。</p>
<figure id="14231141" aria-describedby="caption-14231141" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231141-677278-450x345.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14231141-677278-450x345.jpg" alt="王妍臻-抽象猫头鹰。" width="499" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14231141" class="wp-caption-text">王妍臻-抽象猫头鹰。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展览的亮点展品〈图腾柱〉即是从搜集资料、构思发想，由双方通力合作，将一个个废弃的垃圾桶加以改造，转化历史图腾为一个令人惊艳的立体作品。◇</p>
