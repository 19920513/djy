<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾东部沿海发生7.2级大地震</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219574-e0722eb966b2823893761f10fc493dc3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>援助0403花莲震灾专案捐款资讯。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年04月06日讯】<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">7</span>时<span lang="EN-US">58</span>分，台湾东部海域发生芮氏规模<span lang="EN-US">7.2</span>级地震，是台湾自<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年南投集集<span lang="EN-US">921</span>大地震以来所遭受最大规模的地震。至截稿时间为止，震灾已造成<span lang="EN-US">10</span>人死亡、<span lang="EN-US">1,099</span>人伤，另尚有<span lang="EN-US">705</span>人受困及<span lang="EN-US">11</span>人失联。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219573" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219573-af4e873b3c6722ffe7602ea743b308f0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14219573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219573-af4e873b3c6722ffe7602ea743b308f0-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219573" class="wp-caption-text">图为花莲遭地震重创的校园。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="x_MsoNormal">获悉地震消息后，新南威尔斯州上议院副议长<span lang="EN-US">Rod Roberts</span>、上议院反对党党鞭<span lang="EN-US">Chris Rath</span>、新州议会友台小组主席<span lang="EN-US">Anthony Roberts</span>、新州公平交易影子厅长<span lang="EN-US">Tim James</span>、西雪梨大学校长<span lang="EN-US">Barney Glover</span>及新州<span lang="EN-US">Waverley</span>市议员<span lang="EN-US">Will Nemesh</span>等澳洲友人，分别于面晤或以致电及简讯方式向驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处吴正伟处长表达关切，并对震灾伤亡者及其亲友致上诚挚慰问之意，祈祷灾区早日复原，台湾人民能早日平安度过此次震灾，重建家园。</p>
<p class="x_MsoNormal">台湾位处地震频仍的环太平洋火山带上，美国密苏里科技大学<span lang="EN-US">(Missouri University of Science and Technology)</span>地震研究学者<span lang="EN-US">Stephen Gao</span>及美国东北大学<span lang="EN-US">(Northeastern University)</span>公共政策学教授<span lang="EN-US">Daniel Aldrich</span>分别接受美联社采访指出，历经<span lang="EN-US">921</span>大地震后，台湾政府与人民吸取经验并制定及实施严格建筑法规，并全力推行防灾安全教育，均有助将此次震灾的伤亡减至最低，亦可做为位处地震带相关国家的参考。</p>
<p class="x_MsoNormal">面对花莲巨震，中华民国总统蔡英文于第一时间指示相关单位全力营救伤患与受困失联的国人，并坐镇联合防灾中心视导东部铁公路抢修情形，及时恢复因强震中断的花东疏运。蔡总统另偕同副总统赖清德、行政院长陈建仁及副院长郑文灿捐出一月所得，抛砖引玉，共同响应花莲灾后重建及复原工作的推动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219569" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219569-05b285aa75a98bfdab08b91d8110ae3f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14219569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219569-05b285aa75a98bfdab08b91d8110ae3f-600x363.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219569" class="wp-caption-text">蔡英文总统坐镇中央灾害应变中心指示全力救援。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14219572" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219572" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219572-ff6c4ef9e1c9a911f5d9db4125e02eb0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14219572" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219572-ff6c4ef9e1c9a911f5d9db4125e02eb0-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219572" class="wp-caption-text">图为赖清德副总统亲赴花莲勘灾并向现场军、警、消等救灾人员致谢。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处提供）</figcaption></figure>
