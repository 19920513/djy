<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】越野版皮卡 2024 Honda Ridgeline TrailSport</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224675-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2024 Honda Ridgeline TrailSport。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年04月13日讯】继测试了Passport及Pilot的TrailSport版，这一次是Honda Ridgeline TrailSport，将这皮卡的越野特性更加强化，令开着这皮卡无论在道路或野外，也能轻松面对。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224657" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224657-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14224657" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224657-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_09-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224657" class="wp-caption-text">2024 Honda Ridgeline TrailSport。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二代Honda Ridgeline自2017年推出，以取代第一代车款，前脸设计更为流线，带点同厂SUV的设计。在结构上Ridgeline与一般Pickup也是使用车身与底盘分开制造，在后期才拼合“Body on Frame”结构不同。Honda Ridgeline是探用车身底盘一体的设计，称为“Unibody”，Ridgeline使用的是Honda的ACE（Advanced Compatibility Engineering）车架，在车体坚固度及抗扭性也很高。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224658" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224658-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14224658" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224658-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224658" class="wp-caption-text">尾闸两种开启方法。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一代Ridgeline另一出众的尾闸设计，拥有两种开启方法，第一种是一般皮卡的方法，也就是“向下翻”，好处是占用地方少，而且也可作为搬运平台，但是关上时因闸门的重量而会较费力气；另一种就是Ridgeline独有的横揭开（从右往左开），需要后方有多一点位置，因可利用车牌下方的踏板，爬上车卡也较为容易，而且关上闸门时也没“往上关”般费劲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224661-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14224661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224661-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224661" class="wp-caption-text">尾闸两种开启方法。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，在货卡下方有一个隐藏的储物位置，后备轮胎也藏在这，空间也挺大的，可以用作放置一些贵重物品，也可锁上，比放东西在车厢内更安全。但是也有缺点，就是如果货卡上有东西的话，使要先将东西移开，才可开启。后排座位的座垫可向椅背靠折，成为另一个高而宽的储物空间，足够放入自行车等大型物件，而且像汽车的后排座椅一样，可六四分别折叠，弹性更大。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224662-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_21.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14224662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224662-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_21-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224662" class="wp-caption-text">货卡下方的隐藏储物位置。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>TrailSport版在外型上与其他版本的最大分别，是车身上多处的“TrailSport”徽章，四个轮胎是专为越野的245／60R18 105T All-Terrain，轮圈也是专为TrailSport版而设的18吋灰色合金轮圈。看不到的又有</p>
<p>越野专用的悬挂系统。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224663-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14224663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224663-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224663" class="wp-caption-text">“TrailSport”徽章。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Ridgeline的动力来自一副3.5升V6引擎，有VCM系统，可在不需要时减少供油给某些汽缸，减少耗油。最大马力为280匹，比上一代增加30匹；峰值扭矩262磅呎，也比旧款多出15磅呎，最大拖力为5,000磅。车身重量也比旧有减少了78磅，所以动力更显充沛。至于在初推出时的两驱版本，都已经全部改为只有四驱选择，四驱系统名为i-VTM4全轮驱动系统。变速箱是9速自动变速箱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224666" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224666-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_24.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14224666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224666-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224666" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新Ridgeline在设备上也紧追潮流，中控台装置了9吋的触屏，而且可兼容Apple CarPlay或Android Auto，同时又可有USB、蓝牙连接、XM收音机等功能。恒温系统也分为三区设计，即前座左、右及后排三区。中控台USB端口有齐Type A及Type C各一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224667" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224667-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_26.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14224667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224667-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224667" class="wp-caption-text">三区恒温系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安全方面，Ridgeline有Honda Sensing系统，包括i-ACTIVSENSE是一套全方位的防御系统，内里由多项技术组合而成，包括盲点系统〈Blind Spot Information System〉、碰撞缓解刹车系统〈Collision Mitigation Braking System〉、道路偏离缓解系统〈Road Departure Mitigation System〉等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224668" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224668-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_27.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14224668" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224668-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_27-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224668" class="wp-caption-text">无线充电板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>测试期间开了Ridgeline到另一城市，来回超过300英里，中途加了一次汽油，而且汽车电脑显示平均耗油量约为 20多一点MPG。虽然Honda Ridgeline TrailSport是一辆不错的皮卡，但是耗油也挺大的。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224671-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_32.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14224671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224671-2024_Honda_Ridgeline_Trailsport_32-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224671" class="wp-caption-text">后排座椅可向上折起增载物空间。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
