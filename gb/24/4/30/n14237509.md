<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>疑受李斯特菌感染 大统华等品牌熟食被召回</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237539-1.png" />
<h6>（加拿大食品检验局提供图片）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年04月30日讯】（大纪元记者杨清清加拿大温哥华报导）由于可能受到李斯特菌污染，加拿大食品检验局（CFIA）正在召回三个品牌的肉类和蔬菜熟食，包括：精武牌（Kingwuu）、大统华厨房（T&amp;T Kitchen）和绝味牌（Juewei）。</p>
<p>被召回的产品包括精武出品的凉拌猪头肉和灯影肉丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14237525" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14237525" style="width: 478px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237525-5.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14237525" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237525-5.png" alt="" width="478" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14237525" class="wp-caption-text">（加拿大食品检验局提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14237526" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14237526" style="width: 518px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237526-6.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14237526" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237526-6.png" alt="" width="518" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14237526" class="wp-caption-text">（加拿大食品检验局提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大统华厨房也召回了几种产品，包括香辣腐竹、香辣鸭脖、香辣鸭舌、香辣鹅胗、香辣牛肚和香辣板鸭肠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14237528" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14237528" style="width: 470px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237528-2.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14237528" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237528-2.png" alt="" width="470" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14237528" class="wp-caption-text">（加拿大食品检验局提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14237530" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14237530" style="width: 497px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237530-3.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14237530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237530-3.png" alt="" width="497" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14237530" class="wp-caption-text">（加拿大食品检验局提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14237532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14237532" style="width: 475px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237532-4.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14237532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237532-4.png" alt="" width="475" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14237532" class="wp-caption-text">（加拿大食品检验局提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>被召回的绝味牌产品包括五香牛腱、五香鸭脖、五香猪蹄、麻辣土豆、麻辣腐竹、麻辣牛板筋、麻辣牛肚、麻辣凤爪、麻辣鸡翅尖、麻辣鸭板肠、麻辣鸭头、麻辣鸭掌、麻辣鸭舌、麻辣鸭脖、麻辣毛豆、麻辣鹅胗、麻辣海带、麻辣莲藕片等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14237534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14237534" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237534-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14237534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237534-7-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14237534" class="wp-caption-text">（加拿大食品检验局提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14237536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14237536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237536-8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14237536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14237536-8-600x405.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14237536" class="wp-caption-text">（加拿大食品检验局提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些产品主要是在卑诗省和亚伯达省销售，其他省份可能也有销售。加拿大食品检验局正在对这些产品进行调查，可能会召回更多产品。</p>
<p>此次召回是由绝味食品公司发起的，但目前还没有与这些产品相关的疾病报告。</p>
<p>但是，如果您确实有任何被召回的产品，请不要食用，而应该将它们扔掉或退回到购买地。</p>
<p>虽然受李斯特菌污染的食物可能看起来或闻起来没有变质，但仍会导致人患病。症状包括呕吐、恶心、持续发烧、肌肉酸痛、严重头痛和颈部僵硬。</p>
<p>老年人、免疫力低下的人和孕妇尤其容易感染。◇</p>
