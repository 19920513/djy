<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加东自驾游：抵达格罗斯莫恩国家公园 美景如画</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118742-20210727_canada_helen_chou_216-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文?<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/21/7/20/n13101382.md#1">加东自驾游：出发第一天 佩姬湾小渔村和灯塔</a></p>
<p class="title">今天两个女人要从北悉尼(North Sydney)摆渡上纽芬兰岛的西南端钱纳尔巴斯克港(Channel-port aux Basques)。从北悉尼到钱纳尔巴斯克港184公里，坐船要7个多小时。摆渡轮一般每天中午有一班，半夜有一班。能够坐上半夜那一班是最好的，省时间，还可以省一夜的酒店。我们因为是临时起意，说走就走，没买到半夜的那一班。船上是有卧铺的，我们订票时也都卖完了。好在大家都有对号入座的座位，将就将就吧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118708-20210727_canada_helen_chou_202.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118708-20210727_canada_helen_chou_202-600x450.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118708" class="wp-caption-text">前往纽芬兰钱纳尔巴斯克港的客轮。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>早上睡了个懒觉，慢吞吞开到码头，在停车场坐在车里排队上船，听从指挥停好车上楼找到座位后接着打盹，养精蓄锐。反正外面下着雨也看不到什么美景。七个多小时后我们在雨中抵达纽芬兰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118713" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118713-20210727_canada_helen_chou_203.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118713-20210727_canada_helen_chou_203-600x268.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="268" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118713" class="wp-caption-text">阴雨中的钱纳尔巴斯克港。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从船窗望出去，阴雨中的钱纳尔巴斯克港有点像童话里的小人国。</p>
<p>等我们下去找到车子，再坐在车里等着一辆辆车离船上码头，再到镇上找到预订的酒店，已经很晚了。出门觅食，居然找不到一家看的过去的饭店。最后吃了一顿平生最难吃的“中餐”回酒店睡觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118720" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118720-20210727_canada_helen_chou_204.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118720-20210727_canada_helen_chou_204-600x415.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118720" class="wp-caption-text">开启纽芬兰之旅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二天一早离开钱纳尔巴斯克港，一路向北再往西，前往这次纽芬兰岛上的重头戏 – 格罗斯莫恩国家公园(Gros Morne National Park)。一路上先是下雨，然后阴天，然后晴天，然后又下雨……</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118723-20210727_canada_helen_chou_205.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118723-20210727_canada_helen_chou_205-600x345.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="345" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118723" class="wp-caption-text">格罗斯莫恩国家公园第一印象。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3小时后正式进入格罗斯莫恩国家公园。这个公园坐落在纽芬兰岛的西岸，面积有1805平方公里，是加拿大大西洋省份中第二大的国家公园。它的名字来源于公园内的纽芬兰第二高峰 – 格罗斯莫恩山。Gros Morne源于法语，字母意思是“非常孤独，”在这里表示“孤独站立的大山。”我们过几天会去看一看、爬一爬这座孤独站立的大山。今天先从公园穿过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118724" style="width: 380px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118724-20210727_canada_helen_chou_206.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13118724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118724-20210727_canada_helen_chou_206-600x800.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="380" b="507" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118724" class="wp-caption-text">格罗斯莫恩国家公园第一印象。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再往前开一小时左右，就到了公园里一个风景如画的小镇洛基港(Rocky Harbour)。这里有个景点叫Green Point Geological Site，绿点地质化石湾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118726" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118726-20210727_canada_helen_chou_207.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118726-20210727_canada_helen_chou_207-600x374.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="374" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118726" class="wp-caption-text">通往绿点地质化石湾的路。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13118727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118727" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118727-20210727_canada_helen_chou_208.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118727-20210727_canada_helen_chou_208-600x416.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118727" class="wp-caption-text">绿点地质化石湾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大约5亿年前，造物主做了一个Lady M千层蛋糕，然后转了90度让它垂直站起来，就是我们现在看到的地质层。地质学家在2000年时把这里定为全球界线层型剖面和点位。呃，别问我啥意思。我只知道这蛋糕的一层，一毫米就是100年！我每走一步，几万年，几十万年，就这么“唰”一下过去了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118730" style="width: 381px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118730-20210727_canada_helen_chou_209.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13118730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118730-20210727_canada_helen_chou_209-600x773.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="381" b="491" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118730" class="wp-caption-text">千层蛋糕般的岩石层。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13118732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118732" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118732-20210727_canada_helen_chou_210.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118732-20210727_canada_helen_chou_210-600x428.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118732" class="wp-caption-text">绿点地质化石湾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>岩石上的草特别长，特别软，一步步踩上去软到心里。踩过5亿年前的岩石，又回到人间四月天。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118733-20210727_canada_helen_chou_211.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118733-20210727_canada_helen_chou_211-600x417.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118733" class="wp-caption-text">绿点地质化石湾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>坐在坡顶红椅上，远眺大海，恍然间不知今夕是何年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118734" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118734-20210727_canada_helen_chou_212.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118734-20210727_canada_helen_chou_212-600x450.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118734" class="wp-caption-text">绿点地质化石湾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样贴耳聆听大海的声音，心中是会升起莫言的敬畏的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118736" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118736-20210727_canada_helen_chou_213.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118736-20210727_canada_helen_chou_213-600x416.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118736" class="wp-caption-text">洛基港小镇美如画。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13118738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118738" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118738-20210727_canada_helen_chou_214.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118738" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118738-20210727_canada_helen_chou_214-600x400.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118738" class="wp-caption-text">洛基港小镇美如画。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13118740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118740" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118740-20210727_canada_helen_chou_215.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118740" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118740-20210727_canada_helen_chou_215-600x399.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118740" class="wp-caption-text">洛基港小镇美如画。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从洛基港往南10分钟是另一个如仙境般的小镇Norris Point，诺里斯点。这里山水环抱，民风淳朴。我们预订了一家在水边的民宿。和房东老太太交接后我们出门继续赏美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13118742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118742-20210727_canada_helen_chou_216.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13118742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118742-20210727_canada_helen_chou_216-600x344.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="600" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118742" class="wp-caption-text">诺里斯点的山山水水。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13118744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13118744" style="width: 380px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118744-20210727_canada_helen_chou_217.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13118744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13118744-20210727_canada_helen_chou_217-450x600.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="380" b="507" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13118744" class="wp-caption-text">诺里斯点的山山水水。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
