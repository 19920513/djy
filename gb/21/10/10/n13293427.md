<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：纽约皇后区庆双十升旗礼 侨胞欢腾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293443-152953-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>皇后区庆祝双十国庆现场。（林宜君/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年10月10日讯】（大纪元记者林宜君<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>报导）无畏疫情，海外侨胞周六（9日）在纽约市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%87%E5%90%8E%E5%8C%BA.md#1">皇后区</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B.md#1">法拉盛</a>闹区举办双十<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%BA%86.md#1">国庆</a>活动，中华民国驻外使节、纽约州参议员、众议员代表、市议员均莅临现场，同民众手持美国星条旗与青天白日满地红旗，欢庆中华民国110岁生日，肯定中华民国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE.md#1">台湾</a>自由民主精神，祝贺台美关系深化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13293445" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13293445" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293445-152955.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13293445" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293445-152955.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13293445" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%87%E5%90%8E%E5%8C%BA.md#1">皇后区</a>双十<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%BA%86.md#1">国庆</a>升旗典礼与会来宾合照。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13293444" aria-describedby="caption-13293444" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293444-152954-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293444-152954-450x300.jpg" alt="驻纽约经文处处长李光章。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13293444" class="wp-caption-text">驻<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>经文处处长李光章。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13293438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13293438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293438-152947-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13293438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293438-152947-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13293438" class="wp-caption-text">皇后区双十国庆升旗典礼。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有别于去年在体育场举办升旗典礼，今年的双十国庆在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B.md#1">法拉盛</a>的喜来登酒店（Sheraton LaGuadia East Hotel）户外进行。9日一早，民众涌入典礼场地，一手拿着美国国旗与中华民国国旗，一手拿着相机记录庆典，场面相当热络。</p>
<p>双十国庆筹备主委林映君身着酒红旗袍，华丽亮相，这是她连续第六年接下统筹规划皇后区双十国庆典礼的重责大任。</p>
<p>林映君致词时表示，虽然疫情还未消除，但是她坚持要继续举行升旗典礼，因为对海外侨胞而言，这面国旗的意义格外珍贵。</p>
<p>升旗典礼开始时，现场先演奏美国国歌，接着才是中华民国国歌。随着国旗冉冉升起，现场侨胞朗声唱起异乡游子久违的国歌；当青天白日满地红的国旗在空中飘扬，林映君带领现场民众高呼“中华民国万岁”、“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE.md#1">台湾</a>加油”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13293436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13293436" style="width: 487px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293436-152945-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13293436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293436-152945-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="487" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13293436" class="wp-caption-text">前方人物为皇后区双十国庆筹备主委林映君。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13293442" aria-describedby="caption-13293442" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293442-152951-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293442-152951-450x300.jpg" alt="纽约中华公所主席于金山。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13293442" class="wp-caption-text">纽约中华公所主席于金山。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻纽约经文处处长李光章表示，拜登政府继续深化与中华民国台湾的关系，而台湾目前是美国第九大的出口贸易国家，并感谢纽约州众议员金兑锡（Ron Kim）等人于2019年提案，推动“友我决议”（第01495号及第439号决议）。</p>
<figure id="13293437" aria-describedby="caption-13293437" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293437-152946-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293437-152946-450x300.jpg" alt="由左至右为：纽约中华公所主席于金山、驻纽约经文处处长李光章、法拉盛市议员顾雅明。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13293437" class="wp-caption-text">由左至右为：纽约中华公所主席于金山、驻纽约经文处处长李光章、法拉盛市议员顾雅明。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13293435" aria-describedby="caption-13293435" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293435-152944-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293435-152944-450x300.jpg" alt="皇后区双十国庆活动现场。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13293435" class="wp-caption-text">皇后区双十国庆活动现场。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>法拉盛市议员顾雅明（Peter Koo）表示，他认同中华民国的三民主义精神，法拉盛在30年前相当荒芜，是由台湾人开始拓垦，一家商店接着一家商店开，带动法拉盛的繁荣经济，现在连酒店与商城都有了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13293441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13293441" style="width: 504px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293441-152950-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13293441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293441-152950-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="504" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13293441" class="wp-caption-text">法拉盛市议员顾雅明。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13293434" aria-describedby="caption-13293434" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293434-152943-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293434-152943-450x300.jpg" alt="皇后区双十国庆活动现场。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13293434" class="wp-caption-text">皇后区双十国庆活动现场。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13293440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13293440" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293440-152949-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13293440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293440-152949-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="498" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13293440" class="wp-caption-text">州众议员金兑锡办公室代表杨爱伦。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13293433" aria-describedby="caption-13293433" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293433-152942-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13293433-152942-450x300.jpg" alt="皇后区双十国庆活动现场。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13293433" class="wp-caption-text">皇后区双十国庆活动现场。（林宜君/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
