<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>重阳敬老 狮子会与长辈会送礼祝福</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324061-1-A29F3534-1DE6-4E39-BBDC-15F868595B19-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>台湾人狮子会、长辈会10月21日庆祝重阳节，表达对长者敬老尊贤。（袁玫／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年10月23日讯】（大纪元记者袁玫<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>报导）黄历9月9日<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8D%E9%98%B3%E8%8A%82.md#1">重阳节</a>，又称<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%AC%E8%80%81%E8%8A%82.md#1">敬老节</a>，这一天有重九祭祖、敬老崇孝的传统。10月21日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E4%BA%BA%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">美国台湾人狮子会</a>和长辈会举行重阳敬老活动，给80岁以上的长辈们送礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13324066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13324066" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324066-4-7B906AF0-6A0A-4EEF-B31F-8509DD2FCE33.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13324066" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324066-4-7B906AF0-6A0A-4EEF-B31F-8509DD2FCE33-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13324066" class="wp-caption-text">台湾人狮子会、长辈会10月21日举办庆祝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8D%E9%98%B3%E8%8A%82.md#1">重阳节</a>活动。台湾会馆董事长陈柏宇致词向在场长辈献上敬意。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻洛台北经文处副处长林晖程也携带精美礼物及新护照赠予长辈会百岁人瑞，他说，他代表处长来参加这个有意义的活动，希望经文处的服务因疫情松缓能慢慢走上轨道，更完善服务侨民，也祝福长者健康快乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13324064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13324064" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324064-3-3286EF7B-DB10-4DDF-9BB5-A1FD49E57241.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13324064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324064-3-3286EF7B-DB10-4DDF-9BB5-A1FD49E57241-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13324064" class="wp-caption-text">台湾人狮子会与长辈会10月21日举办庆祝重阳节活动，经文处副处长林晖程赠送礼物给百岁人瑞。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>狮子会会长谢秀缎表示，敬老尊贤是台湾很好的传统文化，狮子会每年都对长辈会的长者们表达心意，今年亦不例外带来礼物和礼金表达狮子会的敬意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13324067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13324067" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324067-5-225F0D84-E01E-40EB-94BF-9B35FA4AE42E.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13324067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324067-5-225F0D84-E01E-40EB-94BF-9B35FA4AE42E-600x606.jpeg" alt="" width="500" b="505" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13324067" class="wp-caption-text">台湾人狮子会会长谢秀缎致词。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13324063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13324063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324063-2-AF2DF6BA-5AA7-4C42-B6FD-5648F91626B0.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13324063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324063-2-AF2DF6BA-5AA7-4C42-B6FD-5648F91626B0-600x425.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13324063" class="wp-caption-text">台湾人狮子会与长辈会10月21日举办庆祝重阳节活动，赠送长者礼物和礼金。图左为狮子会会长谢秀缎、右为长辈会会长李美红。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>长辈会会长李美红感谢大家的热情支持及赠与，并公布下一次的活动是在12月庆祝圣诞节，到时也会通知长辈们参加同贺。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13324068" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13324068" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324068-6-849A29B0-A4DC-4B49-8204-37C8D37E8335.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13324068" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13324068-6-849A29B0-A4DC-4B49-8204-37C8D37E8335-600x411.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13324068" class="wp-caption-text">台湾人长辈会会长李美红致词。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
