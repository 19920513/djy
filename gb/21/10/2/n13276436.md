<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>吴姗儒4千万珠宝闪耀登金钟 大霈优雅迷人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276478-2110020749231487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第56届电视金钟奖星光大道于10月2日晚间在台北市国父纪念馆举行，图右为入围最佳女主角的大霈（李霈瑜）身穿细肩带露背白色礼服；吴姗儒配戴顶级珠宝，闪耀逼人。（三立／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年10月02日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）第56届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E8%A7%86%E9%87%91%E9%92%9F.md#1">电视金钟</a>奖星光大道于10月2日晚间在台北市国父纪念馆举行，入围最佳女主角的大霈<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E9%9C%88%E7%91%9C.md#1">李霈瑜</a>）身穿细肩带露背白色礼服，获得外界一片赞赏；而准新娘<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%A7%97%E5%84%92.md#1">吴姗儒</a>更是配戴要价4千万的顶级珠宝，闪耀逼人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13276704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276704" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276704-2110020930472560.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276704" title="电视金钟奖 金钟56 大霈" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276704-2110020930472560.jpg" alt="电视金钟奖 金钟56 大霈" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276704" class="wp-caption-text">大霈<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E9%9C%88%E7%91%9C.md#1">李霈瑜</a>）身穿细肩带露背白色礼服，获得外界一片赞赏。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13276586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276586" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276586-2110020846172560.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276586" title="电视金钟奖 金钟56 吴姗儒（Sandy）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276586-2110020846172560.jpg" alt="电视金钟奖 金钟56 吴姗儒（Sandy）" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276586" class="wp-caption-text">准新娘<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%A7%97%E5%84%92.md#1">吴姗儒</a>（Sandy）配戴要价4千万PIAGET珠宝、身穿闪耀银色礼服成为瞩目焦点。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>典礼主持人视网膜率先登场，爱犬柚子也穿上西装，可爱卖萌；红毯主持人由黄豪平搭档美女主播蔡尚桦，一身红色灯笼袖礼服展现大方气度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13276659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276659" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276659-2110020912212560.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276659" title="电视金钟奖 金钟56 视网膜" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276659-2110020912212560.jpg" alt="电视金钟奖 金钟56 视网膜" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276659" class="wp-caption-text">2021第56届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E8%A7%86%E9%87%91%E9%92%9F.md#1">电视金钟</a>奖典礼主持人视网膜率先登场，爱犬柚子也穿上西装，可爱卖萌。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13276536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276536-2110020709371487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276536" title="电视金钟奖 黄豪平 蔡尚桦" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276536-2110020709371487.jpg" alt="电视金钟奖 黄豪平 蔡尚桦" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276536" class="wp-caption-text">2021第56届电视金钟奖星光大道由黄豪平（右）与蔡尚桦搭档主持。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大霈（李霈瑜）身穿细肩带露背白色礼服，简单优雅的衬托出好身材；准新娘吴姗儒（Sandy）配戴要价4千万PIAGET珠宝、身穿闪耀银色礼服成为瞩目焦点，特别感谢未婚夫鼓励她尽情做自己；简嫚书以一袭银色缀珠礼服走上红毯，散发复古华美的氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13276538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276538-2110020813422560.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276538" title="电视金钟奖 金钟56 简嫚书" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276538-2110020813422560.jpg" alt="电视金钟奖 金钟56 简嫚书" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276538" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾简嫚书以一袭银色缀珠礼服走上红毯，散发复古华美的氛围。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>温贞菱一袭黑色连身裤展现率性；Janet（谢怡芬）的黑色单片式开衩礼服，时尚俐落；钟瑶的西装拼接豹纹短裙礼服与妆容相得益彰，散发冶艳气质。</p>
