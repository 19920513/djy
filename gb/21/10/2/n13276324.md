<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—绿幛（彩墨）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276331-36f3bfac148e9b0626f7cbdfc81028c6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—绿幛（彩墨）。（局部）。（图片来源：徐明义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E5%B9%9B.md#1">绿幛</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）</p>
<p>有一次，我去散步时，捡到一块人家丢弃的椭圆形海棉，仔细一看，有很多不规则的小孔洞。嗯，好像可以拿来作画呢。于是就拿回来沾墨沾色试试，画就许多张不同的构图，这是其中之一。（另一张题为樱花季，于专辑P. 66）</p>
<p>平时我若捡到树枝、草茎、细沙、羽毛、菜瓜布、玉米鞘、棕榈须……等，我都会拿回来试试。因为作了很多怪异的作品，吸引了“公共电视——文人政事”节目主持人蔡诗萍先生的注意。蒙他邀访，在公视频道“文人政事第348集”播出“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89.md#1">徐明义</a>实验玩乐水墨画”（日期2013?10?16）播出一小时，后再数次重播。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13276331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276331" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276331-36f3bfac148e9b0626f7cbdfc81028c6.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13276331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276331-36f3bfac148e9b0626f7cbdfc81028c6.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="577" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276331" class="wp-caption-text">困知勉行—<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89%E7%94%BB%E9%9B%868.md#1">徐明义画集8</a>—<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E5%B9%9B.md#1">绿幛</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）。67×67cm。（图片来源：<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89.md#1">徐明义</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>Green banner/ink and color painting</h2>
<p>Once, when I went for a walk, I picked up a discarded oval sponge.? Took a close look, there are many irregular holes. Well, it seemed that I can use it for painting. So, I took it back and dipped it with ink for trying, drew many different compositions and this is one of them.</p>
<p>Usually, if I picked up branches, grass stems, sands, feathers, vegetable melon cloth, corn sheaths, palm whiskers, etc., I took them back and gave a try. Due to present too many weird subjects, which have caught the attention of Mr. Chua Sze-ping, the host of &#8220;Literati Affairs – Public Television &#8220;. I was invited and interviewed in the program of &#8220;Xu Ming yi Experimental Ink Painting Pleasure&#8221; broadcasting (2013/10/10/16) on the public TV channel &#8220;Theaters for Scholars series 348&#8221; for one hour.＠</p>
