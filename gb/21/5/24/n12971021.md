<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>有灵魂的牛肉罗宋汤 风味让人惊艳的秘诀</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971271-596571183356355cd5bfa1b4cb95d857-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>小火熬煮的牛肉罗宋汤，营养可口100分。（野人文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：简单煮就满足家人的味蕾！这一道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%AE%8B%E6%B1%A4.md#1">罗宋汤</a>包含各种食材的精华，运用火侯的控制让蔬果尽情展现其鲜甜，一点都不困难，让老手实用新手受用的好食谱，重点是把握好料理秘诀就能煮得很好吃。</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%AE%8B%E6%B1%A4.md#1">罗宋汤</a></span></h2>
<p>这道料理制作有两重点，其一是蔬菜都要切成小丁口感才会好，其二是要把所有的蔬菜，以中小火炒透炒出甜味，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%A4%E5%93%81.md#1">汤品</a>的风味才会让人惊艳，要记得没有把蔬菜炒透的罗宋汤是没有灵魂的！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（4 ～ 6 人份）</span></h3>
<p>牛肋条?600 克<br />
洋葱?1 颗（300 克）<br />
红萝卜?1 根（200 克）<br />
西洋芹?2 根（150 克）<br />
高丽菜?1/4 颗（250 克）<br />
马铃薯?1 颗（300 克）<br />
牛番茄?2 颗（300 克）<br />
蒜头?6 瓣（30 克）<br />
月桂叶?1 片<br />
百里香（Thyme）?5 根<br />
欧芹（Parsley）?3 根<br />
料理白酒?50cc<br />
水?1200cc<br />
番茄糊?3 大匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味料（成品调味用）</span></h3>
<p>盐?1/4 小匙（炒番茄用）<br />
盐、糖、黑胡椒粉?1/2 小匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>① 蒜头切末、洋葱切块、牛番茄、红萝卜、西洋芹、马铃薯切小丁、高丽菜切小片、牛肋条切3至5cm块备用。<br />
② 锅内下2大匙橄榄油，放入牛肋条煎至上色取出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971272" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971272-73e5aa0b4274ae760fea13fa1dc986ae.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971272-73e5aa0b4274ae760fea13fa1dc986ae-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971272" class="wp-caption-text">锅内下2大匙橄榄油，放入牛肋条煎至上色取出备用。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>③ 利用锅中余油，中小火将洋葱（5分钟）、红萝卜（5分钟）、西洋芹（3分钟）炒透。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971273" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971273-7dffba69ca4b8b23941c7db92de957ad.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971273" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971273-7dffba69ca4b8b23941c7db92de957ad-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971273" class="wp-caption-text">利用锅中余油，中小火将洋葱、红萝卜、西洋芹炒透。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>④ 加入蒜末、番茄及1/4小匙盐，炒至番茄呈泥状（5分钟）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971269" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971269-c115049f253162be875d5557ba1d65e0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971269" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971269-c115049f253162be875d5557ba1d65e0-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971269" class="wp-caption-text">加入蒜末、番茄及1/4小匙盐，炒至番茄呈泥状。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑤ 加入高丽菜小片，炒至软化且水分释出（5分钟）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971287-3499e6206ff94225872065a70b984c4a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971287-3499e6206ff94225872065a70b984c4a-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971287" class="wp-caption-text">加入高丽菜小片，炒至软化且水分释出。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑥ 加入马铃薯小丁及番茄糊， 炒至所有食材上色（1分钟） 。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971288" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971288-c3cd89915214920698f879d75f2aac6d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971288-c3cd89915214920698f879d75f2aac6d-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971288" class="wp-caption-text">加入马铃薯小丁及番茄糊， 炒至所有食材上色。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑦ 加入白酒，大火滚煮至水分收干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971292" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971292-86bd03985d931cdd252b525af52a2748.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971292-86bd03985d931cdd252b525af52a2748-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971292" class="wp-caption-text">加入白酒，大火滚煮至水分收干。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑧ 内锅放入牛肉、百里香、月桂叶及炒好的蔬菜（再次强调务必全部炒透如图中所示）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971293" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971293-2bb4c337a8f4309dda2175e5e32c0ce1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971293-2bb4c337a8f4309dda2175e5e32c0ce1-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971293" class="wp-caption-text">内锅放入牛肉、百里香、月桂叶及炒好的蔬菜。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑨ 内锅加水淹过食材，外锅共4杯水蒸至牛肋条软化即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971294" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971294-bbe0a59fc435fa5b5b1dcb9eb895d054.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971294-bbe0a59fc435fa5b5b1dcb9eb895d054-450x447.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971294" class="wp-caption-text">内锅加水淹过食材，外锅共4杯水蒸至牛肋条软化即完成。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑩完成后加入调味料，撒上切碎欧芹即完成！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971295" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971295-f0fbdc6a65cd4420576efd4ef42c1964.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971295-f0fbdc6a65cd4420576efd4ef42c1964-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971295" class="wp-caption-text">完成后加入调味料，撒上切碎欧芹即完成！（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
