<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>调配果树盆栽专用的培养土  4步骤一学就会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969395-P.19-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>只要四个步骤就能调配出栽培果树用的土壤。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>从以前到现在园艺的书上都会写，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E5%85%BB%E5%9C%9F.md#1">培养土</a>应混合多少比例赤玉土、川砂、腐叶土、鹿沼土或蛭石等。看起来很简单，但其实意外的困难。不管是混合的顺序、各自的水分含量、天候，还是混合后要放置的时间等等，都不能不一一考量。所以不太推荐用这种繁复的方法混合土壤。</p>
<p>其实只要购买<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E5%85%BB%E5%9C%9F.md#1">培养土</a>专门业者调配出的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%9C%E6%A0%91.md#1">果树</a>用培养土”，就很方便。就算不是果树专用的培养土，一般贩售的花草用土（非廉价的种类）中，赤玉土含量30～40％的土壤也很适合（直径小于30cm的盆器用30％，大的则用40％）。</p>
<h2>各式培养土</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12969380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969380" style="width: 301px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969380-P.18-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12969380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969380-P.18-1.jpg" alt="栽培土" width="301" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969380" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%9C%E6%A0%91.md#1">果树</a>专用类型培养土。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>果树专用类型</strong>：最近市面上出现很多蔬菜或花卉专用的培养土种类。事先就均衡调配好基础土壤和改良土壤，在使用上非常方便。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969381" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969381" style="width: 298px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969381-P.18-2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12969381" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969381-P.18-2.jpg" alt="培养土" width="298" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969381" class="wp-caption-text">园艺用培养土。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>园艺用</strong>：一般贩售的园艺用培养土。只要再以3比1的比例混入赤玉土，就会变成非常好用的土壤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969383" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969383" style="width: 295px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969383-P.18-3.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12969383" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969383-P.18-3.jpg" alt="培养土" width="295" b="373" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969383" class="wp-caption-text">蓝莓专用培养土。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>蓝莓专用</strong>：蓝莓专用土。蓝莓喜欢弱酸性的土质，移植或换盆时若能使用专用的土壤就很方便。</p>
<h2>基本用土</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12969385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969385" style="width: 285px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969385-P.18-4.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12969385" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969385-P.18-4.jpg" alt="培养土" width="285" b="268" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969385" class="wp-caption-text">赤玉土（中粒）。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12969388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969388" style="width: 286px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969388-P.18-5.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12969388" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969388-P.18-5.jpg" alt="培养土" width="286" b="265" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969388" class="wp-caption-text">市售培养土。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>

市售的培养土（右），已经先均衡调配好基础土壤和改良土壤，可以立即使用。</p>

<p>石灰粒（左）就是粒状的石灰，用来中和酸性的土壤。粒状溶解的速度比粉状慢，效果更能持久。<br />
熔磷（右）是含有20％磷酸和29％钙的缓效碱性材料。</p>
<h2>土壤的制作方法</h2>
<p>1. 将市售培养土和赤玉土倒入容器。两者的比例约为7：3。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969395-P.19-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12969395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969395-P.19-1-600x413.jpg" alt="培养土" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969395" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 用铲子均匀混合培养土和赤玉土。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969397" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969397-P.19-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12969397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969397-P.19-2-600x425.jpg" alt="培养土" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969397" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 加入碱性的熔磷和中和酸性土壤的石灰粒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969400" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969400-P.19-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12969400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969400-P.19-3-600x379.jpg" alt="培养土" width="600" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969400" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 再次均匀混合后就完成了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969402" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969402-P.19-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12969402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969402-P.19-4-600x390.jpg" alt="培养土" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969402" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>优良培养土的条件</h2>
<p>1. <strong>丰富的有机质</strong>：能够储蓄养分，有助于活化微生物的活动。<br />
2. <strong>保水性、保肥性佳</strong>：土壤能充分保持水分和养分。<br />
3. <strong>排水、透气性佳</strong>：团粒状的土壤能排出多余水分，帮助根部呼吸。<br />
4. <strong>可促进细根的生长茂盛</strong>：柔软、酸性适度的土壤，能够促进细根的成长。<br />
5. <strong>不使用二次发酵的堆肥</strong>：使用成熟的堆肥才不会因浇水等产生发酵热，伤害到果树根部。</p>
<h2>栽培知识</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%86%E6%A0%BD.md#1">盆栽</a>栽培要以排水性和透气性为第一考量。若说盆栽栽培的关键在于浇水的技术，其实也不为过。排水性佳、具有适度保肥力和保水力的用土，需要以赤玉土、腐叶土或黑土为基底，再添加必要的土壤做调整。用土如果长期潮湿的话，容器下层就容易缺氧，导致根部无法生长。必须保持稍微干燥，下层的氧气充足，才会长出很多新根，根量多的话，即使只有少量的水和肥料也能有效率地吸收。换句话说，等到土壤表面稍微泛白干涸再充分浇水即可。</p>
