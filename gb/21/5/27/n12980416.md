<p><strong>生态悦读趣：</strong></p>
<h2><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%B2%E8%91%B5.md#1">蒲葵</a>Livistona chinensis <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%95%E6%A6%88.md#1">棕榈</a>科</strong></h2>
<p>又称扇叶<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%B2%E8%91%B5.md#1">蒲葵</a>、球蒲葵、木葵、铁力木、扇椰子、葵扇子、蓬扇树。常绿乔木。树干通直不分枝，株高可达15公尺，外表粗糙。单叶丛生于顶端，扇形深裂；叶柄有刺，叶柄呈三角形，边缘有锯齿状刺；叶大呈掌状分裂，裂片成线形，叶端有分裂，而裂开的地方成弧形下垂。穗状圆锥花序，直立，花黄色。雌雄同株。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12980426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12980426" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980426-497924_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12980426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980426-497924_medium-600x445.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12980426" class="wp-caption-text">穗状圆锥花序，直立，花黄色。雌雄同株。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>果实蓝绿色，长椭圆形，成熟后变成黑褐色，是鸟儿们的美食，内有种子一粒。庭园树或行道树。树干可制成伞柄、屋柱，叶又可制伞。叶鞘具纤维，可制作绳索。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12980428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12980428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980428-497926_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12980428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980428-497926_medium-600x799.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12980428" class="wp-caption-text">扇叶摇晃树下睡，炎炎夏日南风吹。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12980427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12980427" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980427-497925_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12980427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980427-497925_medium-600x799.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12980427" class="wp-caption-text">单叶丛生于顶端，扇形深裂；叶柄有刺，叶柄呈三角形，边缘有锯齿状刺；叶大呈掌状分裂，裂片成线形，叶端有分裂，而裂开的地方成弧形下垂。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
