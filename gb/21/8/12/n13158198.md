<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中华民俗艺术工作坊“永恒传奇”再现</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158183-DSC03253-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>龙腾秘境。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年08月13日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨威斯顿镇报导）经过去年因疫情的停演之后，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%B7%A5%E4%BD%9C%E5%9D%8A.md#1">中华民俗艺术工作坊</a>（CFAW）于8月7日恢复了在瑞吉斯学院（Regis College）举办的年度公演“永恒传奇”，下午和晚上的两场表演吸引数百观众到场观看和喝采。</p>
<p>在威斯顿镇（Weston）的瑞吉斯学院剧场，观众和工作人员都戴着口罩进场。而演出之后，主办方也没有像往年一样举办招待会。但今年的观众比往年更加热情，13个节目的每一个都会多次迎来大家的掌声、欢呼和喝采。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13158177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13158177" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158177-DSC03453.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13158177" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158177-DSC03453-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13158177" class="wp-caption-text">众多观众前来观看表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演出以声势浩大的击鼓表演拉开序幕，而后还有各种传统和现代<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88.md#1">舞蹈</a>，以及舞龙、舞狮、扯铃等<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">民俗艺术</a>表演。今年的演出主题是“永恒传奇”。据节目册介绍，这象征传统东方文化和现代西方文化的融合，以及这种独特文化的永久传承。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13158184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13158184" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158184-DSC03226.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13158184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158184-DSC03226-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13158184" class="wp-caption-text">齐鼓震天。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13158182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13158182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158182-DSC03354.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13158182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158182-DSC03354-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13158182" class="wp-caption-text">功夫传奇。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13158179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13158179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158179-DSC03426.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13158179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158179-DSC03426-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13158179" class="wp-caption-text">绿意盎然。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13158197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13158197" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158197-DSC03288.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13158197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158197-DSC03288-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13158197" class="wp-caption-text">狮舞吉祥。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%B7%A5%E4%BD%9C%E5%9D%8A.md#1">中华民俗艺术工作坊</a>团长张昆介绍，这次演出的节目策划断断续续进行，总共历经一年多的时间。在演出之前，孩子们的排练也有好几周了，而且很多已经毕业的舞团成员也都回来参演。</p>
<p>张昆笑言，大家都非常用心地排练，但由于疫情仍在，他们在排练场地还是要“小心翼翼”地采取防疫措施。</p>
<p>对于明年的演出，张昆说，目前还没有计划，因为“要先把今年的做好”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13158178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13158178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158178-DSC03437.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13158178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13158178-DSC03437-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13158178" class="wp-caption-text">演出之后，演员谢幕。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有观众在表演后称赞，今年的节目使用了很多干冰，感觉像幻化的仙境。她说，男团员的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88.md#1">舞蹈</a>技巧比往年提升，令她印象深刻。◇</p>
