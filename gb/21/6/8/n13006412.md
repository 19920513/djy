<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>六四32周年 卡城市民中领馆前烛光悼念</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13006497-20210607-Movement-for-Democracy-in-China-64-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>烛光悼念六四。（卡城民主促进会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月08日讯】2021年6月4日是天安门<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AD%E5%9B%9B%E5%B1%A0%E6%9D%80.md#1">六四屠杀</a>32周年，逾50名卡城市民于晚上10时，出席由“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A1%E5%8A%A0%E5%88%A9%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E6%B0%91%E4%B8%BB%E4%BF%83%E8%BF%9B%E4%BC%9A.md#1">卡加利中国民主促进会</a>”主办的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AD%E5%9B%9B%E7%83%9B%E5%85%89%E9%9B%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">六四烛光集会</a>，悼念1989年6月4日在天安门的死难者，并声援香港市民被禁进入维园悼六四。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13006500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13006500" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13006500-20210607-Movement-for-Democracy-in-China-64-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13006500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13006500-20210607-Movement-for-Democracy-in-China-64-03-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13006500" class="wp-caption-text">烛光悼念六四集会。（卡城民主促进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13006501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13006501" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13006501-20210607-Movement-for-Democracy-in-China-64-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13006501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13006501-20210607-Movement-for-Democracy-in-China-64-02-600x250.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13006501" class="wp-caption-text">烛光悼念六四集会。（卡城民主促进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参加者以安全分隔距离，手持电子蜡烛及“平反六四”、“还政于民”、“毋忘六四”等标语牌，在中领馆前默哀悼念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13006507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13006507" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13006507-20210607-Movement-for-Democracy-in-China-64-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13006507" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13006507-20210607-Movement-for-Democracy-in-China-64-04-600x425.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13006507" class="wp-caption-text">烛光悼念六四集会。（卡城民主促进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13006508" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13006508" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13006508-20210607-Movement-for-Democracy-in-China-64-05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13006508" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13006508-20210607-Movement-for-Democracy-in-China-64-05-450x548.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="548" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13006508" class="wp-caption-text">烛光悼念六四集会。（卡城民主促进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据主办单位卡城民促会汤会长表示，原先估计因疫情下不会有太多人参加，但想不到竟有50多人出席。部分参加者表示，因看到香港人被禁进入维园，连燃点烛光悼念六四的自由都没有，激起他们的愤慨，决定前来参加，要把维园的烛光照遍世界！</p>
