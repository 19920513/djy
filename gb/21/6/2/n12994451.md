<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台美人传统周 洪家栋推出台湾花东美景摄影展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994455-IMGP5035-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台美人传统周，洪家栋推出“台湾花东超美景色”摄影展。（洪家栋）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年06月03日讯】（大纪元记者袁玫<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>报导）美国六桂宗亲会会长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E5%AE%B6%E6%A0%8B.md#1">洪家栋</a>以行动展现对大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E7%BE%8E%E4%BA%BA%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F%E5%91%A8.md#1">台美人传统周</a>的支持，推出线上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E7%BB%8F%E5%85%B8%E7%B3%BB%E5%88%97%E5%B1%95.md#1">台湾摄影经典系列展</a>，分享他用镜头所捕捉到台湾花东如诗如画的美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994457-IMGP2915X.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994457-IMGP2915X-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994457" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E7%BE%8E%E4%BA%BA%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F%E5%91%A8.md#1">台美人传统周</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E5%AE%B6%E6%A0%8B.md#1">洪家栋</a>推出“台湾花东超美景色”摄影展。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12994477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994477-IMGP5171.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994477-IMGP5171-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994477" class="wp-caption-text">台美人传统周，洪家栋推出“台湾花东超美景色”摄影展。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12994481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994481-IMGP5263A.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994481-IMGP5263A-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994481" class="wp-caption-text">台美人传统周，洪家栋推出“台湾花东超美景色”摄影展。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<p>洪家栋会长2019年举办过精彩的北投风景摄影展，获得很大回响。2021年，他响应台美人传统周，将2019年2月、2020年1月和2月所摄作品成集与大家分享。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994460-IMGP3055TM.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994460-IMGP3055TM-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994460" class="wp-caption-text">台美人传统周，洪家栋推出“台湾花东超美景色”摄影展。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12994461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994461-IMGP3264.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994461-IMGP3264-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994461" class="wp-caption-text">台美人传统周，洪家栋推出“台湾花东超美景色”摄影展。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12994462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994462-IMGP3300TM.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994462-IMGP3300TM-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994462" class="wp-caption-text">台美人传统周，洪家栋推出“台湾花东超美景色”摄影展。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他说，大家都知道花莲有好山好水。他原本计划走两个花东古道：锥麓古道和亲不知子海上古道，后来又发现了难以割舍的美景，如锥麓吊桥、太鲁阁、九曲洞、长春祠、砂卡礑步道、砂卡礑大桥、四八高地-花莲、清水断崖、石门洞（麻糬洞）、石梯坪鱼港、石梯坪海岸、台东加路兰海岸、花莲港红灯塔、回澜吊桥、知本勇男桥、三仙台跨海桥、碟石滩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994466" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994466-IMGP3435.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994466-IMGP3435-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994466" class="wp-caption-text">台美人传统周，洪家栋推出“台湾花东超美景色”摄影展。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
