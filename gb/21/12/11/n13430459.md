<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【热炒料理】爽脆清香又营养 地中海蔬食锅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430478-9fbbfd073c66b9dfa86a4e2629fedf00-600x400.png" />
<h6>热火快炒10分钟就可以完成一道地中海蔬食料理。（山岳文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：结束了一整天的工作，回到家还要准备晚餐，是否感到心有余力不足呢？《<span style="color: #000000;">一锅搞定！美味炒锅料理40道》这一本书中提到，</span>“炒锅料理”解决了这一项难题，只要在炒锅内滴几滴<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84%E6%B2%B9.md#1">橄榄油</a>，倒入鲜切蔬菜，大火热炒一下子就可以完成一道色泽、风味和营养满分的家常菜。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">地中海<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E9%A3%9F.md#1">蔬食</a>锅</span></h2>
<p>份量︰4人份<br />
备料时间︰5分钟<br />
烹饪时间︰10分钟</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>1根茄子，切块<br />
1颗青椒，切细<br />
2支百里香<br />
1颗洋葱，切细<br />
2瓣蒜头，切碎<br />
2根栉瓜，切圆片<br />
10颗小番茄，每颗均切成四等分</p>
<figure id="attachment_13430479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430479" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430479-a6c58a71832fe8b07f4c0f2fd1c071bd.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13430479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430479-a6c58a71832fe8b07f4c0f2fd1c071bd-450x295.png" alt="" width="450" b="295" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430479" class="wp-caption-text">料理“地中海<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E9%A3%9F.md#1">蔬食</a>锅”的材料和成品。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">经典作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 倒1汤匙<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84%E6%B2%B9.md#1">橄榄油</a>到炒锅里。</p>
<p>2. 将栉瓜、青椒、茄子、洋葱、蒜头和百里香倒入锅里，以大火炒约10分钟。当蔬菜都呈现金黄色泽，再倒入番茄。</p>
<p>3. 撒上适量盐巴和胡椒粉调味后上桌，趁热品尝。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">变换口味</span></h3>
<p>● 将莫札瑞拉乳酪放到乡村面包上，然后放进烤箱烘烤。取出后，再将炒好的蔬菜铺在面包上一起品尝。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13426092" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13426092" style="width: 121px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426092-df0b42a4e72cb4c0b1a064b5afb3b953.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13426092" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426092-df0b42a4e72cb4c0b1a064b5afb3b953-450x558.jpg" alt="" width="121" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13426092" class="wp-caption-text">《一锅搞定！美味炒锅料理40道》书封。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
