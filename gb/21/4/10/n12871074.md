<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】夏威夷果仁炒牛柳粒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871112-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>夏威夷果仁炒牛柳粒。（图／梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】</strong>喜欢吃<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%A3.md#1">辣</a>的人有福了！在乍暖还冬的季节，来一盘有点辛辣却兼具肉质鲜甜的热炒菜，不只缓解身体对温度高低起伏的不适应，相对也会比较有胃口。</p>
<p>这一道菜以<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9D%9A%E6%9E%9C.md#1">坚果</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F%E5%A8%81%E5%A4%B7%E6%9E%9C%E4%BB%81.md#1">夏威夷果仁</a>）拌炒<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E6%9F%B3%E7%B2%92.md#1">牛柳粒</a>，增加了咀嚼的口感，只要搭配白饭，就可以让你吃上好几碗，整个停不下来。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F%E5%A8%81%E5%A4%B7%E6%9E%9C%E4%BB%81.md#1">夏威夷果仁</a>炒<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E6%9F%B3%E7%B2%92.md#1">牛柳粒</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>夏威夷果仁2oz<br />
青<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%A3.md#1">辣</a>椒3oz<br />
白洋葱4oz<br />
牛柳粒10oz<br />
姜蒜蓉少许<br />
XO酱1汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871103" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871103-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871103-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871103" class="wp-caption-text">由左至右，夏威夷果仁、牛柳粒、青辣椒、白洋葱、姜蒜蓉。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>水1/4杯<br />
糖1/4茶匙<br />
粟粉半茶匙<br />
麻油少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871104" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871104-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871104" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871104-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871104" class="wp-caption-text">麻油、粟粉、ＸＯ酱、糖、生抽。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【牛肉腌料】</span></h3>
<p>生抽、糖、栗粉各适量、少许水、少许生油</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 牛肉切成手指头粒大小，用生抽、糖，粟粉加少许水拌匀，腌约15分钟，最后加少许生油捞匀备用。</p>
<p>2. 洋葱和青辣椒切成粗粒后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871105" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871105-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871105-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871105" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱和青辣椒切成粗粒后备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热镬下油，先把牛肉粒炒至八成熟捞起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871106" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871106-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871106-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871106" class="wp-caption-text">热镬下油，先把牛肉粒炒至八成熟捞起。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 再放入姜蒜蓉、洋葱和辣椒爆炒一会，加入XO酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871107" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871107-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871107-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871107" class="wp-caption-text">再放入姜蒜蓉、洋葱和辣椒爆炒一会，加入XO酱。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 接着放回牛肉粒翻炒，搅匀调味汁液，快手倒入兜匀即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871108" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871108-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871108-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871108" class="wp-caption-text">接着放回牛肉粒翻炒，搅匀调味汁液，快手倒入兜匀即完成。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将菜肴装盘后，在上面放上夏威夷果仁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871126" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871126-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871126-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871126" class="wp-caption-text">菜肴装盘后，在上面放上夏威夷果仁。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h3>
<p>这道菜非常适合喜欢吃辣的人，菜肴中有多种香辛料，再加上XO酱以及夏威夷果仁，尝起来非常美味！◇</p>
