<p>新闻出处：新唐人英语新闻</p>
<p>Chronic Diseases on the Rise in China<br />
中国慢性疾病增加</p>
<p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />
1. <b>Chronic</b>: adj. [ˋkrɑn?k]慢性的<br />
2. <b>Disease</b>: n. [d?ˋziz]疾病<br />
3. <b>on the rise</b>: ph.增加<br />
4. <b>couple with</b>:ph. [ˋk?p!] [w?e]结合<br />
5. <b>sedentary</b>: adj. [ˋs?dn?t?r?]久坐的<br />
6. <b>lack of</b>: ph. [l?k] [ɑv]缺少<br />
7. <b>contribute to</b>: ph. [k?nˋtr?bjut] [tu]促成<br />
8. <b>respiratory</b>: adj. [r?ˋspa?r??tor?]呼吸的<br />
9. <b>state</b>: v. [stet]说明、声明<br />
10. <b>account of</b>: ph. [?ˋka?nt]说明、对…负有责任<br />
11. <b>prevention</b>: n. [pr?ˋv?n??n]预防<br />
12. <b>GDP</b>:abbr. gross domestic product 国内生产总值</p>
<p>A plate of greasy noodles, vodka-like alcohol, and a cigarette for lunch—this appears to be a typical lunch for middle-class Chinese.一盘油腻的面、类似伏特加味道的酒，和一根香烟,，这似乎是中国中产阶级人士的典型午餐。</p>
<p>Unhealthy diet and habits <b>coupled with sedentary</b> work and <b>lack of </b>exercise are <b>contributing to </b>many chronic health problems in China.<br />
不健康的饮食习惯，加上久坐不动的工作和缺乏运动，导致在中国有许多慢性病的健康问题。</p>
<p>As China moves from a mainly poor, rural community to a richer, urban society, chronic diseases such as heart disease, stroke, cancer, chronic<b> respiratory </b>diseases and diabetes become the leading cause of death—heart disease and cancer, the two top killers.<br />
因为中国从一个多数贫穷的农村，变成一个富有的城市社会，慢性疾病如：心脏、中风，癌症，慢性呼吸道疾病和糖尿病，成为主要死亡的原因。心脏疾病和癌症是两个头号杀手。</p>
<p>The World Health Organization <b>states</b> that chronic diseases are the cause of more than 80 percent of deaths in China in 2008.<br />
世界卫生组织指出，2008年，中国死亡人数中，超过80%来自慢性疾病。</p>
<p>China Daily reported the Chinese Health Ministry Deputy Director said several months ago that chronic illnesses <b>account for</b> 85 percent of all deaths in China.<br />
中国日报报导，中国卫生部副主任说，几个月前，中国死亡人数有85%归因于慢性疾病。</p>
<p>The<b> prevention</b> of chronic illnesses is expensive. A July World Bank report states that lowering the death rate of heart diseases by one percent a year over 30 years could cost as much as article content begin -->1 trillion or 68 percent of China’s 2010<b> GDP</b>.<br />
预防慢性疾病很昂贵。世界银行七月的一份报告指出，一年降低心脏病死亡率一个百分点，30年会花费11兆美元或2010年中国国内生产总值的68％。<br />
