<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>沙田大排档 驰名原只红烧乳鸽 人气小炒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140106371366-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（米芝Gi提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月14日讯】厅去多了总想再吃地道风味菜色，踏入十一月初冬，早晚气温相当清凉，上星期六专程去一直做街坊生意的德记<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%8E%92%E6%A1%A3.md#1">大排档</a>，叹最有名的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E4%B9%B3%E9%B8%BD.md#1">红烧乳鸽</a>和撚手小菜。</p>
<p><strong>完整影片：</strong></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-465edc08-42d9-467e-4f67-83b6cb3b5a65" data-id="player-465edc08-42d9-467e-4f67-83b6cb3b5a65"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/465edc08-42d9-467e-4f67-83b6cb3b5a65?r=16x9&amp;s=1920x1080&amp;d=60&&cat=ncid1251282&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F11%2F14%2Fn10850643.md#1"></a>
<p>德记由旧式屋邨冬菇亭营业到现已经几十年，街坊由年轻到老吃足几十年，但德记依旧几乎晚晚爆场，水准一定保持至今，特别是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E4%B9%B3%E9%B8%BD.md#1">红烧乳鸽</a>，很多搬走了的旧街坊也经常专程回来聚旧兼寻味。</p>
<p>我和朋友特意下午六时到德记，因时间尚早一定会有位，通常到七时便会爆场，要站着等位。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%8E%92%E6%A1%A3.md#1">大排档</a>与餐厅不同，没有拿筹等叫号入座的做法。伙记热情招呼我们坐下，一坐下洗好碟筷后我便立刻点菜，当然不少得人气红烧乳鸽，还点了椒盐骨、姜葱鱼腩煲和金银蛋浸菠菜。点菜后人开始多，未够六时半便差不多坐满人了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10850903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10850903" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140106521366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10850903" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140106521366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10850903" class="wp-caption-text">每一台必点的人气红烧乳鸽、椒盐骨、姜葱鱼腩煲和金银蛋浸菠菜。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>先上来一定是炸得香味四溢的红烧乳鸽，乳鸽配有虾片，还可随个人喜好加上喼汁和淮盐。乳鸽炸得金黄香脆，入口肉质鲜嫩，还有肉汁渗出来，连骨头都完全入味，是每一台必点的人气菜式，食过一定会翻寻味。乳鸽还可选择整只上、不斩件，整只上吃得更过瘾，隔离台每人一只大啖咬，吃得滋味开怀。有些食客看到也选择不斩件整只上，一起豪迈吃地！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10850885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10850885" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140105381366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10850885" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140105381366-600x404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10850885" class="wp-caption-text">红烧乳鸽配有虾片，还可随个人喜好加上喼汁和淮盐。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10850915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10850915" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140106241366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10850915" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140106241366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10850915" class="wp-caption-text">乳鸽还可选择整只上、不斩件。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10850920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10850920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140106071366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10850920" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140106071366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10850920" class="wp-caption-text">隔离台每人一只乳鸽，吃得滋味开怀。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>跟着上来的是椒盐骨，卖相很有避风塘风味，肉质鲜嫩，不嚡而且很入味、够镬气。金银蛋浸菠菜，菠菜除了有益，现在吃也合时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10850872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10850872" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140105531366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10850872" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140105531366-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10850872" class="wp-caption-text">椒盐骨卖相很有避风塘风味，肉质鲜嫩，不嚡而且很入味、够镬气。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10850878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10850878" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140106431366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10850878" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140106431366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10850878" class="wp-caption-text">金银蛋浸菠菜。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后是等了差不多30分钟的姜葱鱼腩煲。这次他们没用传统铁煲上有点失色，铁煲会更够香，鱼皮也会更脆。这次用瓦煲姜葱味突出，鱼腩也很新鲜嫩滑，但不及传统铁煲的爆香。希望下次再来时，改回用铁煲就好了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10850687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10850687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140107081366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10850687" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811140107081366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10850687" class="wp-caption-text">姜葱鱼腩煲这次他们没用传统铁煲，改瓦煲姜葱味突出，鱼腩也很新鲜嫩滑，但不及传统铁煲的爆香。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们吃到晚上七点，这里已经座无虚席，其他人要站着等位了。这里除了远近驰名的乳鸽，鸡粥也很出色，粥底很软绵，且没加味精。所有小炒都很够镬气，海鲜也很新鲜很受欢迎。而且所有食材每日限量卖完就没有，不留隔夜，因此晚上八时多入座的话，很多小炒也已卖完。虽然八时吃晚餐也不算晚，但我曾试过晚上8时15分入座，但基本上很多人气小炒包括炒蚬、鱼腩、鱼头都卖完了，所以想吃到各种人气小炒，一定要尽早来到。#</p>
