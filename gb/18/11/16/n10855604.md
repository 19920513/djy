<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>警大校运会展现“力与美”  徐国勇勉展现实力与风度</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ce5a97877a5607ee69cb56ce453d980d-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>内政部长徐国勇（左2）为警大选手加油。（中央警大提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月16日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）中央警察大学16日第37届校运会，校运会除了运动竞赛外，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E5%A4%A7.md#1">警大</a>学员更大玩创意，扮成佩佩猪等不同主题进场，各班期学员生分别扮演嘻哈风军团、佩佩猪军团、斧头帮、海草帮、爱丽丝梦游仙境、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%A9%E5%85%B7%E6%80%BB%E5%8A%A8%E5%91%98.md#1">玩具总动员</a>、探险活宝等主题进场，创意十足、充满活力朝气，为激烈竞争的气氛增添逗趣活泼的嘉年华趣味，到场的内政部长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E5%9B%BD%E5%8B%87.md#1">徐国勇</a>表示，要成为治安工作干部，除须具备专业知能外，更需锻炼强健体魄，期勉所有参赛选手都能展现实力与风度，发挥运动员精神创造佳绩，为各自团队争取最高的荣誉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10855614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10855614" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/9fd79e5d4dfad5fcb269f033eed60ccb.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10855614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/9fd79e5d4dfad5fcb269f033eed60ccb-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10855614" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E5%A4%A7.md#1">警大</a>第37届校运会开幕进场，创意十足。（中央警大提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10855615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10855615" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/fb1caeae0c6ffb39a33d8b9b056aea55.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10855615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/fb1caeae0c6ffb39a33d8b9b056aea55-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10855615" class="wp-caption-text">警大第37届校运会开幕进场，创意十足。（中央警大提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警察大学一年一度的运动会是“力与美”的动态成果展现，今(107)年警察大学运动会主要是田径及拔河项目的竞技，20人制的拔河比赛，一直是警察大学最传统、也最能凝聚团队向心力的比赛，藉由短短几分钟，将场内外同学凝聚成生命共同体，同心协力为同一目标努力，这种追求团队荣誉的力量，正是警察大学核心价值“荣誉”的体现，也是未来治安干部所必须具备的特质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10855610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10855610" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6bdfab5e47ccd47db570f6b2bd3f653f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10855610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6bdfab5e47ccd47db570f6b2bd3f653f-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10855610" class="wp-caption-text">内政部长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E5%9B%BD%E5%8B%87.md#1">徐国勇</a>（左中）20人制的拔河比赛鸣枪。 （中央警大提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10855613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10855613" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/f8a98f83dd608e057e4f9345bce3392b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10855613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/f8a98f83dd608e057e4f9345bce3392b-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10855613" class="wp-caption-text">警大第37届校运会，仪队入场。（中央警大提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内政部进一步表示，近年来，警察大学同学除了在校内的比赛不断有良好表现外，在全国大专院校的比赛中，更屡创佳绩，像在今年“全国大专运动会”，警察大学同学就荣获4金、6银、7铜佳绩，并有1项打破全校纪录。这些都是全校师生平时全心投入各种教育训练，积极练习、备战，养成勇于挑战自我，追求卓越的表现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10855608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10855608" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/b05ca039c900f4469b5ad48d33ac4709.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10855608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/b05ca039c900f4469b5ad48d33ac4709-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10855608" class="wp-caption-text">警大第37届校运会开幕进场，创意十足。（中央警大提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10855607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10855607" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a752b35c586d85ca3b7272db76716553.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10855607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a752b35c586d85ca3b7272db76716553-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10855607" class="wp-caption-text">警大第37届校运会开幕进场，创意十足。（中央警大提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>校运会警察大学校友总会长何煖轩，赞助10万元奖金，让全体同学努力拚搏奋战之余，都能够获得最好的后援，何煖轩期勉学弟妹积极争取个人荣誉与团体荣耀，为青春岁月刻下永恒纪录与回忆。◇</p>
