<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>冬季进补 双11商机加码</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3dabf4682ffebc2b7b53e909f9a6e378-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>酒香四溢大有肉块。 （业者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月08日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）近日气温变化多端，迎来民间习俗<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9B%E8%A1%A5.md#1">进补</a>最佳时机，网购平台观察，近期“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%94%85.md#1">火锅</a>”、“进补”等相关商品关键字搜寻量较上一周暴增三成，站上相关商品备货量较上月激增2倍，销售量也成长二成，显见冬季进补商机开始发酵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10837986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10837986" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1334d117383975a0313d7ada009b262f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10837986" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1334d117383975a0313d7ada009b262f-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10837986" class="wp-caption-text">软嫩澳洲牛熬煮5～6小时，搭配螺旋丸和花枝浆。 （业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10837985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10837985" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3dabf4682ffebc2b7b53e909f9a6e378-e1541660159491.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10837985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3dabf4682ffebc2b7b53e909f9a6e378-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10837985" class="wp-caption-text">酒香四溢大有肉块。 （业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然今年暖冬，但近日不时降雨，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%90%E5%A4%A9%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">乐天市场</a>看好吃货商机，预估冬季<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9B%E8%A1%A5.md#1">进补</a>商品将进入买气高峰，加上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8C11.md#1">双11</a>商机，11月9日至11月11日特推出最后三天压轴优惠，让消费者暖心也暖胃，透过食补恢复元气体力。◇</p>
