<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>立冬养生吃麻油鸡 暖胃补元气</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%8B%E5%86%AC.md#1">立冬</a>是冬天第一个节气，宣告冬天即将开始。但是在亚洲南部，立冬其实还不算太冷，反而要多注意日夜温差和呼吸道问题喔！</p>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%8B%E5%86%AC.md#1">立冬</a>”在习俗中是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9B%E8%A1%A5.md#1">进补</a>日，根据中医“天人相应之理 ”藉由进补恢复元气，温养五脏，抵御寒冬的来临 。要依个人的体质来做适当的温补，才能补出健康。</p>
<p><strong>饮食小叮咛：</strong>以增加热量为主，要增苦少咸，不可盲目<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9B%E8%A1%A5.md#1">进补</a>。</p>
<p><strong>时令蔬菜：</strong>白萝卜、木耳、芹菜、莲藕、冬笋。</p>
<p><strong>当季水果：</strong>梨、苹果、柿子、甘蔗。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">5步做出“补冬麻油鸡”</span></h2>
<p>每年冬天到来，一定要来上一碗热呼呼的麻油鸡，再搭配面线实在太享受了！</p>
<p>麻油鸡是冬季的必备进补汤品，适宜体质虚寒、怕冷、手脚易冰凉的人。黑麻油和姜可以暖脾胃，驱除体内寒气。黑芝麻本是性平的食物，可延缓衰老、滋补肝肾，经过高温榨取后的黑麻油则会转变为燥热性质，但不建议在料理中加太多。</p>
<p>另需注意的是，体质偏燥热，有口干、冒痘、感冒、经痛、发炎的人，不适合吃麻油鸡进补。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10836971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10836971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/15002409_1467182016628615_6087274869455891515_o.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10836971 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/15002409_1467182016628615_6087274869455891515_o-600x351.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10836971" class="wp-caption-text">经高温榨取的黑麻油转变为燥热性质，能驱除体内寒气，但不宜吃多。（图/C2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>食材：</strong>土鸡腿 2只、鸡骨架 2付、老姜 1块。</p>
<p><strong>调味料：</strong>米酒 1.5瓶、黑麻油 1大匙、冰糖少许。</p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #008000;">▲<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%BB%E6%B2%B9%E9%B8%A1%E5%81%9A%E6%B3%95.md#1">麻油鸡做法</a>。</span></p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1. 鸡骨架煮20分钟，熬成鸡高汤，然后捞起骨头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10836929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10836929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/Screen-Shot-2018-11-07-at-2.56.48-PM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10836929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/Screen-Shot-2018-11-07-at-2.56.48-PM-600x336.png" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10836929" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%BB%E6%B2%B9%E9%B8%A1%E5%81%9A%E6%B3%95.md#1">麻油鸡做法</a>之第一步：熬鸡高汤。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将老姜拍出香气，开火，用黑麻油煸姜，炒至香味散出后，将老姜放入汤锅熬煮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10836928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10836928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/Screen-Shot-2018-11-07-at-2.55.27-PM.png"><img class="wp-image-10836928 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/Screen-Shot-2018-11-07-at-2.55.27-PM-600x334.png" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10836928" class="wp-caption-text">麻油鸡做法之第二步：黑麻油煸姜。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 用炒锅里剩下的黑麻油炒鸡肉，炒至七分熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10836931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10836931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/Screen-Shot-2018-11-07-at-2.58.05-PM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10836931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/Screen-Shot-2018-11-07-at-2.58.05-PM-600x337.png" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10836931" class="wp-caption-text">麻油鸡做法之第三步：黑麻油炒鸡肉。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 加米酒淹过鸡肉，盖上锅盖，煮一分钟至煮滚，让酒精蒸发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10836932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10836932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/Screen-Shot-2018-11-07-at-3.00.50-PM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10836932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/Screen-Shot-2018-11-07-at-3.00.50-PM-600x337.png" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10836932" class="wp-caption-text">麻油鸡做法之第四步：加米酒淹过鸡肉炖煮。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 把鸡肉和汤倒入汤锅，加入少许冰糖，煮十分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10836933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10836933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/Screen-Shot-2018-11-07-at-3.04.32-PM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10836933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/Screen-Shot-2018-11-07-at-3.04.32-PM-600x335.png" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10836933" class="wp-caption-text">麻油鸡做法之第五步：鸡肉和汤倒入汤锅熬煮。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
