<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>冬天最后一个节气！大寒必煮罗宋汤</h2>
<a href="https://dtjix0ckmebpi.cloudfront.net/X2fPkeD7m"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%AF%92.md#1">大寒</a>是冬天的最后一个节气，再接下来就是立春了。有句谚语：“小寒大寒，冷成冰团”，进入冬季最冷的时期，这段时间要重视身体的保暖。</p>
<p>中医认为冬季是生机潜伏、万物蛰藏的时间，这个时候人体的阴阳消长代谢也是处于缓慢的状态，因此养生要着眼于“藏”。意思是这个时期要控制自身精神的活动，保持平静心态。</p>
<p>而冬季是进补的好时机，可以吃些红色食材如甜菜根、番茄、红甜椒、胡萝卜等。红色食材能帮助增强人体抗寒力，还能温补气血。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%AF%92.md#1">大寒</a>料理×温补气血<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%AE%8B%E6%B1%A4.md#1">罗宋汤</a></span></h2>
<p>来自乌克兰的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%AE%8B%E6%B1%A4.md#1">罗宋汤</a>，加入了牛肉及甜菜根，是冬季暖身的最佳汤品之一。上桌前可以加点莳萝，酸奶可依个人口味加到自己汤盘里搅拌享用！</p>
<p><b>食材：</b>甜菜根2颗、牛肩胛肉450g、高丽菜1/2颗、小红萝卜6条、马铃薯2颗、洋葱1颗、红甜椒2/3颗、黄甜椒1/3颗；</p>
<p><strong>香草束：</strong>月桂叶1片、百里香3束、洋香菜3束、大葱1片；</p>
<p><strong>调味料：</strong>番茄酱1大匙、苹果醋适量、盐适量、黑胡椒适量、蒜泥适量；</p>
<p><strong>搭配：</strong>酸奶1小匙、莳萝少许。</p>

</div></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #808080;"><em>（完整制作视频，制作步骤如下。）</em></span></p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1. 高丽菜切丝，小红萝卜、甜菜、马铃薯、洋葱及红黄甜椒切小块。</p>
<p>2. 将甜菜根炒软，加入一大匙番茄酱和适量苹果醋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10078946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10078946" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/luosongtang2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10078946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/luosongtang2-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10078946" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%AF%92%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">大寒料理</a>罗宋汤步骤之二：甜菜根炒软。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 牛肩胛肉切块放入锅中，加水1.5公升炖煮，水滚时将浮泡捞净，放入香草束。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10078951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10078951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/luosongtang3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10078951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/luosongtang3-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10078951" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%AF%92%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">大寒料理</a>罗宋汤步骤之三：牛肉炖汤，加入香草束。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 洋葱炒至焦香，放入萝卜、辣椒炒软。出锅后和甜菜、马铃薯块一起倒入牛肉汤。煮至水滚，加入高丽菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10078953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10078953" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/luosongtang4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10078953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/luosongtang4-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10078953" class="wp-caption-text">大寒料理罗宋汤步骤之四：食材倒入牛肉汤。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 慢炖30～40分钟。加入适量盐、黑胡椒、蒜泥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10078954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10078954" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/luosongtang5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10078954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/luosongtang5-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10078954" class="wp-caption-text">大寒料理罗宋汤步骤之五：慢炖后，加入调料。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将煮好的罗宋汤盛入小盘，在汤面加入酸奶、莳萝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10078973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10078973" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/luosongtang7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10078973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/luosongtang7-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10078973" class="wp-caption-text">大寒料理罗宋汤步骤之六：加入酸奶和莳萝。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
