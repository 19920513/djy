<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>荃湾大河道 越夜越精彩</h2>
<a href="https://d3dnxm84b62m8k.cloudfront.net/L9lhuc55f"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月10日讯】荃湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%B2%B3%E9%81%93.md#1">大河道</a>四通八达，商铺林立，最近进驻了很多有名的小食店，令本身人流已经很多的大河道，更兴旺、更热闹了。</p>
<p>未到六点时，台湾过江龙<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E8%99%8E%E5%A0%82.md#1">老虎堂</a>已有人龙，等候了大约半个小时，有名的黑糖厚鲜奶珍珠已在我手上。一试很浓鲜奶香，珍珠也够烟韧，但黑糖味不突出。我个人觉得很多手摇店也够好喝了，不一定要喝这间，还要排很久不值得！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10773722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10773722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159021366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10773722" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159021366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10773722" class="wp-caption-text">未到六时，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E8%99%8E%E5%A0%82.md#1">老虎堂</a>已有人龙。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10773720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10773720" style="width: 444px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159051366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10773720 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159051366.jpg" alt="" width="444" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10773720" class="wp-caption-text">黑糖厚鲜奶珍珠入口有浓鲜奶香，珍珠也够烟韧，但黑糖味不突出。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>跟着走到旁边的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%BE%A1%E6%B2%90%E5%B2%9A.md#1">花羡沐岚</a>买它的招牌花羡水果漾，我很爱喝水果茶，但这里的果漾不是茶来的，大大杯鲜橙汁底，杯里很多水果，有橙、提子、车厘茄、苹果，一试便爱上，好喝又解渴！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10773740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10773740" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159111366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10773740" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159111366.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10773740" class="wp-caption-text">同样是来自台湾的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%BE%A1%E6%B2%90%E5%B2%9A.md#1">花羡沐岚</a>。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10773735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10773735" style="width: 428px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159081366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10773735 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159081366.jpg" alt="" width="428" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10773735" class="wp-caption-text">花羡水果漾有很多水果，有橙、提子、车厘茄、苹果。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>行着行着惊喜地发现<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E6%9C%97%E9%AC%BC%E5%B7%B4%E5%A3%AB.md#1">元朗鬼巴士</a>，这档闻名已久，很多时在夜晚排长龙的元朗鬼巴士竟然也进驻荃湾呢！什么都想试就买一个小杂锦外加多一串生肠，整盒有生肠、脆肠、反肠、火鸡肾、牛柏叶、扎肉、红肠、猪心、猪舌、猪耳还有猪头肉，虽然是（小）杂锦分量也不少，很抵吃，而且很美味！重点很干净爽脆没有内脏气味，店员也很贴心，食物放到一半便先倒酱汁，最后放满再倒一次酱汁，令食客吃的时候每一件都能均匀沾上酱汁，难怪这么出名，果然名不虚传！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10773777" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10773777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092223221366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10773777" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092223221366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10773777" class="wp-caption-text">很多时在夜晚排长龙的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E6%9C%97%E9%AC%BC%E5%B7%B4%E5%A3%AB.md#1">元朗鬼巴士</a>也进驻荃湾了。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10773786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10773786" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159261366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10773786" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159261366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10773786" class="wp-caption-text">小杂锦外加一串生肠，分量也不少，很抵吃，而且很美味。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>然后再看到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%9E%E5%92%8C%E8%B1%86%E5%93%81.md#1">赞和豆品</a>大排长龙，大大碗豆腐花都只不过是5元，难怪多人到不得了，我当然不会错过啦！我试他最基本的黄糖豆腐花，又香又滑又大碗，真是反通胀益街坊，物超所值！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10773801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10773801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159191366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10773801" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159191366-600x425.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10773801" class="wp-caption-text">和豆品大排长龙。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10773803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10773803" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159231366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10773803" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810092159231366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10773803" class="wp-caption-text">大大碗豆腐花都只不过是5元。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吃了这几间的食物和饮料肚已经撑得胀胀的，还有山西刀削面皇、饺子县、传统茶冰厅自家制蝴蝶酥、椰挞、果酱曲奇和特色甜品等等我想吃但吃不下了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10773823" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10773823" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810100214001366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10773823" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810100214001366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10773823" class="wp-caption-text">传统茶冰厅自家制面包很吸引。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里中、西、日、台、泰式美食，还有甜品和传统小食，应有尽有，而且大部分都好吃，谁说香港没有夜市的？这里便是香港夜市，而且越夜越热闹，越夜越精彩！#</p>
<figure id="attachment_10773812" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10773812" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810100210451366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10773812" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810100210451366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10773812" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%B2%B3%E9%81%93.md#1">大河道</a>越夜越热闹。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
