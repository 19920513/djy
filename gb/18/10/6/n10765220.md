<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这14堂珍贵的人生功课 你学会了哪几堂？</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Video-link-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10440919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Video-link-2-450x51.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="51" /></a></p>
<p><strong>1. 我学会──</strong><br />
我无法使别人爱上我，<br />
但我可以做个值得爱的人。</p>
<p><strong>2. 我学会──</strong><br />
就算别人无法满足我对爱的需求，<br />
他们也已经尽力了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10767878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10767878" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1807182038032483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10767878" title="Happy young couple enjoying the day in a cold winter on the beach." src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1807182038032483-600x400.jpg" alt="Happy young couple enjoying the day in a cold winter on the beach." width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10767878" class="wp-caption-text">就算别人无法满足我对爱的需求，他们也已经尽力了。(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3. 我学会──</strong><br />
就算家人不在身边，<br />
非亲非故者给予的照顾，让我再度相信人性可贵。</p>
<p><strong>4. 我学会──</strong><br />
别人会忘记我说过的话、做过的事，<br />
但却永远记得我给他们的感受。</p>
<p><strong>5. 我学会──</strong><br />
当我面临困难时可能对我落井下石的人，<br />
也可能会为我挺身而出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10767880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10767880" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1808231620332483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10767880" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1808231620332483-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10767880" class="wp-caption-text">当我面临困难时可能对我落井下石的人，也可能会为我挺身而出。(shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6. 我学会──</strong><br />
未经思考的愚蠢之举，<br />
可能让我遗憾终生。</p>
<p><strong>7. 我学会──</strong><br />
就算我的人生跌落谷底，<br />
地球仍然不会停止转动。</p>
<p><strong>8. 我学会──</strong><br />
成长环境和教育背景可能造就人格，<br />
但我必须为自己负责。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看影片完整版…</span></strong><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-d0782eef-6ec7-4280-4b0b-aeb2847f5980" data-id="player-d0782eef-6ec7-4280-4b0b-aeb2847f5980"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/d0782eef-6ec7-4280-4b0b-aeb2847f5980?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F10%2F6%2Fn10765220.md#1"></a></p>
<p><strong>9. 我学会──</strong><br />
用金钱衡量人的成就，<br />
是最笨的方式。</p>
<p><strong>10. 我学会──</strong><br />
墙上悬挂的功名，<br />
决不保证我是个好人。</p>
<p><strong>11. 我学会──</strong><br />
成熟与智慧来自痛苦的经历和挫折，<br />
跟过多少次生日无关。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10767923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10767923" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1303260357361528.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10767923" title="beautiful birthday candles on red background" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1303260357361528-600x398.jpg" alt="beautiful birthday candles on red background" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10767923" class="wp-caption-text">成熟与智慧来自痛苦的经历和挫折，跟过多少次生日无关。(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>12. 我学会──</strong><br />
愿意原谅别人还不够，<br />
有时我们需要原谅的人是自己。</p>
<p><strong>13. 我学会──</strong><br />
英雄总是不顾一切、不计代价去做对的事！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10767883" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10767883" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1805212330232483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10767883" title="happy family having a picnic" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1805212330232483-600x409.jpg" alt="happy family having a picnic" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10767883" class="wp-caption-text">我学会<b>──</b>总是把温暖关爱的话说给家人，因为不知道与他们的缘分还有多久。(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>14. 我学会──</strong><br />
总是把温暖关爱的话说给家人，<br />
因为不知道与他们的缘分还有多久。</p>
<p>如果这些道理让你觉得受用，请与人分享吧！</p>
