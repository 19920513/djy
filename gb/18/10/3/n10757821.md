<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月03日讯】主打传统日式<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E6%88%BF.md#1">茶房</a>风味的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%BB%E5%B8%83%E8%8C%B6%E6%88%BF.md#1">麻布茶房</a>”，以<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E5%BC%8F%E7%94%9C%E7%82%B9.md#1">日式甜点</a>如霜淇淋、刨冰及芭菲等作招徕，加上正宗的日本拉面、主菜、丼及一系列健康轻食，深受喜爱日式风味的朋友欢迎。</p>
<p>我最喜欢他们的雪糕圣代和一系列甜品，适逢40周年，“麻布<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E6%88%BF.md#1">茶房</a>”最近推出火锅及甜品优惠，喜欢的朋友要留意！第一个优惠就是推出以半价的价钱就可以享用到日式火锅+雪糕甜品，最便宜的套餐不超过100元就能享用到（连加一服务费）。第二个优惠就是38元甜品下午茶，一共25款甜品选择，还有咖啡或茶配。还有惠顾午餐送霜淇淋真的很优惠！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10757979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757979" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022202481366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757979" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022202481366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757979" class="wp-caption-text">优惠期间安格斯牛肉火锅可以配圣代。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10757982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757982" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022202531366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757982" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022202531366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757982" class="wp-caption-text">豚肉火锅。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10757983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757983" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810030347181366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757983" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810030347181366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757983" class="wp-caption-text">圣代有不同的味道，朋友选了芒果圣代。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前几天我和朋友午餐时间去，虽然店不大，但是乐富分店的人流较少，加上当天又是闲日，因此坐得很舒服，我们点了两个豪华午餐，分别是海鲜泡菜稻庭乌冬锅和海虾天妇罗冷稻庭乌冬，两个餐除了包前菜和饮品，每个也送冲绳烧紫蕃薯霜淇淋，有四款选择（宇治金时、红豆、黑芝麻、黑蜜黄豆粉），我们选了宇治金时和黑蜜黄豆粉。另还额外叫了一个三色豪华日式刨冰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10757899" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757899" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203281366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757899" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203281366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757899" class="wp-caption-text">三色豪华日式刨冰、冲绳烧紫蕃薯霜淇淋（宇治金时）及冲绳烧紫蕃薯霜淇淋（黑蜜黄豆粉）。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两个餐的主食几乎同步上桌，海鲜泡菜稻庭乌冬锅是铁锅上，泡菜汤底酸中带辣不错，稻庭乌冬入口很滑，口感烟韧。海鲜份量也适中，有虾、青口、鱿鱼，还有豆腐和菜。餐饮配咖啡，咖啡是即磨的很不错。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10757883" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757883" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203011366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757883" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203011366-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757883" class="wp-caption-text">海鲜泡菜稻庭乌冬锅。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10757889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757889" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203171366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757889" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203171366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757889" class="wp-caption-text">乌冬入口很滑，口感烟韧。海鲜份量也适中，有虾、青口、鱿鱼。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海虾天妇罗冷稻庭乌冬，乌冬冷食更滑，口感更烟韧，配上酱汁更好吃，天妇罗炸得香脆不油腻。想不到餐饮配奶茶也不错，有茶味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10757891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757891" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203091366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757891" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203091366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757891" class="wp-caption-text">海虾天妇罗冷稻庭乌冬。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10757892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757892" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203051366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757892" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203051366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757892" class="wp-caption-text">天妇罗炸得香脆不油腻。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10757935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203211366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757935" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203211366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757935" class="wp-caption-text">餐饮：咖啡和奶茶，咖啡是即磨的很不错，奶茶有茶味。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到甜品了，两个霜淇淋和一上来我立刻两眼发光，我一向很喜欢<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%BB%E5%B8%83%E8%8C%B6%E6%88%BF.md#1">麻布茶房</a>的软雪糕，平常吃一个连加一要成四十多元，现在适逢40周年是免费送啊！霜淇淋很软滑充满香牛奶味，下面是冲绳烧紫蕃薯，紫蕃薯入口微暖，充满蕃薯香。不过由于蕃薯是暖的，因此雪糕要快点吃，否则很快会溶掉。宇治金时配有十胜红豆蓉，抹茶味不特出但带很香的红豆味。黑蜜黄豆粉也充满黄豆香味，很不错！三色豪华日式刨冰配有三款糖水（黑糖、枫糖、炼乳）三款中我最喜爱黑糖觉得最好吃，白玉丸子很烟韧，麻薯也很好吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10757937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757937" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203311366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757937" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203311366.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757937" class="wp-caption-text">冲绳烧紫蕃薯霜淇淋有四款选择（宇治金时、红豆、黑芝麻、黑蜜黄豆粉），我们选了宇治金时和黑蜜黄豆粉。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10757898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10757898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203251366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10757898" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810022203251366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10757898" class="wp-caption-text">三色豪华日式刨冰配有三款糖水（黑糖、枫糖、炼乳）。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
