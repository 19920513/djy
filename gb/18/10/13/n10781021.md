<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“喷射背包”飞上天跟汽车竞速 猜猜谁快？</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10611616 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月11日讯】超级英雄不只在大萤幕上，现在不小心在路上都会碰到一、两个！美国一名男子与整个实验团队做“喷射背包”实验，只见他做好万全准备，在跑道上蓄势待发，下一秒“咻”地就飞走了！宛如“钢铁人”，看起来飞行速度比汽车还快，好刺激呀！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10784721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784721" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e4e86140073273c400150173df6200dc.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784721 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e4e86140073273c400150173df6200dc-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784721" class="wp-caption-text">这厉害的装备，该不会就是传说中能变身“钢铁人”的喷射背包？！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10784722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b2fa59fb7591dc6eadc4fa55c78ba9b8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784722 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b2fa59fb7591dc6eadc4fa55c78ba9b8-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784722" class="wp-caption-text">不过侧面也有点像喷洒农药桶的罐子，突然很接地气。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“喷射背包”（jet pack）或称“火箭背包”（rocket pack），这种单人飞行装置由一组喷射引擎或火箭引擎，发动后驾驶人即被推动升空，藉由控制方向可以在空中上下左右移动。最初这样的想法只出现在科幻小说，不过现在已经成为“可触及的现实”。</p>
<p>这位实验飞行员不仅要“试飞”，团队更大费周章准备了赛道和汽车，要让喷射背包的喷射引擎和汽车引擎一决高下，看谁速度快！飞行员先让自己飘浮空中，等待哨音响起，他和汽车倏忽向前，消失在远方，看得旁人拍手叫好、大呼过瘾。最终似乎还赢了比赛！</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #339966;">看完整影片（Video credit: JukinVideo）?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-5a7ea35d-e201-4cda-4a14-da0ea667b852" data-id="player-5a7ea35d-e201-4cda-4a14-da0ea667b852"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/5a7ea35d-e201-4cda-4a14-da0ea667b852?r=16x9&amp;s=1920x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F10%2F13%2Fn10781021.md#1"></a></p>
<p>钢铁人的飞行衣构想据说源自军用设备，据科技网站gizmodo报导，美国军方曾于越战时期深入研究喷射背包实体化的可能性，并附上修饰照片示意图，指出喷射背包可以进行救援并迅速回避。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10784725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784725" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1468c31bedda92f1c6e9c1aceac351e1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784725 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1468c31bedda92f1c6e9c1aceac351e1-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784725" class="wp-caption-text">来到了专业赛道。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10784726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784726" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2111bbe767257760b4ea356f34053e11.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784726 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2111bbe767257760b4ea356f34053e11-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784726" class="wp-caption-text">一人一边，左方汽车，右方喷射背包。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>然而，由于喷射背包的启动有太多限制，包括重力、可用燃料效率低落、气流不稳定等诸多挑战，喷射背包一直未能发挥像科幻小说般描述的那样具有实用价值，更多时候它们被用在休闲娱乐或特技表演。</p>
<p>英国发明家布朗宁（Richard Browning）花费数年研发理想中的喷射背包。2017年，他在英国雷丁镇（Reading）上空试飞成功，率先打破金氏世界纪录，创下穿戴式喷射动力装备的最高纪录──每小时32.02英里（约每小时51.53公里）的速度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10784728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784728" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/07291773dcb82e9bc5c04fc39179d736.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784728 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/07291773dcb82e9bc5c04fc39179d736-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784728" class="wp-caption-text">究竟谁会胜出？（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10784729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784729" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/05c9b8d124272c0a9fb9fbc4031e7121.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784729 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/05c9b8d124272c0a9fb9fbc4031e7121-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784729" class="wp-caption-text">超近距离看到喷射背包升空，机会难得呀！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今（2018）年英国飞行节（Bournemouth Air Festival）中，布朗宁和另一位喷射背包飞行员、重力工业飞行训练总监格拉比西博士（Dr. Angelo Grubisic）进行表演，布朗宁飞行1.4公里，创新纪录；格拉比西也超越过去的最快速度，飞出时速74公里的成绩。</p>
<p>但格拉比西右臂的发动机在表演时冒出火焰，之后便失速坠海，幸好救援人员即时出动，将满身易燃易爆设备的他从海中救起，尽管表演出了大包，但所幸人员均安。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10784730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784730" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/7228b68ec0c890d77fcbaa975b6a0b63.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784730 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/7228b68ec0c890d77fcbaa975b6a0b63-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784730" class="wp-caption-text">三、二、一，出发！汽车和喷射背包都急速向前冲，好过瘾！真是太酷啦！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>美国公司JetPack Aviation则打出训练3小时就能掌握喷射背包飞行技巧的广告，预计2019年推出个人喷射背包产品JB10，售价25万美元（约新台币750万元）。该公司今年也在英国举行的赛车嘉年华会（又称古德伍德速度节，Goodwood Festival of Speed）展示了最新商品JB11。</p>
