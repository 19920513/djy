<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>日式居酒屋巨型炭火烧鳗鱼饭</h2>
<a href="https://d2ktwgchvgcnzd.cloudfront.net/G0ww64o1a"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月24日讯】踏入这间位于长沙湾转角处的居酒屋，内部陈设是间小木屋，配合十分精致的摆设及柔和灯光，带有浓厚居酒屋的怀旧气息。日本传统的居酒屋是给打工一族下班后相聚、闲聊、放松的地方，因此格局布置温暖舒适，让人放松心情。这间<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%B3%E9%81%93%E4%B9%90%E5%B1%85%E9%85%92%E5%B1%8B.md#1">味道乐居酒屋</a>的台凳、墙身都是木，每个角落都经过悉心布置，日本酒樽、怀旧海报招牌、乌丝灯炮，打造出传统居酒屋的格局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10805020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805020" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232226001366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10805020" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232226001366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805020" class="wp-caption-text">这间居酒屋的台凳、墙身都是木，每个角落都经过悉心布置，日本酒樽、怀旧海报招牌、乌丝灯炮，打造出传统居酒屋的格局。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我是为了这里的招牌巨型炭火烧<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B3%97%E9%B1%BC%E9%A5%AD.md#1">鳗鱼饭</a>而来的，这个人气鳗鱼饭和其它日式料理吸引大量食客慕名而至，经常满座大排长龙，为疏导人流每台用餐时间也只限70分钟，真的很夸张！</p>
<p>进入餐厅，坐下打开餐牌看图片就已经垂涎三尺，但我和朋友只得两人为免叫了一台又吃不完（重点只得70分钟），所以还得用心拣。除了传闻每日卖过百份的招牌原条炭火烧<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B3%97%E9%B1%BC%E9%A5%AD.md#1">鳗鱼饭</a>是必拣外，还拣了黑蒜汁烧鹿儿岛黑毛猪定食，以及另一人气海胆三文鱼籽甜虾蓉丼，和流心芝士鱼饼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10804995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10804995" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225471366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10804995" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225471366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10804995" class="wp-caption-text">原条炭火烧鳗鱼饭、黑蒜汁烧鹿儿岛黑毛猪定食和流心芝士鱼饼。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>午餐时间定食更会包括味增汤、沙律和炖蛋。先上来是招牌原条炭火烧鳗鱼饭，目测整条鳗鱼足足有27cm长，匙羹放在鳗鱼旁边显得很迷你。整条鳗鱼又长又肥美，炭烧后带有微焦香味，咬落外脆内鲜嫩，完全没有腥味，而且也不多小骨，一是吃便知是上等货。侍应还说酱汁是用鳗鱼骨、冰糖等材料熬制6小时而成的，难怪这么美味，不像一般行货鳗鱼汁的味道，原来是自家制，令人吃不停口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10804977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10804977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225381366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10804977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225381366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10804977" class="wp-caption-text">原条炭火烧鳗鱼饭。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10804981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10804981" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225431366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10804981" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225431366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10804981" class="wp-caption-text">鳗鱼饭的酱汁是用鳗鱼骨、冰糖等材料熬制6小时而成。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10804986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10804986" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225341366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10804986" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225341366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10804986" class="wp-caption-text">午餐时间定食包括的炖蛋。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10804989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10804989" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232231421366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10804989" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232231421366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10804989" class="wp-caption-text">午餐时间定食包括的味增汤。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10804991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10804991" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232231461366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10804991" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232231461366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10804991" class="wp-caption-text">午餐时间定食包括的沙律。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>跟着是另一人气料理海胆三文鱼籽甜虾蓉丼，新鲜的甜虾打成幼滑的虾蓉，朋友试吃说甜虾蓉打得像还薯蓉般软绵绵，与略带咸的三文鱼籽十分搭配。吃时以少许豉油调味，味道鲜美、口感丰富。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10805004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805004" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225301366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10805004" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225301366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805004" class="wp-caption-text">海胆三文鱼籽甜虾蓉丼，甜虾蓉打得像还薯蓉般软绵绵，与略带咸的三文鱼籽十分搭配。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>跟住上来的是制作需时15至20分钟的烧鹿儿岛黑毛猪配黑蒜汁，个人觉得烧得比较干、有些嚡，但猪肉味香浓，自家制的黑蒜汁很特别、味道很好！最后流心芝士鱼饼要趁热吃，芝士味淡，但鱼味浓。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10805015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225511366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10805015" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225511366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805015" class="wp-caption-text">烧鹿儿岛黑毛猪配黑蒜汁，猪肉味香浓，自家制的黑蒜汁很特别。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10805009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225561366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10805009" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232225561366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805009" class="wp-caption-text">流心芝士鱼饼的芝士味淡，但鱼味浓。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10805016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805016" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232226081366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10805016" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810232226081366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805016" class="wp-caption-text">流心芝士鱼饼咬下去是满满的芝士。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>极力推介大家试这个原条炭火烧鳗鱼饭，真的很味美。香港来说，这里做很真的很出色，骤眼看每一台都会叫一份，真的很有人气。这个鳗鱼饭很巨型，除了鳗鱼很大条，饭的份量也很多，女生一人一定吃不完，男生一份一定够饱，午餐时和朋友分享吃的话，价钱会比较便宜。◇</p>
