<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>减重最常吃这3种高蛋白食物 教你不同吃法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1111-5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>减重时最常吃的高蛋白食物包括鸡蛋、鸡胸肉、豆腐，变换食谱可以变换口味。（Shutterstock/大纪元制图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%8F%E9%87%8D.md#1">减重</a>时最常吃的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E8%9B%8B%E7%99%BD.md#1">高蛋白</a>食物有3种：鸡蛋、鸡胸肉、豆腐。但每天吃这3种食材，任何人都会腻。所以，这个小单元就是要教大家利用这几个常见食材，打造出不同风味的高蛋白质食谱。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">注：</span></strong>这里的食材，主要以水煮蛋1颗、豆腐1块，和鸡胸肉1块为基准。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">享受不同风味！<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%8F%E9%87%8D.md#1">减重</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E8%9B%8B%E7%99%BD.md#1">高蛋白</a>食谱 </span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">一、水煮蛋</span></h3>
<p><strong>微辣芥末籽蛋（热量：95 kcal）</strong></p>
<p>搭配食材：法国芥末籽酱1小匙＋一点点的干辣椒碎。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/00.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10804356 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/00-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a></p>
<p><strong>水煮蛋麦片饼干（热量：156 kcal）</strong></p>
<p>搭配食材：杂粮饼干4片＋小番茄2颗＋黑橄榄切片1/2小匙。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/33-7.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10804353 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/33-7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="272" /></a></p>
<p><strong>水煮蛋沙拉棒（热量：119 kcal）</strong></p>
<p>食材：西洋芹1/2根＋搅拌蛋泥1份＋碎洋葱1大匙＋ 美乃滋1大匙＋柠檬汁1/2小匙＋胡椒少许。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/22-7-e1540344788476.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10804352 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/22-7-e1540344788476-600x347.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="247" /></a></p>

<p><strong>糙米咸鸡胸肉（热量：140 kcal）</strong></p>
<p>搭配食材：糙米粒1大匙＋盐少许。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/222-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10804381 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/222-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="308" /></a></p>
<p><strong>芥末籽优酪酱（热量：162 kcal）</strong></p>
<p>搭配食材：调味优酪乳2大匙＋法国芥末籽酱1小匙＋蜂蜜少许。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/333-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10804382 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/333-1-600x382.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="287" /></a></p>
<p><strong>微辣美乃滋（热量：121 kcal）</strong></p>
<p>搭配食材：美乃滋1大匙＋柠檬汁1小匙＋辣椒酱1/2小匙。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/111-7.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10804380 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/111-7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="258" /></a></p>

<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/bfe6f80ffe66261aa6e264fb7fbb8e8a.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10804403 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/bfe6f80ffe66261aa6e264fb7fbb8e8a.jpg" alt="" width="537" b="468" /></a></p>
<p><strong>酱油紫苏叶（热量：95 kcal）</strong></p>
<p>搭配食材：切碎紫苏叶2片＋水1小杯＋酿造酱油1小匙＋植物油1/2小匙＋香油1/2小匙＋胡椒少许。</p>
<p><strong>黑芝麻美乃滋（热量：125 kcal）</strong></p>
<p>搭配食材：黑芝麻粉1大匙＋美乃滋1/2大匙＋料理用米酒1小匙＋酿造酱油1/2小匙。</p>
<p><strong>番茄巴萨米可醋（热量：91 kcal）</strong></p>
<p>搭配食材：切碎小番茄3颗＋洋葱末1大匙＋巴萨米可醋1小匙＋植物油1/2小匙＋盐少许＋胡椒少许。</p>
