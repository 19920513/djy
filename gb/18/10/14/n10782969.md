<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>群狗听到点名乖乖从命 最后一只表现最搞笑</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年10月15日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>常以各种方式取悦人们，可能无厘头的跳舞、唱歌，也可能玩接飞盘、算数学、捉迷藏的游戏，令人感到狗狗世界真奇妙。现在，就来看点不一样的：乖狗齐齐被“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B9%E5%90%8D.md#1">点名</a>”。</p>
<p>匈牙利的肖普朗（Sopron）市有群<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%AA%E6%98%8E.md#1">聪明</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>。只见一位女训练师站在它们面前，逐一点着它们的名：“金洁（Ginger），内莉（Nelly），莱克西（Lexi），奥克利（Oakley）……”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10784022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784022" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/51d5646254ad8e9722cb91bd14d6f8e7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784022 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/51d5646254ad8e9722cb91bd14d6f8e7-450x372.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="372" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784022" class="wp-caption-text">女子面对着众狗狗，准备命令它们出门。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>

<p><a src=""></a><a href="https://d1kgsi70b35buf.cloudfront.net/K3iBPc91e"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youmaker.com/2018/0403/6a5b3bc9-45e0-43ea-5c7a-48e60d56cdf5?r=16x9&amp;s=1920x1080&amp;cat=video/daily-favorites&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Finspiring.ntd.tv%2Fvideo%2Fdogs-wait-patiently-until-their-names-are-called-but-watch-closely-what-the-the-last-dog-does.md#1" width="671" b="377" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>这群<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%AA%E6%98%8E.md#1">聪明</a>狗儿，每只都知道自己<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8D%E5%AD%97.md#1">名字</a>，它们只要听到女子喊到自己名字，就会乖乖地走出门外。有些狗狗开心摇尾巴，有些慢慢走出，有些快快冲出，有些还会成双出去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10784023" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784023" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/27b068043563a2f2ac59276518213257.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784023 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/27b068043563a2f2ac59276518213257-450x379.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784023" class="wp-caption-text">第一只狗狗金洁出门。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每只狗狗个性都不太一样，同样的是，它们都很听话，且完全不会与同伴<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8D%E5%AD%97.md#1">名字</a>搞混。经一番努力后，女子终于点完名了，这里总共聚集了16只认识自己名字的狗狗……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10784028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784028" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e8dad76a7ce5dafa4cb556f540e44102.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784028 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e8dad76a7ce5dafa4cb556f540e44102-450x366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784028" class="wp-caption-text">一同出门的两只狗狗，恩佐（Enzo）和克迪希（Cadish）。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中最搞笑的是最后一只狗狗“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%9E%E9%9F%B3.md#1">回音</a>（Echo）”，真是狗如其名，其它狗狗听到名字很快就反应过来。这只狗狗听到主人命令，就像在听“回音”一样。它心想：“……咦？主人在叫我呢。”现场人看到它呆萌、慢半拍的模样，忍不住笑了出来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10784032" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10784032" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b33fa622ae23cade8507325ce81847d7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10784032 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b33fa622ae23cade8507325ce81847d7-450x365.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10784032" class="wp-caption-text">最精彩的在后头——慢半拍的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%9E%E9%9F%B3.md#1">回音</a>”。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不少网友都被“回音”的搞笑举动深深吸引了，在留言区不断喊着它的名字，有人写道：“天呐，‘回音’让我笑到流泪。”“我爱‘回音’。”</p>
<p>也有网友站在它的立场说道：“‘回音’很困惑。”“我想，‘回音’是累了。”</p>
<p>当然，不仅“回音”受欢迎，其它的狗狗也有人喜欢，如莱克西、西思科（Sisco），还有成双出去的狗狗。</p>
<p>有位名叫thicc custard的网友更留言：“我可以拥有它们全部吗？”</p>
