<h6>马儿向“同伴”示好。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年10月14日讯】（大纪元记者陈安综合报导）动物看到自己同类的雕像、照片、面具等等这些“人造物”，好像掉入迷中，容易认为它们是“真<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8C%E4%BC%B4.md#1">同伴</a>”，好奇向前打招呼，模样逗趣可爱。现在，就来看看<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC.md#1">马</a>儿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%AF%E8%AE%A4.md#1">误认</a>“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8C%E4%BC%B4.md#1">同伴</a>”的情形吧。</p>
<h2>老<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC.md#1">马</a>不识“小马”真面目</h2>
<p>美国犹他州的亨茨维尔市（Huntsville）有位女子牵着一只马儿，没多久，旁边有人说：“让它过来闻这个。”画面紧接着出现一只“冷静的小马”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10782934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10782934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/30ce568b835aac44b4fd85c6e4cb2831.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10782934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/30ce568b835aac44b4fd85c6e4cb2831-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10782934" class="wp-caption-text">女孩示意马儿来认识新朋友。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>女子用手拍了小马几下，发出“嘭嘭嘭嘭嘭”的轻脆响亮声，并抚摸了它，向马示意：“乖马儿，快，来认识新朋友。”</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看完整影片?</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-5932ba95-6f90-4850-4bb1-3c8f16888b64" data-id="player-5932ba95-6f90-4850-4bb1-3c8f16888b64"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/5932ba95-6f90-4850-4bb1-3c8f16888b64?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F10%2F14%2Fn10782501.md#1"></a></p>
<p>听那清脆响亮声，很明显可知这是一只“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%87%E9%A9%AC.md#1">假马</a>”，这只大黑马却似乎把它视为“真马”了。它向小马方向走去，试图与之培养感情，用头碰碰它，并发出鼻息声：“唿唿唿唿唿。”模样温馨又逗趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10782701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10782701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/8f659c250625d0581144d347c360cb91.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10782701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/8f659c250625d0581144d347c360cb91-600x376.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10782701" class="wp-caption-text">马儿向“同伴”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BA%E5%A5%BD.md#1">示好</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在旁的女子见状一直偷笑，另一位女子还轻声暗示马儿说：“那是假的。”可是马儿怎么听得懂人话？它依然忘我，继续向不动于衷的小马<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BA%E5%A5%BD.md#1">示好</a>……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10782700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10782700" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/97b949e2884936d51d0986a008d0024c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10782700 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/97b949e2884936d51d0986a008d0024c-600x457.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="457" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10782700" class="wp-caption-text">小马的耳朵暗藏危险。（视频截图/大纪元制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>画面虽有趣，现场却暗藏着危险──“小马耳朵尖尖的，有可能会伤到马儿。”现场未露脸的女子不忘贴心提醒：“确保不要伤到它眼睛。”</p>
<h2>白马不识“假面马”</h2>
<p>加拿大渥太华一名女子带着“独角兽”面具，深深吸引着一只可爱的白马。白马与“假面马”培养感情，现场气氛正充满浓情蜜意时，女子竟把头套摘掉，马儿看到这心仪对象原来是长这样，立马逃离现场。</p>
