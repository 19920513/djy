<h6></h6>

<p>【大纪元2018年04月08日讯】<span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12616" lang="ZH-TW">由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2%E5%B1%80.md#1">多伦多教育局</a>?</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12617" lang="EN-CA">（TDSB）</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12619" lang="ZH-TW">与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A3%AB%E5%98%89%E5%A0%A1%E5%9B%BD%E8%AF%AD%E5%AE%B6%E9%95%BF%E4%BC%9A.md#1">士嘉堡国语家长会</a>（</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12620" lang="EN-CA">Scarborough Mandarin Parents Association）</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12621" lang="ZH-TW">举办的2018多伦多文化<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F%E4%BB%A4%E8%90%A5.md#1">夏令营</a></span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12623" lang="ZH-TW">，已于4月7日开始报名。</span></p>
<p><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12623" lang="ZH-TW">该活动已连续举办五年，今年的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F%E4%BB%A4%E8%90%A5.md#1">夏令营</a>由7月3日至7月27日，每周一至五上午9时至下午4时</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12624" lang="ZH-TW">，</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12625" lang="ZH-TW">假?</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12626" lang="EN-CA">Silver Springs?</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12627" lang="EN-CA">Public School (?</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12628" lang="EN-CA">222 Silver Springs Blvd.</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12629" lang="EN-CA">?)</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12630" lang="EN-CA">?</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12631" lang="ZH-TW">举办。上、下课前后均可提供托儿服务。欢迎</span><span id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1523074957170_12631" lang="ZH-TW">4岁至14岁的小朋友报名参加。</span></p>
