<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园工厂火噬5消防员 吴慷仁：希望有天堂</h2>
<a href="https://d1nm4aa0eu3bxh.cloudfront.net/C3ut8Njoh"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年04月29日讯】（大纪元记者张原彰台北报导）桃园平镇<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%AC%E9%B9%8F.md#1">敬鹏</a>工厂深夜火警造成5名<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E9%98%B2%E5%91%98.md#1">消防员</a>殉职，另有2人重伤，全台各界听闻此消息纷纷感到难过并表示哀悼，同时向消防员们致意，男星<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%85%B7%E4%BB%81.md#1">吴慷仁</a>相当感到心疼，他在脸书上发文说，“愿他们好走，卸下任务的重担”。</p>
<p>最近正准备开拍公视新戏的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%85%B7%E4%BB%81.md#1">吴慷仁</a>相当关心时事，日前他曾发表言论，要求台湾政府需要管控广告片的质量，不应让色情与低俗等因子充斥在广告片上，网友们纷纷表示赞同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10346417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10346417" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180429000005L.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10346417 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180429000005L-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10346417" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市平镇区<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%AC%E9%B9%8F.md#1">敬鹏</a>工业工厂28日深夜发生大火，警消获 报到场灌救后，有7名消防队员与2名工厂员工失联，警 消全力灭火及搜救失联人员。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次桃园的平镇发生重大火警，吴慷仁也相当关心，他在听闻<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E9%98%B2%E5%91%98.md#1">消防员</a>殉职的憾事后，感到相当的难过，并表示出哀悼。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>观看完整影片?</strong></span></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-b382151f-9c7b-4a14-4784-ec47daab5fc8" data-id="player-b382151f-9c7b-4a14-4784-ec47daab5fc8"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/b382151f-9c7b-4a14-4784-ec47daab5fc8?r=16x9&amp;s=1920x1080&navurl=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F4%2F29%2Fn10346968.md#1&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F4%2F29%2Fn10346967.md#1"></a>
<p>吴慷仁说，“这两天连续都能听到自己心跳的声音，而今天早上看到了我们警消的牺牲，每每这种消息都让人心跳漏了一拍。”</p>
<p>吴慷仁说，“顿时都有种想哭的感觉，愿他们好走，卸下任务的重担”，他在文末以关键字的方式特别强调，“真的希望有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%A0%82.md#1">天堂</a>”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10346394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10346394" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180429PHO0054l.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10346394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180429PHO0054l-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10346394" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市平镇区敬鹏工厂28日晚间大火，造成消防人员5死。殉职消防员家属29日在殡仪馆，神情哀凄。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网友纷纷留言，“看到殉难人员脸书留言悼念前殉难同仁，真令人涕零 ”、“珍惜当下！面对无常，只求无愧！好好地珍惜自己的每一个时分”、“很多家庭捐出了家人，帮助了更多人”、“会的，他们会上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%A0%82.md#1">天堂</a>的”。</p>
