<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>受邀参加悉尼人权会议 前加拿大小姐获褒奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>加拿大小姐林耶凡在悉尼的国际人权会议上获得嘉奖。（燕楠／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年12月01日讯】（大纪元记者燕楠<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%89%E5%B0%BC.md#1">悉尼</a>报导）11月28日晚，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7%E5%B0%8F%E5%A7%90.md#1">加拿大小姐</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E8%80%B6%E5%87%A1.md#1">林耶凡</a>受邀参加在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%89%E5%B0%BC.md#1">悉尼</a>举办的第九届人权教育国际会议，西悉尼帕拉玛塔市市长为表彰她在促进人权方面所做出的努力，给她颁发了荣誉证书。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10884844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10884844" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10884844 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference3-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10884844" class="wp-caption-text">帕拉玛塔市长威尔森给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E8%80%B6%E5%87%A1.md#1">林耶凡</a>颁发荣誉证书。（燕楠／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该会议的召集人是澳洲人权教育委员会主席、西悉尼大学公平与多样性研究室主任、澳大利亚勋章获得者奥兹多斯基（Dr. Sev Ozdowski AM），他致力于在全球推广人权教育。本届会议，奥兹多斯基特意邀请了林耶凡作为演讲嘉宾。</p>
<p>林耶凡在演讲中介绍了自己为人权发声付出的努力和遇到的困难。讲述了2015年她参加世界小姐竞选的动机，就是想让她的声音被更多的人听到，让更多的人关注发生在中国的人权迫害。然而，她虽然成功获得了那一年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7%E5%B0%8F%E5%A7%90.md#1">加拿大小姐</a>的头衔，但却因为中共干涉，在后来的世界小姐评选中处处受阻，甚至让她还在国内的父亲也受到中共国安的威胁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10884843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10884843" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10884843 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference2-450x587.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="587" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10884843" class="wp-caption-text">林耶凡在人权教育国际会议上演讲。（燕楠／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10884846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10884846" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10884846 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference5-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10884846" class="wp-caption-text">林耶凡在人权教育国际会议上演讲。数百名来自多个国家的学者、人权专家和人权活动家聆听了她的演讲。（燕楠／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林耶凡的演讲让中共侵犯人权和渗透海外的行径再次受到关注。</p>
<p>最后，为林耶凡颁发证书的帕拉玛塔市长威尔森（Andrew Wilson）再次感谢远道而来的加拿大小姐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10884845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10884845" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10884845 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference4-600x388.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10884845" class="wp-caption-text">林耶凡受到人权教育国际会议的基金会召集奥兹多斯基（Dr. Sev Ozdowski，右）邀请来悉尼参加会议。（燕楠／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10884841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10884841" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10884841 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/2018-11-29-humanrights-conference1-600x388.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10884841" class="wp-caption-text">林耶凡出席人权教育国际会议。（燕楠／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
