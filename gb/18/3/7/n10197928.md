<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>田中千绘茶道戏身段优雅 跪拍4小时险腿软</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212371487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>田中千绘在现场大展茶道技艺，优雅的身段受到全场称赞。（台视、东森／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年03月07日讯】最新台剧《高塔公主》于6日首度公开主视觉，并于今天发布第一部剧情版预告，引发网友热议。剧中饰演化妆品公司CEO的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B0%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%83%E7%BB%98.md#1">田中千绘</a>，日前拍摄了自己在剧中的开场戏，在现场大展茶道技艺，优雅的身段受到全场称赞，让她超害羞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10198018" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10198018" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212211487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10198018" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212211487.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="603" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10198018" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B0%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%83%E7%BB%98.md#1">田中千绘</a>首次穿母亲的和服亮相。（台视、东森提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>她第一次在萤幕前穿妈妈的粉色和服亮相，开心地说：“过去在其他作品中曾穿过戏服的和服，但穿着特别从日本带回来的妈妈的和服还是第一次。”</p>
<p>在日本大学时期就学过茶道的她，笑说自己学到的只是皮毛，“我当初是为了吃茶点才跑去学的，进去才发现茶道的学问超深。虽然动作看起来很简单，但其实有很多的细节和讲究。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10198019" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10198019" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212311487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10198019" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212311487-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10198019" class="wp-caption-text">田中千绘拍摄和服茶道戏，程序十分繁琐。（台视、东森提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>多年没再接触茶道的她，光确认茶具以及茶道基础顺序等，就和老师练习了将近两个小时。“因为光现场的茶具和步骤就有十六道程序，而且连角度、力道也有所讲究，这些都全是不能有差错的。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10198022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10198022" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212281487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10198022" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212281487-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10198022" class="wp-caption-text">田中千绘拍茶道戏一跪就是四小时。（台视、东森提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然她直呼紧张，但也被老师赞说领悟和记忆力都很好。从练习到拍摄完成，田中千绘便整整跪坐了超过4个小时，导演看着跪到腿软的田中千绘，又听在日本学茶道至少要跪上8小时，大叹日本女孩真的很辛苦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10198025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10198025" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212341487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10198025" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212341487.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10198025" class="wp-caption-text">田中千绘向老师学习茶道步骤。（台视、东森提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10198026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10198026" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212251487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10198026" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1803070212251487.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10198026" class="wp-caption-text">田中千绘被茶道老师称赞有天分。（台视、东森提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>田中千绘一听打趣笑回：“所以我才会来台湾！开玩笑的啦！”此话一出立刻逗笑全场。</p>
<p>《高塔公主》由孟耿如、莫允雯、郑茵声、王牧语、田中千绘、周群达Duncan、利晴天领衔主演，目前正开拍中。</p>
