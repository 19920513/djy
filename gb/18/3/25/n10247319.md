<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>惠好春耕体验人文艺术空间田野飨宴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/5fea2c20c6a90e260826408d01f6b7fb-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>惠好春耕合影 。（郭千华／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年03月25日讯】（大纪元记者郭千华台湾宜兰报导）惠好<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E6%96%87%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">人文艺术</a>空间位于好山好水的宜兰县员山乡惠好社区旁，视野宽阔宁静幽雅，24日下午举行惠好春耕余明顺、沈暨、黄天化、黄增添四人展，吸引许多享受慢生活的民众及爱好艺术的人群前来观赏，让这放松又温馨氛围开启了开幕茶会的序幕，更缔造民众与艺术家互动的深度对话。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%B6%E8%89%BA%E5%AE%B6.md#1">陶艺家</a>余明顺表示，用柴烧方式创作陶艺，抱持谦虚地与大家互动观摩学习。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%B6%E8%89%BA%E5%AE%B6.md#1">陶艺家</a>黄天化也表示，从莺歌辗转到宜兰，分享创作作品心得，为拿捏水分留下的时间点并控制柴烧火侯，烧制出完美作品实在不容易，一切都需经验累积而来的成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10247323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10247323" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/3/25/n10247319.md#1/%e9%99%b6%e8%97%9d%e5%ae%b6%e9%bb%83%e5%a4%a9%e5%8c%96%e4%bd%9c%e5%93%81" rel="attachment wp-att-10247323"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10247323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/fb2b0d246579857b8e1352bda2733420-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10247323" class="wp-caption-text">陶艺家黄天化作品。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>泥塑家黄增添说自己本来喜爱绘画，然后木雕、最后泥塑，一路走来感到基本功是创作根本，在烧制上请益前辈，更阅读有关书籍无数，作品中表现人生百态情境及喜怒哀乐的不同表情，真是维妙维肖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10247324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10247324" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/3/25/n10247319.md#1/%e6%b3%a5%e5%a1%91%e5%ae%b6%e9%bb%83%e5%a2%9e%e6%b7%bb%e4%bd%9c%e5%93%81" rel="attachment wp-att-10247324"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10247324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/bc88c4d4b75e6a8f7ec9bddd73a96acb-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10247324" class="wp-caption-text">泥塑家黄增添作品 。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A6%E6%B3%95%E5%AE%B6.md#1">书法家</a>沈暨他感恩杨春生老师提携才有今天的创作，作品中呈现各种不同书写方式，其中以行书及楷书结合是自己创作技巧的呈现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10247322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10247322" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/3/25/n10247319.md#1/%e9%99%b6%e8%97%9d%e5%ae%b6%e4%bd%99%e6%98%8e%e9%a0%86-%e6%9b%b8%e6%b3%95%e5%ae%b6%e6%b2%88%e6%9a%a8%e4%bd%9c%e5%93%81" rel="attachment wp-att-10247322"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10247322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/ae95d10554efd1cf743ad2ecc0e36379-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10247322" class="wp-caption-text">陶艺家余明顺 <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A6%E6%B3%95%E5%AE%B6.md#1">书法家</a>沈暨作品。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>远从桃园而来杨春生老师看了优秀作品，感觉哑口无言，书法韵强、柴烧陶艺及泥塑各具风格，别有特色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10247321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10247321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/3/25/n10247319.md#1/%e6%83%a0%e5%a5%bd%e6%98%a5%e8%80%95%e5%90%88%e7%85%a7" rel="attachment wp-att-10247321"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10247321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/168765d53a4741fdae79630d57ffe2b1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10247321" class="wp-caption-text">惠好春耕合照 。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>惠好<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E6%96%87%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">人文艺术</a>空间创办人朱正宗发现后院近距离相当罕见且存在的百年以上名望古厝，与一线之隔的贫困土角厝，凸显贫富差距的建筑与人文历史的强烈对比，用心打造出现今艺术与人文荟萃结合的惠好人文艺术空间。惠好春耕展期；3月24日至4月15日止，邀请民众一起来体验人文艺术空间田野飨宴。</p>
