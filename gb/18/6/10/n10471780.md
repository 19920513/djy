<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>草皮“很烫”？宝宝腿肌超有力 两腿翘高高</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10554314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月16日讯】小孩们看到整片青葱绿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E7%9A%AE.md#1">草皮</a>，大多会想上去滚一滚、跑一跑，但是，这位小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E5%AE%9D.md#1">宝宝</a>却小心翼翼地不让自己幼绵绵的婴儿肌触碰到一丁点草叶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10471816" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10471816" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-62.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10471816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-62-600x504.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="504" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10471816" class="wp-caption-text">一位只穿着尿布的小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E5%AE%9D.md#1">宝宝</a>坐在青葱绿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E7%9A%AE.md#1">草皮</a>上，小心翼翼地不让<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%8C%E8%82%A4.md#1">肌肤</a>碰到草。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10471815" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10471815" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-79.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10471815" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-79-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10471815" class="wp-caption-text">两条短短<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%BF.md#1">腿</a>抬高高，双手向前伸直、平衡，看起来像在做“肌力训练”。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一只白白嫩嫩的小猪猪，噢，不是啦！是小宝宝，包着尿布坐在草皮上，仔细一看，这姿势好像有点奇特，两条短短<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%BF.md#1">腿</a>抬高高，双手向前伸直、平衡，看起来像在做某种“肌力训练”，宝宝皱着眉头认着盯着草皮，莫非这草皮“很烫”吗？</p>
<p>一旁摄影的妈妈看到这搞笑一幕，忍不住哈哈大笑，“宝贝，这草皮有什么问题吗？”宝宝听到妈妈笑声，抬起头望向前方，好像领悟了什么一样，瞬间露出甜死人的愉悦笑容，看了都要融化啦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10471818" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10471818" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-57.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10471818" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-57-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10471818" class="wp-caption-text">宝宝听到妈妈笑声，抬起头望向前方，瞬间露出甜死人的愉悦笑容。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管露出笑容，宝宝还是不让自己软Q嫩肌碰到草皮，双脚一下张开开，一下合紧紧，所有动作都是抬在空中进行的，不得不佩服这宝宝的腿肌超有力，“马麻说不能乱踩草皮。”宝宝蜷曲的身体像肉包一样，看了忍不住想“抱紧处理”，拯救这不停扭动的小宝贝。看来宝宝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%8C%E8%82%A4.md#1">肌肤</a>触觉非常敏感，不想让刺刺的草皮伤了自己。</p>
<p>这位妈妈表示，宝宝Lachie当时9个月大，直到现在2岁半都不会在草地上赤脚走路，当时录影时不知道宝宝有发育迟缓和感官问题，目前正接受治疗，努力帮助这孩子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10471819" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10471819" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-52.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10471819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-52-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10471819" class="wp-caption-text">宝宝不让自己软Q嫩肌碰到草皮，双脚一下张开开，一下合紧紧。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10471814" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10471814" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-52.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10471814" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-52-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10471814" class="wp-caption-text">妈妈说：宝宝有发育迟缓和感官问题，目前正接受治疗。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管影片非常有趣，但也从中发现，原来小宝宝讨厌什么的背后，可能也意味着什么。</p>
