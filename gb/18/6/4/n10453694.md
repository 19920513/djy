<a href="https://d11leod1im7q2u.cloudfront.net/B4iwVmLjx"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月08日讯】一只美丽优雅的加拿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%9B%81.md#1">大雁</a>，静静地在湖边的窝孵<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B.md#1">蛋</a>。它温柔地翻转5颗蛋，用鸟喙整理铺在蛋周围的细绒毛，接着，它张开翅膀轻轻坐下。大雁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A6%88%E5%A6%88.md#1">妈妈</a>很爱宝宝，它会在这儿待上25～28天等幼鸟孵化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10453762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10453762" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-31.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10453762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-31-600x384.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="384" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10453762" class="wp-caption-text">加拿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%9B%81.md#1">大雁</a>温柔地翻转5颗<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B.md#1">蛋</a>，用鸟喙整理铺在蛋周围的细绒毛。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘘!如果有幸在野外看见加拿大雁正在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%B5%E8%9B%8B.md#1">孵蛋</a>，请一定保持安静、保持距离。除了避免打扰大雁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A6%88%E5%A6%88.md#1">妈妈</a>，也是为了别让大雁爸爸激动地攻击“侵略者”。</p>
<p>加拿大雁是很社会型的鸟类，幼鸟会跟着父母一直到长大迁徙，成年的两只大雁配偶，终身都会生活在一起。可以说，加拿大雁是一种“亲情”强烈的鸟类。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10453766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10453766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10453766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-19-600x384.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="384" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10453766" class="wp-caption-text">大雁妈妈会在这儿待上25～28天等幼鸟孵化。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不是所有鸟妈妈都会自己<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%B5%E8%9B%8B.md#1">孵蛋</a>，然而，大雁妈妈除了孵蛋，更会舍命护蛋。曾有报导，大雁妈妈尽管遭遇火灾或利物攻击，仍趴在蛋上不愿离去。</p>
<p>希望大雁宝宝顺利孵化，大雁一家也都平平安安！</p>
