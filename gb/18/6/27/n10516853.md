<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.先将<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E8%85%BF.md#1">火腿</a>中间切掉一个长方形，并将其部分切丁。</p>
<p>2.外圈边框下锅煎到酥脆，在中间打入鹌鹑蛋，盖上锅盖1分钟，即可起锅备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10516947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10516947" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e3687d03193d0dcf57805cdc323d26b9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10516947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e3687d03193d0dcf57805cdc323d26b9-450x281.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10516947" class="wp-caption-text">香煎火腿鹌鹑蛋。 （影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.<strong>蔬菜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炒饭</a>：</strong>热锅抹上一层奶油，将切丁的火腿放入锅中拌抄，再加入混合蔬菜、白饭、葱末，翻炒至锅内食材煮熟，撒点胡椒粉提味。</p>
<p>4.<strong>泡菜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炒饭</a>：</strong>热锅放入点食用油，加入火腿丁拌炒，再放入泡菜、白饭均匀翻炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10516950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10516950" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f44970b328674a98b8f9a101c6908c0b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10516950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f44970b328674a98b8f9a101c6908c0b-450x423.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10516950" class="wp-caption-text">双色香Q炒饭。 （影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.将两种口味炒饭，放入铺有保鲜膜的容器中，并轻微按压铺平，再将炒饭连同保鲜膜一同取出。</p>
<p>6.在中间裹上一片海苔，放上火腿煎蛋，即可美味上桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10517051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10517051" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e0140528ffe969bb373d056d368d5263.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10517051" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e0140528ffe969bb373d056d368d5263-450x386.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10517051" class="wp-caption-text">在炒饭中间裹上一片海苔 。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10517050" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10517050" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/97c79b23d3174c2e6f3c6002346fe6a4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10517050" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/97c79b23d3174c2e6f3c6002346fe6a4-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10517050" class="wp-caption-text">最后，放上火腿煎蛋，即可美味上桌。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【备注】口味咸淡及食材，皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</p>
