<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>高速公路惊见失控马匹 好心女孩英勇出手</h2>
<a href="https://d2cj9ah31kn8fl.cloudfront.net/L2nCxxveq"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年06月16日讯】（大纪元记者裴晏报导）在休士顿一条交通繁忙的高速公路上，出现一只不该出现的动物——一匹漂亮的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC.md#1">马</a>儿！它身上有马鞍，可是应该坐在上面驾驭它的主人却不见了！看到它在高速行驶的车潮中慌张奔跑，实在太危险了！一个闪神就可能被撞上了，这可怎么办才好？</p>
<p>有许多车辆看见了，也试着慢速闪躲避免撞到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC.md#1">马</a>匹。后来一辆白色卡车靠近它，坐在副驾驶座上的女孩勇敢探出车外，伸手试着拉马匹的缰绳。但是，这是有难度的，因为车辆得控制在比马匹的速度快一点点又不能太快，否则够不着马的缰绳??</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-46316f24-46b7-44c6-526e-e978ef16be9d" data-id="player-46316f24-46b7-44c6-526e-e978ef16be9d"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/46316f24-46b7-44c6-526e-e978ef16be9d?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F15%2Fn10488264.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<p>这个惊险的画面被后面的车辆乘客用手机拍下来，可以看到在许多车辆同时高速行进时，很难靠近马匹，同时还得注意旁边高速行驶的车辆。</p>
<p>这位出手相救的爱心卡车司机开车技术很好，他慢慢靠近马匹左侧，但由于速度快，女孩还是好几次错过了拉住缰绳的机会。不过，他们不气馁，决心要救到马匹才罢手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10488489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10488489" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/96b501efcf04e90f7c2fb49795475938-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10488489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/96b501efcf04e90f7c2fb49795475938-1-450x409.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10488489" class="wp-caption-text">这位男性车主发现白色卡车正试图救下失控的马匹时，立即上前支援。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>刚好后面的男性车主也很有爱心，他立即加速开车到马匹的右侧，并提点女孩“小心，你快抓住了”，还一边安抚马匹，显然他很了解马的习性。后来女孩抓到了缰绳，男子又指示他们：“你们减速慢行，慢慢靠右边，马匹会跟着你的方向跑！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10488492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10488492" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2475e4d873c39682276e4420d2bd4ffa-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10488492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2475e4d873c39682276e4420d2bd4ffa-1-450x441.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="441" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10488492" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>幸运的马匹，在最危急的时候遇到两个好心人联合出手，解除了危机。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10488286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10488286" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/b514544d79f0eb64ebfab8d3d85e1f60.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10488286 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/b514544d79f0eb64ebfab8d3d85e1f60-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10488286" class="wp-caption-text">爱心救援成功！白色卡车领着马匹慢慢靠右行驶，离开了险象环生的高速公路，大家都松了一口气。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
