<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>白狮子遇到狗狗 秒变绅士牵手吻手背</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-14.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10512508 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-14-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月27日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90.md#1">狮子</a>是万兽之王，在多数人的印象中是一种凶猛危险的野兽。很多动物都是狮子的猎物，但这段视频中的大白狮子对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>却干了一件令人惊掉下巴的事。</p>
<p>视频显示，一只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>在路上遇到一头白毛大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90.md#1">狮子</a>，大家都以为狗狗肯定成为狮子的腹中餐了。但下一幕剧情逆转，令人实在想不到。</p>
<p>狗狗站在原地，白狮子缓缓走过来。来到狗狗身边后，狮子抬起一只前爪，抓住狗狗的一只前爪，放在嘴边亲了一下，宛如一个绅士。狗狗和狮子还头碰头，十分亲密的样子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10512497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10512497" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/images-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10512497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/images-1-600x425.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10512497" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗站在原地，白狮子缓缓走过来。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>脸书用户塔格莱（Anita Tagle）评论说：“这头狮子住在一个名为‘黑豹白虎基金会’（Black Jaguar White Tiger Foundation）的墨西哥动物避难所。在墨西哥，购买狮子是合法的。这头狮子从一座小小的联排别墅中被救出。”</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片?</span></strong></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-1058824f-1282-45ed-4a44-9d70facba620" data-id="player-1058824f-1282-45ed-4a44-9d70facba620"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/1058824f-1282-45ed-4a44-9d70facba620?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F26%2Fn10512439.md#1"></a>
<p>塔格莱写道，当人们把狮子带走的时候，注意到了那只狗，狗狗感到很不安。人们便一起将狗狗也带走了。狗狗和狮子不想分开。狮子叫米基（Miki），狗狗则叫卡米拉（Camilla）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10512485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10512485" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/640_9b00bee60e6665317d3227934753f1f0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10512485 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/640_9b00bee60e6665317d3227934753f1f0-450x421.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10512485" class="wp-caption-text">狮子抬起一只前爪，抓住狗狗的一只前爪，放在嘴边亲了一下，宛如一个绅士。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有网友观看视频后说：“这就像一个强大的骑士亲吻公主的手。”“画面看起来超有爱。”</p>
<p>也有人说：“养狗的人知道，当一只狗狗咬住你的手时，它是想握你的手，所以狮子伸出爪子‘握手’。但狮子也亲吻（舔）狗狗的爪子。那太酷了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10512486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10512486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/d3121604.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10512486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/d3121604-600x303.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10512486" class="wp-caption-text">白狮子和狗狗有着深厚的友情。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有人说：“那只白狮子真漂亮！”“真是一个绅士。”</p>
