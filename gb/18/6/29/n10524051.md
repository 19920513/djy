<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>400斤大海龟被冲上海岛 救援队尽心送它回家</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10442784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-3-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月30日讯】（大纪元记者伊遐报导）美国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E5%8D%A1%E7%BD%97%E6%9D%A5%E7%BA%B3%E5%B7%9E.md#1">南卡罗来纳州</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%94%E6%B2%BB%E6%95%A6.md#1">乔治敦</a>（Georgetown）东临大西洋。有一天，乔治敦美国南卡海洋生物水族馆接到电话，得知一只栖息于大西洋外海的海龟被冲进内海湾的南岛附近，还活着，请他们去救援。</p>
<p>这是一只棱皮龟（Leatherback sea turtle）。它们背面没有角质板，所以和其它乌龟不同，它的背上是一层柔软的皮革质皮肤，并带有棱脊线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/careful.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10524132" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/careful-600x429.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="429" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524132" class="wp-caption-text">小心别碰破它的皮！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>凯莉．硕瓦森（Kelly Thorvalson）——南卡水族馆海龟救援保育组负责人表示，接到这样的请托非常惊喜。因为通常<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%B1%E7%9A%AE%E6%B5%B7%E9%BE%9F.md#1">棱皮海龟</a>被冲上岸就活不了了；还是活生生的这一点本身就很难得了！在美国纪录中，这是罕见的活存例子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Happy.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10524146 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Happy-600x395.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524146" class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%B1%E7%9A%AE%E6%B5%B7%E9%BE%9F.md#1">棱皮海龟</a>被冲上岸还是活生生的这一点本身就很难得了！”（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>救援队到南岛佑奇自然保护区把这只棱皮海龟运回救援中心，佑奇（Yawkey）也就成为海龟的名字了。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>观看完整影片?<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-65b4bfda-658d-4af8-4163-c78e229aacef" data-id="player-65b4bfda-658d-4af8-4163-c78e229aacef"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/65b4bfda-658d-4af8-4163-c78e229aacef?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F29%2Fn10524051.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<p>凯莉还表示佑奇毕竟是个庞然大物，不知是否能撑过住院疗养期而生还大海。</p>
<p>救援队为佑奇量体重的时候，超出了磅秤的最高标数！秤面显示“错误”。他们猜测佑奇至少215公斤，大概不到十岁，还算是个小海龟。</p>
<p>小佑奇刚到时确实是奄奄一息。救援队给它抗生素、维他命、营养液，四天后小佑奇开始活跃，恢复了生机！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524141" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524141" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Caring.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10524141" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Caring-600x392.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524141" class="wp-caption-text">救援队悉心疗养海龟小佑奇，给它抗生素、维他命、营养液……（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下一步就是要尽快送它回到大海，因为要设置一个适合它的生态环境几乎是不可能的。第一它吃水母，谁弄得了那么多水母给它吃呢？再者，它的皮肤很细，容易刮伤。所以故意把它放在刚刚好够大的小水盆里，不鼓励它大游特游。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/swimming-tub.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10524168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/swimming-tub-600x425.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524168" class="wp-caption-text">让海龟泡泡澡！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>救援队先为小佑奇置入微晶片以便日后追踪。然后用起重机吊起再放入一个特制的放生箱，四面可以自动放倒，省去搬抬的折腾，让小佑奇能顺畅地迎向大海，然后要把它运到大西洋海滨。</p>
<p>有一大群人送行，凯莉说：“这是毕生难遇的时刻，大家都有说不出的高兴。团队很欣慰救援成功，并感恩有这样的机缘。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524150" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/farewell.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10524150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/farewell-600x387.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524150" class="wp-caption-text">小佑奇很高兴要重回大海了！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>放生箱坦开了，小佑奇往前划呀划， 顺着波浪滑进无际的大洋。请观赏暖心影片，您会注意到它的嘴角绽放着微笑哟！</p>
