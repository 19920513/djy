<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这块平常木头 经他雕琢焕发生命 观者叹奇</h2>
<a href="https://doqmgyop4u892.cloudfront.net/J3jxL1Fby"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10442784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-3-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月08日讯】（大纪元记者伊遐报导）从一块木头开始，这位男士用手一雕一琢地创造出一把非凡的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E6%9D%96.md#1">手杖</a>，足以赢得所有绅士的点头赞叹。</p>
<p>杖头的外观一开始还很模糊，但设计的主要核心很快就活灵活现了——是一头威猛慑人的狼。他首先修出头部的大轮廓，用细电锯盘修平头型的弧度。从手柄开始，沿着<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E6%9D%96.md#1">手杖</a>的柱体部分向下移动。</p>
<p>接下来他以各种工具雕刻繁复的细节：圆柱状的磨刀用来磨润狼耳，尖细头的雕刀用来修饰狼的毫毛，用软毛笔上色，用烧灼的方式带出狼毛的质地……透过他用的工具就可以知道这个人是一位经验丰富的专业<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E9%9B%95.md#1">木雕</a>艺术家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10464887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10464887" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Mike.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10464887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Mike-600x405.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10464887" class="wp-caption-text">原先是画家的麦克．史汀奈特（Mike Stinnett）热爱山林与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E9%9B%95.md#1">木雕</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>麦克．史汀奈特（Mike? Stinnett）喜爱山林，原先是画家，后来爱上木雕。一起来看看令人屏息称奇的制作细节，他还有一只可爱的狗狗小帮手哦。</p>
<p><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>观看完整影片?</strong></span></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-8962a67c-dc8c-40ad-5b93-85dbf454a665" data-id="player-8962a67c-dc8c-40ad-5b93-85dbf454a665"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/8962a67c-dc8c-40ad-5b93-85dbf454a665?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x960&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F6%2Fn10461551.md#1"></a>
<p>虽然手柄的顶端以狼的头部呈现，但下面的设计开始有些变化。手杖的中间部分呈现出时尚的钻石格子图案，下半部分让人想起麋鹿角。虽然不太寻常，但整体设计看起来非常和谐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10464854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10464854" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/deer-antler.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10464854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/deer-antler-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10464854" class="wp-caption-text">细心地给下半部的麋鹿角润色。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>精致的手杖经过打磨抛光，继续又有更加精细的手法带来画龙点睛的最后修润。总的来说，这款手杖非常优雅，栩栩如生的狼散发出深沉的生命力，仿佛都能让人听到月夜中的嚎鸣，的确是大师级的艺术作品。</p>
