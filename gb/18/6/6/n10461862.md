<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>游客们来见识最大袋熊 它坐小车出场萌翻了</h2>
<a href="https://d124e4v75n9sum.cloudfront.net/W5fKxfeif"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10445614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月11日讯】在一般情况下，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%8B%E7%86%8A.md#1">袋熊</a>在野外可以活10~15年。这只名叫帕特里克（Patrick）萌萌的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E5%A4%A7%E8%A2%8B%E7%86%8A.md#1">澳洲大袋熊</a>，被认为是年龄最大的驯养袋熊，它竟然活到了31岁。</p>
<p>“它从一开始就表现出了平和而温柔的性格。”澳洲巴拉瑞特野生动物园（Ballarat Wildlife Park）的总经理格雷格·帕克（Greg Parker）告诉ABC新闻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10464787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10464787" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/vid-n674b-NTD-oldest-biggest-wombat.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10464787 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/vid-n674b-NTD-oldest-biggest-wombat-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10464787" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>帕特里克从小是一个孤儿，它在动物园被人工饲养。后来，曾经有三次想把它放回野外。但是，因为帕特里克没有能力保护自己，不被其它<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%8B%E7%86%8A.md#1">袋熊</a>攻击，所以只能让它在动物园里一直生活下去。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看完整影片?</strong></span></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-c23b18f2-f186-45df-4715-3ff4ed395915" data-id="player-c23b18f2-f186-45df-4715-3ff4ed395915"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/c23b18f2-f186-45df-4715-3ff4ed395915?r=16&#215;9&#038;s=1280&#215;720&#038;d=70&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F6%2Fn10461862.md#1"></a>
<p>在动物园里，可爱的帕特里克也很受游客们的欢迎。工作人员也常常抱着它，还会把它放在小车里，推着它到处走走看看。帕特里克生活得很放松自在，它还有自己的脸书网页。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10464859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10464859" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Untitled7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10464859 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Untitled7-600x321.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10464859" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E5%A4%A7%E8%A2%8B%E7%86%8A.md#1">澳洲大袋熊</a>出场啰。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>去年4月，这只体重达36公斤的大袋熊走到了一生的终点。但是，它的脸书页面并没有关闭，仍然会继续介绍动物园里的其它袋熊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10464786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10464786" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Untitled432.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10464786 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Untitled432-600x327.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="327" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10464786" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
