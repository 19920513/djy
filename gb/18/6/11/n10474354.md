<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>温馨！逃过抓捕 非洲孤儿犀牛亲保育妈妈</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10445614 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年06月25日讯】这只可爱的小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8A%80%E7%89%9B.md#1">犀牛</a>，跟<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E8%82%B2%E5%91%98.md#1">保育员</a>亲密地在一起，看上去感情真得很不错，小犀牛用嘴舔着保育员、亲吻着她。很难想像这只可爱的犀牛，不久前，才因为妈妈被偷猎者猎杀后，成了一只孤儿犀牛。</p>
<p><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>观看完整影片?</strong></span><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-23a839f5-02c6-4bac-4c52-cb23fe1137e7" data-id="player-23a839f5-02c6-4bac-4c52-cb23fe1137e7"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/23a839f5-02c6-4bac-4c52-cb23fe1137e7?r=16x9&amp;s=836x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F11%2Fn10474354.md#1"></a></p>
<p>还很年幼、才刚刚长出<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8A%80%E7%89%9B.md#1">犀牛</a>角的小犀牛，虽然侥幸逃过了盗猎者的抓捕，但成了孤儿的它、缺少了妈妈的照顾，其实也很难在丛林野外里存活。</p>
<p>还好有犀牛<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A4%E5%84%BF%E9%99%A2.md#1">孤儿院</a>（The Rhino Orphanage）的帮忙，帮助小犀牛健康成长，学会基本的生存技能后，再回归到野外。这个位在南非林波波省的孤儿院，由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E4%BF%9D%E8%82%B2.md#1">野生动物保育</a>学者戴文特所创立，目前收容了近20只犀牛宝宝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10474403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10474403" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2018-06-11_174051.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10474403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2018-06-11_174051-600x476.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="476" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10474403" class="wp-caption-text">画面中的这只小犀牛名叫Grace，不久前，才因为妈妈被偷猎者猎杀，而成了一只孤儿犀牛，好在有犀牛<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A4%E5%84%BF%E9%99%A2.md#1">孤儿院</a>的帮忙，才帮助它存活下来。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于犀牛角对亚洲人而言被视为珍贵的中药材，犀牛盗猎与黑市交易的威胁始终存在。拥有世界75%犀牛的南非，也是偷猎猖獗的国家，犀牛受伤和被杀害的事件层出不穷。</p>
<p>而数量快速减少的犀牛，已被联合国列为保护濒危物种之一。事实上，犀牛宝宝出生后，常常是与妈妈形影不离，直到断奶之后，才会单独行动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10474408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10474408" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2018-06-11_174133.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10474408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2018-06-11_174133-600x496.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="496" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10474408" class="wp-caption-text">为了成功抚养这些孤儿犀牛，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E8%82%B2%E5%91%98.md#1">保育员</a>有时和小犀牛感情好到就像它的妈妈一样。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所以，为了成功抚养这些孤儿犀牛，保育员有时和小犀牛感情好到就像它的妈妈一样。就像影片中，早晨到野外散步时，这只小犀牛亲切地亲吻着保育员，仿佛在感谢她的照料。</p>
<p>但是准备接近要将其释放到野外前，这种关系就会被打破，这是为了帮助它们能适应并于野外，并能独立生存。但网友们忧心，一但离开了保护区，这只可爱的犀牛又将面临着偷猎者的威胁，真是令人担忧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10474412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10474412" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2018-06-11_174226.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10474412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2018-06-11_174226-600x473.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="473" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10474412" class="wp-caption-text">这只温馨的小影片，希望能唤起人们保育观念，减少犀牛被偷猎。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
