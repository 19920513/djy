<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>酥脆馅多 意大利起司腊肠面包</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10563827" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link-1-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月17日讯】法国圆<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">面包</a>中心挖空的料理，大多都是用来盛浓汤，而这道创意料理，是用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%8A%E8%82%A0.md#1">腊肠</a>、起司、蔬菜做成的咸馅，类似咸派的概念，至于挖出的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">面包</a>，也能另做一锅浓汤，沾汤品尝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493385" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-94.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10493385 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-94-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493385" class="wp-caption-text">意大利起司<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%8A%E8%82%A0.md#1">腊肠</a>面包。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p>圆法国面包 1个<br />
义式番茄酱 1杯<br />
莫札瑞拉起司 300克<br />
意大利香肠（salami） 60克<br />
意大利辣香肠（pepperoni） 100克<br />
洋葱 1/2颗（切丝）<br />
甜椒 1/2颗（切丝）<br />
甜罗勒叶 1/2杯（可用九层塔替代）<br />
白切达起司 1杯</p>
<p>（以上的材料、口味可随意调整、变化。）<br />
※“1杯”=16汤匙=240 ml；“1汤匙”=1 Tbsp=15 ml=3茶匙；“1茶匙”=1 tsp = 5 ml</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 洋葱、甜椒切成丝，并加点奶油下锅炒熟，加盐调味。<br />
2. 烤箱预热至摄氏175度／华氏350度。<br />
3. 切开面包顶，中心挖空，并涂上一层义式番茄酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493378" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-147.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10493378 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-147-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493378" class="wp-caption-text">切开面包顶，中心挖空。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 放上2片莫札瑞拉起司，铺上意大利香肠，再放上炒过的洋葱和甜椒。<br />
5. 铺上罗勒叶，放上2片莫札瑞拉起司，抹一层义式番茄酱，铺上意大利辣香肠，再放1片莫札瑞拉起司，放上炒过的洋葱和甜椒，铺一层意大利香肠，抹上义式番茄酱。（铺上罗勒叶之后，馅料顺序可随意一层一层铺上。）</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493381" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493381" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-117.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10493381 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-117-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493381" class="wp-caption-text">馅料顺序可随意一层一层铺上。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 盖上面包盖。<br />
7. 在面包顶上铺上弄碎的白切达起司、意大利辣香肠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493384" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493384" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-103.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10493384 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-103-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493384" class="wp-caption-text">在面包顶上铺上弄碎的白切达起司、意大利辣香肠。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 放进烤箱，烤20～25分钟。<br />
9. 取出烤箱后，即可享用！</p>
