<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>即兴发挥披萨DIY 正餐或咸食点心都OK</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10568732 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-9-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月03日讯】这道料理真的非常简单，而且可大可小，食材也很随意，想加多少料就加多少料，因此这里不特别写下每种材料的分量，就让你即兴发挥啰！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493981" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-101.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-101-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493981" class="wp-caption-text">星<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%AB%E8%90%A8.md#1">披萨</a>。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%AB%E8%90%A8.md#1">披萨</a>面团：</strong><br />
中筋面粉 450公克<br />
盐 5公克<br />
酵母粉 4公克<br />
温水 250公克<br />
橄榄油 40公克</p>
<p><strong>馅料：</strong><br />
意大利辣香肠<br />
培根<br />
莫札瑞拉起司<br />
青酱<br />
烤肉酱<br />
黑胡椒 少许<br />
蛋 1颗</p>
<p>（以上的材料、口味可随意调整、变化。）</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p><strong>面团：</strong><br />
1.中筋面粉加入盐及酵母粉拌匀，倒入温水不断揉压，直至面团成型。<br />
2.倒入橄榄油，一直揉到面团有筋性、不会黏手。<br />
3.面团盆包上保鲜膜，静置1小时～1个半小时，让面团发酵到2倍大。<br />
4.将面团切成需要的大小，擀平。<br />
5.多余面团可用密封袋装起来冰进冷冻，使用前取出、退冰即可。</p>
<p><strong>披萨：</strong><br />
1.烤箱预热至摄氏200度／华氏400度。<br />
2.将面团擀平，以圆心为交叉点划4刀，成星芒状。<br />
3.青酱均匀涂抹在面团上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493977" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-153.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-153-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493977" class="wp-caption-text">青酱均匀涂抹在面团上。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10493979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493979" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-124.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-124-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493979" class="wp-caption-text">铺上配料。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.铺上意大利辣香肠。<br />
5.铺上莫札瑞拉起司。<br />
6.撒上黑胡椒。<br />
7.利用刚刚切出的星芒和外围面皮，包住馅料。<br />
8.将培根等距离包住披萨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493980" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-110.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-110-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493980" class="wp-caption-text">培根等距离包住披萨，披萨面皮刷上蛋汁。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.在培根上刷上烤肉酱。<br />
10.打一颗蛋，将蛋汁刷在面皮上。<br />
11.将做好的半成品放入烤箱，烘烤20分钟（摄氏200度／华氏400度）。<br />
12.取出烤箱。完成。</p>
<h2>【备注】</h2>
<p>可切一些红萝卜段、小黄瓜段当配菜，沾取田园沙拉酱食用。</p>
