<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>沙田三十年历史鸡粥</h2>
<a href="https://duxu97yt14q37.cloudfront.net/K7emj1xag"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月19日讯】这间海鲜酒家在河畔花园已经30年，基本上我是从小吃到大，小时候是跟家人去吃，长大进入社会后，间中也会带同事和朋友去吃。难得的是这里食物水准还能保持至今，侍应也是做了多年的老伙计，和老街坊彼此熟络闲话家常，不像现在大部分食肆的服务都是冷冰冰机械式、毫无温度。</p>
<p>现在香港人工作工时那么长，想找一间比较晚关门，能和良朋好友共聚，慢慢闲聊的食肆不多，要不就关得早，要不就限时要赶快吃。还好这里想坐多久便坐多久，虽然装修是很一般的酒楼格局，但座位多坐得也算舒适。重点是它关得晚，放工后逛完商场后再去吃也不迟，而且每晚九时半后的宵夜时段，还有多款特价小菜呢！</p>
<p>来到强记怎少得有名的沙田<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E7%B2%A5.md#1">鸡粥</a>和红烧乳鸽呢！刚好当日<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E5%A3%AB%E7%84%97%E9%BE%99%E8%99%BE%E9%85%8D%E4%BC%8A%E9%9D%A2.md#1">芝士焗龙虾配伊面</a>有优惠价，我们当然也要了，还有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%92%E7%9B%90%E9%B8%A1%E8%BD%AF%E9%AA%A8.md#1">椒盐鸡软骨</a>、白灼海虾、豉椒炒大蚬和金银蛋浸苋菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10725682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10725682" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445421366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10725682" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445421366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10725682" class="wp-caption-text">除了有名的沙田<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E7%B2%A5.md#1">鸡粥</a>和红烧乳鸽，还有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E5%A3%AB%E7%84%97%E9%BE%99%E8%99%BE%E9%85%8D%E4%BC%8A%E9%9D%A2.md#1">芝士焗龙虾配伊面</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%92%E7%9B%90%E9%B8%A1%E8%BD%AF%E9%AA%A8.md#1">椒盐鸡软骨</a>、白灼海虾、豉椒炒大蚬。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里上菜很快，差不多一个接一个的上来，很快便上齐了。鸡粥粥底软绵，米粒已完全爆开入口不必咀嚼，鸡入口嫩滑，而且粥没有添加味精。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10725686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10725686" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445321366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10725686" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445321366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10725686" class="wp-caption-text">鸡粥粥底软绵，没有添加味精。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>红烧乳鸽吃得出不是刚新鲜出炉，皮不够脆也有点过了火候，但肉质还是颇嫩的，蘸淮盐或喼汁味道更好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10725689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10725689" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445221366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10725689" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445221366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10725689" class="wp-caption-text">红烧乳鸽。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再来就是芝士焗龙虾配伊面，如果大家想吃西式那只焗芝士，那一定会失望，因为这是中式的，没有厚厚的一层芝士和烤焗的口感，它是比较接近忌廉芡汁的一种，龙虾非常新鲜爽口弹牙，伊面就比较软淋不够烟韧，两者份量也不少，抵吃！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10725692" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10725692" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445111366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10725692" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445111366-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10725692" class="wp-caption-text">芝士焗龙虾配伊面。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>豉椒炒大蚬一如以往的好吃，没有沙。金银蛋苋菜虽然是一般的菜式，但份量比一般酒楼多，新鲜热辣。当天的白灼海虾不够新鲜，肉质不爽口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10725696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10725696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445081366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10725696" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445081366-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10725696" class="wp-caption-text">金银蛋苋菜。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10725698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10725698" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445271366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10725698" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445271366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10725698" class="wp-caption-text">白灼海虾。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后是椒盐鸡软骨，炸得金黄香脆，入口爽脆惹味，绝不会吃到一口油，我们一粒接一粒的吃不停口，多吃也不觉油腻。这里的椒盐小菜是做得非常出色的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10725699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10725699" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445371366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10725699" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445371366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10725699" class="wp-caption-text">椒盐鸡软骨，炸得金黄香脆，入口爽脆惹味。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我和朋友们由晚上7时坐到12时多才离开，慢慢吃不用急着结账交台，这里也邻近巴士站和马铁站，交通也很方便，而且营业至凌晨2时，大家到沙田，即使夜晚也不担心没有东西吃。#</p>
<figure id="attachment_10725701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10725701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445161366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10725701" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809190445161366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10725701" class="wp-caption-text">（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
