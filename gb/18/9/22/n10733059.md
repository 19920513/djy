<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>7只小萌狗荒岛上获救 它们的名字令人叫绝</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月25日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）受困<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%92%E5%B2%9B.md#1">荒岛</a>的生活，想必许多人都不想要。这样的情况，不仅人类会遇到，有7只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>也遭遇了，人们用望远镜发现它们踪影后，展开了一段暖心<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>行动。</p>
<p>7月29日周日晚上朱尼尔．库克（Junior Cook）与朋友莱昂．科隆布（Leon Colombe）在加拿大曼尼托巴省的克罗斯湖（Cross Lake）附近钓鱼，突然间，他们听到奇怪的哭叫声。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看完整影片?</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-1ebe2024-6c8e-47b0-4189-3aa705d2f659" data-id="player-1ebe2024-6c8e-47b0-4189-3aa705d2f659"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/1ebe2024-6c8e-47b0-4189-3aa705d2f659?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F9%2F22%2Fn10733059.md#1"></a></p>
<p>库克住在湖附近，当时他跑去拿双筒望远镜仔细一看，发现远处一岛屿上有一些小小会动的东西，以为是狼，就没想太多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10737781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10737781" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Untitled23.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10737781 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Untitled23-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10737781" class="wp-caption-text">库克垂钓时听到奇怪的哭叫声。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二天早上他们再次听到奇怪哭声，决定要坐船一探究竟……靠近<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%92%E5%B2%9B.md#1">荒岛</a>时候们终于看清，那群黑黑的东西原来是被遗弃的一窝小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10735628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10735628" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6beac2000c7cba6a12d5bc3a1ad1a8f8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10735628 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6beac2000c7cba6a12d5bc3a1ad1a8f8-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10735628" class="wp-caption-text">他们开船前往荒岛查看情况，一群<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md#1">小狗</a>狗聚集到岸边观望着。（视频截图/大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>只见约4月大的毛茸茸<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md#1">小狗</a>们在岸边摇着尾巴迎接。他们下船查看，发现狗狗们有点瘦，大概是饿了不少天吧。</p>
<p>两人不忘给狗狗食物吃，回家后，一边联系“挪威之家动物<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>”（Norway House Animal Rescue）救援组织的主管德比．凡德克霍夫（Deb Vandekerkhove），一边筹划着如何<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%AF%E6%95%91.md#1">拯救</a>可怜的荒岛小狗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10737787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10737787" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/collage-9-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10737787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/collage-9-2-450x446.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10737787" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://www.facebook.com/NorwayHouseRescue" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Courtesy of JR Cook and Norway House Animal Rescue</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>要<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%AF%E6%95%91.md#1">拯救</a>这些狗狗可不是那么简单，首先要取得它们信任。两人连续两天、每天三次运送食物、新鲜饮用水、医疗用品到岛屿，库克甚至还带来自家的“狗屋”，好让狗狗能够有一些基本的保护。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10735622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10735622" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/0d39251a036469085b9af2600e5fd895.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10735622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/0d39251a036469085b9af2600e5fd895-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10735622" class="wp-caption-text">他们不仅给小狗狗屋，也给它们食物吃。（视频截图/大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过一番暖心照顾，到了星期三，他们终于带狗狗离开荒岛，前往寄养家庭，狗狗们由此展开新生活。</p>
<p>“他们就像什么都没发生一样，正在玩玩具。”凡德克霍夫表示，“我们花了两天去搏得狗狗的信任，两只小母狗需要一点时间去知道人们不会去伤害它们，其它的狗狗则表现得很好，它们很好互动。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10737725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10737725" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/38235122_1751266448327922_8879082897152147456_n-600x800-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10737725 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/38235122_1751266448327922_8879082897152147456_n-600x800-1-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10737725" class="wp-caption-text">可爱的小母狗玛丽安。(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/NorwayHouseRescue" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Courtesy of JR Cook and Norway House Animal Rescue</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>要怎么给它们取名呢？1960年代的荒岛喜剧片《吉力根岛》（Gilligan&#8217;s Island）是令人叫绝的灵感来源。影片描写的是七男女被困在一个未知的小岛面对滔滔风暴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10735635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10735635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6188d3d6204953cbacfebaffbf14cb3e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10735635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6188d3d6204953cbacfebaffbf14cb3e-600x620.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="620" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10735635" class="wp-caption-text">救狗人员后来依照狗狗个性用喜剧吉力根岛的角色名称命名。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>那么怎么决定哪只狗狗取什么名字呢？原来是依照小狗个性。凡德克霍夫说：“你会知道，谁是笨瓜，谁是老板。”</p>
<p>“挪威之家动物救援”在脸书贴文中写道：“玛丽．安（Mary Ann）很小而且安静……教授（Professor）也很安静，玩玩具对它来说和兄弟姐妹相处一样简单。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10735639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10735639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/53a0eaa312d474446ef9dfe4f8e1f64e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10735639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/53a0eaa312d474446ef9dfe4f8e1f64e-600x621.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="621" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10735639" class="wp-caption-text">金杰（左）、豪威尔夫妇（右）。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“‘电影明星（Ginger，金洁）’很漂亮、偏好与玛丽．安在一起。豪威尔夫妇（Thurston Howell III和Mrs. Howell，又称Lovey）非常喜欢互动，它们对于人家丢的东西总会忍不住去追……还有一对好兄弟，船长（Skipper）与吉力根（Gilligan），几乎是形影不离。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10737724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10737724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/38207501_10155305192657315_4018536973126860800_n-600x450-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10737724 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/38207501_10155305192657315_4018536973126860800_n-600x450-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10737724" class="wp-caption-text">船长与吉力根。(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/NorwayHouseRescue" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Courtesy of JR Cook and Norway House Animal Rescue</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>小狗狗最后被送往温尼伯宠物救助站（Winnipeg Pet Rescue），在这地方，会有兽医照顾它们几星期，直到救援狗狗的人们确定狗狗安然无恙、可准备寻找“永久的家”。</p>
<p>虽然不知是谁将这些狗狗遗弃在荒岛上，但最重要的是它们终于获救啦。谢谢好心人努力救援，不然真不知它们要怎么生存下去。</p>
