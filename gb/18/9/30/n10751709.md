<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>狗狗在睡觉 猫：大啖狗肉的时刻到了</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年10月02日讯】（大纪元记者陈安综合报导）家里的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E7%8C%AB.md#1">猫猫</a>攻击<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>是很常见的事，不过，狗的体型很大，猫要攻击可能不是那么方便；而狗狗<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9D%A1%E8%A7%89.md#1">睡觉</a>时，就是小猫咪发起攻击的好时机了，甚至还可以大享“生狗肉大餐”。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E7%8C%AB.md#1">猫猫</a>妄想吃“狗肉”</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10752053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10752053" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/b035964b18cc0a127779c8bcac6ac470.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10752053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/b035964b18cc0a127779c8bcac6ac470-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10752053" class="wp-caption-text">猫咪向镜头方向看。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小猫咪站在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>身旁，看了一下镜头，心想：“主人想做什么？！”</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看完整影片…</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-c983578e-cf93-45ee-7904-bfed9c1cd8d9" data-id="player-c983578e-cf93-45ee-7904-bfed9c1cd8d9"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/c983578e-cf93-45ee-7904-bfed9c1cd8d9?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F9%2F30%2Fn10751709.md#1"></a></p>
<p>猫看了镜头大约10秒左右，舔了一下舌头，它……到底想做什么？！没想到，猫咪竟转头往狗狗的脸上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%AC.md#1">咬</a>。天呐，这模样，根本就是在吃东西嘛，猫猫一副享用“狗头大餐”的样子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10752058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10752058" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3452cb24d086c89491c1046942447ee1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10752058" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3452cb24d086c89491c1046942447ee1-600x357.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10752058" class="wp-caption-text">猫咪试图“大啖狗肉”。（视频截图/大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>说也奇怪，狗狗好像不会痛，只一脸烦躁，心想：“小子走开，不要干扰我<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9D%A1%E8%A7%89.md#1">睡觉</a>。”霸气地一脚拨开猫咪身体。猫咪根本没在怕前面这只大家伙，又立刻上前吃狗肉，狗狗又拨开……它们就这样不断上演着你吃我拨的戏码。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10752062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10752062" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/f98cfb6d822217fbf188fed8c1afb19f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10752062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/f98cfb6d822217fbf188fed8c1afb19f-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10752062" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗不断阻止猫咪吃自己的肉。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>狗狗的行为有点像在拨令人烦躁的“蚊子”，只是这只“蚊子”比较大一点。</p>
<p>猫咪有时吃脸颊肉，有时吃下巴肉，有时吃狗腿肉……可惜这是一只“活狗”，不好大快朵颐，只能用狗肉来“按摩牙齿”……</p>
