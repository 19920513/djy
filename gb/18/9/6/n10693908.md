<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>法式小品开胃菜“白酒小蘑菇佐长棍面包”</h2>
<a href="https://dervszfjn9udk.cloudfront.net/W1zkvGMjx"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10611616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月01日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E8%83%83%E8%8F%9C.md#1">开胃菜</a>通常是于主菜前先上桌的料理，其目的是为了刺激味蕾，以达到增加食欲的功用。常见的开胃菜有鱼子酱、鹅肝酱、熏鲑鱼、焗烤蜗牛等，因主要的目的是开胃，因此食材的量不多，也比较精致。</p>
<p>世界三大料理之一的法国菜，被喻为最能表现厨师内涵的料理，每一道菜对厨师而言，都是一项艺术的创作。</p>
<p>这道充满南法风情的“白酒小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%98%91%E8%8F%87.md#1">蘑菇</a>佐<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E6%A3%8D%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">长棍面包</a>”，使用长棍面包，加上新鲜的食材、独特的调理，让你在家也能轻松享受异国料理！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10824978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10824978" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-13.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10824978 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-13-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10824978" class="wp-caption-text">“白酒小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%98%91%E8%8F%87.md#1">蘑菇</a>佐<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E6%A3%8D%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">长棍面包</a>”，在家也能轻松享受异国料理！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【准备材料】</h2>
<p>法国长棍面包 1片<br />
小蘑菇 200公克<br />
洋葱 20公克<br />
红葱头 5公克<br />
牛番茄 20公克<br />
冷冻毛豆仁 20公克<br />
虾夷葱 2公克<br />
黑葡萄干 20公克<br />
蔓越莓 20公克<br />
罗勒叶 5片<br />
荷兰芹 3公克<br />
柠檬汁 15毫升<br />
白酒 20毫升<br />
盐 适量<br />
研磨黑胡椒 适量<br />
橄榄油 20毫升</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 红葱头、洋葱切末；小蘑菇洗净去头、切对半；牛番茄去皮、去籽后切丁；挤些柠檬汁；荷兰芹、罗勒叶、虾夷葱切碎。<br />
2. 法国长棍面包切片，面包片入锅用干锅烙出香气备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10825049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10825049" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/2-6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10825049 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/2-6-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10825049" class="wp-caption-text">红葱头、洋葱切末。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10825051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10825051" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10825051 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3-3-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10825051" class="wp-caption-text">荷兰芹、罗勒叶、虾夷葱切碎。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10825053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10825053" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/4-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10825053 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/4-2-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10825053" class="wp-caption-text">面包片入锅用干锅烙出香气备用。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 锅中放入橄榄油，以中火翻炒小蘑菇，再放一些盐让小蘑菇出水；放入洋葱、红葱头、虾夷葱，炒到金黄色。<br />
4. 加入番茄丁、毛豆仁、黑葡萄干、蔓越莓干，再加入白酒，放一点盐、研磨黑胡椒调味。<br />
5. 关火后，放入荷兰芹末、罗勒叶末。拌入一点橄榄油增加亮度。<br />
6. 最后放在长棍面包片上，再淋上柠檬汁即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10825055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10825055" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/5-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10825055 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/5-1-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10825055" class="wp-caption-text">放入荷兰芹末、罗勒叶末。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10824978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10824978" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-13.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10824978 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-13-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10824978" class="wp-caption-text">将炒好的配料放在长棍面包片上，再淋上柠檬汁即可！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
