<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北京街头艺人教老外“吹糖” 小鼠造型真逗</h2>
<a href="https://dh48pl2d5tlkv.cloudfront.net/H0fW1JJZq"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10610147 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月29日讯】公鸡、小猪、牛、葫芦还有鱿鱼，这些米白色的各式造型小玩意儿是什么东西呢？只见这位街头手艺人拿个小锅子里头盛着黏糊糊的糖稀，原本有点半透明<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%A6%E8%8A%BD%E7%B3%96.md#1">麦芽糖</a>色的糖稀，经过搅拌逐渐变白，好像龙须糖的制作过程。手艺人正在为外国旅客制作<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B3%96%E4%BA%BA.md#1">糖人</a>，来看看他们的“合作”成果吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10848161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10848161" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/64352abbe39ac22f913c741b7c2f66c0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10848161 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/64352abbe39ac22f913c741b7c2f66c0-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10848161" class="wp-caption-text">这种用热腾腾的糖稀和空气做出小摆设的技艺叫作“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B9%E7%B3%96.md#1">吹糖</a>”。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10848162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10848162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/556f64c3063b9beabbe89a803872d2d8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10848162 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/556f64c3063b9beabbe89a803872d2d8-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10848162" class="wp-caption-text">外国旅客正在体验，但是他不敢太用力，反而有点吹不起来。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>西洋的翻糖蛋糕能做出各种缤纷的绚丽造型，如华丽的花朵、城堡、结婚蛋糕等，而东方的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%A6%E8%8A%BD%E7%B3%96.md#1">麦芽糖</a>和蔗糖其实也有自己的特色风格，这种技术就是“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B9%E7%B3%96.md#1">吹糖</a>”，吹出的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B3%96%E4%BA%BA.md#1">糖人</a>”有人物、动物、花草等形象，通常以十二生肖为主。</p>
<p>在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E4%BA%AC.md#1">北京</a>的吹糖人，用一根麦秸秆前端沾上一小团糖稀，糖稀通常用蔗糖和麦芽糖加热调制，吹糖人接着让外国旅客含着麦秆吹气，“吹呀、吹呀！”吹糖人敦促着，一旁帮忙翻译的中国友人即对外国旅客解释，这是要他“blow”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10848163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10848163" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6c60a07bc5093294480734652cd884b8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10848163 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6c60a07bc5093294480734652cd884b8-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10848163" class="wp-caption-text">空气慢慢从糖稀的细管进入，小鼠的肚子渐渐鼓起来了。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>就在吹糖人的指令下，外国旅客把糖稀逐渐像气球一样吹鼓起来，吹糖人也随即又捏又转，把这圆球糖稀的造型渐渐捏像样，似乎是一只可爱的小鼠。吹糖人要外国旅客分几个阶段吹吹停停，纤细的小鼠变得圆圆胖胖，大伙看得兴致勃勃。</p>
<p>吹糖人将小鼠糖人画上了眼睛和耳朵，再蘸上些糖稀做鼻子还有鼠须，最后再用一小团糖稀连接麦秆和小鼠糖人，能拿着把玩的“工艺品”就完成啰！年轻的外国旅客开心地笑了，这是他第一次参与制作可爱小糖人呢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10848164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10848164" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1c38baddd4ccf59ea2a656c361a420d2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10848164 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1c38baddd4ccf59ea2a656c361a420d2-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10848164" class="wp-caption-text">点缀一下装饰用的眼睛、耳朵和胡须。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吹糖人制作的糖人，在物资匮乏的年代，曾是孩子们很喜欢的玩物，不仅有趣好玩，而且还能吃掉，现在则因为卫生考量，通常只是买来观赏，糖人也从哄孩子的零嘴成了民间工艺品。吹糖的技术据说从明末清初就有，不过糖人的制作从宋代就开始，但那时多数是平面造型，类似糖画。</p>
<p>中国早年每到快过年时，街头巷尾就会出现一些民间吹糖艺人，他们挑着扁担，摇着波浪鼓吆喝招揽生意。吹糖艺人把炉子用文火慢慢熬着糖，香甜的味道很快就吸引小孩子，几分钱就能换一个糖人。吹糖的技艺有搓、捏、吹、揉、按、拨等，花样精彩，小孩即使没钱买也爱凑热闹看个过瘾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10848167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10848167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1f0711f99ac339015ac3acad8b102db9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10848167 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1f0711f99ac339015ac3acad8b102db9-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10848167" class="wp-caption-text">可爱的老鼠“糖人”就完成啰！（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10848168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10848168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/657a31a720ae4deceb8e458ce7ef1246.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10848168 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/657a31a720ae4deceb8e458ce7ef1246-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10848168" class="wp-caption-text">胖嘟嘟的小鼠糖人，看起来真讨喜，外国旅客也玩得很开心呢！（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吹糖人从糖锅里舀起一勺糖稀揉成一团，沾上少量淀粉，在圆球上压一个深坑，把里面凹成空心状，保持温度的同时迅速插入一根细细的麦管，鼓着腮帮子朝里面吹气，一边吹一边捏出动物的腿、脖子、脑袋等。不一会儿，维妙维肖的动物造型就在吹糖人师傅娴熟的技艺下，一个又一个的成形了，给无数小朋友们留下了难忘的童年记忆。</p>
