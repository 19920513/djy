<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自转烧烤放题</h2>
<a href="https://d53tcmkdq4r1q.cloudfront.net/D7kpYKjSz"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月26日讯】踏入秋季最适合一家大细或一班好友BBQ，我很喜欢BBQ的，但BBQ通常要多人才成事，沿途又要拿大包小包、到达后又要加炭起火、又要串食物，烧的过程还要小心食物烤过头变黑炭，真是未享受先要辛苦劳动一番，烧完最后还要收拾、清理干净好一切，还有整个身都会沾上浓浓的烤肉味很难受！</p>
<p>之前收到风观塘开了一间<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/bbq%E6%94%BE%E9%A2%98.md#1">BBQ放题</a>餐厅，这间不同于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A7%E7%83%A4%E6%94%BE%E9%A2%98.md#1">烧烤放题</a>，这里的烧烤串会自动转不需人手拿着烧的，方便又干净，我当然立刻要去试试！预订时发现要大约一个月之后才有位，真系意料之外。</p>
<p>踏入这间<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%86%E5%AF%86%E7%83%A7.md#1">密密烧</a>，铺位面积不大，内里不足十张台，坐着有挤迫感，与后面的食客几乎椅背贴椅背。台左侧有一个大布袋给我们放随身的手袋、背包和个人物品，台中间就是固定的烧烤炉，右边架子上就放有三款调味粉包括：孜然粉、辣粉和盐供给食客随意加。最贴心是每桌竟放有一枝俗称“自拍神棍”的自拍器给食客拍照，因为这间<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/bbq%E6%94%BE%E9%A2%98.md#1">BBQ放题</a>人手不多，员工忙分身不暇，为免拒绝帮食客拍照而遭投诉，就每台也放一枝自拍器几客人随便拍个够，真贴心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236211366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742576" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236211366-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742576" class="wp-caption-text">铺位面积不大，内里不足十张台。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742573" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252235571366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742573" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252235571366-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742573" class="wp-caption-text">右边架子上放有三款调味粉包括：孜然粉、辣粉和盐供给食客随意加。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里有三十多款食物选择，肉食还清楚列名出自哪个国家入口令人惊喜，我们基本上把大部分食物也点了来试。未点之前已厨师肠和大啡菇两种比较易熟的食品先上，还附送一包焦糖瓜子给我们吃着等呢！很快串好的食物整齐地一盆盆上来，放在一个多层架上，还附上自家酱汁给我们自己涂在食物上，侍应还细心提示食物由生转到熟的大概时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742520" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252244041366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742520" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252244041366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742520" class="wp-caption-text">每张台附送一包焦糖瓜子让客人吃着等。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742590" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236011366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742590" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236011366-600x396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742590" class="wp-caption-text">串好的食物整齐地一盆盆上来。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10743634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10743634" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809261106191366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10743634" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809261106191366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10743634" class="wp-caption-text">快串好的食物整齐地一盆盆上来，放在一个多层架上。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>烧烤炉最多可放13串，一放上便会自动转，看着一串串的食物由生转到熟感觉方便有趣又治愈。可惜烧烤炉不是用真炭火，而是内藏发热线的烤箱，因此食物烧出来没有炭火香味，也没烤肉香味，但干净。烧烤炉顶部有一个架，方便食客已经烧好的食物整串摆上去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742583" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252244081366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742583" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252244081366-600x383.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742583" class="wp-caption-text">烧烤炉最多可放13串，一放上便会自动转。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236061366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742577" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236061366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742577" class="wp-caption-text">烧烤当然少不了各种肉类，但是牛肉已经有5种。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>食物质素不错，我和朋友们一致觉得最好吃是鱼肉年糕，鱼肉香甜年糕软腍，鱼片头也很爽口弹牙。薯角配盐很夹，烤番薯细细粒也很甜，鸡软骨和鸭舌也烤得香脆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742549" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236111366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742549" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236111366-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742549" class="wp-caption-text">鱼肉年糕（左）和墨汁墨鱼丸。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236161366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742553" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236161366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742553" class="wp-caption-text">新鲜菠萝（左）和薯角。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742567" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236261366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742567" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236261366-600x395.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742567" class="wp-caption-text">左起：新鲜菠萝、甜蕃薯、薯角、三色甜椒、圣女茄。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742617" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236351366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742617" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252236351366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742617" class="wp-caption-text">蔬菜加上调味粉后很好吃。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10743636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10743636" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809261106161366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10743636" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809261106161366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10743636" class="wp-caption-text">烧烤炉顶部有一个架，方便食客已经烧好的食物整串摆上去。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吃着吃着整个桶也插满我们吃完的烤串。之后加单的食物也很快来到，但建议第一次最好不要点大量肉类，因为我们第一次上的全部都是肉类，要先烧好两、三盘肉类后，多层架才有空间放蔬菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742570" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252244151366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10742570" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809252244151366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742570" class="wp-caption-text">吃着吃着整个桶也插满我们吃完的烤串。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们一行6人限时3.5小时，时间是足够的，也真的吃得很饱，还包罐装饮品任饮，价钱也算合理，重点是吃完就走，不用执拾。由于没有炭火身上难闻的烤肉味相对较少。</p>
<p>传统的BBQ是享受烧烤的过程，这个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A7%E7%83%A4%E6%94%BE%E9%A2%98.md#1">烧烤放题</a>则适合懒人一族和喜欢新奇事物的人！</p>
