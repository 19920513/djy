<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>道地法国风味料理“莳萝白酒奶油淡菜”</h2>
<a href="https://db1uzmukbk5lb.cloudfront.net/B1l6CW1Kh"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10615386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月25日讯】法式料理可以分成两大类，南法料理和北法料理。北法料理走的是沿袭宫廷风格的高级路线，是重视细节、摆盘的“法式宫廷菜”；而南法料理是法国风土和历史所孕育的地方菜路线，重口味、分量大，是很道地的“妈妈菜”。</p>
<p>在法国料理中，“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B7%A1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">淡菜</a>”是相当常见的食材。“淡菜”听起来像是某种蔬菜，其实它是一种贝类，淡菜也称为贻贝、孔雀蛤，淡菜的营养价值极高，且胆固醇低，但淡菜和一般贝类一样，属于高普林食物，所以痛风患者建议不要食用喔！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689615" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-25.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-25-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689615" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B3%E8%90%9D.md#1">莳萝</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E9%85%92.md#1">白酒</a>奶油<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B7%A1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">淡菜</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【准备材料】</h2>
<p>淡菜 250公克<br />
新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B3%E8%90%9D.md#1">莳萝</a> 5公克<br />
白葡萄酒 30毫升<br />
豆蔻粉 2公克<br />
百里香 3公克<br />
月桂叶 1片<br />
洋葱 50公克<br />
无盐奶油 20公克<br />
蒜头 10公克<br />
鲜奶油 50毫升<br />
盐 适量<br />
黑胡椒粉 适量</p>
<h2>【做法】</h2>
<p>1. 淡菜洗净备用。<br />
2. 洋葱、蒜头、莳萝切碎。<br />
3. 在锅中加入奶油，加入洋葱末、蒜头末炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689616" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2-20.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2-20-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689616" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱、蒜头、莳萝切碎。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10689617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689617" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3-21.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3-21-450x418.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689617" class="wp-caption-text">在锅中加入奶油，加入洋葱末、蒜头末炒香。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 放入淡菜稍微拌炒，加入豆蔻粉、百里香，再放入白葡萄酒加盖闷煮约1分钟。<br />
5. 加入少许盐、鲜奶油、黑胡椒粉、无盐奶油，最后加入莳萝碎拌匀即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689618" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-12-450x418.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689618" class="wp-caption-text">放入淡菜拌炒，加入豆蔻粉、百里香，再放入白葡萄酒。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10689613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689613" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5-12-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689613" class="wp-caption-text">加入少许盐、鲜奶油、黑胡椒粉、无盐奶油。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>※贴心提醒：</strong><br />
1.蒜头要去心，才不会苦，取碎宜用切的，不要用拍的。<br />
2.刀子利，切洋葱就不会流泪。<br />
3.莳萝即是茴香，可去海鲜腥味。</p>
