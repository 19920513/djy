<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>创意秋蟹料理“万里蟹金瓜石榴球”</h2>
<a href="https://divmj5v44rbg.cloudfront.net/E0aL3ic4y"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10615386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月23日讯】秋天是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9E%83%E8%9F%B9.md#1">螃蟹</a>最肥美的季节，趁着秋蟹肥美来一道美味的螃蟹料理，“万里蟹金瓜石榴球”是把精致、小巧的小石榴造型的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%99%BD%E7%9A%AE.md#1">蛋白皮</a>小球里，包的是手拆花蟹肉和金瓜泥，用不同的烹饪技巧与创意，就能把平凡的食材变成让人眼睛为之一亮的料理！</p>
<p>万里蟹其实不是指一种<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9E%83%E8%9F%B9.md#1">螃蟹</a>的种类，而是台湾的海产行销的品牌，万里蟹主要有三个品种：花蟹、三点蟹及石蟳，产地主要来自新北市富贵角外海的“西北渔场”，占了台湾八成以上的海蟹捕捞量，万里区渔民也发展出台湾第一的捕蟹产业。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689372" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-24-450x419.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689372" class="wp-caption-text">万里蟹金瓜石榴球。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【准备材料】</h2>
<p>花蟹 1只<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>泥<br />
蛋白 3个<br />
玉米粉<br />
水莲<br />
青花菜</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 花蟹煮熟后拆肉。<br />
2. 3颗蛋白加点玉米粉拌匀，用不沾锅煎<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%99%BD%E7%9A%AE.md#1">蛋白皮</a>备用（只煎一面）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689373" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2-19.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2-19-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689373" class="wp-caption-text">花蟹煮熟后拆肉。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10689374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689374" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3-20.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3-20-450x419.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689374" class="wp-caption-text">用不沾锅煎蛋白皮备用。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将蟹肉加点<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>泥调味，用蟹肉包进蛋白皮里，包成石榴球状，再用水莲当线绑紧蛋白皮成绣球状，蒸熟盛盘。<br />
4. 青花菜烫熟，南瓜泥加少许的水煮熟，并放在蟹肉球旁边做装饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689375" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689375" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-11-450x421.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689375" class="wp-caption-text">将蟹肉加点南瓜泥调味。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10689377" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689377" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5-11-450x420.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689377" class="wp-caption-text">蟹肉包进蛋白皮里，包成石榴球状。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
