<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>她打开冰箱 发现丢失的婚戒“升级”感动尖叫</h2>
<a href="https://dhltq9qxjnmw2.cloudfront.net/B4gYxDz8z"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月07日讯】为了给心爱的女人戴上一枚订婚钻戒，多少男人煞费苦心设计浪漫桥段。可是，对于已婚多年的夫妻来说，重新找到类似的惊喜并不容易，一位丈夫就通过巧送<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%92%E6%8C%87.md#1">戒指</a>，为妻子带来非同寻常的惊喜。</p>
<p>影片中，这名女子拉开自家的冰箱，她的视线立即被吸引，冰箱里面藏着一个“礼品袋”，她回头瞅了一眼丈夫，觉得这很有趣，忍不住大笑，她的丈夫也跟着哈哈笑起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10696281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10696281" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/t23.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10696281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/t23-450x584.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="584" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10696281" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妻子取出礼品袋，从中拿出一个盒子，打开一看，发现里面装的竟然是一枚<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%92%E6%8C%87.md#1">戒指</a>。她立即大声尖叫，前俯后仰，围着原地转了一圈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10696277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10696277" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/321.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10696277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/321-450x582.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="582" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10696277" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>她迅速戴上戒指，冲到丈夫身边，与他拥抱、亲吻。原来，她刚刚丢了一枚心爱的结婚戒指。丈夫如此及时地送上新戒指，真的让她激动不已。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10696279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10696279" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4212.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10696279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4212-450x602.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10696279" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“你丢了一个戒指吗？”丈夫问她，她矢口否认。</p>
<p>“我今天早上看到过那枚戒指。”丈夫却接着说。这时旁边的儿子马上将原来的戒指重新套在了母亲的手上。</p>
<p>原来，丈夫把她原来的戒指藏了起来，只为了送她一枚从<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%AA%E6%8B%9C.md#1">迪拜</a>购买的新戒指，果然，这一举动让妻子惊喜不已。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10696280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10696280" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/phot.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10696280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/phot-450x592.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="592" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10696280" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%AA%E6%8B%9C.md#1">迪拜</a>以<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%A0%E5%AE%9D.md#1">珠宝</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%96%E9%A5%B0.md#1">首饰</a>销售火爆而世界闻名，为博女人心，许多人选择去迪拜购买求婚钻戒。这可能也是这位妻子得到新戒指时如此激动的原因之一吧。</p>
