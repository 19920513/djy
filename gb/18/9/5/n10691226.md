<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>深水埗足料糖水 超值价钱</h2>
<a href="https://dycmgeziwp90b.cloudfront.net/K2yWwtyyl"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月05日讯】朋友介绍深水埗荔枝角道有一间很好吃的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B3%96%E6%B0%B4.md#1">糖水</a>店，用料丰富好吃，更重要是平民价钱。虽然对很多人来说不就脚不方便，但仍吸引很多食客捧场，还日日爆场，甚至有游客专程去吃。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%B7%E7%91%9E%E7%94%9C%E5%93%81.md#1">康瑞甜品</a>主打台湾仙草、芋圆、番薯、小丸子做配料的中式<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B3%96%E6%B0%B4.md#1">糖水</a>，充满台湾特色，又不失传统糖水口味。</p>
<p>虽然糖水店真的不就脚，我晚上10点多才去，但到门口发现一样大排长龙，门口派等位纸的侍应非常友善又好笑容，与食客有讲有笑，服务不错。我们等了大约30分钟就有位，虽然坐到出门口，但是我们没所谓。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10691787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10691787" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203011366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10691787" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203011366-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10691787" class="wp-caption-text">晚上10点多才去，但到门口发现一样大排长龙。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里甜品和小食大约十多款，我们点了招牌主打特色“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%99%E8%8D%89%E7%99%BE%E9%85%8D.md#1">仙草百配</a>”，还有桂枣雪耳汤底配芋圆、椰奶红豆沙配芋圆和什果啫喱四人分吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10691795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10691795" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203151366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10691795" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203151366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10691795" class="wp-caption-text">这次我们点了招牌主打特色“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%99%E8%8D%89%E7%99%BE%E9%85%8D.md#1">仙草百配</a>”，还有桂枣雪耳汤底配芋圆、椰奶红豆沙配芋圆和什果啫喱。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>特色仙草百配用料丰富，整碗充满仙草、珍珠、西米、红豆、番薯圆和芋圆，仙草与淡奶混合后入口爽甜顺滑，芋圆及番薯圆口感烟韧，亦吃到淡淡的芋头和蕃薯香味，西米软绵而不烂，整碗糖水口感丰富甜度适中，令人回味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10691797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10691797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203341366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10691797" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203341366-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10691797" class="wp-caption-text">特色仙草百配用料丰富，整碗充满仙草、珍珠、西米、红豆、番薯圆和芋圆。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>椰奶红豆沙配芋圆口感香滑，充满红豆香，配上烟韧芋圆令口感更丰富。桂枣雪耳汤底配芋圆比较清润。而杂果啫喱有五件，外表晶莹剔透，每粒都不同颜色、花纹和味道。啫喱中间还配了一粒椰果，不过吃下去口感不像啫喱，像大菜糕，味道普通没什么特别。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10691800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10691800" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203301366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10691800" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203301366-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10691800" class="wp-caption-text">椰奶红豆沙配芋圆口感香滑，充满红豆香。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10691803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10691803" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203261366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10691803" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203261366-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10691803" class="wp-caption-text">桂枣雪耳汤底配芋圆比较清润。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10691807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10691807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203211366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10691807" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809050203211366-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10691807" class="wp-caption-text">杂果啫喱每粒都不同颜色、花纹和味道，中间还配了一粒椰果。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后结账才九十多元真的物超所值，这个价钱在其它甜品店只能吃2碗糖水而已，而且还没这么丰富的配料。真是反通胀益街坊，难怪日日爆场！#</p>
