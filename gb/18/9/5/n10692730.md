<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>太壮观了！一只只蝠鲼冲出海面“噗通”落下</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年10月22日讯】动物学家及野生动物摄影师寇沃尔汀（Mark Cawardine）在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E8%A5%BF%E5%93%A5.md#1">墨西哥</a>的北下加州（Baja California）用空拍机拍摄到令人叹完观止的画面。</p>
<p>影片呈现一望无际的蓝色海洋，里头有数千只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9D%A0%E9%B2%BC.md#1">蝠鲼</a>在水中奔腾，并从海洋中跳跃，像拍动翅膀一样舞动胸鳍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10745631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745631" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/7d8bc489b3f20b32eb15f99df8f0d44f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745631 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/7d8bc489b3f20b32eb15f99df8f0d44f-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745631" class="wp-caption-text">美丽优雅的姿态被摄影机和照相机捕捉下来。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10745632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745632" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/afa467d4f2a1fda6fbd9cc5875cf54a0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745632 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/afa467d4f2a1fda6fbd9cc5875cf54a0-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745632" class="wp-caption-text">美丽优雅的姿态被摄影机和照相机捕捉下来。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10745633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/71f919b24aad46b6d431220cc5bc6cef.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745633 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/71f919b24aad46b6d431220cc5bc6cef-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745633" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9D%A0%E9%B2%BC.md#1">蝠鲼</a>落回海中，发出“噗通噗通”的声音，就像在开派对一样热闹。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10745634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745634" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2cec1c4c84a2336037728cf8835a2f38.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745634 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2cec1c4c84a2336037728cf8835a2f38-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745634" class="wp-caption-text">此起彼落的“噗通”声，真像蝠鲼间的“竞舞”！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>

<p>模样帅气的蝠鲼，在当地渔民口中有个可爱的昵称──<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9E%E8%A1%8C%E7%8E%89%E7%B1%B3%E9%A5%BC.md#1">飞行玉米饼</a>（flying tortillas）。</p>
<p>虽然是海中生物，它们却可以奋力冲出水面最多2米高。而且这些蝠鲼的体积相当庞大，居然有3英尺（约91公分）宽！</p>
<p>当蝠鲼以这种“飞行”方式跳跃，回落到海平面时会发出声波，飞得越高，落下时发出的声波越强，随着水花飞溅，一只只蝠鲼“噗通、噗通”地弹跳落下，像是场有趣的运动会。</p>
<p>寇沃尔汀每年都会到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E8%A5%BF%E5%93%A5.md#1">墨西哥</a>的下北加州拍摄<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%B8%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲸鱼</a>，最近的一次旅行，他看到了很有趣的东西。</p>
<p>“这些蝠鲼在（北下加州）东海岸突然伸出‘翅膀’冒出水面！像是在我们展现它们高超技巧。”寇沃尔汀表示，这样的场面实在令人非常难忘。</p>
<p>关于蝠鲼这种奇妙的行径，科学家并没有做出定论，人类不清楚它们确切到底为什么要这么做。有人提出假设，认为这是一种求偶仪式，或另类的沟通方式，另一些人认为可能蝠鲼在觅食，或摆脱寄生虫，当然也不排除最直觉的可能性──它们在玩！</p>
<p>蝠鲼长到最大的体型，身长可以达8公尺，体重1吨以上，非常惊人。蝠鲼的扁平身体，胸鳍强大，看起来像翅膀一样，胸鳍前方有两个类似耳朵的突起，用以收集食物，蝠鲼的牙齿细小，主要以浮游生物和小型鱼类为食，常出没在珊瑚礁附近觅食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10745635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/aa6c44982b4a4c3ffed9ad9e2e3d0183.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745635 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/aa6c44982b4a4c3ffed9ad9e2e3d0183-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745635" class="wp-caption-text">科学家为这种行为做了许多假设。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
