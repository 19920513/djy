<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>巧克力优格蛋糕卷 甜而不腻送礼自用两相宜</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10603615 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月16日讯】自然、健康、美味的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A7%E5%85%8B%E5%8A%9B.md#1">巧克力</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%98%E6%A0%BC.md#1">优格</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a>”，是大小朋友的最爱，选用食品级的法国巧克力做成蛋糕体，并用原味优格作为内馅材料，绵密的蛋糕卷吃起来甜而不腻，快来试试看吧！</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a>是相当受欢迎的甜点，轻软、湿润的蛋糕体包着滑顺的鲜奶油，也可以在内馅中加入喜欢的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A7%E5%85%8B%E5%8A%9B.md#1">巧克力</a>、果干等食材来增加风味，经过装饰后，送礼或与亲朋好友分享都十分适合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10680193" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10680193" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1-213.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10680193" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1-213-450x417.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10680193" class="wp-caption-text">巧克力<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%98%E6%A0%BC.md#1">优格</a>蛋糕卷。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【准备材料】</h2>
<p><strong>●蛋糕：</strong><br />
蛋黄 60g<br />
蛋白 90g<br />
苦甜巧克力 适量<br />
液体油 15g<br />
牛奶 30g<br />
香草荚酱 适量<br />
细砂糖 60g<br />
低筋面粉 50g<br />
防潮糖粉 适量</p>
<p><strong>●内馅：</strong><br />
原味优格 100g<br />
鲜奶油 100g<br />
蜂蜜 15g<br />
吉利丁（明胶） 3g</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 蛋黄（天然乳化剂）加入少许香草荚酱拌匀，再加糖再拌至微白，加入液体油、牛奶、融化的巧克力拌匀。<br />
2. 蛋白稍微打发后加糖，打发后再下全部的糖（糖可以安定蛋白），最后用低速打至硬性发泡。<br />
3. 将蛋白霜挖一部分放入作法1.搅拌均匀后，加入过筛的低筋面粉拌匀，再将剩下的蛋白霜全部加入拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10680195" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10680195" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/2-127.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10680195 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/2-127-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10680195" class="wp-caption-text">蛋黄加入少许香草荚酱拌匀，再加糖再拌至微白。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10680196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10680196" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5-36.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10680196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5-36-450x419.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10680196" class="wp-caption-text">将蛋白霜挖一部分放入作法1.搅拌均匀。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将面糊倒入烤盘，用刮板将面糊抺平，放入已经预热的烤箱（摄氏200度），烤12～15分钟即可。<br />
5. 烤好的蛋糕轻敲一下后取出烤盘，倒扣在桌上。（盖湿布可防止蛋糕体太干燥）<br />
6. 内馅部分，将鲜奶油加入蜂蜜、优格；将吉利丁隔水融解后，先取部分鲜奶油内馅加入吉利丁中，混合均匀后，再倒回鲜奶油内馅中拌匀（避免内馅结块）即可。<br />
7. 将蛋糕体于结束端以45度斜切，并在蛋糕体内里轻轻画线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10680198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10680198" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/7-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10680198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/7-10-450x420.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10680198" class="wp-caption-text">将蛋糕体于结束端以45度斜切，并在蛋糕体上轻画线。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 放上鲜奶油内馅抺平。<br />
9. 卷起蛋糕体，放冰箱冷藏30分钟。<br />
10. 在蛋糕卷上放一白纸，洒上防潮糖粉，空白处可挤花做变化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10680239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10680239" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/8-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10680239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/8-8-450x421.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10680239" class="wp-caption-text">放上鲜奶油内馅抺平，卷起蛋糕体，放冷藏30分钟。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
