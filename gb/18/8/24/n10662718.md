<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金门纪念八二三炮战一甲子 老兵感慨遗族不舍</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月24日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E4%BA%8C%E4%B8%89.md#1">八二三</a>战役胜利已经一甲子，国防部举办60周年纪念活动，邀请八二三参战官兵、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%97%E6%97%8F.md#1">遗族</a>代表重回战场──<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%97%A8.md#1">金门</a>，不少<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E5%85%B5.md#1">老兵</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%97%E6%97%8F.md#1">遗族</a>激动落泪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10662774" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10662774" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000072L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662774" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000072L-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10662774" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E4%BA%8C%E4%B8%89%E7%82%AE%E6%88%98.md#1">八二三炮战</a>60周年，国防部扩大举办追思仪式，当年殉职的官兵墓碑上都被放置鲜花，由后人表达无私的感谢与思念。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">观看完整影片?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-ee675737-9db2-4fff-53b2-e594c26583d2" data-id="player-ee675737-9db2-4fff-53b2-e594c26583d2"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/ee675737-9db2-4fff-53b2-e594c26583d2?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F24%2Fn10662718.md#1"></a></p>
<p>年过八旬的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E5%85%B5.md#1">老兵</a>看到同姓的战友不仅悲从中来，在墓碑前驻立良久。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E4%BA%8C%E4%B8%89.md#1">八二三</a>参战老兵粱立兴表示：“讲起来实在很伤心啦！八二三的时候是很激烈的，同姓的人过世的是很少。受伤的那个程度啊，断手断脚，大肠都流出来，连脑袋都破了。”</p>
<p>八二三参战老兵张明坤提到：“后来的炮弹很多很多，在排队的时候就这么刚好一颗炮来，（一班）十二个人，只有副班长还没出来没有被射到，其他都被射到。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10662775" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10662775" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000073L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000073L-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10662775" class="wp-caption-text">不少参战官兵及遗族行经太武山公墓时，望着一旁的墓碑若有所思。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10662773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10662773" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000023L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000023L-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10662773" class="wp-caption-text">当年参战官兵、遗族代表向在八二三战役牺牲的官兵献花致敬。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而特地从山东烟台赶来的遗族，在墓碑上摆了满满的水果，诉说对父亲的无尽追思。</p>
<p>中国山东烟台民众、72岁的张成思说：“从小没看见父亲，当然现在看见了，也就是说，我泪也是止不住，眼泪也是止不住。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10662777" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10662777" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000129L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662777" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000129L-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10662777" class="wp-caption-text">来自山东的72岁张成思（图）23日在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%97%A8.md#1">金门</a>太武山公墓，抚摸着父亲墓碑。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10662770" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10662770" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000130L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662770" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000130L-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10662770" class="wp-caption-text">来自山东的张成思（前）从未见过父亲的他说，能有张父亲的照片，就满足了。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走过一甲子，当年幸存的老兵已进入暮年，遗孀更是白发苍苍。国防部长严德发致词时，特别推崇老兵们“只见一义，不畏生死”的大爱精神，“当年因为有各位无惧漫天炮火、不畏牺牲戍守前线，奠定了中华民国生存发展以及安定繁荣。”</p>
<p>回首60年前的腥风血雨，国防部表示，会矢志捍卫国家与人民的安全，让“八二三战役”的光辉历史，永传后世。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10662771" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10662771" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000018L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180823000018L-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10662771" class="wp-caption-text">国防部长严德发（图）上台致词，感谢当年官兵不畏炮火，在金门驻守。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
