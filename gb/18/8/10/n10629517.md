<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>用在地食材制作日式月饼 让家乡味传千里</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/f53cbc7812a39799e10bc02b36afd646-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>剑湖山王子大饭店，今年特别推出用在地食材制作四色日式桃山月饼。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）中秋节快到了，云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%89%91%E6%B9%96%E5%B1%B1%E7%8E%8B%E5%AD%90%E5%A4%A7%E9%A5%AD%E5%BA%97.md#1">剑湖山王子大饭店</a>，今年特别结合在地<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E6%9D%90%E5%88%B6%E4%BD%9C.md#1">食材制作</a>日式桃山<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%88%E9%A5%BC.md#1">月饼</a>，食材完全采用斗六特选柚子、 林内的烟熏桂圆、古坑精选咖啡及柳丁，不仅在颜色上强化时尚的元素，更展现云林在地优质的农特产品！</p>
<p>王子饭店为行销新推出的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%88%E9%A5%BC.md#1">月饼</a>，由西点房吴俊德吴主厨亲手教学月饼制作，手工制作过程完全公开，并提供免费限量200份日式桃山月饼予现场用餐顾客试吃，希望让家乡味传遍千里！传遍每个人的心里！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10629520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10629520" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/8/10/n10629517.md#1/%e5%8a%8d%e6%b9%96%e5%b1%b1%e7%8e%8b%e5%ad%90%e5%a4%a7%e9%a3%af%e5%ba%97%ef%bc%8c%e6%8e%a8%e5%87%ba%e7%9a%84%e5%9b%9b%e8%89%b2%e6%97%a5%e5%bc%8f%e6%a1%83%e5%b1%b1%e6%9c%88%e9%a4%85%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10629520"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10629520" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/18d8491c88f3d2f7df57703d2f3c128c-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10629520" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%89%91%E6%B9%96%E5%B1%B1%E7%8E%8B%E5%AD%90%E5%A4%A7%E9%A5%AD%E5%BA%97.md#1">剑湖山王子大饭店</a>，推出的四色日式桃山月饼。（廖素贞／大纪元）)</figcaption></figure>
<p>协理陈冠锡表示，桃山月饼是源自日本的桃山县，最适合搭配咖啡和茶来品尝，口感松软入口即化，我们的桃山月饼的皮是用大部分的白凤豆，添加少许的低筋面粉及橄榄油，而传统月饼都是用面粉再加奶油，吃起来负担较重！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10629522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10629522" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/8/10/n10629517.md#1/%e7%82%ba%e8%a1%8c%e9%8a%b7%e6%96%b0%e6%8e%a8%e5%87%ba%e7%9a%84%e6%9c%88%e9%a4%85%ef%bc%8c%e7%94%b1%e8%a5%bf%e9%bb%9e%e6%88%bf%e4%b8%bb%e5%bb%9a%e5%90%b3%e4%bf%8a%e5%be%b7%e8%a6%aa%e6%89%8b%e6%95%99" rel="attachment wp-att-10629522"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10629522" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/d15255b67259b2392e1e73ee02c3c5e7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10629522" class="wp-caption-text">为行销新推出的月饼，由西点房主厨吴俊德亲手教学月饼制作方法。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为让异乡游子也能够品尝到家乡的好滋味，即日起订购剑湖映月魅力四色日式桃山月饼，不限金额即赠蔚蓝西餐厅自助Buffet七五折优惠券，并由官网幸福驿站销售网同步销售，线上买两盒还可享免运服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10629523" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10629523" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/8/10/n10629517.md#1/%e5%85%a5%e6%a8%a1%e6%88%90%e8%a1%8c%e7%9a%84%e6%a1%83%e5%b1%b1%e6%9c%88%e9%a4%85%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10629523"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10629523" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/e1d3006d12eb6621bfc75ffc3bd3d0be-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10629523" class="wp-caption-text">入模成行的桃山月饼。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
