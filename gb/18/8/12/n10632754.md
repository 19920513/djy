<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>部落敲信钟 到金峰乡小旅行吃野餐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/d6675c1e2c3d9171c11b8eb4a7f54942-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东县长黄健庭(左3)和金峰乡长宋贤一(右3)等在TTStyle原创馆前草地广场敲响报信钟，欢迎各界体验金峰乡丰收的农特产。（龙芳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月12日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）台东县<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E5%B3%B0%E4%B9%A1.md#1">金峰乡</a>周末敲响报信钟，象征“金夏峰野餐”市集正式展开，希望游客在市集选购金峰乡优质的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E7%89%B9%E4%BA%A7.md#1">农特产</a>品和部落手工艺品，并享受排湾风味的摇摇饭等部落野餐美食，进一步也能到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E5%B3%B0%E4%B9%A1.md#1">金峰乡</a>实际体验山林之美、部落风情。</p>
<p>金峰乡在经济部中小企业处地方产业发展基金的补助下，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>县政府在金峰乡推动“红金宝赞金峰”产业体验升级活化计划，透过红金宝─红藜、小米、洛神等部落<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E7%89%B9%E4%BA%A7.md#1">农特产</a>品、各式手工艺品，搭配部落特色民宿与农坊、温泉的游程体验，希望带进游客并带动金峰乡整体的产业发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10632757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10632757" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/8/12/n10632754.md#1/%e9%87%91%e5%b3%b0%e9%84%89%e8%be%b2%e7%89%b9%e7%94%a2%e8%b1%90%e6%94%b6%ef%bc%8c%e9%82%80%e5%90%84%e7%95%8c%e5%93%81%e5%9a%90%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10632757"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10632757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/a828ddce12ed6c091dc5c2d623b5dd36-450x330.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10632757" class="wp-caption-text">金峰乡农特产丰收，邀各界品尝。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>金峰乡长宋贤一大力推荐金峰的红藜、洛神是农特产之宝，而“部落野餐”也独一无二，里面融入了排湾族传统“食”文化概念，游客在体验部落美食之余，也能对金峰乡各部落文化有更深入的认识。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10632758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10632758" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/8/12/n10632754.md#1/%e5%88%b0%e9%87%91%e5%b3%b0%e9%84%89%e5%b0%8f%e6%97%85%e8%a1%8c%e5%90%83%e9%87%8e%e9%a4%90%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10632758"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10632758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1d7cdb620101ed7ead8698d5f2e80c8b-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10632758" class="wp-caption-text">到金峰乡小旅行吃野餐。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
