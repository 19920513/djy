<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>最受欢迎的短途步道</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-3-top-photo.jpg" />
<h6>大纪元合成图。
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月07日讯】（大纪元记者徐亦扬报导）</p>
<p><strong>一英里溪<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">步道</a></strong><br />
<strong>One Mile Creek Trail</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10620603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620603" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-3-photo.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10620603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-3-photo.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620603" class="wp-caption-text">大纪元合成图</figcaption></figure>
<p>一英里溪<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">步道</a>是当地受欢迎的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%AD%E9%80%94%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">短途步道</a>。沿着小溪，穿过山毛榉树林，你会在林中听到蜜雀（Tui）、铃鸟（Bellbird）、扇尾鹟（fantail）等本地原生鸟类的大合唱。</p>
<p>在林中，你会看见一条渠水管线，沿着走便到了废弃的水坝。水坝曾经是小镇供水以及后来水力发电的源头。</p>
<p>步道的终点是Skyline缆车。如果你想远行，可选择继续前往本洛蒙德山步道（Ben Lomond Track）。</p>
<p>步道的起点始于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%87%E5%90%8E%E9%95%87.md#1">皇后镇</a>-格林诺奇公路上One Mile 圆盘旁的电力发电所。1923年6月，皇后镇的煤气厂发生爆炸，这一事故使皇后镇出了名，但也几乎让它从地图上消失。而这也促使了一英里发电站的建成和使用。一个引水坝被建成，管道铺设到发电站。这项工程，包括皇后镇周围电力网的铺设于1924年9月完工。</p>
<p><strong>本洛蒙德山步道（Ben Lomond Track）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10620605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620605" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-42.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10620605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-42.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620605" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
<p>若想体验“会当凌绝顶”的感觉，那就继续往前走。本洛蒙德峰高1748米，最高点距<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%87%E5%90%8E%E9%95%87.md#1">皇后镇</a>市中心4公里。山顶可瞭望瓦卡蒂普湖和周边群山，因海拔比皇后镇山高出很多，所以视野也更加辽阔，湖景也看得更加完整。因皇后镇天气多变，这条步道更适合在夏天去。</p>
<p>本洛蒙德是靠近皇后镇的一座山，它早期由牧羊人邓肯?麦克奥斯兰（Duncan McAusland）以苏格兰高地上的本洛蒙德山命名。</p>
<p><strong>山毛榉树林（ Beech Forest）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10620607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620607" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-43.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10620607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-43.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620607" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
<p>山毛榉树林一般生长在南阿尔卑斯山和北岛山脉的寒冷山区。红山毛榉是长得最高的山毛榉品种，它们在肥沃的土地上占主要地位。银叶山毛榉和山地山毛榉在海拔更高的地方则更具生长优势，后者在干燥的地区尤为明显。</p>
<p><strong>扇尾鹟（Fantail）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10620608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620608" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-44.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10620608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-44.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620608" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
<p>扇尾鹟，亦称扇尾翔食雀，原产于南亚到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>的森林空旷地、河岸和海滩，因时常摆动并扩展其长长的圆尾而得名。它们体色灰、黑、褐或棕色相间，通常有显着的白色区域，尤其在腹部、眼眉和尾上。灰扇尾鹟长16公分，是澳大利亚、新西兰和附近岛屿的常见种。</p>
<p><strong>图伊鸟（Tui）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10620609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620609" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-45.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10620609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-45.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620609" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
<p>图伊鸟，名字来源于其毛利名字Tui，是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>著名的本土鸟类，在境内绝大部分地区都能被看到。它们和麻雀是亲戚，均属雀形目，是吸蜜鸟科中体型最大的种类之一。它们拥有短而宽的翅膀，肌肉有力，这让它们能快速扇动翅膀并灵活地转弯爬升和俯冲。</p>
<p><strong>铃鸟（Bellbird）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10620610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620610" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-4.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10620610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-track-4.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620610" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
<p>铃鸟是新西兰特有的鸟类，大小和麻雀差不多，身体呈暗橄榄绿，眼睛为红色。它们的叫声清脆悦耳，以花蜜为食，兼食昆虫。前年，生物学家发现了世界上第一只拥有雌雄同体特征的铃鸟，并且其叫声也兼具双性特征。也有研究发现，铃鸟具备抵御外来物种入侵的神奇能力。</p>
