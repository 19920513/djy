<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>推动主人轮椅的竟是它 女子闹市目睹感人一幕</h2>
<a href="https://d348xnvyypk4sr.cloudfront.net/O7qSnS3Kw"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月28日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳编译报导）汪星人的忠诚不二，人所共知，有许多感人故事在坊间流传，而你见过狗狗在街上努力帮主人推轮椅的吗？近期，菲律宾一位女子在街头就目睹了这一温馨景象，感动之余将影片上传分享，引起众多网友的共鸣。</p>
<p>6月下旬的一天，这位名叫芮玮拉（Misis Faith Revilla）的年轻女子正在达沃市与丈夫驾车回家，忽然看到一位坐轮椅的残疾男子——46岁的阿拉康（Danilo Alarcon）正沿着神学院大道（Seminary Drive）徐行。在他的轮椅后面，狗狗“迪公”（Digong）正将头紧紧贴着椅背，一边向前顶，一边小跑。</p>
<p>这一幕深深感动了芮玮拉，她拿出手机拍摄，并且和丈夫尾随而行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10670734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670734" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/322-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10670734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/322-1-450x506.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670734" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗“迪公”（Digong）正将头紧紧贴着椅背，一边向前顶，一边小跑。（Viral Press视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我最初的反应……我真的很激动，心潮起伏……”她告诉ABS-CBN新闻。</p>
<p>只见迪公尽职尽责地跟随着阿拉康，一路上，忠诚的狗狗用自己的小脑门和鼻子奋力推动着主人的轮椅前行。有了迪公的帮助，阿拉康出行显然更省力。</p>
<p>几周后的7月12日，芮玮拉和丈夫再次在马比尼（Mabini）街上遇到了这位主人和他可爱的狗狗。夫妇俩停下了车，走上前和阿拉康聊天，还请他们到自家开的自助餐厅吃饭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10670736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670736" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/Untitlewd.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10670736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/Untitlewd-450x531.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="590" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670736" class="wp-caption-text">芮玮拉和丈夫再次在马比尼（Mabini）街上遇到了这位主人和他可爱的狗狗。（Viral Press视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><center><a style="border: none; overflow: hidden;" src=></a><a href="https://d348xnvyypk4sr.cloudfront.net/O7qSnS3Kw"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/www/master/t/ntdtv/facebook.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(facebook视频或图片.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/post.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fphoto.php%3Ffbid%3D2015307358502462%26set%3Da.175400325826517%26type%3D3&amp;width=500&amp;show_text=false&amp;height=497&amp;appId" width="500" b="497" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></center>&nbsp;</p>
<p>在交谈中，芮玮拉得知，7个月大的迪公从生下来就和主人一起生活，阿拉康本人多年前因摩托车车祸造成脊髓损伤，瘫痪至今。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10670735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670735" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/Untitledew.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10670735 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/Untitledew-600x333.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670735" class="wp-caption-text">芮玮拉请他们到自家开的自助餐厅吃饭。（Viral Press视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芮玮拉随后在个人脸书上分享了几段暖心视频，并附言：“这只神奇的动物正在神学院大道帮主人推轮椅，见证这一非凡景象，我们感到很荣幸。我的意思是，我以为这样的事只发生在电视节目中，而这却是非常真实的场景。”</p>
<p>她接着写道，“我不知怎样才能形容我此刻的心情。愿神明保佑他们俩。”</p>
<p>芮玮拉显然也和阿拉康及他的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BF%A0%E7%8B%97.md#1">忠狗</a>建立了深厚友谊。她拍摄的2段迪公推车的影片在网路疯传，已获得20多万次观看和上千个赞。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_10673331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10673331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/40362457_2095695990463598_3234856534261366784_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10673331 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/40362457_2095695990463598_3234856534261366784_n-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10673331" class="wp-caption-text">阿拉康带着迪公来做客的合影。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/princess121815/posts/2095696027130261?__xts__[0]=68.ARCUIwuD_XunsedFpWJ4FDZ7O8FzVd7YjYAyXuHVrj2HKBFWcucPfAoUTypwJ8SqURmfp7YC_SWrUUKlfG9omgPjyBHvSet5PUCMvLehZ11T3bKv0UgmKHg49hCbUchWDzwPp9U&amp;__tn__=-R" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Misis Faith L Revilla</a>)</figcaption></figure>芮玮拉告诉大纪元，有一位女网友已捐资为阿拉康购买了一辆新轮椅。看来，这只狗狗对主人无条件的爱，正在产生同样温馨的回响！</p>
