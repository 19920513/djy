<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看过这么爱洗澡的鸟儿吗？还会开水龙头</h2>
<a href="https://dfxrfb9070id6.cloudfront.net/L9yXGLNSs"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月17日讯】这只宝蓝色的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>站在流理台边，伸出脚爪—— 打开<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E9%BE%99%E5%A4%B4.md#1">水龙头</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%97%E6%BE%A1.md#1">洗澡</a>啰！首先，伸脚冲冲水；接着，一个优雅的弯头过水，“全身都要洗，因为我最爱干净了。”主人还很贴心的将水流切换成莲蓬头，这样洗起来应该更过瘾，好享受啊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10399705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10399705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/902c5490c8a5a5744e6756dd29cc2afc.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10399705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/902c5490c8a5a5744e6756dd29cc2afc-600x350.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10399705" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>竟然还会“开<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E9%BE%99%E5%A4%B4.md#1">水龙头</a>”。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过，鸟儿对节约用水可能没什么概念。只见它豪迈的洗着，主人看不下去，好几次偷偷的将水量转小，没想到它马上老大不高兴，再度将水量转大。都说鹦鹉很聪明，真的不能小看它们。</p>
<p>看来，除了学人类说话、唱歌、跳舞，鹦鹉还暗藏许多尚待我们去发掘的特殊才艺喔。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10399708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10399708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/54798265b20ce44ed727108768de7fda.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10399708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/54798265b20ce44ed727108768de7fda-600x314.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10399708" class="wp-caption-text">鹦鹉<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%97%E6%BE%A1.md#1">洗澡</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>平常鸟儿会以鸟喙来清洁自己身上的羽毛，我们在野外也看过鸟儿在水坑里洗澡的画面；而豢养在家中的话，还是需要定期帮它们洗澡，去除羽毛上的寄生虫，以保持健康。但是，洗澡也要挑选天气，气温太冷不适合，以温水为佳，且洗完自然风干较好。</p>
