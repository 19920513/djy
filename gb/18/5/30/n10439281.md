<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>旋转跳跃全都难不倒 狗狗堪称舞林高手</h2>
<a href="https://dvucua6a0b85v.cloudfront.net/R4nckSV5r"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月01日讯】<span class="s1">你家的狗狗拥有什么特殊<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8D%E8%89%BA.md#1">才艺</a>呢？握手、坐下似乎是基本功，而有些身手矫健的狗狗经过训练，能够在草地上和主人玩接飞盘&#8230;..没错，就是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%A7%E7%BE%8A%E7%8A%AC.md#1">牧羊犬</a>。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">影片中的狗狗与主人默契十足，随着主人一连串的动作变换，它也有条不紊地精准配合。各种花式走位全都难不倒，甚至还能站着跳！一人一狗一搭一唱，合作无间的精彩舞蹈令人拍案叫绝。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10439288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10439288" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-2-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10439288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-2-12-450x438.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="438" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10439288" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗与主人一起<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%B3%E8%88%9E.md#1">跳舞</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">“旋转、跳跃、我闭着眼。”虽然不能像歌词一样闭眼啦，但狗狗柔软的身躯与流畅的舞步，还是看得人惊呼连连。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">主角狗狗是边境<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%A7%E7%BE%8A%E7%8A%AC.md#1">牧羊犬</a>，让我们来认识一下：边境牧羊犬是原产自苏格兰的牧羊犬，主要协助农场放牧，并以精力旺盛、体格精实且容易学习杂技运动而闻名。他们被学界认为是最聪明的犬种，智商大约等同于一个六七岁小孩，因此在犬类竞技与牧羊犬竞赛中往往表现亮眼。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10439291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10439291" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-1-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10439291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-1-13-600x377.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10439291" class="wp-caption-text">合作无间的精彩舞蹈。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">原来如此，难怪这只狗狗会这么厉害。一定要看到最后喔！</span></p>
