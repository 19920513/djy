<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>食物无法让流浪狗卸下心防 他们这样收服它的心</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/video-link-3-450x38.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10327568 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/video-link-3-450x38.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="38" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月16日讯】（大纪元记者林海柔报导）狗狗涂力（Tulip，意为郁金香）在街头流浪，看似活泼、精神奕奕的它，其实生病了需要送医治疗。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>组织义工想靠近，取得它的信任并不容易，拿食物诱惑也无法让它卸下心防。最终义工是如何收服流浪狗的心呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10396621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10396621" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-68.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10396621 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-68-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10396621" class="wp-caption-text">流浪狗涂力躲着人不愿靠近。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>洛杉矶狗狗<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>组织“Hope for Paws”（希望之爪）收到救援通知，赶到哥斯大黎加去，救援义工丽莎（Lisa Chiarelli）和同伴预计在那里停留一周，密切配合哥斯大黎加<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87.md#1">狗救援组织</a>，准备和当地义工斯科特（Scott Bradley）一起救援需要帮助的流浪狗。</p>
<p>他们第一个救援任务正是流浪狗涂力，斯科特开车载丽莎到一处民宅空地找狗狗涂力，它躲在车底下不愿让人靠近，之后虽然跑出来了，但依然和人保持距离。最让人心疼的是接下来的这一幕。</p>
<p>丽莎拿出食物希望涂力靠过来吃，这时民宅主人饲养的几只家犬纷纷跑过来抢食，涂力小心地凑过来，它个头较小，天真好奇的模样在家犬中显得特别孤单。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10396699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10396699" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-1-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10396699 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-1-3-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10396699" class="wp-caption-text">流浪狗涂力（右一）站在家犬身旁显得更孤单。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>家犬们吃完丽莎手上的食物回去后，还会有主人疼爱它们，而涂力呢？在民宅的空地上逗留，它的身份也就是一只流浪狗。它和家犬们站在一起的身影，格外惹人怜爱！</p>
<h2>发自真心的关怀 狗狗感受得到</h2>
<p>狗狗会吃救援义工手上的食物，但就是不愿放心靠近，它听着义工吹口哨呼唤它，虽然不愿搭理，但却会竖起耳朵注意听。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10396752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10396752" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-1-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10396752 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-1-3-600x425.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10396752" class="wp-caption-text">当义工吹口哨吸引狗狗注意时，它不理会却竖起耳朵注意听。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>或许狗狗观察许久感受到人类的善意，当斯科特慢慢靠近涂力将它抱在怀里时，狗狗全身放松地乖乖配合，那一刻它已经接受他们的关爱了。3个人围着狗狗温柔说话，相信涂力一定感受到爱吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10396760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10396760" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/7-18.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10396760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/7-18-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10396760" class="wp-caption-text">流浪狗涂力好像也喜欢被义工抱抱。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>狗狗皮肤有些问题，碰触时会不舒服，然而，它却愿意让救援义工抚摸它的身体，这是信任和接受的表现。他们带涂力上医院，最后被诊断患有肿瘤和细菌感染。</p>
<p>斯科特开车带大家回到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B6%E5%AE%B9%E6%89%80.md#1">收容所</a>，先帮涂力洗药浴，再让狗狗舒服躺在睡垫里睡着了。丽莎和同伴参观当地收容所后叹为观止，这个收容所建在雨林中，环境非常好，对被<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%83%E5%85%BB.md#1">弃养</a>的狗狗来说这里真是不可思议的舒适。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10396771" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10396771" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/17-4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10396771 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/17-4-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10396771" class="wp-caption-text">到了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B6%E5%AE%B9%E6%89%80.md#1">收容所</a>，涂力第一次睡得这样舒适。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从影片中可以感受得到，救援义工真心付出的爱，感动了狗狗，也感动了我们！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10396790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10396790" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/23-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10396790 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/23-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10396790" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗收容所环境相当好。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10396799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10396799" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/18-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10396799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/18-3-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10396799" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗收容所里面有草坪，还有游泳池。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
