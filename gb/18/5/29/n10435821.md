<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>奖励南部旅游 宝来花赏温泉公园五折</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/98e9aa0f23362660a12ef23c36a556c4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>十八罗汉山 （高市观光局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月29日讯】（大纪元记者李怡欣台湾高雄报导）交通部<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89.md#1">观光</a>局砸四千万元，针对平日旅游订定<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%A5%E5%8A%A9.md#1">补助</a>激励方案，以提高旅客赴南部旅游诱因，鼓励旅行团前往台南、高雄、屏东、澎湖及台东等县市旅游。为配合中央推动奖励南部湾域旅游，高市府观光局针对旅行业者，自即日起至8月底止推出“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E6%9D%A5.md#1">宝来</a>花赏温泉公园”5折优惠价，鼓励业者带团来高旅游消费，体验山城的悠闲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10435829" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10435829" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/380112ff360e97e9189fd682217b3c9a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10435829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/380112ff360e97e9189fd682217b3c9a-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10435829" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E6%9D%A5.md#1">宝来</a>花赏公园手汤 （高市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89.md#1">观光</a>局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10435824" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10435824" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5320454f46f1ec122868f4ac9a918b80.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10435824" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5320454f46f1ec122868f4ac9a918b80-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10435824" class="wp-caption-text">神威天台山 （高市观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10435826" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10435826" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/ac2cc32e2d9bfe00e013d9c13ac77c05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10435826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/ac2cc32e2d9bfe00e013d9c13ac77c05-450x296.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10435826" class="wp-caption-text">彩蝶谷 （高市观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高市观光局鼓励民众善用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%A5%E5%8A%A9.md#1">补助</a>，畅游茂林国家风景区，高市观光局长曾姿雯表示，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AD%E9%BE%9F.md#1">六龟</a>区处于中央山脉和平原交接处，荖浓溪通过、景致优美，推荐游客沿台27线先到“新威森林公园”散步桃花心木林道，呼吸丰富的芬多精、再往上行经有台湾小桂林之称的“十八罗汉山自然保护区”欣赏特殊的地质景观，然后再到蝴蝶栖息的天堂“彩蝶谷”看漫天飞舞的蝴蝶，绝对值得亲身体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10435827" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10435827" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/a19eb888a8ad9a3ba7f472d95f9bc05e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10435827" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/a19eb888a8ad9a3ba7f472d95f9bc05e-450x680.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="680" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10435827" class="wp-caption-text">新威森林公园 （高市观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除此之外，宝来温泉富含碳酸氢钠泉，有“美人汤”之称，更获2018全台10大好汤肯定，泡汤不分夏冬都让人感到身心舒畅、手足暖暖的新选择；除自然景观外，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AD%E9%BE%9F.md#1">六龟</a>区有多元宗教文化，可来一趟心灵的飨宴之旅。</p>
<p>配合中央奖励南部湾域旅游，即日起至8月底止，凡旅行业者带团来“宝来花赏温泉公园”泡手足汤者，可享5折优惠价。只要符合相关规定之旅行业者即可申请，包括补助住宿费每人每日500元、交通费用(游览车、船舶、火车、飞机等)二分之一，每团补助上限为3万元。</p>
<p>曾姿雯说，高雄观光资源相当丰富，山海河港、城乡与都市景观集于一身，期盼搭配中央所推动的优惠措施，吸引更多旅行业者包装具高雄在地特色之深度游程，将高雄不同的多元面貌介绍给更多人知道。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_10435828" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10435828" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/b0dcd2ae1c021a610d0855a791e099ad.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10435828" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/b0dcd2ae1c021a610d0855a791e099ad-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10435828" class="wp-caption-text">宝来花赏公园 （高市观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
