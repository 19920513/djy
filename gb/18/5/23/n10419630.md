<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看似普通石头一块 35年后当他掰开 美呆了！</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/video-link-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-9535808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/video-link-1-450x38.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="38" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年05月23日讯】（大纪元记者裴晏报导）一个10岁的英国小男孩在海滩看见一块长相奇怪的石头，表面纹路很独特，就把它捡起来带回家存放。不知为何，石头对他来说有一种特别的吸引力，所以男孩一直珍藏着。没想到石头却藏着惊人的秘密，而且他直到45岁才发现它的价值！</p>
<p>这位男子名叫多恩（Sy Donne），因为喜爱研究石头，他长大后读了地质系，可见他与石头有特殊缘分。能凭空捡到这样一块稀世珍宝肯定不是偶然，他在不知情下还完整无缺的收藏了35年。他发现石头的地点是在英格兰北约克郡一个滨海小镇惠特比（Whitby）。</p>
<p>由于他长年下来收集太多石头了，被妻子要求出清整理，他在整理中无意看到石头有裂缝，结果石头掰开后竟是这样神奇的面貌！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10422225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10422225" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Screen-Shot-2018-05-02-at-5.38E.12-PM-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10422225 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Screen-Shot-2018-05-02-at-5.38E.12-PM-1-450x402.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10422225" class="wp-caption-text">他在整理中无意看到石头有裂缝。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-b31656b2-020c-40ea-66a1-5f632f77cfb5" data-id="player-b31656b2-020c-40ea-66a1-5f632f77cfb5"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/b31656b2-020c-40ea-66a1-5f632f77cfb5?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x960&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F5%2F23%2Fn10419630.md#1"></a></p>
<p>这可比它原本独特的外表更加不可思议几万倍呢！因为石头是活生生的“侏罗纪时期历史见证”！专家确认，这块石头是已有1亿8千万年历史的菊石化石，价值约为3,000美金（约台币100,000元）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10422224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10422224" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/maxresdefault-10.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10422224 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/maxresdefault-10-450x471.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="471" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10422224" class="wp-caption-text">石头掰开后竟是这样神奇的面貌！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从影片里的石头内部菊石看，远古海洋生物的螺丝纹结构还是非常清楚完整，这种生物是生存于2亿4千万年到6千5百万年前之间的物种。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10419659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10419659" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/654f3cb4c2fee242fbf119af85a16934.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10419659 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/654f3cb4c2fee242fbf119af85a16934-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10419659" class="wp-caption-text">远古海洋生物的螺丝纹结构还是非常清楚完整。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>生性珍爱石头的多恩，当然不会出售跟着自己35年的宝物，对他来说，石头可是无价之宝呢！</p>
