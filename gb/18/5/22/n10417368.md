<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“手工菠菜面”名称特别 制作过程也超特别</h2>
<a href="https://d3vg08phoazui6.cloudfront.net/C9mjvyXSa"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月25日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E6%9D%A1.md#1">面条</a>是中国常见的传统食物，而关于面条的最早文字记录，在东汉时期就已经出现了。这段影片中，一位美食爱好者在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E5%AE%89.md#1">西安</a>拍下了路边现做<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E5%B7%A5.md#1">手工</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%A0%E8%8F%9C.md#1">菠菜</a>面的画面。就让我们来见识一下“隐藏大厨”的功力吧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10417392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10417392" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/eb0a9aa083fc31dc0cb883956806477f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10417392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/eb0a9aa083fc31dc0cb883956806477f-600x396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10417392" class="wp-caption-text">这东西抓起来就变成<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E6%9D%A1.md#1">面条</a>啰。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将醒过的绿色面团摊开，杆平后卷起，撒上面粉，再卷起、摊开；重复数次后，面团已被杆得薄薄的，将其层层叠叠折好，拿出锋利的菜刀开始切面条。最后，将细细的面条摊开甩一甩，放在盛有面粉的铁盘上，完成！</p>
<p>这一连串的过程看下来，熟练又顺畅，非常疗愈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10417381" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10417381" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/c43d3ae3344028586e76a1bae1f331dd.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10417381" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/c43d3ae3344028586e76a1bae1f331dd-600x345.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="345" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10417381" class="wp-caption-text">变成面条啰。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>瞧，外头的客人吃得津津有味，是不是也让你好想大喊：“老板，来一碗！”虽然米其林星级餐厅是美味的保证，但有时这样的庶民小吃，更是令人流连忘返。</p>
<p>根据“Storyful”的介绍，影片拍摄者经营一个美食部落格，他会放上世界各地新奇、精彩的食物制作影片，例如东京的棉花糖艺术以及曼谷的“黑色”可丽饼。</p>
