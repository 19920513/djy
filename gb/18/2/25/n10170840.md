<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黄子佼跨界策展 号召160位艺人网红彩绘义卖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1802250201011487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黄子佼（左起）跨界策展，号召艺人网红彩绘义卖。（新光三越提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年02月25日讯】由台湾知名百货举办的狗年灯展活动，透过<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%AD%90%E4%BD%BC.md#1">黄子佼</a>三度领军策展，邀请以爱狗出名并横跨演艺圈和艺术界的才子，以及网路红人参加彩绘、签名加上原创设计共逾300只狗狗大军，于24日上午11点起于Yahoo奇摩拍卖展开公益拍卖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10170885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10170885" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1802250206321487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10170885" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1802250206321487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10170885" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%AD%90%E4%BD%BC.md#1">黄子佼</a>三度领军策展，号召逾300只大狗、小狗一起庆元宵。（新光三越提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10171144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10171144" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1802250234251487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10171144" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1802250234251487-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10171144" class="wp-caption-text">配合狗年灯展活动，萧敬腾参与作品彩绘。（新光三越提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10170887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10170887" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1802250200581487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10170887" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1802250200581487-600x503.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="503" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10170887" class="wp-caption-text">配合狗年灯展活动，张轩睿参与作品彩绘。（新光三越提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场活动共号召了贾静雯、修杰楷带着咘咘与Bo妞、玖壹壹、张轩睿、郭书瑶、黄路梓茵Lulu、KID林柏昇、小宇、王维琳、李千娜、李玉玺、宋芸桦、那对夫妻、林心如、林志颖、林俊杰、吴思贤（小乐）、品冠、徐佳莹、毕书尽、陈彦允、陈菀婷、郭静、温昇豪、黄明志、黄鸿升（小鬼）、蔡凡熙、刘以豪、潘玮柏、萧敬腾、简恺乐等160位艺人力挺参与行善。</p>
<p>而公益拍卖所得将捐赠社团法人中华民国保护动物协会，一起为流浪动物付出关怀与微薄心力。</p>
