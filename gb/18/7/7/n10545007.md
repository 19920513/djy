<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鸊鷉求偶超有趣 有如跳“水上芭蕾”</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月07日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E4%B8%8A%E8%8A%AD%E8%95%BE.md#1">水上芭蕾</a>各位一定不陌生，不过，你见到“动物”会跳水上芭蕾的吗？</p>
<p>影片中，有一对大名为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%8A%E9%B7%89.md#1">鸊鷉</a>（音“僻提”）的阿根廷水鸟，一见对方就胸顶胸，不断晃动长长的脖子，那两只长颈一上一下的模样有如<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E7%A7%A4.md#1">天秤</a>左右一直晃动，一副超兴奋的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10548305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10548305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/45c320190e0c9ef86bea4ae6be72821e.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10548305 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/45c320190e0c9ef86bea4ae6be72821e-450x271.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10548305" class="wp-caption-text">两只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%8A%E9%B7%89.md#1">鸊鷉</a>一直“摆头”，有如<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E7%A7%A4.md#1">天秤</a>晃呀晃。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中一只鸊鷉，还会突然间将自己头后仰至背，前身弹起，轻轻一跃，头如槌子向前打入水面，整只消失无影无踪，这……到底在干嘛？！</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-3689d753-6d3b-4b76-59db-9976f3140c84" data-id="player-3689d753-6d3b-4b76-59db-9976f3140c84"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/3689d753-6d3b-4b76-59db-9976f3140c84?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F7%2Fn10545007.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>精彩的还在后面。它们两位再次跳“天秤舞”，突然间转换成“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E4%B8%8A%E8%8A%AD%E8%95%BE.md#1">水上芭蕾</a>”。</p>
<p>两位看似在水中“站立”，身体抖来抖去，左右摆动头部“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E9%9D%A2%E7%9B%B8%E8%A7%91.md#1">面面相觑</a>”，偶而往上啄，好像在边跳舞边“吃蚊子”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10548344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10548344" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c07d61e4704626b03715a780cf5fa447.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10548344 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c07d61e4704626b03715a780cf5fa447-450x256.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="256" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10548344" class="wp-caption-text">它们偶而会伸起头，有如在“吃蚊子”。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>原来，它们这一连串水上奇异行为是在求偶呀，这世上真是无奇不有。</p>
<p>鸊鷉科鸟类是一夫一妻制，据说这种奇异求偶现象每年都会再来一次。更特别的是，并不是我们看到的每一只鸊鷉都是这样跳的，每对鸊鷉舞步都不太一样，而这也是公母鸟双方确认对方是否自己“元配”的重要方式。</p>
<p>阿根廷鸊鷉是1974年才发现的物种，栖息在南美洲南部巴塔哥尼亚偏远湖泊中。由于受到外来入侵种威胁（其中最大威胁是水貂），大约在1991年至2016年这段期间内，鸊鷉族群数量大幅滑落超过80%，保育等级属“极危”，至今只剩不到500对具有繁殖能力的个体。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10548332" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10548332" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/86d842d598907882b3b792e23afc8344.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10548332 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/86d842d598907882b3b792e23afc8344-450x287.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10548332" class="wp-caption-text">鸊鷉跳舞时<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E9%9D%A2%E7%9B%B8%E8%A7%91.md#1">面面相觑</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而在台湾，与阿根廷鸊鷉最像的鸊鷉科鸟种为冠鸊鷉，最为常见的鸊鷉科鸟类则是小鸊?，在关渡自然公园与大安森林公园就有机会看到小鸊?。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10548345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10548345" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/40495d5c474d764791e218dea157e1f8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10548345 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/40495d5c474d764791e218dea157e1f8-450x281.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10548345" class="wp-caption-text">鸊鷉跳“水上芭蕾”中。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网友看后纷纷回应：“哇，这样都不会头晕耶。”“现在知道探戈怎么来的了，哈哈哈。”“脖子有点酸、头有点痛。”“哈哈哈，压力好大，感觉要一直偷瞄舞伴自己有没有跳错。”</p>
