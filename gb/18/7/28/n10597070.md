<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月31日讯】（大纪元记者裴晏报导）日式料理<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E7%A2%97%E8%92%B8.md#1">茶碗蒸</a>是很受欢迎的家常小点心，不过要做出细致又滑嫩的蛋体，以及味道鲜美浓郁的汤汁，可得按部就班讲究一点，那么秘诀在那里呢？来看看这道菜的示范。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-f9d0f771-51ef-49dd-5299-05bed366a043" data-id="player-f9d0f771-51ef-49dd-5299-05bed366a043"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/f9d0f771-51ef-49dd-5299-05bed366a043?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F28%2Fn10597070.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;">食材：</span></h2>
<p>鸡腿肉 1块<br />
虾 10尾<br />
胡萝卜 5片<br />
新鲜香菇 5朵<br />
波菜 1把<br />
鸿禧菇（又称鸿喜菇） 适量<br />
全蛋 3个<br />
鸡汤 450 cc<br />
料酒 1.5茶匙<br />
盐 ?茶匙<br />
糖 ?茶匙<br />
酱油 1茶匙</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法：</span></h2>
<p>1. 鸡腿肉去骨去皮，并切除多余脂肪，再划几刀剖面，待入锅时肉才能快速转金黄。预热油锅，以中火将鸡肉煎至两面金黄。鸡肉煎至半熟后就起锅，切小块备用，入蒸锅后再与蛋体一起蒸至全熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10597202" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597202" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/8154d1e67f8e3fad74826f075ef1a034.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10597202 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/8154d1e67f8e3fad74826f075ef1a034-450x404.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597202" class="wp-caption-text">起油锅，以中火将划开的鸡腿肉片煎至两面金黄。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10597218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597218" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/7ab6af6e6adc2a15215f6b4bf8fb5f07.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10597218 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/7ab6af6e6adc2a15215f6b4bf8fb5f07-450x397.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597218" class="wp-caption-text">将煎出焦色和香气的半熟鸡肉切成小块备用。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将胡萝卜片剪出造型备用。</p>
<p>3. 将新鲜香菇划十字，并切成小块备用。</p>
<p>4. 将煮好的波菜切成小段备用。</p>
<p>5. 剔除虾的泥肠，备用。</p>
<p>6.?将食材放进蒸碗内，鸡肉块、虾、鸿禧菇和蛋汁，再放波菜段、红萝卜和香菇于上方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10597236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597236" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/63b6cd8b3898bb1304cd8d9853e9edf8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10597236 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/63b6cd8b3898bb1304cd8d9853e9edf8-450x410.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597236" class="wp-caption-text">将食材放进蒸碗。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 打散3个全蛋，加入料酒、糖、酱油、盐和鸡汤拌匀，并过滤蛋汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10597243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597243" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/a368fb034062c7c747d95f604f9c8109.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10597243 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/a368fb034062c7c747d95f604f9c8109-450x411.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597243" class="wp-caption-text">在搅拌碗内打散3个全蛋。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10597244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597244" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/cbc01267843865c5c39bb37b012e9b47.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10597244 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/cbc01267843865c5c39bb37b012e9b47-450x409.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597244" class="wp-caption-text">过滤蛋汁后，加进鸡汤基底。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 将过蛋汁注入放好食材的蒸碗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10597245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597245" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/9aebb586378059f6a17635385477fc3c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10597245 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/9aebb586378059f6a17635385477fc3c-450x412.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597245" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 滚水时入锅用大火蒸，在锅内放好蒸碗，盖上锅盖蒸2分钟后，再转小火蒸9分钟。 在锅盖边放一根筷子让出一个可透热气的缝隙。</p>
<p>10. 熄火后，静置3分钟即成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10597252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597252" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/74415c513e2ee7f5b905caee90a472ed.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10597252 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/74415c513e2ee7f5b905caee90a472ed-450x408.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597252" class="wp-caption-text">转小火蒸时，用筷子撑起一个缝隙透气，避免过多热气。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10597253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597253" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/5b038e26c64754b23bacddfc5c1556fd.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10597253 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/5b038e26c64754b23bacddfc5c1556fd-450x407.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597253" class="wp-caption-text">完成品。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10597254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597254" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/f0186a9a4aa00e89edfad1de6ec387e8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10597254 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/f0186a9a4aa00e89edfad1de6ec387e8-450x409.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597254" class="wp-caption-text">只要用一点心就会制作出滑嫩顺口的蛋体。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;">主厨秘诀：</span></h2>
<p>细致滑嫩的蛋体主要是由于这3个要领：</p>
<p>1.? 蛋汁要过筛，过滤出黏液并捞出表面泡沫，否则容易出现气泡。讲究点可以过滤2次去除杂质。</p>
<p>2. 蒸煮时，先用大火蒸再改小火，不求快速熟透，才能确保蛋体整体的滑嫩口感。如果用大火持续蒸到全熟，蛋体会出现像蜂窝一样的坑洞，失去滑顺口感和影响视觉美观度。</p>
<p>3. 以大火蒸时要加盖。先让表面凝固，再转小火并留锅盖缝隙，排除过多热气可保持蒸蛋表面光滑漂亮。</p>
<p>4. 水（鸡汤）和蛋汁的基本比例是2:1，可依个人喜好的软硬度调整。用鸡汤、香菇汤或蔬菜高汤当基底，可提升<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E7%A2%97%E8%92%B8.md#1">茶碗蒸</a>的鲜甜和浓郁度。</p>
<p>5. 选用海鲜类的食材最好用鲜料而非干货，例如龙虾或干贝。</p>
<p>6. 完成的茶碗蒸可洒上新鲜的帕马森干酪、巧达起司或莫札瑞拉起司，增加浓郁度。</p>
