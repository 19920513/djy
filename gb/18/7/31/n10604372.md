<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新唐人大厨上菜：炒牛肉片柔软滑嫩的秘诀</h2>
<a href="https://db8nk66phkh66.cloudfront.net/N7eLdpLnv"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月31日讯】西兰花（花椰菜）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">炒牛肉</a>，是一道经典的中式家常菜。怎样才能炒得柔软嫩滑呢？让新唐人电视台大厨来告诉你秘诀，在家里也可以做出餐馆级美味。</p>
<p><strong>主料：</strong>新鲜牛肉200克</p>
<p><strong>配料：</strong>西兰花180克、胡萝卜50克</p>
<p><strong>调味料：</strong>橄榄油、酱油、蚝油、糖、淀粉、生鸡蛋、大蒜末、红辣椒</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片&gt;&gt;&gt;<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-2d3fc0cd-9bd5-45e9-45f8-64e8d0e879c0" data-id="player-2d3fc0cd-9bd5-45e9-45f8-64e8d0e879c0"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/2d3fc0cd-9bd5-45e9-45f8-64e8d0e879c0?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F31%2Fn10604372.md#1"></a></span></strong></p>
<p><strong>制作方法：</strong></p>
<p>1. 将牛肉切成薄片。注意牛肉要断纹切，请参考影片操作示范。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10608981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10608981" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/beef14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10608981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/beef14-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10608981" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 把切好的牛肉片放进容器中，加入调味料：半汤匙酱油、1个生鸡蛋、半汤匙淀粉、半汤匙橄榄油，搅拌均匀；然后放进冰箱中腌制6小时。（1汤匙合15毫升。）</p>
<figure id="attachment_10608978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10608978" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/beef02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10608978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/beef02-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10608978" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 把切好的西兰花和胡萝卜片，放进滚水中烫一分钟，捞出沥干水分备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10608985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10608985" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/veggi14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10608985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/veggi14-450x447.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10608985" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 在干净的锅中加入1汤匙橄榄油，开大火，待油热后撒入大蒜末，稍加搅拌；马上加入腌制好的牛肉片，翻炒直至牛肉片略微变为油褐色；加入1汤匙蚝油、半汤匙酱油和半汤匙糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10608982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10608982" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/beef23.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10608982" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/beef23-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10608982" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再加入烫过的西兰花和胡萝卜；继续搅拌，直到牛肉片都被酱汁包裹。最后，加入红辣椒片，稍微搅拌一下，即可出锅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10608980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10608980" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/beef3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10608980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/beef3-450x427.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10608980" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
