<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小鹦鹉跟主人乐玩丢追球 两只狗狗淡定处理</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10574187 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-10-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年07月27日讯】丢追<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%90%83.md#1">球</a>游戏是所有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>的最爱，但居然有两只狗狗，可以冷静又淡定地看着主人跟其他小动物玩，忍住想要又叫又跳地追上前去抢<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%90%83.md#1">球</a>，为什么呢？而且狗狗还露出一脸“欣慰”的表情，好像看到自己小孩终于长大的父母，真是有趣！</p>
<p>原来，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>们是在“视察”丢追球新手小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>的学习进度。主人帮小鹦鹉准备了适合它体型大小的塑胶藤球，里面还放着小铃铛能吸引鹦鹉注意，小鸟乖巧地看着球，主人轻轻把球滚出去，它扭着鸟屁屁跟在球球后面，叼小球回来，表现得很不错！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10576923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10576923" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2018-07-20_143256.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10576923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2018-07-20_143256-600x567.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10576923" class="wp-caption-text">主人找了两只“狗教练”负责校正这只丢追球新手<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>的姿势。狗狗超级尽责，非常认真盯着小鹦鹉的动作。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #ff0000;">观看完整影片?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-08e2641a-438b-4f1e-4574-2411c495ce04" data-id="player-08e2641a-438b-4f1e-4574-2411c495ce04"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/08e2641a-438b-4f1e-4574-2411c495ce04?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F20%2Fn10576567.md#1"></a></p>
<p>一只黑白相间，另一只棕白相间，两只边境<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%A7%E7%BE%8A%E7%8A%AC.md#1">牧羊犬</a>专注地看着球球和小鹦鹉的动线，仿佛在心中暗暗打分数：“嗯……没有漏接，看来我平时教得好。”小鹦鹉成功将球球捡回来，就可以获得主人奖励的鸟饲料，看起来好吃又好玩，小鹦鹉真开心。</p>
<p>接着是第二轮训练，球球滚出去，小鹦鹉冲啊！要去追球球，但好像一时太兴奋了，还是想着零食太开心，忘了“嘴边任务”，回程的路上有点东倒西歪。这时，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%BA%E5%95%86.md#1">智商</a>很高的两只边境<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%A7%E7%BE%8A%E7%8A%AC.md#1">牧羊犬</a>居然做出“相视而笑”的转头，好聪明～</p>
<figure id="attachment_10576941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10576941" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2018-07-20_143444.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10576941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2018-07-20_143444-600x582.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="582" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10576941" class="wp-caption-text">小鹦鹉成功将球球捡回来，就可以获得主人奖励的鸟饲料，看起来好吃又好玩，小鹦鹉真开心。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网友觉得，边境牧羊犬真的很聪明，知道那颗小球不是自己的玩具，所以静静看小鸟跟主人玩。也有网友搞笑脑补，认为聪明的边境牧羊犬现在乖乖，是因为在主人面前要维持形象，等主人离开，它们一定会抢走小鹦鹉的球！但更多网友其实不在乎狗狗的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%BA%E5%95%86.md#1">智商</a>，纯粹认为小动物太可爱了！</p>
