<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>奥利佛主厨上菜：英式姜爆和风牛肉烧</h2>
<a href="https://d259d2wq6kf78s.cloudfront.net/I2e8684Bx"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月21日讯】（大纪元记者裴晏报导）许多欧美人士近年来透过网路影片开始在家学习亚洲做菜方式。去年英国达人主厨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%B0%E7%B1%B3%C2%B7%E5%A5%A5%E5%88%A9%E4%BD%9B.md#1">杰米·奥利佛</a>出版的一本新书《<ahref="http://www.books.com.tw/products/0010787292?sloc=reprod_i_2" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">5种食材：简易、快速美味食谱</a>》，里面就有一道深受好评的亚洲菜：“姜爆和风<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>烧”，来看看这道简易料理的做法（由新唐人主厨示范）。</p>
<p>如同奥利佛的一贯作风，这道家常菜备料简单，做法快速，符合这本新书的推广宗旨：只用5种食材就能迅速做出餐厅级料理。书的理念是：越简单的料理（仅需5种材料）就越美味，他示范的做法大都在10个步骤以内就能完成。</p>
<p>这道“英式”姜爆和风<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>烧的姜丝和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%B3%E5%99%8C.md#1">味噌</a>，是欧美人士比较陌生的食材，奥利佛融合了三个绝佳的天然调味料，姜丝、味噌和蜂蜜，让西方人能快速学会经典亚洲口味。他还注明这道菜“只需16分钟”！</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-20ebafce-89b1-4d7d-4c3d-cf6a2be7c337" data-id="player-20ebafce-89b1-4d7d-4c3d-cf6a2be7c337"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/20ebafce-89b1-4d7d-4c3d-cf6a2be7c337?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F20%2Fn10578252.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">食材：（5种）</span></strong></p>
<p>沙朗牛排 300克 （厚度达1.5公分最理想）<br />
姜丝 1/4杯<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%B3%E5%99%8C.md#1">味噌</a> 一大匙<br />
青江菜（上海青） 250克<br />
蜂蜜 2茶匙</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法：</span></strong></p>
<p>1. 用刀将沙朗牛排的油花部分切除，并切小块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10578266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578266" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/4f2c3a4dfcb2ad2dbe7c49af8120ab21.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10578266 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/4f2c3a4dfcb2ad2dbe7c49af8120ab21-450x350.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578266" class="wp-caption-text">切除油花部分。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10578299" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578299" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/d7a0880ea922022018e34ed349f44fcd.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10578299 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/d7a0880ea922022018e34ed349f44fcd-450x403.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578299" class="wp-caption-text">将油花切小块。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 起油锅，将油花煎至金黄，爆出油脂。再加入姜丝，一起炒熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10578303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578303" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/962b5bf34906544b3e3d0f157625ee72.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10578303 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/962b5bf34906544b3e3d0f157625ee72-450x407.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578303" class="wp-caption-text">将油花和姜丝炒熟。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10578267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578267" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/5de64cca373521efc0619e4ff8740513.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10578267 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/5de64cca373521efc0619e4ff8740513-450x366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578267" class="wp-caption-text">金黄色的配料完成，起锅备用。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将牛排切成约3公分宽，加入味噌。起油锅，将牛肉块炒熟，约5分钟。再加入蜂蜜调味拌炒1分钟。加入蜂蜜后的肉块颜色更有光泽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10578287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/0dfea40b3789f989aeb01b52c539cd0d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10578287 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/0dfea40b3789f989aeb01b52c539cd0d-450x407.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578287" class="wp-caption-text">以味噌入味的牛肉块，立即有了亚洲风味，就不需要其他酱汁了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将青江菜对半切开，入滚水约1分钟快速烫熟，保持青脆口感和色泽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10578296" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578296" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/23d769df8d1779d56eebdd2dbb759ec2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10578296 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/23d769df8d1779d56eebdd2dbb759ec2-450x372.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="372" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578296" class="wp-caption-text">用1分钟将配角“青江菜”烫熟。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 将青江菜铺在盘上，放上味噌牛肉，再撒上姜爆油花即成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10578584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578584" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/332998bf801b5b0ca0ee4cb3ada94b29.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10578584 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/332998bf801b5b0ca0ee4cb3ada94b29-450x410.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578584" class="wp-caption-text">完成品。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
