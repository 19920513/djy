<p>【大纪元2018年11月04日讯】早晨忙碌准备上班、上学，有时候又吃腻了外面的早餐店……好烦恼吗？！这时来点小确幸，趁着周末午后，居家打造超萌的兔兔大军，陪你愉快迎接崭新的每一天。还能搭配你独一无二不同的小巧思，创造出各种超萌新吃法，让每天一早都有满满活力！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10577569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10577569" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/307eda3cb42ab49a2c119f5da264fc0e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10577569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/307eda3cb42ab49a2c119f5da264fc0e-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10577569" class="wp-caption-text">超萌的兔兔大军，陪你对战午后瞌睡虫！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【食材准备】</h2>
<p>酵母 2茶匙<br />
糖 2汤匙<br />
牛奶 1杯<br />
盐巴 1茶匙<br />
面粉 3杯<br />
奶油 2汤匙</p>
<p><span style="color: #003366;"><strong>看完整影片?</strong></span><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-91c06589-9cb7-4ab1-70de-8a5c03cb83c7" data-id="player-91c06589-9cb7-4ab1-70de-8a5c03cb83c7"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/91c06589-9cb7-4ab1-70de-8a5c03cb83c7?r=16x9&amp;s=720x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F20%2Fn10577556.md#1"></a></p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.加入酵母、糖、牛奶后，静待10分钟，让酵母醒过来。</p>
<p>2.倒入盐巴、面粉、奶油均匀搅拌，将容器中材料均匀混合。</p>
<p>3.将面团取出，使用双手“揉”5～10分钟；接着，放回容器中，盖上棉布，静待1小时，等面团发酵。</p>
<p>4.先将发酵完成的面团，撷取一小部分，稍后作为耳朵和尾巴使用；其余面团平均分成8等分，并揉成圆状。</p>
<p>5.取其中一个面团，黏上一对细长的兔耳朵，和圆圆的兔尾巴，再用藜麦作为兔子的眼睛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10577588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10577588" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/dd0128b8f578b590d97488f8923ee764.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10577588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/dd0128b8f578b590d97488f8923ee764-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10577588" class="wp-caption-text">黏上一对细长的兔耳朵，和圆圆的兔尾巴，再用藜麦作为兔子的眼睛。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.完成后，放上烤盘，在表面均匀涂上蛋液。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10577590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10577590" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/91ff94725a00eb039bf981067b5b7191.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10577590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/91ff94725a00eb039bf981067b5b7191-450x372.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="372" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10577590" class="wp-caption-text">放上烤盘，在表面均匀涂上蛋液。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.放进烤箱烘烤20分钟，温度约控制于摄氏190度／华氏375度。等烤箱跳起来，就完成啰！</p>
<p>接下来，可以发挥你无限的想像力，变化<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%94%E5%85%94%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">兔兔面包</a>的创新吃法呦！食材口味皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10577570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10577570" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/88b622a5be8dab6e7c981e5ae45fba5f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10577570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/88b622a5be8dab6e7c981e5ae45fba5f-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10577570" class="wp-caption-text">还可以发会你无限的想像力，变化<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%94%E5%85%94%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">兔兔面包</a>的创新吃法呦！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
