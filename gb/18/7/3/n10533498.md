<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>来块苹果甜甜圈 幸福滋味让你吮指回味</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月23日讯】遇到梦幻甜点，你是不是时常也毫无抵抗力，马上就心动被收买啦？这款居家假日甜食，使用新鲜水果食材，搭配香浓、甜甜酱料，让你在家也可以轻松独享健康又疗愈的好滋味。</p>
<p>家庭聚会、姐妹约会一同分享也可以很梦幻！甜蜜、幸福的好滋味，让你忍不住吮指回味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10533521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10533521" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/39f907320841df738d149c8113a4f531.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10533521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/39f907320841df738d149c8113a4f531-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10533521" class="wp-caption-text">打造下午茶梦幻首选甜点，使用新鲜水果让你吃得安心。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【食材准备】</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B9%E6%9E%9C.md#1">苹果</a> 3颗<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96.md#1">焦糖</a> 适量<br />
松饼粉 2杯<br />
水 1/2杯<br />
巧克力 适量<br />
炼乳 些许</p>

<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.先将<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B9%E6%9E%9C.md#1">苹果</a>切片，需要有点厚度，增添口感。</p>
<p>2.中间苹果心的部分，用圆形模具去除掉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10533522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10533522" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/0e741824f1c27e4cc5efdeef323bf0da.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10533522" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/0e741824f1c27e4cc5efdeef323bf0da-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10533522" class="wp-caption-text">先将苹果切片，再使用圆形模具，将苹果心的部分去除掉。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.苹果外圈均匀裹上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96.md#1">焦糖</a>，再依序放于烤盘上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10533524" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10533524" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/cc376c548d142d46a325609426aae27f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10533524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/cc376c548d142d46a325609426aae27f-450x333.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10533524" class="wp-caption-text">苹果外圈均匀裹上焦糖。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.接着，在容器中倒入2杯松饼粉、1/2杯水，搅拌均匀呈浓稠状。</p>
<p>5.将刚才裹上浓浓焦糖的苹果片，在裹粉中完全包覆后，即可下锅油炸。</p>
<p>6.油锅中，苹果片浮起来，呈现可口的金黄色，就可以捞起锅。</p>
<p>7.最后，单面均匀沾上香浓巧克力酱，再淋上甜蜜炼乳，就完成啰！</p>
<p><strong>食材皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10533528" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10533528" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/4d7f588b70e4150d22774d24ac775ce7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10533528" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/4d7f588b70e4150d22774d24ac775ce7-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10533528" class="wp-caption-text">最后淋上炼乳，即可享用啰！甜蜜滋味让你忍不住吮指回味。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
