<a href="https://dh9kqnflpbxz3.cloudfront.net/H5xyjRKiu"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月20日讯】（大纪元记者裴晏报导）炸鱼片是台湾人爱吃的家常料理，如果讲究一点，酥炸鱼片就变成糖醋鱼片或土魠鱼羹。一般的炸鱼片或鱼柳都是金黄色的外皮，不过影片里由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%94%90%E4%BA%BA%E4%B8%BB%E5%8E%A8.md#1">新唐人主厨</a>示范的是不一样的创意变化：“鲜绿版”的酥<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E9%B1%BC%E6%9F%B3.md#1">炸鱼柳</a>。只要加上一种“神秘”食材，就能做出口感和色泽独特的炸鱼柳！</p>

<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-77b23fb4-26cf-4bea-6fbc-628e3e11bce6" data-id="player-77b23fb4-26cf-4bea-6fbc-628e3e11bce6"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/77b23fb4-26cf-4bea-6fbc-628e3e11bce6?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F19%2Fn10575584.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<h3><strong>鲜绿酥炸石斑鱼柳</strong></h3>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></strong></p>
<p>红色石斑鱼 一条<br />
低筋面粉 200克<br />
太白粉 100克<br />
苏打粉 5克<br />
泡打粉 1克<br />
干燥海苔粉 10克<br />
速发干酵母 2克<br />
水 1.5杯<br />
食用油 适量<br />
盐 适量<br />
白楜椒 适量<br />
料酒 1大匙</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法：</span></strong></p>
<p>1. 石斑鱼去骨、去皮后切块状，并放入碗中腌渍备用。腌渍酱汁以盐、料酒和白楜椒调制。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10575602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575602" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet1.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575602" class="wp-caption-text">将鱼身去骨、去皮、切块。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10575605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575605" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet2.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet2.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575605" class="wp-caption-text">腌渍鱼块，使之入味，增加风味及肉质滑嫩度。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 调制炸粉浆：将低筋面粉、太白粉、苏打粉、泡打粉、干酵母、食用油和水混合，并加入海苔粉搅拌均匀备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10575606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575606" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet3.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575606" class="wp-caption-text">调制炸粉浆，并加入海台粉，添增最后成品的鲜味和翠绿色泽。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10575607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575607" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet4.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet4.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575607" class="wp-caption-text">将鱼块放入碗中裹上炸粉浆。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 起油锅至中低油温约100度左右，以防太快变焦黄。</p>
<p>4. 将鱼柳浸入炸粉浆，再逐一放入油锅，不要一次全下，防止粘黏成团。待鱼柳转金黄色，捞起沥干油分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10575608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575608" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet5.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet5.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575608" class="wp-caption-text">中低油温油炸，待鱼块转金黄色再捞起沥干油分。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 第二次下油锅，以中高温迅速过油，将鱼柳外表炸至酥脆，降低含油量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10575609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575609" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet6.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet6.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575609" class="wp-caption-text">第二次下油锅，以较高温迅速过油，以增加脆度及逼出油分。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 加了海苔粉的酥<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E9%B1%BC%E6%9F%B3.md#1">炸鱼柳</a>，翠绿的酥皮外衣配上粉白的滑嫩鱼肉，更显得美味可口！</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>主厨提醒：</strong></span></p>
<ol>
<li>鱼肉不能太湿，否则不易裹上粉浆。如果是解冻的鱼肉，水分多，可以用厨房纸巾吸干水分再浸入炸粉浆，或沾点太白粉再裹浆。</li>
</ol>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">2. 油温和火侯控制因各人厨具不同差异性大，因此可以采“目测法”：当粉浆滴入油锅中一半后还未沉到锅底时就浮起来，那就表示油温已足够开始炸鱼了。</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">3. 炸粉浆可因个人口感喜好而调整筋度，加入糯米粉或太白粉可降低筋度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10575610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575610" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet7.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet7.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575610" class="wp-caption-text">完成品。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10575611" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575611" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet-8.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575611" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fired-fillet-8.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575611" class="wp-caption-text">加入了海苔粉的炸鱼柳，外皮看起来翠绿可口，让已经习惯“金黄色”炸鱼柳的我们，眼睛立刻为之一亮！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
