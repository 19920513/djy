<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>刘香慈率《女兵》演员唱军歌 小娴抖擞亮相</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807050602252384-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>演员刘香慈（中）领军陈谦文（左起）、小娴、杨晴、罗平及全体演员穿着全套军装，唱军歌进场。（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月05日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）台剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E5%85%B5%E6%97%A5%E8%AE%B0.md#1">女兵日记</a>》5日在台北举办首映会，连长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E9%A6%99%E6%85%88.md#1">刘香慈</a>领军排长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%99%B4.md#1">杨晴</a>、罗平，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%A8%B4.md#1">小娴</a>、陈谦文穿着海军白甲式军常服出场，接着班长李宣榕带领全体班兵精神答数，整队交给刘香慈齐唱军歌，现场宛如阅兵典礼，相当有气势。</p>
<p>这次<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E9%A6%99%E6%85%88.md#1">刘香慈</a>从“最美士官长”升官变成“最帅气连长”，剧中面对论及婚嫁的男友，想进入家庭又不想要放弃军旅生活的她，在感情与事业的拉锯成为当前面临的一大难题。而<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%A8%B4.md#1">小娴</a>在经历婚变风波后，也精神抖擞的亮相记者会展现笑容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10539577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10539577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807050602062384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10539577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807050602062384-600x400.jpg" alt="" title="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10539577" class="wp-caption-text">演员刘香慈（左2）7月5日穿着海军白甲式军常服出席首映会，李宣榕（右1）带领全体班兵精神答数。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10539590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10539590" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807050602192384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10539590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807050602192384-600x400.jpg" alt="" title="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10539590" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）小娴、刘香慈、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%99%B4.md#1">杨晴</a>及全体演员穿着全套军装，唱军歌进场。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10539583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10539583" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807050601592384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10539583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807050601592384-600x400.jpg" alt="" title="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10539583" class="wp-caption-text">演员刘香慈（中）领军（左起）陈谦文、小娴、杨晴、罗平穿着海军服出场。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>众演员每天早上4、5点就要起床，6点开始接受严格的操练，在屏东的基地里面，一共待了将近4个月的时间训练。</p>
<p>TVBS首度自制八点档连续剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E5%85%B5%E6%97%A5%E8%AE%B0.md#1">女兵日记</a>》，剧情描述一群女生自愿从军，从最初互看不顺眼，经历无情的磨练、互相扶持，最后变成海军陆战队的心路历程。12分钟片花吸引2百万的点阅人数。</p>
