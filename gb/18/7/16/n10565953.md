<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>让专业的来！牛仔甩出套索帅气制伏公牛</h2>
<a href="https://d29mno3lcgt6u3.cloudfront.net/A6bqtBfTo"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10554314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月20日讯】一般的野生动物突然出现在街道上，总令人忍不住驻足想多看几眼。但当这只动物是具攻击性的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E7%89%9B.md#1">公牛</a>时，大家肯定会选择“三十六计”走为上策。毕竟一不小心可能成为公牛的目标，相当危险！</p>
<p>这件事情发生在休士顿，一只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E7%89%9B.md#1">公牛</a>突然出现在人群聚集的街道上，公牛脾气相当暴躁。这也让原本平静的街道，气氛突然变得很紧张……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10566430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10566430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/ab.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10566430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/ab-600x466.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="466" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10566430" class="wp-caption-text">一只公牛出现在人群聚集的街道上，公牛的暴冲让人心情不禁紧张了起来。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公牛奔跑的路径实在难以捉摸！唯一能够把握的原则只有一个，那就是“不要出现在公牛的前面”！蓝衣男子勇敢出面，希望能抚平公牛紧张的情绪，但事情似乎没这么容易。公牛看着眼前的男子，不但没平静下来，反而作势对他来个急速冲撞！</p>
<p>公牛继续冲到前面的停车场，这时它的速度渐渐慢下来，暂时休息片刻。看来人群对公牛来说也是种无形的压力！？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10566432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10566432" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/ac.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10566432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/ac-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10566432" class="wp-caption-text">身穿蓝衣的保全出面想安抚平公牛的紧张情绪，但事情可没这么容易！公牛不但没平静下来，反而作势要来个急速冲撞！（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>就在此刻，达达的马蹄声划破空气中的凝重氛围，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94.md#1">牛仔</a>一个个的相继出现。牛仔们的出现带给大家希望，人群不由自主地向公牛的方向移动脚步，想看<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%85%E6%B0%94.md#1">帅气</a>牛仔们“大显身手”一番。</p>
<p>一个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94.md#1">牛仔</a>骑在马上，与公牛来个面对面对决。在公牛还没反应过来的时候，他甩出手上的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%97%E7%B4%A2.md#1">套索</a>，套索正中公牛的头！牛仔一拉绳索，公牛就像是被施了魔法一般，立刻乖乖地跟着牛仔回家！</p>
<p>这个突如其来的奇特事件，真是令人大呼惊奇，也不得佩服牛仔的身手不凡！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10566440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10566440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/af.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10566440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/af-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10566440" class="wp-caption-text">牛仔一个个相继出现，带给人群希望。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10566445" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10566445" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/ag.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10566445" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/ag-600x437.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10566445" class="wp-caption-text">牛仔骑在马上，对着公牛甩出手上的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%97%E7%B4%A2.md#1">套索</a>，正中红心！一拉绳索，公牛竟像是被施了魔法一样，立刻乖乖跟着牛仔回家！（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
