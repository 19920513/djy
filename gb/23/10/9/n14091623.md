<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>驻休士顿办事处举办112年国庆酒会 各界共襄盛举</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091630-554135a2cad65a8558dc4b68fd786e4b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>萧伊芳处长发表国庆演说。
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年10月10日讯】（大纪元讯）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%BB%E4%BC%91%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8F%B0%E5%8C%97%E7%BB%8F%E6%B5%8E%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E5%8A%9E%E4%BA%8B%E5%A4%84.md#1">驻休士顿台北经济文化办事处</a>于10月6日午间在休士顿亚洲协会德州中心举办112年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%BA%86%E9%85%92%E4%BC%9A.md#1">国庆酒会</a>，计有500余名宾客出席。除美国国会联邦众议员Al Green(D-TX9)及Sylvia Garcia(D-TX29)亲自到场祝贺外，联邦参议员John Corny(R-TX)、Ted Cruz(R-TX)、联邦众议院外交委员会主席Michael McCaul(D-TX10)及众议员Dan Crenshaw(R-TX2)均派其选区主任代表出席；同时，美国国务院外国使领馆处(OFM)驻休士顿副主任Scott Bell，德州众议员Gene Wu(D-TX137)及Stan Kitzman(R-TX85)，奥克拉荷马州众议员Daniel Pae(R-OK62)，休士顿警察局局长Troy Finner及副局长Ban Tien，Stafford市市长Ken Matthew、美国驻帛琉前大使倪约翰(John Hennessey-Niland)及驻卡达前大使Chase Untermeyer也受邀出席参与盛会。美南地区侨、商、学各界人士同庆，充分展现对中华民国政府的高度支持与向心力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14091632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14091632" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091632-14a96770e55bcbd664f3a6448610107f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14091632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091632-14a96770e55bcbd664f3a6448610107f-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14091632" class="wp-caption-text">萧伊芳获联邦众议员Sylvia Garcia颁赠贺函。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14091633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14091633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091633-d1e5817f59311b450a360a40e1dcbf1a.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14091633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091633-d1e5817f59311b450a360a40e1dcbf1a-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14091633" class="wp-caption-text">萧伊芳处长获德州联邦众议员Al Green颁赠贺函。</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻休士顿经文处处长萧伊芳以“台湾-不可或缺之坚韧伙伴”为主轴发表演说，强调台湾在守护民主及人权、强化产业供应链及抵御强权扩张之努力及贡献，也阐述台美坚实合作关系之具体进展，并说明经文处近年来与美南地区官、产、学界各项丰硕之合作成果，在半导体产业及人才培育领域尤见进展，台湾将持续与美国携手共同维护台海、印太及全球之稳定、和平及繁荣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14091636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14091636" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091636-2e9a9c486c41dc51adf7babd08a058cc.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14091636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091636-2e9a9c486c41dc51adf7babd08a058cc-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14091636" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%BB%E4%BC%91%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8F%B0%E5%8C%97%E7%BB%8F%E6%B5%8E%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E5%8A%9E%E4%BA%8B%E5%A4%84.md#1">驻休士顿台北经济文化办事处</a>处长萧伊芳与贵宾合影。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14091635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14091635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091635-61e2391d16d9854db384639b6f99ac0d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14091635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091635-61e2391d16d9854db384639b6f99ac0d-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14091635" class="wp-caption-text">萧伊芳处长与德州众议员Gene Wu及Stan Kitzman合影。</figcaption></figure>
<p>联邦众议员Al Green致词时忆及前访台美好经验，转述拜登总统亲口承诺对台湾之支持，诚盼台美关系更上层楼；联邦众议员Sylvia Garcia则呼应G众议员，矢言将在国会持续支持各项友台法案；另德州州众议员Gene Wu及Stan Kitzman则盛赞台湾侨商界对德州及美国之卓越贡献，并祝贺国庆。本次国庆亦获美南各州政府及议会、联邦参、众议员以贺函或贺状表达祝贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14091634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14091634" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091634-121ffc9682d57947cbee6de9f4e99db8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14091634" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091634-121ffc9682d57947cbee6de9f4e99db8-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14091634" class="wp-caption-text">萧伊芳处长与奥克拉荷马州众议员Daniel Pae合影。</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次驻休士顿经文处在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%BA%86%E9%85%92%E4%BC%9A.md#1">国庆酒会</a>现场以巨幅LED电视墙播放国庆文宣影片“众志成城、台湾向前”、国情影片“台湾良善力量”、国庆主视觉及活动背版图像，增进与会政要及侨民对中华民国政、经发展现况及参与国际组织等诉求，宣传台湾在外交与全球性议题上之努力及贡献。会场布置方面，除制作台湾轮廓搭配国旗颜色之气球摆设，亦获休士顿知名壁画艺术家GONZO247赠予巨幅画作祝贺国庆，深获在场来宾好评并纷纷与萧处长合影留念，酒会现场宾主尽欢，气氛融洽。</p>
