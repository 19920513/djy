<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：独自乘列车冒险 拉布拉多犬安全回家</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14086597-Storm-the-golden-Labrador-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff

<h6>拉布拉多犬斯托姆（Storm）登上火车，被火车站工作人员接走并与主人团聚。（Metro Trains Melbourne提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年10月02日讯】（英文大纪元记者Louise Chambers报导／王万舍编译）一条<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%89%E5%B8%83%E6%8B%89%E5%A4%9A%E7%8A%AC.md#1">拉布拉多犬</a>（Labrador）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E8%87%AA.md#1">独自</a>搭上通勤列车，小狗因其<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%92%E9%99%A9.md#1">冒险</a>行为而在网上走红。小狗乘坐列车大约八站后，它的通勤之旅终于有了一个圆满的结局。这只勇敢的小狗被火车站工作人员接走并与主人团聚。</p>
<p>早上8点20分，名叫斯托姆（Storm）的金色<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%89%E5%B8%83%E6%8B%89%E5%A4%9A%E7%8A%AC.md#1">拉布拉多犬</a>在澳大利亚墨尔本的Hoppers Crossing车站在无人陪伴的情况下<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E8%87%AA.md#1">独自</a>登上了一列郊区通勤列车。火车开往Flinders Street车站。监控摄像头拍到这只小狗摇着尾巴，耐心地站在黄线后面，等待火车门打开。</p>
<p>小狗快速巡视了车厢，然后在火车开动前几秒跳上火车。</p>
<figure style="width: 584px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436089-Storm-Labrador-4.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436089-Storm-Labrador-4.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="584" b="485" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">金色拉布拉多犬斯托姆登上火车的视频截图。（Metro Trains Melbourne提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 594px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436071-Storm-Labrador-5.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436071-Storm-Labrador-5.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="594" b="424" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">金色拉布拉多犬斯托姆在Flinders Street火车站被人捡到。（Metro Trains Melbourne提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>然而，一路上有人干预斯托姆的单独<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%92%E9%99%A9.md#1">冒险</a>，小狗在到达Flinders Street车站后，另一个安全摄像头拍到这只小狗走出火车后，被维多利亚公共交通（PTV）的工作人员抓住了它的项圈。另一名工作人员用皮带拴住了它。</p>
<p>墨尔本地铁公司（Metro Trains Melbourne）与“北墨尔本丢失狗狗之家”（North Melbourne&#8217;s Lost Dogs Home）合作，终于将斯托姆归还给它的主人。“令人兴奋的消息是，我们能够让斯托姆与它的主人团聚，小狗主人从悉尼远道赶来！”他们在7月24日分享的视频中跟帖表示。他们通过斯托姆身上的芯片找到了它的主人。</p>
<figure style="width: 588px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436033-Storm-1.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436033-Storm-1.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="588" b="441" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Metro Trains Melbourne提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 587px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436093-Storm-5.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436093-Storm-5.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="587" b="783" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">工作人员轻轻拍抚斯托姆。（Metro Trains Melbourne提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“如果这只狗跳上你的火车，那将是最棒的一天！”一位网友评论道。另一位网友写道：“它只是需要一次属于自己的冒险！”</p>
<p>斯托姆的冒险并没有随着它从火车上被护送走而结束。这只幸运的小狗被带到了Flinders Street车站的背后，在那里它受到了所遇到的所有人的拍抚。在被接走之前，它还享受地在城市的南岸散步。斯托姆感激不已的主人后来分享了它在家乡公园里享受球赛的照片。</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436036-Storm-4.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436036-Storm-4.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Metro Trains Melbourne提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 532px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436059-Storm-Labrador-1.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/01/id5436059-Storm-Labrador-1.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="532" b="483" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">斯托姆回家了。（Metro Trains Melbourne提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宠物在墨尔本火车上无人陪伴的情况并不常见。但只要主人遵守规则，宠物就可以出来溜达：小型宠物必须使用合适的运输工具出行，而狗则必须拴上皮带，并远离过道、出口和座椅。</p>
