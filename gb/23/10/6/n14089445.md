<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>德国国王湖  初秋的梦幻之地</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089446-639617-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>国王湖的湖水呈现翡翠绿色，清澈见底，是著名的旅游胜地。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月06日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾报导）2023年10月04日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E5%9B%BD.md#1">德国</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%8E%8B%E6%B9%96.md#1">国王湖</a>（K?nigssee）再次展现其壮丽的美景，初秋的色彩让这片湖泊更加动人。坐落于阿尔卑斯山区，靠近<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A5%E5%9C%B0%E5%88%A9.md#1">奥地利</a>边境的这个湖泊以其翡翠绿的湖水和清澈见底而闻名于世，被誉为德国最美丽的湖泊之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14089447" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14089447" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089447-639618.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14089447" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089447-639618-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14089447" class="wp-caption-text">早晨云雾缥缈的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%8E%8B%E6%B9%96.md#1">国王湖</a>，梦幻～（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14089448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14089448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089448-639619.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14089448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089448-639619-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14089448" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E5%9B%BD.md#1">德国</a>国王湖位于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A5%E5%9C%B0%E5%88%A9.md#1">奥地利</a>边境，周围为壮丽的阿尔卑斯山山脉。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国王湖被壮观的阿尔卑斯山脉环绕，呈现出如梦如幻的景色。湖畔还拥有一座建于12世纪的圣巴尔多禄茂教堂，它的酒红色外观犹如一座洋葱教堂，使人仿佛穿越时空回到中世纪。当你在湖上航行时，船夫会拿出小喇叭演奏，这里吹出的音乐会在湖泊中回响，带来非常有趣的声音效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14089452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14089452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089452-639625.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14089452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089452-639625-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14089452" class="wp-caption-text">群山环绕的国王湖。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14089451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14089451" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089451-639622.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14089451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089451-639622-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14089451" class="wp-caption-text">酒红色系圆圆尖尖的，外观像是洋葱教堂。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这已经是笔者第二次造访国王湖，首次是在2015年，当时是搭乘巴士从萨尔兹堡前来。这一次，笔者带着家人重温了这个美丽的地方，而现在自驾更加方便。</p>
<p>国王湖的湖水清澈见底，翡翠绿的色泽令人陶醉，让它成为一个受游客喜爱的旅游胜地。早晨时分，湖泊被轻轻的云雾包围，更加梦幻迷人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14089449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14089449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089449-639620.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14089449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089449-639620-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14089449" class="wp-caption-text">湖水呈现翡翠绿色，清澈见底。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国王湖航船旅行的一个特色是，当船只行驶到湖中的一半，即距离圣巴尔多禄茂教堂一半航程的位置时，船长会停下船，吹响号角，湖面上会回响出多重的回声，为游客带来一个难忘的音乐表演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14089450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14089450" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089450-639621.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14089450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089450-639621-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14089450" class="wp-caption-text">走一趟国王湖，仿佛穿越时空隧道回到中世纪。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国王湖自1909年起实行了严格的保护措施，只允许电动船、手划船和脚踏船在湖上航行，以确保湖泊的水质保持纯净。</p>
