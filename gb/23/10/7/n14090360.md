<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>来胜利星村漫游 逛台湾最大眷村改建的文创市集</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090364-639859-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>游客可以来寻宝，看到底有几个不同造型的偶。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月07日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>报导）台湾最大的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%B7%E6%9D%91%E6%94%B9%E5%BB%BA.md#1">眷村改建</a>文创聚落——“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%9C%E5%88%A9%E6%98%9F%E6%9D%91.md#1">胜利星村</a>创意生活园区”，拥有69栋历经日治时期保留下来的眷舍。这个地方见证了传统的日式建筑和现代文创艺术的完美融合，转化为一个充满特色的眷村聚落，也储藏着无数的故事与力量。</p>
<figure id="14090365" aria-describedby="caption-14090365" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090365-639860-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090365-639860-450x338.jpg" alt="室内也很有特色。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14090365" class="wp-caption-text">室内也很有特色。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14090363" aria-describedby="caption-14090363" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090363-639858-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090363-639858-450x338.jpg" alt="拥有台湾特色的店家。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14090363" class="wp-caption-text">拥有台湾特色的店家。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14090362" aria-describedby="caption-14090362" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090362-639857-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090362-639857-450x338.jpg" alt="保有日本风味。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14090362" class="wp-caption-text">保有日本风味。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>目前，这个园区汇集了住宿、餐饮、独立书店、视觉艺术、时尚设计、音乐和表演艺术等多个特色店家。无论是白天还是晚上，这里都充满了生气，非常适合规划半日游或一日游行程，来细味眷村内的故事和日常生活。</p>
<p>８日下午三点将有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>县社会处举办的“112年屏东县新住民创意市集”活动登场，民众可以来感受一下眷舍改建后的独特风貌，欣赏历史与现代的交融。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14090361" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14090361" style="width: 439px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090361-639856-300x225.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14090361" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090361-639856-300x225.jpg" alt="" width="439" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14090361" class="wp-caption-text">张晓风的故居。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
