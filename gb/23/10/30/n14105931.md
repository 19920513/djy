<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>乐见浊水溪改善沙尘 居民生活品质提升</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105933-644219-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>云林西螺居民乐见河川局在浊水溪持续推进“扬尘计划”，特别针对南端西侧溪床进行改善工作。（魏进福提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月30日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%9E%BA.md#1">西螺</a>居民乐见河川局在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%8A%E6%B0%B4%E6%BA%AA.md#1">浊水溪</a>持续推进“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%AC%E5%B0%98.md#1">扬尘</a>计划”，特别针对南端西侧溪床进行改善工作。这项计划包括在溪床上铺设稻草席和撒播多种草籽，借助成长后的芒花群遮挡，降低市区沙尘漫天飞舞的情况。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14105935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105935-644221.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14105935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105935-644221-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105935" class="wp-caption-text">这项计划包括在溪床上铺设稻草席。（魏进福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14105934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105934-644220.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14105934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105934-644220-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105934" class="wp-caption-text">撒播多种草籽，借助成长后的芒花群遮挡，降低市区沙尘漫天飞舞的情况。（魏进福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14105936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105936" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105936-644222.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14105936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105936-644222-600x275.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105936" class="wp-caption-text">改善工作正在进行中，居民的生活品质也正在逐渐改善，（魏进福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曾经，每逢秋季东北季风来袭时，整个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%9E%BA.md#1">西螺</a>小镇上空的风沙滚滚，即使门窗紧紧关闭，强风仍能把沙尘带进屋内，让家具和桌椅都沾满了厚厚的细沙子。烹饪和用餐时，沙尘也成了一种不受欢迎的调味料，居民长期忍受风沙之苦，感到困扰不已。</p>
<p>长期以来，居民在秋冬季节不得不忍受沙尘侵袭，而每当选举时，候选人总是承诺要解决<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%8A%E6%B0%B4%E6%BA%AA.md#1">浊水溪</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%AC%E5%B0%98.md#1">扬尘</a>问题，但当当选后，这个承诺经常被遗忘。因此，小镇居民一直是这个问题的受害者。</p>
<p>近年来河川局重视扬尘问题，改善工作正在进行中，居民的生活品质也正在逐渐改善，“沙尘舞季风”和“吃饭拌沙”以后可能成了老年人的回忆。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14105932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105932-644218.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14105932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105932-644218-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105932" class="wp-caption-text">乐见浊水溪改善沙尘，居民生活品质提升。（魏进福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14105937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105937" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105937-644223.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14105937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105937-644223-600x287.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105937" class="wp-caption-text">曾经以前东北季风一来，风沙滚滚，即使门窗紧紧关闭，强风仍能把沙尘带进屋内。（魏进福提供）</figcaption></figure>
