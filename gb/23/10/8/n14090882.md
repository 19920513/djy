<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国庆表演抢先看“翡翠骑士”全台首演在高雄</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090884-639977-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>翡翠骑士整齐划一又千变万化的华丽队形编制，以及活力十足的笑容，获得现场如雷掌声。（高市府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月08日讯】（大纪元记者方金媛台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%9B%84.md#1">高雄</a>报导）“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BF%A1%E7%BF%A0%E9%AA%91%E5%A3%AB.md#1">翡翠骑士</a>”日本东京农业大学第二高等学校吹奏乐部今年首度受邀来台，将在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%BA%86.md#1">国庆</a>大会演出，全台<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%96%E6%BC%94.md#1">首演</a>就献给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%9B%84.md#1">高雄</a>！10月8日下午高雄港七号码头涌入众多人潮，抢先观赏这场精彩的演出，并报以最热烈的掌声。翡翠骑士也史无前例加码两首“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%8F%AF%E6%9B%B2.md#1">安可曲</a>”，感谢现场观众的热情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14090883" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14090883" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090883-639976.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14090883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090883-639976-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14090883" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BF%A1%E7%BF%A0%E9%AA%91%E5%A3%AB.md#1">翡翠骑士</a>整齐划一又千变万化的华丽队形编制，以及活力十足的笑容，获得现场如雷掌声。（高市府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14090885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14090885" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090885-639978.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14090885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090885-639978-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14090885" class="wp-caption-text">翡翠骑士整齐划一又千变万化的华丽队形编制，以及活力十足的笑容，获得现场如雷掌声。（高市府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高雄市长陈其迈表示，诚挚欢迎翡翠骑士来到台湾，也感谢他们第一场演出就在高雄，热情精湛的表演让台湾民众深受感染、非常感动。希望翡翠骑士与高雄有个美好回忆，也欢迎他们日后再回到高雄相聚！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14090886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14090886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090886-639979.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14090886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090886-639979-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14090886" class="wp-caption-text">翡翠骑士整齐划一又千变万化的华丽队形编制，以及活力十足的笑容，获得现场如雷掌声。（高市府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>翡翠骑士在高雄的全台<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%96%E6%BC%94.md#1">首演</a>，148人的盛大编制一出场就吸引全场欢声雷动，久候多时的观众纷纷拿起相机捕捉珍贵的画面。翡翠骑士活力灿烂的笑容、整齐划一又千变万化的华丽队形编制，令观众目不暇给。此外，也带来多首耳熟能详的动画歌曲，让现场观众感到熟悉又亲切，安可声不断！翡翠骑士感受到民众的热情，大方加码献出乐团T-square歌曲“宝岛”和原预计在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%BA%86.md#1">国庆</a>大会演出的“动漫组”两首<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%8F%AF%E6%9B%B2.md#1">安可曲</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14090889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14090889" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090889-639982.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14090889" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090889-639982-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14090889" class="wp-caption-text">“翡翠骑士”全台首演10月8日下午在高雄港七号码头登场（高市府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动现场也有农二高学生家长专程南下见证翡翠骑士来台首场演出，学生家长看到孩子可以到台湾表演感到相当荣幸，对于在IG上看到许多预告翡翠骑士访高演出的宣传影片，也感到惊喜并感受市府的用心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14090887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14090887" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090887-639980.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14090887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090887-639980-600x445.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14090887" class="wp-caption-text">高雄女中仪队参与现场演出（方金媛／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14090888" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14090888" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090888-639981.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14090888" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090888-639981-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14090888" class="wp-caption-text">树德家商表演艺术科参与现场演出（高市府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次“翡翠骑士前进大港”国庆抢先看，高市府也邀请高雄女中仪队、高雄高商乐旗队、树德家商表演艺术科，以及啦啦队世界锦标赛台湾代表队参与现场演出，让台日青年世代透过音乐建立友谊、深化交流。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14090890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14090890" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090890-639983.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14090890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14090890-639983-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14090890" class="wp-caption-text">现场观众纷纷拿起相机，捕捉“翡翠骑士”珍贵的画面。（方金媛／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
