<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>SHINee以出道15年纪录片共享回忆 11月上映</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094545-231013051311100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国人气男团SHINee出道15年推出首部专属纪录片《My SHINee World》。（车库娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年10月13日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）韩国人气男团SHINee将推出出道15年首部专属纪录片《My SHINee World》，该纪录片记录了SHINee的第六次演唱会“SHINee WORLD VI [PERFECT ILLUMINATION]”以及第五次演唱会“SHINee WORLD”的内容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14094537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094537-231013032947100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094537" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094537-231013032947100707-600x324.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094537" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团SHINee出道15年推出首部专属纪录片《My SHINee World》。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2008年以话题曲《Replay》出道的SHINee，之后接连以《Juliette》《Ring Ding Dong》《Sherlock (Clue + Note)》《View》《1 of 1》《Don’t Call Me》，以及2023年的《HARD》等热门歌曲成为高人气的偶像团体。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14094538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094538" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094538-231013032950100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094538" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094538-231013032950100707-600x324.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094538" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团SHINee出道15年推出首部专属纪录片《My SHINee World》。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14094542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094542" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094542-231013032959100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094542" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094542-231013032959100707-600x324.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094542" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团SHINee出道15年推出首部专属纪录片《My SHINee World》。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纪录片《My SHINee World》中除了可看到SHINee于2008年出道时的模样，也可一睹SHINee成员一同回忆过去，还收录了只有本作才看得到的真心话访谈，他们将与粉丝分享令人难忘的回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14094540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094540" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094540-231013032954100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094540" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094540-231013032954100707-600x324.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094540" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团SHINee出道15年推出首部专属纪录片《My SHINee World》。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《My SHINee World》采用了杜比全景声技术。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14094541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094541" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094541-231013032952100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094541" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094541-231013032952100707-600x324.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094541" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团SHINee出道15年推出首部专属纪录片《My SHINee World》。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14094547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094547" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094547-231013032945100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094547" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094547-231013032945100707-600x324.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094547" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团SHINee出道15年推出首部专属纪录片《My SHINee World》。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而从2010年7月发售的第二张正规专辑主打歌《Lucifer》，到今年6月发售的第八张正规专辑主打歌《HARD》等舞台，观众都能从大银幕再次感受到SHINee和SHINee World（官方粉丝名）的热情能量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14094543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094543" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094543-231013033004100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094543" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094543-231013033004100707-600x324.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094543" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团SHINee出道15年推出首部专属纪录片《My SHINee World》。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14094518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094518" style="width: 420px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094518-231013032957100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094518" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094518-231013032957100707.jpg" alt="" width="420" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094518" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团SHINee出道15年推出首部专属纪录片《My SHINee World》。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《My SHINee World》预计11月在台湾上映。</p>
