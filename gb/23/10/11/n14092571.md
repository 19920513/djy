<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>专家分享料理秘笈 普罗旺斯烤羊排外酥内嫩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096930-P.191-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>外酥内嫩的普罗旺斯烤羊排。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong><strong>：</strong>如何做出外表金黄酥脆、肉质软嫩多汁的普罗旺斯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E7%BE%8A%E6%8E%92.md#1">烤羊排</a>？日本专家分享了四大料理诀窍，包括<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E6%9D%90%E6%8C%91%E9%80%89.md#1">食材挑选</a>、食材处理、羊肉排保汁要领、温度掌控。待羊排腌渍好后，放入烤箱不到<strong>12</strong>分钟，香嫩且口感细致的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E7%BE%8A%E6%8E%92.md#1">烤羊排</a>便可上桌。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%BD%97%E6%97%BA%E6%96%AF%E7%83%A4%E7%BE%8A%E6%8E%92.md#1">普罗旺斯烤羊排</a></strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>材料（</strong><strong>1</strong><strong>片）</strong></span></h3>
<p>羊肋排 2根</p>
<p>香草面包粉 适量</p>
<p>盐、粗粒黑胡椒 各少许</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>＊香草面包粉（容易制作的分量）</strong></p>
<p>面包粉?200g<br />
罗勒?30g<br />
干燥巴西利 10g<br />
蒜头（磨泥）1瓣<br />
橄榄油 60ml<br />
盐 40g<br />
黑胡椒粒 20g<br />
起司粉 20g</p>
<p>注：<strong>*</strong><strong>羊里肌肉：品质高级的背部肌肉</strong></p>
<p>相当软嫩的瘦肉，在羊肉当中属于比较高价的部位。背骨与肋骨相连的带骨里肌肉，称为“Long Loin”（长条肋排）。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>前置处理</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.羊肋排是由长条肋排分切而成的，先仔细削除最上面脂肪比较硬的部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14096931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14096931" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096931-P.191-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14096931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096931-P.191-2-450x348.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14096931" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将脂肪部分朝下，在肋骨与肋骨之间下刀切开，如此即可轻松分切。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14096932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14096932" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096932-P.191-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14096932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096932-P.191-3-450x346.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14096932" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.先用菜刀的刀尖切断筋膜，若没有将脂肪和筋膜断开，烤的时候肉会收缩变小。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14096933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14096933" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096933-P.191-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14096933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096933-P.191-4-450x346.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14096933" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.接着在肋排上撒满盐和黑胡椒粒，作为预先调味。在稍微高的位置上方撒，才能够均匀沾上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14096934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14096934" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096934-P.191-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14096934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096934-P.191-5-450x350.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14096934" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.用食物调理机将香草面包粉的材料磨细，均匀沾裹在肉上面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14096935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14096935" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096935-P.191-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14096935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096935-P.191-6-450x347.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14096935" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>正式烹调</strong></span></h3>
<p>6. 在烤盘上架好烤网，将步骤5放在烤网上，放入以215℃预热的烤箱里，烘烤9～12分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14096936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14096936" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096936-P.191-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14096936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14096936-P.191-7-450x348.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14096936" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
