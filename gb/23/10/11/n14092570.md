<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>高手授招！没有羊骚味的“成吉思汗烤羊肉”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092875-P.187-8-572x400.jpg" />
<h6>先冷冻处理是切羊舌的秘诀。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>想品尝肉质软嫩的北海道“成吉思汗烤<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%82%89.md#1">羊肉</a>”，羊肉的“前置处理”是关键。东京“成吉思汗<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>店FUJIYA”的主厨，北海道出身，拥有出神入化的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%80%E5%B7%A5.md#1">刀工</a>，根据羊肉各部位的特征，传授切法密技，去除羊骚味，并让肉质口感变软嫩。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%82%89.md#1">羊肉</a>的处理方式&amp;</strong><strong>切法</strong></span></h2>
<p><strong>北海道最有名的成吉思汗<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>，如何处理一大块羊肉？</strong></p>
<p>重量超过2公斤的<strong><span style="color: #800000;">羊肩肉</span></strong>，这个部位<strong><span style="color: #800000;">有很多脂肪和筋</span></strong>，想要吃出美味，必须先做好前置处理。这里特别请到东京中目黑的人气名店“成吉思汗FUJIYA”店长东藤先生亲自示范，以高超的刀法传授实际处理羊肉块的方法。<strong><span style="color: #800000;">秘诀是先仔细地去除筋等比较硬的部分，再顺着粗大的筋和纤维分切，则可分开各部位。</span></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092850" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092850-P.184-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092850-P.184-1-450x295.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="295" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092850" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>如何处理羊肩肉块</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14092853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092853" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092853-P.185-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092853-P.185-1-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092853" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1.摊开羊肩肉块，顺着筋切成三等分。这是北海道成吉思汗烤肉最常使用的部位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092854" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092854-P.185-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092854-P.185-2-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092854" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.一边往肩里肌部分的侧边下刀，一边分开肉块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092855" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092855-P.185-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092855-P.185-3-450x444.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092855" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.分开到一定程度后，用菜刀切掉肩里肌肉筋膜相连的部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092856" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092856-P.185-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092856-P.185-4-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092856" class="wp-caption-text">烧烤前，先切断筋膜，才能烤出软嫩肉质。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.在羊排肉平坦部分的筋膜处下刀，把筋膜切除。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092857" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092857-P.185-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092857-P.185-5-450x447.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092857" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.切到一定的程度后，用菜刀断开五花肉筋膜相连的部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092858" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092858-P.185-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092858-P.185-6-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092858" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.<strong><span style="color: #800000;">将多余的脂肪和筋膜清除掉，确实处理干净后，就能去除羊骚味。</span></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092859" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092859-P.185-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092859-P.185-7-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092859" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.对于附着在瘦肉等其他部位的筋膜部分，也以相同的方式，用菜刀切除。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092860" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092860-P.185-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092860-P.185-8-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092860" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.全部都处理完成后，最后以片刀的方式切除带筋的部分。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>让肉质更软嫩的基本羊肉切法</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14092864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092864" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092864-P.186-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092864-P.186-4-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092864" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下刀时，<strong><span style="color: #800000;">以斜角度切片，会和肉的纤维呈垂直方向</span></strong>，当纤维被切断后，肉质会变软嫩。此外，斜切薄片可增加肉的表面积，煎烤出的口感更佳。</p>
<p><strong>配合各部位特征的切法让肉质变软嫩好吃！</strong></p>
<p>肩里肌肉和瘦肉是筋比较少的部位，因此只需直接以斜角度切片即可。</p>
<p>而肉质扎实、脂肪和筋比较多的肉排和五花肉，则需要在切工上多花点工夫。肉排如果直接煎的话，肉质会变硬，因此要先用松肉器等调理器具拍断筋膜之后，再以斜角度切片。至于五花肉，也是在分切成容易入口的大小之后，再用菜刀切断肉片上的筋膜。</p>
<p>羊舌因为很柔软、不易分切，最好先冷冻后再切。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>各部位的切法</strong></span></h2>
<p><strong>羊排肉</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092867" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092867-P.187-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092867" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092867-P.187-1-450x347.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092867" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1.由于有很多筋膜，因此先用松肉器（一种敲打肉的工具）等调理器具，一边按压表面、一边切断筋膜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092868" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092868-P.187-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092868-P.187-2-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092868" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.两面都切断筋膜之后，以斜角度下刀切片。如此一来，吃起来就会有软嫩的口感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092869" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092869-P.187-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092869-P.187-3-450x324.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092869" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>羊五花肉</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092866" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092866-P.186-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092866" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092866-P.186-6-450x324.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092866" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1.五花肉要顺着筋膜分切，切成适合用于烤肉的大小。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092872" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092872-P.187-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092872-P.187-5-450x323.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092872" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.用菜刀细切以便切断筋膜，如此即可变成软嫩的口感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092873" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092873" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092873-P.187-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14092873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092873-P.187-6-450x321.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092873" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>羊舌</strong></p>

<p>用菜刀从边缘开始，将冷冻过的羊舌切成薄片。冷冻的状态比较好切。</p>
