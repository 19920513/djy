<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【全植物蔬食料理】天贝全植碗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14106631-e3b540d82b149160b60939acafab0d93-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>天贝全植碗。（台湾东贩提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>手把手带您进入全植物饮食的世界，享受全新食感的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E9%A3%9F%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">蔬食料理</a>乐趣，多样化的当季食材搭配，藉由舒心零负担的全植料理让身体吸收全方位营养，作者多年秘笈无私分享，让全<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9%E8%94%AC%E9%A3%9F.md#1">植物蔬食</a>料理制作更加轻松惬意！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E8%B4%9D.md#1">天贝</a>全植碗</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>食材</strong></span></h3>
<p>A<br />
?芦笋?6根<br />
?红薯?1/2个<br />
?荷兰豆?20g<br />
?松子?5g<br />
?南瓜子?少许<br />
?奇亚籽?少许<br />
?植物油?5?</p>
<p>B<br />
?<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E8%B4%9D.md#1">天贝</a>?30g<br />
?红椒粉?5g<br />
?古法酱油?5?<br />
?橄榄油?10?<br />
?枫糖浆?5?</p>
<p>C<br />
?小番茄?6颗<br />
?柠檬汁?15?<br />
?<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8B%92.md#1">罗勒</a>?10g<br />
?黑胡椒?1g</p>
<figure id="attachment_14106630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14106630" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14106630-3b43f8e870768faf5301cddb91a8f621.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14106630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14106630-3b43f8e870768faf5301cddb91a8f621-450x154.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="154" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14106630" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>作法</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.芦笋取最嫩的部分，平底锅中刷一层油，将处理好的芦笋、荷兰豆煎熟。红薯蒸熟，切块备用。</p>
<p>2.将天贝切成薄片，食材B的调料调匀，将天贝浸在调料中，冷藏腌渍入味后煎至两面微微焦脆。</p>
<p>3.将小番茄切丁，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8B%92.md#1">罗勒</a>洗净后沥干、切碎，与柠檬汁、黑胡椒混合均匀，倒入密封罐中，冷藏一两个小时，做成酱汁。</p>
<p>4.将食材A和天贝组合摆盘，倒入酱汁。</p>
