<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2023第二届市长杯国际时尚创意竞赛展演秀</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098006-641975-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023第二届市长杯国际时尚创意竞赛展演秀，嘉义市长黄敏惠出席盛会，期盼藉盛大赛事将台湾美容美发推向国际舞台。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月18日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市女子美容商业同业公会主办的“2023璀灿嘉艺.玩美起飞第二届市长杯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">国际时尚</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9B%E6%84%8F%E7%AB%9E%E8%B5%9B.md#1">创意竞赛</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%95%E6%BC%94%E7%A7%80.md#1">展演秀</a>”于今（18）日在嘉义市港坪运动公园体育馆举行，汇聚了来自韩国sea东南亚细亚美容协会、Savages Barber野人团队、业界专业人士及全国各大高中职大专院校师生等参与盛会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098008" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098008-641976-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14098008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098008-641976-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098008" class="wp-caption-text">韩国sea东南亚细亚美容协会合照。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次竞赛共有200名参赛者报名，评审阵容包括资深业者、教育界名师以及专业技术者，嘉义市长黄敏惠出席盛会，期盼藉盛大赛事将台湾美容美发推向国际舞台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098010" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098010-641978-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14098010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098010-641978-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098010" class="wp-caption-text">市长杯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">国际时尚</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9B%E6%84%8F%E7%AB%9E%E8%B5%9B.md#1">创意竞赛</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%95%E6%BC%94%E7%A7%80.md#1">展演秀</a>。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄敏惠市长表示，嘉义市是一个追求美学的城市，重视美学教育并向下扎根，结合在地人文、艺术文化一起带动嘉义成长，感谢嘉义市女子美容商业同业公会理事长黄玉珍与其带领的理监事们及所有参与团队，“璀灿嘉艺.玩美起飞”市长杯今年已迈入第二年，本次活动有泰北、马来西亚、韩国、大陆等多国美容美发业者齐聚一堂，可以看见国际业者对时尚与美的精益求精。期许赛事能创造一个互相交流的平台，把国际舞台带来嘉义，海内外业者彼此互相观摩，激荡出全新火花，拓展嘉义展望未来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098009" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098009-641977-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14098009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098009-641977-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098009" class="wp-caption-text">市长杯国际时尚创意竞赛展演秀。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次活动秋冬展演秀也是一大亮点，包含韩国sea东南亚细亚美容协会的宫廷秀展演、弘光科技大学美发造型设计系吕佩芸老师领军的师生团队，以及Savages Barber野人团队带来的精彩演出，为美容美发业者带来灵感，推动嘉义美学展望国际舞台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098011" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098011-641979-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14098011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098011-641979-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098011" class="wp-caption-text">合照。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14098012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098012" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098012-641980-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14098012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098012-641980-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098012" class="wp-caption-text">合照。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
