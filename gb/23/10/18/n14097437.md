<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>阿兵哥感恩办酬宾烤肉  众宾客反响热烈欢畅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097445-IMG_1036-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>众人陆续到达会场。（吕尚明/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年10月18日讯】（大纪元记者吕尚明亚特兰大报导）多年来，约翰溪市（Johns Creek）的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%85%B5%E5%93%A5.md#1">阿兵哥</a>餐馆(DYNASTY? RESTAURANT)，是亚特兰大北郊华人喜爱的道地台湾风味中餐馆。和善的老板陈中兴（外号“小兵”）与老板娘杜娟，秉着和气生财的原则也成为大家的好朋友。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14097447" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14097447" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097447-IMG_1040-e1697587858447.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14097447" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097447-IMG_1040-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14097447" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>香气四溢。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14097446" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14097446" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097446-IMG_1044-e1697587733325.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14097446" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097446-IMG_1044-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14097446" class="wp-caption-text">主持人台商会前会长张淑芬女士，与大厨小兵。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>然而，三年前疫情袭卷全球，许多餐饮业受到重创。小兵与杜娟集思广益后，开创网路群组，借着LINE等社交软体每周广传特餐讯息。在旧雨新知的顾客极力相挺之下，营业完全不受疫情影响。为了对宾客们表达感谢，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%85%B5%E5%93%A5.md#1">阿兵哥</a>餐馆于10月15日(星期日)下午在JONES BRIDGE PARK公园举办了筹谢宾客户外<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>及美食餐会。通知发出后，主顾们反响热烈，在短短几天之内的报名就达到200人的上限。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14097448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14097448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097448-IMG_1056-e1697587975389.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14097448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097448-IMG_1056-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14097448" class="wp-caption-text">热腾腾的盐酥鸡来了。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大厨小兵大展身手，指挥着热心帮忙的志工，餐会中端出了令人眼花撩乱的十多样佳肴：牛排、羊肉片、五花肉的烤肉，小兵的自制台湾香肠，还有盐酥鸡、卤味鸭翅、大肠肉羹面线、凉拌海蜇头、五香茶叶蛋、自制韩国泡菜、夫妻肺片、台式油饭、落地丁香、牛肉绿豆粉丝、凉拌冬粉、五香卤花生、炸鸡翅、凉拌黄瓜、台式锉冰，以及自制古早味冬瓜茶为饮料，真是丰盛到了眼睛看着想吃但是肚皮撑不下的盛况。当天餐会的情景可于以下连结观赏：https://ganjing.one/U8h0sS</p>
<figure id="attachment_14097454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14097454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097454-IMG_1061-e1697588480434.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14097454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097454-IMG_1061-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14097454" class="wp-caption-text">众人大快朵颐。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14097453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14097453" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097453-IMG_1065-2-e1697588345162.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14097453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097453-IMG_1065-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14097453" class="wp-caption-text">拿到美食的欢喜两姐妹。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会餐中亦有兵哥大摸彩，礼品与礼金是多位小兵夫妇的好友们与社区热心人士慷慨解囊赞助。J&amp;J Strings的张董事长赞助了最大奖，小提琴二把及UKULELE小吉他三把，此外还有台商会前副会长潘兰生、亚特兰大飞行航空学校校长王多禄、眷村联谊会会长叶竣图、华美银行经理边铨、American Family Insurance-Hong Lu Agency的高妹、彭文汉、厨柜公司店东DAVID、MR CJ、STEVE LEE、TGA高尔夫球队、张群、黄鸿展、郝尉国、邱婷兰、Ms. Winmate等人的慷慨赞助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14097452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14097452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097452-IMG_1071-e1697588229897.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14097452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097452-IMG_1071-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14097452" class="wp-caption-text">摸彩活动开始。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14097455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14097455" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097455-IMG_1073-e1697588755418.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14097455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14097455-IMG_1073-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14097455" class="wp-caption-text">小娃娃好奇地看爸妈获得的奖品。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿兵哥餐馆每周提供多套美味特餐，而且小兵还精益求精，时常出创新餐点。在传统佳节时，还会以接龙方式让大家订购年糕、台式肉粽等美食。并且为了方便更广区域的顾客们订菜，他们每周固定时间还会将预定的餐点送到百佳超市、Doraville H Mart、Marietta/East Cobb。详情请与杜娟洽询。</p>
