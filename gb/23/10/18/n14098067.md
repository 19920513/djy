<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>安庆国小王子绫 才华洋溢的小艺术家</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098071-641984-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>王子绫经常参加手作市集，与朋友一起在假日或活动中摆摊。（苏美芳提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月18日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林虎尾镇安庆国小的六年级学生，王子绫，已经在不同领域中取得了令人瞩目的成就。最近，她通过了甜品C级认证，并积极迎向第二级证照的学习，充满了学习的热情。此外，她第一次参加了全国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%8F%E5%9C%9F.md#1">黏土</a>大赛，令人惊喜地获得了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%93%E4%B8%9A%E8%AF%84%E5%AE%A1%E5%A5%96.md#1">专业评审奖</a>的第二名，同时也在人气票选中排名第二，成就了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8C%E9%87%8D%E8%8D%A3%E8%AA%89.md#1">双重荣誉</a>。这个暑假对她而言是充实且有意义的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098072" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098072" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098072-641985.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14098072" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098072-641985-600x623.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="468" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098072" class="wp-caption-text">充满童趣的作品。（苏美芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14098073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098073" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098073-641986.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14098073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098073-641986-600x690.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="518" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098073" class="wp-caption-text">送老师教师节的作品。（苏美芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14098069" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098069" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098069-641982.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14098069" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098069-641982-600x928.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="696" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098069" class="wp-caption-text">获得了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%93%E4%B8%9A%E8%AF%84%E5%AE%A1%E5%A5%96.md#1">专业评审奖</a>的第二名，同时也在人气票选中排名第二。（苏美芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>更令人惊讶的是，王子绫在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%8F%E5%9C%9F.md#1">黏土</a>艺术方面取得了出色的成就。她的母亲苏美芳回忆，当她五岁时，为了不让她在假日和社区的孩子们一起玩得又脏又乱，因为妈妈注重外表和整洁，所以报名参加了绘画课程。但令人意外的是，仅仅两个月后，她告诉妈妈她想学习黏土艺术。经过时间的累积，她逐渐展现出卓越的才华，现在已经在六年级时拿到了黏土捏塑设计三级合格证，近期将朝第二级迈进。</p>
<p>此外，王子绫经常参加手作市集，与朋友一起在假日或活动中摆摊，热情地与游客分享她的手工艺品，同时还担任讲师，现场指导游客体验轻黏土艺术的创作。在学业方面，她的表现优异，还在学校乐团担任圆号手。</p>
<p>在成长的过程中，王子绫连续八年借阅了嘉义竹崎乡图书馆的书籍，展现了对知识的渴望和对阅读的热爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098070" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098070-641983.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14098070" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098070-641983-600x649.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="488" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098070" class="wp-caption-text">让她通过甜品认证的作品之一。　（苏美芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14098068" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098068" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098068-641981.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14098068" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098068-641981-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098068" class="wp-caption-text">担任讲师，指导民众体验轻黏土艺术的创作。（苏美芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
