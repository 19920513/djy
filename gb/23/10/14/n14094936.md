<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东管处月光海音乐会 永续元素获国际大奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094937-640905-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>月光海音乐会不提供免洗餐具，运用叶子当餐具。（交通部东管处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月14日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾台东报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A4%E9%80%9A%E9%83%A8.md#1">交通部</a>观光署东部海岸国家风景区管理处（以下简称<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E7%AE%A1%E5%A4%84.md#1">东管处</a>）以“台湾东海岸月光．海音乐会－<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B8%E7%BB%AD.md#1">永续</a>活动的实践”，荣获荷兰非营利组织“绿色目的地基金会”（Green Destinations Foundation）“环境与气候”类“2023<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A8%E7%90%83%E7%99%BE%E5%A4%A7.md#1">全球百大</a>目的地故事奖”（2023 Green Destinations Top 100 Stories）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E7%AE%A1%E5%A4%84.md#1">东管处</a>为了在东海岸推动深度艺术旅游，2015年开始了东海岸大地艺术节，第二年更融入音乐元素，在都历游客中心举办“月光．海音乐会”，从此成为东海岸的亮点活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14094938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094938" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094938-640906.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094938-640906-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094938" class="wp-caption-text">东管处2023GD百大故事获奖证书。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A4%E9%80%9A%E9%83%A8.md#1">交通部</a>东管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14094939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094939" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094939-640907.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094939-640907-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094939" class="wp-caption-text">东管处<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%88%E5%85%89%E6%B5%B7%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">月光海音乐会</a>场景。（交通部东管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14094940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094940-640908.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094940-640908-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094940" class="wp-caption-text">创艺市集，游客用压金借餐具。（交通部东管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>东管处表示，月光．海音乐会每年选择6月到9月圆月缓缓升起的日子举办，以月光、天空与海洋作为舞台背景，取代一次性舞台输出印制物，传递着月亮的祝福和珍爱大地的精神。同时，宣导环保精神，游客除自备个人用品外，市集摊商的征选也优先考虑环保店家，加上尊重在地文化、爱护自然环境和回馈在地社区的理念，7年累积的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B8%E7%BB%AD.md#1">永续</a>元素，已融为月光海音乐活动的DNA。</p>
<p>东管处在2023年初开始加入绿色目的地评鉴计划，希望让世界知道东海岸对永续旅游的努力，东管处表示，本次获奖故事主轴不是音乐会多盛大、舞台卡司多隆重，而是举办大型活动的同时，依旧坚持着减废、环保、尊重在地文化与土地平衡的核心理念；这是东海岸努力永续生活的每一位伙伴、摊商和每年2万多名来参加<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%88%E5%85%89%E6%B5%B7%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">月光海音乐会</a>游客共同的成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14094941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094941" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094941-640909.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094941-640909-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094941" class="wp-caption-text">月光海创意市集。（交通部东管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14094942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14094942" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094942-640910.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14094942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14094942-640910-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14094942" class="wp-caption-text">月光海音乐会游客自备餐具。（交通部东管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
