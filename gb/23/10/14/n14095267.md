<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>艺游诏安同乐二崙” 活动多元多样吸引人潮</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月14日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县2023年“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%8F%E5%AE%89%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E8%8A%82.md#1">诏安客家文化节</a>”系列活动，主题为“艺游诏安同乐<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%8C%E5%B4%99.md#1">二崙</a>”14日上午隆重开幕。活动充满多元多样的内容，吸引了众多民众前来参加，包括副县长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E6%B7%91%E4%BA%9A.md#1">谢淑亚</a>、客委会秘书廖育佩等多位重要嘉宾也来到场。</p>
<p>在活动开幕式上，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E6%B7%91%E4%BA%9A.md#1">谢淑亚</a>副县长表示对客委会对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>诏安客家文化的支持表示感谢，同时也赞扬<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%8C%E5%B4%99.md#1">二崙</a>乡过去各届乡长对客家文化节所付出的努力。她强调客家文化所散发出的勤奋、节俭、耐劳等美德是值得我们学习的，并期许客家文化能持续继承与发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14095268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14095268" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14095268-640974.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14095268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14095268-640974-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14095268" class="wp-caption-text">诏安社区理事长廖明仁带来社区养的羊咩咩，让小朋友直呼好可爱。（廖明仁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14095269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14095269" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14095269-640975.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14095269" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14095269-640975-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14095269" class="wp-caption-text">三和国小为诏安客家话重点推广学校。（三和国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14095273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14095273" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14095273-640979.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14095273" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14095273-640979-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14095273" class="wp-caption-text">大华社区妈妈带来精彩表演。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>二崙乡长钟东荣表示，感谢上级长官对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%8F%E5%AE%89%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E8%8A%82.md#1">诏安客家文化节</a>的高度重视，并表扬二崙乡公所每年举办诏安客家文化活动的用心。钟东荣也向各学校表示敬意，特别是对于他们在保留和培育诏安客家文化方面的努力。</p>
<p>当日的活动内容丰富多元，包括艺文表演、客家文化展览、传统客家美食品尝、客语教学、客家文学展示、手作坊、公益宣导等。以及在地作家文学展，在二崙地区生长的名作家有季季、宋泽莱、钟文音、林永福、杨索等。学校师生、社区妈妈舞蹈团、客家文化爱好者等纷纷参与表演，为现场增添缤纷色彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14095271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14095271" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14095271-640977.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14095271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14095271-640977-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14095271" class="wp-caption-text">会场提供许多小时候的童玩，让来宾忍不住也想回味一下。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14095270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14095270" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14095270-640976.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14095270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14095270-640976-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14095270" class="wp-caption-text">当日的活动内容丰富多元，包括艺文表演、客家文化展览。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
