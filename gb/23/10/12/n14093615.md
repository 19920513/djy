<p>【大纪元2023年10月12日讯】（<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">大纪元记者夏楚君<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>悉尼编译报导）</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">在周一晚（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">10</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">9</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">日）发生的支持巴勒斯坦抗议活动引发争议后，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E5%AF%B9%E5%85%9A%E9%A2%86%E8%A2%96.md#1">反对党领袖</a>达顿（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">Peter Dutton</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）呼吁政府对那些支持<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E9%A9%AC%E6%96%AF.md#1">哈马斯</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%97%E8%AE%AE%E8%80%85.md#1">抗议者</a>处以严厉的惩罚，包括<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%B1%E9%80%90.md#1">驱逐</a>出境。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">周四（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">10</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">12</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">日）</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">达顿呼吁总理取消那些支持<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E9%A9%AC%E6%96%AF.md#1">哈马斯</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%97%E8%AE%AE%E8%80%85.md#1">抗议者</a>签证，并抨击抗议者的行为不符合澳洲人的身份。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">他</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">对</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">2GB</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">电台表示，那些支持哈马斯的抗议者应该被赶出澳洲。达顿说，抗议活动将载入历史，澳洲人不应该因宗教信仰而受到迫害。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">“他们（高喊反犹太口号的抗议者）应该向犹太社区道歉，我们在歌剧院看到的场景将载入我们国家的历史，我认为我们不应该低估它们在海外被解读的程度。”他说。</span></span></p>
<p>“<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">我不希望世界上任何地方的人们认为这些场景代表了我们民族的身份。”</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">社媒的视频显示，<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">在周一晚</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">的支持巴勒斯坦抗议活动中，人群高喊“</span></span>f** the Jews”“Gas the Jews”。</span></p>
