<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】意大利香肠白藜麦饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098755-75cc262b0eff08f7e475f04aa9cd84f5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>藜麦混合白米，搭配调味的意式香肠，简单几步就能煮出美味锅巴饭。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>市售<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9.md#1">意大利</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%82%A0.md#1">香肠</a>已经调过味，用来煮<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%97%9C%E9%BA%A6.md#1">藜麦</a>饭非常方便；不仅藜麦的苦涩味被淹没，激发出来的谷物香气凝聚在锅底黄澄澄的锅巴上，每吃一口都让人陶醉。</p>
<h2>4人份的食材准备：</h2>
<p>1杯长粒大米，淘洗干净<br />
1/4杯白<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%97%9C%E9%BA%A6.md#1">藜麦</a>，下锅前浸水1个半小时或泡至容易捏碎的程度<br />
2 1/4杯沸水<br />
2根<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9.md#1">意大利</a>生肉<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%82%A0.md#1">香肠</a>，甜和辣各一根<br />
1小撮莳萝（Dill）嫩叶（增强香肠中的茴香香气），切碎<br />
1/4茶匙盐<br />
2汤匙橄榄油</p>
<h2>厨具准备：</h2>
<p>1个三层厚底陶瓷锅（或其它蓄热好的平底锅），3夸脱容量</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098757" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098757-6b04f10e0c839e2c9f5b1ca9c26be532-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14098757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098757-6b04f10e0c839e2c9f5b1ca9c26be532-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098757" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>步骤：</h2>
<p>1. 烧一壶沸水待用。<br />
2. 锅中放入橄榄油，香肠去掉肠衣；开中火煸炒至断生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098766-576c5ccd72423baa13bfeb44f9f78f57-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14098766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098766-576c5ccd72423baa13bfeb44f9f78f57-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098766" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 加入大米和白藜麦，炒三分钟左右。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098770" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098770" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098770-d11306738587c88a9e1d2af9a1d632e6-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14098770" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098770-d11306738587c88a9e1d2af9a1d632e6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098770" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 开大火，锅中加入沸水，保持沸腾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098772" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098772" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098772-34a488a3a85cb452900c6ec6c27d50f1-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14098772" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098772-34a488a3a85cb452900c6ec6c27d50f1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098772" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 用锅铲拌匀所有食材就盖上大火煮4分钟，中途别开锅盖。</p>
<p>6. 改中火煮约8分钟，保持中度沸腾状态。</p>
<p>7. 透过玻璃盖看米饭表面，当米粒之间的孔洞只冒零星透明气泡，就改中小火焖18分钟。想吃更耐嚼的锅巴，就多煮几分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098770" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098770" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098770-d11306738587c88a9e1d2af9a1d632e6-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14098770" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098770-d11306738587c88a9e1d2af9a1d632e6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098770" class="wp-caption-text">刚煮好的米饭。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 掀开锅盖，马上趁热拌入莳萝叶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098776" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098776" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098776-0b611331ef536291ac1b2ba1dab92789-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14098776" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098776-0b611331ef536291ac1b2ba1dab92789-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098776" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 锅底黄澄澄的锅巴相当诱人食欲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098755" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098755-75cc262b0eff08f7e475f04aa9cd84f5-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14098755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098755-75cc262b0eff08f7e475f04aa9cd84f5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098755" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 盛盘上桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14098791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14098791" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098791-e3b22079049e72a2c4958721226b3528-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14098791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14098791-e3b22079049e72a2c4958721226b3528-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14098791" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
