<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>日本名牌TOTO 东陶产品：安全、干净、舒适，零接触式 颠覆体验</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100034-Screenshot-2023-10-20-232953.png" />
<h6>极致快适、极致美感，这就是全新TOTO顶级智能马桶盖所带来的前所未有的新体验。（商家提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年10月21日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/toto%E4%B8%9C%E9%99%B6%E6%A0%AA%E5%BC%8F%E4%BC%9A%E7%A4%BE.md#1">TOTO东陶株式会社</a>由大仓和亲先生于1917年创立。 TOTO主要生产销售卫生陶器、浴室、水龙头、台盆等产品以及环境材料（瓷砖）、精密陶瓷、光通信部件等“新领域产品”。TOTO连续两年成为日本消费者口碑最好的企业。TOTO在日本市场占有率第一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100049" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100049-Screenshot-2023-10-21-001623.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14100049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100049-Screenshot-2023-10-21-001623-600x144.png" alt="" width="600" b="144" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100049" class="wp-caption-text">TOTO主要生产销售卫生陶器、浴室、水龙头、台盆等产品以及环境材料（瓷砖）、精密陶瓷、光通信部件等“新领域产品”。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/toto%E5%93%81%E7%89%8C.md#1">TOTO品牌</a>还以卓越的品质享誉世界。它已经进入以下国家或地区并设立有子公司： 美国、墨西哥、巴西、中国大陆、香港、新加坡、台湾、菲律宾、泰国、越南、马来西亚、印度尼西亚、韩国、沙乌地阿拉伯、印度、德国、英国、法国。</p>
<p>1980年，TOTO推出的温水智能马桶盖卫洗丽Washlet，给大家带来清洁、舒适的卫浴体验。</p>
<h2>每天和您亲密接触的 当然要最洁净美观</h2>
<p>极致快适、极致美感，这就是全新<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/toto%E9%A1%B6%E7%BA%A7%E6%99%BA%E8%83%BD%E9%A9%AC%E6%A1%B6%E7%9B%96.md#1">TOTO顶级智能马桶盖</a>所带来的前所未有的新体验。凝聚匠心和先进科技的产品设计，在舒适性、清洁性、便利性上为您呈现尽善尽美的新成果。</p>
<h2>电解水除菌清洁</h2>
<p>业界首创！革新的清洁方式！电解除菌水能轻松分解污物，使污垢难以残留。马桶容易受到污垢、细菌的污染，难免会出现污渍。电解除菌水能够针对马桶内壁清洁除菌，而且不含任何化学物质和清洁剂，让您每天都能放心使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100036" style="width: 544px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100036-Screenshot-2023-10-20-182717.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14100036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100036-Screenshot-2023-10-20-182717.png" alt="" width="544" b="547" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100036" class="wp-caption-text">电解除菌水能有效抑制细菌污物的生成。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>TOTO电解水除菌技术是业界领先的电解水除菌技术，可以轻松地将普通冲洗未能清除的细菌污物去除干净，使马桶、便座底部、喷嘴保持长久清洁。</p>
<p><strong>前喷雾</strong></p>
<p>马桶使用前，自动喷出自来水雾，使马桶表面湿润，让污物不易沾黏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100038" style="width: 549px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100038-Screenshot-2023-10-20-183118.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14100038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100038-Screenshot-2023-10-20-183118.png" alt="" width="549" b="547" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100038" class="wp-caption-text">马桶使用前，自动喷出自来水雾，使马桶表面湿润，让污物不易沾黏。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>温热便座</strong></p>
<p>温水洗净便座的温热便座功能，温度可自由调节，冬天也能舒适如厕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100041" style="width: 544px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100041-Screenshot-2023-10-20-183418.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14100041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100041-Screenshot-2023-10-20-183418.png" alt="" width="544" b="544" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100041" class="wp-caption-text">保持便座温暖，冬天也能舒服如厕。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>自动除臭</strong></p>
<p>使用完毕后，从“除臭”切换为吸入量2倍的自动强力除臭，彻底清除异味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100043" style="width: 549px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100043-Screenshot-2023-10-20-183509.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14100043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100043-Screenshot-2023-10-20-183509.png" alt="" width="549" b="546" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100043" class="wp-caption-text">就座后，马桶会启动除臭功能，使用后切换自动强力除臭，不留一丝异味。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>暖风烘干</strong></p>
<p>暖风烘干功能以舒服的温风，将水汽吹干，让臀部倍感干爽，如厕后更舒适。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100048" style="width: 550px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100048-Screenshot-2023-10-21-001028.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14100048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100048-Screenshot-2023-10-21-001028.png" alt="" width="550" b="547" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100048" class="wp-caption-text">暖风烘干功能以舒服的温风，将水汽吹干，让臀部倍感干爽，如厕后更舒适。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>自动掀盖功能</strong></p>
<p>不须碰触马桶便盖，接近或离开马桶时便盖自动开关。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100046" style="width: 547px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100046-Screenshot-2023-10-20-183346.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14100046" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100046-Screenshot-2023-10-20-183346.png" alt="" width="547" b="546" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100046" class="wp-caption-text">采用非接触式的感应设计，使用步骤更简单便利，容易保持清洁，带来更舒适、轻松的享受。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>水龙卷冲洗（Tornado Flush）</strong></p>
<p>360°强劲水流，无死角冲洗，还能有效省水。传统洗落式冲水马桶，死角较多，不易清洗，容易藏污纳垢。相比之下，水龙卷冲洗在确保有效马桶洗净的同时，还达到了省水、静音的要求。强劲的水流持续回旋数回，形成360度涡流，无死角冲洗圆弧设计的马桶内壁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100047" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100047" style="width: 543px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100047-Screenshot-2023-10-20-183641.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14100047" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100047-Screenshot-2023-10-20-183641.png" alt="" width="543" b="550" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100047" class="wp-caption-text">独创的龙卷水流，延无棱内壁持续回旋数回，漩涡式加速而下，强劲冲洗马桶内壁的同时，也达到静音的要求，实现冲洗品质的突破。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
