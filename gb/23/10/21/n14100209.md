<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>10种腌制橄榄 起司拼盘的必备地中海美食</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100215-shutterstock_1035296740-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>橄榄的种植历史可以追溯到几千年前。腌制橄榄亦作为西餐不可或缺的开胃菜、调味品。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年10月23日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）食用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84.md#1">橄榄</a>以地中海地区最负盛名，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%8C%E5%88%B6%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84.md#1">腌制橄榄</a>是当地的美味开胃菜，也是调味品。如果你正在为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E6%8B%BC%E7%9B%98.md#1">起司拼盘</a>寻找<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%AD%E6%B5%B7%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">地中海美食</a>，点亮用餐气氛，腌制<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84.md#1">橄榄</a>是不错的选项。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%8C%E5%88%B6%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84.md#1">腌制橄榄</a>不单颜色多样，风味从温和到浓郁皆有，与不同食物搭配相得益彰。本篇提供10种受欢迎的橄榄，帮你轻松选购。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100216-90e57e6401168d0f25e07d2a4d1ff70b-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100216-90e57e6401168d0f25e07d2a4d1ff70b-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100216" class="wp-caption-text">美国一家超市的新鲜绿橄榄。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>腌制方法</h2>
<p>橄榄并不是采摘下来就那么美味的，新鲜食用橄榄味道苦涩，经过腌制会焕发香气，风味百变。腌制过程有如酿酒般浪漫可期，每一个品种都会发展出风味特征。以下介绍4种常用腌制方法。</p>
<p>水腌（Water Curing）：作法是将橄榄浸泡水中，每天换水以去除橄榄的苦涩味，剩下的就要交给时间了。此法制作的橄榄味道温和。</p>
<p>盐水腌（Brine Curing）：橄榄浸泡在盐水中，苦味退去，变成味蕾喜欢的咸味，后味通常甘甜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100219" style="width: 594px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100219-0fe38bf4c9d5b80f67a9f4eb2bf185b8-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100219-0fe38bf4c9d5b80f67a9f4eb2bf185b8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="594" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100219" class="wp-caption-text">美国一家超市的腌制橄榄。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>干盐腌（Dry Salt Curing）：被撒上盐的橄榄脱水干瘪， 富有嚼劲兼具风味。</p>
<p>碱液腌（Lye Curing）：此法使用碱性液体腌制以激发橄榄的美味。</p>
<h2>颜色多样</h2>
<p>市售橄榄有绿色、紫色和黑色等，颜色通常代表橄榄的成熟度和加工方法。绿色说明橄榄是在未成熟时采摘的，黑橄榄则是树上成熟的，黑绿色是半熟橄榄。</p>
<h2>受欢迎的种类</h2>
<p>卡拉马塔（Kalamata）：卡拉马塔橄榄起源于希腊南部的伯罗奔尼撒半岛（Peloponnese）。深紫色的果肉在盐水中腌制，果味持久、微咸，是受欢迎的零食。它们在沙拉、意大利面、炖菜和橄榄酱中亦表现出色，常被称为百搭橄榄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100231" style="width: 591px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100231-shutterstock_2203724927-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100231-shutterstock_2203724927-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="591" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100231" class="wp-caption-text">卡拉马塔橄榄。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>切里尼奥拉（Cerignola）：生长在意大利普利亚区（Puglia）的切里尼奥拉，是个头最大的橄榄品种，所以非常适合填馅，馅料包括肉类、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>等。切里尼奥拉有红色品种，绿色的质地最硬实，黑色的最软。尝试将它们与马苏里拉起司、刺山柑（capers）或凤尾鱼搭配，或者切碎放在沙拉、意大利面中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100232" style="width: 554px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100232-shutterstock_1718645278-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100232-shutterstock_1718645278-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="554" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100232" class="wp-caption-text">意大利普利亚区的绿色大橄榄切里尼奥拉。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>萨索斯（Thassos）：来自希腊的深棕色橄榄，柔软的薄皮拥有像葡萄干一样的皱纹。它们合搭蓝纹<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>、柑橘类食物，混合橄榄油与牛至（Oregano）享用也很对味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100235" style="width: 550px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100235-shutterstock_117361687-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100235-shutterstock_117361687-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="550" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100235" class="wp-caption-text">来自希腊的深棕色萨索斯橄榄。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尼翁（Nyons）：它们生长在法国尼翁（Nyons），深色外皮布满细小皱纹，以朴实的甜味着称，作为零食或制成橄榄酱非常不错。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100237" style="width: 564px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100237-shutterstock_1365659648-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100237" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100237-shutterstock_1365659648-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="564" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100237" class="wp-caption-text">尼翁橄榄。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿尔贝奎纳（Arbequina）：西班牙加泰隆尼亚（Catalonia）橄榄，拥有从粉棕色到焦橘色，深紫色、黑色等一系列令人注目的颜色。经过盐水腌制，紧实的肉质散发奶油般浓郁的果味，适合零食、沙拉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100238" style="width: 595px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100238-shutterstock_1441718222-450x301.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100238-shutterstock_1441718222-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="595" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100238" class="wp-caption-text">醋腌阿尔贝奎纳橄榄。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尼索瓦兹（Ni?oise）：来自法国南部的迷你橄榄，咸中带酸，是普罗旺斯料理的爱用食材。它们带来浓郁的药草味，适合海鲜菜肴、披萨和沙拉，例如经典的尼斯沙拉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100239" style="width: 565px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100239-shutterstock_2034371342-450x316.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100239-shutterstock_2034371342-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="565" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100239" class="wp-caption-text">黑色尼索瓦兹橄榄点缀的尼斯沙拉。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>皮肖利（Picholine）：法国著名的青橄榄皮肖利，在盐水中浸泡，质地酥脆，味道像似柠檬又略有淡淡的八角香气，适合作开胃菜、炖菜。它们在法国里维埃拉（Riviera）和摩洛哥均有种植。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100240" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100240" style="width: 556px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100240-shutterstock_1365659648-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100240" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100240-shutterstock_1365659648-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="556" b="370" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100240" class="wp-caption-text">橄榄树上的皮肖利。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>西西里绿橄榄（Castelvetrano）：这些鲜绿色的橄榄生长于西西里岛西南部的Valle del Belice地区，味道咸甜可口，常作为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E6%8B%BC%E7%9B%98.md#1">起司拼盘</a>、意大利面、沙拉和披萨的美味补充。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100254" style="width: 519px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100254-shutterstock_361477538-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100254-shutterstock_361477538-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="519" b="519" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100254" class="wp-caption-text">西西里绿橄榄。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曼萨尼拉（Manzanilla）：烟熏咸味，略带苦味是这些绿橄榄的风味特征。肉质厚实的它们特别适合沙拉。曼萨尼拉橄榄来自西班牙塞维利亚（Sevilla），流行风味是在中间填充红椒（pimentos）的酿橄榄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100241" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100241" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100241-shutterstock_684906862-450x334.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100241" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100241-shutterstock_684906862-450x334.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100241" class="wp-caption-text">填充红椒（pimentos）的西班牙曼萨尼拉橄榄。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>加埃塔（Gaeta）：来自意大利的加埃塔，从绿棕色到深紫色、黑色都有。它们有干盐腌制和盐水腌制两种，盐水腌制的肉质较多汁。这些咸酸兼具的嫩橄榄非常适合意大利面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100242" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100242" style="width: 565px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100242-shutterstock_1022217049-450x298.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14100242" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100242-shutterstock_1022217049-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="565" b="374" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100242" class="wp-caption-text">意大利加埃塔橄榄。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
