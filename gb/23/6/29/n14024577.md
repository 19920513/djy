<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“欢乐菲律宾”7月多伦多街头分享文化美食</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024585-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>欢乐菲律宾（Fun Philippines）是夏日多伦多的街头盛会之一。
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月29日讯】“欢乐<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%B2%E5%BE%8B%E5%AE%BE.md#1">菲律宾</a>”（Fun Philippines?）是多伦多夏季的街头盛会之一。今年7月22日和23日举办两天，与公众分享菲律宾文化美食。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14024577.md#1/5c1153c526f76f88a33c1f27113d2da8" rel="attachment wp-att-14024587"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14024587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024587-5c1153c526f76f88a33c1f27113d2da8-450x197.png" alt="" width="578" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>“欢乐<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%B2%E5%BE%8B%E5%AE%BE.md#1">菲律宾</a>”的命名源自于菲律宾的一个旅游宣传活动，旨在展现菲律宾人天生快乐、乐观、坚强，能在逆境中找到快乐。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14024577.md#1/screenshot-2023-06-27-114403" rel="attachment wp-att-14024589"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14024589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024589-Screenshot-2023-06-27-114403-450x140.jpg" alt="" width="579" b="180" /></a></p>
<p>通过美食、娱乐和大规模聚会，庆祝菲律宾文化。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14024577.md#1/screenshot-2023-06-27-114216" rel="attachment wp-att-14024588"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14024588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024588-Screenshot-2023-06-27-114216-450x250.jpg" alt="" width="578" b="321" /></a></p>
<p>“欢乐菲律宾”活动中，将有超过120个摊位，长达1公里 ，预计吸引20万名参观者，400多名志愿者 ，还有40多个社区组织。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-14024586 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024586-3-450x258.jpg" alt="" width="579" b="332" /></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">活动还将为每个人提供一个展示其才能、产品和服务的机会。</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14024577.md#1/screenshot-2023-06-27-114053" rel="attachment wp-att-14024598"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14024598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024598-Screenshot-2023-06-27-114053-450x276.jpg" alt="" width="579" b="355" /></a></p>
<p>免费入场！</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14024577.md#1/screenshot-2023-06-27-114454" rel="attachment wp-att-14024599"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14024599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024599-Screenshot-2023-06-27-114454-450x157.jpg" alt="" width="579" b="202" /></a></p>
<p><strong>“欢乐菲律宾”Fun Philippines?</strong></p>
<p><strong>活动地址</strong>： 多伦多小马尼拉社区（Bathurst St. and Wilson Ave. Toronto, Ontario, Canada）</p>
<p>活动日期：2023年7月22日和23日</p>
<p>更多详情请看 <ahref="https://www.funphilippinestoronto.com/">网站</a></p>
