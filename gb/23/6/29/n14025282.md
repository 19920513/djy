<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多最大排骨节 本周末百年纪念公园开场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025283-Screenshot-2023-06-29-135943-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月30日讯】由安省节庆集团主办的2023年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>最大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8%E8%8A%82.md#1">排骨节</a>（Toronto’s Largest Ribfest），本周末将在位于多伦多怡陶碧谷的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BE%E5%B9%B4%E7%BA%AA%E5%BF%B5%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">百年纪念公园</a>（Centennial Park）举行。从6月30日开始，一连四天的活动，将让本地居民和来自各地的游客大饱口福。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14025282.md#1/2-1295" rel="attachment wp-att-14025285"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14025285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025285-2-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_14025284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14025284" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14025282.md#1/1-2216" rel="attachment wp-att-14025284"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14025284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025284-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14025284" class="wp-caption-text">过去活动照片（文图由主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>免费入场！共有10支来自北美洲的顶级烤排骨团队，提供屡获殊荣的美味烧烤排骨、嫩撕猪肉、多汁的熏牛胸肉、烧烤鸡、熏香肠，以及巨大的“Dino”烤牛排。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14025282.md#1/3-1042" rel="attachment wp-att-14025286"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14025286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025286-3-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a></p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14025282.md#1/5-624" rel="attachment wp-att-14025288"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14025288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025288-5-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a>活动还将提供各种节日美食，如炸洋葱花、Tiny Tom的迷你甜甜圈、烤玉米、薯条奶酪、漏斗蛋糕，还有手工酿制的冰镇啤酒。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14025282.md#1/4-749" rel="attachment wp-att-14025287"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14025287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025287-4-450x563.png" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a></p>
<p>整个周末将有现场音乐表演，各种本地乐队也将到场献艺。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14025282.md#1/6-452" rel="attachment wp-att-14025289"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14025289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025289-6-450x561.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="561" /></a></p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14025282.md#1/7-384" rel="attachment wp-att-14025290"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14025290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025290-7-450x561.png" alt="" width="450" b="561" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_14025291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14025291" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/6/29/n14025282.md#1/8-331" rel="attachment wp-att-14025291"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14025291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025291-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14025291" class="wp-caption-text">过去活动照片（文图由主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>2023年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>最大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8%E8%8A%82.md#1">排骨节</a></h2>
<p>活动地点：</p>
<p>多伦多<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BE%E5%B9%B4%E7%BA%AA%E5%BF%B5%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">百年纪念公园</a>（256 CENTENNIAL PARK RD, ETOBICOKE, ON）</p>
<p>活动时间：</p>
<p>2023年6月30日至7月3日</p>
