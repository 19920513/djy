<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小小摄影师彩色校园 复国幼儿园专属摄影展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024824-621779-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>小小摄影师专注摄影。（复国幼儿园提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月29日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）灵活运用现有场地及人事营造值得回忆的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B1%95.md#1">摄影展</a>，嘉义市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8D%E5%9B%BD%E5%B9%BC%E5%84%BF.md#1">复国幼儿</a>园28日于园内老房子故事屋举办“彩色校园”小小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B1%95.md#1">摄影展</a>，这次展出的作品皆出自小班幼儿之手，幼儿运用相机或手机纪录了校园及老房子的每一个色彩画面。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8D%E5%9B%BD%E5%B9%BC%E5%84%BF.md#1">复国幼儿</a>园园长陈玉如表示，透过孩子的视角摄影的眼，看到多彩多姿的美丽校园，孩子的摄影作品令人感到十分惊艳，很难想像这些作品是出自三、四岁小班宝贝的影像纪录，实在非常珍贵。琼燕老师和雨欣老师的用心与创意，以及家长的热情和参与，给予我们小小摄影师最大的鼓励。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14024826" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024826" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024826-621780.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14024826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024826-621780-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024826" class="wp-caption-text">小小解说员向家长介绍作品 。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14024827" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024827" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024827-621781.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14024827" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024827-621781-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024827" class="wp-caption-text">小小摄影师欢迎大家。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>企鹅三班琼燕和雨欣两位老师表示，幼儿从主题课程“色彩大发现”出发，化身为颜色小侦探，寻找校园色彩时发现镜头下的世界好漂亮；进而主动拿取学习区的幼儿相机，进行拍照。为了让拍摄的照片清晰不模糊，特地邀请摄影专家余信贤会长叔叔，来教孩子拍照的技巧，孩子拍出校园中蓝色的天空、黄色的向日葵花、红色的脚踏车、红色的溜滑梯、草地里的小黄花和悠游水中的彩色鱼等作品，每张经由孩子视角拍的照片，都充满着初体验的美好呈现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14024828" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024828" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024828-621782.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14024828" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024828-621782-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024828" class="wp-caption-text">小小摄影师与家长大合照。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影展当天孩子穿上自己彩绘的围兜兜，担任小小解说员，小手牵着大手邀请家长来到老房子一同来欣赏；更配合色彩主题，每个家庭提供一道道有颜色的点心，在摄影展之后，举办“多彩多吃”的亲子同乐茶会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14024829" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024829" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024829-621783.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14024829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024829-621783-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024829" class="wp-caption-text">园生合照。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>透过这次小小摄影师的展览活动，也让家长了解孩子在学校的主题学习活动，并深入认识学校里的老房子，不仅珍贵也别具意义。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14024830" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024830-621784.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14024830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024830-621784-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024830" class="wp-caption-text">园长聆听小小摄影师介绍作品。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14024831" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024831" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024831-621785.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14024831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024831-621785-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024831" class="wp-caption-text">校园摄影展。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
