<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苏俊宾感谢桃园劳工朋友促进桃园经济发展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013995-618759-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市副市长苏俊宾与桃园市产业总工会成员合影。（桃园市府新闻处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月11日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）桃园市副市长苏俊宾11日前往杨梅区，出席“勞工法令宣导暨歌唱聯欢活动”。苏俊宾表示，代表张善政市长前来，要向所有劳工朋友表达感谢，因为有劳工朋友长期对桃园的付出，才有桃园今日的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E6%B5%8E%E5%8F%91%E5%B1%95.md#1">经济发展</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14013996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14013996" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013996-618760.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14013996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013996-618760-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14013996" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市副市长苏俊宾与劳工朋友挥手打招呼。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A7%E4%B8%9A%E6%80%BB%E5%B7%A5%E4%BC%9A.md#1">产业总工会</a>是桃园产业环境及劳工政策发展的“防腐剂”，避免政府怠惰与疏忽的重要因素，也是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%B3%E5%B7%A5%E6%9D%83%E7%9B%8A.md#1">劳工权益</a>进步的重要推手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14013997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14013997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013997-618761.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14013997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013997-618761-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14013997" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市副市长苏俊宾与劳工朋友开心自拍。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苏俊宾指出，桃产总对于改善<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%B3%E5%B7%A5%E6%9D%83%E7%9B%8A.md#1">劳工权益</a>的建议，市府皆会以正面的态度来面对，如今有好的成果也是过去点滴累积而来，未来市府将持续秉持相同的模式与桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A7%E4%B8%9A%E6%80%BB%E5%B7%A5%E4%BC%9A.md#1">产业总工会</a>共同合作，期盼在不断协调与改进的过程中，为劳工朋友创造更好的工作环境。◇</p>
