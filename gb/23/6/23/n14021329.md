<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Dawson&#8217;s Landing社区第四期地块开始发售</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021807-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-00-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Dawson’s Landing社区。（Qualico集团提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月23日讯】Qualico Communities-卡尔加里宣布了切斯特米尔（Chestermere）领先社区Dawson&#8217;s Landing社区最新发布的住宅<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E5%9D%97.md#1">地块</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14021809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021809" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021809-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14021809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021809-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-01-600x378.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021809" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%95%E7%A4%BA%E5%B1%8B.md#1">展示屋</a>。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新一期<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E5%9D%97.md#1">地块</a>预售6月22日开始，其中包括来自建筑商Broadview Homes、NuVista Homes、Truman Homes和Sterling Homes的令人兴奋的新住宅。“对于那些希望以更低的价格购买更宽敞住宅的人来说，前置<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%BA%93.md#1">车库</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8C%E6%8B%BC%E5%B1%8B.md#1">双拼屋</a>是一种新的住宅类型。”卡尔加里Qualico Communities营销与沟通协调莫妮卡·比利克（Monica Bilyk）表示。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14021810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021810" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021810-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14021810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021810-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-03-600x360.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021810" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%95%E7%A4%BA%E5%B1%8B.md#1">展示屋</a>。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有其它多种住宅类型供选择：Morrison Homes和Truman Homes的后巷车道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8C%E6%8B%BC%E5%B1%8B.md#1">双拼屋</a>，Trico Homes的后巷车道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%B1%8B.md#1">独立屋</a>以及Broadview Homes、NuVista Homes和Sterling Homes的前置<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%BA%93.md#1">车库</a>独立屋。房屋起价40多万元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14021819" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021819" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021819-026-20221030-highres.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14021819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021819-026-20221030-highres-600x400.jpg" alt="Dawson’s Landing社区，独立屋，双拼屋" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021819" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区展示屋。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>除了第四期地块发布外，居民现在还可以享受Dawson&#8217;s Landing社区首个已完工的游乐场和两个Stormwater Kidney路径区。“作为第一个建造Stormwater Kidney的社区，雨水池和公园为社区居民提供了很好的体验。”比利克表示。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14021815" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021815" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021815-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14021815" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021815-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-02-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021815" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区公园。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>
