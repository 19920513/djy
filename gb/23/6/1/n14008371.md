<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾高尔夫协会走过疫情 “台湾杯”迎向阳光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008447-DSC08136-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>开球前的大合照。(岳磊/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年06月02日讯】(大纪元记者吕尚明亚特兰大报导) 挥别疫情后，乔治亚州台湾高尔夫球协会（TGA）于5月28 日（周日）在亚特兰大北郊苏万尼(Suwanee)的Bear’s Best高尔夫球场举办了2023年的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%9D%AF.md#1">台湾杯</a>”高尔夫球友谊赛。今年的球赛以“走过疫情，迎向阳光 ! ”为号召，得到了球友们的踊跃支持，最后报名人数甚至超过了球场原来给的上限112人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14008485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14008485" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008485-DSC_1102-e1685672402304.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14008485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008485-DSC_1102-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14008485" class="wp-caption-text">TGA历任与现任会长与欧宏伟主任合影。(吕尚明／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>乔治亚州气候温和，并有闻名遐迩的高尔夫球“名人赛”每年在奥古斯塔举行，因此这里的高尔夫球文化气息浓厚。华人中也有很多高尔夫球的爱好者，从这次友谊赛的盛况就可见一斑。当然这次球赛办得如此成功，是拜于会长宋弘彦与TGA灵魂人物、秘书长Jessica杨的努力。老天爷似乎听到了Jessica的祈求，当天晴空万里，气温和煦，是打球的最佳天气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14008450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14008450" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008450-DSC_0907-e1685670093863.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14008450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008450-DSC_0907-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14008450" class="wp-caption-text">“台湾之光”Sarah Hsu与弟弟Daniel Hsu合影。(吕尚明／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>参加比赛的球友来自不同的年龄层，被TGA会员们称为“台湾之光”的年轻女孩Sarah Hsu也参与女子组。Sarah的父母都来自台湾，她刚从Oglethorpe University大学毕业，主修生物心理学。虽然年纪轻，Sarah已经有九年的球龄，并于今年3月在Oglethorpe University的校内高尔夫球赛中获得冠军，最近还代表其大学参加NCAA的全美大学高尔夫球比赛。不过Sarah表示，她目前打算要攻读牙医学院，还没有要在未来参与其它赛事的准备。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14008457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14008457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008457-DSC_1010-e1685670474878.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14008457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008457-DSC_1010-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14008457" class="wp-caption-text">众望所归，欧宏伟主任(左)与TGA会长宋弘彦(右)颁发总冠军奖杯给Sarah Hsu。(吕尚明／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>球友们到达后，先在club house内享用了“阿兵哥”餐馆提供的美味台式便当以及经典锉冰，然后便浩浩荡荡的乘坐数十辆球车直攻果岭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14008492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14008492" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008492-DSC_0860-e1685672835447.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14008492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008492-DSC_0860-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14008492" class="wp-caption-text">出发打球去(吕尚明／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14008494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14008494" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008494-DSC08046-e1685673019342.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14008494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008494-DSC08046-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14008494" class="wp-caption-text">浩荡车阵。(岳磊／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14008455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14008455" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008455-DSC_0841.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14008455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008455-DSC_0841-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14008455" class="wp-caption-text">比赛前努力挥杆练习。(吕尚明／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14008495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14008495" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008495-DSC08088-e1685673190912.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14008495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008495-DSC08088-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14008495" class="wp-caption-text">开球前解说，前会长Nick杨(左)，现会长宋弘彦(右)。(岳磊／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过了数小时在艳阳下挥杆后，球友们三三俩俩地回到club house休息并享用TGA为大家准备的清凉啤酒。大约6点钟，众人都回到club house之后，“阿兵哥”餐馆的老板阿兵与杜娟夫妇俩就为大家带来他们精心烹调的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%9D%AF.md#1">台湾杯</a>！ 台湾味！”晚宴，有笋干圆蹄、椒盐大虾、樟茶鸭、油饭、炒米粉、葱油土鸡等令人目不暇给的美味，更有TGA主办方最后隆重追加的蟹黄鲍鱼!</p>
<figure id="attachment_14008488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14008488" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008488-DSC_0922-e1685672528363.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14008488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008488-DSC_0922-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14008488" class="wp-caption-text">主厨阿兵(前)为大家端出盛宴。(吕尚明／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
