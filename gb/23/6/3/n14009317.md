<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>5种脸型刷腮红显瘦画法 让你增添好气色</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14011043-20230606_makeup_03-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>依脸型的不同，刷腮红的方法也不同， 不仅会改变脸的印象，更有小脸的效果。（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%AE%E7%BA%A2.md#1">腮红</a>怎么画？腮红的颜色和质感都很多元，不同的涂法会给人不一样的印象。依<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%84%B8%E5%9E%8B.md#1">脸型</a>的不同，刷腮红的方法也不尽相同，善用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%96%E5%A6%86.md#1">化妆</a>技巧能营造出小脸的视觉效果。快来看看不同<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%84%B8%E5%9E%8B.md#1">脸型</a>刷腮红的位置吧！</p>
<p>仅仅抹上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%AE%E7%BA%A2.md#1">腮红</a>，血色感立即呈现，给人朝气活力的印象。此外，颜色、质感、抹的方式却会改变整体的氛围，小脸的效果也值得期待。重点是左右两边的颜色要均匀。</p>
<h2>腮红的种类</h2>
<p>由于各类型的显色都不同，就依你想要呈现的妆容来选择吧！建议初学者使用粉饼状的腮红，即使不小心抹得太厚也容易调整。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009319" style="width: 191px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009319-P.94-2.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14009319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009319-P.94-2.jpg" alt="" width="191" b="198" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009319" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>霜状</h3>
<p>仿佛是由内而外呈现出来的血色感，显色佳。跟其他的腮红比起来，特征是质地滋润服贴肌肤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009320" style="width: 279px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009320-P.94-3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14009320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009320-P.94-3.jpg" alt="" width="279" b="182" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009320" class="wp-caption-text">慕斯般的质地，容易和肌肤融合在一起。云雾腮红，#00535／NARS JAPAN。（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>慕斯状</h3>
<p>特征是宛如舒芙蕾般的蓬松质地，用手指就能推开。和肌肤合而为一且服贴，给人可爱的印象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009321" style="width: 235px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009321-P.94-4.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14009321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009321-P.94-4.jpg" alt="" width="235" b="195" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009321" class="wp-caption-text">携带方便， 外出补妆也能使用。柔亮腮红，#N01／CEZANNE（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>粉饼状</h3>
<p>虽是粉饼但抹后的质感轻柔。有光泽的也有雾面的，种类相当多，很容易挑选到自己喜欢的款式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009322" style="width: 194px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009322-P.94-5.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14009322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009322-P.94-5.jpg" alt="" width="194" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009322" class="wp-caption-text">不粘腻，自然服贴。植萃胭唇水染液，#003／ADDICTION BEAUTY。（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>唇蜜状</h3>
<p>如水般地扩散，颜色持久不脱妆。特征是能呈现真实的血色感。增加水润光泽感。</p>
<h2>腮红的基本</h2>
<p>基本上是将腮红刷在脸颊较宽的地方，以增加血色感。刷腮红的方法则依腮红的种类而有所不同，但建议初学者使用腮红刷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009323" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009323-P.95-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14009323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009323-P.95-1.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="245" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009323" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14009324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009324" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009324-P.95-2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14009324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009324-P.95-2.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009324" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>注意不要刷太浓也不要刷到鼻子以下。太浓、位置不对，都是腮红常见的错误。重点是少量且薄薄地一层一层堆叠让它显色，才会有漂亮的腮红。为避免脸看起来长，腮红绝不要刷到鼻子以下。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>point：</strong></span>脸朝正面刷腮红，就能左右对称了！</p>
<h2>基本篇：增加自然好气色</h2>
<p>刷腮红饼?腮红霜的方法<br />
用大支的腮红刷沾腮红饼然后刷在脸颊上， 腮红霜则是用轻拍的方式拍在脸颊上。</p>
<h3>腮红饼</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14009325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009325" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009325-P.96-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14009325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009325-P.96-1-300x195.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="195" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009325" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 在手背上调整一下粉量再刷在脸颊<br />
刷子沾粉后，先在手背上调整一下粉量再刷在颧骨上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009326" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009326" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009326-P.96-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14009326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009326-P.96-2-300x195.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="195" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009326" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 往发际的方向刷开<br />
边旋转刷子边往发际的方向刷腮红，增添自然的血色感。</p>
<h2>完成！</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14009327" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009327" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009327-P.96-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14009327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009327-P.96-3-450x418.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009327" class="wp-caption-text">轻柔有血色感的肌肤。（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>腮红霜</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14009329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009329" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009329-P.96-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14009329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009329-P.96-5-300x193.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="193" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009329" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>1. 用海绵沾取腮红霜</strong><br />
用海绵轻压腮红霜。因为海绵比手指更能均匀又漂亮地抹在脸上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009330" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009330-P.96-6.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14009330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009330-P.96-6-300x195.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="195" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009330" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2. 用海绵拍在脸颊上</strong><br />
以轻拍的方式抹上腮红霜。</p>
<h2>完成！</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14009331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009331-P.96-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14009331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009331-P.96-7-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009331" class="wp-caption-text">显色佳，有光泽感！（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>应用篇：不同脸型刷腮红的方法</h2>
<p>依脸型的不同，刷腮红的方法也不同， 不仅会改变脸的印象，更有小脸的效果。来看看不同脸型的刷腮红的位置吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009333" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009333-P.97-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14009333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009333-P.97-1.jpg" alt="" width="499" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009333" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>圆形脸：圆形脸不仅看起来孩子气也缺少长度，因此就用腮红来改变印象。从耳旁向内侧斜斜地刷上腮红，将给人成熟的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009334" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009334" style="width: 442px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009334-P.97-2.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14009334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009334-P.97-2.jpg" alt="" width="442" b="385" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009334" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>长形脸：眼睛到嘴巴之间稍长，可用腮红解决看起来拉长的问题。眼睛到嘴巴之间的正中间稍微上面一点的地方，刷横向长椭圆形，以缩短脸部中间的距离。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009335" style="width: 489px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009335-P.97-3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14009335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009335-P.97-3.jpg" alt="" width="489" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009335" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本垒板形脸：腮帮子过于显眼的本垒板型脸。为避免视线集中在腮帮子上，在颧骨的地方将腮红刷横向长椭圆形，便会给人俐落的印象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009336" style="width: 456px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009336-P.97-4.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14009336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009336-P.97-4.jpg" alt="" width="456" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009336" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>倒三角形脸：和圆形脸相比，倒三角形脸过于锐利且容易给人难相处的感觉，而腮红则可呈现温和感。颧骨刷圆形腮红，会给人柔和的印象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009337" style="width: 518px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009337-P.97-5.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14009337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009337-P.97-5.jpg" alt="" width="518" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009337" class="wp-caption-text">（图／瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>菱形脸：脸颊较宽的菱形脸，刷腮红的重点在消除显眼的颧骨。在颧骨下方刷倒三角形的腮红，让脸部重心稍微往下移。</p>
