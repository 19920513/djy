<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>主厨料理DIY：培根猪肉卷闪电泡芙佐芒果酱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009192-P.86-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>培根猪肉卷闪电泡芙佐芒果酱汁。（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：西餐的灵魂就在于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%B1%E6%B1%81.md#1">酱汁</a>，学会调制配搭各种食材的佐酱，就能在家复制餐厅的美味。本文由专业大厨教你做好西餐，不必花大钱上课，一次学会职人主厨才知道的西式主菜“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E6%A0%B9.md#1">培根</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E8%82%89.md#1">猪肉</a>卷闪电<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E8%8A%99.md#1">泡芙</a>佐<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%92%E6%9E%9C.md#1">芒果</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%B1%E6%B1%81.md#1">酱汁</a>”的料理程序与呈现技法。</p>
<h3>材料</h3>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E6%A0%B9.md#1">培根</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E8%82%89.md#1">猪肉</a>卷</strong></p>
<p>苹果 1颗_去皮、切丁<br />
奶油 30公克<br />
糖 50公克<br />
猪里肌 200公克_切2公分薄片<br />
盐、黑胡椒碎 适量<br />
培根 10片</p>
<p><strong>闪电<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E8%8A%99.md#1">泡芙</a>内馅</strong></p>
<p>蘑菇 50公克<br />
洋葱 10公克_切碎<br />
大蒜 5公克_切碎<br />
红萝卜 30公克_去皮、切小丁<br />
黄栉瓜 30公克_切小丁<br />
绿栉瓜 30公克_切小丁<br />
鲜奶油 60公克<br />
帕玛森起司 20公克<br />
黑胡椒 5公克<br />
海盐 5公克</p>
<p><strong>闪电泡芙</strong></p>
<p>牛奶 125公克<br />
奶油 40公克<br />
糖 5公克<br />
盐 适量<br />
低筋面粉 63公克<br />
全蛋 2颗</p>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%92%E6%9E%9C.md#1">芒果</a>酱汁</strong></p>
<p>芒果 40公克<br />
柠檬汁 10cc<br />
水 5cc</p>
<p><strong>装饰</strong></p>
<p>红牛血菜苗<br />
豌豆苗</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009193" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009193" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009193-P.87.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009193" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009193-P.87-600x440.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="440" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009193" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">Chef tips</span></strong></p>
<p>如果家里没有舒肥棒，可以取一锅水煮滚后改成小火，用温度计测温，等到水温降到80℃时，将猪肉卷放入，调整好火力，同时必须将温度保持好。</p>
<h3>作法</h3>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;">Ⅰ 培根猪肉卷</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14009194" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009194" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009194-P.88-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009194" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009194-P.88-1-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009194" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 苹果丁沥干水分，放入锅中加入奶油、糖以中小火炒至焦糖色，熄火，盛出后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009195" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009195" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009195-P.88-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009195" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009195-P.88-2-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009195" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 猪小里肌修掉多余的油脂，断筋后，用肉槌均匀的拍扁，拍的时候要拿捏好力道，以免把肉片拍破，并用盐、黑胡椒碎调味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009196" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009196-P.88-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009196-P.88-3-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009196" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 砧板上先铺上一层耐热保鲜膜，再铺入猪小里肌肉片，在最靠近身体的一侧放入焦糖苹果，卷起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009197" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009197-P.88-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009197-P.88-4-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009197" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 耐热保鲜膜铺在底层，将培根一一铺平，把猪肉卷放置中间，再连同保鲜膜一起卷起塑形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009198" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009198-P.88-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009198-P.88-5-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009198" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 抓紧耐热保鲜膜将肉片卷起，直到耐热保鲜膜紧紧的包覆住，两边的收口扭紧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009199" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009199-P.88-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009199-P.88-6-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009199" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 外层再以铝箔纸包卷，包卷时，要从靠近身体这一侧开始卷起，卷得紧实一点，放入水温80℃煮18分钟，即可取出，去除铝箔纸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009200" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009200-P.89-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009200-P.89-1-600x452.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009200" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 去除保鲜膜后擦干水分，放入平底锅中，以少许的橄榄油煎至上色，取出，切成四等分。</p>
<p>Ⅱ 闪电泡芙佐芒果酱汁</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009201" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009201-P.89-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009201-P.89-2-600x452.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009201" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 平底锅下沙拉油加入大蒜、洋菇、红萝卜、黄绿栉瓜、洋葱炒香，加入鲜奶油及起司缩至浓稠以黑胡椒及海盐调味做成内馅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009202" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009202" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009202-P.89-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009202-P.89-3-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009202" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 将牛奶、奶油、盐、糖煮滚加入低筋面粉炒匀，待降温到60℃分次加入全蛋拌匀，放入挤花袋中挤出约7cm×3cm的大小。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009203" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009203-P.89-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009203-P.89-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009203" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 移入已预热烤箱，以200℃烤15分钟，改150℃烤20分钟，取出。将泡芙对半切开，放入适量的内馅，其他泡芙也依序完成，排入盘中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009204" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009204" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009204-P.89-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009204-P.89-5-600x447.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009204" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 将芒果切块，放入食物料理鸡中打成泥状，过筛加入柠檬汁、水，加热后即为芒果酱汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009205" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009205-P.89-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14009205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009205-P.89-6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009205" class="wp-caption-text">（图／台湾广厦出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12. 将培根猪肉卷也排入盘中，放上装饰蔬菜，淋上酱汁即完成。</p>
