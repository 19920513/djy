<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>1200定期票7 /1上线 桃捷提醒走专用通道</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025483-621912-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园机场捷运线各车站，请务必走专用通道才能享有1200定期票优惠。（桃园大众捷运股份有限公司提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月30日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）基北北桃1200<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9A%E6%9C%9F%E7%A5%A8.md#1">定期票</a>7月1日起正式启用啰，即起到年底，持1200定期票进出桃园机场捷运线各车站，请务必走<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%93%E7%94%A8%E9%80%9A%E9%81%93.md#1">专用通道</a>才能享有1200<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9A%E6%9C%9F%E7%A5%A8.md#1">定期票</a>优惠，若走一般闸门通道，将被重复扣款，享受不到优惠了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14025484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14025484" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025484-621913.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14025484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025484-621913-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14025484" class="wp-caption-text">询问处窗口贴等等醒目的引导标志，让乘客能清楚辨识，顺利进出车站。（桃园大众捷运股份有限公司提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14025485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14025485" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025485-621914.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14025485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025485-621914-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14025485" class="wp-caption-text">持有1200定期票乘客也能走一般闸门通道直接进出车站。（桃园大众捷运股份有限公司提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园机场捷运线21处车站都设有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%93%E7%94%A8%E9%80%9A%E9%81%93.md#1">专用通道</a>，搭配立牌、吊牌、地贴、红绒立柱、询问处窗口贴等等醒目的引导标志，让乘客能清楚辨识，顺利进出车站。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14025486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14025486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025486-621915.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14025486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14025486-621915-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14025486" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E6%8D%B7.md#1">桃捷</a>提醒走专用通道。（桃园大众捷运股份有限公司提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园大众捷运股份有限公司正积极进行各车站收费系统更新作业，力拼年底前完成，让持有1200定期票乘客也能走一般闸门通道直接进出车站。◇</p>
