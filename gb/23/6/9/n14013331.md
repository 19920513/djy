<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>高人气打卡景点 “岗山之眼”天空步道</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013345-1-325074-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>高雄冈山景点“岗山之眼”天空步道，是高人气打卡景点，以唯美小提琴作为设计意象。（曾晏均／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>芒种时节，仲夏的天气酷暑难耐，为了贪那一抹清风拂面的感觉，直想向山靠近一点，山上的大树、山上的小草小花，还有令人舒压的虫鸣鸟叫……，偷个浮生半日闲的午后，来一个说走就走的郊游，选一处离尘不离城的小山景点，好好感受旖旎的自然风光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14013351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14013351" style="width: 531px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013351-1-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14013351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013351-1-1.jpg" alt="" width="531" b="525" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14013351" class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B2%97%E5%B1%B1%E4%B9%8B%E7%9C%BC.md#1">岗山之眼</a>”入口意象。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高雄冈山景点“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B2%97%E5%B1%B1%E4%B9%8B%E7%9C%BC.md#1">岗山之眼</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E7%A9%BA%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">天空步道</a>，是高<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E6%B0%94%E6%89%93%E5%8D%A1%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">人气打卡景点</a>，以唯美小提琴作为设计意象，无论白天登高漫步或傍晚赏落日黄昏都各有特色，登上步道可以用360度视角，环视鸟瞰大高雄平原，远眺北大武群山、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%85%AC%E5%BA%97%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">阿公店水库</a>等美景，曾被国际知名旅游杂志《孤独星球》选为推荐旅游地。</p>
<p>“岗山之眼”是高40公尺、全长88公尺的天空回廊，整体以音乐为建筑设计主题，结合了提琴、琴弦、音符等视觉元素。斜张桥的设计形式，搭配纯白色桥体、主塔桥是提琴、钢索为琴弦，营造一种走进“云端音乐世界”的浪漫意象。其中有一段桥面还用透明玻璃铺面，一脚踏上去，底下悬空，胆小如我不由的胆战心惊，凭添了一次空中漫步的刺激体验。</p>
<p>园区步道沿途林荫郁郁葱葱，无障碍设施完善，即使是推着坐轮椅的长辈，或是推着娃娃车亲子游，也可以通行顺畅，园区不大，随意走走逛逛，不用两个小时。下了山，顺道到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%85%AC%E5%BA%97%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">阿公店水库</a>骑脚踏车环湖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14013354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14013354" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013354-4_20230604.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14013354" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013354-4_20230604-600x985.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="985" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14013354" class="wp-caption-text">“岗山之眼”以斜张桥的设计形式，搭配纯白色桥体、主塔桥是提琴、钢索为琴弦，营造一种走进“云端音乐世界”的浪漫意象。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14013353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14013353" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013353-72fd71d2b8c04114c065efd9b3b52dbc.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14013353" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013353-72fd71d2b8c04114c065efd9b3b52dbc-600x585.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="585" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14013353" class="wp-caption-text">天空廊道末端的回旋梯设计意象，是与提琴相应和的口琴。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14013355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14013355" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013355-2d76feaaa61e2e5ceca1cd99cf168c01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14013355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013355-2d76feaaa61e2e5ceca1cd99cf168c01-600x272.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="272" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14013355" class="wp-caption-text">从冈山之演远眺阿公店水库和北大武山。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>阿公店水库自行车道 金黄花朵灿烂盛开</h3>
<p>出了“岗山之眼”，车子沿着堤防往前开，会看到“阿公店水库风景区管理处”的停车场，入口处侧边有YouBike租借站，“阿公店环湖自行车道”就在停车场旁边。</p>
<p>夏日的阿公店水库风光明媚，环湖自行车道的阿勃勒金黄色花朵开得正艳丽灿烂，在水库骑单车环湖，可饱览阿公店水库的美好景色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14013364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14013364" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013364-d8fecf33c48c6bc9b8c295ae4cf9c01f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14013364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013364-d8fecf33c48c6bc9b8c295ae4cf9c01f-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14013364" class="wp-caption-text">夏日的阿公店水库风光明媚，环湖自行车道的阿勃勒金黄色花朵开得灿烂。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14013362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14013362" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013362-b1c1eac23fa91882552889bbd192f80e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14013362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14013362-b1c1eac23fa91882552889bbd192f80e-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14013362" class="wp-caption-text">枯水期的阿公店水库蓄水率降低。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>好心情才会有好风景！郊外满满地绿意舒缓了焦躁的心情，大自然疗愈能量果然无敌，沮丧紧迫的心情一扫而空。由此我更加确定，旅行，不必在乎目的地，重要的是看风景的心情，趁著有脾气时装潇洒，放飞绷得太紧的神经，让心灵去旅行！@*</p>
