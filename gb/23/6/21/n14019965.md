<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>梦舞团联袂新英格兰武术学院 演出喜见盛况</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019952-DSC08069-Pano-e1687314855818.jpg" />
<h6>6月17日，CJT梦舞团携新英格兰武术学院举办年度演出，演员们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年06月21日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨勒星顿镇报导）在跌宕起伏的音乐声中，舞扇和长剑轮番登场，优雅的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88.md#1">舞蹈</a>和刚健的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A6%E6%9C%AF.md#1">武术</a>同台呈现，赢得数百观众的热烈欢呼和掌声。这是“CJT<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%A6%E8%88%9E%E5%9B%A2.md#1">梦舞团</a>”6月17日年度演出的压轴节目“Endless |?恒”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019949" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019949-DSC08026.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14019949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019949-DSC08026-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019949" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%A6%E8%88%9E%E5%9B%A2.md#1">梦舞团</a>带来古典舞表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">周六晚在麻州勒星顿镇（Lexington）的勒星顿基督教学院（Lexington Christian Academy）礼堂，梦舞团联合新英格兰<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A6%E6%9C%AF.md#1">武术</a>学院（New England Wushu Academy），为满场观众带来了风格多样的精彩演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019948-DSC07998.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14019948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019948-DSC07998-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019948" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团表演现代舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14019944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019944" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019944-DSC07927.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14019944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019944-DSC07927-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019944" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团成员表演优美的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88.md#1">舞蹈</a>。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这场年度演出以具有古典风味的仕女伞舞拉开序幕。在一个多小时的演出中，梦舞团为观众们带来了古典舞、现代舞、芭蕾舞、嘻哈舞等各种节目；<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%8B%B1%E6%A0%BC%E5%85%B0%E6%AD%A6%E6%9C%AF%E5%AD%A6%E9%99%A2.md#1">新英格兰武术学院</a>的师生们也带着刀剑兵器上台，跳跃翻腾，展现刚强的武术。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019946" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019946-DSC07962.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14019946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019946-DSC07962-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019946" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%8B%B1%E6%A0%BC%E5%85%B0%E6%AD%A6%E6%9C%AF%E5%AD%A6%E9%99%A2.md#1">新英格兰武术学院</a>带来身形矫健的武术表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“我们都是年轻人，然后就很想要碰撞一些火花，”梦舞团创始人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E5%90%9B%E6%9F%94.md#1">蔡君柔</a>说，“我们在波士顿是非常不一样的团体，我们是一个结合不一样风格的舞团。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019947" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019947-DSC07993.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14019947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019947-DSC07993-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019947" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团带来古典舞表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E5%90%9B%E6%9F%94.md#1">蔡君柔</a>说，将柔美的舞蹈和快节奏的武术结合在一起并不容易，在筹划和排练过程中有过不少波折，有过“彻夜的讨论”，但终于成功地做出了这些节目。她还笑言，其实压轴的节目只在最近几周排练了3次，“很开心竟然成功了”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019945-DSC07941.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14019945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019945-DSC07941-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019945" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团带来Hip-Hop舞蹈表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14019950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019950-DSC08035.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14019950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019950-DSC08035-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019950" class="wp-caption-text">压轴节目结合舞蹈与武术。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当晚，梦舞团和新英格兰武术学院总共有约150人参与了演出，其中包括多位在舞蹈、武术中比赛获奖的学生。观众群也非常庞大，包括演员们的亲属、赞助商代表、社会各界来宾等等。侨教中心主任潘昭荣也到场观看了演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019953" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019953-DSC08082.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14019953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019953-DSC08082-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019953" class="wp-caption-text">CJT梦舞团创办人蔡君柔与侨教中心主任潘昭荣合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在演出过后，蔡君柔上台感谢各位舞者和武者辛勤排练，也感谢所有幕后工作人员长时间的付出。她还与演出团队一起感谢了所有赞助商的支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019951-DSC08061.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14019951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019951-DSC08061-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019951" class="wp-caption-text">蔡君柔感谢各位来宾的支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“我们希望向西方传播我们东方艺术的底蕴，然后也向东方介绍我们西方舞蹈的美。”她说。一同上台的还有新英格兰武术学院创办人Calvin王健豪，以及其他舞蹈和武术老师。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019954" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019954-DSC08088.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14019954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019954-DSC08088-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019954" class="wp-caption-text">CJT梦舞团创办人Mia蔡君柔与新英格兰武术学院创办人Calvin王健豪合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">另外，舞台上还播放了演出的幕后花絮，包括演员们的排练过程和舞蹈学生们的反馈。学生们都说，她们非常喜欢蔡君柔老师的舞蹈课，她是幽默、有耐心，而且对跳舞很有热情的好老师。<span lang="ZH-TW">◇</span></p>
