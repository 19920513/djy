<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>男子神明面前捞钱 桃园警1小时内追缉落网</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14011412-618155-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园警方起获3800余元硬币。（桃园市桃园警察分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月07日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）6日23时40分许，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E8%AD%A6.md#1">桃园警</a>察分局青溪派出所巡佐何政裕及警员陈倍如、吴阡羽巡逻经过中山路时，有民众反映看到1名男子跳进<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%A6%8F%E5%AE%AB.md#1">景福宫</a>前的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B8%E6%84%BF%E6%B1%A0.md#1">许愿池</a>内捞硬币。</p>
<figure id="14011413" aria-describedby="caption-14011413" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14011413-618156-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14011413-618156-450x600.jpg" alt="男子回到景福宫前的许愿池内捞硬币旁。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14011413" class="wp-caption-text">男子回到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%A6%8F%E5%AE%AB.md#1">景福宫</a>前的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B8%E6%84%BF%E6%B1%A0.md#1">许愿池</a>内捞硬币旁。（桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E8%AD%A6.md#1">桃园警</a>察分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>员警立即依民众提供之特征进行追查，不到1小时就查获该名连姓男子（30岁），并起获3800余元硬币，全案依法移请侦办。</p>
<figure id="14011414" aria-describedby="caption-14011414" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14011414-618157-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14011414-618157-450x253.jpg" alt="景福宫前的许愿池。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14011414" class="wp-caption-text">景福宫前的许愿池。（桃园市桃园警察分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>庙方对员警迅速查获嫌犯表示肯定及感谢，并呼吁民众有困难庙方愿意尽力协助，切勿擅自在许愿池捞硬币，做出对神明不敬的行为。◇</p>
