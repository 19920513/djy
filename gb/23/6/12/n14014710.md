<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>明慧学校暨TCML结业典礼 美食歌舞欢庆端午节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014707-DSC07664-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>6月10日，波士顿明慧学校暨TCML举办端午节庆祝，经文处处长孙俭元夫妇、明慧学校校长刘珊玲、以及TCML老师合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年06月12日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨剑桥市报导）在风和日丽的6月10日，剑桥市（Cambridge）公立图书馆的活动厅里非常热闹。人们欣赏歌舞表演，吃粽子，喝珍珠奶茶，欢庆节日。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">值<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%AF%E5%8D%88%E8%8A%82.md#1">端午节</a>即将到来之际，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%8E%E6%85%A7%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">明慧学校</a>暨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%8D%8E%E8%AF%AD%E6%96%87%E5%AD%A6%E4%B9%A0%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">台湾华语文学习中心</a>（TCML）在这里举办结业典礼和节日庆典，得到当地人们的欢迎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14014697" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014697" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014697-DSC07519.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014697" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014697-DSC07519-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014697" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任潘昭荣、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%8E%E6%85%A7%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">明慧学校</a>校长刘珊玲，以及TCML师生合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>TCML</strong><strong>展示华语教学成果</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>明慧学校于今年2月成立<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%8D%8E%E8%AF%AD%E6%96%87%E5%AD%A6%E4%B9%A0%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">台湾华语文学习中心</a>，开设成年人华语课程。历时3个多月后，10多名成人学生学会了日常打招呼、餐厅点菜等基本会话。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在周六的庆祝活动中，成人学生们分别展示了在大学路上打招呼，以及在餐厅与服务员对话的基本华语，令观众们拍手称赞。此外，活动厅外设有TCML摊位，也得到许多观众的关注和咨询。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14014704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014704-DSC07637.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014704-DSC07637-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014704" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿明慧学校TCML的成人学生们展示华语会话。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元夫妇，以及侨教中心主任潘昭荣都到场参加活动，并且对明慧学校TCML的教学表达赞许。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14014698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014698" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014698-DSC07557.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014698" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014698-DSC07557-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014698" class="wp-caption-text">6月10日，经文处处长孙俭元在波士顿明慧学校暨TCML结业典礼中致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">孙俭元表示，很高兴看到明慧学校TCML有这么多学生，也很感谢老师们尽心尽力地从事华语教学工作。经文处及侨教中心也将继续支持明慧学校的教学。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“尽管这是暑假，我也想鼓励所有学生继续练习华语。也希望大家未来继续使用台湾的资源来学习华语。”孙俭元说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14014702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014702" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014702-DSC07623.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014702-DSC07623-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014702" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿明慧学校的母子舞蹈班学生表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">潘昭荣谈到，明慧学校成立TCML短短几个月，就有了10多名成人学生，这超乎他的预期，令他感到高兴。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“我相信（明慧）学校也在这边把华语教学环境做得很好，所以我们才看到越来越多的人愿意到我们学校来学习华语文。”潘昭荣说。他代表侨委会委员长徐佳青祝福大家<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%AF%E5%8D%88%E8%8A%82.md#1">端午节</a>快乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14014699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014699" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014699-DSC07567.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014699-DSC07567-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014699" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任潘昭荣在波士顿明慧学校暨TCML结业典礼中致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14014706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014706-DSC07659.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014706-DSC07659-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014706" class="wp-caption-text">观众们在TCML摊位咨询。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>明慧学校学年结业</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>欢庆端午</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">成人学生们学业优秀，儿童学生们的表现也是不遑多让。当天孩子们的表演有唐诗接力朗诵、介绍端午节由来、戏剧“三个小和尚”、舞蹈“木兰从军”、合唱“我是明慧好学生”，还有有奖猜谜等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14014703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014703" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014703-DSC07628.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014703-DSC07628-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014703" class="wp-caption-text">儿童学生们表演舞蹈“木兰从军”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14014705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014705-DSC07655.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014705-DSC07655-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014705" class="wp-caption-text">儿童学生们表演合唱。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在介绍端午节的由来时，西人孩子吐字清晰，说话流畅，华语水平与华人移民的孩子不相上下，赢得观众们的热烈掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14014700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014700" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014700-DSC07580.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014700-DSC07580-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014700" class="wp-caption-text">西人学生们用流利的华语介绍端午节的由来。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14014701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014701-DSC07596.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014701-DSC07596-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014701" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿明慧学校儿童学生们表演戏剧。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">明慧学校校长刘珊玲谈到，该校今年2月庆祝农历新年，同时成立了台湾华语文学习中心，并开设2个成人华语班。她希望，看到学生们今天的表现后，更多成人学生将加入明慧学校TCML学习华语。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14014722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014722-DSC07670.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014722-DSC07670-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014722" class="wp-caption-text">现场有折纸粽子的摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14014708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014708-DSC07665.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014708-DSC07665-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014708" class="wp-caption-text">现场有折纸艺术的摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14014709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14014709" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014709-DSC07668.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14014709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14014709-DSC07668-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14014709" class="wp-caption-text">人们品尝粽子美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在表演结束后，现场还有粽子、茶叶蛋、珍珠奶茶等美食，吸引观众们纷纷留步。走廊上有明慧学校水墨画班的作品展览，也有手工艺班的老师们教折纸，也吸引不少人驻足。◇</p>
