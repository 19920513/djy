<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东湾“传奇火锅” 流水的食材 不绝的美味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023039-001_-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“传奇火锅”是东湾首家旋转火锅店。（旧金山大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月27日讯】文：余亨之?大纪元<br />
螃蟹、干贝、蛤蜊、鱿鱼、海螺、虾、雪花肥牛、羊肩肉、五花肉、时令蔬菜、花式糕点……回转带如流水一般，将近百种食材源源不断地传送到食客身边。</p>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E5%A5%87%E7%81%AB%E9%94%85.md#1">传奇火锅</a>是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE.md#1">东湾</a>首家<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%8B%E8%BD%AC%E7%81%AB%E9%94%85.md#1">旋转火锅</a>，提供十多种海鲜和美国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%8C%E7%89%9B.md#1">和牛</a>、欧洲和牛和神户牛肉等极品肉类。在别的自助<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%94%85%E5%BA%97.md#1">火锅店</a>，这个价格是吃不到和牛的。”老板Alen说。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E5%A5%87%E7%81%AB%E9%94%85.md#1">传奇火锅</a>的就餐环境也独树一帜，它是湾区首家采用全息投影的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%94%85%E5%BA%97.md#1">火锅店</a>，不同的季节，投影在墙壁上的激光影像也有所不同。现在夏季，墙壁上呈现的是缤纷多彩的鱼、水母，令人仿佛置身于海底世界，赏心悦目，远离夏季的燥热，胃口大开。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE.md#1">东湾</a>“传奇火锅店”有10种锅底</h2>
<p>传奇火锅的原味骨汤，是用鸡骨、猪筒骨、牛骨，炖7个小时以上，熬出的乳白色浓汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14023041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14023041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023041-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14023041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023041-01-600x824.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="824" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14023041" class="wp-caption-text">无限量吃到饱的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%8B%E8%BD%AC%E7%81%AB%E9%94%85.md#1">旋转火锅</a>店。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在此基础上，还有厚味重油、够麻够辣的牛油锅；陈香回甜，凝神养心的养生药膳锅；菌香四溢，清淡爽口的美味菌汤锅；集藤椒的清香和椒麻于一身的秘制藤椒锅；酸甜开胃的番茄锅；浓香甜美、黄澄澄的南瓜金汤锅；酸爽可口的老坛酸菜锅，酸辣的冬阴功锅，清淡甘甜的味噌锅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14023043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14023043" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023043-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14023043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023043-05-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14023043" class="wp-caption-text">传奇火锅有10种汤头供选择。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>10种肉类</h2>
<p>传奇火锅的肉类涮品，有雪花肥牛、羊肩肉、安格斯牛、黑猪肉、猪五花肉、鸡胸肉、鱼肉等十种。另外再加$5，可以无限量地享用澳洲<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%8C%E7%89%9B.md#1">和牛</a>、美国和牛、大米龙等极品牛肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14023042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14023042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023042-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14023042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023042-03-600x355.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="355" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14023042" class="wp-caption-text">有10种肉类供选择。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中大米龙，是和牛的后腿肉，肉质润滑，脂肪含量较低，可以用来煎牛排，也可以切成薄片涮烤，是澳洲和牛制作牛排性价比最高的肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14023044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14023044" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023044-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14023044" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023044-04-600x823.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="823" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14023044" class="wp-caption-text">加$5就可以尝到和牛肉片。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，还有小龙虾、螃蟹、蛤蜊、鱿鱼、海螺、干贝、阿拉斯加蟹脚、牛百叶、火腿肠、鱼丸、牛肉丸、蛋饺、蘑菇、豆腐皮、海带、时令蔬菜等70种涮品，非常丰富。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14023045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14023045" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023045-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14023045" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023045-02-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14023045" class="wp-caption-text">无限量吃到饱的旋转火锅店。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>传奇火锅也有自制的夫妻肺片、泡椒凤爪等开胃小菜，和多款花色西点。旁边的调料桌上摆着蒜泥、香菜末、葱末、辣椒油、蚝油、沙茶酱、韭菜花酱、芝麻酱、豆腐乳酱、甜辣酱等十多种。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14023046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14023046" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023046-46ee9ca08702bc3a533012a0e959fb2f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14023046" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023046-46ee9ca08702bc3a533012a0e959fb2f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14023046" class="wp-caption-text">东湾传奇火锅店内环境（余亨之／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们两张桌子的人都点了Wagyu选项，额外支付5美元，多么划算！和牛任意吃，超好！我们点了美国和牛、澳大利亚和牛、雪花肥牛和猪肉。所有的肉，都是我在任何All You Can Eat火锅餐厅吃过的最好的。传送带上的海鲜和蔬菜也很新鲜很好吃。没有关于食品质量的投诉，而且服务非常好。我唯一要说的是——我们都暴饮暴食了！”Michael W.（Elk Grove, CA）在yelp上留言。◇</p>
