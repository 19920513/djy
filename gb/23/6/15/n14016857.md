<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：美韩史上最大实弹演习精彩瞬间</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016864-GettyImages-1258704001-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023年6月15日，美韩联合军演，韩国的K-2坦克在位于波川（Pocheon）的星津消防训练场（Seungjin Fire Training Field）开火。(Jung Yeon-Je/POOL/AFP via Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年06月15日讯】（大纪元记者李言综合报导）几千名军人于周四（6月15日）参加了史上规模最大的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%9F%A9.md#1">美韩</a>联合<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9E%E5%BC%B9%E6%BC%94%E4%B9%A0.md#1">实弹演习</a>，由韩国总统亲自坐镇，以回应朝鲜在该地区日益制造紧张局势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14016862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14016862" style="width: 613px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016862-GettyImages-1258704235.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14016862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016862-GettyImages-1258704235-613x400.jpg" alt="" width="613" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14016862" class="wp-caption-text">2023年6月15日，游客在波川（Pocheon）的星津消防训练场（Seungjin Fire Training Field）观看<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%9F%A9.md#1">美韩</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%94%E5%90%88%E5%86%9B%E6%BC%94.md#1">联合军演</a>。(Jung Yeon-Je/POOL/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>韩国总统<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%B9%E9%94%A1%E6%82%A6.md#1">尹锡悦</a>的办公室说，举行此次演习也是为了纪念美韩两国结盟70周年和韩国建军75周年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14016863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14016863" style="width: 585px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016863-GettyImages-1258704288.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14016863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016863-GettyImages-1258704288-585x400.jpg" alt="" width="585" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14016863" class="wp-caption-text">2023年6月15日，美韩<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%94%E5%90%88%E5%86%9B%E6%BC%94.md#1">联合军演</a>，韩国的K-2坦克在位于波川（Pocheon）的星津消防训练场（Seungjin Fire Training Field）开火。(Jung Yeon-Je/POOL/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>共有2,500名士兵参加了这次在首尔东北部波川（Pocheon）星津消防训练场（Seungjin Fire Training Field）举行的演习。演习中不乏“尖端”军事资产亮相，包括韩国的F-35战斗机和K9自行榴弹炮以及美国的F-16喷气机和灰鹰（Gray Eagle）无人机。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14016835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14016835" style="width: 620px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016835-GettyImages-1258704137.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14016835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016835-GettyImages-1258704137-620x400.jpg" alt="" width="620" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14016835" class="wp-caption-text">2023年6月15日，美韩联合军演，韩国的阿帕奇（Apache）AH-64直升机在波川（Pocheon）的星津消防训练场（Seungjin Fire Training Field）发射信号弹。(Jung Yeon-Je/POOL/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%B9%E9%94%A1%E6%82%A6.md#1">尹锡悦</a>办公室在一份声明中说，“通过实际演习和实弹训练，旨在检验在朝鲜挑衅的情况下，开展联合行动的能力，以实现‘通过力量实现和平’。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14016865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14016865" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016865-GettyImages-1258703843.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14016865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016865-GettyImages-1258703843-603x400.jpg" alt="" width="603" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14016865" class="wp-caption-text">2023年6月15日，游客在波川（Pocheon）的星津消防训练场（Seungjin Fire Training Field）观看美韩联合军演。(Jung Yeon-Je/POOL/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>演习的初始阶段旨在展示对朝鲜核和导弹威胁以及全面攻击的反应。声明说，演习后期，盟军通过对关键目标的空中和炮火精确打击，为反击做好准备，以便“彻底摧毁朝鲜军事威胁”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14016867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14016867" style="width: 606px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016867-GettyImages-1257869545.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14016867" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14016867-GettyImages-1257869545-606x400.jpg" alt="" width="606" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14016867" class="wp-caption-text">2023年5月25日，韩国军用无人机在波川（Pocheon）的星津消防训练场（Seungjin Fire Training Field）参加美韩联合军事演习。(Yelim Lee/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
