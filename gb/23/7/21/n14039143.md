<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：美洲狮在蓝色冰川上休息的美丽画面</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039251-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-6-copy-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年07月21日讯】（英文大纪元记者<ahref="https://www.theepochtimes.com/photographer-portrays-puma-chilling-on-an-iceberg-that-broke-off-glacier-in-mountains-of-patagonia_5352086.md#1">Anna Mason</a>报导／张雨霏编译）栖息在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E5%A1%94%E5%93%A5%E5%B0%BC%E4%BA%9A.md#1">巴塔哥尼亚</a>巨大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E5%B7%9D.md#1">冰川</a>上的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E6%B4%B2%E7%8B%AE.md#1">美洲狮</a>（puma）能够适应极度寒冷的天气，甚至以能游过<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E6%A0%B9%E5%BB%B7.md#1">阿根廷</a>湖而闻名。</p>
<p>当摄影师路易斯?亚历杭德罗?阿查雷斯（Luis Alejandro Acharez）在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E5%B7%9D.md#1">冰川</a>国家公园（Los Glaciares National Park）与一只在漂浮的冰山上惬意休息的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E6%B4%B2%E7%8B%AE.md#1">美洲狮</a>相遇时，他感受到了巨大的视觉冲击。这只大型猫科动物帅气的外表与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E6%A0%B9%E5%BB%B7.md#1">阿根廷</a>高山上海蓝色的冰块相互映衬，美不胜收。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14039243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14039243" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039243-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-19-copy.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14039243" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039243-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-19-copy.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14039243" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/luigi.fotografia.en.patagonia/">Luis Alejandro Acharez</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿查雷斯先生在2021年7月的一次特别旅行中捕捉到这一美丽的画面。冰川国家公园位于阿根廷圣克鲁斯省西南部，与智利接壤，是很多种类奇特的野生动物的家园。</p>
<p>那是一个下午，46岁的阿查雷斯正在执行记录斯佩加兹尼冰川（spegazzini Glacier）、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%8C%E6%99%AE%E8%90%A8%E6%8B%89%E5%86%B0%E5%B7%9D.md#1">乌普萨拉冰川</a>（Upsala Glacier）和佩里托莫雷诺冰川（Perito Moreno Glacier）的任务，期间他们乘坐的双体船接到了一通无线电呼叫。“另一艘船突然告诉我们，他们在乌普萨拉航道的入口处看到一只美洲狮。”阿查雷斯先生告诉《大纪元时报》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14039246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14039246" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039246-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-6-copy.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14039246" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039246-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-6-copy.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14039246" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/luigi.fotografia.en.patagonia/">Luis Alejandro Acharez</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14039247" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14039247" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039247-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-6-copy.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14039247" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039247-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-6-copy.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14039247" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/luigi.fotografia.en.patagonia/">Luis Alejandro Acharez</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%8C%E6%99%AE%E8%90%A8%E6%8B%89%E5%86%B0%E5%B7%9D.md#1">乌普萨拉冰川</a>是南美洲最大的冰山之一，面积超过330平方英里（合870平方公里），具有独特的锯齿状冰峰，使其看起来美得超凡脱俗。</p>
<p>当靠近乌普萨拉航道上的冰山区域时，大约50名游客和船员都看到了这只趴卧在浮冰上的美洲狮。这位专业摄影师马上拿出他的佳能6D Mark II全画幅数码单反相机进行拍摄。不出所料，当他回到家并在网络上分享这些照片时，立即引起了很大的轰动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14039250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14039250" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039250-secre-hidden-drawers-compartments-desk-king-carlo-alberto-11-copy-2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14039250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039250-secre-hidden-drawers-compartments-desk-king-carlo-alberto-11-copy-2.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14039250" class="wp-caption-text">左：阿查雷斯的近期照；右：阿查雷斯和游客乘坐双体船游览阿根廷冰川国家公园的冰山。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/luigi.fotografia.en.patagonia/">Luis Alejandro Acharez</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“这些照片确实引发了强烈反响”，阿查雷斯先生说，“当天下午，我就收到了大量的反馈信息，我的粉丝呈指数级增长，并且还受到了很多国内和国际媒体的关注。”</p>
<p>阿查雷斯来自<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E5%A1%94%E5%93%A5%E5%B0%BC%E4%BA%9A.md#1">巴塔哥尼亚</a>地区（横跨阿根廷与智利两国）的特雷利乌市（Trelew），在投身专业摄影行业之前曾当过多年水手。他现在是一名获得冰川国家公园授权的旅游摄影师，他相信，随着疫情后航运业的恢复，可能有机会看到比平时更多的动物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14039245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14039245" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039245-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-6-copy.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14039245" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039245-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-6-copy.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14039245" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/luigi.fotografia.en.patagonia/">Luis Alejandro Acharez</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14039248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14039248" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039248-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-6-copy.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14039248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14039248-Luis-Alejandro-Acharez-puma-cougar-iceberg-6-copy.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14039248" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/luigi.fotografia.en.patagonia/">Luis Alejandro Acharez</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>美洲狮又称山狮，是一种栖息于美洲的大型食肉目猫科动物，其分布范围从加拿大的育空地区一直延伸到南美洲的安第斯山脉南段，是西半球大型野生陆栖哺乳动物中分布范围最广的猫科。该物种适应性极强，在美洲大部分的栖息地中都有它们的身影。</p>
<p>生物学家利用GPS跟踪技术在2010年进行的一项<ahref="https://www.researchgate.net/publication/233287883_Swimming_by_pumas_Puma_concolor_in_Patagonia_Rethinking_barriers_to_puma_movement">研究</a>中，获得了一只雄性美洲狮在智利巴塔哥尼亚的科克伦湖（Lago Cochrane）游泳觅食的具体证据。此外，还有美洲狮在委内瑞拉、美国亚利桑那州、科罗拉多河以及佛州大沼泽地游泳的相关记录。</p>
