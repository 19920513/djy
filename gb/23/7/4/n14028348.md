<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【视频】庆祝美国独立日 波士顿重温《独立宣言》</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028363-DSC08332-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>7月4日，波士顿庆祝美国独立日，游行队伍经过“自由之路”。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年07月05日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/7%E6%9C%884%E6%97%A5.md#1">7月4日</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E6%97%A5.md#1">美国独立日</a>，波士顿在小雨中举办第247届年度庆祝活动，纪念<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD.md#1">美国</a>开国先贤，重温《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%AE%A3%E8%A8%80.md#1">独立宣言</a>》。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src="https://www.ganjing.com/embed/1fvlts6bfmhZcUbYbOW4CA9DC1981c" allowfullscreen></a>
</div>
<p>当天上午9点，人们在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>市政府广场的国旗下唱<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD.md#1">美国</a>国歌，宣读效忠誓词。波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）、市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）等政要，以及大批民众在广场上观礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14028358" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14028358" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028358-DSC08307.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14028358" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028358-DSC08307-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14028358" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/7%E6%9C%884%E6%97%A5.md#1">7月4日</a>，波士顿庆祝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E6%97%A5.md#1">美国独立日</a>，政要们参加庆祝典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14028360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14028360" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028360-DSC08315.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14028360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028360-DSC08315-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14028360" class="wp-caption-text">“麻州古老荣誉炮兵连”（Ancient and Honorable Artillery Company）向国旗致敬。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14028361" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14028361" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028361-DSC08321.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14028361" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028361-DSC08321-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14028361" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）参加庆祝典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14028362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14028362" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028362-DSC08327.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14028362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028362-DSC08327-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14028362" class="wp-caption-text">乐队演奏“星条旗永不落”（Stars and Stripes Forever）。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后，游行队伍从市政府广场出发，沿着“自由之路”来到“粮仓墓园”（Granary Burying Ground），缅怀葬于此处的先贤。</p>
<p>队伍中有穿着殖民时期服装的乐队和礼枪队，有历史悠久的“麻州古老荣誉炮兵连”（Ancient and Honorable Artillery Company），后面还跟着大批民众。一路上响彻礼枪的轰隆声和人们的欢呼声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14028364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14028364" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028364-DSC08337.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14028364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028364-DSC08337-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14028364" class="wp-caption-text">游行队伍经过“自由之路”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14028365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14028365" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028365-DSC08344.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14028365" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028365-DSC08344-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14028365" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭和家人参与游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14028366" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14028366" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028366-DSC08350.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14028366" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028366-DSC08350-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14028366" class="wp-caption-text">乐队参与游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>游行队伍穿过波士顿市中心的街道，来到“旧州府大楼”（Old State House），仿照247年前的情景，由古老荣誉炮兵连的成员在楼上向民众宣读《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%AE%A3%E8%A8%80.md#1">独立宣言</a>》。庆祝典礼在人们的欢呼声和“USA”呼喊声中落下帷幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14028367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14028367" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028367-DSC08377.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14028367" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028367-DSC08377-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14028367" class="wp-caption-text">“麻州古老荣誉炮兵连”在旧州府大楼上宣读《独立宣言》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14028369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14028369" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028369-DSC08382.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14028369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028369-DSC08382-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14028369" class="wp-caption-text">“麻州古老荣誉炮兵连”在旧州府大楼上宣读《独立宣言》。楼下有大量民众围观。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14028368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14028368" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028368-DSC08380.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14028368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14028368-DSC08380-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14028368" class="wp-caption-text">“麻州古老荣誉炮兵连”在旧州府大楼上宣读《独立宣言》。楼下有大量民众围观。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1776年7月18日，波士顿人在旧州府大楼的阳台下首次听到《独立宣言》。每年的7月4日，人们都会在旧州府大楼重演那个历史时刻。◇</p>
