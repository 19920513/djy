<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多8月波斯美食节邀您品味多元文化</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14030829-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年07月09日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E6%96%AF%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E8%8A%82.md#1">波斯美食节</a>Persian Food Festival，将于8月5日至7日举办。为期三天充满乐趣的活动，为您提供尽情品味波斯美食、音乐和文化的机会。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/8/n14030821.md#1/1-2241" rel="attachment wp-att-14030827"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14030827" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14030827-1-450x254.jpg" alt="" width="478" b="270" /></a></p>
<p>流传数百年的传统波斯美食，以其充满活力的风味、香料的芬芳而著称，将带给您回味无穷的味蕾体验。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-14030828 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14030828-3-450x568.jpg" alt="" width="490" b="618" /></p>
<p>波斯烹饪，吸纳了各个地烹饪精华，将甜、咸、酸与温和味道巧妙地融合。核心在于运用香草和香料，如藏红花、姜黄、肉桂、豆蔻和玫瑰水，赋予每道菜肴独特的风味和香气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14030833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14030833" style="width: 488px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/8/n14030821.md#1/4-757" rel="attachment wp-att-14030833"><img class=" wp-image-14030833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14030833-4-450x400.jpg" alt="" width="488" b="434" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14030833" class="wp-caption-text">过往活动图片。（主办方提供文图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2023年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E6%96%AF%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E8%8A%82.md#1">波斯美食节</a>将在大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>地区的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%97%E6%B2%BB%E6%96%87%E5%B1%B1.md#1">列治文山</a>举办，免费入场。 请带上您的家人和朋友一起前往，享受现场娱乐表演，体验和欣赏波斯文化和美食的丰富多彩。</p>
