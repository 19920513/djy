<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>警察大白熊进打诈嘉市队 召唤神队友齐反诈</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033529-624200-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>	大棠文创设计的警察大白熊贴图。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年07月13日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市政府警察局推广数位化犯罪预防宣导不遗余力，今年初扩充绿幕虚拟摄影棚设备成立“新时代全媒体影棚”，现在更以本市著名地标“沉睡的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E7%99%BD%E7%86%8A.md#1">大白熊</a>”为新一代<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E8%AF%88.md#1">反诈</a>宣导大使。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14033530" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14033530" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033530-624201.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14033530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033530-624201-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14033530" class="wp-caption-text">感谢大棠文创。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E8%AF%88.md#1">反诈</a>宣导大使将当前反诈重点工作结合缉毒．扫黑、肃枪、少年保护、妇幼安全及防制酒驾等宣导观念，归纳出五大宣导主题，并与本市五间庙宇（嘉邑北社尾保安宫、嘉义文财殿、嘉邑城隍庙、嘉义市奉天宫、九华山地藏庵）合作，对应神明护佑众生的形象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14033531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14033531" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033531-624202.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14033531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033531-624202-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14033531" class="wp-caption-text">警察<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E7%99%BD%E7%86%8A.md#1">大白熊</a>揭幕仪式。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>总统蔡英文于7月10日提出“打诈六个不，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%88%E9%AA%97.md#1">诈骗</a>走无路”：要求先汇款不要给、脸书没蓝勾勾的不要点、网路交友借钱不要信、稳赚不赔群组不要加、收到不明简讯不要理、要求给个资的不泄漏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14033532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14033532" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033532-624203.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14033532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033532-624203-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14033532" class="wp-caption-text">宣誓反诈。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>因此，要拓展识诈宣导广度，唤起民众反诈意识，并凸显嘉义市独特创意宣导主轴“沉睡大白熊苏醒，加入打诈嘉市队只要有心，人人都是神队友！”嘉义市警特与“大棠文化企业社”及“锦田云端科技股份有限公司”跨域合作，共同开发“警察大白熊实体公仔”及“神队友AR扩增实境互动游戏”，将犯罪预防宣导精神融入艺术创作中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14033533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14033533" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033533-624204.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14033533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033533-624204-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14033533" class="wp-caption-text">打诈六个不，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%88%E9%AA%97.md#1">诈骗</a>走无路。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>并运用科技以反诈宣导为主轴，开发互动模组及Q&amp;A题库，使宣导更具永续教育意义，未来可流通应用于校园宣导、有奖征答活动中，吸引青年学子参与、增加民众触及反诈资讯机会，达到入里、入户、入心效果，使全民反诈零死角，共同提升市民防诈免疫力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14033534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14033534" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033534-624205.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14033534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033534-624205-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14033534" class="wp-caption-text">警察大白熊打诈神队友。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄敏惠市长指出，透过了解犯罪和诈骗的原因，我们可以做更多的预防工作，从而减少犯罪的发生，并减少人民受害。这次的整合是我们与庙宇、艺术文化、科技和基层社区的力量共同努力的结果。希望以设计思维的组织，让嘉义市成为一个最健康、最幸福的城市。再次感谢各界的齐心努力，携手全民一起努力，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E5%87%BB%E7%8A%AF%E7%BD%AA.md#1">打击犯罪</a>，让嘉义市成为一个最健康、最幸福的城市。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14033535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14033535" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033535-624206.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14033535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033535-624206-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14033535" class="wp-caption-text">感谢锦田云端科技股份有限公司。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警察局长陈炯旭表示，希望不仅<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E5%87%BB%E7%8A%AF%E7%BD%AA.md#1">打击犯罪</a>，更成为市民的好朋友。多元的犯罪预防宣导模式，教育民众认识犯罪并远离风险。融入活泼有趣的互动模组和知识性的Q&amp;A题库，成功拉近与民众的距离。透过犯罪预防宣导融入艺术创作，运用科技作为反诈宣导的主轴，开发互动模组和Q&amp;A题库，使宣导具有永续教育意义。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14033536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14033536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033536-624207.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14033536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14033536-624207-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14033536" class="wp-caption-text">大合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另特别感谢东吴高职最大建教合作伙伴“老四川餐饮”大力支持，让餐饮科建教班新生职场素人，透过该局成为Z世代反诈小尖兵。</p>
