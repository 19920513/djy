<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾热气球开幕光雕 鹿野高台人潮爆满</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026703-622210-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾国际热气球嘉年华开幕光雕音乐会7月1日晚在鹿野高台登场。（台东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年07月02日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）台湾国际<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E6%B0%94%E7%90%83.md#1">热气球</a>嘉年华开幕<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%89%E9%9B%95%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">光雕音乐会</a>7月1日晚登场，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%BF%E9%87%8E%E9%AB%98%E5%8F%B0.md#1">鹿野高台</a>人潮爆满，首次参与热气球光雕音乐会的妈祖球和HELLO KITTTY、喔熊球等，总计20颗国内外造型球进行光雕展演，还有700架<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E4%BA%BA%E6%9C%BA.md#1">无人机</a>群联合热气球共同进行演出，现场热闹滚滚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026704-622211.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14026704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026704-622211-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026704" class="wp-caption-text">台湾国际<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E6%B0%94%E7%90%83.md#1">热气球</a>嘉年华<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%89%E9%9B%95%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">光雕音乐会</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E4%BA%BA%E6%9C%BA.md#1">无人机</a>群联合热气球共同进行演出。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14026705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026705-622212.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14026705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026705-622212-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026705" class="wp-caption-text">台湾国际热气球嘉年华光雕音乐会，无人机群联合热气球共同进行演出。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14026706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026706-622213.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14026706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026706-622213-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026706" class="wp-caption-text">台湾国际热气球嘉年华开幕光雕音乐会，马来西亚创作歌手黄明志压轴演出。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台东县政府表示，去年全国首创“热气球光雕X无人机群飞”表演成功，大受民众期待，今年无人机群飞共700架，以3D方式呈现妈祖球外，台湾IP人偶也随音乐排列出泡汤、红叶棒球、捣麻糬及农田稻穗等具有台东意象图案。音乐会邀请阎奕格及马来西亚创作歌手黄明志压轴演出。</p>
<p>台东县长饶庆铃欢迎全国各地球迷及游客，今年以“希望”做为开幕光雕音乐会主题，感谢交通部观光局及赞助企业支持。交观处表示，今年热气球光雕音乐会举办9场次，7/20及8/28闭幕光雕音乐会无人机群飞均会以700架次规模进行展演，接下来将在三仙台、池上大坡池、知本温泉区、太麻里曙光园区及国际地标陆续举办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026707-622214.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14026707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026707-622214-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026707" class="wp-caption-text">台湾国际热气球嘉年华开幕光雕音乐会7月1日晚在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%BF%E9%87%8E%E9%AB%98%E5%8F%B0.md#1">鹿野高台</a>登场。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
