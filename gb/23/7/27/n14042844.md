<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>做好防台措施之际 加强清除病媒蚊孳生源</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042845-626780-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>落实防疫措施，远离传染病威胁。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年07月27日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市政府卫生局吁请民众做好<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E5%8F%B0.md#1">防台</a>措施之际，加强巡视居住周遭环境，一并清除或收纳好容器，避免大雨后积水，造成<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%97%85%E5%AA%92%E8%9A%8A.md#1">病媒蚊</a>孳生。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E9%A3%8E.md#1">台风</a>过后请记得再次巡视，清除随风飘来的容器，千万不要让病媒蚊有机可乘！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14042846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14042846" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042846-626781.png"><img class=" wp-image-14042846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042846-626781-600x600.png" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14042846" class="wp-caption-text">消除<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%97%85%E5%AA%92%E8%9A%8A.md#1">病媒蚊</a>最爱的地方。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫生局提醒，豪雨过后容易积水，是病媒蚊孳生、繁殖的时机，请民众主动清除户内外易积水的轮胎、铁铝罐、帆布、宝特瓶、盆栽底盘等容器；一般常见屋顶、阳台、天沟、大楼地下室等，排水孔可能被落叶堵塞造成积水，此类停滞型的积水是埃及斑蚊及白线斑蚊等病媒蚊喜欢停留产卵的处所，大约经过1周左右的时间，即可羽化成蚊，成为传播<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BB%E9%9D%A9%E7%83%AD.md#1">登革热</a>病毒的媒介。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14042847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14042847" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042847-626782.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14042847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042847-626782-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14042847" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E9%A3%8E.md#1">台风</a>来袭前，落实防疫四步骤。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫生局也提醒民众，台风过后，务必注意饮食卫生，饮用水需彻底煮沸或饮用包装水。清理家园时，务必穿着雨鞋或防水长靴、戴防水手套及口罩，做好个人防护措施，请勿赤足及穿着拖鞋打扫环境，避免被生锈器物（如铁钉、铁片等）刺伤或割伤引发破伤风，也可避免感染钩端螺旋体、类鼻疽等疾病。卫生局呼吁身体如有不适的症状，请尽速就医，相关资讯可至疾管署全球资讯网<ahref="https://www.cdc.gov.tw" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://www.cdc.gov.tw</a>）查阅，或拨打卫生局咨询专线05-2341150洽询。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14042848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14042848" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042848-626783.png"><img class=" wp-image-14042848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042848-626783-600x600.png" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14042848" class="wp-caption-text">防疫4招报你知。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14042849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14042849" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042849-626784.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14042849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042849-626784-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14042849" class="wp-caption-text">容器减量功德无量。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
