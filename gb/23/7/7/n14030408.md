<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>穿越感官之旅：7月多伦多探索中东之味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14030431-Screenshot-2023-07-07-101717-600x400.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年07月08日讯】第六届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E4%B8%9C%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E8%8A%82.md#1">中东美食文化节</a>Taste of the Middle East， 将于7月14日在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>市政厅前的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/nathan-phillips-square%E5%B9%BF%E5%9C%BA.md#1">Nathan Phillips Square广场</a>开启，带您深入探索充满迷人音乐、艺术、优雅舞蹈和美食的中东文化。</p>
<p>音乐是中东的心跳，编织出神奇的魔法，共鸣于当地人和游客的灵魂。乌德琴的迷人旋律、达布卡的节奏感和引人入胜的歌声，创造出一场听觉盛宴，将您带入另一个世界。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14030428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14030428-3f1d26a7347dcc382adada76a3d557aa-450x665.png" alt="" width="450" b="665" /></p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/7/n14030408.md#1/53c698533d10b5f0dc58c65ed984d98d" rel="attachment wp-att-14030421"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14030421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14030421-53c698533d10b5f0dc58c65ed984d98d-450x286.png" alt="" width="450" b="286" /></a></p>
<p>舞蹈是中东文化的重要组成部分，体现着舞者的优雅，讲述着祖先的故事，带来节日的喜庆。</p>
<p><img class="size-medium wp-image-14030418 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14030418-1-1-450x298.png" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/7/n14030408.md#1/2-1306" rel="attachment wp-att-14030419"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14030419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14030419-2-450x291.png" alt="" width="450" b="291" /></a></p>
<p>艺术是中东璀璨文化的另一面。无论是装点清真寺墙壁的精美几何图案，还是古老手稿展示的迷人书法，中东的艺术语言传达着深远的意义。</p>

<p>2023年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E4%B8%9C%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E8%8A%82.md#1">中东美食文化节</a>Taste of the Middle East，除了音乐、艺术和舞蹈，更有散发着诱人香气的中东风味美食。</p>
