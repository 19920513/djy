<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第二届多伦多湖滨排骨节7月21日开烤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14029994-c765d4f35815bdd1cc08efbf7fb81fc1-600x400.png" />
<h6>2023年多伦多湖滨排骨节Lakeshore Ribfest于7月21日至23日举行。
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年07月07日讯】由安省节庆集团主办的第二届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>湖滨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8%E8%8A%82.md#1">排骨节</a>Lakeshore Ribfest，将于7月21日开烤。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/7/n14029975.md#1/351148739_556243389790333_9222188081705737463_n" rel="attachment wp-att-14029986"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14029986" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14029986-351148739_556243389790333_9222188081705737463_n-450x390.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="390" /></a></p>
<p>屡获殊荣的烧烤团队，将为来宾奉上令人垂涎的烧烤熏排骨、嫩拉猪肉、多汁的熏牛胸肉、烧烤鸡、熏香肠和巨大的“Dino”牛排，还有现场音乐助兴。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14029988" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14029988-358127639_755453053249416_8083832028358548041_n-450x208.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="208" /></p>

<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/7/n14029975.md#1/357485018_313494124343077_7183433464733686850_n" rel="attachment wp-att-14029987"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14029987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14029987-357485018_313494124343077_7183433464733686850_n-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
