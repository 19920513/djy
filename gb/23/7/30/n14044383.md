<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>久坐伤身 教你在办公室就能做的伸展运动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13788465-headaches-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>对于久坐的上班族来说，工作一段时间后站起来活动一下筋骨是很有必要的。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>整天坐在办公室并不是很耗体力的工作，但久坐会让身体长时间处于同一姿势。要留意自己在伏案工作时有没有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%AF%E8%85%B0%E9%A9%BC%E8%83%8C.md#1">弯腰驼背</a>，滑动滑鼠的姿势会不会造成手腕疼痛。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%A9%E9%A2%88%E9%85%B8%E7%97%9B.md#1">肩颈酸痛</a>、拉伤以及<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%95%E7%AE%A1%E7%BB%BC%E5%90%88%E7%97%87.md#1">腕管综合症</a>是久坐办公的人常见的职业伤害。最好每个小时站起来活动一下，伸展个几分钟，并且以正确坐姿办公。</p>

<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>伸展</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>贴颈式</strong></p>
<p><strong>训练部位：</strong>旋转肌袖</p>
<p><strong>目标：</strong>提高活动范围。</p>
<ul>
<li>坐在稳固的椅子上、腰背打直。</li>
</ul>
<figure id="attachment_14044432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044432" style="width: 376px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044432-102-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14044432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044432-102-1.jpg" alt="" width="376" b="566" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044432" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<ul>
<li>右手放在左肩。</li>
</ul>
<figure id="attachment_14044433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044433" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044433-102-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044433-102-2-450x678.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="678" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044433" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<ul>
<li>左手放在右肘，慢慢地往喉咙的方向加压，手肘和鼻子在一直线上，停留几个呼吸。换边，重复相同的步骤。</li>
</ul>
<figure id="attachment_14044434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044434" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044434-102-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044434-102-3-450x678.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="678" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044434" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>变化式：</strong>改以站姿重复相同的步骤作练习。</p>
<p><strong>?</strong></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>过头伸展</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>训练部位：</strong>肩膀、旋转肌袖</p>
<p><strong>目标：</strong>提高灵活度。</p>
<ul>
<li>坐在稳固的椅子上、腰背打直。</li>
</ul>
<figure id="attachment_14044435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044435" style="width: 346px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044435-103-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14044435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044435-103-1.jpg" alt="" width="346" b="462" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044435" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<ul>
<li>右手往天花板的方向伸直。</li>
</ul>
<figure id="attachment_14044436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044436-103-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044436-103-2-450x707.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="707" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044436" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3、右手臂弯曲，左手放在右肘上，将右前臂放在脑后，轻轻往下压，下压到舒适的位置，维持一小段时间。换另一侧重复相同的步骤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14044437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044437" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044437-103-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044437-103-3-450x648.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="648" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044437" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>变化式：</strong>改以站姿重复相同的步骤。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>扩胸伸展（利用廊道或门口）</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>训练部位：</strong>胸、肩膀</p>
<p><strong>目标：</strong>提高肩部灵活度。</p>
<p><strong>起始动作：</strong>站在门框中间，双手以舒适的高度放在门框的两侧。</p>
<p>1、身体慢慢前倾，将重心放在肩膀的前方，缓缓地伸展肩部。停留二十到三十秒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14044438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044438" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044438-138-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044438-138-1-450x692.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="692" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044438" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><strong>变化式：</strong>在没有合适的门框可当使用时，可以请同伴握住自己的手腕，轻轻地将手臂往后拉展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14044439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044439" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044439-138-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044439-138-2-450x443.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044439" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>?</strong><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>主动动作</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>手臂摆动（向前和向后）</strong></p>
<p><strong>训练部位：</strong>肩膀</p>
<p><strong>目标：</strong>增加肩部的屈伸与伸展范围。<strong>?</strong></p>
<p>1、起始动作，将健侧手臂放在桌上或是其他稳定的平面，以作为支撑，然后弯下身体。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14044428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044428" style="width: 395px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044428-89.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14044428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044428-89.jpg" alt="" width="395" b="342" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044428" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2、沿着身体的一侧，轻柔地前后摆动患侧的手臂，放下手臂时要用肩膀的肌肉，而不是手臂本身的重量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14044430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044430" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044430-90-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044430-90-2-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044430" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14044429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044429" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044429-90-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044429-90-1-450x384.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="384" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044429" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3、如果没有感到疼痛，可以稍微加大摆动的幅度。换边，重复相同的步骤练习。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14044431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044431" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044431-90-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044431-90-3-450x391.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044431" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>变化式： </strong><strong>想要增加拉力的话，可以手握哑铃。</strong></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>前举</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>训练部位：</strong>三角肌</p>
<p><strong>目标：</strong>提高肩膀屈曲的力量</p>
<p><strong>1</strong><strong>、</strong>以正确姿势站立，患侧的脚踩着弹力带，同边的手握着弹力带的另一端。调整弹力带的长短便能改变阻力的大小。阻力设定在要举起手的位置，手打直但不锁死。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14044442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044442" style="width: 307px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044442-166-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14044442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044442-166-1.jpg" alt="" width="307" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044442" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2、慢慢的把直臂举到与肩同高，没有不适的话，可以一直向上举。感到不适时，便降低手臂，回到起始位置。换边重复相同的步骤训练。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_14044443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044443" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044443-166-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044443-166-2-450x646.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="646" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044443" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>变化式：</strong>也可以改用哑铃来进行此项训练（详见184页）</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>肩胛夹挤</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>训练部位：</strong>肩部的稳定肌群</p>
<p><strong>目标：</strong>改善身体的姿势与肌力。</p>
<ul>
<li>以正确姿势站立，手臂抬到肩膀的高度，手肘弯成九十度，手指朝向天花板。</li>
</ul>
<figure id="attachment_14044440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044440" style="width: 342px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044440-157-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14044440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044440-157-1.jpg" alt="" width="342" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044440" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<ul>
<li>将手肘往臀部的方向压低，像是要把它们放到裤子后面的回袋里。自然呼吸不憋气，停留五到十秒的时间。此项练习的目的在打开胸膛，收缩上背肌群、让肩膀可以向后向下开展。</li>
</ul>
<figure id="attachment_14044441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14044441" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044441-157-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14044441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14044441-157-2-450x430.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14044441" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>弹力带Y字伸展</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>训练部位：</strong>上胸</p>
<p><strong>目标：</strong>提高肩带的力量。</p>
