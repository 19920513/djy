<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>本拿比6月房价持续上涨</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年07月14日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">柯瑞斯</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>地区的房价继续逐月上涨，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AC%E6%8B%BF%E6%AF%94.md#1">本拿比</a>也不例外。根据大温地产局（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">REBGV</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）最新公布的报告，全市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%B1%8B.md#1">独立屋</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E5%AF%93.md#1">公寓</a>价格均有所上涨。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">REBGV </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">经济和数据分析总监安德鲁</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">利斯（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Andrew Lis</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">表示，尽管借贷成本上升，但相对于大温哥华地区的买家群体而言，可用的转售库存仍然太少。这是人们继续看到所有细分市场价格逐月上涨的根本原因。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">上个月在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AC%E6%8B%BF%E6%AF%94.md#1">本拿比</a>，典型房产的基准价格从本拿比北部的约</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 103 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元到本拿比东部的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 119 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元不等，南本拿比所有房产类型的基准价格为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 112 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元。北本拿比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月环比上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.9%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，东本拿比环比上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，南本拿比环比上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，但比去年价格略有下降。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">北本拿比的住宅基准价较一年前下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 3.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，但仍较三年前上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 26.8%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。自</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份以来，本拿比东部的联排价格略有下降，基准价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">863,500</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，但比三年前上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">27.1%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">REBGV </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">呼吁省政府调整首次购房者免征房产转让税的门槛。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">REBGV </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">指出，该地区<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E5%AF%93.md#1">公寓</a>的基准价格目前为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 76.7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元，而目前首次购房者的免税门槛为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">52.5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元。这是一个简单的政策调整，可以帮助更多的首次购房者立即买得起房。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> REBGV </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的数据，上个月，本拿比共售出</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">65 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%B1%8B.md#1">独立屋</a>，中位价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">200 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">67 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套城市屋，中位价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">99 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 258 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套公寓，中位价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">73.8 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">责任编辑：李盈</span></p>
