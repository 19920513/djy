<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>爱吃风味羊肉吗 9种香料一定要试</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000937-shutterstock_209564083-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>想获得更多的羊肉料理灵感，可以参考西餐善用香料的作法，例如中东的薄荷羊肉串、普罗旺斯香草羊排等。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年05月20日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%82%89.md#1">羊肉</a>（lamb）软嫩汁甜，可以简单清煮享用本味，也可以用更多<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E6%96%99.md#1">香料</a>激荡出丰富的味蕾体验。说到香料羊肉的作法，除了大名鼎鼎的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%9C%E7%84%B6.md#1">孜然</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E8%8C%B4%E9%A6%99.md#1">小茴香</a>，其实还有几种常见香料也能与羊肉融合出意想不到的好滋味。</p>
<h2>1. 迷迭香（Rosemary）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14000935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000935-shutterstock_319847762.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14000935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000935-shutterstock_319847762-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000935" class="wp-caption-text">干的或新鲜迷迭香皆适合搭配<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%82%89.md#1">羊肉</a>，作腌汁或出锅前撒一些都能激发羊肉鲜味。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>迷迭香原产于地中海，细细小小的叶片散发类似松叶的香气，新鲜或晒干都具足风味。用于烹饪，迷迭香烤鸡久负盛名，迷迭香羊肉亦经典搭配，如迷迭香加红酒慢炖羊肉、羊肉香肠等；而在需要快煮的羊排料理中，它能很快释放香气托出肉的鲜咸味。另外，用剥完叶子的迷迭香茎秆串羊肉串可增加香气。</p>
<h2>2. 百里香（Thyme）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14000931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000931-shutterstock_182026997.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14000931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000931-shutterstock_182026997-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000931" class="wp-caption-text">百里香烤羊排和小蕃茄。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>百里香的叶子虽然只有芝麻大小，但香气不可小瞧。它的优点是几乎与任何肉类合搭，也作为高汤、浇汁的灵魂<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E6%96%99.md#1">香料</a>。煎牛排、羊排快出锅时，撒上有一点似薄荷清凉，又有一点木质气息的百里香叶子，菜肴的咸鲜味立马丰富起来。</p>
<h2>3. 牛至（Oregano）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14000934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000934-shutterstock_6680098.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14000934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000934-shutterstock_6680098-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000934" class="wp-caption-text">混合牛至、大蒜、柠檬汁、迷迭香和橄榄油制作的羊肉腌汁。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>牛至用途广泛，以药用价值和芳香精油为人熟知。在烹饪方面，它是地中海美食不可或缺的香料，希腊的传统羊肉料理多有应用；同时它也是意大利披萨的知名香料，因而有“披萨草”之称。使用时，不同品种的牛至风味各异，希腊牛至的风味要比意大利牛至强烈，而干牛至胜于新鲜的。大蒜是极佳的羊肉调料，有的牛至品种与大蒜很合搭，比如盆栽马郁兰（pot marjoram）。</p>
<h2>4. 马郁兰（Marjoram）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14000938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000938" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000938-shutterstock_1277693386.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14000938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000938-shutterstock_1277693386-600x419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000938" class="wp-caption-text">马郁兰与牛至长相相似，但风味各异。图为新鲜的马郁兰。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>马郁兰的叶子形状与牛至很像，但味道更温和，散发淡淡的甜花香。两种香草都是牛至属植物。以区别野生马郁兰（wild marjoram or Origanum vulgare）和盆栽马郁兰（pot marjoramor or Or iganum onites）等牛至品种，马郁兰也称甜马郁兰（sweet marjoram）。烹饪时，取一些新鲜或干的碎叶子加入腌泡汁或混合调料中即可。</p>
<h2>5. 鼠尾草（Sage）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14000936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000936" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000936-shutterstock_1449988703.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14000936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000936-shutterstock_1449988703-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000936" class="wp-caption-text">干杏肉炖羊肉的调料中加入了鼠尾草。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鼠尾草是薄荷大家族的一员，有数百个品种分布于世界各地。它的气味似樟脑与胡椒的混合，又略有柠檬清香，入菜调味添加少量即能增强风味，用量过多食物会变苦。欧洲、美国和英国常用的几种肉类香料中，鼠尾草占有一席之地。美国感恩节烤火鸡，鼠尾草是不可或缺的香料，然而它与羊肉也合搭。</p>
<h2>6. 普罗旺斯香草（Herbes de Provence）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14000939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000939" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000939-shutterstock_2260866891.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14000939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000939-shutterstock_2260866891-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000939" class="wp-caption-text">普罗旺斯香草是一种香草混合，其中可能包含牛至、迷迭香、百里香、马郁兰和薰衣草等。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>想知道多种香草融合在一起的香味有多么奇妙，一定要试试普罗旺斯香草。法国人称之万能调料，炖、烤肉类、酱料、烘焙和沙拉等都会用到。它的配方与意大利混合香料相似，都是使用传统欧洲香草进行混搭，例如迷迭香、百里香、牛至、鼠尾草和马郁兰等。</p>
<h2>7. 薄荷（Mint）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14000930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000930-shutterstock_735572311.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14000930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000930-shutterstock_735572311-600x460.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="460" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000930" class="wp-caption-text">煎羊排搭配薄荷蒜泥酱。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>羊肉搭配新鲜薄荷是英格兰和中东地区的传统吃法。中东著名的羊肉串（Lamb kofta）呈现了大蒜与薄荷的完美融合。此外，新鲜薄荷还可与柠檬、酸奶等作成腌汁腌制羊肉，也可制成抹酱裹上厚厚一层享用，以及调制薄荷酱蘸着吃也很美味。烤羊肩肉、酸奶烤羊排、杏仁羊排等都有使用薄荷的作法。</p>
<h2>8. 欧芹（Parsley）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14000933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000933-shutterstock_1026310627.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14000933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000933-shutterstock_1026310627-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000933" class="wp-caption-text">欧芹可与羊肉一起炖煮，也可用于摆盘点缀。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>欧芹搭配蒜泥烘烤的面包片香气微妙，百吃不厌。烤羊肉时，如果手边没有其它香草，这组配方也会带来意想不到的美味。当然，搭配其它香草会带来更多风味层次。选择欧芹，平叶欧芹（flat-leaf parsley or Italian parsley）的香气较卷叶欧芹（curly parsley）丰富些。</p>
<h2>9. 月桂叶（Bay leaves）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14000932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000932-shutterstock_411768433.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14000932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000932-shutterstock_411768433-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000932" class="wp-caption-text">红酒汁慢煮羊腿。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>月桂叶非常适合慢煮料理，可以在炖饭、炖肉、慢烤或高汤中添加整叶，摆盘前取出。慢煮时，月桂叶的香气在慢火中缓缓释放，使品尝的每一口食物都有香草味，但又不喧宾夺主，例如月桂叶搭配红酒醋慢烤肌内脂肪均匀细腻的羊肩肉，就是一道无与伦比的美味佳肴。</p>
