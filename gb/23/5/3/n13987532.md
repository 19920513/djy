<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>本周波士顿华埠治安报告（5/5/2023）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12944547-DSC00998-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿华埠牌楼。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年05月04日讯】<span lang="ZH-TW">本周<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0%E6%B2%BB%E5%AE%89.md#1">波士顿华埠治安</a>报告由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F%E5%B1%80.md#1">波士顿警察局</a>提供。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-TW"><b><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%97%E7%AA%83.md#1">盗窃</a>商店</b></span></h3>
<p>4<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>24<span lang="ZH-TW">日下午</span>3:39<span lang="ZH-TW">，警员收到报告说</span>Stuart<span lang="ZH-TW">街</span>191<span lang="ZH-TW">号</span><wbr />CVS<span lang="ZH-TW">商店遭到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%97%E7%AA%83.md#1">盗窃</a>。该店经理说，<wbr />他看到嫌犯偷拿了店内的多件物品。<wbr />经理向警员展示了摄像头拍下的画面。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">警员在</span>Tamsworth<span lang="ZH-TW">街找到了嫌犯，后者承认自己从</span>CVS<span lang="ZH-TW">偷<wbr />了东西。他还承认自己有一把刀在身后的口袋里。<wbr />警员搜查了嫌犯的随身物品，并没收了这把刀。<wbr />嫌犯将因为盗窃商店而被法庭起诉。</span></p>
<h3><b>4<span lang="ZH-TW">月治安报告</span></b></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">波士顿警方在</span>5<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>3<span lang="ZH-TW">日的华埠治安会议中报告，</span>4<span lang="ZH-TW">月华埠地区的治安<wbr />非常平静。当地上个月发生了</span>3<span lang="ZH-TW">起盗窃案和</span>1<span lang="ZH-TW">起入侵商业设施案。<wbr />盗窃案包括</span>1<span lang="ZH-TW">起偷盗包裹，</span>1<span lang="ZH-TW">起入侵汽车盗窃物品，还有</span>1<span lang="ZH-TW">起入侵工<wbr />地盗窃物品。另外，警方</span>4<span lang="ZH-TW">月在华埠地区总共从事了</span>8<span lang="ZH-TW">次逮捕。</span>◇</p>
