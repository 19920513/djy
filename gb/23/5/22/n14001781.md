<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】五柳龙胆石斑～鱼肉酥嫩 酸甜带劲</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001830-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一片片的龙胆石斑鱼片下锅油炸，香酥鲜嫩，淋上酸甜五柳枝。在炎炎夏日到来时，让你食欲大开，下饭的好滋味。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><a class="gjw-iframe" title="2023年3月10日 五柳大石班" src="https://www.ganjing.com/zh-TW/embed/1fmi5iqgv1i3b9JIV64GrSt5Q13e1c" width="640" b="360" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p><strong>小编语</strong> “<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E6%9F%B3.md#1">五柳</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E6%96%91.md#1">石斑</a>”是香港常见的贺年菜，也是宴客的美味佳肴。由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8.md#1">梁厨</a>不藏私传授主厨手艺，不用整条<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鱼</a>下锅油炸，而是将珍珠龙胆<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E6%96%91.md#1">石斑</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鱼</a>切片，油炸后的口感与香气，酥脆表皮、鲜嫩肉质，一口咬下两种多层次口感，进入口中化开的鲜鱼带甘甜，加上“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E6%9F%B3.md#1">五柳</a>酸甜汁”，好吃到无法说出话来！在炎炎的夏日，食欲不振时，这一道菜一定让你好下饭，而且营养达标。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">五柳甜酸汁大石斑</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>珍珠龙胆石斑一条1.5磅<br />
甜杂锦菜4安士<br />
荞头4粒<br />
洋葱、红椒切丝各2 oz<br />
食用油1杯<br />
葱丝、芫荽适量（装饰用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14001822" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14001822" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001822-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14001822" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001822-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14001822" class="wp-caption-text">珍珠龙胆石斑，肉质鲜嫩、胶质满满。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【腌料】</span></h3>
<p>盐、胡椒粉和生粉各适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>水1杯<br />
白醋1汤匙<br />
糖1汤匙<br />
茄汁4汤匙<br />
粟粉水1汤匙（勾芡用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14001823" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14001823" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001823-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14001823" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001823-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14001823" class="wp-caption-text">调味料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 石斑鱼解冻后洗净，用刀起出鱼的头、尾和鱼肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14001824" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14001824" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001824-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14001824" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001824-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14001824" class="wp-caption-text">石斑鱼解冻后洗净，用刀起出鱼的头、尾和鱼肉。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 用盐、胡椒粉腌鱼肉，再抹上薄薄一层生粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14001825" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14001825" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001825-c8ea4290d40033fc0373d5f6676cc8cc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14001825" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001825-c8ea4290d40033fc0373d5f6676cc8cc-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14001825" class="wp-caption-text">用盐、胡椒粉腌鱼肉，再抹上薄薄一层生粉。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅下油1杯，转中大火，把鱼半煎炸至金黄熟透后，盛出装在碟上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14001827" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14001827" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001827-abbe891aa4231ae9032094723f04d70a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14001827" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001827-abbe891aa4231ae9032094723f04d70a-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14001827" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油1杯，转中大火，把鱼半煎炸至金黄熟透后，盛出装在碟上。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 倒掉多余的油，爆香洋葱、红椒及甜杂菜丝。加入水、白醋、糖和茄汁煮滚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14001828" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14001828" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001828-0e624556768bd7e4ac0899a8f91cf69f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14001828" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001828-0e624556768bd7e4ac0899a8f91cf69f-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14001828" class="wp-caption-text">制作酸甜“五柳”，要加入水、白醋、糖和茄汁煮滚。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 接着用粟粉水勾芡，淋在鱼上即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14001829" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14001829" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001829-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14001829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001829-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14001829" class="wp-caption-text">接着用粟粉水勾芡，淋在鱼上即完成。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【小贴士】</span></h3>
<p>?<strong>“甜杂锦菜”的俗称为“五柳料”，是将木瓜、青瓜、红萝卜丝等食材，加入醋和糖腌制而成。</strong>所谓五柳料，即三种甜杂锦菜丝，再加洋葱及红椒共5种材料。</p>
<p>?这次所用的鱼“珍珠龙胆石斑”，是来自台湾养殖的鱼类，已经去除肠脏、腮和鳞，所以非常干净且容易处理。</p>
<p>?鱼在摆盘时，应摆好头、尾、鱼身及鱼翅，排成一条鱼的形状。◇</p>
