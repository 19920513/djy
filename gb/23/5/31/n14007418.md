<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>林心如突预告台东开民宿 周兴哲等成员曝光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14007430-2305310946271487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>总制作人林心如推出光系列IP第二季《光开门就很忙了》。（TVBS提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年05月31日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E5%BF%83%E5%A6%82.md#1">林心如</a>今（31）日突然宣布推出新节目打造宠物友善民宿，集结<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E8%B0%A8%E5%8D%8E.md#1">杨谨华</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E6%84%8F%E6%B6%B5.md#1">陈意涵</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%AD%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%99.md#1">郭雪芙</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E5%85%B4%E5%93%B2.md#1">周兴哲</a>、章广辰和陈昊森。而作为民宿店长，林心如将带领团队克服困难和挑战，并在节目中展现其领导力和创造力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14007432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14007432" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14007432-2305310946341487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14007432" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14007432-2305310946341487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14007432" class="wp-caption-text">《光开门就很忙了》首支预告曝光，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E5%BF%83%E5%A6%82.md#1">林心如</a>经营民宿吐心声。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《光开门就很忙了》节目目标是打造一个独特而难忘的宠物友善民宿体验，并呈现成员们的经营方式。谈到找来这几位嘉宾成员班底的契机，林心如表示：“应该是一种直觉与信任。”</p>
<p>林心如说自己与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E8%B0%A8%E5%8D%8E.md#1">杨谨华</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E6%84%8F%E6%B6%B5.md#1">陈意涵</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%AD%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%99.md#1">郭雪芙</a>原本就熟识，找她们一起经营，可以很安心。</p>
<p>而在民宿中，主厨是不可或缺的角色，马上想到“型男主厨”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E5%85%B4%E5%93%B2.md#1">周兴哲</a>，这也是两人相隔9年再次合体，林心如说：“曾吃过他做的菜，他很擅长烹饪，有他就不用担心旅客的餐点。”</p>
<p>另外，民宿也需要体力活，章广辰主要任务是二厨，陈昊森则负责房务打理工作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14007434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14007434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14007434-2305310946311487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14007434" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14007434-2305310946311487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14007434" class="wp-caption-text">《光开门就很忙了》释出预告，图左起首席大厨章广辰、餐饮总监周兴哲。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《光开门就很忙了》选在台东进行取景拍摄，班底成员们一同在民宿录制十几天，总制作人林心如回忆起那段日子形容：“恍如一场梦，每天早上起床很累，晚上大家聚在一起又吃喝，聊得舍不得睡，这些过程让我们收获了一个很棒的友谊与团结的情感。”</p>
<p>此外，《光开门就很忙了》也是人气歌手周兴哲与新生代男星陈昊森首次参与实境节目。</p>
<p>今（31）日正式公开首支“开门篇”形象预告<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2hFW98BQvSc" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点这里</a>），曝光每位成员们职务内容，而成员们经营宠物友善民宿《光开门就很忙了》，即将于今年暑假正式上档。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14007436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14007436" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14007436-2305310946291487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14007436" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14007436-2305310946291487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14007436" class="wp-caption-text">《光开门就很忙了》首支预告曝光，郭雪芙为点心担当。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14007437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14007437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14007437-2305310948551487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14007437" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14007437-2305310948551487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14007437" class="wp-caption-text">《光开门就很忙了》首支预告曝光，杨谨华担任美学总监。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
