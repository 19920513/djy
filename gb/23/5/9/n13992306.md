<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】阿拉斯加蟹柳带子配荞麦面</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992319-12-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>阿拉斯加蟹柳带子配荞麦面（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语 </strong>清爽海味餐让你胃口大开！在家也可以轻松制作饭店餐点，而且一次补足蛋白质、膳食纤维。准备好蟹脚、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%A6%E5%AD%90.md#1">带子</a>，佐以粤菜经典调味“姜汁酒”，法式<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E8%99%BE%E6%B1%A4.md#1">龙虾汤</a>，和爽口日式<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%9E%E9%BA%A6%E9%9D%A2.md#1">荞麦面</a>，一盘上桌综合多国料理的元素，非常能满足口感。赶快来动手做看看吧！让饭店名厨梁志生教你零失败料理。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>阿拉斯加<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9F%B9%E6%9F%B3.md#1">蟹柳</a> 1.5磅<br />
日本急冻<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%A6%E5%AD%90.md#1">带子</a> 1磅（16至20）<br />
法式<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E8%99%BE%E6%B1%A4.md#1">龙虾汤</a> 1杯<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%A5%E5%85%B0.md#1">芥兰</a> 10棵<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%9E%E9%BA%A6%E9%9D%A2.md#1">荞麦面</a> 1扎<br />
姜汁酒 适量<br />
半个 柠檬<br />
草莓适量（装饰用）</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>油盐水1杯（用来烫<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%A5%E5%85%B0.md#1">芥兰</a>）</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 用剪刀把阿拉斯加蟹脚的壳剪开，取出蟹肉备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13992321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13992321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992321-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13992321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992321-3-450x249.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="249" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13992321" class="wp-caption-text">用剪刀把阿拉斯加蟹脚的壳剪开，取出蟹肉备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 冷冻带子放入滚水，加入姜汁酒及半个柠檬榨汁（连皮放入）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13992322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13992322" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992322-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13992322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992322-4-450x251.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="251" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13992322" class="wp-caption-text">冷冻带子放入滚水，加入姜汁酒及半个柠檬榨汁。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 熄火盖上盖子，浸泡5分钟后取出带子沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13992323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13992323" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992323-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13992323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992323-5-450x254.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13992323" class="wp-caption-text">取出带子沥干备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 荞麦面用水煮开至熟，约3分钟，捞出沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13992324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13992324" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992324-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13992324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992324-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13992324" class="wp-caption-text">荞麦面用水煮开至熟，约3分钟，捞出沥干备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 芥兰用油盐水烫熟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13992325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13992325" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992325-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13992325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992325-7-450x251.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="251" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13992325" class="wp-caption-text">芥兰用油盐水烫熟备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 热锅下油，将带子两面煎至金黄后取出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13992333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13992333" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992333-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13992333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992333-8-450x245.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="245" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13992333" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，将带子两面煎至金黄后取出。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 再把<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9F%B9%E6%9F%B3.md#1">蟹柳</a>稍微煎一下，取出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13992335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13992335" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992335-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13992335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992335-9-450x245.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="245" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13992335" class="wp-caption-text">再把蟹柳稍微煎一下，取出备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 龙虾汤煮热之后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13992336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13992336" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992336-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13992336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992336-10-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13992336" class="wp-caption-text">龙虾汤煮热之后备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 摆盘：荞麦面放盘子中央，放上带子及蟹脚，芥兰摆旁边，淋上龙虾汁，并放上草莓作装饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13992337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13992337" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992337-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13992337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13992337-11-450x245.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="245" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13992337" class="wp-caption-text">摆盘。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【食谱说明】</span></h3>
<p>因为龙虾汤已经够味道，所以不用再加调味。◇</p>
