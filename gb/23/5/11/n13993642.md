<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>竹南农会推食农教育“酱凤梨”DIY 副县长吁多采购凤梨</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993650-613834-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>苗副县长邓桂菊表示：现在《食农教育法》通过，民众对食用当季有概念，吃健康新鲜水果很重要，正值凤梨盛产，希望孩子多吃，有益身体健康，还可以入菜煮凤梨鸡汤等，希望大家一起帮助农民，食用在地当季蔬菜水果。（许享富／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年05月11日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苗栗县竹南镇<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E4%BC%9A.md#1">农会</a>推广<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E5%86%9C%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">食农教育</a>，结合<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%93%E5%AD%A3.md#1">当季</a>盛产<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%A4%E6%A2%A8.md#1">凤梨</a>推出DIY活动，11日安排“食农教育-凤梨推推推”带领学员制作“酱凤梨”。苗栗县副县长邓桂菊也到场关心大家，并且推销<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%93%E5%AD%A3.md#1">当季</a>食材，鼓励大家踊跃采购。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13993643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993643" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993643-613829.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13993643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993643-613829-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993643" class="wp-caption-text">竹南镇<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E4%BC%9A.md#1">农会</a>响应农委会的农食<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%A4%E6%A2%A8.md#1">凤梨</a>推广活动制作酱凤梨（凤梨豆酱）。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13993645" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993645" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993645-613831.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13993645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993645-613831-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993645" class="wp-caption-text">竹南镇农会的凤梨推推推，享受酿造豆酱的乐趣 。 （许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾的气候和环境很适合种凤梨，台湾目前全年都有产凤梨，不仅新鲜，又甜又多汁，台湾还有许多品种。竹南镇农会举办“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E5%86%9C%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">食农教育</a>-凤梨推推推”活动，除了凤梨促销以外，11日于农会推广教育大楼举办“酱凤梨”DIY食农体验，响应产销调节多元应用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13993649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993649-613833.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13993649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993649-613833-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993649" class="wp-caption-text">黄梨（凤梨）的多元化美食。 （许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13993651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993651-613835.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13993651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993651-613835-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993651" class="wp-caption-text">黄梨水晶粽。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苗栗县副县长邓桂菊今日前往竹南镇农会关心行销活动，希望学员们学会之后，料理出更丰盛的佳肴，同时也期待乡亲多采购，帮助农民也吃得健康。农会指导老师细心教导学员制作酱凤梨，现场展示许多美味料理，包括酱凤梨焖虱目鱼、凤梨苦瓜鸡汤，还有解暑的凤梨冰茶、甜点凤梨娘惹糕、凤梨酥、凤梨果酱等，推广当地优质农特产。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13993644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993644-613830.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13993644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993644-613830-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993644" class="wp-caption-text">竹南镇农会总干事蔡信芬表示，纤维酵素高对人体有益，配合农委会及苗栗县农会结合农会体系帮忙协助凤梨行销，认识凤梨，创意食谱佳肴，农会自营社区小铺，也为农友行销农特产品及其它县农会产销合作，为农会带来商机。 （许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13993646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993646-613832.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13993646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993646-613832-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993646" class="wp-caption-text">凤梨豆酱做法。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>竹南镇农会总干事蔡信芬表示，凤梨甜又好吃，纤维酵素高对人体有益，而目前面临凤梨产销过量，农委会及苗栗县农会等单位结合农会体系帮忙协助凤梨行销，因此今天以凤梨推推推DIY酱凤梨体验，教导学员们认识凤梨、制作酱凤梨，未来可加在料理之中，发展更多食谱佳肴，农会自营社区小铺也有贩售凤梨，欢迎乡亲多多选购。◇</p>
