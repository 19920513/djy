<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园电动公车抢救避难演练 增加逃生机会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006497-616756-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>电动公车抢救及避难演练，提升员工的应变能力及消防抢救安全。（桃园消防局第一大队大林消防分队提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年05月30日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）电动车辆具有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E4%BF%9D.md#1">环保</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%82%E8%83%BD.md#1">节能</a>的优势，路上能见度及厂牌越来越多，但由于电池起火后难以扑灭及高压电线路易触电，让消防人员的救灾工作增添风险及困难。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006495" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006495-616754.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14006495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006495-616754-600x631.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="473" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006495" class="wp-caption-text">模拟<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%8A%A8%E5%85%AC%E8%BD%A6.md#1">电动公车</a>于厂内充电时起火，员工发现立即启动自卫消防编组，除了灭火、通报及避难引导。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>消防局第一大队大林消防分队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大林消防分队与桃园客运修车厂共同办理<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%8A%A8%E5%85%AC%E8%BD%A6.md#1">电动公车</a>抢救及避难演练，提升员工的应变能力及消防抢救安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006496" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006496-616755.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14006496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006496-616755-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006496" class="wp-caption-text">关闭充电桩及配电盘的电源。（桃园消防局第一大队大林消防分队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演练过程模拟电动公车于厂内充电时起火，员工发现立即启动自卫消防编组，除了灭火、通报及避难引导外，还必须关闭充电桩及配电盘的电源，移除周围电池零件及可燃物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006498" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006498" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006498-616757.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14006498" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006498-616757-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006498" class="wp-caption-text">搭公车如发生事故或火灾时也切勿惊慌。（桃园消防局第一大队大林消防分队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>消防人员演练如何将电动公车断电、破坏车体时应避开高压电线路及对车体和电池降温，提升员工灾害应变能力及确保救援过程中消防人员及民众的安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006499" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006499" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006499-616758.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14006499" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006499-616758-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006499" class="wp-caption-text">救火训练。（桃园消防局第一大队大林消防分队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大林消防分队长黄章玮表示，演练前厂方针对电动公车及充电桩的构造、电池及高压电线路配置进行详细说明及解说，不同厂牌电动公车的构造及线路配置亦不尽相同，消防人员必须熟练断电程序及破坏车体时应避开高压电线，透过演练降低抢救时触电风险并提升人员救灾安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006500" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006500-616759.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14006500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006500-616759-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006500" class="wp-caption-text">应冷静配合司机引导逃生。（桃园消防局第一大队大林消防分队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园客运修车厂庄镇州厂长也提醒，民众搭乘公车或大众运输工具时，应注意灭火器、车窗击破器及紧急出口位置。如发生事故或火灾时也切勿惊慌，应冷静配合司机引导逃生，车上亦配备灭火器可供灭火，车侧及车顶设有紧急出口可供逃生。当浓烟影响逃生时，可使用车窗击破器敲击车窗玻璃四个角落，建立开口快速逃生。◇</p>
