<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2023PNE夏季嘉年华将于8月开锣</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年05月26日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">柳晴</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>的夏天已至，不少人都在规划周末的行程，太平洋国家<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%9A%E8%A7%88%E4%BC%9A.md#1">博览会</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(PNE)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">近日公布了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2023</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年夏季<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%8E.md#1">嘉年华</a>的节目单供您选择。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">今年的PNE<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%8E.md#1">嘉年华</a>将于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">19</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日举行，这是卑诗省历史最悠久、规模最大的家庭友好型娱乐活动。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">24</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">PNE</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">首席执行</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Shelley Frost</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">在新闻发布会上说：“很高兴</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">PNE</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%9A%E8%A7%88%E4%BC%9A.md#1">博览会</a>能够重新运作，为所有年龄段的人士提供独特的娱乐活动。我们有一系列精彩的新节目、新娱乐项目和景点，迫不及待地期待大家来体验。”</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">今年夏日音乐会再度回归，“我们对今年的演出阵容感到兴奋”，创意总监</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Patrick Roberge</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">说，“包括格莱美获奖者、说唱偶像及</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">PNE</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">粉丝最喜爱的歌星。我们真的希望每一个夜晚都充满了夏日乐趣。”</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">美食展示会是今年新增项目，在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Safeway</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">烹饪舞台上，本地的烹饪专家将示范如果制作美食和日常料理，你还可以在室内的烹饪市场找到特色食品和厨房配件。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">PNE</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">孩子们会见到他们最喜爱的小动物们，从农场动物到经久不衰的超级狗表演，当你坐下来品尝美食时还可以观看一个全新的放映节目</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">——</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">《动物趣事（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Animal Antics</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）》。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">门票：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">PNE</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">博览会的门票必须购买特定日期的门票，只有在网上门票没有售罄的情况下，才会在门口提供。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">PNE</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">建议所有客人提前购票，以免失望。门票（不含游乐设施）从</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">25</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元不等，取决于年龄和购买时间。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E4%B9%90%E5%9C%BA.md#1">游乐场</a>的游乐券是</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，或者你可以购买</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">32-55</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元的通票。有很多方法可以节省你的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">PNE</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">门票。详情请登录PNE Fair查询:</span><ahref="http://www.pne.ca/"><span style="font-weight: 400;"> www.pne.ca</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">时间：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">PNE</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的博览会于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">19</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日上午</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">点至晚</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">点开放（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">21</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">28</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日关闭）</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">地点：温哥华E Hastings街2901号?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
