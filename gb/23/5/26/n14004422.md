<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组合植栽DIY：墨西哥铁树多层次陶盆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004530-666169dcb0a76ebeee8e388d4a0bbb42-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>墨西哥铁树多层次陶盆。（苹果屋出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>以带有椭圆形叶片、极具魅力的墨西哥<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%81%E6%A0%91.md#1">铁树</a>，搭配流畅树形的灰绿冷水花，两种风格一起栽种在陶瓷花盆内，虽然外型不同，但颜色与彩度相近，视觉上相互融合。</p>
<h2>材料</h2>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9.md#1">植物</a>&amp;容器</strong></p>
<p>墨西哥<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%81%E6%A0%91.md#1">铁树</a>（幸运树）、灰绿冷水花、陶瓷花盆</p>
<p><strong>介质&amp;装饰</strong></p>
<p>轻石、观叶<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9.md#1">植物</a>培养土、鹅卵石（各种大小）、收尾材料（灰色小石头）</p>
<p><strong>工具</strong></p>
<p>花盆垫片、铲子、浇水壶</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004429" style="width: 512px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004429-P.53-2.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14004429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004429-P.53-2.jpg" alt="" width="512" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004429" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14004428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004428-P.53-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14004428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004428-P.53-1-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004428" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>Gardener’s Note</strong></span></h2>
<p>一个花盆也能有多层次的栽种，植物紧密种在一起和分散开的感觉大不相同，这个作品以墨西哥铁树搭配周围的灰绿冷水花，呈现截然不同的气氛。</p>
<p>由于花盆质地和植物本身都呈现柔和感，因此选用圆形的鹅卵石收尾。若罗列摆放多个大小相近的石头，不免会有单调、人为感，所以使用大小不一的石头，带出层次感，并营造自然的氛围。</p>
<p>进行<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%8D%E6%A0%BD.md#1">植栽</a>设计时，难免会遇到素材过多，不知从何下手的情况，但别忘了“simple is the best”，简洁有力有时候才是最好的选择。如果想要营造华丽感，与其加入各种收尾材料，不如加强设计亮点，这么做也有助于提升作品的质感。</p>
<h2>How to Make</h2>
<p>1. 在花盆的排水孔处先铺上垫片，以利排水、防止介质流失。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004430" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004430-P.54-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004430-P.54-1-300x301.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004430" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 略压植物的外盆，让土和盆分离之后，一手抓盆，另一手抓植物的底部，将植物脱盆。检查根部状态，剪除过黑和过软的部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004431" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004431-P.54-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004431-P.54-2-300x298.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004431" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将植物放到花盆旁边，大致测量排水层的高度，再铺上轻石。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004432" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004432-P.54-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004432-P.54-3-300x299.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004432" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 放入观叶植物培养土，铺至八到九分满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004433" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004433-P.54-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004433-P.54-4-300x299.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004433" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>TIP 观叶植物培养土是将基本用土加上轻石或蛭石以7：3的比例调配。</p>
<p>5. 先决定墨西哥铁树的位置，再挖一个洞种入。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004434" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004434-P.55-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004434-P.55-1-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004434" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将灰绿冷水花分成2-3份。</p>
<p>TIP 不建议从根部开始分离，最好是先把茎整理过，由上而下分离比较好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004435" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004435-P.55-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004435-P.55-2-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004435" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 先决定最大株的灰绿冷水花的位置，再挖开土种入。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004436" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004436-P.55-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004436-P.55-3-300x299.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004436" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 在其他空位种入小株的灰绿冷水花，并且让植物自然往下垂放。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004437" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004437-P.55-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004437-P.55-4-300x301.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004437" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 种好植物后，再整理好土壤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004438" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004438-P.56-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004438-P.56-1-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004438" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 充分浇水，用手略微压实可让土壤牢固。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004439" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004439-P.56-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004439-P.56-2-300x298.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004439" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 接着在最空的地方放上大颗的鹅卵石。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004440" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004440-P.56-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004440-P.56-3-300x299.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004440" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12. 在大鹅卵石旁边放入小鹅卵石。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004441" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004441-P.56-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004441-P.56-4-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004441" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>13. 为避免鹅卵石呈现一直线，在后方的空位再补上一些小鹅卵石。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004442" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004442-P.57-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004442-P.57-1-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004442" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>14. 最后方的空间也补上小鹅卵石。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004443" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004443-P.57-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004443-P.57-2-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004443" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>15. 在剩下的空间里，均匀放上收尾材料。墨西哥铁树的根部较为脆弱，放置时要稍微避开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004444" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004444" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004444-P.57-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14004444" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004444-P.57-3-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004444" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>照顾方式</h2>
<p>建议摆放在室内光线充足的明亮地方。两种植物都不适合过湿的环境，土壤干燥时再浇水即可。但由于浇水时间间隔长，容易忽略湿度，要记得定期喷雾。假如太过干燥，可能会发生介壳虫的虫害，务必留意。墨西哥铁树通常一年会长叶一次，灰绿冷水花相对生长较快，若太过繁盛导致不成比例，就要定期修剪，维持适当的树形。</p>
