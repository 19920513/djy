<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园杨梅分局救援考生顺利应考</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001158-615328-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>考生发现自己跑错考场，着急向警方求助。（桃园市杨梅警分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年05月21日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）桃园市杨梅区一名林姓国中生，20日早上7时许抵达杨梅治平高中准备参加会考之际，才发现自己跑错考场，着急向警方求助，员警得知后立即载送她至正确考场，让林姓<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%83%E7%94%9F.md#1">考生</a>得以顺利<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%94%E8%80%83.md#1">应考</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14001159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14001159" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001159-615329.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14001159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001159-615329-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14001159" class="wp-caption-text">员警得知后立即载送她至正确考场。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市杨梅警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%A2%85%E5%88%86%E5%B1%80.md#1">杨梅分局</a>草湳派出所警员陈建宏、黄臆錤于20日早上7时许巡逻时，一名15岁林姓国中生向警方求助，表示她参加国中会考，抵达杨梅治平高中考场后才发现她正确考场是在杨梅永平工商，但家长已离开，且身上只有些许零钱，警方得知后，立即驱车将学生载至正确考场地点，让学生顺利<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%94%E8%80%83.md#1">应考</a>，而林生也向警方表示万分感谢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14001160" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14001160" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001160-615330.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14001160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14001160-615330-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14001160" class="wp-caption-text">学生顺利应考，而林生也向警方表示万分感谢。（桃园市杨梅警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警方呼吁，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%83%E7%94%9F.md#1">考生</a>与家长在赴考前几天，可实际到场观察，并计算交通时间以事先规划路线，应试当日可提早出门，避免突发状况发生而来不及因应。◇</p>
