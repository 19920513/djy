<h6>Jeep Wrangler 4xe驶入水洼。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年05月13日讯】（大纪元记者李奥美国西雅图报导）以泥泞（Mud）为主题的活动，是美国西北汽车报业协会（NWAPA）于每年四月份举办的活动，在离西雅图市西南的Ridge Motosports Park进行。由美西北地区的各界车评人参与测试及评分，最后选出各类别最佳汽车及年度户外活动车（NW Outdoor Activity Vehicle of the Year）。</p>
<p><a width="560" b="315" src=""></a><a href="https://d27hok1oawuvj8.cloudfront.net/T1mGLMpth"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/M6H7ec1yC-Y" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen></a></p>
<p>Mudfest因疫情关系停办了数年，于去年重新举行，这次是大纪元记者第二次参加，活动分为赛道及越野测试，今年测试的汽车共有22辆，今年分组与去年不同，这屇汽车种类分为“两排座休旅车（Two-Row Family SUV）”、“三排座休旅车（Three-Row Family SUV）”、“豪华休旅车（Luxury SUV）”和“皮卡（Pickup Truck）”，最后会经投票选出以上四种类别车辆外，也会选出“极限能力车（Extreme Capability Vehicle）”和“最超值休旅车（Best Value SUV or Pickup）”，以及顺应电车潮流的“电动化多用途车（Electrified Utility Vehicle）”。</p>

<figure id="attachment_13995176" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13995176" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995176-S3530028.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995176-S3530028-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-13995176" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13995176" class="wp-caption-text">Rolls-Royce Cullinan。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在“两排座休旅车”类别中，测试车共有7辆，分别有Honda CR-V Hybrid、Jeep Compass Trailhawk、Jeep Wrangler 4xe、Kia Sportage X-Pro、Mazda CX-50 Meridian、Subaru Crosstrek和Volkswagen Tiguan。<br />
“三排座休旅车”有6辆，为Honda Pilot TrailSport、Hyundai Palisade XRT、Kia Telluride X-Pro、Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV、Nissan Pathfinder Rock Creek和Toyota Sequioa TRD Pro。<br />
“豪华休旅车”类别包括Acura RDX A-Spec、BMW X7 M60i、Genesis Electrified GV70、Lexus RX 500h、Rolls-Royce Cullinan及Wagoneer Series II。<br />
“皮卡”类别的测试车包括Ford F-150 Raptor R、Hyundai Santa Cruz及Toyota Tundra TRD Pro。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13995177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13995177" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995177-S3540001.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995177-S3540001-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-13995177" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13995177" class="wp-caption-text">越野路段测试。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13995178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13995178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995178-S3540013.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995178-S3540013-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-13995178" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13995178" class="wp-caption-text">Acura RDX A-Spec。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13995179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13995179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995179-S3540015.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995179-S3540015-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-13995179" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13995179" class="wp-caption-text">Jeep Wrangler 4xe挑战极端路段。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一日测试在柏油路面及赛道上进行，场内设置了加速区、刹车区测试汽车的加速及刹车，并在设置的路障间行驶，测试汽车的过弯表现。继而可驶入赛道上走一圈，试验汽车的实际行驶表现。今年测试区域也有增大，让各测试者有更长路段测试汽车。可惜第一天不时下雨及冰雹，对刹车距离、加速及过弯都影响甚大。<br />
?<br />
第二天在设置的越野路段行驶，有碎石路段、水坑、凹凸不平路段、不同倾斜度的山坡等，务求测试出汽车的越野能力及涉水能力。其中有些路段为极端（Extreme）路况，只会让部分经强化的车辆测试。部分车厂会有厂方人员陪同，讲解该款汽车的特别功能，例如Jeep Wrangler所配备的Sway Bar功能。整天都在雨水与泥泞中进行测试，可以进一步了解同为休旅车，却有非常不同的行车表现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13995180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13995180" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995180-S3540019.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995180-S3540019-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-13995180" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13995180" class="wp-caption-text">Jeep Compass Trailhawk。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13995181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13995181" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995181-S3540024.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13995181-S3540024-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-13995181" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13995181" class="wp-caption-text">Kia Sportage。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>经过两天的道路及越野测试，最后经过各车评人投票选出各类别冠亚军，以及年度户外活动车：<br />
最佳两排座休旅车 – Jeep Wrangler 4xe 20th Anniversary Rubicon; 亚军 – Kia Sportage X-Pro?最佳三排座休旅车 – Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV；亚军?– Toyota Sequoia TRD Pro?最佳豪华休旅车 –?Genesis Electrified GV70; 亚军 –?Wagoneer Series II?最佳皮卡 –?Ford F-150 Raptor R；亚军 –?Hyundai Santa Cruz?最佳极端越野能力车 –?Jeep Wrangler 4xe 20th Anniversary Rubicon；亚军 –?Ford F-150 Raptor R?最佳电动化多用途车 -Genesis Electrified GV70； 亚军 –?Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV?最超值休旅车 –?Subaru Crosstrek 亚军 –?Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV<br />
最后，今年的2023年“最佳户外用途车”经投票选出为Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV，亚军为Genesis Electrified GV70。<br />
?<br />
经过两天不断测试不同汽车的表现，身心疲累，但是测试超过45万美元的Rolls-Royce Cullinan以及12多万的BMW X7 M60i，驾驶这些豪华车在充满泥泞的越野路段纵横，是一次不错的体验！◇</p>
