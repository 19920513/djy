<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《营业中》惊见大鳄上门 吴映洁吓喊：这我不行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13900746-2301060337501487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《嗨！营业中》将播出第13集，伙伴们接到新任务。图左为大鳄造访。（好看娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年01月06日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）实境节目《嗨！<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A5%E4%B8%9A%E4%B8%AD.md#1">营业中</a>》本周将播出第13集，伙伴们接到新任务“柴城那里”主题日一起迎接可爱动物和宠物主人，一开始是可爱动物区的动物，接下来就有珍禽异兽级萌宠出现，让伙伴们频频发出惊叹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13900748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13900748" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13900748-2301060337331487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13900748" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13900748-2301060337331487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13900748" class="wp-caption-text">《嗨！<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A5%E4%B8%9A%E4%B8%AD.md#1">营业中</a>》将播出第13集，来宾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E4%BD%B3%E8%A7%81.md#1">谢佳见</a>（左）与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%9F%E6%AC%A3%E5%87%8C.md#1">钟欣凌</a>（中）带着爱犬光临。（好看娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>首先是金钟视后<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%9F%E6%AC%A3%E5%87%8C.md#1">钟欣凌</a>带着宠物狗狗和戏剧男星<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E4%BD%B3%E8%A7%81.md#1">谢佳见</a>宝贝柴犬莅临，人气歌手娄峻硕担任本集小帮手，为宠物和主人的餐点忙碌张罗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13900749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13900749" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13900749-2301060337411487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13900749" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13900749-2301060337411487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13900749" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%97%A8%EF%BC%81%E8%90%A5%E4%B8%9A%E4%B8%AD.md#1">嗨！营业中</a>》将播出第13集，伙伴们接到新任务。来宾钟欣凌（右）看到象龟光临直呼太夸张。（好看娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着还有麝香猪的到来、55公斤象龟一来就占地盘踩住浩子的脚，让浩子哇哇大叫。下一秒来宾钟欣凌直说“你们真的太夸张！”这时，门口缓缓出现了黑色悍马车，竟然走下来了两只羊驼分别麦可和GaGa。</p>
<p>店长吴映洁见状大叫：“羊驼来了！”羊驼GaGa一看到草皮立刻在草上翻滚模样十分可爱，萌翻众人。连一旁的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%8E%E4%BA%9A%E7%BA%B6.md#1">炎亚纶</a>也直说：“好不真实！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13900751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13900751" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13900751-2301060337461487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13900751" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13900751-2301060337461487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13900751" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%97%A8%EF%BC%81%E8%90%A5%E4%B8%9A%E4%B8%AD.md#1">嗨！营业中</a>》将播出第13集，伙伴们接到新任务。图为羊驼造访。（好看娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>然而，接着让人觉得更不可思议的宠物也大驾光临，一只巨型大鳄鱼出现在门口，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%8E%E4%BA%9A%E7%BA%B6.md#1">炎亚纶</a>一看到连忙逃开，直接躲在店长吴映洁的后面。吴映洁受惊吓大叫：“这个我不行！我手会断掉。”接着大伙儿纷纷落荒而逃。</p>
<p>究竟伙伴们将如何招待这些神奇宝贝？《嗨！营业中》将在周六台视晚上8点首播。</p>
