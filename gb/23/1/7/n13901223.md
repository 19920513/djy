<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】涡轮增乐趣 2023 Nissan Altima SR VC-Turbo</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901250-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月07日讯】这星期一共测试了两辆日系汽车，一辆是豪华品牌Lexus的NX 450h+，以省油及耗油量低为主，而且是Plug-In Hybrid，在日常使用上下班，回家充电就可减少使用燃油。至于另一辆，虽然都是日本品牌，却有另一番乐趣，Nissan Altima SR VC-Turbo是以驾驶乐趣而设计。</p>
<p><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://dhw62qyyxrv4n.cloudfront.net/F4dSWFgLl"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/u-gXu2rozb4" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Nissan Altima自2019年已经更新改款，现在是第六代车款，2023年款会有外貌小升级，车头车尾部分的设计更新，新车颜色及轮圈设计都有不同。前脸采用全新V-motion进风口栏栅设计，SR版在车前后都有“SR”徽章，装有SR专用银黑五幅式轮圈。Altima的C柱黑色设计，营造出“浮顶（floating roof）”的效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13901227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13901227" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901227-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13901227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901227-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13901227" class="wp-caption-text">2023 Nissan Altima SR VC-Turbo。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%A5%A5.md#1">李奥</a>／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一般Nissan Altima不论任何型号都配备2.5升直四DIG引擎，最大马力只有188匹，峰值扭矩180磅呎；如配备AWD版本，马力会减少到182匹，扭矩也减少至178磅呎。测试的SR VC Turbo版是马力最强的一款，装有2升直四VC Turbo涡轮增压引擎，将马力提升到248匹及273磅呎扭矩。变速箱则只有一款，就是Xtronic CVT，反应比以前的CVT快了很多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13901228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13901228" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901228-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13901228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901228-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_07-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13901228" class="wp-caption-text">SR专用银黑五幅式轮圈。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%A5%A5.md#1">李奥</a>／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从SV、SR到SL三种型号，都可选择加装四驱系统，只有S入门版及最高版SR VC Turbo这两款只提供前驱（FWD）选择，不用四驱可减少动力损耗，但是在下雨下雪的日子便要小心，开着大马力前驱汽车比较容易打滑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13901230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13901230" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901230-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13901230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901230-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13901230" class="wp-caption-text">V-motion进风口栏栅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内装方面，测试的SR VC Turbo是黑色内装，配以银色装饰，并有红色缝线。方向盘及变速杆顶部都以皮革包裹。引擎启动键设在变速杆前方位置，更为方便，在中控台底部是手机无线充电板。中控台顶部是一个12.3吋触控式萤幕，集多功能于一身，导航地图搜寻有一个G字，是Google的地图系统。音响系统设有AM／FM／SXM收音机，而且可支援无线Apple Car Play及Android Autos，车内也有Hot Spot无线热点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13901232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13901232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901232-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_24.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13901232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901232-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13901232" class="wp-caption-text">2升直四VC Turbo涡轮增压引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>座位采用NASA认可的“零重力（Zero Gravity）”的低疲劳脊柱支撑座椅，可减少车程为乘客带来的疲累。驾驶席是8方向电动座位，前乘客座则有4方向电动控制。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13901233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13901233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901233-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_35.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13901233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901233-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_35-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13901233" class="wp-caption-text">座位采用零重力低疲劳脊柱支撑座椅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Altima的所有型号均装有“Nissan Safety Shield 360”， 此安全套装技术包括具有行人检测功能的自动紧急制动、盲点警告系统、后方交叉交通警报、车道偏离警告、远光灯辅助和倒车自动刹车等。除了Safety Shield 360，Altima还有智能巡航控制系统。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13901235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13901235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901235-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_26.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13901235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901235-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13901235" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板设计传统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13901236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13901236" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901236-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_27.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13901236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901236-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_27-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13901236" class="wp-caption-text">12.3吋触控式萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Altima SR VC Turbo版的动力表现出色，引擎声浪响亮，排气声也是雄厚，可惜仍配Xtronic CVT变速箱，令动力输出变得太柔和，动力澎湃感失色不少，希望Nissan可将手动变速列为选碰，并可加入四驱系统，那么Altima SR VC Turbo将大大增加驾驶乐趣！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13901238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13901238" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901238-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13901238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13901238-2023_Nissan_Atlima_SR_02-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13901238" class="wp-caption-text">2023 Nissan Altima SR VC-Turbo。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
