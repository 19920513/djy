<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>阿伯茨福德医院迎来卑诗省新年宝宝</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13897715-91ece87cd836c1711a4cbaa4bc972731-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023年新年宝宝于1月1日12时02分在阿伯茨福德地区医院出生。（菲沙卫生局图片）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月02日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">杨清清</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4%E5%AE%9D%E5%AE%9D.md#1">新年宝宝</a>是个女婴！”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>府工作人员1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">发推祝贺<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4%E5%AE%9D%E5%AE%9D.md#1">新年宝宝</a>来到这个世界。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2023</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年卑诗省第一个新生儿于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">时</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">02</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E4%BC%AF%E8%8C%A8%E7%A6%8F%E5%BE%B7.md#1">阿伯茨福德</a>地区医院（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Abbotsford Regional Hospital</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）出生。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Gabriella</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">体重</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">磅</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">盎司。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">据</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Global New</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">报导，</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">刚刚当上爸爸的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Arben Camayang</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">说：“我们没想到她来得这么快，我们本以为要等上</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">天。”??</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">整个生产过程仅用了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分钟。第一次当妈妈的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Thea Villaneuva</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">说一切都很顺利，“宝宝做得很好，医生和护士都很棒，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Gabriella</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">真的很健康。”　　　　</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">菲沙卫生局也欢迎新年宝宝来到这个世界，并向她的父母表示祝贺。</span></p>
<h2><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E4%BF%9D%E7%BD%97%E5%8C%BB%E9%99%A2.md#1">圣保罗医院</a>迎新年女婴 重9磅</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">同日，天佑护理中心（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Providence Health Care</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）宣布，位于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E4%BF%9D%E7%BD%97%E5%8C%BB%E9%99%A2.md#1">圣保罗医院</a>于1月1日凌晨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">时</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">54</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分迎来了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2023</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年的第一个婴儿。女婴</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hana顶着一头黑黑的小卷毛，白白胖胖，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">体重超过</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">磅（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4125</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">克）。◇</span></p>
