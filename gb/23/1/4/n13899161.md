<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>县长颁发乡镇市民代表会正副主席当选证书</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899165-593908-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>与会嘉宾合影。（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月04日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%97%E6%A0%97.md#1">苗栗</a>报导）苗栗县长钟东锦1月4日起，陆续颁发乡镇市民代表会主席、副主席<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%93%E9%80%89%E8%AF%81%E4%B9%A6.md#1">当选证书</a>，并致赠贺匾，期望精诚<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%A2%E7%BB%93.md#1">团结</a>，齐心协力为地方<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%BA%E8%AE%BE.md#1">建设</a>努力打拼。</p>
<p>今天上午，县长钟东锦由县府民政处长彭基山陪同下，至竹南镇民代表会颁发主席何秀绵、副主席李城的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%93%E9%80%89%E8%AF%81%E4%B9%A6.md#1">当选证书</a>，并分别致赠“民意良首”、“辅政为民”贺匾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13899162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13899162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899162-593905.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13899162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899162-593905-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13899162" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%97%E6%A0%97.md#1">苗栗</a>县长钟东锦颁发镇代会主席当选赠书。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13899164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13899164" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899164-593907.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13899164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899164-593907-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13899164" class="wp-caption-text">苗栗县长钟东锦颁赠当选证书。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长钟东锦恭贺何秀绵、李城获得代表支持与肯定，连任主席、副主席，希望能够群策群力，县府、县议会、乡镇市公所、代表会、村里长大家一条心，齐心协力<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%BA%E8%AE%BE.md#1">建设</a>家乡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13899163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13899163" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899163-593906.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13899163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899163-593906-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13899163" class="wp-caption-text">苗栗县长钟东锦颁赠贺匾。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>各乡镇市主席、副主席：苗栗市陈仁杰、王封台；头份市温宜静、张家荣；竹南镇何秀绵、李城；苑里镇洪晃琦、林俊廷；后龙镇花丽卿、周政发；通霄镇徐淑云、黄启森；卓兰镇詹永光、詹秉宪；公馆乡赵兴邦、彭旻萱；铜锣乡林九炲、赖碧云；三义乡林盈富、张玉凤；大湖乡谢见德、陈永豪；造桥乡陈裕福、廖义勇；头屋乡涂金台、范贤达；西湖乡古召善、吴峻毅；三湾乡邱腾森、彭秀珠；狮潭乡林昌元、谢发汶；南庄乡黄顺源、庄新妹；泰安乡陈道光、叶纯辉。◇</p>
