<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新年将至  桃园杨梅警卖场预防犯罪宣导</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13905862-595544.jpg" />
<h6>杨梅警分局前往辖内超市、各大卖场进行预防犯罪宣导，传递各项预防犯罪相关资讯。（桃园市杨梅警分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月13日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）桃园市政府警察局杨梅分局鉴于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">新年</a>将至，民众开始采买年货，为提醒民众加强新年期间“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E6%8A%A2%E3%80%81%E9%98%B2%E7%AA%83%E3%80%81%E5%8F%8D%E8%AF%88%E9%AA%97.md#1">防抢、防窃、反诈骗</a>”，前往辖内超市、各大卖场进行预防<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8A%AF%E7%BD%AA%E5%AE%A3%E5%AF%BC.md#1">犯罪宣导</a>，传递各项预防犯罪相关资讯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13905863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13905863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13905863-595545-e1673582220277.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13905863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13905863-595545-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13905863" class="wp-caption-text">宣导在人潮众多的卖场及市场采买年货时应注意自身财务，留意扒窃趁乱窃取皮包或随身物品。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%A2%85%E8%AD%A6.md#1">杨梅警</a>分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>杨梅分局草湳派出所长张杰凯、警员吴俊宪、张博竣等人，于辖区全联超市、爱买、灿坤等大卖场向民众宣导在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">新年</a>期间，在人潮众多的卖场及市场采买年货时应注意自身财务，留意扒窃趁乱窃取皮包或随身物品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13905864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13905864" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13905864-595546.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13905864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13905864-595546-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13905864" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%A2%85%E8%AD%A6.md#1">杨梅警</a>分局表示，年节期间，切记财不露白。（桃园市杨梅警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>杨梅警分局也表示，年节期间，切记财不露白，如有发现不法，应立即拨打110，警方将持续净化辖区治安，以达治安平稳、交通顺畅、民众安心之目标，让民众能安心度过佳节。◇</p>
