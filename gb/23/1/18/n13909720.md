<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>诚圣宫各种可爱动物石雕像  盼迁至诚圣宫摆设</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909721-596420.jpg" />
<h6>每个吉祥物是为70年代手雕泥作，经过专业人士的巧手，个个光彩亮丽。（诚圣宫提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月18日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）名闻遐迩的芦竹区坑口彩绘村，因航空城计划而受征收即将步入历史，为抢救这些珍贵的文化资产，让当地居民能够永怀思念，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%9A%E5%9C%A3%E5%AE%AB.md#1">诚圣宫</a>积极争取，将其中各种可爱<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E7%9F%B3%E9%9B%95%E5%83%8F.md#1">动物石雕像</a>，迁移至诚圣宫大门前<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%86%E8%AE%BE.md#1">摆设</a>，并聘请专业师傅，进行修补及重新彩绘上色，赋予其新生命。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13909722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13909722" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909722-596421-e1674017833331.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13909722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909722-596421-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13909722" class="wp-caption-text">庙方在地方文物的抢救与保存工作上，付出的心力不少。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%9A%E5%9C%A3%E5%AE%AB.md#1">诚圣宫</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每个吉祥物是为70年代手雕泥作，经过专业人士的巧手，个个光彩亮丽，仿佛有了新生命，吸引了不少游客拍照打卡，俨然成为诚圣宫及芦竹区另一个独具一格的特色地标。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13909723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13909723" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909723-596422-e1674017805979.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13909723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909723-596422-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13909723" class="wp-caption-text">庙方在地方文物的抢救与保存工作上，付出的心力不少。（诚圣宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每个吉祥物各有其寓意，透过现场工作人员的讲解，也让前来参访的游客们都印象深刻，同时也感叹庙方在地方文物的抢救与保存工作上，所付出的心力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13909724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13909724" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909724-596423-e1674017753916.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13909724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909724-596423-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13909724" class="wp-caption-text">庙方在地方文物的抢救与保存工作上，付出的心力不少。（诚圣宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>诚圣宫大门旁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%86%E8%AE%BE.md#1">摆设</a>了一尊重达2023台斤的“龙龟米”，系由405包白米所构成，其浩大龙龟模样，着实吸睛。而“2023台斤”也象征着为新的2023年祈福，希望大家都能在新的一年，鸿图大展，万事如意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13909725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13909725" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909725-596424-e1674017721961.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13909725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909725-596424-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13909725" class="wp-caption-text">每个吉祥物各有其寓意，透过现场工作人员的讲解，也让前来参访的游客们都印象深刻。（诚圣宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>于新年期间(除夕至元宵)举办一系列活动，凡前来诚圣宫与吉祥物合照打卡并上传社群媒体 者，即可获赠“赚赚兔钥匙圈”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13909726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13909726" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909726-596425-e1674017693194.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13909726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909726-596425-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13909726" class="wp-caption-text">每个吉祥物各有其寓意，透过现场工作人员的讲解，也让前来参访的游客们都印象深刻。（诚圣宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参加除夕夜开庙门活动者(晚上9点15开始)，可获赠总价值新台币600元之三大好礼(诚圣宫发财金、赚赚兔钥匙圈、诚圣宫有缘发财钱母)。正月十三，庙方也将举办“开斗”、“点灯”、“求神龟”、“神尊飞銮过火”等一系列活动，欢迎大家前来共襄盛举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13909727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13909727" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909727-596426-e1674017660308.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13909727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13909727-596426-450x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13909727" class="wp-caption-text">每个吉祥物各有其寓意，透过现场工作人员的讲解，也让前来参访的游客们都印象深刻。（诚圣宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>正月十五，滨海协会也将主办欢庆元宵活动，有超强卡司的精彩的影视歌星表演及摸彩活动，欢迎大家做阵来逗闹烈，迎新年，贺新年。诚圣宫欢迎阖家莅临参香，帝君保佑阖家平安健康、财源广进。◇</p>
