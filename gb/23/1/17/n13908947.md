<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《喜事》年节照播 周晓涵等演员吐过节烦恼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908958-2301170050351487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>周晓涵（右）在中国新年常被问何时买房买车。图为剧照。（TVBS提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年01月17日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）8点档台剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%B2%B9%E5%96%9C%E4%BA%8B.md#1">加油喜事</a>》年假不停播，近日更与身心科医师李旻珊合作，调查网友的“过年焦虑”，主要演员<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%99%93%E6%B6%B5.md#1">周晓涵</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E5%AD%9D%E5%AE%89.md#1">周孝安</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B9%E6%89%BF%E6%81%A9.md#1">邹承恩</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%99%B4.md#1">杨晴</a>、臧芮轩、刘育仁也分别聊到新年的烦恼。</p>
<p>戏里刚顺利结婚的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%99%93%E6%B6%B5.md#1">周晓涵</a>，透露身边亲友太了解自己，不再过问结婚、生子，反而会问她什么时候买新车、买新房；育有一子的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B9%E6%89%BF%E6%81%A9.md#1">邹承恩</a>则要大家别再问二胎，笑说：“麦搁问啊！”</p>
<p>李旻珊医师建议，面对亲友的关心，不妨化被动为主动，告知自己的近况、计划，借此满足对方的好奇心，同时掌握想说多少的主控权。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13908961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13908961" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908961-2301170050371487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13908961" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908961-2301170050371487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13908961" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%99%B4.md#1">杨晴</a>苦恼过年要准备什么年菜。图为剧照。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>喜欢下厨的杨晴和臧芮轩，与不少网友一样为准备年菜而烦心，杨晴焦虑过年期间要买多少年菜、哪些要自己煮；臧芮轩一家过往都外出用餐，今年家人指定在家团圆，因此，这阵子她忙着上网订购，却发现许多年菜都售罄，忍不住喊：“到底要吃什么啦？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13908962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13908962" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908962-2301170050321487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13908962" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908962-2301170050321487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13908962" class="wp-caption-text">臧芮轩苦于订不到年菜。图为剧照。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>身心科医师李旻珊表示，这次与《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%B2%B9%E5%96%9C%E4%BA%8B.md#1">加油喜事</a>》合作，公布网友过年的焦虑排行，前三名分别是“晚睡、晚起被碎念”、“被问生子、薪水”、“准备年菜、礼品及红包”，第一名显示大部分的人都担心被碎念，令李旻珊医师直说很有趣。</p>
<p>除了上述所提到的焦虑外，周晓涵、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E5%AD%9D%E5%AE%89.md#1">周孝安</a>及邹承恩与不少网友也担心同一件事，就是吃太多变胖。周晓涵得为年后的代言拍摄忌口，周孝安表示自己每年都苦恼年夜饭要吃多少，笑说：“武子汉（剧中名）突然变胖的话，我要怎么交代？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13908965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13908965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908965-2301170050251487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13908965" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908965-2301170050251487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13908965" class="wp-caption-text">周孝安担心自己过年变胖。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13908964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13908964" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908964-2301170050271487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13908964" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908964-2301170050271487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13908964" class="wp-caption-text">邹承恩（中）要大家别再问生二胎的事。图为剧照。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>邹承恩则因去年过年吃太好、胖太多，今年警惕要格外注意口腹之欲。武家老幺刘育仁跟剧中角色一样乐天，热爱过年的气氛，唯一烦恼的就是“收假焦虑症”，感叹假期太快结束。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13908966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13908966" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908966-2301170050301487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13908966" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908966-2301170050301487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13908966" class="wp-caption-text">刘育仁喜欢过年，但担心年假太快结束。图为剧照。（TVBS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《加油喜事》年节期间依旧播出。剧中，三代婆媳刘秀雯、陈亚兰、周晓涵的互动成为观众关注的焦点。该剧在小年夜（20日）、大年初二（23日）到初六（27日）晚间8点陪大家欢喜过年。</p>
