<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>眷村联谊会偕韩国瑜后援会举办新年晚宴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13916936-1674342485145-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新年晚宴活动剪影(眷村联谊会提供)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年01月27日讯】乔治亚州<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%B7%E6%9D%91%E8%81%94%E8%B0%8A%E4%BC%9A.md#1">眷村联谊会</a>与亚特兰大韩国瑜之友会，于2023年1月20日(星期五)傍晚6时，假阿兵哥餐馆举办<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4%E6%99%9A%E5%AE%B4.md#1">新年晚宴</a>，席开10桌，近100人参加。由因疫总统选举及2年疫情未曾举办，今年邀请函发出，一周内报名近满额，深感会员、会友们的热情响应。</p>
<p>当日下午4时志工依约早到布置现场，6时顺利迎接各方来宾盛装赴宴，6点30分准时开场。晚宴为健康首次未提供任何酒水，显得现场有序又不喧哗。主办单位、侨团、来宾赞助大摸彩活动的彩礼多近50件，包括65吋液晶大电视。阿兵哥餐馆东主亲自主厨晚宴美食，主客均满意非常。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13916938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13916938" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13916938-1674342487428-e1674865716233.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13916938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13916938-1674342487428-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13916938" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4%E6%99%9A%E5%AE%B4.md#1">新年晚宴</a>活动剪影(<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%B7%E6%9D%91%E8%81%94%E8%B0%8A%E4%BC%9A.md#1">眷村联谊会</a>提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13916944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13916944" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13916944-Resized_16743424890601-e1674866001374.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13916944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13916944-Resized_16743424890601-600x800.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13916944" class="wp-caption-text">新年晚宴活动剪影(眷村联谊会提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>新年晚宴因疫情之故缩小办理而未安排主持人，由会长叶竣图及副会长梁穗嫈共同完成了主席、司仪、主持人的任务，会长叶竣图特鸣谢侨务咨询委员何智达医师、侨务陆复泰委员、范琪君委员、侨务顾问张世杰医师、重阳会前会长陈兆?、亚特兰大会长吴俊彦、北美台商会副总会长袁丹青(本会前会长) 、联华报东主叶友庄、亚特兰大荣光会、空军大鹏联谊会的赞助金及摸彩彩礼，甚至有赞助者因事不克前来而托来宾转送来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13916945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13916945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13916945-Resized_1674311743151-e1674866092871.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13916945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13916945-Resized_1674311743151-600x800.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13916945" class="wp-caption-text">新年晚宴活动剪影(眷村联谊会提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚宴中宣布今后举办任何晚宴不提供酒水及今年将成立志工团，需要时配合侨务委员推动侨务活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13916946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13916946" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13916946-1674342485341-e1674866197743.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13916946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13916946-1674342485341-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13916946" class="wp-caption-text">新年晚宴活动剪影(眷村联谊会提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后感谢志工及赴宴之各界来宾。亦是办任何晚宴呈现素质层次最高的一次，从志工到接待赴宴及宴席中都井井有序而顺利完成。敬祝大家阖家安康.事事如意及新春愉快!</p>
<p>乔治亚州眷村联谊会? 会长 叶竣图</p>
<p>亚特兰大韩国瑜之友会? 总召集人 王多禄</p>
<p>敬启</p>
