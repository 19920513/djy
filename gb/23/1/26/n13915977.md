<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>七律：微尘</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13917556-shutterstock_29523859-2-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #003366;"><em><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;">人生苦短，世事愁长；一叶飘零，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%AE%E5%B0%98.md#1">微尘</a>散落。</span></span></em></span><br />
<span style="color: #003366;"><em><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"> 回天有路，入土无时；望远风悲，凭谁命托？</span></span></em></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%AE%E5%B0%98.md#1">微尘</a>上下总含风，漂草泊泥零北东。</span></span></span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;">何处侧身尝得得，几时临命不匆匆。</span></span></span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;">大千无寄愁无绝，方寸有怀梦有穷。</span></span></span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;">飞落<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E8%8A%B1.md#1">杨花</a>休细看，故人高韵写丝桐。</span></span></span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;">注：</span></span><br />
<span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"> ⑴ 微尘，“况我此之身，天地一微尘”（宋代滕岑《和陶渊明饮酒诗》）。</span></span><br />
<span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"> ⑵ 含风，带着风；被风吹拂着。</span></span><br />
<span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"> ⑶ 得得，任情自得状。</span></span><br />
<span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"> ⑷ 大千，“大千世界”的省称。</span></span><br />
<span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"> ⑸ 方寸，指心，因心在胸中方寸间，故称“方寸之心”。</span></span><br />
<span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"> ⑹ <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E8%8A%B1.md#1">杨花</a>，“细看来，不是杨花，点点是离人泪”（苏轼《水龙吟·次韵章质夫杨花词》）。</span></span><br />
<span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"> ⑺ 写，倾吐，倾诉；抒发。</span></span><br />
<span style="vertical-align: inherit;"><span style="vertical-align: inherit;"> ⑻ 丝桐，指琴，古人练丝为弦，削桐为琴；亦指乐曲。@*</span></span></p>
