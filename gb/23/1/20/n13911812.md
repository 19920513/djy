<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>满地宝艺术中心将举办中国新年庆典</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911815-P1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月20日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">邱晨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大温哥华报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">传统的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E7%9A%87%E5%8E%86%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">中国皇历新年</a>即将来临，为您推荐一个适合全家人共度新年的好去处。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">大年初一（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">22</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日），</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E7%9B%88%E5%88%A9%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87.md#1">非盈利组织</a>复兴五千年文明协会（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Reviving 5000 Years of Civilization Association</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）将在满地宝艺术中心（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Port Moody Arts Centre</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）举办一场别具一格的中国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">传统文化</a>推广活动，现场有古筝、琵琶表演、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E9%81%93.md#1">茶道</a>演示（配英文解说）、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4%E5%89%AA%E7%BA%B8.md#1">新年剪纸</a>与传统国画艺术展示，同时还将举办中国传统文化专题讲座。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">主办方代表</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cathy Tan</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">告诉大纪元记者，这一新年活动深受中西方民众的欢迎，在历年举办的活动中，许多人对中国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">传统文化</a>着迷。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13911817" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911817" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911817-54554f902011df56fccc5d0b.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13911817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911817-54554f902011df56fccc5d0b-600x630.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="630" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911817" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E9%81%93.md#1">茶道</a>表演将带观众一起体验中国茶道的“和、静、怡、真”。（复兴五千年文明协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">中国茶道自然谦和，讲究“和、静、怡、真”。以茶养性、以茶养心、以茶养德，其礼仪与内涵，却需行家指点，才能了解一二。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">一张喜庆的红纸，如何巧妙剪出新年贺词呢？而中国国画也是奥妙无穷，画家会现场作画，展示国画的艺术精华。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">您可以登录<ahref="https://www.eventbrite.ca/e/lunar-new-year-tickets-511230673707">https://www.eventbrite.ca/e/lunar-new-year-tickets-511230673707</a>预订座位。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">时间：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">22</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日（周日）下午</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2:00– 3:30</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">地点：Port Moody Arts Centre，Saint Johns 街2425 号，满地宝（Port Moody）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span></p>
