<h6>1月29日大年初八，波士顿华埠醒狮贺岁，迎来众多游客。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年01月31日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）1月29日上午，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">波士顿华埠</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E7%8B%AE.md#1">舞狮</a>贺年庆典在明媚的阳光下鸣锣开场，得到现场成千上万游客的庆祝。同时，市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）也呼吁大家要经常光顾华埠，在本地的商家消费。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-db6a909c-8535-4056-9cfc-e504014053af" data-id="player-db6a909c-8535-4056-9cfc-e504014053af"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/db6a909c-8535-4056-9cfc-e504014053af?r=16x9&amp;d=212&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F23%2F1%2F31%2Fn13919065.md#1"></a>
<p>当天上午10点，华埠的街道上开始回响锣鼓和鞭炮的声音。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>地区的各大武馆和狮团举着各自的旗帜，带领着五颜六色的彩狮，穿过摩肩接踵的人潮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13919030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919030" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919030-DSC03918.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919030-DSC03918-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919030" class="wp-caption-text">1月29日上午，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E7%8B%AE.md#1">舞狮</a>团体举旗穿过华埠街道。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13919033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919033" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919033-DSC03956.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919033-DSC03956-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919033" class="wp-caption-text">1月29日上午，彩狮在Harrison街的主舞台前献舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>狮子们首先在Harrison街菲利普广场的主舞台下翻腾献舞，然后伴随着锣鼓声穿过街道，拜访华埠的每一个商家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13919038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919038-DSC04031.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919038-DSC04031-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919038" class="wp-caption-text">在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">波士顿华埠</a>，贺岁彩狮穿过人潮。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13919040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919040-DSC04081.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919040-DSC04081-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919040" class="wp-caption-text">1月29日华埠舞狮贺岁，彩狮拜访当地商家，送上祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“欢迎大家在这么一个好天气下来到这里，庆祝黄历兔年。兔子常常是活跃的，非常聪明的。今天是开启新一年的很棒的一天。”纽英崙<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E5%85%AC%E6%89%80.md#1">中华公所</a>主席雷国辉说。</p>
<p>华埠的新年舞狮庆典由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E5%85%AC%E6%89%80.md#1">中华公所</a>主办，今年有11个舞狮团参加，包括华林、广教、黄氏、洪青、巾帼等等。人群一直热闹到下午4点才渐渐散去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13919039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919039-DSC04055.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919039-DSC04055-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919039" class="wp-caption-text">彩狮贺岁，鞭炮炸响。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13919041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919041-DSC04098.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919041-DSC04098-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919041" class="wp-caption-text">彩狮贺岁，鞭炮炸响。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在舞狮表演的同时，Tyler街中华公所驻地内还有璞石中华文化工作室带来的文化村活动，包括书法、捏面人、拓印等项目，吸引了不少游客前来参与。其中麦德福（Medford）的华裔市议员曾御宸（Justin Tseng）也陪同担任璞石义工的母亲徐中欣前来助阵。</p>
<p>另外，华埠主街机构也在Boylston街的中华贸易大楼内举办庆祝活动，以舞蹈表演、灯笼制作等文化项目吸引了大批游客。</p>

<figure id="attachment_13919044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919044" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919044-DSC04124.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919044" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919044-DSC04124-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919044" class="wp-caption-text">在中华公所内，璞石中华文化工作室带来书法艺术，得到游客的关注。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>“我想呼吁每个人，今天去支持本地商家，他们真的需要。这里有很棒的食物，各种各样的东西，奶茶、粉面、饺子、点心，应有尽有。但是在今天之后也请再来。”市长吴弭说。她还用中文向大家送上祝福：“新年快乐，恭喜发财，万事如意。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13919046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919046" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919046-DSC04139.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919046" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919046-DSC04139-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919046" class="wp-caption-text">1月29日大年初八，众多游客来参加波士顿华埠的舞狮庆典。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>当天参与开幕式庆祝的嘉宾还有驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元、波士顿市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）、警察局长考克斯（Michael Cox）、麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）、黄子安（Donald Wong）等10多位。</p>
