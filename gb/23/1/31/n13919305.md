<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>年假10天台东涌入百万人潮 创观光高产值</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919308-597448-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新年10天连假，台东各景点涌入观光人潮。（台东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月31日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）疫情趋缓加上新年连续10天假期，台东市区及<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89.md#1">观光</a>景点人潮涌现，根据<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>县政府统计，期间台东的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A4%E9%80%9A.md#1">交通</a>车流约31.8万车次，整体交通入辖（含火车、航空）旅游人次约104.9万人次。住房率统计，连假前两日台东县整体住房率约七成，台东市区住宿率达八成以上，整体观光产值估算约近30亿元台币。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13919310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919310-597449.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919310-597449-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919310" class="wp-caption-text">新年10天连假，台东各景点涌入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89.md#1">观光</a>人潮。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13919306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919306-597446.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919306-597446-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919306" class="wp-caption-text">新年10天连假，台东各景点涌入观光人潮。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13919307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13919307" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919307-597447.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13919307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13919307-597447-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13919307" class="wp-caption-text">新年10天连假，台东各景点涌入观光人潮。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台东县政府<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A4%E9%80%9A.md#1">交通</a>及观光发展处说明，主要景点游客人次统计约57万人次，较去年同期（初一至初六）相比增加17.39%。各景点游客人次最多的前三名分别为：池上大坡池及伯朗大道、知本温泉、小野柳。分析原因除疫情趋缓外，今年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E5%81%87.md#1">年假</a>长，旅客愿意多花天数选择远距离且深度的景点来旅行，加上寒流接连报到，也带动温泉景点人潮提升。</p>
<p>接下来228连假，县府表示，台东不同季节各有不同的大自然面貌，还有多元文化以及丰富的农渔产，想要到台东体验慢活氛围，不妨选择淡季前来，更能感受在地纯朴善良的人情味。◇</p>
