<h6>Nissan Pathfinder Rock Creek。（李奥／大纪元） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月21日讯】最近测试了不少专为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E9%87%8E.md#1">越野</a>而设计的特别版，例如Honda Passport TrailSport、Toyota 4Runner TRD Sport，以及今次的Nissan Pathfinder Rock Creek。Nissan Pathfinder Rock Creek是基建于第五代Pathfinder，位于SV与SL版本中间。</p>
<p><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://doyvntponycdh.cloudfront.net/C1bEofrxz"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/CXv2Rl2WdeQ" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>去年试驾的Pathfinder Platinum是红色，今次的Pathfinder Rock Creek也是差不多，看来Pathfinder最适合红色，所以厂方才刻意安排。稍为不同的是，今年的是红黑色，也就是Dual Tone，红色是车身部分，黑色是车顶部分，包括所有支柱。</p>
<p>这辆2023年Nissan Pathfinder是属于第五代车型，全新设计的巨大前脸使用了V-Motion设计，C型的大灯和浮动车顶是新Pathfinder的特式，细长的日间行车灯，有如上扬的眉毛，和大灯都是LED灯。</p>
<p>LED尾灯比旧款更细长，尾闸设计粗犷，闸面上有巨大镀铬的Pathfinder徽章。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13912088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912088" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912088-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13912088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912088-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_07-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912088" class="wp-caption-text">Nissan Pathfinder Rock Creek。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nissan Pathfinder Rock Creek特别版在车侧及尾闸，都贴上了“Rock Creek”徽章。</p>
<p>另外，装置了特别的18吋黑银色特别版铝合金轮圈，在车顶也装上了黑色的行李架，可载重高达220磅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13912089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912089" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912089-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_17.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13912089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912089-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912089" class="wp-caption-text">车顶也装上了黑色的行李架。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2023年的Pathfinder配备了295匹马力的3.5升直喷V6引擎，扭矩为270磅呎，配置全新9速自动变速箱。这款全新的变速箱也有助于提高燃油经济性和驾驶舒适性，而在切线或出弯时也表现佳。</p>
<p>Rock Creek装配了Paddle Shift手动变档拨片，输出顺滑、强劲、直接。</p>
<p>新Pathfinder具有6,000磅最大拖力，可拖拉船只、ATV、拖车等。另外，拖车摇摆控制是标准配置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13912090" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912090" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912090-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13912090" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912090-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912090" class="wp-caption-text">3.5升直喷V6引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>既然Pathfinder Rock Creek是为了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E9%87%8E.md#1">越野</a>而设，当然全新智能四驱系统也是标配。</p>
<p>地形模式选择有七种，包括标准、雪地、沙地、泥地、运动、经济和牵引，在各种路面也可适应，地形模式旋扭中间是“下坡限速控制”。</p>
<p>Rock Creek特别为越野调节了悬挂系统。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13912091" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912091" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912091-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_32.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13912091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912091-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_32-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912091" class="wp-caption-text">地形模式选择有七种。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Pathfinder Rock Creek版内饰采用了新材料和开放式设计，仪表板只是传统的两圆仪表，跟去年测试的Platinum版拥有大大的萤幕仪表，相差甚远。</p>
<p>中控台也只有8吋彩色触摸屏，内置了NissanConnect系统，有Wi-Fi热点、Apple CarPlay及Android Auto连接，但没有无线手机充电板和13个扬声器的Bose高级音响。</p>
<p>环视监视器比以前分辨率更高、图像可视角度更广，而且特别为越野而设。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13912093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912093-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_42.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13912093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912093-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_42-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912093" class="wp-caption-text">内饰采用了新材料和开放式设计。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了避免碰撞，Pathfinder装有“安全盾牌360”安全系统，包括带行人探测的自动紧急制动、盲点警告、后方交叉交通警报、车道偏离警告、远光灯辅助和后方自动制动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13912094" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912094" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912094-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_36.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13912094" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912094-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_36-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912094" class="wp-caption-text">前座背垫及手枕上也有“Rock Creek”的绣字。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13912095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912095" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912095-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_35.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13912095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912095-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_35-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912095" class="wp-caption-text">前座背垫及手枕上也有“Rock Creek”的绣字。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Rock Creek是标配7人座位，第二排是两个独立的Captain Seat，但也可一键式将座位前倾及移前，让乘客可更容易地进出第三排。第二排座位中间是一个集杯座及储物间的小箱子。</p>
<p>第三排座椅可60/40分离式折叠，提供了最大的灵活性。</p>
<p>另外，前座背垫及手枕上也有“Rock Creek”的绣字。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13912096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912096" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912096-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_31.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13912096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912096-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_31-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912096" class="wp-caption-text">9速自动变速。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新的双齿轮电动转向系统，结合更宽的265/60R18全地形轮胎，提供更稳定、快速的反应，转向角率提高了40%，也减少了转向力度。</p>
<p>前悬挂使用了新连接系统，加强了滚动刚度28%，后悬挂则采用多连杆设计，并更新了减震器和阻尼器，使后滚动刚度增强14%。</p>
<p>ProPILOT辅助系统结合了转向辅助和智能巡航控制，在繁忙的市内和高速公路上都能自动加速、制动和转向。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13912098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912098" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912098-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13912098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912098-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912098" class="wp-caption-text">265/60R18全地形轮胎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Pathfinder Rock Creek是适合一些有家庭又爱挑战野外的人士，带同小朋友一同往野外露营。所以如果只是在市区或只走高速公路的话，Rock Creek的耗油量比SL版要高一点，Rock Creek的公路MPG为23，而SL版是25。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13912100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912100" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912100-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_25.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13912100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912100-2023_Nissan_Pathfinder_25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912100" class="wp-caption-text">8吋彩色触摸屏。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
