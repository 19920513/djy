<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>南澳乡泰雅文化馆常设展1月18日开幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910513-596588.jpg" />
<h6>南澳乡泰雅文化馆常设展‘我们的南澳，我们的根’于1月18日开展（南澳乡公所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月19日讯】（大纪元记者谢月琴台湾宜兰报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%BE%B3.md#1">南澳</a>乡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E9%9B%85%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E9%A6%86.md#1">泰雅文化馆</a>经过1年空间修缮，在111年底完成含文物典藏上架后，‘我们的南澳，我们的根’常设展于1月18日开展了。开幕活动中也邀请在地耆老<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%BE%B7%E6%B8%85.md#1">陈德清</a>(哈勇家演员)来举行祈福仪式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13910514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13910514" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910514-596589-e1674105709340.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13910514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910514-596589-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13910514" class="wp-caption-text">泰雅族<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%BE%B3.md#1">南澳</a>群的传统织布（南澳乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过1年常设展空间修缮，终于在111年底完成含文物典藏上架，常设展区以南澳群泰雅文化传承为轴心，呈现“泰雅族南澳群的迁移史、传统家屋、传统文物、泰雅族口簧琴与世界口簧琴、泰雅族工艺品、传统生态以及教育推广，透过文化迁移及文物展示来诉说南澳的历史。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13910515" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13910515" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910515-596590-e1674105644973.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13910515" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910515-596590-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13910515" class="wp-caption-text">泰雅族南澳群的迁移史（南澳乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>南澳是一个山海晴雨、人文动静皆宜的地方，充满了历史故事，地处山林环绕、自然生态资源丰富、独特编织工艺精湛且有文化意涵，藉由南澳乡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E9%9B%85%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E9%A6%86.md#1">泰雅文化馆</a>常设展文物典藏展示，文化艺术融入生活价值的延续，告诉我们的根就是这里“南澳”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13910516" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13910516" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910516-596591-e1674105617424.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13910516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910516-596591-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13910516" class="wp-caption-text">泰雅族传统文物及工艺品2 (1)（南澳乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>部落有形及无形文化随着时间慢慢消逝之前，南澳乡泰雅文化馆藉历年所收集之文物及田调资料，由常设展区来展示属于南澳乡在地传统文化，另增加影音方式来介绍生态区及传统家屋的建构，开幕活动中也邀请在地耆老<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%BE%B7%E6%B8%85.md#1">陈德清</a>(哈勇家演员)来举行<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E9%9B%85%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F.md#1">泰雅传统</a>祈福仪式，增添庄严又感人的一面，我们欢迎各位一起来共襄盛举，感受属于“我们的南澳、我们的根”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13910517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13910517" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910517-596592-e1674105585133.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13910517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910517-596592-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13910517" class="wp-caption-text">泰雅族传统文物及工艺品2 (2)（南澳乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13910518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13910518" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910518-596593-e1674105525733.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13910518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910518-596593-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13910518" class="wp-caption-text">泰雅族传统文物及工艺品3（南澳乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动期间，于展馆内拍照上传至您的脸书（Facebook）或Instagram（IG）并标注“南澳乡泰雅文化馆、‘我们的南澳，我们的根’”，即可兑换精美小礼物一份喔！送完为止。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13910519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13910519" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910519-596594-e1674105793842.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13910519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13910519-596594-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13910519" class="wp-caption-text">泰雅族传统家屋2 (1)（南澳乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
