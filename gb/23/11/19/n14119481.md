<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>轿车被撞成废铁 国1内坜段3车连环撞4受伤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119487-648089-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>车辆几乎被撞烂，让人觉得惨不忍睹。（国道警察局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月19日讯】（大纪元记者陈建霖台湾桃园报导）19日凌晨3时18分，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%81%93.md#1">国道</a>1号北向56.5公里内坜段，发生一起3车追撞的事故，造成车辆撞烂几乎全毁，车壳喷飞至内、外侧车道，所幸仅造成4人受伤，3名驾驶皆未酒驾，救护人员给予清洗伤口、包扎止血等救护处置后送医，国道警方呼吁驾驶人应依<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%9F%E9%99%90%E8%A1%8C%E8%BD%A6.md#1">速限行车</a>，并保持<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%85%A8%E8%B7%9D%E7%A6%BB.md#1">安全距离</a>。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%81%93.md#1">国道</a>警方调查，整起事故起因为42岁的廖姓女子驾驶自小客车行驶内侧车道，因车辆失控，先是擦撞内侧护栏后停在内侧车道，后方45岁欧性姓男子驾驶自小客车，因闪避不及擦撞廖女车辆，另28岁詹姓女子，也是闪避不及再撞上廖女车辆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119483-648085.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119483-648085-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119483" class="wp-caption-text">国1内坜段3车连环撞，车撞烂2驾驶幸运仅轻伤。（国道警察局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖女的车辆因为一连受到2次撞击，车辆几乎被撞烂，幸运的仅有手部擦伤，詹姓女驾驶也是手部擦伤，3辆车的驾驶酒测值均为0。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119484" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119484-648086.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119484-648086-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119484" class="wp-caption-text">45岁欧姓男子驾驶自小客车，因闪避不及擦撞廖女车辆。（国道警察局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4名受伤；周姓女性31岁、手指及手肘擦伤，由中坜分队救护车送往桃园医院；吕姓女性36岁、头部疼痛无外伤，由中坜分队救护车送往桃园医院；廖姓女性41岁、后颈及左臂疼痛，由中坜分队救护车送往桃园医院；詹姓女性28岁、躯干及肢体疼痛，由内坜分队救护车送往中坜天晟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119485" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119485-648087.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119485-648087-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119485" class="wp-caption-text">车辆撞烂几乎全毁，车壳喷飞至内、外侧车道。（国道警察局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14119482" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119482" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119482-648084.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119482" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119482-648084-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119482" class="wp-caption-text">廖女的车辆因为一连受到2次撞击，车辆几乎被撞烂。（国道警察局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14119486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119486-648088.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119486-648088-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119486" class="wp-caption-text">廖女的车辆受到2次撞击，车辆几乎被撞烂。（国道警察局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国道警方呼吁，汽车驾驶人应遵守交通安全规则，依<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%9F%E9%99%90%E8%A1%8C%E8%BD%A6.md#1">速限行车</a>，并保持<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%85%A8%E8%B7%9D%E7%A6%BB.md#1">安全距离</a>，随时注意前方动态，汽车发生事故车辆尚能行驶，应立即开启双黄警示灯，车辆尽速移至安全处所，人员站立护栏外或护栏边，切勿站立车尾处，面对来车全程注意安全，防止二次事故发生。◇</p>
