<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>二崙公馆社区志工扫街 成为二崙最美风景</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119467-648080-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>社区团结的力量，成为二崙最美的风景之一。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月19日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%8C%E5%B4%99.md#1">二崙</a>公馆社区<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E4%BF%9D%E5%BF%97%E5%B7%A5.md#1">环保志工</a>队在19日的假日，展开了一场令人振奋的清扫行动，针对该社区辖区内的绿园道进行清除盆栽积水、清扫园道落叶与垃圾的工作，并以这份热情为防范登革热提前作好准备。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119466" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119466-648079.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119466-648079-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119466" class="wp-caption-text">志工队的不懈努力与奉献精神为我们提供了一个可持续发展的未来。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一群年轻志工挥动着竹扫把，专心地清扫地上的落叶，笑容洋溢在他们的脸上。志工队中有家族档，有亲子档，每一位志工都充满活力与热情。最年长的志工，享寿84岁的曹新出表示：“参与扫街既是为了公益，也是一种运动养生的方式，感到非常快乐。”此外，还有几位特地从北部返乡的乡亲加入行列，张君美激动地表示，志工队已经成为家乡最美的风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119468" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119468-648081.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119468-648081-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119468" class="wp-caption-text">曹新出表示：“参与扫街既是为了公益，也是一种运动养生的方式，感到非常快乐。”（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14119463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119463" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119463-648076.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119463-648076-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119463" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%8C%E5%B4%99.md#1">二崙</a>公馆社区志工扫街，成为二崙最美风景。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场志工清扫行动不仅让社区更加美丽整洁，还凝聚了社区居民的情感，展现了大家共同关怀环境的决心。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>二崙公馆社区<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E4%BF%9D%E5%BF%97%E5%B7%A5.md#1">环保志工</a>队的积极参与，不仅改善了环境质量，还彰显了社区团结的力量，成为二崙最美的风景之一。</p>
<p>该志工队的不懈努力与奉献精神为我们提供了一个可持续发展的未来，同时也激励着更多人投入环保行动，为社区和环境创造更美好的明天。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119470" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119470-648083.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119470-648083-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119470" class="wp-caption-text">清扫园道落叶与垃圾的工作，并以这份热情为防范登革热提前作好准备。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14119469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119469" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119469-648082.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119469-648082-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119469" class="wp-caption-text">志工队中有家族档，有亲子档。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
