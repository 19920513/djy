<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>奇妙之旅 新北中坑古道与崩山坑古道</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110809-645756-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>遇见迎宾大茶壶，旁边有一条原始山道，通往崩塌山坑古道。 （程丽叶提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月06日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">新北市</a>报导）新北市双溪区拥有着令人惊艳的大自然风光，以及丰富的登山步道。11月初，一群勇敢的登山爱好者，踏上令人兴奋的徒步旅程，走访了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9D%91%E5%8F%A4%E9%81%93.md#1">中坑古道</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%A9%E5%B1%B1%E5%9D%91%E5%8F%A4%E9%81%93.md#1">崩山坑古道</a>，并以威惠庙为出发点，完成了一趟充满冒险和美景的旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110803" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110803-645751-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14110803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110803-645751-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="398" b="531" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110803" class="wp-caption-text">来到一座朴拙典雅的石砌拱桥，桥下流水潺潺。（程丽叶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一大早，登山队伍在威惠庙集结，享受了事先准备的咖啡，让大家神清气爽，迎接这次的探险之旅。</p>
<p>队伍抵达百年土地公庙，当地人称之为水尾土地公庙，这儿便是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9D%91%E5%8F%A4%E9%81%93.md#1">中坑古道</a>的起点。这里的梯田平整翠绿，呈现出一片宁静的景象，而留心观察的人会发现有人精心整理了这片土地。</p>
<p>开始前进，发现步道上有刻着“中坑古道”的石柱，上面还有淡兰古道的双扇蕨标志，这条步道是淡兰古道的中段。</p>
<p>途中遇到了青蛙石，这块石头像是在亲吻，令人联想到童话故事中的王子。继续前行，他们进入一片绿荫茂盛的森林，享受着大自然的芬多精。路径上有牛粪提醒着人们要小心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110810" style="width: 395px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110810-645757-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14110810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110810-645757-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="395" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110810" class="wp-caption-text">这块石头像是在亲吻，令人联想到童话故事中的王子。（程丽叶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14110807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110807" style="width: 422px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110807-645755-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14110807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110807-645755-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="422" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110807" class="wp-caption-text">新北中坑古道与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%A9%E5%B1%B1%E5%9D%91%E5%8F%A4%E9%81%93.md#1">崩山坑古道</a>。（程丽叶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到一座朴拙典雅的石砌拱桥，桥下流水潺潺，桥边的两棵九芎木也让人联想到古诗中的景象。</p>
<p>中午，登山队抵达中坑头土地公庙，这是古道的最高点，海拔597米，并在这里休息片刻。续行的旅程将会带他们前往不同的山头，包括阔濑和枋山坑山。</p>
<p>继续前行，遇见迎宾大茶壶，旁边有一条原始山道，通往崩塌山坑古道，然而，他们没有选择探寻这未知的路况，而是继续在主路上行进。山坡上开满了金黄灿烂的台湾山菊，为道路增添了活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110804" style="width: 416px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110804-645752-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14110804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110804-645752-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="416" b="555" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110804" class="wp-caption-text">柑脚山置高点。（程丽叶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下午，他们经过了古厝地遗迹，然后继续前行，夕阳越过树林照在溪水中，将其染上耀眼的金黄色，视觉上看起来去宛如流动的黄金液体，但走近后却只是一片空无一物的现实。</p>
<p>最终，他们到达了崩山坑古道的出口，选择左边行进，距离威惠庙约1公里，途中经过了厚雨农庄，路旁排列的瓮充满了创意，并继续前行。</p>
<p>最后结束健行探险，整个行程步行约7.5小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110805" style="width: 404px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110805-645753-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14110805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110805-645753-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="404" b="539" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110805" class="wp-caption-text">进入一享受着大自然的芬多精。（程丽叶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14110806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110806" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110806-645754-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14110806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110806-645754-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="535" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110806" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">新北市</a>双溪区拥有着令人惊艳的大自然风光，以及丰富的登山步道。（程丽叶提供）</figcaption></figure>
