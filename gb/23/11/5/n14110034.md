<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>二水跑水节 全台唯一水陆路跑马拉松</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110037-645409-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5日上午6点40分，半马选择先行出发。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月05日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%B0%E5%8C%96.md#1">彰化</a>报导）5日成功举办“2023<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%8C%E6%B0%B4.md#1">二水</a>国际跑水节”，成为全台唯一的水陆路跑马拉松，吸引来自全球的二千多名选手参与，并吸引日本及香港团体前来体验。此次盛大活动得到彰化县长王惠美，与经济部长王美花的参与，为故乡送上丰厚的大礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110038-645410.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110038-645410-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110038" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%B0%E5%8C%96.md#1">彰化</a>各界一起来共襄盛举。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%8C%E6%B0%B4.md#1">二水</a>国际跑水节由交通部观光署、参山国家风景区管理处、经济部水利署、农业部农村发展及水土保持署，以及二水乡公所等一起合办的地方盛事。除了是一场马拉松比赛，也是对二水文化、历史和物产的一次深度探索。</p>
<p>跑水节的亮点之一是水陆路跑，参赛选手在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%A0%A1%E5%9C%B3.md#1">八堡圳</a>旁穿上大会提供的拖鞋，跑进水圳中，体验先民开凿水圳的辛劳，这项活动吸引了，来自六岁到八十岁不同年龄层的二千多名选手共同参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110039-645411.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110039-645411-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110039" class="wp-caption-text">全国唯一<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%91%E6%B0%B4%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89.md#1">跑水马拉</a>松赛，好玩又刺激。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14110040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110040-645412.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110040-645412-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110040" class="wp-caption-text">跑水妈祖也跟着下水跑起来。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>马拉松选手原玉表示，国一曾脑中风瘫痪的她，二十年后靠跑步复健过来的，并鼓励其他人也勇敢接受挑战，相信未来也能在台湾马拉松赛事中见面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110041-645413.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110041-645413-600x603.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="603" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110041" class="wp-caption-text">马拉松赛的勇者：原玉。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经济部长王美花4日也回到故乡二水参加跑水祭活动，称赞这次的活动结合台湾水路的历史源头<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%A0%A1%E5%9C%B3.md#1">八堡圳</a>的开发，成为世界首创的水中奔跑马拉松。此外，经济部还宣布提供约4,800万元的补助款，用于改善鼻仔头的河岸，希望完工后能提供更多的休憩空间，保护二水的文化传承。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110036-645408.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110036-645408-600x544.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="544" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110036" class="wp-caption-text">托鞋备好，因为到八堡圳就可以到河里，体验古人辛苦抢水的情景。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14110035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110035" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110035-645407.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110035-645407-600x522.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="522" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110035" class="wp-caption-text">二水<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%91%E6%B0%B4%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89.md#1">跑水马拉</a>松比赛最年长八十岁。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
