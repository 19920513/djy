<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>彰化国际身障者日 多元参与让爱永续</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110047-645415-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>王惠美县长表示，通过这次表扬活动，赞许了绩优服务单位的用心服务。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月05日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%B0%E5%8C%96.md#1">彰化</a>报导）为庆祝国际身心障碍者日，彰化县政府于5日上午在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BE%E5%A4%B4.md#1">社头</a>果菜市场，隆重举办了“多元参与 让爱永续-112年度彰化县国际身心障碍者日”活动。活动由彰化县县长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E6%83%A0%E7%BE%8E.md#1">王惠美</a>亲自主持，以表扬本县的模范身心障碍者、模范身心障碍家庭照顾者，以及绩优身障服务单位，以感谢他们为社会带来温暖和正能量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110049" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110049-645417.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110049-645417-600x534.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="534" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110049" class="wp-caption-text">国际身心障碍者日是联合国于每年的12月3日被定为国际身心障碍者日。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14110047" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110047" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110047-645415.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110047" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110047-645415-600x303.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110047" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E6%83%A0%E7%BE%8E.md#1">王惠美</a>县长表示，通过这次表扬活动，赞许了绩优服务单位的用心服务。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在活动中，王惠美县长表示，通过这次表扬活动，赞许了绩优服务单位的用心服务，以及家庭照顾者的贡献，并表示对身障朋友们克服身体障碍，拥有充实精彩生活的敬佩之情。</p>
<p>为了帮助身心障碍者克服交通接送问题，县府媒合了民间力量，提供小型和大型复康巴士，供身心障碍朋友使用，并提供多种预约方式，包括电话、LINE和网络预约，以方便有需求的朋友们。此外，县府也积极运用公益彩券盈余，用于推广<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%B0%E5%8C%96.md#1">彰化</a>县的社会福利服务，以使更多的县民受益。</p>
<p>王惠美县长最后恭喜所有获奖者，呼吁大家携手努力，使彰化县变得更加美好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110048" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110048-645416.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110048-645416-600x459.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="459" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110048" class="wp-caption-text">呼吁大家携手努力，使彰化县变得更加美好。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14110050" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110050" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110050-645418.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110050" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110050-645418-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110050" class="wp-caption-text">5日上午有义剪活动。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国际身心障碍者日是联合国于1992年订立的，每年的12月3日被定为国际身心障碍者日，目的是唤起社会对身心障碍者权益的关注，使他们能够公平参与和融入社会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14110051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110051" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110051-645419.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110051" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110051-645419-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110051" class="wp-caption-text">免费体验按摩。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14110046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14110046" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110046-645414.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14110046" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110046-645414-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14110046" class="wp-caption-text">彰化国际身障者日，多元参与让爱永续活动现场。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
