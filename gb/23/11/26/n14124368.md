<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>梦幻“紫色派对” 杨梅26日闪亮登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124373-649568-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>梦幻“紫色派对”真美。   （桃园市杨梅区公所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月26日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）“2023桃园仙草嘉年华公所主题日活动－紫色派对”于11月26日于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%A2%85.md#1">杨梅</a>分局上湖派出所旁农地办理，希望藉由此活动让民众更深入地走进杨梅、体验杨梅的美好。</p>
<p>活动的舞台表演可说是精彩绝伦，特别邀请到曾入围过25届金曲奖最佳客语歌手奖的黄于娟以及曲风广泛跨世代的宠爱乐团，除了歌唱演出之外，更是邀请大朋友、小朋友们都喜欢的小丑尚恩杂耍秀以及有趣又好玩的细菌哥哥气球泡泡秀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14124369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124369" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124369-649564.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14124369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124369-649564-600x220.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="220" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124369" class="wp-caption-text">“2023桃园仙草嘉年华公所主题日活动－紫色派对”登场。（桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%A2%85.md#1">杨梅</a>区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14124374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124374-649569.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14124374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124374-649569-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124374" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%A6%E5%B9%BB.md#1">梦幻</a>“紫色派对”真吸引人。 （桃园市杨梅区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14124372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124372" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124372-649567.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14124372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124372-649567-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124372" class="wp-caption-text">有趣又好玩的细菌哥哥气球泡泡秀。（桃园市杨梅区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除舞台表演之外，活动也邀集杨梅休闲农业区的耀辉休闲牧场带领大家一起彩绘小牛，民众可以自由作画在小牛身上，亲手创作一个独一无二又具有特色的可爱小牛。同时也邀请到肥嘟嘟多肉植物园来到现场教学多肉组盆DIY，一同感受到大自然带来的疗愈，透过手作体验也可以训练肌力、专注力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14124370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124370" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124370-649565.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14124370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124370-649565-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124370" class="wp-caption-text">邀请到肥嘟嘟多肉植物园来到现场教学多肉组盆DIY。（桃园市杨梅区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14124371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124371" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124371-649566.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14124371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124371-649566-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124371" class="wp-caption-text">于“杨梅休闲农业区”粉丝专页按赞，即可获得日式极简折叠椅。（桃园市杨梅区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>杨梅区长施永恭表示，现场除了手作体验之外，只要于11月26日当天上午于活动现场拍照打卡，并于“杨梅休闲农业区”粉丝专页按赞，即可获得日式极简折叠椅，数量有限，详细活动资讯请参考官方粉丝团“杨梅休闲农业区”公告，邀请大家赶快来现场打卡换好礼。◇</p>
