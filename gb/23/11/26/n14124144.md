<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>冬季恋歌 云林萨克斯风之夜震撼登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124151-649542-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>云林县萨克斯风管乐团成员个个音乐高手。（云林县萨克斯风管乐团提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月26日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A8%E5%85%8B%E6%96%AF%E9%A3%8E.md#1">萨克斯风</a>管乐团25日晚上，在云林表演厅举办一场壮丽的音乐会，标题为“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E5%AD%A3%E6%81%8B%E6%AD%8C.md#1">冬季恋歌</a>~云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A8%E5%85%8B%E6%96%AF%E9%A3%8E.md#1">萨克斯风</a>之夜”。这个音乐盛会呈现令人难以忘怀的音乐体验，包括多首经典曲目和重新编排的合奏曲，由国内知名音乐总监陈国郎老师亲自操刀指导。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14124145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124145" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124145-649536.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14124145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124145-649536-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124145" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>县萨克斯风管乐团成员许平和、詹惠枝老师。（许平和提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云林县萨克斯风管乐团由一群业余音乐爱好者组成，他们对萨克斯风音乐充满热情，并特聘知名萨克斯风音乐老师陈国郎，担任音乐总监和指导老师。这个团队致力于共同学习、提升音乐水平，并推广萨克斯风音乐文化，同时积极参与公益演出和艺术文化推广活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14124148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124148" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124148-649539.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14124148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124148-649539-600x466.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="466" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124148" class="wp-caption-text">云林县萨克斯风管乐团团员和粉丝合影。（叶昭吟提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14124146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124146-649537.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14124146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124146-649537-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124146" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E5%AD%A3%E6%81%8B%E6%AD%8C.md#1">冬季恋歌</a> ，云林萨克斯风之夜震撼登场。（云林县萨克斯风管乐团提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>斗南扶轮社社长王智霖，音乐老师叶昭吟以及300E复合区中文版狮子会副社长郭月娇，不约而同前往聆赏，对陈国郎老师以及团员们高超技巧赞不绝口，并表示主持人李学人校长表现相当称职之外，现场演唱“夜来香”，是今晚的彩蛋，笑称他是被教育界耽搁的歌手。这场音乐为观众带来一场冬季之夜的音乐盛宴，让人们在美丽的音乐中感受文化和艺术的美丽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14124152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124152" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124152-649543.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14124152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124152-649543-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124152" class="wp-caption-text">冬季恋歌音乐会吸引各界贵宾前来参加。（叶昭吟提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14124150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124150" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124150-649541.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14124150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124150-649541-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124150" class="wp-caption-text">冬季恋歌音乐会满满的人潮。（郭月娇提供）</figcaption></figure>
