<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>乔州2023年秋季最安全医院名单出炉</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年11月18日讯】（大纪元记者吕尚明亚特兰大编译报导）在美国仍未有政府订定的医疗服务安全的评定标准的情况下，一些大企业与医疗服务采购组织于2000年一同创立了称为“蛙跳组”（The Leapfrog Group）的非营利机构，宗旨在于研究与比较各医院提供的医疗服务的安全度，以鼓励医疗界如蛙跳般大幅度提升安全水准。</p>
<p>蛙跳组每年发表两次全美医院安全度报告，他们最近公布了最新的2023年秋季报告。报告显示<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%94%E5%B7%9E.md#1">乔州</a>以及全美在疫情期间医院感染率增高后，现在安全度大幅提升。</p>
<p>蛙跳组在全美评定了约三千家医院，包括<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%94%E5%B7%9E.md#1">乔州</a>的80家。乔州有24家医院获得A级评定，17家获得B级评定，34家获得C级评定，最后有5家获得D级评定。乔州获得A级的医院如下：</p>
<p>Wellstar West Georgia Medical Center, Lagrange</p>
<p>Piedmont Augusta Hospital, Augusta</p>
<p>Wellstar Kennestone Medical Center, Marietta</p>
<p>Memorial Health University Medical Center, Savannah</p>
<p>Wellstar Paulding Hospital, Hiram</p>
<p>Northeast Georgia Medical Center Barrow, Winder</p>
<p>Atrium Health Floyd Medical Center, Rome</p>
<p>Emory St. Joseph&#8217;s Hospital, Atlanta</p>
<p>Colquitt Regional Medical Center, Moultrie</p>
<p>Fairview Park Hospital, Dublin</p>
<p>Memorial Health Meadows Hospital, Vidalia</p>
<p>Piedmont Macon Medical Center, Macon</p>
<p>Southern Regional Medical Center, Riverdale</p>
<p>AdventHealth Redmond, Rome</p>
<p>Doctors Hospital of Augusta, Augusta</p>
<p>Piedmont Macon North Hospital, Macon</p>
<p>Piedmont Mountainside Hospital, Jasper</p>
<p>Emory Johns Creek Hospital, Johns Creek</p>
<p>Piedmont Columbus Regional Northside, Columbus</p>
<p>Northeast Georgia Medical Center Braselton, Braselton</p>
<p>Tift Regional Medical Center, Tifton</p>
<p>Memorial Satilla Health, Waycross</p>
<p>Coffee Regional Medical Center, Douglas</p>
<p>Piedmont Athens Regional Medical Center, Athens</p>
