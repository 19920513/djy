<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】多莓麦片酥</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119294-0d192d686315ac4cfdd5876c42b79583-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>烤水果是冬日的舒适甜点。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>今天分享的酥皮<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>甜点，你不会在烘焙店找得到，但它却是流行的居家烘焙食谱。</p>
<p>酥香的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%A6%E7%89%87.md#1">麦片</a>下面是厚厚的多汁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%93%E6%9E%9C.md#1">莓果</a>，非常适合与亲友一起分享。吃不完时冷冻或冷藏保存，从冰箱取出搭配冰淇淋享用，即使在冬天也让人停不住大快朵颐。</p>
<p>烘烤这款点心不需要任何经验，准备食材需要花费十分钟。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%A6%E7%89%87.md#1">麦片</a>下面的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>可以换成自己喜欢的品种，枫糖浆也可以根据自己的甜度调整用量。</p>
<h2>材料准备：</h2>
<p>8×8英寸烤盘<br />
3杯冷冻多莓水果（包括草莓、黑莓、蓝莓和覆盆子），可用新鲜水果替换<br />
100克+50克枫糖浆<br />
50克碎核桃<br />
1杯老式燕麦片（rolled oats）<br />
30克面粉<br />
1/4茶匙盐<br />
60克无盐黄油，融化</p>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1. 预热烤箱热风对流功能375华氏度。</p>
<p>2. 融化黄油：冰箱取出黄油切小块放碗中，用微波炉加热25秒，取出搅拌。<br />
3. 搅拌碗中放入燕麦片、盐、面粉、碎核桃一起拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119290" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119290-18fb7d6779c82fe166339c5a079f2a27.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119290-18fb7d6779c82fe166339c5a079f2a27-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119290" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 接着加入融化的黄油和100克枫糖浆，再次拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119292-a5d2043917cde30b85781bee6f10d649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119292-a5d2043917cde30b85781bee6f10d649-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119292" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 烤盘中放入3杯多莓水果，上面铺燕麦混合物，然后浇上50克枫糖浆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119291" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119291-bfea47155e856f21ee8223a4140b446a.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119291-bfea47155e856f21ee8223a4140b446a-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119291" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 烤30分钟至表面燕麦金黄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119293" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119293-5178112a31b151aefcfcbac670b019b2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119293-5178112a31b151aefcfcbac670b019b2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119293" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
