<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】娇小而实用 2023 Toyota Corolla Cross Hybrid XSE</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119008-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023 Toyota Corolla Cross Hybrid XSE。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月18日讯】Toyota的紧凑型SUV一向是C-HR，C-HR虽然细小，功能较很好，但也由于尺寸太小，内里空间也狭窄，载人载货也更困难，所以紧凑型SUV在北美也纷纷退位，又改变尺寸变得更大，好像Nissan Juke及Honda HR-V。</p>
<p><a title="【新车试驾】娇小而实用 2023 Toyota Corolla Cross Hybrid XSE" src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/zh-TW/embed/1g9949auv4f1INUmmoEwL459p1gs1c" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Toyota C-HR也退位予这周测试的Toyota Corolla Cross，首先在尺寸上，Corolla Cross为176.8吋长、71.9吋宽，车高则希64.8吋，比C-HR的171.2吋长、70.7吋 宽、61.6吋 高，是大了一个码。而且由于CH-R是采用四门Coupe的设计，所以车尾闸的倾斜角度压得更低，故此也令载货区更小。而Corolla Cross更接近RAV4的方型设计，所以可使载货区增大，达到21.5立方呎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14118990" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118990" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118990-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118990" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118990-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118990" class="wp-caption-text">2023 Toyota Corolla Cross Hybrid XSE。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Corolla Cross的外形有着Toyota近年的设计，测试的Hybrid版前脸与纯汽油版不一样，汽油版与今代RAV4比较接近，而Hybrid版则与上一代Facelift后的样子相似，也是在厂徽周围密封，在上方有一横线，再在下方有一大梯型进风口，所以有“似曾相识”的感觉。而Corolla Cross尾闸更像Lexus RX，倍增亲切！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14118992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118992" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118992-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118992" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118992-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_09-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118992" class="wp-caption-text">头灯装有LED饰灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14118993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118993" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118993-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118993-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118993" class="wp-caption-text">进风口设计与上一代RAV4相似。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14118994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118994-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118994-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118994" class="wp-caption-text">银黑双色轮圈。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在底盘方面，Corolla Cross使用TNGA-C，与 Corolla的一样，再在此基础上加上电子四驱系统（Electronic AWD system），令Hybrid版的四驱系统更简单。Hybrid的动力系统由一副2升直四引擎，再加上一个53kW的永磁同步马达，组合而成，总马力输出达到196匹，由0到60英里／时的加速厂方数据为8秒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14118995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118995" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118995-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_24.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118995-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118995" class="wp-caption-text">2升直四引擎加53kW马达。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一个为什么Corolla Cross使用此名字，就是它的关系密切，亲密到内装是同一设计，令驾驶者分不清是在驾驶Corolla或是Corolla Cross。由于测试的Corolla Cross是最高版本XSE，所以仪表板是一个7吋的萤幕，比其他版的半实体半萤幕（4.2吋）要好。中控台的轻触萤幕尺寸达到8吋，内里运行EnTune 3.0 Audio系统，附有6个扬声器，以蓝牙技术接驳手机，让驾驶者可透过车内音响通话或播放手机内的音乐，测试车音响已升级为JBL高级音响及9个扬声器。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14118996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118996" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118996-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_36.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118996-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_36-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118996" class="wp-caption-text">内装是跟Corolla同一设计。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Corolla Cross Hybrid 将提供三种等级：S、SE和XSE。S和SE版标配17吋合金轮圈、8吋触控萤幕、LED头灯和尾灯以及智慧钥匙。SE版另增加了隐私玻璃、车顶行李架路轨和换档拨片，安全装备则有盲点监视器（BSM）和后方交叉交通警报（RCTA）。最高级的XSE版，是以SE为基础，并升级配备有18吋合金轮圈、高级LED头灯、尾灯和雾灯、Softex座椅以及加热前座椅和电动驾驶座椅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14118997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118997-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_26.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118997-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118997" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板是7吋萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14118998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118998" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118998-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_27.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118998-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_27-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118998" class="wp-caption-text">中控台萤幕尺寸达到8吋。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日常生活中，一个人开车的时候占了大多数，所以像Corolla Cross这种尺寸是刚好的，载货时又可把后排座位放下，便有40.3立方呎的空间。而Hybrid版的1.8升引擎加马达，虽没有惊人的动力，起步加速也不是很快，但起步后在高速公路上也不会落后于人！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14118999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118999" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118999-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_29.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118999-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118999" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14119001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119001" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119001-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_35.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14119001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119001-2023_Toyota_Corola_Cross_Hybrid_35-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119001" class="wp-caption-text">Softex座椅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
