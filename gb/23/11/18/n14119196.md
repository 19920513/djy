<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2023云林巾彩耕地艺术节 虎尾同心公园盛大登场！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119199-648021-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“2023云林巾彩耕地艺术节”会场。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月18日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B0%BE.md#1">虎尾</a>同心公园18日至19日，欢迎大朋友小朋友一起来参与“2023云林巾彩耕地艺术节”，由行政院云嘉南区联合服务中心，和社团法人云林县<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%9B%E5%B7%BE.md#1">毛巾</a>产业科技发展协会合办的盛大活动，已迈入第五届。今年更特别感谢虎尾魅力商圈的加持，活动结合了市集、音乐、摸彩等多元活动，为虎尾带来了愉快的假日时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119200" style="width: 490px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119200-648022-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14119200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119200-648022-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="490" b="368" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119200" class="wp-caption-text">现场有精彩的表演节目。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“巾彩耕地”一词的由来源于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%9B%E5%B7%BE.md#1">毛巾</a>产业，从泥土开始，生长出棉花，再将棉花织成布，最终制成毛巾，这个产业从最原始的土地发展而来，成就了云林独特的产业风华和文化底蕴。今年的“云林巾彩耕地艺术节”更加丰富多彩，活动场地不仅有台湾毛巾业者市集，还特别以“巾彩动物派对”为主题，展示了10只以毛巾制作的巨型动物，还有稻草大地游戏园区、纸风车剧团的精彩演出、YOYO家族及其他表演团体的艺文活动等等。会场内还展出在地艺术家和亲子们一同共创的巨型毛巾艺术作品，多个艺术展区、巾彩艺术廊道、毛巾生活体验馆等设施，将让参观者感受到毛巾与艺术的视觉震撼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119201" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119201-648023-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14119201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119201-648023-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="440" b="587" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119201" class="wp-caption-text">特别以“巾彩动物派对”为主题，展示了10只以毛巾制作的巨型动物。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14119198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119198" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119198-648020-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14119198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119198-648020-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119198" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B0%BE.md#1">虎尾</a>是台湾毛巾的故乡。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的活动更与虎尾魅力商圈合作，紧密联系了70多间商家，为参与者提供了多重礼遇。前500名兑换集点卡的参与者将获得魅力商圈抵用券1张，可在虎尾镇内的参与店家使用。此外，前500名入场的参与者，还有机会拿到开园好礼，于毛巾品牌摊位消费集点更有机会抽取多项奖品，包括65吋超大电视、脚踏车、毛巾礼盒、运动毛巾等。这是一个难得的机会，不要错过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119197" style="width: 510px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119197-648019-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14119197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119197-648019-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="510" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119197" class="wp-caption-text">泡泡区，小朋友玩的很开心。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14119200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119200" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119200-648022-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14119200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119200-648022-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119200" class="wp-caption-text">现场有精彩的表演节目。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
