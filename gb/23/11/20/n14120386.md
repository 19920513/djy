<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>行政院长陈建仁  访视铜锣乡杭菊产业</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120387-648471-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>行政院长陈建仁与来宾体验采菊乐趣（许享富／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月20日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%97%E6%A0%97.md#1">苗栗</a>报导）行政院<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%BB%BA%E4%BB%81.md#1">陈建仁</a>院长由农业部次长陈添寿、副县长邓桂菊等人陪同，参访<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%9C%E9%94%A3.md#1">铜锣</a>乡秋美人农场，了解该乡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%AD%E8%8F%8A.md#1">杭菊</a>产业的发展，听取乡农会总干事曾义孝的简报，品尝乡农会研发的杭菊茶冻等产品，并下田采菊花，体验田园风情。</p>
<figure id="14120394" aria-describedby="caption-14120394" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120394-648478-450x675.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120394-648478-450x675.jpeg" alt="秋美人农场张焕文指导院长辨别菊花好坏与采取的方法，尝试采菊乐" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14120394" class="wp-caption-text">秋美人农场张焕文指导院长辨别菊花好坏与采取的方法，尝试采菊乐（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14120391" aria-describedby="caption-14120391" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120391-648475-450x294.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120391-648475-450x294.jpeg" alt="行政院长陈建仁，发言人林子伦与中办执行长徐定祯，品尝乡农会的杭菊茶、杭菊茶冻 赞" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14120391" class="wp-caption-text">行政院长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%BB%BA%E4%BB%81.md#1">陈建仁</a>，发言人林子伦与中办执行长徐定祯，品尝乡农会的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%AD%E8%8F%8A.md#1">杭菊</a>茶、杭菊茶冻 赞（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14120389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120389" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120389-648473.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14120389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120389-648473-450x675.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120389" class="wp-caption-text">农业部长陈添寿表示，政府以照顾农民福利为目标，协助农民自主管理，技术提升，农粮署严格的检测把关，食的安全。同时协助行销。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14120388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120388" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120388-648472.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14120388" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120388-648472-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120388" class="wp-caption-text">行政院长陈建仁表示，政府推出3保1金照顾农民权益。同时农业政策朝智慧化、技术化、人力问题解决等，朝台湾新农业前进。以永续、安全与农粮食价格稳定为目标。也感谢县府、乡农会的辅导及产销班的努力，让<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%9C%E9%94%A3.md#1">铜锣</a>杭菊产业发展走向国际化。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>铜锣乡农会去年成立杭菊特色作物集团产区，面积共计14公顷，通过产销履历认证，落实契作农户的田间安全用药教育指导及建立企业化管理模式，引导产、制、销合作经营，创造品牌特色，建构具产业竞争力之杂粮供应体系。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120392" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120392-648476.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14120392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120392-648476-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120392" class="wp-caption-text">铜锣乡农会理事长叶东维赠送杭菊礼盒（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秋美人农场2017年由班长张焕文创立，经县府、乡农会及农粮署辅导下，长达5、6年100%农药安全检测合格；而杭菊产销班第1班，更荣获112年全国十大绩优农业产销班。</p>
<p>行政院长陈建仁感谢县府、乡农会的辅导及产销班的努力，让铜锣杭菊产业发展越来越具有规模，希望未来能够推广到国际，让全世界看见台湾，成为台湾的代名词，是台湾的骄傲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120393" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120393-648477.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14120393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120393-648477-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120393" class="wp-caption-text">杭菊生产过程不落地、同时严格检验，零农药检出，食的安全把关（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>副县长邓桂菊表示，陈建仁院长关心苗县杭菊产业是对地方农业的肯定，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%97%E6%A0%97.md#1">苗栗</a>拥有独特的山海景色，丰富的闽客文化，县府团队将持续协助地方发展特色产业，推广在地高品质的农产品。</p>
