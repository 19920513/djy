<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾拼布艺术节 北港晒被踩街平安“袋”着走</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123783-649446-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>于25日上午在北港镇水道头文化园区隆重登场。（蔡享润提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月25日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县政府在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%B8%AF.md#1">北港</a>举办的“云林厚工学”系列活动中，隆重举办“台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%BC%E5%B8%83.md#1">拼布</a>艺术节”，于25日上午在北港镇水道头文化园区隆重登场。主办单位拼布发展协会的理事长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E7%A7%80%E6%83%A0.md#1">周秀惠</a>表示，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%BC%E5%B8%83.md#1">拼布</a>艺术节引领民众参与街头晒出各种拼布创作，同时透过义卖拼布作品支持公益事业，使拼布艺术不再仅限于静态展示，而能透过互动展示变得更具活力与生活化，这创举吸引众多支持者，顶着大太阳，用行动一起走上街，把作品做动态展示。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14123781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123781" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123781-649444.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14123781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123781-649444-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123781" class="wp-caption-text">台湾拼布艺术节 ，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%B8%AF.md#1">北港</a>晒被踩街平安“袋”着走。（蔡享润提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14123785" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123785" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123785-649448.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14123785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123785-649448-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123785" class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>县笨港文创拼布发展协会”联合“The MEGA Yunlin 云林厚工学”活动。（蔡享润提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“云林县笨港文创拼布发展协会”联合“The MEGA Yunlin 云林厚工学”活动，共同筹划在厚工学卫星展馆北港水道头文化园区，举办“台湾拼布艺术节”，展示今年度最大规模的户外拼布晒被展览，共有来自全台二百余名拼布爱好者参与，展现拼布职人的高超技艺。</p>
<p>台湾拼布艺术节的晒被踩街活动，从2018年由拼布达人徐昕辰发起，已在全台各县市蓬勃举办，今年由云林接下举办权，期望弘扬拼布生活艺术，传承拼布工艺理念，为拼布文化注入更多元的元素，并推动云林观光产业与艺术文化的多元发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14123787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123787" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123787-649450.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14123787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123787-649450-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123787" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E7%A7%80%E6%83%A0.md#1">周秀惠</a>指出，台湾拼布艺术节的晒被踩街活动，从2018年由拼布达人徐昕辰发起，已在全台各县市蓬勃举办。（蔡享润提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14123782" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123782" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123782-649445.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14123782" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123782-649445-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123782" class="wp-caption-text">拼布艺术节引领民众参与街头晒出各种拼布创作。（蔡享润提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14123784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123784" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123784-649447.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14123784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123784-649447-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123784" class="wp-caption-text">周秀惠表示透过义卖拼布作品支持公益事业，使拼布艺术不再仅限于静态展示。（蔡享润提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周秀惠表示，这次的拼布活动展现拼布艺术的创作趣味，并让人们了解创作背后的故事与设计，分享拼布艺术在国内外的发展。同时，各地的艺术家和素人创作者透过作品展示，展现拼布的艺术美学。此外，拼布爱好者还准备了七十余个朝天宫香火袋造型的“拼布平安袋”，所得款项全部捐给公益慈善团体。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14123789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123789" style="width: 447px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123789-649452.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14123789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123789-649452-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="447" b="795" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123789" class="wp-caption-text">准备了70余个朝天宫香火袋造型的“拼布平安袋”，所得款项将全部捐给公益慈善团体。（周秀惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>25日上午数百人一同参与这项大型晒被踩街活动，每人携带一幅长、宽各100公分的拼布图，踩街沿着北港中山路前进，前往朝天宫妈祖庙朝圣，形成一条充满拼布之美的长河。现场还设有“拼布市集”、“公益义卖”和“团体走秀”等周边活动，大家一同平安“袋”着走，共享拼布艺术的热情和美好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14123786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123786" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123786-649449.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14123786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123786-649449-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123786" class="wp-caption-text">有来自全台二百余名拼布爱好者参与，展现拼布职人的高超技艺。（蔡享润提供）</figcaption></figure>
