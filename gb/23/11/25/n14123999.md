<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小校濂洞国小丝竹乐 脱颖而出赢得优等</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124001-649499-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>112学年度新北市学生音乐比赛中，脱颖而出，赢得了优等奖的最佳殊荣。（濂洞国小提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月25日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">新北市</a>报导）位于新北市十三层遗址附近的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BF%82%E6%B4%9E%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">濂洞国小</a>，是一所50人以下的偏乡小校，要参加国乐比赛，充满着挑战。但<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9D%E7%AB%B9%E4%B9%90%E5%9B%A2.md#1">丝竹乐团</a>却在112学年度新北市学生音乐比赛中，脱颖而出，赢得了优等奖的最佳殊荣，鼓舞了全校的师生及家长。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9D%E7%AB%B9%E4%B9%90%E5%9B%A2.md#1">丝竹乐团</a>在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BF%82%E6%B4%9E%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">濂洞国小</a>已经存在了20多年，学校一直努力维护并传承着国乐文化的精髓。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14124000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124000" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124000-649498-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14124000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124000-649498-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124000" class="wp-caption-text">小校濂洞国小丝竹乐 ，脱颖而出赢得优等。（濂洞国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄恕懿校长，担任校长已经七年，一直致力于实现学校的五育均衡发展。他表示，今年的挑战更是艰钜，上台参赛的10位学生中，有5位是新手，24日的比赛对他们来说是初次体验。在短短的时间内，要赶上学长姐们的水准绝非易事，但这些学生怀着极大的热忱，努力学习，同时也感谢学长姐们的耐心教导和等待。这种彼此支持的氛围让学弟妹们感到非常安心，他们一起努力，稳稳地演奏完毕曲目，实现了他们的目标，表现非常出色！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14124002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124002" style="width: 519px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124002-649500-300x225.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14124002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124002-649500-300x225.jpg" alt="" width="519" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124002" class="wp-caption-text">濂洞国小挑战艰钜，上台参赛的10位学生中，有5位是新手，24日的比赛对他们来说是初次体验。（濂洞国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14124003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124003" style="width: 369px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124003-649501-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14124003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124003-649501-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="369" b="492" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124003" class="wp-caption-text">濂洞国小丝竹乐团同学，上台前不断的练习。（濂洞国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>校长黄恕懿特别感谢巫兴昌、刘纪言老师的精湛指导，以及每周三陪伴孩子们练习的矞心老师、小娄老师、敏馨老师、淑娟老师和治民主任。同时，他也衷心感谢孩子们的家长们，因为他们的支持和鼓励，是这一项壮举取得成功的重要关键。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14124004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124004" style="width: 586px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124004-649502-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14124004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124004-649502-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="586" b="440" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124004" class="wp-caption-text">校长黄恕懿特别感谢巫兴昌、刘纪言老师的精湛指导。（濂洞国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14124005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14124005" style="width: 561px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124005-649503-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14124005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14124005-649503-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="561" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14124005" class="wp-caption-text">位于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">新北市</a>十三层遗址附近的濂洞国小，是一所50人以下的偏乡小校，要参加国乐比赛，充满着挑战。（濂洞国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
