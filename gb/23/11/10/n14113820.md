<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>希腊小馆Kisamos Taverna</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113849-1-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>餐厅老板Marino自幼酷爱烹饪。（梁月／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月10日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">梁月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大温哥华采访报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">渔人码头，记忆中总是伴随着和煦的暖阳以及带着牡蛎味的海风。码头边各种类型的餐听，一应俱全。在多到令人眼花缭乱的餐听中，一家清爽的蓝底招牌上写着大大的白色</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">KISAMOS</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">字样的餐厅跃入眼帘。驻足细看，招牌左边写着“传统天然希腊美食”。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><a title="Kisamos Taverna希腊餐厅 —— 一场与地中海的美丽邂逅" src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/zh-TW/embed/1g8ne0epmp02b5N2o3Y9slThR1e11c" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a>　</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">想到在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E9%A4%90.md#1">中餐</a>扎堆的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%97%E6%B2%BB%E6%96%87.md#1">列治文</a>，平日吃惯了高油、高钠，但满足思乡胃的中餐，今日不如尝试一下希腊的传统美食，与遥远的地中海来一场邂逅。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kisamos</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">不算大，步入餐厅，店内巨幅油画瞬间把我带入了那个美丽的国度。画中蓝白的小船拴着缆绳，静静地躺在水面上；威武的海神波塞顿，目光坚毅地望着远方</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">&#8230;&#8230;</span></p>
<h2><strong>经典希腊<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E4%B8%B2.md#1">烤串</a>Souvlakia</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14113842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14113842" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113842-2.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14113842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113842-2-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14113842" class="wp-caption-text">希腊<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E4%B8%B2.md#1">烤串</a>Souvlakia。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kisamos餐厅以希腊克里特岛上的一个小镇命名，开业20多年来，一直经营传统正宗的希腊美食。餐厅老板Marino 的父母拥有几家餐馆，耳濡目染之故，店主自小酷爱烹饪。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">打开菜单，多是由异域名称直译的菜名。见我一筹莫展，老板笑着询问是否需要帮忙。老板推荐了他们的招牌“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Souvlakia</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”小烤串系列，有猪肉串、牛肉串、羊肉串、虾串等多种选择。腌制过的肉撒上多层次的地中海香料，经过烘烤香气十足。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Souvlakia</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">是希腊最经典的平民美食，肉串搭配香料拌饭、烤土豆、希腊沙拉、柠檬和一小份</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Tzaziki</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（希腊酸奶黄瓜酱），很纯正的地中海美味。这一份餐就同时包含了优质蛋白质、低升糖碳水和多种类蔬菜，是现代集追求美味和需求健康饮食的人不可多得的选择。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">喜爱卷饼的，可以点一份烤肉卷饼（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Souvlakia Pita Wrap</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">），这是到希腊旅游必吃的一道街头美食。</span></p>
<h2><strong>主打招牌菜 慢炖羊肉</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14113843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14113843" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113843-3.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14113843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113843-3-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14113843" class="wp-caption-text">慢炖羊肉。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">House Specials</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”特色推荐里面，老板推荐“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Roast Lamb</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”。这是一款精致慢炖羊肉，搭配特色薄荷肉汁，给人一种入口即化但却不厚重不油腻的清新滋味。</span></p>
<h2><strong>素食系列Lahanika</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14113844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14113844" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113844-4.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14113844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113844-4-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14113844" class="wp-caption-text">千层面Boureki。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">如果是素食主义者也不必担心，菜单“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Lahanika</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”系列就是营养素食餐。比如“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Boureki</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”是类似千层面，但原材料全部都替换成了素食，如意大利青瓜、土豆等，同时也搭配烤土豆，沙拉和希腊酸奶酱。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14113845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14113845" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113845-5-1.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14113845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113845-5-1-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14113845" class="wp-caption-text">店内素食主打菠菜派。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Spanakopita</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”菠菜派也是店内素食主打。菠菜派是家喻户晓的希腊传统美食，它的历史可追溯到公元前五世纪。可作为开胃小菜或主食使用。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">最后还有一个“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Casseroles</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”砂锅慢炖系列也是餐厅的招牌菜。餐厅自制的红酒番茄酱，慢炖麦粒面，搭配大虾加上精致摆盘，也是一道平衡了美味和营养的菜肴。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14113846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14113846" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113846-6-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14113846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113846-6-6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14113846" class="wp-caption-text">Casseroles砂锅慢炖系列。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">用餐高峰过后，餐厅老板</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Marino</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">笑着过来问口味如何？我告诉他，这是一场打开我与地中海美食的开端，并且称赞他们后厨出餐效率高，味道赞！</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Marino</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">表示，他们整个后厨团队像一家人一样融洽，把这份快乐，用心通过美食传递给顾客。周末时段非常繁忙，如有聚会，一定记得订位！</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">KISAMOS</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">餐厅周三至周一营业时间为中午</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">点到晚上</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">30</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；周二只有半天营业时间，下午</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">点到晚上</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">30</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
