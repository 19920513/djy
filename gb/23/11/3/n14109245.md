<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“幸福农村绿色照顾计划成果展”展现长者的青春活力</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109246-645270-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>二崙乡农会“112年幸福农村暨绿色照顾计划推动联合成果展”3日在二崙乡农会举行，县长张丽善及县议员廖伟晴、二崙乡长钟东荣，肉品市场副总经理邱良阅都到场与大家同乐。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月03日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）二崙乡农会3日举办“112年幸福<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E6%9D%91.md#1">农村</a>暨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E8%89%B2%E7%85%A7%E9%A1%BE%E8%AE%A1%E5%88%92.md#1">绿色照顾计划</a>推动联合成果展”，现场除了家政班色香味俱全的创意料理，还有高龄者的手工艺作品，更有社区妈妈的土风舞及歌唱表演，现场热闹非凡！展现长者的青春活力。县长张丽善及县议员廖伟晴、二崙乡长钟东荣，肉品市场副总经理邱良阅都到场与大家同乐！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14109247" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14109247" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109247-645271-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14109247" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109247-645271-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14109247" class="wp-caption-text">崙西社区妈妈土风舞班热力四射的表演，很难相信她们都是65岁以上，甚至80岁的长者。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14109250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14109250" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109250-645275-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14109250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109250-645275-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14109250" class="wp-caption-text">现场展示家政班色香味俱全的创意料理。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14109251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14109251" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109251-645276-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14109251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109251-645276-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14109251" class="wp-caption-text">创意料理~ 卷发的女孩。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14109249" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14109249" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109249-645274-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14109249" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109249-645274-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14109249" class="wp-caption-text">高龄者的手工艺作品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张丽善说，农会在农民心目中不仅仅是个存贷款及农事耕作相关的功能，更是一个可以就近、就地照顾农民的机构，非常感谢二崙乡农会加入长照行列，守护我们的乡亲，大大提升农民的生活品质。</p>
<p>二崙乡农会总干事李达彦表示，二崙农会于109年即开始办理“绿色照顾站”，至今已服务超过5000人次的长者，提供二崙乡长者一个互动共助的场所，让咱地方的长辈，来到农会由农会来照顾，有个活动及学习的场域。二崙农会，目前家政班共有36班，将近700二崙位的班员，是二崙乡农会最有实力的基层团体，除了平时办理的技艺传承、生活知识讲习活动外，农会还会在每年底办理成果展，提供家政班员一个展示的舞台，今年度成果展更特别邀约绿照长者一起参与！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14109252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14109252" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109252-645278-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14109252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109252-645278-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14109252" class="wp-caption-text">不老顽童表演的用水桶打鼓，打出节奏整齐的鼓声，展现长者青春不老的活力。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14109254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14109254" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109254-645279-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14109254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109254-645279-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14109254" class="wp-caption-text">充满活力的崙西社区妈妈土风舞班。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天首先登场的是不老顽童表演的用水桶打鼓，在老师的带领下，打出节奏整齐的鼓声，展现长者青春不老的活力！紧接着社区妈妈及社区阿公精彩的歌唱表演，还有社区妈妈热力四射的土风舞演出，很难相信她们都是65岁以上，甚至80岁的长者，平时忙于农务的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E6%9D%91.md#1">农村</a>阿嬷！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14109248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14109248" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109248-645273-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14109248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14109248-645273-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14109248" class="wp-caption-text">68岁的陈丽云是二崙乡歌唱比赛的常胜军，今天她也参加社区妈妈歌唱演出，稳健的台风，不愧是个训练有素的唱匠。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>68岁的陈丽云是农会家政班及绿色照顾的成员，热爱歌唱的她，平常就参加二崙老人会的歌唱班，是歌唱比赛的常胜军，今天她也参加社区妈妈的歌唱演出，稳健的台风，不愧是个训练有素的唱匠。</p>
