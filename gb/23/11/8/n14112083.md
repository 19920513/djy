<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>开南大学重视茶文化传承及茶道教学永续发展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112088-646085-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>林玥秀校长感谢陈右人博士带来了“桃园茶叶的历史、现况与未来发展契机”的精彩讲座。（开南大学提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月08日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E9%87%91.md#1">茶金</a>历史荣光中，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E7%AB%B9%E8%8B%97.md#1">桃竹苗</a>一直是台湾茶产业重镇，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E5%8D%97%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">开南大学</a>作为在地高教基地，重视<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">茶文化</a>传承及茶道教学的永续发展，积极参与传承台湾茶文化的工作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14112086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14112086" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112086-646084.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14112086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112086-646084-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14112086" class="wp-caption-text">轻松对谈，分享茶知识与经验。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E5%8D%97%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">开南大学</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开南大学“台湾茶叶与茶艺介绍”通识课程，8日邀请茶改场前场长陈右人博士，以“桃园茶叶的历史、现况与未来发展契机”为题，发表讲座。活动中，茶艺社的学生积极参与，使用复兴区拉拉山茶叶，协助泡茶与司茶，为活动增添亮点。林玥秀校长表示，配合教育部高教深耕计划，以及发展美学与茶艺是校务发展既定方向。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14112085" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14112085" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112085-646083.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14112085" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112085-646083-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14112085" class="wp-caption-text">席地而坐，一同品茶，茶款为梨山高山乌龙茶。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>茶道蕴藏中华文化悠久的美学与智慧，在咖啡随手可得的当下，希望透过课程融合“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">茶文化</a>”的传统与时尚之生活美学，藉由品茶香、赏茶器、食茶点等多种观点呈现，培育学生们的美学素养。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14112089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14112089" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112089-646086.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14112089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112089-646086-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14112089" class="wp-caption-text">茶艺师与学校参与老师合影。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开大这学期中，陆续举办“学茶随师吃茶”系列活动，其中邀请了桃园在地两位茶艺师，曹淑英和符湘萍，为大日生与外籍生们展示茶艺的优雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14112090" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14112090" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112090-646087.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14112090" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112090-646087-600x380.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14112090" class="wp-caption-text">学生们练习泡茶技巧。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14112084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14112084" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112084-646082.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14112084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112084-646082-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14112084" class="wp-caption-text">外籍生仔细品尝不同阶段的茶风味。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动中，台生主导茶席的布置，亲自为外籍生泡茶和奉茶。候汤时，学生自由自在地交谈，不仅拉近了彼此的距离，也创造了外籍生难忘的回忆，同时也让大家更深刻体验到品茶的乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14112091" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14112091" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112091-646088.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14112091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112091-646088-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14112091" class="wp-caption-text">学生们练习泡茶技巧。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>观光院教师邀请中研院的余舜德博士和茶艺达人温淑萍女士，为研究生讲授“茶文化与冻顶茶的历史”。课堂中，学生们不再分你我，而是席地而坐，一同品茶，进行深入的对话和交流，为台湾茶文化的传承开创了全新的沟通方式。</p>
<p>参与茶计划的空运系彭效武教授说，课程带入茶艺术、带入生活，也将茶道融于生活知识，藉由一杯随手可得的茶汤，来提升对学生们对茶文化有更深的认识，这也是大学教育致力于传承台湾茶文化的承诺。◇</p>
