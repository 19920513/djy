<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东南国中隆重举行66周年校运会 赛事精彩热闹</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114265-646690-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>东南国中任职20年和30年的伙伴。（廖俪芬提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月11日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%9E%BA.md#1">西螺</a>镇的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E5%8D%97%E5%9B%BD%E4%B8%AD.md#1">东南国中</a>于10日和11日，连续两天欢庆其66周年校庆运动会，趣味竞赛、田赛、径赛等精彩活动纷纷登场，然而，最让人期待的当属一年一度的校友接力赛。今年特别之处在于，校内的教职员也积极参与，现场气氛热烈，加油声不断。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114268" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114268-646693.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14114268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114268-646693-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114268" class="wp-caption-text">场外啦啦队，卖力的为选手加油。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>已在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E5%8D%97%E5%9B%BD%E4%B8%AD.md#1">东南国中</a>任教二十年的校长黄佩芬在开幕式上鼓励学生，勇敢表现自己，也不要忘了安全第一。曾担任东南国中校长四十年的董事长戴渝也亲临现场，表达对师生的感谢之情。他感动地说：“谢谢老师们的认真负责，与东南国中一同成长。”东南国中一直以来都以学生的素质，和校园的美景而闻名，每周六开放校园参观时，吸引许多游客前来拍照喂鱼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114274" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114274" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114274-646698.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14114274" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114274-646698-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114274" class="wp-caption-text">全国国中杯羽球锦标赛 国女团第二名。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，东南国中羽球队刚取得亮眼的成绩，获得全国国中杯羽球锦标赛国女团第二名，而国女单打也有出色的表现，温舒伃获得全国第二名，蔡昀静获得第五名。这个成就让学校再次在全国性的体育赛事中崭露头角，引以为傲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114270" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114270-646694.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14114270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114270-646694-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114270" class="wp-caption-text">100公尺决赛。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14114272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114272" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114272-646696.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14114272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114272-646696-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114272" class="wp-caption-text">三年级200公尺赛事。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>东南国中的校庆运动会不仅是一个欢庆校庆的场合，更是凝聚师生情感的时刻。这次的活动结合了竞赛和庆祝，让师生们共同欢度了一个难忘的时刻。我们期待着东南国中未来更多的成就和精彩活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114266" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114266-646691.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14114266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114266-646691-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114266" class="wp-caption-text">铅球踯远。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14114273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114273" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114273-646697.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14114273" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114273-646697-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114273" class="wp-caption-text">二年级趣味竞赛。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
