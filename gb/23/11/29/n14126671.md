<h6>大雪到来，寒冷天气为人们的健康带来不同影响，该如何应对这些影响？（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">12月7日是24<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%82%E6%B0%94.md#1">节气</a>中的第21个节气——<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%9B%AA.md#1">大雪</a>，“大者，盛也，至此而雪盛矣”。至大雪时节，意味着真正的冬天已经来临。</span><span class="s1">中医认为，冬季在生活作息上，要早睡晚起，以养精蓄锐，补充能量；</span><span class="s2">日常</span><span class="s1">要注意<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E6%9A%96.md#1">保暖</a>、保养<span class="s3">心血管</span>；饮食上要注意补肾，多吃温补食物，如羊、牛肉，以及多吃海带、紫菜等含碘让身体变暖等。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">寒冷天气，也是心脑血管疾病高发季节。其实，寒冷天气为人们的健康带来不同影响，该如何应对这些影响？以下是其中8种常见的影响。</span></p>
<h2 class="p3"><span style="color: #188638;"><span class="s1"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%86%B7.md#1">天冷</a></span><span class="s4">8</span><span class="s1">个常见症状和应对方法</span></span></h2>
<h2 class="p6"><span class="s1">1. </span><span class="s5">头</span><span class="s1">痛或偏</span><span class="s5">头</span><span class="s1">痛</span></h2>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">温度降低会导致人体血管收缩，尤其冬天户外活动，大</span><span class="s5">脑</span><span class="s1">中的血管会迅速收</span><span class="s5">缩</span><span class="s1">，血流量减少，导致头疼。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">不只是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%86%B7.md#1">天冷</a>时，夏日强烈的阳光，遇到暴雨等天气</span><span class="s5">变</span><span class="s1">化时，都可能扰乱大</span><span class="s5">脑</span><span class="s1">中化学物质的平衡，</span><span class="s5">导</span><span class="s1">致偏</span><span class="s5">头</span><span class="s1">痛发作。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1"><strong>对策：</strong></span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">关注气候</span><span class="s5">变</span><span class="s1">化和疼痛的关系。梅奥</span><span class="s5">诊</span><span class="s1">所建议，每次</span><span class="s5">头</span><span class="s1">痛发作</span><span class="s5">时</span><span class="s1">，</span><span class="s5">记</span><span class="s1">下可能引起疼痛的任何与气候相关的因素，以备未来应对和预防可能的</span><span class="s5">头</span><span class="s1">痛发作。</span></p>
<h2 class="p6"><span class="s1">2. 皮肤干燥</span></h2>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">天气</span><span class="s5">变</span><span class="s1">冷，空气中的水分通常也会减少，</span><span class="s5">导</span><span class="s1">致皮肤干燥。尤其在有暖气或空调的室内，空气湿度大大降低。长期皮肤干燥会</span><span class="s5">损</span><span class="s1">害皮肤的保</span><span class="s5">护</span><span class="s1">层，导致皮肤粗糙、老化。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><strong><span class="s1">对策：</span></strong></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">通</span><span class="s5">过</span><span class="s1">温和的</span><span class="s5">护</span><span class="s1">理和护肤品来保</span><span class="s5">护</span><span class="s1">皮肤。将普通洗面奶</span><span class="s5">换</span><span class="s1">成温和的洗面奶，每周2次去角</span><span class="s5">质</span><span class="s1">，以去除干燥的死皮。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">化妆一族可以选择使用含有有机硅（聚二甲基硅</span><span class="s5">氧烷</span><span class="s1">）的护肤霜，</span><span class="s5">锁</span><span class="s1">住皮肤水分，令粗糙的皮肤变平滑，</span><span class="s5">让</span><span class="s1">皮肤更容易上</span><span class="s5">妆</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">此外，也不要忘</span><span class="s5">记</span><span class="s1">在有暖气的卧室或办公环境使用加湿器，空气湿度增加时，皮肤会更加滋</span><span class="s5">润</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14126725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14126725" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14126725-shutterstock_2375961457.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14126725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14126725-shutterstock_2375961457-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14126725" class="wp-caption-text">有暖气的卧室或办公环境使用加湿器，空气湿度增加时，皮肤会更加滋润。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2 class="p6"><span class="s1">3. 情绪低落</span></h2>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">由于日照</span><span class="s5">时间</span><span class="s1">短，秋冬季是季</span><span class="s5">节</span><span class="s1">性情感障碍的多发季节。睡眠医学博士</span><span class="s5">罗</span><span class="s1">伯特·S·</span><span class="s5">罗</span><span class="s1">森伯格（Robert S. Rosenberg) 表示，患有季</span><span class="s5">节</span><span class="s1">性情感障碍的人会分泌大量称</span><span class="s5">为“</span><span class="s1">血清素</span><span class="s5">转运</span><span class="s1">蛋白”（SERT）的蛋白</span><span class="s5">质</span><span class="s1">，这种蛋白质会降低快</span><span class="s5">乐</span><span class="s1">激素血清素的</span><span class="s5">浓</span><span class="s1">度。 </span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">可以晒太阳预防冬季抑郁症，最好花更多</span><span class="s5">时间</span><span class="s1">在有阳光的</span><span class="s5">户</span><span class="s1">外活动；如果环境光线不佳，也可使用灯箱（室内光而不是阳光），</span><span class="s5">让</span><span class="s1">自己尽可能多地接触光</span><span class="s5">线</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<h2 class="p6"><span class="s1">4. </span><span class="s5">维</span><span class="s1">生素D缺乏</span></h2>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">人体生成</span><span class="s5">维</span><span class="s1">生素D的主要方式之一，是通</span><span class="s5">过</span><span class="s1">自然阳光照射将皮肤中的胆固醇</span><span class="s5">转</span><span class="s1">化</span><span class="s5">为维</span><span class="s1">生素D3。然而，在寒冷的季</span><span class="s5">节</span><span class="s1">由于人体得到光照不足，导致</span><span class="s5">维</span><span class="s1">生素D缺乏很常见。缺乏</span><span class="s5">维</span><span class="s1">生素D可能引起肌肉无力、痛</span><span class="s5">觉过</span><span class="s1">敏和嗜睡等。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><strong><span class="s1">对策：</span></strong></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">建</span><span class="s5">议</span><span class="s1">食用</span><span class="s5">鲑鱼</span><span class="s1">和金</span><span class="s5">枪鱼</span><span class="s1">等多脂</span><span class="s5">鱼</span><span class="s1">类、</span><span class="s5">维</span><span class="s1">生素D强化牛奶、橙汁和</span><span class="s5">补</span><span class="s1">充</span><span class="s5">剂</span><span class="s1">来增加</span><span class="s5">维</span><span class="s1">生素D</span><span class="s5">摄</span><span class="s1">入量。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">在美国，</span><span class="s5">维</span><span class="s1">生素D3的每日建</span><span class="s5">议摄</span><span class="s1">入量</span><span class="s5">为</span><span class="s1">600 IU （国际单位）。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14126723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14126723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14126723-shutterstock_520086745.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14126723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14126723-shutterstock_520086745-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14126723" class="wp-caption-text">富含维生素D食物。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2 class="p6"><span class="s1">5. 呼吸道症状</span></h2>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">冬天也是呼吸道疾病的高发季节，因为寒冷的天气会</span><span class="s5">导</span><span class="s1">致气管肌肉</span><span class="s5">痉</span><span class="s1">挛，使气道</span><span class="s5">变</span><span class="s1">窄，</span><span class="s5">导</span><span class="s1">致呼吸困</span><span class="s5">难</span><span class="s1">。而有过敏体质的人，则可能引发咳嗽甚至哮喘。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">为减少冷空气对呼吸道的刺激，建</span><span class="s5">议</span><span class="s1">外出时戴上口罩，用口罩呼吸时会减弱冷空气气流，令吸入的空气变得相对温暖。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14126726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14126726" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14126726-shutterstock_572396224.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14126726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14126726-shutterstock_572396224-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14126726" class="wp-caption-text">用口罩呼吸时会减弱冷空气气流，令吸入的空气变得相对温暖。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2 class="p6"><strong><span class="s1">6. 患上感冒</span></strong></h2>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">冬季是不同流感病毒活跃的季节之一，然而，大多数病毒在症状出</span><span class="s5">现</span><span class="s1">前几天就具有</span><span class="s5">传</span><span class="s1">染性，因此人们通常是在不知不</span><span class="s5">觉</span><span class="s1">中接触到感冒病毒。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><strong><span class="s1">对策：</span></strong></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s5">预</span><span class="s1">防感冒的最佳方法是</span><span class="s5">经</span><span class="s1">常</span><span class="s5">彻</span><span class="s1">底洗手，并避开咳嗽或打</span><span class="s5">喷</span><span class="s1">嚏的人。</span></p>
<h2 class="p6"><span class="s1">7. 关</span><span class="s5">节</span><span class="s1">疼</span></h2>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">很多人都有在寒冷或潮湿的日子关节疼痛发作的经历，除了寒气入侵，寒冷的天气会降低关</span><span class="s5">节</span><span class="s1">周</span><span class="s5">围</span><span class="s1">的气</span><span class="s5">压</span><span class="s1">，</span><span class="s5">导</span><span class="s1">致关</span><span class="s5">节</span><span class="s1">周</span><span class="s5">围</span><span class="s1">的</span><span class="s5">软组织</span><span class="s1">像气球一</span><span class="s5">样</span><span class="s1">膨</span><span class="s5">胀</span><span class="s1">，</span><span class="s5">给</span><span class="s1">关</span><span class="s5">节带</span><span class="s1">来</span><span class="s5">额</span><span class="s1">外的</span><span class="s5">压</span><span class="s1">力，</span><span class="s5">导</span><span class="s1">致疼痛。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">适度运动。骨骼需要强壮的肌肉来支撑，不</span><span class="s5">运动</span><span class="s1">会削弱提供支撑的肌肉，使关</span><span class="s5">节</span><span class="s1">承受更大的</span><span class="s5">压</span><span class="s1">力。因此，</span><span class="s5">预</span><span class="s1">防关</span><span class="s5">节</span><span class="s1">疼痛最有效的方法之一就是保持适度</span><span class="s5">运动</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s5">对</span><span class="s1">关</span><span class="s5">节压</span><span class="s1">力</span><span class="s5">较</span><span class="s1">小的有</span><span class="s5">氧运动</span><span class="s1">包括行走、</span><span class="s5">骑</span><span class="s1">自行</span><span class="s5">车</span><span class="s1">、游泳和水中有</span><span class="s5">氧运动</span><span class="s1">。适度</span><span class="s5">锻炼</span><span class="s1">不</span><span class="s5">仅</span><span class="s1">可以</span><span class="s5">让</span><span class="s1">身体暖和起来，</span><span class="s5">缓</span><span class="s1">解关</span><span class="s5">节</span><span class="s1">僵硬，</span><span class="s5">还</span><span class="s1">有助于防止体重增加，因为体重增加会</span><span class="s5">给</span><span class="s1">关</span><span class="s5">节带</span><span class="s1">来</span><span class="s5">压</span><span class="s1">力。</span></p>
