<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>本拿比一卧室公寓租金同比上涨16%</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月24日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">柯瑞斯</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大温哥华编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据租房网站</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rentals.ca</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">最新的全国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%9F%E9%87%91.md#1">租金</a>报告，与加拿大其他主要城市相比，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AC%E6%8B%BF%E6%AF%94.md#1">本拿比</a>是同比增幅最高的城市之一。本拿比的一卧室单元<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%9F%E9%87%91.md#1">租金</a>价格上涨了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 16%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，一卧室单元平均租金仍位居加拿大第二，两卧室单元租金位居第三高。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?10 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AC%E6%8B%BF%E6%AF%94.md#1">本拿比</a>一卧室单元的平均价格为每月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2,647</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。该市一套两卧室单元的平均租金为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3,341</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2022</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月上涨了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">然而，数据也显示，租金也分别比上个月下跌</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.1%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2023</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，本拿比公布的一卧室单元和两卧室单元平均每月价格为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2,700</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3,411 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">地产咨询服务公司都市化(</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Urbanation</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">)总裁肖恩</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">希尔德布兰德(</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Shaun Hildebrand</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">)表示，虽然</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份加拿大整体租金继续走高，但多伦多和温哥华等城市的租金大幅放缓，这表明负担能力的挑战正在导致租房者转向价格较低的市场。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">有数据显示，温哥华仍是加拿大房租最贵的城市，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">一卧室单元租金为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2,872</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，两卧室单元</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3,777</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>的平均挂牌租金是所有省</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">份</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">中最高的，为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2,639</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rentals.ca</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的数据也显示，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>租金环比减</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.6%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，已是连续第二个月下降，导致</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月年增率从</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">降至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9.8%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在全国范围内，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%80%E5%8D%A7%E5%AE%A4%E5%85%AC%E5%AF%93.md#1">一卧室公寓</a>套房的平均租金为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1,938 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，两卧室套房的平均租金为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2,311</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">同时，尽管价格略有上涨，但许多有兴趣的购屋者仍希望透过本拿比进入卑诗省不可预测的房地产市场。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份，本拿比东部住宅物业的基准价格达到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">119.26</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">,</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">同比上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">％；本拿比北</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">102.09</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">,</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">同比上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">％；本拿比南</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">112.2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元，同比下跌</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">％。本拿比东单户<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%B1%8B.md#1">独立屋</a>的基准价格为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">189.48</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">,</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">同比上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.8%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；本拿比北为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">207.93</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元，同比上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.5%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；本拿比南为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">219.97</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元，同比上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.1%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">大温地区</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月的销量总计</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,996 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套，比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2022</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,924</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套销量增长</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 3.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，但比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年季节性平均值下跌</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">29.5%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。◇</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
