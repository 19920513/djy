<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亲子健行之旅 探索最美猫囒山步道</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%8A%95.md#1">南投</a>报导）南投县的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%88%E6%BD%AD.md#1">日月潭</a>，以其迷人的湖景和壮观的自然美景而闻名，不仅是情侣度假的好地方，还是亲子游览的理想目的地。最近，一条被誉为“最美步道”的猫囒山步道，正吸引着游客的目光，这条步道不仅适合亲子健行，还让游客有机会欣赏到日月潭茶改场的壮观红茶园、和已经红透的咖啡豆。</p>
<p>猫囒山步道位于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%88%E6%BD%AD.md#1">日月潭</a>湖畔，其轻松的路线使之成为亲子活动的理想选择。游客可以自行开车前往步道口，然后沿着步道约1小时往返，感受大自然的美景。在步道上，您甚至可能遇到这条步道的“猫”，这可不仅仅是个名字，它真的就像它的名字一样迷人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14118592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118592" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118592-647949.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118592-647949-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118592" class="wp-caption-text">欣赏到日月潭茶改场的壮观红茶园。（王兹嬅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14118591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118591" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118591-647948.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118591-647948-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118591" class="wp-caption-text">亲子健行之旅，探索最美猫囒山步道。（王兹嬅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>步道一开始是柏油路，轻松惬意的步行中，您可以聆听导览人员的解说，深入了解日月潭红茶的历史和背后的故事。步道两旁是茶改场用来研发新品种茶叶的茶园，这些茶园看似稀疏，但实际上是研究茶叶新品种的重要场所。每一个新品种的研发都需要数十年的时间，如日月潭最有名的台茶18号，就是在这里研发出来的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14118595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118595" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118595-647952.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118595-647952-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118595" class="wp-caption-text">日月潭茶改场。（王兹嬅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14118593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118593" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118593-647950.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118593-647950-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118593" class="wp-caption-text">能欣赏到九份二山和集集大山的壮观景色。（王兹嬅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14118594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118594" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118594-647951.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118594-647951-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118594" class="wp-caption-text">步道的观景台可远眺日月潭。（王兹嬅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走到步道中点，您可以回顾山下的景色，一片绿意盎然的茶树坡地，令人心旷神怡。步道两旁种满了橄榄树，为您提供清新的阴凉，让您在山上的漫步更加舒适。步道的观景台可远眺日月潭，让您一览湖光山色，而继续往上走则能欣赏到九份二山和集集大山的壮观景色。</p>
<p>步道的一个亮点是茶园观景台，从这里您可以一眼望去，看到满山的日月潭红茶树。如果您喜欢红茶，记得在回家前购买一罐日月潭红茶，品味其香气和独特的茶香，同时也支持台湾的好茶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14118596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118596" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118596-647953.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118596-647953-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118596" class="wp-caption-text">气象站。（王兹嬅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14118597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14118597" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118597-647954.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14118597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14118597-647954-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14118597" class="wp-caption-text">轻松的路线使之成为亲子活动的理想选择。（王兹嬅提供）</figcaption></figure>
