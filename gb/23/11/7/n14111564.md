<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】山药核桃粥的2种做法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111567-4a8f5408f929d5b6d5caf89aa456e24e-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>历代医家推崇喝粥养生，糜粥养阴，山药核桃粥容易自制又好喝。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>秋之时，种了一年的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E8%8D%AF.md#1">山药</a>收获了，搭配核桃煮粥正当季。这里介绍Instant pot（快煮电压力锅）和明火2种煮粥方法。</p>
<h2>电压力锅煮法</h2>
<p>设置电压力锅的煮粥功能15分钟，自动排气后就可以享用了，省时省力。</p>
<p>150克新鲜日本<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E8%8D%AF.md#1">山药</a>，去皮，切成1厘米薄片</p>
<p>40克脱皮核桃仁</p>
<p>1000毫升水</p>
<p>1/4茶匙盐</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111568" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111568-f000e7a5f9b3749a5d914e547cb18570.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14111568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111568-f000e7a5f9b3749a5d914e547cb18570-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111568" class="wp-caption-text">山药<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%B8%E6%A1%83%E7%B2%A5.md#1">核桃粥</a>的食材准备。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>厨具准备：</h3>
<p>instant pot，容量3夸脱</p>
<p>1.烧一壶开水浸泡核桃5分钟，捞出轻轻一撕，皮即脱落。脱皮核桃煮粥清甜，苦味少。<br />
2.锅中装入所有食材。<br />
3.启动煮粥功能（porridge），设置煮15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111570" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111570-7c262557b06f9c7b19b7d358de7b587d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14111570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111570-7c262557b06f9c7b19b7d358de7b587d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111570" class="wp-caption-text">启动煮粥功能。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.煮好后，不够咸再加盐调味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111571-a37a6c5b921758ea2cb66cdb607a119b.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14111571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111571-a37a6c5b921758ea2cb66cdb607a119b-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111571" class="wp-caption-text">脱皮核桃熬出来的粥颜色清亮。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>明火煮粥的方法</h2>
<p>明火煮粥需要守在火灶旁边调整火候约半个小时。此法费时，但粥味浓厚。</p>
<p>4人份的食材准备：</p>
<p>100克大米，淘洗干净</p>
<p>150克新鲜日本山药，去皮，切成5毫米薄片</p>
<p>40克核桃仁（可用开水浸泡去皮）</p>
<p>1500毫升水</p>
<p>1/2茶匙盐</p>
<p>厨具准备：</p>
<p>3.5夸脱铸铁锅（或蓄热好的多层底煮锅，若锅底太薄粥易糊底）</p>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1.锅中加入清水煮沸。<br />
2.放入大米、掰碎的核桃（碎核桃易煮软）。<br />
3.大火煮沸，盖上盖子（留一个指缝，不盖严实）中火煮20分钟，保持中度沸腾。中途不需要勤搅拌。<br />
4.时间到了，大米八成熟就放入山药开大火煮沸。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111568" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111572-e1b0568ba7cb953c17d9fccd8ae2b9c3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-14111572" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111572-e1b0568ba7cb953c17d9fccd8ae2b9c3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111568" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.阖上锅盖，改小火焖煮30分钟。（日本山药肉质紧实不易煮烂，若用普通菜山药则缩短时间）<br />
6.时间到了试吃，山药熟了就加盐调味，并提高火力煮约3分钟至粥稠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111568" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111573-e4c2d6dee879e226868426023def0fb5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-14111573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111573-e4c2d6dee879e226868426023def0fb5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111568" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>料理技巧：开始旺火煮沸时可以不时搅拌，帮助释放热气以防止糊锅。最后改小火焖煮尽量少搅拌，否则粥过于粘稠，容易糊底。</p>
