<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台南官田丰收季节 菱角田采收已展开</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111558-645966-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台南官田丰收季节 ,菱角田采收已展开。（黄荣群提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月07日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8D%97.md#1">台南</a>报导）随着秋风渐凉，台南<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%98%E7%94%B0.md#1">官田</a>区的台1线省道上，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%B1%E8%A7%92.md#1">菱角</a>田的采收工作已经陆续展开，农民纷纷摆上菱角招牌，推出新鲜熟菱角供应。官田一直以来是台湾菱角的重要产地，这项农业活动不仅带来美味的菱角产品，还对当地水雉生态保护起到积极作用，更是摄影行家取景的绝佳时机。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111559" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111559-645967-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14111559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111559-645967-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111559" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%B1%E8%A7%92.md#1">菱角</a>的产季通常在每年的9月下旬至12月上旬。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14111560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111560" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111560-645968-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14111560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111560-645968-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111560" class="wp-caption-text">只要沿着省道和县道前进，您都能轻松找到许多贩售新鲜菱角的摊位。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>菱角的产季通常在每年的9月下旬至12月上旬，这时菱角富含淀粉，主要以煮熟的方式食用，产品包括蒸气熟菱角、菱角猪脚、菱角排骨、菱角虱目鱼丸等多种美食。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%98%E7%94%B0.md#1">官田</a>区的菱角种植历史悠久，每到产季，这里都变得热闹非凡。</p>
<p>不论您是自行开车或搭乘公共交通工具，只要沿着省道和县道前进，您都能轻松找到许多贩售新鲜菱角的摊位。其中，位于葫芦埤公园中的“官田传承幸福菱角”甚至提供旅客下水田体验采收菱角的乐趣，让游客更贴近这项古老的农业传统。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111561" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111561-645969-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14111561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111561-645969-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111561" class="wp-caption-text">这项农业活动不仅带来美味的菱角产品，还对当地水雉生态保护起到积极作用。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>菱角是台湾饮食文化的一部分，不论是夜市、路边摊，都可以找到贩售蒸菱角的小摊位，甚至有著名的“菱角酥”。然而，许多人可能对菱角的真实生长环境不太了解。如果您对菱角产地感到好奇，不妨来<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8D%97.md#1">台南</a>官田的官田传承幸福菱角，亲自体验菱角的生长过程，并品味最新鲜的菱角美食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111563" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111563-645971-450x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14111563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111563-645971-450x338.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111563" class="wp-caption-text">水雉在菱角田很常见。（官田水雉生态教育园区提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14111562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111562" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111562-645970-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14111562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111562-645970-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111562" class="wp-caption-text">官田一直以来是台湾菱角的重要产地。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
