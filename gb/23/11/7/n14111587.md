<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澎湖季节限定 海岛的蓝白合</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111590-645986-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>没有了熙攘的游客，这座小岛却展现出独特的迷人风采。（陈明宗提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月07日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%8E%E6%B9%96.md#1">澎湖</a>报导）11月的澎湖，或许是最美的秋天。在过了中秋佳节之后，澎湖的观光季节进入了休眠模式，没有了熙攘的游客，这座小岛却展现出独特的迷人风采。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111591" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111591-645987-450x760.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14111591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111591-645987-450x760.jpg" alt="" width="351" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111591" class="wp-caption-text">这个季节的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%8E%E6%B9%96.md#1">澎湖</a>，也带来了风光明媚的风景。（陈明宗提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14111588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111588" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111588-645984-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14111588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111588-645984-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="398" b="597" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111588" class="wp-caption-text">澎湖季节限定<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E7%99%BD%E5%90%88.md#1">蓝白合</a>。（陈明宗提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此时的澎湖，处处都有惊奇等待着发现。没有了喧哗，只有宁静；没有了嘈吵，只有安详。这片宁静的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%B2%9B.md#1">海岛</a>，仿佛一幅宁静如梦的画卷。</p>
<p>澎湖县产业创生经济发展协会理事长陈明宗表示，澎湖的美景不应该被藏匿，而是应该与更多人分享。在这个休眠的季节，一个人独自欣赏大自然的美景，便是一种极致的享受。风吹着特别的云朵，配上湛蓝的天空，呈现出最美的风景，仿佛是置身于梦境之中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111589" style="width: 519px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111589-645985-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14111589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111589-645985-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="519" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111589" class="wp-caption-text">在过了中秋佳节之后，澎湖的观光季节进入了休眠模式。（陈明宗提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>澎湖的秋日风情，不仅是一个让人放松的好地方，这个季节的澎湖，也带来了风光明媚的风景，更是一种内心深处的宁静和满足。</p>
<p>如果你渴望一个宁静的秋日假期，澎湖的秋天将会是一个绝佳的选择。来这座小岛，享受大自然的礼物，感受静谧如梦的美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14111592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111592" style="width: 548px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111592-645988-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14111592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111592-645988-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="548" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111592" class="wp-caption-text">如果你渴望一个宁静的秋日假期，澎湖的秋天将会是一个绝佳的选择。（陈明宗提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14111593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111593" style="width: 381px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111593-645989-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14111593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111593-645989-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="381" b="572" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111593" class="wp-caption-text">连月亮都要来凑<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E7%99%BD%E5%90%88.md#1">蓝白合</a>。（陈明宗提供）</figcaption></figure>
