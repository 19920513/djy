<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多元才艺自信增能 崙背国小之星圆满落幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125860-650146-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>崙背国小校长李秉承表示人工智慧逐渐主宰各个领域的时代，自信和表达能力变得至关重要。（李秉承提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%99%E8%83%8C%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">崙背国小</a>之星才艺大赛，经过两个多月的筹备，在热烈掌声中圆满成功的落幕！这场赛事不仅展现了孩子们多元的才能表演，更突显他们在未来AI主宰的年代中，自信表达的重要性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14125858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125858-650144-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14125858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125858-650144-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125858" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%99%E8%83%8C%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">崙背国小</a>学生个个技艺高巧。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_14125854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125854" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125854-650140-600x899.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14125854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125854-650140-600x899.jpg" alt="" width="351" b="526" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125854" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国小直笛社表演。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14125853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125853-650139-600x280.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14125853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125853-650139-600x280.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125853" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国小之星才艺大赛，掌声中圆满成功的落幕！（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在这场令人振奋的才艺大赛中，崙背国小的小朋友们自信地登上舞台，向观众展现了各种独特的才华。从音乐表演到舞蹈，从诗歌朗诵到科学实验，每一个节目都充满了创意和热情。观众们见证了未来的希望，这些孩子们不仅具备了多样化的技能，还拥有了坚定的自信。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14125855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125855" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125855-650141-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14125855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125855-650141-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="355" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125855" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国小的小朋友们自信地登上舞台。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14125856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125856" style="width: 530px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125856-650142-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14125856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125856-650142-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="530" b="353" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125856" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国小国标舞社表演。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>崙背国小校长李秉承表示，这次才艺大赛的目的不仅在于展现孩子们的才华，更在于培养他们的自信心。在人工智慧逐渐主宰各个领域的时代，自信和表达能力变得至关重要。这场比赛鼓励学生勇敢地走上舞台，表达自我，并将他们的才能充分发挥。</p>
<p>李秉承更感谢所有参与的工作人员，辛劳的付出，让这次活动圆满落幕。同时，感恩家长会百分百的赞助，肯定与鼓励孩子的表现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14125857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125857" style="width: 542px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125857-650143-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14125857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125857-650143-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="542" b="361" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125857" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国小乌克丽丽社表演。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14125859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125859" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125859-650145-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14125859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125859-650145-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="359" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125859" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国小学区以诏安客家人为主。<br />（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
