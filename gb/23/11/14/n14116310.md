<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>水林乡茑松艺术中学“草花社” 种下希望绽放美丽</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116311-647414-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>茑松艺术中学“草花社”，种下希望绽放美丽。（茑松艺术中学提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月14日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%97.md#1">水林</a>乡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%91%E6%9D%BE%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E4%B8%AD%E5%AD%A6.md#1">茑松艺术中学</a>“草花社”，这学期由新进的卢恺婷老师成立，意外地引领学生们在绿篱计划中，经历了一场美丽的草花之旅。尽管社团只有四位来自舞蹈班的女学生，但她们的热情和毅力却让人赞叹不已，不仅让校园绽放美丽，也为自己种下了成就感的种子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14116312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14116312" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116312-647415-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14116312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116312-647415-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14116312" class="wp-caption-text">尽管社团只有四位来自舞蹈班的女学生，但她们的热情和毅力却让人赞叹不已。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%91%E6%9D%BE%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E4%B8%AD%E5%AD%A6.md#1">茑松艺术中学</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>茑松艺术中学的草花社社员，虽然目前只有四位热爱草花的女学生组成，她们分别来自503班的周思月、叶千祎，以及403班的庄钰善和詹萓安。尽管社团规模较小，这四位女生却展现了惊人的自律和毅力。在草花种植活动中，她们不怕指甲脏，不下课休息，甚至不畏汗水沿着脸庞滑落。更令人印象深刻的是，她们在栽种草花的过程中竟然展现出各种软Q的Spiderman姿势，让旁人看了不禁佩服她们，连休闲时间都在练舞蹈基本功的精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14116316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14116316" style="width: 509px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116316-647419-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14116316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116316-647419-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="509" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14116316" class="wp-caption-text">她们在栽种草花的过程中，展现出各种软Q的Spiderman姿势。（茑松艺术中学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14116315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14116315" style="width: 395px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116315-647418-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14116315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116315-647418-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="395" b="592" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14116315" class="wp-caption-text">卢恺婷老师表示，学生们的热情和毅力让她感到骄傲。（茑松艺术中学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>茑松中学的绿篱计划目的是希望美化校园，提供学生们一个参与校园环境改善的机会。草花社的成立正好契合了这一目标，并且让学生们体验到了草花种植的乐趣。卢恺婷老师表示，学生们的热情和毅力让她感到骄傲，并且这次经历将成为她们宝贵的回忆之一。</p>
<p>草花社的学生们常常坚持要到做到最后一分钟，展现了卓越的自律和责任感。他们的努力不仅让校园变得更加美丽，还为学生们种下了一份珍贵的成就感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14116313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14116313" style="width: 393px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116313-647416-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14116313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116313-647416-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="393" b="590" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14116313" class="wp-caption-text">在草花种植活动中，她们不怕指甲脏，不下课休息，甚至不畏汗水沿着脸庞滑落。（茑松艺术中学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14116314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14116314" style="width: 374px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116314-647417-450x667.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14116314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116314-647417-450x667.jpg" alt="" width="374" b="554" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14116314" class="wp-caption-text">茑松中学的绿篱计划目的是希望美化校园，提供学生们一个参与校园环境改善的机会。（茑松艺术中学提供）</figcaption></figure>
