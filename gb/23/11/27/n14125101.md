<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“椬梧滞洪池”免费拍到饱 海边夕阳更是绝美无敌</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125104-649885-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>椬梧滞洪池原本是台糖椬梧农场的甘蔗林，但由于海水倒灌的意外事件，这里变成了椬梧湿地。（蔡茂源提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月27日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A3%E6%B9%96.md#1">口湖</a>乡拥有100公顷大的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%AC%E6%A2%A7%E6%BB%9E%E6%B4%AA%E6%B1%A0.md#1">椬梧滞洪池</a>”，被誉为“云林小日月潭”的隐藏版湖泊秘境。这个地方拥有广阔的湖泊美景、宜人的环湖步道以及阴凉的树林空地，而且最吸引人的是，海边即便是冬季，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%95%E9%98%B3.md#1">夕阳</a>仍然绝美无敌！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14125102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125102" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125102-649883.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14125102" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125102-649883-600x270.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125102" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A3%E6%B9%96.md#1">口湖</a>乡拥有100公顷大的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%AC%E6%A2%A7%E6%BB%9E%E6%B4%AA%E6%B1%A0.md#1">椬梧滞洪池</a>”。（蔡茂源提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14125103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125103" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125103-649884.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14125103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125103-649884-600x270.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125103" class="wp-caption-text">被誉为“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>小日月潭”的隐藏版湖泊秘境。（蔡茂源提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>椬梧滞洪池原本是台糖椬梧农场的甘蔗林，但由于海水倒灌的意外事件，这里变成了椬梧湿地，生态丰富，吸引了各种鸟类栖息，湖泊更是如梦如幻，被形容为台湾的第二座日月潭。</p>
<p>对于喜欢骑脚踏车的游客来说，椬梧滞洪池也是一个绝佳的地方，环湖步道大约需要30分钟的骑行时间，一路上可以欣赏到浓密的森林风光，享受清新的空气。</p>
<p>想要来这边散步的人，步行到湖边后，你会被设置在湖边的凉亭所吸引，这里的湖泊美景让人感到宁静与舒适。</p>
<p>而在冬天的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%95%E9%98%B3.md#1">夕阳</a>时分，邻近四湖的海边也有美不胜收的风景，摄影爱好者蔡茂源经常来到这里等待夕阳，捕捉到许多令人叹为观止的美丽画面，海边的风车为美图增色不少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14125105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125105-649886.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14125105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125105-649886-600x270.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125105" class="wp-caption-text">摄影爱好者蔡茂源经常在四湖海边等待夕阳。（蔡茂源提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云林小日月潭，不受东北季风的影响，每一个季节都有不同的魅力，无论是赏湖泊美景、骑脚踏车还是欣赏夕阳，都是一个绝佳的选择。来云林，别忘了探访这个高CP值的免费景点，让你的旅程更加丰富多彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14125106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125106-649887.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14125106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125106-649887-600x270.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125106" class="wp-caption-text">风车当配角，四湖的夕阳更美了。（蔡茂源提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14125107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125107" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125107-649888.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14125107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125107-649888-600x270.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125107" class="wp-caption-text">蔡茂源捕捉到许多美丽画面。（蔡茂源提供）</figcaption></figure>
