<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第十届渣打台北公益马拉松 北市长蒋万安鸣枪开跑</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114947-646895-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>台北市长蒋万安今日出席了此次活动，为三公里路跑选手敲锣起跑，并鸣枪为跑者们开始比赛。（台北市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月12日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台北报导）12日总统府前举办一场盛大的运动盛事，“2023第十届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A3%E6%89%93.md#1">渣打</a>台北<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E7%9B%8A.md#1">公益</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89%E6%9D%BE.md#1">马拉松</a>”，吸引了上万名跑步爱好者参加。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">台北市</a>长蒋万安今日出席了此次活动，为三公里路跑选手敲锣起跑，并鸣枪为跑者们开始比赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114945" style="width: 543px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114945-646893-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14114945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114945-646893-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="543" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114945" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A3%E6%89%93.md#1">渣打</a>台北<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E7%9B%8A.md#1">公益</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89%E6%9D%BE.md#1">马拉松</a>已经迈入第十届。（李明贤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14114944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114944" style="width: 509px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114944-646892-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14114944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114944-646892-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="509" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114944" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">台北市</a>议员李明贤跑完全程后开心的表示，当他面临工作和生活上的压力时，常常会透过跑步来纾解。（李明贤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蒋万安在活动中表达了对渣打集团长期以来对路跑活动的大力支持的感谢之情。这次的渣打台北公益马拉松已经迈入第十届，象征着十年的稳健发展，也代表着活动的成功，祝福所有参赛者都能在这一天跑出优异的成绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114948" style="width: 443px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114948-646896-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14114948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114948-646896-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="443" b="591" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114948" class="wp-caption-text">总统府起跑。（选手提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台北市议员李明贤跑完全程后开心的表示，当他面临工作和生活上的压力时，常常会透过跑步来纾解。对他来说，跑步是一种极具愉悦感的方式，可以同时锻炼身体和心灵。</p>
<p>最后，蒋万安再次感谢渣打银行、励馨基金会以及视障者家属协会，他们共同投入公益事业，让这场路跑活动充满意义。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114950" style="width: 532px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114950-646901-450x462.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14114950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114950-646901-450x462.jpg" alt="" width="532" b="546" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114950" class="wp-caption-text">全马的路程，沿着台北市跑，考验跑者耐力。（李明贤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14114949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114949" style="width: 445px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114949-646897-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14114949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114949-646897-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="445" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114949" class="wp-caption-text">选手开心的跑完，完成初体验。（选手提供）</figcaption></figure>
