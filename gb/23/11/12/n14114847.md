<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>暴风肆虐 卑诗省超40万用户电力中断</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114849-11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023年11月11日晚6时，列治文部分路段没有信号灯，酒店、商店漆黑一片。（大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月12日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">杨清清</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大温哥华报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日晚间至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日上午，一场狂风肆虐<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>南岸地区，超过</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">40</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万用户经历了不同时间的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%8A%9B%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%AD.md#1">电力中断</a>。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">截至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日晚</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">时</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">30</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分左右，大温地区仍有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">23,011</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">户卑诗水电公司（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">BC Hydro</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">用户</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的电力尚未恢复。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%97%E6%B2%BB%E6%96%87.md#1">列治文</a>地区，大风导致</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bridgeport</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">地区</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 3,500 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">多户今晨停电。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">卑诗水电公司报告称，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日上午<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%97%E6%B2%BB%E6%96%87.md#1">列治文</a>有六个地区受到停电影响，其中最大的停电地区位于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">River</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">路和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Vanguard</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">路之间，以及</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bridgeport</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">路和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Sea Island</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">路之间。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">此外由于电线掉落，威廉姆斯路（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Williams Road</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Steveston</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">高速公路（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Steveston Highway</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）之间的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 5 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">号路（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">No. 5 Road</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）以西至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Seahan Crescent </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的地区<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%8A%9B%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%AD.md#1">电力中断</a>。影响到卑诗水电局的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 258 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个客户。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14114848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114848-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14114848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114848-12-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114848" class="wp-caption-text">2023年11月11日晚6时，列治文部分路段没有信号灯，酒店、商店漆黑一片。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">晚</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">时，有列治文居民发现部分路段“路灯、红绿灯都没了，酒店、商店全部漆黑一片。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">此外，位于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Westminster Highway </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> River Road </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">之间的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 5 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">号路以东也有一处停电，影响到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 270 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个用户。◇</span></p>
