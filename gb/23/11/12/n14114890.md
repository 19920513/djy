<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】玉米红萝卜羊肉汤～驱寒滋补</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114915-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>驱寒滋补优质汤品——“玉米红萝卜羊肉汤”。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong> 在春秋时代吴地流传一句民间俗语：“冬至<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9B%E8%A1%A5.md#1">进补</a>，春天打虎”。在寒冷的冬季，特重食补，养精蓄锐就是为了来年做准备。那么，驱寒不上火的食谱就非常适用于众人。</p>
<p>今天为您介绍的这一道食谱“玉米<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C.md#1">红萝卜</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%82%89%E6%B1%A4.md#1">羊肉汤</a>”，来自香港大厨之手，只要四个步骤就可以上桌，料理步骤最重要的除了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%82%89.md#1">羊肉</a>先用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C%E6%B1%81%E9%85%92.md#1">姜汁酒</a>去血水，其中水滚后才放入食材更为重要，这是锁住食材营养精华的关键。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">玉米<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C.md#1">红萝卜</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%82%89%E6%B1%A4.md#1">羊肉汤</a></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14114914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114914" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114914-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114914-6-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114914" class="wp-caption-text">冬季滋补煲汤是最佳选择之一。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>玉米3条<br />
红萝卜3条<br />
西芹4条<br />
洋葱1个<br />
陈皮1块<br />
蜜枣4粒<br />
姜3片<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%82%89.md#1">羊肉</a>2磅<br />
水16碗<br />
盐适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114909" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114909-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114909-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114909" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1.转中火，羊肉用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C%E6%B1%81%E9%85%92.md#1">姜汁酒</a>川烫，浮泡和肥油要记得捞掉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114910" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114910-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114910-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114910" class="wp-caption-text">羊肉用姜汁酒川烫。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.玉米去壳切小段，红萝卜去皮切小块，西芹和洋葱切小块，陈皮和蜜枣洗净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114911" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114911-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114911-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114911" class="wp-caption-text">食材切小段。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.汤煲内放入16碗水，水滚后放入所有材料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114912" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114912-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114912-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114912" class="wp-caption-text">水滚后放入所有材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.大火煮滚15分钟后再转为中小火多煮2小时，最后加入盐调味。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114913" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114913-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114913-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114913" class="wp-caption-text">大火煮滚15分钟后再转为中小火多煮2小时。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
