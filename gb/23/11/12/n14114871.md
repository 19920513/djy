<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】豉汁苦瓜牛肉～爽脆回甘 滑嫩肉香</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114881-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>豉汁苦瓜牛肉～爽脆回甘 滑嫩肉香。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong> “<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%A6%E7%93%9C.md#1">苦瓜</a>”对不同的人其味蕾会有不同的感受，怕吃苦也是人的天性，但是保有苦味余韵，一吃却是回甘滋味会令人留恋，这就是“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%89%E6%B1%81.md#1">豉汁</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%A6%E7%93%9C.md#1">苦瓜</a>牛肉”这道菜肴风味微妙的特色之一。</p>
<p>热<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">炒牛肉</a>爆出的油脂和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%B1%89%E9%85%B1.md#1">豆豉酱</a>、蚝油等调味酱汁融合，以及苦瓜的“精心处理（作法5）”，成就了这一道菜，让人见识到家常<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">粤菜</a>的好滋味，这也是很多人的心头好呢！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>苦瓜1.5磅<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">炒牛肉</a>1磅<br />
姜少许<br />
蒜蓉少许<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%B1%89%E9%85%B1.md#1">豆豉酱</a>2茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114872" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114872-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114872-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114872" class="wp-caption-text">材料（牛肉、苦瓜、姜、蒜蓉、豆豉酱）。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【腌料】</span></h3>
<p>生抽、糖、胡椒粉、粟粉各少许</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>水1杯<br />
蚝油1汤匙<br />
糖1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
麻油少许<br />
美极鲜酱油少许<br />
粟粉1汤匙（加开水勾芡用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114873" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114873" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114873-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114873-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114873" class="wp-caption-text">调味酱品。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1.苦瓜一开二，挖去瓜瓤切成小块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114874" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114874-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114874" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114874-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114874" class="wp-caption-text">苦瓜一开二，挖去瓜瓤切成小块备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.牛肉加入少许生抽、糖、胡椒粉、粟粉，拌匀后腌15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114875" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114875" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114875-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114875-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114875" class="wp-caption-text">牛肉加入少许生抽、糖、胡椒粉、粟粉，拌匀后腌15分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.热锅下油，先把牛肉炒至八成熟捞起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114876" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114876-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114876-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114876" class="wp-caption-text">把牛肉炒至八成熟捞起。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.爆香姜、蒜蓉和豆豉酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114877" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114877-92b67bd4b2cf2e842e1e80badaa68680.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114877-92b67bd4b2cf2e842e1e80badaa68680-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114877" class="wp-caption-text">爆香姜、蒜蓉和豆豉酱。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.放入苦瓜翻炒一会，加入1杯水及蚝油、糖和胡椒粉，煮5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114878" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114878-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114878-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114878" class="wp-caption-text">放入苦瓜翻炒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.再放回牛肉兜匀，用粟粉水勾芡，加入麻油和美极鲜酱油即完成。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114879" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114879" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114879-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14114879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114879-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114879" class="wp-caption-text">再放回牛肉兜匀，用粟粉水勾芡，加入麻油和美极鲜酱油即完成。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
