<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东港渔港火灾渔船 变成摄影师的口袋密境</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108109-644912-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>屏东县东港渔港内港。（黄荣群提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>报导）屏东县<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B8%AF.md#1">东港</a>渔港内港，位于镇海滨国小附近，一直以来是渔船停泊的安全港湾。然而，八月发生了一场意外，数艘渔船突然起火。不过，这一场灾难也意外地让屏东县东港渔港，变成屏东<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1.md#1">摄影</a>协会常务理事黄荣群的口袋密境，每一张照片都令人赞叹，与蓝天相映成趣。</p>
<figure id="14108110" aria-describedby="caption-14108110" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108110-644913-450x225.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108110-644913-450x225.jpg" alt="与蓝天相映成趣。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14108110" class="wp-caption-text">与蓝天相映成趣。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14108114" aria-describedby="caption-14108114" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108114-644915-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108114-644915-450x300.jpg" alt="成为摄影爱好者的绝佳景点。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14108114" class="wp-caption-text">成为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1.md#1">摄影</a>爱好者的绝佳景点。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14108113" aria-describedby="caption-14108113" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108113-644914-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108113-644914-450x300.jpg" alt="八月发生数艘渔船突然起火，这一场灾难也意外地，变成摄影师的口袋密境。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14108113" class="wp-caption-text">八月发生数艘渔船突然起火，这一场灾难也意外地，变成摄影师的口袋密境。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>起火的渔船属于CT3型号，长期停泊在渔港内港，船上并没有渔工。当火势迅速扩大时，有五艘渔船被紧密停泊在一起，形成一片火海。当地居民迅速行动，切断了其中两艘渔船的牵绳，将它们推离火源，同时启动了附近的其他渔船，将它们赶紧移离现场，以避免火势进一步扩大。</p>
<p>这一场火灾意外地让渔港内港，成为摄影爱好者的绝佳景点。</p>
<figure id="14108116" aria-describedby="caption-14108116" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108116-644917-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108116-644917-450x300.jpg" alt="荒凉感，但照片呈现反而是另一种美。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14108116" class="wp-caption-text">荒凉感，但照片呈现反而是另一种美。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14108115" aria-describedby="caption-14108115" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108115-644916-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14108115-644916-450x300.jpg" alt="每一张照片都令人赞赏。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14108115" class="wp-caption-text">每一张照片都令人赞赏。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
