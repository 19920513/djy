<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>花莲玉里农民勤耕耘 迎接第二期稻米采收季节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121174-648717-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>没正式进入收割之前，每天早上仍然有农民前往田地巡视，享受这个季节的仙境般美景。（康明义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月21日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E8%8E%B2.md#1">花莲</a>报导）阳光普照的11月下旬，花莲<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%89%E9%87%8C.md#1">玉里</a>的田间已经开始传来割稻机收获的声音，预告农民们即将迎接农田的采收季节。还没正式进入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B6%E5%89%B2.md#1">收割</a>之前，每天早上仍然有农民前往田地巡视，享受这个季节的仙境般美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14121179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121179-648722.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14121179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121179-648722-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121179" class="wp-caption-text">采收前的风景如诗如画。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>农民们早早开始检修割稻机、大卡车、烘谷机、拼装车和堆高机，为即将到来的二期<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B6%E5%89%B2.md#1">收割</a>做好万全准备。然而，电池的故障成了他们的困扰，这意味着又一次的荷包失血。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%89%E9%87%8C.md#1">玉里</a>青农康明义表示，他种植的香米田区，自从第一次台风后就一直涌出泉水，虽然泉水通常会在一段时间后停止，但今年接连袭来的台风带来的雨水使得泉水一直涌流不息，使得这片田地变成了一个无需巡水的神奇田区。</p>
<p>根据惯例，二期收割前常常伴随着阴雨天气，今年也不例外，小雨绵绵不绝，预计泉水在收割前难以枯竭。这将使得割稻机在泥泞的田地中操作，会变得更加困难。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14121176" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121176" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121176-648719.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14121176" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121176-648719-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121176" class="wp-caption-text">纵谷玉里田间风景。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14121175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121175-648718.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14121175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121175-648718-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121175" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E8%8E%B2.md#1">花莲</a>玉里农民勤耕耘，迎接农田采收季节。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在第二期耕作期间，面临了三个台风的侵袭，插秧后不久就受到了大雨的影响，导致小秧泡水几天。然而，尽管台风带来了挑战，稻作的生长也稍微延缓，使得收割时间比往年稍晚。</p>
<p>不过，这几天田地中传来割稻机收获的声音，农民们经过风雨洗礼，终于迎来了收获的时刻。尽管遇到了一些困难，但农民们充满信心，相信只要有良好的天气和坚韧的意志，他们能够克服一切困难，完成采收的任务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14121178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121178-648721.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14121178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121178-648721-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121178" class="wp-caption-text">玉里<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A8%BB%E7%B1%B3.md#1">稻米</a>进入收割期。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14121177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121177" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121177-648720.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14121177" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121177-648720-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121177" class="wp-caption-text">台风带来了挑战，稻作的生长也稍微延缓，使得收割时间比往年稍晚。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
