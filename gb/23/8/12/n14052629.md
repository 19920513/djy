<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】新酒旧瓶 2023 Acura Integra A-Spec with Technology</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052640-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023 Acura Integra A-Spec。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年08月12日讯】Honda的豪华品牌Acura在北美拥有不少拥护者，尤其由Honda Civic豪装起来的车系，以往有被名为EL、CSX及ILX，最新一代以旧有名字“Integra ”来继承这任务，可算是Acura Integra的重新复活。</p>
<p><a class="gjw-iframe" title="2023 Acura Integra A Spec 新酒旧瓶" src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/zh-TW/embed/1g1ik1hnoud7f7LQ50i9LrTpX1l01c" width="640" b="360" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>以往的Integra通常有三门揭背或五门揭背两款，然而这次Acura只有一款五门揭背版，实用性当然更高，外型也相当运动化，前脸是Acura最新的五角钻石形进风口栏栅。旧有Integra最令人印象深刻的，相信那四个小圆头灯应该是第一位吧，但是新款Integra却没有沿用那设计，头灯是由多个LED灯组成的平凡设计，配以特式的脉搏跳动般的LED饰灯，有点像Kia K5，K5是橙色的，而Integra则是全白色。引擎盖上有三条折线，将Integra的动感再提高。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052630" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052630-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052630-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_08-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052630" class="wp-caption-text">2023 Acura Integra A-Spec。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>测试车是Integra A-Spec型号，车侧有“A-Spec”徽章，非常显眼，相反Integra车章却没有被使用，只是在左前头灯下方及右尾灯下方各有一个“Integra ”名字的凹痕，是比较特别，但在远一点却看不清楚。尾部由于是揭背款式，所以设计更接近Coupe，尾闸开关藏在中间的凹位，这个设计相信是向旧有Integra 致敬。尾部的鸭尾翼外观作用比实际作用更大。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052631" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052631-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052631-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052631" class="wp-caption-text">前“A-Spec”标志。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14052632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052632" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052632-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_28.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052632-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_28-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052632" class="wp-caption-text">后“A-Spec”标志。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>动力来自一副排气量 1.5升直四涡轮增压引擎，与Honda Civic一样，最大马力输出为200匹，扭矩则有192磅呎，基本上跟去年测试的Honda Civic Si一样，只是Integra车身重量更重，测试车Integra A-Spec with Technology车重达3,073磅。除了最高版本A-Spec with Technology外，所有Integra均会装备CVT无段变速箱，而A-Spec with Technology有CVT及6速手动变速选择，价钱也是一样。以前只有入门型号才会采用手动变速，自动变速会贵上约1,000元，现在手动变速变得更罕有，将来当电动车更普及，手动变速相信也会绝种了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052633-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052633-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052633" class="wp-caption-text">1.5升直四涡轮增压引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Acura Integra也有为手动变速花了一点心思，装有引擎转速匹配系统，在侦察到降档时会自动提高引擎转速，放开离合器时就会完美接合，“Heel and Toe”这类繁复动作，任何人都可轻松做到。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052634" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052634-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_33.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052634" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052634-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052634" class="wp-caption-text">6速手动变速已极少见了。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，Acura Integra装有经调校的悬挂系统，增加了Integra的驾驶乐趣，备有“自适应减震系统”能够更精确控制减震器设置。驾驶模式（名为Integrated Dynamics System）有四种模式，包括“运动（Sport）”、“舒适（Comfort）”及“一般（Normal）”，另外 A-Spec with Technology会有“个人（Individual）”模式，驾驶者可自己控制各项设定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052635-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_29.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052635-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052635" class="wp-caption-text">10.2吋全彩色LCD萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>进入车厢，Acura Integra虽称不上顶级豪华，但也比一般Civic升级不少。前台的蜂巢网设计类似Civic，车厢内以银黑红三色配搭，座椅及门内侧采用仿麂皮绒，并有独特缝线，更为舒服及特别。变速杆手握位置以皮革包裹，杆顶部及中间位置则是磨沙金属，入档感觉精准扎实。引擎自动熄火开关、电子手刹车及行车模式键都设在变速杆左方，而后方则是两个前后排列的杯座。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052636" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052636-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_30.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052636-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_30-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052636" class="wp-caption-text">9吋触控中控台萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控台上部分装有9吋触控萤幕，除了装有导航地图，也可支援Apple CarPlay及Android Auto，而且都是Wireless无线版。中控台底部有两个可提供2.5A电力的USB插头，一个是Type A，另一个并最新的Type C，不论新或旧设备都可有位置充电。Acura标配了Acura高级音响，而A-Spec with Technology则配ELS STUDIO 3D高级音响，有16个扬声器。测试车的恒温系统是两区。Acura Integra采用全数位萤幕，是10.2吋全彩色LCD萤幕，在转换行车模式时仪表板主题颜色也会改变，A-Spec with Technology另装有约5.3吋的抬头显示器（Head-Up Display）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052637" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052637-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_42.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052637" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052637-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_42-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052637" class="wp-caption-text">Integra的“豪华”车厢。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然Acura Integra A-Spec with Technology是现时最高级版的Integra，但Acura已准备推出以Civic Type R为蓝本的Integra S，动力更达到 320匹，排气量同样是2升涡轮增压引擎，也会配备6速手动变速，一定是比A-Spec更为好玩！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052638-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_31.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052638-2023_Acura_Integra_A_Spec_31-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052638" class="wp-caption-text">前台的蜂巢网设计。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>长/宽/高：185.8吋 / 72.0吋 / 55.5吋</p>
<p>重量：3,073磅</p>
<p>动力系统：1.5升直四涡轮增压VTEC引擎</p>
<p>马力：200匹@6,000</p>
<p>扭力：192磅呎@1,800-5,000</p>
<p>传动系统：6速手动变速箱</p>
<p>耗油量（市区）：9.1升/百公里</p>
