<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自制东南亚常见的甜点抹酱：香兰咖椰酱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060533-ce4ce43e6829e3db220cd0d4ad13e491-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香兰咖椰酱夹土司，是一道美味的早餐。（幸福文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>咖椰酱（Kaya）是一种在马来西亚及新加坡常见的甜点酱料，马来语中的Kaya 表示丰富，指成品有着非常丰富的味道及质感。咖椰酱是用鸡蛋或鸭蛋、砂糖及椰浆等材料隔水加热煮至浓稠而成，可用来涂抹面包或制作甜点。</p>
<p>咖椰酱的起源不可考，但普遍认为是由马六甲的葡萄牙后裔发明，因为16 世纪时马六甲被葡萄牙占领殖民，咖椰酱与葡萄牙传统美食Doce De Ovos 非常相似。</p>
<p>咖椰酱通常有两种版本，一种是添加了香兰叶制成，色泽为绿色，带有芋头香，而另一种则没有添加香兰叶，色泽为浅棕色。制作过程使用隔水加热（Bain-Marie）的方式熬煮，才能防止鸡蛋凝固结块，煮出细致滑顺的成品。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E6%A4%B0%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">咖椰吐司</a>是新加坡连锁咖啡店亚坤（Ya Kun Coffeestall）的招牌产品，是由从中国海南移民到新加坡的黎亚坤与太太开发出来的。将炭烤至酥脆的吐司面包横剖成两半，再涂抹自制的咖椰酱与切成薄片的奶油，搭配淋上酱油的半熟蛋及咖啡，这就是亚坤最出名的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A9%E9%A4%90.md#1">早餐</a>。</p>
<p>几年前到新加坡曾经吃过一次，到现在都还念念不忘。咖椰酱材料很单纯，很适合家庭操作，如果没有香兰叶就直接省略做成原味咖椰酱，其余作法完全相同。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>材料</strong>（份量：150g）</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E5%85%B0%E5%92%96%E6%A4%B0%E9%85%B1.md#1">香兰咖椰酱</a></span></h3>
<p>香兰叶3 枝（约18g）</p>
<p>椰浆（椰奶）100g</p>
<p>细砂糖45g</p>
<p>鸡蛋1 颗（净重约50g）</p>
<p>注：椰浆可以用罐头包装或使用椰奶粉冲泡</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">香兰<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E6%A4%B0%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">咖椰吐司</a></span></h3>
<p>吐司2 片</p>
<p>冰的含盐奶油30g</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E5%85%B0%E5%92%96%E6%A4%B0%E9%85%B1.md#1">香兰咖椰酱</a>适量</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">制作香兰咖椰酱</span></h3>
<p>1.香兰叶淸洗干净剪小段，放入果汁机中，加入椰浆再搅打成泥状，备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060347" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060347" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060347-339f37fe2a9097014e615ec7e4fcc8c0.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060347-339f37fe2a9097014e615ec7e4fcc8c0-450x414.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="276" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060347" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14060350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060350" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060350-5a3fdfded0a9aaf2c15e36551e858521.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060350" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060350-5a3fdfded0a9aaf2c15e36551e858521-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060350" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14060353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060353" style="width: 304px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060353-aa9127fae4fa1f4468259a6e72b464d3.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060353" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060353-aa9127fae4fa1f4468259a6e72b464d3-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="304" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060353" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将香兰椰浆汤汁过滤出来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060357" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060357" style="width: 303px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060357-903e001a35e108104cef7b38539ef6db.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060357" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060357-903e001a35e108104cef7b38539ef6db-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="303" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060357" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 加入细砂糖及鸡蛋搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060360" style="width: 302px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060360-760965a946c4dc2b3dedec4be2ceb20e.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060360-760965a946c4dc2b3dedec4be2ceb20e-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="302" b="328" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060360" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14060366" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060366" style="width: 303px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060366-27a75005f325394de8e8b28973f809b2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060366" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060366-27a75005f325394de8e8b28973f809b2-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="303" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060366" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 另外取一个较大的盆子，加水煮至沸腾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060369" style="width: 299px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060369-4a01a708f5b787a130847dc246b22e8a.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060369-4a01a708f5b787a130847dc246b22e8a-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="299" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060369" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 使用隔水加热的方式，以中火加热，一边煮一边搅拌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060374" style="width: 295px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060374-6ad87b2b055fb0cb940fc6180c14f002.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060374-6ad87b2b055fb0cb940fc6180c14f002-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="295" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060374" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 煮至搅拌的时候出现“明显浓稠的漩涡状”，即可离火（约10-15 分钟）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060378" style="width: 299px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060378-80ef5cbdf1903179b699e5a7f8d13a6b.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060378-80ef5cbdf1903179b699e5a7f8d13a6b-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="299" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060378" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 放至完全冷却，装入煮沸晾干的玻璃瓶中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060382" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060382" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060382-e2d7e0e189bbac862cba32f9d7a16cc0.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060382" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060382-e2d7e0e189bbac862cba32f9d7a16cc0-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060382" class="wp-caption-text">香兰咖椰酱（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">制作香兰咖椰吐司</span></h3>
<p>8.将吐司2 片烤至酥脆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060385" style="width: 305px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060385-d74dabf5835bce608c2adcd537420ffb.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060385" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060385-d74dabf5835bce608c2adcd537420ffb-450x474.jpg" alt="" width="305" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060385" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>9. 在吐司表面抹上香兰咖椰酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060389" style="width: 303px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060389-aff7af5e7c94081e6d8278c716999d40.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060389-aff7af5e7c94081e6d8278c716999d40-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="303" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060389" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 将冰的含盐奶油切成4 小片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060393" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060393-81e5742f7b8b277fe1f130440ab2e319.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060393-81e5742f7b8b277fe1f130440ab2e319-450x489.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060393" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11.最后放上奶油切片夹起，即可享用。</p>
