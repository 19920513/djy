<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>韩志旼等人演《摸心第六感》 曝想要的超能力</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052438-230811090657100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>演员韩志旼主演韩剧《摸心第六感》剧照。（Netflix提供）、SUHO（金俊勉）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年08月11日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）Netflix最新韩剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%B8%E5%BF%83%E7%AC%AC%E5%85%AD%E6%84%9F.md#1">摸心第六感</a>》（英文：Behind Your Touch）10日举行记者会，主要演员<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BF%97%E6%97%BC.md#1">韩志旼</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%EF%A7%A1%E6%9A%8B%E8%B5%B7.md#1">李暋起</a>、SUHO（金俊勉）及导演金鉐润皆出席现场。</p>
<p>《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%B8%E5%BF%83%E7%AC%AC%E5%85%AD%E6%84%9F.md#1">摸心第六感</a>》是导演金鉐润和编剧李楠圭时隔四年再度携手合作，而<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BF%97%E6%97%BC.md#1">韩志旼</a>在剧中饰演拥有感应超能力的幽默兽医，文壮烈饰演热血刑警，剧中最神秘的角色则由SUHO担任，三人与导演藉由记者会的问答，满足观众的期待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052426" style="width: 493px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052426-230811085109100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052426" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052426-230811085109100707.jpg" alt="" width="493" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052426" class="wp-caption-text">演员韩志旼（右）、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%EF%A7%A1%E6%9A%8B%E8%B5%B7.md#1">李暋起</a>出席韩剧《摸心第六感》记者会。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该剧故事发生在宛如世外桃源无尽市，一位名叫奉艺奋（韩志旼 饰）的热心乡村兽医意外获得读心术的超能力，使她能够看到动物和人类的过去。她与一名来自首尔雄心勃勃的精英刑警文壮烈（李暋起 饰）携手合作，文壮烈需要奉艺奋的超能力以重返首尔的职位。不过，这对“笨拙”的搭档卷入了一系列的杀人案件，而两人的合作关系也远远超乎了他们的想像。</p>
<p>被问到为何会将剧名取名为“???”并简称“Hip”，导演解释说，其实“Hip”在韩国有“独特”的意思，加上主角奉艺奋其实要用手触摸各种动物的屁股，才能发挥自己的超能力，所以特别用“Hip”来去代表奉艺奋独有的超能力，也同时带有双关的意思。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052427" style="width: 493px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052427-230811085115100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052427" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052427-230811085115100707.jpg" alt="" width="493" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052427" class="wp-caption-text">演员韩志旼（右）、李暋起出席韩剧《摸心第六感》记者会。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导演金鉐润还分享：“这次必须将喜剧、浪漫、悬疑、惊悚元素交互融合在一起，实际上是非常难的挑战。虽然剧情可能会走向悬疑，但相信观众看到最后一定还是能找到幽默的元素，并开怀大笑！”</p>
<p>韩志旼也说：“原本以为这是一部浪漫喜剧，结果到头来发现完全不是！不过我一直很想要有一个喜剧的角色，况且这次结合这么多元素还有不同的情境在奉艺奋身上，大家一定能够发现这个角色有趣的地方！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052430" style="width: 408px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052430-230811085121100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052430" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052430-230811085121100707.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052430" class="wp-caption-text">演员韩志旼出席韩剧《摸心第六感》记者会。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14052429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052429" style="width: 497px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052429-230811085133100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052429" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052429-230811085133100707.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052429" class="wp-caption-text">演员韩志旼出席韩剧《摸心第六感》记者会。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后，被问及自己最想要何种超能力时，由于SUHO在男团EXO中的设定是拥有“水”的超能力，他便说自己已有“创造水”的能力，所以不需要再有超能力。但这个回答引起其他人反驳，韩志旼、李暋起都说，大家从来都没有看过这个超能力。演员们的幽默回应使记者会现场充满笑声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052434" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052434-230811085127100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052434" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052434-230811085127100707.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052434" class="wp-caption-text">人气男团EXO队长SUHO（金俊勉）出席韩剧《摸心第六感》记者会。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14052425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052425" style="width: 418px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052425-230811085139100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052425" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052425-230811085139100707.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052425" class="wp-caption-text">人气男团EXO队长SUHO（金俊勉）出席韩剧《摸心第六感》记者会。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>韩志旼则说，自己希望能够穿越时空，去见自己离开的亲友跟宠物。而李暋起提到，每次被问到这题，他的答案都会改变，他想要有一个属于自己独一无二的超能力，但现在没有正确答案。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14052437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052437" style="width: 481px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052437-230811085124100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052437" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052437-230811085124100707.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052437" class="wp-caption-text">演员李暋起出席韩剧《摸心第六感》记者会资料照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14052436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14052436" style="width: 408px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052436-230811085130100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14052436" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14052436-230811085130100707.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14052436" class="wp-caption-text">演员李暋起出席韩剧《摸心第六感》记者会。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>拥有欢笑、幽默、浪漫、悬疑、惊悚元素的最新韩剧《摸心第六感》将于8月12日于Netflix公开。</p>
