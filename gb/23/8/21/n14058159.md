<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：一只被遗弃的流浪狗获救后面目一新</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14058161-spspca-matt-matted-dog-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>被遗弃的狗毛发浓密，处于“最糟糕的情况”。(Courtesy of Tori Houston and South Plains SPCA)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年08月21日讯】（大纪元记者Anna Mason报导／赵孜济编译）人们在玉米地里发现了一只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%9B%E5%8F%91.md#1">毛发</a>脏乱的被遗弃的狗。他们在网上呼吁，寻求帮助。这之后，南平原爱护动物协会（South Plains SPCA）的一名寄养者收养了这只狗。她震惊地发现这是他们遇到过的“最严重的被遗弃的狗的案例”。</p>
<p>“它有这么多毛，你无法分辨哪儿是前面，哪儿是后面？”来自德克萨斯州拉伯克的托??休斯顿（Tori Houston）告诉《大纪元时报》，“那是哪条腿？哪儿是耳朵？你看不到它的眼睛……它的皮毛又脏又紧。”</p>
<p>“这太可怕了。我从未见过这样的事情。我们甚至不知道这只狗是什么性别。”</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429092-spspca-matt-the-matted-dog-1200x1110.jpeg?_gl=1*1lzxdyp*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429092-spspca-matt-the-matted-dog-1200x1110.jpeg?_gl=1*1lzxdyp*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." width="600" b="555" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">当马特（Matt）被带到南平原爱护动物协会（South Plains SPCA）时，它的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%9B%E5%8F%91.md#1">毛发</a>又脏又乱。（由Tori Houston和South Plains SPCA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>休斯顿已经在动物救援组织工作了大约六年，当她得知一只狗陷入如此可悲的状态这么久时，她感到震惊。</p>
<p>小狗被带到安全地带，并被命名为马特（Matt）。南平原爱护动物协会试图给它洗澡，但他们的努力无济于事。</p>
<p>根据Facebook的帖子，这只狗有腐烂的皮肤斑点，成千上万的山羊头小刺（一种植物种子）、小棍、草杆，还有甲虫夹在皮毛里、卡在皮肤内。由于它的皮毛紧贴在皮肤上，马特身上也遭受了严重的瘀伤。</p>
<p>此外，马特的指甲有整整2英寸（5厘米）长，一条皮带嵌入它的毛发中，导致项圈嵌入脖子。</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429088-matt-the-dog-spspca-1200x1083.jpeg?_gl=1*ilsjl5*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429088-matt-the-dog-spspca-1200x1083.jpeg?_gl=1*ilsjl5*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." width="600" b="541" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由Tori Houston和South Plains SPCA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429086-matt-the-dog-rescued-spspca.jpeg?_gl=1*ilsjl5*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429086-matt-the-dog-rescued-spspca.jpeg?_gl=1*ilsjl5*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA" width="450" b="459" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由Tori Houston和South Plains SPCA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然救援人员成功地将皮带切成几段，但每次他们试图剪掉它的毛发时，马特都会痛苦地大喊大叫。因此，休斯顿女士联系了南平原爱护动物协会主席金?莫耶斯（Kim Moyers），向她寻求最佳行动方案。</p>
<p>“她和我殚精竭虑，我试图给这只狗剃毛吗？或者我们需要做什么？”休斯顿告诉《大纪元时报》。</p>
<p>经过一番讨论，她们得出结论，为了解决缠结的毛皮问题，必须给马特注射镇静剂。马特随后被送往方舟动物医院（Ark Animal Hospital）。</p>
<p>当马特被施加镇静剂之后，凯拉美容服务（Kayla&#8217;s Grooming Service）的专业美容师凯拉?瑟蒙（Kayla Thurmon）施展了她的“美容魔法”。她剃掉了它的毛，并<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%AE%E5%89%AA.md#1">修剪</a>了它的指甲。后来，兽医团队收集了血液样本并在体内植入了微芯片。</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429093-spspca-matted-dog-rescued-1200x1600.jpeg?_gl=1*30vtx2*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429093-spspca-matted-dog-rescued-1200x1600.jpeg?_gl=1*30vtx2*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">剃毛时的马特。（由Tori Houston和South Plains SPCA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429082-heavy-matted-dog-found-in-cornfield-1200x1600.jpeg?_gl=1*8hn8l5*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429082-heavy-matted-dog-found-in-cornfield-1200x1600.jpeg?_gl=1*8hn8l5*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由Tori Houston和South Plains SPCA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429081-3-lbs-fur-matt-the-dog.jpeg?_gl=1*8hn8l5*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429081-3-lbs-fur-matt-the-dog.jpeg?_gl=1*8hn8l5*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马特减掉了3磅的皮毛。（由Tori Houston和South Plains SPCA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在减掉了三磅（1.3公斤）的毛发后，马特的救援人员重现了一只美丽而健康的狗。</p>
<p>“它感觉自己像只全新的狗！”南平原爱护动物协会在Facebook帖子中写道。</p>
<figure style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429087-matt-the-dog-shaved-and-groom-spspca-e1692067156384.jpeg?_gl=1*g4eg3z*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429087-matt-the-dog-shaved-and-groom-spspca-e1692067156384.jpeg?_gl=1*g4eg3z*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." width="449" b="549" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由Tori Houston和South Plains SPCA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>马特没有心丝虫病和蜱病。然而，由于贫血，它需要接受药物治疗，并且由于极度的眼睛刺激，它每天需要滴几次眼药水。一旦它通过血液检查，马特也需要牙科护理。</p>
<p>在撰写本文时，休斯顿女士估计马特需要大约六周的治疗才能被其他家庭收养。</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429084-matt-taking-shower-after-rescued-1200x1600.jpeg?_gl=1*vxxt9f*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429084-matt-taking-shower-after-rescued-1200x1600.jpeg?_gl=1*vxxt9f*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由Tori Houston和South Plains SPCA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着马特的困境传播开来，许多人以捐款的形式向该组织伸出援手。</p>
<p>“这真是太好了。”休斯顿女士说，她目前正在照顾马特，直到它完全康复，可以被长期收养为止。</p>
<p>“它很可爱，很有爱心”，休斯顿女士说，“我们每天都在发现新事物。它喜欢散步。它喜欢玩吱吱作响的球和玩具，它是一只很可爱的狗。”◇</p>
<figure style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429089-matt-with-tori-houston-1200x1600.jpeg?_gl=1*1amth3r*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429089-matt-with-tori-houston-1200x1600.jpeg?_gl=1*1amth3r*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA." width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马特和休斯顿女士。（由Tori Houston和South Plains SPCA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429083-matt-in-foster-house-1200x1600.jpeg?_gl=1*1amth3r*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://img.theepochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/28/id5429083-matt-in-foster-house-1200x1600.jpeg?_gl=1*1amth3r*_gcl_au*MzE4ODA4LjE2OTIzODQxMzA" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">改头换面后的马特。（由Tori Houston和South Plains SPCA提供）</figcaption></figure>
