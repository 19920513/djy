<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Taste Of Manila美食节多伦多菲律宾社区庆10周年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14051279-1280-3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年08月09日讯】由菲律宾艺术家、娱乐和社区协会（SPARC）主办，马尼拉美食节（Taste Of Manila）将于2023年8月19日至20日在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>的小马尼拉社区（Bathurst St夹Wilson Ave）举办10周年庆。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/9/n14050612.md#1/1280-5" rel="attachment wp-att-14051281"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14051281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14051281-1280-450x693.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="693" /></a></p>
<p>马尼拉美食节是安省<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>市原创菲律宾街头节，得到了多伦多市和省府的全力支持，是<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">深受喜爱的多伦多传统街头活动。</span></span> <span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">每年8月，马尼拉美食节都会吸引30多万名游客，来自不同社区的人士聚集在一起，庆祝充满活力的菲律宾精神。</span></span></span></p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/9/n14050612.md#1/1280-1-3" rel="attachment wp-att-14051277"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14051277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14051277-1280-1-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a>值马尼拉美食节10周年庆，主办者承诺，将举办一场前所未有的激动人心的街头派对。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/9/n14050612.md#1/1w2a0051_43216712835_o-scaled" rel="attachment wp-att-14051283"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14051283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14051283-1w2a0051_43216712835_o-scaled-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a></p>
<p>沿Bathurst St街，从Wilson Ave/Bathurst St一直到York Downs Dr/Bathurst St的2公里路段，将有135个摊位。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/9/n14050612.md#1/1280-4-2" rel="attachment wp-att-14051280"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14051280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14051280-1280-4-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a></p>
<p>正宗的菲律宾美食是该节日的最大亮点，游客将有机会品尝香脆的春卷、冰甜的halo halo、令人难以抗拒的美味烤乳猪，以及令人感到贴心的煎饼等美食。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/9/n14050612.md#1/1280-2-4" rel="attachment wp-att-14051278"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14051278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14051278-1280-2-450x254.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="254" /></a></p>
<p>到多伦多的小马尼拉社区体验一场超越国界的盛宴吧！马尼拉美食节还提供现场音乐和表演、儿童活动和时装秀等。</p>
