<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美食美酒 别错过8月11至13日康山烤排骨节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047005-369e48bd431f0b3ac46b1f1be80155ad-600x400.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年08月03日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%B7%E5%B1%B1.md#1">康山</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8%E8%8A%82.md#1">烤排骨节</a>（Thornhill Rib &amp; Beer Fest）将于 8月11日至13日在大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>地区康山Dufferin Clark Community Centre社区中心举行。美味的烧烤排骨、精酿啤酒和现场娱乐表演，将带给您多元的难忘体验。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/3/n14046975.md#1/1280-4" rel="attachment wp-att-14046985"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14046985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14046985-1280-450x296.jpg" alt="" width="557" b="366" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">北美大名鼎鼎的烧烤店，将在摊位上为来宾奉上恰到好处的多汁排骨。排骨节还提供铁板香肠、多汁的汉堡，以及配菜和素食等。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/3/n14046975.md#1/1280-1-2" rel="attachment wp-att-14046983"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14046983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14046983-1280-1-450x295.jpg" alt="" width="558" b="366" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">无论是喜欢粘甜的烧烤酱，还是浓郁干酱的朋友，种类繁多的佳肴美酒，总有一款能够满足您的味蕾。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/3/n14046975.md#1/1280-2-3" rel="attachment wp-att-14046984"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14046984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14046984-1280-2-450x602.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="748" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">来<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%B7%E5%B1%B1.md#1">康山</a>社区，在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8%E8%8A%82.md#1">烤排骨节</a>尽情享受美食美酒，还有充满激情的音乐吧！</p>
