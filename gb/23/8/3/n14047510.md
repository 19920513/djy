<h2><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E7%BB%A3%E7%9C%BC.md#1">绿绣眼</a>Zosterops japonicus绣眼鸟科</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14047513" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14047513" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047513-4eb9940c235a8e728c88b41eb012e5fb.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14047513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047513-4eb9940c235a8e728c88b41eb012e5fb-600x447.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14047513" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E7%BB%A3%E7%9C%BC.md#1">绿绣眼</a>是庭园及都市的行道树上，最容易看到的小巧可爱鸟儿，橄榄绿的羽色出现在美人树或山樱花树上时更显突出。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14047519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14047519" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047519-623660_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14047519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047519-623660_medium-600x894.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="894" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14047519" class="wp-caption-text">体长约11公分；背部羽毛为绿色，胸和腰部为灰色，腹部白色；翅膀和尾部羽毛泛绿光；明显的特征就是眼睛的周围环绕着白色绒毛状短羽，形成鲜明的白眼圈，故名绣眼。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>绿绣眼又称青笛仔（台语）、青啼仔、暗绿绣眼鸟、相思仔、白眼圈、粉眼儿、粉燕儿、白眼儿、白日眶等。为中国著名的观赏鸟。体长约11公分；背部羽毛为绿色，胸和腰部为灰色，腹部白色；翅膀和尾部羽毛泛绿光；明显的特征就是眼睛的周围环绕着白色绒毛状短羽，形成鲜明的白眼圈，故名绣眼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14047514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14047514" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047514-20469b366847d5436dcc862beec7e2ef.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14047514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047514-20469b366847d5436dcc862beec7e2ef-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14047514" class="wp-caption-text">中国著名的观赏鸟。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14047523" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14047523" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047523-7cd4b717f91b3c67146128cad94f96fd.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14047523" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047523-7cd4b717f91b3c67146128cad94f96fd-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14047523" class="wp-caption-text">它们是古代诗人及画家最喜描述的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E7%B1%BB.md#1">鸟类</a>之一。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>绿绣眼是庭园及都市的行道树上，最容易看到的小巧可爱鸟儿，橄榄绿的羽色出现在美人树或山樱花树上时更显突出。鸣声清脆悦耳，清晨时常在居家窗口向人问早，让人有股一日之计在于晨的温馨感受。它们更是古代诗人及画家最喜描述的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E7%B1%BB.md#1">鸟类</a>之一。爱吃小型虫，昆虫在其食物中占95%，其余的是在各种植物开花时啄食花蜜以及果实。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14047520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14047520" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047520-623661_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14047520" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14047520-623661_medium-600x797.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="797" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14047520" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
