<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>雪梨侨界组队为台湾而跑</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054043-1A-600x400.png" />
<h6>出发点海德公园分批合照：黄梅芳（左一）会长、施博祥（左十）主任、卢金烟（右一)总会长、林雅雯（右七）会长、张明仁（右九）会长；前排（右二）洪秀华会长。（主办方提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年08月15日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">雪梨</a>City2Surf城市到海滩<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%85%88%E5%96%84.md#1">慈善</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89%E6%9D%BE.md#1">马拉松</a>去年在疫情后恢复正常举办，共有约六万人参赛，今年8月13日已恢复疫情前的规模超过八万人参赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054042" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054042-1B-533x400.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14054042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054042-1B-533x400.png" alt="" width="599" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054042" class="wp-caption-text">出发点海德公园部分发起单位、选手、义工及赞助单位大合照。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">雪梨</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%A8%E7%95%8C.md#1">侨界</a>心系台湾，计有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>新南威尔斯国际杰人会、国立政治大学校友会澳洲雪梨分会、澳洲台湾妇女联谊会、澳龙摄影协会、雪梨荣光联谊会、澳洲台湾妇友会、雪梨宝岛合唱团、世界华人工商妇女企管协会澳洲雪梨分会及澳洲职场人等九个社团，及Iron Gate Estate 鐡门葡萄酒庄、E.Tel Communications、Taipei Fubon Commercial Bank台北富邦银行、TRANSCO Electrical、Yeh’s Group Australia及Bluechip Infotech等六家台商企业、继去年的成功经验，再度发起共组TW Runners队参赛，一起为台湾而跑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054041" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054041-1C-710x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14054041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054041-1C-710x400.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054041" class="wp-caption-text">出发点海德公园分批合照：张明仁（左三）会长、洪秀华（左五）会长、林雅雯（左八）会长、黄金环（右五）顾问、卢金烟（右五）总会长。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>City2Surf是一个非常有意义的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%85%88%E5%96%84.md#1">慈善</a>欢乐<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89%E6%9D%BE.md#1">马拉松</a>活动，自1971年开始在全球各大城市开跑后，受到普遍欢迎，跑者也可为自己指定的慈善机构募款。TW Runners指定受赞助的慈善团体为在地的生命线LifelineHarbour to HawkesburySydney，疫情期间生命线增加大量的请求协助电话，因而须寻求更多善心人士的支持。雪梨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%A8%E7%95%8C.md#1">侨界</a>也配合发动爱心关怀捐款，将请生命线出席庆祝双十国庆晚会接受捐款。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054044" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054044-1D-533x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14054044" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054044-1D-533x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054044" class="wp-caption-text">出发点海德公园分批合照：林雅雯（右四）会长、卢金烟（右一）总会长。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冬末的雪梨清晨仍有凉意，气温低于十度，但丝毫未减51位TW Runners为台湾而跑的热情，在雪梨华侨文教服务中心施博祥主任、国际杰人会澳洲总会卢金烟总会长、林雅雯会长、张明仁会长、黄梅芳会长、洪秀华会长等多位侨领的带领下，分批与选手、义工及到场帮忙加油打气的乡亲合照，并一起高喊为台湾加油！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054046" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054046-1G-533x400.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14054046" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054046-1G-533x400.png" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054046" class="wp-caption-text">选手跑抵终点合照：林雅雯（右三）会长、卢金烟（左二）总会长。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14054047" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054047" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054047-1F-533x400.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14054047" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054047-1F-533x400.png" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054047" class="wp-caption-text">选手跑抵终点合照：施博祥（左三）主任（亚军）、张昱仁（右二）首席代表、林友清（右三）经理。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14054050" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054050" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054050-1I-533x400.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14054050" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054050-1I-533x400.png" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054050" class="wp-caption-text">费心打扮的TW Runners合照。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>选手报名非常踊跃，共有51名参加TW Runners队，彰显旅居澳洲雪梨地区的侨胞，对祖国中华民国台湾的爱护及支持。TW Runners更以行动为台湾而跑，其中有数位选手并非侨胞，而是特别来支持台湾的。活动也受到许多台商企业的支持，中华航空China Airlines、华硕电脑Asus、E-Tel Communications、Iron Gate Estate鐡门葡萄酒庄及Tasterite &#8211; Go Planty等多家企业捐赠精美礼物及饮料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054048" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054048-1E-533x400.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14054048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054048-1E-533x400.png" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054048" class="wp-caption-text">选手抵达终点合照：施博祥（右五）主任、张明仁（右七）会长。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14054045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054045" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054045-1H-533x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14054045" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054045-1H-533x400.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054045" class="wp-caption-text">卢金烟（右一）总会长与澳洲选手Peter MacMillan（中）、印度裔选手Dharani Parthy（左一）合照。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14054049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054049" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054049-1J-533x400.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14054049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054049-1J-533x400.png" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054049" class="wp-caption-text">脸上贴国旗及台湾黑熊的TW Runners合照。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>选手身穿精心设计有台湾黑熊图案的队服，分批于海德公园出发，奋力奔跑14公里，抵达终点邦代海滩。比赛结果最后由James Kai Chun Wang以1小时2分19秒夺冠，施博祥主任居次，Chun-Hao Lee季军。主办单位颁赠每位选手及义工一大袋精美的礼物，与到场祝贺的乡亲们，一起在美丽的邦代海滩享受冬日的太阳及美味的饮料点心，互约明年一定邀请更多的TW Runners为台湾而跑！</p>
