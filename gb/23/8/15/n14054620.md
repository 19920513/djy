<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英国海边名镇嘉年华 法轮功腰鼓队荣获二等奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054664-2fcd5716-d062-4846-840b-30533701f152-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>2023年8月13日，斯凯格内斯?(Skegness) 嘉年华庆典活动，市长和腰鼓队学员合影。（Molly/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年08月16日讯】（英国大纪元报导）<span class="s1"><b>2023</b></span><b>年</b><span class="s1"><b>8</b></span><b>月</b><span class="s1"><b>13</b></span><b>日，</b>英国法轮功学员组成的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E9%BC%93%E9%98%9F.md#1">腰鼓队</a>，再一次受邀参加了英国著名海滨度假名镇之一的<ahref="https://www.soundofhope.org/term/773099?lang=b5"><span class="s2">斯凯格内斯</span></a><span class="s1">?(Skegness) </span>举行的<ahref="https://www.soundofhope.org/term/80661?lang=b5"><span class="s2">嘉年华</span></a>庆典活动。身着金黄色服装的<b><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E9%BC%93%E9%98%9F.md#1">腰鼓队</a></b>，<b>伴随着</b>《法轮大法好》等乐曲，击鼓行进，鼓声动听，个个神彩奕奕。令当地民众耳目一新，赢得了民众的喜爱，并荣获这次活动的二等奖。市长亲自颁发奖状，和腰鼓队的成员一起合影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054623-WhatsApp-Image-2023-08-14-at-22.49.27.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14054623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054623-WhatsApp-Image-2023-08-14-at-22.49.27-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054623" class="wp-caption-text">2023年8月13日，斯凯格内斯?(Skegness) 嘉年华活动，荣获二等奖。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14054624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054624" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054624-8d62e22a097e71d266e610b88b7826fa.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14054624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054624-8d62e22a097e71d266e610b88b7826fa-600x881.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="881" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054624" class="wp-caption-text">2023年8月13日，斯凯格内斯?(Skegness) 嘉年华活动，市长和腰鼓队负责人合影。（李芸/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><ahref="https://www.soundofhope.org/term/80661?lang=b5">嘉年华</a></span>花车游行是该镇多年来最丰富多彩的庆典之一。游行队伍在下午一点钟出发，延着列治文路<span class="s2"> (Richmond Road) </span>行进，然后向右转入蓝牡蛎路<span class="s2"> (Lumley Road), </span>最后在当地海滨地标<span class="s2">&#8211;</span>钟塔向左转入大游行街<span class="s2"> (Grand Parade), </span>途经商业区和海滨大道，吸引许多的当地民众夹道观看，非常高兴的向游行的表演者鼓掌欢迎，举着手机拍照。人们很高兴的接过法轮功学员派发的传单。</p>
