<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2023南庄部落漫步峡谷 登山健行9/9登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056060-630393-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>2023南庄部落健行活动，漫步、轻旅行。（许享富／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年08月17日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）2023<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E5%BA%84.md#1">南庄</a>部落<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%A5%E8%A1%8C.md#1">健行</a>活动，是为行销狮头山风景区辖域特色及推动<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%A8%E8%90%BD%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89.md#1">部落观光</a>，提升南庄地区原住民族部落观光能见度。</p>
<p>透过<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BB%E5%B1%B1.md#1">登山</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%A5%E8%A1%8C.md#1">健行</a>及音乐会等活动，深度探索部落文化、感受山林美景，发展独具魅力的参与式<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%A8%E8%90%BD%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89.md#1">部落观光</a>新体验，与山林间零距离的对话，并结合部落传统技艺及音乐舞蹈表演让心灵丰满饱足，达到部族团结谐和的圆融情境，本次活动内容如“登山健行”、“部落音乐会”、“部落轻旅行”、“体验原民DIY”等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14056063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14056063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056063-630396.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14056063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056063-630396-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14056063" class="wp-caption-text">2023<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E5%BA%84.md#1">南庄</a>部落健行活动，漫步、轻旅行。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14056062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14056062" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056062-630395.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14056062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056062-630395-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14056062" class="wp-caption-text">2023南庄部落健行活动，漫步、轻旅行。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参山国家风景区管理处长曹忠猷说喜欢漫步山林，9月9日上午9时30分有南庄石壁部落健行，全程3.6公里，好走不累。沿途有饮水和当地水果，原民烤大猪可品尝。还有音乐会、多项体验活动、还有50吋电视等摸彩活动。报名健行活动游客请于约定时间至集合地点搭乘专车前往。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14056061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14056061" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056061-630394.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-14056061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056061-630394-600x900.jpeg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14056061" class="wp-caption-text">参山国家风景区管理处长曹忠猷说2023南庄部落健行活动，是为行销狮头山风景区辖域特色及推动部落观光，提升南庄地区原住民族部落观光能见度。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14056064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14056064" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056064-630397.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-14056064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056064-630397-600x900.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14056064" class="wp-caption-text">南庄乡长罗春莲欢迎来南庄漫游，体验多元文化美、风景与特优农特产品。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14056065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14056065" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056065-630398.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-14056065" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14056065-630398-600x888.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="666" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14056065" class="wp-caption-text">林郁证长老竭诚来欣赏<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%9F%E6%B0%91%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">原民文化</a>、风景与美食，同时音乐会与体验原民DIY活动（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>报名小旅行的游客请于约定时间至集合地点搭乘专车。接驳巴士时刻表与详细内容。请上主题网站。游客停留南庄住宿，9月10日举办部落轻旅行，分东河、蓬莱两条路线，各开放20人次报名。欣赏部人文与风景美。请上网Facebook搜寻“趣游参山：狮头山、梨山、 八卦山”查询。或上活动主题网站：https://sites.google.com/view/hatala-raisinay/查询。</p>
