<h2><strong>桂花Osmanthus fragrans木犀科</strong></h2>
<p>又称木犀、岩桂、九里香 、桂、木犀花。原始的桂树都生长在岩壁上，所以又叫“岩桂”。常绿权木，高度可达10公尺，全株平滑，木质茎，多生枝桠。叶密集对生，革质，椭圆形，先端尖锐，边缘有细锯齿。花腋生呈簇状，花冠四裂，银白色，盛开时芳香四溢，具有浓郁香味且经久不散。5~10月开花，花期长，连续绽放不断。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14067007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067007-469477_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14067007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067007-469477_medium-600x803.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="803" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067007" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14067005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067005-469475_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14067005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067005-469475_medium-600x905.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="905" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067005" class="wp-caption-text">原始的桂树都生长在岩壁上，所以又叫“岩桂”。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14067006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067006-469476_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14067006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067006-469476_medium-600x903.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="903" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067006" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾的桂花树由于99%以上都是开雄性花的植株；因为雌雄异株关系，所以我们常见桂花，却很少见到桂花的果实。台湾嫁娶习俗以桂花陪嫁，象征高贵多子。常作为庭园树。花可入酒、浸盐蜜、作香茶及桂花卤等。全株可入药，根或根皮可治胃痛、牙痛、风湿麻木、筋骨疼痛；花可化痰、散瘀。止牙痛、口臭；果实可治肝胃气痛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14067000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067000" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067000-469478_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14067000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067000-469478_medium-600x445.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067000" class="wp-caption-text">花腋生呈簇状，花冠四裂，银白色，盛开时芳香四溢，具有浓郁香味且经久不散。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
