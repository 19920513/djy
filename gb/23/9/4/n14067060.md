<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>来北约克美食节 体验文化美食与娱乐的碰撞</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月05日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E7%BA%A6%E5%85%8B.md#1">北约克</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E8%8A%82.md#1">美食节</a>（Taste of North York）<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">是一场美食盛宴，也是享受充满活力的娱乐的聚会，更是体验各种文化之旅，9月8日至10日在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E7%BA%A6%E5%85%8B.md#1">北约克</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E5%B0%94%EF%BC%8E%E6%8B%89%E6%96%AF%E7%89%B9%E6%9B%BC%E5%B9%BF%E5%9C%BA.md#1">梅尔．拉斯特曼广场</a>（Mel Lastman Square）举行。</span></span></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/4/n14067060.md#1/c8e79ceb9f090630938a51881f3da2e3" rel="attachment wp-att-14067081"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14067081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067081-c8e79ceb9f090630938a51881f3da2e3-450x440.png" alt="" width="450" b="440" /></a></p>

<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/4/n14067060.md#1/1280-7" rel="attachment wp-att-14067077"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14067077" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067077-1280-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a></p>
<p>多样化的美食，也将带您开启独特的味蕾之旅。大规模的露天街头美食市场，将提供来自各种不同文化的数百种美食，让您尽情享受北约克多元化的佳肴。</p>
<p>品尝美味、跟随音乐的节奏摇摆，来北约克美食节吧。</p>
