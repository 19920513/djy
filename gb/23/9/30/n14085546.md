<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>屏东万年溪秋日美景 栾树换妆迎接多彩季节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085550-638451-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>全长约5.5公里的万年溪，其中的2公里沿岸被种植满了台湾栾树。（黄荣群提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月30日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）时序转入秋天，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">屏东市</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%87%E5%B9%B4%E6%BA%AA.md#1">万年溪</a>两旁，台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BE%E6%A0%91.md#1">栾树</a>宛如换妆的奇幻时刻，把这条河川装点得如诗如画。全长约5.5公里的万年溪，其中的2公里沿岸种满了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%A0%BE%E6%A0%91.md#1">台湾栾树</a>。此时，这些栾树正由翠绿转变成金黄，并渐渐变为浓郁的红色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14085551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085551" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085551-638452.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14085551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085551-638452-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085551" class="wp-caption-text">成为摄影爱好者的天堂。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14085548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085548-638449.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14085548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085548-638449-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085548" class="wp-caption-text">美丽的秋分景色。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14085552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085552-638453.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14085552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085552-638453-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085552" class="wp-caption-text">成为摄影爱好者的天堂。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>满目的栾花点缀着整条<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%87%E5%B9%B4%E6%BA%AA.md#1">万年溪</a>两旁，营造出绚丽多彩的景致，加上蓝天白云，使得万年溪更加美不胜收。万年溪扮演着<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">屏东市</a>生态的串联者，而河畔的柳树随风摇曳，为这幅画面增添了诗情画意的色彩。此外，河岸两侧设有座椅、木栈道和观景台，提供民众一个优雅的漫游场所。荫树的遮蔽下，漫步于人行道上，也不会感到酷热。</p>
<p>台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BE%E6%A0%91.md#1">栾树</a>是台湾特有的树种，全球仅此一地。主要分布在低海拔和阳光充足的区域。除了具备抗污染的特性外，它还能够吸收空气中的废气，因此在台湾的众多地方都可以见到栾树的身影。</p>
<p>摄影家黄荣群表示万年溪的美景，尤其在这个秋高气爽的季节格外引人入胜。在阳光普照的日子里，这片景致更加令人陶醉，为大自然的美丽赠送了一个极为宜人的画面。无论在哪个景点，欣赏美丽都取决于观赏者的心境，而每个景点都有其独特的吸引之处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14085553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085553-638454.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14085553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085553-638454-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085553" class="wp-caption-text">蓝天白云的倒映一起呼应美景。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
