<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>布农部落休闲农场 海葵折损企待整修和关注</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075611-636131-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>表演剧场：猎人回来报战功。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）台东县<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%B6%E5%B9%B3%E4%B9%A1.md#1">延平乡</a>的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E5%86%9C%E9%83%A8%E8%90%BD.md#1">布农部落</a>休闲农场”，经过二十年族人的努力，成为了一个独特的旅游景点，但这一次海葵台风却重重打击布农族人的希望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075612" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075612" style="width: 511px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075612-636132-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14075612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075612-636132-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="511" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075612" class="wp-caption-text">当地木艺家阿旦的作品。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每天上午10:30和下午14:00，游客可以欣赏到布农族特别的八部合音、猎人表演，感受这富有穿透力的音乐，了解布农族的生活和祭典。此外，游客还可以参加各种活动，表演开演前一小时，可以先去体验射箭、皮雕DIY工作坊，带着自己的手作品回家，留下珍贵的回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075613" style="width: 492px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075613-636133-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14075613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075613-636133-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="492" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075613" class="wp-caption-text">台风过后的景象。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E5%86%9C%E9%83%A8%E8%90%BD.md#1">布农部落</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>布农部落工作人员白铃屋表示海葵台风重创<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>，布农部落园区里的大树、民宿区，还有园区里的木雕作品，包括电力和热水设备，三个月内都无法修复，因为需要一些资金。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075614" style="width: 479px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075614-636134-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14075614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075614-636134-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="479" b="359" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075614" class="wp-caption-text">厨房全毁。（布农部落提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>布农部落休闲农场承载<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%B6%E5%B9%B3%E4%B9%A1.md#1">延平乡</a>布农族人，老中青三代的经济命脉，很多学子从小就在这边表演打工长大的，自给自足的情况下，让他们能够安身立命，到外地求学后，再返乡跟族人一起打拼，所以重新出发，是他们目前最大的愿望。</p>
