<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>柴鱼渣别丢 教你做传统日式柴鱼佃煮</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078651-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>传统日本家庭式柴鱼佃煮。（日日幸福出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%83%E7%85%AE.md#1">佃煮</a>是传统日本家庭式的烹调方式，成品甜中带咸，风味非常特别，基本材料就是酱油及糖，也会添加一些味醂及米酒提味添香，成品因为含水低，所以适合较长时间保存。</p>
<p>煮完日式高汤过滤出来的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%B4%E9%B1%BC.md#1">柴鱼</a>渣不要急着丢弃，以<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%83%E7%85%AE.md#1">佃煮</a>方式好好利用，就是一道配粥配饭的小菜，甜甜咸咸好唰嘴。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%B4%E9%B1%BC.md#1">柴鱼</a>佃煮</span></h2>
<p>约2～3人份</p>
<p><strong>材料</strong><strong>：</strong></p>
<p>煮完高汤滤出的柴鱼45g、熟白芝麻1茶匙</p>
<p><strong>调味料：</strong></p>
<p>酱油1大匙、米酒1大匙、味醂1大匙、糖1.5大匙</p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1.依序将所有调味料放入锅中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078613" style="width: 297px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078613-4a9aefe8a2f7832c4d8dfd04db99fb22.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14078613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078613-4a9aefe8a2f7832c4d8dfd04db99fb22-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="297" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078613" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.混合均匀小火煮沸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078616" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078616-627309772de7d42a39b3f77a6f7fd2fa.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078616-627309772de7d42a39b3f77a6f7fd2fa-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078616" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.将煮完高汤滤出的柴鱼加入，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078619" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078619-64c188cb2679709058cc6bb4b7437b72.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078619-64c188cb2679709058cc6bb4b7437b72-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078619" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.小火持续熬煮至汤汁收干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078621" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078621-7e50adf3732b4f278db02e34b6967deb.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078621-7e50adf3732b4f278db02e34b6967deb-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078621" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.最后将熟白芝麻加入混合均匀即可。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>小叮咛：</strong></span></h2>
<p>柴鱼佃煮冷却后，密封放冰箱冷藏保存，约可以保存1个月左右。</p>
<p><strong>●工具与材料说明</strong>：</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078625" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078625-6335a1a3e2f75ddf6d08ded9e230cbc9.png"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078625-6335a1a3e2f75ddf6d08ded9e230cbc9-300x452.png" alt="" width="300" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078625" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>常见量匙份量</strong>：</p>
<p>1大匙（1T）=15cc<br />
1茶（小）匙（1t）=5cc<br />
1/2茶匙（1/2t）=2.5cc<br />
1/4茶匙（1/4t）=1.25cc</p>
<p><strong>柴鱼片</strong><strong>：</strong>由鲣鱼鱼腹部位的肉经过煮熟、干燥及发霉等程序制成坚硬如石头的柴鱼块，再用钢刀刨成如木屑般的薄片状食用。是日式高汤及味噌汤汤底重要的材料，可以增加汤的鲜味及甜味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078607" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078607-010e554a7c1f6e7116ed898b44b795a2.png"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078607-010e554a7c1f6e7116ed898b44b795a2-300x452.png" alt="" width="300" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078607" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>味醂</strong></span><strong>：</strong>日式传统调味料，由甜糯米加上麹酿成，带甜味及少许酒味，可以取代米酒去腥并增加甘甜，并且增添食物光泽。<span style="color: #993300;">如果没有</span>的话，<span style="color: #993300;">可以用米油：糖：冷开水以2：1：2的比例混合均匀代替。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14078604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078604" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078604-b30c43aa796179b50b9d4b9307aaec95.png"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078604-b30c43aa796179b50b9d4b9307aaec95-300x452.png" alt="" width="300" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078604" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
