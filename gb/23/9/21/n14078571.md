<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>让生活增添新口味 面包沾食英伦蛤蜊浓汤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079689-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>英伦蛤蜊浓汤。（日日幸福出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>假日时间比较多，我喜欢烤个面包，再搭配一些西式餐点，让家人转换一下口味。</p>
<p>熬煮一锅可口的浓汤绝对少不了，刚出炉的面包沾食着材料丰富的浓汤，是我很喜欢的吃法。</p>
<p>平凡一成不变的日子也因为餐桌上的风景不同，而让生活添加了许多欢乐与色彩。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>英伦<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%A4%E8%9C%8A%E6%B5%93%E6%B1%A4.md#1">蛤蜊浓汤</a></strong></span></h2>
<p>约5～6人份</p>
<h3><strong>材料：</strong></h3>
<p>马铃薯500g、西洋芹1/2支、洋菇5～6朵、熟蛤蜊肉（clams）250g、牛奶400g、动物性鲜奶油100g、煮马铃薯水500g、无盐奶油30g、面粉2大匙</p>
<h3><strong>调味料：</strong></h3>
<p>盐1茶匙、黑胡椒1/4茶匙</p>
<h3><strong>步骤：</strong></h3>
<p>1.马铃薯去皮切约1.5cm丁状；洋菇切片；西洋芹切小丁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078605" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078605-a6a8a3616e18e90e6003e374760e1057.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078605-a6a8a3616e18e90e6003e374760e1057-300x419.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078605" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.马铃薯加入足量的水，煮约12～15分钟，至竹签可以轻易插入后，将煮软的马铃薯丁捞起，煮马铃薯剩下的水要保留。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078606" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078606-5140be09b87069a6701264e0bbba0471.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078606-5140be09b87069a6701264e0bbba0471-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078606" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.将无盐奶油放入锅中，以小火加热融化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078608" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078608-3cf6cfe74c1f1398292316b9480cb5f2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078608-3cf6cfe74c1f1398292316b9480cb5f2-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078608" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.加入面粉，用打蛋器混合均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078609" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078609-9ffedd862b863cf8b7a9c1ebb59c8c14.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078609-9ffedd862b863cf8b7a9c1ebb59c8c14-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078609" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.分数5～6次将牛奶及动物性鲜奶油加入混合均匀（每一次加入都要用打蛋器搅拌均匀，才可以再度添加）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078612" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078612" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078612-fd3d132c52bc74cca9bf691f2eea0016.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078612-fd3d132c52bc74cca9bf691f2eea0016-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078612" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.最后将煮马铃薯的汤汁500g加入混合均匀，煮至沸腾（如果煮马铃薯的汤汁不够，以水或牛奶补足）。</p>
<p>7.依序加入马铃薯丁、西洋芹、洋菇及熟蛤蜊肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078614" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078614-1cdee92f2369ea4b3f2d8687ca11a029.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078614-1cdee92f2369ea4b3f2d8687ca11a029-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078614" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.加入调味料。</p>
<p>9.以中小火加热，边煮边搅拌，煮至沸腾后，改以小火再煮5～6分钟即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078617" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078617-d91d214ed0550a52fc0d7a185b0cee1b.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078617-d91d214ed0550a52fc0d7a185b0cee1b-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078617" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>工具与材料说明</strong>：</p>
<p><strong>常见量匙份量</strong>：</p>
<p>1大匙（1T）=15cc<br />
1茶（小）匙（1t）=5cc<br />
1/2茶匙（1/2t）=2.5cc<br />
1/4茶匙（1/4t）=1.25cc</p>
<p><strong>奶油：</strong>由牛奶中的脂肪提炼出来，增添料理香气及滑顺口感。奶油分为无盐及有盐两种，在料理中使用都可以；若使用含盐奶油，请自行斟酌配方中添加盐的份量。</p>
<p><strong>动物性鲜奶油：</strong>由牛奶提炼，用于料理中做白酱、浓汤等。鲜奶油开封后，要密封放冰箱冷藏，开口部分要保持干净；使用完马上放冰箱，这样应该可以放20～30天，不可以冷冻，一冷冻就油水分离，影响口感。</p>
