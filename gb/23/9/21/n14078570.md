<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>平底锅作出好料理：橙汁鸭胸</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079670-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>橙汁鸭胸。（日日幸福出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>编者按：</strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%99%E6%B1%81.md#1">橙汁</a>鸭胸是知名的料理，不用烤箱、气炸锅，仅用平底锅就可作出一样好吃的家庭版<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%99%E6%B1%81%E9%B8%AD%E8%83%B8.md#1">橙汁鸭胸</a>。这一道曾经与家人共享的料理，一直铭刻在作者心中，勾起她许多难忘的回忆。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%99%E6%B1%81.md#1">橙汁</a>鸭胸</strong></span></h2>
<p>约3～4人份</p>
<p>早上翻找资料，抽屉中忽然出现一张父亲笑的开心的照片，一想到再也不能看到他老人家，再也不能跟他说说话，我的胸口又开始翻滚，眼泪不争气的滑落，这真是好难释怀的一段时间。</p>
<p>生老病死虽然是人生必经之路，但面对亲爱的家人却又如此不舍，我还有好多功课要学习。有一天，我也会步向生命终站，和亲爱的家人分别。</p>
<p>但是我期盼家人不要哀伤难过，留着我的爱，带着祝福的心好好往前走，继续为身边的人奉献自己的光和热。我知道，爸爸一定也是如此希望的。</p>
<h3><strong>材料：</strong></h3>
<p>鸭胸肉2片（约400g）</p>
<p><strong>腌鸭胸肉调味料：</strong>盐1/2茶匙、黑胡椒粉1/4茶匙</p>
<p><strong>柳橙酱汁：</strong>柳橙汁120cc、柳橙皮1颗、蜂蜜50g、白兰地45cc、红酒醋45cc、无盐奶油30g</p>
<h3><strong>步骤：</strong></h3>
<p>1.鸭胸肉皮的部分，用刀切出交错纹路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078618" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078618-8d32fd36a6dcd5423c333aa58e62f494.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078618-8d32fd36a6dcd5423c333aa58e62f494-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078618" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.加入腌鸭胸肉调味料，用手仔细搓揉均匀，腌渍15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078622" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078622-211a4a1697a0f1ed618c0ca1bcd93caf.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078622-211a4a1697a0f1ed618c0ca1bcd93caf-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078622" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.柳橙刷洗干净，其中一颗将皮括下切成细丝，然后榨汁取120cc。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078624" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078624-fddd78d8d8964d75ab99e566ecd3a3d1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078624-fddd78d8d8964d75ab99e566ecd3a3d1-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078624" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.平底锅中倒入1大匙油，油温热后，将鸭皮朝下放置。</p>
<p>5.中小火煎7～8分钟，多余的油脂倒掉。</p>
<p>6.将鸭胸翻面，盖上盖子，小火再煎8～10分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078629-0a33cc5eadf9387106069e6a64ccc3ee.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-small wp-image-14078629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078629-0a33cc5eadf9387106069e6a64ccc3ee-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a>7.煎鸭胸时，同时依序将柳橙酱汁材料（无盐奶油除外）放入另一锅中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078628" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078628-5f417c35ca965d43b018f2ce706959d1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078628-5f417c35ca965d43b018f2ce706959d1-300x381.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078628" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.以小火加热，熬煮约8～10分钟至浓稠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078626" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078626" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078626-793a664447ded810a1704686ae4050ed.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14078626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078626-793a664447ded810a1704686ae4050ed-300x363.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078626" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.最后加入无盐奶油混合均匀即可。</p>
<p>10.鸭胸煎至熟透金黄即可起锅，切成片状排入盘中。</p>
<p>11.将柳橙酱汁淋上即可。</p>
<h3>●<strong>工具与材料说明：</strong></h3>
<p><strong>常见量匙份量</strong>：</p>
<p>1大匙（1T）=15cc<br />
1茶（小）匙（1t）=5cc<br />
1/2茶匙（1/2t）=2.5cc<br />
1/4茶匙（1/4t）=1.25cc</p>
<p><strong>奶油：</strong>由牛奶中的脂肪提炼出来，增添料理香气及滑顺口感。奶油分为无盐及有盐两种，在料理中使用都可以；若使用含盐奶油，请自行斟酌配方中添加盐的份量。</p>
