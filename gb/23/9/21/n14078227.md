<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾之宝！“台湾栾树”开花期特别吸引人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078232-636831-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>褒忠台19甲路段。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月21日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）夏末秋初，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%A0%BE%E6%A0%91.md#1">台湾栾树</a>再次以其迷人的色彩和多变的风采，吸引着游客和植物爱好者的目光。台湾独特的树种，因其从春到冬四季不同的色彩变化而闻名，也因此有多个别名，包括“苦楝舅”“灯笼树”和“台湾金雨树”。</p>
<figure id="14078228" aria-describedby="caption-14078228" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078228-636827-450x738.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078228-636827-450x738.jpg" alt="台湾栾树的奇妙之处在于它在不同季节，展现出的色彩变化。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14078228" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%A0%BE%E6%A0%91.md#1">台湾栾树</a>的奇妙之处在于它在不同季节，展现出的色彩变化。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14078230" aria-describedby="caption-14078230" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078230-636829-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078230-636829-450x675.jpg" alt="一进花季，就会有许多摄影爱好者前来取景拍照。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14078230" class="wp-caption-text">一进花季，就会有许多摄影爱好者前来取景拍照。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14078229" aria-describedby="caption-14078229" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078229-636828-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078229-636828-450x675.jpg" alt="夏末秋初，台湾栾树再次以其迷人的色彩和多变的风采。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14078229" class="wp-caption-text">夏末秋初，台湾栾树再次以其迷人的色彩和多变的风采。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾栾树的奇妙之处在于它在不同季节展现出的色彩变化。春天，树冠满布嫩绿，令人赏心悦目。随着秋天的到来，树上的小花开始盛放，呈现金黄色的圆锥花序，犹如一场金色的雨，让人感受到大自然的神奇。而当入冬时，嫩红的蒴果变成暗红色。</p>
<figure id="14078234" aria-describedby="caption-14078234" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078234-636833-450x231.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078234-636833-450x231.jpg" alt="褒忠台19甲路段。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14078234" class="wp-caption-text">褒忠台19甲路段。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14078233" aria-describedby="caption-14078233" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078233-636832-450x225.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078233-636832-450x225.jpg" alt="东势自行车道" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14078233" class="wp-caption-text">东势自行车道（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这棵树的特别之处不仅在于其华丽的花和蒴果，还在于其叶子的变化。从满株绿叶到开花时呈黄色，结果时又转为红褐色，最终蒴果干枯成为褐色而掉落，共有四种不同的叶色，使得观赏期特别长，有人称之为“四色树”。</p>
<p>被誉为世界十大名木之一，在台湾各地都可以找到它的踪迹，像<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>虎尾高铁、东势自行车道，最壮观的应该是褒忠中央公路台19线道，一进花季，就会有许多摄影爱好者前来取景拍照。◇</p>
