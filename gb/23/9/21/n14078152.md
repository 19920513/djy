<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“闻曲忆情” 赵宝玲携星乐队唱歌谈人生</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078134-DSC01263-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>9月16日晚上，“闻曲忆情”音乐会在阿灵顿镇（Arlington）举办。主唱赵宝玲演绎经典歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年09月21日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨阿灵顿镇报导）“以前我有非常严重的心律失常的现象，自从加入了乐队，这种现象消失了。歌唱我爱了一辈子，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90.md#1">音乐</a>也陪了我一辈子，无论是伤痛，还是惊喜，它都在我的生命里面。”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>中华葱油饼的创始人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B5%E5%AE%9D%E7%8E%B2.md#1">赵宝玲</a>在灯光闪耀的舞台上说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">她分享的，是在她的个人演唱会上表演时的感想。9月16日晚上，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B5%E5%AE%9D%E7%8E%B2.md#1">赵宝玲</a>携<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%9F%E4%B9%90%E9%98%9F.md#1">星乐队</a>在麻州阿灵顿（Arlington）的“Regent Theatre”举办了这场“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%97%BB%E6%9B%B2%E5%BF%86%E6%83%85.md#1">闻曲忆情</a>”晚会，歌唱之余，亦分享她的人生历程，得到观众的热烈反响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078140-DSC01347.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078140" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078140-DSC01347-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078140" class="wp-caption-text">9月16日晚上，“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%97%BB%E6%9B%B2%E5%BF%86%E6%83%85.md#1">闻曲忆情</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90.md#1">音乐</a>会在阿灵顿镇（Arlington）举办。主唱赵宝玲演绎经典歌曲《龙船调》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>深情演绎经典曲目</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">周六晚上的音乐会以一段回忆视频拉开序幕，以温情的叙述展现了赵宝玲与家人来到美国时的生活、情感。随着“好一朵美丽的茉莉花”歌声在伴唱席响起，赵宝玲和她的音乐老师之一万兵传缓缓进入舞台，唱响经典的《茉莉花》歌曲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078129" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078129" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078129-DSC01151.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078129" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078129-DSC01151-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078129" class="wp-caption-text">赵宝玲和万兵传演唱《茉莉花》，拉开音乐会的序幕。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在接下来的晚会中，赵宝玲演唱了《我等到花儿都谢了》、《Autumn Leaves》、《龙船调》等不同风格、不同语言的歌曲，同时为每一首歌搭配了特色的礼服和表演，赢得观众的热烈喝采和掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078130" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078130-DSC01174.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078130" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078130-DSC01174-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078130" class="wp-caption-text">赵宝玲为每一首歌曲都准备了特色的服装。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">除了赵宝玲之外，为这场音乐会献上独唱、合唱或演奏的表演者还包括歌唱家万兵传、黄靖懿、周军培，波城飞歌歌友会，还有星乐团及其年轻主唱桑永骥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078138" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078138-DSC01322.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078138" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078138-DSC01322-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078138" class="wp-caption-text">歌唱家黄靖懿演唱美声歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14078135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078135" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078135-DSC01285.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078135" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078135-DSC01285-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078135" class="wp-caption-text">赵宝玲和周军培演唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“在我的歌库里边起码有100多首的曲子。今天晚上送给大家的曲子，是我从这100多首里边精选出来的。”赵宝玲说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">对于观众们的厚爱，赵宝玲说她非常感动。她之前曾担心450张票太多了，但没想到在很短时间内就发完了，而且直到演出当天，还有人问她有没有票。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">随着最后一首歌曲结束，观众席爆发热烈掌声。很多观众涌到台上，向表演者们献花，并与他们合影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078139-DSC01336.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078139-DSC01336-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078139" class="wp-caption-text">波城飞歌歌友会登台演唱。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14078131" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078131" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078131-DSC01198.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078131" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078131-DSC01198-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078131" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%9F%E4%B9%90%E9%98%9F.md#1">星乐队</a>的年轻主唱桑永骥演唱《海阔天空》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>“闻曲忆情”谈人生</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当晚，赵宝玲的家人们也都来到了音乐会现场。赵宝玲在演唱《月亮代表我的心》时，高兴地下台与两个女儿拥抱。她的丈夫周哲民上台献上一束玫瑰花，并在观众的掌声中笑言：“为讨我老板的欢心，我豁出去了。宝玲老是说我没有浪漫，没有送花给她，送了也是后院摘的海棠花。那这次就送了一束玫瑰花，如果她不满意，还可以送多一次。这辈子不满意，下辈子还可以再继续送。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078137" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078137" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078137-DSC01303.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078137" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078137-DSC01303-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078137" class="wp-caption-text">赵宝玲的丈夫周哲民上台献花，表达爱意。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14078132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078132-DSC01217.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078132" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078132-DSC01217-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078132" class="wp-caption-text">赵宝玲以《月亮代表我的心》献给两个女儿。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">赵宝玲与家人们的生活故事，也是穿插在音乐会中的重要主题。她用古诗词将音乐会分成4个部分，也代表着她人生的4个阶段：“人生若只如初见，何事秋风悲画扇”，“行到水穷处，坐看云起时”，“采菊东篱下，悠然见南山”，“但愿人长久，千里共婵娟”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078136" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078136-DSC01287.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078136" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078136-DSC01287-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078136" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿星乐队为演唱者伴奏，另外也有自己的演奏节目。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">赵宝玲与观众分享了她在求学、工作、创业、家庭，乃至学唱歌过程中的艰苦和成绩，伤感和喜悦。她谈到了在她日以继夜地工作时，家里的女儿对她的想念；也讲到在面对创业的巨大压力期间，她与丈夫时常爆发的争吵。她也讲到了看到女儿结婚时的喜悦，还有退休后在家摆弄园艺，收到丈夫摘下的海棠花时的闲趣……</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078142" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078142" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078142-DSC01357.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078142" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078142-DSC01357-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078142" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会最后，表演者们上台谢幕。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14078143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078143" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078143-DSC01378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078143" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078143-DSC01378-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078143" class="wp-caption-text">多名观众上台献花给赵宝玲和其他表演者。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“我们的爱情很简单，跟普通人一样，从初恋到热恋到走进婚姻，少不了吵架，但是却让我们更能互相包容和理解。”赵宝玲说。在叙述的同时，星乐队音乐总监甄若矛用电子琴为她演奏出温柔的背景乐曲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078145-DSC01403.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078145-DSC01403-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078145" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会结束，表演者和工作人员合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在工作人员合影时，这场音乐会的导演郑蔚说，她之所以支持赵宝玲的晚会，是因为她受到宝玲和她家人的感动。尤其在制作视频时，听到赵宝玲的大女儿说特别需要母亲在身边，这让郑蔚感动落泪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078144" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078144-DSC01393.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14078144" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078144-DSC01393-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078144" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会主唱赵宝玲、导演郑蔚（赵宝玲右）分别谈及感想。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“虽然是非常平凡的一生，但这平凡的一生里面处处充满了坚持。这是我们每个人身上都需要的。”郑蔚说。◇</p>
