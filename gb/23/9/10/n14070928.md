<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>二崙乡长杯歌唱比赛圆满成功！恭贺获奖者</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070929-634698-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>9日晚间二崙乡公所举办社会组歌唱比赛决赛（李政雄提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%8C%E5%B4%99.md#1">二崙</a>乡，拥有悠久的农业历史和丰富的艺文底蕴，为了增添艺文氛围，乡公所9日晚间举办乡长杯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%8C%E5%94%B1%E6%AF%94%E8%B5%9B.md#1">歌唱比赛</a>社会组决赛。此次音乐盛事，鼓励了乡民，透过歌声表达热情和活力，并凝聚了社区，促进了团结。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070932-634701.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070932-634701-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070932" class="wp-caption-text">热舞开场，炒热现场气氛。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070933-634702.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070933-634702-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070933" class="wp-caption-text">舞蹈团技艺超群，获得满堂彩。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>比赛一公开，即吸引了众多歌唱爱好者踊跃参加，现场气氛热烈。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%8C%E5%B4%99.md#1">二崙</a>乡乡长钟东荣表示，这反映了他的政策愿景，即长者安享晚年，年轻人在当地找到就业机会，实现乐活生活。他相信，这样的活动将使二崙乡更加稳定、更加团结、更具活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070930-634699.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070930-634699-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070930" class="wp-caption-text">冠军由廖春子夺得。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070935-634704.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070935-634704-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070935" class="wp-caption-text">五位评审和热情的观众。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070931-634700.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070931-634700-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070931" class="wp-caption-text">所有得奖者，过关斩奖相当不容易。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070934" style="width: 410px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070934-634703.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14070934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070934-634703-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="410" b="729" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070934" class="wp-caption-text">粉丝慕名而来。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>社会组决赛获奖名单。第一名廖春子、第二名钟长旺、第三名廖冠钦、第四名廖文鸿、第五名杨胜雄、第六名颜如煜，9日决赛圆满成功。</p>
