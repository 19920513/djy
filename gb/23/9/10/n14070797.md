<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>埤头“东螺溪韭菜花田”疗愈系雪白花海再现</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070798-634588-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>入园清洁费50元。（黄连兴提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%B0%E5%8C%96.md#1">彰化</a>报导）9月初秋，彰化埤头<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E8%9E%BA%E6%BA%AA.md#1">东螺溪</a>旁的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%AD%E8%8F%9C%E8%8A%B1%E6%B5%B7.md#1">韭菜花海</a>，再度盛大开放，久等的花海浪漫回归，且更加宏大壮观。连续三年，彰化一直保有韭菜花海，尽管位置不同，美丽依然不减。今年，花海主题以“童真童趣”为核心，为游客们带来全新视觉盛宴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070801-634591.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070801-634591-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070801" class="wp-caption-text">装置艺术和白色花海相映，好美丽。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070804" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070804-634594.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070804-634594-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070804" class="wp-caption-text">雪白加上绿影好美丽。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070800" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070800-634590.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14070800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070800-634590-600x899.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="679" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070800" class="wp-caption-text">游客在漫游花海时，能够捕捉到最梦幻的照片回忆。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070805-634595.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070805-634595-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070805" class="wp-caption-text">今年多了紫色LOVE装置艺术。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2023年的韭菜花田不仅保留了浪漫氛围，还引入了“童真童趣”元素，希望在后疫情时代疗愈游客的心灵，为乡亲打造一片美丽的雪白花海。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070799" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070799-634589.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14070799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070799-634589-600x899.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070799" class="wp-caption-text">由于降雨情况，第一区9月初才满开。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>园区主人表示由于降雨情况，第一区9月初近满开，而第二区约有2成花朵盛开，预计最美的花况将在九月下旬展现。花海与秋千造景完美融合，营造出浪漫氛围，非常适合跟好友们共同拍摄网美照片。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070802" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070802" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070802-634592.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070802-634592-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070802" class="wp-caption-text">云林民众黄樱花特别盛装前往打卡。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070803" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070803-634593.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070803-634593-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070803" class="wp-caption-text">数大便是美，仰拍角度抓住模特儿的美。　（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，今年的花田还增添了可爱的小屋和经典的LOVE造景，让游客在漫游花海时，能够捕捉到最梦幻的照片回忆。</p>
