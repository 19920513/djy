<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】花雕醉鸡杏鲍菇～消暑冷盘 酒香鲜甜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070951-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>花雕醉鸡杏鲍菇。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：秋老虎大发威，虽然节气已经过白露，但是天气还是相当炎热，这时候在家做<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B7%E7%9B%98.md#1">冷盘</a>，家人吃饭时一定开胃。“花雕<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%86%89%E9%B8%A1.md#1">醉鸡</a>”要好吃，和酒香腌渍是否入味、鸡肉软嫩有弹性脱离不了关系，口感和气味都兼具就是一盘美味佳肴。以下由梁厨亲自传授秘诀，教你如何做“花雕醉鸡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B7%E7%9B%98.md#1">冷盘</a>料理”，从腌渍鸡肉、蒸煮到浸泡鸡肉、杏鲍菇，详细解说，零厨艺也不会失败的大厨菜。</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">花雕<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%86%89%E9%B8%A1.md#1">醉鸡</a>杏鲍菇</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>有机走地鸡1只（约3.5磅，洗净后抹干水）<br />
姜4片<br />
葱2条<br />
八角4粒<br />
绍兴花雕酒4汤匙<br />
盐2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉1茶匙<br />
杏鲍菇2条</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【花雕酒汁材料】</span></h3>
<p>鸡汤2杯<br />
绍兴花雕酒1杯<br />
当归2片<br />
红枣6粒<br />
杞子1汤匙<br />
鱼露2汤匙<br />
黄糖1汤匙<br />
蒸完鸡过滤后的汁约1杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070943" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070943-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14070943" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070943-1-450x334.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070943" class="wp-caption-text">花雕酒汁材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 用盐、胡椒粉抹匀鸡身内外，再把姜、葱、八角塞进鸡腔，加花雕酒腌30分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070944" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070944-89ea362c87f58b9e3f9bf96da09dad09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14070944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070944-89ea362c87f58b9e3f9bf96da09dad09-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070944" class="wp-caption-text">用盐、胡椒粉抹匀鸡身内外，再把姜、葱、八角塞进鸡腔，加花雕酒腌30分。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 蒸锅内烧滚水，把鸡只放入隔水蒸25分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070945" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070945-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14070945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070945-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070945" class="wp-caption-text">鸡只放入隔水蒸25分。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 鸡汤加红枣、当归、杏鲍菇先煮30分。加杞子、花雕酒、鱼露和糖煮3分后放凉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070946" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070946-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14070946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070946-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070946" class="wp-caption-text">鸡汤加红枣、当归、杏鲍菇先煮30分。加杞子、花雕酒、鱼露和糖煮3分后放凉。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 鸡蒸熟后取出，放凉后再切大块。留下过滤后的鸡汁约1杯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070947" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070947-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14070947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070947-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070947" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蒸熟后取出，放凉后再切大块。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 将鸡汁加入“作法3”的花雕汁中，再放入鸡肉，放进冰箱浸泡过夜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070948" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070948-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14070948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070948-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070948" class="wp-caption-text">鸡汁加入“作法3”的花雕汁中。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 第二天取出鸡肉，杏鲍菇切厚片，放上红枣、杞子，再淋上花雕汁。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070949" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070949-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14070949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070949-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070949" class="wp-caption-text">杏鲍菇切厚片。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070950" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070950-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14070950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070950-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070950" class="wp-caption-text">第二天取出鸡肉，放上红枣、杞子，再淋上花雕汁。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
