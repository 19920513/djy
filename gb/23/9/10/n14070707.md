<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>彰化禽品节 畜产派对庆300 精彩活动盛大登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070716-634576-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>庆300精彩活动盛大登场！（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%B0%E5%8C%96.md#1">彰化</a>报导）为庆祝彰化县建县300年，县政府举办“2023彰化<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A6%BD%E5%93%81%E8%8A%82.md#1">禽品节</a> &#8211; 畜产派对庆300”系列活动之“彰化禽品节”，于10日（星期日）上午，在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%98%E6%9E%97.md#1">员林</a>市圆林园滞洪池生态公园草坪区热闹登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070714" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070714-634574.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070714-634574-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070714" class="wp-caption-text">“2023<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%B0%E5%8C%96.md#1">彰化</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A6%BD%E5%93%81%E8%8A%82.md#1">禽品节</a> &#8211; 畜产派对庆300”系列活动之“彰化禽品节”。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070713" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070713-634573.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070713-634573-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070713" class="wp-caption-text">彰化县养殖户代表齐聚一堂。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070708-634568.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070708-634568-600x280.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070708" class="wp-caption-text">社区成员提供精彩的表演节目。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动亮点包括YOYO姊姊亲子唱跳秀，吸引许多大小朋友一起同乐，现场气氛相当活泼有趣，还有限量免费禽品品尝、平价土鸡促销、特色市集等等，规划了游园小火车，以及建县300年特别推出的，只需300元的烤鸡野餐套组，限量300组供民众报名参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070715" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070715-634575.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070715-634575-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070715" class="wp-caption-text">yoyo姐姐受到亲子的欢迎。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>彰化县长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E6%83%A0%E7%BE%8E.md#1">王惠美</a>表示，彰化是全台禽畜养殖最大宗的县市，二颗蛋就会有一颗来自彰化</p>
<p>此外，为了回馈支持者，我们也举办了留言抽好礼活动。只要您在彰化禽品节脸书粉丝专页按赞追踪，并在专属贴文处留言，即有机会参加摸彩抽大奖活动，更有机会获得精美小礼物或品尝兑换券。</p>
