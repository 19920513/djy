<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>首届台湾音乐祭 雨中唱响华语经典</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073207-DSC01021-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>9月9日，侨教中心外迎来波士顿地区首届“波音旺来”台湾音乐祭。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年09月14日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨牛顿市报导）在9月9日下午的阴雨天气下，牛顿市（Newton）侨教中心外的草坪上回响着情感充沛的歌唱与充满热情的喝采。这是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>地区第一届“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E9%9F%B3%E6%97%BA%E6%9D%A5.md#1">波音旺来</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E7%A5%AD.md#1">台湾音乐祭</a>。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src="https://www.ganjing.com/embed/1g5889v78kh2u8cCC8j8tzxsy1cj1c" allowfullscreen></a>
</div>
<figure id="attachment_14073213" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073213" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073213-DSC01068.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073213" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073213-DSC01068-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073213" class="wp-caption-text">9月9日，侨教中心外迎来<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>地区首届“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E9%9F%B3%E6%97%BA%E6%9D%A5.md#1">波音旺来</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E7%A5%AD.md#1">台湾音乐祭</a>，得到上千观众热捧。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14073212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073212" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073212-DSC01065.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073212-DSC01065-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073212" class="wp-caption-text">9月9日，侨教中心外迎来波士顿地区首届“波音旺来”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE.md#1">台湾</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90.md#1">音乐</a>祭，得到上千观众热捧。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天，尽管天气预报显示有雨，但到了下午，仍有上千人来到侨教中心外的舞台附近，等待<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90.md#1">音乐</a>会开场。不少人撑着雨伞，或穿着主办方提供的雨衣。然而在即将开演之际，天空传来轰隆雷声，雨势渐渐变大。主办方为确保大家安全，让所有人进到海德社区中心（Hyde Community Center）的篮球场内躲雨，直到1个小时之后雨停了，才让乐队登上舞台开唱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14073204" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073204" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073204-DSC01013.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073204-DSC01013-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073204" class="wp-caption-text">由于下雨，主办方让观众进入室内躲避了1小时。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14073208" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073208" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073208-DSC01030.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073208-DSC01030-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073208" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会恢复后，两位吉他手热情演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这突如其来的大雨并未浇灭大家的热情。伴随着台上深情的歌声和乐声，台下观众们也爆发出此起彼伏的掌声与喝采。首先登台的是波士顿地区颇有名气的“You &amp; Me”乐队，随后还有多位资深乐手和歌手上台演出，唱响经典华语歌曲。最后，歌手们还献唱了一系列的周杰伦歌曲作为压轴节目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14073211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073211" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073211-DSC01064.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073211-DSC01064-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073211" class="wp-caption-text">歌手与乐手们深情演绎。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14073199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073199" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073199-DSC00935.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073199-DSC00935-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073199" class="wp-caption-text">主办方表演者、义工和嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“非常感谢今天下雨，让天气变得凉爽。”资深吉他手黄崇校下台之后笑言。这位“You &amp; Me”乐队的主力成员说，原本他们准备了7首歌，但因为下雨而不得不缩短时间。尽管如此，他还是表演得“非常高兴”，“很爽”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14073209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073209" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073209-DSC01047.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073209-DSC01047-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073209" class="wp-caption-text">现场有捞鱼游戏摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14073201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073201" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073201-DSC00971.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073201-DSC00971-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073201" class="wp-caption-text">客家乡亲会支持<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE.md#1">台湾</a>参与联合国活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">除了音乐演出之外，活动现场还有10多个游戏摊位，以及台湾龙舟队带来的龙舟模拟器体验活动。湿漉漉的草坪上人来人往，非常热闹。侨教中心主任潘昭荣也在现场协助演出工作。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏在开场致词中说，他非常高兴参加这个音乐祭。他尤其感谢“You &amp; Me”乐队及所有表演者为大家奉献节目。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“我们这里有非常棒的演出，他们会带来优秀的台湾音乐和文化。”廖朝宏说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14073202" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073202" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073202-DSC00983.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073202-DSC00983-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073202" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14073203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073203" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073203-DSC01007.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073203-DSC01007-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073203" class="wp-caption-text">海德社区中心主任莱斯（John Rice）致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14073215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14073215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073215-DSC01071.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14073215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073215-DSC01071-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14073215" class="wp-caption-text">主办方摄影师向观众致意。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">海德社区中心主任莱斯（John Rice）说，他很高兴看到，除了每年5月的“牛顿台湾日”之外，当地又迎来一个新的庆典。他感谢经文处的长期合作，也感谢所有表演者共同带来这场“台湾音乐祭”。◇</p>
