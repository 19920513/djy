<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多秋季自行车大促销 9月23日仅此一天</h2>

<p>【大纪元2023年09月04日讯】<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">一年一度的秋季<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E8%A1%8C%E8%BD%A6%E5%A4%A7%E4%BF%83%E9%94%80.md#1">自行车大促销</a>又回来了！自行车爱好者别错过9月在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>举办的2023年秋季自行车大促销活动（Bicycle Fall Blowout Sale），享受独家优惠，并有机会赢得令人垂涎的Bianchi自行车等。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>秋季<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E8%A1%8C%E8%BD%A6%E5%A4%A7%E4%BF%83%E9%94%80.md#1">自行车大促销</a>，将于9月23 日在Enercare Centre展览馆举办，上午10点到下午5点，仅此一天。</span></span></span></p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/3/n14066456.md#1/369965046_698267545663008_1306479896137065541_n" rel="attachment wp-att-14066474"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14066474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066474-369965046_698267545663008_1306479896137065541_n-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a></p>
<p>令人难以置信的优惠，绝对不容错过的机会。无论您是休闲骑手、周末勇士还是专业自行车手，<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">本次活动展示的各种自行车、配件和装备，定有一款能吸引您，并且价格令人惊喜</span></span></span>。</p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/3/n14066456.md#1/369967730_698276755662087_5294274513342067017_n" rel="attachment wp-att-14066475"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14066475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066475-369967730_698276755662087_5294274513342067017_n-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a></span></span></span></p>
