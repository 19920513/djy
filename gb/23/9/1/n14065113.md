<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>专家预测卑诗房市明年重返升势</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月01日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">柯瑞斯</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大温哥华编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>房地产协会</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">( BCREA)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">预测，受央行连续加息影响，本省今年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E5%B1%8B%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE.md#1">房屋销售</a>量和平均价将会有小幅下跌，但预计明年销量及售价均会双双反弹，其中住宅均价会回升至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">100</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>房地产协会首席经济学家布伦登</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">奥格蒙森（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Brendon Ogmundson</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）表示，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2023</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年卑诗省房地产市场比预期更具弹性。根据该协会收集的数据趋</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">势</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">BCREA </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">预计</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2023 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E5%B1%8B%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE.md#1">房屋销售</a>同比下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.8%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，平均房价将下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">976,600</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在大温房地产委员会</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> (REBGV) </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">管辖范围内，预计</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2023</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年房屋销售量同比下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 4.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，而平均房价将同比小幅上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 0.2%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">127.5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%B2%E6%B2%99%E6%B2%B3%E8%B0%B7.md#1">菲沙河谷</a>房地产协会预计，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2023</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年其管辖范围内的房屋销售量将同比下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，平均房价同比下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5.2%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">104</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">由于库存低，销售活动低于平均水平，今年大部分时间房价在上涨。奥格蒙森指出，由于加拿大央行意外地进一步提高政策利率，并且预计加拿大央行将在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2024 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年末或最晚至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2025 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年中期降低利率，销量可能会进一步放缓。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">卑诗省房地产协会目前预测</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2024 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年全省房屋销售将同比增长</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 6.1%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，其中大温增长</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 7.1%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%B2%E6%B2%99%E6%B2%B3%E8%B0%B7.md#1">菲沙河谷</a>增长</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 3.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2024</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年全省平均房价将再次突破七位数，同比上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。其中，大温平均房价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 129 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元，同比增长</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1.2%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；菲沙河谷平均房价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 108 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元，同比增长</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 3.8%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。◇</span></p>
