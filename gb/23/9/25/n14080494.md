<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>七绝：赏心稻粱肥（外八首）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080505-001A-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>晴雨偏宜秋草瘦，赏心最是稻粱肥。（图片由朝晖提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>序：古人云：“自古逢秋悲寂寥，我言秋日胜春朝。”南方的秋色与春景相比别有一番风味，分享一组图片及配画诗：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体; color: #000080;"><strong>一、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%83%E7%BB%9D.md#1">七绝</a>：赏心稻粱肥</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><strong>欲同花语莫相违，一路寻芳近翠微。<br />
晴雨偏宜秋草瘦，赏心最是稻粱肥。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">《平水韵》上平五微</p>
<p>注释：1、晴雨：晴天或下雨。北宋.郭载：“晴雨一川皆好景，烟花三月总牵愁。”三、四句的言外之意是说，为什么晴日及下雨秋草反而清瘦了呢？原来天地的精华都去滋润了田地里的庄稼。这是对丰收的期盼，也是对辛苦一年农民的祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14080499" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080499" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080499-002.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14080499" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080499-002-450x346.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080499" class="wp-caption-text">落照山衔尘世外，疏林水映画图中。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体; color: #000080;"><strong>二、七律：落照与清辉</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><strong>一钩新月又秋风，千嶂霏霏绿雾濛。<br />
落照山衔尘世外，疏林水映画图中。<br />
赋多神秀孤芳赏，心有纯真万虑空。<br />
天地为怀归路近，清辉惟愿九州同。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">《平水韵》上平一东</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">注释：1、霏霏：泛指浓密盛多。2、绿雾：青茫茫的雾气。3、落照：晚霞、夕阳。南宋·陆游：“乔木苍烟外，孤村落照间。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14080501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080501" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080501-003.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14080501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080501-003-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080501" class="wp-caption-text">一钩新月又秋风，千嶂霏霏绿雾濛。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14080565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080565" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080565-004.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14080565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080565-004-450x342.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="342" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080565" class="wp-caption-text">蜘蛛竹中落，板栗涧边肥。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体; color: #000080;"><strong>三、五律：翠微之约</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><strong>溪山风拂面，雾露湿侵衣。<br />
流水秋声远，野花人迹稀。<br />
蜘蛛竹中落，板栗涧边肥。<br />
待到重阳日，还来就翠微。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">《平水韵》上平五微</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">注释：1、落：落脚、停留。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14080573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080573" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080573-007.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14080573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080573-007-450x392.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080573" class="wp-caption-text">朱果添秋静，金风送晚凉。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>四、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%83%E7%BB%9D.md#1">七绝</a>：妙手制香</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>妙手和成座上珍，轻烟不&#33879;世间尘。<br />
由来雅正能离俗，除却浮华见本真。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">《平水韵》上平十一</p>
<figure id="attachment_14080566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080566" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080566-005.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14080566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080566-005-300x418.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080566" class="wp-caption-text">蜘蛛在竹林中结网落脚（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14080571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080571" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080571-006.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14080571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080571-006-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080571" class="wp-caption-text">妙手和成座上珍，轻烟不着世间尘。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体; color: #000080;"><strong>五、五律：秋静</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><strong>朱果添秋静，金风送晚凉。<br />
望中芳草碧，行处野花香。<br />
浥露亭亭立，含烟淡淡妆。<br />
荣枯皆过眼，物我两相忘。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">《平水韵》下平七阳</p>
<figure id="attachment_14080575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080575" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080575-008.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14080575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080575-008-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080575" class="wp-caption-text">一缕幽香化清净，百般尘虑付烟消。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体; color: #000080;"><strong>六、七绝：红袖添香</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><strong>一缕幽香化清净，百般尘虑付烟消。<br />
浑如国色心如玉，不染铅华意自娇。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">《平水韵》下平二萧</p>
<p>注释：1、红袖：指美女。元 关汉卿《金线池》楔子：“华省芳筵不待终，忙携红袖去匆匆。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14080578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080578" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080578-009.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14080578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080578-009-450x348.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080578" class="wp-caption-text">淡泊经风雨，逍遥入翠微。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体; color: #000080;"><strong>七、五律：入翠微</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><strong>不用三台贵，无求驷马归。<br />
光阴嗟物变，岁月叹人非。<br />
淡泊经风雨，逍遥入翠微。<br />
浮名兼薄利，莫与素心违。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">《平水韵》上平五微</p>
<p>注释：1、三台：喻三公（古代高官）。唐·高适《奉酬睢阳李太守》诗：“三台冀入梦，四岳尚分忧。”2、驷马：指显贵者所乘的驾四匹马的高车。喻厚禄。3、人非：物是人非。宋 欧阳修：“昔憩甘棠长旧围，重来城郭叹人非。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14080579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080579" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080579-010.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14080579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080579-010-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080579" class="wp-caption-text">流水光阴身外事，赏心诗句个中藏。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体; color: #000080;"><strong>八、七律：秋思</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">序：古人“秋思”之词多悲凉。白居易《寄王秘书》：“霜菊花萎日，风梧叶碎时。怪来秋思苦，缘咏秘书诗。”陆游《秋思》云：“鱼复城边逢雁飞，白头羁客恨依依。”“鱼复城”亦称“白帝城”今为重庆的奉节县。南方的秋天与西北方不同，有收获、有零落、还有成长。不须要“为赋新词强说愁”。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><strong>南国新秋别样妆，缤纷不用染风霜。<br />
才听野鸟鸣深树，又逐鲜花上短墙。<br />
流水光阴身外事，赏心诗句个中藏。<br />
消磨俗虑观云海，寄托清思有暗香。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">《平水韵》下平七阳</p>
<p>注释：1、缤纷：繁盛貌。《楚辞?离骚》：“佩缤纷其繁饰兮，芳菲菲其弥章。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14080581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080581" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080581-012.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14080581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080581-012-450x663.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="663" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080581" class="wp-caption-text">风吹香馥郁，枝动舞婆娑。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体; color: #000080;"><strong>九、五律：小池青荷</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><strong>舍外一池荷，欣承雨露多。<br />
风吹香馥郁，枝动舞婆娑。<br />
幽处常如许，尘埃莫奈何。<br />
思君清致色，伴我碧山阿。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">《平水韵》下平五歌</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">注释：1、阿：山边、水边均可用“阿”来形容。唐?杜甫《别唐十五诫因寄礼部贾侍郎》：“复为万里别，送子山之阿。”唐?孟浩然《归至郢中》：“远游经海峤，返棹归山阿。”@*</p>
