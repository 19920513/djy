<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大手牵小手 租税游乐园宣导活动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075170-636001-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>16日在屏东县立图书馆总馆的宣导活动。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）为使民众更加了解地方税屏东县公共建设的相互重要性，16日在屏东县立图书馆总馆热闹来举办“大手牵小手~租税游乐园宣导活动”，让民众认识并且愿意积极缴纳税捐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075169-636000.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075169-636000-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075169" class="wp-caption-text">租税相关知识的宣导。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075172-636003.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075172-636003-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075172" class="wp-caption-text">色彩鲜艳的主视觉。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075175-636006.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075175-636006-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075175" class="wp-caption-text">财税局与各机关的政令宣导。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075174-636005.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075174-636005-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075174" class="wp-caption-text">财税局与各机关的政令宣导闯关游戏。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次活动结合各机关团体及音乐表演聚集人潮，宣导税捐、云端发票便利性，并推广各项简政便民措施、建立正确租税观念，增进征纳双方谐和，屏东县政府财税局与财政部南区国税局屏东分局，联合在屏东县立图书馆总馆户外草地，共同举办“大手牵小手~租税游乐园”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%9F%E7%A8%8E%E5%AE%A3%E5%AF%BC.md#1">租税宣导</a>活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075167-635998.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075167-635998-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075167" class="wp-caption-text">民众参与闯关游戏。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075168-635999.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075168-635999-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075168" class="wp-caption-text">亲子团一起来关关。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075171-636002.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075171-636002-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075171" class="wp-caption-text">吸睛可爱的餐车。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075173-636004.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075173-636004-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075173" class="wp-caption-text">最大奖是5,000元礼券2名。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动包括精彩的音乐表演，当中穿插税务有奖征答及摸彩活动，其中摸彩奖品有梦特娇乳胶枕、双耳蒸锅…，最大奖是5,000元礼券2名。现场集结20家市集、气垫城堡，还有财税局与各机关的政令宣导闯关游戏，完成闯关任务者，就可兑换精美的礼物1份，现场有许多亲子团一起前来挑战闯关游戏。</p>
