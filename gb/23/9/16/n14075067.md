<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>慢活台东 在自然醒慢活祭中寻找自我</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075078-635974-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>紫色的浪漫氛围。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）16日至17日在台东<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%81%E8%8A%B1%E6%9D%91.md#1">铁花村</a>，有一场特别的身心灵休闲美学体验，这是台湾首个全身心休闲美学品牌的活动，旨在为参与者提供一个完美的机会，让他们过着信念的生活，重新与自己联系，并在自然的怀抱中找到。</p>

<figure id="attachment_14075070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075070" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075070-635966-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14075070" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075070-635966-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075070" class="wp-caption-text">绿色植栽让人觉得疗愈。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
