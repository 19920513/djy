<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【无鱼坊的摄影心视界】优雅蓝鹊林中娇</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076401-625892_medium-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾蓝鹊Urocissa caerulea鸦科。（图／郑清海提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>优雅<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E9%B9%8A.md#1">蓝鹊</a>树上跳</strong><br />
<strong>长尾山娘林中娇</strong><br />
<strong>蓝白羽色博青睐</strong><br />
<strong>子代护幼真奇妙</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14076398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076398-625889_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14076398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076398-625889_medium-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076398" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14076399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076399" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076399-625890_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14076399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076399-625890_medium-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076399" class="wp-caption-text">虹膜为黄色、喙及脚为红色。头、颈及胸部羽色为黑色，其他部分为蓝色。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E9%B9%8A.md#1">蓝鹊</a>Urocissa caerulea鸦科</strong></h2>
<p>又称长尾山娘、长尾阵、山娘仔。体长约65公分，尾部约为总身长的2/3。虹膜为黄色、喙及脚为红色。头、颈及胸部羽色为黑色，其他部分为蓝色。尾上覆羽为黑色，飞羽末端有白斑。有12根长尾羽，末端为白色，左右对称，以中间的尾羽最长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14076397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076397" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076397-625888_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14076397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076397-625888_medium-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076397" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14076396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076396-625887_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14076396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076396-625887_medium-600x894.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="894" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076396" class="wp-caption-text">尾上覆羽为黑色，飞羽末端有白斑。有12根长尾羽，末端为白色，左右对称，以中间的尾羽最长。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蓝鹊为群居<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E7%B1%BB.md#1">鸟类</a>，生活在台湾低海拔阔叶林，社会行为十分复杂，繁殖期约为3~5月，繁殖、育幼期间有强烈的护巢行为。每次约生4至6颗蛋。属杂食性动物，食物包括植物的果实或根茎、蛋、两栖类和小型哺乳类等，也会摄食厨余和腐肉，甚至还会吃下其夭折幼鸟。台湾蓝鹊会储存及分享食物，在育雏时前一年生的子代，会帮亲鸟共同照顾幼鸟，非常特别。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14076403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076403" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076403-625893_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14076403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076403-625893_medium-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076403" class="wp-caption-text">优雅蓝鹊树上跳，长尾山娘林中娇。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
