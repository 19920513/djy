<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：非洲青年自学成才 用废品造新颖跑车</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072110-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-1-e1692004367215-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年09月12日讯】（英文大纪元记者<ahref="https://www.theepochtimes.com/bright/self-taught-man-builds-his-own-sports-car-from-junkyard-scrap-and-it-looks-incredible-5459057">Anna Mason</a>报导／张雨霏编译）来自<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2.md#1">非洲</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E7%BA%B3.md#1">加纳</a>的一名青年从零开始，依靠捡来的废料成功地为自己打造了一辆功能齐全、造型独特的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%91%E8%BD%A6.md#1">跑车</a>。</p>
<p>21岁的凯尔文?奥达泰?克鲁克尚克<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/kelvincars_gh/">Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank</a>）住在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E7%BA%B3.md#1">加纳</a>首都阿克拉（Accra），一直梦想着成为一名赛车手和飞行员。他于2017年开始了自己的DIY<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6.md#1">汽车</a>制造项目，尽管他没有接受过该专业的任何正式培训，也没有适当的物力和财力资源，甚至靠收集废品然后出售以筹集资金。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14072118" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072118" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072118-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-13.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072118" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072118-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-13.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="535" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072118" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14072120" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072120" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072120-Kelvin-e1692787421997.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072120" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072120-Kelvin-e1692787421997.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="516" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072120" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些困难并不能阻止克鲁克尚克先生实现拥有一辆<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%91%E8%BD%A6.md#1">跑车</a>的梦想。他利用自己的智慧和才华建造出一辆具有海豚般形象特征的漂亮<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6.md#1">汽车</a>。</p>
<p>克鲁克尚克先生告诉《大纪元时报》，他目前没有机械工程学位，但他对机械有着敏锐的“直觉”，通过自行研究学习如何构建自己的原型车。最终，他成功组装了一辆亮蓝色的跑车，并将其命名为“Kelsus P1”。该车配有车速表、转速表、运动型鸥翼门，甚至还带有个性化的徽章以及设计新颖的“背鳍”和“气孔”。</p>
<p>一路走来，这位大胆的年轻企业家引起了其他人的注意，当地的室内装潢师、电工和汽车喷漆技师开始介入并提供帮助。从开始创作至今已经五年了，克鲁克尚克先生对他的作品感到非常兴奋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14072113" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072113" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072113-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-15.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072113-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-15.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072113" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14072112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072112" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072112-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-14.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072112-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-14.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072112" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14072114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072114" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072114-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-17.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072114-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-17.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072114" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14072115" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072115" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072115-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-18.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072115-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-18.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072115" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我一直对机器制造感兴趣”，他说，“我从制作玩具模型车开始，还参加过一些当地的比赛。每当看到跑车我总是很兴奋，这就是为什么我开始建造自己的原型车。”</p>
<p>克鲁克尚克先生通过做各种兼职，一点一点地赚到足够的钱来购买二手车零件。他跑了很多地方以便获得最优惠的价格，同时收集废品出售。然而，由于在获取资金、焊接机和汽车零部件等方面都有难度，加之没有足够的工作空间，他一直很难取得重大进展。</p>
<p>这是一个缓慢而富有挑战性的过程，但随着时间的推移，他的汽车开始成形，不久，他开始见证自己的梦想转变为“有形的现实”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14072121" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072121" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072121-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruikshank.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072121" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072121-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruikshank.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072121" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该样车根据海豚的特点和特征进行建模，采用了流线型设计，顶部饰有一个“背鳍”。发动机罩盖上的“气孔”可以引入冷空气，以便起到冷却发动机的作用。这辆车搭载的是摩托车发动机，他工作了很长时间才攒够这笔钱。“Kelsus P1”还配备LED内饰车灯、用于播放音乐的集成蓝牙以及一键启动功能。</p>
<p>“这只是一个样品车。”这位拥有远大志向的加纳人说，并解释他如何设法组建一支团队，以实现国际制造标准的目标。</p>
<p>不用说，克鲁克尚克先生的家人和朋友都为他感到无比自豪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14072111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072111" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072111-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-8.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072111-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-8.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="534" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072111" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14072110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072110" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072110-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-1-e1692004367215.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072110-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-1-e1692004367215.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072110" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他不仅参加了公开展览，他的项目还引起了全球关注，他本人甚至还获得了奖学金。展望未来，除了将他的汽车提升到一个新的水平，并致力于开发其它以可再生能源为基础的产品外，他还计划攻读工程学位。</p>
<p>“我凭借自己的辛勤付出和敬业精神造出了这辆车，我的朋友和家人都为我的成就感到高兴和自豪。我从有公信力的媒体机构获得的认可和赞赏，加上来自世界各地人们的支持，真的让他们感到惊讶。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14072119" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072119" style="width: 402px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072119-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-10.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072119-Kelvin-Odartei-Cruickshank-car-10.jpg" alt="" width="402" b="536" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072119" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14072117" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14072117" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072117-Kelvin-car-3-e1692004308812.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14072117" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072117-Kelvin-car-3-e1692004308812.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="538" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14072117" class="wp-caption-text">（Kelvin Odartei Cruikshank提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他从这个过程中学到的最大教训是不仅专业知识很重要，团队合作也很重要。</p>
<p>“成功建造任何东西都要依靠团队的力量。”他说，“一路上，我得到了关键人员的援助和帮助，他们看到了我的愿景并以某种方式支持我。当你追求自己的梦想时，总会遇到障碍。一定要有耐心和毅力，困难会在适当的时候得到解决。我凭着信念、意志力、决心和努力，渡过了所有难关。”◇</p>
