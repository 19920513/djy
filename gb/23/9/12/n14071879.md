<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>周三法拉盛办活动 助粮食券被盗刷者提交补偿申请</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年09月12日讯】（大纪元记者林丹纽约报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%AE%E9%A3%9F%E5%88%B8.md#1">粮食券</a>金额被盗者8月21日起可申请补偿，市议员<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E6%95%8F%E4%BB%AA.md#1">黄敏仪</a>（Sandra Ung）和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B.md#1">法拉盛</a>亚平会（AAFE）将于9月13日（周三）早上10点到下午3点，在法拉盛亚平会社区服务中心举办活动，协助粮食券金额被盗者、现金补助福利（cash assistance benefits）被<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%97%E5%88%B7.md#1">盗刷</a>者提交补偿申请。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14071880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071880" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071880-168909.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14071880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071880-168909-600x776.jpg" alt="" width="448" b="579" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071880" class="wp-caption-text">活动海报。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E6%95%8F%E4%BB%AA.md#1">黄敏仪</a>办公室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>预约请拨打：646-360-0253，或发邮件到：xiaodong_zhang@aafe.org。活动地点：133-29 41st Ave., 2 FL Flushing NY 11355。</p>
