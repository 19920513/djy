<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>耆英会白礼顿楼恢复中秋联欢 各族裔同乐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077026-DSC01701-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>9月18日，中华耆英会白礼顿楼与布鲁克林老人中心共同举办中秋联欢会，与各族裔长者同庆佳节。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年09月19日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨布鲁克林镇报导）时隔3年，布鲁克林老人中心（Brookline Senior Center）的活动厅再次响起华语歌声，迎来中国式的舞蹈。这是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E8%80%86%E8%8B%B1%E4%BC%9A.md#1">中华耆英会</a>白礼顿楼（Brighton House）带来的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B%E8%8A%82.md#1">中秋节</a>联欢会，其丰富的节目赢得观众的热烈掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14077024" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077024" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077024-DSC01675.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077024" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077024-DSC01675-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077024" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E8%80%86%E8%8B%B1%E4%BC%9A.md#1">中华耆英会</a>歌唱组表演节目。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14077020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077020" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077020-DSC01635.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077020" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077020-DSC01635-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077020" class="wp-caption-text">乐赵令瑜和徐思敏担任司仪。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">9月18日下午，不同族裔的耆英观众总计上百人聚集在布鲁克林老人中心，观赏中华<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%86%E8%8B%B1%E4%BC%9A.md#1">耆英会</a>带来的中秋庆祝节目。这次联欢会由乐赵令瑜和徐思敏担任司仪，其中乐赵令瑜曾获1964年的第四届“中国小姐”冠军。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14077030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077030" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077030-DSC01750.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077030-DSC01750-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077030" class="wp-caption-text">表演者和嘉宾们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在节目介绍和嘉宾致词之后，10场表演轮番登场。从舞蹈“相思月”开始，接下来有粤曲演唱、英文歌曲表演、传统服装走秀、歌剧选段等不同风格的节目。这其中的服装走秀不仅是中华<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%86%E8%8B%B1%E4%BC%9A.md#1">耆英会</a>舞蹈组的表演，还是热心观众们展示自己风貌的舞台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14077025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077025" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077025-DSC01690.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077025-DSC01690-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077025" class="wp-caption-text">中华耆英会舞蹈组表演服装走秀。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14077023" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077023" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077023-DSC01668.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077023" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077023-DSC01668-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077023" class="wp-caption-text">乐赵令瑜和谭润金表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在联欢会的后半段，歌唱家万兵传、黄靖懿、王子钊演唱了“我的太阳”、“饮酒歌”等歌曲。他们嘹亮且充满感情的演绎，赢得观众的热烈喝采和掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14077029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077029" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077029-DSC01745.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077029-DSC01745-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077029" class="wp-caption-text">男高音万兵传和女高音黄靖懿演唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14077028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077028" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077028-DSC01714.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077028-DSC01714-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077028" class="wp-caption-text">男低音王子钊演唱歌曲，李润钢琴伴奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">据中华耆英会白礼顿楼主任梅丽梨介绍，与布鲁克林老人中心合作的这个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B%E8%8A%82.md#1">中秋节</a>联欢会从1998年开始举办，其目标是将亚裔的文化推广到包括布鲁克林在内的不同社区。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“我们希望将这种文化，从布鲁克林老人中心，从我们中华耆英会推向麻省，推向全世界。”梅丽梨说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14077022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077022" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077022-DSC01654.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077022" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077022-DSC01654-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077022" class="wp-caption-text">中华耆英会白礼顿楼主任梅丽梨欢迎大家参与中秋联欢会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">她还谈到，即便在疫情期间，耆英会也没有停止为大家庆祝中秋；而这次能够回归2019年的活动形式，真是很美好。她感谢大家前来参与这次活动，并期待大家未来继续支持中华耆英会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14077021" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077021" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077021-DSC01642.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077021" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077021-DSC01642-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077021" class="wp-caption-text">中华耆英会副行政主任张昆祝大家节日快乐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天，布鲁克林老人中心主任Ruthann Dobek和中华耆英会副行政主任张昆也都来到现场，与耆英们共庆中秋节。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“上次我看到大家是3年多之前了，我们终于团聚，真是太好了！祝福每个人享有幸福快乐的中秋节。”张昆说。◇</p>
