<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>112年环境知识竞赛嘉义市初赛颁奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076841-636559-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>社会组受奖合照。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月19日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E5%A2%83%E7%9F%A5%E8%AF%86.md#1">环境知识</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%9E%E8%B5%9B.md#1">竞赛</a>有机会给市民对环境作更进一步的认识，嘉义市政府环境保护局于112年9月16日于嘉义大学新民校区举办“112年环境知识竞赛嘉义市初赛”，共吸引近400位的顶尖好手前来挑战，争取最高殊荣代表嘉义市进入全国赛的殿堂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14076842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076842" style="width: 511px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076842-636560-600x398.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14076842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076842-636560-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="511" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076842" class="wp-caption-text">高中职组受奖合照。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参赛组别依年龄分为4个组别，包含国小、国中、高中（职）组及社会组，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%9E%E8%B5%9B.md#1">竞赛</a>方式采“淘汰赛”及“骤死赛”二阶段办理，赛事进入骤死赛时透过嘉义市政府环境保护局脸书粉丝专页直播，以感受现场竞赛紧张的氛围，并为选手加油打气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14076843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076843" style="width: 497px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076843-636561-600x389.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14076843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076843-636561-600x389.jpg" alt="" width="497" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076843" class="wp-caption-text">国中组受奖合照。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>初赛命题涵盖2050净零排放、气候变迁及循环经济等环境议题，在经过激烈的竞赛，各组前5名将代表嘉义市参加11月18日由环境部办理的112年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E5%A2%83%E7%9F%A5%E8%AF%86.md#1">环境知识</a>竞赛全国总决赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14076844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076844" style="width: 525px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076844-636562-600x422.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14076844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076844-636562-600x422.jpg" alt="" width="525" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076844" class="wp-caption-text">国小组受奖合照。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次获各组前5名如下：国小组第一名港坪国小陈育贞同学、第二名博爱国小何品希同学、第三名垂杨国小蔡佳勋同学、第四名垂杨国小王裕杰同学、第五名兴嘉国小陈毅恩同学；国中组第一名北兴国中叶峻颖同学、第二名北兴国中刘家毓同学、第三名北园国中许晁榕同学、第四名北兴国中林品妤同学、第五名北兴国中黄苡瑄同学；高中组第一名嘉华中学叶祐睿同学、第二名嘉华中学林孟儿同学、第三名嘉华中学叶筠琦同学、第四名嘉华高级中学郑钰馨同学、第五名嘉义高中赖立庸同学；社会组第一名谢圣雅、第二名陈正光、第三名郭雅玲、第四名童皆瑜、第五名单赢田。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14076845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076845" style="width: 517px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076845-636563-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14076845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076845-636563-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="517" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076845" class="wp-caption-text">社会组第一名。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市政府环境保护局表示，办理环境知识竞赛以竞赛方式增进趣味性外，同时也让民众了解近年环境议题及时事，以培养环境素养并将知识落实于生活中，让环境教育从小扎根，朝向永续发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14076846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076846" style="width: 520px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076846-636564-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14076846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076846-636564-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="520" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076846" class="wp-caption-text">高中职组第一名。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14076847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076847" style="width: 529px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076847-636565-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14076847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076847-636565-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="529" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076847" class="wp-caption-text">国中组第一名。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14076848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076848" style="width: 507px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076848-636566-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14076848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076848-636566-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="507" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076848" class="wp-caption-text">国小组第一名。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
