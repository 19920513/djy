<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>越南文化节NRG盛大登场 美食歌舞丰富热闹</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077970-01-600x400.png" />
<h6>越南小姐选美佳丽和主持人合影。（大纪元图片）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年09月21日讯】（大纪元记者易永琦<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%91%E6%96%AF%E9%A1%BF.md#1">休斯顿</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E8%8A%82.md#1">越南文化节</a>上周六在NRG中心盛大举行。今年的活动以“越南的味道”为主题，展示越南三个主要地区——北部，中部和南部的文化。 庆祝活动以传统灯笼游行开场，邀请市长特纳参加，以庆祝中秋节。现场还有儿童传统服装比赛，越南青少年小姐文化节奖学金选美比赛，以“梦想”为主题的艺术比赛，以越南传统<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>为特色的饮食比赛，传统的越南国际象棋锦标赛和现场决赛，以及适合成人和儿童的各种传统游戏。 除了许多越南食品摊位外，还将有不少信息服务摊位，包括健康检查，公民教育，社区合作伙伴和越南人拥有的商家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14077974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077974-05.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077974-05-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077974" class="wp-caption-text">巨大的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E8%8A%82.md#1">越南文化节</a>”标志物。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14077972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077972-03.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077972-03-600x339.png" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077972" class="wp-caption-text">热闹的舞狮<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%A8%E6%BC%94.md#1">表演</a>让现场气氛大振。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14077973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077973" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077973-04.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077973-04-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077973" class="wp-caption-text">中央舞台上举行活跃的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%A8%E6%BC%94.md#1">表演</a>。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14077975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077975" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077975-06.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077975-06-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077975" class="wp-caption-text">现场的多彩灯笼庆祝中秋节的到来。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场游客熙熙攘攘，携家带口的人们穿梭于各个摊位，宽敞的场地分为几大区块，入口处是一个巨大的越南节的标志建筑，附近是一个中式碑坊，和用花搭建的照相背景，人们纷纷拿出手机自拍。中央大舞台上举行热闹的表演，舞狮，武术表演，舞蹈等等，还有越南小姐选美比赛，非常热闹。最受观众喜爱的当数越南<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>餐点，从越南三明治，到熏肉饭，越南汤面，卤肉，素食，各式冰品，美味的食物吸引人们大排长龙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14077971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077971-02.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077971-02-600x337.png" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077971" class="wp-caption-text">现场忙碌的食物贩售摊位。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14077978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077978" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077978-09.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077978-09-600x337.png" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077978" class="wp-caption-text">越南文化节上各类小吃。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14077977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077977-08.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077977-08-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077977" class="wp-caption-text">越南文化节上各类小吃。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>

<p>现场还有很多传统的儿童游戏，包括水中钓鸭子，投篮，扔飞镖等等。动中有静，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%91%E6%96%AF%E9%A1%BF.md#1">休斯顿</a>图书馆在现场设立摊位，举办儿童读书会，还有人聚精会神地下棋，似乎完全不受现场喧嚣热闹的气氛影响，自得其乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14077979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14077979" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077979-10.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14077979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077979-10-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14077979" class="wp-caption-text">现场游戏吸引了许多年轻人游玩。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>越南文化节是休斯顿地区的越南裔美国人发起的，旨在向休斯顿和周边地区推广越南文化。该活动由越南文化科学协会主办。</p>
