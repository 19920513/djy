<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澳洲必胜客遭网攻 百万客户信息或遭泄漏</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14077504-GettyImages-108787493-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为位于昆州TOWNSVILLE的一家必胜客分店。（Ian Hitchcock/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月20日讯】<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">（大纪元记者夏楚君澳洲悉尼报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E5%BF%85%E8%83%9C%E5%AE%A2.md#1">澳洲必胜客</a>（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">Pizza Hut Australia）</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">已经向其<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E6%88%B7.md#1">客户</a>发送了一封电子邮件，通知他们发生了一起网络安全事故。此前，数据泄漏监测网站称，必胜客的一百万客户或受到此事件的影响。</span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">周三（9月20日），必胜客在发给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E6%88%B7.md#1">客户</a>的邮件中表示，公司在</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">9</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月初发现了一起网络安全事故，“未经授权的第三方访问了公司的部分数据”。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">该公司补充说，它 “立即采取了行动”，来保护客户的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%A1%E6%81%AF.md#1">信息</a>。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">必胜客在邮件中表示，受影响的数据涉及澳洲客户数据库中的客户记录详情和在线订单交易。其中包括客户姓名、送货地址、电子邮件地址和联系电话等<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%A1%E6%81%AF.md#1">信息</a>。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">邮件写道，“根据我们的调查和针对该事件采取的措施，我们相信我们数据库中只有一小部分客户的个人信息受到了影响。就此事件，我们已通知这些客户以及澳洲信息专员办公室</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。”</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"> “<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">我们的日常运营不会受到影响，我们将继续为客户安全地处理网上或电话订单。”</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">9</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月初，</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">DataBreaches.net</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">网站曾报导说，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E5%BF%85%E8%83%9C%E5%AE%A2.md#1">澳洲必胜客</a>客户数据遭<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E5%AE%A2.md#1">黑客</a>攻击。该网站称，此次数据泄漏事件或导致一百多万顾客受到影响。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">D</span></span><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">a</span></span><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">taBreaches.net</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">网站称，骇客组织</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">ShinyHunters</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">在</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">1</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">2 </span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">个月前通过亚马逊网路服务，利用多个入口获得了访问权限，获取了</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">3,000</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万笔订单记录以及超过</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">100</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万澳洲必胜客客户的信息。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">此骇客组织向</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">DataBreaches.net</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">网站提供了包含</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">20万</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">条记录的样本文件，其中包含敏感的客户信息，例如姓名、电子邮件地址、密码、家庭住址、手机号码和信用卡号码。 幸运的是，信用卡号和密码已加密。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">ShinyHunters</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">要求支付</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">30</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万元来删除这些数据， 如果不支付赎金，数据很可能会被泄露或在暗网出售。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #2b2b2b;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">去年，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E5%AE%A2.md#1">黑客</a>针对澳洲发起了一系列网路攻击事件，电信巨头</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">Optus</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">和医保巨头</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">Medibank</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">的数百万客户数据遭泄漏，许多澳洲人仍处于戒备状态。</span></span></span></p>
