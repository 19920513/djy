<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>住房及燃油成本上涨 8月通胀升至5.2%</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/174263371-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在住房和燃油成本飙升下，澳洲8月份的年通胀率升至5.2% (Scott Barbour/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月27日讯】（<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">大纪元记者夏楚君澳洲悉尼报导）澳洲</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">8</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月份</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%9A%E8%83%80.md#1">通胀</a>指标出现四</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">个月来的首次上升，年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%9A%E8%83%80.md#1">通胀</a>率升至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">5.2%</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">澳洲统计局周三（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">9</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">27</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">日）发布的最新数据显示，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">住房</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%87%83%E6%B2%B9.md#1">燃油</a>成本的强劲增长导致了这一小幅上涨，高于上个月的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">4.9%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">“本月的年增长率为</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">5.2%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，高于</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">7</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月份的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">4.9%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。年通胀率仍低于</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2022 </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">12</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">8.4%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">的峰值”，统计局价格统计主管马夸特（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Michelle Marquardt</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）说。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">数据显示，</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">8</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月份年度通胀涨幅的最大推动因素是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%87%83%E6%B2%B9.md#1">燃油</a>（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">+9.1%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">住房</a>（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">+6.6%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）、交通（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">+7.4%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）、食品和非酒精饮料（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">+4.4%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）以及保险和金融服务（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">+8.8%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">马夸特表示，燃油价格的年度变化仍然不稳定，燃油价格较</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">12</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">个月前上涨</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">13.9%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，其中</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">8</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月上涨了</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">9.1%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">就住房价格而言，房价的年度涨幅为</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">6.6%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，低于</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">7</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月份的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">7.3%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。但由于房屋租赁市场的紧张，租金价格持续上涨，截至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">8</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">12</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">个月内，租金价格从</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">7</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">7.6%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">升至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">7.8%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">截至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">8</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月份的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">12</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">个月内，电力价格年涨</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">12.7%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，天然气价格年涨</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">12.9% </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">数据显示，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>和蔬菜价格下跌了</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">8.3%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，这对于面临生活成本压力的消费者来说是个好消息。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">不过，不同食品杂货的价格差异很大。</span></span></p>
<p>“<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">食品通胀继续缓解，但各类食品之间仍存在差异。在过去的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">12</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">个月中，面包和谷物产品以及乳制品的价格上涨了</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">10%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">以上，而<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>和蔬菜的价格则由于生长条件的改善而比</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">12</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">个月前下降了</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">8.3%<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">”，</span></span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">马夸特说。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">每月通胀结果将影响储银的利率决定，如果数据显示价格压力持续存在，储银则可能会进一步升息。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">储银董事会将于下周召开会议，做出在新</span></span><span style="color: #4d5156;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">储备银行行长布洛克（</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">Michele Bullock</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #4d5156;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">领导下的首次利率决定。 </span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">董事会先前表示，如果通胀比预期持续，可能需要进一步上调</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">4.1%</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">的官方现金利率。</span></span></p>
