<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“但愿血常有~千里共首捐”民众热烈支持捐血活动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070302-634456-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“但愿血常有~千里共首捐”公益捐血活动。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月09日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）“但愿血常有~千里共首捐”公益<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%90%E8%A1%80.md#1">捐血</a>活动，是由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>县螺晖青年协会、西螺狮子会、一贯道发一崇德云林道场祥合区，和云林县产业总工会等四个不同领域的团队联合主办。</p>
<p>活动于9日在西螺大桥咖啡广场举行，时间为上午9点至下午4点，活动期间设置两台<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%90%E8%A1%80.md#1">捐血</a>车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070308-634462.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070308-634462-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070308" class="wp-caption-text">9月9日在西螺大桥咖啡广场举行。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动营造捐血公益 嘉年华氛围，创造“三多”-礼品多、摊位多、表演 多，更设计“首捐舞台”让首捐者可以用大声公呐喊 “我捐血~我骄傲”,吸引年轻人用自己的姿态做有 意义的事情。不仅互动游戏，亦可拍照打卡上传，更 设计“首捐卡”，让捐血不仅是助人，更成为荣耀的 勋章。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070305" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070305-634459.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070305-634459-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070305" class="wp-caption-text">本活动营造捐血公益 嘉年华氛围,创造“三多”-礼品多、摊位多、表演多。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>镇长廖秋萍表示好多的公益团体响应，共同举办，创下了西螺地区捐血活动的规模和典范！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14070307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070307" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070307-634461.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14070307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070307-634461-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="798" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070307" class="wp-caption-text">西螺镇长廖秋萍受到民众欢迎。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14070301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14070301" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070301-634455.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14070301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14070301-634455-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14070301" class="wp-caption-text">好多的公益团体一同举办的捐血活动，创下了西螺地区捐血活动的规模和典范！（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动的捐血伴手礼有11种，包括螺晖青年马克杯、西螺农会米、瑞春酱油、狮子会雨伞、苹果、萝卜、烤地瓜、台塑生医抗菌喷雾、长庚生技能量水、口罩、面包和兑换券等，市值超过千元。此外，首捐者还可以获得兑换券、萝卜糕和福兴宫幸福摇摇乐吊饰等额外福利。</p>
