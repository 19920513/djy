<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>余风采堂春宴迎兔年 颁发奖学金</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13940961-DSC09055-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙余风采堂2月25日晚举办兔年春宴，向宗亲子女颁赠奖学金。（余风采堂提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年03月02日讯】（大纪元讯）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8B%B1%E5%B4%99%E4%BD%99%E9%A3%8E%E9%87%87%E5%A0%82.md#1">纽英崙余风采堂</a>于2月25日晚在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>帝苑大酒楼举办<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%AE%B4.md#1">春宴</a>，宗亲非常踊跃参与，筵开27桌。许多宗亲一家老少到场庆祝兔年到来，祈望疫情早日结束，兔年一切如意吉祥，身体健康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13941005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941005-IMG_4907.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941005-IMG_4907-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941005" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8B%B1%E5%B4%99%E4%BD%99%E9%A3%8E%E9%87%87%E5%A0%82.md#1">纽英崙余风采堂</a>2月25日晚举办<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%AE%B4.md#1">春宴</a>迎兔年，职员合影。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%99%E9%A3%8E%E9%87%87%E5%A0%82.md#1">余风采堂</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">晚宴期间，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%99%E9%A3%8E%E9%87%87%E5%A0%82.md#1">余风采堂</a>美东总长余文博与元老余超群颁发奖状给文博总长的友人Jack吴先生，以表彰他义务参与余风采堂设施维修的贡献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13941009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941009-IMG_4949.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941009-IMG_4949-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941009" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂2023年春宴颁奖，左起：Jack吴先生、余文博、余丽媖。（余风采堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">两位宗长还向现任主席余宝爱和余立昌，以及楼业主任余宗涛颁发奖状，表扬他们同心协力为公所管理维修事务，劳苦功高。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13941008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941008-IMG_4922.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941008-IMG_4922-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941008" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂2023年春宴颁奖，左起：余文博、余宝爱、余超群。（余风采堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13941006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941006-IMG_4912.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941006-IMG_4912-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941006" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂2023年春宴颁奖，左起：余超群、余立昌、余文博。（余风采堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13941007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941007-IMG_4919.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941007-IMG_4919-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941007" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂2023年春宴颁奖，左起：余超群、余宗涛、余文博。（余风采堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13940962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13940962" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13940962-YeeFung.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13940962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13940962-YeeFung-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13940962" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂2月25日晚举办兔年春宴，宗长和妇女组成员合影。（余风采堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">余风采堂一如往年向宗亲子弟颁发奖学金。今年获奖的有13人，包括：余慧欣、余绮雯、余家宝、余家玲、余万慈、麦诗美、余慧珊、余了了、余慧敏、余树豪、余万辉、余慧秀、余湘儿。同时，90岁以上的长者宗亲亦得到公所赠予的礼物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13940960" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13940960" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13940960-DSC09007.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13940960" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13940960-DSC09007-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13940960" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂兔年春宴，向长者宗亲赠送礼物。（余风采堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">接下来，余风采堂妇女组带来舞蹈表演，令晚宴增添不少光彩和掌声，不枉费她们几个月来的排练。晚宴中还有卡拉OK和抽奖活动，宗亲们热情参加。晚宴结束后，大家都带着笑容回家。</p>
