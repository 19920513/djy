<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>香港饶宗颐文化馆洋紫荆盛放 群花包围成打卡热点</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943886-Ben-16@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年03月06日讯】（大纪元记者刘沛林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E6%B8%AF.md#1">香港</a>综合报导）随着口罩令取消，香港步入3月花季，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%B6%E5%AE%97%E9%A2%90%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E9%A6%86.md#1">饶宗颐文化馆</a>的洋紫荆盛放，可供市民<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E8%8A%B1.md#1">赏花</a>之余亦是打卡好去处。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%B6%E5%AE%97%E9%A2%90%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E9%A6%86.md#1">饶宗颐文化馆</a>是有百年历史的三级历史建筑群，坐落在荔枝角山岗上，现址曾先后用作医院、检疫站、监狱、传染病医院和精神病疗养院，亦是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E6%B8%AF.md#1">香港</a>特区政府发展局“活化历史建筑伙伴计划”的首批活化项目，文化馆范围有过千棵植物，大部分更是原生于此，是城市中难得的绿洲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13943876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943876" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943876-Ben-3@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13943876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943876-Ben-3@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943876" class="wp-caption-text">饶宗颐文化馆的洋紫荆盛放，可供市民<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E8%8A%B1.md#1">赏花</a>之余亦是打卡好去处。（刘骏轩／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13943879" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943879-Ben-1@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13943879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943879-Ben-1@1200x1200-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943879" class="wp-caption-text">市民可乘撘港铁或巴士前往饶宗颐文化馆。（刘骏轩／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13943880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943880" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943880-Ben-6@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13943880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943880-Ben-6@1200x1200-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943880" class="wp-caption-text">假期去饶宗颐文化馆散步赏花，放松身心。（刘骏轩／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13943883" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943883" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943883-Ben-20@1200x1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13943883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943883-Ben-20@1200x1200-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943883" class="wp-caption-text">市民赏花之余更可观鸟。（刘骏轩／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近期馆内的郁金香已经逐渐凋谢，但有正值花期的洋荆接力盛开，成为市民另一打卡热点。白花羊蹄甲又名白花洋紫荆，盛开时带有淡淡幽香，紫色的洋紫荆，香港市民对一定不会陌生，因为它是香港的市花。除了赏花，市民不妨预留时间到饶宗颐文化馆内的展厅参观，见证建筑群在香港历史中变迁，或到馆内咖啡馆品尝下午茶。</p>
<p>饶宗颐文化馆位于九龙美孚青山道800号，该处交通便利，由港铁美孚站 B 出口，前行至中华电力变电站，登上行人天桥，左转至青山道休憩公园，于交通灯处横过青山道，即抵达饶宗颐文化馆。市民亦可乘撘巴士31B、32、35A、36B、286X，至“饶宗颐文化馆”。◇</p>
