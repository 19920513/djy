<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉市复国幼儿园专属好礼欢庆儿童节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962616-607491-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>	复国幼儿园合照。（复国幼儿园提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年03月31日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）一年一度的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E8%8A%82.md#1">儿童节</a>即将到来，在这个属于儿童的节日里，为了带给孩子们充满爱与欢乐的感受，嘉义市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8D%E5%9B%BD%E5%B9%BC%E5%84%BF%E5%9B%AD.md#1">复国幼儿园</a>于30日上午举办儿童欢乐音乐会，特地邀请海洋家族的哥哥姊姊们，莅园演出精彩的故事音乐剧场“小红帽与大野狼”，并且带领全园进行小朋友最爱的“欢乐唱跳秀”，与孩子们提前一起欢庆儿童节！</p>
<p>教育处处长林立生表示，复国教学团队精心设计亲子线上互动游戏“老房子大冒险”送给孩子，这是一款以幼儿园周边木造老房子为主题的冒险游戏，亲子可以透过游戏探索嘉义市木造老房子，了解它们的历史和文化，是独树一帜、充满趣味而且富有意义的礼物，复国精心准备的礼物与正在进行的教学课程以及校园和社区特色环环相扣，可见<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8D%E5%9B%BD%E5%B9%BC%E5%84%BF%E5%9B%AD.md#1">复国幼儿园</a>用心之处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13962617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13962617" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962617-607492.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13962617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962617-607492-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13962617" class="wp-caption-text">复国教学团队精心设计亲子线上互动游戏“老房子大冒险”。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13962618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13962618" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962618-607493.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13962618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962618-607493-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13962618" class="wp-caption-text">大合照。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>复国幼儿园园长陈玉如表示，在这特别的日子里，复国家族安排了海洋家族欢乐音乐会、老房子大冒险亲子线上互动游戏及家长会特制独一无二的“复国校景拼图”等<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E8%8A%82.md#1">儿童节</a>限定的专属好礼来相送。除此之外，教学团队也规划一系列的趣味体能循环游戏，让孩子们在榕树爷爷大草原尽情地奔跑玩耍，享受运动及游戏的乐趣；也要感谢嘉邑城隍庙赠送“卡通儿童洁牙组”给全园的小宝贝，祝福孩子们健康快乐的成长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13962619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13962619" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962619-607494.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13962619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962619-607494-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13962619" class="wp-caption-text">复国幼儿园以故事音乐剧场欢度儿童节。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>儿童欢乐音乐会让孩子能透过充满童趣且耳熟能详的故事，感受莫札特的音乐飨宴，将音乐更贴近的带入孩子的体验当中。随着高潮迭起的剧情，孩子们陶醉其中，与表演者共同完成一个深刻难忘的演出，现场气氛欢乐热闹，相信这也成为孩子儿童节的一大惊喜。同时也颁发模范儿童奖牌，一起见证和分享得奖的荣耀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13962620" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13962620" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962620-607495.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13962620" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962620-607495-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13962620" class="wp-caption-text">精彩儿童周活动。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>儿童节是专属孩子们的节日，学校的用心让孩子们沈浸在爱的氛围里，希望在这特别的时刻，能让每一位小宝贝脸上充满灿烂笑容，感受满溢的欢乐与幸福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13962621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13962621" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962621-607496.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13962621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962621-607496-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13962621" class="wp-caption-text">欢庆儿童节。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13962622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13962622" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962622-607497.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13962622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962622-607497-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13962622" class="wp-caption-text">合照。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13962623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13962623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962623-607498.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13962623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13962623-607498-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13962623" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友们开心过儿童周。（复国幼儿园提供）</figcaption></figure>
