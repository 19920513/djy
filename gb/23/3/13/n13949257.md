<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>真人《小美人鱼》新预告片 水下王国及女巫世界</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931929-20230217-mark-Disney00-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>真人版《小美人鱼》剧照。（迪士尼提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年03月13日讯】（大纪元记者叶子杰报导）周日奥斯卡颁奖典礼上，海莉·贝莉发布了真人版《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%BE%8E%E4%BA%BA%E9%B1%BC.md#1">小美人鱼</a>》正式预告片。片中小美人鱼唱出了唯美高音，扣人心弦，而在水下王国，小美人鱼为了人类王子不顾父王的劝言，到女巫世界用声音换取了双脚。</p>
<p>自从迪士尼于上个月释出真人版《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%BE%8E%E4%BA%BA%E9%B1%BC.md#1">小美人鱼</a>》（The Little Mermaid）预告影片后，获得影迷热情响应。周日晚上，贝利和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%9B%E8%8E%89%E8%8E%8E%EF%BD%A5%E9%BA%A6%E5%8D%A1%E9%94%A1.md#1">玛莉莎?麦卡锡</a>走上奥斯卡颁奖典礼舞台，为即将于5月上映的真人版《小美人鱼》发布新的正式预告片。</p>
<p>真人版《小美人鱼》正式预告片：<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dMLXeGdEPzk" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">请点此观看影片</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_13949305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13949305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949305-20230313-mark-disney27.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13949305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949305-20230313-mark-disney27-450x189.jpg" alt="真人版《小美人鱼》" width="450" b="189" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13949305" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照，小美人鱼“爱丽儿”（海莉·贝莉 饰）。（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13949304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13949304" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949304-20230313-mark-disney28.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13949304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949304-20230313-mark-disney28-450x189.jpg" alt="真人版《小美人鱼》" width="450" b="189" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13949304" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照，海洋女巫“乌苏拉”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%9B%E8%8E%89%E8%8E%8E%EF%BD%A5%E9%BA%A6%E5%8D%A1%E9%94%A1.md#1">玛莉莎?麦卡锡</a> 饰）。（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《小美人鱼》第一部正式预告片让观众更了解小美人鱼“爱丽儿”的海底世界。从人类王子“亚力克”（乔纳?霍尔-金 饰）遭遇船难开场，爱丽儿把他救上岸，对英俊潇洒的亚力克王子一见倾心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13949303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13949303" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949303-20230313-mark-disney29.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13949303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949303-20230313-mark-disney29-450x189.jpg" alt="真人版《小美人鱼》" width="450" b="189" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13949303" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照，小美人鱼“爱丽儿”（海莉·贝莉 饰）、人类王子“亚力克”（乔纳?霍尔-金 饰）。（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>爱丽儿的父王特里顿国王（哈维?尔巴登 饰）严厉告诫她，禁止她再度接近人类，但爱丽儿很想到人类世界去找亚力克。为此海洋女巫“乌苏拉”（麦卡锡 饰）发出了魔性笑声，诱使天真的爱丽儿答应暗中交易，用自己的声音去交换双腿，变成人类。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13949286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13949286" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949286-20230313-mark-disney22.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13949286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949286-20230313-mark-disney22-450x165.jpg" alt="真人版《小美人鱼》" width="450" b="165" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13949286" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照，女巫世界。（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13949289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13949289" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949289-20230313-mark-disney25.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13949289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949289-20230313-mark-disney25-450x185.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="185" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13949289" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照，小美人鱼“爱丽儿”（海莉·贝莉 饰）被女巫诱惑。（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而螃蟹大臣暨音乐家“赛巴斯汀”、可爱跟班“小比目鱼”和热心聒噪聒噪的海鸥“史卡托”也在片中可爱登场，再度勾起影迷们的青春回忆。最后小美人鱼以美妙歌喉再度引吭高歌经典名曲《Part of Your World》，澎湃汹涌的升高音唱功十足震撼人心，仿佛述说着她美丽与坎坷的故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13949285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13949285" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949285-20230313-mark-disney21.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13949285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949285-20230313-mark-disney21-450x164.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="164" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13949285" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照，特里顿国王（哈维?尔巴登 饰）。（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13949287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13949287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949287-20230313-mark-disney23.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13949287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949287-20230313-mark-disney23-450x162.jpg" alt="真人版《小美人鱼》" width="450" b="162" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13949287" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照，水下王国。（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>声音方面，赛巴斯汀、小比目鱼和史卡托则分别由热门音乐剧《汉密尔顿》东尼奖得主唱将男星戴维德?迪格斯、《不存在的房间》童星雅各?特伦布雷与《疯狂亚洲富豪》女星奥卡菲娜献声配音、大展歌喉与喜感，坚强卡司阵容令人期待。</p>
<p>《小美人鱼》经典动画原版创下全球超过两亿美元票房佳绩，荣获奥斯卡最佳原创配乐与歌曲。而改编的全新真人版除了美不胜收的水底特效，还有群鱼飞舞的获奖名曲《Under the Sea》华丽场面、烟火与爆破的绚烂画面，以及栩栩如生的真实动物角色和精致合成的小美人鱼电脑生成图像（CGI）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13949302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13949302" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949302-20230313-mark-disney30.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13949302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949302-20230313-mark-disney30-450x189.jpg" alt="真人版《小美人鱼》" width="450" b="189" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13949302" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照，小美人鱼“爱丽儿”（海莉·贝莉 饰）。（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13949288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13949288" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949288-20230313-mark-disney24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13949288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949288-20230313-mark-disney24-450x186.jpg" alt="真人版《小美人鱼》" width="450" b="186" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13949288" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照，王子的城堡。（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>歌曲方面，动画原版作曲家亚伦孟肯与《魔法满屋》乐坛才子林·曼努尔·米兰达首度搭档创作词曲，为原本没有献唱的角色量身订做和经典曲目一样动听的新歌；《芝加哥》金奖导演劳勃·马歇尔再度施展音乐剧背景的编舞长才，设计出目不暇给的众多歌舞桥段，肯定令人大呼过瘾。</p>
<p>至于今天的奥斯卡颁奖典礼上，贝利已是连二任获邀担任典礼的颁奖人，她与麦卡锡一同在典礼上首度公开真人版《小美人鱼》正式预告片，也为典礼直播再掀起一波高潮。</p>
<p>真人版《小美人鱼》将于5月26日在美国上映，而台湾将抢先在2023年5月25日上映。</p>
