<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宫庙香油钱也敢偷 窃贼集团遭大溪警逮送办</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13955606-605719-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大溪区一间庙宇香油钱遭窃。（桃园市大溪警分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年03月22日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）大溪区一间庙宇<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E6%B2%B9%E9%92%B1.md#1">香油钱</a>遭窃，大溪分局获报后成立专案小组，案经大溪分局侦查队及南雅派出所积极侦办，掌握相关情资锁定<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8A%AF%E5%AB%8C.md#1">犯嫌</a>，陆续将黄姓等6名犯嫌查缉到案，全案讯问后移送桃园地方检察署侦办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13955607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13955607" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13955607-605720.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13955607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13955607-605720-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13955607" class="wp-caption-text">窃盗集团横行南桃园地区，专挑宫庙<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E6%B2%B9%E9%92%B1.md#1">香油钱</a>下手。（桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%BA%AA%E8%AD%A6.md#1">大溪警</a>分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警方表示，以黄姓男子（27岁）为首的窃盗集团，横行南桃园地区，专挑宫庙香油钱下手，行径嚣张，警方获报后立即成立专案小组，经清查及掌握相关情资锁定对象，持桃园地方检察署核发搜索票及拘票，并向保安警察大队申请支援警力，兵分多路将该集团成员缉捕到案，并起获作案工具、赃款相关证物及毒品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13955608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13955608" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13955608-605721.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13955608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13955608-605721-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13955608" class="wp-caption-text">大溪区一间庙宇香油钱遭窃。（桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%BA%AA%E8%AD%A6.md#1">大溪警</a>分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大溪警分局表示，获报后立即组成专案小组积极侦办，多方清查掌握情资，锁定窃嫌行踪并顺利逮捕窃嫌并申请检方羁押，警方对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%BB%E5%AE%89.md#1">治安</a>工作绝对不遗余力，努力净化辖内治安。◇</p>
