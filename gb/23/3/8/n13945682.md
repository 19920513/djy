<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义市营养午餐创意摆盘送上独“嘉”祝福</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945683-603391-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>	垂杨国小为中华队加油。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年03月08日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）众志成城，世界棒球经典赛8日晚点燃战火，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E9%98%9F.md#1">中华队</a>在A组的预赛首战将对上巴拿马，不少球迷已磨拳擦掌准备到场为球员呐喊助威，棒球原乡嘉义市热情不落人后，别出心裁号召全市国中小学及幼儿园发挥巧思，将营养午餐摆盘成“中华队加油”字样为中华队集气，在让学童吃得营养均衡又开心的同时，更为中华队送上“独嘉”的祝福。</p>
<p>嘉义市政府教育处长林立生表示，棒球原乡嘉义市重视运动发展与培育，例如今年经典赛的练习赛在嘉义市开打，中职练习赛、二军赛嘉义也在嘉义市；不仅以最好的场地提供球员集训、练习的空间，黄敏惠市长更于赛前亲自为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E9%98%9F.md#1">中华队</a>送上象征“强棒出击”的木雕，祝福中华队从嘉义市出发，一路打出好成绩，“进军日本东京巨蛋，决战美国迈阿密！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945684" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945684-603393.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945684-603393-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945684" class="wp-caption-text">民生国中的中华队加油营养午餐。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13945685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945685" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945685-603394.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945685" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945685-603394-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945685" class="wp-caption-text">创意午餐。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天在经典赛开打前夕，嘉义市各国小中学将营养午餐<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9B%E6%84%8F%E6%91%86%E7%9B%98.md#1">创意摆盘</a>出“中华队加油”字样，全市国中小棒球队也响应号召拍照打卡，不只希望为中华队集气送上祝福，也期待营养美味的午餐凝聚孩子们对运动赛事关注，让棒球运动和代表嘉义市的KANO坚毅精神继续扎根。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945686" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945686-603395.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945686" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945686-603395-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="1066" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945686" class="wp-caption-text">林森国小以鸡蛋祝福中华队完封对手。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市民生国中学务主任童俊凯表示，迎接经典赛到来，民生国中体育班近日以为中华队加油的标语为教室布置换新装，今日午餐厨妈们更发挥创意以海苔点缀米饭排出“中华队加油”字样，不仅在午餐时间带给学生们惊喜，更让学生们为中华队加油的心沸腾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945687-603396.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945687-603396-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945687" class="wp-caption-text">创意午餐。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今日响应以营养午餐为中华队送上祝福的活动回响热烈，垂杨国小以球棒和棒球排列出大大的“CT加油”字样，林森国小更创意用鸡蛋排出比分，祝福中华队完封对手，就连复国幼儿园的小小朋友也开心享用以小蕃茄、葡萄排列成的中华队加油字样，送上来自小小球迷们的祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945688" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945688-603397.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945688" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945688-603397-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945688" class="wp-caption-text">复国幼儿园以营养午餐为中华队加油。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13945689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945689" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945689-603398.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945689" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945689-603398-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945689" class="wp-caption-text">民生国中的创意布置教室为中华队加油。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
