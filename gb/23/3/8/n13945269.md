<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“华埠地标”波城安良工商会120周年喜迎春</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945272-IMG_1242-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿华埠的安良工商会大楼有着“Welcome to Chinatown”标志，被称为华埠地标。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年03月08日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）有巨大的“Welcome to Chinatown”红字和显眼的中国古典风格亭子，波士顿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E8%89%AF%E5%B7%A5%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">安良工商会</a>大楼可谓是“唐人街最亮眼的地标”，3月5日晚<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%AE%B4.md#1">春宴</a>的主持人梅宇国说。</p>
<p>今年迎来120周年的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E8%89%AF%E5%B7%A5%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">安良工商会</a>周日在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>帝苑酒楼举办晚宴，与政要嘉宾及<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%A8%E5%9B%A2.md#1">侨团</a>代表总共近400人齐贺兔年新春。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945259-DSC05124.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945259-DSC05124-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945259" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿安良工商会职员及嘉宾们向全体来宾敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全美安良工商会1893年创建于纽约。波士顿分会亦在10年后的1903年创办，至今是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>最古老和有影响力的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%A8%E5%9B%A2.md#1">侨团</a>之一。</p>
<p>由于疫情影响，波城安良工商会上一次<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%AE%B4.md#1">春宴</a>已是2020年2月了。如今这个传统得以恢复，迎来本地侨团，波士顿、纽约、罗德岛和芝加哥的安良会员，以及多位麻州政要，总共近40桌来宾热闹地庆祝黄历兔年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945265" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945265-DSC04951.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945265-DSC04951-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945265" class="wp-caption-text">安良醒狮团贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13945264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945264" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945264-DSC04940.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945264-DSC04940-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945264" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）、市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）等嘉宾给醒狮送上红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>“高朋满座”贺兔年</strong></h3>
<p>周日晚的宴会以舞狮贺岁拉开序幕。安良醒狮团的3头彩狮在锣鼓声中翻腾进场，送财献瑞。到场赴宴的波士顿市议员墨菲（Erin Murphy）亲自“披挂上阵”，摇着醒狮穿过席间，向宾客们送上祝福。在表演过后，满头大汗的她直呼很累也很有趣。</p>
<p>接下来，波士顿安良工商会会长陈伟民致词欢迎来宾。“兔年我想祝大家身体健康，心想事成，生意兴隆。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945263" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945263-DSC05020.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945263-DSC05020-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945263" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议会向波士顿安良工商会赠予贺状。左起：安良主席伍伟业、陈伟民、市议员墨菲（Erin Murphy）、费连（Ed Flynn）。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）到场参与了晚宴。她首先用中文祝福大家“新年快乐”，然后感谢安良工商会为繁荣社区贡献诸多努力。</p>
<p>“我们会一直支持你们，与你们合作，庆祝你们的成绩。”吴弭说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945260" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945260-DSC05049.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945260-DSC05049-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945260" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾莅临波士顿安良工商会的春宴。左起：中华公所主席雷国辉、安良会长伍伟业、市长办公室华埠联络员黄楚岚、至孝笃亲公所主席陈文珊、安良会长陈伟民、市议员墨菲（Erin Murphy）、市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）、麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元、洪门总理余丽媖、安良总理陈仕维。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两位主持人梅宇国、阮鸿灿介绍说，吴弭在选举期间曾两次拜访华埠的安良大楼，得到安良工商会、洪门、华埠商会、中华公所等各团体的支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945262" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945262-DSC05026.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945262" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945262-DSC05026-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945262" class="wp-caption-text">安良工商会3月5日晚春宴的司仪梅宇国和阮鸿灿。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚赴宴的嘉宾还包括波士顿市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）、麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元、侨教中心主任潘昭荣、纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉等等。</p>
<p>雷国辉说，今晚有这么多远道而来的朋友，“高朋满座，济济一堂”，让人非常高兴。他感谢安良工商会与中华公所合作举办多项繁荣华埠的活动，例如乒乓球比赛、象棋比赛、排球比赛等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945267" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945267-DSC04858.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945267" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945267-DSC04858-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945267" class="wp-caption-text">全美安良工商会总理陈仕维夫妇和邓遐勋夫妇参加3月5日晚的春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13945257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945257-DSC05082.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945257-DSC05082-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945257" class="wp-caption-text">全美安良总理陈仕维和邓遐勋，波士顿会长陈伟民和伍伟业向宾客们敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宴席间，全美安良工商会总理陈仕维和邓遐勋，以及波士顿安良工商会会长陈伟民和伍伟业来到每一桌向宾客们敬酒。扮演“财神”的阮鸿灿穿上了新的蓝色“官袍”，把红包发给每桌来宾，赢得大家的欢呼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13945261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945261-DSC05056.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945261-DSC05056-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945261" class="wp-caption-text">“财神”向来宾们送福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13945277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13945277" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945277-DSC05029.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13945277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13945277-DSC05029-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13945277" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿安良工商会3月5日春宴高朋满座。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在聚会欢庆的同时，安良工商会也依照传统向公益机构捐款。今天获捐的团体是广教中文学校、王氏YMCA、中华耆英会、侨立中文学校和中华颐养院。◇</p>
