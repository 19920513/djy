<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>世界各地发酵食物荟萃 一文了解22种（下）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941661-shutterstock_1575860851-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>世界各地皆有承传已久的发酵工艺，经过发酵的食物变得别具风味或营养更丰富。许多发酵食物已贯穿大部分人的一日三餐，比如酸奶、面包、酸菜、酱料、起司和酒类。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年03月02日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E9%85%B5.md#1">发酵</a>是一种古老的食品工艺，食物经过发酵味道会改变，甚至产生新的营养物质。数千年来发酵食品在全球历经演变，形成的种类不胜枚举。以下介绍别具一格的22种，其中不乏承传千年的名产。【<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/3/2/n13941631.md#1">点这里阅读：世界各地发酵食物荟萃 一文了解22种（上）</a>】</p>
<h2>11. 菲律宾米<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E7%B3%95.md#1">发糕</a></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941647-shutterstock_116640229.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941647-shutterstock_116640229-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941647" class="wp-caption-text">菲律宾流行的米<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E7%B3%95.md#1">发糕</a>有多种颜色和风味。</figcaption></figure>
<p>菲律宾流行的米发糕（Puto）有多种口味和颜色，通常作为早餐，或配咖啡、热巧克力享用，也可佐咸味菜肴吃。Puto的传统主料是米粉，也有不使用米粉的作法，比如用木薯制作的Puto Lanson。</p>
<h2>12. 印度甜咸糕</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941651-shutterstock_385558012.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941651-shutterstock_385558012-600x439.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="439" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941651" class="wp-caption-text">印度甜咸糕。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>印度甜咸糕（Dhokla）是先用大米和豆类磨成浆混合姜黄粉、芝麻等调料进行<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E9%85%B5.md#1">发酵</a>，之后上锅蒸熟。食用时搭配绿辣椒、香菜等，口感松软，味道甜咸。此外，也有仅用鹰嘴豆粉制作的版本。</p>
<h2>13. 西非玉米糕</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941655" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941655" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941655-shutterstock_1440190862.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941655" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941655-shutterstock_1440190862-600x613.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="613" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941655" class="wp-caption-text">西非的传统玉米糕是当地人的主食，搭配辣酱和菜肴一起吃。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>西非加纳、牙买加等地区的传统主食是用玉米粉发酵制成的玉米糕（Kenkey）。它与墨西哥的玉米蒸糕（Tamale）类似，外层用玉米叶或蕉叶包裹，特别之处是没有馅料且作成圆柱形。经过发酵，玉米糕的味道尝起来微酸，吃的时候通常裹上当地的特制辣酱。</p>
<h2>14. 酸芋泥</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941650" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941650-shutterstock_315782543.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941650-shutterstock_315782543-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941650" class="wp-caption-text">波利尼西亚群岛和夏威夷周边地区的人们以发酵的芋泥（图中小碗）为主食。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在波利尼西亚群岛和夏威夷等地，人们以发酵酸芋泥（Poi）为主食。这种芋泥呈糊状，带有一些芋头本身的紫色，口感细腻。</p>
<h2>15. 格瓦斯</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941652-shutterstock_641321392.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941652-shutterstock_641321392-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941652" class="wp-caption-text">俄罗斯及周边地区时兴的发酵饮品格瓦斯。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>俄罗斯及周边地区时兴的发酵饮品格瓦斯（Kvass），是用黑麦面包、酵母、糖、葡萄干或蜂蜜混合发酵制成。由于益生菌作用，饮品中带有微微气泡，味道类似淡啤酒。也因其发酵时间短，所以被视为无酒精饮品；如果延长发酵时间，它的酒精浓度会增加，但也只有约1.5%。</p>
<h2>16. 开菲尔</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941680" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941680-shutterstock_735031507.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941680-shutterstock_735031507-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941680" class="wp-caption-text">开菲尔的发酵原理与一般<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%B8%E5%A5%B6.md#1">酸奶</a>类似，但营养价值不同，质地也更稀薄。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>发酵乳品开菲尔（Kefir）源自高加索地区。现代技术制作的开菲尔（Kefir）是将开菲尔菌加入牛奶中发酵而成，质地比普通<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%B8%E5%A5%B6.md#1">酸奶</a>稀薄。因其具有特殊营养价值而受到世界各地区欢迎。</p>
<h2>17. 杜格酸奶</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941648" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941648-shutterstock_1991913959.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941648-shutterstock_1991913959-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941648" class="wp-caption-text">伊朗一带的传统饮品杜格酸奶。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>杜格酸奶（Doogh）长久以来都是伊朗一带的传统饮品。它用酸奶加盐和气泡水制成，味道既咸又酸，在炎热的天气和饭后享用非常爽口。</p>
<h2>18. 墨西哥玉米浆</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941653-shutterstock_1393453643.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941653-shutterstock_1393453643-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941653" class="wp-caption-text">图为巧克力味的墨西哥玉米浆。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>玉米是墨西哥的重要农作物，当地人用玉米制作各种美食，包括热玉米浆（Atole）。这款饮品由棕糖、水、肉桂和用碱制过的玉米粉煮成，有时会加入水果或巧克力增加风味，作为早餐热饮或搭玉米粉蒸糕一起享用。</p>
<h2>19. 越南酸肉</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941646-shutterstock_729491764.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941646-shutterstock_729491764-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941646" class="wp-caption-text">越南酸肉。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>酸肉（Nem Chua）是越南特有的小吃，由香蕉叶包裹生的碎猪肉和猪皮，再加以特制调料发酵而成，口感Q弹，味道酸爽。直接吃、油炸后蘸辣酱吃都很有风味。</p>
<h2>20. 泰国酸肉</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941654-shutterstock_1379672363.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941654-shutterstock_1379672363-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941654" class="wp-caption-text">泰国北部的酸肉肠搭配鸡蛋料理。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>泰国也有用猪肉和猪皮混合发酵而成的特色酸肉，生吃或烹饪成其它菜肴皆可。泰国各地区有不同风味的酸肉，如泰国北部的烤酸肉（Chin Som Mo）是用香蕉叶包裹酸肉在火上烤熟了吃。</p>
<h2>21. 海豹腌海雀</h2>
<p>格陵兰岛（Greenland）的因纽特人有食用海豹腌海雀（Kiviak）的传统。他们将现捕的海雀（auks）整只放入掏空内脏的海豹尸体中，接着将塞满海雀的海豹尸体缝合并放在冻土层中发酵。发酵时间一到，人们便打开海豹尸体取出海雀，剥掉鸟羽毛后生食肉和内脏。</p>
<h2>22. 瑞典酸鲱鱼</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13941645" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13941645" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941645-shutterstock_1603518619.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13941645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13941645-shutterstock_1603518619-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13941645" class="wp-caption-text">酸鲱鱼罐头。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>酸鲱鱼（Surstr?mming）以罐头形式出售。在春末，瑞典人将在波罗的海捕获的鲱鱼泡在盐水中发酵制成酸鲱鱼。发酵好的鲱鱼气味相当浓烈，因此多半在水中开启罐头以减淡气味。酸鲱鱼在瑞典北部最为盛行。吃的时候搭配面包、饼干、洋葱、酸奶油和绿香葱等。◇</p>
