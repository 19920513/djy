<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一房二用 10种隐形床打造复合空间</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13947013-shutterstock_1110933545-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在你的家中设置隐形床，长沙发是经典选项之一。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年03月10日讯】你是否喜欢家里有一处偶尔可以打盹休憩或看书追剧慵懒度过一天的空间？或是偶尔亲友来访过夜，有一个灵活使用的下榻空间？<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9A%90%E5%BD%A2%E5%BA%8A.md#1">隐形床</a>正是个好点子，下次居家装修时，不妨发挥设计巧思，布置一个可以使用隐形床的空间。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>什么是“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9A%90%E5%BD%A2%E5%BA%8A.md#1">隐形床</a>”</strong></span></h2>
<p>简单来说，隐形床就是想用时可作为床，不用时便于“隐藏”的家具。有些家庭需要一房二用，或人数多时需要增加睡铺，或是作为乐享慢活时光的空间；隐形床恰好可以发挥作用，可以在工作室、餐厅、卧房、书房、客厅或客卧导入这样的设计。</p>
<p>然而隐形床未必都能节省空间，有些款式每天收合的话较不实用，这要看你的需求来选择。以下几种常见的设计，各有不同特色。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E5%8F%91%E5%BA%8A.md#1">沙发床</a></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13946781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946781" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946781-shutterstock_2213568147.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946781-shutterstock_2213568147-600x400.jpg" alt="Elegant Living Room Furnished In Modern Apartment With A Sofa" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946781" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E5%8F%91%E5%BA%8A.md#1">沙发床</a>款式很多样，最好挑选高品质的泡棉比较耐用。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>可折叠式沙发床拉开之后就是一张床，不用时可收合起来；这种款式能灵活变换空间，不仅适合摆放在室内，换上防水防尘沙发布也可以在户外使用。若想将庭院变成临时卧室，再加个活动顶篷就能轻松实现了。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>客床放在餐厅区</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13946783" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946783" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946783-shutterstock_2228912697.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946783" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946783-shutterstock_2228912697-600x360.jpg" alt="Modern Dining And Kitchen Interior With Dining Table And Chairs" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946783" class="wp-caption-text">折叠式沙发椅可当作餐椅使用。示意图。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>想像一下，家里的餐桌椅和外面的餐厅一样是复合式搭配，一排是单椅，另一排是可折叠沙发，很有新鲜感。餐厅区放置沙发床，平时作为餐厅沙发椅使用，当需要时拉出来就是客人留宿的床<b>。</b></p>
<h2><strong style="color: #008000;">把床嵌入壁上</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13946785" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946785" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946785-shutterstock_1629447364.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946785-shutterstock_1629447364-600x400.jpg" alt="Modern Zen Peaceful Bedroom Japan Style Bedroom With Shelf Wall" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946785" class="wp-caption-text">在厚墙、壁柜或衣柜里打造简便床铺，不占空间。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你的墙壁够深足以容纳一个睡垫的话，作为临时睡铺不会占用太多空间，下方还可做储物柜收纳被褥很方便。这类设计也可以套用在衣柜间，另外加装漂亮的布帘以保护隐私。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B5%E5%A6%83%E6%A4%85.md#1">贵妃椅</a>变身床铺</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13946790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946790" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946790-DSCN1860.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946790-DSCN1860-600x450.jpg" alt="贵妃椅,沙发" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946790" class="wp-caption-text">家里摆放一张<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B5%E5%A6%83%E6%A4%85.md#1">贵妃椅</a>，更加突显典雅质感。（林海柔/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>双人贵妃椅也是沙发的一种，给人感觉舒适典雅。最常见的尺寸长约170～185cm，一侧扶手以较柔和的角度倾斜是空间的一大亮点，搭配一只装钉橡胶轮的复古木箱，好看又实用。</p>
<p>当你需要多张床时，从木箱里拿出枕头被单来铺上，再将木箱紧靠贵妃椅尾端，固定好轮子，简单变身为舒适单人床。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>拉出式沙发</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13946798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946798-shutterstock_2103223820.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946798-shutterstock_2103223820-600x400.jpg" alt="Woman Making Sleeping Place For Guest In Living Room Convertible" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946798" class="wp-caption-text">比起L型沙发，拖拉式沙发床可以收合较不占空间。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>长型或L型沙发很适合当做隐形床，如果你想将客厅留出更多空间，拉出式沙发也是经典的隐形床。关键是挑选外观好看的，还要沙发结构坚固耐用，坐垫品质好一些的才好，确保躺在床垫上舒适不塌陷。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>实用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A6%BB%E6%A6%BB%E7%B1%B3.md#1">榻榻米</a></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13946802" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946802" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946802-shutterstock_1637591188.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946802-shutterstock_1637591188-600x373.jpg" alt="Living Room Wood Japanese Interior Design 3d Rendering" width="600" b="373" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946802" class="wp-caption-text">整个地板铺上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A6%BB%E6%A6%BB%E7%B1%B3.md#1">榻榻米</a>，随地坐卧都很舒适。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在地板或架高平台上安置榻榻米，给人很放松的感觉，而且空间感更大；铺在客厅可当做地板踩踏，尤其适合小户型，随地而坐或躺卧，方便又舒适。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>有日光的阅读角落</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13946804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946804" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946804-shutterstock_363995738.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946804-shutterstock_363995738-600x400.jpg" alt="Image Of Relax Area With Large Sofa And Window" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946804" class="wp-caption-text">窗台边光线明亮，打造阅读的角落好放松。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13947025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13947025" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13947025-shutterstock_278721365-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13947025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13947025-shutterstock_278721365-1-600x400.jpg" alt="Rattan Sofa In Terrace At Night Time" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13947025" class="wp-caption-text">在阳台摆张仿中式的坐卧沙发床，也是很棒的休憩区。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在客厅找一处光线明亮的僻静区域，打造一个适合午睡或阅读的角落，多放几个蓬松的靠枕，柔软的毯子，让人可以放松舒适地靠卧着，这个有趣的角落还能提供给客人当睡铺。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>在屋里挂一张吊床</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13946807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946807-shutterstock_1984409936.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946807-shutterstock_1984409936-600x400.jpg" alt="View From Above Of Cozy Light Bedroom In Bohemian Style" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946807" class="wp-caption-text">利用夹层角落放张摇摆床，增添度假气息。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>现代感的起居室或客厅吊挂着一张摇摆床，更能增添休闲氛围。如果空间不够大的话，也能将这浪漫的隐形床摆放在夹层角落，以温馨的布置将客卧升等。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>花园金属索吊床</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13946810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946810" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946810-shutterstock_1024449781.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946810-shutterstock_1024449781-600x400.jpg" alt="Swinging Outdoor Bed" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946810" class="wp-caption-text">花园中的大吊床也很适合客人短暂休憩。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>隔着大片落地窗玻璃，花园、花房看起来和室内融为一体，庭院也是增加休闲活动或睡眠空间的好方法——摆放可折叠的三人藤椅，休闲木摇椅，或是吊挂金属绳索的吊床，享受清闲的午后时光。</p>
<p>而市面上还有一种电动升降床，外型类似金属索吊床，不用时只需按下电动开关就能将床收高在天花板，很适合小空间的时尚设计，增加活动空间。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>壁床</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13946817" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13946817" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946817-shutterstock_1991034758.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13946817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13946817-shutterstock_1991034758-600x400.jpg" alt="Crescent Beach Fl Usa - June 14 2021 Living Room" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13946817" class="wp-caption-text">壁床拉合时很花费力气，如须经常使用床铺的话，建议不要选用。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>墨菲床（Murphy Bed）也有称壁床，从外观看来，你以为的壁柜或书柜，其实往下一拉就是一张床，大多装置在需要一房二用的空间，按照空间需求订制尺寸、样式，款式繁多——还可隐藏在移动式墙壁后，电视机后面，或是折叠收合在矮柜中。</p>
<p>需注意的是，壁床内部的弹簧、五金配件，使用过于频繁的话容易减损寿命，需定期检查；这种隐形床使用时较费力，如果需要频繁收合的话，就要好好评估一下了。</p>
