<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>麻州Holyoke第70届圣派翠克节游行 万众欢庆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954727-DSC_0141-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>3月19日麻州霍利约克举办圣派翠克节游行，花车经过，观众欢呼。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年03月21日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨麻州霍利约克市报导）在3月19日的刺骨寒风下，麻州西部的霍利约克市（Holyoke）有成千上万的人穿着厚外套，甚至裹着毛毯坐在街头。他们等待着<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E6%B4%BE%E7%BF%A0%E5%85%8B%E8%8A%82.md#1">圣派翠克节</a>（St. Patrick’s Day）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>的开始。</p>
<p>当天一早，来自不同地区的约10万民众涌入人口只有4万的小城霍利约克，参加今年盛大的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E6%B4%BE%E7%BF%A0%E5%85%8B%E8%8A%82.md#1">圣派翠克节</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>。当地的这个游行，今年迎来70周年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13954726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954726" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954726-DSC_0138.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954726-DSC_0138-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954726" class="wp-caption-text">众多观众迎着寒风参加圣派翠克节游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13954720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954720" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954720-DSC_0124.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954720-DSC_0124-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954720" class="wp-caption-text">退休的警察们参加游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在约2英里的游行路线旁，许多人都穿着绿色的衣服或饰物，庆祝这个源自爱尔兰的节日。各种游行方阵在人群的挥手和欢呼声中缓缓穿过了道路，来到游行终点的霍利约克市政厅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13954724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954724-DSC_0137.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954724-DSC_0137-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954724" class="wp-caption-text">霍利约克的圣派翠克节游行有穿着传统服装的乐队。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13954729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954729" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954729-DSC_0144.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954729-DSC_0144-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954729" class="wp-caption-text">霍利约克的圣派翠克节游行有精彩的乐队演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“这是个美好的日子，有来自全世界的人们拜访霍利约克和麻州西部。我希望每个人都度过美好的时光，享受愉快，和睦相聚。”全程参与游行的霍利约克市长加西亚（Joshua A. Garcia）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13954732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954732" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954732-DSC_0153.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954732-DSC_0153-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954732" class="wp-caption-text">当地的高中生乐队参加圣派翠克节游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13954731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954731-DSC_0151.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954731-DSC_0151-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954731" class="wp-caption-text">霍利约克的圣派翠克节游行有踩着高跷的女士们。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同样全程参与游行的市议员道尔曼（Peter R. Tallman）说，他热爱这个他生活了一辈子的城市，热爱当地的圣派翠克节庆典。“出来的人们都非常友好。游行的各部分都很精彩，乐队非常精彩，娱乐节目很不错。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13954733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954733-DSC_0157.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954733-DSC_0157-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954733" class="wp-caption-text">消防员们参加霍利约克圣派翠克节游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13954730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954730" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954730-DSC_0147.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954730-DSC_0147-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954730" class="wp-caption-text">当地的公益团体参加圣派翠克节游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>霍利约克的圣派翠克节游行有穿着传统服装或鲜艳礼服的乐队，有各式豪车和复古车辆，有当地高中生组成的乐队和啦啦队，也有一些公益组织和剧团等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13954728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954728" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954728-DSC_0142.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954728-DSC_0142-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954728" class="wp-caption-text">在霍利约克市街头，观众们热情地迎接圣派翠克节游行队伍。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纽约法轮功学员的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%9B%BD%E4%B9%90%E5%9B%A2.md#1">天国乐团</a>”再次受邀参加这个庆典。这也是他们第三次参加霍利约克的圣派翠克节游行。去年，天国乐团被评为最佳游行队伍。今年，民众依然对他们的表现印象深刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13954721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954721" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954721-DSC_0128.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954721-DSC_0128-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954721" class="wp-caption-text">纽约“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%9B%BD%E4%B9%90%E5%9B%A2.md#1">天国乐团</a>”参加霍利约克圣派翠克节游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13954722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954722-DSC_0131.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954722-DSC_0131-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954722" class="wp-caption-text">纽约“天国乐团”参加霍利约克圣派翠克节游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13954718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954718" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954718-20230319_115430.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954718-20230319_115430-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954718" class="wp-caption-text">纽约“天国乐团”参加霍利约克圣派翠克节游行。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13954719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954719" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954719-20230319_115505.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954719-20230319_115505-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954719" class="wp-caption-text">纽约“天国乐团”参加霍利约克圣派翠克节游行。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“他们（天国乐团）一直都表现出色，每年都有很多乐队成员，他们表现很精彩。今天天气很冷，这里经常都很冷，但这是很愉快的时光。”与父亲一起观看游行的当地居民斯图亚特（Jessica Stewart）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13954723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13954723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954723-DSC_0132.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13954723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13954723-DSC_0132-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13954723" class="wp-caption-text">游行领队之一Russell McNiff（右2）已连续58年参加霍利约克的圣派翠克节游行了。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的游行庆典在30?F左右的寒风中举办。但是游行领队之一麦克尼夫（Russell McNiff）说，天气并没有阻碍人们的热情参与。“这是我第58次游行。58年前我曾是游行庆典主席，我也曾担任游行领队。我觉得每一次游行都很棒。”他说。◇</p>
