<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>男子修复技能高超 让70年代老福特再次上路</h2>
<a href="https://d7b58bkqqk9ju.cloudfront.net/K3wMWxbDw"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年04月06日讯】（英文大纪元记者<ahref="https://www.theepochtimes.com/mechanic-buys-rusted-79-ranchero-gt-thats-been-sitting-for-20-years-now-she-runs-looks-wow_5120908.md#1">Louise Chambers</a>报导／张雨霏编译）美国宾夕法尼亚州一位对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E7%88%B7%E8%BD%A6.md#1">老爷车</a>充满热情的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%BA%E6%A2%B0%E5%B8%88.md#1">机械师</a>充分发挥自己娴熟的技能，没费太多周折就让一辆已闲置20年之久的老爷车重获新生。</p>
<p>36岁的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%BA%E6%A2%B0%E5%B8%88.md#1">机械师</a>克里斯（Chris）在费城北部的一个车库里工作，当他在附近的罗耶斯福德区（Royersford）看到一辆翡翠色1979款<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A6%8F%E7%89%B9.md#1">福特</a>Ranchero GT待售时，他马上产生了要<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%AE%E5%A4%8D.md#1">修复</a>它的想法。</p>
<p>这辆老<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A6%8F%E7%89%B9.md#1">福特</a>的行驶里程已达96,000英里（约合155,000公里），卖方为原车主的孙女。所有其他潜在买家都想要这辆车的零部件，但这位孙女不忍心看到心爱的Ranchero被拆得七零八落。然而，克里斯承诺让这辆<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E7%88%B7%E8%BD%A6.md#1">老爷车</a>再次上路，因此，他最终顺利将其拖回自己的车库。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13966329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966329" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966329-NoNonsenseKnowHow-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13966329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966329-NoNonsenseKnowHow-1.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966329" class="wp-caption-text">（NoNonsenseKnowHow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13966328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966328" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966328-et-NoNonsenseKnowHow-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13966328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966328-et-NoNonsenseKnowHow-1.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="361" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966328" class="wp-caption-text">（NoNonsenseKnowHow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13966330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966330" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966330-NoNonsenseKnowHow-5.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13966330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966330-NoNonsenseKnowHow-5.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966330" class="wp-caption-text">（NoNonsenseKnowHow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>事实上，这辆Ranchero仍保留着所有原厂件，包括一个5.0升Coyote发动机，尽管它已经銹迹斑斑，并且后视镜还带有原装的整流罩。</p>
<p>原车主为他的所有车辆喷上了颜色适中的翡翠色金属漆，作为他公司的统一品牌。这辆车的内部装饰也很讲究，克里斯为此感到很吃惊，所以他认为原车主应该是一个很有品味的人。</p>
<p>克里斯对汽车进行分阶段处理，首先清洁外部，然后用真空吸尘器清除内部的污垢、垃圾、动物粪便以及表面的銹迹等。接下来，他检查了汽车的发动机状况和机油，并拆掉了没电的电池。</p>
<p>他清洗完发动机舱后，成功恢复了它的动力系统。然后，他检查了刹车线，并换上了装在车身底部的原装备胎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13966338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966338" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966338-NoNonsenseKnowHow-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13966338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966338-NoNonsenseKnowHow-4.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966338" class="wp-caption-text">（NoNonsenseKnowHow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13966331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966331" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966331-NoNonsenseKnowHow-8.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13966331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966331-NoNonsenseKnowHow-8.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="349" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966331" class="wp-caption-text">（NoNonsenseKnowHow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13966332" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966332" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966332-NoNonsenseKnowHow-9.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13966332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966332-NoNonsenseKnowHow-9.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966332" class="wp-caption-text">（NoNonsenseKnowHow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>克里斯接着清理了所有金属零部件的表面銹迹并拆掉已破损的顶棚，然后让臭氧消毒机在车内运行了一整夜，以清除空气污染物。第二天，他尝试打开点火装置，结果这辆Ranchero马上动起来了。</p>
<p>“这充分证明了福特的品质！甚至没有丝毫迟疑，快得有些令人难以置信！”克里斯说。</p>
<p>Ranchero的首次成功试驾不仅证明了汽车工艺的高超，也证明了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%AE%E5%A4%8D.md#1">修复</a>者技能的精湛。不出所料，车子一上路运行也非常顺畅，前刹车效果良好，变速箱虽然笨重但仍很实用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13966333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966333" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966333-NoNonsenseKnowHow-16.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13966333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966333-NoNonsenseKnowHow-16.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966333" class="wp-caption-text">（NoNonsenseKnowHow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13966334" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966334" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966334-NoNonsenseKnowHow-17.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13966334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966334-NoNonsenseKnowHow-17.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966334" class="wp-caption-text">（NoNonsenseKnowHow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13966335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966335" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966335-NoNonsenseKnowHow-18.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13966335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966335-NoNonsenseKnowHow-18.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966335" class="wp-caption-text">（NoNonsenseKnowHow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在这项修复工作取得巨大的成功后，克里斯决定将这辆Ranchero挂牌出售，希望能找到一位同行机械师或老爷车爱好者，给这辆刚上路的“新车”多一点关爱。</p>
