<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东浪嘉年华落幕 逾3万人体验杉原湾海景沙滩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13965917-607923-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>东浪嘉年华活动吸引不少民众在台东杉原湾体验沙滩海景。（台东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年04月06日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾台东报导）2023<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B5%AA.md#1">东浪</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%8E.md#1">嘉年华</a>系列活动，4月1日起连续假期在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%89%E5%8E%9F%E6%B9%BE.md#1">杉原湾</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E6%BB%A9.md#1">沙滩</a>进行，白天有沙滩各项游憩活动，晚间则有连续进行的沙滩<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">音乐会</a>，台东县政府表示，共计吸引逾3万人到杉原湾体验。</p>
<figure id="13965919" aria-describedby="caption-13965919" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13965919-607925-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13965919-607925-450x300.jpg" alt="东浪嘉年华活动吸引不少民众在台东杉原湾体验沙滩海景。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13965919" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B5%AA.md#1">东浪</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%8E.md#1">嘉年华</a>活动吸引不少民众在台东<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%89%E5%8E%9F%E6%B9%BE.md#1">杉原湾</a>体验<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E6%BB%A9.md#1">沙滩</a>海景。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13965918" aria-describedby="caption-13965918" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13965918-607924-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13965918-607924-450x300.jpg" alt="东浪嘉年华活动吸引不少民众在台东杉原湾体验沙滩海景。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13965918" class="wp-caption-text">东浪嘉年华活动吸引不少民众在台东杉原湾体验沙滩海景。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县府表示，今年东浪嘉年华让大家享受不一样的海滩时光，感受杉原湾南岛风情独特魅力，同时把杉原湾变成<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">音乐会</a>场景，不少民众连续每天都来报到，人潮将杉原湾及台11线挤爆。</p>
<p>沙滩游憩活动由台湾沙滩排球挑战赛开启序幕，大型风筝展演、亲子沙雕、亲子趴板及南岛沙滩部落体验接连登场。每晚的沙滩音乐会，共24组卡司轮番上阵，每天涌入大批民众到场卡位，近距离与歌手同嗨。</p>
<p>在地莿桐部落为做好活动期间垃圾清理及现场环境维护工作，晚会期间也加入维护环境，从舞台前到沙滩、戏水区，族人不间断地进行清洁工作，迅速还原场地清洁，为活动画下完美句点。</p>
<figure id="13965921" aria-describedby="caption-13965921" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13965921-607927-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13965921-607927-450x300.jpg" alt="东浪嘉年华沙滩音乐会，DJ-KOO具俊晔演唱。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13965921" class="wp-caption-text">东浪嘉年华沙滩音乐会，DJ-KOO具俊晔演唱。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13965920" aria-describedby="caption-13965920" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13965920-607926-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13965920-607926-450x300.jpg" alt="东浪嘉年华沙滩音乐会，民众热情支持。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13965920" class="wp-caption-text">东浪嘉年华沙滩音乐会，民众热情支持。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
