<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>老宅私房美食餐馆Cozy Dining  美酒佳肴 人生享受</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966234-Cozy-Dining-logo-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>位于悉尼南区Sutherland的老宅私房美食餐馆。（Cozy Dining提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年04月06日讯】在悉尼南区的Sutherland?，有一家别具一格由文物保护的老式民宅改造的餐馆，其进餐环境独特优雅，给人一种在私家宅邸享受美食的感觉。</p>
<p>餐馆内浮雕花纹的屋顶，老式的家具，彩色的台灯与镶嵌彩色玻璃的窗户和谐一体，柔和的灯光下，精致的餐具和摆设打造了优雅的就餐氛围，舒适得给人一种强烈的想立马在此享受美食的欲望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13966235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966235-oozy-Dining--600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13966235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966235-oozy-Dining--600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966235" class="wp-caption-text">位于悉尼南区Sutherland的老宅私房美食餐馆。（Cozy Dining提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>餐馆就餐的区域被分为室内，凉台和室外街景树荫下，而无论那种就餐区，Cozy Dining所营造的都是非常舒适悠闲的就餐环境，配上与优雅环境Cozy Dining?相匹配的精致菜式更是令人锦上添花。五款套餐菜式精美，配搭精巧，比悉尼高档餐馆的Fine Ding仿佛更胜一筹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13966237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966237" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966237-dry-aged-OP-Ribeye-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13966237" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966237-dry-aged-OP-Ribeye-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966237" class="wp-caption-text">经过Dry aged 过程的牛排，多余的水分从牛肉中散失掉。使得牛排更嫩，更美味。（Cozy Dining提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Cozy Dining的厨师拥有15年的烹调经验，曾在悉尼著名的餐厅担任厨师，为Cozy Dining烹制出精美高级品尝菜套餐，特别是碳烤牛排，?经过Dry aged?过程的牛排，多余的?水分从牛肉中散失掉。?由此产生更浓的牛肉风味和味道，使得牛排更嫩，更美味，还搭配了来自猎人谷和西澳精选的葡萄酒。在Cozy Dining轻松浪漫的用餐环境中，精美菜肴和相配的美酒带给客人无与伦比的美好生活就如此的享受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13966238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966238" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966238-Trout-Gravlax-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13966238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966238-Trout-Gravlax-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966238" class="wp-caption-text">招牌鳟鱼Gravlax。（Cozy Dining提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Cozy Dining?以招牌鳟鱼Gravlax作为开胃菜开始了八道套餐品尝之旅。?经过腌制及冷熏后的鳟鱼肉质细嫩鲜美，配上玫瑰色的脆甜菜根和茴香蒜味酸奶酱，味道层次丰富爽口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13966239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13966239" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966239-Charred-Black-Opal-Wagyu-MB9-Taco-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13966239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13966239-Charred-Black-Opal-Wagyu-MB9-Taco-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13966239" class="wp-caption-text">脆皮红薯黑宝和牛。（Cozy Dining提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来招牌开胃菜是脆皮红薯黑宝和牛，选用高品质的和牛，经炭火稍微燎烤，切碎后拌上烤辣椒配蒜花，一款独特口味的菜品使味蕾大开，?颇有特色，搭配来自猎人谷的精选葡萄酒，保证满足您的所有渴望。</p>
<p>Cozy Dining?的菜品丰富，配菜精美，如煎鲜贝，鞑靼牛肉，海鲜意面，煎Dry aged澳洲盲曹，蘑菇意饭，玫瑰奶酪土豆面团， 柠檬冰糕，白巧克力慕斯， 还有Cozy Dining?自烤的手工面包。</p>
