<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园消防第四大队呼吁防范扫墓错误易致火灾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964914-607742-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>横渡体验。（桃园消防第四大队提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年04月04日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）今年春季降雨少，各地天干物燥，易使山林田野因些许火星致生火灾；适逢清明连假，前往公墓祭拜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%AB%E5%A2%93.md#1">扫墓</a>民众众多，点香祭拜及燃烧金纸如未妥善处理，极易引发公墓火警。</p>
<figure id="13964915" aria-describedby="caption-13964915" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964915-607743-450x247.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964915-607743-450x247.jpg" alt="住警器热水器解说。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13964915" class="wp-caption-text">住警器热水器解说。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E6%B6%88%E9%98%B2.md#1">桃园消防</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AC%AC%E5%9B%9B%E5%A4%A7%E9%98%9F.md#1">第四大队</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市消防局<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AC%AC%E5%9B%9B%E5%A4%A7%E9%98%9F.md#1">第四大队</a>龙潭分队选在4、5日，于龙潭分队举办为期两日的消防宣导体验营，活动不仅包含清明<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%AB%E5%A2%93.md#1">扫墓</a>宣导专区，还有最受欢迎的射水体验区、地震体验专区、横渡体验区、水灭火器操作区、CPR操作及救护宣导区、住宅防火对策2.0专区、消防衣着装体验、消防车辆及器材展示区，期待吸引更多家长及小朋友参与，使整个活动现场热闹欢乐。</p>
<figure id="13964916" aria-describedby="caption-13964916" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964916-607744-450x242.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964916-607744-450x242.jpg" alt="射水体验。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13964916" class="wp-caption-text">射水体验。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E6%B6%88%E9%98%B2.md#1">桃园消防</a>第四大队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13964917" aria-describedby="caption-13964917" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964917-607745-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964917-607745-450x253.jpg" alt="山域抢救体验。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13964917" class="wp-caption-text">山域抢救体验。（桃园消防第四大队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>因应本次活动龙潭分队于三月中旬时已于辖区内国小举办“清明扫墓四不二记得”创意征图活动，让小朋友用创意发想，亲手绘制画作来加深防灾印象，并委托学校老师遴选优秀作品于朝会时给予表扬并同时向学生宣导防灾观念，学校方面也肯定桃园市政府消防局用心。</p>
<figure id="13964918" aria-describedby="caption-13964918" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964918-607746-450x309.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964918-607746-450x309.jpg" alt="得奖作品。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13964918" class="wp-caption-text">得奖作品。（桃园消防第四大队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园消防第四大队长郭百伦表示：由于近日天气较为炎热，且雨情不佳，天干物燥特别容易引发杂草及公墓火警，令同仁疲于奔命。经与里长及相关单位协调，于公墓皆有设置取水桶及储水袋供民众扫墓时扑灭火星，望能降低公墓火警发生的频率。</p>
<figure id="13964919" aria-describedby="caption-13964919" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964919-607747-450x308.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964919-607747-450x308.jpg" alt="得奖作品。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13964919" class="wp-caption-text">得奖作品。（桃园消防第四大队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园消防第四大队呼吁市民前往公墓扫墓祭拜时，牢记“四不二记得”口诀: 不任意燃烧杂草、不随意丢烟蒂、不让冥纸飞扬、不燃放爆竹烟火、记得扑灭余烬、记得随手收拾垃圾等，共同努力防范公墓火灾发生。◇</p>
