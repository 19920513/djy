<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：暮归</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13980772-sunset-g244c8944e_1920-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>(Pixabay)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>已是日暮黄昏<br />
走过了多少路途<br />
历经了多少阴晴圆缺<br />
已难记得<br />
家园的记忆却时时相随</p>
<p>那些阳光灿烂的日子远了<br />
天色渐渐暗下<br />
渐渐矇眬<br />
体力渐渐衰竭<br />
还得坚忍支撑下去</p>
<p>一步步向前走<br />
走过那丛树林<br />
越过那条河流<br />
可预期的，前方还会遭遇<br />
许多崎岖坎坷</p>
<p>已是日暮黄昏<br />
天色渐渐暗下<br />
虽有矇眬和衰竭相要胁<br />
还是要走<br />
走归梦中的家园</p>
<p><strong>Back Home in the Evening</strong> Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>It’s the evening<br />
And is difficult for me to remember<br />
Many routes I have past<br />
As well as many yīn qíng yuán quē I have experiented<br />
But there follows me the remembers of my homeland very often</p>
<p>It’s far from me those sunny and brilliant days<br />
The weather is dark gradually<br />
And dim gradually<br />
The physical strength is exhaustion gradually<br />
But I must prop up firmly still</p>
<p>I go forward step by step<br />
Walk across the grove<br />
Pass across the river<br />
It can be predicted there are many forked roads<br />
And vicissitudes in front of me</p>
<p>It’s the evening<br />
The weather is dark gradually<br />
Though there are gloom and exhaustion put press on me<br />
I must go forward still<br />
To the homeland of my dream</p>
