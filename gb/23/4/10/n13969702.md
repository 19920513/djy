<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小犀牛丧母成孤儿 获救后与饲养员感情深厚</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969714-ET-Web-rhino-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>肯尼亚一头失去母亲的黑犀牛幼崽幸运获救。(?Sheldrick Wildlife Trust/Mia Collis)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年04月10日讯】（英文大纪元记者<ahref="https://www.theepochtimes.com/rhino-4-who-was-rescued-as-a-calf-after-his-mom-died-remains-very-attached-to-the-keepers-that-have-cared-for-him_5127132.md#1">Anna Mason</a>报导／张雨霏编译）当一头仅6个月大的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E7%8A%80%E7%89%9B.md#1">黑犀牛</a>因自然原因而失去母亲后，它会很容易成为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%B7%E7%8C%8E%E8%80%85.md#1">偷猎者</a>和掠食者的目标。庆幸的是，这头幼小的犀牛很快被救援人员发现并获得全方位的照顾，现在已经和它的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%B2%E5%85%BB%E5%91%98.md#1">饲养员</a>们形成了很紧密的情感纽带。</p>
<p>这头<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E7%8A%80%E7%89%9B.md#1">黑犀牛</a>被命名为“阿波罗”（Apollo），现已4岁的它每天都在长时间享受泥浴，在它最喜欢的树下打盹，并尽情地享受来自<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%B2%E5%85%BB%E5%91%98.md#1">饲养员</a>的背部抓痒兼按摩。直到它长到足够大时，它才会回归属于它的野外生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969706" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969706-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Georgina-Preston-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13969706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969706-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Georgina-Preston-1.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969706" class="wp-caption-text">(? Sheldrick Wildlife Trust/Mia Collis)</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿波罗与谢尔德里克野生动物基金会<ahref="https://www.sheldrickwildlifetrust.org/orphans/apollo">Sheldrick Wildlife Trust</a>，简称SWT）的“邂逅”始于2019年9月，当时位于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%AF%E5%B0%BC%E4%BA%9A.md#1">肯尼亚</a>内罗毕的一个团队与SWT的警犬队合作，对一群经验丰富的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%B7%E7%8C%8E%E8%80%85.md#1">偷猎者</a>的藏身处展开空中搜索。</p>
<p>直升机飞行员安迪?佩恩（Andy Payne）突然接到来自<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%AF%E5%B0%BC%E4%BA%9A.md#1">肯尼亚</a>野生动物协会（KWS）的紧急电话，该协会通知他西察沃国家公园（Tsavo West National Park）的一位高级管理员急需有人协助他去营救一头黑犀牛幼崽。这位管理员发现那头小犀牛无助地站在死于炭疽病的母犀牛的尸体旁，这头雌性黑犀牛是早已为人所熟知的“阿莫伊”（Amoy）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969708" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969708-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Mia-Collis-1-copy.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13969708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969708-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Mia-Collis-1-copy.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969708" class="wp-caption-text">(? Sheldrick Wildlife Trust/Mia Collis)</figcaption></figure>
<p>SWT在<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/SheldrickTrust/posts/pfbid0CRhDwqCT9mjDxB1WK7rRiH21yAfL1bKDFUnmDWQFmuBJRbB1JfAfznYaBdxkobfdl">脸书</a>的一篇帖子中写道：“接下来，一场激烈的追逐赛在西察沃国家公园内展开，因为狡猾的小犀牛让救援人员一路追了几英里远。多亏了地面和空中人员的不懈努力，我们终于成功救下了小阿波罗。”</p>
<p>这头小牛很快被直升机送到SWT的卡卢库幼崽保育室（Kaluku Neonate Nursery），该基金会最有经验的饲养员提前在那里做好迎接它的准备。阿波罗抵达后，先接受了多针注射液，其中包括一针镇定剂，因为当时它处于“极端的”压力之下。</p>
<p>“令人欣慰的是，它渐渐趋于平静，很快就喝了一瓶补液。”该组织说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969709" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969709-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Mia-Collis-3.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13969709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969709-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Mia-Collis-3.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969709" class="wp-caption-text">(? Sheldrick Wildlife Trust/Mia Collis)</figcaption></figure>
<p>毋庸置疑，对于所有关心孤儿犀牛的人来说，第一个晚上都会有些担心吊胆。然而，到了第二天，阿波罗就允许它的饲养员用扫帚反复擦蹭它的肚皮。</p>
<p>“他们需要花时间与它建立信任”，SWT的一位代表告诉《大纪元时报》，“它很快就适应了它的新家庭。”</p>
<p>不久之后，阿波罗就跟着它的饲养员们四处走动，它非常喜欢吸吮它的奶瓶，并享受专门为它特制的泥浴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969707-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Georgina-Preston-2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13969707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969707-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Georgina-Preston-2.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969707" class="wp-caption-text">(? Sheldrick Wildlife Trust/Mia Collis)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13969711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969711" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969711-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Mia-Collis-6.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13969711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969711-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Mia-Collis-6.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969711" class="wp-caption-text">(? Sheldrick Wildlife Trust/Mia Collis)</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然有许多饲养员参与照顾阿波罗，但它唯独与两个特别擅长照顾孤儿犀牛的尤斯图斯（Justus）和金欧（King&#8217;oo）有着非常紧密的联系。</p>
<p>与此同时，阿波罗最喜欢的活动包括享受长时间的泥沙浴和在它最喜欢的猴面包树下小睡。在4岁生日那天，它享受了一次悠闲惬意的泥浴，同时饲养员还不断给它挠痒痒。</p>
<p>至于阿波罗的未来，SWT表示它将重新融入栖息在察沃公园的野生黑犀牛种群。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969713" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969713-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Mia-Collis-9.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13969713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969713-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Mia-Collis-9.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969713" class="wp-caption-text">(? Sheldrick Wildlife Trust/Mia Collis)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“这是一个渐进的过程，因为犀牛的领地意识很强，必须慢慢引入新成员，尤其是新的雄性犀牛。”该代表说。</p>
<p>以收养和救助大象和犀牛孤儿&#33879;称的SWT，已成功收养了17头犀牛孤儿。该组织非常感谢所有付出时间和精力帮助拯救小阿波罗的人。</p>
<p>“每一头犀牛的生命都弥足珍贵，尤其是考虑到肯尼亚仅存900多头黑犀牛这一事实。”SWT在脸书帖子中写道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969710-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Mia-Collis-5.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13969710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969710-Apollo_Copyright_Sheldrick-Wildlife-Trust_and_Mia-Collis-5.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969710" class="wp-caption-text">(?
