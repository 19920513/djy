<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：寻找沙滩上的脚印</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969842-sand-g263e9822c_1920-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>(Pixabay)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>又到当年被称为夏威夷海边的沙滩<br />
我一头便急急奔赴，栽进去寻找<br />
只是怎么都找不到我当年留下的脚印了</p>
<p>那是个满是松软细沙的沙滩<br />
那些年我常和家人来这里<br />
看海，漫步，戏水，玩挖沙马、追沙马游戏<br />
留下不少我深深的脚印<br />
连同不少我欢乐的笑声和青春活力<br />
我想这次重来一定可以寻找得到<br />
没想到它们已经无处寻</p>
<p>为什么呢？我怀疑着<br />
浪涛滚滚的海依旧滚滚翻涌着<br />
满是松软细沙的沙滩依旧在<br />
我那些当年留下的深深脚印为什么不见了？<br />
是谁给偷走了？藏到哪里去了？<br />
寻遍沙滩，甚至像当年挖沙马一样挖沙寻找<br />
挖呀挖呀，挖得满头大汗，就是寻找不到<br />
　<br />
猛回头才发现后面跟着一个人<br />
他告诉我，那些脚印<br />
如同我的茂密黑发<br />
被滚滚海浪卷走了</p>
<p>唉，被滚滚海浪卷走了<br />
包括那些深深的脚印、欢乐的笑声和青春活力<br />
包括开发大鹏湾前那片宽阔的水域<br />
包括当年最早规划开发大我三岁的柯姓县长<br />
那年邀我乘坐他的竹筏到水面钓鱼的卢姓学弟<br />
还有那原始风味，诸般情趣<br />
唉，都已经去了了了，唉……</p>
<p><strong>Look for the Footprints on the Sandy Beach</strong> Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>Once again while I come to the sandy beach called Howaii those year<br />
I run hurriedly frame up to look for<br />
Only I can’t find the footprints which I left those years</p>
<p>It’s the sandy beach which full of soft fine sands<br />
I often come with my families those years<br />
To appreciate the sea, to stroll, to play water, to dig and chase stalk- eyed ghost crab<br />
Left down many of my deep footprints<br />
As well as my happy laugh and young vitality<br />
I deem I can find them certainly this time<br />
But it’s not expected nowhere to find them</p>
<p>Why? I doubt<br />
The rolling sea is also rolling still<br />
The sandy beach full of soft fine sands is here still<br />
Why do the deep footprints I left those years can’t find?<br />
Who steals them? Where does they conceal?<br />
I look for everywhere the sandy beach, even look for the sand as I dig those years<br />
I dig and dig, dig into sweat streaming my whole body, it’s no find</p>
<p>Turn back I suddenly find there is a person who follows me<br />
He tells me, those footprints<br />
Was rolling away by the spray<br />
Like my thick blacken hairs</p>
<p>Alas, rolling away by the spray<br />
Includes the deep footprints, joyful laugh and young vitality<br />
Includes the wide area of bay Da-Peng<br />
Includes plan to develop here the county leader Ke who elder three years old than me<br />
As well as invited me to fish on his bamboo raft the younger male schoolmate Lu<br />
And the original taste, much interest<br />
Alas, it’s all gone already, alas ……@</p>
