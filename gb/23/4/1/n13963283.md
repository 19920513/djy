<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>头份竹南地区亲子运动嘉年华 4千人同乐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963284-607551-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>苗栗县长钟东锦期盼该活动 促进亲子关系（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年04月01日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）县府教育处4月1日上午在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%B9%E5%8D%97.md#1">竹南</a>运动公园举办<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B4%E4%BB%BD.md#1">头份</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%B9%E5%8D%97.md#1">竹南</a>地区“童在一起．幸福有你”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%B2%E5%AD%90%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8.md#1">亲子运动</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%8E.md#1">嘉年华</a>，约2千个家庭至少4千人参与，家长、小朋友冒雨一起玩亲子互动及闯关游戏，充满了欢乐。</p>
<p>为促进亲子感情与培养儿童良好运动习惯，县府教育处今年规划分别在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B4%E4%BB%BD.md#1">头份</a>竹南地区、通苑地区、苗栗地区、大湖地区办理4场次亲子活动，今天登场的是头份竹南地区场次。</p>
<figure id="13963290" aria-describedby="caption-13963290" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963290-607556-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963290-607556-450x300.jpg" alt="闯关游戏" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13963290" class="wp-caption-text">闯关游戏（苗县府 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天的亲子活动，虽在雨中举办，但丝毫未减乡亲的参与热情，至少约4千位家长、小朋友穿着雨衣或打着雨伞陆续赶到会场，在雨中参与亲子互动游戏或闯关游戏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13963289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13963289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963289-607555-600x401.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13963289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963289-607555-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13963289" class="wp-caption-text">亲子趣味游戏（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在开幕仪式中，县长钟东锦、立委陈超明夫人吴丽利、副议长张淑芬、县议员萧咏萱、陈光轩、陈碧华、邱毓兴、徐功凡服务处主秘、叶忠伦助理、头份市长罗雪珠、竹南镇长方进兴、县府教育处长叶芯慧等人共同启动这项活动，并祝小朋友儿童节快乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13963288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13963288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963288-607554-600x363.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13963288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963288-607554-600x363.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13963288" class="wp-caption-text">亲子趣味游戏（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长钟东锦指出，为因应社会快速发展的趋势和少子女化的挑战，亲子教育更应重视，今天办理的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%B2%E5%AD%90%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8.md#1">亲子运动</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%8E.md#1">嘉年华</a>活动，不仅能锻炼幼儿体能，亦能增进亲子关系，从小培养孩子运动习惯，欢迎乡亲和小朋友携手一起来参与，促进家庭和乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13963287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13963287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963287-607553-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13963287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963287-607553-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13963287" class="wp-caption-text">雨天档不住民众的热情（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天的活动现场安排县府税务局、政风处、地政处、教育处、环保局等单位的宣导摊位，且设计多种有趣的亲子互动游戏，让孩子们透过游戏达到运动健身的效果，并增进亲子之间的感情，此外，现场更设置了灌篮高手、眼明手快、童玩弹珠台等20余项手脑并用的闯关摊位，同时准备礼物赠予每个参加活动的小朋友，让家长带领孩子一同挑战，愉快度过周末假期。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13963285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13963285" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963285-607552-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13963285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13963285-607552-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13963285" class="wp-caption-text">该活动吸引许多家长和学童参与（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
