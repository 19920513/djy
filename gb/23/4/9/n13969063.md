<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】白果腐竹鲜百合浸油菜心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969085-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>白果腐竹鲜百合浸油菜心（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>小编语</strong></span>?清明时节一过，气温渐渐回升，这时食用清爽润肺、好消化的美味佳肴，有益身心。这次推荐的美食菜谱，来自有40多年经验的饭店主厨，详细步骤搭配实作照片，在家也能轻易做出饭店佳肴。主要材料有银杏（俗称：<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E6%9E%9C.md#1">白果</a>）、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%90%E7%AB%B9.md#1">腐竹</a>和鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BE%E5%90%88.md#1">百合</a>，所谓药食同源，百合宁心安神、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E6%9E%9C.md#1">白果</a>温肺益气，连茹素者都视为健康好菜！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>白果18粒<br />
鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%90%E7%AB%B9.md#1">腐竹</a>4 oz<br />
鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BE%E5%90%88.md#1">百合</a>2 oz<br />
红萝卜花4片<br />
油菜心1磅<br />
蔬菜汤2杯</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>蘑菇粉1茶匙<br />
盐1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 油菜心去头去尾，洗净备用。鲜百合切除末段少许，拆散后洗净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969078" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969078-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13969078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969078-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969078" class="wp-caption-text">油菜心去头去尾，鲜百合切除末段少许，两者都洗净备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 鲜腐竹和白果，洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969079" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969079-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13969079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969079-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969079" class="wp-caption-text">鲜腐竹和白果，洗干净备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 汤煲内放入蔬菜汤，加入调味料，汤滚后放入油菜心烫熟，捞起放在盘中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969077" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969077" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969077-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13969077" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969077-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969077" class="wp-caption-text">汤煲内放入蔬菜。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 再放入白果、腐竹、百合和红萝卜，煮2分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969080" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969080-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13969080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969080-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969080" class="wp-caption-text">放入白果和红萝卜。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 然后连同材料一起倒入放有油菜心的盘中排放好，便可食用。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13969081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13969081" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969081-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13969081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13969081-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13969081" class="wp-caption-text">将全部材料放入已经装有油菜心的盘中，进行摆盘。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
