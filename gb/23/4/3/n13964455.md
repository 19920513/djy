<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>张赫张娜拉出演喜剧 《特务家族》4月中登台</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964464-20230403-mark-disney08-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>张赫X张娜拉《特务家族（Family）》间谍喜剧剧照，张赫。（迪士尼+提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年04月03日讯】（大纪元记者叶子杰报导）犹如《间谍家家酒》的韩国真人版间谍影集《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%B9%E5%8A%A1%E5%AE%B6%E6%97%8F.md#1">特务家族</a>》，讲述了平常伪装成平凡上班族的杀手“权道勋”与梦想成为完美家人的妻子“姜侑拉”之间发生的喜剧故事，4月17日于Disney+上线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13964462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13964462" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964462-20230403-mark-disney06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13964462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964462-20230403-mark-disney06-450x300.jpg" alt="《特务家族（Family）》" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13964462" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E8%B5%AB.md#1">张赫</a>X<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E5%A8%9C%E6%8B%89.md#1">张娜拉</a>《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%B9%E5%8A%A1%E5%AE%B6%E6%97%8F.md#1">特务家族</a>（Family）》间谍喜剧剧照。（迪士尼+提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《特务家族（Family: The Unbreakable Bond）》由张正道与李正默共同执导，并由郑宥善（曾编剧《那个男人，吴秀》）执笔，该片是继<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E8%B5%AB.md#1">张赫</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E5%A8%9C%E6%8B%89.md#1">张娜拉</a>共同出演《明朗少女成功记》、《命中注定我爱你》、《Drama Festival-久违的再见》之后，时隔9年第四度合作的影集。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13964461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13964461" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964461-20230403-mark-disney05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13964461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964461-20230403-mark-disney05-450x675.jpg" alt="《特务家族（Family）》" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13964461" class="wp-caption-text">《特务家族（Family）》间谍喜剧剧照，张娜拉。（迪士尼+提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《特务家族》故事描述国家情报院特工“权道勋”（张赫 饰）多年来向所有人隐藏他的间谍身份，白天是贸易公司朝九晚五白领上班族，晚上则作为特工伺机而动，就连自己的妻子“姜侑拉”（张娜拉 饰）也被蒙在鼓里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13964460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13964460" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964460-20230403-mark-disney04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13964460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964460-20230403-mark-disney04-450x300.jpg" alt="《特务家族（Family）》" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13964460" class="wp-caption-text">张赫X张娜拉《特务家族（Family）》间谍喜剧剧照。（迪士尼+提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>权道勋为了不让秘密败露，不只日以继夜的工作，更不忘给妻子梦寐以求的甜蜜家庭生活，却殊不知自己的枕边人也向自己隐瞒了一个惊人秘密。</p>
<p>《特务家族》将于4月17日在香港、台湾、美国以及各地区Disney+同步上线，并于每周一、二播出最新集数。</p>
<p>《特务家族》预告片：<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BbekZGEg_PA" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">请点此观看影片</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_13964457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13964457" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964457-20230403-mark-disney01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13964457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13964457-20230403-mark-disney01-450x300.jpg" alt="《特务家族（Family）》" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13964457" class="wp-caption-text">《特务家族（Family）》间谍喜剧剧照，张赫。（迪士尼+提供）</figcaption></figure>
