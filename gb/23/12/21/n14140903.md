<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】黑松露牛肝菌白酱烩龙虾尾～圣诞菜谱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140918-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黑松露牛肝菌白酱烩龙虾尾，圣诞菜谱。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>编者按：圣诞佳节将至，梁厨示范的这道“黑松露<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%9D%E8%8F%8C.md#1">牛肝菌</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E9%85%B1.md#1">白酱</a>烩<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E8%99%BE.md#1">龙虾</a>尾”，制作方法并不困难，建议你不妨趁着圣诞节期间，在家里尝试这道美味料理。</p>
<h3>【材料】</h3>
<p>太平洋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E8%99%BE.md#1">龙虾</a>尾5条<br />
生粉适量<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%9D%E8%8F%8C.md#1">牛肝菌</a>0.8 oz<br />
白磨菇片8 oz<br />
洋葱1/4个切粒<br />
蒜蓉、姜蓉、干葱粒各少许<br />
黑松露片1汤匙<br />
鲜奶油1/2杯<br />
鸡汤2杯<br />
牛油2 oz<br />
面粉2 oz</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140909-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14140909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140909-1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140909" class="wp-caption-text">材料。(梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【调味】</h3>
<p>盐1/2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
糖1茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140910" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140910-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14140910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140910-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140910" class="wp-caption-text">调味料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【作法】</h3>
<p>1. 龙虾尾解冻后切大块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140911" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140911-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140911-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140911" class="wp-caption-text">龙虾尾解冻后切大块。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 牛肝菌用水浸泡5分钟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140912" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140912-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140912-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140912" class="wp-caption-text">牛肝菌用水浸泡5分钟备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅下油，龙虾尾洒上少许生粉拌匀，放进锅中煎香至八成熟，取出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140913" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140913-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140913-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140913" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，龙虾尾洒上少许生粉拌匀，放进锅中煎香至八成熟，取出备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 叧外取一个小锅子，热锅后放入牛油、面粉，煮成糊状备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140914" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140914-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140914-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140914" class="wp-caption-text">叧外取一个小锅子，热锅后放入牛油、面粉，煮成糊状备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再次热锅下油，放入姜、蒜、洋葱和干葱炒香，加入牛肝菌、白磨菇翻炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140915" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140915-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140915-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140915" class="wp-caption-text">再次热锅下油，放入姜、蒜、洋葱和干葱炒香，加入牛肝菌、白磨菇翻炒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放入龙虾尾翻炒，加入鸡汤及调味料煮滚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140916" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140916-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140916-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140916" class="wp-caption-text">放入龙虾尾翻炒，加入鸡汤及调味料煮滚。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 接着加入“作法4”的面糊及鲜奶油，轻轻搅拌。摆盘后放上黑松露片。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140917" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140917-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140917-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140917" class="wp-caption-text">接着加入“作法4”的面糊及鲜奶油，轻轻搅拌。摆盘后放上黑松露片。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14140919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140919" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140919-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140919-11-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140919" class="wp-caption-text">黑松露牛肝菌<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E9%85%B1.md#1">白酱</a>烩龙虾尾，圣诞菜谱。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
