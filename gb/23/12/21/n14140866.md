<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】烤火鸡腿杂菜～圣诞菜谱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140874-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>圣诞佳节将至，这道“烤火鸡腿杂菜”，美味又营养，特别适合家庭聚餐时享用。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：圣诞佳节将至，梁厨示范的这道“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E7%81%AB%E9%B8%A1.md#1">烤火鸡</a>腿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%82%E8%8F%9C.md#1">杂菜</a>”，美味又营养，特别适合家庭聚餐时享用。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>火鸡腿4块<br />
洋葱1个<br />
西芹 5条<br />
莲藕8 oz<br />
羽衣甘篮 12个<br />
彩虹胡萝卜 1.5磅</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140867" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140867-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14140867" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140867-1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140867" class="wp-caption-text">材料。(梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>有味盐 3汤匙<br />
黑胡椒粉1汤匙<br />
白酒1杯<br />
鸡汤 2杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140868" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140868-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14140868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140868-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140868" class="wp-caption-text">调味料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 火鸡腿洗净抹干水，在肉的部分划上几刀，以便入味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140869" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140869-3.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14140869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140869-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140869" class="wp-caption-text">火鸡腿洗净抹干水，在肉的部分划上几刀，以便入味。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 把有味盐和黑胡椒粉均匀涂抹在鸡腿上，腌2小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140870" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140870-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140870-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140870" class="wp-caption-text">把有味盐和黑胡椒粉均匀涂抹在鸡腿上，腌2小时。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将所有蔬菜切成大块。预热烤箱至425°F，烤盘铺上锡箔纸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140871" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140871" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140871-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140871" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140871-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140871" class="wp-caption-text">将所有蔬菜切成大块。预热烤箱至425°F，烤盘铺上锡箔纸。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 蔬菜铺在盘底，鸡腿在上，倒入白酒、鸡汤，放进烤箱烤25分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140872" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140872-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140872-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140872" class="wp-caption-text">蔬菜铺在盘底，鸡腿在上，倒入白酒、鸡汤，放进烤箱烤25分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再把烤箱温度降至350°F，烤20分钟。插入温度计，达到165°F便熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140873" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140873" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140873-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140873-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140873" class="wp-caption-text">再把烤箱温度降至350°F，烤20分钟。插入温度计，达到165°F便熟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 取出鸡腿，盖上锡箔纸保温，留小部分蔬菜。<br />
7. 剩下的蔬菜连汁液一起放进搅拌机搅拌做成汁酱，调好味道。<br />
8. 取出鸡腿的骨头，肉切成厚片，蔬菜放碟中，上面铺上鸡肉，淋上汁酱便成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14140876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14140876" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140876-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14140876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14140876-9-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14140876" class="wp-caption-text">取出鸡腿的骨头，肉切成厚片，蔬菜放碟中，上面铺上鸡肉，淋上汁酱便成。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h3>
<p>? 用烤完的蔬菜做成汁酱，好吃又营养。◇</p>
