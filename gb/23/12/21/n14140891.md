<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新州现疫情 儿童和年轻患者急诊人数激增</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213043-GettyImages-1248157933-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2020年6月4日一名疑似COVID-19染疫患者被送往悉尼圣文森特医院（St Vincent's Hospital）急诊部红色区的隔离病房。(Lisa Maree Williams/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月21日讯】<span style="color: #000000;">（大纪元记者夏楚君澳洲悉尼编译报导）</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">圣诞假期前夕，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">新州</a>出现的新一波COVID-19（中共病毒）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%96%AB%E6%83%85.md#1">疫情</a>导致儿童和年轻</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">患者前往<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%80%A5%E8%AF%8A.md#1">急诊</a>室就诊的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E6%95%B0.md#1">人数</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BF%80%E5%A2%9E.md#1">激增</a>。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">新州</a>卫生厅表示，过去两周，前往<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%80%A5%E8%AF%8A.md#1">急诊</a>就诊的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', HelveticaNeue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">COVID-19</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">患者持续增加，尤其是儿童和</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', HelveticaNeue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">17</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', HelveticaNeue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">34</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">岁的年轻患者。</span></span></span></p>
