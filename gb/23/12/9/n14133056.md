<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】五指毛桃煲西施骨汤～祛湿健脾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133064-6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>益气提神的“五指毛桃煲西施骨汤”。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>前言：</strong>“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E6%8C%87%E6%AF%9B%E6%A1%83.md#1">五指毛桃</a>”对广东人而言是家户喻晓的汤料食材，在台湾相对少见，它本身带有椰子的香气，多用于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%85%B2%E6%B1%A4.md#1">煲汤</a>。五指毛桃煲<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%96%BD%E9%AA%A8%E6%B1%A4.md#1">西施骨汤</a>，不会肥腻，气味清香可口，既能益气、提神、解乏，又能祛湿、健脾胃，男女老少都适合饮用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E6%8C%87%E6%AF%9B%E6%A1%83.md#1">五指毛桃</a>煲<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%96%BD%E9%AA%A8%E6%B1%A4.md#1">西施骨汤</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>五指毛桃4 oz<br />
西施骨1.5磅<br />
生姜3片<br />
南北杏1.5 oz<br />
蜜枣4粒<br />
陈皮1块<br />
红萝卜适量<br />
盐适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133058" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133058-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14133058" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133058-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133058" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 五指毛桃洗干净，用水浸泡一会。西施骨切大块，用热水烫过后冲洗干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133060" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133060-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14133060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133060-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133060" class="wp-caption-text">西施骨切大块，用热水烫过后冲洗干净。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 南北杏、蜜枣、陈皮冲洗干净，生姜轻轻拍扁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133061" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133061-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14133061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133061-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133061" class="wp-caption-text">南北杏、蜜枣、陈皮冲洗干净，生姜轻轻拍扁。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 汤煲内放入15碗水，把所有材料放入，猛火煲滚后转为小火。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133063" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133063-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14133063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133063-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133063" class="wp-caption-text">汤煲内放入15碗水，把所有材料放入，猛火煲滚后转为小火。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 继续煲约2个半小时，加入适量盐调味，即可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133062" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133062-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14133062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133062-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133062" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%85%B2%E6%B1%A4.md#1">煲汤</a>约2个半小时后，加入适量盐调味，即可食用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
