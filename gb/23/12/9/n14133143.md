<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“猫里茶粄陶染客庄走读”苗栗市长力挺产业发展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133145-652301-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>猫里茶粄陶染客庄走读 。（苗栗市公所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月09日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%97%E6%A0%97.md#1">苗栗</a>报导）苗栗市公所于12/9、10举办了别开生面的“猫里茶粄陶染客庄走读”系列活动，积极推广苗栗市茶、粄、陶、染等优质客庄产业，同时提供群众免费报名苗栗市长邱镇军诚热情邀请全国民众踊跃参与，共同体验“猫里茶粄陶染客庄读”系列活动。本次活动将让参与者触摸、视觉、听觉、嗅觉走和味觉这五个活动种感官，深切感受茶、粄、陶、染四大工艺所发挥的独特魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133144" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133144-652300.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14133144" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133144-652300-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133144" class="wp-caption-text">猫里茶粄陶染客庄走读。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%97%E6%A0%97.md#1">苗栗</a>市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>焦点活动将集中在茶、粄、陶、染四大技艺的表演，透过互动体验，让参与者更了解这些技巧的取得成果。距离，促进交流与合作。</p>
<p>提供更丰富的体验，特别邀请猫里休闲农业区邱文正领导长、苗栗市嘉盛社区张永昌总消耗、凝蓝工坊负责人谢后兰老师以及舞陶负责人谢羽杰老师，他们将援建援助参与者深入体验茶、粄、陶、染四大工艺的奥妙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133146-652302.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14133146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133146-652302-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133146" class="wp-caption-text">猫里茶粄陶染客庄走读讲解。（苗栗市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自活动报名资讯上线以来，民众的热情参与让主办方惊喜。报名资讯一经公开，立即引发报名网站的挤爆，秒杀的状态更是在眨眼间完成。各项体验行程名额瞬间满额，展示了人民群众对此活动的热切期待与支持。</p>
<p>这项活动受到邱镇军领导的特别重视，并表示：苗栗市拥有丰富的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E5%BA%84%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">客庄文化</a>底蕴，特别是在茶、粄、陶和染等方面，具有极高的发展潜力。推动当地产业升级，市府积极参与并举办此次活动，希望透过实际体验，让更多人并喜爱苗栗市的客庄之美，带动苗栗市客庄休闲旅游产业蓬勃发展，为地方经济注入新活力。市公所承诺将持续举办类似的推广活动，不断努力，让更多的人认识、了解、喜爱这片充满活力的客庄土地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133147-652303.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14133147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133147-652303-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133147" class="wp-caption-text">猫里茶粄陶染客庄走读成果。（苗栗市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14133148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133148" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133148-652304.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14133148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133148-652304-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133148" class="wp-caption-text">猫里茶粄陶染客庄走读成果二。（苗栗市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14133149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133149-652305.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14133149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133149-652305-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133149" class="wp-caption-text">猫里茶粄陶染客庄走读成果三。（苗栗市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
