<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>烘烤美味马铃薯 秘诀是选对品种</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133158-shutterstock_2281778405-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>烘烤淀粉含量多的马铃薯，可以吃到松散口感，例如褐皮马铃薯。喜欢爽脆的马铃薯，就要挑选蜡质品种。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月09日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a>种类繁多，煮蔬菜汤的品种用来<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E7%83%A4.md#1">烘烤</a>不一定好吃。为烘烤食谱选择马铃薯，主要看其淀粉含量。通常这些信息不会标示在包装上，但可以根据品种来判断质地。</p>
<p>美国市售<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a>分为三类质地：蜡质型（Waxy potatoes）、通用型（All-purpose potatoes）和粉质型（Starchy potatoes）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133172-73169e7242f95bdc19e4667b4204bfe7-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14133172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133172-73169e7242f95bdc19e4667b4204bfe7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133172" class="wp-caption-text">美国的黄皮马铃薯（Yellow Potatoes）属蜡质型。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蜡质马铃薯淀粉少，烹煮时可以保持形状，不糊锅。常见品种包括红皮马铃薯（Red Bliss）、拇指马铃薯（Fingerling potato）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133156" style="width: 596px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133156-6265858b9f71decaf66ee51504165e5b-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14133156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133156-6265858b9f71decaf66ee51504165e5b-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="596" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133156" class="wp-caption-text">图为美国市售拇指马铃薯。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14133157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133157" style="width: 596px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133157-9b8d829b09f60153621e3112ebe8949b-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14133157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133157-9b8d829b09f60153621e3112ebe8949b-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="596" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133157" class="wp-caption-text">图为美国市售红皮马铃薯。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>相比之下，粉质马铃薯受到薯条、薯泥（mashing）和烘焙（baking）食谱的欢迎。因为它们的水分少、淀粉多，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E7%83%A4.md#1">烘烤</a>后质地粉糯，也容易烤出诱人食欲的棕色。褐皮马铃薯（Russet potatoes）、爱达荷州褐皮马鈴薯（Idaho potatoes）都属于这类。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133171-95dd09978d90d396e8138d39f341f859-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14133171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133171-95dd09978d90d396e8138d39f341f859-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133171" class="wp-caption-text">爱达荷州白皮马铃薯（White Potatoes）的味道和质地与褐皮马铃薯相似，可作通用品种。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你正在寻找适合烘烤的马铃薯，可以考虑如下8种。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133159" style="width: 594px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133159-5fbd20eb216db09bb670861180741c20-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14133159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133159-5fbd20eb216db09bb670861180741c20-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="594" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133159" class="wp-caption-text">美国常见的褐皮马铃薯。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>1. 褐皮马铃薯</h2>
<p>褐皮马铃薯有不少培育品种，在北美广泛种植。但美国市场多以Russet potatoes通称，Russet burbank是热门品种之一。这种马铃薯芽眼少，果肉的颜色为象牙白，烘烤后呈现干而松散的粉质。</p>
<p>Russet burbank的种子最早由美国植物学家路德?伯班克（Luther Burbank）发现。当时处于19世纪爱尔兰发生马铃薯疫病之后，人们正在寻找具有抗病性的马铃薯品种。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133161" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133161-shutterstock_77926864-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14133161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133161-shutterstock_77926864-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133161" class="wp-caption-text">爱达荷州褐皮马鈴薯。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>2. 爱达荷州褐皮马鈴薯</h2>
<p>爱达荷州的官方蔬菜是马铃薯。该州为当地种植的马铃薯注册了商标“Grown in Idaho?”。褐皮马铃薯Russet Burbank是他们的热销品种。这些马铃薯加热后会是美味的沙质口感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133162" style="width: 596px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133162-shutterstock_244601539-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14133162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133162-shutterstock_244601539-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="596" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133162" class="wp-caption-text">马里斯派珀马铃薯（Maris Piper potatoes）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>3. 马里斯派珀马铃薯</h2>
<p>马里斯派珀马铃薯（Maris Piper potatoes）是英国最常见的品种之一。它们糖分低，不太甜，是英式薯条的爱用品种。英国名厨杰米·奥利弗（Jamie Oliver）就曾在他的美食影片中教授如何烤（roast ）这种马铃薯，并称赞其质地蓬松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133163" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133163-shutterstock_47300851-450x313.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14133163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133163-shutterstock_47300851-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133163" class="wp-caption-text">爱德华国王马铃薯（King Edward potatoes）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>4. 爱德华国王马铃薯</h2>
<p>在英国，爱德华国王马铃薯是用途多元的品种，烘烤、油炸尤其出色。它们的外皮棕、粉色相间，煮熟的质地也像马里斯派珀马铃薯一样蓬松。过去这种马铃薯有多个名字，但在1902年为了纪念该年英王爱德华七世（King Edward VII）登基，更名为爱德华国王马铃薯（King Edward potatoes）。从那时起，它们愈加为人熟知。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133164" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133164-shutterstock_1209637012-600x496.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14133164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133164-shutterstock_1209637012-600x496.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="496" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133164" class="wp-caption-text">荷兰奶油马铃薯（Dutch cream potatoes）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>5. 荷兰奶油马铃薯</h2>
<p>“薯”如其名，光亮的外皮里面是顺滑的果肉。荷兰奶油马铃薯（Dutch cream potatoes）多才多艺，可以做成<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF%E6%B3%A5.md#1">马铃薯泥</a>、薯条，烘焙和烘烤也很棒。然而它们淀粉较少，所以烘烤后不会四散在烤盘上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133165-shutterstock_48002881-600x480.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14133165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133165-shutterstock_48002881-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133165" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%B2%E7%A9%BA%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">育空黄金马铃薯</a>（Yukon Gold potatoes）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>6. <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%B2%E7%A9%BA%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">育空黄金马铃薯</a></h2>
<p>育空黄金马铃薯（Yukon Gold potatoes）由加拿大历经数年培育而成，直到1980年代才问世。金黄色的外皮和果肉是它们的标志。这些外表出众的马铃薯，因用途广泛深受欢迎。因为淀粉量适中，用于烘烤、薯条、薯片、薯泥都适合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133166-shutterstock_1435430582-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14133166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133166-shutterstock_1435430582-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133166" class="wp-caption-text">锡贝戈马铃薯（Sebago potatoes）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>7. 锡贝戈马铃薯</h2>
<p>如果你在澳洲，锡贝戈马铃薯（Sebago potatoes）应该进入你的购物清单。这些拥有白色肉质、大小适中的马铃薯属于通用品种，可以用作烘焙、蒸、炸等各式料理。它们最初在缅因州培育，于1940年进入澳洲，至今仍然很受欢迎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133167" style="width: 593px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133167-shutterstock_2096671651-600x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14133167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133167-shutterstock_2096671651-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="593" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133167" class="wp-caption-text">黑马铃薯（Negra potatoes）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>8. 黑色马铃薯</h2>
<p>这些深色的秘鲁品种被称为黑色马铃薯（Negra potatoes）。它们吸收汤汁后不易散开变成糊状，如果计划尝试多种烹饪方式，正好可以一薯多用。特别的是，它们黄白色的果肉散发微妙的甜味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133168-shutterstock_1476444224-600x397.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14133168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133168-shutterstock_1476444224-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133168" class="wp-caption-text">紫色马铃薯（Purple Majesty potatoes）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>9. 紫色马铃薯</h2>
<p>紫色马铃薯（Purple Majesty potatoes）端上餐桌，可能会抢过主菜的风头——它的肉质是亮眼的紫色。紫色也意味着它们富含抗氧化物剂。这些马铃薯在北美很常见，质地与育空马铃薯相似，用于烘烤、烘焙或制作薯泥都不会让你失望。</p>
