<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>华埠圣诞树雨中亮灯 华埠商家居民同乐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131350-IMG_4853-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>12月3日傍晚，波士顿华埠Harrison街的圣诞树已经亮灯了。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月07日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）随着人们齐声倒数的声音落下，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E.md#1">圣诞</a>老人和波士顿市长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%BC%AD.md#1">吴弭</a>（Michelle Wu）拉动摇杆，点亮<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E6%A0%91.md#1">圣诞树</a>，将节日气氛带到这个古老的社区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131346" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131346-DSC04938.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14131346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131346-DSC04938-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131346" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E.md#1">圣诞</a>老公公、老太太，还有市长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%BC%AD.md#1">吴弭</a>等嘉宾为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E6%A0%91.md#1">圣诞树</a>亮灯。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">12月3日下午，人们冒着寒冷的风雨聚集在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>Harrison街的菲利浦广场，庆祝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>市长“魔法电车和圣诞树亮灯之旅”。华埠商会也联合当地商家，为人们带来面包和热饮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131348" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131348-DSC04968.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14131348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131348-DSC04968-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131348" class="wp-caption-text">华埠商会联合当地商家，为参加圣诞树点灯活动的人们带来食物。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14131347" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131347" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131347-DSC04950.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14131347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131347-DSC04950-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131347" class="wp-caption-text">华埠商会联合当地商家，为参加圣诞树点灯活动的人们带来食物。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“今天我们华商会很高兴，虽然在这样的天气下，大家仍然这么踊跃来见证这个一年一度的圣诞树亮灯仪式。今年虽然天公不作美，但大家的热情，还是喜气洋洋的！”华埠商会的陈仕维说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131343" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131343-DSC04856.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14131343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131343-DSC04856-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131343" class="wp-caption-text">华埠商会的陈仕维和陈文珊在雨中参加圣诞树亮灯活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">下午3点多，天色已经开始变暗。圣诞花车播着欢快的音乐，载着圣诞老人和政要嘉宾们来到华埠的菲利浦广场。当地商家和居民们也都打着雨伞、穿着雨衣参与活动。随着圣诞树亮灯，花车喷出彩带，人群也爆发热烈的欢呼声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131344" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131344-DSC04881.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14131344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131344-DSC04881-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131344" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭乘坐“魔法电车”来到华埠。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14131345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131345" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131345-DSC04898.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14131345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131345-DSC04898-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131345" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）参加华埠的圣诞树亮灯庆祝。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">跟随“魔法电车”为波士顿各个社区亮灯的嘉宾，除了圣诞老公公、老太太之外，还有波士顿市长吴弭、市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）、市议员路易琼（Ruthzee Louijeune）、桑塔纳（Henry Santana）、麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131349" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131349-DSC04979.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14131349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131349-DSC04979-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131349" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾们参加波士顿华埠圣诞树亮灯庆典。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“今年是我们第27届年度魔法电车之旅，除了延续传统之外，我们还希望做得更多。”吴弭说。她说，今年市政府与Goya食品公司合作，在年底节假季期间，为有需要的家庭提供食物，欢迎大家向工作人员咨询和申请。◇</p>
