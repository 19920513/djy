<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>温哥华街坊会将举办周年晚会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14127843-20221204-VKADinner-504-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一年一度的温哥华街坊会周年晚会 VKA Annual Gala将于2023年12月2日登场。（温哥华街坊会提供图片）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月01日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">一年一度的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%97%E5%9D%8A%E4%BC%9A.md#1">街坊会</a>周年晚会</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> VKA Annual Gala</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">即将登场。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">本届晚会将于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日在列治文鸿星海鲜酒家举行，让大温地区的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E6%B8%AF%E7%A7%BB%E6%B0%91.md#1">香港移民</a>及各界朋友聚首一堂。届时将有游戏与表演，以及拍卖活动。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">　　</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">国会议员<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E6%85%A7%E8%B4%9E.md#1">关慧贞</a>将应邀莅临致词，并于现场颁发首次推出的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%97%E5%9D%8A%E4%BC%9A.md#1">街坊会</a>新抵埠港人奖学金”，两名香港留学生将各得</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1000</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">奖学金。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">同场亦有本地商家如“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">852</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">港点煮”、其他</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">机构</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">如“台加文化协会”、“华埠掌故”、“香港屋”、“公民协进会”等亦会派出代表参与。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“温哥华街坊会”（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Vancouver Kaifong Association</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，简称</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">VKA</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）为服务<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E6%B8%AF%E7%A7%BB%E6%B0%91.md#1">香港移民</a>社群的注册非牟利义务组织，三年多以来透过各种活动维系并协助香港新移民适应并投入加拿大生活。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">日期：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日（星期六）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">时间：下午</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6:00 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">开放，下午</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6:30 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">入席</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">地点：鸿星海鲜酒家（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8181 Cambie Rd. #2200, Richmond, BC V6X 3X9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">活动详情：</span><ahref="https://tickets.vankaifong.com/annual-gala-2023/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">https://tickets.vankaifong.com/annual-gala-2023/</span></a>?<span style="font-weight: 400;">?◇</span></p>
