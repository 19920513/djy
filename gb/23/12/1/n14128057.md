<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】气炸锅培根酥</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128065-3c37a82e1f4e7deeb45d1f13b72495a7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>酥皮搭配培根的花样造型，看上去超有食欲，日常家庭用餐或聚会都适合。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E6%A0%B9.md#1">培根</a>总是煎着吃？花点心思做造型，培根也可以是点亮用餐气氛的好食材。今天分享的培根酥用到培根和市售<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%A5%E7%9A%AE.md#1">酥皮</a>两种材料，12种造型十分钟完成，有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%94%E7%82%B8%E9%94%85.md#1">气炸锅</a>和普通烤箱两种做法。</p>
<h2>料理贴士：</h2>
<p>这里选用脂肪少的中心切<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E6%A0%B9.md#1">培根</a>。觉得培根油脂多，造型前可以切掉培根脂肪。此外，最好使用烤架烘烤，以帮助分离培根油脂，避免点心浸泡其中。</p>
<p>另外，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%A5%E7%9A%AE.md#1">酥皮</a>可以自制。市售则有黄油（又称奶油）、起酥油或植物油等多种选择。</p>
<p>酥皮可以提前一天放在冷藏室解冻。如果从冷冻室取出直接用，需要室温解冻约30分钟。解冻时，若酥皮捏上去可延展，说明解冻过度。这时要将酥皮放入冷藏室至少20分钟再用。</p>
<p>此外，分批烘烤的培根酥，待烤部分需要冷藏保存。</p>
<p>酥皮上面可以刷上芥末等调料增加风味。卷酥皮时，中间要留出面皮蓬发的空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128061" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128061-4e8577ce4a07fda0adaf569255be1472.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128061-4e8577ce4a07fda0adaf569255be1472-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128061" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备：</h2>
<p>培根（center cut）<br />
一张酥皮（Puff pastry sheets），尺寸9.75X10.5英寸<br />
半个鸡蛋，打散成蛋液</p>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1. 将酥皮轻轻展开，用擀面杖或手掌在折痕处按压，使之平展。然后切成12条，逐一摆入培根捏出造型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128062" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128062-c2709c6e84737bf1265d9cff3d1344ba.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128062-c2709c6e84737bf1265d9cff3d1344ba-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128062" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 放入冰箱冷藏至少20分钟至变硬。时间快到时，预热烤箱400华氏度并打散蛋液。<br />
3. 培根酥入炉前刷上蛋液。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128064" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128064-4fa7322149733b0d86e018ad6af0ce57.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128064-4fa7322149733b0d86e018ad6af0ce57-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128064" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 若用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%94%E7%82%B8%E9%94%85.md#1">气炸锅</a>，以350华氏度烤18分钟至表面金黄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128060" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128060-a6a368654900dcfcfdd8e8c32eac9a92.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128060-a6a368654900dcfcfdd8e8c32eac9a92-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128060" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 若用烤箱，以400华氏度烤15分钟至表面金黄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128063-1bce426d4cc22dc6e6c7fe6e7cd5a6f1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128063-1bce426d4cc22dc6e6c7fe6e7cd5a6f1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128063" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 趁热享用培根酥。</p>
