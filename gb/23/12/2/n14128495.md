<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>关山工商校庆登场 林依晨现身抢走目光焦点</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128497-650894-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>林依晨身穿大红T恤，素颜出席，成为了全场的焦点。（周颢承提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）台东<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E5%B1%B1.md#1">关山</a>工商在2日举行创校68周年校庆运动会，盛况空前。除了现场来了许多来宾祝贺，被誉为演艺圈“零负评女神”的艺人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E4%BE%9D%E6%99%A8.md#1">林依晨</a>，也陪同公公前来进行外套捐赠，瞬间成为焦点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128496" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128496-650893-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14128496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128496-650893-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128496" class="wp-caption-text">第二届北海扶轮社社长林光裕，他的媳妇，著名演员<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E4%BE%9D%E6%99%A8.md#1">林依晨</a>，也亲自前来参加活动。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在这个充满欢乐和激情的校庆运动会上，台北“北海扶轮社”特别赶来参与。昨天，该扶轮社已拜会<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E5%B1%B1.md#1">关山</a>镇长彭成丰，而今天，多位曾任社长或社员也慷慨捐赠校务推动基金，展现了对学校的支持。特别是该扶轮社的第二届社长林光裕，他的媳妇，著名演员林依晨，也亲自前来参加活动。林光裕捐赠全校师生每人一件外套，这一慷慨之举引起了全场的关注。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128498" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128498" style="width: 579px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128498-650895-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14128498" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128498-650895-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="579" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128498" class="wp-caption-text">林依晨也分享了她的人生态度，鼓励同学们尽早确立目标，并坚持无惧面对各种挑战，勇往直前。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14128499" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128499" style="width: 554px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128499-650896-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14128499" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128499-650896-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="554" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128499" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E5%B1%B1%E5%B7%A5%E5%95%86.md#1">关山工商</a>在2日举行创校68周年校庆运动会。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林依晨身穿大红T恤，素颜出席，成为了全场的焦点。她展现了亲切和无明星架子的一面，不仅与来宾合影，还不时瞄向场外玩耍的可爱女儿。然而，在开幕典礼即将结束时，烟火燃放的巨大爆炸声响让林依晨顾不得他人，迅速离开现场，原来是为了保护女儿免受惊吓。然而，当爆炸声响结束后，她仍然不让热情的“粉丝”失望，继续与大家合影，展现了她的亲和力和热情。</p>
<p>校长王怡婷对“北海扶轮社”的支持表示感激，强调这些年来该扶轮社在学校的各项事务中都给予了重要的支持。此外，林依晨也分享了她的人生态度，鼓励同学们尽早确立目标，并坚持无惧面对各种挑战，勇往直前。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128501" style="width: 563px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128501-650898-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14128501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128501-650898-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="563" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128501" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E5%B1%B1%E5%B7%A5%E5%95%86.md#1">关山工商</a>校庆运动会，盛况空前。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14128500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128500" style="width: 536px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128500-650897-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14128500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128500-650897-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="536" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128500" class="wp-caption-text">关山工商校庆热闹登场。 （周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
