<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>9种装饰法提升房间温馨感 如度假般轻松自在</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128355-shutterstock_1714586167-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>布置一个舒适雅致的房间，能让睡眠品质更好，更加放松舒压。 (Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>善用几种装饰方法就可以让我们走进房间时感受到一种安定、温暖而愉悦的氛围，那种好似拥抱着你，让人放松休息，犹如沉浸在一本好书、一杯茶香中朴实而自然的气息。</p>
<p>融合大自然元素是提升房间温馨质朴实感最经典的方式，你只需要几个简单的装饰物件就能让卧室更加舒适、有气氛～</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>一、搭配亚麻、藤制品</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14128361" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128361" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128361-shutterstock_2082456313.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128361" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128361-shutterstock_2082456313-600x400.jpg" alt="Bedroom In New Luxury Home With Hardwood Floors Custom Headboard" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128361" class="wp-caption-text">房间里添加亚麻地垫、亚麻沙发布能让空间更加温馨柔和。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在卧室加装天然材质的物品能增添朴实、自然风格，比如挂上亚麻布窗帘、藤编灯罩、藤条床头板，悬挂一些藤蔓墙艺术，或者装有植物盆栽的藤编篮等，简约又有质感。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>二、从浅色床单开始</strong></span></h2>
<p>卧室里最显眼的物品就是床了，铺上米白、白灰或奶油色床单、枕头套，柔软舒适的纯绵布让人想倒卧在蓬松的被褥上；避免过多使用亮白、鲜艳色调，而淡雅的小碎花绵布看起来也很自然。<strong>?</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>三、铺上厚实的针织毯</strong></span></h2>
<p>冬季的房间里铺上一条又大又厚的针织毯，让你的床看起来更加暖和、温馨，选择米色、灰褐色或棕褐色显得雅致、沉静。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>四、创造清爽空间</strong></span></h2>
<p>过多的设计会让房间显得凌乱，容易造成视觉负担，影响心情。规划一下你想采用的装饰元素，适当的留白能提升舒适感。还有尽量避免电线缠绕，将外露的电器电线收纳整齐，会让卧室空间更加清爽。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>五、增添植物元素</strong></span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>1.?</strong><strong>贴上植物印花壁纸</strong></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14128363" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128363" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128363-shutterstock_2276643691.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128363" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128363-shutterstock_2276643691-600x399.jpg" alt="Interior Design Luxury Home Decoration Modern House And Details Beautiful" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128363" class="wp-caption-text">植物图腾增添了空间的生动、艺术感。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在房间的一片或部分墙面贴上植物印花壁纸，这样房间多了大自然的清新感；采用绿色等中性色调的田园山水图腾，能让卧室的氛围多几分闲情逸致。</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>2.?</strong><strong>植物印花抱枕</strong></span></p>
<p>卧室里放几个植物印花抱枕也是妆点大自然风格的好方法。可以从低饱和度的色调入手，如天空蓝、粉黄、粉绿色，如果抱枕缝有荷叶边会更棒。</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>3.?</strong><strong>摆放鲜花或干燥花</strong></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14128369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128369" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128369-shutterstock_1792750597.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128369-shutterstock_1792750597-600x400.jpg" alt="Portrait White Frame Mockup On Retro Wooden Bedside Table Modern" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128369" class="wp-caption-text">应景的干燥花妆点卧室很美丽。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>房间里引进一盆室内植物、放一束鲜花或干燥花，让人感觉充满了大自然的清新朝气；摆上当季的花卉种类，一年四季都能增添卧室空间质感。</p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">六、调和墙壁油漆颜色</span></strong></h2>
<p>朴实的房间墙面颜色大多选用大地色为主调——浅棕、灰、绿、乳白、赤棕等色混合搭配更有层次，可以将一面墙或天花板漆上有抛光效果的亮光漆，房间看起来会更加有生气。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>七、选用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9E%E6%9C%A8%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%B7.md#1">实木家具</a></strong></span></h2>
<p>装置几个天然的的木制品会让你的房间更显朴实雅致，想想看你要从哪里开始～</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>1.?</strong><strong>朴实木凳</strong></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14128370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128370" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128370-shutterstock_2191116087.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128370-shutterstock_2191116087-600x895.jpg" alt="Interior Photography Of A Coastal Boho Style Bedroom With A" width="600" b="895" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128370" class="wp-caption-text">粗犷的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9E%E6%9C%A8%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%B7.md#1">实木家具</a>散发着自然朴实的氛围。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>房间里摆放着粗犷的木凳能增添朴实的气息——在床尾放一张长长的实木凳，或在墙角摆个厚实的小矮凳，最好选购“粗雕造型”特别有原始的大自然韵味。</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>2.?</strong><strong>木质墙壁</strong></span></p>
<p><strong>在卧室的部分墙面</strong>上打造木镶板，并涂上黄绿色或浅棕色系，透着美丽的木纹纹理很有大自然风味；淡雅的色调让人感觉舒适。</p>
<p><strong>3.?</strong><strong>天花板木梁</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14128377" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128377" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128377-shutterstock_528244336.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128377-shutterstock_528244336-600x400.jpg" alt="Komiza Vis Croatia - Aug 15 2015 - Traditional Old" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128377" class="wp-caption-text">将一根木柱立在地面或用做屋梁，都能营造特别的大自然风格。 (Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你家里正要进行<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A4%E5%86%85%E8%A3%85%E4%BF%AE.md#1">室内装修</a>，那么不妨考虑在天花板加装实木横梁，裸露的木头会让整个房间的大自然风格更加鲜明。<strong>?</strong></p>
<p><strong style="color: #000000;">4.?</strong><strong style="color: #000000;">木条装饰</strong></p>
<p>自制一根实木窗帘杆或简单挂衣杆，最好保有木质原色、有点弯曲的线条能更加突显房间朴实的大自然风格。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>八、不使用主灯</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14128379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128379-shutterstock_2100503194.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128379-shutterstock_2100503194-600x400.jpg" alt="Modern Luxury Beautiful Interior With Panoramic Windows Design Bedroom With" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128379" class="wp-caption-text">卧房里安装嵌灯，搭配壁灯或立灯，能营造出多种微妙氛围。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>巧妙的灯光照明能营造房间的氛围，因此挑选适合自己卧室的灯具很重要，为了打造舒适放松的柔和气氛，建议房间采用“一室多灯”的配置，尽量使用间接照明的方式，不使用天花板主灯。</p>
<p>色温也是寝室氛围的重要因素，尽量避免使用刺眼的亮白日光灯，低色温的灯光感觉较柔和，一般大多会使用2700K～3000K的暖色光。</p>
<p>我们可以在床头背墙加装隐藏式灯带；如果有阅读、梳妆、更衣的照明需求，可在床头安装壁灯或桌灯；还可以用圆形或方形开口的嵌灯，增加明亮度，多点式的照明配置能营造舒适放松的空间。<strong>?</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>九、用简单绘画和艺术品装饰墙壁</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14128384" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128384" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128384-shutterstock_1984414667.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128384" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128384-shutterstock_1984414667-600x400.jpg" alt="Bohemian Style Bedroom With Comfortable King Sized Cozy Bed With" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128384" class="wp-caption-text">简单地布置淡雅床幔，能让卧房更加雅致、浪漫。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你希望房间是朴实中带有艺术感，增添一些独特风格，可以选用大自然主题的艺术品，以中性色调来调和房间的静谧与自在。</p>
<p>比如毛绒绒的地毯、人造毛皮的抱枕、漆上淡金色的实木家具、圆弧形床头板、灯罩，陶磁花瓶等装饰都能让朴实的房间“有气质”。</p>
<p>只要避免过多的装饰设计、鲜艳色调，多增添淡雅色、适度的留白，加一点简单的线条或纹理，就能让房间氛围更加朴实自然有质感。◇</p>
