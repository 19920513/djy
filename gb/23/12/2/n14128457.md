<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>落实清源专案  杨梅警屡破大麻森林</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128458-650868-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黄姓及张姓男子(均为53岁)栽种大麻，共查扣大麻植株289余株。（桃园市杨梅警分局   提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月02日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%A2%85%E8%AD%A6.md#1">杨梅警</a>分局落实清源专案，在新竹湖口区与杨梅交界之铁皮屋及桃园新屋区民宅分别查获黄姓及张姓男子(均为53岁)栽种<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%BA%BB.md#1">大麻</a>，共查扣大麻植株289余株、栽种设备及手机等证物，两案均由桃园地检署起诉毒品危害防制条例第12条2项之栽种大麻罪。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%A2%85%E8%AD%A6.md#1">杨梅警</a>分局清查辖内治安人口时，发现黄男疑似有栽种<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%BA%BB.md#1">大麻</a>情事，锁定黄嫌出入之新竹湖口与桃园杨梅交界之铁皮工厂埋守，于112年6月6日黄男出入之际上前，随即发现黄男以铁皮工厂掩护之大麻植栽栽种场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128459" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128459-650869.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128459-650869-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128459" class="wp-caption-text">黄男以铁皮工厂掩护之大麻植栽栽种场。（桃园市杨梅警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14128460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128460-650870.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128460-650870-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128460" class="wp-caption-text">新屋区也查获张男栽种大麻。（桃园市杨梅警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>扩大追查发现，另名张姓男子疑似也在栽种大麻，经专案小组密集跟监搜证，又于6月13日持搜索票，在新屋区也顺利查获张男栽种大麻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128461-650871.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14128461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128461-650871-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128461" class="wp-caption-text">共查扣大麻289株、栽种工具等证物。（桃园市杨梅警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两场合计共查扣大麻289株、栽种工具等证物，查扣的大麻放置于分局礼堂俨然小型大麻森林，杨梅警将持续落实清源专案，查缉各类毒品工厂。◇</p>
