<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>崙背国小夺冠 云林县长杯跆拳道锦标赛缔造佳绩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131663-651895-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>崙背国小感谢崙背道馆教练用心指导。（李秉承提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月07日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%99%E8%83%8C%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">崙背国小</a>的小选手们，在云林县长杯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%86%E6%8B%B3%E9%81%93%E9%94%A6%E6%A0%87%E8%B5%9B.md#1">跆拳道锦标赛</a>场上崭露头角，赢得丰富的奖牌，更荣获国小组的“团体总冠军”。</p>
<figure id="14131664" aria-describedby="caption-14131664" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131664-651896-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131664-651896-450x600.jpg" alt="崙背国小荣获国小组的“团体总冠军”。" width="350" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14131664" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%99%E8%83%8C%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">崙背国小</a>荣获国小组的“团体总冠军”。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14131659" aria-describedby="caption-14131659" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131659-651891-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131659-651891-450x300.jpg" alt="崙背国小夺冠！ 云林县长杯跆拳道锦标赛缔造佳绩。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14131659" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国小夺冠！ <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>县长杯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%86%E6%8B%B3%E9%81%93%E9%94%A6%E6%A0%87%E8%B5%9B.md#1">跆拳道锦标赛</a>缔造佳绩。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>这些小选手们，为了追求梦想，克服种种困难，利用晚上的课余时间在道馆努力训练，勇敢站上赛场，将平常的练习成果充分展现。他们的毅力和奋斗精神令人赞赏。</p>
<p>在这次秋季县长杯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%86%E6%8B%B3%E9%81%93.md#1">跆拳道</a>锦标赛上，崙背国小的小选手们总共赢得19面金牌、14面银牌和9面铜牌，成绩骄人。更令人瞩目的是，他们荣获了国小组的“团体总冠军”，展现出色的成果和团队精神。</p>
<figure id="14131662" aria-describedby="caption-14131662" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131662-651894-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131662-651894-450x337.jpg" alt="校长李秉承期待未来他们会有更好的发展，继续为云林县带来好成绩。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14131662" class="wp-caption-text">校长李秉承期待未来他们会有更好的发展，继续为云林县带来好成绩。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14131661" aria-describedby="caption-14131661" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131661-651893-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131661-651893-450x337.jpg" alt="崙背国小的孩子们展现多才多艺的特点，为学校和崙背乡带来了光彩。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14131661" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国小的孩子们展现多才多艺的特点，为学校和崙背乡带来了光彩。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>崙背国小校长李秉承感谢崙背道馆教练们的用心指导，教练团队不仅教导了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%86%E6%8B%B3%E9%81%93.md#1">跆拳道</a>技巧，更培养了小选手们的毅力和自信。同时，崙背国小的孩子们展现出多才多艺的特点，为学校和崙背乡带来了光彩。</p>
<figure id="14131660" aria-describedby="caption-14131660" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131660-651892-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131660-651892-450x337.jpg" alt="崙背国小的小选手们总共赢得19面金牌、14面银牌和9面铜牌。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14131660" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国小的小选手们总共赢得19面金牌、14面银牌和9面铜牌。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
