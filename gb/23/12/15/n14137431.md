<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北一女、政大两校友会合办聚餐 各自换届</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137423-1-1-1Xiaoyouhui-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿北一女和政大校友会联合主办年终餐会全体与会者合影，其中有几位建中校友来共襄盛举。（章佩儿/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月16日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿摩顿市报导）岁末将至，12月9日中午，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%BF%E5%A4%A7.md#1">政大</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B%E4%BC%9A.md#1">校友会</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E4%B8%80%E5%A5%B3.md#1">北一女</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B%E4%BC%9A.md#1">校友会</a>在摩顿市合办年终聚餐活动，当天60多位双方校友携亲眷、朋友一同参加，还有几位建中校友也来凑热闹。侨教中心主任潘昭荣也应邀出席并致词。之所以合办，实乃两会的干部很多重叠，更绝的是政大会长林丽珠这边卸任，随即在北一女那边接任新会长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137422" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137422-1-1-2Xiaoyouhui.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137422-1-1-2Xiaoyouhui-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137422" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E4%B8%80%E5%A5%B3.md#1">北一女</a>校友会干部合影。左起:公关林游岚、新会长林丽珠、副会长廖珮妤、前会长吴杏玫、财务林晓岚、文书魏君容、卸任会长饶雨涵。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年恰逢北一女中成立120周年，校友梁雅惠提前为庆祝今年12月12日北一女双甲子校庆所剪辑的影片，令远在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>的校友也能感受到母校喜悦的气氛。</p>
<p>北一女校友会会长饶雨涵当天兼主持人，她先邀请大家观看影片，内容回顾波士顿校友会从创办至今所参加的活动和值得记录的时刻。接着财务林晓岚对全年活动收支进行了总结和汇报，饶雨涵还为大家介绍了今年刚刚入会的魏君容以及几位干部。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137421" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137421-1-1-4Xiaoyouhui.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137421-1-1-4Xiaoyouhui-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137421" class="wp-caption-text">贵宾侨教中心主任潘昭荣（中）应邀抽出幸运奖。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后宣布新旧任干部交接，饶雨涵将会长的棒子交给副会长林丽珠，并介绍新团队——副会长廖珮妤、活动及公关林游岚 、财务及通讯林晓岚、文书魏君容，而饶雨涵将续任荣誉理事。</p>
<p>同时，林丽珠作为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%BF%E5%A4%A7.md#1">政大</a>校友会会长对今年政大参加的活动也进行了回顾，包括海外侨胞座谈会、双十游行、国庆晚会、台湾夜市、与北一女校友会合办野餐会&#8230;。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137428-1-1-5Xiaoyouhui.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137428-1-1-5Xiaoyouhui-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137428" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿北一女校友会历届会长合影。左起创会会长赵小怡、江佩蓉、李欣、吴萍萍、吴杏玫、饶雨涵、林丽珠。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林丽珠刚接下北一女会长，因此政大会长的棒子就交给新会长林游岚，并介绍新年度政大校友会干部——副会长吴宇怀、公关龙熙皓、文书吴欣纯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137424" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137424-1-1-3Xiaoyouhui.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137424-1-1-3Xiaoyouhui-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137424" class="wp-caption-text">政大校友会合影。前排左一康韶和、左三新会长林游岚、左四陈家骅、左五卸任会长林丽珠、右一公关龙熙皓，后排左一副会长吴宇怀。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后，饶雨涵与林丽珠联合主持了抽奖活动，主办单位用心准备了很多饶有意义的礼品给“幸运嘉宾”，包括印有北一女中及建国中学字样的袋子。政大校友中最年长的康韶和获得一大瓶酒，把他乐坏了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137425" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137425-1-1-6Xiaoyouhui.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137425-1-1-6Xiaoyouhui-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137425" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿建中校友会会长郑新霖。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137427" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137427-1-1-7Xiaoyouhui.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137427-1-1-7Xiaoyouhui-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137427" class="wp-caption-text">政大校友中最年长的康韶喜获一大瓶酒。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次建中校友会来了李小玉、林致中、周万钦、康韶和、游理聿等会员，会长郑新霖则表示，很高兴能够和两个校友会合办活动，期待未来能有更多的交流与合作。◇</p>
