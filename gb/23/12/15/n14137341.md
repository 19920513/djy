<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>风之频率引领屏东 落山风艺术季盛大登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137347-653559-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>屏东县长周春米15日为落山风艺术季开幕。（黄荣群提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月15日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>报导）屏东县政府举办的“2023<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%BD%E5%B1%B1%E9%A3%8E.md#1">落山风</a>艺术季”于15日隆重登场，为期三个月至2024年2月25日。今年艺术季以“风的频率Frequency of The Wind”为主题，致敬文学巨匠海明威的经典之作《老人与海》。这场以艺术呈现<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%8E%E7%9A%84%E5%8F%98%E5%8C%96.md#1">风的变化</a>为核心的艺术季，汇聚了来自台湾、韩国、日本等17位优秀艺术家，共同展现对自然的敬畏与对生活的独特诠释。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137344" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137344-653556.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137344-653556-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137344" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%BD%E5%B1%B1%E9%A3%8E.md#1">落山风</a>艺术季致敬海明威“老人与海”。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137346" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137346-653558.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137346-653558-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137346" class="wp-caption-text">这场以艺术呈现风的变落山风艺术季汇聚了来自台湾、韩国、日本等17位优秀艺术家。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的艺术季由Art Front Gallery策展，跨国合作呈现了12组户外艺术品和2档室内特展，将现代艺术与大自然完美结合。</p>
<h2>艺术家作品亮点</h2>
<p>1. 清水久和的“风の摄政王”</p>
<p>被强风吹动的狂野飞机头，展现了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>地区落山风的在地日常。</p>
<p>2. 东弘一郎的“风车轮Wind Wheel”</p>
<p>利用屏东废弃的Pbike轮胎打造的风车，运用风力转动，将多变的自然纳入创作元素。</p>
<p>3. Art Front Gallery的“Sailing for Hope”</p>
<p>集结日本及菲律宾知名艺术家，呈现人类为了探索未来在海上寻求希望的航行，展现典型日本大地艺术季的艺术能量。</p>
<p>4. 吉井宏的“Friends of The Wind”</p>
<p>日本雕塑艺术家首次台湾个展，在“待（呆）．风楼展区”创造出空中水族馆的氛围，以风为雏形塑造各种角色生物，融合美丽的色彩、滑稽的调性和幽默的风格。</p>
<h2>地方特色展示</h2>
<h3>1. 沙滩装置艺术</h3>
<p>包括大型“Sylph”、赖彦勋的“那只旗鱼的鼻骨”和詹维欣的“南南”等作品，让人们透过艺术感受风的想像。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137342" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137342-653554.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137342-653554-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137342" class="wp-caption-text">落山风艺术季展期至明年2月25日。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137343" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137343-653555.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137343-653555-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137343" class="wp-caption-text">今年艺术季以“风的频率Frequency of The Wind”为主题。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137345" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137345-653557.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137345-653557-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137345" class="wp-caption-text">落山风艺术季迎跨年和新年观光热潮。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>2. 社区老屋展区“风告诉我”</h3>
<p>由艺术家邱雨玟打造，将老屋打造成时光轮转的空间，传达渔村今昔痕迹和人在自然中的坚韧力量。</p>
<p>艺术季展期至明年2月25日，民众可预期在此感受来自艺术的力量，同时也可参与多样的互动体验，和购买独一无二的纪念品。在艺术与大自然的融合中，让屏东成为艺术的圣地，引领着风的频率，与大地共振。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137348" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137348-653560.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137348-653560-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137348" class="wp-caption-text">落山风艺术季，大型仿生兽漫步海口港。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
