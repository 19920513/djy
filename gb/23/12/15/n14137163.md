<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>土库后埔国小足球队 荣获秋季县长杯亚军</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137164-653502-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>后埔国小中、高年级同获云林县秋季县长杯亚军。（郑桂君提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月15日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%9F%E5%BA%93.md#1">土库</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8E%E5%9F%94%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">后埔国小</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%B3%E7%90%83.md#1">足球</a>队，在云林县秋季县长杯足球锦标赛中表现亮眼，中年级和高年级分别荣获亚军的殊荣。在全县16支队伍的激烈角逐中，球队展现出优异的技术和拼搏精神，深受校内师生以及家长们的支持与鼓励。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137165" style="width: 525px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137165-653503-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137165-653503-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="525" b="295" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137165" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8E%E5%9F%94%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">后埔国小</a>中年级最终以5：3的比分获得亚军。（郑桂君提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>校长黄丽鸿对全校师生以及家长们表示感谢，对于学生们的拼搏精神感到由衷的骄傲，虽然与冠军擦身而过，但教练和家长们均感受到“虽败犹荣”的自豪。</p>
<p>接下来，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%9F%E5%BA%93.md#1">土库</a>后埔国小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%B3%E7%90%83.md#1">足球</a>队的冠、亚军两支队伍将代表<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>县参加明年一月份的全国国民小学世界杯足球赛，家长们将全力支持，持续加强训练，为云林县争光，为学生们提供更多学习和成长的机会。</p>
<p>家长郑桂君特别感谢土库后埔国小的校长黄丽鸿、主任吴怡霏，以及教练团队的苏振华教练。她表示，这三天来他们不仅给予小选手们充分的支持与鼓励，还在比赛中全程陪伴、照顾孩子及记录比赛，表现出色且辛苦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137166" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137166-653504-450x331.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14137166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137166-653504-450x331.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="331" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137166" class="wp-caption-text">后埔国小家长全力支持足球队的发展。（郑桂君提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137167" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137167-653505-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14137167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137167-653505-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137167" class="wp-caption-text">教练们在培训孩子们足球技术的同时，更注重教导礼节与规矩。（郑桂君提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教练们在培训孩子们足球技术的同时，更注重教导礼节与规矩。越是优秀的球员，越应该具备有礼貌和守秩序的品格。比赛中的情绪稳定及受挫力也是教练们强调的重要素养，这将对孩子们未来的成长产生积极的影响。</p>
<p>最后，比赛中展现的“球品”与“人品”，尊重对手的态度也赢得了家长的赞赏。教练们希望这些经验不仅能够在足球场上发光发热，更能成为孩子们未来人生中的宝贵财富。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137168" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137168-653506-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14137168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137168-653506-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137168" class="wp-caption-text">冠、亚军两支队伍将代表云林县参加明年一月份的全国国民小学世界杯足球赛。（郑桂君提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137169" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137169-653507-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14137169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137169-653507-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137169" class="wp-caption-text">在比赛中，孩子们展现出努力拼搏的样子，成为后埔国小的骄傲。（郑桂君提供）</figcaption></figure>
