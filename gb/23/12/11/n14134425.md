<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第66届多伦多国际游艇展明年1月举办10天</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134427-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月12日讯】北美最大规模的室内游艇展——<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E6%B8%B8%E8%89%87%E5%B1%95.md#1">国际游艇展</a>（Toronto International Boat Show），被视为加拿大游艇生活爱好者每个冬天的盛会。第66届展会，将于2024年1月19日至28日，在多伦多湖滨的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%BD%E6%BA%90%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">能源中心</a>（Enercare?Centre）举办。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/12/11/n14134425.md#1/31466e6e168764f58feca00c7efab4d7" rel="attachment wp-att-14134443"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14134443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134443-31466e6e168764f58feca00c7efab4d7-450x693.png" alt="" width="450" b="693" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">除了参观最新款的游艇，了解各类船上设备及驾艇旅游知识，现场购买心仪的游艇<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">（无论大小）</span></span></span>，更有机会赢取高达1万元的奖金。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/12/11/n14134425.md#1/03-457" rel="attachment wp-att-14134429"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14134429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134429-03-450x450.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">不论是钓鱼艇，豪华游艇，帆船，水上摩托艇或是独木舟，以及各种游艇上的用品和服务，行业专家将与您分享最前沿的见解。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/12/11/n14134425.md#1/02-547" rel="attachment wp-att-14134428"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14134428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134428-02-450x450.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">在寒冷的冬日里，约上三五亲朋好友，到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E6%B8%B8%E8%89%87%E5%B1%95.md#1">国际游艇展</a>的世界上最大的室内湖上学习驾驶游艇，体验冲浪，欣赏水上滑水表演吧。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/12/11/n14134425.md#1/4a09d004ab7e97b5f00cc71a74cb1b96" rel="attachment wp-att-14134430"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14134430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134430-4a09d004ab7e97b5f00cc71a74cb1b96-450x338.png" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">更多精彩活动等着您！</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14134431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134431-9c94356ca5a78245d1af60eab52d4791-450x328.png" alt="" width="450" b="328" /></p>
