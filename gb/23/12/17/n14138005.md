<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>寒冬送暖 桃园市育仁国小种菜煮菜做公益</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138006-653686-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>小朋友一早就开心的采收育仁国小“可食地景街”的有机蔬菜。（桃园市育仁国小 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月17日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）寒流来袭，但爱心不减，个子不大、爱心很大的育仁国小学童，在过年前夕，老师们要给小朋友不一样的新年礼物，就是做公益，将在校园亲手种植的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%89%E6%9C%BA%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">有机蔬菜</a>摘下到在地的蓝埔关怀据点煮给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%B1%E5%8A%BF%E4%B9%A1%E4%BA%B2.md#1">弱势乡亲</a>享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138007-653687.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14138007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138007-653687-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138007" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%89%E6%9C%BA%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">有机蔬菜</a>的高丽菜真肥大。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E5%B8%82.md#1">桃园市</a>育仁国小 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138008-653688.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14138008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138008-653688-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138008" class="wp-caption-text">所有的蔬菜都是带领小朋友采用友善无毒的自然农法种植，蔬菜非常的天然健康。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E5%B8%82.md#1">桃园市</a>育仁国小 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小朋友一早就开心的采收育仁国小“可食地景街”的有机蔬菜，包括高丽菜、芥菜和结头菜，辅导主任黄焕勇说，所有的蔬菜都是带领小朋友采用友善无毒的自然农法种植，蔬菜非常的天然健康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138009-653689.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14138009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138009-653689-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138009" class="wp-caption-text">孩子将亲手种植的蔬菜煮给关怀站的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%B1%E5%8A%BF%E4%B9%A1%E4%BA%B2.md#1">弱势乡亲</a>享用。（桃园市育仁国小 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小朋友从松土、种植、抓虫、自制有机肥与天然驱虫液等，从实作中友善环境，培养正确、安全及健康的饮食观念。并将食农教育融入陶艺、木艺、园艺、草编等，营造具美感的可食地景街，可食地景街里不只有孩子的健康力，更有许多孩子的创造力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138010-653690.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14138010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138010-653690-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138010" class="wp-caption-text">孩子将亲手种植的蔬菜煮给关怀站的弱势乡亲享用。（桃园市育仁国小 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138013-653693.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14138013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138013-653693-600x419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138013" class="wp-caption-text">育仁国小农情传蜜意。（桃园市育仁国小 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园观音蓝埔关怀据点徐桂本理事长与徐文宏总干事表示，感谢育仁国小师生来关怀据点做公益，乡亲都很高兴他们的到来，大家一起为社会尽一些心意，让社会充满爱与关怀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138011" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138011-653691.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14138011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138011-653691-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138011" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友帮忙清洗蔬菜。（桃园市育仁国小 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138012-653692.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14138012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138012-653692-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138012" class="wp-caption-text">厨工与小朋友一起炒菜，大家一起为社会尽一些心意。（桃园市育仁国小 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>育仁国小校长张雄腾表示，做公益不用等长大，当孩子开始参与公共事务，就会发现孩子的成长速度与幅度是超乎想像的，所以带孩子将亲手种植的蔬菜煮给关怀站的弱势乡亲享用，培养孩子在实作过程中学习，鼓励孩子从小完成利他助人的好事，建立良好的品德。◇</p>
