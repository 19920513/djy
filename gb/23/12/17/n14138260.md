<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>南女艺文走廊 “绿概念艺术”罗荣源谢碧芬联展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138262-653840-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台南女中艺文活动，展览日期至2023年12月30日止。（苏昭蓉提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8D%97%E5%A5%B3%E4%B8%AD.md#1">台南女中</a>校友会即日起主办一场令人期待的图书馆艺文走廊展覧活动，集结南一中、南女两校校友会的艺术文化交流画展。此次展览更特别邀请到罗荣源<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%BA%E7%AD%91%E5%B8%88.md#1">建筑师</a>与谢碧芬老师，夫妻俩携手举办“绿概念艺术”联展，展览期间细腻呈现他们独特的艺术观点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138267" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138267-653845.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14138267" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138267-653845-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138267" class="wp-caption-text">粉丝开心收到谢碧芬设计的小礼物。（苏昭蓉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138266" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138266-653844.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14138266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138266-653844-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="798" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138266" class="wp-caption-text">谢碧芬亲自导览。（苏昭蓉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展览主题： “绿概念艺术”罗荣源、谢碧芬夫妻联展<br />
展览日期： 至2023年12月30日止。</p>
<p>展览作者简介与创作理念：</p>
<p>【宏观VS微观】：罗荣源，一位从小就不守规矩的创作者，自<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%BA%E7%AD%91%E5%B8%88.md#1">建筑师</a>转型为画家，三十年来挥洒画笔，展现强烈的个人风格。他以建筑师的理工背景及美学素养，创造出宏观视野的作品，深受数学、物理、宇宙天文的启发。罗荣源在作品中充满想像力，表达对生活的隐喻，不拘泥于学院约束，展现建筑师与画家两重身份。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138263" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138263-653841.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14138263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138263-653841-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138263" class="wp-caption-text">罗荣源建筑师与谢碧芬老师夫妻携手举办“绿概念艺术”联展。（苏昭蓉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138264" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138264-653842.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14138264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138264-653842-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138264" class="wp-caption-text">慕名而来的校友相当多，一起来赏画。（苏昭蓉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【微观世界】意外旅程：谢碧芬，一位多才多艺的女性艺术家，在艺术绿色主义的引领下，展现了对艺术的深刻理解。她以剪贴、自动性绘画、复合媒材呈现作品，细致的个性在画中展现，将目光停留在微小的范围，展现丰富的微观世界。谢碧芬将绿色主义提升为超现实存在，带领观者进入一个无限可能的异世界。</p>
<p>【艺术家罗荣源与艺术家谢碧芬理性与感性的情话绵绵】：纪向（画家、艺评家、诗人）在评论中表示，罗荣源的作品呈现超现实的存在，实体与虚构的巧妙结合，展现出他对无限存在可能的探索。而谢碧芬以环保主义为引领，创造出理性与感性同堂的艺术呈现，将现实与理论结合，打造出幻化超现实的存在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138265" style="width: 530px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138265-653843.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14138265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138265-653843-600x344.jpg" alt="" width="530" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138265" class="wp-caption-text">南女中校友谢碧芬赠画给母校。（苏昭蓉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展览期间将提供观众一个难得的机会，同时体验宏观与微观的对话，感受理性与感性的完美交融。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138261" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138261-653839.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14138261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138261-653839-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138261" class="wp-caption-text">南女艺文走廊 “绿概念艺术”罗荣源谢碧芬联展。（苏昭蓉提供）</figcaption></figure>
