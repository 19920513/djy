<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大埤幸福‘稻’了  稻草艺术、表演热闹呈现</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137627-653591-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“2023云林县大埤乡稻草酸菜季活动-幸福‘稻’了”，16日上午热闹开幕。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）“2023云林县大埤乡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A8%BB%E8%8D%89.md#1">稻草</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%B8%E8%8F%9C.md#1">酸菜</a>季活动-幸福‘稻’了”，为期两天的活动16日上午盛大开幕，吸引全台乡亲踊跃参与，共同感受大埤的农村魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137632" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137632-653596.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137632-653596-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137632" class="wp-caption-text">大埤幼儿园精彩表演。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>去年大埤乡以<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A8%BB%E8%8D%89.md#1">稻草</a>迷宫引起轰动，今年更以崭新的稻草艺术呈现，为大埤乡注入全新活力。此次活动以“幸福稻了”为主题，结合在地企业、小农、社区及社群，共同推广大埤的特色，让更多人深入了解这片土地的独特之处。为了提前为民众打造难忘的活动体验，大埤乡“台湾岛语文化教育协会”携手推出“埤东×埤西社区深度之旅”，串联地方景点，让游客提前感受大埤的魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137629" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137629-653593.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137629-653593-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137629" class="wp-caption-text">稻草创作。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137630" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137630-653594.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137630-653594-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137630" class="wp-caption-text">稻草艺术廊道特殊的镜面，反射的出来的景色相当特别。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137633-653597.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137633-653597-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137633" class="wp-caption-text">稻草装置艺术成为热门打卡点。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今日现场不仅有稻草×<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%B8%E8%8F%9C.md#1">酸菜</a>产业体验和艺文表演等丰富亲子活动，还制作了福气吉祥的艺术装置，预告着即将到来的龙年，传递“好运龙总来”的新年祝福。为了推广国产猪肉及本乡特有农产，现场还准备了农村风味的酸菜鲁肉饭及汤品，让民众在品味美食的同时感受在地风情。</p>
<p>除此之外，各摊位上展售的在地特色商品，展现了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>在地的独特魅力，让民众有机会将这份美好带回家，共同分享云林的好物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137631" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137631-653595.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137631-653595-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137631" class="wp-caption-text">美食兑换受到民众青睐。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次活动将持续两天，呈现出大埤朴质的农村气息，让参与的民众充分体验并了解当地的产业特色。两天的表演丰富多元，特别为亲子设计的体验活动和市集更是吸引众多家庭前来共襄盛举，营造了一片欢乐的氛围。祝福大家在大埤体验幸福‘稻’了，共同度过难忘的时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137628" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137628-653592.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137628-653592-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137628" class="wp-caption-text">活动以“幸福稻了”为主题，结合在地企业、小农、社区及社群。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
