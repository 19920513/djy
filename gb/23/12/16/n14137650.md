<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>缘定云林冬恋物语一日游 云县首场单身联谊成功</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137651-653598-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>未来云林县府将人口政策作相关业务串联，形成幸福产业链。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县政府16日成功举办首场县民单身联谊活动，以“缘定云林冬恋物语一日游”为主题，为县内单身男女打造了一场充满浪漫氛围的社交盛事。县长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E4%B8%BD%E5%96%84.md#1">张丽善</a>亲自现身祝福，期望透过这次活动促成更多美好姻缘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137652-653599.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137652-653599-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137652" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E6%94%BF%E5%A4%84.md#1">民政处</a>长萧德恕表示，本次活动参加者都十分积极，让活动充满欢乐愉快的气氛。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在“缘定<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>冬恋物语一日游”中，参与者齐聚云林县政府前广场，展开一场富有创意和活力的单身联谊活动。县长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E4%B8%BD%E5%96%84.md#1">张丽善</a>为男女主角们送上祝福，并期待这场活动成为爱情的契机，搭起幸福的桥梁。</p>
<p>活动由云林县政府出发，带领参与者前往日月潭风景区，其中包括参拜香火鼎盛的龙凤宫月老庙，大家诚心祈求在这场特殊的活动中找到理想的另一半。联谊活动以小组形式进行，游湖闯关活动增进了彼此之间的了解，加深了爱心指数。</p>
<p>旅程最后男女主角们进行互相投票，成功配对的情侣不仅获得配对礼，有机会获得电影券，为下次约会唱出新的续曲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137653-653600.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137653-653600-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137653" class="wp-caption-text">县长张丽善期待本次活动能有佳偶配对成功，一起参加明年的集团结婚。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137655" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137655" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137655-653602.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137655" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137655-653602-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137655" class="wp-caption-text">云林县单身联谊活动“缘定云林冬恋物语一日游”幸福出发~（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137654-653601.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137654-653601-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137654" class="wp-caption-text">最后由男女主角云林县府<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AA%E5%A9%9A%E8%81%94%E8%B0%8A.md#1">未婚联谊</a>活动，如果配对成功的话，可获得配对礼电影券2张，为下次约会唱出续曲。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长张丽善表示，这是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E6%94%BF%E5%A4%84.md#1">民政处</a>首度办理的县民单身联谊活动，报名人数空前，展现了活动的热烈参与度。未来，县政府将进一步串联人口政策，形成幸福产业链，从交往、结婚、生子、到做月子，还有幼儿照顾，一连串的好礼，将提供给今天参与者之外，云林县民也同样享有，期待这次活动能促成更多佳偶的成功配对，一同参与明年的集团结婚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137656" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137656" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137656-653603.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14137656" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137656-653603-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137656" class="wp-caption-text">云林县府首场<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AA%E5%A9%9A%E8%81%94%E8%B0%8A.md#1">未婚联谊</a>活动，16日举行。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
