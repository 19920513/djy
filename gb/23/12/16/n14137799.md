<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>虎尾溪铁道音乐市集盛大登场 五分车成亮点</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137807-653664-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>云林县精致农业生产合作社与云林县农业经理人协会，携手呈献2023虎尾溪铁道音乐市集。（李政雄提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县精致农业生产合作社与云林县农业经理人协会，携手呈献2023虎尾溪<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%81%E9%81%93.md#1">铁道</a>音乐市集，为期一天的盛会于16日在马光有机栽培园区隆重举行。此次活动不仅结合了音乐表演，更融入各项农事体验和食农教育活动，展现台湾西半部最大有机园区的迷人风采。</p>
<p>活动当天，游客聆听溪圳糖香虎尾潮的乐声、欣赏社区舞蹈表演，在马光有机园栽培区感受台湾西半部最大有机园区的魅力。五分糖铁车声缓缓驶入园区，开始诉说这片土地的美好故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137803" style="width: 518px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137803-653660-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137803-653660-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="518" b="291" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137803" class="wp-caption-text">虎尾社区大学表演。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137804" style="width: 510px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137804-653661-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137804-653661-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="510" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137804" class="wp-caption-text">虎尾社区大学表演。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137805" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137805-653662-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14137805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137805-653662-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137805" class="wp-caption-text">虎尾社区大学表演（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，活动特别亮点是民众可以站上日治时期长官巡视火车(135巡道车)，漫步在波斯菊花海中，聆听鸟鸣声，展开一场充满历史与自然风光的交汇之旅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137801" style="width: 364px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137801-653658-450x740.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137801-653658-450x740.jpg" alt="" width="364" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137801" class="wp-caption-text">活动特别亮点是民众可以看到日治时期长官巡视火车(135巡道车)，漫步在波斯菊花海中。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>音乐市集不仅提供丰富的音乐、舞蹈表演，还有食农教育活动，让参与者在品尝美食的同时，了解农业生产的过程，所以16日虎尾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%81%E9%81%93.md#1">铁道</a>音乐市集活动，为地方乡亲献上一场丰富多彩的活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137800" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137800-653657-450x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14137800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137800-653657-450x338.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137800" class="wp-caption-text">马光有机栽培园区前，一大片波斯菊花海。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
