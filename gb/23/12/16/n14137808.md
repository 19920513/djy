<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中华花艺特展“探春—遇见古典花学的春天 ” 云林开展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137817-653656-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>县长张丽善及艺文界人士到场共襄盛举（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）由国立虎尾科技大学廖敦如教授策划，“探春—遇见<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E5%85%B8%E8%8A%B1.md#1">古典花</a>学的春天 <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E8%8A%B1%E8%89%BA.md#1">中华花艺</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%B9%E5%B1%95.md#1">特展</a>”，16日于云林县政府文化观光处展览馆1楼在充满文人气息的氛围下盛大开展，县长张丽善及国内艺文界人士近百人齐聚一堂，体验古典插花艺术之美！并欢迎民众在12/31日前来文观处赏花！</p>
<p>开幕茶会由茑松艺术高中舞蹈科学生，以12月份之水仙花神舞揭开序幕，并结合宋朝文人四艺之“点茶”的茶席，为“探春”花展开幕！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137818" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137818" style="width: 540px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137818-653665-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137818" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137818-653665-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="540" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137818" class="wp-caption-text">开幕茶会结合宋朝文人四艺之“点茶”的茶席，为“探春”花展增添许多人文气息。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137819" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137819" style="width: 521px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137819-653666-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137819-653666-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="521" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137819" class="wp-caption-text">开幕茶会由茑松艺术高中舞蹈科学生，以12月份之水仙花神舞揭开序幕。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>策展人廖敦如表示，“探春不为桑、探春不为麦；日日出西围，只望花柳色”是唐朝诗人孟郊描写春游的心境，“花”是天地慧黠，古典插花艺术是一项即将失传且待传承的文化技艺，在大寒节气里，27位花艺家用温暖的在地花卉与艺术形式迎向“立春”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137811" style="width: 528px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137811-653641-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137811" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137811-653641-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="528" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137811" class="wp-caption-text">展出的作品之一。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花艺也是一项非常具有文化资产保存价值的技艺，透过这次的展览，让云林优秀的花艺家呈现自己创作，展现云林不同的艺术；另外云林县有非常丰富的花卉产业，包括文心兰、洋桔梗、桂竹等都运用在展览设计中，让艺术可以结合产业，让艺术有跨领域呈现，欢迎云林的乡亲在展览期间可以来共襄盛举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137812" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137812" style="width: 527px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137812-653642-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137812-653642-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="527" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137812" class="wp-caption-text">写景花。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张丽善表示，在云林有缤纷的花卉，洋吉梗、文心兰、兰花等等美不胜收，在岁末时刻，花艺家应用在地的花材，并结合<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E8%8A%B1%E8%89%BA.md#1">中华花艺</a>瓶、盘、缸、碗、篮、筒等六大花器，透过写景、造型、心象、理念等表现手法，把中华文化传统美学与云林在地花卉结合起来，透过花艺专家把云林花卉变得更美丽，也让大家欣赏不同花器与不同花艺所呈现的美学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137815" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137815" style="width: 524px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137815-653650-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137815" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137815-653650-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="524" b="349" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137815" class="wp-caption-text">作品繁华春色。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137809" style="width: 494px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137809-653639-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137809-653639-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="494" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137809" class="wp-caption-text">廖敦如(左)和她的作品，右为文观处长陈璧君。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137810" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137810-653640-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14137810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137810-653640-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137810" class="wp-caption-text">展出的作品之一。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137813" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137813" style="width: 515px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137813-653643-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14137813" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137813-653643-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="515" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137813" class="wp-caption-text">造型花。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文化观光处长陈璧君表示，中华花艺是全方位艺术，极具东方文化辨识度及古典美学的内涵度，此展览一共有35件作品，参展的花艺家有27位，除了县内优秀的花艺家之外，还有多位旅居外地也是云林女儿之花艺家，也应邀此展览回云林参展。</p>
<p>本次展览一共分为四个主题区，分别为“器用为美”区，展现中华花艺六大花器之美；主题二“型形得趣”区—介绍中华花艺四大花型；主题三“文人游心”区—呈现文人的闲情雅趣；主题四“岁寒迎曦”&#8211;透过年花设计展现甲辰年之迎春创作。</p>
<p>一场“探春”的花艺展，以花入境，藉花表意，邀请民众一同品花、赏花，遇见<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E5%85%B8%E8%8A%B1.md#1">古典花</a>学的春天。展期12/16日—12/31日止。</p>
