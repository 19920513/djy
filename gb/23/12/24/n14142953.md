<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>关怀弱势 恩主公慈善会举办寒冬送暖爱心园游会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142954-655243-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>恩主公慈善会会长颜忠义议员与表演团队合影。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）由云林县恩主公慈善会举办的【关怀饥饿、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%92%E5%86%AC%E9%80%81%E6%9A%96.md#1">寒冬送暖</a>】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%BF%83%E5%9B%AD%E6%B8%B8%E4%BC%9A.md#1">爱心园游会</a>，24日上午于该会馆观心园热闹举行，吸引上千人参与，现场热闹滚滚，今年除了发送近600份物资给中低收入户及边缘户，还有爱心义剪、春联挥毫、发票兑换、节目表演及民众最期盼的抽奖活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142955" style="width: 522px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142955-655245-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14142955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142955-655245-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="522" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142955" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友太鼓表演。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>笃信“关公”的恩主公慈善会会长颜忠义议员表示，慈善会多年来秉持关圣帝君守护众生的精神，已连续举办9年“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%92%E5%86%AC%E9%80%81%E6%9A%96.md#1">寒冬送暖</a>、关怀饥饿”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%BF%83%E5%9B%AD%E6%B8%B8%E4%BC%9A.md#1">爱心园游会</a>，针对斗南、大埤、古坑三乡镇公所立案之边缘户、中低收入户，除了发送每户一份日常生活物资外，还赠送春联、100元园游卷和摸彩券及免费义剪，让这些中低收入户提前感受过年的气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142957" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142957" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142957-655248-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14142957" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142957-655248-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142957" class="wp-caption-text">云林县理烫发美容联谊会也参与“义剪” 活动。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，今年慈善会不只做公益，也帮助地方产业发展，除募集近60个爱心摊位参与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%89%E5%8D%96.md#1">义卖</a>活动，将义卖所得全数捐助这次的寒冬送暖活动，在园游会的后方，也提供给斗南得奖的优质商家设摊，来推广他们的产品！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142960" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142960" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142960-655254-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14142960" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142960-655254-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142960" class="wp-caption-text">云林县议会议长黄凯这次也共襄盛举，赞助10份的摸彩奖品，并专程到场抽奖。 2114 丰田社区发展协会也加入义（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14142959" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142959" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142959-655252-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14142959" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142959-655252-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142959" class="wp-caption-text">丰田社区发展协会也加入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%89%E5%8D%96.md#1">义卖</a>摊位行列。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云林县议会议长黄凯这次也共襄盛举，赞助10份的摸彩奖品，并专程到场抽奖，他非常肯定颜忠义议员的热心公益，长年关怀弱势的善举；云林县救国团真善美联谊会，也爱心不落人后的参与这次义卖活动，会员们把自家生产的养生鸡蛋及柳丁，拿出来义卖；恩主公黄老师书法班也参与春联挥毫，黄老师说，为了这次活动，他们事前就先写了500副春联，希望让每个人都能拿到春联开心过年！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142956" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142956" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142956-655247-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14142956" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142956-655247-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142956" class="wp-caption-text">恩主公黄老师书法班也参与这次春联挥毫。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14142962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142962" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142962-655260-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14142962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142962-655260-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142962" class="wp-caption-text">救国团真善美联谊会，也爱心不落人后的参与这次义卖活动，会员们把自家生产的养生鸡蛋及柳丁，拿出来义卖。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>颜忠义说，“消除贫穷、终止饥饿”是联合国SDGs永续发展目标的首要项目，感谢各界善心团体、善心人士、志工群、会员们大家的爱心认购及付出，让这次的爱心活动能顺利圆满，也让弱势家庭感受社会的温暖！</p>
