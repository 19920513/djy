<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园改装排气管从“标准罚”改为“行为罚”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143837-655553-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>警方执行静桃专案，拦查噪音车至指定地点检测噪音分贝。（桃园市桃园警分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月26日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）为改善<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%99%AA%E9%9F%B3.md#1">噪音</a>车扰民问题，桃园市政府于今年9月22日起正式公告，将改装<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E6%B0%94%E7%AE%A1.md#1">排气管</a>从“标准罚”改为“行为罚”，只要违规行为经查证属实，可开罚3000元至3万元罚锾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14143838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143838" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143838-655554.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14143838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143838-655554-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143838" class="wp-caption-text">利用线上各项勤务加强拍照通报给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E4%BF%9D%E5%B1%80.md#1">环保局</a>，依照《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%99%AA%E9%9F%B3.md#1">噪音</a>管制法》第8条径行裁处。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市桃园警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14143839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143839-655555.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14143839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143839-655555-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143839" class="wp-caption-text">强力执行“静桃专案”，提醒各驾驶人留意，避免荷包失血。（桃园市桃园警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园警分局自9月22日公告后，利用线上各项勤务加强拍照通报给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E4%BF%9D%E5%B1%80.md#1">环保局</a>，依照《噪音管制法》第8条径行裁处，已经达154件。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14143840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143840" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143840-655556.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14143840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143840-655556-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143840" class="wp-caption-text">检测噪音情形。（桃园市桃园警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另持续携手环保及监理单位，执行联合稽查检测噪音车勤务，噪音超标当场开单告发的也达304件，拍照函请环保局通知复验的更达2500件，后续仍将强力执行“静桃专案”，提醒各驾驶人留意，避免荷包失血。◇</p>
