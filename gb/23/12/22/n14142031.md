<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】石榴烤火鸡~甜酸好滋味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142045-070875fe13e7d780efe79ec9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>自制的新鲜石榴糖浆甜酸好滋味，除了烤火鸡香喷喷，烤鸡也不赖。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月27日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E6%A6%B4.md#1">石榴</a>寓意美好，滋味酸酸甜甜惹人喜爱。平时石榴多是榨汁冷吃，其实用来烤肉也相当美味呢。今天给大家带来一道用新鲜石榴烘烤的火鸡，脆脆的火鸡皮涂满石榴糖浆，甚是美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142052" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142052-a0d8c57ddbad32f837eecca1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14142052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142052-a0d8c57ddbad32f837eecca1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142052" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14142049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142049" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142049-f756979039401f873aaa2165.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14142049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142049-f756979039401f873aaa2165-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142049" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>预腌制新鲜火鸡，18磅</p>
<p>鸡腔填料：新鲜香草，百里香、鼠尾草、迷迭香、1个柠檬切片、1个洋葱切片</p>
<p>鸡皮涂料：半条融化黄油混合黑胡椒、新鲜百里香</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E6%A6%B4.md#1">石榴</a>糖浆：1个石榴的果肉混合2汤匙糖熬煮成蜜汁</p>
<p>厨具准备</p>
<p>火鸡烤架、厨用剪刀、肉类温度计、厨房纸巾、锡箔纸和厨用手套。（选购、处理火鸡点<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/11/22/n14121902.md#1">这里</a>）</p>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1.开启烤箱热风烤功能（conv roast），预热325华氏度。<br />
2.剪掉颈部多余脂肪，留下鸡皮包裹鸡脖子空洞。取出鸡腔里的内脏和鸡脖子另作他用，擦干血水。移除鸡尾部的白色环扣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142217" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142217-cfc708b0fc0e3f1bb24da018a59505c3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14142217" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142217-cfc708b0fc0e3f1bb24da018a59505c3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142217" class="wp-caption-text">(作者提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.微波炉20秒融化黄油，加入黑胡椒、新鲜百里香拌匀，涂抹鸡皮。<br />
4.鸡翅尖别到鸡背，取锡箔纸包裹鸡腿尖端和覆盖鸡胸，以避免过早烤焦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142047" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142047" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142047-5894cc75b3336f0bd58acfec.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14142047" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142047-5894cc75b3336f0bd58acfec-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142047" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.煮石榴糖浆：石榴果肉榨汁，加糖熬煮约20分钟变成蜜汁，用网筛滤去杂质使用。<br />
6.火鸡烤3小时撤下锡箔纸，再烤40分钟出炉。出炉前刷石榴糖浆1至2次（酱汁薄厚依个人喜好），烤20分钟。<br />
7.出炉静置30分钟再分切。（分切方法点<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/11/22/n14121902.md#1">这里</a>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142046" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142046-1474cba15d8ca9b2ff9ae18a.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14142046" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142046-1474cba15d8ca9b2ff9ae18a-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142046" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
