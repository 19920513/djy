<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>周海媚病例单在网络曝光 临终细节惊人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14135008-DSC_8748.jpg" />
<h6>第53届电视金钟奖颁奖嘉宾周海媚彩排记者会于2018年10月5日在台北举行。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月13日讯】（大纪元记者夏松报导）12月12日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%B5%B7%E5%AA%9A.md#1">周海媚</a>工作室发文称，周海媚因病医治无效，于11日离世，未提及<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%BB%E4%B8%96.md#1">去世</a>原因。不过，网传消息与医院电子病历透露，周海媚被送到医院时，已丧失意识，心跳停止。</p>
<p>11日晚，有网友在微博爆料港星<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%B5%B7%E5%AA%9A.md#1">周海媚</a>离世，之后有媒体求证，周海媚工作人员挂断电话，随后“周海媚”冲上微博热搜第一。</p>
<p>据面馆业主刘伟纪透露，周海媚从北京<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A1%BA%E4%B9%89.md#1">顺义</a>家送往医院时，已经死亡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14134982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14134982" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134982-9a23b7a715adee3ec7947330563e9a10.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14134982" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134982-9a23b7a715adee3ec7947330563e9a10.png" alt="" width="448" b="472" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14134982" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>刘伟纪随后说：“周海媚的死亡消息略为修改，所以删了前面那个，朋友希望我等通稿，目前的确切讯息是死在（北京<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A1%BA%E4%B9%89.md#1">顺义</a>）MOMA万万树（小区）家中，进一步等家属发讣告与医院的死亡证书后再官宣。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14134984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14134984" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134984-20231212_17024009195489.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14134984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134984-20231212_17024009195489-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14134984" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周海媚<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%BB%E4%B8%96.md#1">去世</a>传闻发酵后，陆续有圈内人出面辟谣。</p>
<p>对此，刘伟纪表示，“那些都别信，我的消息是最亲近的关系，我都有周海媚的微信，她以前老来我这儿吃饭。”“从爆出消息到现在都还没人出来证实为假，我问的是她身边的好友，这种就是在等通稿，已经有报120记录。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14134983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14134983" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134983-20231212_17024009195604.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14134983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134983-20231212_17024009195604-600x328.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="328" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14134983" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14134987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14134987" style="width: 445px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134987-d3313021b535e05190dd01c6c7964500.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14134987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134987-d3313021b535e05190dd01c6c7964500.png" alt="" width="445" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14134987" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，自称是知情者的网友爆料，周海媚11日下午在北京顺义自家别墅突然晕倒，工作人员紧急报警送医。爆料者描述，周海媚被发现时已经不行了。</p>
<p>更为详细的信息，来自一张网传北京<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB%E9%99%A2.md#1">中医院</a>顺义医院门诊电子病历。病历内容显示，12月11日11时11分，急诊科接待了一位名为周海媚的57岁女子，“发现意识丧失，心跳停止1小时”。</p>
<p>现病史：患者半素独居，今天10:00时许同事发现患者倒地，二便（大小便）失禁，意识丧失，遂拨打120前来。近1周患者咳嗽、喘憋，于家中自行吸氧，昨日（10日）夜间喘憋严重。（同事代诉）</p>
<p>既往史：高血压、系统性红斑性狼疮。</p>
<p>体格检查：意识丧失，全身僵硬、冰凉，可见尸班，双侧瞳孔散大，结膜出血，对光反应消失，心跳停止，呼吸停止。</p>
<p>西医诊断：心脏停搏，意识丧失，呼吸停止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14134995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14134995" style="width: 649px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134995-20231212_17024058751746.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14134995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14134995-20231212_17024058751746.jpg" alt="" width="649" b="365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14134995" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网传北京<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB%E9%99%A2.md#1">中医院</a>顺义医院门诊电子病历所显示信息，与周海媚本人生活、身体状况高度吻合。</p>
<p>11日，也有媒体从周海媚好友香港演员田启文处获悉：周海媚在北京一家医院接受治疗，周海媚此前长期受红斑狼疮困扰。据悉，周海媚1998年患上红斑狼疮，曾多次复发，严重时关节会红肿发炎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14135005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14135005" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14135005-PO_X1704.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14135005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14135005-PO_X1704-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14135005" class="wp-caption-text">周海媚。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14135007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14135007" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14135007-20150420-fanbingbing-kiri-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14135007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14135007-20150420-fanbingbing-kiri-9-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14135007" class="wp-caption-text">周海媚。（蔡雯文／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周海媚因出演《倚天屠龙记》获封“最美周芷若”，上周三（12月6日）刚刚度过57岁生日。生日当天，其工作室还转发了她的短视频，并写道：“今天，57岁啦！感恩你们多年来的喜爱和陪伴，愿大家健康、平安、喜乐！”</p>
<p>周海媚最后一次公开亮相是今年11月，她出席了第九届雅安大熊猫与自然电影周活动。12月7日，周海媚工作室还曾发布动态，更新了她的照片。</p>
