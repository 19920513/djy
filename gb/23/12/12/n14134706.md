<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>素里1月1日起新增快捷巴士R6</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月12日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">杨清清</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大温哥华报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日，</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%90%E8%81%94.md#1">运联</a>（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">TransLink</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）宣布，明年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月将在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%A0%E9%87%8C.md#1">素里</a>启动新的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E4%BA%A4%E5%BF%AB%E6%8D%B7.md#1">公交快捷</a>线</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">R6 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这条新路线可使斯科特路（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Scott Road</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">的公交车行程单向提速</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分钟，在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%A0%E9%87%8C.md#1">素里</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E8%A7%92%E6%B4%B2.md#1">三角洲</a>最繁忙的公交走廊上为乘客提供多达</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 20% </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的载客能力。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">　　</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">R6 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">将于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日开通，是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%90%E8%81%94.md#1">运联</a>自</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年以来最大的一次服务扩展。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日期间，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">R6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">路将按假日时间表运营，于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日正式开通，随后将提供正常服务。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">这将是运联在大温哥华地区提供的第六条快捷巴士线服务，具有高发车频率、享有公交优先车道、有限站点、乘客可从所有门同时上车等特点。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">　　</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">目前斯科特路走廊是菲沙河以南最繁忙的公交走廊，每</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">周</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的公交乘客超过</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">30,000</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人次，是大温哥华地区乘客增长最强劲的公交走廊。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">作为运联冬季服务更新的一部分，从</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日开始，将对</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">条公交线路进行调整，包括在一天中最繁忙的时段增加</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">条线路的服务。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">乘客可登录</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> translink.ca/servicechanges</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，了解有关具体线路变化的更多信息。公交车站的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">QR</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">码也将首次引导乘客查看翻译成简体中文、繁体中文和旁遮普语的服务变更信息。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">　　</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">公交服务变更每年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月进行一次；这些变更基于季节性模式和乘客需求。运联</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">表示，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">将继续监控整个地区的乘客数量，以确保在最需要的地方提供服务。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span></p>
