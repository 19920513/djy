<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台中第二市场 老饕必访的古早味美食天堂</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132484-652132-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>今年11/28开始卖冬天的肉圆汤。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月08日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台中报导）台中<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E8%BD%A6%E7%AB%99.md#1">火车站</a>附近的第二市场，一直以来都是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E9%A5%95.md#1">老饕</a>客心中的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>热点。这个位于台中市中区三民路与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%A4%A7%E9%81%93.md#1">台湾大道</a>一段的市场，汇集了数十家古早味小吃摊，为游客提供丰富多元的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>选择。无论是早晨的早餐、午间的午餐，还是宵夜，这里都能满足你的味蕾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132483-652131.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14132483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132483-652131-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132483" class="wp-caption-text">台中第二市场，八十岁了。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14132486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132486-652134.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14132486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132486-652134-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132486" class="wp-caption-text">台中第二市场里面，有好几家不同风格的咖啡厅。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 相思面店 &#8211; 菜头粿糯米肠，特制的辣椒酱和麻酱，游客力推。 早晨6点多就开始营业的相思面店，是上班族的早餐首选。他们主打“吃粗饱”的概念，提供多种早餐组合，包括菜头粿、芋头粿和蛋。这些美食在铁板上煎得香脆，淋上特制的米酱，味道绝配。此外，相思面店的干面淋上自制的麻酱，香气十足，让人回味无穷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132488" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132488-652136.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14132488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132488-652136-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132488" class="wp-caption-text">相思面店，菜头粿、糯米肠淋上特制的米酱。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14132485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132485" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132485-652133.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14132485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132485-652133-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132485" class="wp-caption-text">相思面店 ，现煎菜头粿、糯米肠。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 老赖豆浆红茶</p>
<p>台中第二市场内有一家著名的饮料摊，就位于相思面店的对面，名为老赖豆浆红茶。这里的豆浆红茶是必点之一，无论是搭配萝卜糕或其他小吃，都能满足你的口腹之欲，而且不用排队太久，快速品尝美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132487-652135.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14132487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132487-652135-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132487" class="wp-caption-text">老赖红茶平日的人潮。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 阿月寿司</p>
<p>阿月寿司是第二市场的隐藏宝藏，提供平价的寿司、生鱼片和海鲜丼。他们早上八点就开始营业，是想在白天享受寿司美味的理想之地。请注意，他们的营业时间只到下午两点，所以不要太晚造访。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132482" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132482" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132482-652130.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14132482" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132482-652130-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132482" class="wp-caption-text">阿月寿司，早上八点就可以吃到日本料理了。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 承记肉圆汤</p>
<p>承记肉圆汤是市场里的另一个美食热点，虽然冬天不卖米苔目和粉粿，但他们冬天的汤圆(这边称肉圆汤)和肉羹同样美味可口。汤圆外皮有着令人喜爱的口感，肉馅也令人赞不绝口，配上茼蒿菜，很有妈妈的味道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132481-652129.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14132481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132481-652129-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132481" class="wp-caption-text">老赖红茶和承记肉圆汤，二个一起吃，游客称绝配。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台中第二市场不仅以美食闻名，还保留着80年前日治时期的风貌，使游客在品味美食的同时，能够感受到这个市场的独特魅力。市场内的屋顶和楼梯等古迹，都值得一一探索，让您在视觉和味觉上都享受丰富的旅程。</p>
<p>无论您是当地居民还是游客，台中<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E8%BD%A6%E7%AB%99.md#1">火车站</a>第二市场绝对是一个令人愉快的美食天堂，值得多次光临，一探其古早味美食的魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132489-652137.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14132489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132489-652137-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132489" class="wp-caption-text">台中第二市场不仅以美食闻名，还保留着80年前日治时期的风貌。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
