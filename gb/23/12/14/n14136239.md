<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉兰国小幼儿园启用 循鲁凯族传统仪式庆贺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136240-653216-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东县金峰乡嘉兰国小新建幼儿园落成启用，采鲁凯族传统嫁娶婚礼仪式庆贺。（台东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月14日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）台东县金峰乡嘉兰国小新建<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BC%E5%84%BF%E5%9B%AD.md#1">幼儿园</a>14日举行落成启用典礼，学校特别使用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%81%E5%87%AF%E6%97%8F.md#1">鲁凯族</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F.md#1">传统</a>嫁娶婚礼的仪式，用秋千架以及各式各样聘礼，迎娶金峰乡立幼儿园到嘉兰国小附设幼儿园，祝贺两园所能共同为幼儿的学习成长而努力，期盼崭新的园所能使<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">文化</a>扎根部落。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14136241" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14136241" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136241-653217.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14136241" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136241-653217-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14136241" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>县金峰乡嘉兰国小新建<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BC%E5%84%BF%E5%9B%AD.md#1">幼儿园</a>落成启用，采<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%81%E5%87%AF%E6%97%8F.md#1">鲁凯族</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F.md#1">传统</a>嫁娶婚礼仪式庆贺。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14136242" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14136242" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136242-653218.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14136242" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136242-653218-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14136242" class="wp-caption-text">台东县金峰乡嘉兰国小新建幼儿园落成启用，采鲁凯族传统嫁娶婚礼仪式庆贺。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14136243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14136243" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136243-653219.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14136243" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136243-653219-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14136243" class="wp-caption-text">台东县金峰乡嘉兰国小新建幼儿园落成启用，采鲁凯族传统嫁娶婚礼仪式庆贺。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县府教育处说明，新幼儿园园舍为二层楼附电梯的建筑，建置无障碍设施，教室共2间，设计概念以“空间的开放”与“使用者的安全与多元运用”为原则，融入嘉兰村美好风光景色及国小整体设计，让学校具备美感又实用的学习空间。</p>
<p>县长饶庆铃表示，嘉兰国小位于东64县道旁，为社区型的学校，原本幼儿园空间不足，中央及地方通力合作，投入近3000万元兴建，并提升园内设施设备，收托人数也从1班15人增加至2班60人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14136245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14136245" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136245-653221.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14136245" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136245-653221-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14136245" class="wp-caption-text">台东县金峰乡嘉兰国小新建幼儿园。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
