<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第二届多伦多假日艺术品集市本周六仅一天</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136538-1-600x400.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月15日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%87%E6%97%A5.md#1">假日</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%93%81%E9%9B%86%E5%B8%82.md#1">艺术品集市</a>（Toronto Art Crawl Holiday Market ）第二届活动，将于本周六（12月16日）带来一场绝对不容错过的购物盛宴。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/12/14/n14136533.md#1/4-888" rel="attachment wp-att-14136541"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14136541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136541-4-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">主办方邀请您本周六，前往令人惊叹的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>湖滨俱乐部，位于展览中心（Exhibition Place, 15 Saskatchewan Rd）的Grand Bizarre，度过充满艺术奇迹、独特发现和欢庆节日的一天。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/12/14/n14136533.md#1/3-1209" rel="attachment wp-att-14136540"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14136540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136540-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="450" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">120 多位当地艺术家和供应商，将带来各种手工珍品，包括艺术、摄影、木制品、珠宝、服装、配件、蜡烛、时装、甜点、沐浴和美容产品，还有迷人的书籍。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/12/14/n14136533.md#1/2-1520" rel="attachment wp-att-14136539"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14136539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136539-2-450x400.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="533" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">为节日购物清单上的每位亲朋寻找独一无二的完美礼物？来多伦多<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%87%E6%97%A5.md#1">假日</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%93%81%E9%9B%86%E5%B8%82.md#1">艺术品集市</a>吧，支持当地的工匠，并把独特的物品带回家，赋予这个假日特别的意义。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/12/14/n14136533.md#1/e70a1fbc278086d5c4a6cfda2a5dc7b4" rel="attachment wp-att-14136548"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14136548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136548-e70a1fbc278086d5c4a6cfda2a5dc7b4-450x269.png" alt="" width="600" b="358" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">享受节日购物气氛的同时，品尝各种甜点，让自己沉浸在一丝丝甜蜜之中。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/12/14/n14136533.md#1/5-721" rel="attachment wp-att-14136542"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14136542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136542-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">与当地艺术家和企业交流，获取艺术、文化和欢庆节日的灵感吧。<strong>免费入场！</strong></p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/12/14/n14136533.md#1/f7642ce2b285b7d4f61eddfd801e5a6b" rel="attachment wp-att-14136543"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-14136543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136543-f7642ce2b285b7d4f61eddfd801e5a6b-450x345.png" alt="" width="600" b="460" /></a></p>
<h2 class="p1"><strong>多伦多假日艺术品集市</strong></h2>
<p class="p1">时间：</p>
<p class="p1">12 月 16 日（星期六），中午 12 点至 5 点</p>
<p class="p1">地点：</p>
<p class="p1">Grand Bizarre（15 Saskatchewan Rd , Exhibition Place）</p>
