<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>草悟道圣诞村开跑 8米高“圣诞萌犬”陪你过节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136046-653127-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>草悟道街头现身近8公尺、4公尺高的“BIG DOG”，由日本艺术家“matsui”创作。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月14日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%AD.md#1">台中</a>报导）随着冬日的脚步悄悄临近，台中<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E6%82%9F%E9%81%93.md#1">草悟道</a>街区和紧邻的勤美诚品绿园道，再度焕然一新，举办一年一度的盛大冬日<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E5%85%B8.md#1">庆典</a>-“草悟道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E6%9D%91.md#1">圣诞村</a>”，今年已迈入第14届的活动，以全新品牌“PARKLANE CHRISTMAS”呈现，打造出一个缤纷欢乐的圣诞村，为大家带来无尽的欢笑与惊喜。</p>
<p><strong>【<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E6%9D%91.md#1">圣诞村</a>五大打卡亮点】</strong></p>
<p><strong>1. BIG DOG：超巨型“圣诞日本萌犬” </strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E6%82%9F%E9%81%93.md#1">草悟道</a>街头现身近8公尺、4公尺高的“BIG DOG”，由日本艺术家“matsui”创作，以“Sharing Love”为主题，呈现出巨大的圣诞狗狗和红色小碗，疗愈感十足。这限定作品不仅可爱好拍，更蕴含了圣诞节的分享精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14136048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14136048" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136048-653129.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14136048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136048-653129-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14136048" class="wp-caption-text">“草悟道圣诞村”！今年已迈入第14届的活动。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2. 好多好多红帽子：巨红圣诞帽 </strong></p>
<p>勤美诚品绿园道户外竟然出现了一群戴着巨大圣诞帽的帽子们，进入其中，仿佛穿越至圣诞的神奇世界，银河流域中满布圣诞文字光点，瞬间燃点节庆氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14136049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14136049" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136049-653130.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14136049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136049-653130-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14136049" class="wp-caption-text">好多好多红帽子：巨红圣诞帽。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3. 圣诞帽泡泡：梦幻泡泡雨中的圣诞奇景 </strong></p>
<p>在勤美诚品绿园道，一座高达6米的圣诞帽宛如从天而降，灯球闪烁映照在镜球上，打造梦幻泡泡雨。进入这片云端空间，慵懒的懒骨头与圣诞造型充气枕，让人尽情坐躺，共同享受这场冬季限定的泡泡雨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14136047" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14136047" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136047-653128.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14136047" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136047-653128-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14136047" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞帽泡泡：梦幻泡泡雨中的圣诞奇景在勤美诚品绿园道。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>4. 音乐快闪市集：唱片行汇聚音乐热情 </strong></p>
<p>12月16日，草悟道圣诞村邀请国内外各大音乐唱片行，推出1日限定的音乐快闪市集，带给大家满满的音乐热情，让节庆氛围更添热闹。</p>
<p><strong>5. 街头艺人、马戏表演：缤纷精彩不容错过 </strong></p>
<p>整个圣诞节周末，草悟道街头将弥漫着街头艺人的欢笑声和马戏表演的惊奇瞬间，增添了更多的精彩元素，让游客在欢笑声中共度难忘时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14136050" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14136050" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136050-653131.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14136050" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136050-653131-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14136050" class="wp-caption-text">12月16日，草悟道圣诞村邀请国内外各大音乐唱片行，推出1日限定的音乐快闪市集。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14136051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14136051" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136051-653132.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14136051" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14136051-653132-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14136051" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%AD.md#1">台中</a>草悟道和勤美诚品绿园道，再度焕然一新。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
