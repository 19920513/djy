<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新泽西蛋港市圣诞点灯游行 法轮功队伍添姿彩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139337-signal-2023-12-16-211436_002-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>2023年12月16日，法轮功腰鼓队带来《法轮大法好》和《铃儿响叮当》的乐曲。（腰鼓队提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月19日讯】（大纪元记者林丹<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E6%B3%BD%E8%A5%BF.md#1">新泽西</a>州蛋港市（Egg Harbor City）12月16日晚举行<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E.md#1">圣诞</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B9%E7%81%AF%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">点灯游行</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md#1">法轮功</a>腰鼓队、莲花舞蹈队参加，这是他们第二年参加这个游行，给当地的民众带去中华传统文化和法轮大法的福音。<br />
<a title="Egg harbor City NJ Christmas Parade on Dec 16 2023" src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/zh-TW/embed/1gclm5p67f27KWxD0AxwjBkHU1hm1c" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_14139348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139348" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139348-IMG_2762.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14139348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139348-IMG_2762-533x400.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139348" class="wp-caption-text">2023年12月16日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md#1">法轮功</a>腰鼓队带来《法轮大法好》和《铃儿响叮当》的乐曲。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139349" style="width: 592px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139349-IMG_2895.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14139349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139349-IMG_2895-592x400.jpg" alt="" width="592" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139349" class="wp-caption-text">法轮功莲花舞蹈队为游行增添色彩。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>游行中，数十辆挂满彩灯的车子流淌在装点着<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E.md#1">圣诞</a>装饰的街道上。腰鼓队表演了《法轮大法好》以及圣诞歌曲《铃儿响叮当》，她们英姿飒爽地打着腰鼓，令人精神振奋，增添喜庆气氛；穿着彩色的中国传统服装、腰间别着莲花的舞蹈队边走边舞，别具风采。</p>
<p>当地居民非常高兴法轮功游行队伍再次到来，他们看到表演，鼓掌、点赞。老太太Kelly热烈鼓掌并说：“我很喜欢！太漂亮了！我去年也看到她们来到这里。”她还跟她的孙子说：“她们很漂亮，是吗？”居民Petti说：“很漂亮！我很喜欢！”并问：“她们来自哪里？”</p>
<p>游行主办方安排了释放人造雪花的环节，当腰鼓队和莲花队经过雪花时，在明亮的灯光的照射下，尤为绚丽。</p>
<p>蛋港市位于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E6%B3%BD%E8%A5%BF.md#1">新泽西</a>大西洋城-哈蒙顿大都会区（Atlantic City-Hammonton?metropolitan）。在1614年，当荷兰船只“富图恩号”（Fortuyn）在Mullica River登陆时，欧洲人在蛋港城的所在地建立了定居点，第一批定居者因发现该地区有大量的海鸥蛋，而把该地区命名为“Eyren Haven”。到后来改名为“蛋港市”（Egg Harbor）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139352" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139352-IMG_2889.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14139352" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139352-IMG_2889-533x400.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139352" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞彩车。（林丹/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139351" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139351-IMG_2879.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14139351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139351-IMG_2879-533x400.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139351" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞彩车。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
