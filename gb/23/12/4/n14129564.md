<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>秀峦军舰岩与军舰岩吊桥 壮观美景尽览</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129566-651346-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新竹县尖石乡美丽山景。（吴淑珠提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月04日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%AB%B9.md#1">新竹</a>县报导）位于新竹<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%96%E7%9F%B3%E4%B9%A1.md#1">尖石乡</a>的秀峦<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9B%E8%88%B0%E5%B2%A9.md#1">军舰岩</a>及军舰岩<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8A%E6%A1%A5.md#1">吊桥</a>，以其壮丽的自然景观吸引游客，为探索大自然的绝佳去处。坐落于大汉溪上游的玉峰溪谷间，秀峦军舰岩因其三角形状的岩壁，占据着广大面积，垂直耸立于溪水之上，形状恰似军舰，因此得名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14129565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14129565" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129565-651345.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14129565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129565-651345-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14129565" class="wp-caption-text">秀峦<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9B%E8%88%B0%E5%B2%A9.md#1">军舰岩</a>与军舰岩<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8A%E6%A1%A5.md#1">吊桥</a>，壮观美景尽览。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前往秀峦军舰岩的路线经过120县道，沿着<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%96%E7%9F%B3%E4%B9%A1.md#1">尖石乡</a>方向行驶，转尖石一号大桥竹60线，经过宇老和田埔，最后抵达秀峦部落。在距离秀峦军舰岩意象入口约3分钟路程的地方，游客可以远眺军舰岩、军舰岩吊桥和成片的野生枫树林。初冬季节时，这里的枫树呈现多彩的色彩，给人一种心旷神怡的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14129567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14129567" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129567-651347.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14129567" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129567-651347-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14129567" class="wp-caption-text">尖石竹-60道山景夕阳。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14129569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14129569" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129569-651349.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14129569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129569-651349-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14129569" class="wp-caption-text">军舰岩附近的枫树，呈现多彩的色彩。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秀峦军舰岩的入口位于秀峦道路上，靠近控溪部落。一路上，游客可以欣赏到芒花和军舰岩的美景。这段路是土石步道，当天气不佳时需注意步伐。从这里步行约5分钟就能轻松抵达军舰岩吊桥。</p>
<p>军舰岩吊桥全长约60公尺，桥柱上刻有泰雅族图纹，于2015年3月建成。它是通往控溪部落的一条便道，也是近距离欣赏秀峦军舰岩和控溪吊桥的理想地点。站在吊桥上俯瞰玉峰溪，可以看到溪床上的大小岩石和秀峦野溪温泉，对于想要前往的游客，只需步行约170公尺即可抵达。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14129572" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14129572" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129572-651356.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14129572" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129572-651356-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14129572" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%AB%B9.md#1">新竹</a>尖石秀峦部落景色。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>穿过军舰岩吊桥后，游客可以继续前行，沿着石阶走上山，这是通往屯野生炮台步道的路线之一。据悉，这座炮台建于日治时期，用于监控泰康、新光、养老等泰雅族部落，共有屯野生台山、芝生毛台山、乌来山炮台三座。整个来回的路程需要约一小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14129568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14129568" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129568-651348.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14129568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129568-651348-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14129568" class="wp-caption-text">位于新竹尖石乡的秀峦军舰岩及军舰岩吊桥，以其壮丽的自然景观吸引游客。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在步行一段路之后，游客将来到玉峰溪河床上，视野开阔，可以欣赏到壮观的军舰岩。这里的蓝天与岩石的结合给人一种令人陶醉的感觉，并且伴随着溪水声和清凉的风，令人感到惬意。然而，由于这个地方阳光有限，所以游客在这里仍然需要注意保暖。</p>
<p>站在军舰岩吊桥的左侧仰头望去，游客可以清楚看到秀峦野溪温泉，由于溪水非常清澈，甚至有机会可以偶遇苦花鱼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14129571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14129571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129571-651351.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14129571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129571-651351-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14129571" class="wp-caption-text">玉峰溪水非常清澈干净。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14129570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14129570" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129570-651350.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14129570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14129570-651350-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14129570" class="wp-caption-text">游客将来到玉峰溪河床上，视野开阔。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
