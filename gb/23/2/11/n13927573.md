<h2><strong>大理花Dahlia pinnata菊科</strong></h2>
<p>大理花又称大丽花、天竺牡丹、大理菊、苕菊，洋芍药、洋牡丹。常见多年生草本庭园花卉。叶对生，羽状复叶。纺锤状块根。头状花序花顶生，花色繁多花形大小差异很大。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13927578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13927578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13927578-593018.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13927578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13927578-593018-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13927578" class="wp-caption-text">常见多年生草本庭园花卉。叶对生，羽状复叶。纺锤状块根。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13927579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13927579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13927579-593019.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13927579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13927579-593019-600x606.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="606" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13927579" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>夏季休眠外，其余季节皆可见开花。属半寒性植物，喜欢光线充足、凉爽或是通风良好环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13927582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13927582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13927582-593016.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13927582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13927582-593016-600x903.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="903" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13927582" class="wp-caption-text">大理花头状花序花顶生，花色繁多花形大小差异很大。</figcaption></figure>
