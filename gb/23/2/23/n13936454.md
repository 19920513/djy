<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>温哥华维多利亚购房所需收入激增</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月23日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">柯瑞斯</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">房价可能会下跌，但这并不意味着负担能力有所提高，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>的两个城市在加拿大购房所需的收入大幅飙升，名列榜首。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">据Daily Hive报导，来自金融机构</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">RateHub</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的数据令人惊讶的是，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>买房所需的收入并没有出现显着的变化。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%A4%9A%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A.md#1">维多利亚</a>名列榜首，温哥华紧随其后，位居第二。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">温哥华在需要多少收入方面仍然名列前茅，它只是没有像<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%A4%9A%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A.md#1">维多利亚</a>去年那样显着增长。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>两个城市的房价在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2022 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2023 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月期间都有下跌，但购房所需的收入却增加了不少。</span></p>
<h2><strong>维多利亚增加25,500元</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2022 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，维多利亚州的平均房价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 878,500 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2023 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年降至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 866,700</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，降幅为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 11,800 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2022 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年，根据</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 5.25% </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%8B%E5%8A%9B%E6%B5%8B%E8%AF%95.md#1">压力测试</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%A9%E7%8E%87.md#1">利率</a>和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2.91% </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的抵押贷款<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%A9%E7%8E%87.md#1">利率</a>计算的必要年收入为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 143,750 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2023 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年，根据</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7.37% </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%8B%E5%8A%9B%E6%B5%8B%E8%AF%95.md#1">压力测试</a>率和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 5.37% </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的抵押贷款利率，这一数字增加到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 169,250 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。因此，即使房价下跌了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 11,800 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，所需收入却增加了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 25,500</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。另外值得注意的是，与加拿大其他城市相比，维多利亚的房价跌幅最小。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">去年，世代挤压（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Generation Squeeze</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">的一项研究表明，温哥华年轻人必须在五年内每天放弃</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 26 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">杯拿铁，才能在温哥华买得起房子。</span></p>
<h2><strong>温哥华增加 23,350 元</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> RateHub </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的新数据，这一现实并没有太大改变。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2022 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，温哥华的平均房价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">119</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2023 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，下降到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1,111,400 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，减少了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 78,600 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。收入方面，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2022</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，基于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5.25%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的压力测试利率和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.91%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的抵押贷款利率，买房所需的收入为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">189,450</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。然而，在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2023</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，基于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 7.37% </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的压力测试利率和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 5.37%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的抵押贷款利率，该数字跃升至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 212,800 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，增加了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 23,350 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。截至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2023 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，温哥华的房屋平均成本以及所需的总收入继续领先加拿大。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">RateHub </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的数据显示，与</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2022 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月相比，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个主要市场中有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 9 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个购买房产所需的收入有所增加。卡尔加里位列第三，仅次于温哥华，所需的收入变化增加了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 20,310 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，其次是哈利法克斯和蒙特利尔。哈利法克斯和卡尔加里是加拿大仅有的两个房价上涨的主要城市。◇</span></p>
