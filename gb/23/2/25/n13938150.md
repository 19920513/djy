<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>食在佛罗伦斯 10道经典佳肴推荐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938155-shutterstock_1224476515-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>佛罗伦斯著名的美食汇聚地中央市场（The Mercato Centrale）。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年02月25日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）许多人称<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%9B%E7%BD%97%E4%BC%A6%E6%96%AF.md#1">佛罗伦斯</a>为艺术之都，也有人说它是意式料理的宝藏之地，所有对意式美食垂涎已久的热情，皆可在这座城市找到最好的安放。的确，徜徉这座城，邂逅它无处不在的美，除了美景，留住视线的还有那遍布街道的各色佳肴。享受意乡食风贵精不贵多，不妨从10道经典美食开始。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13938158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938158" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938158-shutterstock_371225494.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938158" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938158-shutterstock_371225494-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938158" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%9B%E7%BD%97%E4%BC%A6%E6%96%AF.md#1">佛罗伦斯</a>中央市场内的一间食品店。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一、T骨牛排（Florentine Beefsteak）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13938170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938170-shutterstock_352992917.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938170-shutterstock_352992917-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938170" class="wp-caption-text">佛罗伦斯的名菜T骨牛排。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在佛罗伦斯（Florence），契安尼娜（Chianina）T骨牛排（T-bone）是许多人的最爱。这种牛排通常以重量论价，每块重量从1公斤到4公斤不等，质感厚重。牛排的烹饪极为讲究，在上火烤之前，要经过一段时间的熟成。烤好的牛排仅用盐和胡椒等简单调味，使牛肉最自然的本味得以淋漓尽致地展现。烤好端上桌的牛排外层焦香，内部呈嫩粉色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13938171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938171-shutterstock_1283993155.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938171-shutterstock_1283993155-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938171" class="wp-caption-text">牛排在上火烤之前要放置在专用储藏室经过一段时间熟成。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>T骨牛排在佛罗伦斯流行了几百年，一如既往保持美味的奥秘在于大有来头的牛只。契安尼娜牛是意大利土生土长的珍贵古老牛种，以体型硕大肌肉发达而闻名。初期作为役畜饲养，其肉用价值被发现后才育成肉牛品种。</p>
<h2>二、牛肚包（Beef Tripe）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13938173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938173-shutterstock_1972996385.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938173-shutterstock_1972996385-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938173" class="wp-caption-text">佛罗伦斯的标志性小吃牛肚包。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到了佛罗伦斯，想尝尝隐于市井的小吃，理所当然要吃牛肚包了。牛肚包（Lampredotto）的作法类似汉堡，即在两片面包之间夹入煨煮了好几个小时的牛肚碎块，再淋上原汁和惹味的酱料。原本干硬的面包在酱汁浸入后变得湿润，夹上软烂入味的牛肚一起吃，口感和味道融合得相当好。</p>
<p>炖牛肚是意大利古老的民间菜肴，风味因各地区而异。佛罗伦斯牛肚包在中世纪就已是寻常人家的美味，许多人到了这里，都会去中央市场品尝大名鼎鼎的牛肚包。不过，由于主料来自牛的内脏（第四个胃），牛肚包纵然美味，当地人却是有人欢喜，有人厌。</p>
<h2>三、野猪肉酱意面（Pappardelle with Wild Boar Sauce）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13938175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938175-shutterstock_769734886.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938175-shutterstock_769734886-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938175" class="wp-caption-text">野猪肉酱意面。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>佛罗伦斯出产自己的特色意面。在9月至2月的狩猎季到佛罗伦斯，将能品尝到秋天才有的美味——野猪肉。此时，人们会用野猪肉制成肉酱搭配宽扁的丝带意面（Papardelle）享用，这便是托斯卡纳地区（Tuscany region）的传统美食。</p>
<p>狩猎野猪是意大利人由来已久的消遣方式，烹饪猪肉自然是拿手好戏。传统的作法是将短时间干式熟成的野猪肉浸在葡萄酒酱汁中过夜，使肉质产生独特风味后再烹成肉酱搭配丝带意面吃。有时，这种意面也配以野兔肉或鹅肉作成的肉酱。</p>
<h2>四、牛肝菌松露意面（Pasta with Porcini and Truffle Mushrooms）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13938176" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938176" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938176-shutterstock_1525151033.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938176" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938176-shutterstock_1525151033-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938176" class="wp-caption-text">牛肝菌松露意面。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>提到托斯卡纳的另一种秋季珍馐，那便是当地乡村富产的松露。这片地方是世界著名松露产区，当地人常用在野地寻获的新鲜松露洗净刨成薄片撒在意面上，或加入炒鸡蛋和汤中享用。松露的味道极为微妙，意大利厨师善用牛肝菌，用牛肝菌搭配松露产生出平衡的风味，充满山野味道。到佛罗伦斯不尝牛肝菌松露意面未免太可惜。</p>
<h2>五、鸡肝酱面包片（Crostini）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13938178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938178-shutterstock_1376013782.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938178-shutterstock_1376013782-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938178" class="wp-caption-text">鸡肝酱面包片。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>佛罗伦斯的开胃菜鸡肝酱面包片也很知名，不少人吃过之后大赞其味惊为天人。制作时将鸡肝用酒烹煮过，所以具足风味。吃的时候，把鸡肝酱抹在一小片意大利面包片上一起入口。在当地餐厅很容易找到这道菜。</p>
<h2>六、起司拼盘（Tagliere）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13938179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938179-shutterstock_1035296740.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938179-shutterstock_1035296740-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938179" class="wp-caption-text">熟成肉和起司组合的拼盘。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>熟成肉和起司这两种意式特产有一种完美吃法：拼盘。在餐馆或酒吧，甚至农庄都可以品尝到。它是很棒的开胃菜和夏季小食，在聚会的餐桌上也会经常见到。拼盘的制作非常简单，用料没有统一规范，将食材依序摆上即可，完全可以自购食材制作。最简单的小拼盘会有新鲜或陈化的起司、熟成肉片和酱料。大拼盘就丰盛一些，不仅起司种类多，还会放上烤蔬菜、面包片等。</p>
<h2>七、葡萄面包（Bread with Grapes）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13938180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938180" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938180-shutterstock_2069429093.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938180-shutterstock_2069429093-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938180" class="wp-caption-text">意大利传统的葡萄面包。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Schiacciata是受意大利人欢迎的一种咸味扁面包。它与常见的扁面包Focaccia很像，只是质地更薄脆，常用来作三明治。依照佛罗伦斯的传统，当葡萄丰收时，人们会将成熟的葡萄铺在面包上烘烤，作甜食享用。出炉的葡萄面包底层酥脆，表面柔软湿润、充满鲜果香甜。</p>
<h2>八、佛罗伦斯甜蛋糕（Schiacciata Fiorentina）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13938182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938182-shutterstock_2263483011.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938182-shutterstock_2263483011-600x395.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938182" class="wp-caption-text">佛罗伦斯甜蛋糕。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>几乎每间佛罗伦斯蛋糕店都售买这种甜蛋糕。扁平的黄色蛋糕上面覆盖了一层薄薄的白色糖粉，中间用可可粉印出佛罗伦斯市徽图案。蛋糕绵软湿润，口感微甜。在过去，它是当地嘉年华期间食用的甜品，现在不分时节都可以吃得到。</p>
<h2>九、冰淇淋（Gelato）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13938183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938183-shutterstock_1535996054.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938183-shutterstock_1535996054-600x419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938183" class="wp-caption-text">佛罗伦斯冰淇淋。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走在佛罗伦斯街头，不时看到有人手中拿着冰淇淋。意式冰淇淋从这个城市发源，迄今已有数百年。然而，街头冰淇淋店林立，如果没有美食攻略，要找到好品质的冰淇淋就需费点功夫了。可以多走几家进行外观对比，看到颜色鲜艳、装饰夸张的不要买（里面可能有色素）；若是天然食材作的冰淇淋，颜色看起来会接近食材原色，不妨先少买些试吃，口味合适再买大包装大块朵颐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13938184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938184" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938184-shutterstock_1288220263.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938184-shutterstock_1288220263-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938184" class="wp-caption-text">佛罗伦斯一家冰淇淋店的柜台。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>十、意式杏仁饼干（Cantucci）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13938187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13938187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938187-shutterstock_2128434752.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13938187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13938187-shutterstock_2128434752-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13938187" class="wp-caption-text">意式杏仁饼干的经典吃法是蘸餐后甜葡萄酒享用。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>佛罗伦斯随处可见意式杏仁饼干。世界各地有许多这类饼干版本，泛称biscotti；其使用榛子或开心果替代杏仁，以及加入黄油使质地变脆，甚至松软。事实上，传统意式杏仁饼干与之大为不同。</p>
<p>由于面团没有黄油，且要经过两次烘烤（整形成长条面团烤一次，切片后再烤一次），所以意式杏仁饼干的质地相当硬实，需蘸咖啡等饮品软化了吃。用饼干蘸着同样源自托斯卡纳的餐后甜葡萄酒（Vin Santo）享用，是最经典的吃法。</p>
