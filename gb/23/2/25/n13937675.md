<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《决胜的挥拍》迎最终回 冠军赛结果受瞩目</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937682-20230223-FTV-007-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《决胜的挥拍》剧中五虎将培养出好感情，图左起为吴东谚、谢佳见、程予希、王瞳、沈建宏。（民视提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年02月25日讯】（大纪元记者林森山报导）台湾和马来西亚跨国合制的青春偶像剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B3%E8%83%9C%E7%9A%84%E6%8C%A5%E6%8B%8D.md#1">决胜的挥拍</a>》，将在本周六晚间10点（当地时间）于民视播出最终回，除了团队是否夺下冠军受瞩目之外，男女主角的感情也备受关注。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13937679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13937679" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937679-20230223-FTV-004.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13937679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937679-20230223-FTV-004-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13937679" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B3%E8%83%9C%E7%9A%84%E6%8C%A5%E6%8B%8D.md#1">决胜的挥拍</a>》男主角<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E4%BD%B3%E8%A7%81.md#1">谢佳见</a>。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13937677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13937677" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937677-20230223-FTV-002.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13937677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937677-20230223-FTV-002-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13937677" class="wp-caption-text">《决胜的挥拍》女主角<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A8%8B%E4%BA%88%E5%B8%8C.md#1">程予希</a>。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了剧里的大专杯羽球赛，主演<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E4%BD%B3%E8%A7%81.md#1">谢佳见</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A8%8B%E4%BA%88%E5%B8%8C.md#1">程予希</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E4%B8%9C%E8%B0%9A.md#1">吴东谚</a>、沈建宏在体育场拍了整整两天，剧中担任队长的程予希表示对自己这一场戏相当要求，希望在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%A0%E5%86%9B%E8%B5%9B.md#1">冠军赛</a>可以拿出好表现。</p>
<p>程予希坦承拍摄前很紧张，尤其首次拍摄运动类型的戏剧，想做到完美；为了让身体保持暖身的状态，当机器在拍别的场景时，她依然在场边不断的挥拍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13937678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13937678" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937678-20230223-FTV-003.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13937678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937678-20230223-FTV-003-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13937678" class="wp-caption-text">程予希为《决胜的挥拍》苦练羽球，成果非凡获得满堂彩。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而且在拍摄的过程中也险酿意外，现场因为灯光角度，程予希对打接球时完全看不到羽球，这消失的羽球下一秒直接打到她左眼，这一幕吓坏全场。</p>
<p>程予希说当下她马上闭上眼睛，所以没有受伤，“我现在可是球场上的女汉子，就算受点伤也没关系的”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13937676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13937676" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937676-20230223-FTV-001.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13937676" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937676-20230223-FTV-001-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13937676" class="wp-caption-text">程予希拍摄《决胜的挥拍》，差点因为灯光酿意外。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>拍摄本剧，沈建宏表示自己很开心，因为从来没有参加球赛运动的戏剧，这是从头到尾扎实的练习，角色还一路比到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%A0%E5%86%9B%E8%B5%9B.md#1">冠军赛</a>，很新鲜，也觉得自己蛮帅的。</p>
<p>而<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E4%B8%9C%E8%B0%9A.md#1">吴东谚</a>则表示可以跟沈建宏组成双打很幸福，两人在里面也是真的体力活，有空档就会不停的挥拍，虽然很累但很好玩。</p>
<p>吴东谚也提到有几场戏为了扑接球造成双脚膝盖瘀青，但对他来说是很棒的回忆；现在他也养成习惯，有空就会去打羽球，把手感维持住。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13937680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13937680" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937680-20230223-FTV-005.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13937680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937680-20230223-FTV-005-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13937680" class="wp-caption-text">《决胜的挥拍》谢佳见最后一集在教练席上观赛，忍不住手痒想上场打球。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谢佳见表示最后结局的这一场戏，自己几乎都是坐在教练椅上看他们挥拍，笑说“在场边看手很痒，很想加入他们”。他也表示拍这部戏最开心就是认识了王瞳、程予希、吴东谚、沈建宏。</p>
<p>谢佳见认为，大家从开拍到现在就是“关关难过关关过”，很开心大家在辛苦的过程中，能够有很棒的体验，也谢谢观众从第一集陪伴大家到最后。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13937681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13937681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937681-20230223-FTV-006.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13937681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937681-20230223-FTV-006-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13937681" class="wp-caption-text">《决胜的挥拍》剧里程予希跟谢佳见的恋情备受关注。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
