<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大温地区2022年市选募款与花费一览</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月06日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">唐仁</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大温哥华报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E9%80%89%E4%B8%BE%E5%B1%80.md#1">卑诗选举局</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">( Elections BC )</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">发布的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2022 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年市选财务报表，在两个角逐最激烈的城市中，温哥华市长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%88%E8%A7%82%E5%81%A5.md#1">沈观健</a>团队的募款金额超过</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 190 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元，是前市长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%98%E8%BF%BA%E8%BF%AA.md#1">甘迺迪</a>团队募款总额的三倍。而<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%A0%E9%87%8C.md#1">素里</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%82%E9%80%89%E8%80%85%E7%AD%B9%E6%AC%BE.md#1">参选者筹款</a>和花费总金额创下历史纪录。</span></p>
<h2><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%88%E8%A7%82%E5%81%A5.md#1">沈观健</a>团队花费170万元</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">ABC </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">报告了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1,006,987 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元的捐款和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 414,003 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元的其他收入以及收到的转</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">帐</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，总计</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 1,420,991 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。该党表示，在市选中花费近</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">170</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">元用于击败前市长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%98%E8%BF%BA%E8%BF%AA.md#1">甘迺迪</a>（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kennedy Stewart</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">），并赢得了市议会和公园局的多数控制权。</span></p>
