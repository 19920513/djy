<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义铁高车站施工 6日起人行天桥封闭一年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923915-598586-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>	民众抽取通行票证。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月06日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）为配合“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89%E5%B8%82.md#1">嘉义市</a>区<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%81%E8%B7%AF%E9%AB%98%E6%9E%B6%E5%8C%96.md#1">铁路高架化</a>计划”C612标嘉义高架化车站施工需要，嘉义车站前、后站行人动线共分成3阶段调整，目前进行至第2阶段，从今(6)日起，封闭拆除嘉义高架站区范围内的站外行人天桥进行改建作业，预计于1年后113年2月完工复旧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923916-598587.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13923916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923916-598587-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923916" class="wp-caption-text">民众抽取通行票证。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89%E5%B8%82.md#1">嘉义市</a>政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第2阶段行人动线调整期间，搭乘台铁的旅客动线及无障碍电梯等措施均维持不变；而往来通行前、后站或本市先期交通转运中心的民众，因站外行人天桥封闭，请改至车站前、后站剪票口，抽取临时通行票券，经由站内行人天桥前往前、后站或本市先期交通转运中心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923917" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923917-598588.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13923917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923917-598588-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923917" class="wp-caption-text">民众询问向导员问题。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为因应各阶段車站行人动线改道，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%81%E8%B7%AF%E9%AB%98%E6%9E%B6%E5%8C%96.md#1">铁路高架化</a>施工单位交通部铁道局与嘉义车站已设置立牌看板、向导员、取票机及指标系统等相关配合措施，并派员协助引导旅客与市民，民众如有遇到任何动线疑义，亦可询问站内或配戴向导员识别证的工作人员，顺利进出嘉义车站前、后站或本市先期交通转运中心。交通处也呼吁旅客善用楼梯，将无障碍电梯留给年长、行动不便或携带大型行李的旅客通行，并感谢旅客的体谅与配合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923918" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923918-598589.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13923918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923918-598589-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923918" class="wp-caption-text">前站动线。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>交通处表示，嘉义高架化车站施工期间，各阶段行人动线调整及配套措施，已偕交通部铁道局及台湾铁路管理局多次召开会议共同研商，期许以对旅客、民众影响最小、维持无障碍通行及撙节公帑等原则，拟订最适方案，呼吁旅客及利用站区往返的民众，届时请依照改道导引告示、牌面、标示及向导员的指引，并请注意通行安全，期间造成不便，再请市民及旅客惠予配合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923919" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923919-598590.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13923919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923919-598590-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923919" class="wp-caption-text">后站动线。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13923920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923920-598591.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13923920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923920-598591-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923920" class="wp-caption-text">站内告示。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13923921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923921-598592.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13923921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923921-598592-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923921" class="wp-caption-text">站外告示。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
