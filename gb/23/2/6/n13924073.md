<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台东元宵绕境祈福 重现民俗节庆热闹气氛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924075-598602-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东元宵绕境祈福，祈国泰民安、风调雨顺。（台东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月06日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）因疫情停办2年的台东<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5%E8%8A%82.md#1">元宵节</a>绕境<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%88%E7%A6%8F.md#1">祈福</a>活动，2月5、6日（正月15、16日）一连两天在台东市区恢复举办，共51间庙宇、团体参加绕境，祈国泰民安、风调雨顺，民众沿街观看，疫情后重新感受<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97.md#1">民俗</a>节庆的热闹气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13924074" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13924074" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924074-598601.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13924074" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924074-598601-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13924074" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>元宵绕境<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%88%E7%A6%8F.md#1">祈福</a>，祈国泰民安、风调雨顺。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13924076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13924076" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924076-598603.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13924076" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924076-598603-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13924076" class="wp-caption-text">台东元宵绕境祈福，祈国泰民安、风调雨顺。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>绕境活动出发前，由台东县长饶庆铃、台东县议会议长吴秀华、台东市长陈铭风、民政处长钟青柏、天后宫管理委员会主委林有德等人在台东天后宫主持开锣仪式，并点燃起马炮。连续两天的绕境活动在6日晚间7点各队伍陆续返回天后宫后结束。</p>
<p>天后宫表示，今年共有51队报名参与，游行路线5日游行台东市区东西向街道，6日游行台东市区南北向街道，5日下午并在南京路广场进行了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97.md#1">民俗</a>艺阵展演。玄武堂和邯郸堂也分别配合各单位邀约进行多场炮炸寒单爷活动，吸引许多民众观看。</p>
<p>台东县府与天后宫今年共推绕境公约，补助参加绕境队伍2万元，共同发扬元宵绕境传统文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13924078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13924078" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924078-598605.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13924078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924078-598605-600x362.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13924078" class="wp-caption-text">马兰荣家共祈国泰民安，祥和家昌。（马兰荣家提供）</figcaption></figure>
