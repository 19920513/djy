<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：印度火烈鸟年度大迁徙 场面异常壮观</h2>
<a href="https://d1ntfewljph5k2.cloudfront.net/V0r73j1wp"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年02月09日讯】（英文大纪元记者Anna Mason报导／张雨霏编译）每当摄影师拉吉?莫汉（Raj Mohan）目睹<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6.md#1">印度</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E7%83%88%E9%B8%9F.md#1">火烈鸟</a>大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%81%E5%BE%99.md#1">迁徙</a>时，他都会被深深地迷住。</p>
<p>“它们让我着迷。老实说，无法用言语描述我当时的感受。”莫汉告诉《大纪元时报》。</p>
<p>这位32岁的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6.md#1">印度</a>IT专家每年坚持前往印度南部泰米尔纳德邦的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E5%88%A9%E5%8D%A1%E7%89%B9%E6%B9%96.md#1">普利卡特湖</a>（Pulicat Lake）参加一年一度的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E7%83%88%E9%B8%9F.md#1">火烈鸟</a>盛会”，这是一项为期三天的促进当地生态旅游的官方活动。每年的1月份，成千上万只绚丽的粉红色鸟儿都会聚集在60公里长的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E5%88%A9%E5%8D%A1%E7%89%B9%E6%B9%96.md#1">普利卡特湖</a>中繁殖。</p>
<p>莫汉部署了一架无人机，并捕捉到了它们无比壮观的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%81%E5%BE%99.md#1">迁徙</a>场面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13926216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13926216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926216-Famingo3-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13926216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926216-Famingo3-4.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13926216" class="wp-caption-text">（Raj Mohan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13926219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13926219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926219-Famingo9-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13926219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926219-Famingo9-4.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13926219" class="wp-caption-text">（Raj Mohan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13926217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13926217" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926217-Famingo7-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13926217" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926217-Famingo7-4.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13926217" class="wp-caption-text">（Raj Mohan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“这些鸟类是从不同的大陆迁徙到这片温暖的水域，这一事实足以鼓舞人们前往这个地方来观赏。”莫汉说。</p>
<p>这种长腿、蹼足的鸟类以生长在泻湖浅水区的大量微小植物和藻类为食，此外，螃蟹、蜗牛和小鱼以及湖边稻田里的蠕虫和昆虫也是它们可口的美食。</p>
<p>莫汉是一位忠实的旅行者和探险家，他花费数小时仔细观察这些美丽火烈鸟的群体行为，当他注意到它们形成有趣的队形时，他马上操控无人机从上方拍下难得的画面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13926218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13926218" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926218-Famingo8-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13926218" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926218-Famingo8-4.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="587" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13926218" class="wp-caption-text">（Raj Mohan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13926220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13926220" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926220-Famingo11-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13926220" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926220-Famingo11-4.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13926220" class="wp-caption-text">（Raj Mohan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13926227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13926227" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926227-Famingo5-3.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13926227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926227-Famingo5-3.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="564" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13926227" class="wp-caption-text">（Raj Mohan提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>“我发现，无论在什么情况下，它们总是聚集在一起，成群结队地一起向前移动以保护彼此。它们是典型的群居鸟类。”他说。</p>
<p>大约十年前，这位计算机科学工程专业的毕业生开始在旅行途中用手机拍照。后来，在熟悉相机操作之后，他对航空摄影产生了兴趣。然而，学习这种技能需要足够的耐心和毅力，但他多年的坚持得到了回报。</p>
<p>莫汉的照片《粉红之心》（Heart of Pink）成为2022年度Nature in Focus摄影大赛的获奖作品，他说他很荣幸能在这样一个享有盛誉的平台上获奖，并且他的照片获得了“令人难以置信的正面回应”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13926226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13926226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926226-Famingo4-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13926226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926226-Famingo4-4.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="355" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13926226" class="wp-caption-text">获奖作品《粉红之心》。（Raj Mohan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“有些人说他们从来不知道在印度也可以看到火烈鸟，有些人说这是他们见过的最好的火烈鸟<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%AA%E6%8B%8D.md#1">航拍</a>照片，等等。”他说。</p>
<p>除了标志性的火烈鸟作品外，莫汉还捕捉到了印度喜马拉雅山上“危险但美丽”的道路，以及一辆火车穿过“卡纳塔克邦的绿色之路”的壮观画面，这些作品都在网络上广为流传。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13926221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13926221" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926221-Famingo14-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13926221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926221-Famingo14-4.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="564" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13926221" class="wp-caption-text">印度喜马拉雅山上“危险但美丽”的道路。（Raj Mohan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13926222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13926222" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926222-Famingo15-3.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13926222" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13926222-Famingo15-3.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="564" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13926222" class="wp-caption-text">印度喜马拉雅山上“危险但美丽”的道路。（Raj Mohan提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>“每个地方都很美，每个地方都很重要。你所要做的就是环顾四周，向尽可能多的人展示世界的美丽。”他说，“先有梦想，然后去尝试，最终实现它！这就是我的信念。”</p>
