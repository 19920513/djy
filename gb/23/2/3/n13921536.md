<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>通缉男装路人过家门不入 难逃员警法眼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921537-598017-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>员警在旁，林男察觉有异，立即假装路人过家门不入径往前走。（桃园警察分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月03日讯】（大纪元记者陈建霖台湾桃园报导）2日19时15分许，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F%E5%88%86%E5%B1%80.md#1">桃园警察分局</a>景福派出所副所长黄福源及警员詹学庆、谢雨峻，前往林姓男子（64岁、因涉嫌不能安全驾驶案件判刑8个月，未到案执行遭发布<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%9A%E7%BC%89.md#1">通缉</a>中）民安路住处埋守执行查缉。</p>
<figure id="13921538" aria-describedby="caption-13921538" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921538-598018-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921538-598018-450x253.jpg" alt="林男回到家门前，掏出钥匙准备开门。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13921538" class="wp-caption-text">林男回到家门前，掏出钥匙准备开门。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F%E5%88%86%E5%B1%80.md#1">桃园警察分局</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林男回到家门前，掏出钥匙准备开门，突然察觉有异，立即假装路人过家门不入径往前走，员警立即尾随拦查，查询其身份时，林男否认自己身份。</p>
<figure id="13921539" aria-describedby="caption-13921539" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921539-598019-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921539-598019-450x253.jpg" alt="黄福源一把抓住他，叫出他的全名。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13921539" class="wp-caption-text">黄福源一把抓住他，叫出他的全名。（桃园警察分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄福源一把抓住他，叫出他的全名，林男知道身份已曝光，只好乖乖配合，员警依法将其逮捕解送归案。◇</p>
