<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“褒忠吃饭担” 万人吃平安饭  祈求新年平安顺利</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923276-598376-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023褒忠马鸣山五年千岁吃饭担文化节，副总统赖清德与云林县长张丽善也到场一起挑饭担，体验褒忠传统民俗文化！（云林县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月05日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）有百年历史的云林县马鸣山五年千岁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%83%E9%A5%AD%E6%8B%85.md#1">吃饭担</a>文化节因疫情停办2年，今年扩大举行，由东势乡同安村主办，参加人潮超过往年，上万人齐聚会场，今年较特别的是年轻人带着小孩参加的特别多。副总统<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%96%E6%B8%85%E5%BE%B7.md#1">赖清德</a>与云林县长张丽善也到场一起挑饭担，下午三点30分，在南北旗集合后，在“开动”一声令下，大家井然有序地一起享用油饭、酸菜鸭、米粉等料理。看不到过去打包的现象！</p>
<p>副总统<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%96%E6%B8%85%E5%BE%B7.md#1">赖清德</a>说，久仰马鸣山吃饭摊之名，比过年还热闹，果然名不虚传，过去褒忠、东势都曾出现瘟疫，最终由五年千岁化解，因前2年停办，今年有更多人来响应，他代表蔡总统祝福大家身体健康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923275" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923275-598372-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13923275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923275-598372-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923275" class="wp-caption-text">下午三点30分，在南北旗集合后，在“开动”一声令下，大家井然有序地一起享用油饭、酸菜鸭、米粉等料理。看不到过去抢着打包的现象。（云林县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长张丽善说，欢迎全国民众来到东势乡同安村共襄盛举，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%83%E9%A5%AD%E6%8B%85.md#1">吃饭担</a>活动因疫情停办2年，今年在马鸣山镇安宫在主委章金树带领下，扩大举办，也鼓励民众自带餐具，不使用一次性抛弃餐具；如今新冠肺炎灾情持续，五年千岁透过元宵节祈安绕境，祈求疫情早日消退。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923277" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923277-598378-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13923277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923277-598378-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923277" class="wp-caption-text">下午三点30分，南北旗集合后，与会长官挑着饭担进会场。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年吃饭担活动以“传承”为主轴，号召年轻年轻人回乡参加，有传承角色扮演、扛轿体验、阵头表演以及绕境祈福等，场面壮观热闹。</p>
<p>来自斗六的黄妈妈和先生带着二岁及四岁的小孩来参加“吃饭担”活动，连二岁的儿子，也一起吃起<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E5%AE%89%E9%A5%AD.md#1">平安饭</a>，她说：祈求全家吃了平安饭后，新的一年都能平安！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923278" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923278-598379-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13923278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923278-598379-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923278" class="wp-caption-text">吃饭担活动现场的舞蹈表演。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台自台中的沈妈妈，也是一家四口来吃<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E5%AE%89%E9%A5%AD.md#1">平安饭</a>，她说：二年的疫情，孩子们一直关在家里，快闷坏了！所以特别带他们出来走走，一方面体验民俗文化，也希望五年千岁能带给全家平安！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923279" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923279-598390-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13923279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923279-598390-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923279" class="wp-caption-text">黄妈妈正在喂二岁的儿子吃平安饭，祈求新的一年孩子能平平安安。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>褒忠在地民众许先生表示，他今年已76岁，从懂事到现在，吃饭担这个民俗活动就一直流传到现在，大家来吃个平安饭，就觉得回去后能受到五年千岁的庇佑，家庭平安顺利，能安定民心，是个很值得传承下去的民俗！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923280" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923280-598391-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13923280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923280-598391-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923280" class="wp-caption-text">今年年轻夫妻带着小孩参加吃饭担活动特别多。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文观处长陈璧君表示，马鸣山五年千岁元宵平安绕境吃饭担已有百年历史，已登录为无形文化资产，每年祈安绕境走过五股十四庄，祈求民众平安健康，兼具地方特色及文化典范性，是难得一见的地方盛事，欢迎大家元宵节到东势乡同安村吃平安餐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923281-598392-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13923281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923281-598392-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923281" class="wp-caption-text">年轻爸爸正在喂小孩吃平安饭。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13923282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923282-598403-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13923282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923282-598403-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923282" class="wp-caption-text">来自台中的沈妈妈也带着全家人来吃明安饭。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
