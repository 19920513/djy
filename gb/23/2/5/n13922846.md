<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“岩韵风华”─ 胶彩画创作联展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922849-598275-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“岩韵风华”─ 胶彩画创作联展 开幕合影。（桃园市文化局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月05日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园艺术亮点<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%B6%E5%BD%A9%E7%94%BB.md#1">胶彩画</a>家张瑞蓉，长期连结全国各地热爱胶彩艺术创作者，深耕与推广胶彩艺术至全国，并广受民众喜爱。“岩韵风华”胶彩创作联展，即桃园胶彩画家张瑞蓉结合台中简锦清、杨玉梅、林必强等三位胶彩画家联展，展出近期作品40余件，即日起至2月19日，于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD%E5%B8%82%E6%94%BF%E5%BA%9C%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E5%B1%80.md#1">桃园市政府文化局</a>3楼第一展览室展出，作品精彩，邀市民朋友们莅临欣赏。</p>
<p>展览主题“岩韵风华”发想自<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%B6%E5%BD%A9%E7%94%BB.md#1">胶彩画</a>的特色，创作时画布敷色的矿物质颜料，取自天然岩矿，调合动物胶后绘制成作品，经由创作者构思再造其色彩绚烂及多元层次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13922847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13922847" style="width: 568px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922847-598273.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13922847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922847-598273.jpg" alt="" width="568" b="750" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13922847" class="wp-caption-text">简锦清 月夜。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13922848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13922848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922848-598274.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13922848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922848-598274-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13922848" class="wp-caption-text">“岩韵风华”─ 胶彩画创作联展 展览一角。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近年来胶彩画创作更是创新多元，并广为创作者及观赏者所喜爱，藉由本次展览与大家面对面，欣赏四位老师长期从事胶彩画创作及耕耘，风格迥异独特创作样貌，画幅中简锦清作品显露“温润雅致”、张瑞蓉以“岩韵花容”展现胶彩风华、杨玉梅的作品“清新秀丽”、林必强作品以“真境自然”等四大特色，期盼让大家对胶彩画，有耳目一新的时代感受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13922850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13922850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922850-598276.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13922850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922850-598276-600x497.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="497" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13922850" class="wp-caption-text">林必强 皓月。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13922851" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13922851" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922851-598277.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13922851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922851-598277-600x743.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="743" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13922851" class="wp-caption-text">张瑞蓉 荷趣。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本展藉由结合东、西方多元媒材与写生技法相互融合，成为新时代胶彩风貌，也将结合桃园在地文化景观人文元素，激荡出胶彩艺术丰饶成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13922852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13922852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922852-598278.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13922852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13922852-598278-600x484.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="484" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13922852" class="wp-caption-text">杨玉梅 无语。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>简锦清、张瑞蓉、杨玉梅、林必强四位胶彩画家，一生以胶彩画传承为志业，提携进辈长期耕耘胶彩画坛有成，藉由展出与桃园艺文团体交流，让瑰丽的胶彩艺术在桃园生根茁壮，欢迎市民朋友们踊跃前往欣赏。◇</p>
