<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>真人版《小美人鱼》新预告片 呈现美丽海洋世界</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931929-20230217-mark-Disney00-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>真人版《小美人鱼》剧照。（迪士尼提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年02月17日讯】（大纪元记者叶子杰编译报导）迪士尼16日推出真人翻拍版的《小美人鱼》最新第二部预告片，30 秒的片段呈现出美丽的海洋世界、小美人鱼“爱丽儿”与亚力克王子，最后还出现了反派乌苏拉。本片将于5月上映。</p>
<p>真人版《小美人鱼》最新第二部预告：<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VQCfbmhIjzo">请点此观看影片</a></p>
<p>真人版《小美人鱼》首发预告：<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0-wPm99PF9U">请点此观看影片</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_13931972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13931972" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931972-20230217-mark-Disney07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13931972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931972-20230217-mark-Disney07-450x169.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="169" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13931972" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新的预告片开场，随着小美人鱼<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%8E%89%C2%B7%E8%B4%9D%E8%8E%89.md#1">海莉·贝莉</a>（Halle Bailey）唱着〈Part of Your World〉，带领观众进入美丽的海洋世界景观。小美人鱼快乐地翻滚在海洋世界里。新预告片中首次出现“亚力克王子”和“乌苏拉”形象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13931968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13931968" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931968-20230217-mark-Disney01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13931968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931968-20230217-mark-Disney01-450x209.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="209" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13931968" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>真人版《小美人鱼》由芝加哥电影制片人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%B3%E5%8B%83%C2%B7%E9%A9%AC%E6%AD%87%E5%B0%94.md#1">劳勃·马歇尔</a>（Rob Marshall）执导，22岁非裔歌手贝莉饰演主角小美人鱼“爱丽儿”，乔纳·霍尔-金（Jonah Hauer-King）饰演“亚力克王子”，玛莉莎·麦卡锡（Melissa McCarthy）饰演反派“乌苏拉”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13931970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13931970" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931970-20230217-mark-Disney04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13931970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931970-20230217-mark-Disney04-450x181.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="181" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13931970" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其他还有，奥斯卡奖得主哈维?尔巴登（Javier Bardem）饰演“特里顿国王”，雅各·特伦布雷（Jacob Tremblay）饰演“比目鱼”，戴维德·迪格斯（Daveed Diggs）饰演“塞巴斯丁”。百老汇传奇人物林-曼努尔·米兰达（Lin-Manuel Miranda）和亚伦·孟肯（Alan Menken）合作为《小美人鱼》配乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13931969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13931969" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931969-20230217-mark-Disney03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13931969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931969-20230217-mark-Disney03-450x223.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="223" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13931969" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《小美人鱼》描述特里顿国王最小的女儿爱丽儿想要了解海洋以外的世界，在探访海面时爱上了潇洒的亚力克王子。虽然美人鱼禁止与人类互动，但爱丽儿想顺从自己的内心。她与邪恶的反派海女巫乌苏拉达成协议，让她有机会可以体验陆地的生活，但最终却将她的生命——以及她父亲的王位——置于危险之中。</p>
<p>真人版《小美人鱼》将于5月26日在台湾及美国上映。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13931971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13931971" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931971-20230217-mark-Disney06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13931971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13931971-20230217-mark-Disney06-450x216.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="216" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13931971" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《小美人鱼》剧照（迪士尼提供）</figcaption></figure>
