<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>领养不弃养 桃园动保处欢迎可爱动物领养</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937098-601501-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>吸蜜鹦鹉。（桃园市农业局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）兔年想要兔宝宝吗？桃园市政府动物保护处的“非犬猫特殊宠物”领养活动将在3月8日上午10点办理，想成为兔爸爸、鸽妈妈、鹦鹉饲主的民众，欢迎携带国民身份证及运输笼，踊跃参加!</p>
<figure id="13937105" aria-describedby="caption-13937105" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937105-601508-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937105-601508-450x337.jpg" alt="仓鼠（黄白）。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937105" class="wp-caption-text">仓鼠（黄白）。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13937100" aria-describedby="caption-13937100" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937100-601503-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937100-601503-450x337.jpg" alt="刺猬。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937100" class="wp-caption-text">刺猬。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13937099" aria-describedby="caption-13937099" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937099-601502-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937099-601502-450x600.jpg" alt="兔。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937099" class="wp-caption-text">兔。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国人宠物以犬猫为大宗，但也会有少数非犬猫宠物，因民众弃养或走失，而由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E4%BF%9D%E5%A4%84.md#1">动保处</a>收容，若经公告后仍无饲主领回，则以不定期办理认养活动的方式，让非犬猫宠物能有完善的照养生活环境。</p>
<figure id="13937102" aria-describedby="caption-13937102" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937102-601505-450x339.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937102-601505-450x339.jpg" alt="肥尾守宫。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937102" class="wp-caption-text">肥尾守宫。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13937101" aria-describedby="caption-13937101" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937101-601504-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937101-601504-450x337.jpg" alt="和尚鹦鹉。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937101" class="wp-caption-text">和尚鹦鹉。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13937111" aria-describedby="caption-13937111" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937111-601512-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937111-601512-450x337.jpg" alt="鸽子。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937111" class="wp-caption-text">鸽子。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>预计于2023年3月8日上午10点于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E4%BF%9D%E5%A4%84.md#1">动保处</a>(桃园市桃园区县府路57号)办理的非犬猫特殊宠物领养活动，有包括雷克斯兔、刺猬、爬虫类、鹦鹉及仓鼠等，共计12只动物开放领养。动保处强调，有意领养之民众除需设籍桃园市并年满18岁，也会要求拥有饲养特殊宠物的能力；领养成功的市民必须参加1小时的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E5%91%BD%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">生命教育</a>课程，课程内容包括如何饲养、照护特殊宠物，如饲料、饮水，以及环境与卫生管理及异常状况的注意事项及护理工作。</p>
<figure id="13937104" aria-describedby="caption-13937104" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937104-601507-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937104-601507-450x337.jpg" alt="仓鼠（黄）。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937104" class="wp-caption-text">仓鼠（黄）。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13937103" aria-describedby="caption-13937103" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937103-601506-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937103-601506-450x337.jpg" alt="仓鼠（咖白）。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937103" class="wp-caption-text">仓鼠（咖白）。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13937110" aria-describedby="caption-13937110" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937110-601511-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937110-601511-450x600.jpg" alt="雷克斯兔。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937110" class="wp-caption-text">雷克斯兔。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市农业局长陈冠义呼吁，越来越多人饲养非犬猫宠物，民众在饲养宠物前应谨慎评估自身能力和住家环境，并提供宠物妥善饲养空间，每种宠物都有它喜好的生活环境，应该针对饲养宠物属性提供适合场域，避免不良的环境，造成动物逃逸或伤害。动保处将持续宣导推广<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E5%91%BD%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">生命教育</a>及落实饲主责任，“懂它再养它，终养不弃养”。</p>
<figure id="13937108" aria-describedby="caption-13937108" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937108-601510-450x584.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937108-601510-450x584.jpg" alt="道奇兔。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937108" class="wp-caption-text">道奇兔。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13937106" aria-describedby="caption-13937106" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937106-601509-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13937106-601509-450x337.jpg" alt="球蟒。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13937106" class="wp-caption-text">球蟒。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动详情可参考桃园动保处的官方网站<ahref="https://animal.tycg.gov.tw/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://animal.tycg.gov.tw/</a>）或脸书粉丝专页，并办理领养作业，让我们一起关注动物保护，为这个社会带来更多正能量！◇</p>
