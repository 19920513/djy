<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】试驾黑线特别版GX 2023 Lexus GX 460 Black Line Special Edition</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932341-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_03-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023 Lexus GX 460 Black Line。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月18日讯】2023年Lexus GX是GX的第二代产品，自2009年推出以来，历经了两次Facelift小升级，以保持GX的新鲜感，而在2023年则推出“Black Line Special Edition”特别版，在价钱及设备上，都是介乎于Premium版与Luxury版之间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13932318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13932318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932318-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13932318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932318-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13932318" class="wp-caption-text">2023 Lexus GX 460 Black Line。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>GX 460的外型设计就好像方块一样，高度与宽度是同等尺寸，Black Line特别版在外观上有少许不同，首先是18吋六幅式钢琴黑色是黑色轮圈，雾灯旁有特别的饰版。另外前脸的Spindle进风口栏栅也采用黑色，车窗的框透也同样是黑色，车顶架及尾灯底色也是黑色，务求凸显出“Black Line”特式。车两侧装有踏脚板，要登入离地距高的GX车厢更为方便。GX的另外一个特点是它的尾闸，横揭式的尾闸设计在现今市场已近淘汰，缺点是没有电动辅助开启，而且开启时也占位置。此外，GX的尾闸玻璃窗也可独立上揭打开，方便放入一 些小东西，或在越野时透透气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13932320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13932320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932320-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13932320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932320-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13932320" class="wp-caption-text">头灯下方装有清洗器。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13932321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13932321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932321-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13932321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932321-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_09-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13932321" class="wp-caption-text">雾灯旁有特别的饰版。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Lexus GX 460 Black Line Special Edition与其他版本一样，采用4.6升V8汽油引擎，马力输出301匹，扭矩329磅呎。搭配6速自动变速箱，有点落伍，就算是RX 350也在使用8速的自动变速箱。然而，GX的四驱系统就更为出色，GX 460装有4H及4L四驱选择，更有中置差速锁开关，“多种越野模式选择（Multi-Terrain Select）”及可升降车身高度，就只有最高版本Luxury才配备，还好Black Line有“动力动态悬挂系统（KDSS）”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13932322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13932322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932322-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_32.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13932322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932322-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_32-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13932322" class="wp-caption-text">4.6升V8汽油引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Lexus内装一向都是非常豪华，多功能方向盘有加热功能，方向盘上的多功能按钮可使驾驶者手不离方向盘，便可改变音响或仪表板显示等工作，令人可更专心驾驶。此外，前排座椅均装有加热及冷冻功能、十方向电动前座椅、驾驶座有两个位置记忆、前后共三区的恒温控制、两侧后视镜折叠等。中控台萤幕是触控式控制，可用作音响系统控制、倒车的影像显示、导航系统及汽车设定多种用途。2023年款GX的中控台设计，经过重新排列，萤幕移到顶部，送风口则在萤幕下方，恒温系统按键变小并以一行排列，4H及4L四驱控制及DAC也移上到中控台中间位置。CD播放机也仍然保留，在变速杆右方位置则变成了Remote Touch的控制板。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13932324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13932324" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932324-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_45.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13932324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932324-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_45-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13932324" class="wp-caption-text">Lexus内装一向都是非常豪华。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>GX 460 Black Line Special Edition装有倒车镜头，以及Panoramic View前后左右四个摄像头。头灯下方装有清洗器，在头灯亮着时，按下清洗键后，清洗器会向前伸出，并以高压水柱清洗头灯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13932325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13932325" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932325-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_31.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13932325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932325-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_31-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13932325" class="wp-caption-text">载货空间为46.7立方呎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中排座位是普通的Bench Seat，可坐三人，座椅是白色，第三排两座椅是标配，要电动开合也是Premium以上才备有。在打开第三排座椅后载货区会变小，只可放入小量东西，而且因为左右可独立折叠，所以载人载货的组合也更随意。GX的车内空间宽敞，中排座位脚部空间大，前座上方装有天窗，但尺寸不大。驾驶席在熄火时会自动后移，让驾驶者有更多空间进出。载货空间为46.7立方呎，当第三排立起时便变为11.6立方呎，而放倒第二排座位后，载货空间又会变成64.7立方呎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13932326" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13932326" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932326-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_15.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13932326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932326-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_15-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13932326" class="wp-caption-text">Lexus GX 460车侧装有踏脚板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>GX 460装备了一副马力强大引擎，用作推动逾5千磅的车身，0-60mph需时7.8秒，而拖力则有6,500磅，起步加速缓慢，中高速时表现比较出色。由于车身高及重，在市内高速入弯时有点转向不足，但在高速公路上均速转弯则很顺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13932329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13932329" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932329-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_17.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13932329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932329-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13932329" class="wp-caption-text">黑色轮圈配合Black Line主题。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>GX 460 Black Line Special Edition承继了GX的各方优点，而将多个部分改为黑色，作为特别版的卖点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13932331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13932331" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932331-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13932331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932331-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_23-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13932331" class="wp-caption-text">GX的尾闸玻璃窗也可独立上揭打开。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驾驶席高高在上的感觉最美妙，但上下车时需要使用车侧脚踏板。引擎声雄壮响亮，气势凌厉，虽然起步不快，但引擎声响足以震慑旁边汽车！Lexus旗舰SUV的 LX系列在今年推出全新设计，有差不多“年纪”的GX，会在明年或2023年改款吗？</p>
<figure id="attachment_13932332" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13932332" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932332-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_33.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13932332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13932332-2023_Lexus_GX460_Black_33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13932332" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板设计传统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
