<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>车银优拍摄品牌画报 演绎经典与休闲风春装</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13934681-2302210415421487.jpg" />
<h6>车银优日前为韩国某男装品牌拍摄画报。（LIBERCLASSY提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年02月21日讯】（大纪元记者乐慧萱编译报导） 20日，韩国某男装品牌公开了代言人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E9%93%B6%E4%BC%98.md#1">车银优</a>拍摄的2023年S/S画报。</p>
<p>此次画报以西班牙语中意为避风港的“Querencia”为主题，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E9%93%B6%E4%BC%98.md#1">车银优</a>在茂密的树丛中度过疗愈和休闲的时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13934682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13934682" style="width: 457px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13934682-2302210415541487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13934682" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13934682-2302210415541487-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="457" b="686" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13934682" class="wp-caption-text">车银优日前为韩国某男装品牌拍摄画报。（LIBERCLASSY提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在公开的画报中，车银优从经典的西装款式到卫衣、夹克、半裤、开衫等色彩缤纷的休闲风格，展示了该品牌2023年S/S新装造型。此次公开的服装将在韩国该牌旗下卖场及官方购物中心销售。</p>
<p>该品牌相关人士表示，此次2023春秋画报，旨在通过品牌代言人车银优来表达品牌特色Liberal（自由）和Classy（高级、干练），“蕴含着希望因新冠肺炎而疲惫生活结束后，即将到来的的春天成为治愈和休息的时间的意思。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13934683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13934683" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13934683-2302210415471487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13934683" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13934683-2302210415471487-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="680" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13934683" class="wp-caption-text">车银优日前为韩国某男装品牌拍摄画报。（LIBERCLASSY提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，由金南佶、李多熙、车银优主演的韩剧《Island》第二部将于2月24日上线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13934684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13934684" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13934684-2302210415521487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13934684" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13934684-2302210415521487-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="680" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13934684" class="wp-caption-text">车银优日前为韩国某男装品牌拍摄画报。LIBERCLASSY提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13934685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13934685" style="width: 459px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13934685-2302210415451487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13934685" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13934685-2302210415451487-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="459" b="689" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13934685" class="wp-caption-text">车银优日前为韩国某男装品牌拍摄画报。（LIBERCLASSY提供）</figcaption></figure>
