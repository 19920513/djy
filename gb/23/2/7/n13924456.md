<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加拿大100家最浪漫餐厅 卑诗省20家餐厅入选</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月07日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">上官石溪</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加拿大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%83%85%E4%BA%BA%E8%8A%82.md#1">情人节</a>马上要到了，如果你正在为心爱的人寻找一个完美的地方来提供美酒佳肴，那么你很幸运。因为订餐小程序</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">OpenTable</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">刚刚发布了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2023</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年加拿大</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">100</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">家最浪漫的餐厅名单。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">它是通过</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">“分析</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">OpenTable</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">食客的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">100</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">多万条评论”，同时权衡食物、环境、服务等因素，最终列出百强名单。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">该名单包括<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">家餐厅，其中</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">家在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>，分别是</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Botanist</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hy&#8217;s Steakhouse Vancouver</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kisa Tanto</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">LIFT Bar Grill View</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Lupo Restaurant &amp; Vinoteca</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；以及</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Osteria Savio Volpe</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">三家在大温地区，包括位于兰</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">里</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cascades</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">赌场的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">ATLAS Steak + Fish</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Haven Kitchen + Bar</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，以及位于本拿比的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hart House</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">餐厅。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">OpenTable</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的数据，维多利亚也是另一个浪漫餐厅的热点，在名单上有六家餐厅上榜，包括：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Finn&#8217;s Seafood</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chops</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hart House</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Finn&#8217;s Seafood, Chops and Cocktails; Glo Restaurant + Lounge; Vista 18 Restaurant + Lounge; Zambri&#8217;s; </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Tea at The Empress.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">其余上榜的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>的餐厅还包括：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Oliver</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">市的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Miradoro at Tinhorn Creek Winery; Malahat</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">市的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Alpina Restaurant at Villa Eyrie Resort in; Lake Country </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">镇的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Block One Restaurant at 50th Parallel Winery; Brentwood Bay</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Butchart Gardens The Dining Room; </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">基隆拿市的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Home Block Restaurant at CedarCreek Estate Winery; </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">以及惠斯勒的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Joe Fortes Whistler.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">该网站还围绕即将到来的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%83%85%E4%BA%BA%E8%8A%82.md#1">情人节</a>晚餐发布了一些统计数据和建议，包括建议至少提前一</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">周</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">预订，以确保在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日当天订到。另外，还可以考虑在</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">“非高峰时段</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">”就餐，以便在最热门餐厅能有更好的机会预订到一张桌子。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">OpenTable</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">还在其最近的报告中谈到了就餐预算问题，它指出</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">72%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的受访者计划在情人节用餐时每人花费超过</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">41</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，大多数受访者说，分担</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">帐</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">单是</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">“完全可以接受的”。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">如果你想平均每人花费稍低于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">41</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">&#8211;</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，比如两位食客每人</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">39.99</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，宜家（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">IKEA</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）将在其宜家瑞典餐厅举办有三道菜的情人节大餐（包括著名的瑞典肉丸作为主菜的选择），包括北欧水果汽水和无酒精鸡尾酒，酒精饮料则需要额外付费。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span></p>
